1Call out if you please. Will anyone answer you?

2For anger kills a fool,

3I have seen a fool taking root,
but I immediately pronounced a curse on his home.

8However, if I were you, I would appeal to God
and would present my case to Him. # Jb 13:18; 23:4; Lm 3:59
9He does great # Jb 9:10; 37:5; Ps 71:19 and unsearchable things,
wonders without number. # Jb 42:5; Ps 40:5; 72:18
10He gives rain to the earth
and sends water to the fields. # Jb 36:27-28; 38:26-28; Jr 14:22
11He sets the lowly on high,
and mourners are lifted to safety. # 1Sm 2:7; Ps 113:6-7; Mt 5:4
12He frustrates the schemes of the crafty

13He traps the wise in their craftiness

14They encounter darkness by day,
and they grope at noon

15He saves the needy from their sharp words # Lit from the sword of their mouth ; Ps 55:21; 59:7 # Ps 55:21; 59:7

16So the poor have hope,
and injustice shuts its mouth. # Ps 63:11; 107:42; Rm 3:19
17See how happy the man is God corrects;

18For He crushes but also binds up;
He strikes, but His hands also heal. # Dt 32:39; 1Sm 2:6; Hs 6:1
19He will rescue you from six calamities;
no harm will touch you in seven.
20In famine He will redeem you from death,
and in battle, from the power of the sword. # Ps 33:19; 37:19; 144:10
21You will be safe from slander # Lit be hidden from the whip of the tongue

26You will approach the grave # Jb 3:22; 10:19; 17:1 in full vigor, # Gn 15:15; 25:8; Jb 21:23
