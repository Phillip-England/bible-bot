A Call to Good Works
11Dear friends, I urge you as strangers and temporary residents # 1Pt 1:1 to abstain # 1Tm 4:3 from fleshly # Rm 7:23; Php 3:3 desires that war against you. # Or against the soul # Gl 5:17; Jms 4:1 12Conduct # 2Pt 3:11 yourselves honorably among the Gentiles, # Or among the nations, or among the pagans so that in a case where they speak against you as those who do what is evil, they will, by observing your good works, glorify God on the day of visitation. # The day when God intervenes in human history, either in grace or in judgment # Is 10:3; Jr 6:15; 8:12; 10:15; Lk 1:68; 7:16; 19:44
13Submit # Rm 13:1-7; Ti 3:1 to every human authority # Or creature because of the Lord, whether to the Emperor # Lit king as the supreme authority # Rm 13:1 14or to governors as those sent out by him to punish those who do what is evil and to praise those who do what is good. 15For it is God’s will # Gl 1:4; Eph 1:9 that you silence the ignorance # 1Pt 3:16 of foolish people by doing good. 16As God’s slaves, # Rm 1:1; Ti 1:1; Rv 2:20 live as free people, but don’t use your freedom as a way to conceal evil. # 1Co 14:20 17Honor everyone.
