2 Kings 13

13
Israel’s King Jehoahaz
1In the twenty-third year of Judah’s King Joash # 2Kg 12:1 son of Ahaziah, Jehoahaz # 2Kg 10:35 son of Jehu became king over Israel in Samaria and reigned 17 years. 2He did what was evil in the Lord’s sight and followed the sins that Jeroboam son of Nebat had caused Israel to commit; he did not turn away from them. # 1Kg 12:26-33 3So the Lord’s anger burned against Israel, # Dt 31:17; Jdg 2:14 and He surrendered them to the power of Hazael # 2Kg 8:12; 12:17 king of Aram and his son Ben-hadad # 2Kg 13:24-25 during their reigns.
4Then Jehoahaz sought the Lord’s favor, and the Lord heard him, for He saw the oppression the king of Aram inflicted on Israel. # Ex 3:7-9; Nm 21:7-9; 2Kg 14:26 5Therefore, the Lord gave Israel a deliverer, # Jdg 2:18; 2Kg 13:25; 14:25,27 and they escaped from the power of the Arameans. Then the people of Israel dwelt in their tents as before, 6but they didn’t turn away from the sins that the house of Jeroboam had caused Israel to commit. Jehoahaz walked in them, # 2Kg 13:2 and the Asherah pole also remained standing in Samaria. 7Jehoahaz did not have an army left, except for 50 horsemen, 10 chariots, and 10,000 foot soldiers, because the king of Aram had destroyed them, # 2Kg 10:32-33 making them like dust at threshing. # Am 1:3
8The rest of the events of Jehoahaz’s reign, along with all his accomplishments and his might, are written in the Historical Record of Israel’s Kings. # 2Kg 1:17-18; 10:34-36 9Jehoahaz rested with his fathers, and he was buried in Samaria. His son Jehoash # Lit Joash became king in his place.
Israel’s King Jehoash
10In the thirty-seventh year of Judah’s King Joash, Jehoash son of Jehoahaz became king over Israel in Samaria and reigned 16 years. 11He did what was evil in the Lord’s sight. He did not turn away from all the sins that Jeroboam son of Nebat had caused Israel to commit, but he walked in them. # 1Kg 12:28-33
12The rest of the events of Jehoash’s reign, along with all his accomplishments and the power he had to wage war against Judah’s King Amaziah, # 2Kg 14:15-16 are written in the Historical Record of Israel’s Kings. 13Jehoash rested with his fathers, and Jeroboam # 2Kg 14:23 sat on his throne. Jehoash was buried in Samaria with the kings of Israel.
Elisha’s Death
14When Elisha became sick with the illness that he died from, Jehoash king of Israel went down and wept over him and said, “My father, my father, the chariots and horsemen of Israel! ” # 2Kg 2:12
15Elisha responded, “Take a bow and arrows.” So he got a bow and arrows. # 1Sm 20:20 16Then Elisha said to the king of Israel, “Put your hand on the bow.” So the king put his hand on it, and Elisha put his hands on the king’s hands. 17Elisha said, “Open the east window.” So he opened it. Elisha said, “Shoot! ” So he shot. # Jos 8:18 Then Elisha said, “The Lord’s arrow of victory, yes, the arrow of victory over Aram. You are to strike down the Arameans in Aphek # 1Kg 20:26 until you have put an end to them.”
18Then Elisha said, “Take the arrows! ” So he took them. Then Elisha said to the king of Israel, “Strike the ground! ” So he struck the ground three times and stopped. 19The man of God # 2Kg 5:20 was angry with him and said, “You should have struck the ground five or six times. Then you would have struck down Aram until you had put an end to them, but now you will only strike down Aram three times.” 20Then Elisha died and was buried. # 2Kg 2:1,11
Now Moabite raiders # 2Kg 3:7; 24:2 used to come into the land in the spring of the year. 21Once, as the Israelites were burying a man, suddenly they saw a raiding party, so they threw the man into Elisha’s tomb. When he touched Elisha’s bones, the man revived and stood up! # Mt 27:52
God’s Mercy on Israel
22Hazael # 1Kg 19:17; 2Kg 8:12-13 king of Aram oppressed Israel throughout the reign of Jehoahaz, 23but the Lord was gracious to them, had compassion on them, # 1Kg 8:28 and turned toward them because of His covenant with Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. # Ex 2:24 He was not willing to destroy them. Even now He has not banished them from His presence. # 2Kg 14:27
24King Hazael of Aram died, and his son Ben-hadad # 2Kg 13:3 became king in his place. 25Then Jehoash son of Jehoahaz took back from Ben-hadad son of Hazael the cities that Hazael had taken in war from Jehoash’s father Jehoahaz. Jehoash defeated Ben-hadad three times and recovered the cities of Israel. # 2Kg 10:32
