14The Lord said to Moses, “The time of your death is now approaching. Call Joshua and present yourselves at the tent of meeting so that I may commission him.” When Moses and Joshua went and presented themselves at the tent of meeting, 15the Lord appeared at the tent in a pillar of cloud, # Ex 13:21-22; 33:9-10; Ps 99:7 and the cloud stood at the entrance to the tent. # Nm 12:5

They will abandon Me # Dt 28:20; Jg 10:19; 1Sm 8:8; 1Kg 11:33; 2Kg 22:17; Jr 1:16; 2:13; 5:7,19; 16:11; 19:4 and break the covenant # Dt 4:13,23; 5:2-3; 17:2; 29:1,9-14,21,25 I have made with them. 17My anger will burn against them on that day; # Nm 32:10; Is 13:9,13; Lm 1:12 I will abandon them and hide My face from them so that they will become easy prey. # Lit will be for devouring # Ps 63:10; Jr 2:14; Ezk 34:8 Many troubles and afflictions will come to them. On that day they will say, ‘Haven’t these troubles come to us because our God is no longer with us? ’ 18I will certainly hide My face on that day because of all the evil they have done by turning to other gods. 19Therefore write down this song # Dt 32:1-43 for yourselves and teach it to the Israelites; have them recite it, # Lit Israelites; put it in their mouths so that this song may be a witness for Me against the Israelites. 20When I bring them into the land I swore to give their fathers, a land flowing with milk and honey, # Dt 11:9 they will eat their fill # Dt 11:15 and prosper. # Lit be fat They will turn to other gods and worship them, despising Me # ** and breaking My covenant. # Lv 26:15,44; Dt 4:31; Jos 23:16 21And when many troubles and afflictions come to them, this song will testify against them, because # Lit because the mouths of their descendants will not have forgotten it. 23The Lord commissioned Joshua son of Nun, “Be strong and courageous, for you will bring the Israelites into the land I swore to them, and I will be with you.” # Jos 1:1-9
