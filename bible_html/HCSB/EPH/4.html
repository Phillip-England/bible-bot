Living the New Life
17Therefore, I say this and testify in the Lord: You should no longer walk as the Gentiles walk, in the futility of their thoughts. 18They are darkened in their understanding, excluded from the life of God, because of the ignorance that is in them and because of the hardness of their hearts. 19They became callous and gave themselves over to promiscuity for the practice of every kind of impurity # 1Th 4:17 with a desire for more and more. # Lit with greediness
20But that is not how you learned about the Messiah, 21assuming you heard about Him and were taught by Him, because the truth # 2Th 2:10 is in Jesus. 22You took off # Or Jesus. This means: take off (as a command) # Rm 13:12 your former way of life, # 2Pt 3:11 the old self # Lit man ; = a person before conversion # Rm 6:6; 7:6; 1Co 5:7-8; 2Co 3:14; Col 3:9 that is corrupted by deceitful desires; 23you are being renewed # Or desires; renew (as a command) in the spirit # Ps 51:12 of your minds; 24you put on # Or minds; and put on (as a command) # Rm 13:12 the new self, the one created # Mk 13:19; Jn 1:3; Rv 3:14 according to God’s likeness # Col 3:10; 2Pt 1:4 in righteousness and purity of the truth.
25Since you put away # Pr 31:25 lying, Speak the truth, each one to his neighbor, # Zch 8:16 # Zch 8:16 because we are members of one another. 26Be angry and do not sin. # Ps 4:4; 37:8; Mt 18:21; Jms 1:19-20 # Ps 4:4 Don’t let the sun go down on your anger, 27and don’t give the Devil an opportunity. 28The thief must no longer steal. Instead, he must do honest work with his own hands, so that he has something to share # 1Th 2:8; 1Tm 6:18 with anyone in need. 29No foul language is to come from your mouth, but only what is good for building up someone in need, # Lit for the building up of the need so that it gives grace to those who hear. 30And don’t grieve God’s Holy Spirit. # Ps 51:11; Jn 1:33; Ac 2:4; Rm 8:9; Gl 5:25; Ti 3:5; Rv 3:22 You were sealed by Him # Or Spirit, by whom you were sealed for the day of redemption. # Php 1:6 31All bitterness, anger and wrath, shouting and slander must be removed from you, along with all malice. 32And be kind # Mt 11:30; Lk 5:39; 6:35; Rm 2:4; 1Co 15:33; 1Pt 2:3 and compassionate # 1Pt 3:8 to one another, forgiving # Ps 25:11; Mt 9:2; Mk 2:5; Lk 24:47; Col 3:12-13 one another, just as God also forgave you # Other mss read us in Christ.
