Psalms 18

18
Praise for Deliverance
For the choir director. Of the servant of the Lord, David, who spoke the words of this song to the Lord on the day the Lord rescued him from the hand of all his enemies and from the hand of Saul. # 2Sm 22 He said:
1I love You, Lord, my strength.
2The Lord is my rock,
my fortress, and my deliverer, # Ps 62:7; 94:22
my God, my mountain where I seek refuge,
my shield # Gn 15:1; Dt 33:29; Ps 3:3; 5:12; 28:7; 119:114 and the horn of my salvation, # 1Sm 2:1,10; Lk 1:69
my stronghold.
3I called to the Lord, who is worthy of praise, # Ps 48:1; 96:4; 145:3
and I was saved from my enemies. # Ps 138:7
4The ropes of death were wrapped around me;
the torrents of destruction terrified me. # Ps 124:4
5The ropes of Sheol entangled me;
the snares of death confronted me. # Ps 116:3
6I called to the Lord in my distress,
and I cried to my God for help.
From His temple He heard my voice, # Ps 3:4
and my cry to Him reached His ears. # Ps 34:15
7Then the earth shook and quaked;
the foundations of the mountains trembled; # Is 13:13; Hg 2:6
they shook because He burned with anger.
8Smoke rose from His nostrils,
and consuming fire came from His mouth;
coals were set ablaze by it. # Or ablaze from Him # Ps 21:9; 50:3
9He parted the heavens and came down, # Ps 144:5; Is 64:1
a dark cloud beneath His feet.
10He rode on a cherub and flew,
soaring on the wings of the wind. # Ps 104:3
11He made darkness His hiding place,
dark storm clouds His canopy around Him. # Ps 97:2
12From the radiance of His presence, # Ps 50:2; Is 60:3
His clouds swept onward with hail and blazing coals.
13The Lord thundered from # Some Hb mss, LXX, Tg, Jer; other Hb mss read in heaven;
the Most High projected His voice. # Some Hb mss read voice, with hail and fiery coals # Ps 29:3; 104:7
14He shot His arrows and scattered them;
He hurled # Or multiplied lightning bolts and routed them. # Ps 144:6
15The depths of the sea became visible,
the foundations of the world were exposed,
at Your rebuke, Lord,
at the blast of the breath of Your nostrils. # Ex 15:8; Ps 106:9; Nah 1:4
16He reached down from heaven
and took hold of me;
He pulled me out of deep waters. # Ps 144:7
17He rescued me from my powerful enemy
and from those who hated me,
for they were too strong for me. # Ps 35:10; 59:1; 142:6
18They confronted me in the day of my distress,
but the Lord was my support. # Ps 55:22; 59:16
19He brought me out to a spacious place; # Ps 31:8; 118:5
He rescued me because He delighted in me. # Ps 41:11
20The Lord rewarded me
according to my righteousness; # Ps 7:8
He repaid me
according to the cleanness of my hands. # Ps 24:4
21For I have kept the ways of the Lord
and have not turned from my God to wickedness. # Jb 23:11; Ps 119:101
22Indeed, I have kept all His ordinances in mind # Lit Indeed, all His ordinances have been in front of me
and have not disregarded His statutes. # Lv 18:4-5
23I was blameless toward Him # Dt 18:13; Ps 37:18; 119:1
and kept myself from sinning.
24So the Lord repaid me
according to my righteousness, # 2Ch 6:23
according to the cleanness of my hands in His sight. # Ps 24:4
25With the faithful
You prove Yourself faithful;
with the blameless man
You prove Yourself blameless;
26with the pure
You prove Yourself pure,
but with the crooked
You prove Yourself shrewd. # Mt 16:27; Rv 22:12
27For You rescue an afflicted people, # Ps 72:12
but You humble those with haughty eyes. # Ps 138:6
28Lord, You light my lamp;
my God illuminates my darkness. # Ps 112:4; Is 42:16
29With You I can attack a barrier, # Or ridge
and with my God I can leap over a wall. # Mt 19:26; Php 4:13
30God — His way is perfect; # Dt 32:4
the word of the Lord is pure. # Ps 19:7
He is a shield to all who take refuge in Him. # Ps 91:4; 144:2
31For who is God besides Yahweh? # Dt 32:39; 1Sm 2:2; Ps 86:8
And who is a rock? Only our God. # Ps 62:2
32God — He clothes me with strength # Is 45:5
and makes my way perfect.
33He makes my feet like the feet of a deer # Hab 3:19
and sets me securely on the heights. # Or on my high places # Dt 32:13; Is 58:14
34He trains my hands for war; # Ps 144:1
my arms can bend a bow of bronze. # Jb 29:20
35You have given me the shield of Your salvation;
Your right hand upholds me, # Ps 89:21
and Your humility exalts me.
36You widen a place beneath me for my steps, # Ps 31:8; 118:5
and my ankles do not give way. # Ps 66:9; Pr 4:12
37I pursue my enemies and overtake them;
I do not turn back until they are wiped out. # Ps 44:5
38I crush them, and they cannot get up;
they fall beneath my feet. # Ps 36:12
39You have clothed me with strength for battle; # 1Sm 2:4
You subdue my adversaries beneath me. # 1Ch 17:10; Ps 81:14
40You have made my enemies retreat before me; # Or You gave me the necks of my enemies # Ex 23:27
I annihilate those who hate me. # Dt 7:10
41They cry for help, but there is no one to save them # Ps 50:22 —
they cry to the Lord, but He does not answer them. # Mc 3:4
42I pulverize them like dust before the wind; # 2Kg 13:7
I trample them # Some Hb mss, LXX, Syr, Tg; other Hb mss read I poured them out like mud in the streets. # Is 10:6; Mc 7:10
43You have freed me from the feuds among the people;
You have appointed me the head of nations; # 2Sm 8:1-14
a people I had not known serve me. # Is 55:5
44Foreigners submit to me grudgingly;
as soon as they hear, # Lit At the hearing of the ear they obey me. # Dt 33:29; Ps 66:3
45Foreigners lose heart
and come trembling from their fortifications. # Mc 7:17
46The Lord lives — may my rock be praised!
The God of my salvation is exalted. # Ps 62:2,6
47God — He gives me vengeance # 2Sm 4:8; Ps 94:1
and subdues peoples under me. # Ps 47:3; 144:2
48He frees me from my enemies.
You exalt me above my adversaries;
You rescue me from violent men. # Ps 59:1; 140:1
49Therefore I will praise You, Yahweh, among the nations;
I will sing about Your name. # Rm 15:9
50He gives great victories to His king; # Ps 144:10
He shows loyalty to His anointed,
to David and his descendants forever. # 2Sm 7:12-13; Ps 89:4,29
