13“If I have rejected the cause of my manservant or my maidservant,
when they brought a complaint against me,

When he #Ps. 17:3makes inquiry, what shall I answer him?
15Did #ch. 34:19; Prov. 14:31; 22:2; [Eph. 6:9]not he who made me in the womb make him?

16“If I have #[ch. 22:7] withheld anything that the poor desired,
or have #ch. 11:20; 17:5caused the eyes of the widow to fail,

19if I have seen anyone #ch. 29:13 perish for #ch. 24:7, 10lack of clothing,

22then let my shoulder blade fall from my shoulder,

23For I was #ch. 13:11in terror of calamity from God,
and I could not have faced his #ch. 13:11majesty.

29“If I have #Prov. 17:5rejoiced at the ruin of him who hated me,

31if the men of my tent have not said,
‘Who is there that has not been filled with his #Ex. 16:12; 1 Sam. 25:11meat?’
32(#[Gen. 19:2, 3; Judg. 19:20, 21; Matt. 25:35; Heb. 13:2]the sojourner has not lodged in the street;

34because I stood in great fear of #[Ex. 23:2]the multitude,
