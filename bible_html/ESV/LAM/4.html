Lamentations 4

4
The Holy Stones Lie Scattered
1 # [Isa. 1:22; Jer. 6:30] How the gold has grown dim,
how the pure gold is changed!
The holy stones lie scattered
# ch. 2:19 at the head of every street.
2The precious sons of Zion,
worth their weight in #Ps. 19:10 fine gold,
how they are regarded as #See Jer. 19:11earthen pots,
the work of a potter’s hands!
3Even jackals offer the breast;
they nurse their young;
but the daughter of my people has become cruel,
like the ostriches in the wilderness.
4The tongue of the nursing infant #Ps. 22:15 sticks
to the roof of its mouth for thirst;
# [ch. 2:11] the children beg for food,
but no one gives to them.
5Those who once feasted on delicacies
perish in the streets;
# [2 Sam. 1:24] those who were brought up in purple
embrace ash heaps.
6 # Matt. 10:15; Luke 10:12 For the chastisement#4:6 Or iniquity of the daughter of my people has been greater
than the punishment#4:6 Or sin of Sodom,
# Gen. 19:25; 2 Pet. 2:6; Jude 7 which was overthrown in a moment,
and no hands were wrung for her.#4:6 The meaning of the Hebrew is uncertain
7Her princes were purer than snow,
whiter than milk;
their bodies were more ruddy than coral,
the beauty of their form#4:7 The meaning of the Hebrew is uncertain was like sapphire.#4:7 Hebrew lapis lazuli
8 # ch. 5:10; Job 30:30; [Ps. 119:83] Now their face is blacker than soot;
they are not recognized in the streets;
their skin has shriveled on their bones;
it has become as dry as wood.
9Happier were the victims of the sword
than the victims of hunger,
who wasted away, pierced
by lack of the fruits of the field.
10 # See Jer. 19:9 The hands of #[1 Kgs. 3:26; Isa. 49:15] compassionate women
# [2 Kgs. 6:29] have boiled their own children;
# Deut. 28:57 they became their food
during the destruction of the daughter of my people.
11 # Ezek. 5:13 The Lord gave full vent to his wrath;
he poured out his hot anger,
and #See Jer. 17:27he kindled a fire in Zion
that consumed its foundations.
12 # [Isa. 52:15; 53:1] The kings of the earth did not believe,
nor any of the inhabitants of the world,
that foe or enemy could enter
the gates of Jerusalem.
13This was for #[ch. 2:20]; See Jer. 5:31; 23:21the sins of her prophets
and #[ch. 2:20]; See Jer. 5:31; 23:21the iniquities of her priests,
who shed in the midst of her
the blood of the righteous.
14 # [Isa. 59:10] They wandered, blind, through the streets;
they were so defiled with blood
# [Num. 19:16] that no one was able to touch
their garments.
15“Away! #[Lev. 13:45]Unclean!” people cried at them.
“Away! Away! Do not touch!”
So they became fugitives and wanderers;
people said among the nations,
“They shall stay with us no longer.”
16 # ch. 2:17 The Lord himself#4:16 Hebrew The face of the Lord has scattered them;
he will regard them no more;
# [Isa. 24:2] no honor was shown to the priests,
# ch. 5:12 no favor to the elders.
17 # Ps. 119:82, 123; [Jer. 3:23] Our eyes failed, ever watching
# Ps. 119:82, 123; [Jer. 3:23] vainly for help;
in our watching we watched
for #Jer. 37:7, 8a nation which could not save.
18 # ch. 3:52 They dogged our steps
so that we could not walk in our streets;
# Ezek. 7:2, 3, 6; Amos 8:2 our end drew near; our days were numbered,
for our end had come.
19Our pursuers were #Jer. 4:13; [2 Sam. 1:23; Hab. 1:8]swifter
than the eagles in the heavens;
they chased us on the mountains;
they lay in wait for us in the wilderness.
20 # [Gen. 2:7] The breath of our nostrils, #[ch. 2:9; 2 Kgs. 25:5, 6] the Lord’s anointed,
was captured #[Ezek. 12:13; 17:20; 19:4, 8] in their pits,
of whom we said, #[Judg. 9:15; Ezek. 31:6, 17]“Under his shadow
we shall live among the nations.”
21 # [ch. 1:21] Rejoice and be glad, O daughter of Edom,
you who dwell in #Job 1:1; Jer. 25:20 the land of Uz;
but to you also #See Jer. 25:15, 16the cup shall pass;
you shall become drunk and strip yourself bare.
22 # [Isa. 40:2] The punishment of your iniquity, O daughter of Zion, is accomplished;
he will keep you in exile no longer;#4:22 Or he will not exile you again
but #Obad. 10your iniquity, O daughter of Edom, he will punish;
he will uncover your sins.
