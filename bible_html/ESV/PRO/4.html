A Father’s Wise Instruction

2for I give you good #Job 11:4precepts;

3When I was a son with my father,

4he #1 Chr. 28:9; [Eph. 6:4] taught me and said to me,
# [ch. 3:1] “Let your heart hold fast my words;
# ch. 7:2; Lev. 18:5; Isa. 55:3 keep my commandments, and live.
5 # ch. 2:2 Get wisdom; get #[See ver. 1 above]insight;

6Do not forsake her, and she will keep you;
# [2 Thess. 2:10] love her, and she will guard you.

11I have #See 1 Sam. 12:23taught you the way of wisdom;
I have led you in the paths of uprightness.
12When you walk, #[Job 18:7; Ps. 18:36; 119:45] your step will not be hampered,
and #ch. 3:23if you run, you will not stumble.
13 # ch. 3:18 Keep hold of instruction; do not let go;
guard her, for she is your #ver. 22; [John 1:4; 1 John 5:12]life.
14 # ch. 1:15; Ps. 1:1 Do not enter the path of the wicked,
and do not walk in the way of the evil.
15Avoid it; do not go on it;
turn away from it and pass on.
16For they #[Ps. 36:4]cannot sleep unless they have done wrong;
they are robbed of sleep unless they have made someone stumble.

20 # ver. 10 My son, be attentive to my words;
incline your ear to my sayings.
21 # ch. 3:21 Let them not escape from your sight;
# [ch. 2:1] keep them within your heart.
22For they are #ver. 13; ch. 8:35; 21:21; Deut. 32:47; [1 Tim. 4:8]life to those who find them,
