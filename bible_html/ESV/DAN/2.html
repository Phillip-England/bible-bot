Daniel Interprets the Dream
31“You saw, O king, and behold, a great image. This image, mighty and of exceeding brightness, stood before you, and its appearance was frightening. 32#[ver. 38] The head of this image was of fine gold, #[ver. 39]its chest and arms of silver, its middle and #[ver. 39]thighs of bronze, 33#[ver. 40]its legs of iron, its feet partly of iron and partly of clay. 34As you looked, a stone was cut out #[ch. 8:25; Job 34:20; Lam. 4:6; 2 Cor. 5:1] by no human hand, and it struck the image on its feet of iron and clay, and #ver. 35, 40, 44, 45; Isa. 8:9; [Matt. 21:44; Luke 20:18]broke them in pieces. 35Then the iron, the clay, the bronze, the silver, and the gold, all together were broken in pieces, and became #Ps. 1:4 like the chaff of the summer threshing floors; and the wind carried them away, so that #[Rev. 20:11] not a trace of them could be found. But the stone that struck the image became #[Isa. 2:2] a great mountain #[Ps. 80:9]and filled the whole earth.

Daniel Is Promoted
