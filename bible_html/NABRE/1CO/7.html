1 Ikorinton 7

7
1Konye nuka akiro nuipotu kiwadikakis eoŋ. Ejok kama kekiliokit kinyek atiror aberu. 2Konye kanuka adoteu, kojau ŋolikiliokit kakeberu, ŋiniberu da kokilenike. 3Kotacak ŋolikiliokit akeberu nuibusakinito ŋes, kwape kwaŋin da kotacak ŋiniberu nuibusakinito okilenike. 4Mam aberu alopet eyait akekuan, dimarai okilenike; kwape kwaŋin da mam ekiliokit elopet eyait akekuan, dimarai akeberu. 5Sirimikanaros bonik, alemar diat icamanaros adaun apak, tetere iluŋakinos ailip; kosodete konyet aimorikikin bobo, kinyek Satan acodun yes kanuka amamus ayaikina. 6Enera ŋun kacamun, kwabo mere kaicorakinet. 7#Mat 19:12. Konye emiela ituŋa kere araut kwape kaeoŋ elopet. Konye ejai ŋinidiopet ainakineteke naebuni kama ejai Edeke, idiopet kopone ece, idiopet da kopone ece.
8Konye alimokini lumam edukokina ka apuserun da, ebe ejok kamakec arai esalito kwape kaeoŋ. 9#1 Tim 5:14. Konye arai mam epedorete ayaikina bonik, kodukokinos. Naarai ejok adukokin itia ainok.
10 # Mat 5:32; 19:9; Mk 10:11-12; Luk 16:18. Ecorakini luedukokina, kwabo mere eoŋ, dimarai Ejakait, ebe, kinyek aberu aisac okilenike. 11Konye arai esaci, kosal mam edukokina, arai bo kopuco kokilenike. Bobo da kinyek ekiliokit aireŋear akeberu.
12Alimokini ice, eoŋ mere Ejakait, ebe, arai ejaas onace kaberu namam iyuunit, ecamuni ŋin da aiboienen kaŋes, kinyek aireŋear ŋin. 13Arai da ejaas aberu kekiliokit lomam iyuunit, ŋes da ecamuni aiboienen kaŋin, kinyek anyoun kama kokilenike. 14#Rom 11:16. Naarai italaunitai ekiliokit lomam iyuunit kanuka akeberu, aberu da namam iyuunit italaunitai kanuka okilenike. Arai mere kwaŋin tieraas ikusidwe lumam elaete, konye kwana eraas lukalaunak 15#Rom 14:19. Konye arai enyouni lomam iyuunit, konyou do ŋesi; kakiro nuikote nen mam onac arai kinac eenitai. Naarai abu Edeke konyarau oni toma ainapakin. 16#1 Pet 3:1. Ijo aberu, ijenuni biai, wolo bo itajari okilenikon? Ijo da ekiliokit, ijenuni biai, wolo bo itajari akoniberu?
17 # 1 Kor 7:20,24. Konye na bon, kolosenenei ŋinidiopet kwape kekorakinitor Ejakait, ŋinidiopet da kwape kenyaraunitor Edeke. Ecoranakini kwaŋin kotoma okanisan kere. 18Kenyaraunitai kereka etuŋanan ekepitai? Kinyek araun lomam ekepitai. Kenyaraunitai kereka etuŋanan lomam ekepitai? Kinyek akepio. 19#Gal 5:6; 6:15; Rom 2:25. Naarai akepio mam erai ibore, amamus akepio da mam erai ibore, dimarai aricit aicorakineta nuka Edeke. 20Kosalak ŋolituŋanan kanyaraunio nakenyaraunere. 21Irai kereka ebulesit nakinyaraunere? Siripodo ŋun, Konye arai ipedori araun loeosikinitai, kitwasama gwai kwaŋin. 22#Pes 6:6; Plm 1:16. Naarai lokenyaraunio erai ebulesit, erai loeosikinitai loka Ejakait; kwape kwaŋin da lokenyaraunio erai loeosikinitai erai ebulesit loka Kristo. 23Aponi kogweli yes kakais nuipu; sirikiraut ibulesin luka ituŋa. 24Aso iinacan, kosal ŋinidiopet nepepe ka Edeke kwape kenyaraunere,
25 # 1 Tim 1:12,13. Nuka abekon emamei eoŋ aicorakinet naka Ejakait, konye kapesikis yes akiro kwape etuŋanan lomunot kanuka aitim naka Ejakait. 26Aomit eoŋ ebe kanuka ican luka tinenikwana ejok arai kesal ituŋanan kwape ejaar. 27Kirotakinitai kereka jo kaberu? Sirimoot alacakinio, Emamei kereka jo aberu? Sirimoot aberu. 28#Luk 21:23. Konye arai imanyi, mam idiakit, arai da edukokin apese, mam ŋin ediakit. Konye luikote nen eanyunete ican luka akuan, ido akoto aiyar yes ŋul. 29#Rom 13:11; Luk 14:26. Enera na, iinacan, ebe eŋoŋar apak; kanuka ŋun koraut luejaas kaŋor kwape kaluemameete, 30luemonyete da kwape kalumam emonyete, ka luiyalamasi kwape kalumam iyalamasi, luegwelete da kwape kaluemameete iboro, 31#1 Yok 2:15-17. luitwasamaete akwap koraut kwape kalumam itwasamaete, Naarai etubor epone loka akwapin kanu.
32Akoto yes koraut lumam iyaloŋoŋoete. Lomam emanyi iyaloŋoŋoi akiro nuka Ejakait, kwape epedoria aitelel Ejakait; 33#Luk 14:20; Pes 5:29. konye loamanyit iyaloŋoŋoi akiro nuka akwapin, kwape epedoria aitelel akeberu, 34ido ejai atiaket. Aberu namam edukokinitai, arai bo apese, iyaloŋoŋoi akiro nuka Ejakait, tetere eraun nakalaunan toma akuanake ka omoyoke da. Kwabo naedukokina iyaloŋoŋoi akiro nuka akwapin, kwape epedoria aitelel okilenike. 35Enera ŋun kodumut yes ajokis, mam akoto ailok yes koic, dimarai aitetemikin nuibusakinito, kijaanakinete Ejakait komamei yenitapaari aomisiokus.
36Arai eomit etuŋanan ebe mam ikonakinit ejok naisinerit, arai imiela noi, ido ibusakinit, aso kiswama ŋesi kwape ekotor, komany ŋes, mam ediaki. 37Konye loicasit kotauke, komamei ibore yenitinaikinit, loepedori ayait akoteke, itogogoŋit da kotauke aricit naisinerit, iswamai ejok. 38Kwaŋin loemanyi naisinerit, iswamai ejok; lomam emanyi da iswamai ejok aitelekarit.
39 # Rom 7:2. Irotakinio aberu kokilenike apak kere naejaria ŋol. Konye arai ketwana okilenike, eosikinitai adukokin loekot, konye toma Ejakait. 40Konye aomit eoŋ ebe iyalama aitelekarit arai esali kwaŋin, Ido aomit ebe ajai kEmoyo loka Ejakait.
