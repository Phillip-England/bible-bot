Isaia 37

37
1 # 2 Ika 19:1; 2 Siit 6:24; Sab 50:15; Sek 13:9; Luk 18:1; Rom 12:12; 1 Ses 5:17. Kosodi kanen, kepupu ekabaka Kesekia, kocil inapeneke, konap epuko, koloma togo loka EJAKAIT. 2#2 Ika 22:14. Kijuka Eliakim, loapolokinit ekal, ka Sebuna ekewadikan, ka imakio luka isasedotin, konapitos ipukoi, mama ejai Isaia enabi okoku loka Amos. 3#2 Ika 19:3; Kos 5:9; Sab 39:11; 50:15. Kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Inera Kesekia kwanata ebe, Apaaran na erai apaaran naka ican, naka aitoronio, ka naka amoromoroo; naarai adol idwe aurio, emamei da agogoŋ nakaidoun. 4#1 Sam 7:8; 2 Ika 19:4; Yak 5:14,18. Motoko iirari EJAKAIT Ekonideke akiro nuka Rabasake, loejukuni ekabaka loka Asuria ejakaitike amoromor Edeke lokajaran, ka aitoron akiro nuapupu EJAKAIT Ekonideke: aso kitonyou ailipikon kanuka aatoŋot naedeun.”
5Kopotu do ikeiaanakinak luka ekabaka Kesekia neejai Isaia. 6Kotema Isaia kes atiar, “Kolimokis ejakaitikus kwanata ebe, ‘Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, Sirikiyoŋ akiro nuipup, nukamoromorooto eoŋ ikejaanakinak luka ekabaka loka Asuria. 7Koany, ebwaikin emoyo tomake, epupuni ŋesi eemut, eboŋori bukee; ebirokini eoŋ da ŋes kekileŋ akwapuke.’’’
8 # Yos 15:39,42; 2 Siit 11:9; Nek 11:30; Yer 34:7. Koboŋo do Rabasake, kodum ekabaka loka Asuria ejiosi kaLibuna: naarai abu kopupunite ebe aboŋo ŋesi koLakis. 9Kopup inerai nuka Tiraka ekabaka loka Etiopia ebe, “Koany, alomu aitiji jo.” Kepupu ŋun, kiyakik ŋesi Kesekia ikiyakia ebe, 10“Ilimokinete Kesekia ekabaka loka Yuda kwanata ebe, ‘Kinyek Edekekon loimuno aiŋal jo ebe, Mam Yerusalem ijaikinio akan naka ekabaka loka Asuria. 11Koany, ipupunit ijo nueswamakis ikabakan luka Asuria akwapin kere, amudianarete kes cut: do konyet jo kiyuunio? 12#Age 11:31. Apotu kereka idekesio luka atekerin koyuununete kes, nuamudianarete lukapapaaka, Gosan, ka Karan, ka Resep, ka idwe luka Eden luajaas Telasar? 13#Yer 49:23. Ai bo ejai ekabaka loka Kamat, ka ekabaka loka Arupad, ka ekabaka loka ere loka Separabaim, loka Kena, ka Iba da?’”
14Kijau Kesekia ebalua kakan naka ikiyakia, kosiom; kolot Kesekia togo loka EJAKAIT, kopesia kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 15Kilip Kesekia EJAKAIT, kobala ebe, 16#Any 25:22.“Wu EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Edeke loka Isirael, loiboie kokerubin, ijo ŋes Edeke, ijo bon, loka ajakanuto kere nuka akwap; ijo kolo isubi akuj ka akwap. 17#Dan 9:18. Kitoik akonikit, wu EJAKAIT, kiira; kokweny, wu EJAKAIT, koany; kopup akiro kere nuka Senakerib, nuejuku ŋesi amoromoroo Edeke lokajaran. 18Abeit, wu EJAKAIT, apotu ikabakan luka Asuria komudiasi atekerin kere, ka akecekwap da. 19Apotu koeakasi idekesiokec akim: naarai mam keraas idekesio, konye aswam naka akanin nuka ituŋa, ikito ka amor; ŋes kemudiarere kes. 20Aso do, wu EJAKAIT Ekosideke, kitajarai sio kakanike, tetere ejenunete ajakanuto kere nuka akwap ebe, ljo ŋes EJAKAIT, ijo bon ba.”
21Kosodi Isaia okoku loka Amos aijukakin Kesekia ebe, “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, ebe, Naarai ilip ijo eoŋ asurokin Senakerib ekabaka loka Asuria, 22na ŋes akirot naener EJAKAIT mama ejai ŋesi ebe:
“Kimwaŋit akoku nabekot naka Sion jo,
Ekienar jo kisekelei:
Emuŋumuŋ akoku naka Yerusalem akouke neijai.
23Iŋai bo imoromor ijo, kisekel?
Iŋai bo itopolooritor ijo ekoniporoto, ka aiteluor akonikonye kuju?
Isurok Lokalaunan loka Isirael.
24Imoromor EJAKAIT kokiyakiakon,
Kinera atiar, ‘Kaipues naka agalinika
Adokari oswion luka iroŋan,
Atutubeta nukotoma nuka Lebanon;
Ajepi iresiake lueojak, ka iberosinike luejokuka;
Alomari da aiboisike naiwasie,
Amagoro naka emanikorike loideŋeriat.
25Abok, amat akipi,
Etooni da kakakeju icorin kere luko Misiri.’
26Mam ibu kopup kwape eswamaa kolo,
Kasub agear apeŋo?
Kwana da etodolit,
Korai ijo loimudiari ireria lukebwaitai
Araun aapoŋor nukamejei,
27Kanuka ŋun lueboiete kotoma keraas luedit agogoŋ,
Koburut, kitirirata;
Kosubito bala inya luka ais, kwape ikui,
Kwape kinya luejaas kuju togo,
Kwape emanikor loka akima eriŋa epolooro,
28Konye ajeni eoŋ akoniiboie,
Alomarikon ka alomunikon,
Aikerepoonutukon kaneajai.
29 # Yob 41:2; Sab 32:9; Isa 30:28; Esk 29:4; Amo 4:2; Yak 3:3. Kanuka aikerepoonutukon kaneajai,
Ido naarai adolu emokodekon akiika,
Ŋes ebwaikinia ekagolu okumesikon,
Arukanaka da aitukukon,
Kenyakari jo orot loilomunia.
30“Na ŋes eraun aanyunet mamakon: okaru lo inyamete yenebwaŋuni bon, okaru lokiareit yenitocuni kaii; okaru lokiwuniet koyeata, kodwenya, kiraata imanikoria luka emusabibu, konyama araitoke. 31Edupi aatoŋot naideuni naabu koku kokek loka Yuda alias kwap, kora araito kokuju. 32#2 Ika 19:31. Naarai elomari aatoŋot koYerusalem, luekuete da komoru Sion: itodolikini ŋun adunyanut naka EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
33“Aso inera kwanata EJAKAIT nuka ekabaka loka Asuria ebe, Mam edoluni ore lo, karaida agumakin emal, karaida adolokin kebuku, karaida aitutu etut asurokinia ŋes. 34Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Orot lokelomunia, ŋopeŋol eboŋoria, ido mam edoluni ore lo. 35#2 Ika 20:6; Isa 27:3. Naarai egaŋi eoŋ ere lo aitajar, kanukaŋ, ido kanuka ekakejaanakinan Daudi.”
36 # 2 Ika 19:35. Koloma emalaika loka EJAKAIT oidukurun luka Iyasurian, kodany ilukumin akwatat ka ilukumin akaisakanykauni ka lukany: kenyout ituŋa tupuruc sek, koany, koraas kere ikatwak. 37Kosodi Senakerib ekabaka loka Asuria anyoun, kolot, koboŋo, kiboikin Ninebe. 38Kosodi kanen, emai ŋesi kotogo loka Nisurok ekedeke, koarata Aduram-melek ka Saresar odweke ŋes kekileŋ: kokereasi akwap naka Ararat. Kirum Esar-kadon okokuke aiboisike.
