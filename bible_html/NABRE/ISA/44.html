Isaia 44

44
1 # Isa 41:8; Yer 30:10. “Konye kwana kiira, wu Yakob ekakejaanakinan;
Ka Isirael loaseunit.
2 # Ikis 32:15. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT lokisubi,
Lokiluŋuluŋi jo ameun kakoik, lokiŋarakini ebe;
Sirikikurianar, wu Yakob ekakejaanakinan;
Ka jo da, Yesurun, loaseunit.
3 # Mal 3:10; Yok 7:38. Naarai abukokini akipi ŋin yenejakit akure,
Aguuruto da akwap naconit:
Abukokini ekamoyo akoniwur,
Arereŋuka da odwekon:
4Elibunete kwape anya,
Kwape ikito kama eleleata akip.
5 # Any 13:9; Nek 9:38. Ebala ediopet ebe, ‘Arai eoŋ loka EJAKAIT’;
Ece da emaikini bon ekiror loka Yakob;
Ece da iwadikakini akanike ebe, ‘Loka EJAKAIT:
Emaikini bon ekiror loka Isirael.”
6 # Isa 48:12; 43:1; 41:4; Apu 1:8,17; 2:8; 22:13. Inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Ekabaka loka Isirael,
Ekalakunanike da, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio:
Ebe: Arai eoŋ losodit, arai lokawasia da;
Kwabo emamei ecedeke dimarai eoŋ.
7Iŋai bo eputos keoŋ? Kolimo do,
Kitejen kitemonok kaŋukan.
Iŋai bo elimorit apeŋo akiro nuepote?
Kitejenut ŋun nuejaunete rwaru,
8 # Ikis 4:35; 1 Sam 2:2; 2 Sam 22:32. Sirikiyoŋa, karaida akurianar:
Mam apeŋo kalimonokini jo ka aitodianakin?
Iraas yesi da ikakajenak.
Ejai Edeke ece dimarai eoŋ?
Eebo, emamei Atiiba; mam eoŋ ajeni adio da.”
9 # Isa 41:24; 42:17-20; Ikis 4:28; Sab 115:4. Ŋul luesubete aput naegerer eraas kere aamameak; emamei ameda kotoma oborokec luisiyalamikinete: ikajenakakec da mam eanyunete karaida ajenun; tetere irumete ileic. 10#Yer 10:5; Kbk 2:18. Iŋai bo asubi edeke, arai kecalari aput naetatao naemamei ameda? 11#Sab 97:7; Isa 42:17. Koany, irumete ileic ipaupake kere; ikasubak da eraas ituŋa: kotukonokinai kere nepepe, kobwaata; ekuriakaros, irumete kere ileic.
12 # Isa 40:19. Etatai ekatatan aep, iswamai toma atotol, iluŋuluŋi kanyulia, esubi kakekan nakagogoŋ: eebo, eriebi eteŋei, emameun agogoŋuke; mam emasi akipi, iilor. 13Itemi ekekeon ekokoro; igiri kekalamu; esonyi kesonyet, itemi kaitemisio, ituputoori kwape aput naka ituŋanan, kwape ejaar atemelel naka ituŋanan, aiboi kotogoke. 14Ejepi iresia, eyaŋaari da etekwa ka elua, itogogoŋi kanukeŋ ediopet kokito luka amagoro: eraikini eberos, itani akiru. 15Kosodi konyet araun akito nuka ituŋanan; elemuni ipieri; eebo, inoki, ipoio inyamat: eebo, esubunia edeke, emai ŋes; esubunia aput na’ gerer, ekukonokini kakonyeneke. 16Icwei ŋesi atutubet kakim; atutubet ŋipeŋin ŋes enyamia akiriŋ; epei nupeon, imonikin: eebo, ipieri, kobala ebe, “Bu, amwanu eoŋ, aany akim.” 17Nuesalito esubunia edeke, aputoke naegerer; ekukonokini ŋes emai da, ilipi ŋes, kobala ebe, “Koyuunai; naarai irai ijo ekadeke.”
18 # Sab 81:12; Isa 45:20; Rom 11:8-10; 2 Ses 2:11. Mam ejenete, karaida aomoom: naarai etumudak ŋesi akecekonye, tetere mam epedorete aanyun; ikecetauon da tetere mam epedorete ajenun. 19Emamei da yeniituni, emamei da aijen karaida amisiikin atemar ebe, “Ecweik eoŋ atutubet kakim; eebo, epoo atap katotoleke; ape akiriŋ, kanyam da; asubunia teni nuesalito ibore yeniketal? Akukonokini kereka apuŋure naka ekitoi?” 20#Kos 4:12; Rom 1:21; 2 Ses 2:11. Enyami ŋesi ekurwon: etapaa etau loeŋalatai tetere mam epedori ayuun eketau, karaida atemar atiar, “Mere ejai aiŋal akanika nakoteten?”
21Kiitu akiro nu, wu Yakob;
Ka Isirael da, naarai irai ijo ekakejaanakinan;
Eoŋ keluŋuluŋuni; irai ijo ekakejaanakinan;
Wu Isirael, mam eoŋ kemurwokini jo.
22 # Isa 1:18; 43:25; 53:11,12; Yer 33:8; Mik 7:18,19; Kol 1:14; Isa 43:1; 1 Kor 6:20; 1 Pet 1:18,19. Amwata adiakanaretakon kwape edou loejamijami, Aronisiokon da kwape elisina:
Koboŋu neajai; naarai kalaku jo.
23 # Sab 69:34; Isa 42:10; Apu 18:20. Koye, ijo akuj, naarai eswama EJAKAIT;
Kikareoto yes atutubeta nukokwap nuka akwap;
Kogeut ayoore, yes iroŋan,
ljo amagoro, ka ŋolikitoi kotomake;
Naarai alaku EJAKAIT Yakob,
Itopolooritai koIsirael.
24 # Yob 19:25; 9:8; Isa 43:1,14; 40:22; Sab 104:2. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT, ekalakunanikon,
Ŋol lokiluŋuluŋi jo ameun kakoik ebe:
“Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, loasubi kere;
Loapesit bon akuj; loaterarit akwap;
Iŋai ca ejai keoŋ?
25 # Yer 50:36; 1 Kor 1:20. Loerebokini aanyuneta nuka ikeŋalak,
Esikerepikini ikaroak;
Loetoboŋori lukacoa kau,
Loesibanibani akeeeijen:
26 # Sek 1:6; Mat 5:18. Loetogogoŋi akirot naka ekakeiaanakinan,
Etodoli da ainerun naka ikiyakiaka;
Loenera nuka Yerusalem ebe, ‘Edumuni luiboikinos toma’;
Nuka ireria luka Yuda da ebe, ‘Edukio,
Eoŋ da etonyouni aiboisiokec nuiswapito’:
27 # Yer 50:38. Loalimokini ataro ebe, ‘Koono,
Eoŋ da etooni icorinikon’;
28 # 2 Siit 36:22,23; Esr 1:2. Loenera nuka Kuro ebe, ‘Ŋes ekakecokon,
Itodoli ŋes! kere nuakoto eoŋ’;
Enera da nuka Yerusalem ebe, ‘Edukio’;
Nuka eyekalu da ebe, ‘Epesio akonipeteta.’’’
