Isaia 10

10
1Itukwi ŋul luisilakinete ikisila lumam edolito,
Ka ikewadikak luiwadikaete nuka aitapaar:
2Luireŋearete lukican kotube,
Luedemarete luebeito lukican lukotuŋaka,
Eraunos apuserun nuŋatunakec,
Ikokiok da lumejaunakec!
3 # Kos 9:7; Luk 19:44. Ikwaikinete ai kapaaran naka aiticanio,
Kamudiar da naebuni kama elwana?
Ikerete da neejai iŋai ayuunio?
Ai bo da inyeiniata akusibar?
4Isudedeete kwap kienan,
Ebirokinos kwap kalukearitai.
Karaida kiswamata ŋun kere, mam ca ejoniarit akenyunyura, Konye eriŋa akekan erioro.
5 # Isa 14:24-27; Nak 1:1–3:19; Sep 2:13-15. ljo ba Eyasuriat, esuretait loka anyunyuraka,
Ebela loka aisiopoka!
6Ejukakini ŋes asurokin ateker naitisilo,
Ecorakini ŋes asurokin ituŋa luka akanyunyura,
Aiŋat luŋatuna, ameja da lumelauna,
Aipudapuda kes kwape erit loka irotin.
7 # Mik 4:12. Konye mam elosikinit aswam kwaŋin,
Eketau da mam eomit kwaŋin;
Konye ejai amudiar otauke,
Agelar da atekerin kwabo mere nuikidioko.
8 # 2 Ika 18:24. Naarai ebala ebe,
“Mam kereka eraas ikapolokoka kere ikabakan?
9 # Amo 6:2; 2 Siit 35:20; Yer 46:2; Mar 13:21; 2 Sam 8:9; 2 Ika 23:33; Isa 10:9; 2 Ika 16:9. Kalino mam kereka eputosi ka Karakemis?
Kamat da mam eputosi ka Arapak?
Samaria da mam eputosi ka Damasiko?
10Kwape adolio akakan ajakanuto nuka aputosia,
Nuejaas kaputosia nuegerer nuitelekarito ŋun nuejaas Yerusalem ka Samaria;
11Kwape keswamakinia Samaria ka aputosiake,
Mam kereka eswamakini Yerusalem kwaŋin ka aputosiake?”
12Kosodi kanen naedaunia rwaru EJAKAIT akeswam kere komoru Sion ka Yerusalem, eticani araito nuka etau loegogoŋ loka ekabaka loka Asuria, ka aiwoŋonut naka akonyeke nueomiara. 13#Any 15:9; Sab 20:7; Isa 37:24. Naarai atema atiar,
“Eswama kagogoŋ naka akakan, ka akaeoa da;
Naarai arai loaeobe:
Kebelok iikorun luka atekerin,
Kaŋat abarakec,
Abu kalem kwape emaswa ŋul luibolete koicoloŋo.
14Adum akakan abar naka atekerin kwape kakais;
Ido kwape ituŋanan yenetukonokini abei nuejalitai,
Atukokinit eoŋ kwape kwaŋin akwap kere:
Emamei yenikibwakibwaki abebenok,
Karaida yenikeooŋi aituk, karaida yenikididia.”
15 # Yer 51:20; Rom 9:20,21. Edakakini kereka aep aluikin ŋol loejepia?
Itopoloor kereka emusemen aluikin ŋol loirii ŋes?
Kwape ti eriaria ebela ŋul luikiarete ŋes,
Arai kwape ti etonyounia asuretait ŋin yenimam erai akitoi!
16Kanuka ŋun ŋes ijukunia EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Adokoror mama ejaas ituŋake luketumito;
Ido inokokinio aitisisiet kwap kaibusesuke
Kwape aitisisiet naka akim.
17Aica naka Isirael eraun akim,
Lokalaunanike da eraun aipeloret naka akim;
Icwei isidopori da ikukwake
Ka ikodokodoke kapaaran adiopet.
18Emudiari aibuses naka amagoroke, ka naka imanikoriake luideŋeriaete,
Aijar ka akuan da;
Ejaun kwape edokororaror lokadekan.
19Ikidiokoros luedeunos luka ikito luka amagoroke,
Epedori ikoku aiwadik kes.
20Kosodi kanen kapaaran kaŋin, mam bobo aatoŋot naka Isirael, ka ŋul da luideunos luka ekek loka Yakob egoŋakinos kaŋol kaloedanyi kes; konye egoŋakinos ka EJAKAIT, Lokalaunan loka Isirael, kabeit. 21Eboŋuni aatoŋot, aatoŋot naka Yakob, neejai Edeke Lokapedor. 22#Mik 5:3; Rom 9:27. Naarai karaida kesubito ituŋakon Isirael bala asiŋe naka aŋolol, eboŋuni aatoŋot bon: inerunio amudiar, nailapara kadolit. 23#Isa 28:22; Dan 9:27. Naarai itodoli EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, amudiar nainerunio, kokidiŋ kakwap kere,
24Aso inera kwanata EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe, “Wu yes ikatuŋa luibolete koSion, sirikikuriakaros Eyasuriat: karaida kiile yes kebela, ikiari esuretaiteke asurokin yes, kwape ejaar epone loko Misiri. 25#Dan 11:36. Naarai eriŋa esalit apak edit noi, itodolikinio aisiop ka anyunyuraka kamudiariokec. 26Isidiki EJAKAIT loka ajoresio edaaset asurokin ŋes, kwape kearit loka Midian lokejai komoru Oreb: ejaun ekebela da kaŋolol, ikiari kwape ejaar epone loko Misiri. 27#Sab 105:15. Kosodi kanen kapaaran kaŋin, enyouni abwokonike kokeperikon, ajookotoke komurisinikon, emudiario da ajookot kanuka ainyonyoikin akinyet.”
28 # Nek 11:31. Adolu ŋesi Aiat, atubor koMiguron;
Kiŋadak abwokonike koMikimas:
29 # 1 Sam 13:23. Atuboros karidat; koperoto Geba:
Kitamarana Rama; aker Gibea loka Saul.
30 # 1 Sam 25:44. Kiluluk keporotokon, ijo akoku naka Galim!
Kopup ijo Laisa! Koboŋok ŋes, ijo Aŋatot!
31 # Yos 15:31. Aker Madumena;
Etukonokinos luibolete koGebim akerit,
32 # 1 Sam 21:1. Toma apaaran na naka lolo eperi Nob:
Iniŋi akekan aluikin emoru loka akoku naka Sion,
Eukot loka Yerusalem.
33Koany, ibesi EJAKAIT loka ajoresio atenuna ketasuro:
Ejepario lueojak, itisilio luitopolooro.
34Ejepi kasuat amonieik nuka amagoro,
Ibiror Lebanon kanuka lokapedor.
