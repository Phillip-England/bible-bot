Isaia 43

43
1 # Isa 44:6; 42:6; 1 Kor 1:9; 2 Tim 1:9. Konye kwana inera kwanata EJAKAIT loabu kosub jo, wu Yakob,
Ŋol Iokiluŋuluŋi jo, wu Isirael, ebe:
“Sirikikurianar, naarai kalaku jo;
Kanyarit jo kokirorikon, irai Lokaŋ,
2 # Sab 66:12; Ikis 31:6-8; Dan 3:25. Arai itori toma akipi, ajaenena kajo;
Toma ocorin, mam kiraparete:
Arai itori toma akim, mam kinomori;
Mam eiriani akim kama ijai,
3 # Awa 21:18. Naarai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekonideke,
Lokalaunan loka Isirael, eketajaranikon;
Ainak Misiri araun lugelisikon,
Etiopia da ka Seba kanukon.
4 # Any 19:5,6. Naarai irai loka akais nuipu kakonyeneka,
Lokayoŋio, kamina jo;
Ŋes ainakinia ituŋa kanukon,
Atekerin kanuka aiiarakon.
5 # Isa 41:10,14; 44:2. Sirikikurianar; naarai ajai kaio:
Ayauni eoŋ aurikon kokide, Kenotuni jo koto:
6Alimokini agolitomei ebe; Koosik;
Eŋalakimak da ebe, Sirikitikok:
Koyau ikadwe lusap kama elwana,
Ka lupesur da kosaŋe loka akwap.
7 # Isa 63:19; 29:23; Sab 100:3; Yok 3:3; 2 Kor 5:17; Pes 2:10. Ŋinidiopet yenemaikinio ekakiror,
Yenabu kasub kanuka aibusesuka;
Keluŋuluŋ ŋes; eebo, kasub eoŋ ŋes.”
8 # Isa 6:9; 42:19. Kitolomutu imudukiok luejaas akonye,
Ka ikimiŋiok luejaas aki!
9Kinotunos atekerin kere, kotukonokinos ituŋa;
Iŋai bo idio kakesi epedori alimokin ŋun,
Aitodikin oni akiro nusodit?
Koyaut kesi ikajenakakec, kitojokai;
Kopuputu kesi, kobalas ebe, Abeit ŋin.
10 # Yok 1:7; Asw 1:8; Bur 12:1; Apu 1:5; Isa 44:8; 41:8. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, “Iraas yesi ikakajenak ka ekakejaanakinan
lokaseunit:
Tetere kiienete kiyuunete eoŋ,
ljeŋunete ebe eoŋ Ŋopenol;
Emamei Edeke lokesubitai ageikin eoŋ,
Mam ejaun da lokatupakini.
11Eoŋ, eoŋ obe, arai EJAKAIT;
Emamei eketajaran dimarai eoŋ,
12 # Ikis 32:16. Alimo eoŋ, etajar, etodik,
Amamei edeke lomoit kamakus.”
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, “Kanuka ŋun iraas yesi ikakajenak,
Eoŋ da arai Edeke.
13 # Sab 90:2; Awa 8:23; Mik 5:2; Mat 19:26. Eebo, agear lolo, eoŋ ŋopeŋol;
Kwabo emamei yenepedori ayuun kakanika:
Eswamai eoŋ, iŋai bo itikokini?”
14Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT, ekusikalakunan,
Lokalaunan loka Isirael ebe,
“Kanukus ŋes kejukaria Babulon,
Esijesari kes kere kwape luekerete,
Kes Ikaludean, toma ameerin nuka aiyalamakec.
15 # Kos 13:10. Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, lokalaunanikus;
Lokasuban loka Isirael, ekabakakus.”
16 # Sab 77:19. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT,
Loesubi erot toma aŋolol,
Esokot da toma akipi nukagogoŋ;
17 # Any 14:4. Loitolomuni agali ka ebalasi, ajore ka apedor;
Eperete nepepe, mam enyounete;
Erikaros, itudeŋio kwape eusi, ebe:
18“Sirikiitut akiro nusodit,
Karaida aomoom akiro nukasonya.
19 # 2 Kor 5:17; Apu 21:5. Koany, eswamai eoŋ akirot nakitetet;
Elibuni kwape kwana; mam yes ijenunete?
Asubi eoŋ erot toma ais,
Icorin da toma amodiŋ.
20 # Isa 48:21; Luk 1:74; Pes 1:5. Ketopoloorete eoŋ itiaŋ luka ais, ikuwei, ka ikuului;
Naarai ainakini akipi kotoma ais,
Icorin da kotoma amodiŋ,
Aitamasia ituŋaka, luseuŋunaka;
21Ituŋa luasubuni eoŋ
Tetere elimonorete aiturioka.
22 # Mal 1:13. “Konye mam kiwonet eoŋ, wu Yakob;
Ketapasa jo, wu Isirael.
23Mam kiiununei eoŋ ibaren ludidik luka ainakinetakus
nuicweio;
Mam kitubusikinit eoŋ kasubanakon.
Mam kesilaŋirikinit jo ainanakin eoŋ ainakineta,
Karaida aitapas jo kaluisupuruununo,
24 # Isa 1:14; Mal 2:17; Yud 1:4. Mam kigwelanakinit eoŋ eitola kapesan,
Karaida aisimonikin eoŋ kasiepai nuka asubanakon:
Konye ibu ijo kisilaŋirikinite eoŋ karonisiokon,
Kitapas eoŋ kanumam adolitekon,
25 # Isa 44:22; Yer 31:34; Mik 7:18,19; Esk 36:22. “Eoŋ, eoŋ obe, ŋopeŋol loamwatari adiakanaretakon kanukaŋ;
Mam eituni akonironisio.
26Kisiitunoi; kapurokinos:
Kopet akonikiro, tetere kitojokio.
27 # Rom 5:12. Kodiaka papaakon losodit,
Kopaasi ikejulakakon kamakaŋ.
28 # Isa 47:6; Aku 2:2; Sab 79:4; Yer 24:9; Dan 9:11; Sek 8:13. Kanuka ŋun ketisil ikapolok luka aibois nakalaunan,
Kejaik Yakob araun ekenit,
Ka Isirael da amoromoroo.
