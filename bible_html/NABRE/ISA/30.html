Isaia 30

30
1 # Ikis 29:19; Rom 2:5; 2 Tim 3:13. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
“Itukwi idwe luijerukaka,
Luinerunete konye mere neajai eoŋ;
Luimukokinos keimukos, konye mere kekamoyo,
Tetere iyatanakinete aronis neejai aronis:
2 # Mar 27:21; 1 Ika 22:7; Yer 21:2. Luelosete aijesar Misiri,
Komam keŋisete kaitukuka;
Tetere egogoŋoros kagogoŋ naka Parao,
Amunokin etoribe loka Misiri!
3 # Yer 37:5,7. Kanuka ŋun eraun agogoŋ naka Parao ileicikus,
Amunokin etoribe loka Misiri eraun aitirirakus.
4Naarai ejaas ikapolokoke Soan,
Adolut ikiyakiake Kaŋes.
5 # Yer 2:36. Irumete kes kere ilele
Kanuka ituŋa lumam epedorete aisimedaun kes,
Lumam eraas aiŋarakinet karaida ameda,
Konye ileic, ido amorosit.”
6 # Kos 8:9. Abwokon naka itiaŋ luka Eŋalakimak.
Kotoma akwap naka ican ka aburun
Naeponiata aŋatuny ka eŋatuny,
Emusaga ka emun loesarani loeporitoi,
Edakete abarakec kokeperin luka asigirian nudidik,
Ikeceboro da luka akais nuipu karukia nuka akaalai,
Mama ejaas ituŋa lumam isimedaunete kes.
7 # Yer 37:7. Naarai iŋarakini Misiri atai, atitai:
Ŋes amaikinia ŋes “Rakab naidanyanyai.”
8Aso kolot, kiwadikak amoru kakonyenekec,
Kigirigir da oitabo,
Tetere eraun ekajenan kanuka apaarasia nuepote ikar kere.
9 # Ikis 32:20. Naarai eraas ituŋa luijerukaka, idwe luesabaka,
Idwe lueŋerito akiirar ekisil loka EJAKAIT.
10 # Yer 11:21; 1 Ika 22:13. Luetemarete ikadwarak ebe, “Sirisesenete”;
Luetemarete inabin da ebe, “Sirikidwarakis sio akiro nuetenete,
Kolimokinai sio akiro nuepataka, Kodwarakinai nukaiŋal:
11 # Asw 13:8. Irwa yesi korot, kopaasi kosokot,
Kitamamaut Lokalaunan loka Isirael kakonyenekosi.”
12Aso ebala Lokalaunan loka Isirael kwanata ebe,
“Naarai imenata akirot na,
Imunokinos aimwaŋ ka amagulul,
Igoŋakinos yesi mamakec:
13Ŋes ejaunor namam adolit na mamakus,
Kwape acilat naekoto aibiror,
Eneara kiŋa karwatat naeoj,
Itiuni aibirorike ebuni atipet.
14 # Sab 2:9; 2 Pet 2:4,5. Abilunike ikoni kwape ebwaŋabwaŋaunor amot naka ekajean,
Ebwaŋabwaŋai komam aitim;
Tetere mam edumunos katutubetake
Atibolok naeyaŋaarere akim kokyala,
Arai naewokere akipi koico.”
15 # Isa 7:4; Sab 81:11; Awa 1:24; Yer 44:16; Mat 23:37. Naarai inera kwanata EJAKAIT Edeke, Lokalaunan loka Isirael, ebe,
“Kitajario yes kanuka aboŋunun ka aiyeŋunun;
Ejaun akusigogoŋ toma aiyamakina ka aicasit.”
Konye yes inerito. 16Konyc ipotu kobalas ebe,
“Mam, naarai kikeri kabalasin”;
Kanuka ŋun ipote akerit:
Ebe da, “Kibwaneni luetiroko”;
Kanuka ŋun etiroko lukirukioto yes.
17 # Leb 26:8; Ikis 28:25; Yos 23:10. Ekeri elukumit kairetakin naka ediopet;
Ikerete yesi kairetakin naka ikany:
Aitodol naisalakiniata kwape” epir kaidi naka eroŋat,
Kwape aanyunet koukot.
18 # 2 Pet 3:9; Sab 2:12; Awa 16:20; Yer 17:7. Kanuka ŋun idareuni EJAKAIT, tetere esianikini yes,
Kanuka ŋun da itopolooritai ŋes, tetere kitimi yes.
Naarai EJAKAIT ŋes Edeke loka etube;
Isirereŋitoi kere luidareunete ŋes.
19Naarai yes ituŋa lukoSion luibolete koYerusalem: mam bobo imonyete; abeit esianikini ŋesi yes naepupio aimonyokus; naepupunio, ŋes konyet kibonokinio yes, 20Ido karaida einakini EJAKAIT yes inyamat luka aitaaritoi ka akipi nuka aiticanitoi, konye mam bobo eŋopi Ekusikesisianakinan, konye eanyunete akusikonye Ekusikesisianakinan. 21#Ikis 5:32; Yos 1:7; 2 Ika 22:2; Awa 4:27. Epupunete da akoniki akirot kokaukon naebala ebe, “Lo ŋes erot, kitoroto kotoma”; naipaariata kakan nakoteten, ido naipaariata kediany. 22#2 Siit 31:1; Isa 2:20. Imunaasi ailadad naka akusiputosia nuegerer nuka epuesa, ka arapeta nuka esabu nuka aputosiakus nuecalara: icakarete kes kwape ibore yenimam elai; ilimokinete ebe “Irwa!”
23 # Mat 6:33; 1 Tim 4:8. Isitepi ŋesi akiru kanuka ikinyomokus, luiweete alup; inyamat da luka eiyas loka alup, ituraŋaŋas, ipuakaros: kapaaran kaŋin edakas ibarenekus oronyia luepolok, 24Imoŋin da ka asigirian nuekoriete alup enyamete lunyaman lueiaas abalaŋ, luepjesitai keritei ka ipukupuket. 25Ejaunos da icorin ka aguuruto nuka akipi toma ŋoliroŋat loeoj, ka ŋoliukot loeoj, kapaaran naka earit loepol, naibirunotor itogoi lueojak. 26#Isa 60:19; Sek 2:5; Apu 21:23. Ido da eraun aica naka elap kwape aica naka akoloŋ, aica da naka akoloŋ iyatakina irwan akanykaarei, kwape aica naka apaarasia akanykaarei, kapaaran naeenia EJAKAIT ariebo naka ituŋake, naitaŋaleunia amusuŋunukec nukinomonomoo.
27Koany, ebuni ekiror loka EJAKAIT kama elwana,
Enoki kanyunyurake, kotoma apuru naepol naitutumai:
lleleba akeituk aisiop.
Esubit akeŋajep da bala akim naidopori.
28 # 2 Ses 2:8. Esubit eiyeŋaneke bala aleles nailapanar,
Naedoli omurisiŋ,
Aikeke atekerin kaikekeis naka amudiario:
Aibwaikin abokonya nuka ituŋa amunyo naitapaari.
29ljaunos yesi kekosio kwape loka akuar naka epucit lokalaunan; ailele da nakotau, kwape naelosio ituŋanan kalamaru abunore oroŋat loka EJAKAIT, Atiiba naka Isirael. 30Isiirari da EJAKAIT ekeporoto loka aibuses, itodikini aijesun naka akanike, aisiop naka anyunyurakeŋ, airian naka akim naidopori, ajielelitoi naka akiru, ka ekwam, ka akako da. 31Ebilibilio Eyasuriat kanuka eporoto loka EJAKAIT, loinomi kebela. 32Ŋiniinometait da naka ebela loeseunitai, loinomi EJAKAIT ŋes, ejaun kisiman ka adeudeun: ejiikinos ŋesi ka kes kojiisio luka aijaŋijaŋ. 33#Yer 7:31. Naarai aponi Topet kitemokinitete sek; eebo, etemonokinitoi kanuka ekabaka; etemoki kidul kopol: oupake ejai akim ka akito nuipu; isinepi eiyeŋane loka EJAKAIT ŋes, kwape aleles naka acukuman.
