Isaia 49

49
1 # Mat 1:20; Luk 1:15; Yer 1:5. Kopupunoi, yes apujana;
Kiirasi da yes ituŋa luilwaka:
Abu EJAKAIT kanyarau kakoik;
Kolimo ekakiror kaliliget naka totoka.
2 # Kos 6:5; Bur 4:12; Apu 1:16. Etusub akaituk bala ekileŋ loekwana,
Kebwa kotoribe loka akanike;
Ketarau ekojo loiputut,
Ocoeke da ŋes karicitor:
3 # Sek 3:8; Mat 17:17; Yok 13:31. Katema eoŋ atiar, “gjo ekakejaanakinan;
Isirael, ŋes ketubusikinere.”
4 # Esk 3:19. Konye katema atiar, “Abu ketaaritoi taai,
Atwania akagogoŋ aloŋ, atwania taai:
Konye abeit ejai akatubokinio neejai EJAKAIT,
Ekaropit da ejaas ka Ekadeke.”
5Natikwana inera EJAKAIT lokasubi eoŋ kotoma akoik
Araun ekekejaanakinan,
Ainyakakin ŋes bobo Yakob,
Ido kotukonokinai Isirael neejai:
(Naarai ebusu eoŋ kakonye nuka EJAKAIT,
Ekadeke da arau akagogoŋ.)
6 # Isa 9:2; 42:6; Luk 2:32; Asw 13:47; 26:23. Eebo, ebala ebe,
“Erai akirot naebenen noi jo araun ekakejaanakinan
Aitonyoun atekerin nuka Yakob,
Ka aitajar luapiaŋunos kotoma Isirael:
Kainakin jo da araun aica mama ejaas lukacekwapin, Koraenena akaitajar aitodol awasia naka akwap.”
7Inera kwanata EJAKAIT,
Ekalakunan loka Isirael, ido Ekalaunanike,
Mama ejai ŋol loemenito ituŋa, ŋol loelait ateker,
Ekejaanakinan loka ikayaitok, ebe:
“Eanyunete ikabakan, enyounete;
Lukapolok da, emaete;
Kanuka EJAKAIT loerai emunot,
Ekalaunan loka Isirael, loabu koseu jo.”
8 # Sab 69:13; 2 Kor 6:2. Inera kwanata EJAKAIT ebe,
“Kaboŋok jo kapak nakacamunia,
Ido keŋarak kapaaran naka aitajario:
Ido kekuniai jo,
Kainak araun aitutuket mama ejaas ituŋa,
Aiticat akwap, aisurum airumeta nukamejei;
9 # Isa 42:7; Sek 9:12. Kolimonokini ŋul lueenitai ebe, ‘Kolomasi,’
Ka ŋul luejaas kamuton ebe, ‘Kitoduunos.’
Enyamenenete korotin,
Komor kere luelapat nen eraun eronyikec,
10 # Sab 22:26; 121:6; 23:2; Yok 6:35; Apu 7:16-17. Mam kes eriebenenei eteŋei, karaida akure;
Kwabo mam enomonori akoloŋ karaida aibuse:
Naarai ŋol loitimi iŋarenenokini kes,
Eyanaanari da atanin,
11Ido etuluori ikaroŋan kere araun erot,
Iturio da ikarotin.
12Koany, epote lu kama elwana;
Ido, koany, epote lu kagolitomei koto da;
Ido epote lu kakwap naka Sinim,
13Koye, ijo akuj; kolakar, ijo akwap;
Kogeut ayoore, yes iroŋan:
Naarai etalaka EJAKAIT iketuŋja,
Ido itimi luke lukiticanio.
14Konye kobala Sion ebe, “Kajalak YAWE,
Kemurwoki EJAKAIT.”
15 # Sab 103:13; Mat 7:11; Rom 11:29. “Epedori aberu ikekoku yenenaki aimurwokin,
Komam isionie okoku loka akoikike?”
Eebo, epedorete ŋun aimurwokin,
Konye mam eoŋ kemurwokini jo.
16 # Any 13:9; Eko 8:6; Sek 2:8. Koany, kegiranakinit jo otauon luka akaninika;
Ejaenenas arwatatinikon kakonyeneka tiritir.
17Isunyito ikadukokokon adepar ikamudiarakakon,
Elomarete da ŋul lukitisilete kamakon.
18 # Awa 17:6. Kiteluo akonikonye, ido koany:
Etukonokinos ŋul kere, epote mamakon.
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kwape ajaria,
Abeit inapi kes kere kwape lukisiro,
Iyogogoi kwape ateran.
19 # Isa 54:1,2; Sek 2:4. Naarai aiboisiokon nuiswapito nuesilok,
Ka akwapukon naemudiara,
Abeit kwape kwana ididinik luibojete kotomakon,
Ŋul da lukilikorete elwakaros.
20 # Mat 3:9; Rom 11:11. Etemarete idwe luaurio kapak naka atwaniarikon akiikon ebe,
“Kadidiŋiki eoŋ aibois:
Konyaikinai keboikin.’
21 # Age 42:13; Yer 31:15. Kosodi ijo atemar kotaukon atiar,
“Iŋai bo kaurikini ŋul,
Naarai atwania idweka, do acuduŋun bon,
Arai nakereŋearitai, nairimi lailo ka laije?
Iŋai esai ŋul? Koany, asal bon; ai bo kejaas ŋul?’”
22Ebala EJAKAIT Edeke kwanata ebe,
“Koany, ekeari akakan mama ejaas atekerin,
Asipokini aanyuneteka mama ejaas ituŋa:
Eyaunete odwekon katorobusiokec,
Erioŋakinete adwekon okepirinikec.
23 # Sab 72:9; 34:22; Mik 7:17; Rom 5:5. Eraunos ikabakan lukapapakon lukisaete,
Akabakanakee da nukatotokon nukisaete:
Ekukonokinos jo kitulurito akecekonyen kwap,
Emeete adae nukakejekon;
ljeŋuni da ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT,
Ŋul da lukedareunete mam irumete ileic.”
24 # Mat 12:29; Luk 11:21. Edemario tope lumejan kama ka ikagogoŋok?
Arai bo eyuunio lukikamuna luka ekadoliton?
25Konye ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe,
“Edemario cabo lukikamuna luka ikagogoŋok,
Eyuunio da lumejan luka lusogim:
Naarai kiŋolopikin eoŋ kaŋol loiŋolopas kajo,
Etajari ikonidwe.
26 # Apu 14:20; Sab 9:16; Isa 43:3. Etanyami eoŋ ŋul lukimwanito jo akiriŋikec kikulepek; Emerikinos akeceokot kikulepek, kwape kebino loewiny. Kosodete ejenunete awat kere ebe,
Eoŋ EJAKAIT arai eketajaranikon,
Ekalakunanikon, Ekagogoŋon loka Yakob.”
