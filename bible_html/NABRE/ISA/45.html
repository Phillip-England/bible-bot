Isaia 45

45
1 # Sab 73:23; Isa 41:13. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT Iokinyonyoikitonoke akinyet, ŋes Kuro,
Loetiŋit eoŋ akekan nakoteten, aitonokok atekerin kakonyeneke,
Alacar aŋabes nuka ikabakan;
Aŋaar ikekia kakonyeneke,
Ido mam irutei egolonokinio:
2Ebe: “Keŋarenikini eoŋ jo,
Eiyakini aiboisio nuimugogolito:
Abilibilei ikirigin luka aisinit,
Atubutubei atiketa nuka asua.
3 # Any 33:12. Kainakini jo iboro luka akais nuipu luka amuton,
Abar da naibwaitai naka aiboisio nukaiyeiyea,
Tetere ijenuni ebe Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT,
Edeke loka Isirael, lokanyarit jo kekirorikon,
4 # Isa 44:1; Asw 17:23; Gal 1:8,9; Pes 2:12; 1 Ses 4:5. Kanuka Yakob ekakejaanakinan, ka Isirael loseunaka,
Kanyarit jo kekirorikon:
Kamaikinit jo ekiror, karaida mam kijenunit eoŋ.
5 # Ikis 4:35; Sab 18:32. Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, emamei ece;
Emamei Edeke dimarai eoŋ:
Kaenikini arokoba, karaida mam kijenunit eoŋ.
6 # Sab 102:15; Mal 1:11. Tetere ejenunete ageun kalomun naka akoloŋ,
Kiton to da, ebe emamei eee dimarai eoŋ:
Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, emamei eee,
7 # Amo 3:6. Eoŋ aluŋuluŋi aica, asubuni amuton;
Ayauni ainapakin, asubuni aronis;
Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, loeswamai ŋun kere,
8 # Sab 85:11. “Kotep, ijo akuj, kokuju,
Kobukut idouon adolit:
Koooŋata da alup, tetere itolomunete aitajario,
Isilibunete da adolit;
Eoŋ EJAKAIT asubunit ŋun.
9 # Rom 9:20; Isa 64:8; Yer 18:6. “Itukwi ŋol loepegas ka Lokasubanike!
Atibolok kotoma atiboloka nuka akwap!
Iŋisi tope elupe ŋol lokiluŋuluŋi ebe, ‘Iswamai inyo?’
Arai aswamukon ebe, ‘Emameete ŋes akanin?’
10Itukwi ŋol loebala papake ebe, ‘Iwuriei inyo?’
Arai aberu ebe, ‘Irieba awuruun inyo?’”
11 # Yer 31:9; Isa 29:23. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT,
Lokalaunan loka Isirael, ido Lokasubanike ebe,
“Kiŋitunai nuka akiro nuepote;
Kicorakinai cabo nuka idweka,
Ido nuka aswam naka akaninika.
12 # Yer 27:5. Asubunit eoŋ akwap,
Asubunit da etuŋanan kotomake:
Eoŋ, akakanin epesikinito akuj,
Ecorakinit eoŋ ajoreke kere.”
13 # Esr 1:1; Rom 3:24. Ebala EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe:
“Etonyounit ŋes kadolit, eteteni ikerotin kere:
Eduki ŋesi ekare,
Eosikini atai lukaŋ lukireŋearitai,
Mere kanuka agwelar, karaida aropio.”
14 # Sab 68:31; Isa 49:23; Sek 8:22; 1 Kor 14:25. Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe,
“Aitaaritoi nako Misiri, ka lugwelanara luka Etiopia,
Ka Isabean, ituŋa lukaojak,
Epote mama ijai, eraunos lukon;
Kitupakinete jo; epote kotoma orikoi:
Kikukonokinos, kilipete jo ebe,
‘Abeit ejai Edeke mamakon;
Emamei ece, emamei Edeke ece.’”
15 # Ikis 29:29; Sab 36:6; Isa 8:17; Rom 11:33. Abeit irai ijo Edeke loiŋopi,
Wu Edeke loka Isirael, Eketajaran.
16Irumete kes ileice, eebo, itiriraete, kes kere:
Edakaros akwesikec kere luesubete aputosia.
17 # 2 Sam 23:5; Sab 103:17; Kos 1:7; Rom 11:26. Konye itajari EJAKAIT Isirael kaitajario namam edaun:
Mam yes irumenenete ileice,
Karaida aitirir ikar lumam edaunos.
18Naarai ebala kwanata EJAKAIT loasubuni akuj;
Ŋes Edeke;
Lokiluŋuluŋi akwap, kosub:
Kiticat, mam asubuni atai,
Kiluŋuluŋi tetere iboikinoi kotoma, ebe:
“Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT; emamei eee.
19 # Ikis 30:11; Nek 9:13; Sab 19:8; Rom 7:12. Mam abu kener kaiyeiyea,
Kaibois naka akwap naka amuton;
Mam kabala aur naka Yakob ebe, ‘Komoai eoŋ atai”:
Eoŋ EJAKAIT enera nukadolit,
Alimokini akiro nuetenete.
20 # Yer 44:28; Rom 1:22. “Kotukonokinos, kopotu;
Kikunyutu, yes luikuata luka atekerin:
Emamei kes aijen luedakete akitoi naka aputokec naegerer, Ilipete edeke lomam epedori aitajar,
21Kolimosi, kikeut;
Eebo, kinerutu nepepe.
Iŋai bo kitoduunit ŋin ageun kasonya?
Iŋai bo kelimokinit apeŋo?
Mere Eoŋ EJAKAIT?
Emamei ecedeke dimarai eoŋ;
Edeke loedolit, ido eketajaran;
Emamei ece dimarai eoŋ.
22“Kosesenai, kitaiarunos, isaneta kere luka akwap:
Naarai arai eoŋ Edeke, emamei ece.
23 # Age 22:16; Yer 22:5; Bur 6:13; Age 31:53; Ikis 6:13; Sab 63:11; Rom 14:11; Pil 2:10-11. Eyapoik kanukaŋ,
Alomu akirot kaitukuka kadolit,
Ido mam eboŋuni, ebe,
‘Kakukonokin ŋinikuŋ eoŋ,
Keyapoikini ŋiniŋajep.’
24Ejauni yenikabala ebe,
Ejai adolit ka agogoŋ toma EJAKAIT bon:
Epote ituŋa mamakeŋ, irumete ileice
Ŋul kere luenyunyurikinete ŋes.
25Itojokio aur kere naka Isirael toma EJAKAIT,
Ituro da.”
