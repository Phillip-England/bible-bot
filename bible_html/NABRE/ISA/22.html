Isaia 22

22
1Abwokon naka aditot naka aitapoet.
Anyoika bo ŋun, idokar ijo cut
Oswion luka itogoi?
2Wu jo loileleba eibwoŋ,
Ere lokecelet, ere lokcicur;
Mam luaaranakon eario kekileŋ,
Kwabo mam etwakito koile,
3Akerata ikayaitokokon nepepe,
Apotu ikeketak akabwai koenata kes:
Kere lukedumakinio mamakon aponi koenai nepepe,
Apeata neelwana.
4Kanuka ŋun katema atiar,
“Sirikiteete eoŋ, kamony noi akiyo;
Siritaaritos aipudun eoŋ,
Naarai enataritai akoku naka ikatuŋa.”
5 # Est 3:15; Aku 1:5. Naarai ebunit apaaran naka aburun,
Naka aipudapud, naka abuonokin da,
Kama ejai EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
Kaditot naka aitapoet:
Aitakitak arwatatin, amonyakin iroŋan,
6 # Isa 21:2; Yer 49:35. Arwa Elam akuraru,
Ejaas kagalin nuka ituŋa ka ikebwanenok;
Apuko Kir ebuku,
7Kosodi kanen, ileleba aditonokon nuejok agalin,
Kiderakinos ikebwanenok orute.
8 # 1 Ika 7:2. Kolaca arapetait naka Yuda.
Ibu kosesen kapaaran kaŋin lujiis lukotogo loka amagoro. 9#2 Ika 20:20; 2 Siit 32:4; Eko 4:4. Ipotu koanyutu arorwaitin nukore loka Daudi; ipotu kotukonokis akipi nuka atapar naijesara. 10Ipotu kimarata itogoi luka Yerusalem, kotiyatiyata itogoi anwokia arwatat. 11#Nek 3:16. Ipotu kobokata atapar kidiŋ karwatatin aarei kanuka akipi nuka atapar namojoŋ: konye mam ipotu kosesenata ŋol lokesubi ŋun, karaida aanyun akonyen nuka ŋol kalokolo kiswamai ŋun.
12Kapaaran kaya abu EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
Konyarau ituŋa aimony, ka aibworo,
Ka abareno, ka anapit da ipukoi:
13 # Isa 5:12; Amo 6:3; Luk 17:26-29; Isa 56:12; 1 Kor 15:32. Ido koany, aiyalama ka ailel,
Aiar imoŋin ka aipu imerekekin,
Ainyam akiriŋ, ka aimat ebino.
“Mere kanyama, kamata da,
Naarai kitwaki moi.”
14 # 1 Sam 3:14; Esk 24:13. Kikujokin EJAKAIT loka ajoresio akiika,
Ebala EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe,
“Abeit mam kilucio yes numam adolit nu
Aitodol neitwakiata.”
15 # 2 Ika 18:37; Isa 36:3; 1 Ika 4:6. Inera EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio kwanata ebe, “Ape, kolot mama ejai ekeŋadan ŋol, ŋes Sebuna, loepolokinit etogo, kiner ebe, 16Iswamai inyo kane? Iŋai da ijaatar ijo ne, tape ibokit ailit ne? Ibokikinit ailit kuju, aikeikin aibois atiiba! 17Koany, kirutari EJAKAIT jo kagogoŋ, ijo lokagogoŋ: eebo, kilidi ŋesi jo ba ba. 18Abeit kiiliili jo ka acakar da kwape emupira akwap naelal; itwanikini nina, ŋina da ejaunos agalinikon nukaibuses, ijo ileic luka etogo loka eiakaitikon, 19Kerenei eoŋ jo kaswamukon, kidemario apoloukon. 20#2 Ika 18:18. Kosodi kanen, kapaaran kaya anyarauni eoŋ ekakeiaanakinan Eliakim okoku loka Kilikia: 21#Yob 29:16. etanapi eoŋ ŋes ekonikaso, etogogoŋi ŋes karokobakon, ejaikini ayaitekon akanike; eluori ŋesi papa loka luibojete koYerusalem, loka ekekloka Yuda da. 22#Apu 3:7. Esirioni eoŋ elacetait loka ekek loka Daudi kokeperike; eŋaari ŋesi, emameuni da loegoli; egoli ŋesi, emameuni da loeŋaari, 23#Esr 9:8. Esikakini ŋes kwape kekweru aibois naicasit; eraun ŋesi eicoloŋ loka aibuses kokek loka papake. 24Ikajakinete kesi kamake aibuses kere naka ekek loka papake, aur ka luetupanakinete, ŋinibore kere yenedit, ameun kaiwokito aitodol akaalakanya. 25Ebala EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe, kapaaran kaya, ilokulokun ekweru loisikakinitai aibois naicasit; ejepio, ibiror, emudiario da luebwokit ŋesi; naarai ener EJAKAIT.”
