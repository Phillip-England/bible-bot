Isaia 1

1
1 # Mar 12:6; 2 Ika 15:1. Aitapoet naka Isaia okoku loka Amos, nakeanyununei kanuka Yuda ka Yerusalem, kapaarasia nuka Usia, ka Yotam, ka Akas, ka Kesekia, ikabakan luka Yuda.
2Kiira, ijo akuj, kitoik akit, ijo akwap,
Naarai ener EJAKAIT ebe:
“Abu ketanak ka asa da idwe,
Konye kaŋerokis,
3 # Yer 9:3,6. Ejeni emoŋ papake,
Ejeni da asigiria auj naka ejakaitike;
Konye mam Isirael ejeni,
Mam ikatuŋa eomitos.”
4 # Isa 57:3,4. Wu okwe ateker nakaronon,
ltuŋa luelaŋirikinito numam edolito,
Aur naka luiswamaete aronis,
Idwe luemunaarito!
Enyeis EJAKAIT,
Amena Ekalaunan loka Isirael,
Agitaros, aboŋosi kau.
5 # Isa 9:13; Yer 2:30. Inyo bo ikototor ainomio bobo,
Iyatakinotor da aipaliikin?
Edek akou kere, iiloro etau kere.
6 # Yer 8:22. Anyoun kotau loka akeju aitodol akou,
Emamei aŋaleu;
Nabarai amusuŋun, ka aionokineta,
Ka idola luebosak:
Mam ebuleneneo, kwabo mam eenenenoo,
Kwabo mam inyonyoanakinio akinyet,
7 # Ikis 28:51. Eswap akusikwap; ecwei ikusireria kakim;
Akwapukus da enyamete imo kakonyenekus,
Ido eswap, naarai epikosi ŋes imo.
8Ido asal akoku naka Sion
Kwape keidukuru loejai amana naka emusabibu,
Kwape kaiteis naejai amana, naka akolil,
Kwape ka ere loirigaunito lukasurup.
9 # Age 19:24; Rom 9:29. Kedim EJAKAIT loka ajoresio
Aitasalikin oni aatoŋot naedit noi,
Tikirai kwape ka Sodom,
Tikiiput kwape ka Gomora.
10 # Esk 16:46. Kiirasi akirot naka EJAKAIT,
Yes ijaka lukoSodom;
Kitoikis akit ikisila luka Edekewok,
Yes ituŋa lukoGomora.
11 # Amo 5:21-22; Sab 50:8. Inera EJAKAIT ebe:
“Anyoin ajokis naka asubanakus nuipu kamakaŋ?
Kaidoto ainakineta nuicweio nuka imerekekin lumaseniko,
Ka asiepai naka ibaren luipisio;
Kwabo mam alelakinit aokot nuka itoba,
Karaida nuka idwe luka amerekekin, karaida nuka ikorwa.
12“Naitakanuniata kakonyeneka,
Iŋai bo kiburai aipudapud akalaroi?
13Siriyauŋunete bobo asuban nuka aamameak;
Etalei eoŋ lukisupurununo.
Elap lokelamunan ka asabato, anyara da atukosia,
Mam acamunit, eraas numam edolito,
Eebo, atukonokin da nakalaunan.
14Elait ekatau ilapiokus lukelamunak ka ipucinokus luicorakinitai;
Eraas aitapasitoi kaneajai;
Kapas atitiŋonokin kes.
15 # Yob 27:9; 1 Tim 2:8. Ido nailoianariata akusikanin
Ebwaenenei akakonye kama ijaas;
Eebo, nailipenenata nuipu, mam eiranari:
Imodimoda akusikanin aokot,
16 # Yer 4:14; Rom 12:9. Kilota, kitalaunos;
Kolemasi aronis naka aswamisiokus kakonyeneka;
Kinyeis aswam aronis.
17 # Mik 6:8. Kisisiautu aswam ejok, komoenenete etube;
Koyuuŋunete yenereitai,
Kirabanakinete ikokiok,
Kopuronokinete apuserut.”
18 # 1 Sam 12:7; Mik 6:2; Isa 41:21; 44:22; Sab 51:7; Mik 7:18; Pes 1:6-8. Inera EJAKAIT ebe:
“Kopotu ca do, kenerutu:
Karaida kereŋak adiakanaretakus be,
Ekwaŋuunete kwape kakile;
Karaida kireremata kwape kaokot,
Iliacaunete kwape ka epaaba.
19 # Ikis 28:1. Arai iraas luinupaka ka luirimaka,
Inyamenenete ajokis naka akwap.
20 # Tit 1:2. Konye do kiŋeroto kipaliikis,
Kidanyio kekileŋ:
Naarai ener aituk naka EJAKAIT,”
21Aluor ca ma ere lomunot lodote!
Ŋol loeleleba kolo etube!
Kiboie kolo adolit kotomake,
Konye kwana ikaarak.
22Aluor epuesakon elele,
Inyalakinitai ebinokon kakipi.
23 # 2 Siit 36:14; Yer 5:5; Dan 9:5; Kos 9:15. Epaliikis ikapolokokon,
Eraas ipadic luka ikokolak.
Emina ŋolituŋanan ainakineta nuitumudakete akonye,
Irii da ainakineta.
Mam Isigalikinete ikokiok,
Mam da etube loka apuserut edolokini kes.
24 # Ikis 28:63. Kanuka ŋun inera EJAKAIT,
EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
Lokagogoŋon loka Isirael ebe:
“Bu, eyeŋuni do kama ejaas luketokokolito,
Ido enyaŋi kama ejaas lukasurupoka.
25 # Apu 3:19. Ido ebelokini akakan mamakon,
Aliliauni elelekon cut,
Alemari da luinyalakina tomakon kere.
26Enyakakini ikatubokokon kwape sek,
Ikenerunakakon da kwape kageunet,
Kokau kinyarao Ere loka adolit,
Ere lomunot.”
27Elakuni etube Sion,
Adolit da ŋul lukotomake luibelokinos.
28Konye emudiaros ikcbueanarak ka ikadiakanarak nepepe, Isidoporio cut ŋul luejalete EJAKAIT.
29 # Isa 57:5; 65:3. Naarai kirumete yes ileic kanuka iluan lukiŋobeleete, Edakaros akwesikus kanuka imanikoria Iukiseunete,
30Naarai iraunos kwape elua loeoni akekiot,
Ido kwape emanikor loemameete akip.
31Eraun lokagogoŋ kwape esilot loka amujajut,
Akeswam da kwape aatwenyitwenyit;
Icweikinio kes kere,
Komamei yenitudeŋi.
