Isaia 21

21
1 # Sek 9:14. Abwokon naka amodiŋ naka aŋolol.
Kwape ekutaror itapipirion kari naka Eŋalakimak,
Elomuni kamodiŋ kakwap nasogim.
2 # 1 Sam 24:13; Yer 49:34; Isa 24:16; 13:17. Kalimokin eoŋ aitapoet naetiono;
Ekamodan emodomodoi, ekaŋatan da eŋasi.
Kodoka, ijo Elam; kirigau, ijo Media;
Esililiŋi eoŋ eidinyike kere,
3Kanuka ŋun ileleba aŋabeteka ariebo,
Kekamut aayoi kwape aayoi nuka aberu naidouni:
Ketipipilata komam apedori akiirar;
Kaburunit komam apedori aanyun.
4 # Ikis 28:67. Iyeŋiyeŋa ekatau, ketukurianata nusogim;
Eloeokin akamukat naabu kemiela araun amaran kama ajai.
5 # Dan 5:5. Itemonokinete kesi emesa, epesete imukekan,
Enyamete, emasete da.
Konyoutu, yes ikapolok,
Kinyonyoata ebuku akinyet.
6Naarai kabala EJAKAIT eoŋ kwanata ebe,
“Kolot, kibwaik ekedaran;
Kolimo ŋeasi nueanyuni:
7Naeanyunia ŋesi erioŋet loka ikebwanenok luelosete iareiarei, Erioŋet loka asigiria, erioŋet loka akaalai,
Kodunyak ŋesi noi aipup.”
8 # Kbk 2:1. Kikuna kwape eŋatuny ebe,
“Wu EJAKAIT, abwoenenei tiritir kokuju kaiteis paaran, Kebwaanakinio kaiboisika akuaresia kere.
9 # Isa 13:19; Yer 50:2; Apu 14:8; 18:2. Ido koany, ebunit erioŋet loka ituŋa,
Ikebwanenok luelosete iareiarei.”
Koboŋok kobala ebe,
“Ebiror Babulon, ebiror;
Abilibilunos aputosia kere nuegerer nuka idekesioke kiton akwap.”
10 # Yer 51:33; Mik 4:13; Kbk 3:12. Wu jo, naieomomoka, ido alosika naepietet:
Abu kalimok jo nuabu keira kama ejai EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
Edeke loka Isirael.
11 # 1 Siit 1:30; Esk 35:2; Oba 1:1. Abwokon naka Duma.
Ejai yenikeworoi eoŋ koSeir ebe,
“Ekedaran, anyoika nuka akuar?
Ekedaran, anyoika nuka akuar?”
12Koboŋok ekedaran ebe,
“Ebunit apaaran, ido akuare da;
Arai ikotosi aiŋitun, kiŋita:
Koboŋutu bobo.”
13 # 1 Siit 1:9. Abwokon naka Arabia.
Iperete yesi ais naka Arabia,
Yes luidiaete luka Idedanin.
14 # Yob 6:19. Koinasi akipi ŋin yenajakit akure:
Apotu lueboiete kakwap naka Tema kisiriamasi luakerete
Ka aijaikin da kes ikecenyamat,
15Naarai kekerete kesi kokileŋa,
Kokileŋa lueutunitoi, akabwai nuikesitai,
Kanuka awuruun naka ejie.
16 # Age 25:13; Sab 120:5; Eko 1:5; Isa 60:7. Naarai kabala EJAKAIT eoŋ kwanata ebe, “Eriŋa ekaru ketuboro, kwape ejaatar ikar luka ekeswaman eropit, eoliori aibuses kere naka Kedar: 17#Mar 23:19; 1 Sam 15:29; Isa 1:20; Mat 24:35; Mk 13:31; Luk 21:33; 1 Pet 1:25. ikidiokoros luideunos da kotiai loka ikeketak akabwai, ituŋa lukirigin luka idwe luka Kedar: naarai ener EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael.”
