Isaia 14

14
1 # Sab 102:13,16; Isa 54:7,8; Yer 31:3,4,10,11; Sek 1:17; Isa 60:4,5,10. Naarai itimi EJAKAIT Yakob, eseuni bobo Isirael, ibwaikini kes akwapukec: imorikikini imoit kakes, iketakinos kekek loka Yakob. 2Eyaete da atekerin, kosodete ayaŋaun toma aiboisikec; ejaun ekek loka Isirael kakes kakwap naka EJAKAIT araun ibulesin ka abulesin: ijoreorete kesi ŋul luapotu kijoreosi kes; eyaete ŋul lukeroikinitos kes,
3Kosodi kanen, kapaaran nakisibworaria EJAKAIT jo kaitururukon, ka icanikon, ka aitaaritoi kere nakitaaritere, 4#Isa 13:19; Kbk 2:6. inera awaragan na aluikin ekabaka loka Babulon, itemari atiar:
“Adaun ca ma ekaroikiton!
Adaun ca ma ere lokesabu!
5Abil EJAKAIT ebela loka ikaronok,
Ebela lokajakanut loka ikayaitok;
6Loadanya ituŋa kanyunyura kaidany natir,
Loayaitor atekerin kaisiop,
Karoikinit naemamei yenitikokinit.
7Ibworarit akwap kere, Ililiŋi:
Egeunete ayoore.
8 # Isa 55:12; Esk 31:16. Eebo, iyalamasi iberosin kanukon,
Ka iresia luka Lebanon, ebalasi ebe
‘Ameun kaitisiliokon,
Emamei lokijepit sio.’
9Iriaŋiriaŋa Okatwak kokwap kanukon
Airiamakin kajo ibuni:
Isidiki lukatwak kanukon,
Lukapolok kere luka akwap,
Itonyounit koicoloŋokec
Ikabakan kere luka atekerin.
10Kes kere kiboŋokinete jo,
Ebalasi ebe, ‘Ikalanyanari ijo da kwape kasio?
Iput kwape kasio!
11Ikokororitai aibusesukon kiton Okatwak,
Ka aruo naka adigidigiokon;
Epesikinitai ikur kwapukon,
Iritan da kimukoritere.
12 # Apu 8:10; 9:1. Ibirun ca ma kakuj,
ljo Aacer naka Atupuruc, okoku loka Acilakide!
Kijeparitai ca ma kiton alup,
ljo loibu kitisil atekerin!
13 # Aku 2:1; Dan 8:10; Apu 12:4; Sab 48:2; Mat 11:23; Luk 10:15. Ibu kiner kotaukon ebe,
‘Adokari toma akuj,
Etopoloori ekaicoloŋ aitelekar aacer nuka Edeke;
Ido eboikini omoru loka einos,
Kosaŋe loka ari naka agolitomei:
14 # Sep 2:15; 2 Ses 2:4. Adokari kuju adepar amukurur naka idouon;
Aputokin ka Je loejai kuju kakuj.’
15 # Isa 47:8; Esk 28:8,9; Mat 11:23; Asw 12:23. Konye kikokororio kiton Okatwak,
Atutubeta nukokwap nuka aipany.
16Ŋul lukianyunete jo kiŋanyanakinete akonye,
Kirereete jo, kobalas ebe,
‘Lopelo tope lokitamarani akwap,
Lokijaŋijaŋi ajakanuto:
17Lokitarauni akwap amodiŋ,
Loketiyakini ireriake;
Lomam abu koosik ienanake aboŋor bukec?’
18Ikabakan kere luka atekerin, kes kere,
Ejotooros kaibuses,
Ŋolituŋanan kotogoke.
19Konye jo kicakario kailitikon kwape atenut naelaitai, Konapite lukatwak, luecumucumutai kekileŋ,
Luijesaros kiton amor nuka aipany;
Kwape kates naicakacakao.
20Mam imorikikini kakes kanukio,
Naarai imudiar akonikwap,
Iar ikonituŋa,
Mam elimorio aur naka ikeswamak nueroko ikar kere.
21 # Any 20:5; Yob 18:19; Mat 23:35. Kitemokisi aario naka ikedwe
Kanuka numam adolit nuka lukapapakec;
Tetere mam enyounete, irumete akwap,
Ilelebete ireria akwap kere.
22 # 1 Ika 14:10; Awa 10:7. Ebala EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe, “Anyoikini eoŋ kes,” ebala EJAKAIT ebe, “Abilari koBabulon ekiror ka aatoŋot, okoku ka okoku loka okoku.” 23Ebala EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe, “Etarauni airumet naka eeoc, ka ataparin nuka akipi: apiari da kigweren luka amudiar,”
24 # Isa 10:5-34; Nak 1:1–3:19; Sep 2:13-15. Eyapoik EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, ebala ebe,
“Abeit, kwape aomoomotor, ejaun kwaŋin;
Kwape abu kenerunia, etogogoŋi kwaŋin.
25Abilari Eyasuriat kakwapuka,
Ecakacakai koroŋanaka:
Elemar akejookot kamakec,
Abwokonike da kokeperinikec.”
26Ŋin ŋes ainerun nainerunitai kakwap kere:
Ŋin ŋes akan naerioro mama ejaas atekerin kere.
27 # 2 Siit 20:6; Yob 40:8; Isa 23:9; Yer 4:28. Naarai EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ŋes inerunit,
Iŋai da itapalauni?
Erioro akekan, iŋai da inyakakini?
28 # 2 Ika 16:20; 2 Siit 28:27. Kokaru loatwania ekabaka Akas kojaun abwokon na ebe:
29 # Yer 47:1-7; Esk 25:15-17; Yok 4:4-8; Amo 1:6-8. “Sirikiyalamikin, ijo Pilisitia kere,
Naarai abila ebela lokiinomi:
Naarai kotoma aliasit naka emun elomuni asigirikoloŋ,
Aurike da eraun emun loesarani loeporitoi.
30Enyami yenikiyai yenika lokican, Eperi da yenilwaritoi kainapakina:
Aari eoŋ aliasitikon kebeli, Eario aatoŋotokon.
31Kibwolol, ijo erut, Komony, ijo ere;
Kocalar ijo Pilisitia kere;
Naarai elomuni apuru kari naka agolitomei,
Kwabo emamei yenepaari korioŋetakec.”
32Aso eboŋokini biai ikiyakia luka ateker?
Ebe, “Abu EJAKAIT kibwaik apetetait naka Sion,
Ŋes ekuianarete luereitai luka ikeruna kotomakec.”
