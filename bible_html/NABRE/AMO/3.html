Amos 3

3
1Kiirasi akirot na nakinerak yes EJAKAIT, wu idwe luka Isirael, ka ajen kere naetonyouni alemun kakwap nako Misiri kabala ebe:
2 # Esk 9:6; Dan 9:12; Mat 11:22; Luk 12:47; Rom 2:9; 1 Pet 4:17. “Yes bonik abu kaien kajenasinei kere nuka akwap:
ŋes ketieania yes kanuka numam adolitekus kere.
3“Epedorete kereka iarei aup,
Arai mam imorikikina?
4Egat kereka eŋatuny kamagoro,
Komam emejaunit?
Ikuna kereka iŋatuny kotoma abuake,
Arai mam ikamunit ibore?
5Epedori kereka akweny aipok kokwap,
Komam elokitai elabwata?
Ejetakin kereka aloket kokwap,
Arai mam ipokit ibore?
6 # Esk 33:3. Ekutakinio kereka arupepe kore,
Komam ituŋa ekuriakaros?
Edoikin kereka aronis ore,
Arai mam EJAKAIT eycŋaunit?
7 # Age 6:13; Awa 3:32; Dan 9:22-27; Yok 15:15; Apu 1:1,19. Abeit emameete nuiswamai EJAKAIT Edeke,
Dimarai epukokinit ikebulesin inabin akeiyeiyea.
8 # 1 Kor 9:16. Agatu eŋatuny;
Iŋai bo mam ekurianar?
Ener EJAKAIT Edeke,
Iŋai bo ekurwokini aidwar?”
9Kolimonosi kotogoi luka Asudod,
Kotogoi da kakwap nako Misiri,
Kineras atiar, “Kotukonokinos koroŋan luka Samaria, Koanyut iceleta luetia nen lueiaas toma,
Ka imwaŋito da kwape ejaatar kidiŋ nen.”
10Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
“Naarai mam ejenete aswam naejok,
ŋul luinadaete epali ka aiŋat kotogoikec.”
11 # 2 Ika 17:6. Ŋes inerar EJAKAIT Edeke kwanata ebe:
“Ejaun lokasurup, airiamar akwap iwaitin kere:
Itojoni akonigogoŋ,
Eŋasio da ikonitogoi lukapolou.”
12Inera kwanata EJAKAIT ebe:” Kwape edemunia ekecokon akeie aarei arai itutubet yenikakit kaituk naka eŋatuny; eyuunio kwaŋin idwe luka lsirael luibojete koSamaria kotoma agor naka aperis, ka aicolonisio nuka aliiri nukotut.”
13Inera EJAKAIT Edeke, Edeke loka aioresio ebe:
“Kiirasi, koraut ikaienak kokek loka Yakob.
14 # 2 Ika 23:15. Naarai kapaaran naeticania Isirael adiakanaretake,
Eticani da itemwan luka Bet-el,
Eiepio da amumwar nuka etem,
Kibirokinos kwap.”
15Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
“Atiyakini etogo loka aiporo, ka etogo da loka akamu;
Eoliorete itogoi luka ikel luka etom,
Enaŋete da itogoi lukapolou.”
