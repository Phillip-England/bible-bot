9
1 # Esr 2:59; Nek 7:5; Leb 26:33; 2 Siit 33:11; 36:5. Kwaŋin aponi kimarai Isirael kere kwape ajaar auriokee; ido koany iwadikatai oitabo loka ikabakan luka Isirael; kijoreoroi Yuda Babulon kanuka adiakarikee. 2#Esr 2:70; Nek 7:73; Yos 9:27; Esr 2:43; 8:20. Luageete aiboikin airumetakee, oreriakee kes lu, Isirael, isasedotin, Ilebin, ka Inetinim, 3#Nek 11:1. Kibojete ko Yerusalem lukodwe luka Yuda, ka lukodwe luka Benyamin, ka lukodwe luka Epuraim ka Manase; 4#Age 46:12; Mar 26:20. Utai okoku loka Amikud, okoku loka Omiri, okoku loka Imiri, okoku loka Bani, Iokodwe luka Peres okoku loka Yuda, 5Luka Isilonin; Asaia ekiyaike, ka odweke. 6Lukodwe luka Sera; Yeuel, ka iinaeanakee, akwat akanykape ka akaisakanykaoŋon, 7Lukodwe luka Benyamin; Salu okoku loka Mesulam, okoku loka Kodabia, okoku loka Kasenua; 8ka Ibuneia okoku loka Yerokam, ka Ela okoku loka Usi, okoku loka Mikiri, ka Mesulam okoku loka Sepatia, okoku loka Reuel, okoku loka Ibiniya; 9ka iinacanakec, kwape ajaatar aturiokee, akwat akanykaoŋon ka akaisakany ka lukanykape. Ituŋa ŋul kere kes ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee kwape ajaatar ikekia luka lukapapaakec.
10 # Nek 11:10; 12:19. Lukosasedotin; Yedaia, ka Yekoi-arib, ka Yakin; 11ka Asaria okoku loka Kilikia, okoku loka Mesulam, okoku loka Sadok, okoku loka Meraiot, okoku loka Akitub, ekapolokiton etogo loka Edeke; 12ka Adaia okoku loka Yerokam, okoku loka Pasur, okoku loka Malikiya, ka Maasai okoku loka Adiel, okoku loka Yasera, okoku loka Mesulam, okoku loka Mesilemit, okoku loka Imer; 13ka iinaeanakec, ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee, elukumit ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisakanykapc; ituŋa lukapedor noi aswam aijaanakin naka etogo loka Edeke.
14 # 1 Siit 6:19; Nek 11:15-19. Luka Ilebin; Semaia okoku loka Kasub, okoku loka Asirikam, okoku loka Kasabia, lokodwe luka Merari; 15ka Bakabakar, Keres, ka Galal, ka Matania okoku loka Mika, okoku loka Sikiri, okoku loka Asap; 16#1 Siit 2:54; Nek 7:26; 12:28. ka Obadia okoku loka Semaia, okoku loka Galal, okoku loka Yedutun, ka Berekia okoku loka Asa, okoku loka Elukana, lueboienenete koreria ludidik luka Inetopatin.
17 # 1 Siit 23:5; 26:1; Sab 84:10. Ka ikagolok; Salum, ka Akub, ka Talumon, ka Akiman, ka iinaeanakec: Salum ŋes ekapolon; 18#2 Ika 11:19; Esk 44:23. lu lem kidarenenete erute loka ekabaka ari nakokide: keraas kesi ikagolok luka iidukurun luka idwe luka Lebi. 19#Mar 26:9,11; 1 Siit 6:33,38. Koanyanar Salum okoku loka Kore, okoku loka Ebiasap, okoku loka Kora, ka iinacanake, lukokek loka papaake, Ikorakin, aswam naka aijaanakin, ikagolok luka ikekia luka Ewema lokalaunan: keraas lukapapaakee ikapolok luka iidukurun luka EJAKAIT, ikagolok luka ekek; 20#Any 6:25; Mar 3:32; 25:11; 31:6; Yos 22:30,31; Sab 106:30. Kerai Pinekas okoku loka Eleasar ekapolonikee kolo sek, kojai EJAKAIT kaŋes, 21Kerai Sekaria okoku loka Meselemia ekagolon loka ekek loka ewema loka airiamunun. 22#1 Siit 26:1,2; 1 Sam 9:9. Ŋul kerc luaponi koseunai araun ikagolok luka ikekia, emwaasi akwat aarei ka itomon ka luarei. Aponi nul kimarai kauriokee koreriakee ludidik, luapotu Daudi ka Samuel ekadwaran kotiakut aswam aswamukec nakemunoi. 23Kosodete kesi ka idwekec koanyanarete ikekia lukotogo loka EJAKAIT, ŋes etogo loka ewema lokalaunan, kameanakin. 24#1 Siit 26:13-19. Keiaananas ikagolok owaitin iwonon, kide, ka to, ka eŋalakimak, ka agolitomei. 25#2 Ika 11:5; 2 Siit 23:8. Koponenenete iinaeanakec, koreriakec ludidik, kanunupaarasia akanykaarei apakio kapakio ajaut kakes: 26Naarai ikagolok luonon lukapolok, lukeraas Ilebin, ajaas kaswam naatiakakinitai, kopolokinito agolai ka aiŋadisia nuka iboro kaluketiai kotogo loka Edeke. 27Kiboienenete airimor etogo loka Edeke, naarai aidar nakee, nakee da anaanar ŋinitupuruc.
28Kidarenenete ice kakesi iboro luka aijaanakin; naarai emareneneo nakitolomanarere ka nakitolomununere. 29Kotiakakinitcte ice kakesi aidar iboro lukotogo, ka iboro kere luka aibois nakalaunan, ka akiria nuidieie, ka ebino, ka akinyet, ka lukisupurununo, ka lueur maa. 30#Any 30:23; 37:29; Eko 1:3,13; Yer 6:20; Esk 27:19,22; Mk 14:3. Kitemokis ice kodwe luka isasedotin eiswamae loka lueur maa. 31#Leb 6:21. Aponi kotiakakinitete Matitia, ediopet koLebin, lokerai ekiyai loka Salum Ekorakit aidar iboro lupeon. 32#Any 40:23; Bur 9:2,24; Yok 6:32,33. Kopolokinito ice koinacanakee, lukodwe luka Ikokatin akiro nuka cmugati loka aitodikin, aitemokin ŋes ninipaaran nakasabato,
33 # 1 Siit 6:31; 13:8; 15:16. Ŋul kes ikayook, ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakec luka Ilebin, lueboienenete kagolai Iuemameete aceswam: naarai kiswamaenenere aswamukec apaaran ka akuar. 34Ŋul kes ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakec luka Ilebin, anatar aturiokee, ikapolok: kiboienenete nul koYerusalem,
35 # 1 Siit 8:29. Kiboie Yeicl, papa loka Gibeon, koGibeon, ekiror loka akeberu Maaka: 36Abudon okokuke lokiyai, ka Sur, ka Kis, ka Baal, ka Ner, ka Nadab; 37ka Gedor, ka Akio, ka Sekaria, ka Mikilot. 38Mikilot kouri Simeam. Kibojete kesi da kiinaeanakee koYerusalem, eiduny kiinacanakec, 39#1 Siit 8:33. Ner kouri Kis; Kis kouri Saul; Saul kouri Yonatan, ka Malikisua, ka Abinadab, ka Es-baal. 40Okoku loka Yonatan ŋes Merib-baal; Merib-baal kouri Mika. 41#2 Sam 9:12; 1 Siit 8:35. Odwe luka Mika; Piton, ka Mclek, ka Tarea, ka Akas. 42Akas kouri Yara; Yara kouri Alemet, ka Asumabet, ka Simiri; Simiri kouri Mosa: 43Mosa kouri Binea; ka Repaia okokuke, Eleasa okokuke, Asel okokuke: 44Kejaas Ascl odwe ikanykape, ikecekiroria lu; Asirikam, ka Bokeru, ka Isimael, ka Scaria, ka Obadia, ka Kanan: nul kes odwe luka Asel.
