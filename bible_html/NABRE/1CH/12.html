1 Aisiitonokin 12

12
1 # 1 Sam 27:2,6. Lu kes mil Iuapotu kopotu mama ejai Daudi Sikilag, kapak nakenopitor kanuka Saul okoku loka Kis: koraas kotuŋa lukirigin, ikenara kinakake kojie. 2#Tub 20:16. Kokapitos akabwai, apedorete aitwasam akan nakoteten ka nakokediany da aporoeanaria alica, ka agumanaria imalia da kakabwa; koraas luka iinaean luka Saul luka Benyamin, 3#Yos 21:18; 1 Siit 11:28. Lokapolon ŋes Akieser, kosodi Yoas, odwe luka Semaa Egibeatit; ka Yesiel, ka Pelet, odwe luka Asumabet; ka Bereka, ka Yeku Enatotait; 4#Yos 10:2; 18:21,25; 1 Ika 3:4,5; 9:2; Isa 28:21. ka Isimaia Egibeonit, etuŋanan lokirigi kotoma akaisauni, loapolokinit akaisauni; ka Yeremia, ka Yakasiel, ka Yokanan, ka Yosabad Egederatit; 5Elusai, ka Yerimot, ka Bealia, ka Semaria, ka Sepatia Ekarupit; 6Elukana, ka Isia, ka Asarel, ka Yoeser, ka Yasobeam, Ikorakin; 7ka Yoela, ka Sebadia, odwe luka Yerokam loka Gedor.
8 # 1 Sam 23:14,29; 1 Siit 11:16; 2 Sam 17:10; 23:20; 1 Siit 11:22; Awa 28:1; 2 Sam 2:18. Luka Igadin apotu kogitakinos mama ejai Daudi aibois naebwaitai modiŋ, ituŋa lukirigin luetitin, ituŋa luetuutooritai ejie, luapedorete aitwasam ebuku ka akwara; akonyenekec koputokinos kakonyen nuka iŋatunyo, ketiroko kesi kwape kamorio koron an, 9Eser lokapolon, Obadia lokiareit, Eliab lokiwuniet; 10Misimana Iokiwononet, Yeremia kokianyet; 11Atai lokikanyetekape, Eliel lokikanyetckaarei; 12Yokanan lokikanyetekauni, Elisaba lokikanyetekaon on; 13Yeremia lokitomonet, Makubanai lokitomonet kalodiop. 14Keraas nul lukodwe luka Gad ikapolok luka ajore; nol lokerai lodidi kakesi keriakas kakwatat, lokapolon da kelukumit. 15#Yos 3:15; Yer 12:5; 49:19. Ŋul kes luapotu kodokas Yorodan kolap losodit, kedau ŋesi ailapanar aririke kere; kitekerata nul kere luka aditon, kiton kide, koto da.
16Kopotu lukodwe luka Benyamin ka Yuda mama ejai aibois naebwaitai mama ejai Daudi. 17#Sek 3:2; Yud 1:9. Koloma Daudi airiamakin kakes, Koboŋok kotema atiar, “Arai ca iponito yesi inapakina mama ajai aitjarakin eoŋ, kimorikikin ekatau ka lukus; konye arai iponito abilakin eoŋ ebotet mama ejaas lukasurupoka, naarai emamei aronis kakaninika, kosesen Edeke loka lukapapaawok, kitoron.” 18#2 Sam 17:25; 1 Siit 2:17. Kosodi emoyo abunokin Amasai lokerai lokapolon loka akaisauni, kotema atiar,
“Kirai isio lukon, Daudi,
owaikon, ijo okoku loka Yese.
Ainapakin, ainapakin kojai mama ŋal,
ainapakin da mama ejaas ikeijarakinakakon!
Naarai kiŋarakinit Edekekon.”
Kosodi Daudi aisukunyuun kes, kitarau kes ikapolok luka erioŋet.
19 # 1 Sam 29:2,4. Luka Manase da apotu ice kolunakinos mama ejai Daudi naabunio nepepe ka Ipilisitian asurokin Saul kojie, konye mam apotu kinarakis kes: naarai kinerut ijaka luka Ipilisitian, kotwarata ŋes, koba lasi ebe, “Epedori alunakin mama ejai ekapolonike Saul kosodi ayaun akaŋas akwesiwok.” 20Naalosio ŋesi Sikilag, kolunakinos mamake luka Manase, Aduna, ka Yosabad, ka Yediael, ka Mikael, ka Yosabad, ka Eliku, ka Siletai, ikapolok luka ilukumin lukeraas luka Manase. 21#1 Sam 30:1. Kiŋarakis Daudi asurokin erioŋet loka ikaŋatak; naarai keraas kesi kere ituŋa lukirigin luetitin, koraas ikapolok luka ajore. 22Naarai ŋinipaaran kopotu ituŋa mama ejai Daudi aiŋarakin ŋes, aitodol nakeraunor ajore naepol, kwape ajore naka Edeke.
23 # 2 Sam 2:3; 1 Siit 10:14; 11:10; 1 Sam 16:1. Lu kes itiaisinei luka ikapolok luka ŋul kaluakapitos lujiis, luapotu kopotu mama ejai Daudi Keburon, aibelokin ajakanut naka Saul mamake, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT. 24Idwe luka Yuda lukitinitos ibukui ka akwaras emwaasi ilukumin ikanykape ka akwat akanykauni, luakapitos luiiis. 25Lukodwe luka Simeon, ituŋa lukirigin luetitin ejie, ilukumin ikanykaarei ka akwatat, 26Lukodwe luka Lebi ilukumin iwonon ka akwat akanykape. 27Yekoiada ŋes ekenarenikinon loka ekek loka Aaron, kojaas kaŋes ilukumin iuni ka akwat akanykaarei; 28#2 Sam 8:17; 1 Ika 1:8; 2:25; 1 Siit 6:8; Esk 44:15. Sadok ekatumunan lokirigi loetitiŋ, ka lukotogo loka papake ikapolok akaisaarei ka luarei. 29Lukodwe luka Benyamin, iinaean luka Saul, ilukumin iuni: naarai aitodol apakio nun keraas luitelekarito aipues lukiketakina atupar etogo loka Saul. 30Lukodwe luka Epuraim ilukumin akaisaarei ka akwat akanykauni, ituŋa lukirigin luetitin, ituŋa lukodwaratau kokekia luka lukapapaakec. 31Lukatutubet naka ateker naka Manase ilukumin itomon ka lukanykauni, luabilitai ikiroriakee, aponare aitaraun Daudi ekabaka. 32#Est 1:13. Lukodwe luka Isakar, ituŋa luajenete apakio, ajenun nuibusakinit Isirael aswam: ikapolokokec emwaasi akwat aarei; kiswamata iinacanakec kere kwape ajaar aicorakinetekec. 33Luka Sebulun, luapedorete alomar kaiore, luapedorete aiderakin ejie, lukejaas kiboro lukiponesio luipu lujiis, ilukumin akaisakany; luapedorete aiderakin ejie, luamameete itauon iarei, 34Luka Naputali ikapolok elukumit, nepepe kakes da lukejaas ibukui ka akwaras ilukumin akaisauni ka lukanykaarei. 35Luka Idanin luapedorete aiderakin ejie, ilukumin akaisaarei ka lukanykauni ka akwat akanykape. 36Luka Aser, luapedorete alomar kajore, luapedorete aiderakin ejie, ilukumin akaisaoŋon. 37Okekwa juwai Yorodan, luka Ireubenin, ka Igadin, ka atutubet naka ateker naka Manase, lukejaas kiboro lukiponesio luipu lujiis ilukumin akwatat ka ilukumin akaisaarei.
38Ŋul kere, ituŋa lukajiok, luapedorete aiderakin ejie, apotu kopotu Keburon ketau loetenara, aitaraun Daudi ekabaka loka Isirael kere: luka Isirael ice kere da kejaas ketau ediopet aitaraun Daudi ekabaka, 39Kejaas nina nepepe ka Daudi apaarasia auni, enyamete, emasete: naarai apotu iinacanakec kitemokis kanukec, 40Bobo da nul luedunyas kakes, kiton Isakar ka Sebulun ka Naputali, kibwokut inyamat kasigirian, kakaalai, konyubun, komonin, inyamat luka akiria, alunureta nuka itinin, aibuneta nueokito nuka imusabibun, ka ebino, ka akinyet, ka imoŋin, ka imerekekin, epu noi; naarai kejai aiyalama koIsirae!’
