1 Aisiitonokin 11

11
1 # 2 Sam 5:1; 1 Siit 12:23. Kosodi Isirael kere atukonokin mama ejai Daudi Keburon, kotemasi atiar, “Koany, kirai isio akoitikon ka akirinikon. 2#Sab 78:70,71. Kapakio nua tuboros nukeraar Saul ekabaka, ibu ijo kitoloma kitolom da Isirael: abu EJAKAIT Ekonideke kotema jo atiar, ‘Itanyamenenei ijo ikatuŋa Isirael, iraun ijo ekapolon loka ikatuŋa Isirael.’” 3#2 Sam 5:3; Rom 8:31; 1 Sam 16:1. Kwaŋin koporu imakio kere luka Isirael mama ejai ekabaka Keburon; kitutuk Daudi kakes koKeburon kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: kinyonyoikis Daudi akinyet araun ekabaka loka Isirael, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT kama ka Samuel.
4 # 2 Sam 5:6; Age 10:16; 15:21; Any 3:17; Tub 1:21; 19:10. Kolot Daudi ka Isirael kere Yerusalem (ŋol ŋes Yebus); ajaas kanen Iyebusin lukiboiete kakwap, 5Kotemasi lukiboiete koYebus Daudi atiar, “Mam ilomuni ne.” Konye abu Daudi kirum ere loegogon loka Sion; ŋol ŋes ere loka Daudi. 6Kotema Daudi atiar, “Loegeuni aidany Iyebusin, eraun ŋol ekapolon ka ekayaiton.” Kogeuni do Yoab okoku loka Seruia alomar, kitaraunai ekapolon. 7#2 Sam 5:7. Kiboikin Daudi ere loegogon; kanuka ŋun aponi komaikinai ere loka Daudi. 8Koduk ere airimor, anyoun koMilo ka airimor: kiteteu Yoab aiboisio ace nukore, 9Kopoloor Daudi noi noi; naarai ajaenena EJAKAIT loka ajoresio kaŋes,
10 # 2 Sam 23:8; 1 Sam 16:1,12. Lu kes ikapolok luka ituŋa lukirigin lukejaas kaDaudi, luapotu kitodiara bonik Iukagogon mamake kaiakanutuke, nepepe ka Isirael kere, aitaraun ŋes ekabaka, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Isirael. 11Lo ŋes etiai loka ituŋa lukirigin lukejaas ka Daudi: Yasobeam, okoku loka Ekakumonit, ekapolon loka akaisauni; kodaku ŋol akekwara asurokin akwat auni koar kes katipet.
12Kotupite ŋes Eleasar okoku loka Dodo, Ekokait, lokerai ediopet kotuna iuni lukirigin, 13#1 Sam 17:1. Ajaas ŋes ka Daudi koPas-damin, etukokina Ipilisitian nina ejie komanikor loeleleba esairi; kokerata ituŋa kakonyen nuka Ipilisitian. 14#Awa 21:31. Kobwaata kesi kokidin omanikor, koiiut ŋes, koarata Ipilisitian; kitaiar EJAKAIT kes kaipikor naepol.
15 # Yos 12:15; 2 Sam 23:13; 1 Siit 14:9; Isa 17:5. Kikokororos iuni kotoma akaisauni lukapolok atiiba mama ejai Daudi, kabua naka Adulam; kidukurite aiore naka Ipilisitian aditot naka Repaim. 16Keiai Daudi toma abwas, keiai abwas naka iserikalei luka Ipilisitian Betlekem. 17Kimiela Daudi, kotema atiar, “Arai kainak idiotuŋanan eoŋ akipi aimat nuka atan naka Bet-lekem naidunyas kerute!” 18Kocila luuni ajore naka Ipilisitian, kowokut akipi katan naka Bet-lekem, naidunyas kerute, koyanas, koinutu Daudi: konye koner Daudi aimat, konye kiwela mama ejai EJAKAIT. 19Kotema atiar, “Madi akuj, eoŋ aswam nin: amasi kereka aokot nuka ituŋa kalu lualosito akaŋas naka aijarakec? Naarai apotu koyaut kakaŋas naka aiiarakec.” Kanuka nun koner aimat, Kiswamata ituŋa luuni lukirigin akiro nun,
20 # 1 Sam 26:6-8; 2 Sam 2:18; 3:20; 18:2; 1 Siit 2:16. Abisai, onace loka Yoab, ŋes lokapolon loka luuni; naarai kodaku ŋesi akwarake asurokin akwat auni koar kes, kodum ekiror kotoma luuni. 21#2 Sam 23:19; 1 Kor 15:41. Kitopolooritete ŋes adepar luarei kouni, kitaraunai ekapolonikec: konye mam abu kodolok luuni lusodit.
22 # 2 Sam 1:23; 23:20; 1 Siit 12:8. Benaia okoku loka Yekoiada, okoku loka etuŋanan loetitin leka Kabuseel, loabu kiswama aswamisio nukagogon, abu ŋesi koar odwe luarei luka Ariel loka Moab: kiiesar da, koar eŋatuny kokidin aipany kapak naka akako, 23Koar ŋesi Emisiriat da, etuŋanan lokos loeoja ikubitin ikany; kitiŋite Emisiriat akwara kakanike naaputos kakitoi naesonyere; kolot ŋol mama ejai ŋesi kebela, kodem akwara kakan naka Emisiriat, koar ŋes kakwarake elopet, 24Kiswama Benaia okoku loka Yekoiada akiro nun, kodum ekiror kotuna luuni lukirigin. 25Koany, kitopolooritete ŋes adepar akaisauni, konye mam abu kodolok luuni lusodit: kodakak Daudi ŋes ayait ikedarakake.
26 # 2 Sam 2:18-23; 23:24; 1 Siit 27:7. Bobo da ituŋa lukirigin luka aioresio; Asakel onace loka Yoab, Elukanan okoku loka Dodo loka Bet-lekem; 27Samot Ekarorit, Keles Epelonit; 28Ira okoku loka Ikes Etekoit, Abieser Enatotait; 29Sibekai Ekusatit, Ilai Ekokait; 30Makarai Enetopatit, Keled okoku loka Baana Enetopatit; 31Itai okoku loka Ribai loka Gibea loka idwe luka Benyamin, Benaia Epiratonit; 32Kurai loka aguuruto nuka Gaas, Abiel Erabatait; 33Asumabet Ebakarumit, Eliaba Esaalubonit; 34Odwe luka Kasem Egisonit, Yonatan okoku loka Sage Ekararit; 35Akiam okoku loka Sakar Ekararit, Elipal okoku loka Ur; 36Keper Emekeratit, Akiya Epelonit; 37Kesiro Ekaramelit, Naarai okoku loka Esibai; 38Yoel onaee loka Natan, Mibar okoku loka Kagiri; 39Selek Emonait, Nakarai Eberotit, loadakit lujiis luka Yoab okoku loka Seruia; 40#Yos 15:48; 1 Siit 2:50,53. Ira Itirit, Gareb Itirit; 41#2 Sam 11:3. Uria Ekitit, Sabad okoku loka Alai; 42Adina okoku loka Sisa Ereubenit, ekapolon loka Ireubenin, ka akaisauni nepepe kaŋes; 43Kanan okoku loka Maaka, ka Yosapat Emitinit; 44Usia Esiteratait, Sama ka Yeiel odwe luka Kotam Eroerait; 45Yediael okoku loka Simiri, ka Yoka onaeeke, Etisit; 46Eliel Emakabit, ka Yeribai, ka Yosabia, odwe luka Elunaam, ka Itima Emoabit; 47Eliel, ka Obed, ka Yaasiel Emesobait.
