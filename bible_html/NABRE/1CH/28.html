1 Aisiitonokin 28

28
1 # Yos 23:2; 24:1; 1 Siit 23:2; 27:16; Any 18:25; Ikis 1:15; 1 Siit 27:1,25; 11:10. Kotukonok Daudi ikapolok kere luka Isirael Yerusalem, ikapolok luka atekerin, ka ikapolok luka irioneta lukebeanakinos aijaanakin eka baka, ka ikapolok luka ilukumin, ka ikapolok luka akwatat, ka ikayaitok luka iboro kere ka ibaren luka ekabaka ka odweke, nepepe kikapolokitok, ka ituŋa lukirigin, kes ituŋa kere lukirigin luetitin, 2#Ikis 17:20; 1 Siit 11:1-3; Sab 22:22; Bur 2:11; 2 Sam 7:2; 1 Ika 8:17; 1 Siit 17:2; 2 Siit 6:8,9; Sab 132:3,7. Kosodi Daudi ekabaka aibwo kakejeke, kotema atiar, “Kopupokinai, ikainaean, ka ikatuŋa; keiai otaukan adukokin asaduku naka aitutuket naka EJAKAIT, ka eicoloŋ loka akeje nuka Edekewok etogo loka aiyeŋun; ido abu ketemokinite aiduk. 3#2 Sam 7:5; 1 Ika 5:3; 1 Siit 17:4; 22:8. Konye abu Edeke katema eoŋ atiar, ‘Mam jo idukokini ekakiror etogo, naarai irai ijo etuŋanan lokijiisio, ibu kobuko aokot.’ 4#1 Sam 16:7; Age 49:8; 1 Sam 16:1,12. Konye abu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, kaseu eoŋ kokek kere loka papaka araun ekabaka loka Isirael ikar kere: naarai abu koseu Yuda araun ekapolon; kokek loka Yuda da, ekek loka papaka; kodwe luka papaka kalelak eoŋ aitaraun ekabaka loka Isirael kere: 5#1 Siit 3:1; 14:4-7; 22:9. Kodweka da kere (naarai kainak EJAKAIT eoŋ idwe lusap luipu), abu koseu Solomon aiboikin oicoloŋ loka ajakanut naka EJAKAIT airum Isirael. 6#2 Siit 1:9. Kalimok eoŋ ebe, ‘Solomon okokukon, ŋes eduki ekatogo ka akalaroi: naarai aseu eoŋ ŋes araun okokuka, eoŋ da araun papake. 7#1 Ika 9:4; 11:11; 1 Siit 22:13. Etieasi akejakanut ikar kere, arai kinyikoki aiswamaenen aicorakinetaka ka itubesiaka, kwape lolo.’ 8#Ikis 4:1. Aso do, kakonyen nuka Isirael kere, ekodet loka EJAKAIT, kopupi Edekewok, korieenenete komoenenete aicorakineta kere nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke: tetere irumete akwap na naejok, kinyeiniata araun airumet naka ikusidwe lukitupanakinete yes ikar kere,
9 # Sab 9:10; Yer 9:24; Kos 4:1; Yok 17:3; 2 Ika 20:3; Sab 101:2; 1 Sam 16:7; 1 Ika 8:39; 1 Siit 29:17; Apu 2:23; 2 Siit 15:2; Mat 7:7; Yak 4:8,10. “Ŋo da, Solomon okokuka, kojenu Edeke loka papakon, kiiaanakinenei ŋes ketau loedolit ka aomisio nuekotosi: naarai enici EJAKAIT itauon kere, ejeŋi aitetemonor kere nuka aomisio: arai imoenenei ŋes, kitakananakini jo: konye arai iialenenei ŋes, kirenei jo ikar kere. 10#1 Siit 22:16; Awa 4:23. Aso koeoite; naarai kiseu EJAKAIT jo adukokin aibois nakalaunan etogo: aso kicat, kiswama.”
11 # Any 25:40; 1 Ika 6:2. Kosodi Daudi ainakin Solomon okokuke aput naka alomaret naka eyekalu, ka naka itogoike, ka aiŋadisiake, ka agolaike nukokuju, ka agolaike nukotoma, ka aibois naka eicoloŋ-lokaitim: 12#1 Siit 26:20. Ka aput naka nukejaas omoyoke kere, nuka alaroi nuka etogo loka EJAKAIT, ka agolai kere nuelukunito, naka aiŋadisia nuka etogo loka Edeke, ka naka aiŋadisia nuka iboro luatiakunitai. 13Babo da nuka abeanakineta nuka isasedotin ka Ilebin, ka aswam kere naka aijaanakin kotogo loka EJAKAIT, ka iboro kere luka aijaanakin kotogo loka EJAKAIT. 14Esabu loitemitoi loka iboro luka esabu, iboro kere luka nolipone loka aijaanakin; epuesa loitemitoi loka iboro kere luka epuesa, iboro kere luka ŋolipone loka aijaanakin. 15Itemem da esabu loka ipirin luka atalai, loka atalaike, nuka esabu, loetemem loka nolipir loka atala ka atalaike: ido loka ipirin luka atalai luka epuesa, epuesa loetemem loka nolipir loka atala, ka atalaike, kwape kejaar aijaanakin naka nolipir loka atala. 16#Any 25:23; 1 Ika 7:48; 2 Siit 4:8. Etemem da esabu loka imesan luka imugatin luka aitodikin, kaŋolimesa; epuesa da loka imesan luka epuesa. 17Ka igolun luka akiriŋ, ka atanya, ka akolin, nuka esabu loelai: etemem da esabu loka atuba, kaninituba; etemem da epuesa loka atuba, ka ninituba. 18#Any 30:1; 25:18; 1 Sam 4:4; 1 Ika 6:23; Sab 18:10; 68:17; Esk 10:2. Etemem do esabu loililiauna loka etem loka lukisupurununo: esabu da loka aput naka agali, kes ikerubim, luigararaete abebenokakec, arapaar asaduku naka aitutuket naka EJAKAIT. 19#Any 25:40; 26:30. Kobala Daudi ebe, “Ŋun kere ketejenunete eoŋ kaiwadikaeta kakan naka EJAKAIT, kes aswamisio kere nuka aput kana.”
20 # Ikis 31:7; Yos 1:5-7; 1 Siit 22:3; 1 Kor 16:13; Bur 13:5. Kotema Daudi Solomon okokuke atiar, “Kicat, kotitinior, kiswama nun; sirikikurianar, karaida aiyaloŋoŋ: naarai EJAKAIT Edeke, ŋes Ekadeke ejaenena kajo; mam ŋes isokori kamakon, mam kijali jo, aitodol needaunor aswam kere naka aijaanakin naka etogo loka EJAKAIT. 21#1 Siit 24:1; 25:1; Any 35:25; 36:1. Ido koany, ejaas abeanakineta nuka isasedotin ka Ilebin, nuka aijaanakin kere nakaetogo loka Edeke: ejaun kajo nolitunanan loekoto loepiriana, kanolipone loka aijaanakin kotoma aswam naka ŋolipone: erimakinete da eut ikapolok ka ituŋa kere aicorakinetekon.’
