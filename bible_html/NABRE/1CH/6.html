31 # 1 Siit 16:1; 2 Sam 6:17; 1 Ika 8:4. Lu kes luabu Daudi kitopoloo aijaanakin naka ayoore togo loka EJAKAIT, kedau asaduku aibworar. 32Kijaanakis kayoore koiijaren kewema lokalaunan loka ewema loka airiamunun, aitodol neadukokinia Solomon etogo loka EJAKAIT Yerusalem, kidarata aswamukec kwape ajaar abeanakinikec. 33Lu kes luedarenenete, ka odwekec. 49 # Any 29:38; 30:1; Leb 1:9; 10:19,20; Mar 3:10; 18:7; Bur 5:1; Any 30:7; 1 Sam 2:28; 1 Siit 23:13; Luk 1:9. Kateker naka Benyamin; Geba ka ironyiake, ka Alemet ka ironyiake, ka Anatot ka ironyiake, Ireriakee kere anatar ikekiakec emwaasi ireria itomon ka luuni.
61 # Yos 21:5. Aponi koinakinai luedeuna luka odwe luka Kokat koroet, kokek loka ateker, katutubet naka ateker, atutubet naka Manase, ireria itomon. 62Mama ejaas odwe luka Geresom, kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, kateker naka Isakar, kateker naka Aser, kateker naka Naputali, kateker da naka Manase koBasan, ireria itomon ka luuni. 63#Yos 21:7,34. Aponi koinakinai odwe luka Merari koroet, kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, kateker naka Reuben, kateker naka Gad, kateker naka Sebulun, ireria itomon ka luarei. 64#Mar 35:1-8; Yos 21:41,42. Apotu idwe luka Isirael koinakis Ilebin ireria ka ironyiakec, 65Apotu kesi koinakis koroet kateker naka idwe luka Yuda, kateker naka idwe luka Simeon, kateker naka idwe luka Benyamin, ireria ŋul luebilitai ikiroriakec.
