1 Aisiitonokin 25

25
1 # 1 Siit 12:28; 23:2; 6:33; 15:17; Any 15:20; Mar 11:25; 1 Sam 10:5; 1 Siit 16:4; 23:5; Sab 150:3,5; 1 Kor 14:1; Apu 15:2,4. Bobo da abu Daudi ka ikapolok luka aiore kotiak ice lukodwe luka Asap, ka luka Keman, ka luka Yedutun, aijaanakin, luedwarete kadeudeun, ka isabulin, ka ikacwi: lo ŋes etiaikec kanul kalueswamaete aswam kwape ajaar aijaanakinikec: 2Lukodwe luka Asap; Sakur, ka Yosep, ka Netania, ka Asarela, odwe luka Asap; kakan naka Asap luadwarenenete kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka ekabaka, 3Luka Yedutun: odwe luka Yedutun; Gedalia, ka Seri, ka Yesaia, ka Kasabia, ka Matitia, ikanykape; kakan naka papakec Yedutun kadeudeu, luadwarenenete kaimal kaiturit EJAKAIT. 4Luka Keman: odwe luka Keman; Bukia, ka Matania, ka Usiel, ka Sebuel, ka Yerimot, ka Kanania, ka Kanani, ka Eliata, ka Gidaliti, ka Romamiti-eser, ka Yosebekasa, ka Maloti, ka Kotir, ka Makasiot: 5#1 Sam 9:9; 2 Sam 24:11; 1 Siit 21:9; 26:28; Amo 7:12; 1 Pet 4:11; Age 33:5; 1 Sam 1:17; 1 Siit 28:5; Sab 127:3; 128:3; Isa 3:18. Ŋul kere odwe luka Keman ekadwaran loka ekabaka kakiro nuka Edeke, aikeun amumwarat. Abu Edeke koinak Keman idwe lusap itomon ka luonon ka lupesur iuni. 6Ŋul kere kejaas kakan naka papakee ayoore kotogo loka EJAKAIT, kikacwi, ka isabulin, ka adeudeun, aijaanakin kotogo loka Edeke; Asap, ka Yedutun, ka Keman lukeiaas kaicorakinet naka ekabaka, 7#Sab 150:1; Pes 5:19; Kol 3:16; Yak 5:13. Etiaikee, nepepe kiinacanakec luetuutooritai ayeonokin EJAKAIT, kes kere lukepiriaka, emwaasi akwat aarei ka akaisakanykauni ka ikanykauni. 8#2 Siit 23:13. Apotu kilamilamata ajenun abeanakinikec, kere koriakanara, ludidik ka lukapolok, ekesisianakinan ka ekesisian.
9Kobeik eroet losodit Yosep loka Asap; lokiareit Gedalia; ŋes ka iinaeanake ka odweke emwaasi itomon kaluarei. 10Lokiwuniet Sakur, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei: 11Lokiwononet Isiri, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 12Lokikanyet Netania, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei, 13Lokikanyetekape Bukia, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 14Lokikanyetekaarei Yesarela, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 15Lokikanyetekauni Yesaia, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 16Lokikanyetekaonon Matania, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei. 17Lokitomonet Simei, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei. 18Lokitomonet ka lodiopet Asarel, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei. 19Lokitomonet kaarei Kasabia, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 20Lokitomonet kauni Subael, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei. 21Lokitomonet kaonon Matitia, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 22Lokitomonet kakany Yeremot, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 23Lokitomonet kakanykape Kanaia, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei, 24Lokitomonet kakanykaarei Yosebekasa, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei. 25Lokitomonet kakanykauni Kanani, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 26Lokitomonet kakanykaonon Maloti, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 27Lokakaisaarei Eliata, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 28Lokakaisaarei ka lodiopet Kotir, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 29Lokakaisaarei ka luarei Gidaliti, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei. 30Lokakaisaarei ka luuni Makasiot, odweke ka iinacanake, itomon ka luarei. 31#Apu 4:4; 5:8; 11:16. Lokakaisaarei ka luonon Romamitieser, odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka luarei.
