1 Aisiitonokin 16

16
1 # 2 Sam 6:17-19. Kitolomut asaduku naka Edeke, kibwaikis ŋes kidin owema loebubukinit Daudi kanuke: koinakis ainakineta nuieweio ka ainakineta nuka ainapakin kakonyen nuka Edeke, 2Kedau Daudi ainakin ainakinet naicweio ka ainakineta nuka ainapakin, kilipak ituŋa arereŋ kokiror loka EJAKAIT, 3Kokorak da ninitunanan yenika Isirael, ikiliok ka aŋor, ninidiopet emugati, aturosit naka akiriŋ, ka aluŋuret naka imusabibun lukeokito.
4Koseu ice luka Ilebin aiiaanakin koinaren kasaduku naka EJAKAIT, ainakin asuban ka aimal ka aiturit EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael: 5Asap lokapolon, kotupite ŋes Sekaria, Yeiel, ka Semiramot, ka Yekiel, ka Matitia, ka Eliab, ka Benaia, ka Obed-edom, ka Yeiel, keiaas kisabulin ka adeudeun; ka Asap keiaas kikacwi, aituru kes; 6ka Benaia ka Yakasiel isasedotin kejaas karupepen tiritir, koinaren kasaduku naka aitutuket naka Edeke.
7 # 2 Sam 23:1. Kosodi kapaaran kaŋin kisiau Daudi aitemokin aimal EJAKAIT, kama ka Asap ka iinacanake.
8 # Sab 105:1; 145:1. Kimalata EJAKAIT, kiworoto ekekiror;
Kitejenutu aswamisioke otuŋa,
9 # Sab 95:1,2; 100:1. Koyeikisi ŋes, koyeikisi aiturit ŋes;
Kitatamata aswamisioke kere nukaumokin.
10 # Sab 34:3; Isa 45:25. Kitubusikina kokirorike lokalaunan:
Kolakaros itauon luka ŋul kaluemoete EJAKAIT.
11 # Amo 5:6,14. Komoata EJAKAIT ka akegogoŋ;
Komoata akonyeneke ikar kere.
12 # Sab 103:2; 111:2. Kiitutu akeswamisio nukaumokin nueswama; Nukaumokinike, ka itubesia luka aitukuke.
13Wu yes aur naka Isirael ekeiaanakinanike,
Yes idwe luka Yakob, luseunake.
14Ŋes EJAKAIT Ewokodeke:
Iketubesia eia akwap kere.
15Kiitutu aitutuketcke ikar kere,
Akirot naabu kicorak aturlo alukumit;
16 # Age 15:18; 17:2; 26:3; Nek 9:8; Bur 6:13-18. Aitutuket naetutuki ŋesi kAburaam,
Ka aiyapositike mama ejai Isaak;
17Kitogogon da ŋipeŋin mama ejai Yakob araun ekisil,
Mama ejai Isirael araun aitutuket namam edaun.
18Ebe, “Kainakini jo akwap naka Kanaan,
Ewai loka airumetekon.”
19 # Age 34:30; Bur 11:13. Nakiraatar yesi ituŋa luikidioko;
Eebo, luikidioko noi, kiboiororoto kotomake;
20Kirimete kateker kiton ateker,
Anyoun kajakanut adiopet kiton otuŋa ice.
21 # Age 12:17; 20:3; Any 7:15-18. Mam abu koeamak idiotuŋanan aikonakin kes erono;
Eebo, abu kitoron ikabakan kanukee;
22 # Sab 105:15; 1 Yok 2:27. Kobala ebe, “Sirikitirosi lukinyonyoikitonoka akinyet, Sirikiswamakis ikanabin aronis.”
23 # Sab 47:1; 96:1. Koyeikisi EJAKAIT, yes akwapin kere;
Kitodiasi akeitaiar ninlpaaran.
24 # Isa 12:4. Kolimosi aibusesuke katekerin,
Aswamisioke nukaumokin kotuna kere.
25 # Sab 24:8; 89:6-8; Isa 40:25,26. Naarai epol EJAKAIT, loibusakinit noi aiturio:
Loibusakinit ayonio adepar idekesio kere.
26 # Leb 19:4; Sab 115:4-8; Isa 45:20; Yer 10:3; 1 Kor 8:4. Naarai idekesio kere luka ituŋa eraas aputosia:
Konye EJAKAIT ŋes asubi akui,
27 # Sab 8:1. Aibuses ka apolou ejaas akonyeneke:
Agogoŋ ka aiyalama ejaas aiboisike,
28Koinakis EJAKAIT, yes atekerin nuka ituŋa,
Koinakis EJAKAIT aibuses ka agogoŋ,
29Koinakis EJAKAIT aibuses naibusakinit ekekiror:
Koyautu ainakinet, kopotu kakonyeneke.
Komaata EJAKAIT konapito luelaete,
30Komaran kakonyeneke, akwap kere:
Icasit akwap da, komam epedori aijaŋijaŋa.
31 # Isa 35:10; Luk 2:13; Apu 14:2. Kiyalamikin akuj, kole! akwap;
Kolimosi katekerin ebe, “Irumit EJAKAIT,”
32 # Sab 96:10. Kirurum aŋolol, ka ailelebake;
Kolel ekitui, ka luejaas toma kere;
33Kosodete do ikito luka amagoro ayoore kanuka aiyalama Kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT,
Naarai ebunit atubokin akwap,
34 # Sab 106:1; 107:1; 118:1. Kimalata do EJAKAIT: naarai ejok ŋesi:
Naarai eiaenena akeitim ikar kere.
35 # Sab 106:47,48. Kobalasi ebe, “Kitajarai sio,
Wu Edeke loka aitaiarakosi,
Kotukonokinai sio koyuunai katekerin,
Aimalaenen ekonikiror lokalaunan,
Kebwonananas kotoma aiturit joo
36 # 1 Ika 8:15; Sab 72:18,19; Ikis 27:15; Nek 8:6. Kituritete EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael,
Ameun ikar luno kiton ikar kere!”
Kobalas ituŋa kere ebe, “Amen,” kituroto EJAKAIT.
37Abu kitasal Asap ka iinaeanake kanen, koinaren kasaduku naka aitutuket naka EJAKAIT, aiiaanakin tiritir koinaren kasaduku, kwape kibusakinitor aswam naka ŋinipaaran: 38ka Obed-edom ka iinaeanakee, akaisakanykape ka ikanykauni; Obed-edom da okoku loka Yedutun ka Kosa araun ikagolok; 39#1 Siit 21:29; 2 Siit 1:3; 1 Ika 3:4. ka Sadok esasedoti, ka ikeinaean isasedotin, koinaren kewema lokalaunan loka EJAKAIT toma aibois naieelelei naajai Gibeon. 40#Any 29:38; Mar 28:3. Ainanakin ainakineta nuieweio mama ejai EJAKAIT kotem loka ainakineta nuieweio tiritir tupuruc ka eboŋ, kwape ajaatar kere nuiwadikatai kokisila luka EJAKAIT, luabu ŋesi kicorak Isirael; 41#2 Siit 5:13; 7:3; Esr 3:11; Nek 9:17; Sab 25:10; 33:5; 100:5; Yer 33:11; Yok 2:13; Luk 6:36; Yak 5:11. Keman da ka Yedutun kakes, ka ice luaseunitai, luabilitai ikiroriakee, aimalaenen EJAKAIT, naarai ejaenena akeitim ikar kere; 42Keman da ka Yedutun ka kes keiaas karupepen ka ikacwi kanuka nul kaluituruete, ka iboro luka ikososinei luka Edeke: odwe luka Yedutun da ajaut okek.
43 # 2 Sam 6:19,20; Age 18:19; Yos 24:15. Konyout ituŋa kere, kolot ninitunanan oreke: kobono Daudi ailipakin etogoke arereŋ.
