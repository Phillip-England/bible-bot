1 Aisiitonokin 4

4
1 # Age 38:29; 46:12; Mar 26:20,21; Rus 4:18. Odwe luka Yuda; Peres, Kesuron, ka Karumi, ka Kur, ka Soba!’ 2Reaia okoku loka Sobal kouri Yakat; Yakat kouri Akumai ka Lakad.
Ŋul kes ikekia luka Isoratin. 3Lu kes luka papa laka Etam; Yesereel, ka Isima, ka Idibas: ekiror loka inacikee Kaseleleponi: 4ka Penuel papa loka Gedor, ka Eser papa loka Kusa. Ŋul kes odwe luka Kur, ekiyai loka Epurata, papa loka Bet-lekem. 5Ajaas Asur papa loka Tekoa aŋor aarei, Kela ka Naara, 6Kourik Naara ŋes Akusam, ka Keper, ka Temeni, ka Kaaka-sitari, Ŋul kes odwe luka Naara. 7Odwe luka Kela Seret, ka Isar, ka Etinan. 8Kakos kouri Anub, ka Sobeba, ka ikekia luka Akarel okoku loka Karum. 9#Age 34:19; Sab 112:6; Awa 10:7; Age 3:16. Kitopolooritete Yabes aitelekarit iinaeanake: komaik totoke ekekiror Yabes ebe, “Naarai kedou ŋes kaiturur.” 10#Age 12:8; 1 Siit 16:8; Yob 12:4; 22:27; Isa 12:4; 1 Ika 3:7-13; Sab 21:4; 65:2; Mat 7:7-11. Kiwone Yabes Edeke loka Isirael ebe, “Wu okwe arai kisireren ijo eoŋ kabeit, kitalal eikoruka, koiaenena akanikon keoŋ, kidar eoŋ karonis, komam eyauni aiturur mamakan!” Kocam Edeke nuabu ŋesi kilip. 11Kelub onaee loka Suka kouri Mekir, loarai papa loka Esiton. 12Esiton kouri Bet-rapa, ka Pasea, ka Tekina papa loka Ir-nakas. Ŋul kes ituŋa luka Reka. 13#Yos 15:17. Odwe luka Kenas; Otiniel, ka Seraia: odwe luka Otiniel; Katat. 14#Nek 11:35. Meonotai kouri Opura: Seraia kouri Yoab papa loka Ge-karasim; naarai keraas ikeswamak luepiriaka. 15Odwe luka Kaleb okoku loka Yepune; lru, ka Ela, ka Naam: odwe luka Ela; Kenas. 16Odwe luka Yekalelel; Sip, ka Sipa, ka Tiria, ka, Asarel. 17Odwe luka Esera; Yeter, ka Mered, ka Eper, ka Yalon: kouri ŋin Miriam, ka Samai, ka Isiba papa loka Esitemoa. 18Akeberu Ayudayat kouri Yered papa loka Gedor, ka Keber papa loka Soko, ka Yekutiel papa loka Sanoa. Ŋul kes odwe luka Bitia akoku naka Parao, naamanyit Mered. 19Odwe luka aberu naka Kodia, kinaci naka Nakam, kes lukapapa luka Keila Egarumit, ka Esitemoa Emaakatit. 20Odwe luka Simon; Aminon, ka Rina, ka Ben-kanan, ka Tilon. Odwe luka Isi; Soket, ka Ben-soket, 21#Age 38:1. Odwe luka Sela okoku loka Yuda; Er papa loka Leka, ka Laada papa loka Maresa, ka ikekia luka etogo loka nul kalukesonyete ebaputa loejok, luka etogo loka Asibea; 22#2 Sam 8:2. ka Yokim, ka ituŋa luka Koseba, ka Yoas, ka Sarap, loapolokinit Moab, ka Yasubi-lekem. Ido akiro ŋun eraas nuno, 23ŋul kes ikajeak amoti, ka lueboiete koNetaim ka Gedera: kiboioto kaŋina nepepe kekabaka kanuka akeswam.
24Odwe luka Simeon; Nemuel, ka Yamin, ka Yarib, ka Sera, ka Saul: 25Salum okokuke, Mibisam okokuke, Misima okokuke. 26Odwe luka Misima; Kamuel okokuke, Sakur okokuke, Simei okokuke: 27Kejaas Simci idwe lusap itomon ka lukanykape, ka lupesur akanykape; konye amameete iinacanake idwe luipu, kwabo mam kiyatakina ekekikee, kwape idwe luka Yuda. 28Kiboioto kesi Beer-seba, ka Molada, ka Kasar-sual; 29koBilika da, ka Esem, ka Tolad; 30koBetuel da, ka Koruma, ka Sikilag; 31koBet-marakabot, ka Kasar-susim, ka Bet-biri, ka Saaraim, ŋul kes ireriakec kiton airum naka Daudi. 32Ireriakee ludidik kes Etam, ka Ain, ka Rimon, ka Token, ka Asan, ireria ikany: 33ka ireriakee ludidik kere luerimorito ireria ŋu! kiton Baal. Ŋun kes aiboisiokec, ejaas kes aurikee.
34Mesobab, ka Yamulek, ka Yosa okoku loka Amasia; 35ka Yoel, ka Yeku okoku loka Yosibia, okoku loka Seraia, okoku loka Asiel; 36ka Elioenai, ka Yaakoba, ka Yesokaia, ka Asaia, ka Adiel, ka Yesimiel, ka Benaia; 37ka Sisa okoku loka Sipi, okoku loka Alon, okoku loka Yedaia, okoku loka Simiri, okoku loka Semaia; 38Ŋullualimor ikeeekiroria kes ikapolok luka ikekiakee: kiyatakinos noi itogoi luka lukapapaakec. 39Koloto kiton nealomarere Gedor, kiton ari nakokide naka aditot, amoote inya luka isipaninikec. 40#Age 9:22; 10:6; Sab 78:51. Kodumut inya luelibito ejok, kolal akwap kiyamakina, kinapakina da; naarai lueboioto kaŋina apeŋo keraas luka Kam. 41#2 Ika 18:8. Ŋul da luewadikao ikiroria apote kapaarasia nuka Kesekia ekabaka loka Yuda, komunaasi iwemanakec, ka Imeunim luadumunos kaŋina, komudiasi kes eut, kiton lolo, kiboikinos aiboisikec: naarai ajaas inya kaŋina luka isipaninikec. 42Ice kakesi, kes odwe luka Simeon, ituŋa akwat akany, koloto moru Seir, ikapolokitokokee kes Pelatia, ka Nearia, ka Repaia, ka Usi el, odwe luka Isi. 43#1 Sam 15:8; 30:17; 2 Sam 8:12. Kodanyata lukideunos luka Imalekain luakuete, kiboikinos do nina, kiton lolo.
