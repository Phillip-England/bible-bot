1 Aisiitonokin 26

26
1 # Mar 16:1,2; 26:9-11; 1 Siit 9:17; 15:18; 2 Siit 23:19; Yud 1:11; 1 Siit 6:39. Abeanakin naka ikagolok: luka Ikorakin; Meselemia okoku loka Kore, lokodwe luka Asap. 2Ajaas Meselemia idwe lusap; Sekaria ekiyai, Yediaellokiareit, Sebadia lokiwuniet, Yatiniel, lokiwoŋonet; 3Elam lokikanyet, Yekokanan lokikanyetekape, Eliekoenai lokikanyetekaarei. 4Ajaas Obed-edom idwe lusap; Semaia ekiyai, Yekosaba lokiareit, Yoa lokiwuniet, Sakar lokiwononet, ka Netanellokikanyet; 5#1 Siit 13:14; Age 33:5; Sab 127:3. Amiellokikanyetekape, Isakar lokikanyetekaarei, Peuletai lokikanyetekauni: naarai kisireren Edeke ŋes, 6Aponi kourikinai Semaia da okokuke idwe lusap, Iukeyaito ekek loka papakee: naarai keraas ituŋa lukirigin luetitin. 7Odwe luka Semaia; Otini, ka Repael, ka Obed, ka Elisabad, lukeraas iinaeanake ituŋa luetitin, Eliku, ka Semakia. 8Ŋul kere odwe luka Obed-edom; kes ka odwekee ka iinaeanakee, ituŋa lukapedor ka agogoŋ aijaanakin; akaisakanykape ka luarei luka Obed-edom. 9Ajaas Meselemia idwe lusap ka iinaean, ituŋa luetitin, itomon ka lukanykauni. 10#1 Siit 16:38; Age 4:7; 49:3; Ikis 21:17; 1 Siit 5:1. Kosa da, lokodwe luka Merari, ajaas ŋes idwe lusap; Simiri lokapolon (naarai abu papake kitarau ŋes lokapolon kwabo mam kerai ekiyai); 11Kilikia lokiareit, Tebalia lokiwuniet, Sekaria lokiwononet: odwe ka iinaean kere luka Kosa emwaasi itomon ka luuni.
12Kotoma kanul kobeanakinos ikagolok, ituŋa lukapolok, lukejaas nukisinadio, kwape kiinaeanakee, aiiaanakin kotogo loka EJAKAIT, 13#Yos 18:10; 1 Siit 25:8; 24:5; Awa 18:18; Yok 1:7; Asw 1:26; 10:34; Gal 3:28; Kol 3:11. Apotu kilamilamata, ludidik kwape kalukapolok, kwapc ajaatar ikekia luka lukapapaakee, kanuka nolirute. 14Kobeik Selemia eroet lokokide. Kosodete ailamilamikin Sekaria okokuke, ekapetan akiro lokeeobe; kobeik ekeroet ari naka agolitomei, 15Kobeik Obed-edom ari naka eŋalakimak: ka odweke etogo loka ainad. 16#1 Ika 10:5; 1 Siit 24:31; 25:8; 2 Siit 9:4; Nek 12:24. Kobeik Supim ka Kosa ari nakoto, erute loka Saleket, nekejai eititiria loedokari, kiteonoro ikedarak kaluee. 17Keiaas Ilebin ikanykape ari nakokide, iwonon ninipaaran ari naka agolitomei, iwonon ninipaaran ari naka eŋalakimak, ka iareiarei kanuka etogo loka ainad, 18Kanuka Parabar ari nakoto, iwonon neejai eititiria, ka iarei oParabar. 19Ŋun kes abeanakineta nuka ikagolok; lukodwe luka Ikorakin, ka lukodwe luka Merari.
20 # Ikis 12:6; 1 Ika 7:51; 14:26; 1 Siit 9:26-29; 22:14-16; Mal 3:10; 2 Siit 31:11,12. Luka Ilebin, kopolokinite Akiya aiŋadisia nuka etogo loka Edeke, ka aiŋadisia nuka iboro luketiakunitai, 21#1 Siit 6:17; 23:8. Odwe luka Ladan; odwe luka Igeresonin lukeraas luka Ladan, ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee lukeraas luka Ladan Egeresonit; Yekieli.
22Odwe luka Yekieli; Setam, ka Yoel onaeeke, kopolokinito aiŋadisia nuka etogo loka EJAKAIT, 23#Any 6:18; Mar 3:19; 1 Siit 23:12. Luka Imuramain, luka Isarin, ka luka Ikeburonin, ka luka Iusielin; 24#1 Siit 23:16; 24:20. Kerai Sebuel okoku loka Geresom, okoku loka Musa ekayaiton aiŋadisia. 25#1 Siit 23:18; 24:22. Ka iinacanake; loka Elieser kobu Rekabia okokuke ka Yesaia okokuke, ka Yoram okokuke, ka Sikiri okokuke, ka Selemot okokuke, 26Kopolokinite Selemot ŋol ka ikeinaean aiŋadisia kere nuka iboro luatiakunitai, luapotu Daudi, ka ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee, ikapolok luka ilukumin, ka lukakwatat, ka ikapolok luka aiore kotiakasi. 27#Mar 31:50; Yos 6:19,24; 1 Siit 22:14-16; 2 Siit 15:11,18; 2 Ika 12:14; 22:4,5; Nek 10:32. Luka lunatuna luadumunete koiiisio apotu kotiakasi aiteteun etogo loka EJAKAIT, 28#1 Sam 9:9. Kere da luabu Samuel ekadwaran, ka Saul okoku loka Kis, ka Abiner okoku loka Ner, ka Yoab okoku loka Seruia, kotiak; ŋinibore kere yeniketiakunitai, kerai nin yenikakan naka Selemot ka iinaeanake.
29 # Ikis 1:16; 16:18; 1 Siit 23:4; 2 Siit 19:8-11. Luka Isarin, apotu Kenania ka odweke kiswamata aswam nakokina koIsirael, araut ikapolokitok ka ikatubok. 30Luka Ikeburonin, Kasabia ka iinaeanake, ituŋa Iuetitiŋ, elukumit ka akwat akanykaarei, kopolokinito Isirael okekwa juwai Yorodan ari nakoto; kanuka aswam kere naka EJAKAIT, ido aijaanakin ekabaka. 31#1 Siit 23:19; 2 Sam 5:4; 1 Ika 2:11; 1 Siit 29:27; Mar 32:1,3,35; Yos 21:39; Isa 16:9; Yer 48:32. Luka Ikeburonin kerai Yeriya lokapolon, loka Ikeburonin, kwape ajaar aurikee kwape ajaatar ikekia luka lukapapaakec. Kokaru loka akaisaoŋon loka airum naka Daudi aponi komoai, ido kodumunai kakesi ituŋa lukirigin luetitin koYaser loka Gilead. 32#2 Siit 19:11. Iinaeanake, ituŋa luetitin, emwaasi ilukumin iarei ka akwat akanykaarei, ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee, luabu Daudi kitarau ikapolokitok luka Ireubenin, ka Igadin, ka atutubet naka ateker naka Imanasein, kaŋinikirot mama ejai Edeke, kakiro da nuka ekabaka.
