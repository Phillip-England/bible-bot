Ikatubok 7

7
1 # Tub 6:32; 1 Sam 12:11; Age 22:3; Yos 3:1; 6:12; Lim 9:10; Age 12:6. Kosodi Yerubaal, ŋes Gideon, ka ituŋa kere luajaas kaŋes adilokin anyoun, kidukuroto ariri naka atan naka Karod: #7:1 Karod Apolollke Amaran. koiaas iidukurun luka Midian arikee naka agolitomei, ariri naka eukot loka More, aditot,
2 # Ikis 8:17; 9:4; 32:27; 1 Sam 17:47; Sab 33:16-18; 44:6,7; Awa 25:6; Lim 9:11; Isa 10:13; Yer 9:23,24; Sek 4:6; Rom 3:27; 1 Kor 1:29; 4:7; 2 Kor 4:7; 10:17; Pes 2:9. Kotema EJAKAIT Gideon atiar, “Ipu noi ituŋa luijaatar ijo, eoŋ ainakin Imidianin akanikee, kinyek Isirael aiturokinor eoŋ ebe, ‘Akakan alopet ŋes ketaiar.’ 3#Ikis 20:8. Aso do kolot, kolimo kaki nuka ituŋa ebe, ‘Ŋol loekuriana loemarani, kobono konyou da komoru Gilead.’’’ Kobonosi kotuna ituŋa ilukumin akaisaarei ka luarei; kosalata ilukumin itomon.
4 # 1 Sam 16:6,7. Kotema EJAKAIT Gideon atiar, “Eroko ituŋa ipu noi; koyana kes akipi, egimori eoŋ kes kaŋina kanukon: ejaun do ebe, loatemari eoŋ ebe, ‘Ŋol euparos kajo,’ Ŋopeŋol iuparotor; loatemari eoŋ ebe, ‘Mam ŋol euparos kajo,’ mam ŋol iuparotor.” 5Kwaŋin koyanau ituŋa akipi: kotema EJAKAIT Gideon atiar, “Ŋolituŋanan loemasi akipi kanaiepeke, kwape emasia ekiŋok, igitari ŋol; ka ŋol da loekukokin aimat.” 6Etiai loka ŋul kaluamasete bala ekiŋok, luebwaikinete akanin aitukekee emwaasi akwat auni: konye ituŋa luce kere apotu kokukokinos aimat akipi. 7#1 Sam 14:6; Isa 41:14,18. Kotoma EJAKAIT Gideon atiar, “Ketajari jo kituna akwat auni luamasete bala ekiŋok, eiaikini Imidianin akanikon: koloto do ŋinituŋanan aiboisike.” 8Kitiijata do ituŋa inyamat kakaninikec, ka arupepenekee: kijuka ituŋa kere luka Isirael ninitunanan owemake, konye abu kitasal nul akwat auni: kojaas iidukurun luka Midian kwapuken aditot.
9 # Age 46:2,3; Yob 4:13; Mat 1:20; Asw 18:9,10; 27:23. Kosodi kanen, kakuare kaŋin kotema EJAKAIT ŋes atiar, “Konyou, kijesar oidukurun; naarai ejaik kes akanikon. 10Konye arai ikuriana ijo aijesar, kijesaros ijo ka Pura ekejaanakinanikon oidukurun: 11#Age 24:14; 1 Sam 14:9,10. Ipupuni do nuineras kesi; akaulo kanun egogoŋioros akonikanin aijesar oidukurun.” Kosodi ŋesi ka Pura ekejaanakinanike aijesar kiton otuŋa luakapakina ejie luajaas osaŋe loka iidukurun. 12#Tub 6:5,33; 8:10. Koperete Imidianin ka Imalekain ka idwe kere lukokide kaditot bala emaase kanuka aipues; komam ikaalaikec imarunos, kwape asine naejai ariri naka aŋolol kanuka aipues, 13#Tub 6:15; Isa 41:14,15; 1 Kor 1:27. Kebu Gideon, koany, ajai etuŋanan loalimonokini oupake airujasit, kobala ebe, “Koany, eruia airujasit, ido koany, bala abu aluŋuret naka emugati loka asairi kikokorun oidukurun luka Midian, kodol owema, kocam ŋes kibiror, kobuburia ŋes, koper ewema.” 14#Age 40:8; Mar 23:5. Koboŋok oupake ŋes ebe, “Mere nin ieebore dimarai ekileŋ loka Gideon okoku loka Yoas, etuŋanan loka Isirael: ejaiki EJAKAIT Midian ka ajore kere akanike.”
15Kosodi kepupu Gideon inerai airujasit ka apolouke, abu koma Edeke; kobono oidukurun luka Isirael, kotema atiar, “Konyoutu; naarai ejaiki EJAKAIT aiore naka Midian akaninikus.” 16#Mar 10:9; Yos 6:4; 2 Siit 13:14,15; Amo 3:6. Kotiak ituŋa akwat auni korioijeta iuni, kititin kes kere arupepen ka amoti nuamameete iboro kotoma, kibwaik akalasia nuenokete toma amoti. 17Kotema kes atiar, “Kosesenai eoŋ, kiswamata kwape kwaŋin; ido koany, naadolio aiboisio nukosane loka iidukurun, kosodi kwape eswamaa eoŋ, iswamaete yesi da kwape kwaŋin. 18Naakutakinia arupepe, eoŋ ka ituŋa kere luejaas keoŋ, ikutakinete yesi da arupepen kanoliwai kere loka iidukurun, kobalas ebe, ‘Luka EJAKAIT ka luka Gideon da.’’’
19 # Any 14:24; Luk 12:38; Apu 16:15. Kwaŋin kodolut Gideon ka ituŋa akwatat luajaas kaŋes aibois nakosane loka iidukurun kageunet naka akwenyunet nakokidin, atipet napekedaut aijulakin ikedarak: kokutata arupepen, kobwanabwanata amoti nuetinito kakaninikec. 20Kokutata irioneta iuni arupepen, kobwanabwanata amoti, kitinata akalasia nuenokete kakaninikee nukokediany, ka arupepen kakaninikec nukoteten akusia: kiworoto ebe, “Ekileŋ loka EJAKAIT ka loka Gideen da.” 21#Any 14:13,14,25; 2 Siit 20:17; Isa 30:7,15; 2 Ika 7:7; Yob 15:21,22; Awa 28:1. Kobwaata kesi ninitunanan kaiboisike alukun iidukurun: koker ajore kere; kocelata, kitekerata kes. 22#Yos 6:4,16,20; 2 Kor 4:7; 1 Sam 14:20; Sab 83:9; Isa 9:4; 19:2; 2 Siit 20:23. Kokutata arupepen akwat auni, abu EJAKAIT koinak ekileŋ loka ninitunanan aitiji oupake, ka ajore kere: koker ajore kiton Bet-sita loebei Serera, kiton oikor loka Abel-mekola, loidunyas kaTabat. 23Kotukonokinos ituŋa luka Isirael kotoma Naputali, kotoma Aser, kotoma Manase kere da, kodapata Midian,
24 # Tub 3:27; Rom 15:30; Pil 1:27; Tub 3:28; Yok 1:28. Kijuka Gideon ikiyakia akwap kere nakiukon naka Epuraim, kobala ebe, “Kopotu asurokin Midian, kogeikis kes airum akipi, kiton Bet-bara, ka Yorodan da.” Kotukokinos ituŋa kere luka Epuraim, kirumata akipi kiton Bet-bara, ka Yorodan. 25#Tub 8:3,4; Sab 83:11; Isa 10:26. Koyanaasi ikapolok iarei luka Midian, Oreb ka Seeb; koarakis Oreb moru Oreb, koarakis Seeb acues nakebino naka Seeb, kodapata Midian: koyaut akwes nuka Oreb ka Seeb mama ejai Gideon okekwa loka Yorodan.
