Ikatubok 16

16
1Kolot Samuson Gasa, koany kaŋ ina naadote, koloma mamakeŋ. 2#1 Sam 19:11; 23:26; Sab 118:10-12; Asw 9:24; 2 Kor 11:32,33. Kolimokinai Igasan ebe, “Abu Samuson ne.” Kolukutu ŋes, koperut ŋes akuare kere korute lokore, kililinata akuare kere, kobalas ebe, “Kiwala akwap, kiari ŋes.” 3Koper Samuson kiton kidinebai, konyou kidiŋebai, kitin ikirigin luka erute lokore, ka ategerio aarei, kouto kes, ka atiket da, korioŋak kes okeperinike, kodaka kes aidi naka eronat loitei Keburon.
4Kosodi kanen akaulo kanun, abu ŋesi kominak aberu kaditot naka Sorek, ekekiror Delila. 5#Tub 14:15; Awa 2:16-19; 5:3. Kopotu ijaka luka Ipilisitian neejai nin, kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Kiiodiiod ŋes, kojen neelomunitor akegogoŋ naepol, ka epone lokipedoria aipikor ŋes, tetere kipedori ajen ŋes aitiean ŋes: kiinakini isio jo ninidiopet kasio atutubeta akwat atomon kanadiopet luka epuesa.” 6Kotema Delila Samuson atiar, “Kenainait, kolimokinai neelomunitor akonigogoŋ naepol, ka epone lokienere jo aitican.” 7Kotema Samuson ŋes atiar, “Arai kaenata kesi eoŋ kigegoi ikanykaarei luepalal lumam itoonitoi, ŋes kito akalanyanaror eoŋ, kiputokin kalucetuna.” 8#Awa 6:24-26. Kosodete ijaka luka Ipilisitian ainun ŋin igegoi ikanykaarei luepalal lumam itoonitoi, koenia nin ŋes, 9#Yok 5:14. Ajaas nin ikaperunok ago la nakotoma. Kotema nin ŋes atiar, “Samuson, kibunokis jo Ipilisitian.” Koned igegoi kwape enedar eusi loka amujajut ketiro akim. Kwaŋin mam akegogoŋ ajenio.
10Kotema Delila Samuson atiar, “Koany, kimod ijo eoŋ, kilimokinit da nuka aisab: asa keŋainait, kolimokinai epone lokienere jo.” 11Kotema ŋes atiar, “Arai kaenata kesieon kaunoi nukitetiak numam itwasamatai adioswam, ŋes kito akalanyanaror eoŋ, kiputokin kalucetuna.” 12#Awa 7:22. Kolem Delila aunoi nukitetiak, koenia ŋes, kotema atiar, “Samuson, kibunokis jo Ipilisitian.” Kibojete ikaperunok kagola nakotoma. Koŋeda kes kakaninike bala eusi.
13Kotema Delila Samuson atiar, “Okwe, kimod ijo eoŋ kiton kwana, kilimokinit da nuka aisab: kolimokinai epone lokienere jo.” Kotema ŋes atiar, “Arai kicipak ijo ikatim luieipitai irwan akanykaarei oboro luicipi.” 14Abu nin kiketak nul keloit, kotema ŋes atiar, “Samuson, kibunokis jo Ipilisitian.” Kokweny kotoma ajo, kout eloit ka aieipet da.
15 # Tub 14:16; Awa 2:16; 5:3-14. Kotema nin ŋes atiar, “Itemari ijo biai ebe, ‘Kamina jo,’ komam lekonitau ejaas keoŋ? Kimod eoŋ irwan nu auni, mam da kilimokinit eoŋ neelomunitor akonigogoŋ naepol.” 16Kosodi kanen kieebok ŋin abokobok ŋes kaimaima ninlpaaran kakiroke, abu eketau kokadak kiton atwanare.
17 # 1 Siit 28:9; 2 Siit 15:2; Awa 18:2; Mik 7:5; Mar 6:5; Tub 13:5; Asw 18:18. Abu kolimok ŋes nukotauke kere, kotema ŋes atiar, “Mam ejutet icakit akouka; naarai arai eoŋ Enasirit loka Edeke ageun kakoik naka totoka: arai kebareni akakou ŋes kito enyounio akagogoŋ kamakaŋ, akalanyanar, kiputokin da kalucetuŋa.”
18Kejenu Delila ebe alimoki ŋes nukotauke kere, abu kijuka anyaraun iiaka luka Ipilisitian ebe, “Kopotu do irwan na bon, naarai kalimok eoŋ nukotauke kere.” Kosodete ijaka luka Ipilisitian aponare mamake koyaunito apesan kakanikec. 19#Awa 5:3,4; 7:21; Lim 7:25,26. Abu kitoioto ŋes kakejeke; kosodi nin anyaraun etuŋanan kobareno iketim lueeipitai irwan akanykaarei; kogeu aitican ŋes, konyou akegogoŋ kamake, 20#Mar 14:9,42,43; Yos 7:12; 1 Sam 16:14; 18:12; 28:15,16; 2 Siit 15:2; Sab 33:16; Awa 22:14; Yer 9:23,24; Kos 9:12. Kotema atiar, “Samuson, kibunokis jo Ipilisitian.” Kokweny kotoma ajo, kobala ebe, “Alomari kwape lem sek, kasodi ailakilakaar.” Konye mam ajeni ebe abu EJAKAIT konyou kamaken. 21Kikamut Ipilisitian ŋes, kogorut akekonye; koyanaasi ŋes Gasa, koenata kirikoi luka aisinit; abu kirienenei kaidaris. 22Konye apotu iketim kogeut apol bobo kedaun abaren ŋes,
23Kosodete kotukonokinos iiaka luka Ipilisitian ainakin Dagon ekecedeke asubanit naepol, kalakara da: naarai abalas ebe, “Kijaik edekewok oni Samuson lokasurupiwok akaniniwok.” 24#1 Sam 31:9; 1 Siit 10:9; Sab 97:7; 115:3,8; Dan 5:4,23; 1 Kor 8:4; 10:19,20. Keanyut ituŋa ŋes, apotu kituroto edekekee: naarai abalas ebe, “Kiiaik edekewok oni lokasurupiwok akaniniwok, loemudiarit awokokwap, loaar luipu kaoni.” 25#Tub 9:27; 16:6-9; 18:20; 2 Sam 13:28; 1 Ika 20:12; Est 3:15; Isa 32:13; Dan 5:2-4; Mat 14:6,7. Kosodi kanen, kiyalamikinos ikecetauon, apotu kotemasi ebe, “Konyarautu Samuson, koboliak oni,” Konyaraut Samuson kaidaris; koboliak kes: kibwaikis ŋes kidin opirin, 26Kotema Samuson esapat loarikit ŋes kakan ebe, “Kocamakinai kararapa ipirin luiganito etogo, tetere agonakini kes.” 27#Ikis 22:8; Yos 2:6. Eleleba etogo ikiliok ka aŋor; iiaka kere luka Ipilisitian ajaas nen; ajaas ikiliok ka aŋor kwape ilukumin iuni kuiu togo, lueteete Samuson eboliakini kes.
28 # Yer 15:15. Kiworo Samuson EJAKAIT, kobala ebe, “Wu EJAKAIT Edeke, kenainait kiitunoi, kenaiŋait kitogogonoi, irwan na bon, wu Edeke, tetere enyanari atipet akakonye aarei kaneejaas Ipilisitian.” 29Kitin Samuson ipirin iarei lukokidin lueganito etogo, kogonakin kes, ediopet kakekan nakoteten, ediopet da kakekan nakokediany. 30Kotema Samuson atiar, “Mere katwana nepepe k Ipilisitian.” Kokukum kakegogon kere; kotiyaikin etogo ijaka, ka ituŋa kere luajaas toma. Kwaŋin lukatwak luaari ŋesi kapak naka atwanareke epu adepar luaari ŋesi eiari, 31#Tub 13:25. Kosodete ikeinaean ka ekal kere loka papake aponare, kolemutu ŋes, koyanaut, kidaikis ŋes kidin kaSora ka Esitaol aibois nakidaanakina naka Manoa papake, Abu ŋesi kotubok Isirael ikar akaisaarei.
