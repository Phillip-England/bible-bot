Ikatubok 18

18
1 # Tub 17:6; 21:25; Yos 19:47. Kapaarasia kanun amamei ekabaka koIsirael: kapaarasia kanun da amoot ateker naka Idanin airumet nakiboienenet: naarai kiton apaaran nin mam airumetekec etebcikinitai kes katekerin nuka Isirael. 2#Tub 13:25; Mar 13:17; Yos 2:1; Tub 17:1. Kiiukas idwe luka Dan kokekikec ituŋa ikany kotiaikee kere, ituŋa lukirigin, anyoun koSora ka Esitaol, airot akwap, ka aiŋic da; kotemasi kes atiar, “Koloto, konicata akwap.” Kodolot akwap nakiukon naka Epuraim, togo loka Mika, koperoto nen. 3Kidunyakinos kesi togo loka Mika, apotu kojenata eporoto loka ekatumunan Elebit: kopaasi nen, kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Iŋai bo kiyauni jo ne? Inyo bo iswamai ijo kane? Inyo bo da ejai jo nce?” 4#Tub 17:10; Yok 10:12,13; Asw 18:18,21; 20:33; 1 Tim 3:3; Tit 1:11; 2 Pet 2:3. Kotema kes atiar, “Kekonaki Mika eoŋ kwaŋin, karop eoŋ, araun da eoŋ ekesascdoti.” 5#1 Ika 22:5; Kos 4:12; Tub 17:5. Kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Kiŋaiŋait kinit Edeke tetere kiienuni arai ereren akosilosit nakilosi.” 6#1 Ika 22:6. Kotema esasedoti kes atiar, “Koloto kainapakina. Ejai crotokus loilosete akonyen nuka EJAKAIT,”
7Kosodete ituŋa ŋul ikany anyoun, kodolut Lais, koanyut ituŋa luajaas nen, kwape eboioto kinapakina, kwape ejaar epone loka Isidonin, kiyamakina kinapakina da; naarai amamei kakwap yenayait kes, yenepedori aitisil kes kadiokirot, alwaka kesi koSidonin, amameete kes akiro kere kidiotunanan. 8Koboŋosi neejaas iinaeanakee Sora ka Esitaol: kotcmasi iinacanakec kes atiar, “Anyoika bo?” 9#Mar 13:20; 14:7-9; Yos 2:23,24; 1 Sam 14:48; 1 Ika 22:3. Kotcmasi atiar, “Konyoutu, kaloto asurokin kes, naarai kianyu akwap, ido koany, ejok noi: kanukinyo ipalaritotor? Sirikikalanyakata alosit alomar airum akwap. 10#Ikis 8:9. Nailosioto, idolokinete ituŋa luinapakina, elal da akwap: naarai kijaik Edcke ŋes akanikus; aibois naemamei eipud loka ninibore kere yenejai akwap.”
11Konyout kanen lukokek loka Idanin, koSora ka Esitaol, ituŋa akwat akanykape luakapito luiiis. 12#Yos 15:60. Kolototo, kidukuroto Kiriat-yearim, koYuda: ŋes amaikiniata aibois ŋin Makane-dan, kiton lolo: koany, ejai kau naka Kiriat-yearim. 13Kitoroto nen kiton akwap nakiukon naka Epuraim, kopotu togo loka Mika,
14 # 1 Sam 14:28; Tub 8:27; 17:5; 1 Sam 23:6. Kosodete ituŋa ikany lualosito airot akwap naka Lais, aboŋokin ikeeeinaean ebe, “Ŋenete yesi cbe ejai otogoi lu epod, ka iterapim, ka aput naegerer, ka aput nacaluuna? Aso do koomoomata nuiswamaete.” 15#Age 43:27; 1 Sam 17:22. Kopaasi nen, kodoloto togo loka ekatumunan Elebit, ŋes etogo loka Mika, kinitata ŋes ejauteneke, 16Kobwaata ituŋa akwat akanykape luakapito lujiis, luaraas luka idwe luka Dan, korute loka ere, 17#Age 31:19,30; Tub 6:31; 17:4,5; 2 Ika 19:18; Isa 41:29; 46:12; Mik 5:13. Koloto ituŋa ikany lualosito airot akwap, kolomasi nen, kolemut aput naegerer, ka epod, ka iterapim, ka aput naealuna: kobwo esasedoti korute ka ituŋa akwat akanykape luakapito lujiis. 18Kosodi kelomasi kwi togo loka Mika, kelemut aput naegerer, ka epod, ka iterapim, ka aput naealuna, kotema esasedoti kes atiar, “Inyo bo iswamaete yesi?” 19#Yob 21:5; 29:9; Awa 30:32; Mik 7:16; Age 45:8; Tub 17:10; 2 Ika 6:21; 13:14; Isa 22:21; Mat 23:9. Kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Kililiŋ, korapak lakonikan aitukukon, kaupakinos, koraun papakosi ka esasedotikosi: ejok kereka jo araut esasedoti togo loka cdiopctunanan, arai bo araut esasedoti loka ateker ka ekek loka Isirael?” 20Kolakar etau loka esasedoti, kolem epod, ka iterapim, ka aput naegerer, kouparos kiruna.
21Kwaŋin kibelokinos koloto, kisinarenoto ludidik ka ibaren ka iboro. 22Kelwanikinos kesi kotogo loka Mika, kotukokinos ituŋa luajaas otogoi luedunyas ketogo loka Mika, kodolokis idwe luka Dan. 23Kiworoto kesi idwe luka Dan. Koŋoliasi kau, kotemasi Mika atiar, “Inyo bo kisiriebi jo, tape ibunit kerioŋet loetia nen?” 24Kotema atiar, “Ilemarito yesi ikadekesio lueswamauni eoŋ, ka esasedoti, iyekasi da, inyo do bobo ejai eoŋ? Ikote kiŋisito eoŋ ai ebe, ‘Inyo bo kisiriebi jo?’” 25#2 Sam 17:8. Kotemasi idwe luka “Dan ŋes atiar, “Sirikepupun ekoniporoto kamakosi, kinyeis ituŋa luenyunyuraka abunokin jo, kosodi ijo atwaniar aijarakon, ka ajair naka lukokalekon.” 26Kolototo idwe luka Dan: keanyu Mika ebe agogoŋ kesi adepar ŋes, abu kibelokin, kobono togoke.
27 # Ikis 33:22; Yos 19:47. Koyanaasi ŋul lueswamauni Mika, ka esasedoti loarai loke, kodolut Lais, otuŋa luiyamakina luinapakina da, kodanyata kes kakwanis naka ekileŋ; kicweata ere kakim, 28#Age 49:13; Yos 8:11; Tub 10:12; Mar 13:21; 2 Sam 10:6. Komamei ekayuunan, naarai alwana kaneajai Sidon, amameete da kes akiro kidiotunanan; ajai ere aditot naka Bet-rekob. Kodukoto bobo ere ŋol, kiboikinos nen. 29#Yos 19:47; Age 14:14; Tub 20:1; 1 Ika 12:29; 15:20. Komaikis ekiror loka ere Dan, kwape ajaar ekiror loka Dan papaakee, loaurikinio Isirael: konye kerai sek ekiror loka ere Lais. 30#Tub 13:1; 1 Sam 4:2; Sab 78:60. Kosipokis idwe luka Dan kanukee aput naegerer; ido Yonatan okoku loka Geresom, okoku loka Musa, ŋes ka odweke, kes araas isasedotin luka ateker naka Idanin aitodol apaaran naanatarere akwap. 31#Yos 18:1; 1 Sam 4:1. Kwaŋin kosipokis kesi aput naegerer naka Mika naeswamauni ŋesi, apak kere naajaar etogo loka Edeke Silo.
