Ikatubok 21

21
1 # Tub 20:1; 1 Sam 7:5,6. Apotu ituŋa luka Isirael kiyapoikinito koMisipa ebe, “Mam idio kaoni ekorakini Benyamin akokuke aimany.” 2#Yos 18:1; Tub 20:18,26. Kopotu ituŋa Bet-el, kiboioto kanen kiton eboŋ kakonyen nuka Edeke, kodelosi, komonyata noi, 3Kotemasi atiar, “Wu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, kanukinyo eswamaunor na kotoma Isirael, ebe lolo ateker adiopet atwaniar koIsirael?” 4#Age 8:20; 12:7; Any 20:24,25; Tub 6:26; 1 Sam 14:35; 1 Ika 8:64; Bur 13:10. Kosodi kanen tupuruc, kodilokis ituŋa anyoun, kodukokis etem nen, koinakis ainakineta nuieweio ka ainakineta nuka ainapakin. 5#Leb 27:28,29; Tub 5:23; 1 Sam 11:7; Yer 48:10. Kotemasi idwe luka Isirael atiar, “Iŋai bo kotoma atekerin kere nuka Isiraellomam abunit atukokin mama ejai EJAKAIT? Naarai apotu kiyapoikinito aiyaposit naepol kanuka nol lomam abunit atukokin mama ejai EJAKAIT Misipa ebe, ‘Abeit eario ŋol.’” 6Kiitut idwe luka Isirael kanuka Benyamin onaeekee, kotemasi atiar, “Ejai ateker adiopet naagelar kotoma Isiraellolo. 7Kikonakini oni ai kidumakini nul luesalito aŋor, naarai kiyapo kalimito EJAKAIT ebe mam kikorakini kes adwewok aimany?”
8 # 1 Sam 11:1; 31:11; 2 Sam 2:5,6. Kotemasi atiar, “Iŋai bo adiopet kotoma atekerin nuka Isirael namam abunit mama ejai EJAKAIT Misipa?” Ido koany, amamei edio loka Yabesgilead loabunit atukokin. 9Naarai naemarere ituŋa, koany, amamei ediopet loka luiboienenete koYabes-gilead kanen. 10#Ikis 13:15; Yos 7:24-26; Tub 5:23; 1 Sam 11:7. Kijuka ekodet ituŋa ilukumin itomon ka luarei lukirigin noi, kicorak kes ebe, “Koloto kodanyata luibojete koYabes-gilead kakwanis naka ekileŋ, kanor ka ludidik da, 11#Mar 31:17. Iswamaete kwanata; imudiarete eut ŋclikiliokit, ka ŋiniberu naeperito kekiliokit.” 12#Yos 18:1; 22:9; 1 Sam 1:3; Sab 78:59,60; Yer 7:12,14. Kodumut kaneejaas luiboienenete koYabes-gilead abekon akwat aoŋon, numam ajeneteekiliokit kanuka apero kaŋes: koyanautu kes oidukurun Silo, loejai akwap naka Kanaan,
13 # Tub 20:47; Ikis 20:10. Kijuka ekodet kere kinera kaidwe luka Benyamin luajaas moru Rimon, kolimosi ainapakin kamakec, 14Kobono Benyamin kapak kaŋin; kokorakis kes apesur nuetaiarcte kesi kanor nuka Yabes-gilead: konye apotu kokadata kes. 15Kiitut ituŋa kanuka Benyamin, naarai abu EJAKAIT kibwaik arorwa atekerin nuka Isirael.
16Kosodete imakio luka ekodet atemar ebe, “Kikonakini oni ai kidumakini nul Iuesalito aŋor, naarai amudiar aŋor kotoma Benyamin?” 17Kotemasi atiar, “Ibusakinit koiaun airumet naka nul kaluakuata luka Benyamin, kinyek ateker amwatario koIsirael. 18#Tub 11:35. Konye mam oni kipedori akorakin kes adwewok aimany: naarai eyapoikinito idwe luka Isirael ebe, ‘Kikenoi ŋol loekorakini Benyamin apese aimany.’’’ 19Kotemasi atiar, “Koany, ejai epueit loka EJAKAIT ŋolikaru Silo, loejai agolitomei naka Bet-el, ari nakokide naka erot loepol loenyouni koBet-el kiton Sekem, eŋalakimak da naka Lebona.” 20Kieorakis kesi idwe luka Benyamin ebe, “Koloto, koperut komanikoria luka emusabibu: 21#Any 15:20; 32:6,19; Tub 11:34; 1 Sam 18:6; 21:11; 29:5; Yer 31:4,13. Koanyut, ido koany, arai kelomut adwe nuka Silo ekosio okosio, ilomunete do yesi da komanikoria luka emusabibu, ikamunete yesi ŋinituŋanan akeberu kotoma adwe nuka Silo, kosodete alosit akwap naka Benyamin. 22Kosodi naeponiata lukapapakee arai iinaeanakee aosan mamakosi, kitemari isio kes ebe, ‘Kokorakinai sio kes kasianut: naarai mam kipou kademakisi kes ŋinituŋanan akeberu kojie; kwabo mam yes ipotu kokorakis kes; ti do yes kigwaut akirot.’’’ 23#Tub 20:48. Kiswamata idwe luka Benyamin kwaŋin, koyanaasi aŋor, kwape ajaar etiaikee, loka nun kanuekosiete, nuapotu kesi koyanaasi: kolototo kobonosi airumetekec, kodukoto irereia, kiboioto kotoma. 24Konyout idwe luka Isirael kanen kapak kaŋin, ŋinituŋanan atekerike okekike da, kolototo kanen ŋinituŋanan airumeteke.
25 # Tub 17:6; 18:1; 19:1; Ikis 12:8; Aku 5:14; Rom 13:3-5. Kapaarasia kanun amamei ekabaka koIsirael: eswamai ŋinituŋanan nuadolito kakonyeneke bon.
