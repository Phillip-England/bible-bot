2 Aisiltonokin 8

8
1 # 1 Ika 9:10. Kosodi kanen, ketuboros ikar akaisaarei luadukio Solomon etogo loka EJAKAIT, ka etogoke da elopet, 2ireria luabu Kuram koinak Solomon, koduk Solomon kes, kisiboik idwe luka Isirael ŋina.
3 # Mar 13:21; 2 Sam 8:3. Kolot Solomon Kamat-soba, kipiko ŋes, 4#1 Ika 9:17. Kodukok Tadumor amodiŋ, ka ireria kere lukaiŋad luadukokini Kamat, 5Koduk da Bet-koron Ioimukurur, ka Bet-koron loijesara, ireria luabwaitai, lukejaas arwatatin, ka ikirigin, ka atiketa; 6#Yos 15:11. ka Baalat, ka ireria kere lukaiŋad lukejaas ka Solomon, ka ireria kere luka agalinike, ka ireria luka ikebwanenokoke, ka kere luakoto Solomon aiduk aisiyalamikinor kotoma Yerusalem, ka Lebanon, ka akwap kere naka airumuke, 7#1 Ika 9:20; Age 10:15; 15:18; Ikis 7:1. Ituŋa kere da luedeunos luka Ikitin, ka Imorain, ka Iperisin, ka Ikibin, ka Iyebusin, lumam keraas luka Isirael; 8luka idwekec luedeunos kakes kakwap, lumam idwe luka Isirael emudiara, kolemununei Solomon ikitubusiana kamakee, kiton lolo. 9#Any 19:5; Ikis 23:19; Leb 25:39; Gal 4:28,31. Konye mam abu Solomon kitubulesene idwe luka Isirael kanuka akeswam; konye keraas kesi ikajiok, ka ikapolok luka ikapolokoke, ka ikapolokitok luka agalinike ka ikebwanenokoke. 10#1 Ika 5:16; 9:23; 2 Siit 2:18. Lu kes ikapolok lukapolok luka ekabaka Solomon, kes akwat aarei ka akaisakany, luayaitos ituŋa.
11 # 1 Ika 3:1; 7:8; 9:24. Kitonyou Solomon akoku naka Parao kore loka Daudi mama ejai etogo loabu ŋesi kodukok ŋes: naarai abala ebe, “Mam akaberu iboikini togo loka Daudi ekabaka loka Isirael, naarai eraas aiboisio nukalaunak, nuadolokinit asaduku naka EJAKAIT.”
12Kosodi Solomon ainakin ainakineta nuicweio mama ejai EJAKAIT kotem loka EJAKAIT, loaduki kiŋaren kaneelomarere, 13#Any 29:38; Mar 28:3-26; 29:1; Any 23:14; Ikis 16:16; 1 Ika 9:25. kwape kibusakinitor nuka ŋinipaaran, koinanakini kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka Musa, ka nukasabaton, ka napilamununei elap, kopucin lueseunitai, irwan auni ŋolikaru, kopucit loka emugati lomam itubuunitoi, kopucit loka asabaton, kopucit da loka iswamin. 14#1 Siit 24:1; 2 Siit 5:11; 31:2; Luk 1:5,8; 1 Siit 25:1; 9:17; 26:1; 2 Sam 23:2. Kitemok, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka Daudi papake, abeanakin naka isasedotin kaijaanakinikec, ka Ilebin kaswamukec, aiturit, ka aijaanakin isasedotin kwape epudar aswam naka ŋinipaaran: ikagolok da kabeanakinikec korutei kere: naarai abu Daudi etuŋanan loka Edeke kicorak kwaŋin. 15Mam apotu konyout kaicorakinet naka ekabaka mama ejaas isasedotin ka Ilebin kadiokirot kere, ka nuka iboro luka akais nuipu da.
16Kitemokinoi aswam kere naka Solomon kiton apaaran naka apetetait naka etogo loka EJAKAIT, kiton da neadaunia. Kwaŋin kitodoloi etogo loka EJAKAIT,
17 # 1 Ika 9:26; Ikis 2:8; 2 Ika 14:22. Kosodi Solomon alosit Esion-geber, ka Elot, kariri naka aŋolol kakwap naka Edom. 18#1 Ika 9:27; 2 Siit 9:10,13; Age 10:29; Yob 22:24; Isa 13:12. Kijukak Kuram ŋes kakanin nuka ibulesinike ameerin ka ibulesin luajenete aŋolol; kouparos kaibulesin luka Solomon alosit Opir, koomata kaŋina atalatan akwat aoŋon ka akaisakany nuka esabu, koyaut mama ejai ekabaka Solomon.
