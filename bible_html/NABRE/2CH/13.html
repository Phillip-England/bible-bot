2 Aisiltonokin 13

13
1 # 1 Ika 15:1. Kokaru lokitomonet kakanykauni loka ekabaka Yeroboam kogeu Abiya airum Yuda. 2#2 Siit 11:20. Kirumak ikar iuni koYerusalem: ekiror loka totoke kerai Mikaia akoku naka Uriel loka Gibea. Kejai ejie kidiŋ Abiya ka Yeroboam,
3Kolot Abiya kajore naka ikajiok luetitiŋ, kes ituŋa ilukumin akwat aoŋon lueseunitai asurokin: kiderakin Yeroboam ejie kaŋes kituŋa ilukumin akwat akanykauni lueseunitai, lukeraas ikirigin luetitiŋ. 4#Yos 18:22. Kobwo Abiya komoru Semaraim loejai kakwap nakiukon naka Epuraim, kotema atiar, “Kopupokinai jo Yeroboam ka Isirael kere; 5#Tub 11:21-24; 1 Sam 16:12,13; 2 Sam 7:12-16; Sab 89:20; Luk 1:31-33; Mar 18:19. mam kereka ibusakinit yes ajenun ebe abu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael koinak Daudi ajakanut naka Isirael ikar kere, ŋes, ka odweke kaitutuket naka abalaŋ? 6#1 Ika 11:26; 12:20,27; 2 Siit 10:19. Konye konyou Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, ekejaanakinan loka Solomon okoku loka Daudi, kipaliik ekejakait. 7#Tub 9:4; Sab 26:4; Awa 12:11; Asw 17:5. Kotukonokinos ituŋa luutai mama ejai, odwe luka Belial, lukitogogoŋete asurokin Rekoboam okoku loka Solomon, korai Rekoboam esapat, loka etau loenonok, komam kepedori aitikokin kes.
8 # Kos 8:6. “Kwana do iwomitos yesi asurokin ajakanut naka EJAKAIT kakan naka odwe luka Daudi; iraas yesi ekodet loepol, ejaas kayes itak luka esabu luabu Yeroboam kiswamak yes araun idekesio. 9#2 Siit 11:14,15; Any 29:35. Mam baca yes ipotu kireŋeasi isasedotin luka EJAKAIT, odwe luka Aaron, ka Ilebin, kodumut isasedotin kopone loka ituŋa lukacekwapin? Tape loebuni atiakun bon kemoŋ lotobait ka imerekekin ikanykaarei, epedori araun esasedoti loka ŋul lumam eraas idekesio. 10Konye do isio, EJAKAIT ŋes Edekekosi, mam sio kipou kajalata ŋes; kija isio ka isasedotin luijaanakinete EJAKAIT, odwe luka Aaron, ka Ilebin toma aswamukec: 11#2 Siit 2:4; Leb 24:2-6; Any 27:20,21. icweikinete kesi ŋinitupuruc ka ŋinitibon ainakineta nuieweio, ka lukisupurununo lueuru maa: inapanakinete kesi imugatin luka aitodikin omesa loesegaara; ka epir loka atalai loka esabu ka atalaike ainok ŋinitiboŋ: naarai kiric isio akiro nuka EJAKAIT Ewokodeke; konye ipotu yesi kojalata ŋes. 12#Ikis 20:4; Yos 5:14; Sab 20:7; Mar 10:8; Yob 15:25,26; Asw 5:39; 9:4,5. Ido koany, ejai Edeke kasio kiŋarenikinit, ka isasedotinike karupepen nuka elulu akusia elulu asurokin yes. Yes idwe luka Isirael, sirikisurokis EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakus; naarai mam ikeriakaros.”
13Konye kitolot Yeroboam ikaperunok kokaukec: kojaas kes kiŋaren ka Yuda, ka ikaperunok kaukec, 14Kenoliasi luka Yuda kau, koany, keiai ejie kinaren, kokaukec da: kiworoto mama ejai EJAKAIT, kokuta isasedotin arupepen. 15#Mar 32:4; 2 Siit 14:12. Kosodete ituŋa luka Yuda eibwoŋ: napibwoŋas ituŋa luka Yuda, kosodi kanen, kodany Edeke Yeroboam ka Isirael kere kakonyen nuka Abiya ka Yuda. 16Kokerata idwe luka Isirael kakonyen nuka Yuda: kijaik Edeke kes akanikec. 17Koar Abiya ka ituŋake kes kearit loepol: kibiroros luaaritai luka Isirael ituŋa ilukumin akwat akany lueseunitai. 18#Tub 8:28; 2 Ika 18:5; 1 Siit 5:20; 2 Siit 16:8,9; 20:20; Sab 22:5. Kwaŋin kitonokokai idwe luka Isirael kapaarasia kaŋun, kipikosi idwe luka Yuda naarai kegoŋakina EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakec. 19#Yos 15:9; Yok 11:54. Korukitor Abiya Yeroboam, kirum ireria kamake, Bet-el ka ireriake, ka Yesana ka ireriake, ka Epuron ka ireriake. 20#1 Sam 25:38; 26:10; Esk 24:16; Asw 12:23; 1 Ika 14:20. Mam bobo abu Yeroboam kogogoŋor kapaarasia nuka Abiya: kinom EJAKAIT ŋes, kotwana. 21Konye kogogoŋor Abiya, komany aŋor atomon ka nuoŋon, kouri idwe lusap akaisaarei ka luarei, ka lupesur itomon ka lukanykape. 22#2 Siit 9:29; 12:15. Aswamisio ace nuka Abiya, ka iponesioke, ka akiroke, iwadikatai oitabo loka enabi Ido.
