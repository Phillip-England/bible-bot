2 Aisiltonokin 34

34
1 # 1 Ika 3:7-9; 13:2; 2 Ika 22:1; 1 Siit 3:14,15; 2 Siit 35:25. Kerai Yosia loka ikar ikanykauni egeuni airum; kirumak ikar akaisauni ka Iodiopet koYerusalem. 2Kiswama nuejok kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kolosenenei korotin luka Daudi papaake, komam epaari akan nakoteten karaida nakokediany. 3#1 Sam 1:24; Sab 119:9; Awa 8:17; Lim 12:1; 2 Tim 3:15; 2 Siit 15:2; 1 Ika 13:2; 2 Siit 33:17,22. Naarai kokaru lokikanyetekauni loka airumuke, eriŋa erai esapat, kogeu amoot Edeke loka Daudi papaake: kokaru lokitomonet kaarei kogeu aiteseg Yuda ka Yerusalem alemar aiboisio nuiceleleete, ka Aserim, ka aputosia nuegerer, ka aputosia nuecalara; 4#Leb 26:30; 2 Ika 23:4; Any 32:20. kobilibilai itemwan luka Ibaalim kakonyeneke; kojepeiep aputosia nuka akoloŋ nuiceleleete kokujukec; komunyumuny Aserim, ka aputosia nuegerer, ka aputosia nuecalara, kitarau apua, kipurupuranak atesina nuka ŋul kaluapotu koinakis asuban mamakec, 5#1 Ika 13:2. Kicwei akojo nuka isasedotin kotemwanakec, kiteseg Yuda ka Yerusalem, 6Kiswama kwaŋin koreria luka Manase ka Epuraim ka Simeon, kiton Naputali, ka aapoŋorukec airimor. 7#Ikis 9:21. Kobilibil itemwan, kidecidec Aserim ka aputosia nuegerer araun apua, kojepejep aputosia nuka akoloŋ anatar akwap kere naka Isirael, koboŋo Yerusalem.
8 # 2 Ika 22:3. Kokaru lokitomonet kakanykauni loka airumuke, kedau aiteseg akwap, ka etogo, kijuka Sapan okoku loka Asalia, ka Maaseia ekayaiton ere, ka Yoa okoku loka Yoakas ekesiitonokinan, aiteteun etogo loka EJAKAIT Ekedeke. 9#2 Ika 12:4. Kopotu mama ejai Kilikia esasedoti lopeti, kijaikis apesan nuayaunitai togo loka Edeke, nuatukokinito Ilebin, ikagolok ekek, kakan naka Manase ka Epuraim, ka aatoŋot kere naka Isirael, ka Yuda kere ka Benyamin, ka luiboiete koYerusalem. 10Kijaikis akan naka ikeswamak luiteete etogo loka EJAKAIT; koinakis ikeswamak lueswamaete togo loka EJAKAIT aiteteunia ka apucunio etogo, 11Koinakis ikekeok, ka ikadukok, agwelia amor nuikee, ka akito nuimorikikinete, aiswamaa abaoi nuka itogoi luapotu ikabakan luka Yuda komudiasi. 12#2 Ika 12:15; 22:7; Nek 7:2; Awa 28:20; 1 Kor 4:2. Kiswamata ituŋa kamunonut; ikaanyanarakakec Yakat ka Obadia, Ilebin, lukodwe luka Merari; Sekaria ka Mesulam, lukodwe luka Ikokatin aiteten: ice luka Ilebin da, kere luepedorete airam ejok iboro lueruosi, 13#1 Siit 23:4,5; Esr 7:6; Yer 8:8; Mat 26:3. Kepolokinito kesi luebwokete abwokon, kitetenata kere lueswamaete ŋolipone loka aijaanakin: ajaas koLebin da ikewadikak, ka ikapolokitok, ka ikagolok.
14 # Ikis 31:24-26; 2 Ika 22:8. Nakitolomuniata apesan nuayaunitai togo loka EJAKAIT, kodum Kilika esasedoti eitabo loka ikisila luka EJAKAIT luainakinio kama ka Musa. 15Koboŋok Kilikia kotema Sapan ekewadikan atiar, “Adum eoŋ eitabo loka ikisila kotogo loka EJAKAIT.” Kosodi Kilikia aijaikin Sapan eitabo. 16Kodaka Sapan eitabo mama ejai ekabaka, ido kinyakak ekabaka akiro, ebe, “Iswamaete ikonikejaanakinak kere nukititiŋitai. 17Akorasi apesan nuadumuna kotogo loka EJAKAIT, ejaikis akan naka ikaanyanarak, akan da naka ikeswamak.” 18#Ikis 17:19; Yos 1:8. Kosodi Sapan ekewadikan alimokin ekabaka ebe, “Kejaiki Kilikia esasedoti eitabo.” Kosiom Sapan kakonyen nuka ekabaka.
19 # Ikis 28:1-68; Rom 3:20; 7:7-14; Gal 2:19; 3:10-13; 2 Ika 19:1; Nek 8:9; Sab 94:12,13; 119:120; Yer 23:9,29; Yok 2:13; Kbk 3:16. Kosodi kanen, kiira ekabaka akiro nuka ikisila, kocil ikenapen. 20#2 Ika 22:12. Kicorak ekabaka Kilikia, ka Akikam okoku loka Sapan, ka Abudon okoku loka Mika, ka Sapan ekewadikan, ka Asaia ekejaanakinan loka ekabaka ebe, 21#2 Ika 17:6.“Koloto yesi, kiŋita EJAKAIT kanukan, kanuka ŋul da luideuna koIsirael ka Yuda, akiro nuka eitabo loadumun: naarai epol anyunyura naka EJAKAIT nakibukokina oni, naarai mam lukapapaawok apotu koricito akirot naka EJAKAIT, aiswamaenen kwape ajaatar kere nuiwadikatai oitabo lo.”
22 # Any 15:20; Tub 4:4; Luk 2:36; Asw 21:9; 2 Ika 22:14. Kolot Kilikia, ka ŋul da luecorakini ekabaka, mama ejai Kulida anabi, aberu naka Salum okoku loka Tokat, okoku loka Kasara, ekeŋadan inapen; (eboienenei nin koYerusalem katutubet nakiareit;) kinerut kaŋes ŋun. 23Kotema ŋesi kes atiar, “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, ebe;, Kolimokis etuŋanan lokijukunit yes neajai ebe, 24#Yos 23:16; 2 Ika 21:12; 23:26,27; 2 Siit 36:6,21; Isa 5:5; Yer 25:9; Dan 9:11-16; Amo 3:6; Sek 1:6. Inera kwanata EJAKAIT ebe, Koany, ayauni aronis aibois na, ka neejaas luiboiete kotoma, aikenito kere nuiwadikatai oitabo loasiomi kakonyen nuka ekabaka loka Yuda: 25naarai kajala, esupuruikis lukisupurununo odekesio ice, aitunyunyurikin eoŋ kaswamisio kere nuka akaninikec; ŋes ebukokinor akanyunyura aibois na, komam eduŋeori. 26Konye ekabaka loka Yuda, lokijukuni aiŋitakin EJAKAIT, kolimokis ŋes kwanata ebe, Inera kwanata EJAKAIT Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Nuka akiro nukipupuni, 27#Sab 51:17; 86:5; Dan 9:9. kanu anonokiaror ekonitau, kitasonikin kakonyen nuka Edeke, naipupunio akekiro aluikin aibois na, aluikin da luiboiete kotoma, kitasonikin kakonyeneka, kocil ikoninapen, komony akiyo kakonyeneka; inera EJAKAIT ebe, Keira eoŋ da, 28koany, atukokini jo mama ejaas lukapapaakon, kitukokinio jo ailitikon inapakina, kwabo mam eanyunete akonikonye aronis kere naayauni aibois na, ka luiboiete kotoma.’” Kinyakakinai ekabaka akiro.
29 # 2 Ika 23:1. Kosodi ekabaka aijukar kotukok imakio kere luka Yuda ka Yerusalem. 30Kodoka ekabaka togo loka EJAKAIT, ka ituŋa kere luka Yuda, ka lukiboiete koYerusalem, ka isasedotin, ka Ilebin, ka ituŋa kere, lukapolok ka ludidik kere: kosiomak akiikec akiro kere nuka eitabo loka aitutuket loadumun kotogo loka EJAKAIT. 31#2 Ika 11:14. Kobwo ekabaka kaiboisike, kitutuk aitutuket kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, atupit EJAKAIT, airie aicorakinetake, ka aitejenunetake, ka ikisilake, keketau kere, kakeijar da kere, aitodol akiro kere nuka aitutuket nuewadikatai oitabo lo. 32Kitogogoŋ da ituŋa kere Iuadumuna koYerusalem ka Benyamin, aitodol. Kiswamata lukiboiete koYerusalem kwape ajaar aitutuket naka Edeke, Edeke loka lukapapaakec. 33#1 Ika 11:5; Yer 3:10; 2 Siit 24:2. Kolema Yosia italesia kere kakwapin kere nuaraas nuka idwe luka Isirael, kisidik luadumuna kere koIsirael aijaanakin, ŋes aijaanakin EJAKAIT Ekecedeke. Apaarasiake kere mam apotu konyoutu katupit EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakec.
