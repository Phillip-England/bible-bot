2 Aisiltonokin 24

24
1 # 2 Ika 11:21; 12:1. Kerai Yoas loka ikar ikanykaarei egeuni airum; kirumak ikar akaisaoŋon ko Yerusalem: ekiror loka totoke kerai Sibia naka Beer-seba, 2#2 Siit 25:2; 26:5. Kiswama Yoas nuejokuka kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT apaarasia kere nuka Yekoiada esasedoti. 3Komanyak Yekoiada ŋes aŋor aarei; kouri idwe lusap ka lupesur.
4Kosodi kanen akaulo kaŋun, kokoto Yoas aiteteun etogo loka EJAKAIT. 5#2 Ika 12:4; 2 Siit 29:3; 34:8. Kotukonok ŋesi isasedotin ka Ilebin, kotema kes atiar, “Kolomasi oreria luka Yuda, kotukonokis apesan kama ka Isirael kere aiteteunia etogo loka Ekusideke ŋolikaru, koanyutu ebe isuburikinete yesi akirot.” Konye mam Ilebin apotu kisuburikis, 6#2 Ika 12:7; Any 30:12-16; Mar 1:50; 17:7,8; Asw 7:44. Konyarau ekabaka Yekoiada ekapolon, kotema ŋes atiar, “Kanukinyo mam ca jo itepesenia Ilebin ayaŋaun koYuda ka Yerusalem esolo loka Musa ekejaanakinan loka EJAKAIT, ka loka ekodet loka Isirael kanuka ewema loka aitejenunet?” 7#2 Siit 21:17; 22:3,4; Sab 12:8; 2 Ika 12:4. Naarai apotu odwe luka Atalia, aberu ya nakaronon, kobelaros togo loka Edeke; iboro da lutiakuna kere luka etogo loka EJAKAIT ainakis Ibaalim.
8 # 2 Ika 12:9. Kicorak do ekabaka, kiswamata asaduku, kibwaikis kiŋa orute loka etogo loka EJAKAIT. 9#Any 36:6. Kolimoroi anatar Yuda ka Yerusalem, ainun EJAKAIT esolo loabu Musa ekejaanakinan loka Edeke kicorak Isirael kamodiŋ. 10Kiyalamas ikapolok kere, ka ituŋa kere, koyautu kocakakis asaduku, aitodol neadaunotor. 11#2 Ika 12:10. Kosodi keyaun asaduku mama ejaas ikapolok luka ekabaka kakan naka Ilebin, keanyutu ebe ejaas apesan nuipu, kobu ekewadikan loka ekabaka ka ekaroan loka esasedoti lopeti, kobuko asaduku, koyaŋaasi, kinyakakis aiboisike. Kiswamata kwaŋin ninipaaran kotukonokis apesan nuipu noi, 12Koinak ekabaka ka Yekoiada luiswamaete aswam naka aijaanakin togo loka EJAKAIT; kisiwoteoto ikadukok ka ikekeok aiteteun etogo loka EJAKAIT, ikatatak da asua ka aisinin aiteteunia etogo loka EJAKAIT, 13#Nek 4:7. Kiswamata ikeswamak, kitodoloto aswam ejok, kosipokis etogo loka Edeke kaibusesuke, kitogogoŋoto ŋes, 14#1 Ika 7:50; 2 Ika 12:13; Any 29:38; Bur 7:27; 1 Pet 1:18,19; Apu 5:9. Kedautu kesi, koyaut apesan nuedeunos mama ejai ekabaka ka Yekoiada. Kiswamauniata iboro lukotogo loka EJAKAIT, iboro aijaanakinia, ka ainakinia, ka akaalei, ka iboro luka esabu ka luka epuesa, Koinanakis ainakineta nuicweio togo loka EJAKAIT tiritir apaarasia kere nuka Yekoiada,
15 # Yob 5:26. Konye komojoŋo Yekoiada, kipu apaarasiake noi, kotwana; korai loka ikar akwatat ka akaisauni naketwania. 16#1 Ika 2:10. Aponi kidaikinai ore loka Daudi neejaas ikabakan, naarai abu kiswama nuejok koIsirael, mama ejai Edeke da ka ekekek.
17 # Asw 20:29. Akaulo katwanare naka Yekoiada kopotu ikapolok luka Yuda, kisudedoto mama ejai ekabaka, Kosodi ekabaka akiirar kes. 18#1 Ika 14:23; Yos 22:20; Tub 5:8; 2 Sam 24:1; 2 Siit 19:2; 28:13; Kos 5:10. Kojalata etogo loka EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakec, kijaanakis Aserim ka aputosia: kobunok anyunyura Yuda ka Yerusalem kanuka adiakaretekec kana. 19#2 Siit 36:15; Nek 9:26; Yer 7:25,26; 25:4; Luk 11:47-51. Konye kijukanakini ŋesi kes inabin ainyakun bobo mama ejai EJAKAIT; kitejenikis kes; konye koŋeroto akiirar kes,
20 # 2 Siit 15:1,2; Tub 6:34; Mar 14:41; 1 Sam 13:13,14; Ikis 29:25,26; Yer 2:19. Kodoik emoyo laka Edeke Sekaria okoku loka Yekoiada esasedoti: kobwo kokuju kituŋa, kotema kes atiar, “Inera Edeke kwanata ebe, Kanukinyo itapaariata yesi aicorakineta nuka EJAKAIT, kinyeis akeriakar? Naarai ijalata EJAKAIT, ŋes da kijali yes.” 21#Mat 23:35; Asw 7:58. Kinerutu aluikin ŋes, kibiribiroto ŋes kamor kaicorakinet naka ekabaka alaro naka etogo loka EJAKAIT. 22Kwaŋin mam abu Yoas ekabaka kiitu asianut nakiswamakinit Yekoiada papa loka Sekaria, konye koar okokuke, Etwani kotema atiar, “Kosesen EJAKAIT, kitepesen.”
23 # 2 Ika 12:17. Kosodi kanen, awasia naka ekaru, kobu ajore naka Isurian asurokin ŋes: kopotu Yuda ka Yerusalem, komudiasi ikapolok kere luka ituŋa kotuŋa, kijukasi luŋatunakec kere mama ejai ekabaka loka Damasiko, 24#Leb 26:8,25; Ikis 32:30; Isa 30:17; Ikis 28:25; Isa 10:5. Naarai abu ajore naka Isurian kerioŋet loedit loka ituŋa; kijaik EJAKAIT ajore naepol noi akanikee, naarai apotu kojalata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakec. Kwaŋin kitodolik Yoas etube.
25 # 2 Ika 12:18,20; Sab 10:14. Kenyoutu kesi kamakeŋ, (naarai kinyeis ŋes edeka noi,) kinerutu ikekejaanakinak asurokin ŋes kanuka aokot nuka odwe luka Yekoiada esasedoti, koarakis ŋes oitadake, kotwana: aponi kidaikinai ore loka Daudi, konye mam aponi kidaikinai atesina nuka ikabakan. 26Lu kes luenerunete asurokin ŋes; Sabad okoku loka Simeat Amonit, ka Yekosabad okoku loka Simirit Amoabit. 27Nuka odweke, ka apolou naka aritai nukisibwokio, ka airueakin aiduk etogo loka Edeke, koany, iwadikatai kakiro nuka eitabo loka ikabakan. Kirum Amasia okokuke aiboisike.
