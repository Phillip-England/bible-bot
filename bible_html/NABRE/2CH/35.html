2 Aisiltonokin 35

35
1 # 2 Ika 23:21; Any 12:6; Mar 9:3; Esr 6:19; Esk 45:21. Koric Yosia Aleleŋio mama ejai EJAKAIT koYerusalem: koarata lukaleleŋio kapaaran nakitomonet kaoŋon naka elap losodit. 2#2 Siit 23:18; Esr 6:18; 1 Siit 22:19; 2 Siit 29:5,11. Kibwaik isasedotin aswamukec, kisinyikok kes aijaanakin kotogo loka EJAKAIT. 3#Ikis 33:10; 2 Siit 30:22; 34:14; Mal 2:7; 2 Siit 5:7; 1 Siit 23:26. Kotema ŋesi Ilebin luisisianakinete Isirael kere, luaraas lukalaunak mama ejai EJAKAIT atiar, “Kibwaikis asaduku nakalaunan togo loabu Solomon okoku loka Daudi ekabaka loka Isirael koduk; mam bobo ejaun abwokon okeperinikus: kijaanakisi do EJAKAIT Ekusideke, ka iketuŋa Isirael. 4#1 Siit 9:10; 23:1; 24:1; 2 Siit 8:14. Kitemonokisi kwape ejaatar ikekia luka lukapapaakus, kabeanakinikus, kwape ajaatar aiwadikaeta nuka Daudi ekabaka loka Isirael, ido kwape ajaatar aiwadikaeta nuka Solomon okokuke. 5#Sab 134:1. Kobwaata kaibois nakalaunan kwape ajaatar atiaketa nuka ikekia luka lukapapaakus luka ikusiinacan idwe luka ituŋa, kojaun kaŋolidiopet atiaket naka ekek loka lukapapaa luka Ilebin. 6#Any 19:10; 2 Siit 29:5; 30:3,15; Esr 6:20; Yob 1:5; Sab 51:7; Yok 2:16; Bur 9:13,14. Koarata lukaleleŋio, kitalaunos bonik, kitemokis nuka ikusiinaean, aswam kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT kama ka Musa.”
7 # Mik 6:7; 2 Siit 30:24. Kosodi Yosia ainakin idwe luka ituŋa, ŋul kere luajaas kanen, kosipan, imerekekin ludidik kaikalei, kes kere araun ainakineta nuka aleleŋio, emwaasi ilukumin akaisauni, ka imoŋin Iutoba ilukumin iuni: ŋul keraas koboro luka ekabaka. 8#2 Kor 9:7. Koinakis ikapolokoke araun ainakinet naka akote mama ejaas ituŋa, ka isasedotin, ka Ilebin. Apotu Kilikia ka Sekaria ka Yekiel, ikayaitok luka etogo loka Edeke koinakis isasedotin ibaren ludidik ilukumin iarei ka akwat akanykape, ka imoŋin akwat auni araun ainakineta nuka aleleŋio. 9Konania da, ka Semaia ka Netanel, ijnacanake, ka Kasabia ka Yeiel ka Yosabad, iiaka luka Ilebin, koinakis Ilebin ibaren ludidik ilukumin ikany, ka imoŋin akwat akany.
10 # Esr 6:18. Kwaŋin kitemokin aijaanakin, kobwaata isasedotin kaiboisikec, ka Ilebin kabeanakinikec, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka ekabaka, 11#Leb 1:5,6; 2 Siit 29:22,34. Koarata lukaleleŋio, kobwatanakis isasedotin aokot nuejaunete kakanikec, kojenata Ilebin kes. 12#Leb 3:3. Kolemasi ainakineta nuicweio, tetere einakinete kwape ajaatar atiaketa nuka ikekia luka lukapapaa luka idwe luka ituŋa, ainakin EJAKAIT, kwape iwadikatere oitabo loka Musa. Kiswamata kwaŋin komoniŋ da. 13#Any 12:8; Ikis 16:7; 1 Sam 2:13-15. Kopeoto Iukaleleŋio kakim kwape ajaar aicorakinet: kipooto ainakineta nukalaunak kotoma amotii, ka akeito, kasepulian da, kodakasi katipet mama ejaas idwe kere luka ituŋa. 14Kokau kitemokis nukee, ka nuka isasedotin da; naarai kirododorito isasedotin odwe luka Aaron ainakin ainakineta nuicweio ka asiepai kiton kuare: kanuka ŋun kitemokis Ilebin nukec, ka nuka isasedotin odwe luka Aaron. 15#1 Siit 25:1; 9:17,18; 26:14. Kejaas ikayook, odwe luka Asap aiboisikec, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka Daudi, ka Asap, ka Keman, ka Yedutun ekadwaran loka ekabaka; ajaas ikagolok kaŋolirute: komam ipudas anyoun kaswamukec, naarai kitemokis ikeceinacan Ilebin nukec.
16Kwaŋin kitemokin aijaanakin kere naka EJAKAIT kapaaran kaŋin, airic aleleŋio, ainakin da ainakineta nuicweio kotem loka EJAKAIT, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka ekabaka Yosia. 17#Any 12:15; 13:6; Leb 23:5,8; Mar 28:16,17; Ikis 16:3-8; 2 Siit 30:21; 1 Kor 5:7,8. Koricata idwe luka Isirael luajaas nen aleleŋio kapak kaŋin, ka epucit loka imugatin lumam itubuunitoi apaarasia akanykaarei, 18#2 Ika 23:22,23. Amamei da aleleŋio naikoni nen naaricitai koIsirael ageun kapaarasia nuka Samuel enabi; kwabo mam ediokabaka laka Isirael abu koric aleleŋio kwape kericia Yosia, ka isasedotin ka Ilebin, ka Yuda kere ka Isirael luajaas nen, ka lukiboiete koYerusalem. 19Kokaru lokitomonet kakanykauni loka airum naka Yosia ŋes kericere aleleŋio ŋin,
20 # 2 Ika 23:29; Yer 46:2. Akaulo kaŋun kere, kedau Yosia aitemokin eyekalu, kolot Neko ekabaka loko Misiri asurokin Karakemis eiduny ka Eupurat; koloma Yosia asurokin ŋes. 21#2 Ika 18:25; Isa 36:10; Dan 4:35. Konye kijuka ŋesi ikiyakia mama ejai ebe, “Kimariai inyo eoŋ ka jo, ijo ekabaka loka Yuda? Mam eoŋ kasurokini jo lolo, dimarai asurokin ekek lokijior eoŋ; kecorak Edeke aisunyakin: kinyek ijo aiilokin Edeke, loejai keoŋ, kinyek amudiar jo.” 22#1 Ika 14:2; 22:30; 2 Siit 18:29. Konye koŋer Yosia aibelokin akekonyen, konye kitegelikin bon, kosodi ejie kaŋes, mam abu kiira akiro nuka Neko, nuapote kaituk naka Edeke, kobu ejie aditot naka Megido. 23#1 Ika 22:34. Kogumata ikagumak imalia ekabaka Yosia; kobala ekabaka ikekejaanakinak ebe, “Koyaŋaarai eoŋ: naarai kemusuŋi noi.” 24#2 Ika 23:30; Sek 12:11. Kitodokut ikekejaanakinak ŋes kagali, kibwaikis ŋes agali nakiareit naajai kaŋes, koyaut ŋes Yerusalem; kotwana, aponi kidaikinai atesina nuka lukapapaake. Kibworoto Yuda kere ka Yerusalem Yosia. 25#Yer 22:10; Aku 4:20; Lim 12:5; Yer 9:17; Amo 5:16; Mat 9:23; Yer 22:20. Kokuudian Yeremia Yosia: kinerata ikayook kere ka akayook nuka Yosia kakuudianakec, kiton lolo: kitaraut aicorakinet koIsirael: ido koany, iwadikatai toma akuudian. 26#Sab 112:6; Awa 10:7; 22:1. Aswamisio ace nuka Yosia, ka akeswamisio nuejok, kwape ejaatar nuiwadikatai okisila luka EJAKAIT, 27akeswamisio da, nusodit ka nukawasia, koany, iwadikatai oitabo loka ikabakan luka Isirael ka Yuda.
