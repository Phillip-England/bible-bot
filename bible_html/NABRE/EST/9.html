Esita 9

9
1 # Est 8:12; 3:13; 2 Sam 22:41; Isa 14:2. Kosodi kolap lokitomonet kaarei, ŋes elap Adar, kapaaranike nakitomonet kauni, naabu koapunitor aicorakinet naka ekabaka ka ekekisil aitodolikinio, kapaaran naamunotor lukasurup luka Iyudayan ayait kes; kwabo abu kilocokin abuburiar kes, koyaitos Iyudayan ŋul lukelaito kes. 2#Est 8:11,17; Sab 71:13; Any 23:27. Kotukonokinos Iyudayan oreriakec anatar ibukui kere luka ekabaka Akasuero, aikam nul luemootos aitican kes: amamei da ituŋanan yenepedor airebokin kes: naarai kodoikinite atekerin kere akurian kes. 3#Awa 16:7. Apotu ikapolok kere lukibukui, ka lukibukui, ka ikayaitok, ka ŋul da lukiswamaenenete aswam naka ekabaka kinarakis Iyudayan; naarai kodoikinite kes akurian Morudekai. 4#2 Sam 3:1; 1 Siit 11:9; Sab 1:3; Awa 4:18; Isa 9:7. Naarai kerai Morudekai ekapolon kotogo loka ekabaka, kisiarakina eemutuke anatar ibukui kere: naarai kiyatanakin etuŋanan ŋol Morudekai apoloor noi. 5Kodanyata Iyudayan lukasurupokee kere iposete kes kekilen, ka aiar, ka amudiar, kiswamakis nul lukelaito kes kwape akototor. 6KoSusa togo lokapolou apotu Iyudayan koarata komudiasi ituŋa akwat akany, 7Ka Parasanadata, ka Dalapon, ka Asapata, 8ka Porata, ka Adalia, ka Aridata, 9ka Paramasata, ka Arisai, ka Aridai, ka Baisata. 10#Any 20:5; Est 5:11; Yob 18:19; Sab 21:10; Age 14:14-16; Est 8:11. Koarata da odwe lutomon luka Kaman okoku loka Kamedata, lokasurup loka Iyudayan; konye mam kes apotu kibwaikis akecekan onasit,
11Kapaaran kaŋin aponi koyaunai etiai loka nul luaarakinio Susa togo lokapolou mama ejai eka baka. 12Kotema ekabaka Esita akabaka atiar, “Aarata Iyudayan amudiasi da ituŋa akwat akany koSusa togo lokapolou, ka odwe lutomon luka Kaman da; asa do eswamata anyoika kobukui ice uka ekabakal Inyo bo ca ilipit ijo? Kiinakinio. Arai inyo bo bobo Iinainait? Kiswamai.” 13#2 Sam 21:6,9. Kosodi Esita atemar atiar, “Arai kitoiok ekabaka, kocamakinai Iyudayan luejaas Susa aswam moi da kwape ejaar ekisil loka lolo, kikaiakinai da odwe lutomon luka Kaman akito.” 14Kicorak ekabaka kikwaikinai nepenen: kolimoroi ekisil koSusa togo lokapolou; kikajakinai da odwe lutomon luka Kaman. 15Kotukonokinos Iyudayan luajaas Susa apaaran nakitomonet kaonon da naka elap Adar, koarata ituŋa akwat auni koSusa; konye mam kes apotu kibwaikis akecekan onasit,
16 # Leb 26:7,8; 1 Ses 5:22. Kotukonokinos Iyudayan ice luajaas obukui, kobwoikis aijarakee, koiaut kaibworar kama kalukasurupokec, koarata ilukumin akaisakanykaarei ka lukany kanul kalukelaito kes; konye mam kes apotu kibwaikis akanikec onasit, 17Aponi nun kiswamai kapaaran nakitomonet kauni naka elap Adar; kapaaranike nakitomonet kaonon kibworasi kesi, kituluosi apaaran naka ipucino ka aiyalama. 18Konye apotu Iyudayan luajaas Susa kotukonokinos kapaaran nakitomonet kauni ka apaaran nakitomonet kaonon da; kapaaran nakitomonet kakany kibworasi, kituluosi apaaran naka ipucino ka aiyalama. 19#Ikis 16:11,14; Sab 118:15; Apu 11:10; 1 Sam 25:8; Nek 8:10-12; Est 8:17. Ŋes ituluoriata Iyudayan luka ireria ludidik luibojete koreria luemameete arwatatin apaaran nakitomonet kaonon naka elap Adar apaaran naka aiyalama ka ipucino, apaaran da naejok, naijaanakinotor da akoreta.
20Kiwadika Morudekai ŋun, kijuka ibaluan mama ejaas Iyudayan kere luajaas obukui kere luka ekabaka Akasuero, lueapiete ka luelwaka da, 21#Sab 145:4. kicorak kes aidar apaaran nakitomonet kaonon naka elap Adar, apaaranike nakitomonet kakany da, nolikaru. 22#Sab 30:11; Mat 5:4; Yok 16:20-22; Asw 2:44-46. Kes apaarasia nuadumuniata Iyudayan aibworar kama kalukasurupokee, elap da loebelokinotor ieanikee araun aiyalama, nakaibworo araun apaaran naejok; kituluosi kes apaarasia nuka ipucino ka aiyalama, ka nuka aijaanakinor akoreta, ainakinia da lukican ainakineta.
23Kisumunoto Iyudayan aiswamaenen kwape egeariata, ido kwape ewadikakinia kes Morudekai; 24#Est 3:6,7. naarai abu Kaman okoku loka Kamedata, Egagait, lokasurup loka Iyudayan kere, kopeleik amudiar Iyudayan, abu kilamilam Pur kes iroeta, apiar kes, ka amudiar da; 25#1 Sam 24:12,13; Est 7:10; Sab 7:16; 37:12,13. konye kedol akirot akonyen nuka ekabaka, kicorak kobaluan eboteteke lokaronon loabilakinit ŋesi Iyudayan kobe akouke elopet; ŋes da ka odweke kikajakinai akito. 26Ŋes kemaikinere apaarasia ŋun Purim, kwape ejaar ekiror Pur. Kosodi kanuka akiro kere nuka ebalua kanol, ka nuadautu kesi aanyun kakirot kaŋin, ka nuadoikis kes da, 27#Est 8:17; Isa 56:3,6; Sek 2:11. ŋes kieorakiniata Iyudayan, kisumunot da kanukec, kanuka aurikec da, ka nul kere luimoranakinos kakes, ebe, idarenenete apaarasia nun nuarei kwape ajaar aiwadikaeteke, ido kwape ajaar apak naeseunitai nolikaru. 28Kiitununio apaarasia ŋun kidareneneo da anatar aturio kere, ikekia kere, kanolibuku ka ŋolire; kinyeis apaarasia ŋun nuka Purim aitwatanario koYudayan, karaida aiitunetekec aolior kaurikec.
29 # Est 2:15; 8:10. Kosodi Esita akabaka, akoku naka Abikail, ka Morudekai Eyudayat, aiwadik kapolou kere aitogogoŋ ebalua ŋol lokiareit loka Purim, 30Kijuka ibaluan mama ejaas Iyudayan kere, mama ejaas ibukui akwatat ka akaisaarei ka lukanykaarei luka ajakanut naka Akasuero, kakiro nuka ainapakin ka abeit, 31aitogogoŋ apaarasia ŋun nuka Purim kapakiokee nueseunitai, kwape kicorakinia Morudekai Eyudayat ka Esita akabaka, ido kwape kicorakiniata kesi kanukec ka aurikec da, kakiro nuka ailunukec ka aimony. 32Kitogogon aicorakinet naka Esita akiro nun nuka Purim. Kiwadikai da oitabo.
