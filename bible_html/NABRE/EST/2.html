Esita 2

2
1 # Est 1:19. Akaulo kanun, nakiyamakinor anyunyura naka ekabaka Akasuero, kiitu Basiti, ka nueswama ŋesi, ka naaponi kisilakinai mamakeŋ. 2#Age 12:14,15; 1 Ika 1:2. Kosodete ikejaanakinak luka ekabaka luijaanakinete ŋes atemari ŋes atiar, “Mere komoikinai ekabaka abekon nukatumunak nuitemelelas: 3koseu ekabaka ijaka kobukui kere luka ajakanutuke, kotukonokis abekon kere nukatumunak nuitcmelelas Susa togo lokapolou, togo loka aŋor, toma akan naka Kegai ebwacit loka ekabaka, ekedaran aŋor; kijaikinai iborokee luka aitesegaario: 4apese da naitojokit ekabaka koraun akabaka aibois naka Basiti.” Kitalaka akirot nin ekabaka; kiswama kwaŋin.
5Kojai Eyudayat toma Susa togo lokapolou, ekekiror Morudekai, okoku loka Yair, okoku loka Simei, okoku loka Kis, Ebenyamenit: 6#2 Ika 24:14,15; 2 Siit 36:10,20; Yer 22:24. lokeyanaario ko Yerusalem nepepe ka lueioreunitai ka Yekonia ekabaka loka Yuda, loabu Nebukadanesar ekabaka loko Babulon koyana. 7Kitanu ŋesi Kadasa, ŋes Esita, akoku naka papake lodidi: naarai amamei nin papa karaida toto, etemelel apese ajok asesen noi; ketwana papake ka totoke, koyana Morudekai ŋes araun akokuke, 8Kosodi kanen, kepupun aicorakinet naka ekabaka, ka ekekisil, kotukokinitete apesur nuipu Susa togo lokapolou, akan naka Kegai, koyanaarai Esita togo loka ekabaka, kotoma akan naka Kegai, ekedaran loka aŋor. 9Kisiyalamik ŋes apese, kodum asianut kamaken; kisunya ŋesi ainakin ŋin iboroke luka aitesegaario, ka akorakinetake, ka apesur akanykaarei nuibusakinit ainakinio kotogo loka ekabaka: abu kiloeo ŋes ka apesuruke kitolomik kes aibois naitelekarit ajokis kotogo loka aŋor, 10#Est 3:8; Leb 26:36; Ikis 28:65. Mam abu Esita kitejenu atekerike ka ikeinacan: naarai abu Morudekai kieorite ŋes ebe sirikitejenu. 11Kitorenenei Morudekai nolikolonit kinaren kalaro naka etogo loka aŋor, ajenun kwape ejaar Esita, ka nuedoikinete ŋes.
12Kedol abeikin naka ninipese alomar mama ejai ekabaka Akasuero, kedaun ŋes aiswamakinio kwape ejaar ekisil loka aŋor, ilapio itomon ka luarei, (naarai kedoloto kwaŋin apaarasia nuka aitesegaariokec, kes ilapio ikanykape akinyet naka luesaete, ka ilapio ikanykapc aures namaa, ka iboro luka aiteseg aŋor,) 13ŋes do ebunio apese mama ejai ekabaka, koinakinai ŋes nuekoto kere alosit kaŋes enyouni kotogo loka aŋor alomar togo loka ekabaka. 14Elosi ŋesi eboŋ, kobonun tupuruc toma togo lokiareit loka aŋor, akan naka Saasagas, ebwacit loka ckabaka, loidari abusian: mam bobo nin alomanar mama ejai ekabaka, dimikito isiyalamikinit ekabaka, enyaraunitai ekirorike.
15 # Eko 6:9; Asw 7:10; 2 Tim 4:18. Kosodi nakedolio abeikin naka Esita, akoku naka Abikail papa lodidi loka Morudekai, loabu koya ŋes araun akokuke, alomar mama ejai ekabaka, mam abu kipuda ibore dimarai luaseiki ŋes Kegai ebwacit loka ekabaka, ekedaran loka aŋor. Kominunai Esita kakonyen nuka ŋul kere luasesenete ŋes. 16#Est 3:7. Aponi Esita koyanaarai mama ejai ekabaka Akasuero toma togoke loka akabakane olap lokitomonet, ŋes elap Tebet, okaru lokikanyetekaarei loka airumuke. 17#Age 41:40; 1 Sam 2:8; Est 3:14; Yob 36:7; Sab 75:5; Luk 1:48,49; Yak 2:5; Apu 3:21. Komina ekabaka Esita aitclekarit aŋor kere, kodum ŋes asianut ka aminunio kakonyeneke aitelekarit abekon kere; kibwaik ŋesi aboko naka akabakanc akouke kitarau ŋes akabaka aibois naka Basiti. 18#Age 29:22; Tub 14:10; Est 1:3; Mat 25:1-10; Mk 6:21; Luk 14:8. Kosodi ekabaka aipoikin ikapolokoke ka ikejaanakinakake epucit loepol, ŋes epucit loka Esita; koinak ibukui aitimio, kokor ainakineta, kwape ajaar aikor naka ekabaka.
19 # Est 3:2. Naatukokinotor abekon irwan nakiareit, abu Morudekai kiboikin orute loka ekabaka. 20Mam ber Esita etejenunit ikeinacan ka aketeker; kwape ecorakinia Morudekai; naarai kiswama Esita aicorakinet naka Morudekai, kwape eswamaenena eroko ŋesi esai ŋes. 21Apaarasia kanun iboie Morudekai korute loka ekabaka, konyunyurikinos ibwacin iarei luka ekabaka, Bigitan ka Teres, kanul luidarete ekek, komoata aikamun ekabaka Akasuero. 22#Est 6:1,2. Kojenu Morudekai, kiteienu ŋesi Esita akabaka; kolimok Esita ekabaka nun kokiror loka Morudekai. 23#Age 40:19; Ikis 21:22. Kedautu aiŋic akiro ŋun, apotu kotakanut ebe ikote nen, aponi kes kere iarei kikajakinai okitoi: aponi kiwadikai da oitabo loka aisiitonokin kakonyen nuka ekabaka.
