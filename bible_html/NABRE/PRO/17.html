Awaragasia 17

17
1Ejok aiban neejai aiyapaara,
Itia ainyam epucit kotogo loeleleba aŋolopa.
2Ebulesit lokacoa eyai ikoku yeniswamai nukileie,
Ido irumi airumet koinaean.
3 # Sab 26:2; Awa 27:21; Yer 17:10; Mal 3:3. Amot nes ililiaunere epuesa, eupa da esabu;
Konye EJAKAIT itemi itauon,
4Ibwaikini loiswamai aronis eketau neejaas aituke nueroko:
Iirari da lokenalan aŋajep naka ameŋeranut.
5 # Yob 31:29; Awa 24:17; Oba 1:12. Loemoear lukican emoromoroi Lokasubanike:
Ŋol da loisiyalamikinit ican mam eswaki aiticanio,
6Idwe luka idwe kes aboko naka imakio;
Aibuses da naka idwe ŋes lukapapakee.
7Mam ibusakinit loban einer nuepolok:
Ido bo do aituk nakainal ibusakinit biai lokapolon?
8 # Age 39:21; Dan 6:3. Esubit ainakinet bala amoru nakarereŋ kakonye nuka nol kaloejaatar nin:
Ekerianari neibelokinor kere.
9Ŋol loerapakini aibucaret emoi amina:
Konye loieeeemono etiaki ipapero.
10Elomari noi aitoronio toma otunanan lokaijen,
Aitelekarit ibelai akwatat kaneejai loban.
11Etuŋanan lokaronon cmoi epali bon;
ŋes kijukarere ekiyakia loededeŋ mamakeŋ,
12Eiok etuŋanan airiamakin kadubu naedemaritai ikedwe,
Itia airiamakin ebaŋ kabanaanutuke,
13 # Yer 18:20; Rom 12:17; 1 Ses 5:15. Loetaeakini aronis itia aiokis,
Mam enyouni aronis kokaleke.
14 # Awa 20:3; Asw 6:1; Rom 12:18; 1 Ses 4:11. Ageunet naka eeelet esubit bala etuŋanan loepeduni akipi:
Aso do kinyek epeget, eriŋa ejauna aŋolopa.
15 # Any 23:7; Isa 5:23. Ŋol loitojoki lokaronon, ka nol da loititelekari ekadoliton, Eraas kesi kere luketal kaneejai EJAKAIT,
16Kanukinyo ejaatar loban apesan akanike agwelia acoa, Kwabo emamei ŋes aijen?
17Emina epapero apaarasia kere,
Ido onaee euritai kanuka apakio nuka icano
18Etuŋanan loemamei aijen ititini akan,
Eraun ekabwoaran kakonyen nuka eidunyeteke.
19Loemina eeelet emina aibucar:
da loitoojari ekekike emoi amudiario.
20Ŋol loemagul etau mam edumuni ajokis:
Ŋol da loemagul aŋajep ibirokin aronis.
21Loeurie ebaŋ eanyuni ican:
Papa da loka ebaŋ mam iyalama.
22Etau loelakara nes ekia loejok:
Konye emoyo loebilil itooni akoio,
23 # Any 23:8. Iiauni ekaronon ainakinet katorob,
Aibelokinio irotin luka etube.
24 # Lim 2:14. Ejai acoa akonyen nuka nol kaloeiai aijen:
Konye akonye nuka ebaŋ ejaas osaŋeta luka akwap,
25Okoku loban itakadikini papake,
Ido erai adwaris mama ejai nakeurie nes.
26Ido mam ejok aitican ekadoliton,
Karaida aile isotia kanuka aitenarakec,
27 # Yak 1:19. Loitikokini akekiro ejaas kaiien:
Erunanan Ioeiai emoyo loelilim emisiana.
28Loban da arai ililhji enyaritar lokaeoa:
Arai ebutarit eomitai loecobe.
