Awaragasia 10

10
1Awaragasia nuka Solomon.
Okoku loeeobe einakini papake alakara:
Konye okoku loebanaana itakadikini totoke.
2 # Sab 49:6; Luk 12:19; Dan 4:27. Iboro lukakais nuipu luedumunio kanuka aronis mam imedaete,
Konye adolit nes kiyuuni katwanare,
3 # Sab 10:14. Mam EJAKAIT eeamakini aijar naka ekadoliton eriebi eteŋei:
Konye ireneari aiŋobel naka ekaronon.
4 # Awa 13:4. Akan naelonwana eyauni ican,
Konye akan naekukurana itabari.
5Loetukonokini kakamu ŋes okoku loeeobe:
Konye ŋol loeiotoi kaidweny ŋes okoku loiswamai nukileic.
6 # Est 7:8. Ejaas arereŋesio kakou naka ekadoliton:
Konye erapit akaje aituk naka ekaronon.
7 # Sab 9:5,6; Lim 8:10. Isirereni akiitun ekadoliton;
Konye ebosi ekiror loka ekaronon.
8Iyuunete lukacoa kotauon aicorakineta:
Konye ibirori loinerenenei nukabanaanut.
9 # Sab 23:4; Isa 33:15,16. Ŋol loelosenenei itenara elosenenei kainapakina:
Konye ejenunio lomagul ikerotin.
10Loeedi kakonuke eyauni iean,
Konye ibirori loinerenenei nukabanaanut.
11 # Sab 37:30; 107:42. Aituk naka ekadoliton ŋes atan nakaijar:
Konye erapit akaie aituk naka ekaronon.
12 # Yak 5:20; 1 Pet 4:8; 1 Kor 13:4. Elait isidiki inuneta:
Konye crapi amina aibucanareta kere.
13 # Luk 4:22. Edumunio acoa kaituk naka ŋol kaloemisiana,
Konyc ejai ebela kanuka ŋol kaloemamei ajenun.
14Inadakinete lukacoa aijen:
Konye aituk naka loebanaana isukunyuni amudiario.
15 # Yob 31:24; Awa 18:11; Luk 12:19; 1 Tim 6:17. Abara naka ekabaran ŋes ereke loegogon:
Amudiar naka lukican nes icanikee.
16Aitaaritoi naka ekadoliton itebei ŋes aiiar,
Eiyas loka ekaronon itebei nes aronis.
17Ŋol loibwaikini etau aituutoorio ejai orot loebei aijar:
Konye epaari nol loijalijala aitoronio.
18Ŋol loimunoni elait erai loka aituk nakenalan:
Ebanaana da ŋol loesabanakinenei.
19 # Lim 5:3; Sab 39:1; Awa 17:27,28; Yak 1:19. Kaipues naka akiro mam eswaki aibucar:
Konye ŋol loetiki akenaiep iswamai kacoa.
20Eputos aŋajep naka ekadoliton ka epuesa loseuna:
Etau loka ekaronon erai lootai.
21Itanyami aituk naka ekadoliton luipu:
Konye etwakete lubaŋiok naarai eolikinete acoa.
22 # Age 12:2; Ikis 8:17,18; 1 Sam 2:7,8; 1 Tim 4:8. Arereŋ naka EJAKAIT itabari,
Mam da inyalakini ican kaŋes.
23Aswam aronis esubit bala abolia mama ejai ebaŋ:
Aswam da nukaeoa itojokit ctunanan lokaienun,
24 # Yob 15:21; Mat 5:6. Nuekurianari ekaronon kes nuedoikinete ŋes:
Ido einakinio ekadoliton nuimiela.
25 # Sab 15:5; Mat 7:24; 1 Tim 6:19. Ketubor etapipirio, emamei bobo ekaronon:
Konye icasit ekadoliton ikar kere.
26Kwape ebino loedwar ria kokel, ka apuru toma akonye, Ikoni nen loilonwana kama ejaas ŋul luiyakito nes.
27 # Lim 7:17. Ayoŋit EJAKAIT iyatakini apaarasia:
Konye ituurianitai ikar luka ekaronon.
28 # Sab 16:9; Rom 5:2; Yob 8:13. Amun naka ekadoliton itodoli aiyalama:
Konye eoliori amun naka ekaronon.
29 # Sek 10:12; Pil 4:13; Isa 40:31; Sab 1:6. Erai EJAKAIT abwas naegogon mama ejaas luitenara kotauon:
Konye erai amudiar mama ejaas ikeswamak numam adolit,
30Mam eut ikadolitok elemanario:
Konye mam ikaronok ibojenenete kakwap.
31Itolomuni aituk naka ekadoliton acoa:
Konye emudiario aŋajep naepaarit.
32Ejeŋi aituk naka ekadoliton nuecamunio:
Konye aituk naka ekaronon ejeŋi nuepaarito.
