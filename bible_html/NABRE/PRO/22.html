Awaragasia 22

22
1Tiejok aseun ekiror loejok itia abar naepol,
Ido asianut nakamin itia epuesa ka esa bu.
2 # Sab 49:1,2. Iriamanakinos lukabarak ka lukican:
EJAKAIT ŋes ekasubanikec kere.
3 # Isa 26:20. Eanyuni etuŋanan loecobe aronis, kosodi aiŋop:
Konye itorete lujimat nen, iticanio.
4Eropit lokaitasono ka ayoŋ it EJAKAIT,
ŋes abar, ka aibuses, ka aijar da.
5 # 1 Yok 5:18. Ejaas ikukwa ka iica korot loka lomagul:
Ŋol loidara bon clwaniar kamakee.
6Kitanaik ikoku erot loibusakinit alosenenoo,
Karaida kemoion mam enyouni kanen,
7Eyait lokabaran lokican,
Loeyuuna da erai ebulesit loka loeyuuni.
8 # Yob 4:8; Kos 10:13. Loeyei numam adolit esaki ilou:
Edaun da ebela loka anyunyurake.
9Isirerenitoi loeja kakonye nuesiaka;
Naarai einakini lukican inyamatake.
10Kireŋea ekamwan an, emamuun da epeget;
Eebo, enaŋi aŋolopa ka amoromor.
11Loekot etau loescgara,
Kanuka asianut naka aitukuke ŋes eraunor ekabaka epaperoke,
12Esesenete akonye nuka EJAKAIT ŋol loejai anen,
Konye itapalauni akiro nuka etuŋanan loebili ibotei.
13Ebala loelonwana ebe, “Ejai eŋatuny kina:
Kaari korot.”
14 # Awa 2:16; Lim 7:26. Aituk naka aberu kanaadote nes aipany naidul:
Kenyunyuriki EJAKAIT etuŋanan ibirokini nen.
15Eenitai abaŋaanut kotoma otau loka ikoku:
Konye ibelai lukaleeor irenearete neelwana kamaken.
16 # Yob 20:19-23; 34:26-28; Sab 12:5; Lim 5:8; Amo 2:6-8; 5:11; Isa 3:14,15; Yak 2:13. Ŋol loeroikini lokican adumunia amedake,
Ŋol da loeinakini lokabaran iboro, edolokini ŋol aibabanitoi.
17Kitoik akonikit, kiira akiro nuka lukacoa,
Kibwaik da ekonitau mama ejai akaijen.
18Naarai ejok arai irici kes kotomakon,
Arai icasito kaitukukon.
19Kojai amunonutukon neejai EJAKAIT,
ŋes ketejenunia lolo jo da.
20Mam kereka kewadikakinit jo akiro nuejokuka no;’ Nuka ainerun ka nuka aijen da?
21 # 1 Pet 3:15. Aitejenikini jo cut akiro nukabeit,
Tetere iboŋokini akiro nukabeit ŋul lukijukarito.
22 # Mal 3:5. Sirinasi lokican, naarai icana,
Karaida aroikin luiticanio korute.
23Naarai epurokini EJAKAIT kes,
Ido enatari aijarakec kaŋul kaluenasete.
24Sirikisipaper etuŋanan loenyunyurana;
Siriupasi ketunanan loisiopana:
25Kinyek aisisiaun ikerotin,
Ipoki aijarakon koie.
26Sirijaananas kanul kaluititinete akanin,
Kanul kaluebwoanarete lukapesenin:
27Arai imameete jo lutacakinet,
Kanukinyo ma eyanaaria ŋesi aperitikon kamakon?
28Sirikijul aanyunet naka eikor losek,
Nakesipokinete lukapapaakon.
29 # 1 Ika 11:28; Awa 10:4; Lim 9:10; Mat 25:21; Rom 12:11. Iany etuŋanan ekukurikinit aitaaritoike?
Ebwoi ŋesi kakonyen nuka ikabakan;
Mam ebwoi kakonyen nuka ipasoi.
