Daniel 12

12
1 # Apu 16:18; Isa 11:11; Yer 30:7; Kos 3:5; Yok 3:16; Rom 11:26; Any 32:32; Isa 4:3; Esk 13:9; Luk 10:20; Mat 24:21; Mk 13:19; Apu 7:14; 12:7. “Kapak kaŋin ebwoi Mikael, ejakait lokapolon loebwoikini idwe luka ituŋakon: ido ejaun apak naka aburun, namam apeŋo ejaunit ageun kateker ajaut aitodol apak nin: ido kapak kaŋin eyuunio ikonituŋa, ninidiopet yenetakanuni iwadikatai oitabo. 2#Isa 26:19; Mat 25:46; Yok 5:29. Ekwenyunete da luipu kanul kaluejotoete kotoma apua naka akwap, ice neejai aijar namam edaun, ice da mama ejaas ileic ka amwanio namam edaun. 3#1 Kor 15:41. Eeaunete da ŋul lukacoa kwape amwekimwek naka akuj; nul da luibelokinete luipu mama ejai adolit kwape aacer ikar ka ikar. 4#Apu 22:10. Konye jo, wu Daniel, kimunon akiro, kipue eitabo, aitodol apak nakawasia: ekerete luipu lailo ka laije, iyatakin da aijen.”
5Kasodi eoŋ, Daniel, asesen, ido koany, ebwoete ice iarei, ediopet kowai lo kariri naka ecor, ece juwai kariri naka ecor. 6#Sab 74:9; 1 Pet 1:12; Apu 6:10. Kosodi ediopet kinit etuŋanan loenapit ebaputa, loajai kuiu akipi nukocor ebe, “Itodoli bo ai kiton osaŋe loka nukaumokin nu?” 7#Dan 7:25; Luk 21:24; Apu 10:5; 12:14. Korio erunanan loenapit ebaputa, loajai kuju akipi nukoeor, akekan nakoteten ka akekan nakokediany da kiton akuj, kasodi apupun ŋes iyapoi kolimite ŋol loejarenenei ikar kere ebe, itodoli apak, ka apakio, ka atutubet; ido kedaut kesi abilibil apedor naka ituŋa lukalaunak, kosodete konyet nun kere adaun, 8Kapup, konye mam abu kajen: kasodi atemar ebe, “Wu ekajakait, nuelomunete kanun kere anyoika?” 9Kotema atiar, “Kolot, Daniel: naarai emunon akiro, epueakin da aitodol apak nakawasia. 10#Sek 13:9; Yok 7:17; Apu 22:11. Itesegaaros luipu, italaunos, ililiaunos; konye iswamaete lukaronok nukaronok; emameun yenikaronon yenejenuni: konye ejenunete nul lukacoa. 11#Dan 9:27; 11:31; Mat 24:15; Mk 13:14. Ido ageun kapak naelemarere ainakinet naicweio natir, esipokino da etal loisimejeikini, ejaunete apaarasia alukumit ka akwat aarei ka akaisakanykaoŋon. 12Isirerenitoi ŋin yenidaret, kodol apaarasia alukumit ka akwat auni ka akaisauni kanukany. 13#Isa 57:2; Sab 1:5. Konye kolot ma ijo aitodol naejaunia awasia: naarai ibworari, ibwoi da kotoma nakibeit jo osaŋe loka apaarasia.”
