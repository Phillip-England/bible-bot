Apukokinio 6

6
1 # Apu 4:6; 5:1,2,6,8. Kasodi kaany naapukoria Imerekek adiopet naka aipuceta akanykaarei, keira da ediopet ka lukajarak luoŋon inera bala eporoto loka agiro ebe, “Bia!” 2#Sek 1:8; 6:3,6. Kaany, ido koany, ebalasit lokakwaŋan, kejai ekebwaneniton da akabwa; koinakinai aboko, koloma da aipikor, ido ipikori.
3Naapukoria ŋesi aipucet nakiareit, keira lokajaran lokiareit ebala ebe, “Bia!” 4#Sek 1:8; 6:2. Kolom do ebalasit ece, loereŋ; koinakinai ekebwanenitonike alemar ainapakin kotoma akwap, tetere ituŋa earas; koinakinai da ŋes ekileŋ loepol.
5 # Sek 6:2,6. Naapukoria ŋesi aipucet nakiwuniet, keira lokajaran lokiwuniet ebala ebe, “Bia!” Kaany, ido koany, ebalasit lokirionon, kejai ekebwanenitonike aitemis akanike. 6Keira bala eporoto kokidiŋ lukajarak luoŋon ebala ebe, “Aitemis naka akiria naka adenari, ka aitemisio auni nuka esairi nuka adenari, kwabo mam imunaari akinyet karaida ebino!”
7Naapukoria ŋesi aipucet nakiwoŋonet, keira eporoto loka lokajaran lokiwoŋonet ebala ebe, “Bia!” 8#Esk 5:12; 14:21; 29:5; 33:27; 34:28; Kos 13:14; Yer 15:3. Kaany, ido koany, ebalasit loekwele, ekebwanenitonike da ekekiror Atwanare, kotupa ŋes Okatwak; koinakinai kes apolou kama katutubet nakiwoŋonet naka akwap, koarete kekileŋ, ka ebeli, ka atular, ka itiaŋ luededeŋ luka akwap.
9 # Apu 8:5; 14:18; 16:7. Naapukoria ŋesi aipucet nakikanyet, kaany kokwap ketem, imoyon luka ŋul kalukeario kanuka akirot naka Edeke, kanuka ajenanut da nakejaatar. 10#Sek 1:12; Sab 79:10; Ikis 32:43; Age 4:10; 2 Ika 9:7; Kos 4:1. Kiworoto keporoto loepol ebe, “Ijo Ejakait, Lokalaunan, lokabeit, itodoli ai mam kitubokini, karaida ainyaŋ aokotokosi kama ejaas ŋul luiboiete kakwap?” 11#Apu 3:4; 7:9; Mat 23:32. Koinakinai kes ŋinidiopet anapito nakakwaŋan, kolimokinai da kes aiyeŋun bobo apak naedit, kiton naitodolikinor etiai loka ibulesin lukaupakec, ka ikeceinacan, luepote aario kwape kakesi.
12 # Isa 13:10; Yoe 2:30; Luk 21:25; Esk 32:7,8. Naapukoria ŋesi aipucet nakikanyetekape, kaany, kejai amaran naepol naka akwap; kirioniar akoloŋ bala epuko loka ajul nukirionok, koluor elap da kere bala aokot, 13#Isa 34:4; 13:10. kibirununos aacer nuka akuj toma akwap, kwape eraraununia etini araitoke nuelidio naejanijaŋia ekwam loepol. 14#Apu 16:20. Kilicarai akuj bala eitabo loenunukitoi, kolemarai ŋoliroŋat ka ŋinipujan kaiboisike. 15#Isa 2:10,19,21; 24:21; 34:12; Yer 4:29; Sab 48:4; 2:2. Kosodete ikabakan luka akwap, ka ikapolok, ka ikapolokitok iserikalei, ka ikabarak, ka ikagogoŋok, ka ŋinibulesit ka yenejakakai, koŋopata kotoma abuai, ka atiiban nuka iroŋan, 16#Kos 10:8; Luk 23:30; Isa 6:1; Sab 47:8. kobalas iroŋan ka atiiban ebe, “Kibirunos mamakosi, kitoŋopai sio kakonyen nuka ŋol kaloiboie koicoloŋ, ka anyunyura da naka Imerekek; 17#Yoe 2:11,31; Mal 3:2; Sep 1:14,18; Rom 2:5. naarai adol apaaran naepol naka anyunyurakec, iŋai bo do epedori aibwo?”
