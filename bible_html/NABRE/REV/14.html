Apukokinio 14

14
1 # Esk 9:4; Apu 7:3; 3:12. Kasodi kaany, ido koany, kobwoi Imerekek komoru Sion, ka ituŋa ilukumin akwatat ka akaisaoŋon ka luoŋon nepepe kaŋes, luiwadikatai ekirorike, ka ekiror loka Papake oreetakec. 2#Esk 1:24; 43:2; Apu 1:15. Keira da eporoto loebuni kotoma akuj bala eporoto loka akipi nukepuak, bala da eporoto loka agiro naepol; eporoto da loabu keira kosubite bala eporoto loka ikaramak lueramete adeudeunokec, 3#Sab 33:3; 40:3; 96:1; 98:1; 144:9; 149:1; Isa 42:10; Apu 5:9. Koyeete ekosio lokitetet koiŋaren keicoloŋ, kakonyen da nuka ikajarak luoŋon, ka imakio. Kwabo emamei ituŋanan yenepedori aisisiaun ekosio ŋol, dimarai ŋul ilukumin akwatat ka akaisaoŋon ka luoŋon, lualakunio kotoma akwap. 4#2 Kor 11:2; Pes 5:27. Ŋul kes lumam emunaara kama ejaas aŋor naarai eraas ibekon; ŋul kes luetupito Imerekek kama elosi kere; ŋul kes lualakunio kotoma otuŋa araut araito nugeet nuka Edeke, ka nuka Imerekek, 5#Sep 3:13; Sab 32:2; Isa 53:9. ido mam edumuna aimod kaitukekec, naarai emamei kes aitoronet,
6 # Apu 8:13. Kasodi kaany emalaika ece eporitoi kokidiŋ kakuj, ejaas Akiro Nukajokak nuka ikar kere alimonokin luiboiete kotoma akwap, luka ŋinijena, ka ateker, ka aŋajep, ka ituŋa da; 7#Any 20:11; Sab 146:6. kobala keporoto loepol ebe, “Koyoŋito Edeke, kiturito ŋes, naarai adol esawa loka aitubuke, komaata ŋol lokesubi akuj; ka akwap ka aŋolol, ka atanin da nuka akipi.”
8 # Isa 21:9; Yer 51:7,8; Apu 18:2; Dan 4:30. Kotupak emalaika ece, lokiareit, kobala ebe, “Ebiror, ebiror Babulon lokapolon, loetamasi atekerin kere ebino loka adedeŋ naka adoteuke.”
9 # Apu 13:12-17; Mat 10:28. Kotupak da emalaika ece, lokiwuniet, kobala keporoto loepol ebe, “Arai kema ituŋanan etiaŋ ka aputoke, kedumu da emacar koreeteke arai bo kakanike, 10#Isa 51:17; Age 19:24; Esk 38:22; Sab 75:8; Yer 25:15; Apu 16:19. emasi ŋin da ebino loka adedeŋ naka Edeke, loebukokinitai lomam inyalitai toma aikopo naka anyunyurake, isiriebio ŋes da kotoma akim ka acukuman kakonyen nuka imalaikan lukalaunak, kakonyen da nuka Imerekek, 11#Isa 34:9,10; Apu 19:3. Epuriori da apuru naka aisiriebiokec kuju, ikar ka ikar, kwabo mam iyeŋunete paaran ka kuare da, luemaete etiaŋ ka aputoke, ka ŋin da yenedumuni emacar loka ekirorike.”
12 # Isa 34:10; Apu 13:10; 12:17. Ne ŋes ejai atitiŋikin naka lukalaunak, ŋul luericito aicorakineta nuka Edeke ka aiyuun da naka Yesu.
13 # Isa 57:2; Bur 4:10; 1 Kor 15:58. Abu keira eporoto loebuni kotoma akuj kobala ebe, “Kiwadik ebe: Isirereŋitoi lukatwak, luetwakete kotoma Ejakait amear lolo.” Eebo, ebala Emoyo ebe, “Tetere ibworarete kotoma aitaaritoikec; naarai euparos akeceswamisio kakes!”
14 # Dan 7:13; 10:16; Mat 13:39,41. Kasodi kaany, ido koany, edou lokakwaŋan, kiboie da kodou loeputosi bala okoku loka etuŋanan, kejai aboko naka esabu akouke, ka ecilikiki da loekwana akanike. 15#Yok 3:13. Kolomu emalaika ece koyekalu, kiworoi keporoto loepol mama ejai ŋol loiboie kodou, ebe, “Kijuka ecilikiki, kosak, naarai adol apak naka aisak, naarai akoko noi isakan luka akwap.” 16Kosodi loiboie kodou acakar ecilikikike toma akwap, kosakai do akwap.
17Kolomu do emalaika ece koyekalu lokotoma akuj, kojai ŋes da ecilikiki loekwana. 18#Yok 3:13. Kosodi kolom emalaika ece kotem, kojai apolou kama ejai akim, kiworoi keporoto loepol mama ejai ŋol loejai ecilikiki loekwana, ebe, “Kijuka ecilikikikon loekwana, kode aibuŋeta nuka emusabibu loka akwap, naarai akoko noi imusabibunuke.” 19#Apu 19:15. Kocaka do emalaika ecilikikike toma akwap, kode emusabibu loka akwap, kocakak acuet naepol naka adedeŋ naka Edeke. 20#Isa 63:3; Aku 1:15; Apu 19:15; Yok 3:13. Kicakacakai acuet kokiŋa naka ere, koleleata aokot alomun kacuet, kiton arukanasia nuka ibalasin, kwape alosit naka amairon akwat aarei.
