1 # Apu 1:13,16,20. “Kiwadikak emalaika loka ekanisa lokotoma Epeso ebe: Inera kwanata ŋol loitiŋit aacer akanykaarei kakanike nakoteten, loelosenenei kokidiŋ opirin lukatalai ikanykaarei luka esabu ebe:
2 # 1 Yok 4:1. “Ajeni akoniswamisio, ka aitaaritoi, ka aŋirikinitekon, ido kwape mam ipedoria atitiŋikin ituŋa lukaronok, ido ibu kitem ŋul luenyaras bonik ikiyakia, kwabo mam eraas, kodum ebe eraas ikeŋalak, 3Iŋirikinit ka atitiŋikin kanuka ekakiror, kwabo mam ibu kopas. 4Konye ejai eoŋ akirot na kajo ebe ibu kojal akonimin nasodit. 5Aso kiitu nekibirunor, kitoronikin, kiswama da aswamisio nusodit. Konye arai mam iswamai kwaŋin, abuni mamakon, alemari da epirikon lokatala kaiboisike, dimikito kitoronikin. 6#Sab 139:21. Konye ejai jo na ebe ilait aswamisio nuka Inikolain, nualait eoŋ da. 7#Age 2:9; 3:22,24; Luk 23:43; 2 Kor 12:4; Apu 22:2; Esk 28:13; 31:8. Ŋin yenejai akit, kopup nuelimokini Emoyo ikanisan. Ŋin yenipikori ainakini ŋes ainyam araito nuka ekitoi loka aijar, loejai Paradais#2:7 Paradais apolouke Emanikor loka Edeke. loka Edeke.
8 # Isa 44:6; 48:12; Apu 1:17; 22:13. “Ido kiwadikak emalaika loka ekanisa lokotoma Sumuruna ebe: Inera kwanata ŋol losodit ka lokawasia da, loabu kotwanite ido ejari, ebe:
9 # Apu 3:9; Yak 2:5; 2 Kor 11:14,15. “Ajeni aiticaniokon, ka ikonibakor, (konye irai ekabaran) ka amoromor naka ŋul kaluenyaras bonik Iyudayan, kwabo mam eraas, konye eraas atukos naka Satan. 10#Dan 1:12,14; Mat 10:28; Apu 3:11; 2 Tim 4:8. Sirikiyoŋ nukidoikinete aisirieb jo, Koany, elosi Satan acakakin ice kayes aidaris, tetere kitemio, kiticanio da yes apaarasia atomon. Koraenena lomunot kiton atwanare, eoŋ da kainakini jo aboko naka aijar, 11#Apu 20:14; 21:8. Ŋin yenejai akit, kopup nuelimokini Emoyo ikanisan. Ŋin yenipikori mam epipilikini cut atwanare nakiareit.
12 # Isa 49:2; Bur 4:12; Apu 1:16; 19:15. “Ido kiwadikak emalaika loka ekanisa lokotoma Perugamo ebe: Inera kwanata ŋol loejai ekileŋ loekwana kowaitin iarei kere ebe:
13 # Apu 13:2; 3:8. “Ajeni mama iboio, neejai eicoloŋ loka Satan; ido ikamunit noi ekakiror, kwabo mam ibu koŋer aiyuunika, karaida kapaarasia nuka Antipa ekajenanika, lomunotoka, loaarakinio mamakus, mama iboio Satan. 14#Mar 22:5,7; 31:16; Ikis 23:4; Mar 25:1-3; Yud 1:11. Konye ejai eoŋ akiro nuikidioko kajo, naarai ejaas jo ituŋa luikamunito aisisianakin naka Balaam, loesisianakini Balak acakakin aitiguikinet akonyen nuka idwe luka Isirael, ainyam nueinakinio aputosia, ka adotear da. 15Ejaas jo da kwaŋin ituŋa luikamunito aisisianakin naka Inikolain, 16Aso kitoronikin. Konye arai mam iswamai kwaŋin abunit atipet mamakon, ajiikini kakes kekileŋ loka aitukuka. 17#Any 16:14-15; 16:33-34; Yok 6:48-50; Isa 62:2; 65:15; Sab 78:24; Apu 3:12. Ŋin yenejai akit, kopup nuelimokini Emoyo ikanisan. Ŋin yenipikori ainakini ŋes emanu loebwaitai, ainakini da ŋes amoru naekwaŋ, ido iwadikatai amoru ekiror lokitetet, lomam ituŋanan ejeni nabarai yenijauni.
18 # Dan 10:6. “Ido kiwadikak emalaika loka ekanisa lokotoma Suatira ebe: Inera kwanata Okoku loka Edeke, ŋol loejaas akonyeke bala aipelonoreta nuka akim, akejeke da eputosi kwape aikomon nuepwatat ebe:
19“Ajeni akoniswamisio, ka amin ka aiyuun ka aijaanakin, ka aŋirikinitekon da, ka aswamisiokon nukawasia kwape itelekaritotor kwa nusodit. 20#1 Ika 16:31; 2 Ika 9:22,30; Mar 25:1,2. Konye ejai eoŋ akirot na kajo, naarai inyeinit aberu ŋin, Yesebel, naenyara bon anabi, isisianakini iŋalaari da ikabulesin tetere edotearos, ka ainyam nueinakinio aputosia. 21Abu kainak ŋes apak tetere itoronikin, kwabo mam ekoto aitoronikin kotoma adoteuke. 22Koany, acakakini ŋes ojam, ka ŋul da luedoteete nepepe kaŋes acakakini toma aiticanio naepol, dimikito kitoronikinos kotoma aswamisioke. 23#Sab 7:9; 62:12; Yer 11:20; 17:10. Ido aari ikedwe katwanare. Ejenunete da ikanisan kere ebe eoŋ ŋopeŋol loererei aomisio ka itauon, eoŋ da ainakini ŋinidiopet kayesi kwape ejaatar aswamisiokus. 24Konye kabala yes ice luijaas Suatira luemamei aisisianakin ŋin kere, lumam ijenete nuidulok nuka Satan, kwape ineratar, mam kebwaikini yes abwokon ace; 25#Apu 3:11. nabarai kikamut noi yenijaatar, kiton naabunio. 26#Sab 2:8-9. Ŋin yenipikori, ka aricit aswamisioka kiton awasia, ainakini ŋes apolou kama ejaas atekerin, 27#Apu 12:5. Ŋes da icokiei kes kebela loka asuat, kwape ebwaŋabwaŋatar amoti nuka ekajean, kwape kainakinia da Papaka eoŋ; 28eoŋ da ainakini ŋes etop. 29Ŋin yenejai akit, kopup nuelimokini Emoyo ikanisan.
