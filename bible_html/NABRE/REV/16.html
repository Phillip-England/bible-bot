Apukokinio 16

16
1 # Isa 66:6; Sab 69:24; Yer 10:25; Sep 3:8. Kasodi keira eporoto loepol loebuni kotoma oyekalu elimokini imalaikan ikanykaarei ebe, “Koloto, kobukokis atanyia akanykaarei nuka adedeŋ naka Edeke toma akwap.”
2 # Any 9:10,11; Ikis 28:35. Kosodi losodit alosit, kobukok atanyike toma akwap, koraun edola loerono loetiono kotuŋa luejai emacar loka etiaŋ, luemaete da aputoke, 3#Any 7:17-21. Kobukok emalaika lokiareit atanyike toma aŋolol, koraun aokot bala nuka yenikatwan, kotwaka da iboro kere luejarete, lukejaas toma aŋolol. 4#Any 7:17-21; Sab 78:44. Kobukok emalaika lokiwuniet atanyike toma ocorin ka atanin nuka akipi, koraunos aokot. 5#Sab 119:137; 145:17; Any 3:14; Ikis 32:4; Isa 41:4. Keira emalaika loka akipi ebala ebe, “Idolit ijo, loijai, ido lokijai, Lokalaunan,
Naarai itubok kwaŋin,
6 # Isa 49:26; Sab 79:3. Naarai apotu kobukosi aokot nuka lukalaunak ka nuka inabin,
Ijo iinak kes aokot aimat,
Ibusakinito kes!”
7 # Sab 19:9; 119:137; Apu 9:13; 19:2. Keira da etem ebala ebe:
“Eebo, Ejakait Edeke Lokapedor kere,
Eraas itubesiakon lukabeit ka luedolito da.”
8Kobukok emalaika lokiwoŋonet atanyike toma akoloŋ, aponi koinakinai ŋes anomor ituŋa kakim. 9#Apu 16:11,21; 9:20,21. Konomoroi ituŋa kamwanis naepol, koinyanakis ekiror loka Edeke, loejai apolou kama ejaas aiticaneta ŋun, kwabo mam apotu kitoronikinos, karaida aitopoloor ŋes.
10 # Any 10:21; Isa 8:21,22. Kobukok emalaika lokikanyet atanyike oicoloŋ loka etiaŋ, kitumutonoroi ajakanutuke; kokonyokonyata aŋajepakec kanuka ariebokec. 11#Dan 2:19. Koinyanakis Edeke loka akuj kanuka ariebokec, ka idolakec, kwabo mam apotu kitoronikinos kotoma aswamisiokec.
12 # Isa 11:15; 41:2; 44:27; Age 15:18; Ikis 1:7; Yos 1:4; Yer 50:38. Kobukok emalaika lokikanyetekape atanyike toma ocor lokapolon Eupurate, koonikis akipike, tetere itemokino erot loka ikabakan luepote kokide. 13#Apu 12:9; 13:1,11; Any 8:3; 1 Ika 22:21-23. Abu kaany da imoyon iuni lueroko lueputosi kaidodokai, elomunete kaituk naka apiu, kaituk da naka etiaŋ, kaituk da naka enabi lokeŋalan. 14#Apu 13:13; 19:19. Naarai eraas imoyon luka ajokin luiswamaete aanyuneta, luelosete mama ejaas ikabakan luka akwapin kere, atukonokin kes ojie loka apaaran nakapolon naka Edeke Lokapedor kere, 15#Mat 24:43-44; Luk 12:39-40; Apu 3:3,18; 1 Ses 5:2. (“Koany, abuni kwape ekokolan! Isirereŋitoi yenekwenyi, koricite ikenapen, kinyek alosit imujalal, kinyeis ituŋa da aanyun ileicike!”) 16#2 Ika 9:27; 23:29; Sek 12:11; Tub 5:19,31. Kotukonokis kes aibois naenyaritai kAburanian Arumagedon.
17 # Isa 66:6. Kobukok emalaika lokikanyetekaarei atanyike apak, kolom eporoto loepol koyekalu, ebuni koicoloŋ, ebala ebe, “Eswamaun!” 18#Apu 4:5; 8:5; 11:13,19; Any 19:16; Dan 12:1. Kojau aimiliaunun, ka iporotoi, ka agiro, kojau da amaran naepol naka akwap, namam ejai apeŋo netikolo ajaatar ituŋa akwap, kopol amaran ŋin naetia nen, 19#Isa 51:17; Dan 4:30; Yer 25:15; Apu 14:10. Kotiaka ere lokapolon atutubeta auni, kibiroros da ireria luka atekerin, Kiitu Edeke Babulon lokapolon, ainakin ŋes aimat aikopo naka ebino loka adedeŋ naka anyunyurake. 20#Apu 6:14; 20:11. Koker ŋinipujan, kwabo mam edumuna iroŋan; 21#Any 9:23; Apu 11:19. kodounun akako naepol, naelaŋir kwape atalant, abunore kotoma akuj mama ejaas ituŋa, koinyanakis ituŋa Edeke kanuka aiticanet ŋin naka akako, naarai kepol noi jokan akeitieanet.
