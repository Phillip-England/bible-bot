Apukokinio 21

21
1 # Isa 65:17; 66:22; 2 Pet 3:13; Apu 20:11. Kasodi kaany akuj nakitetet, ka akwap nakitetet; naarai atubor akuj nasodit, ka akwap da nasodit, mam bobo aŋolol ejai. 2#Isa 52:1; 61:10; Apu 3:12; 19:17; Bur 11:10. Kaany da ere lokalaunan, Yerusalem lokitetet, ijesun kotoma akuj, kama ka Edeke, itemokino kwape ateran naisirokinitai okilenike, 3#Esk 37:27; Leb 26:11-12; Sek 2:10; Isa 8:8. Keira da eporoto loepol loebuni kotoma oicoloŋ kobala ebe, “Koany, ejai ewema loka Edeke nepepe kituŋa, iboienenei kakes, eraunos kesi iketuŋa, Edeke da elopet ejaenena kakes. 4#Isa 25:8; 33:10; 35:10; 65:19; Apu 7:17; Yer 31:16. Emwatari akiyo kere kakonyeneke, mam bobo ejaun atwanare, karaida akuudian, karaida aimony, karaida ariebo, mam bobo ejaunos; naarai atuboros nusodit.”
5 # Apu 4:2; 5:1; 2 Kor 5:17; Sab 47:8. Ŋol da loiboie koicoloŋ kobala ebe, “Koany, eteteuni eoŋ kere.”
Kobala da ebe, “Kiwadik, naarai eraas akiro nu numunon ido nukabeit.” 6#Isa 55:1; Apu 1:8; 22:17; Sek 14:8. Katema ŋesi eoŋ atiar, “Eswamaunos! Arai eoŋ Alupa ka Omega, ageunet ka esaŋe da, Ainakini yenejakit akure aimat atai atan naka akipi nuka aijar. 7#2 Sam 7:14; Sab 89:27-28; Apu 2:7. Yenipikori irumi ŋun, araun eoŋ Ekedeke, ŋes da eraun okokuka. 8#Apu 22:15; 20:14; Bur 10:38,39. Konye ikuriesik, ka lumam iyuunito, ka luesiliaka, ka ikaarak, ka idotea, ka ikacudak, ka luemaete aputosia, ka ikeŋalak kere, ejaun akorakineteke toma aŋolol naenoki kakim ka acukuman; ŋes atwanare nakiareit.”
9 # Leb 26:21; Apu 15:1. Kosodi kobu ediopet komalaikan ikanykaarei lukejaas atanyia akanykaarei nueleleba aiticaneta akanykaarei nukawasia, kinera keoŋ, kobala ebe, “Bia, ketodikini jo ateran, aberu naka Imerekek.” 10#Esk 40:2; Isa 52:1. Kadaka kotoma Omoyo oroŋat loepol, loeoja, ketodik eoŋ ere lokalaunan Yerusalem, ijesun kotoma akuj kama ka Edeke, 11#Isa 58:8; 60:1,2,19. ejaas kaibuses naka Edeke, koputosi aicake bala amoru naka akais nuipu noi, kwape amoru naka ayaseper naipiororoi noi, 12#Esk 48:30-35. Kejai arwatat naepol, naeoja, kejaas irutei itomon ka luarei, kejaas imalaikan itomon ka luarei orutei, kiwadikatai da ikiroria luka atekerin atomon ka nuarei nuka idwe luka Isirael orutei. 13Kide irutei iuni, agolitomei irutei iuni, eŋalakimak irutei iuni, to da irutei iuni, 14Kojaas apeteta atomon ka nuarei arwatat naka ere, kotomakec da ikiroria itomon ka luarei luka ikiyakia itomon ka luarei luka Imerekek.
15 # Esk 40:3,5. Kojai ŋol lokinera keoŋ egada lokitemis loka esabu, tetere itemio ere, ka iruteike, ka arwatatake, 16#Esk 43:16; 48:16,17. Etirianitai da ere iwaitin kere, aojauke koriakasi kalalau. Kitem ŋesi ere kegada amairon alukumit ka akwat akany; koriakanara aojauke ka alalauke ka amukururuke da. 17Kitem da arwatatake ikubitin#21:17 ekubit itemi alalau bala l' 6" akwatat ka akaisaoŋon ka luoŋon, kwape ejaar aitemis naka etuŋanan, ŋes naka emalaika. 18#Isa 54:11-12. Adukio naka arwatatake korai naka ayaseper, ere da korai loka esabu loelai, kwape aroet naelai. 19#Isa 54:11,12. Kisiritete apeteta nuka arwatat naka ere kaŋinimoru kere naka akais nuipu; apetetait nasodit ayaseper, nakiareit asapiro, nakiwuniet aget, nakiwoŋonet emeralid, 20nakikanyet naemelomelot; nakikanyetekape asadia; nakikanyetekaarei akurusoliso; nakikanyetekauni aberul; nakikanyetekaoŋon atopasit; nakitomonet akurusopuraso; nakitomonet ka nadiopet ayasit; nakitomonet kanuarei da ametusit, 21Keraas irutei itomon ka luarei ilulun itomon ka luarei, ŋolidiopet korutei kerai elulu ediopet; erot da loka ere kerai esabu loelai bala aroet naipiororoi.
22Mam abu kaany eyekalu kore, naarai eyekaluke ŋes Ejakait Edeke Lokapedor kere ka Imerekek, 23#Isa 24:23; 60:19-20. Mam ere ipuda akoloŋ karaida elap acaikin ŋes; naarai inwakakinit aibuses naka Edeke, atalake da ŋes Imerekek. 24#Isa 60:3,5; Sab 89:27. Elosenenete da atekerin kaicake, eyaunete ikabakan luka akwap aibusesukec tomake; 25#Isa 60:11; Apu 22:5; Sek 14:7. mam cut egolokinio iruteike paaran, naarai mam ejaun akuare ŋina; 26einunete ŋes aibuses ka ayoŋio nuka atekerin. 27#Isa 52:1; Esk 44:9; Dan 12:1. Ido mam elomari cut ibore kere tomake yenemunaara, karaida yeniswamai nukasilianut, ka aiŋalanut, dimarai ŋul bonik luiwadikatai toma oitabo loka aijar loka Imerekek.
