Apukokinio 18

18
1 # Apu 10:1; Esk 43:2. Akaulo kaŋun abu kaany emalaika ece ijesuni kotoma akuj, ejai kapolou naepol; kitacau aibusesuke akwap. 2#Isa 13:21; 21:9; 34:11; Yer 9:11; 50:39; 51:8; Apu 14:8; Dan 4:30. Kiworo keporoto loegogoŋ ebe,
“Ebiror, ebiror Babulon lokapolon!
Arau aibois naiboioto ajokin,
Ka aidaris naka imoyon lueroko,
Aidaris da naka ŋinikweny yenerono yenelaitai;
3 # Isa 23:17; Yer 25:15,27; 51:7; Nak 3:4. Naarai ebiroros atekerin kere kanuka ebino loka adedeŋ naka adoteuke,
Adoteata da ikabakan luka akwap kaŋes,
Abarata da ikagwelanarak luka akwap
Kanuka agogoŋ naka eporoke.”
4 # Isa 48:20; 52:11; Yer 50:8; 51:6,9,45. Kasodi keira eporoto ece ebuni kotoma akuj ebala ebe,
“Ikatuŋa, kolomutu kotomakeŋ,
Kinyeis aimorikikin kadiakanaretake,
Kinyeis ainakinio aiticanetake;
5 # Age 18:20-21; Yer 51:9. Naarai iculul adiakanaretake kiton akuj,
Eitu da Edeke amamus adoliteke.
6 # Sab 137:8; Yer 50:15,29; 2 Ses 1:6. Kotaca ŋes kwape atacia ŋesi,
Kiyatakis ŋes irwan aarei kwape ejaatar aswamisioke:
Toma aikopo naenyalia ŋesi, kinyalakis ŋes irwan aarei.
7 # Isa 47:7-9; Yer 50:29. Kwape etopolooror bon, koporo da,
Kwape kwaŋin koinakis ŋes aisiriebio noi ka akuudian,
Naarai ebala kotauke ebe, ‘Eboie eoŋ kwape akabaka,
Mam arai apuserut, mam cut aanyuni akuudian.’
8 # Isa 47:9; Yer 50:34; Apu 17:16. Kanuka ŋun ŋes eponiata aiticanetake toma apaaran adiopet,
Atwanare, ka akuudian, ka ebeli,
Icweikinio cut kakim;
Naarai egogoŋ Ejakait Edeke loetubokini ŋes.”
9 # Esk 26:16-17. Emonyakinete ŋes, ibwololoete da ikabakan luka akwap luadoteata kaŋes ka eporo da, naeanyuniata apuru naka aicweioke; #Esk 27:30-35; Isa 23:17; Apu 17:2. 10#Dan 4:30; Esk 26:17; Isa 21:9; Yer 51:8; Apu 14:8. kobwoete kama elwana, kanuka akurian naka aisiriebioke, kobalasi ebe,
“Itukwi! Itukwi! Ere lokapolon,
Babulon, ere lokapolon loegogoŋ!
Naarai adolu atubokiniokon toma osawa ediopet.”
11 # Esk 27:31,36. Emonyete ikagwelanarak luka akwap, ekuudianete da kanukeŋ, naarai emamei bobo yenegweli igwelarakec, 12#Esk 27:12-13,22. igwelara luka esabu, ka epuesa, ka amoru nuka akais nuipu, ka ilulun, ka ebaputa loejok, ka lomuge, ka aliri, ka lokareŋan, ka ŋolikitoi loeuru maa, ka ŋinibore kere yenika ikel luka itomei, ka ŋinibore kere yenika ekitoi loka akais nuipu noi, ka aikomon, ka asua, ka amor nuka ijesesinei, 13#Esk 27:13. akinomon, ka lueuru maa, ka emina, ka luesaete, ka lukisupurununo, ka ebino, ka akinyet, ka akiria nuicidie, ka eŋano, ka ibaren, ka amerekekin, ka ibalasin, ka agalin, ka ibulesin, ka aijar da naka ituŋa.
14“Araito da nuimiela ekonitau anyout mamakon,
Iboro kere da luetarioko ka luimilias anyout kamakon, Komam bobo ituŋa edumunete kes!”
15 # Esk 27:31,36. Ikagwelanarak iboro ŋul, luetabari ŋesi, ebwoete kama elwana, kanuka akurian naka aisiriebioke, komonyete ka akuudian, kobalasi ebe,
16 # Apu 17:4. “Itukwi! Itukwi! Ere lokapolon,
Loaponi kitanapai ebaputa loejok,
ka lomuge ka lokareŋan, Kisiritete kesabu,
ka amor nuka akais nuipu, ka ilulun da!
17 # Isa 23:14; Esk 27:26-30. Naarai amudiar abar naetia nen kosawa ediopet.”
Kobwoi da ŋolikapolon loka ataker, ka yenelosi aibois kere kotoma ataker, ka ikekwaŋak, ka ŋul kere da luiswamaete toma aŋolol, kobwoete kama elwana, 18#Esk 27:32; Isa 34:10. ido naaanyuniata apuru naka aicweioke, kiworoto ebe,
“Inyo bo eputosi ka ere lokapolon?”
19 # Esk 26:19; 27:30-34. Kiwelanakis apua akwesikec, kiworoto emonyete ka akuudian ebe,
“Itukwi! Itukwi! Ere lokapolon,
Loetabari kere lukejaas atakerin toma aŋolol kanuka akaisike nuipu! Naarai amudiar kotoma osawa ediopet.
20 # Ikis 32:43; Yer 51:48; Isa 44:23. Kiyalamikin kanukeŋ, ijo akuj,
Ka yes lukalaunak, ka yes ikiyakia, ka yes da inabin,
Naarai kitubok Edeke akirokus kamakeŋ!”
21 # Yer 51:63-64; Esk 26:21; Dan 4:30. Kosodi kodak emalaika loegogoŋ amoru, kwape akukuse naepol, kocakak aŋolol cikil, kobala ebe,
“Kwaŋin ecakakinere Babulon ere lokapolon,
Kwabo mam bobo etakanuni,
22 # Esk 26:13; Isa 24:8. #Yer 7:34; 25:10. Karaida eporoto loka ikaramak adeudeun,
ka luejaas lueruosi,
ka luekusete alamarun,
ka luekusete arupepen,
Mam epupunos bobo kotomakon.
Karaida epirioit loka aswam kere,
mam etakanuni bobo kotomakon;
Karaida eporoto loka akukuse mam epupuni bobo kotomakon;
23 # Yer 7:34; 16:9; 25:10; Isa 23:8; 47:9. Karaida aica naka atala, mam ecai bobo kotomakon;
Karaida eporoto loka eteran ka ateran mam epupuni bobo kotomakon;
Naarai keraas ikagwelanarakakon ikapolok luka akwap,
Naarai toma ocudetekon ŋes eŋalaarere atekerin kere,
24 # Yer 51:9,49; Mat 23:35; Apu 6:10; 17:6; 19:2. Kotakanut da kotomake aokot nuka inabin ka nuka lukalaunak,
Ka nuka ŋul kere luaario kotoma akwap.”
