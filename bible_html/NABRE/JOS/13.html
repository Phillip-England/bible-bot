Yosua 13

13
1 # Age 18:11; Yos 14:10; 24:29; 1 Ika 1:1; Luk 1:7. Amoionit Yosua kokokosanite noi; kotema EJAKAIT ŋes atiar, “Imojoŋit ijo ikokosanit da noi, konye ideuna akwap naepol nakiruma. 2#Any 23:29,31; Ikis 11:23,24; Tub 3:1; Age 10:14; 26:1; Yok 3:4; 1 Sam 27:8; 2 Sam 3:3. Na ŋes akwap naeroko ideuna: akwap kere naka Ipilisitian ka Igesurin kere; 3#Yer 2:18; 1 Sam 6:4,16; Sep 2:5; Ikis 2:23. Ageun koSikor, loejai kinaren naka Misiri, kiton oikor loka Ekuron kari naka agolitomei, loemarakinitai oKanaanin: ijaka ikany luka Ipilisitian; Igasin, ka Isudodain, ka Isikelonain, ka Igitin, ka Iyekuronin; luka Abim da, 4#Yos 19:30; Tub 1:34. Kari naka eŋalakimak: akwap kere naka Ikanaanin, ka Meara loerai loka Isidonin, kiton Apek, kiton oikor loka Imorain: 5#1 Ika 5:18; Sab 83:7; Esk 27:9; Yos 12:7. Akwap da naka Igebalin, ka Lebanon kere, kama eniunia akoloŋ, ageun koBaal-gad kwap kemoru Kerumon kiton neelomarere Kamat: 6#Mar 33:54; Yos 11:8; 23:13; Tub 2:21; Yos 14:1,2. Luibojete kere kakwap nakiukon ageun koLebanon kiton Misirepot-maim, kes Isidonin kere; ereŋeari eoŋ ŋul kakonyen nuka idwe luka Isirael: konye kokorak ijo idwe luka Isirael akwap nin araun airumet, kwape keeorakinia joo 7Aso kokorak akwap na araun airumet naka atekerin akanykaoŋon, ka atutubet naka ateker naka Manase.”
8 # Mar 32:33; Ikis 3:12; Yos 22:4. Apotu Ireubenin ka Igadin kodumut airumetekee nepepe kaŋes, naabu Musa koinak kes kokekwa loka Yorodan kari nakokide, kwape ainakinia Musa ekejaanakinan loka EJAKAIT kes; 9#Mar 21:30; Isa 15:2; Yer 48:18,22. Ageun koAroer, loejai osiep loka aditot naka Arunon, ka ere loejai kidin aditot, ka apuuton kere naka Medeba kiton Dibon; 10ka ireria kere luka Sikon ekabaka loka Imorain, loayait Kesibon, kiton oikor loka idwe luka Amon; 11#Yos 12:5. ka Gilead, ka eikor loka Igesurin ka Imaakatin, ka moru Kerumon kere, ka Basan kere kiton Saleea; 12#Ikis 3:11; Mar 21:24. Ajakanut kere naka Og koBasan, loayait Asitarot ka Ederei (nopenol Ioedeuna loka luedeuna luka Irepaim); naarai abu Musa kodany ŋul, kireŋea da, 13Konye mam idwe luka Isirael apotu kireneasi Igesurin, karaida Imaakatin: konye apotu Gesur ka Maakat kiboikinos kidin kIsirael, kiton lolo, 14#Ikis 18:1; 10:9; 12:12,19; Mar 18:20; Yos 14:3,4. Ateker naka Lebi bon ŋes mam ŋes abu koinak airumet; ainakineta nueinakinere kakim nuka EJAKAIT Edeke loka Isirael, kes airumeteke, kwape abu kolimokinia Ŋes.
15Koinak Musa ateker naka idwe luka Reuben kwape ejaatar ikekiakec, 16#Mar 21:28,30; Ikis 3:12; Yos 12:2. Ageunit eikorukec koAroer, loejai osiep loka aditot naka Arunon, ka ere loejai kidin aditot, ka apuuton kere naka Medeba; 17Kesibon, ka ireriake kere lueiaas apuuton; Dibon, ka Bamot-baal, ka Bet-baal-meon; 18#Mar 21:23. Ka Yakas, ka Kedemot, ka Mepaat; 19#Mar 32:37. ka Kiriataim ka Sibima, ka Seret-sakar komoru loka aditot; 20ka Bet-peor, ka neijululuoro Pisiga, ka Bet-yesimot; 21#Ikis 3:10; Mar 21:24; 31:8. ka ireria kere luka apuuton, ka ajakanut kere naka Sikon, ekabaka loka Imorain, loayait Kesibon, loabu Musa kodany nepepe kiiaka luka Midian, Ebi, ka Rekem, ka Sur, ka Kur, ka Reba, ikapolok luka Sikon, lueboiete kakwap. 22#Mar 22:5; 24:1; 31:8; 2 Pet 2:15; Apu 2:14. Apotu idwe luka Isirael koarata Balaam da okoku loka Beor, ekadwaran, kotoma kanul luaponi koarai. 23Arai Yorodan eikor loka idwe luka Reuben, ka eikoruke. Ŋin ŋes airumet naka idwe luka Reuben kwape ejaatar ikekiakee, ireriakec lukapolok ka ludidik da.
24Koinak Musa ateker naka Gad, kes idwe lukaGad, kwape ejaatar ikekiakee. 25#Mar 32:35; 21:26-29; Ikis 2:19; Tub 11:13; 2 Sam 11:1; 12:26. Arai Yaser eikorukee, ka ireria kere luka Gilead, ka atutubet naka akwap naka idwe luka Amon, kiton Aroerloitei Raba; 26Ageun koKesibon kiton Ramat-misipe, ka Betonim; ageun da koMakanaim kiton oikor loka Debir; 27#Age 33:17; 1 Ika 7:46; Mar 34:11; Ikis 3:17; Yos 11:2; Mat 14:34; Luk 5:1. Kaditot da, Bet-karam, ka Bet-nimura, ka Sukot, ka Sapon, ka ajakanut kere naedeuna naka Sikon ekabaka loka Kesibon, Yorodan ka eikoruke, kiton osaŋe loka aŋolol naka Kinerot, okekwa loka Yorodan kokide. 28Ŋin ŋes airumet naka idwe luka Gad kwape ejaatar ikekiakec, ireriakec lukapolok ka ludidik.
29Koinak Musa atutubet naka ateker naka Manase airumet: arai naka atutubet naka ateker naka Manase kwape ejaatar ikekiakec. 30#Mar 32:41; 1 Siit 2:23. Ageunit eikorukee koMakanaim, Basan kere, ajakanut kere naka Og ekabaka loka Basan, ka ireria kere luka Yair, luejaas Basan, ireria akaisakanykape: 31#Yos 12:4. ka atutubet naka Gilead, ka Asitarot, ka Edcrei, ireria luka ajakanut naka Og koBasan, araas luka idwe luka Makir okoku loka Manese, kes atutubet naka idwe luka Makir kwape ejaatar ikekiakec,
32Ŋun kes airumeta nuakorokoroi Musa kapuutona nuka Moab kokekwa loka Yorodan koYeriko, kokide. 33#Mar 18:20; Yos 18:7; Ikis 10:9; 12:12; 14:27,29; 18:1,2; Esk 44:28. Konye mam Musa abu koinak ateker naka Lebi airumet: EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, ŋes airumetekec, kwape abu kolimokinia kes.
