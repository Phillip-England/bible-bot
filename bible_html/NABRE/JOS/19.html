Yosua 19

19
1 # Age 49:7. Kobeik eroet lokiareit Simeon, ŋes ateker naka idwe luka Simeon, kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: ajai airumetekec kidin kairumet naka idwe luka Yuda, 2#1 Siit 4:28-33; Age 21:14,31; 26:33; Yos 15:28; Nek 11:26. Apotu kesi kirumata Beer-seba, arai Seba, ka Molada; 3ka Kasar-sual, ka Bala, ka Esem; 4#1 Siit 4:29,30. ka Elitolad, ka Betul, ka Koruma; 5ka Sikilag, ka Ber-marakabot, ka Kasar-susa; 6ka Bet-lebaot, ka Saruken; ireria itomon ka luuni ka ireriakec ludidik; 7Ain, ka Rimon, ka Eter, ka Asan; ireria iwonon ka ireriakec ludidik: 8ka ireria ludidik kere lualukunito ireria nul kiton Baalat-beer, Rama loka ari naka Eŋalakimak. Ŋin ŋes airumet naka ateker naka idwe luka Simeon kwape ajaatar ikekiakec. 9Ajai airumet naka idwe luka Simeon toma owai loka idwe luka Yuda: naarai alalakinit noi idwe luka Yuda akorakinetekec: kanuka nun ŋes akorakinere idwe luka Simeon airumet kokidin airumetekec.
10Kobeik eroet lokiwuniet idwe luka Sebulun kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: ajai eikor loka airumetekee kiton Sarid: 11#Age 49:13; Any 23:31; Mar 34:6,7; Yos 12:22; 1 Ika 4:12; 1 Siit 6:68. Kitor eikorukec ari nakoto, kiton Marala, kodol Dabeset; kodol da aguurut naejai kinaren ka Yokoneam; 12Kokudo koSarid ari nakokide mama eniunia akoloŋ kiton oikor loka Kisilot-tabor; kobe Daberat, kitor Yapia; 13Kobe kanen kiton Gat-keper, kiton Et-kasin; kitor Rimon loedolit Nea; 14Kokudo eikor airimor ŋes kari naka eŋalakimak kiton Kanaton: kosan aditot naka Ipita-el; 15#Yos 21:34; Tub 1:30; Yos 11:1; 12:20. ka Katat, ka Nakalal, ka Simuron, ka Idala, ka Bet-lekem: ireria itomon ka luarei ka ireriakec ludidik. 16#Asw 17:26. Ŋin ŋes airumet naka idwe luka Sebulun kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, ireria nul lukapolok ka ireriakec ludidik.
17Kobeik eroet lokiwononet Isakar, kes idwe luka Isakar kwape ajaatar ikekiakec. 18#Yos 15:16; Tub 6:33; 1 Ika 21:1,23; 2 Ika 8:29; 9:15,30; Kos 1:4,5; 1 Sam 28:4; 2 Ika 4:8. Ajai eikorukec kiton Yesereel, ka Kesulot, ka Sunem; 19ka Kaparaim, ka Sion, ka Anakarat; 20ka Rabit, ka Kision, ka Ebes; 21#Yos 21:29. ka Remet, ka En-ganim, ka En-kada, ka Bet-pases; 22#Tub 4:6; 1 Sam 10:3; 1 Siit 6:77; Sab 89:12; Yer 46:18. Kodol eikor Tabor, ka Sakasuma, ka Bet-semes; kosan eikorukec Yorodan: ireria itomon ka lukanykape ka ireriakec ludidik. 23Ŋin ŋes airumet naka ateker naka idwe luka Isakar kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, ireria lukapolok ka ireriakec ludidik,
24Kobeik eroet lokikanyet ateker naka idwe luka Aser, kwape ajaatar ikekiakec. 25#2 Sam 2:16; 1 Siit 6:75. Eikorukec ŋes Kelakat, ka Kali, ka Beten, ka Akisap; 26#1 Ika 18:19; 2 Ika 2:25; 4:25; Isa 35:2; Yer 46:18; Amo 1:2; 9:3; Mik 7:14. ka Alamelek, ka Amad, ka Misal; kodol Karamel kari nakoto, kiton Sikor-libunat da; 27#1 Ika 9:13. Kokudo neeniunia akoloŋ kiton Bet-dagon, kodol Sebulun, ka aditot naka Ipita-el kari naka agolitomei kiton Bet-emek, ka Neiel; kitor Kabul kakan nakokediany, 28#Yok 2:1; Age 10:15,19; Yos 11:8; Tub 1:31; 10:12; Asw 27:3. ka Eburon, ka Rekob, ka Kamon, ka Kana, kiton Sidon lokapolon; 29#2 Sam 5:11; Age 38:5; Tub 1:31; Mik 1:14. Kokudo eikor kiton Rama, ka ere loebwaitai loka Turo; kokudo eikor kiton Kosa; kosan aŋolol kakwap naka Akisib: 30Uma da, ka Apek, ka Rekob: ireria akaisaarei ka luarei ka ireriakec ludidik. 31Ŋin ŋes airumet naka ateker naka idwe luka Aser kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, ireria nul lukapolok ka ireriakec ludidik.
32Kobeik eroet lokikanyetekape idwe luka Naputali, kes idwe luka Naputali kwape ajaatar ikekiakec. 33Ageunit eikorukec koKelep, ageun kolua loajai Saananim, ka Adami-nekeb, ka Yabuneel, kiton Lakum: kosan Yorodan: 34#Ikis 33:23. Kokudo eikor ari nakoto kiton Asinot-tabor, kobe kanen kiton Kukok; kodolok Sebulun kari naka eŋalakimak, kodolok Aser kari nakoto, ka Yuda da koYorodan kaneeŋiunia akoloŋ, 35#Age 10:18; Mar 13:21; 1 Ika 8:65; 2 Ika 14:25; Yer 39:5; Ikis 3:17; Yos 11:2; 12:3; Mk 6:53; Luk 5:1. Ireria luabwaitai kes Sidim, ka Ser, ka Kamat, ka Rakat ka Kineret; 36ka Adama, ka Rama, ka Kasor; 37ka Kedes, ka Ederei, ka En-kasor; 38ka Iron, ka Migidal-el, ka Korem, ka Bet-anat, ka Bet-semes; ireria itomon ka lukanykaonon ka ireriakec ludidik. 39Ŋin ŋes airumet naka ateker naka idwe luka Naputali kwape ajaatar ikekiakee, ireria lukapolok ka ireriakec ludidik,
40Kobeik eroet lokikanyetekaarei ateker naka idwe luka Dan kwape ajaatar ikekiakec. 41#Tub 13:2. Eikor loka airumetekec ŋes Sora, ka Esitaol, ka Ir-semes; 42#Tub 1:35; 1 Ika 4:9. ka Saalabin, ka Aiyalon, ka Itila; 43ka Elon, ka Timuna, ka Ekuron; 44ka Eliteke, ka Gibeton, ka Baalat; 45ka Yekud, ka Bene-berak, ka Gatrimon; 46ka Me-yarikon, ka Rakon, kiteos eikor kaYopa. 47#Tub 18:27-29; 18:1; Age 49:17. Kisukunyikis idwe luka Dan eikorukee: naarai apotu idwe luka Dan kolomasi aitiji Lesem, koyanaasi ŋes, kodanyata luken kakwanis naka ekileŋ, kirumata, kiboikinos toma, komaikis Lesem Dan, ekiror loka papakec. 48#Mar 26:54; Asw 17:26. Ŋin ŋes airumet naka ateker naka idwe luka Dan kwape ajaatar ikekiakee, ireria ŋullukapolok ka ireriakec ludidik.
49Kitananata kwaŋin akorokor airumeta nuka akwap kwape ajaatar iikorunoke; koinakis idwe luka Isirael Yosua okoku loka Nun airumet kamakee: 50#Yos 24:30; 1 Siit 7:24. Apotu koinakis ŋes ere loelipit, ŋes Timunat-sera kotoma akwap nakiukon naka Epuraim, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT: abu koduk ere, kiboikin roma.
51 # Mar 34:17; Yos 14:1; 18:1,10; Tub 21:19; Sab 78:58; Yer 7:12; 26:6. Ŋun kes airumeta nuapotu Eleasar esasedoti, ka Yosua okoku loka Nun, ka ikapolok luka ikalia luka lukapapaa luka atekerin nuka idwe luka Isirael kokorokorata kes airum kailamilam eroet koSilo kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT kokek loka ewema loka airiamunun. Kitananata kwaŋin akorokor akwap.
