Yosua 15

15
1 # Mar 34:3; 33:36. Kobe eroet loka ateker naka idwe luka Yuda kwape ejaatar ikekiakee ageun koikor loka Edom, kiton amodiŋ naka Sin, kari naka eŋalakimak, atutubet nakosane naka eŋalakimak. 2Ageunit eikorukee loka ari naka eŋalakimak katutubet nakosane loka Aŋolol naka Abalaŋ, ageun kagor naitei eŋalakimak: 3Kitor ari naka eŋalakimak naedokarere Akurabim, kobei Sin, kitor ari naka eŋalakimak naka Kades-barunea, kiriria Kesiron, kitor Adar, kokudo koKaraka: 4#Mar 34:5; Age 15:18; 1 Ika 8:65. Kobe Asimon, kitor aguurut nako Misiri; kosan eikor aŋolol: eraun ŋol eikorukus loka ari naka eŋalakimak. 5Aŋolol naka Abalaŋ ŋes arai eikor lokokide, kiton osaŋe loka Yorodan, Ageunit eikor loka ari naka agolitomei kariri naka aŋolol kosane loka Yorodan; 6#Yos 18:17. Kitor eikor Bet-kogila, kiriria agolitomei naka Bet-araba; kitor eikor amoru naka Bokan okoku loka Reuben: 7#Yos 7:26; Isa 65:10; Kos 2:15; 2 Sam 17:17. Kitor eikor Debir ageun kaditot naka Akor, ape agolitomei, kitei Giligal, loitei needokarere Adumim, loejai ari naka eŋalakimak naka ecor: kobe eikor akipi nuka En-semes, kosaŋak En-rogel; 8#Yos 18:16,28; 2 Ika 23:10; Yer 19:2,6; Tub 1:21; 19:10; 2 Sam 5:18. Kitor eikor aditot naka okoku loka Kinom kiton ari naka eŋalakimak naka Eyebusit (nopenol ŋes Yerusalem): kitor eikor kuju keronat loejai kinaren kaditot naka Kinom kari nakoto, loejai atutubet nakosane naka aditot naka Repaim kari naka agolitomei: 9#1 Siit 13:6; Tub 18:12. Kigirunai eikor ageun kokuju keronat kiton atan naka akipi nuka Neputoa, kobe oreria luka emoru Epuron; kigirunai eikor kiton Baala (ŋopeŋol ŋes Kiriat-yearim): 10#Age 38:13; Tub 14:1. Kokudo eikor koBaala ari nakoto kiton emoru Seir, kobe ari naka agolitomei naka emoru Yearim (nopenol ŋes Kesalon), kitor Bet-semes, kiriria Timuna: 11#Yos 19:43; 1 Sam 5:10. Kobe eikorari naka agolitomei kowai loka Ekuron: kigirunai eikor kiton Sikeron, kobe emoru Baala, kosan Yabuneel; kosan eikor aŋolol. 12#Mar 34:6; Ikis 11:24; Esk 47:20. Aŋolol nakapolon ŋes arai eikor loka ari nakoto, ka eikoruke. Ŋol ŋes eikor loalukunit idwe luka Yuda kwape ajaatar ikekiakee.
13 # Yos 14:13; Age 23:2. Abu koinak Kaleb okoku loka Yepune akorakinet kodwe luka Yuda, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT naeeorakinit ŋesi Yosua; akorakineteke ŋes Kiriat-araba, arai Araba ŋol papa loka Anak (nopenol ŋes Keburon). 14#Tub 1:10; Mar 13:22. Kirenea Kaleb odwe iuni luka Anak, Sesai, ka Akiman, ka Talimai, idwe luka Anak. 15#Yos 10:38. Koloma asurokin lueboiete koDebir: arai sek ekiror loka Debir Kiriat-seper. 16Kotema Kaleb atiar, “Ŋol loedanyi luka Kiriat-seper, kosodi airum ere, akorakini eoŋ ŋol akokuka Akisa aimany.” 17#Tub 3:9; Mar 32:12; Yos 14:6. Kosodi Otiniel, okoku loka Kenas onace loka Kaleb, kirum ŋes; abu kokorak ŋes akokuke Akisa aimany. 18#Tub 1:14; Age 24:64; 1 Sam 25:23. Kosodi kanen, kebu mamake abu ŋin kokoto nol ailipun papake emanikor: kodok kasigiriake; kotema Kaleb ŋes atiar, “Inyo bo ikoto ijo?” 19#Age 33:11. Kotema atiar, “Koinakinai eoŋ arereŋ; naarai kiinakinit eoŋ akwap naka ari naka Eijalakimak, koinakinai eoŋ da atanin nuka akipi.” Abu koinak ŋes atanin nukokuju ka nukajesa da.
20 # Age 49:8-12; Ikis 33:7. Ŋin ŋes airumet naka ateker naka idwe luka Yuda kwape ejaatar ikekiakee. 21#Age 35:21. Ireria lukosane luka ateker naka idwe luka Yuda kariri naka eikor loka Edom kari naka Eŋalakimak kes lu: Kabuseel, ka Eder ka Yagur; 22ka Kina, ka Dimona, ka Adada; 23ka Kedes, ka Kasor, ka Itinan; 24#1 Sam 15:4. Sip, ka Telem, ka Bealot; 25ka Kasor-kadata, ka Keriot-kesiron (nopenol ŋes Kasor); 26Amam, ka Sema, ka Molada; 27ka Kasar-gada, ka Kesimon, ka Bet-pelet; 28ka Kasar-sual, ka Beer-seba, ka Bisiotia; 29ka Baala, ka Iim, ka Esem; 30ka Elitolad, ka Kesil, ka Korima; 31#Yos 19:5; 1 Sam 27:6; 30:1; 1 Siit 12:1. ka Sikilag, ka Madumena, ka Sanisana; 32#Nek 11:29. ka Lebaot, ka Silikim, ka Ain, ka Rimon: ireria kere imwaasi akaisaarei ka lukanykaonon ka ireriakec ludidik.
33 # Yos 19:41; Mar 13:23. Kapuuton, Esitaol, ka Sora, ka Asina; 34ka Sanoa, ka En-ganim, ka Tapua, ka Enam; 35#Yos 10:3,5; 12:15; 1 Sam 17:1; Yos 10:10. ka Yaramut, ka Adulam, ka Soko, ka Aseka; 36#1 Sam 17:52. ka Saaraim, ka Aditaim, ka Gedera, ka Gederotaim; ireria itomon ka luoŋon ka ireriakee ludidik.
37Senan, ka Kadasa, ka Migidal-gad; 38#2 Ika 14:7. ka Dilan, ka Misipe, ka Yokiteel; 39#Yos 10:3; 2 Ika 18:14; 19:8; 2 Siit 11:9; 2 Ika 22:1; Yos 12:12. Lakis, ka Bosikat, ka Egilon; 40ka Kabon, ka Lakimam, ka Kitilis; 41ka Gederot, ka Bet-dagon, ka Naama, ka Makeda; ireria itomon ka lukanykape ka ireriakee ludidik.
42Libuna, ka Eter, ka Asan; 43ka Iputa, ka Asina, ka Nesib; 44ka Keila, ka Akisib, ka Maresa; ireria ikanykaoŋon ka ireriakee ludidik.
45Ekuron, ka ireriake ka ireriake ludidik. 46#Yos 13:3; 1 Sam 5:1,6. Ageun ka Ekuron kiton aŋolol, kere luedunyas ka Asudod, ka ireriakee ludidik.
47 # Age 15:18; Mar 34:6. Asudod, ireriake ka ireriake ludidik; Gasa, ireriake ka ireriake ludidik; kiton aguurut nako Misiri, ka aŋolol nakapolon, ka eikoruke.
48Kakwap nakiukon, Samir, ka Yatir, ka Soko; 49ka Dana, ka Kiriat-sana (nopenol ŋes Dabir); 50ka Anab, ka Esitemo, ka Anim; 51#Yos 10:41; 11:16. ka Gosen, ka Kolon, ka Gilo, ireria itomon ka lodiopet ka ireriakee ludidik. 52Arab ka Duma, ka Esan; 53ka Yanim ka Bet-tapua ka Apcka; 54#Age 23:2; Yos 14:15; Tub 1:10. ka Kumita, ka Kiriat-araba, (nopenol ŋes Keburon), ka Sior; ireria ikanykaoŋon ka ireriakee ludidik.
55Maon, ka Karamel, ka Sip, ka Yuta; 56ka Yesereel, ka Yokodeam, ka Sanoa; 57Kain, ka Gibea, ka Timuna; ireria itomon ka ireriakec ludidik. 58Kalukul, ka Bet-sur, ka Gedor; 59ka Maarat, ka Bet-anot, ka Elitekon; ireria ikanykape ka ireriakec ludidik.
60 # Yos 18:14; 1 Sam 7:1,2; 1 Siit 13:6. Kiriat-baal (nopenol ŋes Kiriat-yearim), ka Raba; ireria iarei ka ireriakec ludidik.
61Kotoma amodiŋ, Bet-araba, Midin, ka Sekaka; 62#1 Sam 23:29. ka Nibisan, ka Ere loka Abalaŋ, ka En-gedi; ireria ikanykape ka ireriakee ludidik.
63 # Tub 1:8,21; 19:10-12; Mar 13:29; 2 Sam 5:6; 24:16,18; 1 Siit 11:4; 2 Siit 3:1; Sek 9:7. Konye Iyebusin, lueboienenete koYerusalem, mam idwe luka Yuda apotu kopedosi aireŋear kes: konye apotu Iyebusin kiboioto kidwe luka Yuda koYerusalem, kiton lolo.
