Yosua 6

6
1Kogoloro noi Yeriko kanuka idwe luka Isirael: amamei yenelomari karaida yenelomuni. 2#Yos 2:9; Tub 11:21; 2 Sam 5:19; Nek 9:24; Dan 5:18,19. Kotema EJAKAIT Yosua atiar, “Koany, ainak Yeriko akanikon, ka ekabakake, ka ituŋa lukirigin luetitiŋ. 3Irimorete yesi ere, ituŋa kere lukajiok, irimorete ere irwan atipet. Iswamai ijo kwaŋin apaarasia akanykape. 4#Tub 7:16; Mar 10:8. Itiŋete isasedotin ikanykaarei arupepen akanykaarei nuka amumwara nuka imerekekin koiŋaren kasaduku: ido kapaaran nakikanyetekaarei irimorete ere irwan akanykaarei, kokusete isasedotin arupepen. 5Ejaun do ebe, naekutakiniata noi amumwarat naka emerekek, naipupunioto yesi aruo naka arupepe, ibwoŋakinete ituŋa kere eibwoŋ loepol: etiyakin arwatat naka ere, edokarete ituŋa ŋinituŋanan ederori kiŋarenike.” 6Abu Yosua okoku loka Nun konyarau isasedotin, kotema kes atiar, “Kodakata asaduku naka aitutuket, kitiŋata isasedotin ikanykaarei arupepen akanykaarei nuka amumwara nuka imerekekin koiŋaren kasaduku naka EJAKAIT.” 7Kotemasi kesi ituŋa atiar, “Kotuboros, kirimosi ere, koloto ituŋa luekapakina kiŋaren kasaduku naka EJAKAIT.”
8 # Yos 4:13. Kojaun kwaŋin ebe, naalimokinia Yosua ituŋa, koloto isasedotin ikanykaarei luetinito arupepen akanykaarei nuka amumwara nuka imerekekin kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kokutata arupepen: kotupak kes asaduku naka aitutuket naka EJAKAIT. 9#Mar 10:25; Isa 42:12,13. Koloto ituŋa luakapakina kiŋaren kisasedotin luakusete arupepen, kotupata lukokau asaduku, kokusete isasedotin arupepen elosete. 10Kicorak Yosua ituŋa ebe, “Mam ibwoŋakinete karaida ikusiporotoi apupunio, karaida alimun adiokirot kaitukekus, aitodol apaaran nakecorakinia yes aibwoŋakin; ŋes konyet ibwoŋakiniata.” 11Kwaŋin kisirimo asaduku naka EJAKAIT ere, airimor ŋes irwan atipet: koboŋosi oidukurun, koperoto oidukurun.
12 # Ikis 31:25. Kodilok Yosua anyoun tupuruc, kodakata isasedotin asaduku naka EJAKAIT. 13#1 Siit 15:26; Gal 6:9. Kolosenenete isasedotin ikanykaarei luetiŋito arupepen akanykaarei nuka amumwara nuka imerekekin koiŋaren kasaduku naka EJAKAIT, kokusete arupepen: kiŋarenikinitos ituŋa luakapakina lujiis kes; kotupata lukokau asaduku naka EJAKAIT, kokusete isasedotin arupepen elosete. 14Kapaaran nakiareit da kirimosi ere irwan atipet, koboŋosi oidukurun: kiswamata kwaŋin apaarasia akanykape.
15Kosodi kanen kapaaran nakikanyetekaarei, kodilokis anyoun ecila kide, kirimosi ere kwape ejaar epone ŋol irwan akanykaarei: konye kapaaran kaŋin apotu kirimosi ere irwan akanykaarei, 16#Tub 7:20; 2 Siit 13:14. Kosodi kanen irwan nakikanyetekaarei nakekutakiniata isasedotin arupepen, kotema Yosua ituŋa atiar, “Kibwoŋata; naarai kiinak EJAKAIT yes ere. 17#Age 12:3; Yos 2:4; 1 Sam 15:6; Mat 10:41; 25:40; Bur 6:10; 11:31; Yak 2:25. Itateleori EJAKAIT ere, ŋes ka luejaas toma kere; dimarai Rakab naadote ŋes ejari, ŋes ka ŋul kere luejaas kaŋes togo, naarai abu ŋesi kibwa ikiyakia lukiijukarit. 18#Ikis 7:26; 13:17; Yos 7:1,11,12; Isa 52:11; Rom 12:9; 2 Kor 6:17; Pes 5:11; 1 Ses 5:22; Yos 7:25; 1 Ika 18:17,18; Yok 1:12. Ka yes da, kocoito noi kosaca ibore yenitateleoritai kinyeis naidauniata aitaleor, ayaŋaar ibore yenitateleoritai; kosodete kwaŋin ayaun aiken oidukurun luka Isirael ka aitican kes. 19Konye epuesa kere, ka esabu, ka amoti nuka aisinit ka asuat eraas nukalaunak kama ejai EJAKAIT: eyaŋaunio aiŋadis naka EJAKAIT,” 20#Bur 11:30. Kosodete ituŋa eibwoŋ, kokutata isasedotin arupepen: kosodi kanen kepuput ituŋa aruo naka arupepe, kibwoŋata ituŋa eibwoŋ loepol, kotiyakin arwatat, kodokasi ituŋa ore, ŋinituŋanan kodero kiŋarenike, kirumata ere, 21#Ikis 7:2; 20:16,17; 1 Sam 15:3,8,18,19; 1 Ika 20:42; Yer 48:18; Apu 18:21. Komudiasi cut kere luajaas toma ore, ikiliok ka aŋor, ludidik ka lumojoŋ, imoŋin ka amerekekin, ka asigirian, kakwanis naka ekileŋ.
22 # Yos 2:14; Bur 11:31. Kotema Yosua ituŋa luarei luarosete akwap ebe, “Koloto togo loka naadote, kitolomut aberu, ka luejaatar kere, kwape kiyapoikinioto ŋes.” 23#Yos 2:13. Kolomasi ikatumunak lukarotak, kitolomut Rakab ka papake, ka totoke, ka iinacanake, ka Iuajaatar kere; kitolomut da lukokekike kere; kibwaikis kes kiŋa kiidukurun luka Isirael. 24Kicweikis ere, ka luejaas toma kere: dimarai epuesa ka esabu, ka amoti nuka aisinit ka asuat, apotu kesi kibwaikis ŋul aiŋadis naka etogo loka EJAKAIT. 25#Bur 11:31; Mat 1:5. Konye Rakab naadote, ka ekal loka papake, ka luajaatar kere abu Yosua kitajar; kiboikin mama ejai Isirael, kiton lolo; naarai abu ŋesi kibwa ikiyakia luejukarit Yosua airot Yeriko. 26#1 Ika 16:34; Mal 1:4. Kicorak Yosua kes kaiyaposit kapak kaŋin ebe, “Kikenoi kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT etuŋanan loenyouni aiduk ere lo Yeriko,
Etwani ekiyaike naepesikinia ŋesi apetetake,
Etwani da eŋooritike naesipokinia ŋesi ikekiake.”
27 # Age 39:2; Ikis 31:8,23; Yos 1:5; 3:7; Tub 1:19; 2 Sam 7:9; 8:6; Rom 8:31; Yos 9:1,3; 1 Sam 2:30. Kojaenena EJAKAIT ka Yosua; kojenun da ŋesi kakwap kere.
