Yokana 19

19
1Kosodi Pilato koyaŋa Yesu, koda ŋes. 2Kojeata iserikalei aboko naka ikukwa, kitanapikis akouke, kitanapata da anapito naamuge; 3#Yok 18:22. koponenenete mama ejai, kobalas ebe, “Yoga, Ekabaka loka Iyudayan!” Kibapanasi ŋes. 4Kosodi kolom bobo Pilato kiŋa, kotema kes atiar, “Koany ayaŋauni ŋes kiŋa mama ijaas, tetere ijenunete ebe mam eoŋ aanyunit adioronis kamakeŋ.” 5Kolom do Yesu, enapit aboko naka ikukwa ka anapito naamuge, Kotema Pilato kes atiar, “Koany etuŋanan!” 6Kosodete keanyutu isasedotin lupeti ka ikapolokitok, kiworoto ebe, “Kibubuk, kibubuk ba!” Kotema Pilato kes atiar, “Koyaŋaasi yesi, kibubukis, naarai mam eoŋ aanyunit adioronis kamakeŋ.” 7#Yok 5:18; 10:33; Leb 24:16. Koboŋokis Iyudayan ebe, “Ejai sio ekisil, ido kanuka ekisil kaŋol ibusakinit ŋes atwanare, naarai abu kitarauna bon Okoku loka Edeke.” 8Kosodi nakiiraria Pilato akirot ŋin, kiyatak akurianar; 9koloma bobo oPuraitorion#19:9 Puraitorion ŋes etogo loka ekayaiton. kotema Yesu atiar, “Laali bo kibunio?” Konye mam Yesu eboŋokino. 10Kanuka ŋun kotema Pilato ŋes atiar, “Mam inera keoŋ? Mam ijeni ebe ejai eoŋ apedor aosikin jo, ejai da apedor aibubukin?” 11#Yok 10:18; Asw 2:23. Koboŋok Yesu ebe, “Ti mam jo ijaas kapedor kere mamakaŋ, arai mam kidumunit abunore kokuju; kanuka ŋun ŋol lokejaikini eoŋ akaninikon ŋes erono adepar.”
12 # Asw 17:7. Ageun kapak kaŋin komoote Pilato aosikin ŋes, konye kiworoto Iyudayan ebe, “Arai kiwosik lo, mam irai epapero loka Kaisar; ŋolituŋanan loedakakini bon araun ekabaka itokokoli Kaisar.” 13Nakiiraria Pilato akiro ŋun, kitolom Yesu kiŋa, kiboikin oicoloŋ loka aitub, aibois naenyaritai Amor Nuepesitai, konye kAburanian enyaritai Gabata. 14Kerai da apaaran naka Aitemokin Aleleŋ; kerai bala esawa lokikanyetekape. Kotema Pilato Iyudayan atiar, “Koany, Ekabakakus!” 15#Yok 19:6. Kanuka ŋun kiworoto ŋul ebe, “Kolema, kolema, kibubuk!” Pilato kotema kes atiar, “Ebubukini kereka Ekabakakus?” Koboŋokis isasedotin lupeti ebe, “Emamei sio ekabaka ece dimarai Kaisar.” 16#Mat 27:31-50; Mk 15:20-37; Luk 23:26-46. Kosodi kijaik ŋes akaninikec aibubukinio.
17Koyata Yesu, koloma, erioŋit ekemusalaba kiton aibois naenyaritai Abelekek naka Akou, kAburanian ebe Gologota. 18Kibubukis nen, ka ice iarei nenipe kaŋes, ediopet epuai ka ece da epuai, ka Yesu kidiŋikec. 19Kiwadik Pilato aitejenunet, kikajak omusalaba; ewadikatai ebe, “YESU LOKO NASARET, EKABAKA LOKA IYUDAYAN.” 20Kosioma luipu koYudayan aitejenunet ŋin, naarai keapie aibois nakibubukinitoi Yesu kore; kiwadikatai kAburanian, ka Aromayon, ka Ayonanin da. 21Kosodete isasedotin lupeti luka Iyudayan kotemasi Pilato atiar, “Sirikiwadika ebe, ‘Ekabaka loka lyudayan’, dimarai ebe, ‘Abala ŋesi ebe Eoŋ ekabaka loka Iyudayan.’” 22Koboŋok Pilato ebe, “Nuewadik, ewadik.”
23Nakedauniata iserikalei aibubukin Yesu, koyaŋaasi akenapito kokora katutubeta aoŋon, ŋoliserikalet nakeŋ, ka ekaso da. Konye ekasoke kemamei airiamasit, naarai kejeitai ameun kokuju kiton kwap; 24#Sab 22:18. Kotemanaros bonik atiar, “Sirikiicil, konye kelamilamakisi eroet aanyun iŋai ebeikini.” Tetere edolete nukiwadikatai ebe,
“Akorokorata anapitoka,
Kilamilamata kanuka ekasoka.”
Kiswamata iserikalei kwaŋin.
25Konye kobwoete totoke ka inaci naka totoke, ŋes Mariam aberu naka Kelopa, ka Mariam Magadalene, eapiete kemusalaba loka Yesu. 26#Yok 13:23. Kosodi keanyu Yesu totoke, ka ekesisian lokemina ebwoi kodie, kotema totoke atiar, “Toto, koany okokukon!” 27Kosodi kotema ŋesi ekesisian atiar, “Koany totokon!” Ameun kosawa kaŋol koyaŋa ekesisian ŋin oreke.
28 # Sab 22:15; 69:21; Yok 13:3; 18:4. Kokau kaŋun, kojeni Yesu ebe aŋetakinos kere, tetere itodolikini nakiwadikatai kobala ebe, “Kajak akur.” 29#Sab 69:21. Kejai nen amot naeleleba ebino loedwar; kibwaikis acago naileleba ebino ŋol loedwar kuju akwara, kikiasi kiton aitukuke. 30Kedau Yesu aijaikinio ebino loedwar, kobala ebe, “Aŋetakinos.” Kitulur akekou, kijaik ekemoyo.
31 # Ikis 21:23. Aso naarai kerai apaaran naka Aitemonokin, kinyeis awat asalakin omusalaba apaaran nakasabato (naarai kerai nakasabato ŋin apaaran nakapolon), ŋes kilipiata Iyudayan Pilato akejekec abilio, tetere elemunio. 32Kopotu do iserikalei kobilata akeje nuka losodit, ka nuka lokiareit lokibubukinitoi nenipe kaŋes; 33konye kedolokis Yesu koanyut ebe kedau nan atwanare, mam ebila akejeke, 34Konye ediopet koserikalei kicum amaranake kakwara, atipet kolomutu aokot ka akipi. 35Ŋol da lokeanyuni iteienunit, ido akeitejenunet erai nakabeit, ŋes da ejeni ebe inera abeit, tetere yes da iyuunete. 36#Any 12:48; Mar 9:12; Sab 34:20. Naarai kedoikinete ŋun tetere edoli nakiwadikatai ebe, “Mam ebilio akekoit.” 37#Sek 12:10; Apu 1:7. Ka nakiwadikatai ace da ebala ebe, “Esesenete moolo ŋol lokicumete.”
38 # Mat 27:57-61; Mk 15:42-47; Luk 23:50-55. Kokau kaŋun abu Yosep loka Arimataya lokerai ekesisian loka Yesu, konye kaiyeiyea kanuka akurian Iyudayan, kilip Pilato apedor alemar akuan naka Yesu: kisiik Pilato. Kosodi kolot, kolema akuanake. 39#Yok 3:1-2; Mat 2:11. Kolot Nikodemo da, lokelosit ber mama ejai ŋesi kuare, koyaŋa lukesaete ka icicikwa luinyalal, bala alaŋir naka ilatirin akwatat. 40Kosodete kolemut akuan naka Yesu, konunukikis ogoen luka ebaputa nepepe kalukesai, kwape ejaar epone loka Iyudayan loka aida. 41Nepenen nekibubukinitoi kejai emanikor; ido toma omanikor kejai ates nakitetet namam lem kibwaikinitai idiotuŋanan. 42Kosodi kanuka Aitemonokin naka Iyudayan, kibwaikis Yesu nen, naarai aapie ates.
