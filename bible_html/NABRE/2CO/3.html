1 # 2 Kor 5:12; Rom 16:1; Asw 18:27. Kigeuni kereka aiturokina bobo? Arai bo kwape kicetuŋa kipuda tope ibaluan luka aiturit mamakus, arai kamakus? 2#1 Kor 9:2. Iraas yesi ebaluakosi, iwadikatai otauonokus,#3:2 wadikaete lukapolok ice “kosi” aibois naka “kus”. ejena esiomete da ituŋa kere. 3#Any 24:12; 31:18; 34:1; Awa 3:3; 7:3; Rom 15:16; Yer 31:33; Esk 11:19; 36:26. Itakanete ebe iraas yesi ebalua loka Kristo, lokijaanakini isio, iwadikatai mere kebuino dimarai kEmoyo loka Edeke lokajaran, mere amor, dimarai otauon luka awat. 4Kimunokina Edeke kwaŋin kanuka Kristo, 5#2 Kor 2:6. Mam sio kilosikino aomit bonik araut ibore kanukosi; kwabo ebuni aisilosikiniokosi kama ka Edeke, 6#Yer 31:31; 1 Kor 11:25; Rom 7:6; Yok 6:63. lokisilosikini sio araut ikejaanakinak luka aitutuket nakitetet, mere luka enyukuta, dimarai luka Emoyo; naarai eari enyukuta, konye Emoyo einakini aijar.
7 # Any 34:29. Aso arai aijaanakin naka atwanare naigirigiritai kinyukutan kamoru, abu kobu kaibuses, tetere mam idwe luka Isirael apedorete asesen akonyen nuka Musa kanuka aibuses naka akonyeneke, aibuses ŋin naitapalaunio, 8#Gal 3:2,5. mam kereka aijaanakin naka Emoyo itelekarit kaibuses? 9#Ikis 27:26; Rom 1:17; 3:21. Naarai arai kejai aijaanakin naka atubokinio kaibuses, itelekarit noi aijaanakin naka adolit ajaun kaibuses. 10#Any 34:29. Naarai naitubusikinitai mam kitubusikinitai kakirot kana, kanuka aibuses naedepari noi noi. 11Naarai arai naitapalaunio kejai kaibuses, itelekarit noi naesalenenei ajaun kaibuses.
12Aso kija kamun naetia nen kitwasamai atitiŋ;, naepol, 13#Any 34:33,35. kwabo mere kwape Musa loemukorit akekonyen, kinyeis idwe luka Isirael asesen awasia naka naitapalaunio. 14#Rom 11:25. Konye kitininikitai ikecetauon. Naarai kiton lolo naesiomenenere aitutuket namojoŋ eriŋa esalit aimukoret ŋipeŋin mam epukoritai; naarai toma Kristo itapalaunitai, 15Konye kiton lolo naesiomenenere nuka Musa epesikina aimukoret otauonokec; 16#Any 34:34; Rom 11:23,26. konye naibelonokinor tuŋanan mama ejai Ejakait, elemario aimukoret, 17#Yok 7:39; 8:36; Any 16:7; 24:17; Rom 8:2. Konye erai Ejakait Emoyo, ido neejai Emoyo loka Ejakait, nepenen da ejaikin aosikinio. 18Konye oni kere kiseseni aibuses naka Ejakait kwape toma aroet, kakonyen numam imukoritai, kiloconokinio aputor ŋes, anyoun kaibuses kiton aibuses, naarai epote ŋun kama ka Ejakait, loerai Emoyo.
