1 # Leb 23:5; Mar 9:1-5; 28:16; Ikis 16:1-2. Kobala EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron kakwap nako Misiri ebe, 2#Any 13:4; Ikis 16:1. “Eraun elap lo logeet kolapio mama ijaas: eraun elap losodit laka ekaru mama ijaas. 3Kolimokis ekodet kere loka Isirael ebe, Kapaaran nakitomonet naka elap lo elemunete kesi ŋininujanan imerekek, kwape ejaatar itogoi luka lukapapakee, imerekek kanolitogo. 4Arai ikidioko lukotogo komam edaunete imerekek, komorata ka ekeidunyet loidunyatar eketogo; koyaata kwape eiaar etiai loka ituŋa; kwape enyamia ninitunanan, kimarakinio omcrekek. 5#Leb 22:19; Mal 1:8,14; Bur 9:14; 1 Pet 1:19. Sirienwalit imerekekikus, ikiliokit yenika ekaru ediopet: ilemunete kotoma amerekekin arai toma akinei, 6#Leb 23:15; Mar 9:3; 26:18; Ikis 16:1,6. Inadaete ŋes aitodol apaaran nakitomonet kaonon naka elap kaŋol. Atukot kere naka ekodct loka Isirael eari napebon, 7Eyaŋaarete da aokot, kipikakis ategerio aarei nuka ekek ka acunet da, kotogoi luenyamiata. 8#Mar 9:11; 1 Kor 5:8. Ido enyamete akiriŋ akuare nin, kopeet kakim, ka emugati lomam itubuunitoi; enyamete da kedia kaloedwar. 9Sirikinyamata eionit, karaida ipoitoi, nabarai apcot kakim; akekou ka akekeje ka iboro da luka akoik. 10#Any 23:18. Sirikideut idiobore aitodol tupuruc; konye yenideun aitodol tupuruc kicweata kakim. 11#Ikis 16:5. Inyamete da kwanata; koenito akusiŋabes, koiaas amuk akeiekus, ebela akanikus: kisunyito inyamete: erai ŋin aleleŋ naka EJAKAIT. 12#Any 11:4,5; Amo 5:17; Mar 33:4. Naarai akuare nin etori eoŋ akwap nako Misiri, adanyi da ikiyaik kere kakwap nako Misiri, ituŋanan ka itian da; ido etodoli ikatubesia kodekesio kere luko Misiri: Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT. 13#Bur 11:28. Koraas aokot aanyunet mama iiaas kotogoi luijaas; kaany eoŋ da aokot aleleni yes, kwabo mam ejaun atular amudiar yes, naadanyia akwap nako Misiri.
14 # Any 23:15; 34:18; Leb 23:4,5,6-8; 2 Ika 23:21; Mar 28:17-25; Ikis 16:3-8. “Koraenena apaaran ŋin aisiitunet mamakus, kidarenenete ŋes korai epucit loka EJAKAIT: kotoma aturiokus kere, kidarenenete epucit kokisil lomam edaun. 15#1 Kor 5:7; Age 17:14; Mar 9:13; 28:17; Ikis 16:3. Inyamcnenete imugatin lumam itubuunitoi apaarasia akanykaarei; ageun kapaaran nasodit ilemarete yenikitubuunet kotogoikus: naarai ninitunanan yenenyami imugatin luitubuunitoi ageun kapaaran nasodit aitodol apaaran nakikanyetekaarei, egelario ituŋanan nin kolsirael. 16Kapaaran nasodit ejaun atukonokin nakalaunan; mam iswamao adioswam kere kapaarasia kanun, dimarai luenyami ŋinituŋanan, nul bon iswamaete. 17#Any 13:3. Idarenenete epucit loka imugatin lumam itubuunitoi; naarai kapaaran kaŋin ŋes etolomunia ajorekus kakwap nako Misiri: ŋes idareneniata apaaran nin anatar aturiokus, araun ekisil loka ikar kere. 18#Leb 23:5. Kolap losodit kapaaran nakitomonet kaonon naka clap eboŋ, inyamete imugatin lumam itubuunitoi, aitodol apaaran naka akaisaarei kanadiop naka elap eboŋ. 19#Any 23:15; 34:18; Mar 9:13. Apaarasia akanykaarei mam etakanuni yenikitubuunet kotogoikus; naarai ŋinitunanan yenenyami yenikitubuunitoi, egelario ituŋanan nin kokodet loka Isirael, arai erai emoit, karaida iwuriat. 20Sirikinyama yenikitubuunitoi; konyamete imugatin lumam itubuunitoi kotoma aiboisiokus kere,”
21 # Mar 9:4; Yos 5:10; Esr 6:20; Luk 22:7. Kosodi Musa anyaraun imakio kere luka Isirael, kotema kes atiar, “Koloto kooma idwe luka amerekekin kwape ejaatar ikekiakus, koarata luka aleleŋio. 22Kolemutu da itenun luka esop,#12:22 esop eputosi bala emopim kidomakis toma aokot nuejaas abeseni; kobwatanakis aokot nuejaas abeseni acuneta ka ategerio aarei nuka ekek: sirikelomu idiotuŋanan ke re kokek loka etogoke aitodol tupuruc. 23#Bur 11:28; Esk 9:6; Apu 7:3; 9:4; 2 Sam 24:16; 1 Kor 10:10. Naarai etubori EJAKAIT aidany Imisirian; aso naeanyunia aokot kacuneta ka ategerio aarei nuka ekek, cleleni EJAKAIT ekek, komam ecamakini ekamudiaran alomar otogoikus aidany yes, 24Ido idarenenete akirot nin araun ekisil mamakon kodwekon da, ikar kere. 25Kosodete kidoloto akwap nakiinakini EJAKAIT, kwape kolo kisumunio, idarenenete aiiaanakin nin. 26#Any 13:8,14; Ikis 32:7; Yos 4:6; Sab 78:6. Kosodi nakinisiata idwekus ebe, ‘Aijaanakinikus na, apolouke inyo?’ 27Itemanarete ebe, ‘Ŋes asubanit naka aleleŋ naka EJAKAIT, loalelenikini idwe luka Isirael koMisiri, naadanyia Imisirian koyuu ikaliawok.’’’ Kituluroto ituŋa akecekwes, komaata.
28Koloto idwe luka Isirael kiswamata kwaŋin; kwape ecorakinia EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron, kiswamata kwaŋin.
29 # Mar 8:17; 33:4; Sab 78:51; 105:36; Isa 37:36. Kosodi kidiijebai, kodany EJAKAIT ikiyaik kere luka akwap nako Misiri, ageun ka ekiyai loka Parao, loeboie koicoloŋoke loka ajakanut, kiton ikiyai loka loaenitai kaidaris; ka ikiyaik kere luka ibaren. 30#Age 11:6; Awa 21:13; Esk 7:27; Yak 2:13. Konyou Parao kuare, ŋes ka ikekeiaanakinak kere ka Imisirian kere; kojau aimony naepol akwap nako Misiri; naarai amamci etogo lomam atwanitor ituŋanan, 31#Any 10:9. Konyarau ŋesi Musa ka Aaron kuare, kobala ebe, “Konyoutu, koloto kotuŋaka, yes ka idwe luka Isirael; koloto kijaanakis EJAKAIT, kwape ibalatar. 32#Age 27:34. Koyaata isipaninikus luka amerekekin ka luka aituk, kwape ibalatar, koloto; kilipakis eoŋ da arercn.”
33 # Any 11:8; Sab 105:38; Age 20:3. Kisuburikisi Imisirian ituŋa, aitonyoun kes kakwap atipet; naarai kobalas ebe, “Wuu kitwaka!” 34Koyaata ituŋa atapiokec koroko kitubuuno, koenanakina amoti nukinyonis ogoenekec kokeperinikec. 35#Any 3:21-22; 11:2. Kiswamata idwe luka Isirael kwape ajaar akirot naka Musa; kilipata kesi Imisirian iboro luka epuesa, ka iboro luka esabu, ka igoen. 36#Age 15:14; Any 3:22. Koinak EJAKAIT ituŋa aminunio kakonyen nuka Imisirian, koinanakinai luelipito. Konatata Imisirian.
37 # Mar 33:3,5; Age 47:11; 12:2; 46:3; Any 38:26; Mar 1:46; 11:21. Koloto idwe luka Isirael anyoun koRameses adolore Sukot, ikiliok kwaka ilukumin akwat akanykape lualosete kakeie, komam imarakinitai idwe.
38Ido koupar erioŋet luka imo kakes; ka isipanin luka amerekekin ka luka aituk, ibaren luipu noi. 39#Any 6:1; 11:1. Kopeoto imugatin lumam itubuunitoi luka atap naayaunete kakwap nako Misiri, naarai mam etubuunitoi; naarai aponi kireijeunai koMisiri kisuouriknitete, kwabo mam epoito idisinyamat kere,
40 # Age 15:13; Gal 3:17; Asw 7:6. Apotu idwe luka lsirael kiboioto koMisiri adaun ikar akwat aoŋon ka akaisauni. 41#Any 7:4. Kosodi ketuboros ikar akwat aoŋon ka akaisauni, konyou aiore kere naka EJAKAIT kapaaran kaya koMisiri. 42Ŋes akuare naibusakinit aidario noi kama ejai EJAKAIT kanuka aitolomun kes kakwap nako Misiri: nin ŋes akuare ya naka EJAKAIT naibusakinit idwe luka Isirael aricenen noi anatar aturiokec kere,
43 # Mar 9:14. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron atiar, “Lo ŋes ekisil loka aleleŋ: mam imoit kere enyamenenei: 44#Age 17:12,13. Konye nolibulesit loegwelitai kapesan, kedau akepio, ŋes konyet enyamia. 45#Leb 22:10. Mam emoit ka ekeiaanakinan loiwotei enyamenenete. 46#Mar 9:12; Yok 19:33,36; Sab 34:20. Togo ediopet enyamenenere; sirikiyaŋaasi akirinike kina ketogo; karaida aibil akoitike. 47Erici ekodet kere loka Isirael, 48ido arai iboienenei emoit kajo, kokoto aidar aleleŋ kama ejai EJAKAIT, kokepenenete ikiliokoke kere, ŋes konyet ikunyakinia aidar; eraun bala iwuriat akwap: konye lomam ekepep kere mam enyamencnei, 49#Mar 9:14; 15:15; Gal 3:28. Eraun ekisil ediopet mama ejai iwuriat ka emoit loiboic kayes.”
50Kiswamata kwaŋin idwe luka Isirael kere; kwape ecorakinia EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron, kiswamata kwaŋin. 51#Asw 7:36. Kosodi kapaaran kaya kitolom EJAKAIT idwe luka Isirael kakwap nako Misiri kajorekec.
