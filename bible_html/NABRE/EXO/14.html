14
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Mar 33:7; Yer 44:1. “Kolimok idwe luka lsirael kobonosi kau, kidukuroto kiijaren ka Pi-kakirot, kidiij ka Migidol ka aŋolol, kinaren ka Baal-sepon; idukurcte ariri naka aŋolol, 3#Sab 35:21; 71:11. Etemari Parao nuka idwe luka Isirael ebe, “Etotoliki kes akwap, agoloo amodiŋ.’ 4#Yak 1:13-17; Any 9:16; Rom 9:17-23. Etogogoŋi eoŋ etau loka Parao, etupuni kes; adumuni eoŋ aibuses kaneejai Parao ka ajoreke kere; ejenunete da lmisirian ebe arai Eoŋ EJAKAIT.” Kiswamata kwaniŋ.
5Aponi kolimokinai ekabaka loka Misiri ebe, “Akerata ituŋa.” Kibelokin etau loka Parao ka loka ikekeiaanakinak mama ejaas ituŋa; kobalas ebe, cc Inyo bo yen yenikisub, aisiikin Isirael anyoun kaijaanakin oni?” 6Kitemok agalike, kouparos ka iketuŋa: 7Koyana agalin akwat akanykape nuseuna, ka agalin kere nuko Misiri, ka iiaka ayait kes kere. 8#Any 6:1; 13:9; Mar 33:3. Kitogogon EJAKAIT etau loka Parao ekabaka loka Misiri, kibwapak idwe luka lsirael: naarai alomarcte idwe luka Isirael kakan naarioro. 9#Any 15:9; Yos 24:6. Kibwapakis lmisirian kes, ibalasin kere ka agalin nuka Parao, ka ikebwanenokoke ka akejore, kodolokis kes idukurete ariri naka aŋolol, owai loka Pi-kakirot, kinaren ka Baal-sepon.
10 # Yos 24:7; Nek 9:9; Sab 34:17; 107:6. Keapia Parao, kiteluos idwe luka Isirael akonye, ido koany, kokubokinos Imisirian kes; kokuriakaros noi: komonyata idwe luka lsirael mama ejai EJAKAIT. 11#Sab 106:7. Kobalas Musa ebe, “Kanu emameatar atesin koMisiri, ŋes kiyaunia sio atwakikin modin? Kanukinyo kiswamakinia sio kwana, aitolomun sio koMisiri? 12#Any 6:9. Mere na ŋes akirot nakibala isio jo koMisiri ebe, ‘Kinyeinai sio kejaanakis Imisirian?’ Naarai ejok aijaanakin Imisirian itia atwakikin modin.” 13#2 Siit 20:15,17; Isa 41:10-14. Kobala Musa ituŋa ebe, “Sirikikuriakaros, kobwaata kopalarito, koanyutu aitaiar naka EJAKAIT, nakiswamakini yes lolo: naarai Imisirian luianyunete lolo, mam bobo ianyunete kes ikar kere. 14#Ikis 20:4; Yos 10:14; 23:3; 2 Siit 20:29; Nek 4:20; Isa 30:15; 31:4. Kijiikini EJAKAIT yes, kililinete yesi da.”
15Kobala EJAKAIT Musa ebe, “Kanukinyo imonyia mama ajai? Kolimok idwe luka Isirael kolototo kiŋaren. 16#Any 7:19. Kitonyou ijo ebelakon, korio akanikon aijolol, kotiak ŋes; itorete idwe luka Isirael kidin kaŋolol neconit. 17#Awa 29:1. Ka eoŋ da, koany, etogogoŋi itauon luka Imisirian, edoete atupite kes; adumuni eoŋ aibuses kaneejai Parao, ka ajoreke kere, kagalinike, kokebwanenokoke da. 18Ejenunete lmisirian ebe arai Eoŋ EJAKAIT, naadumunia aibuses kaneejai Parao, kagalinike, kokebwanenokoke da.”
19 # Age 48:16. Konyou emalaika loka Edeke loeŋarenikinit ajore naka Isirael kobono kaukec; konyou epir loka edou koinarenekec, kobwo kokaukec: 20#Isa 8:14; 2 Kor 4:3. Kobu kosipokin kidin ajore naka lmisirian ka ajore naka Isirael; kojai edou ka amuton, konye kocaik kuarc: mam kwi apotu kodolokis nul akuare kere.
21 # Yos 3:16; 4:23; Nek 9:11; Sab 74:13; 106:9. Korio Musa akekan aŋolol; kitukut EJAKAIT ekwam lokokide loegogon aitobonor aŋolol akuare kiya, kitarau aŋolol akwap naconit, kotiakas akip. 22#1 Kor 10:1-2; Bur 11:29; Any 15:19; Mar 33:8; Sab 66:6; 78:13. Kolomasi idwe luka Isirael kidin aŋolol neconit: kosubito akip kamakec bala arwatat kakan nakorcten, kakan da nakokediany. 23Kotupasi Imisirian, kolomasi akaulokec kidiij aŋolol, ibalasin kere luka Parao, ka agalinike, ka ikebwanenokoke. 24Kosodi kanen kotoma akwenyunet naka tupuruc, kosesen EJAKAIT ajore naka Imisirian kotoma opir loka akim ka cdou, kisiyalonon ajore naka Imisirian. 25Kisipok akokoron nuka agalinikec, kireŋeata kolanirikinito; tape ebalas Imisirian ebe, “Kakerata kakonyen nuka Isirael; naarai itijie EJAKAIT Imisirian kanukec.”
26Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, “Korio akanikon aŋolol, tetere eboŋokinete akip oMisirian, agalinikec, ka ikebwanenokokec.” 27#Yos 4:18. Korio Musa akekan aŋolol, Koboŋok aŋolol kagogonuke napiwalari; kokerata Imisirian; kilakilakak EJAKAIT Imisirian kidin aŋolol. 28#Ikis 11:4; Sab 78:53; Nek 9:11; Kbk 3:8. Kobonut akip, korapasi agalin ka ikebwanenok, ajore kere naka Parao naabu koloma aŋolol kokaukec; mam abu kosalak ediopet kakesi, 29#Sab 77:20; 78:52,53. Konye kitcro idwe luka Isirael nceonit kokidin aŋolol; kosubito akip kamakec bala arwatat kakan nakoteten ka nakokediany da.
30 # Sab 106:8-10; 58:10; 59:10. Kwaŋin kitajar EJAKAIT Isirael kapaaran kaŋin kakan naka Imisirian; koany Isirael Imisirian ctwakito kariri kaŋolol, 31#Any 19:9; Yok 2:11; 11:45. Koany Isirael aswam naepol naeswamakini EJAKAIT Imisirian, koyonata ituŋa EJAKAIT: kiyuut EJAKAIT ka Musa ekejaanakinanike.
