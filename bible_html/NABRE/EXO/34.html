34
1 # Ikis 10:1-4. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, “Kikeu amor aarei nueputos kwape kanusodit: ewadikakini eoŋ amor akiro nuajaas toma amor nusodit, nuibu kobwan, 2Kitemokin moi kodoku tupuruc moru Sinai, kitodun mama ajai kaidi naka emoru. 3Ido sirikedoku ituŋanan nepepe kajo, siriketakanu da idiotuŋanan komoru kere; karaida isipanin luka amerekekin ka aituk kinyeis adak kiŋaren kemoru kanol.” 4Kikeu ŋesi amor aarei nuaputos kwape kanusodit; konyou Musa tupuruc sek, kodoka moru Sinai, kwape ecorakinitor EJAKAIT, koyana kakanike amor aarei. 5Kijesun EJAKAIT kodou, kobwo kanen nepepe kaŋes, kolimo ekiror loka EJAKAIT.
6 # Any 20:5-6; Mar 14:18; Ikis 5:9-10; 7:9-10; Nek 9:17; Yok 2:13; Rom 2:4; Sab 108:4. Kitor EJAKAIT kakonyeneke, kolimo ebe, “EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT, Edeke loileleba aitim loesiana, loiyapi anyunyurikin, loepol aitim ka abeit: 7#Yer 32:18; Dan 9:4; Pes 4:32; 1 Yok 1:9; Yos 24:19; Yob 10:14. Loiŋadat aitim mama ejaas ilukumin, loisioniei numam adolit ka aibucareta ka aronis; lomam itojokit luegwaunito akiro; loidopei numam adolit nuka lukapapa kodwekec, kodwe da luka idwekec, kiton atur nakiwuniet ka nakiwononet.” 8Kisunya Musa, kitulur akou kwap, koma. 9#Sab 94:14; Sek 2:12. Kobala ebe, “Arai kwana adumunit asianut kakonyenekon, wu EJAKAIT, kenainait, kolosenenei EJAKAIT kokidinikosi; naarai eraas ituŋa lueninik imurisiŋa; kitimoi sio numam adolitekosi ka aronisikosi, koyanaarai araun airumetekon.” 10#Ikis 29:12; 2 Sam 7:23; Sab 147:20; Isa 64:3. Kotema atiar, “Koany, etutuki: kakonyen nuka ituŋakon kere eswamai nukaumokin, numam lem iswamatai kakwap kere, karaida kateker adio: eanyunete ituŋa kere luijai ijo mamakec aswam naka EJAKAIT, naarai esogim akirot nakeswamakini.
11 # Ikis 12:28. Koric ijo nukecorak lolo: koany, ereŋeari koinarenikon Emorait, ka Ekanaanit, ka Ekitit, ka Eperisit, ka Ekibit, ka Eyebusit da. 12#Tub 2:2. Kocoite, kinyek aitutuk kaluiboiete kakwap nailosete, kinyek araun atacit kamakus: 13#Ikis 16:21; 2 Ika 18:4. Konye ibilete itemwanakec, imunyumunyete da ipirinokec, iiepeiepete Aseraikec: 14#Isa 57:15; Any 20:5. Naarai mam imaete ecedeke kere: naarai EJAKAIT, loerai ekekiror Ekolo, erai Edeke lokekolo: 15#Esk 6:9; 1 Kor 8:4; 10:27. Kinyek aitutuk kaluiboiete kakwap, kolosete eporo mama ejaas idekesiokec, koinakinete idekesiokec asuban, kinyek idiopet anyarar jo, inyami da asubanitike; 16#Esr 9:2; Mar 25:1,2. Kinyek amanyakin odwekon adwekec, adwekec alosit eporo mama ejaas idekesiokec, einakinete odwekon alosit adoteu mama ejaas idekesiokec.
17 # Any 20:4; Leb 19:4; Ikis 5:8; 27:15. Sirikiswamau idekesio lucaluna.
18 # Any 12:14-20; Leb 23:6-8; Mar 28:16-25. Kidarenenei epucit loka imugatin lumam itubuunitoi. Inyamenenei imugatin lumam itubuunitoi apaarasia akanykaarei, kwape kecorakinia jo, kapak naeseunitai kolap Abib; naarai olap Abib ŋes ilomunia kakwap nako Misiri. 19#Any 13:2; 22:29; Esk 44:30; Luk 2:23. Yenepukori aliliget kere erai yenikaŋ; ka ibarenekon kere lucraas ikiliok, ikiyaik luka imoŋin ka imerekekin. 20#Any 13:13; Ikis 16:16; 2 Sam 24:24. Ka ikiyaik luka asigirian ilakuni kimerekek: arai mam ilakuni ŋes, ibili ekemurisin, Ilakuni ikiyaik kere luka odwekon. Komamei da idio yenetakanuni kakonyeneka komamei ibore.
21 # Any 20:9-10; 23:12; 31:15; 35:2; Leb 23:3; Ikis 5:13-14; Luk 13:14. Iswamaenenei apaarasia akanykape, konye kapaaran nakikanyetekaarei iyenuni: Iyenuni kapak naka akor ka apak naka aisak da. 22#Leb 23:15-21,39-43; Mar 28:26-31; Any 23:16. Iricenenei da epucit loka asabaton, ŋes loka araito nuegeete nuka akima, ka epucit loka atukonokin odula kosane loka ekaru. 23Etakanunete ikiliokokon kere irwan auni nolikaru kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Edeke, Edeke loka Isirael. 24#Leb 18:24; Ikis 7:1; 19:8; Yos 24:8-13; Sab 78:55; 80:8; Age 35:5; 2 Siit 17:10; Awa 16:7. Naarai ereŋeari atekerin kakonyenekon, etalalai da iikorunokon: mam idiotuŋanan ipudakin akwapukon, nailosio atakanun kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke irwan auni nclikaru. 25#Any 12:10; 23:18. Mam iinakini aokot nuka asubanitika kimugatin luitubuunitoi; karaida aideun asubanit naka epucit loka aleleŋio aitodol tupuruc. 26#Ikis 14:21; 26:2,10; Nek 10:35; Awa 3:9. Iyauni nugeet nuka araito nuegeete kalupokon togo loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke. Mam ipoi ikalol kakile nuka totoke.” 27#Ikis 31:9; Isa 30:8. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, “Kiwadik ijo akiro nun: naarai kwape ejaatar akiro nun etutuk kaio ka Isirael.” 28#Ikis 9:9,18; Any 31:18; 32:16; Ikis 10:2,4. Kojai ŋesi nina ka EJAKAIT apaarasia akaisaoŋon paaran ka kuare da; mam anyami atap karaida aimat akipi. Kiwadik ŋesi amor akiro nuka aitutuket, aicorakineta atomon.
29 # 2 Kor 3:7-16; Any 32:15; Mat 17:2. Kosodi kanen, nakijesunor Musa komoru Sinai, itinit Musa amor aarei nuka aitejenunet, nakijesunor ŋesi komoru, mam Musa ajeni ebe emilia amuny naka ereeteke kanuka einerike kaŋes, 30Keanyut Aaron ka idwe luka Isirael kere Musa, koany, emilia amuny naka ereeteke; kokuriakaros aikunyakin ŋes. 31Kiworo Musa kes; kobonut Aaron ka ijaka kere luka ekodet neajai ŋesi; kiner Musa kakes. 32#Any 24:3. Kokau kikunyut idwe luka Isirael kere; kicorak kes nun kere nuabu EJAKAIT kinera kaŋes komoru Sinai. 33#2 Kor 3:13. Kedau Musa einer kakes, kiswapa ekereet, 34Konye nakelomanaria Musa akonyen nuka EJAKAIT einer kaŋes, kolacanari aiswapet, aitodol nakelomununia; kolom, kolimok idwe luka Isirael kere nukicorakinio; 35Koanyut idwe luka Isirael ereet loka Musa, ebe emilia amuny naka ereet loka Musa: kiswapa Musa bobo ereeteke, aitodol naalomaria einer kaŋes.
