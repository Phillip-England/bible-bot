Anyoun 5

5
1 # Any 10:9. Kosodi kokau kopotu Musa ka Aaron, kotemasi Parao atiar, “Inera EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lsirael kwanata ebe, ‘Kisiik ikatuŋa alosit, tetere kepoikinete eoŋ epucit kamodiŋ.’’’ 2#2 Ika 18:35; Yob 21:15; Sab 12:3-5; 2 Siit 32:14; Any 3:19. Kiner Parao ebe, “Iŋai bo EJAKAIT? Tape eoŋ apupuni ekeporoto, kasodi aisiikin Isirael alosit? Mam eoŋ ajeni EJAKAIT, ido bobo mam esiikini lsirael alosit.” 3#Any 3:18. Kotemasi ebe, “Abu Edeke loka lburanian kiriamun kasio: kinainait, kisiik sio kaloto alosit naka apaarasia auni toma amodiŋ, kainakisi asuban mama ejai EJAKAIT Ekosideke; kinyek aidany sio katular, arai kekilen.” 4#Awa 28:15; Any 1:11. Kotema ekabaka loka Misiri kes atiar, “Ŋo Musa, ka Aaron kanukinyo isinyeiniata ituŋa aswamisiokec? koloto neejai abwokonikus.” 5#Any 1:7,9. Kiner Parao ebe, “Koany, epuata kwana ituŋa lukotoma akwap, yes da isiyenut kes kotoma abwokonikec.” 6#Any 1:11. Kapaaran kaŋin abu Parao kicorak iketwasamak ituŋa ka ijakakec da ebe, 7“Mam bobo iinanakinete ituŋa anya nusubet amatapalin kwape lem sek; koloto kesi kotukonokis anya kanukec. 8Etiai da loka amatapalin, lopelem iswamaenenete kesi, ŋopenol ŋes itubokinete; sirikitidisiasi nepetai da: naarai elonwaaka; ŋes einakinit kes aimony ebe, ‘Kisiikinai kaloto kainakisi asuban mama ejai Ekosideke.’ 9Kijaikisi do ituŋa aswam naetiono noi, kitaaritotor; kinyeis aipup akiro nuka aiŋal.”
10 # Awa 29:12. Kolomasi iketwasamak ituŋa ka ijakakec da, kolimokis ituŋa ebe, “Inera Parao kwanata ebe, ‘Mam kainakini yes anya. 11Kolototo yesi ikulepek kotukonokis anya kaneidumakinete kere: naarai mam itidisiario akusiswam.’’’ 12Kanuka ŋun kisiarakinos ituŋa toma akwap kere nako Misiri atukonokin akasirim aitaraun anya. 13Kisinyikokis luketwasamakakec ebe, “Kitodoloto aswamisiokus, ipelueik luka ninipaaran, kwape lem iswamaenenaata ejaas anya.” 14#Age 15:13. Koleata iketwasamak luka Parao ijaka luka idwe luka Isiraelluaseunito kesi, kobalas ebe, “Kanukinyo mam yes itodolitotor akusiswam bian ka lolo da, kotoma aisub amatapalin kwape lem sek?”
15Kosodi kopotu ijaka luka idwe luka Isirael, emonyete mama ejai Parao’ ebe, “Kanukinyo ikonakinia ikonibulesin kwaŋin? 16Mam einanakinio ikonibulesin anya, konye kibalai sio ebe, ‘Kosubata amatapalin’: ido koany, eleio ikonibulesin; kwabo erai aibucaret naka ikonituŋa obe.” 17Konye kotema ebe, “Iloŋwaaka, iloŋwaaka yesi: ŋes ineratar yesi ebe, ‘Kisiikinai kaloto ainakin asuban mama ejai EJAKAIT.’ 18Koloto do aswam; naarai mam kiinakinio anya, konye isubete etiai loka amatapalin lopelem.” 19Koanyut iiaka luka idwe luka Isirael ebe ejaas kesi ocan naalimunenere ebe, “Mam inonooretc amatapalinikus, karaida ipelueikikus luka ninipaaran.’ 20Kiriamunos kesi ka Musa ka Aaron, luabwoete korot, eboŋunete kesi kama ejai Parao. 21#Any 6:9; Age 34:30; 1 Sam 13:4; 2 Sam 10:6; 1 Siit 19:6. Kolimokis kes ebe, “Kitei EJAKAIT yes, kotub da; naarai kisilaata yesi sio kakonyen nuka Parao, kakonyen da nuka ibulesinike, aibwaikin ekileŋ akaninikec aaria sio.”
22 # Mar 11:11; 1 Sam 30:6. Koboŋo Musa mama ejai EJAKAIT, kotema atiar, “EJAKAIT, kanukinyo ikonakinia ituŋa lu erono? Kanukinyo da kiyakuunia eoŋ? 23#Mat 14:31; Bur 10:23. Naarai ageun naabunio eoŋ neejai Parao eincr kokirorikon, ekonaki ŋesi ituŋa lu erono; kwabo mam ibu koyuu ikonituŋa nepetai da.”
