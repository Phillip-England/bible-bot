10 # Leb 1:4; 8:14. Iyauni da emoŋ lotobait kinaren kewema loka airiamunun: idokokinete Aaron ka odweke akaninikec omon lotobait. 11Iari ijo emoŋ lotobait kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kokek loka ewema loka airiamunun. 12#Leb 8:15; Any 27:2; 30:2. Ilemuni aokot nuka emoŋ lotobait ijukonokini da amumwara nuka etem kebokoritikon; ibukokini da aokot kere kwap ketem, 13#Leb 3:3. Iwaruni da asiepai kere naerapit lukotoma, ka asiepai naka emany, ka iŋalur iarei, ka asiepaikec, icwei kes kotem. 14#Leb 4:11,12,21; Bur 13:11. Konye akiriŋ naka emoŋ lotobait, ka emukuleke, ka ikijitike icwei ijo kokina kiidukurun; erai ainakinet naka adiakar.
15 # Leb 8:18; 1:4; Isa 53:6; Gal 4:3-5. Iyanaari da emerekek ediopet; idokokinete Aaron ka odweke akaninikec akou naka emerekek. 16Iari do ijo emerekek, iyanaari aokotoke, ibwatanakini otem. 17Itubutubei emerekek, ilosi lukotomake, ka akeieke, kosodi aibwaikin inerioke ka akouke nepepe. 18#Pes 5:2; Pil 4:18; Age 8:21. Icwei emerekek kere kotem: erai ainakinet naicweio mama ejai EJAKAIT: erai asakat naeuru saa, ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT.
19 # Leb 8:22. Iyanaari da emerekek ece; idokokinete Aaron ka odweke akaninikec akou naka emerekek. 20Kosodi ijo aiar emerekek, iyaŋaari aokot, ijukono kini okobet loka akit nakoteten naka Aaron, okoben da luka aki nukoteten nuka odweke, obokorokec lukapolok luka akaninikec nukoteten, obokorokec lukapolok da luka akeienekec nukoteten, ibwatanakini da aokot otem. 21#Any 30:25; Leb 8:30; Bur 9:22. Ilemuni aokot nuejaas otem ka akinyet nakinyonyoanakina ibwatakini Aaron onapeneke da, odweke da, ka onapenekec da nepepe kaŋes: italaunio ŋes, ka inapeneke, ka odweke, ka inapen luka odweke da.
22Ilemuni da asiepai nuka emerekek ka asonyok ka asiepai naerapit lukotoma ka asiepai naka emany ka iŋalur iarei ka asiepaikec, ka amurwo nakoteten; naarai erai emerekek loka atiakunio: 23#Leb 8:26. Ka emugati ediopet, ka aluŋuret adiopet naka imugatin luinyalakinitai akinyet, ka emugati ediopet loebenibena kodita loka imugatin lumam itubuunitoi loejai akonyen nuka EJAKAIT: 24Ibwaikini kere akanin nuka Aaron ka akanin nuka odweke; ijaŋari kes araun ainakinet naijanio kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 25#Leb 8:28. Ŋaari kes kakaninikec, icwei kes kotem kainakinet naicweio, araun asakat naeuru saa kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: ŋes ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT.
26 # Leb 7:31,34; 8:29; Mar 18:11,18; Sab 99:6. Iyanaari atorob naka emerekek loka atiakunio naka Aaron, iianari araun ainakinet naijanio kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: eraun nakon, 27#Ikis 18:3. Italauni da atorob naka ainakinet naijaŋio, ka amurwo naka ainakinet naedakakina, naijanio, naedakakinitai, nuka emerekek loka atiakunio, kes nuka Aaron ka nuka odweke. 28#Leb 10:15; 7:34. Eraunos nuitebeitai Aaron ka odweke ikar kere kodwe luka Isirael: naarai erai ainakinet naedakakinitai: eraun ainakinet naedakakinitai kodwe luka Isirael naka asuban nuka ainakinetakec nuka ainapakin, ŋes ainakinet naedakakinitai mama ejai EJAKAIT.
29 # Mar 20:26; 18:8; 35:25. Eraunos inapen lukalaunak luka Aaron luenapete odweke luetupanakinete ŋes, ainyonyoanakinio kotomakec, atiakunio da kotomakec. 30#Leb 8:35. Enapenenei okokuke loeraun esasedoti aiboisike apaarasia akanykaarei naelomaria owema loka airiamunun aijaanakin toma aibois nakalaunan.
31 # Leb 8:31. Iyanaari emerekek loka atiakunio, ipoi akirinike kaibois nakalaunan. 32#Mat 12:4.
