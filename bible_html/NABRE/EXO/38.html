Anyoun 38

38
1 # Any 27:1; 2 Siit 29:23,24; Sab 51:15-19; Isa 61:8; Esk 44:11; 45:17; Bur 9:14; 13:10. Abu kiswamau etem loka ainakineta nuieweio loka akito nuka sita: aojauke ikubitin ikany, alalauke ikubitin ikany, loka agoria aŋolol nueriakanara; amukururuke da ikubitin iuni. 2Kiswamak da amumwarake kuju kagoriake aoŋon; koraas amumwara nuka ibore idiopet kaŋes: kiladaa ŋes kaisinit. 3#1 Ika 7:45. Abu kiswamau iboro kere luka etem, atubaake, initiake, atanyake, ka igolunake luka akiriŋ, ka ekialake: eswamaunit iboroke kere kaisinit. 4Abu kiswamak etem eboi loka aisinit, kwap kapama naerimorit ŋes kokwap, kodolite kidin osiepion luka etem. 5Abu kotata igilun iwoŋon luka isaneta iwonon luka eboi loka aisinit, luaraas aiboisio nuka adaketa. 6Abu kiswamau adaketa nuka akito nuka sita, kiladaa kes kaisinit. 7Kitolomanak adaketa ogilun kosiepion luka etem, adakia ŋes; abu kiswamau ŋes kabaoi koorori.
8 # Any 30:18; 2 Ika 16:17; 2 Siit 4:2; Sab 26:6; Sek 13:1; Esk 36:25; 2 Kor 7:1; Bur 10:22; 1 Yok 5:6. Abu kiswamau akalaya naka aisinit, ka aigaŋeteke naka aisinit, naka aroeta nuka aŋor kanuejaanakinete kokek loka ewema loka airiamunun.
9 # Any 27:9; Sab 84:2; 92:13. Abu kiswamau alaro: kowai loka eŋalakimak ari naka eijalakimak igoen luegalitoi luka alaro araas luka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim, ikubitin akwatat: 10Emwaasi ipirinokec akaisaarei, ka aigaŋetakec nuka aisinit akaisaarei; igolun luka ipirin ka aikajetakec araas luka epuesa. 11Kowai loka agolitomei ikubitin akwatat, ipirinokec akaisaarei ka aigaŋetakec akaisaarei, nuka aisinit; igolun luka ipirin ka aikajetakee araas luka epuesa. 12Kowai lokoto ajaas igoen luegalitoi luka ikubitin akaisakany, ipirinokec itomon, ka aigaŋetakec atomon; igolun luka ipirin ka aikaietakec araas luka epuesa. 13Kowai lokokide ari nakokide ikubitin akaisakany. 14Igoen Iucgalitoi luka ewai ediopet loka erute araas ikubitin itomon ka lukany; ipirinokec iuni, ka aigaŋetakec auni; 15Kwaŋin epuai da; kakan na ka akan ya da naka erute loka alaro ajaas igoen luegalitoi luka ikubitin itomon ka lukany; ipirinokec iuni, ka aigaŋetakec auni. 16Igoen luegalitoi kere luerimorito alaro araas luka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim. 17Araas aigaŋeta nuka ipirin nuka aisinit: igoluna luka ipirin ka aikajetakee luka epuesa; ka akwesikec da eladaaritai kepuesa; ipirin kere luka alaro ajaas aikajeta nuka epuesa. 18#2 Siit 3:14. Aigalisit naka erute loka alaro arai aswam naka ekasonyan, lobululu, ka lomuge, ka lokarenan, ka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim: aojauke ikubitin akaisaarei, ka amukururuke ikubitin ikany, koputos kigoen kaluegalitoi alaro. 19Emwaasi ikecepirin iwonon ka aigaŋetakec aoŋon, luka aisinit; igolunakec luka epuesa, ka akwesikec ka aikaietakec eladaaritai kepuesa. 20#Any 27:19; 2 Siit 3:9; Esr 9:8; Isa 22:23; Pes 2:21,22. Iloin kere luka ewema lokalaunan ka lualukunito alaro, araas luka aisinit.
21 # Mar 1:50; 9:15; 18:2; 2 Siit 24:6; Asw 7:44; Mar 4:28,33. Lo ŋes etiai loka iboro luka ewema lokalaunan, ŋes ewema lokalaunan loka aiteienunet, kwape aponi kimarere, kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka Musa, araun aiiaanakin naka Ilebin, kakan naka Itamar, okoku loka Aaron esasedoti. 22#Any 31:2,6. Abu Bcsalel okoku loka Uri, okoku loka Kur, loka ateker naka Yuda kiswamau kere luabu EJAKAIT kicorak Musa. 23Kojai Okoliab, okoku loka Akisamak, loka ateker naka Dan, nepepe kaŋes, loarai ekegiran amor, ekeswaman loepiriana, ekasonyan lobululu ka lomuge ka lokarenan, ka ebaputa loejok.
24 # Any 30:13; Leb 5:15; 27:3,25; Mar 3:47; 18:16. Esabu kere loaponi kitwasamai kanuka aswam kere naka aibois nakalaunan, ŋes esabu loainakinio, arai atalatan #38:24 atalata = isekelin 3,000. akaisaarei ka nukanykaonon, ka isekelin #38:24 esekel ŋes apesa. akwat akanykaarei ka akaisauni, kwape ajaar esekel loka aibois nakalaunan. 25#Any 30:11-16. Epuesa loka nul kaluaponi kimarai kokodet arai atalatan akwatat, ka isekelin elukumit ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisakanykaarei ka lukany, kwape ajaar esekel loka aioois nakalaunau: 26#Mat 17:24; Any 30:13,15; Mar 1:46. Ŋininnjanan ebeka, ŋes atutubet naka esekel, kwape ajaar esekel loka aibois nakalaunan, kanuka ŋinitunanan yenabu kotubor mama ejaas luemarar, ageun kaluka ikar akaisaarei ka luedeparito, kes ituŋa ilukumin akwat akanykape ka ilukumin iuni, ka akwat akany ka akaisakany, 27#Any 26:19-32. Araas atalatan akwatat nuka epuesa nutataunct aigaŋeta nuka aibois nakalaunan, ka aigaŋcta nuka aigalisit; aigaŋeta akwatat nuka atalatan akwatat, atalata kanuka aigaŋet. 28Abu kiswamau igoluna luka ipirin kisekelin elukumit ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisakanykaarei ka lukany, kiladaari da akwesikec, kiswamakina da aikaietakec. 29Aisinit naka ainakinet arai atalatan akaisakanykaarei ka isekelin ilukumin iarei ka akwat aoŋon. 30Kiswamaunia nin aigaŋeta nuka ekek loka ewema loka airiamunun, ka etem loka aisinit, ka eboike loka aisinit, ka iboro kere luka etem. 31#Any 27:19; 2 Siit 3:9; Esr 9:8; Isa 22:23; Pes 2:21,22. Ka aigaŋeta nualukunito alaro, ka aigaŋeta nuka erute loka alaro, ka iloin kere luka ewema lokalaunan, ka iloin kere lualukunito alaro.
