26
1 # Any 25:9; 29:42,43; 40:2,17; Asw 7:44,45; Bur 8:2-5; 9:2-11; Apu 21:3; 1 Kor 12:28; Pes 4:11,12; Bur 1:14. Bobo da iswamauni ewema kaigalisio atomon; nuka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim ka lobululu, ka lomuge, ka lokarenan, nepepe kikerubin aswam naka ekeswaman loepiriana ŋes iswamaunia kes. 2Aojau naka ninigalisit korai ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukanykauni, alalau naka niniigalisit ikubitin iwonon: aigalisio kere koraas nuka aitemis adiopet. 3#1 Kor 12:4-12. Iropikini aigalisio akany; iropikini da aigalisio ace akany. 4#Pes 4:13; Kol 2:2,19. Iswamakini da akodeta nubululu oreret loka aigalisit nakawasia kotoma kanueropikinitai: iswamakini da kwaŋin oreret loka aigalisit nakawasia kotoma kanuce nueropikinitai. 5Iswamakini akodeta akaisakany toma aigalisit adiopet, iswamakini da akodeta akaisakany oreret loka aigalisit nakiareit naeropikinitai; kiteosi da akedeta ijun. 6Iswamauni aruceta akaisakany nuka esabu, imorikikini aigalisio nun karuceta kanun: koraun ediopewema.
7 # Any 36:14. Iswamauni aigalisio nuka aiulo nuka akinei araun ewema kokuiu owema: iswamauni aigalisio atomon ka nadiopet. 8Aojau naka niniigalisit korai ikubitin akaisauni, alalau da naka niniigalisit korai ikubitin iwonon: aigalisio nun atomon ka nadiopet koraas kere nuka aitemis adiopet. 9Iropikini aigalisio akany kowaikec bonik, ka aigalisio akanykape kowaikec, ikudokini aigalisit nakikanyetekape koinaren kewema. 10Iswamauni akodcta akaisakany koreret loka aigalisit nakawasia kanun kanueropikinitai, ka akodeta akaisakany koreret loka aigalisit nakawasia kanuce kanueropikinitai.
11Iswamauni aruceta akaisakany nuka aisinit, ibwaikini aruceta nun toma akodeta, imorikikini ewema nepepe, tetere eraun ediopet. 12Ka atutubet nin naesalakini naekudokina naka aigalisio nuka ewema, atutubet nin naka aigalisit naesalakini ijeko kokau kewema. 13Ekubit ediopet owai ediopet, ka ekubit cdiopet owai loce, loesali kaojau naka aigalisio nuka ewema ijeko kosiepion luka ewema, owai lo kowai je da, arapit. 14#Any 36:19; Mar 24:5. Iswamauni eimukoret loka ewema kimukulen luka imerekekin Iuiterenitai, kokuiuke da eimukoret loka imukulen luka etakas.
15Iswamauni abaoi nuka ewema nuka akito nuka sita, nuesipos. 16Aoiau naka ninibao ikubitin itomon, alalau da naka ninibao ekubit ediopet ka atutubet. 17Ejaun ninibao karucetake aarei, nuerucokinito adiopet mama ejai nace: iswamauni kwaŋin abaoi kere nuka ewema. 18Iswamauni abaoi nuka ewema, abaoi akaisaarei kowai loka eŋalakimak kari naka eŋalakimak. 19#Any 38:27. Iswamauni aigaŋeta akaisaoŋon nuka epuesa kwap kabaoi nun akaisaarei; aigaŋeta aarei kwap kabao adiopet neelomarete arucetake aarei ka aigaŋeta aarei kwap kabao ace neelomarete arucetake aarei, 20Kowai lokiareit loka ewema kari naka agolitomei, abaoi akaisaarei; 21Aigaŋetakec nuka epuesa akaisaoŋon; aigaŋeta aarei kwap kabao adiopet, ka aigaŋeta aarei kwap kabao nace. 22Iswamauni da abaoi akanykape kowai lokokau loka ewema ari nakoto, 23Iswamauni da abaoi aarei nuka agoria nuejaas to kewema. 24Koraas aareiarei kokwap, kwaŋin irotakina abaoi nepcpe anyoun kokwap aitodol kuiu ogilut ediopet: ejaunete kwaŋin kere aarei; eraas nuka agoria nun aarei. 25Ejaunete abaoi akanykauni, ka aigaŋetakec nuka epuesa, aigaŋeta atomon ka nukanykape; aigaŋeta aarei kwap kabao adiopet, ka aigaŋeta aarei kwap kabao nace.
26 # Any 36:31; Rom 15:1; Gal 6:2. Iswamauni ariino nuka akito nuka sita; ariino akany nuka abaoi nukowai ediopet loka ewema. 27Ka ariino akany nuka abaoi nukowai lokiareit loka ewema, ka ariino akany nuka abaoi nukoto kewema, ari nakoto. 28Ka ariit nakokidin naejai kidin kabaoi iriikini anyoun kepuai kiton epuai. 29Iladaari abaoi nun ke sa bu, iswamauni da igilunukec lukitolomanakina ariino kesabu: iladaari ariino kesabu. 30#Any 25:9,40; 27:8; Asw 7:44; Bur 8:5. Isipokini ewema kaputoke naaponi kitcdikin jo komoru.
31 # Leb 16:2; 2 Siit 3:14; Mat 27:51; Mk 15:38; Luk 23:45; Bur 9:3. Ido iswamauni aigalisit kegoe kalobululu, ka lomuge, ka lokareijan, ka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim: icipakinio ikerubin ogoe aswam naka ekeswaman kaloepiriana: 32Ikajakini opirin iwonon luka akito nuka sita luiladaaritai kesabu, eraunos igolunakec luka esabu, kotoma aigaŋeta aoŋon nuka epuesa. 33#Bur 6:19; 9:3-5; Any 25:16; 40:21; Yos 4:11; 1 Sam 4:6; 6:19; 1 Ika 8:6; 1 Siit 15:1; Apu 11:19; Leb 16:2; Mat 24:15. Ikajakini aigalisit kwap kogolun, itolomikin asaduku naka aiteienunet toma aigalisit: etiaki aigalisit kamakus aibois nakalaunan ka aibois nakalaunan noi. 34#Any 25:21; 40:20; Leb 16:2; Bur 9:5. Ibwaikini eicolon-Iokaitim toma asaduku naka aiteicnunet aibois nakalaunan noi. 35#Any 40:22-24; Bur 9:2. Ibwaikini emesa kina kaigalisit, ka epir loka atala kiteos kemesa kowai loka ewema ari naka eŋalakimak: ibwaikini cmesa ari naka agolitomei.
36 # Any 36:37. Ido iswamauni aigalisit naka ekek loka Ewema, lobululu, lomuge, ka lokarenan ka ebaputa Ioecipitai kiusio lueriim, aswam naka ekasonyan.
37 # Any 36:38. Ido iswamakini aigalisit ipirin ikany luka akito nuka sita, iladaari kes kesabu; eraunos igolunakec luka esabu: itataikini kes aigaŋeta akany nuka aisinit.
