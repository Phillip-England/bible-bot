1 # Mar 16:1-3; 17:1-8; Sab 77:20; Bur 5:1-4. Kisukuny ijo Aaron onacekon, ka odweke nepepe kaŋes, kodwe luka Isirael, kejaanak eoŋ kosasedotene, Aaron, ka Nadab ka Abiku, ka Eleasar ka Itamar, odwe luka Aaron, 2#Any 29:5,29; 39:1,2; Leb 8:7,30; Mar 20:26-28. Ido iswamakini onacekon Aaron inapen lukalaunak, kanuka aibuses ka ajokis da. 3#Any 31:6; 35:31-35; 1 Ika 3:12; Isa 28:26; Asw 6:3; 1 Kor 12:7-11; Pes 1:17. Ilimokini kere luejaas itauon luka apirianut, lueteleleb eoŋ kemoyo loka acoa, kiswamata inapen luka Aaron aitalaunia ŋes, kejaanak eoŋ kosasedotene. 4Lu kes inapen luiswamaunete kesi; yenikatorob, ka epod,#28:4 epod ŋes anapito naka esasedoti. ka ekaso, ka ekooti loocerer, ka egoe lokieniena akou, ka aenetait naka aŋabet: iswamakinete Aaron onacekon ka odweke inapen lukalaunak, kejaanak eoŋ kosasedotene.
5Eyaŋaarete esa bu, ka lobululu, ka lomuge, ka Lokareŋan, ka ebaputa loejok, 6#Any 39:2; Leb 8:7. Iswamaunete kesi epod loka esabu, ka lobululu, ka lomuge, ka Lokareŋan, ka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim, aswam naka ekeswaman kalocpiriana. 7Ejaunos lukokeperin iarei luimorikikinitai kosanetakec iarei; tctere imorikikina. 8Aenet da naicipitai kapirianut, naejai tomake aenia, koputos akeswam kaswam naka egoeke; loka esabu, ka lobululu, lomugc, lokarenan, ka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim. 9Ido iyaŋaari amor aarei nuemelomeloto, kigiranak nen ikiroria luka idwe luka Isirael. 10Ikiroriakec ikanykape amoru adiopet, ka ikiroria ikanykape luesalitos amoru nakiareit, kwape eiaar auriokec. 11Aswam naka ekegiran amor, kwape igiria emacar, ŋes igiria ijo amor ŋun aarei kwape ejaatar ikiroria luka idwe luka Isirael: ilukuni kes kapesan nuka esabu. 12#Any 39:7; Sek 6:13; Age 9:12-17; Any 12:14; Yos 4:7; Mk 14:9; Asw 10:4; 1 Kor 11:24,25. Ibwaikini da amor nun aarei okeperin luka epod, araun amor nuka aisiitunet kanuka idwe luka Isirael: edakari Aaron ikiroriakec akonyen nuka EJAKAIT kokeperinike iarei araun aisntunet, 13Iswamauni da apesan nuka esabu: 14Ka irikoi iarei luka esabu kaloelai; iswamauni kwape kaunoi, kaswam kanaeojitoi: ienikini da irikoi lueoiitoi toma apesan.
15 # Any 39:8; Leb 8:8; Isa 59:17; 1 Ses 5:7; Pes 6:14. Ido iswamauni yenikatorob yeniketube, aswam naka ekeswaman kaloepiriana: iswamauni kwape kaswam naka epod; yenika esabu, ka yenibululu, ka yenimuge, ka yenikarenan, ka ebaputa loecipitai kiusio lueriim, iswamauni ŋes kwaŋin. 16Eraun yenika agoria aoŋon nueriakanara, ekudokinitai bala eriagi, aojauke eraun atutubet naka ekubit, alalauke da atutubet naka ekubit, 17#Any 30:10; Mal 3:17. Ido ipiripiranakini amor nekiminaikina, ikokoron iwonon luka amor: ekokoro loka asadia, * ka atopasit, * ka karubunikol * ŋes eraun ckokoro losodit; 18Ekokoro lokiareit loka emeralid, * ka asapiro, * ka diamod*; 19Ekokoro lokiwuniet loka eyasit, * ka aget,* ka ametusit*; 20Ka ekokoro lokiwoŋonet loka aberul, * ka naemelomelot, * ka ayaseper#28:20 Nu kere eraas amor nuegelegela nuka etiai loepol.; elukunio esabu loiminaikinere. 21#Mar 1:5; 2:3; Apu 7:4-8; 21:12. Eraunos amor kwape ejaatar ikiroria luka idwe luka Isiracl, atomon ka nuarei, kwape ejaatar ikiroriakec; kwape igiria emacar, niniruŋanan kwape ejaar ekekiror, eraunos kanuka atekerin atomon ka nuarei. 22Ido ibwaikini toma yenikatorob irikoi kwape kaunoi, luka aswam naeoiitoi naka esabu kaloelai. 23Ibwaikini da igilun iarei luka esabu toma yenikatorob, ibwaikini igilun Ŋul iarei osaŋeta iarei luka yenikatorob. 24Ibwaikini irikoi iarei luka esabu lueojitoi toma ogilun iarei lukosaneta luka yenikatorob. 25Ibwaikini isaneta ice luka irikoi nul luarei lueojitoi toma apesan kwa aarei, kosodi aibwaikin okeperin luka epod, kowaike lokoinaren. 26Iswamauni da igilun iarei luka esabu, ibwaikini toma osaŋeta iarei luka yenikatorob, toma atokitoitike, naejai owai Iokotoma loka epod. 27Iswamauni igilun iarei luka esa bu, kosodi aibwaikin toma atutubeta aarei nuka ikeperin luka epod kwapuke, kowai lokoiŋaren, eiduny kaimorikikinetake, kuju aenet naka epod naicipitai kapirianut. 28Eenikinete igilun luka yenikatorob ka igilun luka epod keusi lobululu, tetere iboie kokuju aenet naka epod naicipitai kapirianut, tetere mam yenikatorob ilaiakin kepod. 29#Eko 8:6; Isa 49:15,16; Bur 9:24. Ido enapenenei Aaron ikiroria luka idwe luka Isirael kotoma yenikatorob yeniketube kotauke, naelomanaria aibois nakalaunan, araun aisiitunet natir kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 30#Mar 27:21; Ikis 33:8; Nek 7:65; Leb 8:8; 1 Sam 28:6; Esr 2:63. Ibwaikini Urim ka Tumim #28:30 Urim ka Tumim Apoloukec Aica ka Nuibccokino. toma yenikatorob ycniketube; ejaununos otau loka Aaron, naelomanaria akonyen nuka EJAKAIT: enapenenei Aaron etube loka idwe luka Isirael kotauke tiritir kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT.
31 # Any 39:22; Leb 8:7; 2 Sam 6:14; Kos 3:4. Iswamauni ekaso loka epod korai kere lobululu. 32Ejaun apedot kotomake neitori akou: ejaun atokitoit p-aka aswam naicipitai airimor apedotoke, kwape kapedot naka ekooti loka acuma, kinyek acilio. 33Toma atweleike iswamakin akamamagan nuka lobululu, ka lomuge, ka Lokareŋan, airimor atwelei kere; ka icirin luka esabu kidinikec airimor: 34Ecirit loka esabu ka akamamaga, ka ecirit loka esabu ka akamamaga, airimor atwelei nuka ekaso. 35Ido enapenenei Aaron aiiaanakinia: iiranario da aruokec naelomanaria aibois nakalaunan akonycn nuka EJAKAIT, naelomununia da, kinyek atwanare.
36 # Any 39:30; Leb 8:9; 1 Siit 16:29; Sab 29:2; 93:5; Sek 14:20; Bur 4:15; 7:26. Iswamauni amekemeket nakesabu kaloelai, kigirak ŋes, kwape igiria emacar ebe, IKALAUNAN YENIKA EJAKAIT. 37Ieni keusi lobululu, ido ejaun ogoe lokieniena akou; ejaenena kiŋaren kegoe lokieniena akou. 38#Leb 10:17; 22:9; Isa 53:11; Yok 1:29; Bur 9:28; 1 Pet 2:24; Leb 1:4; 23:11; Isa 56:7. Ido ejaenena oreet loka Aaron, ibwokenenei Aaron numam adolit nuka iboro lukalaunak, luitalaunete idwe luka Isirael kotoma ainakinetakec kere nukalaunak; ejaenena oreet loka Aaron apaarasia kere, tetere ecamunio kes kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT.
39Icipuni ekooti locerer loka ebaputa kaloejok, iswamauni da kebaputa kaloejok egoe lokieniena akou, iswamauni aenetait naka aŋabet, aswam naka ekasonyan.
40 # Any 39:27; Esk 44:17,18. Ido iswamakini idwe luka Aaron ikootin, iswamakini kes aeneta nuka aŋabes, akopiran da iswamakini kes, kanuka aibuses ka ajokis. 41#Any 29:7; 30:30; Leb 10:7. Itanapi kes Aaron onacekon, ka odweke; inyonyoikini kes akinyet, itiakuni kes, italauni kes, tetere keiaanakinete eoŋ kosasedotene. 42#Leb 6:10; Esk 44:18. Iswamakini kes ipalei luka ebaputa arapakinia awatekec nuejaas titai; egeunete kanabes kiton amurwos: 43#Any 20:26; Leb 20:19,20; Mar 18:22; Leb 17:7. Enapenenei Aaron, ka odweke, naelomanariata toma owema loka airiamunun, arai naikunyakiniata otem aijaanakin kotoma aibois nakalaunan; tetere mam ibwokete numam adolit, kosodete atwakare: eraun ekisil kamake ka aurike naetupanakini ikar kere.
