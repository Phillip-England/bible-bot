Akuudian 4

4
1Amasiki ca ma esabu!
Eloeokin ca ma esabu loalai noi!
Aeakanakin amor nuka aibois nakalaunan
Asisiarin nuka ŋclirot.
2 # 2 Kor 4:7. Itelepai luka etiai loepol luka Sion
Lueriakasi kesabu loililiauna,
Emari ca ma kwape amoti nuka elupe,
Aswam naka akanin nuka ekajean!
3 # Yob 39:14. Ikuwei da einakinete ikisin,
Konakete ludidikikee:
Konye ebwor akoku naka ikatuŋa,
Kwape ikuului luka amodiŋ.
4 # Sab 22:15. Emakakin aŋajep naka imukeru
Odokolike kanuka akure:
Ilipitos idwe ludidik inyamat,
Kwabo emamei yenedutakini kes,
5 # Yob 24:8; Luk 15:16. Aoliosi korotin nul luanyamenenete kosogasi:
Ŋul luapolokinete ogoen lukareijak
Epcrete otutio luka ekurwon.
6 # Age 19:24. Naarai numam adolit nuka akoku naka ikatuŋa
Itelckaritos aronis naka Sodom,
Loketiyakinio katipet,
Emamei akan naaponi kibwaikinai.
7Asegara isotiake adepar akako,
Eliaeanito adepar akile,
Arenak awatekee adepar amor nukarcŋak,
Apwatarakec da esubit bala asapiro.
8 # Sab 102:5. Kwana iriono aputokee aitelekar etulia;
Mam ejenunos korotin:
Eminaikin akeeemunyo kakojokec;
Aono, esubit bala akitoi.
9Eiok lukeari ekileŋ itia lukeari eteŋei;
Naarai adokororata,
Icumitai kanu amamikiniata araito
Nukomanikor.
10 # Aku 2:20; Ikis 28:57; Esk 5:10. Apotu aŋor nuitimitoi kipooto ikeeedwe
Kakaninikee akulepek;
Korautu inyamatakee
Kanuka amudiario naka akoku naka ikatuŋa.
11 # Ikis 32:22. Etodolik EJAKAIT akededen,
Abuku akenyunyura naededeŋ;
Enokoki akim Sion,
Etisisi da apetetake.
12 # Ikis 29:24. Mam apotu ikabakan luka akwap kiyuunito,
Karaida luibojete kotoma akwap kere,
Ebe, epedorcte loitokokoli ka lokasurup
Alomar korutei luka Yerusalem,
13 # Yer 5:31; Mat 23:31. Kanuka aronisio nuka inabinoke,
Ka numam adolit nuka isasedotinike,
Luapotu kobukosi aokot
Nuka ikadolitok kokidiŋike.
14 # Yer 2:34. Eturiaitos korotin bala imudukiok,
Amunaaros kaokot,
Mam cur cpedorete ituŋa
Atiror anapitokee.
15Iworoete ituŋa ebe, “Irwa! Mam ilaete!
Irwa! Irwa! Sirikitirosi!”
Kwaŋin koraut ikasiak ka ipasoitin,
Kobalasi ituŋa kotoma atekerin ebe,
“Mam iboikinos bobo kasio.”
16 # Aku 5:12. Esiarisiar kes anyunyura naka EJAKAIT;
Mam eroko ibwaikinit kes etau:
Mam eyoŋete akonyen nuka isasedotin,
Mam eminasi da imakio.
17 # 2 Ika 24:7; Isa 20:5,6. Apasata akonyencwok amoot aiŋarakinio naatai:
Nakidaria kipou kedara ateker
Namam epedori aitaiar oni,
18 # 2 Ika 25:4; Yer 51:33. Emejaete nekilosi kere,
Mam kipedori alosit korotiniwok,
Eapie awasiawok adolun, Etodolikinos awokopaarasia;
Naarai adolu awasiawok.
19Atiroko lukirukitotor
Aitelekarit imorukaalei luka apak:
Kibwapitos koronan, Kiperunete kamodiŋ.
20 # Age 2:7; Yer 52:9. Eiyeŋane loka ikumesiwok,#4:20 Ŋes atemar the ekabaka. Lokinyonyoikitono akinyet naka
EJAKAIT,
Epok kotoma apanyiakee;
Lokipou keneras ebe,
“Kiboie kotoribeke kotoma atekerin.”
21Kolakar, kolel, ijo akoku naka Edom,
Naiboic kakwap naka Us:
Ikopuni akoli mamakon da;
Imerikin, kosodi amujalalar.
22Etodolikin aiticanet naka numam adolitekon,
Ijo akoku naka Sion;
Mam bobo kiioreorio;
Konye iticani numam adolitekon,
Ŋo akoku naka Edom;
Ilepilepi aronisiokon.
