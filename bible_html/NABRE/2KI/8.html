2 Ikabakan 8

8
1Abu Elisa kolimokinite aberu naetaiarikini ŋesi okokuke ebe, “Konyou, kolot ijo ka lukokalekon, kiboikin neipedoria aiboiororor; naarai anyarau. EJAKAIT ebeli; edauni ebeli ikar ikanykaarei kakwap.” #2 Ika 4:35; Age 41:25-32; Leb 26:19-26; 2 Sam 24:13; 1 Ika 17:1; Sab 105:16; Kag 1:11. 2Konyou aberu kiswama kwape ajaar akirot naka etuŋanan loka Edeke: kolot kalukokaleke, kiboiororoi kakwap naka Ipilisitian ikar ikanykaarei, 3Kosodi kanen ketuboros ikar ikanykaarei kobonu aberu anyoun kakwap naka Ipilisitian: koloma amonyakin ekabaka kanuka eketogo ka alupoke, 4Enisit ekabaka Gekasi ekeiaanakinan loka etuŋanan loka Edeke, kobala ebe, “Keŋainait, kolimokinai akiro nuepolok nuabu Elisa kiswama.” #2 Ika 5:27. 5Kosodi kanen, napelimenokini ŋesi ekabaka kwape etaiarunia ŋol loatwanit, koany, kodoluni aberu naetajarikini ŋesi okokuke, komonyak ekabaka kanuka eketogo ka akelup da. Kotema Gekasi atiar, “Ejakaitika, wu ekabaka, na ŋes aberu, lo da okokuke, loetajaruni Elisa.” #2 Ika 4:35. 6Kinit ekabaka aberu, kolimok ŋes, Koseu ekabaka ekapolokiton ediopet kotema atiar, “Kinyakakis kere luaraas luke, ka araito kere naka alupoke ageun kapaaran naanyounio kakwap, kiton lolo.”
7Kobu Elisa Damasiko; adeka Ben-kadad ekabaka loka Suria; aponi kolimokinai ŋes ebe, “Abu etuŋanan laka, Edeke ne.” 8Kotema ekabaka Kasael atiar, “Koyana ainakinet kakanikon, kolot airiamun ketunanan loka Edeke, kinitunia ŋes EJAKAIT ebe, ‘Aŋaleuni adeka na?’ ” #1 Ika 19:15; 1 Sam 9:7,9; 1 Ika 14:3; 2 Ika 5:5; Yos 9:14; Tub 20:18,23; 2 Ika 1:2; 3:11-13. 9Kolot Kasael airiamun kaŋes, koyana ainakinet, naka ninioore yenejok yeniko Damasiko, naebwokito akaalai akaisaoŋon, kobu kobwo kakonyeneke, kotema atiar, “Okokukon Ben-kadad ekabaka loka Suria ŋes keyakuunit neijai ebala ebe, ‘Aŋaleuni adeka na?’” 10Kotema Elisa ŋes atiar, “Kolot, kolimok ŋes ebe, ‘Abeit iŋaleuni.’ Konye ketodikinite EJAKAIT eoŋ ebe abeit etwani.” 11Kosesen noi aitodol naeleicikinio: komony etuŋanan loka Edeke akiyo. #Age 45:2; Sab 119:136; Yer 9:1,18; Luk 19:41. 12Kotema Kasael atiar, “Inyo bo emonyi ekaiakait?” Koboŋok ebe, “Naarai ajeni aronis naiswamakini ijo idwe luka Isirael: icwei abwasinikee kakim, iari itelepaikec kekilen, ilatanari ludidikikee, iŋerereanari akecenor nuepotiete.” #2 Ika 10:32; 12:17; 13:13; Amo 1:3; 2 Ika 15:16; Kos 13:16; Amo 1:13. 13Kotema Kasael atiar, “Konye ekonikejaanakinan ŋes inyo, loerai ekiŋok cur, aswam akirot ŋin naepol?” Koboŋok Elisa ebe, “Ketodik EJAKAIT eoŋ ebe iraun ijo ekabaka loka Suria.” #1 Sam 17:43; 1 Ika 19:15. 14Kosodi ŋesi anyoun kama ejai Elisa, kobu neejai ejakaitike; loketemari ŋes atiar, “Ebala bo Elisa ai?” Koboŋok ebe, “Kalimoki ebe abeit iŋaleuni.” 15Kosodi kanen kapaaran naatupakini, kolem eimukis, kidomak akipi, korapak akonyeneke, kotwana: kirum Kasael aiboisike.
16Kokaru lokikanyet loka Yoram okoku loka Akab ekabaka loka Isirael, korai Yekosapat ekabaka loka Yuda, kogeu Yekoram okoku loka Yekosapat ekabaka loka Yuda airum. #2 Siit 21:3,4. 17Kerai loka ikar akaisauni ka luarei egeuni airum; kirumak ikar ikanykaunl ko Yerusalem. #2 Siit 21:5. 18Kolosenenei korot loka ikabakan luka Isirael, kwape eswamaenenaa ekek loka Akab; naarai amanyit akoku naka Akab: kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 19Konye mam EJAKAIT akoto amudiar Yuda, kanuka Daudi ekekeiaanakinan, kwape kisumunio ŋes ainakin atala kanuka ikedwe ikar kere. #2 Sam 7:13; 22:51; 1 Ika 11:36; 15:4; 2 Siit 21:7; Sab 34:22; 89:4,29; 132:17.
20Kapaarasiake kipaliik Edom anyoun kakan naka Yuda kodakakis ekabakakee kanukec. #Age 27:40; 2 Ika 3:27; 2 Siit 21:8-10; 1 Ika 22:47. 21Kosodi Yoram adokar Sair, ka agalinike kere kaŋes: konyou kuare, kodany Idomein lualukunito ŋes, ka ikapolok luka agalin: kokera ituŋa owemanakee. 22Kwaŋin kipaliik Edom anyoun kakan naka Yuda, kiton lolo. Kosodi Libuna da aipaliikin kapaarasia kanun. #Age 27:40; Yos 21:13; 2 Siit 21:10. 23Aswamisio ace nuka Yoram, ka nuabu kiswama kere mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Yuda? 24Kojotoor Yoram kalukapapaake, kidaikinai kalukapapaake ore loka Daudi: kirum Akasia okokuke aiboisike. #2 Siit 22:6; 21:17.
25Kokaru lokitomonet kaarei loka Yoram okoku loka Akab ekabaka loka Isirael kogeu Akasia okoku loka Yekoram ekabaka loka Yuda airum. 26Kerai loka ikar akaisaarei ka luarei egeuni airum; kirumak ekaru ediopet ko Yerusalem. Ekiror loka totoke ŋes Atalia akoku naka Omiri ekabaka loka Isirael. #2 Siit 22:2. 27Kolosenenei korot loka ekek loka Akab, kiswama nueroko kakonycn nuka EJAKAIT, kwape eswamaenenaa ekek loka Akab; naarai kerai ekamuran loka ekek loka Akab. #2 Siit 22:3,4. 28Kouparos Akasia ka Yoram okoku loka Akab aitiji Kasael ekabaka loka Suria koRamot-gilead: kimusunata Isurian Yoram. 29Kobono ekabaka Yoram aŋaleun amusuŋun koYesereel nuemusunete Isurian koRama, nakeiiotor kaKasael ekabaka loka Suria. Kolot Akasia okoku loka Yekoram ekabaka loka Yuda aanyun Yoram okoku loka Akab koYesereel, naarai adeka. #2 Ika 9:15,16; 2 Siit 22:6.
