2 Ikabakan 14

14
1Kokaru lokiareit loka Yoas okoku loka Yoakas ekabaka loka Isirael kogeu Amasia okoku loka Yoas ekabaka loka Yuda airum. #2 Ika 13:10; 2 Siit 25:1. 2Kerai loka ikar akaisaarei ka lukany egeuni airum; kirumak ikar akaisaarei ka lukanykaonon koYerusalem: ekiror loka totoke kerai Yekoadin naka Yerusalem. 3Kiswama nuejok kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, konye mam abu koriakas kaDaudi papaake: eswamaenenei kwape ajaatar nueswamai papake Yoas. 4Konye mam aponi kolemarai aiboisio nuiceleleete: korina ituŋa einanakinete asuban, ka aisupuruun lueuru maa kaiboisio nuiceleleete. #2 Ika 12:3; 1 Ika 15:14; 22:43. 5Kosodi kanen, napiticasio ajakanut kakanike, abu koar ikejaanakinakake luaarete ekabaka papake: #2 Ika 12:20. 6Konye mam abu koar idwe luka ikaarak: kwape ajaatar nun nuewadikatai oitabo loka ikisila luka Musa, kwape ecorakinitor EJAKAIT ebe, “Mam lukapapa eario kanuka idwe, karaida idwe mam eario kanuka lukapapakec; konye etwani ninitunanan kanuka aronisike.” #Ikis 24:16; Esk 18:4,20.
7Koarak luka Edom ilukumin itomon Aditot naka Abalaŋ, kirum Sela eiiosi, komaik ekiror Yokuteel, kiton lolo. #2 Siit 25:11; 2 Sam 8:13; Yos 15:38.
8Kosodi Amasia aiiukar ikiyakia mama ejai Yekoas, okoku loka Yekoakas okoku loka Yeku, ekabaka loka Isirael, kobala ebe, “Bia kaanyunos kakonye.” #2 Siit 25:17,18; Awa 17:14. 9Kijuka Yekoas ekabaka loka Isirael mama ejai Amasia ekabaka loka Yuda kobala ebe, “Abu ekenep loajai Lebanon kijukak eres loajai Lebanon ebe, ‘Kokorak akokukon okokuka komany’: kitoro etiaŋ loka ais loajai Lebanon, kicakaeak ekenep. #Tub 9:8; 1 Ika 4:33. 10Abeit idany Edom, iturokina etaukon: kibwonak ŋun, kopaik ore: naarai kanukinyo ilomaria akiro numere nukon ka atwaniar, kibirori ijo ka Yuda da kajo?” #Ikis 8:14; 2 Siit 32:25; Esk 28:2,17; Kbk 2:4; 2 Siit 35:21; Awa 26:17; Luk 14:31,32.
11Konye koner Amasia akiirar. Kolot do Yekoas ekabaka loka Isirael: koanyunos kakonye, ŋesi ka Amasia ekabaka loka Yuda koBet-semet, loarai loka Yuda. #Yos 21:16. 12Kireneai Yuda kakonyen nuka Isirael, koker ninitunanan owemake. 13Kikam Yekoas ekabaka loka Isirael Amasia ekabaka loka Yuda, okoku loka Yekoas, okoku loka Akasia, koBet-semet, kobu Yerusalem, kotiyatiya arwatat naka Yerusalem anyoun korute loka Epuraim kiton orute loka agor, ikubitin akwat aoŋon. #2 Siit 25:23; Nek 8:16; 12:39; Yer 31:38; Sek 14:10. 14Konata esabu ka epuesa kere, ka iboro kere luadumunio kotogo loka EJAKAIT, koboro luka akais nuipu lukotogo loka ekabaka, ka lukititina da, kobono Samaria. #1 Ika 7:51; 2 Ika 24:13.
15Aswamisio ace nuka Yekoas nuabu kiswama, ka akegogoŋ, kwape aiior ka Amasia ekabaka loka Yuda, mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael? #2 Ika 13:12. 16Koiotoor Yekoas kalukapapaake, aponi kidaikinai Samaria nepepe kikabakan luka Isirael; kirum Yeroboam okokuke aiboisike.
17Kobut Amasia okoku loka Yoas ekabaka loka Yuda ikar itomon ka lukany kedau Yekoas okoku loka Yekoakas ekabaka leka Isirael atwanare, #2 Siit 25:25. 18Aswamisio ace nuka Amasia mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nukaikabakan luka Yuda? 19Kinerut asurokin ŋes koYerusalem; koker Lakis; konye kiiukasi atupakin ŋes Lakis, koarakinai nina, #2 Ika 12:20,21; 2 Siit 25:27; Yos 10:31. 20Koyaunai ŋes kobalasin: aponi kidaikinai Yerusalem kalukapapaake ore loka Daudi. 21Koyaata ituŋa kere luka Yuda Asaria, lokerai loka ikar itomon ka lukanykape, kitaraut ŋes ekabaka aibois naka papake Amasia. #2 Ika 15:13; 2 Siit 26:1. 22Koduk ŋesi Elat, kinyakak Yuda, kedau ekabaka aiotoor kalukapapaake. #1 Ika 9:26; 2 Ika 16:6; Ikis 2:8.
23Kokaru lokitomonet kakany loka Amasia okoku loka Yoas ekabaka loka Yuda, kogeu Yeroboam okoku loka Yoas ekabaka loka Isirael airum koSamaria, kirumak ikar akaisaoŋon ka lodiopet. 24Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: mam abu konyou karonisio kere nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, nuetidiakaria Isirael. 25Abu kinyaka eikor loka Isirael ageun kaneelomarere Kamat kiton aŋolol naka Araba, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, naener kama ka ekejaanakinanike Yona okoku loka Amitai, enabi, loarai loka Gat-keper. #Mar 13:21; 34:8; Esk 47:16-18; Amo 6:14; Ikis 3:17; Yok 1:1; Mat 12:39; Yos 19:13. 26Naarai koany EJAKAIT aiticanio naka Isirael, erono noi: naarai amamei yeneenitai karaida yenimam eenirai, karaida ekenarakinan neejai Isirael. #Any 3:7,9; Tub 10:16; 2 Ika 13:4; Ikis 32:36. 27Mam enerit EJAKAIT ebe emwatari ŋesi ekiror loka Isirael kokwap kakui: konye kitaiar kes kakan naka Yeroboam okoku loka Yoas.’ #2 Ika 13:5.
28Aswamisio ace nuka Yeroboam, ka nuabu kiswama kere, ka akegogoŋ, kwape aiior, kwape enyakunia Isirael Damasiko ka Kamat, lukeraas luka Yuda, mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael? #2 Sam 8:6; 1 Ika 11:24. 29Koiotoor Yeroboam kalukapapaake, kes ikabakan luka Isirael; kirum Sekaria okokuke aiboisike. #2 Ika 15:8.
