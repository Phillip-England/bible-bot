2 Ikabakan 7

7
1Kotema Elisa atiar, “Kopupokisi akirot naka EJAKAIT: inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, Moi kwape tinenikwana egwelario aitemis naka akiria nuidieie esekel, * ka aitemisio aarei nuka esairi esekel, korute loka Samaria.’ #Any 8:23; Yos 3:5; Sab 46:1. 2Kosodi ekapolon loagonanakin ekabaka akanike aboŋokin etuŋanan loka Edeke ebe, “Koany, arai kiswama EJAKAIT apedon toma akuj, tikepedoo akirot nin ajaut?” Kotema atiar, “Koany, ianyuni kakonikonye konye mam inyami.” #2 Ika 5:18; Age 7:11; 8:2; Mal 3:10; Ikis 3:27; Bur 3:17.
3Kojaas ituŋa iwonon lukatapon neelomarere korute: kotemanaros atiar, “Kanukinyo kiboio oni kane aitodol nekitwakia? #Leb 13:46; Mar 5:2; Ikis 24:8,9; 2 Ika 5:27; Luk 17:12. 4Arai kiitema atiar, Kalomasi ore, ejai ebeli ore, kitwakikini nen: arai kiiboikini nepene kitwaki. Aso do kaloto, kalunakinos ajore naka Isurian: arai kiitajarata oni, kijari; arai kiiarata, kitwaki do.” #Est 4:16; Luk 15:17-19. 5Konyout nape imuki alosit oidukurun luka Isurian: kedoloto ariri naka iidukurun luka lsurian, koany, kemamei ediotuŋanan. 6Naarai abu EJAKAIT kisiira aiore naka Isurian aruo naka agalin, ka aruo naka ibalasin, aruo naka aiore naepol: kotemanaros atiar, “Koany, ebooleikis ekabaka loka Isirael ikabakan luka Ikitin ka ikabakan luka Imisirian aitiji oni,” #Leb 26:36; 2 Sam 5:24; 2 Ika 3:23; 19:7; Yob 15:21; Esk 10:5; Apu 6:15,16; 1 Ika 10:29. 7Kanuka ŋun konyout kokerata nape imuki, kinyeikis iwemanakec, ka ibalasinikec, ka asigirianakec, kwape ajaatar Iidukurun, kokerata kanuka akeceijar. #Yob 18:11; Sab 48:4-6; 53:5; Awa 28:1; Yer 48:8,9. 8Kedoloto lukatapon nul ariri naka iidukurun, kolomasi owema ediopet, konyama komata, kolemut epuesa ka esabu ka inapen, koloto kibwaata; kobonut, kolomasi owema ece, kolemut kanen da, koloto kibwaata.
9Kosodete atemanar atiar, “Mam kiswamai nuejok: lolo ŋes apaaran naka akiro nuejok, oni da kililini: arai kiidara kiwala akwap, kidolokini oni aitieanio: asa do kopotu, kaloto kalimokis lukokal loka ekabaka.’ #Pil 2:14; 2 Pet 1:19; Est 9:17; Isa 41:27; Nak 1:15; Mar 32:23. 10Kopotu kiworoto ekagolon loka ere: kolimonokis kes ebe, “Kidolo isio oidukurun luka Isurian, ido koany, amamei etuŋanan, karaida eporoto loka ituŋanan, konye ibalasin eenen, ka asigirian eenen, ka iweman da kwape kwaŋin.” 11Konyarau ikagolok; kolimokis lukokal loka ekabaka lukotoma. 12Konyou ekabaka kuare, kotema ikekeiaanakinak atiar, “Kekwenyari yes nukiswamakis Isurian oni. Ejenete kesi ebe kiriebi oni eteŋei; kanuka nun ŋes alomariata koidukurun aiŋop ais, kobalas ebe, ‘Kelomut kore, kanatasi ejarete, kilomari do ore.’’’ 13Koboŋok ediopet kokeiaanakinakake kotema atiar, “Kenainait, koyanaasi icetuŋa ibalasin ikany luedeunos, luesalito ore, (koany, esubito kesi bala ekodet kere loka Isirael loedeun kaŋes; koany, esubito bala ekodet kere loka Isirael loamudiar r) keiukasi, kaanyutu.” 14Kosodete koyanaasi agalin aarei kibalasin; kijuka ekabaka atupar aiore naka Isurian, kobala ebe, “Koloto, koanyut.” 15Kibwapakis kes aitodol Yorodan: ido koany, eleleba erot inapen ka iboro, luacakanarete Isurian iwonyete, Kobonut ikiyakia, kolimokis ekabaka,
16Kolomut ituŋa, konata iidukurun luka Isurian. Kogwelanarai do aitemis naka akiria nuidicic esekel, ka aitemisio aarei nuka esairi esekel, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT, #Mar 23:19; Sab 89:2; 119:89; Isa 40:8; 44:26; Mat 24:35; Mk 13:31; 1 Kor 1:9; 1 Ses 5:24; 1 Pet 1:25. 17Kodakak ekabaka ekapolon loagona* esekel ŋes apesa nakin ekabaka akanike apolokin erute: kicakacakata ituŋa ŋes korute, kotwana kwape abalar etuŋanan loka Edeke, loenera kelot ekabaka mama ejai. #Ikis 32:35-40; 2 Ika 6:32; 2 Siit 36:16; Yob 21:28-30; Sab 31:17,18; Awa 13:13; Isa 5:24; Luk 10:16; 1 Ses 4:8; Bur 10:30,31; Yud 1:14,15. 18Kosodi kanen, kwape abalar etuŋanan loka Edeke ekabaka ebe, “Aitemisio aarei nuka esairi esekel, ka aitemis naka akiria nuidicie esekel, ejaun kwaŋin korute loka Samaria moi kwape tinenikwana.’ #2 Ika 7:1. 19Abu ekapolon ŋol Koboŋok etuŋanan loka Edeke kotema atiar, “Koany, arai kiswama EJAKAJT apedon toma akuj, tikepedoo akirot ŋin ajaut?” Kobala ebe, “Koany, ianyuni kakonikonye, konye mam inyami.” 20Kodolok ŋes kwaŋin; naarai kicakacakata ituŋa ŋes korute, kotwana. #Mar 20:12; Yob 20:23; Yer 17:6; Bur 3:18.
