2 Ikabakan 3

3
1 # 2 Ika 1:17. Kogeu Yekoram okoku loka Akab airum Isirael koSamaria kokaru lokitomonet kakanykauni loka Yekosapat ekabaka loka Yuda, kirumak ikar itomon ka luarei. 2#1 Ika 16:31,32. Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT; konye komam aputokin nuka papake ka totoke: naarai abu kolema epir loka Baal loabu papake kiswama, 3#2 Ika 10:28-31; 1 Ika 12:28-32. Konye kiketakin karonisio nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, nuetidiakaria Isirael; mam abu konyou kakesi.
4 # Age 13:2; Yob 1:3; Isa 16:1. Kerai Mesa ekabaka loka Moab ekeeokon loka amerekekin; kijaanakin eka baka loka Isirael ajulo nuka idwe luka imerekekin ilukumin akwatat, ka nuka imerekekin ilukumin akwatat. 5#2 Ika 1:1. Kosodi kanen ketwana Akab, kipaliik do ekabaka loka Moab ekabaka loka Isirael. 6Koloma ekabaka Yekoram koSamaria kapakio kanun, kimar Isirael kere, 7#1 Ika 22:4. Kolot kiyakia mama ejai Yekosapat ekabaka loka Yuda ebe, “Kepaliiki ekabaka loka Moab; kiupari ijo keoŋ aitiji Moab?” Kotema atiar, “Alosi: arai eoŋ kwape iraar ijo, ikatuŋa kwape ikonituŋa, ka ikabalasin da kwape ikonibalasin.” 8Kotema atiar, “Kitoro orot ali? “Koboŋok ebe, “Orot lokamodin naka Edom.”
9 # Any 11:8. Kosodi kolot ekabaka loka Isirael, ka ekabaka loka Yuda, ka ekabaka loka Edom: kirimosi alosit naka apaarasia akanykaarei: komamikis ajore akip, karaida itian luketupito kes. 10#Sab 78:34; Awa 19:3; Isa 8:21. Kotema ekabaka loka Isirael atiar, “Wu okwe! Naarai anyarau Ejakait ikabakan lu luuni aijaikin kes akan naka Moab.” 11#1 Ika 22:7; Amo 3:7; Sab 55:22; Awa 3:5,6. Konye kotema Yekosapat atiar, “Emamei kereka kane enabi loka EJAKAIT, ainitunia EJAKAIT?” Koboŋok ediopet loka ikejaanakinak luka ekabaka loka Isirael cbe, “Ejai Elisa okoku loka Sapat kane, loalianakini akipi akanin nuka Eliya.” 12#2 Ika 2:25. Kotema Yekosapat atiar, “Ejai akirot naka EJAKAIT kaŋes.” Kolot ekabaka loka Isirael ka Yekosapat ka ekabaka loka Edom mama ejai,
13 # Esk 14:3; Tub 10:14; Rus 1:15; 1 Ika 18:19. Kotema Elisa ekabaka loka Isirael atiar, “Kimariai eoŋ inyo kajo?
Kolot ijo mama ejaas inabin luka papakon, ka inabin luka totokon.” Kotema ekabaka loka Isirael ŋes atiar, “Mam; naarai anyarau EJAKAIT ikabakan lu luuni aijaikin kes akan naka Moab.” 14#1 Ika 17:1; 2 Ika 5:16; Sab 15:4. Kotema Elisa atiar, “Kwape ejaria EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, loabwoi eoŋ kakonyeneke, abeit, arai kemam apodo Yekosapat ekabaka loka Yuda ajaut kane, timam kaanolia jo, timam ketei da. 15#1 Sam 10:5; Esk 1:3; 3:14,22; 8:1. Aso do koinunai ekaraman.” Kosodi kanen, kerama ekaraman, kodoik ŋes apedor naka EJAKAIT. 16#2 Ika 4:3. Kotema atiar, “Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe,, Kilelebata alelesia aditot na.’ 17#Sab 107:35. Naarai inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, ‘Mam ianyunete ekwam karaida akiru, konye ilelebunos akip aditot; imasete yesi da, yes, ka ibarenekus, ka itianikus.’ 18#Yer 32:17,27; Mk 10:27; Luk 1:37; Pes 3:20; 1 Ika 20:28. Konye erai na akirot naedit kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: ijaikini da ŋesi Imoabin akaninikus, 19#1 Sam 15:3. Idanyete luka ŋolire loebwaitai, ka nolire Ioititatai, ijepete nolikitoi loejok, inukete atanin kere nuka akipi, imunaarete kamor ŋinimana naejok.” 20#Any 29:39,40. Kosodi kanen, tupuruc, kapak naka ainakin ainakinet, koany, kopotu akipi elomunito korot loka Edom, kilelebun akwap akipi.
21Kepuputu Imoabin kere ebe apotu ikabakan aitiji kes, kotukonokinos, kere luepedorete ainap lujiis ka adepar, kobwaata koikor. 22Konyoutu tupuruc sek, kocaik akoloŋ akipi, koanyutu Imoabin akipi kajuwaikee erenak bala aokot. 23Kotemasi atiar, “Nu kes aokot; abeit amudiaros ikabakan, adanyakinos, ŋinitunanan aar aupake: aso do, Moab, kanata.” 24Kedoloto oidukurun luka Isirael, konyoutu Isiraelin kodanyata Imoabin, kokerata kakonyenekee: kiyatakinos alosit akwap edanyete Imoabin. 25#Isa 16:7,11. Kotiyatiyata ireria; kocakanakis nolinujanan akemoru ninimana naejok, kilelebun; konukata atanin kere nuka akipi, kojepata ikito kere lukajokak; aitodol needeuniata amor nuko Kir-kareset ban; konye kirimirimoto lukaporoceta, atiyatiya. 26Keanyu ekabaka loka Moab ebe ipikorit ejie ŋes, koyaŋa ituŋa akwat akanykaarei lukedakito ikileŋa kaŋes, acilar mama ejai ekabaka loka Edom: konye mam apotu kopedosi. 27#Amo 2:1; Leb 18:21; 20:2; Ikis 12:31; 18:10; 2 Ika 17:17; 2 Siit 28:3; Sab 106:37,38; Yer 7:31; 32:35; Esk 16:20; 23:37. Kosodi alemun okokuke ekiyai lotikirum aiboisike, koinak ŋes araun ainakinet naicweio karwatat, Kojaun anyunyura naepol mama ejai Isirael: konyout kamaken, kobonosi akwapukee.
