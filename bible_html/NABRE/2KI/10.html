2 Ikabakan 10

10
1Kejaas Akab odweke akaisakanykaarei koSamaria. Kiwadika Yeku ibaluan, kijuka Samaria, mama ejaas ikayaitok luka Yesereel, kes imakio, mama ejaas ŋul da luasaete odwe luka Akab kobala ebe, #Ikis 16:18. 2“Aso napeedoluni ebalua lo mama iiaas, naarai ejaas odwe luka ekapolonikus kayes, ejaas da agalin ka ibalasin kayes, ka ere loebwaitai da, ka luiiis; #2 Ika 5:6. 3Komoata loejok ka loibus kodwe luka ekapolonikus, kodakakis oicoloŋ loka papake, koiiut ekek loka ekapolonikus.’ 4Konye kokuriakaros noi, kotemasi atiar, “Koany, mam apotu ikabakan nul luarei kobwaata kakonyeneke: kikonakini isio kibwoi ai?” #2 Ika 9:24,27; Luk 14:31. 5Apotu ekapolokiton loka ekal, ka ekapolokiton loka ere, ka imakio da, ka nul luasaete idwe, kiyakikis Yeku ebe, “Kirai isio ikejaanakinakakon, kiswamai kere nukieorakini ijo sio; mam kitaraun ituŋanan kere ekabaka: kiswama ijo nuejok kakonyenekon.” #Yos 9:11,24,25; 1 Ika 20:4. 6Kosodi ŋesi airucakin aiwadik ebalua kiner atiar, “Arai ijaas kanukan, arai kiirasi eporotoka, koyaut akwes nuka ituŋa odwe luka ekapolonikus, kopotu neaiai Yesereel moi tinenikwana.” Kejaas odwe luka ekabaka, kes ituŋa akaisakanykaarei, kikapolok luka ere, luasaete kes. #Yob 21:19; Any 20:5; Yos 7:24. 7Kosodi kanen kedol ebalua neeiaas, koyaata odwe luka ekabaka, koarata kes, ituŋa akaisakanykaarei, kibwaikis akwesikec obukito, kijukasi kes neejai ŋesi Yesereel. #1 Ika 21:21. 8Kobu ekiyakia, kolimok ŋes ebe, “Ayaun akwes nuka odwe luka ekabaka.” Kotema atiar, “Kotuk atukon aarei neelomarere korute aitodol moi.” 9Kosodi kanen tupuruc, koloma, kobwo, kotema ituŋa kere atiar, “Iraas yesi luidolito: koany, abu keneru asurokin ekapolonika, ka aiar da: konye iŋai aar lu kere? #2 Ika 9:14,24; Kos 1:4. 10Kojenut do ebe mam edoi kwap adio kakiro nuka EJAKAIT nuenerit EJAKAIT nuka ekek loka Akab: naarai eswama EJAKAIT ŋin naenerit kama ka Eliya ekelaanakinanike,’ #1 Sam 3:19; Sab 33:11; 119:89; Isa 14:27; 40:8; Mat 24:35; 1 Pet 1:25; 1 Ika 21:19,21,29. 11Kwaŋin kodany Yeku luedeunos kere lukokek loka Akab koYesereel, ka iketuŋa kere lukapolok, ka ipaperoke luminan, ka isasedotinike, aitodol namam inyeinia ŋes idio yenapaikin. #1 Ika 18:19.
12Konyou kolot, kobu Samarla, Kosodi ejai orot togo loka ikecokok luka aiŋer ajul. 13Kiriamakinos Yeku kiinacan luka Akasia ekabaka loka Yuda, kotema atiar, “Lukanai bo yesi?” Kobonokis ŋes ebe, “Kirai isio iinacan luka Akasia: kilosi ayogan idwe luka ekabaka ka idwe luka akabaka.” #2 Ika 8:29; 2 Siit 22:8. 14Kotema atiar, “Kikamutu eiarete.” Kikamutu kes eiarete, koarakinai aipany naka etogo loka aiŋer ajul, kes ituŋa akaisaoŋon ka luarei; mam abu kideun ediopet kakesi.
15Kenyou ŋesi kanen, kiriamunos ka Yekonadab okoku loka Rekab ebunit airiamun kaŋes; kiyoga Yeku ŋes, kotema atiar, “Ejokuna ekonitau, kwape eiaar etauka kotaukon?” Koboŋok Yesonadab ebe, “Ejok.” Kotema atiar, “Arai kwaŋin, ae akonikan.” Koinak akekan; kitodoka ŋes agali mama ejai. #Yer 35:6; 1 Siit 2:55; Esr 10:19. 16Kotema atiar, “Kauparos, koany ekakokote kanuka EJAKAIT.” Kisiboik agalike. #1 Ika 19:10; Awa 27:2; Mat 6:2; Rom 10:2. 17Kedolo ŋesi Samaria, kodany kere luedeuna luka Akab koSamatia, aitodol neamudiaria, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT, nakelimokinit Eliya, #2 Ika 9:8; 2 Siit 22:8; Yob 18:5-21; 21:17-30; Sab 34:16; 145:20; Awa 2:22; Mal 4:1; 1 Ika 21:21.
18Kotukonok Yeku ituŋa kere, kotema kes atiar, “Abu Akab kijaanak Baal edit; konye Yeku iiaanakini do ŋes noi. #1 Ika 16:31,32. 19Aso do konyarautu inabin kere luka Baal neaiai, ikamaakake kere, ka isasedotinike; sirikemamuun ediopet da: naarai ejai ainakin Baal asubanit naepol noi; ŋin yenemamuuni, mam eiari.” Konye kiswamai Yeku nun amodunia, tetere emudiari ikamaak Baal. #1 Ika 22:6. 20Kotema Yeku atiar, “Kitalautu atukonokin nakalaunan mama ejai Baal.’ Koramai otuŋa kere. 21Kiyakia Yeku anatar Isirael kere: kikokounos ikamaak. Baal kere, mam abu kopaik ediotuŋanan lomam ebunit. Kopotu togo loka Baal; kilelebun etogo loka Baal pas. #1 Ika 16:32. 22Kotema ŋesi ŋol loapolokinit agola naenapere atiar, “Koyau inapen luka luemaete Baal kere.” Kitolomik kes inapen. 23Kolot Yeku, ka Yekonadab okoku loka Rekab, togo loka Baal; kotema ŋesi ikamaak Baal atiar, “Komoutu, koanyut ebe emamei kane ediopet ka lukejaanakinak luka EJAKAIT, dimarai ikamaak Baal bon.” 24Kolomasi toma ainakin asuban ka ainaklneta nuicweio.
Kibwaikinit Yeku ituŋa akaisakanykauni kina, kotema atiar, “Toma otuŋa luayauni eoŋ akaninikus arai keku ediopet, aijarake etaci aiiar naka ŋol.” #1 Ika 20:39. 25Kosodi kanen, kedau ainakin ainakinet naicweio, kotema Yeku ikedarak ka ikapolok atiar, “Kolomasi, koara kes; sirikeku ediopet da.” Kodanyata kes kakwanis naka ekileŋ; kocakasi ikedarak ka ikapolok kes kina, koloto ore loka etogo loka Baal. 26Kitolomut ipirin luajaas togo loka Baal, kicweata. #1 Ika 14:23. 27Kobilibilata epir loka Baal, kobilibilata etogo loka Baal, kitaraut aipany kiton lolo. #Esr 6:11; Dan 2:5; 3:29.
28Kwaŋin komudia Yeku Baal koIsirael. 29Konye mam Yeku abu konyou karonisio nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat nuetidiakaria Isirael, kes itak luka esabu luajaas Bet-el, ka Dan. #Any 32:4; 1 Ika 12:28,29; Kos 8:5,6. 30Kotema EJAKAIT Yeku atiar, “Naarai iswama ejok itodolit nuejok kakonyeneka, iswamak etogo loka Akab kwape ajaatar nukotauka kere, iboikinos do odwekon luka atur nakiwononet oicoloŋ loka Isirael.” #2 Ika 13:1; 15:8,12. 31Konye mam Yeku abu kopodokin alosenen okisila luka EJAKAIT Edeke laka Isirael keketau kere: mam abu konyou karonisio nuka Yeroboam nuetidiakaria Isirael. #Awa 4:23; Leb 26:14,17; Ikis 28:15; 1 Ika 12:30; 13:34; 14:16; 15:29,30,34; 16:2,3,26.
32Kapaarasia kanun kogeu EJAKAIT ainono Isirael: kodany Kasael kes koikoruno kere luka Isirael; #2 Ika 8:12; 13:22. 33Anyoun koYorodan ari nakokide, akwap kere naka Gilead, Igadin, ka Ireubenin ka Imanasen, anyoun koAroer, loidunyas kajesa naka Arunon, ŋes Gilead ka Basan. #Amo 1:3. 34Aswamisio ace nuka Yeku, ka nuabu kiswama kere, ka agogoŋuke kere, mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael? 35Kojotoor Yeku kalukapapaake: aponi kidaikinai Samaria. Kirum Yekoakas okokuke aiboisike. 36Apak naabu Yeku kirumak Isirael koSamaria keraas ikar akaisaarei ka lukanykauni.
