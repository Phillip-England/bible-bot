2 Ikabakan 17

17
1Kokaru lokitomonet kaarei loka Akas ekabaka loka Yuda kogeu Kosea okoku loka Ela airum Isirael koSamaria, kirumak ikar ikanykaoŋon. #2 Ika 15:30.
2Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, konye mere kwape ikabakan luka Isirael luageikinete ŋes. 3Kobu Salumaneser ekabaka loka Asuria asurokin ŋes; koraun Kosea ekei aanakinanike, koinununei ainakineta, #2 Ika 18:9; 2 Sam 8:2. 4Kodum ekabaka loka Asuria ebotet kaneejai Kosea; naarai abu kijuka ikiyakia mama ejai So ekabaka loko Misiri, mam abu koinak ekabaka loka Asuria ainakinet, kwape eswamaa nolikaru: kanuka ŋun kogolok ekabaka loka Asuria ŋes, koen kaidaris. 5Kosodi ekabaka loka Asuria kobu konata akwap kere, kolot Samaria, kirigau ŋes ikar iuni. #2 Ika 18:9. 6Kokaru lokikanyetekaonon loka Kosea, kirum ekabaka loka Asuria Samaria, kiioreo Isirael ayaŋaar Asuria, kibwaik kes Kala, ka Kabor, kariri kecor loka Gosan, koreria luka Imedin, #2 Ika 18:10; Kos 13:16; Leb 26:32; Ikis 28:36,64; 29:27,28; 1 Siit 5:26.
7Kojai kwaŋin, naarai apotu idwe luka Isirael kodiakasi kama ejai EJAKAIT Ekecedeke, loetolomuni kes koMisiri anyoun kakan naka Parao ekabaka loko Misiri, koyonito idekesio ice. 8Kolosenenete kokisila luka atekerin, nuerenei EJAKAIT kakonyen nuka idwe luka Isirael, ka nul da lukibwaikinete ikabakan luka Isirael. #Leb 18:3; Ikis 18:9; 2 Ika 16:3. 9Kokokoanakinos idwe luka Isirael aswam numam ejok nukasurokin EJAKAIT Ekecedeke, kodukoto aiboisio nuiceleleete koreriakee kere, ageun kotogo loeoj loka ikedarak kiton ore lokebwaitai. #Ikis 13:6; Esk 8:12; 2 Ika 18:8. 10Kosipokis ipirin ka Aserim kanoliukot loeoia, ido kokwap kanolikitoi loepesit. #1 Ika 14:23; Isa 57:5; Any 34:13; Ikis 16:21; Mik 5:14; Ikis 12:2; 2 Ika 16:4. 11Kisupuruununete kaŋina lueuru maa kaiboisio kere nuiceleleete, kwape eswamaenenaata atekerin nuereneari EJAKAIT kakonyenekee: kiswamata nukaronok aitunyunyurikin EJAKAIT, 12Kijaanakis aputosia, nualimokinit EJAKAIT kes ebe, “Sirikiswamata akirot na.” #Any 20:3; Leb 26:1; Ikis 5:7; 4:19. 13Konye kitejenik EJAKAIT Isirael, ka Yuda, kama ka nolinabi, ka ŋolikadwaran, ebe, “Kibelokinos yesi anyoun korotinikus lueroko, koricata aicorakinetaka ka ikakisila, kwape ejaatar ikakisila kere lukeeorakinit lukapapaakus, lukejukakinit yes kama ka ikakejaanakinak inabin.” #1 Sam 9:9; Isa 1:16-20; Yer 18:11; 25:5; 35:15. 14Konye konero akiirar, konye kitininikata imurisiijakec, kwape kimurisina luka lukapapaakec, lumam apotu kiyuut EJAKAIT Ekecedeke. #Ikis 31:27; Awa 29:1. 15Konerenenete ikisilake, ka aitutuketeke naetutukit kalukapapaakee, ka aitejenuneta nueteienunit kes; kotupasi aamameak, koraunos lukaamameak, kotupenenete atekerin nuerimorito kes, nuabu EJAKAIT kieoro kes ebe sirikiswamata kwape kakesi. #Ikis 29:25; 32:21; 1 Ika 16:13; 1 Kor 8:4; Sab 115:8; Yer 2:5; Rom 1:21; Ikis 12:30,31. 16Kojala aicorakineta kere nuka EJAKAIT Ekecedese, kosubut aputosia nuecalara, kes itak iarei, kosubut Asera da, komaenenete ajore kere nakokuiu, kijaanakis Baal, #Any 32:8; 1 Ika 12:28; 14:15; 15:13; 16:33; 18:18; 19:10; 2 Ika 11:18. 17Kitotoroenenete ikeeedwe lusap ka lupesur akim, kiswamata nuka airo ka nuka ecudet, kogwelaros aswam nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, aitunyunyurikin ŋes. #Leb 18:21; 20:2; 2 Ika 16:3; 2 Siit 28:3; Sab 106:37,38; Isa 57:5; Yer 7:31; 32:35; Esk 16:20; 23:37; Ikis 18:10; 2 Ika 21:6; Isa 2:6; Yer 27:9; Mik 5:12; Asw 16:16; Gal 5:20; 1 Ika 21:20; Isa 50:1. 18Kanuka nun konyunyurik noi EJAKAIT Isirael, kolema kakonyeneke; mam abu kideun dimarai areker naka Yuda bon. #1 Ika 11:13,32.
19Yuda da mam abu koric aicorakineta nuka EJAKAIT Ekecedeke, konye kolosenenete kokisila luka Isirael Iukibwaikinete, #Yer 3:8. 20Koŋer EJAKAIT aur kere naka Isirael, kitican kes, kijaik kes akanin nuka ikaŋatak, aitodol nakirenearia kes kakonyeneke. #2 Ika 13:3; 15:29. 21Naarai koeila Isirael alemar kotogo loka Daudi; kodakakis Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat araun ekabaka: kiŋalaa Yeroboam Isirael anyoun katupit EJAKAIT, kitidiaka kes aidiak naepol. #1 Ika 11:11,31; 12:20,28. 22Kolosenenete idwe luka Isirael karonisio kere nuka Yeroboam nueswamai; mam apotu konyoutu kakesi; 23Aitodol naalemaria EJAKAIT Isirael kakonyeneke, kwape enerar kama ka Ikekejaanakinak kere inabin. Koyanaarai eut Isirael Asuria anyoun kakwapuke, kiton lolo. #1 Ika 14:16.
24Koyau ekabaka loka Asuria ituŋa anyoun koBabulon, ka Kuta, ka Aba, ka Kamat, ka Separabaim, kibwaik kes oreria luka Samaria aibois naka idwe luka Isirael: kiruma Samaria kiboikinos oreriake. #Esr 4:2; 2 Ika 18:34. 25Kosodi, egeete aiboi kanen, mam ayoŋito EJAKAIT: kanuka nun kijukak EJAKAIT kes iŋatunyo, luaarete ice kakesi. #Pes 2:12. 26Ŋes kelimokiniata ekabaka loka Asuria ebe, “Atekerin nuiyanaari ijo cut, kibwaik oreria luka Samaria, mam ejenete epone loka Edeke loka akwap: ŋes keinakini ijukakini kes iŋatunyo, ido koany, earete kes, naarai mam ejenete epone loka Edeke loka akwap.” 27Kosodi ekabaka loka Asuria aicorakin atiar, “Koyanaas ediopet kosasedotin lukiyaunete kaŋina; koloto kiboioto kaŋina, kisisianak kes epone loka Edeke loka akwap.” #Tub 17:13. 28Kosodi ediopet kosasedotin lualemario koSamaria kobu kiboikin Bet-el, kisisianak kwape ibusakinitor ayoŋit EJAKAIT.
29Konye kosubununei ŋiniteker idekesiokec, kibwaanakinete otogoi luka aiboisio nuiceleleete nuadukito Isamarian, ŋiniteker oreriakec neiboioto. 30Kosubut ituŋa luka Babulon Sukot-benot, kosubut ituŋa luka Kut Nerigal, kosubut ituŋa luka Kamat Asima. 31Kosubut Ibain Nibas ka Taratak, kicweenenete Iseparabin ikeeedwe kakim neejaas Adaramelek ka Anamelek, idekesio luka Separabaim. #Leb 18:21; Ikis 12:31. 32Koyonenenete EJAKAIT, ido kotiakutu kotomakec isasedotin luka aiboisio nuiceleleete, luainanakinete asuban kanukee kotogoi luka aiboisio nuiceleleete. #1 Ika 12:31. 33Koyoŋenenete EJAKAIT, kiiaanakis da ikecedekesio, kwape kejaar epone loka atekerin nuayanaario, #1 Ika 18:21; Isa 29:13; Kos 10:2; Sep 1:5; Mat 6:24; Luk 16:13. 34lswamaete kiton lolo kwape ajaatar iponesio lukolosck.
Mam eyoŋito EJAKAIT, mam iswamaete kwape keiaar apucunetakec, karaida itubesiakec, karaida ikisila arai aicorakinet naecorakinit EJAKAIT idwe luka Yakob, loamaikinit Isirael; #Age 32:28; 35:10; 1 Ika 11:31. 35Loetutuki EJAKAIT kaŋes, kicorak kes ebe, “Sirikiyoŋata icedekesio, karaida akukonokin kes, karaida aiiaanakin, karaida ainakin kes asuban: #Tub 6:10; Any 20:5. 36Konye EJAKAIT, lokitolomuni yes kakwap nako Misiri. Kagogon naepol, ka akan naarioro, ŋol ŋes loiyoŋenenete, ŋol ŋes loikukonokinos, ŋol da loiinanakinete asuban; #Any 6:6; Ikis 10:20; 12:5-12; Apu 15:4. 37Apucuneta ka itubesia, ka ikisila ka aicorakinet luabu ŋesi kiwadikak yes, iricenenete aswam ikar kere; sirikiyoŋa idekesio ice. #Ikis 5:32. 38Aitutuket naetutuk eoŋ kayes, sirikimurwonokinete; karaida aiyoŋ icedekesio. #Ikis 4:23. 39Konye koyonenenete EJAKAIT Ekusideke; kitaiarenenei ŋesi yes kakan naka lukasurupokus kere.” 40Konye mam apotu kopupokis, konye kiswamata kwape ajaar eponekec lolem sek,
41Koyoŋa atekerin nun EJAKAIT, kijaanakis aputosiakee nuegerer ikecedwe da, ka idwe luka ikecedwe, kwape eswamaenenaata Iukapapaakec, iswamaenenete kwaŋin kiton lolo. #Yos 24:14; Sep 1:5; Luk 16:13; Yok 4:24; Apu 3:15.
