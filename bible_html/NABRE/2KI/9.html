2 Ikabakan 9

9
1Konyarau Elisa enabi ediopet kodwe luka inabin, kotema ŋes atiar, “Koen aŋabetekon, koyana ekube lo loka akinyet kakanikon, kolot Ramot-gilead. #1 Ika 20:35; Yer 1:9,10,17; 2 Ika 4:29; 8:28. 2Kidolo nina komou kaŋina Yeku okoku loka Yekosapat okoku loka Nimisi, koloma, kitonyou ŋes kama ejaas iinacanake, kitoloma agola nakotoma. 3Kosodi alemun ekube loka akinyet, kobukok akonke, kotema atiar, ‘Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, Kenyonyoik jo akinyet araun ekabaka loka Isirael.’ Kosodi konaa ekek, koker, sirikiyap.” #1 Ika 19:16.
4Aso abu ekatumunan, ŋes ekatumunan enabi kolot Ramot-gilead. 5Kedolo, koany, iboiete ikapolok luka aiore: kotema atiar, “Ejaas nukejukunitere mama ijai ijo, lokapolon.” Kotema Yeku atiar, “Mama ejai iŋai kasio kere?” Kotema atiar, “Mama ijai ijo, lokapolon.” 6Konyou ŋesi, koloma togo; kobukok akinyet akouke, kotema ŋes atiar, “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael ebe, Kenyonyoik jo akinyet araun ekabaka loka ituŋa luka EJAKAIT, kes Isirael. #1 Sam 2:7,8; 2 Siit 22:7; Sab 75:6,7; 83:18; Yer 27:5; Dan 2:21; 4:17,25; 5:21. 7Ido idanyi ekek loka Akab ekapolonikon, kenyan aokot nuka ikakejaanakinak inabin, ka aokot nuka ikejaanakinak kere luka EJAKAIT, nuabukoros kakan naka Yesebel. #Sab 58:10,11; 116:15; 1 Ika 18:4; 21:15. 8Naarai eoliori ekek kere loka Akab: amudiari ninikoku yenitelepat yenika Akab, ka ŋol loeenitai ka lomam eenitai kotoma Isirael. #1 Ika 14:10; 21:21; Sab 76:8-12; 110:5; 1 Sam 25:22; Ikis 32:36. 9Ido etuluori ekek loka Akab kwape ekek loka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, kwape da ekek loka Baasa okoku loka Akiya. #1 Ika 14:10; 15:29; 21:22; 16:3. 10Enyamakinete iŋokwo Yesebel amana naka Yesereel, emameun yenidai ŋes.” Konaa ekek, koker. #1 Ika 14:10,11; 21:23; 2 Ika 9:35-37; Yer 15:3; 36:30.
11Kosodi Yeku alomun mama ejaas ikejaanakinak luka eiakaitike: kotema cdiopet atiar, “Ejokuka baca? Anyoika keyaunit etuŋanan ŋol lokikerep mama ijai?” Kobala ŋesi ebe, “Ŋenete yesi etuŋanan, ka akekiro da.” #Yer 29:26; Kos 9:7; Mk 3:21; Yok 10:20; Asw 17:18; 26:24; 1 Kor 4:10; 2 Kor 5:13. 12Kobalas ebe, “Isabi; kolimokinai do.” Kotema atiar, “Ebala kwana kwana ebe, “Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, Kenyonyoik jo akinyet araun ekabaka loka Isirael.’’’ 13Kisunyasi ŋolitunanan alemun ekegoe, kopesikis kwapuke kuju kadoketa, kokuta arupepe, kotemasi atiar, “Yeku ŋes ekabaka,” #Mat 21:7; Mk 11:7,8.
14Kwaŋin kinerut Yeku okoku loka Yekosapat okoku loka Nimisi asurokin Yoram. (Kidari Yoram Ramot-gilead, ŋes ka Isirael kere, kanuka Kasael ekabaka loka Suria. 15Konye kebonunit ekabaka Yoram Yesereel aŋaleun amusuŋun nuemusunito Isurian eiiosi kaKasael ekabaka loka Suria.) Kotema Yeku atiar, “Arai ikotosi kwaŋin kinyek ajaut loesii alomar ore, alosit alimokin koYesereel.” #2 Ika 8:29; 2 Siit 22:6. 16Kodoka Yeku agali, kolot Yesereel; naarai aperi Yoram kaŋina, Abu Akasia ekabaka loka Yuda kolosite aanyun Yoram.
17Kobwoi ekedaran kotogo lokokuju koYesereel, koany erioŋet loka Yeku ebunit, kotema atiar, “Aany erioŋet.” Kotema Yoram atiar, “Koseu ekebwanenon ebalasit, kiyakia airiamakin kakes, kobala ebe, ‘Ejai kereka ainapakin?’ ” 18Kolot ediopet ibwanenit ebalasit airiamun kaŋes, kotema atiar, “Inera ekabaka kwanata ebe, ‘Ejai kereka ainapakin?’ ” Kotema Yeku atiar, “Inyo bo ipodo ijo kainapakin? Komana kauka.” Kolimok ekedaran ebe, “Adolo ekiyakia neejaas kesi, konye mam eboŋunit.” 19Kosodi ŋesi aiyakia lokiareit ibwanenit ebalasit, kodolo neejaas kotema atiar, “Inera ekabaka kwanata ebe, ‘Ejai kereka ainapakin?’ ” Koboŋok Yeku ebe, “Inyo bo ipodo ijo kainapakin? Komana kauka.” 20Kolimok ekedaran ebe, “Adolo ŋol da neeiaas, konye mam eboŋunit: eirene eputos bala eirene loka Yeku okoku loka Nimisi; naarai irenei ŋesi komositoi.”
21Kotema Yoram atiar, “Kitemokisi.” Kitemokisi agalike, Kolom Yoram ekabaka loka Isirael ka Akasia ekabaka loka Yuda, ninitunanan agalike, kolomut airiamun kaYeku, kodumakis ŋes mana naka Nabot Eyesereelit. #2 Ika 8:29; 10:13,14; 2 Siit 22:7. 22Kosodi kanen, keanyu Yoram Yeku, kotema atiar, “Ejai kereka ainapakin, Yeku?” Koboŋok ebe, “Ainapakin anyoin, adoteu naka totokon Yesebel ka icudetake luipu luetia nen?” #1 Ika 14:30; Isa 47:9,11; Nak 3:4; Apu 2:20; 17:1-5; 18:3. 23Kibelok Yoram akegali koker, kotema ŋesi Akasia atiar, “Ŋo ba, Akasia, ejai ebotet.” 24Kiket Yeku akabwake kagogoŋuke kere, kogum Yoram kidin opura, koloma emal otauke, kibirokin toma agalike. 25Kosodi Yeku atemar Bidikar ekapolonike atiar, “Kodaku, kocakak ŋes amana naka alup nuka Nabot Eyesereelit: naarai kiitu ebe nakibwanenenoo eoŋ ka jo katupito Akab papake, kibwaik EJAKAIT abwokon na mamake; #1 Ika 21:29; Yer 23:33-38; Nak 1:1; Mat 11:30. 26Inera EJAKAIT ebe, ‘Abeit aany bian aokot nuka Nabot, ka aokot nuka odweke’; inera EJAKAIT ebe, ‘Atacakini jo kamana kana.’ Aso do kodakaa kocakak amana na, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT.” #1 Ika 21:19; 22:38; Est 7:10.
27Konye keanyu Akasia ekabaka loka Yuda ŋun, kokeria orot lokotogo lokomanikor. Kotupa Yeku ŋes, kotema atiar, “Koarakis ŋes da agalike.” Koarakinai needokarere alosit Gur loidunyas ka Ibileam. Kokeria Megido, kotwanik ŋina. #Tub 1:27; 5:19; 1 Ika 9:15; 2 Siit 22:9; 35:22; Sek 12:11. 28Kodakakis ikekejaanakinak ŋes agali koyanaasi Yerusalem, kidaikinai atesike nepepe kalukapapaake ore loka Daudi.
29Kokaru lokitomonet ka lodiopet loka Yoram okoku loka Akab kogeu Akasia airum Yuda.
30Kedolo Yeku Yesereel, kopup Yesebel; kojuk akonyeke, kisiri akouke, kireik kadirisa. #Yer 4:30. 31Kosodi elomari Yeku korute, kotema atiar, “Ejai kereka ainapakin, ijo Simiri, ekaaran ekapolonikon?” #1 Ika 16:9-20. 32Kitonyou akekonyen adirisa, kotema atiar, “Iŋai ejai owaika? Inair “Kireikis ŋes ibwacin iarei arai Juni. 33Kotema atiar, “Koeakutu kwap.” Kwaŋin koeakunai kwap: kodetanakinos aokotoke arwatat, kobalasin da; kicakaeaka ŋes kakeje. 34Kosodi keloma toma, konyam komat; kotema atiar, “Kidara aberu nin naekenokinitoi, kidaata: naarai erai akoku naka ekabaka.” #Awa 10:7; Isa 65:15; 1 Ika 16:31. 35Koloto aida ŋes: konye mam apotu kodumut kaŋesi dimarai abelekek kakou, ka akeie, ka atitiban nuka akanin. 36Kanuka ŋun kobonuru kolimokis ŋes, Kotema atiar, “Na ŋes akirot naka EJAKAIT, naenera toma okejaanakinanike Eliya Etisibit ebe, ‘Enyamakinete inokwo akuan naka Yesebel mana naka Yesereel: #Nak 1:2; 2 Ika 10:30; 1 Ika 21:23. 37Akuan da naka Yesebel eraun bala aoret kais kamana naka Yesereel; kinyeis einer ebe, Na ŋes Yesebel.’’’ #Sab 83:10; Yer 8:2; 16:4.
