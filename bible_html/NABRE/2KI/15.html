2 Ikabakan 15

15
1Kokar loka akaisaarei ka lukanykaarei loka Yeroboam ekabaka, loka Isirael, kogeu Asaria okoku loka Amasla ekabaka loka Yuda airum, #2 Ika 14:21. 2Kerai loka ikar itomon ka lukanykape egeuni alrum; kirumak ikar akaisakany ka luarei koYerusalem, ekiror loka totoke kerai Yekolia naka Yerusalem. 3Kiswama nuejok kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kwape ajaatar kere nueswamai papake Amasia. 4Konye mam aponi kolemarai aiboisio nuiceleleete: korina itutja einanakinete asuban, ka aisupuruun lueuru maa kaiboisio nuiceleleete. #2 Ika 12:3. 5Kitedek EJAKAIT ekabaka, koraun lokatapon aitodol apaaran naatwania, kiboie kotogo loetiakuna, Kopolokinit Yotam okoku loka ekabaka ekal, aitub kotuna luka akwap. #2 Siit 26:19; Leb 13:46. 6Aswamisio ace nuka Asaria, ka nuabu kiswama kere, mam kereka iwadikatai oltabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Yuda? 7Koiotoor Asaria kalukapapaake; aponi kidaikinai kalukapapaake ore loka Daudi: kirum Yotam okokuke aiboisike. #2 Siit 26:23.
8Kokaru loka akaisauni ka Iukanykauni loka Asaria ekabaka loka Yuda kirumak Sekaria okoku loka Yeroboam Isirael koSamaria ilapio ikanykape. 9Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kwape eswamaata lukapapaake; mam abu konyou karonisio nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, nuetidiakaria Isirael, 10Kinerut Salum okoku loka Yabes asurokin ŋes, kicumak akonyen nuka ituŋa, koar; kirum alboisike. #Kos 1:4; Amo 7:9; Yob 18:5,11-21; 20:4-29; Sab 1:4; 37:37; Awa 2:22; 3:33; Lim 8:12,13. 11Aswamisio ace nuka Sekaria, koany, iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael. 12Ŋin ŋes akirot naka EJAKAIT naalimokini Yeku ebe, “Iboikinos odwekon luka aturi nakiwononet oicoloŋ loka Isirael.” Ido kitodolikinoi kwaŋin. #2 Ika 10:30; Yob 23:13; Sab 33:11; Isa 14:27; 40:8; Mat 24:35; 1 Pet 1:25.
13Kogeu Salum okoku loka Yabes airum kokaru loka akaisauni ka lukanykaonon loka Usia ekabaka loka Yuda; kirumak apak naka elap ediopet koSamaria. #2 Siit 26:1; Mat 1:8. 14Konyou Menakem okoku loka Gadi koTirusa, kobu Samaria, kicumak Salum okoku loka Yabes koSamaria, koar ŋes, kirum aiboisike. #Yos 12:24; 1 Ika 14:17; 15:21,33; Eko 6:4. 15Aswamisio ace nuka Salum, ka ainerunike naabu kiner, koany, iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael. 16Kosodi Menakem aidany Tirusa, ka luajaas toma kere, ka iikorunoke, anyoun koTirusa: naarai mam apotu konaikis ŋes, kanuka ŋun ŋes adanyia kes; konercreana aŋor kere nukotoma nuapotiete. #1 Ika 4:24; 2 Ika 8:12; 2 Siit 36:17; Isa 13:16,18; Esk 9:5,6,10; Kos 13:16.
17Kokaru loka akaisauni ka lukanykaonon loka Asaria ekabaka loka Yuda kogeu Menakem okoku loka Gadi airum Isirael, kirumak ikar itomon koSamaria. 18Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT; mam abu konyou karonisio nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat nuetidiakaria Isirael apaarasiake kere, 19Kobu Pul ekabaka loka Asuria asurokin akwap; koinaki Menakem Pul atalata alukumit nuka epuesa, koiai akekan kaŋes altogogonio ajakanut akanike. #1 Siit 5:26; Isa 9:1; Kos 8:9; 2 Ika 14:5. 20Kitelem Menakem apesan kolsirael, kotuŋa kere lukagogon lukabarak, kitelem ŋolituŋanan isekelin akaisakany luka epuesa, ainakin ekabaka loka Asuria, Kobono ekabaka loka Asurla, mam abu kopaik akwap nin. 21Aswamisio ace nuka Menakem, ka nuabu kiswama kere, mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael? 22Koiotoor Menakem kalukapapaake; kirum Pekakia Ŋkokuke aiboisike.
23Kokaru loka akaisakany loka Asaria ekabaka loka Yuda kogeu Pekakia airum Isirael koSamaria, kirumak ikar iarei. #1 Ika 15:25; 16:8; 22:51; Yob 20:5. 24Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: mam abu konyou karonisio nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, nuetidiakaria Isirael. 25Kineru Peka, okoku loka Remalia, ekapolonike asurokin ŋes, kicumak Samaria, arwatat naegogon naka etogo loka ekabaka, nepepe ka Arugob ka Arie; ido keiaas ituŋa akaisakany luka Igileadin kaŋes: koar ŋes, kirum aiboisike. 26Aswamisio ace nuka Pekakia, ka nuabu kiswama kere, koany, iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael.
27Kokaru loka akaisakany ka luarei loka Asaria ekabaka loka Yuda kogeu Peka okoku loka Remalia airum Isirael koSamaria, kirumak ikar akaisaarei. #2 Siit 28:6; Isa 7:1. 28Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: mam abu konyou karonisio nuka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, nuetidiakaria Isirael,
29Kapaarasia nuka Peka ekabaka loka Isirael kobu Tigilat-pileser ekabaka loka Asuria, kirum Iyon, ka Abel-bet-maaka, ka Yanoa, ka Kedes, ka Kasor, ka Gilead, ka Galilaya, akwap kere naka Naputali; kiioreo kes Asuria. #1 Siit 5:26; Isa 9:1; 1 Ika 15:20. 30Kineru Kosea okoku loka Ela asurokin Peka okoku loka Remalia, kicum ŋes, koar, kirum aiboisike, kokaru loka akaisaarei loka Yotam okoku loka Usia. 31Aswamisio ace nuka Peka, ka nuabu kiswama kere, koany iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Isirael.
32Kokaru lokiareit loka Peka okoku loka Remalia ekabaka loka Isirael kogeu Yotam okoku loka Usia ekabaka loka Yuda airum. #1 Siit 3:12; 2 Siit 27:1; Mat 1:9. 33Kerai loka ikar akaisaarei ka lukany egeuni airum; kirumak ikar itomon ka lukanykape koYerusalem: ekiror loka totoke kerai Yerusa akoku naka Sadok. 34Kiswama nuejok kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: kiswama kwape ajaatar kere nueswamai papake Usia. 35Konye mam aponi kolemarai aiboisio nuiceleleete: korina iruna einanakinete asuban, ka aisupuruun lueuru maa kaiboisio nuiceleleete. Koduk ŋesi erute loikumemei, loka etogo loka EJAKAIT. #2 Ika 15:4; 2 Siit 27:3. 36Aswamisio ace nuka Yotam, ka nuabu kiswama kere, mam kereka iwadikatai oitabo loka aisiitonokin nuka ikabakan luka Yuda? 37Kapaarasia kanun kogeu EJAKAIT aiiukun Resin ekabaka loka Suria ka Peka okoku loka Remalia asurokin Yuda. #2 Ika 10:32; 2 Siit 28:6; 2 Ika 16:5; Isa 7:1. 38Koiotoor Yotam kalukapapaake, aponi kidaikinai kalukapapaake ore loka papaake Daudi: kirum Akas okokuke aiboisike.
