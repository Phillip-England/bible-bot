2 Ikabakan 6

6
1Kotemasi odwe luka inabin Elisa atiar, “Koany, edidin aibois nekiboio isio kakonyenekon. #1 Sam 10:12; 2 Ika 2:3; 4:1,38; 9:1. 2Kinainait, kaloto Yorodan, koiepu ŋolituŋanan atekat, kadukioto ŋina aibois nekiboio.” Koboŋok ŋesi ebe, “Koloto yesi.” 3Kotema ediopet atiar, “Keŋaiŋait, kiyu, kouparos kikonikejaanakinak.” Koboŋok ebe, “Kiupari.” 4Kosodi aupar kakes. Kedoloto Yorodan, koiepa akito, 5Konye napeiepi ediopet atekat, kidotun aep kodo akipi: komony kotema atiar, “‘Wu okwe, ekajakait! Naarai erai ailipuna.” 6Kotema etuŋanan loka Edeke atiar, “Ai bo adoo?” Kitodik ŋes aibois. Koiepu akitoi, kocakak nen, kisilepu asuat. #Any 15:25; 2 Ika 2:21; 4:41. 7Kotema atiar, “Kolem,” Korio akekan, kolem.
8Kosodi kojioto ekabaka loka Suria ka Isirael; kinerut kikekejaanakinak atiar, “Abore kamutan ŋes ejaikin ekaswam.” 9Kiyakik ctunanan loka Edeke ekabaka loka Isirael kinera atiar, “Koeoite kinyek aitor abore kamutan naarai itorete Isurian nina.” #2 Ika 13:14; Sab 25:14; Awa 3:32; Isa 6:13; Amo 3:7; Mat 5:13. 10Kijuka ekabaka loka Isirael toma aibois nin naalimokini etuŋanan loka Edeke ikwenyari; koku kaŋina, mere irwan atipct karaida aarei.
11Kosodi kiyalonon noi ekabaka loka Suria kotauke kanuka akirot kaŋin; konyarau ikekejaanakinak kotema kes atiar, “Mam kiteienunete eoŋ ediopet kaoni loejai kanuka ekabaka loka Isirael?” 12Kotema ediopet kokeiaanakinakake atiar, “Mam ekaiakait, wu ekabaka; konye Elisa enabi Ioejai kolsirael ŋes elimonokini ekabaka loka Isirael akiro nuinera ijo kagolakon.” 13Kotema atiar, “Koloto koanyutu neejai, keiuka koomai.” Kolimokinai ebc, “Koany, ejai Dotan.” #Age 37:17. 14Kosodi kijuka ibalasin ka agalin, ka ajore naepol: kopotu kuare kirigaut ere.
15Kosodi ekeiaanakinan loka etuŋanan loka Edeke nakenyou tupuruc, koloma, koany, aiore ka ibalasin ka agalin irigaunito ere. Kotema ekekejaanakinan ŋes atiar, “Itukwi, ekajakait! Kekwaikis ai?” #Any 24:13. 16Koboijok ebe: “Sirikikurianar: naarai ipu nul lueja kaoni adepar lueiaas kakes.” #2 Sam 22:3,31; 2 Siit 32:7; Sab 3:3-8; 18:2; 91:2; Awa 18:10; 21:31; Isa 43:1; Rom 8:31. 17Kilip Elisa kobala ebe, “Wu EJAKAIT, kenainait, kitwekeny akekonye tetere eanyuni.” Kosodi kitwekeny EJAKAIT akonye nuka etelepat; koany; ido koany, ileleba eronat ibalasin ka agalin nuka akim irimorito Elisa. #2 Ika 2:11; Sab 34:7; 68:17; Sek 1:8; 6:1-7; Bur 1:14. 18Kikokororos kesi neejai ŋesi, kilip Elisa EJAKAIT, kotema atiar, “Kenainait kitumudak akonye nuka ituŋa kalu.” Kitumudak akonyekec kwape ajaar akirot naka Elisa. #Age 19:11; Yob 5:14; Asw 13:9. 19Kotema Elisa kes atiar, “Mere lo ŋes erot, ido mere lo ŋes ere: kotupunai, ketodoli eoŋ yes mama ejai etuŋanan loimoete.” Koyana kes Samaria.
20Kosodi kanen, kedoloto Samaria, kotema Elisa atiar, “EJAKAIT, kitwekeny akonye nuka ituŋa kalu, koanyut.” Kitwekeny EJAKAIT akonyekee, koanyutu; ido koany, kejaas toma Samaria. 21Keanyu kes, kotema ekabaka loka Isirael Elisa atiar, “Ipapa, adanyi kes? Adanyi kes?” #2 Ika 2:12; 8:9. 22Koboŋok ebe, “Mam idanyi kes: ikoto kereka aidany nul luinatu kekonikilen ka akabwakon? Kibwaik atap ka akipi akonyenekee, konyama komata, kobonosi mama ejai eiakaitikec.” #Awa 25:21; Rom 12:20. 23Kipoik kes inyamat luipu: kedautu ainyam ka aimat, kotwara kes, koloto mama ejai eiakaitikec, Mam bobo apotu irioneta luka Suria kopotu akwap naka Isirael. #2 Ika 5:2; 24:2.
24Kosodi kanen, kokau kanun, kotukonok Ben-kadad ekabaka loka Suria aioreke kere, kodoka, kirigau Samaria. 25Koiai ebeli loededeŋ toma Samaria: ido koany, kirigaut, aitodol neagwelariata akou naka asigiria atutubeta akaisakanykauni nuka epuesa, ka atutubet nakiwononet naka aoret naka ajele atutubeta akany nuka epuesa, #Leb 26:26. 26Nakitoritor ekabaka loka Isirael arwatat, komonyak aberu ŋes, kotema atiar, “Kiŋarakinai, ejakaitika, wu ekabaka.” 27Kotema atiar, “Arai mam EJAKAIT kinarakini jo, alemuni eoŋ kai lukenarakinia jo? Opejal arai bo acuet ebino?” 28Kotema ekabaka ŋes atiar, “Anyoika bo?” Koboŋok ebe, “Abu aberu na katema eoŋ atiar, ‘Kopala ikokukon, kanyama ŋes lolo, kinyami yenika da moi.’ #Tub 18:23; Isa 22:1. 29Kipou kepooto ikokuka, kanyama: kasodi eoŋ atemar ŋes kapaaran nakiareit ebe, ‘Kopala ikokukon, kanyama.’ Konye ebwa ŋesi ikokuke.” #Leb 26:29; Ikis 28:53,57. 30Kosodi kanen, kepupu ekabaka akiro nuka aberu, kocil inapeneke; ido etorit ŋesi arwatat; kosesenete ituŋa, ido koany, kenapit epuko kakuanake. #Age 37:29,34; 1 Ika 21:27; 2 Ika 5:7; 18:37. 31Kosodi ŋesi atemar atiar, “Keswamak Edeke eoŋ kwaŋin, ka adepar da, arai esalakini akou naka Elisa okoku loka Sapat mama ejai ŋesi lolo.” #Rus 1:17; 2 Sam 3:9,35; 1 Ika 2:23; 19:2; Asw 23:14.
32Konye kiboie Elisa kotogoke, kibojete imakio da kaŋes; kiyakia ekabaka etuŋanan anyoun kamaken: konye eroko edolo ekiyakia mama ejai, kotema ŋesi imakio atiar, “Ianyutu yesi okoku lo loka ekaaran kwape eyakuunia aiepar akouka? Aso do, naebunio ekiyakia, kogolokisi ekek, kotikokis: mam kituikitui naka akeje nuka eiakaitike ejai kauke?” #Sab 118:6,9; Esk 8:1; 20:1; Mal 3:16; Luk 13:32; 1 Ika 18:4. 33Eroko inera kakes, koany, kodol ekiyakia mama ejai: kotema atiar, “Koany, aronis na erai naka EJAKAIT; kanukinyo bo edaritor EJAKAIT?” #Yob 21:15; Sab 27:14; Awa 30:9; Isa 8:17; Kbk 2:3; Mal 3:14; Apu 16:9.
