2 Ikabakan 19

19
1Kosodi kanen, kepupu ekabaka Kesekia, kocil inapeneke, konap epuko, koloma togo loka EJAKAIT. #Sab 3:4,8; 27:14; Awa 16:3; Isa 37:1; Pil 4:6; 1 Pet 5:6,7. 2Kijuka Eliakim, loapolokinit ekal, ka Sebuna ekewadikan, ka imakio luka isasedotin, konapitos ipukoi, mama ejai Isaia enabi okoku loka Amos. 3Kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Inera Kesekia kwanata ebe, Apaaran na erai apaaran naka iean, naka aitoronio, ka naka amoromoroo: naarai adol idwe aurio, emamei da agogoŋ nakaidoun. 4Motoko iirari EJAKAIT Ekonideke akiro kere nuka Rabasake, loejukuni ekabaka loka Asuria ejakaitike amoromor Edeke lokajaran, ka aitoron akiro nuapupu EJAKAIT Ekonideke: aso kitonyou ailipikon kanuka aatoŋot naedeun.” #2 Sam 16:12; 2 Ika 18:35; Sab 50:21; Yak 5:16.
5Kopotu do ikejaanakinak luka ekabaka Kesekia neejai Isaia, 6Kotema Isaia kes atiar, “Kolimokis ejakaitikus kwanata ebe, ‘Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, Sirikiyoŋ akiro nuipup, nukamoromorooto eoŋ ikejaanakinak luka ekabaka loka Asuria. #Isa 37:6; Any 14:13; 2 Ika 6:16; 2 Siit 32:15,17. 7Koany, ebwaikin emoyo tomake, epupuni ŋesi eemut, eboŋori bukee; ebirokini eoŋ da ŋes kekilen akwapuke.’’’ #Yob 4:9; Sab 11:6; Yer 51:1.
8Kobono do Rabasake, kodum ekabaka loka Asuria ejiosi ka Libuna: naarai abu kopupunite ebe abono ŋesi koLakis. #2 Ika 18:14. 9Kepupu inerai nuka Tiraka eka baka loko Etiopia ebe, “Koany, alomu aitiii jo”: kiyakik bobo ŋesi Kesekia ikiyakia ebe, #1 Sam 23:27; Isa 37:9. 10“Itemarete Kesekia ekabaka loka Yuda kwanata ebe, ‘Kinyek Edekekon loimuno aiŋal jo ebe, Mam Yerusalem ijaikinio akan naka ekabaka loka Asuria. #2 Ika 18:5; 2 Siit 32:15-19; Isa 37:10,14. 11Koany, ipupunit ijo nueswamakis ikabakan luka Asuria akwapin kere, amudianarete kes eut: do konyet ijo kiyuunio? 12Apotu kereka idekesio luka atekerin koyuununete kes, nuamudianarete lukapapaaka, Gosan, ka Karan, ka Resep, ka idwe luka Eden luajaas Telasar? #Age 11:31; Esk 27:23. 13Ai bo ejai ekabaka loka Kamat, ka ekabaka loka Arupad, ka ekabaka loka ere loka Separabaim, loka Kena, ka Iba da?’” #2 Ika 18:34; 17:24.
14Kiiau Kesekia ebalua kakan naka ikiyakia, kosiom; kolot Kesekia togo loka EJAKAIT, kopesia kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. #Isa 37:14. 15Kilip Kesekia kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kobala ebe, “Wu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, loiboie kokerubin, ijo ŋes Edeke, ijo bon, loka ajakanuto kere nuka akwap; ijo kolo isubi akuj ka akwap. #Any 25:22; 1 Sam 4:4; Sab 80:1; 99:1; 1 Ika 18:39; Sab 96:5; Isa 44:6. 16Kitoik akonikit, wu EJAKAIT, kiira; kokweny, wu EJAKAIT, koany; kopup akiro nuka Senakerib, nueiuku ŋesi amoromoroo Edeke lokaiaran. #Sab 31:2; 2 Siit 6:40. 17Abeit, wu EJAKAIT, apotu ikabakan luka Asuria komudiasi atekerin ka akeeekwapin, 18Apotu kocakasi idekesiokee akim: naarai mam keraas idekesio, konye aswam naka akanin nuka ituŋa, ikito ka amor; ŋes kemudiarere ŋes. #Sab 115:4. 19Aso do, wu EJAKAIT Ekosideke, kenainait, kitaiarai sio kakanike, tetere ejenunete ajakanuto kere nuka akwap ebe ijo ŋes EJAKAIT Edeke, ijo bon ba.” #Sab 83:18.
20Kosodi Isaia okoku loka Amos aiiukakin Kesekia ebe, “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael ebe, Naarai ilip eoŋ asurokin Senakerib ekabaka loka Asuria, eira, #Sab 65:2. 21Na ŋes akirot naener EJAKAIT mama ejai ŋesi ebe:
“Kimwaŋit akoku nabekot naka Sion jo,
ekienar jo kisekelei;
emuŋumuŋ akoku naka Yerusalem akouke neijai, #Aku 2:13,15; Yob 16:4; Sab 22:7,8.
22“Iŋai imoromor ijo kisekel?
Iŋai loitopolooritor ijo ekoniporoto,
ka aiteluor akonikonyen kuju?
Isurok Lokalaunan loka Isirael. #Sab 71:22; Isa 5:24; 30:11-15; Yer 51:5.
23Imoromor EJAKAIT kokiyakiakon,
kinera atiar, ‘Kaipues naka agalinika
Adokari oswion luka ironan,
atutubeta nukotoma nuka Lebanon.
Ajepi iresiake lueoiak,
ka iberosinike luejokuka;
Alomari da aiboisike naiwasie,
amagoro naka emanikorike loideneriat. #2 Ika 18:18; Sab 20:7.
24Abok, amat akipi nukimo,
etooni da kakakeju
ieorin kere luko Misiri.’
25Mam ibu kopup kwape eswamaa kolo,
Kasub agear apeŋo?
Kwana da etodolit,
Korai ijo loimudiari ireria lukebwaitai
araun aapoŋor nukamejei, #Sab 33:11; Isa 45:7; 10:5.
26Kanuka nun lueboiete kotoma
keraas luedit agogoŋ,
koburut kitirirata;
kosubito bala inya luka ais, kwape kikui,
kwape kinya luejaas kuju togo,
kwape kakima nueokito eriŋa epolooro. #Sab 129:6.
27“Konye ajeni akoniiboie,
ka alomarikon, ka alomunikon,
aikerepoonutukon kaneaiai, #Sab 139:1.
28Kanuka aikerepoonutukon kaneajai,
ido naarai adolu emokodekon akika,
ŋes ebwaikinia ekagolu okumesikon,
ka arukanaka da aitukukon,
kenyakati jo orot loilomunia. #Yob 41:2; Esk 29:4; 38:4; Amo 4:2.
29“Na ŋes eraun aanyunet neijai: okaru lo inyamete yenebwaŋuni bon, okaru lokiareit yenitoeuni kaii; okaru lokiwuniet koyeata, kodwenya, kiraata imanikoria luka emusabibu, konyama araitoke. #1 Sam 2:34; 2 Ika 20:8,9; Isa 7:11; Luk 2:12. 30Edupi aatoŋot naideuni naabu koku kokek loka Yuda alias kwap, kota araito kokuiu, 31Naarai elomari aatoŋot koYerusalem, luekuete da komoru Sion: itodolikini ŋun adunyanut naka EJAKAIT, #Isa 9:7.
32“Kanuka nun inera kwanata EJAKAIT nuka ekabaka loka Asuria ebe, Mam edoluni ore lo, karaida agumakin emal, karaida adolokin kebuku, karaida aitutu etut asurokinia ŋes. 33Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Orot lokelomunia, nopenol ebonoria, ido mam edoluni ore lo. 34Naarai egaŋi eoŋ ere lo aitaiar, kanukan, ido kanuka ekakejaanakinan Daudi.” #2 Ika 20:6; 1 Ika 11:12,13.
35Kosodi kanen, akuare ŋin; koloma emalaika loka EJAKAIT oidukurun luka Iyasurian, kodany ilukumin akwatat ka akaisakanykauni ka lukany: kenyout ituŋa tupuruc sek, koany, koraas kere ikatwak. #2 Siit 32:21; Isa 37:36. 36Kosodi Senakerib ekabaka loka Asuria anyoun, kolot, kobono, kiboikin Ninebe. #Age 10:11; Yok 1:2; 3:3; Nak 1:1; Sep 2:13. 37Kosodi kanen, emai ŋesi kotogo loka Nisurok ekedeke, koarata Aduram-melek ka Sareser ŋes kekileŋ: kokereasi akwap naka Ararat. Kirum Esar-kadon okokuke aiboisike. #Esr 4:2.
