Marako 10

10
1 # Mat 19:1-9. Konyou kaŋina, kolot oikorun luko Yudaya okekwa Yorodan: kotukokinos bobo ikodeta mamakeŋ; kisisianak kes bobo kwape atapitor. #Mk 9:33. 2Kopotu Iparisayon mamakeŋ, kiŋita ŋes ebe, “Ibus kereka etuŋanan aitwar akeberu?” Kokodut ŋes. 3Koboŋok kotema kes atiar, “Anyoika kicorakinit Musa?” 4#Ikis 24:1-4; Mat 5:31,32. Kotemasi atiar, “Abu Musa kocam aiwadik ebalua loka alacakin, ka aitwar.” 5Konye kotema Yesu kes atiar, “Kanuka aninik naka ikusitauon abu ŋesi kiwadik aicorakinet ŋin kanukus. 6#Age 1:27; 5:2. Konye ameun kageunet naka asubunio, ‘Abu kosub yenikiliokit ka yeniberu.’ 7#Age 2:24. Kanuka akirot kaŋin ŋes inyeinia etuŋanan papake ka totoke, kirotakinos kakeberu; 8eraunete ŋul luarei adiopetekuan: mam bobo eraas iarei dimarai adiopetekuan. 9Kwaŋin luadau Edeke airotakin, kinyek ituŋanan aitiak.”
10Kiŋita bobo ikekesisiak akirot ŋin naajaar togo. 11#Mat 5:32; 1 Kor 7:10-11. Kotema kes atiar, “Ŋolituŋanan loetwari akeberu komany ace, adotear, adiaka kama ejai aberuke. #Luk 16:18. 12Ŋin da arai inyeini okilenike, kodukok ece, adotear.”
13 # Mat 19:13-15; Luk 18:15-17. Koinunai idwe ludidik da tetere etirori kes; kitorona ikekesisiak ŋul lukeyaunete kes. 14Konye nakeanyunia Yesu kokadak noi, kotema kes atiar, “Kocamutu idwe ludidik abunore mama ajai, sirikisimika kes; naarai lueputos kalu ajakanut naka Edeke erai nakee. 15#Mat 18:3. Abeit katemari yes atiar, Ŋin yenimam iyuuni ajakanut naka Edeke kwape ikoku yenididi, mam cut elomari toma.” 16#Mk 9:36. Kotun kes, kidokok akekanin, kisirereŋ da.
17 # Mat 19:16-30; Luk 18:18-30. Napenyouni ŋesi alosit orot, kokeru etuŋanan; kokukokin ŋes, kiŋit ebe, “Ekesisianakinan lokajokan, keswama anyoika tetere erumi aijar naka ikar kere?” 18Kotema Yesu ŋes atiar, “Kanukinyo kinyaraaa eoŋ lokajokan? Emamei yenikajokan dimarai ediopet, ŋes Edeke. 19#Any 20:12-16; Ikis 5:16-20; 24:14. Ijeni aicorakineta ebe, Siriari, Siridote, Sirikokoi, Sirisabanakinenei, Sirirodi, Koyoŋitenenei papakon ka totokon.” 20Kotema ŋes atiar, “Ekesisianakinan, aricit ŋun kere ameun keiditeuka.” 21#Mk 8:34; Mat 10:38. Kosesen Yesu ŋes komina, kotema atiar, “Kiwolik adiopekirot: kolot, kogwela luijaatar kere, kokoranak lukican, ijaunete moolo kabar kotoma akuj: kosodi kobu kotup eoŋ.” 22Konyorididior ereeteke kanuka akirot kaŋin, kolot itururwei: naarai kejaas iboro luipu.
23Koŋoleŋole Yesu, kotema ikekesisiak atiar, “Etiono ca ma lukabarak alomar ajakanut naka Edeke!” 24#Sab 62:10; 1 Tim 6:17. Koumokis ikekesisiak kanuka akiroke, Konye Koboŋok Yesu bobo, kotema kes atiar, “Idwe, etiono ca ma lukabarak alomar ajakanut naka Edeke! 25Epatana ekaal aitor apedot naka ekus, itia ekabaran alomar ajakanut naka Edeke.” 26Koumokis noi jokan, kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Aso iŋai bo do epedori aitajario?” 27#Age 18:14; Yob 42:2. Kosesen kes Yesu kotema atiar, “Mam epedorio kotuŋa, konye mam ikote nen kama ejai Edeke: naarai epedoros kere kama ejai Edeke.” 28Kogeu Petero alimokin ŋes ebe, “Koany, kipou isio kenyeis kere, katuputu jo.” 29Kotema Yesu atiar, “Abeit katemari yes atiar, Emamei ituŋanan yeninyeinit etogo, arai iinacan, arai ainacan, arai toto, arai papa, arai idwe, arai amanat, kanukaŋ, kanuka Akiro Nukajokak da, 30konye edumuni irwan akwatat kwape kwana apak na, itogoi, ka iinacan, ka ainacan, ka nukatoto, ka idwe, ka amanat, nepepe kareio; kosodi moolo toma okar luepote aijar naka ikar kere, 31#Mat 20:16; Luk 13:30. Konye luipu lueraas lusodit eraunete lukawasia; ka lukawasia da eraunete lusodit.”
32 # Mat 20:17-19; Luk 18:31-34. Kejaas orot, edokarete alosit Yerusalem; kiŋarenikinit kes Yesu: koumokis; kes da luketupitos kokuriaka. Koyaŋa bobo lutomon kaluarei, kogeu alimonokin kes akiro nuedoikinete ŋes. #Yok 11:16,55; Mk 9:31. 33Kobala ebe, “Koany, kidokari alosit Yerusalem; einakinio Okoku loka etuŋanan akanin nuka isasedotin lupeti ka ikewadikak; etubokinete ŋes atwanare, ijaikinete ŋes mama ejaas Imo, 34Eboliatar, ka aimuanakin aimul, ka aida, ka aiar da; akaulo kapaarasia auni ejaruni.”
35 # Mat 20:20-28. Kikunyut Yakob ka Yokana, odwe luka Sebedayo mama ejai, kotemasi atiar, “Ekesisianakinan, kikoto kiswamak sio kere nukilipi jo.” 36Kotema kes atiar, “Ikotosi keswamakis inyo?” 37Kotemasi ŋesi atiar, “Kocamakinai sio keboikinos ediopet akanikon nakoteten, ediopet da akanikon nakokediany, kotoma aibusesukon.” 38#Luk 12:50; Mk 14:36; Rom 6:3. Konye kotema Yesu kes atiar,
“Mam ijenete nukilipito. Ipedorete tope aimat akoli naamasi eoŋ? Arai aibatisao kaibatis nakebatisatai eoŋ?” 39#Asw 12:2; Apu 1:9. Kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Kipedori.” Kotema Yesu kes atiar, “Imasete bo akoli naamasi eoŋ: ido ibatisao kaibatis nakebatisatai eoŋ: 40konye akiro nuka aiboikin akanika nakoteten arai nakokediany mere nakaŋ aikor: konye einakinio ŋul luitemokinitoi sek.” 41Kosodete nakepupunioto lutomon kogeut anyunyurikin Yakob ka Yokana. 42#Luk 22:25-26. Konyarau Yesu kes kotema atiar, “Ijenete ebe ŋul lueomitai araut ikayaitok luka Imo, eyaete kes kagogoŋ: ejai ikapolokokec apedor kamakec. 43#Mat 23:11; Mk 9:35; Luk 22:26. Konye mam ikote nen kamakus: konye ituŋanan yenekoto araut yenikapolon kamakus, korau ikusikejaanakinan; 44ŋin da yenekoto araut yenisodit kamakus, korau ibulesit yenika ituŋa kere, 45Naarai Okoku loka etuŋanan da mam abu kobu aijaanakinio, dimarai aijaanakin, ka ainakin da akeijar alakunia luipu.”
46 # Mat 20:29-34; Luk 18:35-43. Kodoloto Yeriko: napelosi anyoun ko Yeriko, nepepe kikekesisiak ka ekodet loapol, abu okoku loka Timayo, Batimayo, emuduki ekelipiton, kiboie kariri kerot, 47Nakiiraria ebe erai ŋol Yesu loko Nasaret, kogeu aiworo, kobala ebe, “Yesu, ijo okoku loka Daudi, kitimoi eoŋ.” 48Kiretakis luipu tetere ililiŋi: konye ŋes kiyatak noi aiworo ebe, “Wu ijo okoku loka Daudi, kitimoi eoŋ.” 49Kobwou Yesu, kotema atiar, “Konyarautu ŋes,” Konyaraut emuduk, kotemasi atiar, “Kotitiŋior: konyou, kinyarit ŋesi jo.” 50Kocaka akenapito, kodetun, kobu mama ejai Yesu. 51Koboŋok Yesu kotema ŋes atiar, “Ikoto keswamak jo inyo?” Kotema emuduk atiar, “Raboni, (Ekesisianakinan,) akoto kakweny.” 52Kotema Yesu ŋes atiar, “Kolot: kitaŋaleu jo akoniiyuun.” Atipet kokweny, kotupa ŋes korot.
