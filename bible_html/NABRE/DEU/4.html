Ikisila 4

4
1 # Esk 20:11; Rom 10:5. “Aso natikwana, ijo Isirael, kopup ikisila ka itubesia, lukesisianakini aiswamaenen; tetere ibusete, ilomarete irumete da akwap nakiinakini EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakus. 2#Apu 22:18-19; Ikis 12:32; Yos 1:7; Awa 30:6; Mat 15:9. Siriyatanakinete akirot nakecorakini yes, karaida aiŋoŋar, tetere iricete aicorakineta nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke nukecorakini yes. 3#Mar 25:1-9. Aanyut akusikonye nukesubi EJAKAIT kanuka Baal-peor: naarai ituŋa kere luatuparito Baal-peor, abu EJAKAIT Ekusideke komudia kokidiŋikus. 4Konye yes luipotu kiketakinos kama kEJAKAIT Ekusideke ejari ŋinidiopet kayesi lolo. 5Koany, kesisianak yes ikisila ka itubesia, kwape kecorakinia EJAKAIT Ekadeke, kiswamaenenete kwaŋin kotoma akwap nailomarete airum. 6#Yob 28:28; Sab 19:7; 111:10; Awa 1:7; 2 Tim 3:15; 1 Ika 10:6,9; Dan 1:20; 4:9; Mal 3:12; Asw 4:13. Aso kocoito aiswamaenen; naarai ŋin ŋes acoakus ka aijenekus kakonyen nuka atekerin nuepupunete ikisila ŋul kere, etemarete ebe, ‘Abeit ateker na nakos eraas ituŋa lukacoa ka lukaijen.’ 7#2 Sam 7:23; Any 25:8; 29:45; Leb 26:12; 1 Ika 6:13; Sab 46:1; 145:18; Isa 55:6; Sek 2:10; Pes 2:17; Yak 4:8. Naarai ateker nakos naanyoin, ejai edeke diekec, kwape ejaar EJAKAIT Edekewok nakiwoneeneneo ŋes? 8Ido ateker nakos naanyoin, ejaas ikisila ka itubesia luebeito kwape ejaatar ikisila ŋul kere, luebwaik eoŋ akonyenekus lolo?
9 # Awa 4:23; 23:19; 3:1,3; 4:21; Age 18:19; Ikis 6:7; 11:19; Sab 78:5,6; Awa 22:6; Pes 6:4. “Konye kocoite, kidari noi ekonitau, kinyeis akiro aimurwokin nuaanyut akonikonye, kinyeis da anyoun kotaukon apaarasia kere nuka akoniijar; konye kitejenanakinenei ikonidwe ka idwe luka ikonidwe. 10#Any 19:9,16; 20:18; Bur 12:18. Apaaran nakolo ibwoia kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke koKoreb, nakatemaria eoŋ EJAKAIT atiar, ‘Kotukonokinai ituŋa, eoŋ da esiirari kes akakiro, kisisiaut ayoŋitenen eoŋ apaarasia kere nuebusioto kakwap, kisisianakinete kesi da ikecedwe.’ 11#Any 19:16-18; Bur 12:18-19. Ipŋulotu yesi da kikunyakis kobwaata kokwap kemoru; kosaran emoru kiton otau loka akuj; ka amuton, ka idouon, ka amuton naijesi cucucuc. 12#Any 20:22; 1 Ika 19:12. Kiner EJAKAIT kayes kokidiŋ akim; ipotu kopuput eporoto loka akiro, konye mam eanyuna idiobore; dimarai eporoto bon. 13#Any 31:18; 34:28; Ikis 9:10. Abu kolimok yes akeitutuket, naabu kicorak yes aiswamaenen, kes aicorakineta atomon; ewadikat toma amor aarei. 14#Any 21:1. Kecorak EJAKAIT kapak kaya aisisianakin yes ikisila ka itubesia, tetere iswamaenenete kakwap naidokarete airum.
15 # Yos 23:11; Awa 4:23,27; Yer 17:21; Mal 2:15; Isa 40:18; Asw 17:24-29. “Aso kocounitos noi; naarai mam ipotu koanyut idiobore kapaaran naenerar EJAKAIT kayes koKoreb kokidiŋ akim: 16#Any 20:4; Leb 26:1; Ikis 5:8; 27:15; Rom 1:23. kinyeis amunaun bonik, aikeun aput naepusit idiobore kere, aput naka yenikiliokit arai yeniberu. 17#Rom 1:23. Aput naka etiaŋ loejai akwap, aput naka akweny kere naejaas abebenoka naeporitoi kapak, 18Aput naka ibore kere yeneliat kokwap, aput naka ibore yenika ecor yenejai toma akipi kwap kalup. 19#Ikis 17:3; Yob 31:26; Yer 8:2; Age 2:1; 2 Ika 17:16; 21:3. Ido kinye aiteluor akonikonye akuj, kiany da akoloŋ, ka elap ka aacer, ŋes ajore kere nakokuju, kijodijodoorete jo aima kes, ka aijaanakin, luabu EJAKAIT Ekonideke kokorak ituŋa kere luejaas kwap kakuj kere. 20#Any 19:5; Ikis 7:6; 14:2; 26:18; Tit 2:14; 1 Pet 2:9. Konye abu EJAKAIT kolem yes kasua nuesaranete, koyaŋau koMisiri, araun kamakeŋ ituŋa luka airumet, kwape ejaar lolo. 21#Mar 20:12. Bobo da abu EJAKAIT kanyunyurik eoŋ kanukus, keyapoik eoŋ kenyek aidok Yorodan, karaida alomar akwap ŋin nakajokan nakiinakini EJAKAIT Ekonideke araun airumet: 22#2 Pet 1:13. Konye kebusakinit eoŋ atwanikin akwap na, mam kebusakinit aidok Yorodan: konye idokarete yesi, kirumata akwap ŋin nakajokan. 23Kocounitos, kinyeis aimurwokin aitutuket naka EJAKAIT Ekusideke, naitutuki kayes, aikeun aput naepusit idiobore kere naabu EJAKAIT Ekonideke kisimik. 24#Bur 12:29; Any 24:17; Ikis 9:3; Isa 33:14; Any 20:5; Ikis 6:15; Isa 42:8; 48:11. Naarai erai EJAKAIT Ekonideke akim naidopori, Edeke loka ekolo.
25 # 2 Ika 17:17. “Naiwurienena idwe, ka idwe luka idwe, kodaunito do yesi apak naepol kakwap, kosodete amunaun bonik, aikeun aput naepusit idiobore kere, ka aswam nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke, aitunyunyurikin ŋes: 26#Ikis 30:18,19; Isa 1:2; Mik 6:2. Anyaraunit akuj ka akwap araun akajenakakus lolo, ebe, itwakete cut atipet kakwap nailosete airum okekwa loka Yorodan; mam akusipaarasia ipuaros, konye imudiaros cut. 27#Ikis 28:36; Leb 26:33; Ikis 28:62,64; Nek 1:8; Esk 12:15; 32:26. Kisiarisiarari EJAKAIT yes otuŋa, ideuni etiaikus edit katekerin, nukireŋeikini EJAKAIT yes. 28#Ikis 28:36,64; Yer 16:13; Esk 20:32,39; Asw 7:42. Ijaanakinete kaŋina idekesio, luesubete ituŋa kakecekanin, akito ka amor, lumam esesenete, karaida aipup, karaida ainyam, karaida aiŋuun. 29#Yer 29:13; 2 Siit 15:4; Nek 1:9; Isa 55:6. Konye arai kimo ijo EJAKAIT Ekonideke kaŋina, idumuni ijo ŋes, arai kimo ijo ŋes kekonitau kere ka akoniijar kere. 30#Age 49:1; Ikis 31:20; Yer 23:20; Kos 3:5; Yer 4:1,2; Yok 2:12. Naijaikinor ocan, nakidolokinioto da akiro ŋun kere kapaarasia nukokau iboŋuni mama ejai EJAKAIT Ekonideke, ipupuni do ekeporoto: 31#Any 34:6; Mar 14:18; 2 Siit 30:9; Nek 9:31; Yob 8:8; Sab 86:5,15; 103:8; 111:4; Yok 4:2. Naarai EJAKAIT Ekonideke erai Edeke loketimon, mam kijalakini, karaida amudiar jo, karaida aimurwokin aitutuket naka lukapapaakon naabu kiyapoik kes.
32 # Yob 8:8; Any 15:11; 1 Sam 2:2; 2 Sam 22:31,32; Isa 44:6,8; 45:5,18; Mat 24:31; Mk 12:29. “Naarai kiŋit nuka apaarasia nuatuboros, nukigeikinete jo, ageun kapaaran naasubakinia Edeke ituŋanan akwap, ka anyoun kosaŋe ediopet loka akuj kiton osaŋe loce, arai ejauna akirot naeputos kakirot na nakos, arai epupunitoi naeputotor? 33Epupunito kereka apeŋo ituŋa eporoto loka Edeke inera kokidiŋ kakim kwape ipupunioto yesi, kosodete aijar? 34Arai abu Edeke kotamak alemun ateker kokidiŋ naka aceteker, kaigimoreta, ka aanyuneta, ka nukaumokin, ka ejie, ka akan nakagogoŋ. ka akan naerioro, ka nusogim nukos, kwape ajaatar kere nuabu EJAKAIT Ekusideke kiswama kanukus koMisiri kakonyenekus? 35#Mk 12:29,32; Ikis 32:39; 1 Sam 2:2; 2 Sam 22:32; Isa 45:5,18. Aponi kitodikin jo tetere ijenuni ebe EJAKAIT ŋes Edeke; emamei ece dimarai ŋes. 36#Any 19:9,19; 20:18,22; 24:16; 1 Siit 29:11; 2 Siit 20:6; Bur 12:18. Abu kisiira jo ekeporoto kakuj, tetere kisisianakini jo: abu da kitaany jo akekim naepol kakwap; kopup akekiro kokidiŋ kakim. 37Kanu naarai abu komina lukapapaakon, abu koseu akeceur naetupakini, kitolom jo koMisiri kakepedor naepol, kojaenena kajo: 38Aireŋear atekerin nuepolok nuegogoŋ da adepar jo kakonyenekon, aitolomar jo, ainakin jo akecekwap airum, kwape ejaar lolo, 39#Yos 2:11; Isa 42:8; Dan 4:35. Aso do kojen lolo, kibwaik otaukon, ebe, EJAKAIT erai Edeke kokuju toma akuj, kwap da kakwap: emamei ece. 40#Leb 22:31; Ikis 5:16; 6:3,18; 12:25,28; 22:7; Pes 6:3. Irici ijo ikekisila ka aicorakinetake, nukecorakini jo lolo, tetere ijaun ijo ejok, ka ikonidwe lukitupakinete jo, tetere ipuaros akonipaarasia kakwap nakiinakini EJAKAIT Ekonideke ikar kere.”
41 # Yos 20:8-9; Mar 35:6. Kosodi Musa atiakar ireria iuni kokekwa loka Yorodan kama eŋiunia akoloŋ; 42Tetere ikaaran ekereari ŋina, yeneari iidunyeteke komam epeleikinit, komam elait ŋes lem sek; tetere ekereari ore ediopet kaŋul kosodi aijar: 43Kes lu, Beser kotoma amodiŋ, kapuuton, kanuka Ireubenin; ka Ramot kotoma Gilead kanuka Igadin; ka Golan kotoma Basan, kanuka Imanasin.
44Lu kes ikisila luabu Musa kibwaik akonyen nuka idwe luka Isirael: 45Nu kes aitejenuneta, ka ikisila, ka itubesia luabu Musa kolimok idwe luka Isirael naalomuniata koMisiri; 46#Ikis 3:29. Okekwa loka Yorodan kaditot naitei Betpeor, kakwap naka Sikon ekabaka loka Imorain, loeboie koKesibon, luadanyi Musa ka idwe luka Isirael naalomuniata koMisiri: 47#Mar 21:35; Ikis 3:3,4. Kirumata akekwap, ka akwap naka Og, ekabaka loka Basan, ikabakan luarei luka Imorain, luajaas okekwa loka Yorodan kama eŋiunia akoloŋ; 48#Ikis 2:36; 3:12; Sab 133:3. Anyoun koAroer, naejai ariri naka aditot naka Arunon, kiton moru Sion (ŋopeŋol ŋes Kerumon). 49Ka Araba kere okekwa loka Yorodan ari nakokide, kiton aŋolol naka Araba, kwap neijululuoro Pisiga.
