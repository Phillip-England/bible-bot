Ikisila 33

33
1 # Age 49:28; Luk 24:50; Yok 14:27; 16:33. Na ŋes arereŋ naabu Musa etuŋanan loka Edeke kilipak Isirael eriŋa ŋes ketwana, 2#Any 19:18; Tub 5:4; Kbk 3:3; Dan 7:10; Asw 7:53; Gal 3:19; Bur 2:2. Kotema atiar,
“Abu EJAKAIT kobu koSinai,
Kocaik kes koSeir;
Koca komoru Paran,
Kolom kolukumin itomon luka ikalaunak:
Kakekan nakoteten ajai ekisil loka akim mamakec.
3 # Kos 11:1; Mal 1:2; 1 Sam 2:9; Sab 50:5; Luk 10:39; Asw 22:3. Eebo, emin ituŋa;
Ejaas ikalaunakake kere akonikan:
Apotu kiboikinos akejekon;
Jjaikinio ŋinituŋanan akonikiro.
4 # Yok 7:19. Abu Musa kicorak oni ekisil,
Airumet naka ekodet loka Yakob.
5 # Yob 29:25. Arai ŋesi ekabaka koYesurun,
Naatukonokinere ikapolok luka ituŋa,
Atekerin kere nuka Isirael.
6“Kojar Reuben, komam etwani;
Konye kikidiokoto iketuŋa.”
7 # Age 49:8. Na ŋes arereŋ naka Yuda: kotema atiar,
“EJAKAIT, kopup eporoto loka Yuda,
Kitoloma ŋes neejaas iketuŋa:
Abu koji kakekanin kanukeŋ;
Iŋarakini ijo ŋes aitiji lukasurupoke.”
8 # Any 17:7; 28:30; Mar 20:13; 27:21; Leb 8:8. Naka Lebi da kotema atiar,
“Tumimikon ka Urimikon ejaas ka etuŋananikon loka Edeke, Loibu kigimo koMasa,
Loibu kitepeg kakipi nuka Meriba;
9 # Ikis 13:6; Mat 10:37; 12:48; Mk 10:29; Luk 14:26; Any 32:26; Yer 18:18; Mal 2:5. Loabala nuka papake ka nuka totoke ebe,
‘Mam aanyunit ŋes”:
Mam ŋes abu kolimo ikeinacan,
Karaida ajenun ikedwe:
Naarai aricito akonikirot,
Kidarete aitutuketekon.
10 # Leb 10:11; Any 30:7,8; Sab 51:19. Isisianakinete Yakob itubesiakon,
Ka Isirael da ekisilikon:
Ibwaikinete lukisupurununo akonyenekon,
Ka ainakinet kere naicweio kotemukon.
11 # Sab 20:3. EJAKAIT, kisirereŋ iboroke,
Kocam aswam naka akaninike:
Kicum aipelor aŋabes nuka ŋul kaluenyoikinete ŋes,
Nuka ŋul da luelaito ŋes,
Tetere mam bobo enyounete.”
12Naka Benyamin kotema atiar,
“Iboikin yeniminat yenika EJAKAIT kainapakin eiduny kaŋes; Imukorit ŋes apaaran kere,
Iboienenei da kokidiŋ kikeperinike.”
13 # Age 49:25; 27:28. Naka Yosep kotema atiar,
“Kisirereŋ EJAKAIT akekwap;
Koboro luketiai loepol luka akui, kakopyo,
Kaidules naidipidipakina kwap,
14Koboro luketiai loepol luka araito nuka akoloŋ,
Koboro luketiai loepol luka apol naka ilapio,
15 # Kbk 3:6. Koboro luepolok luka iroŋan lukolo sek,
Koboro luketiai loepol luka iukon lumam enaŋete,
16 # Any 3:2,4; Asw 7:30. Koboro luketiai loepol luka alup ka ailelebakec,
Kacamunio naka ŋol kaloiboie kokitwi;
Kobu arereŋ akou naka Yosep,
Kosiaduk loka ŋol kaloatiakario koinacanake,
17 # 1 Ika 22:11; Sab 44:5. Ekiyai loka emonoke, erai apolou nakeŋ;
Amumwarake eraas amumwara nuka ekosobwan:
Kes eremoria ŋesi ituŋa, kes kere, kiton osaŋe loka akwap:
Eraas kesi ilukumin itomon luka Epuraim,
Eraas da ilukumin luka Manase.”
18Naka Sebulun kotema atiar,
“Sebulun, kolakar kotoma alomarikon;
Isakar da kotoma owemaikon.
19 # Isa 2:3; Yer 50:4,5; Sab 4:5. Enyararete ituŋa oroŋat;
Einakinete kaŋina asuban nuka adolit:
Naarai ijujuete luidilas kaŋolol,
Ka luketiai loepol luibwaitai kasiŋe.”
20 # Yos 13:10; 1 Siit 12:8. Naka Gad kotema atiar,
“Isirereŋitoi ŋol loitalalai Gad:
Iboie bala aŋatuny,
Epoopoi kakan, eebo esiaduk loka akou.
21 # Mar 32:16,21; Yos 4:12. Abu kokorakin atutubet nasodit kanukeŋ,
Naarai eŋadakinitai ewai loka lokesilan
Kobu nepepe kalukapolok luka ituŋa,
Kitodol abeit naka EJAKAIT,
Ka itubesia koIsirael.”
22 # Yos 19:47; Tub 18:27. Naka Dan kotema atiar,
“Erai Dan ikoku yenika eŋatuny,
Yenesomun koBasan.”
23 # Yos 19:32. Naka Naputali kotema atiar,
“Wu Naputali, loimono asianut,
Loileleba arereŋ naka EJAKAIT:
Irumi ijo ari nakoto ka eŋalakimak.”
24 # Yob 29:6. Naka Aser kotema atiar,
“Isirereŋitoi Aser kidwe; Kocamunai koinacanake,
Kidomak ŋesi akekeju akinyet.
25Eraunos agolitokon asuat ka aisinit;
Kwape kapaarasiakon, kwape kwaŋin ejaunor agogoŋukon.”
26 # Any 15:11; Sab 86:8; Yer 10:6; Sab 68:4,33,34; 104:3; Kbk 3:8. “Emamei yeneputos ka Edeke, wu Yesurun,
Loibwaneni akuj aiŋarakin jo,
Kajokisike kakuj.
27 # Sab 90:1. Edeke lomam enaŋi ŋes amecisikon,
Kigagat kakanin numam enaŋete:
Ereŋea lukasurupo kakonyenekon,
Kobala ebe, ‘Komudia.’
28 # Mar 23:9; Yer 23:6; 33:16. Iboie Isirael kainapakin,
Atan naka Yakob ejai bon,
Kakwap naka akima ka ebino;
Eebo etepi akekuj akopyo.
29 # Sab 144:15; 2 Sam 7:23. Kisirereŋitoi jo, wu Isirael:
Iŋai loeputos kajo, ituŋa luitajarit EJAKAIT,
Ebuku loka aiŋarakiniokon,
Ŋol ŋes ekileŋ loka ajokisikon!
Kinonokikinete jo lukasurupokon;
Icakacakai ijo aiboisiokec nuiceleleete.”
