Ikisila 32

32
1 # Sab 50:4; Isa 1:2; Yer 2:12; 6:19. “Kitoik akit, ijo akuj, kener;
Akwap da kopup akiro nuka aitukuka:
2 # 2 Sam 23:4; Yob 29:22,23; Isa 55:10; Kos 6:4; 14:5; 1 Kor 3:6,8; Bur 6:7; Sab 72:6. Edouni aisisianakinika kwape kakiru,
Ijililiun einerika kwape kakopyo;
Kwape kakiru naedit naetepakini amelo,
Kwape kelimilim loilimilimi okui.
3Naarai alimori eoŋ ekiror loka EJAKAIT:
Kitopoloosi yesi Ewokodeke!
4 # 2 Sam 22:31; Dan 4:37; Yer 10:10; Yob 34:10. “Atiiba, edolit aswamuke;
Naarai eraas irotinike kere etube.
Edeke lomunot loemameete numam adolit,
Erai ŋesi lokabeit ido loedolit.
5Amunaunos kamake, mam eraas idweke, Aŋwalata;
Erai atur naepaarit namagul da.
6 # Sab 116:12; Isa 63:16; 2 Sam 7:23; Sab 74:2. Ikwaikinete kereka yesi EJAKAIT kwaŋin,
Yes ituŋa lubaŋiok ka lumam acoa?
Mere ŋesi papakon lokidumunit jo?
Kisub jo kiticat da.
7Kiitu apaarasia nukolo ŋo kasonya,
Koomoom nuka ikar luka aturio nuipu:
Kiŋit papakon, kitodikini ŋesi jo;
Imakiokon da, kilimokinete jo.
8 # Asw 17:26; Sek 9:2; Age 11:8. Naeinakinia Loejai kuju kakuj atekerin airumetekec, Naatiakia idwe luka ituŋa,
Abu kibwaik iikorun luka ituŋa
Kwape ejaar etiai loka idwe luka Isirael.
9 # 1 Sam 10:1. Naarai akorakinet naka EJAKAIT ŋes iketuŋa;
Yakob ŋes eroet loka airumeteke.
10 # Ikis 4:36; Nek 9:20; Sab 32:7,10; 147:19,20; Rom 2:18; 3:2; Sab 17:8; Awa 7:2. “Abu kodumak ŋes akwap nakamodiŋ,
Amodiŋ naelapat naegatatar itiaŋ;
Abu kirimo ŋes, koric ŋes,
Kidar ŋes bala ecopot loka akoŋuke:
11Bala emorukaalet loitetemonokini lukokaike,
Loeporitoi kaneejaas idweke,
Abu korio akebebenoka, koyaŋa kes,
Kodaka kes kabebenokake:
12EJAKAIT bon ŋes eŋarenikinit ŋes,
Amamei edeke lomoit kaŋes.
13 # Isa 58:14; Yob 29:6. Kitodoka ŋes kaiboisio nuiceleleete nuka akwap,
Konyam da amio naka emanikor;
Koinak ŋes aijuju esik loalomuni katiiba,
Ka akinyet naalomuni kamoru naegogoŋ;
14 # Sab 147:14; Age 49:11; Mat 26:28,29; Yok 6:55,56. Akinyet naka aituk, ka akile nuka amerekekin,
Ka asiepai nuka imerekekin ludidik,
Ka imerekekin luka Basan, ka ikorwa,
Ka akima nuturaŋ;
Ibu komat ebino loka aokot nuka emusabibu.
15 # Isa 1:4; Sab 89:26. “Konye kotum Yesurun, kopetonor:
Ibu kotum, koburior, kinutakin:
Kosodi konyet aijal Edeke lokesubi ŋes,
Komen Atiiba naka aitajarioke.
16Apotu kisurumata ŋes ekokote kanuka idekesio lumo, Kitunyunyurikisi da ŋes kitalesia.
17 # 1 Kor 10:20. Apotu koinakis ajokin asuban, numam eraas Edeke,
Koinakis asuban idekesio lumam lem ejenete,
Lukitetiak luapote kokau,
Lumam lem lukapapaakus eyoŋito.
18Mam iwomit nuka Atiiba nakiwuruuni jo,
Kimurwok jo Edeke lokiwuruuni jo.
19“Koany EJAKAIT, kola kes,
Kanuka aitunyunyurikin naka ikedwe lusap ka lupesur.
20 # Mat 17:17. Kotema atiar, ‘Ebwai ekareet kamakec,
Aanyuni ejautene loka esaŋekec:
Naarai eraas atur naepaarit, Idwe lumam eraas lumunon.
21 # 1 Kor 10:22; Rom 10:19; 1 Sam 12:21. Kesurumata ekokote kanuka yenimam erai Edeke;
Ketunyunyurikis kanuka aamameakakec:
Esurumi kes ekokote kanuka ŋul lumam eraas ateker; Etunyunyurikini kes kateker naebaŋaana,
22 # Aku 4:11. Naarai enepu akim kotoma anyunyuraka,
Enoki kiton aipany nakokwap cut,
Enyami akwap ka amioke,
Icwei da apeteta nuka iroŋan.
23“Eculakini kes aronisio;
Adaikini ikakojoi mamakec:
24Idokoraros kanuka eteŋei,
Enomorio kes keimilia kaloesarani,
Ka amudiario naedwar ria:
Ejukakini kes ikel luka itiaŋ,
Aisorit naka lueliatos luka apua,
25 # Aku 1:20. Eari ekileŋ kokiŋa,
Ejaun etasuro agolai;
Emudiari ekatumunan ka abekot,
Yenenaki ka ituŋanan yenebwaŋunito akorwo.
26Abu katema atiar, “Tikesiarisiara kes neelwana,
Tiketanan aiitununokec kotuŋa”:
27 # Sab 140:8. Kedim eoŋ aiyoŋ aitunyunyurikin naka lokasurup,
Kinyeis lukasurupokec asabanakin,
Kinyeis kesi atemar atiar, “Itopolooritai awokokan,
Mere EJAKAIT eswamai nu kere.’”
28“Naarai eraas ateker naemamei ainerun,
Emamei da aijen kotomakec.
29 # Sab 81:13. Arai keraas lukacoa, tikejenut ŋun,
Tieomito nuka esaŋekec loebuni!
30 # Yos 23:10; Sab 44:12. Ikwaikini ai etuŋanan erukitor elukumit,
Ka iarei da aiteker ilukumin itomon,
Dimikito egwelarit Atiibakec kes,
Dimi ijaikinit EJAKAIT kes?
31 # 1 Sam 4:8; Yer 40:3. Naarai atiibakec mam eputos ka Atiibawok,
Eraas lukasurupowok da ikatubok luka ŋun.
32Naarai emusabibukec erai emusabibu loka Sodom,
Loka imanikoria luka Gomora:
Ikecemusabibun eraas imusabibun luka epiu,
Edwarak aibuŋetakec:
33Ekecebino ŋes aisorit naka imunwo lurwopopoete,
Ka aisorit naededeŋ naka imusagan.
34 # Yob 14:17. Mam kereka ŋun eŋadakinitai mamakaŋ,
Kipucuc kotoma aiŋadisiaka?
35 # Rom 12:19; 13:4; Bur 10:30; Sab 94:1. Enyaijit ŋes lokan, ka etacit,
Kapak naejurusiarotor akecekejen:
Naarai aapu apaaran naka amudiariokec,
Ido iwonyunete nuebunokinete kes.
36 # Sab 135:14; Tub 2:18; Sab 90:13; 105:45; Yer 31:20; Yok 2:14. Naarai etubokini EJAKAIT iketuŋa,
Iituni ikebulesin;
Keanyu ebe alemar akecegogoŋ,
Emamei da yenideuna yeneenitai arai yeneosikinitai.
37 # Tub 10:14; 2 Ika 3:13; Yer 2:28. Etemari atiar, ‘Ai bo ejaas idekesiokec,
Atiiba naamunosi kesi;
38Luanyamete asiepai nuka asubanakec,
Luamasete ebino loka ainakinetekec namatat?
Konyoutu kesi, kiŋarakis yes,
Koraunos kesi amecisikus.
39 # Isa 41:4; 45:5; 46:4; 1 Sam 2:6; 2 Ika 5:7; Yob 5:18; Sab 68:20; Isa 43:13; Kos 6:1. “Aso do koany ebe, Eoŋ,
Eoŋ obe, arai ŋopeŋol,
Emamei edeke keoŋ:
Aari, ido etajari;
Emusuŋi, ido etaŋaleuni:
Emamei idio da yenepedori ademari kakanika.
40 # Any 6:8. Naarai ekiari akakan akuj,
Ebe, Kwape ajaria ikar kere,
41Arai kepen ekileŋika loemwekimweki,
Kosodi akakan aikod etube;
Enyaŋi mama ejaas lukasurupoka,
Ataci ŋul lukalaito eoŋ.
42 # Yob 13:24. Etemerikini ikojoika aokot,
Enyami ekakileŋ akiriŋ;
Kaokot nuka luearitai ka iŋatan,
Ageun kakwes nuka itima lueojak luka lukasurupo.’
43 # Rom 15:10; Apu 19:2; 6:10; Sab 85:1. “Kolelata yesi atekerin, ka iketuŋa:
Naarai inyaŋi aokot nuka ikekejaanakinak,
Inyaŋi mama ejaas lukasurupoke,
Itegeli kanuka akwapuke, kanuka iketuŋa da.”
44Kobu Musa kiner akiro kere nuka ekosio kaŋol kaki nuka ituŋa, ŋes ka Kosea okoku loka Nun. 45Kitanaŋ Musa einer akiro ŋun kere mama ejai Isirael kere: 46Kotema kes atiar, “Kibwaikisi itauonokus akiro kere nuketejen yes lolo; nuicorakinete yesi ikusidwe, airic aiswamaenen akiro kere nuka ikisila kalu. 47#Awa 3:2; Rom 10:5. Naarai mam eraas akamameak kamakus; naarai erai aijarakus, kanuka ŋun iyatakinete yesi akusipaarasia kakwap naidokarete airum okekwa loka Yorodan.”
48 # Mar 27:12-14; Ikis 3:23-27. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa kapaaran kaŋin ebe, 49“Kodoka ijo oroŋat lo Abarim, moru Nebo loejai akwap naka Moab, naitei Yeriko; koseseni akwap naka Kanaan, naainakinit idwe luka Isirael araun airumet: 50Kotwanik moru loidokari ijo, koyanaar mama ejaas ikonituŋa; kwape atwanikinia onaeekon Aaron moru Kor, koyaŋaarai mama ejaas iketuŋa: 51#Mar 20:11. Naarai ipotu kibuceasi kama ajai kokidiŋ kidwe luka Isirael kakipi nuka Meriba naka Kades, kamodiŋ naka Sin; naarai mam ipotu kitalaut eoŋ kokidiŋ kidwe luka Isirael. 52Naarai ianyuni akwap kakonyenekon; konye mam ilomari akwap naainakinit idwe luka Isirael.”
