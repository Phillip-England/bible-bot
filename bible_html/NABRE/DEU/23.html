Ikisila 23

23
1“Etuŋanan loimusuŋitai itouke, arai loetuboritai ekiliokaneke, mam elomari atukot naka EJAKAIT.
2“Ikoku yeneurunete ipajan mam elomari atukot naka EJAKAIT; kiton atur nakitomonet mam lukeŋ elomarete atukot naka EJAKAIT.
3 # Nek 13:1-2; 4:3-7; Rus 4:5. “Mam Emorait ka Emoabit elomarete atukot naka EJAKAIT; kiton atur nakitomonet mam lueraas lukec elomarete atukot naka EJAKAIT ikar kere: 4#Mar 22:1-6; Age 14:18; Ikis 2:29; 1 Sam 25:11; 1 Ika 18:4; Isa 63:9; Mat 10:40,42; 25:41-46; Yos 24:9. Naarai mam apotu kijaikis yes inyamat ka akipi korot, nailomuniata koMisiri; ido naarai apotu koropata Balaam okoku loka Beor anyoun koPetor loka Mesopotamia, aiken jo. 5#Awa 26:2. Konye mam EJAKAIT Ekonideke abu kopup Balaam; konye abu EJAKAIT Ekonideke kijulak ekenit araun arereŋ kamakon, naarai abu EJAKAIT Ekonideke komina jo. 6#Esr 9:12. Sirikimo ainapakinikec karaida akerianutukec apaarasiakon kere ikar kere.
7 # Age 25:24-26; Oba 1:12; Any 22:21; 23:9; Ikis 10:19; Leb 19:34. “Sirikila Edomait; naarai erai onacekon: sirikila Emisiriat; naarai ibu korai emoit kakwapuke. 8Elomarete idwekec luka atur kanakiwuniet atukot naka EJAKAIT.
9“Nailomaria koidukurun aitiji lukasurupokon, igitari ijo koboro kere lueroko,
10 # Leb 15:16; Mar 5:2,3; 1 Kor 5:11,13. “Arai kejaun etuŋanan mamakus lomam elai kanuka airujasit naka akuare, elomari ŋol ŋina kiidukurun, mam elomuni oidukurun: 11Konye ejaun, kewar akwap, iloŋi ŋesi kakip: kosodi keloma akoloŋ, elomuni oidukurun.
12“Jjaenena da kaibois kiŋa kiidukurun nailomanari aikiŋan: 13Jjaenena kabasaŋ oborokon lujiis; kosodi naijorio ijo kokitui, iboki ber kabasaŋakon, kosodi aibelokin anukakin ŋun nuelomunete kotomakon: 14#Age 15:1; Leb 26:12; Yer 32:40; 2 Kor 6:16; Any 3:5. Naarai eupasi EJAKAIT Ekonideke kiidukurunukon, ayuun jo, aijaikin jo lukasurupokon; kanuka ŋun ŋes eraunotor iidukurunukon lukalaunak: tetere mam ŋes eanyuni numam elaete kotomakon, kosodi anyoun kamakon.
15 # 1 Sam 30:15. “Sirikinyaka neejai papake ebulesit loesiit kaneejai papake abunore mamakon: 16#Any 22:21; 23:9; Awa 22:22; Yer 7:6; Sek 7:10; Mal 3:5; Yak 2:6. Iboienenei kajo, kamakon, kaibois naeseuni kotoma ediopet koreriakon, loekot ŋesi noi: sirikiroik ŋes.
17 # Leb 19:29; Awa 2:16; Age 19:5; 2 Ika 23:7. “Sirikejaun naadote kotoma adwe nuka Isirael, karaida luitepete ikiliok kotoma odwe luka Isirael. 18Sirikiyaŋau eropit loka naadote, karaida eropit loka ekiŋok, togo loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke kanuka adioyaposit kere: naarai ŋul kere eraas luketal mama ejai EJAKAIT Ekonideke.
19 # Any 22:25; Leb 25:36-37; Ikis 15:7-11; Nek 5:2,7; Sab 15:5; Luk 6:34. “Sirikiyuu onacekon kanuka adumunia ameda; ameda naka apesan, ameda naka inyamat, ameda naka ŋinibore yeniyuunia kanuka ameda: 20#Leb 19:34; Ikis 15:10. Ipedori ayuun emoit kanuka ameda: konye sirikiyuu onacekon kanuka adumunia ameda: tetere kisirereŋi jo EJAKAIT Ekonideke kakirokon kere nuiswamai, kotoma akwap nailosi airum.
21 # Mar 30:1-16; Mat 5:33; Yob 22:27; Sab 61:8; Lim 5:4,5. “Naiyapoikinio ijo EJAKAIT Ekonideke aiyaposit, sirikitilor aitodol: naarai abeit iburai EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo ŋin; eraun da adiakaret mamakon. 22Konye arai kiŋer aiyapo, mam eraun aronis kamakon. 23#Sab 66:13. Idari aiswamaenen ŋin naelomuni kaitukukon; kwape ejaatar nuiyapok EJAKAIT Ekonideke, ainakinet nakakote, naisumunit kaitukukon,
24 # Mat 12:1; Luk 12:15; 1 Kor 6:10; Kol 3:5. “Nailomaria amana naka emusabibu naka eidunyetekon, ipedori ainyam araitoke kwape ikotor; konye sirikibwaik adioda oborekon. 25Nailomaria akima nuka eidunyetekon, ipedori aigitunun akima kakanikon; konye sirikisak kecilikiki akima nuka eidunyetekon.
