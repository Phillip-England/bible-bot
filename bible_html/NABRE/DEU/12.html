Ikisila 12

12
1 # Ikis 4:10; 1 Ika 8:40; Gal 6:9. “Lu kes ikisila ka itubesia luidarenenete aitodol kakwap nakiinak EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakus airum, apaarasia kere nuijariata yesi kakwap. 2#Any 34:33; 2 Ika 16:4; 17:10,11; Yer 3:6. Komudiasi aiboisio kere nuejaanakiniata atekerin nuirumete yesi ikecedekesio, koroŋan lueojak, koukon, kokwap da naka ŋolikitoi kaloepesit: 3#Ikis 7:5; Mar 33:52; Tub 2:2; Any 23:13; Sab 16:4; Sek 13:2. Kotiyanakis itemwanakec, komunyumunyata ipirinokec, kicweata Aserimikec: kojepejepata aputosia nuegerer nuka idekesiokec; komudiasi ekecekiror kaibois kaŋin. 4Konye sirikiswamakis kwaŋin EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 5#Ikis 26:2; Yos 9:27; 1 Ika 8:29; 2 Siit 7:12; Sab 78:68. Konye aibois naeseuni EJAKAIT Ekusideke aibwaikin ekekiror katekerinikus kere, komoenenete aiboisike, koponenenete nen: 6#Leb 17:3; Ikis 14:22. Koyaŋaununete nen ainakinetakus nuicweio, ka asubanakus, ka lukitomonetakus, ka ainakinet naedakio naka akusikan, ka aiyaposiokus, ka ainakinetakus nuka akote, ka ikiyaik luka isipaninikus luka aituk ka luka amerekekin: 7#Ikis 14:26; Leb 23:40; Lim 3:12; 5:18. Konyamenenete kanen kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke, kolakanaros kotoma kaŋinibore kayeniswamaete, yesi ka ikaliakus, nakisirereŋio EJAKAIT yes. 8Siriswamaete iboro kere lukiswamai oni kane lolo, ŋinituŋanan luedolito kakonyeneke: 9Naarai eroko yes kedolo aiyeŋun ka airumet nakiinakinit yes EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 10Konye kidokata Yorodan, kiboikinos da akwap nakisurumi yes EJAKAIT Ekusideke, kisiyeŋun yes kaneejaas lukasurupokus kere lukilukunito, kosodete iboikinos da inapakina; 11#Ikis 14:23; 15:20; 16:2; Yos 18:1; 1 Ika 8:29; Sab 78:68; 87:2. Kosodi kanen iyaŋaunete kere lukecorak yes aibois naeseuni EJAKAIT Ekusideke aisiboikin ekekiror; ainakinetakus nuicweio, ka asubanakus, lukitomonetakus, ka ainakinet naedakio naka akanikon, ka aiyaposiokus kere nuseuna nuiyapoikinete EJAKAIT: 12#Ikis 10:9; 14:29. Kolakanaros kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke, yes ka ikusidwe lusap ka lupesur, ka ikusikejaanakinak, ka akusikejaanakinak ka Elebit loejai orekon, naarai emamei ŋes akorakinet karaida airumet kamakus. 13#Leb 17:4. Kocoite ebe mam iinakini ainakinetekon naicweio kaŋiniibois naianyuni: 14Konye kaibois naeseuni EJAKAIT kotoma adiopet katekerinikus, iinakini kanen ainakinetakon nuicweio, nen da iswamaenena kere nukecorakini jo.
15 # Age 9:3; Ikis 14:5; 15:22. “Konye ipedori aiar ka ainyam akiriŋ koruteikon kere, kwape ejaunor eipud kere loka etaukon, kwape kisirereŋio EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo: epedorete lumam elaete ka luelaete da ainyam ŋin, kwape amori, ka amiam. 16#Age 9:4; Leb 7:26-27; 17:10-14; 19:26; Ikis 15:23; 1 Sam 14:33,34; Asw 15:20,29. Konye sirinyamenenete aokot; kobukonorete kwap bala akipi. 17Sirikinyamak oruteikon yenikitomonet yenika akimakon, ka ebinokon, ka akinyetekon, karaida ikiyaik luka isipaninikon luka aituk ka luka amerekekin, karaida aiyapositikon kere naiyapoi, karaida ainakinetakon nuka akote, karaida ainakinet naedakio naka akanikon; 18#Ikis 14:23; Lim 3:12,13; 5:18-20. Konye konyamak kes akonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke kaibois naeseuni EJAKAIT Ekonideke, ijo ka okokukon, ka akokukon, ka ekejaanakinanikon, ka akejaanakinanikon, ka Elebit loejai orekon: kolakanar kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke kotoma kanuiswamai kere. 19#Ikis 14:27. Kocoite kinyek aijal Elebit apaarasia kere nuiboionono ijo kakwap.
20 # Age 15:18; 28:14; Any 34:24; Ikis 11:24; 19:8. “Naitalalaaria EJAKAIT Ekonideke eikorukon, kwape kisumunio jo, kosodi ijo atemar atiar, ‘Anyami akiriŋ “naarai imiela ekonitau ainyam akiriŋ; ipedori ainyam akiriŋ kwape ejaar eimiel loka ekonitau. 21Arai elwana kaneijai ijo aibois naeseuni EJAKAIT Ekonideke aibwaikin ekekiror, iari do yenika esipanikon loka aituk arai loka amerekekin, lokiinak EJAKAIT jo, kwape kecorakinia jo, inyamakini oruteikon, kwape ejaar eimiel loka ekonitau. 22Kwape enyamere amori ka amiam, inyami ijo da ŋun kwaŋin: lumam elaete ka luelaete da enyamete ŋun kopone ediopet. 23#Leb 17:10-14; Age 9:4. Konye kitogogoŋ na ebe mam inyamete aokot: naarai aokot ŋes aijar; sirikinyam aijar nepepe kakiriŋ. 24Sirinyamenenei kes; kobukonori kwap bala akipi. 25#Ikis 4:40; Isa 3:10; Any 15:26; Ikis 13:18; 1 Ika 11:38. Sirinyamenenei kes; tetere ijaun ejok, ka idwekon lukitupanakinete, naiswamaa ijo nuedolito kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 26#1 Siit 21:22,24; Mar 5:9,10; 18:19. Konye koyaŋanari iborokon lukalaunak luijaatar, ka aiyaposiokon, kolot aibois naeseuni EJAKAIT. 27#Leb 1:5-13; 17:11. Iinakini ainakinetakon nuicweio, akiriŋ ka aokot kotem loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke: ebukokinio aokot nuka asubanakon otem loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke, inyami ijo akiriŋ. 28#Sab 25:12,13; Awa 1:33; 3:1-4; Lim 8:12. Kidarenene kopupununei akiro nu kere nukecorak jo, tetere ijaun ejok ka ikonidwe lukitupanakinete jo ikar kere, naiswamaa nuejok nuedolito da kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke,
29 # Any 23:23; Yos 23:4; Sab 78:55. “Naemudiaria EJAKAIT Ekonideke atekerin kakonyenekon, nuilosi ijo airum, kosodi ijo airum kes, iboikin da akwapukec; 30Kocoite jo kinyek alokio atupari kes kemudiar kes kakonyenekon; kinyek aiŋitun idekesiokee ebe, ‘Jjaanakinete atekerin nu ikecedekesio biai? Keswama eoŋ da kwape kwaŋin.’ 31#Leb 18:3,26-30; 2 Ika 17:15; 2 Siit 33:2; 36:14; Yer 32:35. Sirikiswamak kwaŋin EJAKAIT Ekonideke: naarai eswamakis ikecedekesio nuketal kere kaneejai EJAKAIT, nuelait ŋesi; naarai icweanakinete ikecedwe lusap ka lupesur mama ejaas idekesiokec.
32 # Ikis 4:2; Apu 22:18-19; Yos 1:7; Awa 30:6. “Ŋinikirot kere nakecorakini yes, ŋipeŋin iricete aiswamaenen: sirikiyatakis ace, karaida aiŋoŋoor ace.
