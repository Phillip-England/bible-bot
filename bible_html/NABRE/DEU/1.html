Ikisila 1

1
1 # Mar 32:6,20,29; Yos 9:1; 22:4,7. Nu kes akiro nualimokini Musa Isirael kere okekwa loka Yorodan kamodiŋ, kotoma Araba naiteos kaSup, kidiŋ Paran, ka Topel, ka Laban, ka Kaserot, ka Di-sakab. 2#Mar 13:26; Ikis 9:23. Erai alosit naka apaarasia atomon kanadiopet anyoun koKoreb ibei moru Seir kiton Kades-barunea. 3#Mar 33:38. Kosodi kokaru loka akaisaoŋon, kolap lokitomonet ka lodiopet, kapaaran nasodit naka elap, abu Musa kolimok idwe luka Isirael kwape ajaatar kere nuabala EJAKAIT ŋes aicorakin kes; 4#Mar 21:21-35; Nek 9:22; Sab 135:11; 139:19. Kedau aidany Sikon ekabaka loka Imorain, lueboiete koKesibon, ka Og ekabaka loka Basan loeboie koAsitarot, ka Ederei: 5Kokekwa loka Yorodan kakwap naka Moab, abu Musa kogeu alimor ikisila lu ebe, 6#Any 3:11; 9:1; Mar 10:11. “Abu EJAKAIT Ewokodeke kotema oni koKoreb atiar, ‘Iboioto yesi noi koroŋat kalo: 7Kibelokinos, kobwota, koloto akwap nakiukon naka Imorain, ka aiboisio kere nuidunyatar, Araba, akwap nakiukon, ka apuutona, ka Eŋalakimak, ariri naka aŋolol, akwap naka Ikanaanin, ka Lebanon, kiton ocor lokapolon, ecor Eupurat. 8#Age 12:7; 15:18; 28:13. Koany, ebwaik akwap akonyenekus: kolomasi, kirumata akwap naabu EJAKAIT kiyapoik lukapapaakus, Aburaam, ka Isaak, ka Yakob, ainakin kes ka aurikec naetupanakini.’
9 # Any 18:18; Mar 11:14. “Abu katema yes kapak kaya ebe, ‘Mam eoŋ bon apedori adakite yes: 10#Age 15:5; 22:17; 28:14; Any 32:13; Ikis 10:22; 28:62; 1 Siit 27:23. Abu EJAKAIT Ekusideke kitupuu yes, ido koany, isubito yesi lolo bala aacer nuka akuj kanuka aipues. 11#Age 22:17; 26:4; Any 32:13. Kitupuu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakus etiaikus lo irwan alukumit, kisirereŋ yes, kwape kisumunio yes! 12#1 Ika 3:8; 2 Kor 11:28. Ekwaikini eoŋ ai apedori bon adakit aitapasitoikus, ka alaŋirukus, ka epegetekus? 13Koseutu ituŋa luecoak, luka aijen, ka luejenas, kwape ejaatar atekerinikus, adakakini eoŋ apolokinit yes.’ 14Ipotu koboŋokis eoŋ ebe, ‘Ejokuna sio aswam akirot nainer ijo.’ 15Kwaŋin abu kadakak lukapolok luka atekerinikus, ituŋa luecoak luejenas, araun lukipolokinito yes, ikapolok luka ilukumin, ikapolok luka akwat, ikapolok luka akaisakany, ka ikapolok luka itomon, ka ijaka, kwape ejaatar atekerinikus. 16#Any 23:2-8; Yok 7:24; Leb 24:22. Abu kecorak ikatubokokus kapak kaya ebe, ‘Kopupoto akiro nuka iinacanakus, kotubokis kadolit etuŋanan ka onaeeke, ka imoit yenejai kaŋes. 17#1 Sam 16:7; Awa 24:23; Yak 2:1; Awa 28:21; 29:4,25; 2 Siit 19:6; Any 18:22,26. Mam itiakito ituŋa kotube; ipupete ludidik ka luepolok kopone ediopet; mam ikuriakaros akonyen nuka ituŋanan; naarai erai etube loka Edeke: akirot nakitionikini yes iyaunete mamaka, apupuni eoŋ ŋes.’ 18Abu kecorak yes kapak kaya akiro kere nukibusakinito aswam.
19 # Mar 10:12; Ikis 8:15; Yer 2:6; Mar 13:26. “Kipou kaloto anyoun koKoreb, ketoroto amodiŋ ŋin kere naepol naesogim naipotu koanyut, korot loebei akwap nakiukon naka Imorain, kwape kecorakinitor EJAKAIT Edekewok; kadoloto Kades-barunea. 20Abu katema yes atiar, ‘Idolut akwap nakiukon naka Imorain, nakiinakinit EJAKAIT Edekewok. 21#Mar 13:30; 14:8,9; Yos 1:9; Sab 27:1-3; 46:1,7,11; Isa 41:10; 43:1,2; Luk 12:32; Bur 13:6. Koany, ebwaiki EJAKAIT Edekekon akwap akonyenekon: kolot kirum, kwape kilimokinia EJAKAIT Edeke loka lukapapaakon; sirikikurianar, karaida ajuruŋuŋ.’ 22Ipotu kikunyut neajai, ŋinituŋanan kayesi, kotemasi atiar, ‘Ketiliŋaasi ituŋa, korotakis oni akwap, tetere kiinunete oni akirot naka erot lokibusakinit aitor, ka ireria lukidolokini.’ 23#Mar 13:3. Abu ketojok akirot ŋin: kaseu ituŋa itomon ka luarei, ediopetuŋanan kaŋiniteker: 24Apotu kibelokinos koloto oroŋat, kodoloto aditot naka Esukol, korotata. 25Kolemut araito nuka akwap kakaninikec, koinut oni, koinut da akirot ebe, ‘Ejok akwap nakiinakinit EJAKAIT Ewokodeke.’
26 # Ikis 9:23; Bur 3:16; Mar 14:1-4; Sab 106:24. “Konye ipotu koŋeroto alosit, kipaliikis aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT Ekusideke: 27#Ikis 9:28. Ipotu kirurumata kowemanakus ebe, ‘Kanu naarai kilait EJAKAIT oni, ŋes kitolomunia kakwap nako Misiri, aijaikin oni akan naka Imorain, amudiar oni, 28#Mar 13:28-33; Ikis 9:1,2. Ai bo kilosi oni? acalasi iwokoinacan iwokotauon, ebe, “Epolok ituŋa, eojak da adepar oni; epolok ireria, ebwaitai da kiton akuj; bobo da kianyu odwe luka Anakim kaŋina.” 29Kasodi eoŋ atemar yes ebe, ‘Sirikiyaloŋoŋoto, karaida akurianar kes. 30#Any 14:14; Nek 4:20. EJAKAIT Ekusideke lokiŋarenikinit yes, ŋes kjiiikini yes kwape ejaatar kere nuabu kiswama kanukus koMisiri kakonyenekus; 31#Asw 13:18; Any 19:4; Ikis 32:11; Isa 46:3,4; 63:9; Kos 11:3. Kamodiŋ da, neianyuniata kwape kidakio EJAKAIT Ekusideke yes, kwape edakia etuŋanan ikokuke, korotin kere luilosete, aitodol naidolunioto aibois na.’ 32#Bur 3:19; Sab 106:24; Yud 1:5. Konye kakirot kaŋin, mam ipotu kiyuut EJAKAIT Ekusideke, 33#Any 13:21; Sab 78:14; Mar 10:33; Esk 20:6. Loabu kiŋarenikinite yes korot, amoikin yes aibois naibubukincte ikusiweman, kotoma akim kuare, aitodikin yes erot loitorete, kotoma odou da paaran.
34 # Bur 3:18; Ikis 2:14. “Kopup EJAKAIT akusikiro, konyunyurikin, kiyapo ebe, 35#Mar 14:22; Sab 95:11. ‘Abeit mam idiopet kotuŋa kalu luka aturi kana nakaronon eanyuni akwap naejok naeyapoik ainakin lukapapaakus, 36Dimarai Kaleb, okoku loka Yepune, ŋes eanyuni; ainakini ŋes ka ikedwe akwap naecak ŋesi: naarai atupa cut EJAKAIT.’ 37#Mar 27:14; Ikis 3:26; 4:21; 34:4; Sab 106:32. Abu da EJAKAIT kanyunyurik eoŋ kanukus ebe, ‘Mam jo da ilomari ŋina: 38#Any 24:13; 33:11; 1 Sam 16:22; Ikis 31:7,23. Yosua, okoku loka Nun, loebwoi kakonyenekon, ŋes elomari ŋina: kitogogoŋ ijo ŋes; naarai ŋes isurumi Isirael akwap. 39#Isa 7:15,16; Esk 18:20. Bobo da ludidikikus, luibalasi yesi ebe eraunos imejan, ka idwekus, lumam lolo ejenete ajokis karaida aronis, kes elomarete, ainakini kes akwap, irumete. 40Konye yes, kibelokinos, koloto modiŋ korot loebei Aŋolol Nakareŋan.’
41“Kosodete yesi aboŋokin eoŋ ebe, ‘Kidiaka isio kama ejai EJAKAIT, kilosi ejie, kwape ejaatar kere nukicorak EJAKAIT Ewokodeke oni.’ Ipotu koenata ŋinituŋanan lujiisike, koomitos araut akirot naebenen adokar oroŋ at. 42Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, ‘Kolimok kes ebe, Sirikidokasi, karaida ejie; naarai mam eoŋ ajai kayes; kinyeis lukasurupokus aidany yes.’ 43#Mar 14:44,45. Abu kalimok yes kwaŋin, koŋeroto apupun; konye ipotu kipaliikis aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT, koŋasikinos adokar oroŋat. 44#Ikis 28:25; 32:30; Sab 118:12; Isa 7:18. Kopotu Imorain lueboiete koroŋat kaŋol asurokin yes, korukioto yes, kwape erukio ao, kodanyata yes koSeir kiton Koruma. 45#Yob 27:9; Sab 66:18; Awa 1:24; Isa 1:15; Yer 11:7-14; Sek 7:11; Yok 9:31. Ipotu koboŋut aimony akonyen nuka EJAKAIT; konye mam EJAKAIT abu kopup ekusiporoto, karaida aitoikin yes akit. 46#Mar 13:25; 20:1,22; Tub 11:17. Kwaŋin ipotu kiboioto koKades apaarasia nuipu, kwape ejaatar apaarasia nuipotu kiboioto kaŋina.
