Ikisila 28

28
1 # Ikis 11:13-17; Any 15:26; Leb 26:3; Sab 106:3; 111:10; Isa 1:19; 3:10; Yer 11:4; 12:16; 1 Siit 14:2; Rom 2:10. “Kosodi kanen arai kipup ijo kojokan eporoto loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke airic aiswamaenen aicorakinetake kere nukecorak jo lolo, kitopoloori EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo adepar atekerin kere nuka akwap: 2#Sek 1:6. Epote da arereŋesio nu kere mamakon, kidolokinete jo, arai kipup ijo eporoto loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke. 3#Sab 128:1,4; Age 39:5. Kisirereŋio jo kore, kisirereŋio da komanikor. 4#Age 22:17; 1 Tim 4:8. Isirereŋio aur naka akonikuan, ka araito nuka alupokon, ka aur naka ibarenikon, eiyas loka aitukukon ka ludidik luka esipanikon. 5Isirereŋio editakon ka amotikon nakigures. 6#Sab 121:8. Isirereŋio nailomununia, kisirereŋio da nailomanaria.
7 # 2 Sam 22:38. “Edanyi EJAKAIT lukasurupokon lukinyoikinete jo kakonyenekon: elomunete asurokin jo korot ediopet, ekerete kakonyenekon korotin ikanykaarei, 8#Leb 25:21; Awa 3:10; Ikis 15:10. Icorakini EJAKAIT arereŋ odulaakon, ka aswamisiokon kere nuiswamaenenei; kisirereŋi ŋesi jo kakwap nakiinakinit EJAKAIT Ekonideke. 9#Age 17:7; Any 19:5; Isa 1:26; 62:12; Tit 2:14. Kiticasi EJAKAIT yes araun ituŋake lukalaunak kwape kiyapoikinio yes; arai iricito yesi aicorakineta nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke alosenen korotinike. 10#2 Siit 7:14. Eanyunete ituŋa kere luka akwap ebe kimaikinitai ekiror loka EJAKAIT; kiyoŋete jo. 11#Awa 10:22. Itupui EJAKAIT ajokisiokon, kaur naka akuanakon, aur da naka ibarenikon, araito da nuka alupokon, kakwap naabu EJAKAIT kiyapoik lukapapaakon ainakin jo. 12#Yak 1:18. Kiŋaikini EJAKAIT jo aiŋadisike naejok ainakin akwapukon akiru kapakike, aisirereŋ aswam kere naka akonikan: iyuununei atekerin nuipu, konye mam jo iyuun kamakec. 13Kiinakini EJAKAIT ijo araun ekaliŋan konye mere ekerikakinan; ijaun ijo kuju bon, mam ijaun kwap; arai kipup ijo aicorakineta nuka EJAKAIT Ekonideke, nukecorak ijo lolo airic aiswamaenen; 14#Yos 1:7; 2 Ika 22:2; Awa 4:27. Komam ipaari ainyein adio kakiro nukecorak yes lolo, apaar teten arai kediany, atupar icedekesio aijaanakin kes.
15 # Leb 26:14; Aku 2:17; Dan 9:11; Mal 2:2; Rom 2:8,9. “Konye kereka, arai mam jo ipupuni eporoto loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke airic aiswamaenen aicorakinetake kere ka ikisilake nukecorak jo lolo; kibunokinete jo ikenito lu kere, kidolokinete da. 16Kikenio jo kore, kikenio jo komanikor. 17Ikenio editakon ka amotikon nakigures. 18#Isa 3:1; Kbk 3:17. Ikenio aur naka akuanakon, ka araito nuka alupokon, eiyas loka aitukukon, ka ludidik luka esipanikon. 19Kikenio jo nailomununia, kikenio da nailomanaria.
20 # Awa 3:33; Sek 5:3,4; Mal 2:2; Sab 80:16; Isa 30:17; 51:20. “Kijukakini EJAKAIT jo iken, aiticaneta, ka airetakin kotoma aswamisiokon kere, aitodol nakimudiarere jo, aitodol da naiolioro ijo atipet; kanuka aronis naka aswamisiokon, nukijalia ijo eoŋ. 21#Leb 26:25; Mar 14:12; Yer 16:4; 24:10; Amo 4:10; Mat 24:7. Kitulanari EJAKAIT jo aitodol nakimudiaria jo kakwap nailomari airum. 22#Leb 26:16. Kidanyi EJAKAIT jo keluera, ka ekamidi, ka abuun, ka amwanis naepol, ka ekileŋ, ka ekure, ka airouroun; kibwapete jo aitodol naiolioro. 23Akujukon da naejai kuju akoukon eraun aisinit, ka alup nuejaas kwapukon eraunos asuat. 24ltuluori EJAKAIT akiru naka akwapukon apua ka asiŋei: kibunokinete jo kokuju, aitodol nakimudiarere jo.
25 # Leb 26:17; Ikis 32:30; Isa 30:17; Yer 15:4; 24:9; Esk 23:46. “Kidanyi EJAKAIT jo kakonyen nuka lukasurupokon: ilomari korot ediopet asurokin kes, ikeri korotin ikanykaarei kakonyenekec: iraun etasuro kama ejaas ajakanuto kere nuka akwap. 26Eraun atesikon akiriŋ naka akweny kere, ka itiaŋ luka akwap, emamuun yenetwarari kes. 27#Any 9:9; 15:26; 1 Sam 5:6; Sab 78:66. Kitedeki EJAKAIT jo abusio nuka Misiri, ka ajimii, ka ijip, ka aipii, numam ipedori aŋaleun. 28Kitedeki EJAKAIT jo ikerep, ka amudukanut, ka arieŋun naka etau: 29Ituriaitoi paaran, kwape eturiaitor emuduk kamuton, komam ikerianar koponesiokon: kiroikinio kiŋasio jo duc, emamuun yenikitajari jo, 30#Yob 31:10; Yer 8:10. Isineri ijo apese, konye eperete ecetuŋanan kaŋes: iduki ijo etogo, konye mam iboikini toma: irai emanikor loka emusabibu, konye mam inyami araitoke. 31Eario emoŋokon kakonyenekon, konye mam jo inyami ŋes: kidemario asigiriakon kakonyenekon kadeden, konye mam kinyakakinio: einakinio lukasurupokon amerekekinikon, emamuun yenikitajari jo, 32Ekorakinio icetuŋa ikonidwe lusap ka lupesur kosesenete akonikonye, konye itegete kanuka aimuit kes apaaran kere: ido kimamikinete jo nuipedori aswam. 33#Leb 26:16; Yer 5:17. Enyami ateker namam jo ijeni araito nuka alupokon, ka aitaaritoikon; konye kiroikinio jo kicikakinitete duc: 34Kikerepikini ijo kanuka nueanyunete akonikonye. 35Ibwaikini EJAKAIT abusio nueroko noi: akuŋinikon, ka akejenekon, numam ipedori aŋaleun, ageun kotau loka akejukon kiton osiaduk loka akoukon.
36 # 2 Ika 17:4; 24:12,14; Yer 39:1; 2 Siit 33:1; 36:1; Yer 16:13. “Kiyaŋaari EJAKAIT jo, ka ekabakakon loidakakini ayait jo, mama ejai ateker namam jo ijeni, ijo ka lukapapaakon, ijaanakini kaŋina idekesio ice akito ka amor. 37#1 Ika 9:7,8; Yer 24:9; 25:9; Sek 8:13; Sab 44:14. Iraun aumokinet, ka awarag, ka ikienis kotuŋa kere lukireŋeikini EJAKAIT jo. 38#Mik 6:15; Kag 1:6; Any 10:4; Yok 1:4. Iyei ikinyom luipu omanikor, konye isakun idis; naarai enyami emaase kes. 39Irai amanat nuka emusabibu icapi kes konye mam imasi ebino, karaida aideŋ araito; naarai enyami ekurut kes 40Ejaunos iseitunin oikorunokon kere, konye mam inyonyoikin akinyetekec naarai edounenete araito nuka eseitunikon. 41#Yer 52:28. Iwurie idwe lusap ka lupesur konye mam eraunos lukon; naarai eŋatario. 42Enyamiari emaase ikitokon kere ka araito nuka alupokon. 43Iyatanakini emoit loejai mamakon adokari kuju adepar jo; iyatanakini ijo aidoor kwap. 44#Esr 9:7; Aku 1:5. Kiyuununei ŋes jo, konye mam jo iyuuni ŋes: eraun ŋesi ekaliŋan, ka jo da ekerikakinan 45Kibunokinete jo ikenito ŋul kere, kibwapete jo, kidolokinete jo, aitodol nakimudiarere jo; naarai mam ibu kopup eporoto loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke airic aicorakinetake ka ikisilake luabu kicorak jo: 46#Mar 26:10; Isa 8:18; Esk 5:15; 14:8. Eraunos kaneijai ijo aanyunet ka aumokinet, kaneejai aurikon da ikar kere.
47 # Nek 9:35-37. “Naarai mam ijaanakinit EJAKAIT Ekonideke kailel, kaiyalama naka etau, kanuka aidiles naka iboro kere: 48#Isa 47:6; Yer 27:12; 28:14; Mat 11:29. Kanuka ŋun ijaanakini ijo lukasurupokon luijukuni EJAKAIT asurokin jo, koriebi eteŋei, kojakite akur kimujalal, kipuda iboro kere: idokokini ajookot naka asuat omurisinikon aitodol nakimudiaria joo 49#Yer 5:15; 6:22,23; Luk 19:43; Yer 48:40; 49:22; Aku 4:19; Esk 17:3; Kos 8:1. Eyaŋauni EJAKAIT ateker kama elwana asurokin jo, anyoun kosaŋe loka akwap, kwape eporitor emorukaalet, ateker namam jo ijenuni akeŋajep. 50#Dan 8:23; 2 Siit 36:17; Isa 47:6. Ateker naededeŋ akekonyen, namam epod imakio karaida aitim ludidik: 51#Isa 1:7; 62:8; Yer 5:15-17. Enyami aur naka ibarenikon, ka araito nuka alupokon, aitodol nakimudiarere: mam ŋes kinyeinia jo akima, arai ebino arai akinyet, arai eiyas loka aitukukon, karaida ludidik luka esipanikon aitodol nakimudiaria jo. 52#2 Ika 25:1; Yer 37:8; Mat 22:7. Kirigauni jo koruteikon kere, aitodol naetiyaikinotor arwatatinikon nueojak nuebwaitai, nuibu komuno, anatar akwapukon kere: kirigauni jo koruteikon kere nakiinak EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo. 53#Leb 26:29; 2 Ika 6:28,29; Yer 19:9; Aku 2:20; 4:10; Luk 21:23. Inyami ijo aur naka akonikuan, akiriŋ naka idwekon lusap ka lupesur lukiinak EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo; kirigaunitete, kicikakinitete, kanekicikakinete lukasurupokon jo. 54Etuŋanan loenonok kotomakus, loijamamai eraun loitei onaceke kakoŋu naerono, ka aberu naka atorobuke, ka Iuideun: kodweke luideikina ŋes: 55Komam einakini idio kakesi akiriŋ naka idweke luenyami ŋesi, naarai emamei yenideikina ŋes; kirigaunitete, kicikakinitete kanekicikakinete lukasurupokon jo koruteikon kere. 56Aberu naenonok naijamamai kotomakus, namam tiketamaki aicakakin akekeju alup kanuka aijamamake ka anonokuke, itei ŋesi okilenike loka atorobuke, ka okokuke ka akokuke kakoŋu naerono; 57#2 Ika 6:28-29; Aku 4:10. Ka aŋasiepeke naelomuni kakejeke, ka ikedwe lueuruuni; naarai enyami kes kaiyeiyea kanuka eipud iboro kere: kirigaunitete, kicikakinitete, kanekicikakinete lukasurupokon jo koruteikon
58 # Any 6:3; Sab 99:3; Isa 57:15; 29:23; Mal 1:14; Sab 20:1; 83:18; Isa 42:8; Mal 2:3; 4:2; Pil 2:10. “Arai mam irici aitodol akiro kere nuka ikisila lu luiwadikatai oitabo lo tetere iyoŋi ekiror lo lokaibuses loeyoŋitai, EJAKAIT Ekonideke; 59#1 Ika 9:7,9; Aku 1:9,12; 4:12; Dan 9:12; 2 Siit 21:12-15. Kijukakini EJAKAIT jo aiticaneta nukaumokin, ka aiticaneta nuka akoniwur, aiticaneta nukos, nuedaunete apak naepol, ka adekesia nueroko noi, nuedaunete apak naepol. 60Kiinuni bobo jo adekesia kere nuko Misiri, nuibu koyoŋ; kidoŋete jo. 61Ŋinideka da, ka ŋiniiticanet, namam iwadikatai oitabo loka ikisila kalu, kiinuni EJAKAIT jo kes, aitodol nakimudiarere jo. 62#Nek 9:23. Isalete yesi ikidioko, kwabo iraas kolo yesi kwape aacer nuka akuj kanuka aipues; naarai mam ipotu kopupoto eporoto loka EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 63#Isa 62:5; Yer 32:41; Mik 7:18; Sep 3:17; Luk 15:6,10; Awa 1:26; Isa 1:24. Kosodi kanen, kwape esiyalamikinitor EJAKAIT aiswamakin yes nukajokak, ka aitupuu yes; isiyalamikini kwaŋin EJAKAIT aitoolior yes, ka amudiar yes; kilemario do yes kakwap nailomarete airum. 64#Leb 26:33; Nek 1:8; Yer 16:13. Kisiarakini EJAKAIT jo otuŋa kere, ageun kosaŋe ediopet loka akwap kiton osaŋe ece loka akwap; ijaanakini idekesio ice kaŋina, lumam lem ijenunit, ijo karaida lukapapaakon, kes akito ka amor. 65#Age 8:9; Isa 57:21; Esk 5:12,17; Amo 9:4; Leb 26:36. Mam jo idumuni aijamam kotoma atekerin ŋun, emameikini da akonikeju aibworar: konye ijaikini EJAKAIT jo kaŋina etau loemarani, ka akonye nuitegete, ka etau loitururwei: 66Ibuonokini noi kanuka aijarakon; ikurianari kuare ka paaran, mam imuno akoniijar: 67#Yob 7:4. Itemari tupuruc ebe, ‘Tiejok arai kerai eboŋ!’ Eboŋ da itemari ebe, ‘Tiejok arai kerai tupuruc!’ kanuka akurian naka etaukon naikurianaror, ido kanuka nueanyunete akonikonye. 68#Yer 44:7; Kos 8:13; 9:3. Kinyakari EJAKAIT jo Misiri kameerin, korot loabu kalimok jo ebe, ‘Mam bobo ianyuni ŋes’; igwelanaros kaŋina mama ejaas lukasurupokus araun ibulesin ka abulesin, konye emameun ituŋanan yenikigweli yes.”
