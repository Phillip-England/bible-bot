Ikisila 2

2
1 # Mar 21:4. “Kasodete aibelokin kaloto modiŋ korot loebei Aŋolol Nakareŋan, kwape kabalar EJAKAIT eoŋ: kerimosi emoru Seir apaarasia nuipu. 2Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, 3‘Irimosi eroŋat lo apaarasia nuipu: kibelokinos ari naka agolitomei. 4#Age 36:8. Kicorak ijo ituŋa ebe, Itorete oikor loka ikusiinacan idwe luka Esau, luiboiete koSeir; kikuriakaros kesi yes: aso ca kocoito noi: 5#Age 36:8; Ikis 32:8; Yos 24:4; 2 Siit 20:10-12; Sab 115:3; 135:5,6; Dan 4:32,35; Asw 17:26. Sirikiŋolopata kakes; naarai mam kainakini yes akecekwap, mam karaida nepeicakakini etau loka akeju bon, naarai ainak Esau moru Seir airum. 6Igwelete inyamat lunyaman kapesan kamakec; igwelete da akipi numatan kapesan kamakec. 7Naarai kisirereŋit EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo kaswam kere naka akanikon; abu ŋesi kojenu alositekon kamodiŋ kana nakos: ajaenena EJAKAIT Ekonideke kajo ikar lu akaisaoŋon; amamei ibore yenibu kipuda.’ 8#1 Ika 9:26; 2 Ika 14:22; 16:6. Kwaŋin katuboros kaneejaas iinacanawok idwe luka Esau, luiboiete koSeir, anyoun korot loka Araba, anyoun koElat, ka Esion-geber.
“Kebelokinos ketoro orot loka amodiŋ naka Moab. 9#Age 19:37; Mar 21:28; Sab 83:8. Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, ‘Sirikitunyunyurik Moab, karaida aitoŋolop kes kojie: naarai mam kainakini yes airum akekwap; naarai ainak idwe luka Lot airum Ar.’ 10#Age 14:5; Mar 13:22; Ikis 9:2. (Apotu Iemim kiboioto kanen sek, ituŋa luapolok, kipu, koojak bala Inakaim: 11Amaikinitai ŋul Irepaim, kwape Inakaim; konye enyarito Imoabin kes Iemim. 12#Age 14:6; 36:20; 1 Siit 1:38-42. Eboioto Ikorin da koSeir sek, konye kirumata idwe luka Esau aiboisikec; komudiasi kes kakonyenekec, kiboikinos aiboisikec; kwape eswamakinia Isirael akwap naka airumeteke, naainakini EJAKAIT kes.) 13‘Aso konyoutu do, kodokata aguurut Sered.’ Kipou kadokata aguurut Sered. 14#Mar 14:28-35; 26:64; 32:11; Sab 78:33; 90:3-9; Ikis 1:34,35; Esk 20:15. Apaarasia nukinyounio oni koKades-barunea, aitodol nakidokia aguurut Sered, emwaasi ikar akaisauni ka lukanykauni; aitodol naadaunor atur kere naka ituŋa lukajiok koidukurun, kwape eyapoikinitor EJAKAIT kes. 15#1 Sam 5:6-11; 7:13; Sab 95:10,11; 106:26; Bur 3:11-19; 4:1-5. Bobo da aluikinit akan naka EJAKAIT kes, amudiar kes koidukurun, aitodol naadaunotor.
16“Kosodi kanen ketwaka ituŋa kere lukajiok, kodaunos da kotuŋa, 17Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, 18‘Itori ijo lolo Ar, oikor loka Moab: 19#Age 19:38; 14:5. Naikunyakinia neejaas idwe luka Amon, sirikitunyunyurik kes, karaida aitoŋolop: naarai mam kainakini jo airum akwap naka idwe luka Amon: naarai ainak idwe luka Lot airum ŋes.’ 20(Ŋin da emaikinitai akwap naka Irepaim: eboioto Irepaim kanen sek; konye enyarito Imorain kes Isamsumim; 21Ituŋa luapolok, kipu, koojak bala Inakaim; konye abu EJAKAIT komudia kes kakonyenekec: kirumata kesi, kiboikinos aiboisikec: 22#Age 36:8; Yob 12:23. Kwape abu ŋesi kiswamakinia idwe luka Esau, luiboiete koSeir, naamudiaria Ikorin kakonyenekec; kirumata kesi, kiboikinos aiboisikec kiton lolo: 23#Yos 13:3; Yer 25:20; Sep 2:4; Age 10:14; Amo 9:7. Iabim da lueboiete koreria kiton Gasa, apotu Ikaputorim lualomunete koKaputor komudiasi kes, kiboikinos aiboisikec.) 24#Mar 21:13,14; Tub 11:18. ‘Konyoutu, koloto, kitoroto aditot naka Arunon; koany, ainak Sikon Emorait, ekabaka loka Kesibon ka akwapuke akanikon: kogeutu airum ŋes, koŋolopata kitijioto ŋes. 25#Any 15:14; Ikis 11:25; Yos 2:9. Ageun lolo ebwaikini etasurokon ka ailiilikon otuŋa luejaas kwap kakuj kere, luepupunete akirokon, kosodete amaran ka ariebakin kanukon.’
26 # Ikis 20:10; Est 9:30. “Abu kejuka ikiyakia anyoun kamodiŋ naka Kedemot mama ejai Sikon ekabaka loka Kesibon kakiro nuka ainapakin, ebe, 27#Mar 21:21; Tub 11:19. ‘Kisiikinai ketor akwapukon: aderi erot lokapolon, mam apaari akan nakoteten karaida nakokediany. 28#Mar 20:19. Kitwegeli ijo eoŋ inyamat lunyaman kapesan; ka akipi da numatan kapesan: kisiikinai ketor aitor kakeje; 29#Ikis 23:3; Tub 11:17. Kwape apotu idwe luka Esau luiboiete koSeir ka Imoabin luiboiete koAr keswamakiniata; aitodol naadokia Yorodan alomar akwap nakiinakinit EJAKAIT Ekosideke sio.’ 30#Mar 21:23; Yos 11:19,20; Any 4:21; Kos 4:17; Yak 1:13-15. Konye mam Sikon ekabaka loka Kesibon abu kisiik oni aitor akwapuke: naarai abu EJAKAIT Ekonideke kitogogoŋ ekemoyo, kitininik eketau, tetere ijaikini ŋes akanikon, kwape ejaar lolo. 31Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, ‘Koany, ageu aijaikin jo Sikon ka akekwap: kogeu airum, tetere irumi akekwap.’ 32Kosodi Sikon alomun asurokin oni, ŋes ka iketuŋa kere, ejie koYakas. 33#Any 23:31; Ikis 7:2; 20:16; Sab 135:10-12; 136:18-20; Ikis 29:7. Kijaik EJAKAIT Ewokodeke ŋes mamawok; kadanyata ŋes ka odweke, ka ituŋake kere. 34#Leb 27:28; Mar 21:2,3; Ikis 7:2,26; Yos 7:11; 8:25,26; 9:24; 11:14; 1 Sam 15:3-9. Kerumata ireriake kere kapak kaŋin, kamudiasi cut ŋolire loeboioto ituŋa kotoma, ka aŋor ka ludidik; mam kipou kedeutu idio kakesi: 35Dimarai kipou kanatutu ibaren, ka lunatan lukinatuni koreria lukipou kerumata. 36#Ikis 3:12; 4:48; Yos 13:9; Sab 44:3. Anyoun koAroer, loejai ariri naka aditot naka Arunon, kore da loejai aditot, kiton Gilead, amamei ere lokiwojakinit oni: abu EJAKAIT Ewokodeke kijaik kere mamawok: 37#Age 32:22; Mar 21:24; Ikis 3:16. Dimarai akwap naka idwe luka Amon ŋes mam yes ikunyakinete; ariri kere naka ecor Yabok, ka ireria luka akwap nakiukon, ka neabu EJAKAIT Ewokodeke kitikok oni.
