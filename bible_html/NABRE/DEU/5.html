1Konyarau Musa Isirael kere, kotema kes atiar, “Jjo ba Isirael, kiira ikisila ka itubesia lualimokini akiikus lolo, tetere isisiaete kes, kosodete airic aiswamaenen. 2#Any 19:5; 24:7,8; Ikis 4:23; Bur 8:6-13; 9:19-23. Abu EJAKAIT Ewokodeke kitutukik oni koKoreb. 3#Mat 13:17; Bur 8:9. Mam EJAKAIT abu kitutukik lukapapaawok aitutuket ŋin, konye oni, oni lukija ne kere lukijari lolo. 4#Any 19:9,19; 20:22; Ikis 4:33,36; 34:10. Abu EJAKAIT kiner kayes kiteos komoru kokidiŋ kakim, 5#Any 19:16; 20:21; Mar 16:48; Sab 106:23; Yer 30:21; Gal 3:19; Bur 12:18,19; Any 24:2. (Abwoi eoŋ kokidiŋ EJAKAIT ka yes kapak kaŋin, aitodikin yes akirot naka EJAKAIT: naarai ikuriaka yesi akim, mam ipotu kodokasi moru,) ebe,
6 # Any 20:2; Leb 26:1; Ikis 6:4; Sab 81:10. “‘Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekonideke, loketolomuni jo kakwap nako Misiri, kotogo loka ebulesene.
7 # Any 20:3. “‘Sirijaenena kicedekesio nabarai EOŊ.
8 # Leb 26:1; Ikis 4:15-18; 27:15; Any 20:4; Sab 97:7; Asw 17:29. “Sirikisub adioput naegerer, karaida asima naka ibore kere yenejai kuju toma akuj, karaida yenejai kwap toma alup, karaida yenejai toma akipi nuejaas kwap kalup: 9#Any 34:6-7; Mar 14:18; Ikis 7:9-10. Sirikikukonokin kes, karaida aijaanakin kes: naarai EOŊ EJAKAIT Ekonideke arai Edeke lokekolo, edopet kodwe numam adolit nuka lukapapaakec, kiton atur nakiwuniet ka nakiwononet naka ŋul kalukalaito eoŋ; 10#Yer 32:18; Dan 9:4; 1 Yok 1:7. Ido etimi ilukumin luka ituŋa lukaminas eoŋ koricito akaicorakineta.
11 # Leb 19:12; Any 20:7; Yak 5:12; Mat 5:33. “Sirilimit atai ekiror loka EJAKAIT Ekonideke; naarai mam EJAKAIT isikai aitetelekar akirot, ŋin yenelimit atai ekekiror.
12 # Any 16:23-30; 31:12-14; Any 20:8; Nek 13:31. “‘Koricite apaaran nakasabato, aitalaun ŋes, kwape kicorakinia jo EJAKAIT Ekonideke. 13#Any 23:12; 31:15; 34:21; 35:2; Leb 23:3; Esk 20:12. Kitaaritoi apaarasia akanykape, kiswama akoniswamisio kere; 14#Age 2:2; Any 16:29; Bur 4:4. Konye do apaaran nakikanyetekaarei ŋes asabato naka EJAKAIT Ekonideke: apaaran ŋin sirikiswama adioswam kere, ijo, ka okokukon, ka akokukon, ka ekonikejaanakinan, ka akonikejaanakinan, ka ekonimoŋ, ka akonisigiria, ka ikonibaren kere, ka imoit yenejai orekon; tetere ekonikejaanakinan ka akonikejaanakinan iyeŋete kwape kajo. 15Kiitu ijo ebe irai kolo ijo ebulesit kakwap nako Misiri, kitolom EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo kakan nakagogoŋ, ka akan naerioro: kanuka ŋun kicorakinit EJAKAIT Ekonideke jo airic apaaran nakasabato.
16 # Ikis 27:16; Mat 15:4; 19:19; Mk 7:10; 10:19; Luk 18:20; Pes 6:2,3; Leb 19:3; Kol 3:20; Ikis 4:40. “Koyonitenenei papakon ka totokon, kwape kicorakinia jo EJAKAIT Ekonideke: tetere akonipaarasia iyatakinos, tetere da ijaun ejok kakwap nakiinakini EJAKAIT Ekonideke.
17 # Age 9:6; Leb 24:17; Mat 5:21; 19:18; Mk 10:19; Luk 18:20; Rom 13:9; Yak 2:11. “Siriarenenei.
18 # Leb 20:10; Mat 5:27; 19:18; Mk 10:19; Luk 18:20; Rom 13:9; Yak 2:11. “‘Karaida adotenen.
19 # Leb 19:11; Mat 19:18; Mk 10:19; Luk 18:20; Rom 13:9. “‘Karaida akokoenen.
20 # Any 23:1; Mat 19:18; Mk 10:19; Luk 18:20; 1 Ika 21:10. ““Karaida asabanakin ikoniidunyet,
21 # Rom 7:7; 13:9; Mik 2:2; Kbk 2:9; Luk 12:15; Gal 5:14. “Karaida aiŋobelenen aberu naka eidunyetekon; karaida eimiel etogo loka eidunyetekon, alupoke, arai ekekejaanakinan, arai akekejaanakinan, ekemonŋ arai akesigiria, arai ibore kere yenerai yenika eidunyetekon.’
22 # Bur 12:18-19; Any 24:12. “Abu EJAKAIT kolimok ekodetekus kere akiro ŋun keporoto loepol komoru kokidiŋ kakim, kodou, ka amuton naajesi cucucuc: mam abu kiyatak ace da. Abu kiwadik kes amor aarei, kainak eoŋ; 23Kosodi naipupunioto yesi eporoto kokidiŋ kamuton, konoki akim komoru, ipotu kikunyut neajai eoŋ, ikapolok kere luka atekerinikus, ka imakiokus; 24Kotemasi yesi atiar, “Koany, kitodik EJAKAIT Ewokodeke oni akeibuses ka akepolou, kipupu da ekeporoto kokidiŋ kakim: kijenu lolo ebe ineras Edeke kituŋanan, ido ejari. 25Kanukinyo do kitwakia isio? Naarai kinyamaari sio akim na naepol: kitwaki arai kiipupu bobo eporoto loka EJAKAIT Ewokodeke. 26#Ikis 4:33. Naarai iŋai yenejai akuan, yenikepupunit eporoto loka Edeke lokajaran inera kokidiŋ kakim, kwape kipupunio oni, kosodi aijar? 27#Bur 12:19. Kikunyak ijo, kopup kere nuetemari EJAKAIT Ewokodeke: kosodi ijo alimokin sio kere nukilimonokini EJAKAIT Ewokodeke; kipupuni isio, kiswamai da.’
28“Abu EJAKAIT kopup eporoto loka akirokus, nakilimokiniata eoŋ; katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, ‘Apup eporoto loka akiro nuka ituŋa kalu, nukilimokis jo: alimut ejok ŋun kere nuenerata. 29#Ikis 32:29; Sab 81:13; Isa 48:18; Yer 44:4; Mat 23:37; Luk 19:42; 2 Kor 5:20; 6:1; 7:1; Bur 12:25; Ikis 11:1; Sab 119:1-5; Luk 11:28; Yok 15:14; Apu 22:14. Bala ti kojaenena etau loikoni nen kotomakec, kayoŋito eoŋ, koricito akaicorakineta tiritir, tetere ejaunos ejok ka ikecedwe ikar kere! 30Kolot kolimok kes ebe, “Koboŋosi yesi owemanakus.” 31#Mal 4:4; Gal 3:19. Konye ijo, kobwo kane, kalimokini jo aicorakineta kere, ka ikisila, ka itubesia, luisisianakini ijo kes, tetere iswamaenenete kes kakwap naainakini kes airum.’ 32#Ikis 17:20; 28:14; Yos 1:7; 23:6; Awa 4:27. Kanuka ŋun iricete aswam kwape kicorakinia yes EJAKAIT Ekusideke: mam ipaarete akan nakoteten karaida nakokediany. 33#Ikis 10:12; Sab 119:6; Lim 8:12; Yer 7:23; Luk 1:6; 1 Tim 4:8; Ikis 4:40; 12:25,28; 22:7; Pes 6:3. Ilosenenete koroictin kere lukorak yes EJAKAIT Ekusideke, tetere ijarete, ijaunos ejok, iyatakinos da akusipaarasia kakwap nairumete.
