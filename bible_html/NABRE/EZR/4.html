Esera 4

4
1Kepuputu da lukasurupo luka Yuda ka Benyamin ebe adukoto idwe Iuejoreoritai eyekalu loka EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael; 2#Est 8:17; 2 Ika 17:24,32,33. kikunyakis kesi neejai Serubabel, ka ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapakee, kobalas ebe, “Mere kadukoto nepepe kayes: naarai kimoot isio Ekusideke, kwape kayesi; kiinakini isio ŋes asuban ageun kapaarasia nuka Esar-kadon ekabaka loka Asuria lokitodokuni sio nee.” 3#Yok 4:9; Asw 8:21; 2 Siit 36:22,23; Esr 6:3; Isa 44:28. Konye Serubabel, ka Yesua ka ice kere luka ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapa luka Isirael, kotemasi kes atiar, “Mam kipuda yes aimorikikin kasio kotoma aiduk etogo loka Ekosideke; konye sic kiduki bonik etogo loka EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, kwape abu Kuro ekabaka loko Perusia kicorakinia.”
4 # Esr 3:3; Isa 35:3,4; Yer 38:4. Kosodete ituŋa luka akwap kitakalanyanata akanin nuka ituŋa luka Yuda, kisiyalononoto kes kotoma aiduk, 5koropata ikapetak akiro aluikin kes, aitikokin akeeeinerun, apaarasia kere nuka Kuro ekabaka loko Perusia, aitodol naerumia Dario ekabaka loko Perusia.
6Kairum naka Akasuero, ageunet naka airumuke, kiwadikata aosan lukiboiete ko Yuda ka Yerusalem.
7Kapaarasia nuka Aratasasata apotu Bisilam, ka Mituredat, ka Tabeel, ka lukaupake ice kere, kiwadikata mama ejai Aratasasata ekabaka loko Perusia; ewadikatai ebalua keiwadikae loka Suria, kanaiep naka Isurian. 8#Sab 112:6; Sek 1:14; Rom 8:28. Kiwadikata Rekum lokebuku, ka Simisai ekewadikan ebalua mama ejai Aratasasata ekabaka asurokin Yerusalem kopone kalo: 9#2 Ika 17:30,31. Kosodete kiwadikata Rekum lokebuku, ka Simisai ekewadikan, ka iukaupakec ice kere; Idinaitin, Iparasatakain, Itarapelin, Ipasian, Irakebain, Ibabulonin, Isusanikin, Idekain, Ilamein, 10#Esr 7:12. ka atekerin ace kere nukitwobotuni Osunapar lokapolon lokaibuses, kibwaik ore loka Samaria, ka akwap ace da okekwa juwai, ka ace da, 11Ikopuna nu kobalua lokijukakinete kesi: “Aratasasata ekabaka: Ikonikejaanakinak, ituŋa luka okekwa juwai, ka ace da. 12#Amo 7:10; Luk 23:2; Asw 24:5. Kojen ekabaka ebe adolut Iyudayan luanyounete kamakon mamakosi Yerusalem; edukete ere lokepali lokaronon da, adaut arwatatin, eteteut da apeteta. 13#Esr 7:24; Mat 9:9. Aso kojen ekabaka ebe, arai keduki ere lo, kodaunai da arwatatin, mam kes elemete esolo loka ituŋa, ka esolo loka iboro, ka esolo loka ikalotok da, kosodi kokau etwakiarete ikabakan. 14Aso do naarai kinyami abalaŋ naka etogo loka ekabaka, mam ibus sio aanyun aitisilio naka ekabaka, kanuka nun kijukakinit aiteienikin ekabaka; 15#Est 3:8; Asw 17:6,7. konicai oitabo loka aisiitonokin loka lukapapakon: kwaŋin idumuni koitabo loka aisiitonokin ijenuni kwape eraar ere lo ere lokepali, loitisili ikabakan ka ibukui, ebe da apotu kipaliikis apeŋo kotomake: kanuka nun da amudiarere ere lo. 16Kitejenunit ekabaka ebe, arai keduki ere lo, kodaunai da arwatatin, kanuka nun mam ijaun ijo kewai okekwa juwai.”
17Kosodi ekabaka aiiukar aboŋokin mama ejai Rekum lokebuku, ka Simisai ekewadikan, ka lukaupakee ice kere lukiboiete ko Samaria, kakwap ace nakokekwa juwai ebe, “Kinapakinos, ka ace da. 18Aponi kasomakin eoŋ kaitebeikin ebalua lokijukakinete yesi sio. 19Kebwaik ekisil, komoata, apotu kodumutu ebe ere ŋol ageun apeŋo ebilanakini ikabakan ebotet aipaliikin ka amwanit da inerununoo kotomake. 20#1 Siit 18:3; Sab 72:8; Age 15:18. Ido ajaenenas ikabakan lukagogon luapolokinete Yerusalem, luayaitos akwap kere okekwa iuwai; ataeanakinio kes esolo loka ituŋa, esolo loka iboro, ka esolo loka ikalotok da. 21Aso do kibwaikisi ekisil, kitananata ituŋa lu aswam, kinyek ere ŋol adukio, aitodol naebwaikinia eoŋ ekisil. 22Koeoito kinyeis aijalakin kanun: kanukinyo tikepolooror aronis aitwataniar ikabakan?”
23 # Awa 4:16. Kosodi kesomi aikopunet naka ebalua loka eka baka Aratasasata kakonyen nuka Rekum, ka Simisai ekewadikan ka lukaupakec, kisunyas atipet alosit Yerusalem mama ejaas Iyudayan, kitananata aswamukee kagogon ka apedor. 24#Yob 20:5. Kosodi aswam nakotogo loka Edeke loejai Yerusalem aitananio; aponi kitananai aitodol okaru lokiareit loka airum naka Dario ekabaka loko Perusia.
