Esera 8

8
1Lu kes ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakec, na da ŋes aur naka nul kaluauparos kaeon anyoun koBabulon, kairum naka Aratasasata ekabaka. 2#1 Siit 3:22. Kodwe luka Pinekas, Geresom: kodwe luka Itamar, Daniel: kodwe luka Daudi, Katus, 3#Esr 2:3. Kodwe luka Sekania; kodwe luka Paros, Sekaria: nepepe kaŋes aponi kimarakinitete kaur naka ikiliok akwatat ka akaisakany. 4Kodwe luka Pakat-moab, Eliekoenai okoku loka Serakia; nepepc kaŋes da ikiliok akwat aarei. 5Kodwe luka Sekania, okoku Ioka
Yakasiel; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akwat auni. 6#Esr 2:15; Nek 7:20. Kodwe luka Adin, Ebed okoku loka Yonatan; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akaisakany. 7Kodwe luka Elam, Yesaia okoku loka Atalia; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akaisakanykaarei. 8Kodwe luka Sepatia, Sebadia okoku loka Mikael; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akaisakanykauni. 9Kodwe luka Yoab, Obadia okoku loka Yekiel; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akwat aarei ka itomon ka lukanykauni. 10Kodwe luka Selomit, okoku loka Yosipia; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akwatat ka akaisakanykape. 11Kodwe luka Bebai, Sekaria okoku loka Bebai; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akaisaarei ka lukanykauni. 12Kodwe luka Asagad, Yokanan okoku loka Kakatan; nepepe kaŋes da ikiliok akwatat ka itomon. 13Kodwe luka Adonikam, lukawasia; lu kes ikeeekiroria, Elipelet, Yeuel, ka Semaia, nepepe kakes da ikiliok akaisakanykape. 14Kodwe luka Bigibai, Utai ka Sabud; nepepe kakes da ikiliok akaisakanykaarei.
15 # Sab 137:1; Asw 16:13; Awa 27:23; Asw 20:28; Bur 13:17; Esr 7:7; Mar 8:1. Katukonok kes toma ocor loelelei Akaba; kipou kedukuro adaun apaarasia auni: kanic ituŋa, ka isasedotin, komam edumuna ediopet kodwe luka Lebi. 16Kasodi anyaraun Elieser, ka Ariel, ka Semaia, ka Elunatan, ka Yarib, ka Elunatan, ka Natan, ka Sekaria, ka Mesulam, ituŋa Jukapolok; ka Yoiarib, ka Elunatan, luaraas ikesisianakinak. 17Kejuka kes neejai Ido ekapolon loka aibois Kasipia; kalimok nuelimokinete Ido, ka ikeinaean Inetinim, kaibois Kasipia, tetere kiinunete sio ikejaanakinak luka etogo loka Edekewok. 18#Esr 7:28; Awa 3:6; Rom 8:28; Leb 10:10,11; 2 Siit 30:22; Nek 8:7. Kanuka akan nakajokan naka Edekewok naaiai kaoni, koinutu etuŋanan lokaeoa, lokodwe luka Mali, okoku loka Lebi, okoku loka Isirael; ka Serebia, ka odweke ka iinaeanake, itomon ka lukanykauni; 19ka Kasabia, ido nepepe kaŋes Yesaia lokodwe luka Merari, iinaeanake ka odwekec, akaisaarei; 20#Esr 2:43. luka Inetinim, luapotu Daudi ka ikapolok koinakis aiiaanakin Ilebin, Inetinim akwat aarei ka akaisaarei: kolimoroi kere ikecekiroria.
21 # 2 Siit 20:3; Leb 16:29; Isa 58:3,5; Sab 5:8. Kasodi alimor ailuŋ kaŋina, kocor Akaba, tetere kitasonikin kakonyen nuka Edekewok, aimo kaneejai ŋesi erot loitenara, lowok, ka loka idwewok ludidik, ka loka iborowok kere. 22#1 Kor 9:15; 1 Siit 28:9; Esr 7:6,9; Sab 33:18,19; Isa 3:10; Rom 8:28; 1 Pet 3:12; Sab 34:16; 2 Siit 15:2. Naarai keleicik ailip ekabaka erioŋet loka iserikalei ka ikebwanenok aiganakin korot kama ejaas lukasurup: naarai kipou kenerata kekabaka ebe, “Akan naka Edekekosi ejai kanul kere kaluemoete ŋes, kanuka ajokis; konye apedorike ka anyunyurake eluikini nul kere luinyeinete ŋes.” 23#Ikis 4:29; 1 Siit 5:20; 2 Siit 33:13; Isa 19:22; Yer 29:12,13. Kipou do kaluŋata, kenainaata Edekewok akirot nin: kiira ŋesi ainainakosi.
24Kasodi atiakun itomon ka luarei kokapolok luka isasedotin, Serebia, ka Kasabia, ka itomon koinaeanakee kakes, 25#Esr 7:15,16. ketemok kes epuesa, ka esabu, ka iboro, ŋes ainakinet kanuka etogo loka Edekewok naainak ekabaka ka ikekenerunak, ka ikapolokoke, ka Isirael kere luajaas nen: 26Ketemok cut akanikee atalatan nuka epuesa akwat akanykape ka akaisakany, ka iboro luka epuesa atalatan akwatat; esabu da atalatan akwatat: 27ka atiboi nuka esabu akaisaarei nuka idarikin elukumit; ka iboro iarei luka aisinit naejok naiputut, luka akais nuipu bala esabu. 28#Ikis 33:8; Isa 52:11; Leb 22:2,3; Mar 4:4; 1 Siit 23:28; 2 Siit 24:14. Kabala kes atiar, “Iraas yesi lukalaunak mama ejai EJAKAIT, iboro da eraas lukalaunak; epuesa ka esabu kes ainakinet naka akot mama ejai EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakus. 29#1 Siit 26:20-26; Luk 12:37,38. Kokwenyete, kidarete; aitodol naitemioto kes kakonyen nuka ijaka luka isasedotin ka Ilebin, ka ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaa luka Isirael, koYerusalem, kotoma agolai nuka etogo loka EJAKAIT,” 30Kesodete isasedotin ka Ilebin aijaun aitemis naka epuesa ka esabu, ka iboro, ayaŋaar Yerusalem togo loka Edekewok.
31 # Esr 7:6,9,28; Yob 5:19-24; Isa 41:10-14. Kasodete kanyout koeor Akaba kapaaran nakitomonet kaarei naka elap losodit alosit Yerusalem: kojai akan naka Edekewok kaoni koyu kakan naka lukasurup ka ikaperunok korot, 32#Nek 2:11. Kapotu Yerusalem, kadautu da apaarasia auni kanen. 33Kapaaran nakiwononet aponi kitemoi epuesa ka esabu ka iboro kotogo loka Edekewok kakan naka Meremot okoku loka Uria esasedoti; nepepe kaŋes ajai Eleasar okoku loka Pinekas; nepepe kakes ajai Yosabad okoku loka Yesua, ka Noadia okoku loka Binui, Ilebin; 34kere kwape ejaar ekecetiai ka akecelanir da: kiwadikai alaniru kere kapakio kanun.
35 # Esr 6:17. Koinakis idwe luka aijoreorio lukabonunok kaireneario ainakincta nuicweio mama ejai Edeke loka Isirael, imoŋin lutoba itomon ka luarei kanuka Isirael kere, imerekekin akaisakanykaoŋon ka lukanykape, imerekekin ludidik akaisakanykaarei ka lukanykaarei, ka ikorwa itomon ka luarei araut ainakinet naka adiakaret: koraunos nul kere ainakinet naicweio mama ejai EJAKAIT. 36#Esr 7:21; Isa 56:6; 14:1; Sek 8:1-23. Koinakis lukibukui luka ekabaka, ka ikayaitok lukokekwa juwai aicorakineta nuka ekabaka: kisigalikis ituŋa ka etogo loka Edeke.
