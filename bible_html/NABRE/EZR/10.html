Esera 10

10
1 # Dan 9:20; 2 Siit 20:9. Napilipi Esera, elimori, emonyi akiyo, isudedei koinaren ketogo loka Edeke, kotukokinos neaiai ŋesi koIsirael ekodet loepol noi loka ikiliok ka aijor ka idwe: naarai apotu ituŋa komonyata noi akiyo. 2#Any 34:13. Koboŋok Sekania okoku loka Yekiel, ediopet kodwe luka Elam, kotema Esera atiar, “Kibuea kama ejai Edekewok, aimany aŋor numo nuka atekerin nuka akwap: konye kwape kwana ejai amunonut neejai Isirael kanuka nun, 3#Esr 9:4; Sab 119:53,120; Isa 66:2; Ikis 7:2,3. Aso do ketutukoto aitutuket ka Edekewok aireŋear aŋor kere, ka lueurito kesi, kwape ejaar ainerun naka ejakaitika, ka ŋul luemaranete toma aicorakinet naka Edekewok, ido kiswamaunai kwape eiaar ekisil. 4#1 Siit 28:10; Isa 35:3,4. Konyou; naarai akirot nin nakon, isio da kija kajo; kicata, kiswama.” 5#Nek 5:12. Kosodi Esera anyoun, kisiyapo iiaka luka isasedotin, ka lebin, ka Isirael kere, ebe kiswamaenenete kwape ejaar akirot ŋin. Kiyapoto do.
6 # Ikis 9:18. Kosodi Esera anyoun koinaren ketogo loka Edeke, koloma agola naka Yekokanan okoku loka Eliasib: kedolo, mam abu konyam atap, karaida aimat akipi: naarai abu kokadak kanuka aibucar naka nul kaluka aijoreorio. 7Aponi kolimoroi anatar Yuda ka Yerusalem, mama ejaas idwe kere luka aijoreorio, kotukonokinos Yerusalem; 8#Leb 27:28; Yos 6:19. Ido yenimam ebuni toma apak naka apaarasia auni, kwape ejaar ainerun naka ikapolok ka imakio, kotwania ikeboro kere, ka ŋes elopet kogelarai kokodet loka aijoreorio.
9 # 1 Sam 12:18. Kosodete ituŋa kere luka Yuda ka Benyamin kotukonokinos Yerusalem kotoma apaarasia auni; kerai elap lokikanyetekaonon, kapaaran naka akaisaarei naka elap: kiboikinos ituŋa kere aibois naelal koinaren ketogo loka Edeke, komaranete kanuka akirot kaŋin, kanuka akiru da naapol noi. 10Kobwo Esera esasedoti kolimok kes ebe, “Ipotu kibucasi komanya aŋor numo, kosodete aiyatakin adiakar koIsirael. 11#Awa 28:13; Yer 3:13; Isa 1:16,17; Rom 12:2. Aso do kolimosi mama ejai EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lukapapaakus, kiswamata nuitojokit ŋesi: kogitaros katekerin nuka akwap, ka aŋor numo da.” 12Kosodi ekodet kere aboŋokin keporoto loepol ebe, “Kwape inerar ijo akirokosi, kwape kwaŋin kibusakinitor aswam. 13Konye ipu ituŋa, ido ŋes apak naka akiru naepol, mam kipedori aibwo kokina, ido na mere ŋes aswam naka apaaran adiopet arai aarei: naarai kibuea noi kakirot kana. 14#Mar 25:4; 2 Ika 23:26; 2 Siit 28:11-13; 29:10; 30:8; Sab 78:38; Isa 12:1. Aso koseunai ikapolokokosi kokodet kere, ka nul kere luejaas toma oreriakosi luemanyito aŋor numo kopotu kapakio nueseunitai, ido nepepe kanul, imakio luka nolirc, ka ikatubokoke, aitodol neibelokinor anyunyura naededen naka Edekewok anyoun kamawok, aitodol naedaunor akirot nin.” 15Konye kobwaata Yonatan okoku loka Asakel ka Yaseia okoku loka Tikuba aiŋer akirot na: kiganakis kes Mesulam ka Sabetai Elebit,
16Kiswamata idwe luka aijoreorio kwaŋin. Kotiakunai Esera esasedoti, ka ice kokapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakec, kwape ajaatar ikekia luka lukapapaakec, kes kere ebilitai ikecekiroria; kiboikinos kapaaran nasodit naka elap lokitomonet aiŋic akirot ŋin. 17Kodautu akiro nuka ikiliok kere luamanyito aŋor numo koroko apaaran nasodit naka elap losodit etuboro.
18 # Esr 5:2; Kag 1:1,12; Sek 3:1; 6:11. Kotakanut kodwe luka isasedotin luamanyito aŋor numo: kodwe luka Yesua, okoku loka Yosadak, ka iinacanake, Maaseia, ka Elieser, ka Yarib, ka Gedalia. 19#2 Ika 10:15; 1 Siit 29:24; 2 Siit 30:8; Gal 2:9; Awa 6:1; 22:26; Leb 5:15; 6:4-6. Apotu koriosi akecekanin kwape ireneariata do akecenor; ido naarai agwaunito akiro, koinakis emerekek lokosipan kanuka agwaunikec. 20Kodwe luka Imer; Kanani ka Sebadia. 21Kodwe luka Karim; Maaseia, ka Eliya, ka Semaia, ka Yekiel, ka Usia. 22Kodwe luka Pasur; Elioenai, Maaseia, Isimael, Netanel, Yosabad, ka Elasa.
23KoLebin; Yosabad, ka Simei, ka Kelaia (ŋes ŋol Kelita), Petakia, Yuda ka Elieser. 24Kokayook: Eliasib: kokagolok; Salum, ka Telem, ka Uri.
25 # Esr 2:3; 8:3; Nek 7:8. KoIsirael: kodwe luka Paros; Ramia, ka Isia, ka Malakiya, ka Miyamin, ka Eleasar, ka Malakiya, ka Benaia, 26Kodwe luka Elam; Matania, ka Sekaria, ka Yekiel, ka Abidi, ka Yeremot, ka Eliya. 27#Esr 2:8; Nek 7:13. Kodwe luka Satu; Elioenai, Eliasib, Matania, ka Yeremot, ka Sabad, ka Asisa. 28Kodwe luka Bebai; Yekokanan, Kanania, Sabai, Atalai, 29Kodwe luka Bani; Mesulam, ka Maluk, ka Adaia, ka Yasub, ka Seal, ka Yercmot, 30Kodwe luka Pakat-moab; Adana ka Kelal, ka Benaia, ka Maaseia, ka Matania, ka Besalel, ka Binui, ka Manase. 31#Nek 3:11. Kodwe luka Karim; Elieser, ka Isiya, ka Malakiya, ka. Semaia, ka Simeon; 32Benyamin, ka Maluk, ka Semaria. 33Kodwe luka Kasum; Matenai, ka Matata, ka Sabad, ka Elipelet, ka Yeremai, ka Manase, ka Simei. 34Kodwe luka Bani; Maadai, ka Amuram, ka Uel; 35Benaia, ka Bedeia, ka Keluki; 36Bania, ka Meremot, ka Eliasib; 37Matania, ka Matenai, ka Yaasu; 38ka Bani, ka Binui, ka Simei, 39ka Selemia, ka Natan, ka Adaia; 40Makanadebai, ka Sasai, ka Sarai; 41Asarel, ka Selemia, ka Scmaria; 42Salum, ka Amaria, ka Yosep. 43#Mar 32:38; Esr 2:29. Kodwc luka Nebo; Yeiel, ka Matitia, ka Sabad, ka Sebina, ka Ido, ka Yoel, ka Benaia. 44#Age 6:2-5; Any 23:2; Ikis 7:3. Ŋul kere amanyito aŋor numo; ajaas ke kakesi aŋor nuauritotor idwe.
