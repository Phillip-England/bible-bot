Luka 6

6
1 # Mat 12:1-8; Mk 2:23-28. Kosodi kanen nakitoritor amanat apaaran nakasabato, kijejeata ikekesisiak aluŋuren nuka akima, koninata kakanin konyama. #Ikis 23:25. 2#Yok 5:10. Konye ice koParisayon kotemasi atiar, “Kanukinyo iswamaata yesi numam ibus kapaaran nakasabato?” 3#1 Sam 21:1-6. Koboŋok Yesu kotema kes atiar, “Mam isiomito na nakiswamai Daudi, nakeriebia eteŋei, ŋes ka ikepaup; 4#Leb 24:9. kwape kelomaria togo loka Edeke, kolem imugatin luka aitodikin, konyam, koinak da ikepaup; lumam ibus ainyam dimarai isasedotin bonik?” 5Kotema kes atiar, “Okoku loka etuŋanan ŋes ejakait loka asabato.”
6 # Mat 12:9-14; Mk 3:1-6. Kosodi kanen, kanakasabato ace, koloma toma atukos kisisianak: kojai nen etuŋanan, lokewonit akekan nakoteten. 7#Luk 14:1. Kiteikinito ikewadikak ka Iparisayon, aanyun arai itaŋaleuni kapaaran nakasabato; tetere edumunete akirot nakiwosaet ŋes. 8Konye kojeni akeceomisio; kolimok etuŋanan lokejai akan nakewonit ebe, “Konyou, kobwo kokidiŋ.” Konyou ŋesi kobwo. 9Kotema Yesu kes atiar, “Keŋisit yes ebe, Ibus kereka aswam nuejok kapaaran nakasabato, arai bo aswam nueroko, aitaiar aijar arai bo amudiar?” 10Kosesen kes kere, kolimok je ebe, “Korio akonikan.” Kikonak nen: koŋaleu akekan, 11Konye kilelebun kes anyunyura, kinerut bonik nuiswamakinete Yesu,
12 # Mk 3:13-19. Kosodi kanen kapaarasia kaŋun, kolot oroŋat ailip; kilip Edeke akuare kiya, 13#Mat 10:2-4; Yok 6:70; Asw 1:13. Kiwala akwap, konyarau ikekesisiak; koseu itomon ka luarei kotomakec, luabu komaik ikiyakia; 14Simon, loamaikini Petero, ka Anderea onaceke, ka Yakob ka Yokana, ka Pilip ka Batolomayo, 15ka Matayo ka Tomas, ka Yakob okoku loka Alupayo, ka Simon loenyaritai Selot, 16ka Yuda okoku loka Yakob, ka Yuda Isikariot, lokebilakini ŋes ebotet.
17 # Mat 4:23–5:1; Mk 3:7-12. Kikokorun nepepe kakes, kobwo kaibois kanaeriana, ka ekodet loepol loka ikekesisiak, ka ituŋa luipu noi lukepote koYudaya kere ka Yerusalem, ka ariri nakaŋolol nako Turo ka Sidon, lukepote akiirar ŋes, ka aitaŋaleunio akecedekesia: 18ŋul da lukiticanete imoyon lueroko aponi kitaŋaleunoi. 19Kokotos lukokodet kere atiror ŋes; naarai kolom apedor kamakeŋ kitaŋaleu kes kere.
20 # Mat 5:3,4,6,11,12. Kikeu akekonye kosesen ikekesisiak, kotema atiar,
“Isirereŋitoi yes lukican; naarai ajakanut naka Edeke erai nakus,
21 # Apu 7:16,17; Sab 126:5,6; Isa 61:3. “Isirereŋitoi yes lukiriebi eteŋei kwana; naarai imonikinos.
“Isirereŋitoi yes luimonyete kwana: naarai ikieniete.
22 # 1 Pet 4:14; Yok 15:19; 16:2. “Isirereŋitoi yes, nakiŋaruniata ituŋa, kigitarete yes, kimoromoroete, ecakarete ekusikiror bala ibore yenerono, kanuka Okoku loka etuŋanan, 23#2 Siit 36:16; Asw 7:52. Kolelakis ka eicur apaaran ŋin: naarai koany, epol ekusiropit kotoma akui: naarai kikonakis, lukapapaakec inabin kwaŋin.
24 # Yak 5:1. “Konye itukwi yes lukabarak! Naarai idaut adumun akusiitaakario,
25 # Isa 5:22. “Itukwi yes, luimonikinos kwana! Naarai ebuni eteŋei airieb yes.
“Itukwi yes, luikienasi kwana! Naarai ipote aibworo ka aimony.
26 # Yak 4:4; Mik 2:11. “Itukwi yes, lukiturete kwana kere! Naarai kikonakis lukapapaakec inabin lukeŋalak kwaŋin.
27 # Mat 5:39-48. “Konye katemari yes luipupete atiar, Kominasi lukasurupokus, kikonakinete nuejok ŋul lukinaras yes, 28kilipakis arereŋ ŋul lukilamete, kilipakis da ŋul lukireito yes. 29Yenikibapi eitinyo, kibelok loce da; yenikidemari ekonilou, sirikimik ekaso da. 30Koinak ŋinituŋanan kere yenikilipi; yenikidemari jo ikoniboro sirikiburau bobo. 31#Mat 7:12. Kwape ikototor ituŋa aikonakin yes, kikonakis yesi da kes kwape kwaŋin.
32“Arai iminasi ŋul lukiminasi yes, kimalao kanuka inyo? Naarai ikadiakanarak da eminasi lueminasi kes. 33Arai da iswamakinete nuejok ŋul lukiswamakinete yes nuejok, kimalao kanuka inyo? Naarai ikadiakanarak da iswamaete kwape kwaŋin, 34#Leb 25:35,36. Arai da iyuunete ŋul luimunos adumun kamakec, kimalao kanuka inyo? Ikadiakanarak da eyuunete ikadiakanarak tetere edumunete ŋupeŋul bobo. 35Konye kominasi lukasurupokus, kiswamakisi nuejok, koyuut, komam ijuruŋuŋito; epol rwaru ekusiropit, iraunete yesi idwe luka Je kaloejai kuju kakuj: naarai esiana ŋesi mama kalumam imalaete ka lueroko da. 36Koraas luketimok, kwape eraar Papakus loketimon.
37 # Mat 7:1-29. “Siritubonokinete, yes da kinyeis atubokinio: sirisitelekanarete, yes da kinyeis aisitelekario; kisioniete, yes da kisionio; #Mat 6:14. 38#Mk 4:24. koinakisi, yes da kiinakinio; aitemis naejok, naicikakinitai, naijaŋijaŋitai, kiton abukonor, ŋin ŋes kiinakinete ituŋa atorobusiokus. Naarai aitemis naitemioto, ŋipeŋin kitemere yes da.”
39 # Mat 15:14; 23:16,24. Kolimok kes awaragan ebe, “Epedori kereka emuduki aiŋarenikin emuduki? Mam ibirokinos kere iarei toma aipany? 40#Mat 10:24-25; Yok 13:16; 15:20. Mam ekesisian itelekari ekesisianakinanike, konye ŋinituŋanan yenituutooritai cut erianakinos ka ekesisianakinanike. 41Aso kanukinyo isesenia igugut yenejai akoŋu naka onacekon, komam ipodo akitoi naejai akoŋukon kilopet? 42Arai ipedori biai alimokin onacekon ebe, ‘Onaceka, kinyek kalem igugut yeneiai akoŋukon’, komam iseseni akitoi naejai akoŋukon kilopet? Yenikesoswai ijo, kolem ber akitoi naejai akoŋukon, kosodi konyet asesen kotenan alemun igugut kakoŋu naka onacekon.
43“Naarai emamei ekitoi loejok loerai araito nukaronok; karaida ekitoi loerono loerai araito nuejok. 44#Mat 12:33. Naarai ejenunio ŋolikitoi kanuka araitoke. Naarai kotoma okukwa mam ituŋa edeŋete itinin, karaida toma okodokodo aideŋ imusabibun. 45#Mat 12:34,35. Itolomuni ituŋanan yenejok nuejok kotoma kanuejok nuiŋadakinit otauke; ituŋanan da yenikaronon itolomuni nukaronok kotoma kanueroko nuiŋadakinit: naarai inera akeituk nuilapununos kotauke. 46#Mal 1:6; Mat 7:21. Kanukinyo kinyaritotor eoŋ ebe, ‘Ejakait, Ejakait,’ kwabo mam iswamaete nuabala eoŋ? 47Ŋinituŋanan kere yenebuni mamakaŋ, kiira da akakiro ka aswam kes, ketodikini yes kwape eputor: 48Eputos bala ekadukon etogo, loeboki kwap noi, epesikini apetetait atiida; naeleleunia akalel, naeretonokinotor akip nukecor togo ŋol, mam epedori aijaŋijaŋa: naarai edukitoi ejok. 49Konye yenepupuni, komam iswamai, eputos bala lokeduki eketogo alup komamei apetetait; koretonokinos akip nukecor togo ŋol, atipet kibiror; kepol da atiyakinike.”
