Luka 5

5
1 # Mat 13:1-2; Mk 3:9-10; 4:1. Kosodi kanen nakeridakiniata ŋes ikodeta iirarete akirot naka Edeke, kebwoi ŋesi kariri kaŋolol naka Genesaret. 2Koany atakerin aarei nukeiaas oitolima loka aŋolol; konye kedokunito ikalokak ilosete iboyanakec. 3Kodoka ataker adiopet, nakerai naka Simon, kilip ŋes aisukunyar nediokasilon anyoun koitolima, Kiboikin kisisianak ikodeta kojai ataker. 4#Yok 21:6. Nakedau einer, kotema ŋesi Simon atiar, “Koremo ataker kiton ataro, kocakakis iboyanakus aikamia lukecor.” 5#Yok 21:3. Koboŋok Simon kotema atiar, “Ejakait, kipou ketaaritos akuare kiya, komam kidumuni idiobore: konye kanuka akonikirot, acakakini bo iboyan.” 6#Yok 21:6. Kedaut aswam ŋun, kipokata iboro lukecor luipu noi: kokotosi ikeceboyan aŋed; 7kokwetut lukaupakec lukejaas ataker ace aponare aiŋarakin kes. Kopotu, kisilelebata atakerin kere aarei kiton neebuciata alumor. 8Konye keanyu Simon Petero ŋun, kibirokin kwap akuŋin nuka Yesu, kobala ebe, “Wu Ejakait, kinyeinai, naarai arai eoŋ ekadiakanaran.” 9Naarai koumok ŋesi, ka ŋul kere lukejaas kaŋes, kanuka aipues naka lukecor lukikamunete: 10#Mat 13:47. koumokis da Yakob ka Yokana, odwe luka Sebedayo, lukemoranara ka Simon. Kotema Yesu Simon atiar, “Sirikikurianar; amear natikwana iraun ekalokan ituŋa.” 11#Mat 19:27. Nakedauniata aitodokun atakerinikec oitolima, kinyeis kere, kotupasi ŋes.
12 # Mat 8:1-4; Mk 1:40-45. Kosodi kanen, nakejaar ore ediopet, kojai etuŋanan loelelebikina atapon: nakeanyunia ŋesi Yesu, kibirokin kwap ebuburia, kiŋaiŋa kotema atiar, “Ejakait, arai ikoto, ipedori aiswagar eoŋ.” 13Korio Yesu akekan, kotiro ŋes, kotema atiar, “Akoto; kiswagar.” Atipet konyou atapon kamakeŋ. 14#Leb 14:1-32; 13:49. Kicor ŋes sirikelimoki idiotuŋanan; konye ebe, “Kolot, kitodiar mama ejai esasedoti, koinak kanuka aiswagariokon kwape kolo kicorakinitor Musa, araun aitejenunet kamakec.” 15Konye kiyatakinos akekiro aisiarar: kotukonokinos ikodeta luipu akiirar, ido aitaŋaleunio adekesiakec. 16#Mk 1:35. Konye ŋes kogitar kolot amodiŋ, kilip.
17 # Mat 9:1-8; Mk 2:1-12. Kosodi kanen kapaaran adiopet kaŋun, isisianakini ŋesi: ido kiboiete Iparisayon ka ikesisianakinak luka ikisila kanen, lukepote koreria kere luko Galilaya ka Yudaya ka Yerusalem: kojai agogoŋ naka Ejakait kaŋes aitaŋaleun. 18Ido koany, kibwokut ituŋa etuŋanan loesenyit koitada: kokotos aitolomar ŋes toma, ka aibwaikin akonyeneke, 19Nakekurwosi aitolomar kanuka ekodet, kodokasi kuju oswi, kijoliliut ŋes aitorunio ametegulan koitadake kokidiŋ, akonyen nuka Yesu, 20Nakeanyunia ŋesi akeceiyuun kotema atiar, “Ijo ba, esionii akonidiakanareta.” 21#Luk 7:49; Isa 43:25; Yok 5:12. Kogeut ikewadikak ka Iparisayon epeget kobalas ebe, “Tŋai bo lo loinera nukainyanakin Edeke? Iŋai bo epedori aisioni adiakanareta, dimarai Edeke bon?” 22Konye kojeni Yesu ekecepeget, koboŋok kotema kes atiar, “Kanukinyo ipegatatar kotauonokus? 23Ani bo naepatana, atemar ebe, ‘Esionii akonidiakanareta,’ arai bo atemar ebe, ‘Konyou kolot?’ 24Konye tetere ijenunete ebe ejai Okoku loka etuŋanan apedor kakwap aisioni adiakanareta,” (kolimok lokasenyan ebe,) “Katemari jo atiar, Konyou, kibwok ekoniitada, kolot orekon.” 25Atipet konyou kakonyenekec, kibwok akeperit, kolot oreke, itopoloorit Edeke. 26Koumokis kere, kitopoloorito Edeke; kilelebunos akurian, kobalas ebe, “Kiany lolo nukaumokin.”
27 # Mat 9:9-13; Mk 2:13-17. Kokau kaŋun koloma, koany eketeleman esolo, ekekiror Lebi, iboie kaitelemis, kotema ŋes atiar, “Kotupunai.” 28Kinyek kere, konyoi: kotupa ŋes,
29 # Luk 15:1. Kipoik Lebi ŋes epucit loepol koreke: kojau ekodet loepol laka iketelemak esolo ka ke lukemorete kakes, 30#Luk 15:1-2. Kirurumakis Iparisayon ka ikewadikakakec ikekesisiak kobalas ebe, “Kanukinyo inyamiata imasiata da nepepe ka iketelemak esolo ka ikadiakanarak da?” 31Koboŋok Yesu kotema kes atiar, “Lukaŋaleok mam ipudas emurwon; dimarai lukadekak. 32Mam abunit anyarit ikadolitok, dimarai ikadiakanarak tetere itoronikinos.” 33#Mat 9:14-17; Mk 2:18-22. Kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Eluŋete ikesisiak luka Yokana irwan ipu, ka ailip; ikote nen da ikesisiak luka Iparisayon; konye lukon enyamete emasete da.” 34Kotema Yesu kes atiar, “Ipedorete kereka aituluŋ luenyaritai opucit loka eiterane, naejaatar keteran? 35Konye eponito apaarasia, nuedemarere eteran, eluŋete do kapaarasia kaŋun.” 36Kolimok kes awaragan, ebe, “Emamei yeneciluni atutubet kanapito nakitetet, ka adonyokin ŋes anapito namojoŋ; arai kikonaki nen eciluni nakitetet, atutubet da naecilunio kama kanakitetet, mam ecamanaros kanamoion. 37Emamei da yenebukokini ebino loitet oriagin lukemukule lumoioŋ; arai kikonaki nen, ecili ebino loitet iriagin, ebukor ebino, imadiŋikinos iriagin da. 38Konye ebukonokinio ebino loitet oriagin lukitetiak. 39Kwabo emamei yenemasi ebino loikuniatai, yenekoto loitet; naarai ebala ebe, ‘Ejok loikuniatai.’ ”
