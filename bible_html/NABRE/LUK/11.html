Luka 11

11
1 # Luk 5:33. Kosodi kanen, nakilipia kaibois adiopet, nakedaunia, kotema ediopet kosesisiakake atiar, “Ejakait, kisisianakinai sio ailip, kwape kisisianakinia Yokana da ikekesisiak.” 2#Mat 6:9-13. Kotema kes atiar, “Nailipiata, kineras ebe, Papakosi loejai toma akuj, ekonikiror kitalaunai. Akonijakanut kobu. Nuikoto kiswamai kakwap, kwape iswamaere kotoma akuj. 3Koinanakinai sio ikosinyamat luka ŋinipaaran; 4Kisionioi sio akosidiakanareta; naarai kisioniei isio lukapeseninikosi. Sirikiya sio toma acodunio, konye koyuunai sio kama ejai lokaronon.”
5Kotema kes atiar, “Iŋai kayesi yenejai ekepapero, kelot mama ejai kidiŋ kuare, kotema ŋes atiar, ‘Opaperoka, koyuunai ber kimugatin iuni; 6naarai adolu ekapapero mamaka ebuni kalosit, emamei eoŋ idiobore yenainakini ŋes ainyam’; 7#Mat 26:10. eboŋokini lokotogo ebe, ‘Sirikiitapas eoŋ; egolol ekek, kiperi eoŋ nepepe kikadwe koitada; mam apedori anyoun ainakin jo’? 8#Luk 18:5. Katemari yes atiar, Karaida mam enyouni ainakin naarai erai ekepapero, konye kanuka akeiŋaiŋait enyouni einakini ŋes luipuda kere. 9#Mat 7:7-11. Katemari yes atiar, Kilipa, kiinakinio; komoata, idumunete; kikuŋukuŋoto, kiŋaikinio, 10Naarai ŋin yenilipi ijaikinio, yenemoi edumuni, ka yenikuŋukuŋi da enaikinio. 11Iŋai bo kayesi erai papa, arai kilipi ikokuke emugati, einakini tope amoru? Arai bo agar, ido aibois kagar einakini tope ŋes emun? 12Arai kilip abeet, einakini tope ekiriton? 13Aso arai yes lukaronok iienete ainakin ikusidwe ainakineta nuejok, mam kereka itelekarit noi Papakus loejai toma akuj ainakin ŋul luilipete ŋes Emoyo Lokalaunan?”
14 # Mat 12:22-30,43-45; Mk 3:22-27. Kosodi kanen ireŋeari ŋesi ajokit nabaŋ, Kelom ajokit, kiner lobaŋ; koumokis ikodeta. 15#Mat 9:34; 10:25. Konye kobalas ice kakesi ebe, “Ireŋeari ŋesi ajokin kanuka Beelesebul ekapolon loka ajokin.” 16#Mat 12:38; 16:1; Mk 8:11. Kocodut ice, kokotosi aanyunet naebuni kotoma akuj. 17Konye kejeni ŋesi akeceomisio, kotema kes atiar, “Ŋinijakanut naetiakatiakaun asurokin bon emudiar; arai etogo ketogo ibiror. 18Aso arai etiakatiakaun Satan bon, ikonakini ai akejakanut icasi? Naarai ibalasi yesi ebe ereŋeari eoŋ ajokin kanuka Beelesebul. 19Aso arai ereŋeari eoŋ ajokin kanuka Beelesebul, ido bo ikusidwe ireŋearete kanuka iŋai? Kanuka ŋun ŋes eraatar ŋul ikusikatubok. 20#Any 8:19. Konye arai ereŋeari eoŋ ajokin kebokorit loka Edeke, aso adolu ajakanut naka Edeke mamakus, 21Etuŋanan lokagogon ekapi lujiisike idari alaroke, inapakina ikeboro: 22#Kol 2:15. Konye naebunokinia ekagogoŋon loedeparit ŋes, ipikori edemari lujiisike kere luamunokina ŋesi, ka akorokor iŋatanake, 23#Mk 9:40; Luk 9:50. Ituŋanan yenimam ejai keoŋ, ŋes yenikekoloka; ituŋanan da yenimam etukonokini keoŋ, isiarakini.
24“Kelonu emoyo loerono kotuŋanan, itori aiboisio nuemameete akip, emoot nekiyeŋunet; keoliki, ebala ebe, ‘Aboŋori togoka nekabunio.’ 25Keboŋu, edumuni epieitai, epesitai da. 26#Yok 5:14. Kosodi do elosi eupunos kimoyon ice ikanykaarei luitelekarito ŋes aronis; elomarete kesi da esalete nen; ejautene loka ituŋanan kaŋin lokawasia eraun loerono adepar losodit.”
27 # Luk 1:28,42,48. Kosodi kanen, napinera akiro ŋun, kiworo adiopeberu kotoma okodet, kotema atiar, “Isirereŋitoi aliliget nakipotiere jo, ka ikisin da lukinaki.” 28#Luk 8:15,21. Konye Yesu kobala ebe, “Konye ca, nuitelekarito, isirereŋitoi luiirarete akirot naka Edeke, koricata da!”
29 # Mat 12:38-42. Kosodi naketukokinotor ikodeta mamakeŋ, kogeu atemar kes atiar, “Atur na erai atur nakaronon; emoot aanyunet, kwabo mam einakinio aanyunet, dimarai aanyunet naka Yona. #Mat 16:4; Mk 8:12; 1 Kor 1:22. 30#Yok 3:4. Naarai kwape keraar Yona aanyunet kama ejaas ituŋa luko Ninebe, kwape kwaŋin eraunia Okoku loka etuŋanan kama ejai atur na, 31#1 Ika 10:1-10; 2 Siit 9:1-12. Enyouni akabaka naka Eŋalakimak kapaaran naka etube nepepe kituŋa luka atur kana, itetelekari kes akiro; naarai abu ŋesi kobu kosaŋe loka akwap akiirar acoa naka Solomon, ido koany, ejai ne yenitelekarit Solomon. 32#Yok 3:5. Enyounete ikiliok luka Ninebe kapaaran naka etube nepepe katur na itetelekarete ŋes akiro; naarai apotu kesi kitoronikinos kanuka alimonokin naka Yona, ido koany, ejai ne yenitelekarit Yona, 33#Mat 5:15; Mk 4:21; Luk 8:16. Emamei yeninokokini atala kibwaik aipany arai toma obukit, dimarai ikajakini ocoget, tetere epedorete luelomunete aanyun aica. 34#Mat 6:22,23. Atala naka akuan ŋes akonikoŋu; arai oteni akonikoŋu, akonikuan kere ecai; konye arai erono, akonikuan da emutono. 35Aso kocoite kinye aica nakotomakon araun amuton. 36Aso arai ecai akonikuan kere, komamei adiotutubet naemutono, ileleba kere aica pas, kwape katala nakinwakakinit jo kaicake.”
37 # Luk 7:36; 14:1. Napinera, konyara Eparisayot ediopet oreke aimor kaŋes lukapaaran; koloma kiboikin ainyam. 38#Mat 15:2. Keanyu Eparisayot ŋol koumok, naarai mam ber kilosit eroko enyama. 39#Mat 23:1-36. Kotema Ejakait ŋes atiar, “Kwana yes Iparisayon itesegarete kiŋa kakolin ka asanin, konye kotomakus ileleba aiŋat ka aronis, 40Ibaŋiok yesi! Lokesubi lukokuiu mere ŋes lokesubi lukotoma da? 41Konye koinakisi lukotoma araut ainakineta nukaitim; ido koany, elaete kere kamakus.
42 # Leb 27:30. “Konye itukwi yes Iparisayon! Naarai iinanakinete idiopet kotomon yenika alilot ka ecaboi ka ŋinikiyot kere, inyeinete etube ka amin naka Edeke; ibusakinit yes aswam ŋun komam inyeinete kwa da. 43#Luk 20:46. Itukwi yes Iparisayon! Naarai iminasi iicoloŋo lukapolou katukosia, ka aiyogao kagwelanareta. 44Itukwi yes! Naarai iputos katesin numam etakanete, nuitorito ituŋa komam ejenete.”
45Koboŋok ediopet koricakisila kotema ŋes atiar, “Ekesisianakinan, kiner ijo kwaŋin kimoromor sio da.” 46Kotema atiar, “Itukwi yes da iricakisila! Naarai isibwokete ituŋa ibwokon lukalaŋirok, konye yes ikulepek mam itirorete ibwokon kediopet kobokorokus. 47Itukwi yes! Naarai idukete atesin nuka inabin luapotu lukapapaakus koara. 48Aso iraas ikajenak icamunito da aswamisio nuka lukapapaakus; naarai kes lukearete, yes da idukete atesinakec, 49Kanuka ŋun inera acoa naka Edeke ebe, ‘Ejukakini kes inabin ka ikiyakia, earete ice kakes, ereete,’ 50tetere itepesenio atur na aokot nuka inabi; kere, nukebukoros ameun kapetetait naka akwap, 51#Age 4:8; 2 Siit 24:20-21. ameun kaokot nuka Abel kiton aokot nuka Sakaria, lokearakinio kidiŋ ketem ka aibois nakalaunan. Eebo, katemari yes atiar, Itepesenio kotoma atur kana. 52Itukwi yes iricakisila! Naarai ilemut alacetait naka aijen; mam yes ilomarito, ido itikokinito luakotos alomar.”
53Nakelomunia kanen, kogeut ikewadikak ka Iparisayon aridakin jokan, ka aiŋitiŋit akiro nuipu, 54#Luk 20:20. kolokata ŋes tetere ipoki kakirot naelomuni kaitukuke.
