Iromayon 8

8
1Aso do kwana emamei ŋul luejaas toma Kristo Yesu atubokinio. 2#Rom 3:27; 7:23,24. Naarai kaosikis eoŋ ikisila luka Emoyo loka aijar loejai toma Kristo Yesu anyoun kokisila luka adiakar ka atwanare. 3#Yok 1:14; Pil 2:7; Asw 13:38; 15:10; Bur 2:17; 4:15. Naarai ŋun numam apedorito ikisila, kanuka akalanyanut naka akuan, abu Edeke, naejukunia Okokuke elopet kaput naka akuan nakadiakaran ido kanuka adiakar, kotubok adiakar kotoma akuan, 4#Gal 5:16,25. tetere aicorakineta nuka ikisila itodolikinio kotomawok, lumam kilosenenei atupar akuan dimarai atupar Emoyo. 5Naarai ŋul luetuparete akuan eomitos nuka akuan, konye ŋul luetuparete Emoyo, eomitos nuka Emoyo. 6#Rom 6:21. Naarai aomit naka akuan ŋes atwanare, konye aomit naka Emoyo ŋes aijar ka ainapakin. 7#Mat 12:34; Yak 4:4; Yok 8:43; 12:39. Kanuka ŋun aomit naka akuan ŋes aitusurup Edeke; naarai mam enonokikinit ikisila luka Edeke, kwabo mam epedori anonokikin; 8kes da luejaas toma akuan mam epedorete aisiyalamikin Edeke.
9 # 1 Kor 3:16; 12:3. Konye arai iboie Emoyo loka Edeke kotomakus, mam ijaas toma akuan dimarai toma Omoyo. Arai da emamei ituŋanan Emoyo loka Kristo, mam ŋin erai yenike. 10#Gal 2:20; Pil 1:21; 1 Pet 4:6. Konye arai ejai Kristo kotomakus, atwakata awatekus kanuka adiakar, konye ejarete ikusimoyon kanuka adolit. 11#1 Kor 3:16. Konye arai iboie Emoyo loka ŋol lokitajaruni Yesu kokatwak kotomakus, ŋol lokitajaruni Kristo Yesu kokatwak einakini da akusiwat nuetwakete aijar kanuka Ekemoyo loiboie kotomakus.
12 # Rom 6:7,18. Aso do, iinacan, kija kapesen, mere mama ejai akuan atupar aijar naka akuan, 13#Gal 6:8; Pes 4:22-24. naarai arai ijarete atupar akuan, ikotosi atwakare, konye arai kitwataniasi aswamisio nuka akuan kanuka Emoyo, ijarete do. 14Naarai kere luiŋarenikinit Emoyo loka Edeke, ŋul kes odwe luka Edeke, 15#Gal 4:5-7. Naarai mam idumunito emoyo loka ebulesene aboŋor bobo akurian, dimarai idumunito emoyo loka araun odwe. Kanuka ŋol kimonya ebe, “Abba! Papa!” #Mk 14:36; Gal 4:5,6; 2 Tim 1:7. 16#2 Kor 1:22. Emoyo elopet itejenikini imoyonowok ebe kirai idwe luka Edeke, 17#Gal 4:7; Apu 21:7. arai kirai idwe, kirai da ikerumak, ikerumak luka Edeke, luirumete nepepe ka Kristo, arai ca kiiticani nepepe kaŋes, tetere kitubusikinio da kaŋes.
18 # 2 Kor 4:17; Rom 5:2. Aomit eoŋ ebe aiticanio naka apak kana mam ibusakinit aitiirianio kaibuses naekoto apukokinio oni. 19#Kol 3:4. Naarai idarete isuban keimiel noi apukokinio naka odwe luka Edeke; 20#Age 3:17-19; 5:29; Lim 1:2. naarai kitononokikinai isuban aamameak, mere kanuka eipudikec, dimarai kanuka ŋol lokitononokikini kotoma amun; 21#2 Pet 3:13; 1 Yok 3:2. naarai eosikinio isuban da anyoun kobulesene loka aibos, alomar aosikinio naka aibuses naka idwe luka Edeke. 22Naarai kijeni ebe idinyasi isuban kere, eriebasi da nepepe kiton tinenikwana; 23#2 Kor 5:2-4; Gal 5:5. kwabo mere ŋul bon, dimarai oni da ikulepek luejaas araito nugeet nuka Emoyo, kidinya oni da kotomawok, kedareto araut odwe, ŋes atemar ebe alakun naka awatewok 24#2 Kor 5:7. Naarai kiponi kitajari kanuka amun. Konye ketakanu ibore yenikemunoi emamei bobo amun. Naarai iŋai tope emunokina ibore yenetakani? 25Konye arai kimunokina yenimam etakani, kidaret katitiŋikin.
26Kwape kwaŋin da isigalikini Emoyo akalanyanutuwok; naarai mam kijeni kwape ibusakinitor ailip, konye kinainaanakin Emoyo oni keidiny lomam inerio, 27#Sab 139:1; 1 Kor 4:5. Ŋol da loirerei itauon ejeni aomisio nuka Emoyo, kwape ejaatar, naarai iŋaiŋaaŋakini lukalaunak kwape ekotor Edeke, 28#Pes 1:11; 3:11. Kijeni ebe iswamaete iboro kere nepepe kanuka ajokis mama ejaas ŋul lueminasi Edeke, ŋes atemar ŋul lukenyaraunio kanuka alosikiniteke. 29#Kol 1:18; Bur 1:6. Naarai ŋul luabu kojenu sek, abu koseu da sek aituput kes aput naka Okokuke, tetere erauni ŋesi ekiyai kotoma oinacan luipu. 30Ŋul da luabu koseu sek, konyarau da kes; ŋul da luabu konyarau, kitojok da kes; ŋul da luabu kitojok, kitubusik da kes.
31 # Sab 118:6; Mat 1:23. Aso do kinera anu kaŋun? Arai ejai Edeke kanuwok iŋai tope kisurokini oni? 32#Yok 3:16. Ŋol lomam abu kimik Okokuke elopet, konye koinak ŋes kanuwok kere, mam kereka kikorakini iboro kere da nepepe kaŋes? 33#Isa 50:8. Iŋai bo iwosai luseuna luka Edeke? Edeke itojokit; 34#Sab 110:1; 1 Yok 2:1. iŋai bo etubokini? Kristo Yesu loketwani, konye aitelekarit lokitajarunio, loejai akan nakoteten naka Edeke, loiŋaiŋaanakini oni. 35Iŋai bo kitiaki oni kamin naka Kristo? Arai bo ican, arai aridakinio, arai areio, arai ebeli, arai amujalal, arai aronis, arai ekileŋ? 36#Sab 44:22; 2 Kor 4:11. Kwape iwadikatere ebe,
“Kanukon kiario aitiwar akwap,
Kimario bala amerekekin nutubutubo.”
37 # Yok 16:33. Konye kotoma ŋun kere kipikori noi kanuka ŋol lokimina oni. 38Naarai etogogoŋi ebe karaida atwanare, karaida aiiar, karaida imalaikan, karaida lueyaito, karaida nuejaas, karaida nuejaunete, karaida lukapedor, 39karaida amukurur, karaida aidules, karaida isubat kere, mam kipedorete aitiak oni kamin naka Edeke naejai toma Kristo Yesu Ejakaitiwok.
