Aswamisio 26

26
1Kobala Agiripa Paul ebe, “Kinup jo einer kanukon.” Kosodi korio Paul akekan, koiyar, kobala ebe:
2“Wu Ekabaka Agiripa, kekemer naarai kaiyar lolokakonyenekon kanuka akiro kere nukewosatotor Iyudayan, 3aitelekarit naarai ijeni ijo iponesio kere ka aiŋiseta da kere nuejaas oYudayan. Kanuka ŋun kemaimait kopupok eoŋ katitiŋikinit. 4Naarai ejenete Iyudayan kere ekajautene ameun keitelepane, lokejai kageunet kotoma atekereka, ido ko Yerusalem da. 5#Asw 23:6; Pil 3:5. Apotu kajenut ameun apeŋo, arai ekotosi aitejenun, ebe abu karai Eparisayot lokorioŋet loetokit noi kotoma odinikosi. 6#Asw 28:20. Kwana da abwoi kane atubokinio kanuka amun aisumunet nakisumunit Edeke lukapapaakosi, 7#Asw 24:15. naemunosi atekerinikosi atomon ka nuarei adolokin, emaenenete Edeke kainyikokinit paaran ka kuare da. 8Kanukinyo eomitere kamakus araut akirot namam ecamuna ebe itaiari Edeke ikatwak?
9 # Asw 8:3; 22:4-5. “Abeit abu eoŋ elopet kaomite ebe kebusakinit aswam nuipu airebokin ekiror loka Yesu Enasarayot. #Asw 9:1-29; 22:3-21. 10Abu keswama ŋun ko Yerusalem; abu kaen ice kalukalaunak aidaris, kadau ainakinio apedor kaneejaas isasedotin lupeti, ido da abu kekeik akan luaario. 11Ido irwan ipu abu keticanenenei kes katukosia kere, ketiŋaikinite ainyanakin Edeke; kareite kes kaibworit kiton oreria Iukokiŋa da.
12“Kasodi alosit Damasiko kanuka aswam kaŋin, kija kapedor ka anupakinio naka isasedotin lupeti. 13Kosodi kidiŋ paaran, wu ekabaka, abu kaany aica nakebuni kokuju, nakedeparit ailiya naka akoloŋ, kacaikin eoŋ ka ŋul lukija nenipe keoŋ. 14Kebirokinos isio kere kwap, keira eporoto lokinera keoŋ kaŋajep naka Aburanian ebe, ‘Saul, Saul, kanukinyo kireitor eoŋ? Kitionikinit ijo aipet iketetin.’ 15Kabala ebe, ‘Iŋai bo ijo, Ejakait?’ Katema eoŋ Ejakait atiar, ‘Eoŋ Yesu loireit ijo. 16#Esk 2:1,3. Konye konyou, kobwo kakejekon; naarai ketakanikin jo kanuka nu, adakakin jo araun ekejaanakinan ka ekajenan loka akiro nuibu koanyunia eoŋ, ido kaŋun nuketakanikinor, 17#Yer 1:7; 1 Siit 16:35. kayuuni jo kama kituŋakon ka Imo da-lukeyakiari mamakec, 18#Ikis 33:3; Isa 35:5; 42:7,16; Asw 20:32; Pes 2:2; Kol 1:13. aitwekenyun akecekonye, tetere ibelokinos ainyein amuton kesesenata aica, ainyein da apedor naka Satan kosesenata Edeke, kosodete adumun aisionio adiakanareta, ka airumet kokidiŋ; kaŋul kaluitalaunitai kanuka aiyuun naejai tomakaŋ.’
19 # Gal 1:16. “Kanuka ŋun, wu Ekabaka Agiripa, mam abu kepaliik aitapoet nakakuj, 20#Mat 3:8. konye abu kageu alimonokin luko Damasiko, kosodi koYerusalem, kotoma akwap kere da naka Iyudayan, ka Imo da, ebe kitoronikinos, kibelokinos mama ejai Edeke, iswamaete aswamisio nuibusakinito aitoronikin. 21#Asw 21:30,31. Kanuka ŋun ŋes kekamuniata Iyudayan kotoma oyekalu, katamakis aiar da. 22#Luk 24:44-47. Aso kiton lolo adum aisigalikinio naebuni kama ejai Edeke, abwoi aitejenun ludidik ka lukapolok da, kwabo mam eneri akirot dimarai ŋun nukinerasi Musa ka inabin ebe ejaunete: 23#Isa 42:6; 49:6; 1 Kor 15:20. ebe ibusakinit Kristo aiticanio, ido da korau ŋesi losodit ajarun kokatwak, elimonokini aica mama ejaas ituŋakosi ka Imo da.”
24Kosodi nakeiyaror kwaŋin, kotema Pesito keporoto loepol atiar, “Kikerepiki cama ijo, Paul, kijulaki jo aisisiakon naepol.” 25Konye kobala Paul ebe, “Mam kekerepikinit, wu Pesito lokaibuses, dimarai enera akiro nukabeit ka nukapirianut da obe. 26#Yok 18:20. Naarai ejeŋi ekabaka nuka akiro kaŋun, eoŋ da enera kaŋes katitiŋ; naarai etogogoŋit ebe emamei akirot kotoma kaŋun naimunonikinitai ŋes, naarai mam nu eswamas kagor. 27Ekabaka Agiripa, iyuunit tope ijo inabin? Ajeni ebe iyuunit.” 28#Asw 11:26; 1 Pet 4:16. Kotema Agiripa Paul atiar, “Kanaesupaana ikoto aituluor eoŋ da Ekristayot!” 29Koboŋok Paul ebe, “Arai kanaesupaana, arai kanaepol elipi Edeke mere ijo bon, konye kaŋul da kere lukapupete lolo, koraut kwape keoŋ-dimarai aeneta nu.”
30Kosodi kobwo ekabaka, ka ekayaiton ka Berenike, ka ŋul lukiboiete kakes; 31napenyounete, kineranakinos ebe, “Mam etuŋanan lo iswamat akirot naibusakinitor atwanare karaida aenio.” 32#Asw 25:11. Kobala Agiripa Pesito ebe, “Arai kedim etuŋanan ŋol aiyatakinit mama ejai Kaisar ti kelacakin.”
