Aswamisio 14

14
1Kosodi kanen kotoma Ikonium, kolomasi nepepe toma atukos naka Iyudayan, ido kanuka ŋun nuenerasi kiyu ekodet loapol loka Iyudayan ka Iyonanin. 2#Asw 13:45. Konye Iyudayan lumam kiyuunito kisidikoto Imo kitolomikis etau loerono asurokin iinacan. 3#Asw 19:11; Mk 16:20; Bur 2:4. Kosodete kosala nen apak naepol, inerasi katitiŋ kotoma Ejakait, lokitejenuni akirot naka akesianut, kocamak aanyuneta ka nukaumokin aiswamao kakaninikec, 4Konye kotiakas ituŋa lukore; ice owai loka lyudayan, ice da owai loka ikiyakia, 5#Asw 14:19; 2 Tim 3:11. Kiton neatakanunia asurokin naka Imo ka Iyudayan, nepepe kikayaitokokec, aiswamakin kes erono, ka aibiribir kamor. 6#Mat 10:23. Kejenut kes ŋun, kokereasi Lusitura ka Derube, ireria luka Lukaonia, ka akwapin nuedunyatar; 7#Asw 11:19,20. kanen da kolimonokis akiro nukajokak.
8 # Asw 3:2; 9:33; Yok 9:1. Kanen koLusitura kojai etuŋanan iboie, ilejekekas akekeje, eŋwalas ameun kakoik naka totoke, lomam alosi cut. 9#Asw 3:4; Mat 9:28. Abu ŋol kiira Paul inera; kosesen Paul ŋes, koany ebe ejai aiyuun naitanaleuni, 10kolimok keporoto loepol ebe, “Kobwo kotenan kakonikeje.” Kodetun atipet, kosakasak. 11#Asw 28:6. Nakeanyuniata ikodeta nukiswamai Paul, kikeseut iporotoikec, kobalas kaŋajep naka Lukaonia ebe, “Kijesakinos oni idekesio kaput naka ituŋa.” 12Komaikis Baranaba Seu, ka Paul da Kereme, naarai kerai ŋesi lokiŋarenikinit einer. 13Koyau esasedoti loka Seu, lokejai eyekaluke kiŋaren naka ere, imoŋin ka abokoi nuka atur kiton okek, kokoto ainakin asuban nepepe kikodeta. 14Konye nakejenuniata ikiyakia Baranaba ka Paul, kocilata ikecenapen, kokerata kolomasi okodet, kiworoto ebe, 15#Any 20:11; Sab 146:6; Isa 37:16; Yer 32:17; Yak 5:17.“Ikatuŋa, kanukinyo iswamaata nu? Kirai isio da ituŋa lukepone kwape kayesi, kiinunit yes akiro nukaiokak, tetere ibelokinos ainyein akiro nu nukamameak kosesena Edeke lokajaran lokesubi akuj ka akwap ka aŋolol ka iboro da kere luejaas tomakec, 16#Asw 17:30. Katurio nuatuboros abu kojalak atekerin kere alosit kotoma orotinikec; 17#Sab 147:8; Yer 5:24. konye mam kinyein komamei aitejenunet, naarai kiswama nuejok, koinak yes akiru abunore kakuj, ka apakio nuka amio, isimonikini ikusitauon kinyamat ka aiyalama.” 18Kakiro kaŋun, kitikokis ikodeta kationis ainakin kes asuban.
19 # 2 Kor 11:25; 2 Tim 3:11. Konye kodolut Iyudayan luapote koAntiokia ka Ikonium; kisidikoto ikodeta, kibiribiroto Paul kamor, kiriasi kiŋa naka ere, koomito ebe atwana, 20Konye napelukunito ikesisiak, konyou, koloma ore; kapaaran naatupakini kolot Derube nepepe ka Baranaba. 21#Mat 28:19. Kosodi nakedauniata alimonokin akiro nukajokak toma ore ŋol, ka adumun ikesisiak luipu, koboŋosi Lusitura ka Ikonium ka Antiokia, 22#Asw 11:23; 1 Ses 3:3; Mat 7:14. kitogogoŋo itauon luka ikesisiak, ka aikwenyar kes kosalito toma aiyuun ŋin, ebe da kibusakinit alomar toma ajakanut naka Edeke kotoma aiticanio nuipu. 23#Asw 13:3. Nakedauniata aseikin kes imakio kotoma ŋolikanisa, kailip, ka ailuŋ, kibwaikis kes akanin nuka Ejakait lokiyuunito.
24Kitoro Pisidia, kodolot Pampulia. 25Kedaut alimonokin akirot toma Peruga, kijesaros kiton Atalia; 26#Asw 13:1,2. konyout kanen kikwaŋaros kiton Antiokia nekolo elipakinere kes asianut naka Edeke kanuka aswam naadaut aitodol. 27#1 Kor 16:9; Asw 11:18. Nakedolioto, kotukokis ekanisa, kitatamikis akiro kere nukiswamai Edeke nepepe kakes, ebe da aŋaik Imo ekek loka aiyuun, 28Kiboioto kanen apak namam edit nepepe kikesisiak.
