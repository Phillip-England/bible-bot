Rusi 2

2
1 # Rus 3:2,12. Ajai da Naomi onaee loka ekileke, etuŋanan loecasit, ekabaran loka ekek loka Elimelek; ekekiror Boas, 2#Leb 19:9-10; 23:22; Ikis 24:19. Kotema Rusi Amoabut Naomi atiar, “Mere kalot kwana mana airar alunuran nuka akima, ka aitup da ŋin yenikadumakini asianut kakonycneke.” Kotema ŋes atiar, “Ape cabo, akokuka.” 3Abu kolot, kobu airar toma amana naesakiata ikasakak; ido kodum alukat naka amana naka Boas, loarai loka ekek leka Elimelek, 4#Tub 6:12; Sab 118:26; 129:7,8; Luk 1:28; 2 Ses 3:16; 2 Tim 4:22; Kol 4:6. Ido koany, kobu Boas koBet-lekem, kotema ikasakak atiar, “EJAKAIT kojai kayes.” Koboŋokis ŋes ebe, “Kisirereŋ jo EJAKAIT.” 5Kosodi Boas atemar ekebulesit loetasaki ikasakak atiar, “Nakanai apese na?” 6#Rus 1:22. Abu ebulesit loetasaki ikasakak Koboŋok ŋes ebe, “Na ŋes apese Amoabut naaboŋunete kaNaomi anyoun kakwap naka Moab: 7Abu kiner cbe,, Kenaiŋaito, kerarunun ka atukonokin da kaneasakiata ikasakak koisutan da’; kosodi do abunore, eswama apeanan katupuruc kiton kwana, mam ebonorit ore.”
8Kosodi Boas atemar Rusi ebe, “Mere ipupi ijo, akokuka? Sirilosenenei airar mana ace, karaida anyoun kane, konye kojaenena die kapesuruka. 9Kojaenenas akonyekon mana naesakenenoo, kotupanar ijo da kes: mam kereka eeorakinit itelepai ebe Sirikitironorete jo? Ido da nakijakia akure, kolosenenei neejai amot komasi ŋun nuewokunete itelepai.” 10#Age 18:2; 1 Sam 25:23. Kosodi kibiror ebuburia, kisuded, kobala ŋesi ebe, “Inyo bo adumunia asianut kakonyenekon, ijo ajenun eoŋ, kwabo arai eoŋ arroit?” 11#Awa 31:31; Rus 1:14; Luk 5:11,28; 14:33; 18:29,30; Bur 11:8,9,24-26. Koboŋok Boas kobala ŋes ebe, “Aponi kalimonokin cut kere nuibu kiswamak tataakon ageun kaneatwania ekilekon: ido kwape ibu ijo kopalia papakon ka totokon ka akwap da nakolo kiwurikinio, kobu otuŋa lumam apeŋo iieni, 12#1 Sam 24:19; Sab 19:11; 58:11; Awa 11:18; Mat 5:12; 6:1; Luk 6:35; 14:12-14; 2 Tim 1:18; Bur 6:10; 11:6,26; Sab 17:8; 36:7. Kotacak EJAKAIT jo akoniswam, ido EJAKAIT Edeke loka Isirael, loibu aimee kwap kabebenokake korop jo noi.” 13#1 Sam 25:41; Pil 2:3. Kotema atiar, “Mere kadum asianut kakonyenekon ejakaitika; naarai kitalaka, naarai inera kakonibulesit nuka asianut, karaida mam arai adiopet kotoma abulesinikon.”
14Kosodi nakedol apak nakainyam, abu Eoas kobala ŋes ebe, “Bia ainyam atap, kieunyak aodunetekon odia loedwar.” Kiboikin ŋesi awai naka ikasakak; apotu koinakis ŋes imomwa luikawooritai, konyamkimonikin kokurwok. 15Kosodi kenyou airar, kicorak Boas iketelepai, kotema atiar, “Kirarunun ŋesi koisutan da konye siriretanakinete ŋes, 16Komudanakinete ŋes da koisutan, kinyeis, kirar ŋes alopet, konye mam ilimunete.”
17Kosodi ŋesi airar kamana aitiwar akwap; kicom nuabu kotukonokinite kodum bala epa #2:17 epa itemi alaniru loka esairi. 18Kibwoko, koloma ore; abu tataake koany nuatukonokinit ŋesi; koyau koinak lunan edeuni neai nakedau aimonikin. 19Kosodi tataake kobala ebe, “Ai bo lolo kirararit? Ido ai bo kiswamaarit? Koinakinai asuliany ŋol loabu kojen jo.” Kitejenik ŋesi tataake ŋol loeswamaete kaŋes, kinera ebe, “Ekiliokit lokiswamaa lolo ekekiror Boas.” 20#Leb 25:25; Rus 3:10; 2 Sam 2:5; Yob 29:13; 2 Tim 1:16; Awa 17:17; Rus 3:9; 4:6. Kobala Naomi ateranike ebe, “Kisirereŋ EJAKAIT ŋes, lomam enaŋi akesianut mama kalukajarak ka lukatwak da.” Naomi kobala ŋes ebe, “Ekiliokit ŋolŋes lokokekiwok eut, ediopet ka lukalakunakawok.” 21Kiner Rusi Amoabut ebe, “Ee ba, abu ŋesi katema eoŋ ebe, ‘Kojaenena die kitelepaika aitodol naedauniata aisak nuka kere,’” 22Kosodi Naomi atemar Rusi ateranike ebe, “Ejok, akokuka, koupananasi nepepe kabulesinike, kwabo kinyein adumun jo kamana ace kere.” 23Kosodi ŋesi aapienena kabulesin nuka Boas airarenen aitodol naedaunere aisak esairi ka aisak akima; kiboienenei katataake.
