1 Ikabakan 8

8
1 # Mar 10:33; 2 Sam 6:17; 1 Ika 3:15; 1 Siit 13:1-14; 15:3; 2 Sam 5:7. Kosodi Solomon atukokin imakio luka Isirael, ka ikapolok kere luka atekerin, ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapa luka idwe luka Isirael mama ejai ekabaka Solomon Yerusalem, ayaun asaduku naka aitutuket naka EJAKAIT anyoun kore loka Daudi, ŋes Sion. 2#Leb 23:34; Ikis 16:13; 2 Siit 7:8. Kotukonokinos ituŋa kere luka Isirael mama ejai ekabaka Solomon opucit, kolap Etanim, ŋes elap lokikanyetekaarei. 3#Mar 4:15; Ikis 31:9; Yos 3:3,6; 6:4; 1 Siit 15:14. Kopotu imakio kere luka Isirael da, kodakut isasedotin asaduku. 4#1 Ika 3:4; 2 Siit 1:3. Koyaut asaduku naka EJAKAIT, ka ewema loka airiamunun, ka iboro kere lukalaunak luajaas owema; kes iboro luapotu isasedotin ka Ilebin koyaut. 5Kosodi ekabaka Solomon ka ekodet kere loka Isirael luatukonokinos mama ejai ŋesi, ajaas kaŋes kinaren kasaduku, koinakis amerekekin ka imoŋin araun asuban numam epedorio alimun karaida aimarun kanuka aipues. 6#Any 26:33; 1 Ika 6:27. Kitolomasi isasedotin asaduku naka aitutuket naka EJAKAIT aiboisike, toma aibois neinerenenere akirot naka etogo, aibois nakalaunan noi, kwap kabebenoka nuka ikerubin. 7Naarai kigararas ikerubin abebenokakec kuju aibois naka asaduku, korapias ikerubin asaduku ka adaketake. 8#Any 25:14; 2 Siit 5:9. Kewojak adaketa tetere etakani esanekec kaibois nakalaunan koiŋaren kaibois neinerenenere akirot; konye mam ketakanete kokina; ejaas nepenen, kiton lolo. 9#Any 25:21; Ikis 10:2,5; Bur 9:4; Any 40:20; 34:27; Ikis 4:13. Amamei ibore kotoma asaduku dimarai amor aarei nuabu Musa kibwaik koKoreb, nakitutukio EJAKAIT aitutuket kaidwe luka Isirael, nakelomuniata kakwap nako Misiri. 10Kosodi kanen, kedaut isasedotin alomun kaibois nakalaunan, kilelebun edou togo loka EJAKAIT. 11Mam isasedotin apotu kopedosi aibwo aijaanakin kanuka edou: naarai kilelebun aibuses naka EJAKAIT etogo loka EJAKAIT.
12 # Leb 16:2; Sab 18:11; 97:2. Kosodi Solomon einer atiar,
“Abala EJAKAIT ebe iboienenei ŋesi kamuton naejesi ŋaŋ.
13 # Sab 132:14; Yok 4:21-23; Asw 6:14. Abeit kadukok jo etogo aiboikin, Aibois naiboieneno ijo ikar kere.” 14#Yos 22:6; 2 Sam 6:18; 1 Siit 16:2; 2 Siit 6:3. Kibelokin ekabaka, kilipak ekodet kere loka Isirael arereŋ: kobwo ekodet kere loka Isirael. 15#Luk 1:68. Kotema ŋesi atiar, “Kituritete EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, loabu kiner kakeituk mama ejai Daudi papaka, ido kitodol da kakekan ebe, 16#2 Siit 6:5; Ikis 12:11. ‘Ageun kapaaran naketolomunia ikatuŋa Isirael koMisiri, mam abu kaseu ere katekerin kere nuka Isirael aiduk etogo, tetere ejaun nen ekakiror; konye abu kaseu Daudi apolokin ikatuŋa Isirael.’ 17Abu Daudi papaka koomite kotauke adukokin ekiror loka EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, etogo, 18Konye abu EJAKAIT kotema Daudi papaka atiar, ‘Naarai iwomit ijo kotaukon adukokin ekakirot etogo, iswama ejok aomit kotaukon: 19Konye mam jo iduki etogo; konye okokukon, loelomuni kakuanakon, ŋes edukokini ekakiror etogo.’ 20Etodol EJAKAIT akekirot naabu kiner; naarai anyou eoŋ aibois naka Daudi papaka, eboikini oicoloŋ loka Isirael, kwape esumunia EJAKAIT, adukok ekiror loka EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, etogo, 21#Ikis 31:26; 2 Ika 22:8. Nepenen da ebwaik aibois naka asaduku, naejai aitutuket naka EJAKAIT tomake, naetutuki ŋesi kalukapapaawok, nakitolomunia kes kakwap nako Misiri.”
22 # 2 Ika 23:3; 2 Siit 6:12; Yob 11:13; Sab 63:4; Isa 1:15; 1 Tim 2:8. Kobwo Solomon koinaren ketem loka EJAKAIT kakonyen nuka ekodet kere loka Isirael, korio akekanin kuju: 23#Any 15:11; 1 Siit 29:10-13; Sab 35:10; 89:6-8; Yer 10:6-16; Mik 7:18; Ikis 7:9; Nek 1:5; Dan 9:4; Age 17:1; 2 Ika 20:3. Kobala ebe, “Wu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, emamei Edeke loiputosi kaio, kuju toma akuj, arai kokwap kakwap; loitodolenenei aitutuket ka aitim kikonikejaanakinak luelosenenete kakonyenekon kikeeetauon kere: 24Loitodolik ekonikejaanakinan Daudi papaka nun nuiou kisumun ŋes: eebo, ibu kiner kaitukukon, itodolo da kakanikon, kwape ejaatar lolo, 25#Sab 132:12. Aso, wu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, kitodolik ekejaanakinanikon Daudi papaka ŋun nuibu kisumun ŋes ebe, ‘Mam kimamikini ijo etuŋanan loiboikin oicoloŋ loka Isirael kakonyeneka kwape ilosenenoo ijo kakonyeneka.’ 26#2 Sam 7:25. Aso do, wu Edeke loka Isirael, kenainait, kitogogoŋoi akonikirot, nakilimokini ekonikejaanakinan Daudi papaka.
27 # 2 Siit 2:6; Sab 113:4; Isa 66:1; Yer 23:24; Asw 7:49; 17:24; 2 Kor 12:2. “Konye iboie kereka Edeke abeit kakwap? Koany, mam epedori akuj ka akuj naka akuj ailosikin jo; ido konyet etogo lo loadukit eoŋ ŋes bo inyo! 28#Pil 4:6. Konye kocam ijo ailip naka ekonikejaanakinan, ka aiŋaiŋake, wu EJAKAIT Ekadeke, akiirar aimony ka ailip nailipi ekonikejaanakinan kakonyenekon lolo: 29#Dan 6:10. Kokwenyenenete akonikonyen asesen etogo lo apaaran ka akuar, ŋes aibois nakolo ilimit ebe, ‘Ejaenena nen ekakiror’: akiirar ailip nailipi ekonikejaanakinan kaibois kana. 30#2 Siit 20:9; Nek 1:6; Any 34:6,7; Sab 85:2; 130:4; Dan 9:9; Mat 6:12; 1 Yok 1:9. Kiiranari da aiŋaiŋa naka ekonikejaanakinan, naka ikonituŋa Isirael da nailipenenata kesi kaibois kana: eebo, kiiranari ijo ijai toma akuj aiboisikon: ido naipupunio, kisionie,
31“Arai kediak etuŋanan kama ejai eidunyeteke, kisiyapoikinoi aiyaposit, ebuni ŋesi aiyapo koiŋaren ketemukon togo lo; 32#Ikis 25:1. Kopupununei do ijo ijai toma akuj, kiswama, kotubok ikonikejaanakinak, kiteteleka ikaronon, aitoboŋikin ekepone akouke; kitojok da ikadoliton, ainakin ŋes kwape ejaar adoliteke.
33 # Ikis 28:25; Leb 26:39; Yer 20:12. “Naedanyere ikonituŋa Isirael kakonyen nuka lukasurup, naarai adiakasi kamakon; arai kibelokinos mamakon, kolimorete ekonikiror, ilipete, kinainaete jo kotogo kalo: 34Kopupununei do ijo ijai toma akuj, kisionie aronis naka ikonituŋa Isirael, kinyaku kes bobo akwap nakiinakinit lukapapakee.
35“Naegolokinere akui, komamei akiru, naarai adiakasi kamakon; arai ilipete kiteete aibois na, kolimorete ekonikiror, kibelokinos anyoun karonisikee, nakiticania ijo kes; 36#Sab 5:8; 143:8; Isa 35:8; Kos 2:21; 1 Sam 12:23; Yer 6:16. Kopupununei ijo ijai toma akui, kisionie aronis naka ikonikejaanakinak ka naka ikonituŋa Isirael, nakisisianakinia ijo kes erot loejok loibusakinit kes aloseneno; kitetep akiru akwapukon, naibu koinak ikonituŋa araun airumet.
37 # Leb 26:16; Ikis 28:21-52. “Arai keiaun ebeli kakwap, arai kejaun atular, arai kejaun ekure arai airouroun, emaase arai ekutelek; arai kirigaun lukasurupokee kakwap naka ireriakee; ŋinitular kere, ka ŋinidek kere naeiaunor; 38ŋiniilip ka ninikinaina nailipi ituŋanan kere, arai ikonituŋa Isirael kere, yenejeni ninituŋanan atular nakotauke, korio akekanin mama ejai etogo lo: 39#1 Sam 16:7; 1 Siit 28:9; Sab 11:4; Yer 17:10; Asw 1:24. Kopupununei do ijo ijai roma akuj aiboisikon, kisionie, kiswama, koropi ŋinituŋanan kwape ejaatar iponesioke, yenijeni ijo etauke; (naarai ijo, ijo bon ijeni itauon luka idwe luka ituŋa kere:) 40Tetere kiyonete jo apaarasia kere nuiboienenoto kesi kakwap nakiinakinit Iukapapaakosi,
41“Bobo da nuka imoit, yenimere yenika ikonituŋa Isirael, naenyounio ŋesi kakwap naelwana kanuka ekonikiror; 42#Ikis 3:24. (Naarai iirarete kesi nuka ekonikiror Iokapolon, nuka akanikon nakagogonon, nuka akanikon naerioro;) naebunio ŋesi ailip kitei etogo lo: 43#1 Sam 17:46; 2 Ika 19:19; Sab 67:2; 102:15. Kopupununei ijo ijai toma akuj aiboisikon, kiswama kwape ejaatar kere nukiworoi imoit jo; tetere ejenunete atekerin kere nuka akwap ekonikiror, koyoŋito jo, kwape kiyoŋitotor ikonituŋa Isirael, tetere ejenunete ebe emaikinitai etogo lo loadukit eoŋ ekonikiror.
44“Arai kelomasi ituŋakon aitiji Iukasurupokec, korot kere loijukari ijo kes, ilipete EJAKAIT kiteete ere lokiseuni ijo, kiteete da etogo loadukokinit eoŋ ekonikiror: 45Kopupununei do jo ijai toma akui akeeekilip, ka akeceiŋaiŋa, kisigalik akecekirot.
46 # 2 Siit 6:36; Yob 9:2; 14:4; Sab 130:3; 143:2; Awa 20:9; Lim 7:20; Yak 3:2; 1 Yok 1:8. “Arai kediakasi kamakon, (naarai emamei ituŋanan yenimam ediakari) inyunyurikini ijo kes, ijaikini kes lukasurupokec, tetere ijoreorete kes akwap naka lukasurup, naelwana arai naeapie; 47#Leb 26:40; Nek 1:6; Sab 106:6; Dan 9:5. Arai keomoomata kesi kakwap naeioreoritai, kibelokinos bobo, kinainaata ijo kakwap naka ŋul kalukijoreorete kes, kobalasi ebe, ‘Kidiaka isio, kiswama nukepali, kikwaik erono’; 48#Ikis 4:29; Nek 1:9; Awa 23:26; Yer 29:12; Dan 6:10; Asw 8:37; Rom 10:10; 2 Siit 6:38; 28:9; Sab 5:7. Arai kebonut mama ijai ijo kikecetauon kere, kaijarakee kere kakwap naka lukasurupokee, luejoreorete kes, kilipete jo kiteete akwapukec, nakiinakinit lukapapaakec, ere lokiseuni ijo, ka etogo loadukokinit ekonikiror: 49Kopupununei do ijo akeeeilip ka akeceiŋaiŋa ijai toma akuj aiboisikon, kisigalik akeeekirot; 50#Esr 7:6; Sab 106:46. Kisionie ikonituŋa luapotu kodiakasi kamakon, ka aibucaretakec kere nuebuearete kamakon; koinak kes aitimio kakonyen nuka nul kaluejoreorete kes, tetere itimete kes: 51Naarai eraas ikonituŋa, ka airumetekon, naitolomuni kolo koMisiri, anyoun kokidin oupa loka asuat: 52Kokwenyenenete akonikonye aiŋaiŋa naka ekonikeiaanakinan, ka aiŋaiŋa naka ikonituŋa Isirael, kiiranari kes naemonyananata mamakon. 53Naarai ibu kotiak kes katekerin kere nuka akwap, araun airumetekon, kwape ibalar kama ka Musa ekonikejaanakinan, naitolomunia lukapapaakosi koMisiri, wu EJAKAIT Edeke.”
54Kosodi, kedau Solomon ailip ailipasit nin kere ka aiŋaiŋa mama ejai EJAKAIT, konyou ŋesi koinaren ketem loka EJAKAIT, kane nan ekukokina eriorit akaninike akuj. 55#2 Sam 6:18. Kobwo ŋesi, kilipak ekodet kere loka Isirael arereŋ keporoto loepol ebe, 56#Ikis 12:10; Yos 21:45; 23:14. “Kituritete EJAKAIT, loabu kisiyen iketuŋa Isirael, kwape ejaatar kere nuabu kisumun: mam adiokirot kere naeoliorit kaisumunetake nuejok, nuesumunit kakan naka Musa ekejaanakinanike. 57#Ikis 31:6. Kojaenena EJAKAIT Ewokodeke kaoni, kwape aiaenenar kalukapapaawok: kinyek ŋesi anyoun kamawok, karaida ajalakin oni: 58#Sab 119:36; Yer 10:23; 2 Kor 3:5. Tetere isibelokini itauonowok mamake, alosenen korotinike kere, arieenen aicorakinetake, ka ikisilake ka itubesiake, lueeorakinit lukapapaawok. 59Akakiro nu, nuenaina kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, koapikis kesi EJAKAIT Ewokodeke paaran ka kuare, kisigalik akirot naka ekejaanakinanike, ka akirot naka iketuŋa Isirael, kwape ekotor ŋinipaaran. 60#Yos 4:24; 1 Sam 17:46; 2 Ika 19:19; Ikis 4:35; 1 Ika 18:39; Isa 44:6,8,24; Yer 10:10-12. Tetere ejenunete atekerin kere nuka akwap ebe EJAKAIT, ŋes Edeke; emamei ece kere. 61#1 Ika 11:4; 2 Ika 20:3. Kodolioritos do itauonokus mama ejai EJAKAIT Ewokodeke, alosenen kokisilake, arieenen aicorakinetake, kwape lolo.”
62Kosodi ekabaka ka Isirael kere nepepe kaŋes, koinakis asuban mama ejai EJAKAIT. 63Koinak Solomon asubanit naka ainapakin nakeinakini EJAKAIT, imoŋin ilukumin akaisaarei ka luarei, ka amerekekin alukumin akwatat ka akaisaarei. Kwaŋin kotiaku ekabaka ka idwe luka Isirael etogo loka EJAKAIT, 64Kapaaran kaŋipekanin da kitalau ekabaka kidin alaro naajai kinaren ketogo loka EJAKAIT; naarai koinak kanen ainakinet naicweio, ka ainakinet naka akiria, ka asiepai nuka ainakineta nuka ainapakin: naarai adit etem loka aisinit loajai akonyen nuka EJAKAIT ailosikin ainakinet naicweio, ka ainakinet naka akiria, ka asiepai nuka ainakineta nuka ainapakin.
65 # Leb 23:34; Mar 34:8; Yos 13:5; Tub 3:3; 2 Ika 14:25; Age 15:18; Any 23:31; Mar 34:5. Kipo Solomon epueit kapak kaŋin, ka Isirael kere kaŋes, ekodet loepol, anyoun kaneelomarere Kamat kiton aguurut nako Misiri, kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ewokodeke, apaarasia akanykaarei, ka apaarasia akanykaarei, kes apaarasia atomon ka nuoŋon. 66Kapaaran nakikanyetekauni kotwar ituŋa, kilipakis kesi ekabaka arereŋ, koloto owemanakee kolakara, kiyalamas kotauon kanuka ajokis kere naetodiki EJAKAIT Daudi ekejaanakinanike, ka Isirael iketuŋa.
