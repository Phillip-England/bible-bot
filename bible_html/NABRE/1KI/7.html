1 Ikabakan 7

7
1 # 1 Ika 9:10; 10:17; 2 Siit 8:11. Kodau Solomon ikar itomon ka luuni eduki eketogo, kodau eketogo kere. 2Naarai koduk etogo loka ikito luka Lebanon; aojauke ikubitin akwatat, alalauke ikubitin akaisakany, amukururuke ikubitin akaisauni, kotoma okokoron iwonon luka ipirin luka eres, ka abaoi nuka eres kopirin. 3Koraparai kakito nuka eres kokuju agolai akaisaoŋon ka nukany, nuajaas kuju kipirin: ŋolikokoro atomon ka nukany. 4Ajaas apedon okokoron iuni, ido kidokonokino apedon kokokoron iuni. 5Aoiau ka alalau naka ikekia ariakanara, naka apedon da ariakanara: ido kidokonokino apedon kokokoron iuni.
6Kiswamau agola neelomarere kipirin; aojauke ikubitin akaisakany, alalauke ikubitin akaisauni; neelomarere da koinareneke; ipirin ka adoketa koinareneke.
7 # 1 Ika 10:18; Sab 45:6; 110:1; Awa 20:8; 29:4; Isa 9:7. Kiswamau agola naka eicoloŋ neetubio, agola natubes: koraparitete keres anyoun kokwap kiton arwatatin da.
8 # Ikis 17:16,17; 1 Ika 3:1. Etogoke loaperenenei, alaro ace kinaren kagola neelomarere, aponi kiswamai koputosi kaŋin, Kiswamau da etogo loka akoku naka Parao, (naamanyit Solomon) koputosi kagola kaŋin,
9Ŋul kere keraas nuka amor nuka akais nuipu, amor nuekee, kwape ajaar aitemis, kotuboi kisumenon, toma kokina da, anyoun kapetetait, kiton oswi, kwaŋin kina da kiton alaro nakapolon. 10Kerai apetetait naka amor nuka akais nuipu, amor nuepolok, amor nuka ikubitin itomon, ka amor nuka ikubitin ikanykauni. 11Keiaas amor nuka akais nuipu kuiu da, amor nuekee, kwape ajaar aitemis, ka akito nuka eres, 12#Yok 10:23; Asw 3:11. Alaro da naerimorit kejaas ikokoron iuni luka amor nuekee, ka ekokoro loka abaoi nuka eres; koputos kalaro nakotoma naka etogo loka EJAKAIT, ka agola neelomarere togo.
13 # 2 Siit 4:11. Kijuka eka baka Solomon aomare Kiram koTuro. 14#2 Siit 2:14; 4:16; Any 31:3; 28:3; 36:1; Yob 35:11; Isa 28:26; Dan 1:17; Luk 2:40. Kerai ŋesi okoku loka aberu napuserut naka ateker naka Naputali, kerai papake etuŋanan loka Turo, ekeswaman aisinit; eleleba ŋes acoa ka aijen ka apirianut, aswam aswamisinei kere nuka aisinit. Kobu ŋesi mama ejai ekabaka Solomon, kiswama akeswam kere.
15 # 2 Ika 25:17; 2 Siit 3:15; 4:12; Yer 52:21. Naarai abu kotatau ipirin iarei luka aisinit, ŋolipir aojauke ikubitin itomon ka lukanykauni: kolukunite aitemis naka ikubitin itomon ka luarei nolidlopet. 16Kiswamau akwes aarei nuka aisinit naeealara, asipokin kuju opirin: amukurur naka akou adiopet ikubitin ikany, aojao da naka akou ace ikubitin ikany, 17Ajaas iboyan luka aswam nailokonyitai, ka irikoi luka aswam naeoiitai luka akwes nukejaas kuiu opirin, eboi loka akou adiopet, ka eboi loka akou nace. 18Kiswamau kwaŋin akamamagan: kokokoron iarei airimor eboi ediopet arapiar akou nakejai kuju opir; kiswamak akou ace da kwaŋin. 19Araas akwes nuajaas kuju opirin luajaas agola neelomarere nuka aswam bala nakapeeel, ikubitin iwonon. 20#2 Ika 25:17; 2 Siit 3:16; 4:13; Yer 52:23. Ajaas akwes da kuju opirin, kidunyasi kakoik naedunyas kiboyan: emwaasi akamamagan akwat aarei, kokokoron airimor ninikou. 21#2 Siit 3:17; Eko 3:10; Gal 2:9; Apu 3:12; 1 Ika 6:3. Kosipok ipirin agola neelomarere oyekalu: kosipok epir lokoteten, komaik ekekiror Yakin: kosipok epir lokokediany, komaik ekekiror Boas, 22Kejai aswam nakapeeel kuju opirin; kodau kwaŋin aswam naka ipirin.
23 # 2 Ika 25:13; 2 Siit 4:2; Yer 52:17. Kiswamau aŋolol naecalara ikubitin itomon anyoun koreret kiton oreret, kolukuna, aojaoke ikubitin ikany; kolukunite aitemis naka ikubitin akaisauni. 24#2 Siit 4:3. Kojaas ituol luerimorito ŋes kwap kereret alukun, ikubitin itomon, kirimorite aŋolol: kojaas ituol okokoron iarei kocala kere nepepe kotataete. 25#2 Siit 4:4,5; Yer 52:20; Esk 1:10; Apu 4:6,7. Kidokokino omonin itomon ka luarei, kosesenete iuni ari naka agolitomei, kosesenete iuni ari nakoto, kosesenete iuni ari naka eŋalakimak, kosesenete iuni ari nakokide: kidokokino aŋolol kujukee, kojaas iwosikee toma. 26#2 Siit 4:5. Aburesuke alalau naka akan: erereteke kiswamai bala ereret loka atido, bala aturot naka apeeel: abu kigau akaalakanya alukumin aarei.
27Kiswamau aicegeria atomon nuka aisinit; aojau naka aieeger adiopet ikubitin iwonon, alalauke ikubitin iwoŋon, amukururuke ikubitin iuni. 28Ikote aicegeria neta: kojaas abaoi; kojaas abaoi kidiŋ kanuiriikino: 29#Age 3:24; 1 Ika 6:27; Sab 18:10; Esk 1:10; 10:14; Apu 4:6-8. Kojaas iŋatunyo, ka Imoŋin, ka ikerubin abaoi nuajaas kidin kanuiriikino; koiai aiganetait kuiu kanuiriikino: kwap kinatunyo ka imoŋin kojaas lueojitai lueiekosi. 30Kojai ŋiniiceger akokoron aoŋon nuka aisinit, ka nuisibelonokinete nuka aisinit: kojaas aigaŋeta nuka akalaya kagoriake aoŋon: koealara aigaŋeta, kojaas lueojitai kosiep loka ŋiniigaŋet. 31Aitukuke toma aboko kerai ekubit adiopet kuju: kolukuna aitukuke kwape aswam naka aikit, ekubit ediopet ka atutubet: kojaas agirai aitukuke, abaoikec keraas nueriakas nuitenara komam elukuna. 32Kojaas akokoron aoŋon kwap kabaoi: kojaas nuisibelonokinete akokoron aiceger: amukurur naka akokoro ekubit ka atutubet naka ekubit. 33Aswam naka akokoron korai bala aswam naka akokoro naka agali: nuisibelonokinetakee, ka ipirinokec, ka airimetakec, akidinotokec da, kere kecalara, 34Kojaas aigaŋeta aoŋon agoria aoŋon nuka niniiceger: keraas aigaŋeta nuka aieeger alopet. 35Ajai ajeunet kuju kaieeger amukururuke atutubet naka ekubit: akaninike ka abaoike kuju kaiceger koraas nuka adiopeswam. 36Kigir ŋesi ikerubin, ka iŋatunyo, ka idukudukun, necpadalalas akaninike ka abaoike, kwape ajaar apakikee, ka lueojitai airimor. 37Kiswamau aicegeria atomon nuikote nen: kojaas kes kere otatae ediopet, ka aitemis adiopet, ka aput adiopet,
38 # Any 30:17; 2 Siit 4:6; Sek 13:1; Tit 3:5. Kiswamau akalaya atomon nuka aisinit: kigauni akalaya adiopet atakwai akaisaoŋon: ninikalaya ikubitin iwonon: akalaya adiopet kotoma aieeger adiopet kiton aicegeria atomon. 39Kosipok aicegeria, akany owai lokotcten loka etogo, ka akany owai lokokediany loka etogo: kosipok aŋolol owai lokokide loka etogo asesen eŋalakimak.
40Kiswamau Kiram akalaya, ka initia, ka atanyia. Kwaŋin kodau Kiram aswam kere naeswamakini ekabaka Solomon kotogo loka EJAKAIT: 41Ipirin iarei, ka atiboi aarei nuka akwes nuajaas kuju opirin; ka iboyan iarei arapia atiboi nuka akwes nuajaas kuju opirin; 42Ka akamamagan akwat aoŋon nuka iboyan iarei; ikokoron iarei luka akamamagan kanoliboi, arapia atiboi aarei nuka akwes nuajaas kuiu opirin: 43Ka aicegeria atomon, ka akalaya atomon nuka aicegeria; 44Aŋolol adiopet, ka imoŋin itomon ka luarei kwap kaŋolol.
45 # Any 27:3; 38:8; 2 Siit 4:16; Sek 14:20,21. Ka amoti, ka initia, ka atanyia: iboro ŋul kere, lueswamakini Kiram ekabaka Solomon kotogo loka EJAKAIT, araas nuka aisinit naepwatat. 46#2 Siit 4:17; Age 33:17; Yos 3:16; 13:27. Kocala ekabaka kes kApuuton naka Yorodan, kolupe loejai kidin Sukot ka Saretan. 47#1 Siit 22:14. Kinyek Solomon ainiŋ iboro kere, naarai epu kes adepar: mam apedorit adumun alaniru naka aisinit.
48 # Any 37:10,25; 25:30; Leb 24:5. Kiswamau Solomon iboro kere luajaas togo loka EJAKAIT: etcm loka esabu, ka emesa loajaas imugatin luka aitodikin, loka esabu; 49Ka ipirin lukatalai luka esabu loelai, ikany owai lokoteten, ka ikany owai lokokediany, koinaren neinerenenere akirot; atur da, ka atalai, ka arideta, nuka esabu; 50Ka akolin, ka aitudeŋeta, ka atanyia, ka akaalei, ka atubake nuigaete ekurwon, nuka esa bu loelai; ka ipatai luka ikirigin luka etogo lokotoma, aibois nakalaunan noi, ka ikirigin luka etogo, ŋes eyekalu, kere luka esabu.
51Kodaun kwaŋin aswam kere naeswamai ekabaka Solomon kotogo loka EJAKAIT, Koyau Solomon iboro luabu Daudi papake kotiaku, epuesa, ka esabu, ka iboro, kibwaik kes aiŋadisia nuka etogo loka EJAKAIT.
