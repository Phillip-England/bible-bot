1 Ikabakan 21

21
1 # Tub 6:33; 1 Sam 29:1. Kosodi kanen kokau kanun, kejai Nabos Eyesereelit emanikor loka emusabibu lokejai Yesereel, keapia ketogo loti loka Akab ekabaka loka Samaria. 2#Age 3:6; Any 20:17; Ikis 5:21; Kbk 2:9; Luk 12:15; 1 Tim 6:9,10; Yak 1:14,15; 1 Yok 2:16; 1 Sam 8:14. Kosodi Akab kobala Nabos ebe, “Ae emanikorikon loka emusabibu koraun lokaŋ araun emanikor loka edia, naarai eapie kekatogo; kainakini eoŋ jo emanikor ece loka emusabibu loitelekarit aiokis: arai bo itoiokit, kainakini apesan kwape eiaar eketiai.’ 3#Leb 25:23; Mar 36:7; Esk 46:18. Kobala Nabos Akab ebe, “Madi EJAKAIT, eoŋ ainakin jo airumet naka lukapapaaka.’ 4Kobu Akab togoke ekadakinit ido konyunyura kanuka akirot nakelimokini Nabos Eyesereelit ŋes: naarai enera atiar, “Mam kainakini jo airumet naka lukapapaaka.’ Korior kaperisike, kimwana akekonyen, koner ainyam.
5Konye kobu aberuke Yesebel mama ejai, kobala ŋes ebe, “Inyo bo etakadiki ekonitau kwaŋin, tape ineri ainyam?” 6Kobala ŋes ebe, “Naarai ener kaNabos Eyesereelit, kalimok ŋes ebe, ‘Ae ekonimanikor loka emusabibu kagwel kapesan; arai bo isiyalamikinit kesiee keeemanikor’: kabonok ebe, ‘Mam kainakini ekamanikor loka emusabibu.’’’ 7#1 Sam 8:14; Yer 6:7; Mik 2:1; 7:3. Kobala ŋes aberuke Yesebel ebe, “Mere kereka jo iyait ajakanut naka Isirael? Konyou, konyam inyamat, kolakar ekonitau: kainakini eoŋ jo emanikor loka emusabibu loka Nabos Eyesereelit.’
8Kwaŋin kiwadik ŋes ibaluan kokiror loka Akab, kipueak kes akeipucet, kijuka ibaluan mama ejaas imakio ka ikapolok lukejaas oreke, lukiboiete kaNabos, 9Kiwadik obaluan kinera atiar, “Kolimos ailuŋ, kibwaikis Nabos akonyen nuka ituŋa: 10#Ikis 13:13; Tub 19:22; 1 Sam 2:12; 2 Sam 20:1; 23:6; 2 Kor 6:15; Any 22:28; Leb 24:15,16; Asw 6:11; Leb 24:14. Kisibwaata ituŋa iarei, idwe luka Belial kakonyeneke, kitorona ŋes, kobalas ebe, ‘Ibu konerok Edeke ka ekabaka da.’ Koyanaasi konyet, kibiribiroto kamor, kotwana.’ 11Kosodete ituŋa lukoreken, imakio ka ikapolok lukiboiete koreken, kiswamata kwape kieorakinia Yesebel, kwape kiwadikatere obaluan luejukakini ŋesi kes. 12#Yob 15:34; 34:30; Awa 11:9; 29:12; Isa 9:16,17; 32:6,7; 1 Tim 4:2. Kolimos ailuŋ, kibwaikis Nabos akonyen nuka ituŋa, 13#Mar 15:36; Yos 7:24; 2 Siit 24:21; Mk 15:20; Asw 7:58,59; Bur 11:37. Kopotu ituŋa iarei, idwe luka Belial, kiboikinos akonyeneke: kitorona odwe luka Belial Nabos kakonyen nuka ituŋa, atiar, “Abu Nabos konerok Edeke ka ekabaka da.’ Kitolomasi ŋes kina naka ere, kibiribiroto kamor, kotwana. 14Kiyakiasi neejai Yesebel atiar, “Ebiribirii Nabos kamor, atwana.’
15Kosodi kanen, kiira Yesebel ebe ebiribirii Nabos kamor, atwana da, kolimok Yesebel Akab atiar, “Konyou, kirum emanikor loka emusabibu loka Nabos loagirit ainakin jo agwel kapesan: naarai mam Nabos eroko ejari, konye atwana.” 16Kosodi kanen, kiira Akeb ebe atwana Nabos, konyou Akab kijesar omanikor loka emusabibu loka Nabos Eyesereelit airum.
17 # Sab 9:12. Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Eliya Etisibit, atiar, 18#1 Ika 13:32; 2 Siit 22:9. “Konyou kiiesar airiamun ka Akab ekabaka loka Isirael, loiboie koSamaria: koany, ejai omanikor loka emusabibu loka Nabos Eyesereelit airum. 19#Age 4:9-11; 2 Sam 12:9; 1 Ika 22:38. Ido kolimok ŋes atiar,’ Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, “Iar, irum da?’” Kolimok da atiar, ‘Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, “Aibois nakemeoto inokwo aokot nuka Nabos, emeete da kwaŋin inokwo akoniwokot, nupekon.”’”
20 # 1 Ika 18:17; Isa 29:21; Amo 5:10; 2 Ika 17:17; Rom 7:14. Kotema Akab Eliya atiar, “Kiany ijo eoŋ, ijo lokasurupika?”
Koboŋok atiar, “Kaany; naarai igwelari bon aswam nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 21#1 Ika 14:10; Ikis 32:35; 2 Ika 9:8; Sab 94:1-23; 1 Sam 25:22. Koany, ayauni nukaronok mama ijai, ido kapiar jo eut, adaun da kama ejai Akab ŋinikoku yenisapat, ka ŋin yeneenen, ka yenimam eenen koIsirael. 22#1 Ika 15:29; 16:3,11. Ido etuluori ekonikek aputoor loka Yeroboam okoku loka Nebat, ka aputoor ekek loka Baasa okoku loka Akiya, kanuka aitunyunyurikin nakitunyunyurikini, aitidiakanar Isirael. 23#2 Ika 9:36; Sab 7:16; 9:15,16. Ido kiner EJAKAIT nuka Yesebel da atiar, ‘Enyamakinete inokwo Yesebel arwatat naka Yesereel.’ 24#1 Ika 14:11; 16:4. Yenika Akab yenetwanikini ore enyamenenete inokwo; ido nin yenetwanikini ais enyamenenete akweny nuka apak.’
25 # 1 Ika 16:30,31; 11:1,4; Age 6:2-5. (Konye emamei yeneputos ka Akab lokegwelar elopet aswam nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT lokitapaari aberuke Yesebel. 26#Age 15:16; 2 Ika 21:11. Kiswamaenenei noi nuketal katuparit aputosia, kwape ejaatar kere nukiswamaenenete Imorain, lukireneari kolo EJAKAIT kakonyen nuka idwe luka Isirael.)
27 # Age 37:34. Kosodi kanen, kiira Akab akiro nun, koeilicil ikenapen, konap ipukoi, koluŋ, koper kopukoi, kolot etwanit akuan, 28Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Eliya Etisibit, kobala ebe, 29#Sab 66:20; 78:34-37; 2 Ika 9:25. “Iany ijo Akab kwape etasonikinor kakonyeneka? Naarai etasonikin kakonyeneka, mam ayauni aronis ŋin kapaarasiake: konye kapaarasia nuka okokuke ŋes ayaunia aronis ŋin okekike.”
