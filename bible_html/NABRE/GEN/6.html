Ageun 6

6
1 # Yob 1:6; 2:1. Kosodete nakegeuniata iruna eiyas kakwap, kouriata apesur, 2#2 Kor 6:18; Ikis 7:3,4. Koanyut odwe luka Edeke adwe nuka ituŋa ebe ejokuka; komanyata aŋor kotoma kanun kere nuaseununete kesi. 3#Luk 19:42; Gal 5:16,17; 1 Pet 3:20; Sab 78:39. Kotema EJAKAIT atiar, “Mam ekatau epegenenas kitunanan ikar ka ikar, naarai erai ŋesi da akuan: konye eraunos akepaarasia ikar akwatat ka akaisaarei.” 4#Mar 13:33. Kapaarasia kanun kojaas Inepilin roma akwap: ido kokau kanun, nakelomanariata odwe luka Edeke mama ejaas adwe nuka ituŋa, kourianakis kes: nulkes ituŋa lukagogon lukolo sek, ituŋa luajenas,
5 # Rom 1:28-31; Age 8:21; Ikis 29:19; Awa 6:18; Mat 15:19; Mat 24:37; Luk 17:26; 1 Pet 3:20. Koany EJAKAIT aronis naka ituŋanan epol kotoma akwap, ka aitetemonorikere naka aomisio nukotauke erai naerono due. 6#Mar 23:19; 1 Sam 15:11,29; Isa 63:10; Pes 4:30. Kiitu EJAKAIT naarai abu kosub etuŋanan kotoma akwap, kokadak kotauke. 7Kobala EJAKAIT ebe, “Amwatari eoŋ ituŋanan yenabu kasub kakwap; ageun kotunanan, ka etiaŋ, ka lueliatos, ka lueporitos kapak; naarai eitu eoŋ kanuabu kasubunia kes.” 8#Age 19:19; Any 33:12; Luk 1:30; Asw 7:46. Konye kodum Noa asianut kakonye nuka EJAKAIT.
9 # 2 Pet 2:5. Na ŋes aur naka Noa. Arai Noa etuŋanan loedolit, lomam itoronitai katurike: kolosenenei Noa nepepe ka Edeke. 10Kouri Noa isap iuni Sem, ka Kam, ka Yapet.
11Komunaun akwap kakonyen nuka Edeke, kikerepik akwap. 12#Sab 14:2; 33:13. Koany Edeke akwap, ido koany, kemunaun; naarai ŋinibore kere yenejai akuan abu komunau ekerot kotoma akwap.
13 # Esk 7:2; Amo 8:2; 1 Pet 4:8. Kotema Edeke Noa atiar, “Adolu awasia naka ŋinibore kere yeneiai aiiar kakonyeneka: naarai ekerepiki akwap kanukec; asa koany, amudiari eoŋ kes nepepe ka akwap da. 14Kikeu ataker naka ekitoi loka goper; kitemok agolai toma ataker, kilada kemina toma kokina da. 15Lo ŋes epone loitemokinio ŋes: aoiau naka ataker ikubitin #6:15 Ekubit ŋes aitemis naka aojau bala r’ 6’. akwat auni, alalauke ikubitin akaisakany, amukururuke ikubitin akaisauni. 16Kopedok adirisa ataker, ekubit ediopet kuju; kibwaik ekekosiep loka ataker; kiswama ataker kicul itogoi, lokokwap, ka lokiareit, ka lokiwuniet. 17#2 Pet 2:5; Rom 5:12,14. Koany, ayauni eoŋ da akalele naka akipi toma akwap, amudiar akuan kere naejai eiyeŋane loka aijar toma, kwap kakuj; etwani nlnibore kere yenejai toma akwap. 18Konye etogogoŋi eoŋ akaitutuket kajo; ilomari ijo ataker, ijo, ka odwekon, ka akoniberu, ka aŋor nuka odwekon, nepepe kajo. 19Ido imeikini toma ataker epone loka ŋinibore kere yeneiai aijar naka akuan, iareiarei luka nolipone, imeikini toma ataker tetere ejarete nepepe kajo; ido koraas yenikiliokit ka yeniberu. 20Lueporitos toma oponekec, ka ibaren toma oponekec, ka ninibore kere yeneliat kokwap toma oponeke, iareiarei luka ŋolipone epote neiiai, tetere eiarete, 21#Age 1:29,30. Koyana inyamat kere luenyamio, kotukonok neijai; tetere inyami ijo, ka kes da.” 22#Bur 11:7; Age 7:5. Kiswama Noa kwaŋin; kwape kicorakinia ŋes Edeke, kiswama kwaŋin.
