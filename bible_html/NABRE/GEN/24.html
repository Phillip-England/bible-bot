Ageun 24

24
1 # Age 18:11; 13:2; Gal 3:9. Amoionit Aburaam kokokosanite da noi: kisirerenenenei EJAKAIT Aburaam kotoma akiro kere. 2#Age 15:2; 24:10; 39:4-6; 47:29; Aku 5:6. Kolimok Aburaam ekebulesit, ekapolon loka etogoke, loayanaarit kere luajaas kaŋes ebe, “Keŋaiŋait, kibwaik akonikan kwap kamurwoka. 3#Any 34:16; Ikis 7:3; 2 Kor 6:14-17. Eoŋ da kesiyapoikini jo kama ejai EJAKAIT, Edeke loka akuj ido Edeke loka akwapisinei, ebe mam imanyakini ikakoku aberu naelomuni kotoma apesur nuko Kanaan, nukaneeboio eoŋ. 4#Age 28:2. Konye ilosi akwapuka, neejaas iinacanaka, komanyak okokuka Isaak aberu kaŋina.” 5Kotema ebulesit atiar, “Elemuni mam aberu iyuuni abunore keoŋ adolun akwap na; ebusakinit kereka eoŋ ainyakar okokukon akwap nakolo kibunio?” 6#2 Pet 2:20-22. Kotema Aburaam atiar, “Mam inyakari okokuka nepepec da. 7#Age 12:1,7; 13:15; Any 32:13; 23:20; Sab 34:7; Isa 63:9. EJAKAIT, Edeke loka akuj lokalemuni kotogo loka papaka, kakwap da nakaurikinio, ido loabu kinera keoŋ, keyapoik da kobala ebe, ‘Ainakini akoniwur akwap na’; ijukuni nol ekemalaika aiŋarenikin jo, imanyakini okokuka aberu naka nina. 8#Yos 2:17-20. Arai mam aberu iyuuni abunore kajo, emameete nukibeikinete jo kotoma aiyaposit kana, konye sirikinyaka okokuka nina.” 9Kibwaik ebulesit akekan kwap kamurwo naka papake Aburaam, kiyapoik kanuka akirot kaŋin.
10 # Age 27:43. Koyana ebulesit ikaalai itomon, lukotoma okaalai luka papake, kolot; kojaas kiboro kere lukajokak luka papake kotoma akaninike; konyou, kolot Mesopotamia, ore loka Nakor. 11#Any 2:16; 1 Sam 9:11. Kitukukok ikaalai kiŋa naka ere neejai atan naka akipi, napejosi, apak naeponiata aŋor aiwok akipi. 12#Age 24:27; 26:24; Any 3:6,15; Pil 4:6. Kotema atiar, “Wu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka ejakaitika Aburaam, kenainair, koinakinai arereŋ lolo, kitodik ejakaitika Aburaam asianut. 13Koany, abwoi kariri naka atan naka akipi, ido epote apesur nuka ituŋa lukore aiwok akipi; 14#Awa 19:14; Tub 6:17-37; 1 Sam 6:7. Aso kojau kwana, apese naatemari eoŋ ebe, ‘Kooku amotikon, kenainait kamat’; kosodi ŋesi da ebe, ‘Komat, eruanakini da ikonikaalai’; koraun nin naiseikinit ekonibulesit Isaak; nen do ajenunia ebe itodik ekajakait asianut.”
15 # Sab 34:15; Age 11:29. Kwaŋin, eroko inera, koany, kolom Rebeka nakeurie Betuel okoku loka Milika, aberu naka Nakor, onace loka Aburaam, kojai amotike okeperike. 16#Age 26:7. Etemelel da apese noi, abekot namam ejeŋi ekiliokit; kijesar atan, kileleb amotike, kodok. 17#Yok 4:7. Koker ebulesit airiamun kaŋes kotema atiar, “Kenainait, ae adisikipi nuka amotikon, kamat.” 18#1 Pet 3:8; 4:9. Kotema atiar, “Komat, ekajakait.” Kisunya awoun amoti, kibwaik akanike kitamat ŋes, 19Kedau aitamat ŋes kotema atiar, “Aokokini ikonikaalai da, komata kimonikinos.” 20Kisunya abukokin akipi nuka amotike atuba, koker bobo atan aiwok, kowokok ikekaalai kere. 21#Luk 2:19,51. Kite ŋes etuŋanan, kopala ajenun arai abu EJAKAIT kisireren akelosit, arai bo mam.
22 # Any 32:2,3; Isa 3:19,20; 1 Pet 3:3. Kosodete, kedaut ikaalai aimat, kolem etuŋanan egilut loka esabu, alaŋiruke atutubet naka esekel, aikomon da aarei nuka akaninike, alanirukec isekelin itomon luka esabu; 23Kotema atiar, “Iŋai papakon? Kenainait kolimokinai. Ejai apak ore loka papakon kapero isio?” 24Kotema atiar, “Arai eoŋ apese naka Betuel okoku loka Milika, lokeurikini ŋesi Nakor.” 25#1 Pet 4:9. Kotema da atiar, “Kija kanya ka inyamat lukidaunete, ka apak da naperot.” 26#Age 24:52; Any 4:31. Kitulur etuŋanan akekou, koma EJAKAIT. 27#Any 18:10; Rus 4:14; 1 Sam 25:32; 2 Sam 18:28; Luk 1:68; Age 32:10; Sab 98:3; Age 24:48; Awa 3:6. Kotema atiar, “Kituritete EJAKAIT, Edeke loka ejakaitika Aburaam, lomam eswaki aitodikin ekajakait akeitim ka akebeit; ka eoŋ da, abu EJAKAIT keŋarenik korot kiton togo loka iinacan luka ejakaitika.”
28Koker apese, kolimok lukotogo loka totoke kwape ejaatar akiro ŋun, 29#Age 29:5. Ido ajai Rebeka onaceke, ekekiror Laban; koker neejai etuŋanan, aisiriama ŋes katan. 30Kosodi nakeanyunia ŋesi egilut, ka aikomon kakanin nuka inacike, kiira da akiro nuka Rebeka inacike ebe, “Kalimoki etuŋanan kwaŋin”; kodol ŋesi neejai etuŋanan; ido koany, ebwoi kidunyasi kikaalai kaneajai atan. 31#Age 26:29; Tub 17:2; Rus 3:10; Sab 115:15. Kotema atiar, “Kolom ijo lokisirerenit EJAKAIT; inyo bo ibwoia kokiŋa? Naarai etemonok eoŋ etogo, ka apak da naka ikaalai.” 32#Age 43:24; Tub 19:21. Koloma etuŋanan togo, kolac ikaalai; koinak anya ka inyamat kanuka ikaalai, ka akipi ailosia akekeien ka akejen nuka ituŋa kaluajaas nepepe kaŋes, 33#Yob 23:12; Yok 4:34; Pes 6:5-7. Kibwaikinai aimuj akonyeneke ebe, “Kimuj.” Konye kotema atiar, “Mam eoŋ emuji, eriŋa kelimuno nukejukunitere.” Kotema atiar, “Kolim.”
34Kotema atiar, “Arai eoŋ ebulesit loka Aburaam. 35#Age 24:1; 13:2; Yob 1:3. Abu EJAKAIT kisirereŋ noi ejakaitika, ido araun lokapolon; abu koinak da ŋes isipanin luka amerekekin ka aituk, ka epuesa, ka esabu, ka ibulesin ka abulesin, ka ikaalai ka asigirian. 36#Age 21:2,10; 25:5. Abu Sara aberu naka ejakaitika kourik ŋes ikoku kemoion: abu koinak ŋol kere lukejaatar. 37#Age 24:3. Kesiyapoik eoŋ ejakaitika ebe, Mam imanyakini okokuka apese naka apesur nuka Ikanaanin lueboie eoŋ kakwapukec, 38Konye ilosi togo loka papaka, neejai atekerika komanyak okokuka aberu. 39Abu katema ejakaitika atiar, ‘Elemuni bo aberu kogir aupun keoŋ.’ 40#Age 24:7; 5:22-24; 17:1; Any 23:20. Kalimok eoŋ ebe, ‘EJAKAIT, Ioalosenenei eoŋ kakonyeneke, iyakiari ekemalaika nepepe kaio, isirercni akonilosit, imanyakini da okokuka aberu naelomuni koinacanaka kotogo loka papaka, 41#Age 24:8. Kwaŋin mam nukibeete jo kanuka akaiyaposit, arai kidol oinacanaka; ido arai mam kes kiinakinete aberu, emameete nukibeikinete jo kanuka aiyapositika.’
42 # 1 Ika 1:36; Asw 10:7,8,22; Nek 1:11; Sab 90:17; Rom 1:10. “Abu lolo eoŋ atan, kasodi atemar, ‘Wu EJAKAIT, Edeke loka ejakaitika Aburaam, arai teni kisireren erotoka loalosi; 43#Age 24:13. Koany, abwoi eoŋ kariri naka atan naka akipi; asa kojaut kwana, ebe, apese naelomuni aiwok, naabala eoŋ ebe, “Koinakinai akipi kamotikon kamat, kenainait”; 44#Bur 13:2. Kosodi ŋesi da ebe, “Komat ijo, ido awokokini eoŋ ikonikaalai da”: koraun nin aberu naeseikinit EJAKAIT okoku loka ejakaitika.’
45 # Age 24:15; 1 Sam 1:13; Isa 65:24. “Napeneranakin ŋun kotauka, koany, kolom Rebeka kamotike kokeperike; kiiesar atan, kowok; kasodi eoŋ ebe, ‘Kenainait, ae kamat.’ 46Kisunya ŋesi awoun amotike kokeperike, kotema atiar, ‘Komat, eruanakini ikonikaalai da’; kasodi aimat, kiruanak ikaalai da. 47#Esk 16:11,12. Abu kenit ŋes ebe, ‘Irai ijo akoku naka iŋai P” Kotema atiar, ‘Akoku naka Betuel, okoku loka Nakor, loaurikini ŋes Milika’; kebwaik egilut okumesike, ka aikomon da akaninike. 48#Age 24:26; Sab 32:8; 48:14; 107:7; Isa 48:17. Ketulur akouka kwap, kama EJAKAIT, ketur EJAKAIT, Edeke loka ejakaitika Aburaam; loabu keteten eoŋ korot ayaŋaar apese naka onaceke loka ejakaitika kanuka okokuke. 49#Age 47:29; Yos 2:14. Natikwana da arai iyuunitos asubakin ejakaitika asianut ka abeit, kolimokinai; arai mam, kolimokinai; kebelokin teten, arai kediany.”
50 # Sab 118:23; Mat 21:42; Mk 12:11. Kosodete kobonokis Laban ka Betuel atiar, “Alomu akirot nin kama ejai EJAKAIT; mam kipedori alimokin jo nuejok karaida nueroko; 51#Age 20:15. Koany, ejai Rebeka akonyenekon, koyana ŋes, kolot, koraun ŋesi aberu naka okoku loka ejakaitikon kwape atemaria EJAKAIT.”
52Kosodi kiira ebulesit loka Aburaam akecekiro, kokukokin kwap mama ejai EJAKAIT. 53#Any 3:22; 11:2; 12:35; 2 Siit 21:3; Esr 1:6. Koyau ebulesit inapen luka epuesa, ka luka esabu, ka igoen, koinak Rebeka; koinak da onaceke ka totoke iboro luka akais nuipu. 54#Age 24:56,59. Konyamata, komatata, ŋesi ka ituŋa luajaatar, kiwalakis; konyout tupuruc, kotema atiar, “Kotwarai eoŋ, kalot neejai ejakaitika.” 55Kotema onace loka apese ka totoke atiar, “Kosal ber apese kasio adaun apaarasia adis, arai atomon: kosodi konyet alosit.” 56#Age 24:40. Kotema atiar, “Sirikiisibo eoŋ, naarai abu EJAKAIT kisireren akalosit; kotwarai eoŋ kalot neejai ejakaitika.” 57Kotemasi atiar, “Kinyarauni apese, kenitata nuelomunete kaitukuke.” 58Konyaraut Rebeka, kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Ikoto alosit nepepe ketunanan kalo?” Kotema atiar, “Alosi.” 59#Age 35:8. Kotwarata Rebeka kinacikec, ka akasaanike, ka ebulesit loka Aburaam, ka iketuŋa. 60#Age 17:16; 22:17; Rus 4:11. Kilipakis Rebeka arereŋ, kotemasi atiar, “Inacikosi, korau ijo toto naka ilukumin luka ilukumin itomon, akoniwur da kirumia erute loka ŋul kaluelaete kes.” 61Konyou Rebeka, kapesuruke, kibwanenoto ikaalai, kotupasi etuŋanan; koyana ebulesit Rebeka, kolot.
62 # Age 16:14; 25:11. Kobu Isaak korot loka Beer-lakai-roi: naarai kiboienenei kakwap naka ari naka Eŋalakimak. 63#Yos 1:8; Sab 1:2; Dan 6:10; Mat 6:5,6; Mk 1:35; Luk 5:16; Asw 10:9. Koloma Isaak omanikor eboŋ aomoom; kiteluo akekonye, kite, ido koany, eponitos ikaalai. 64#Yos 15:18; 1 Sam 25:23. Kiteluo Rebeka akekonye: keanyu Isaak kodok kokaalai. 65#1 Kor 11:3-10; 1 Ses 4:13. Kotema ŋesi ebulesit atiar, “Iŋai bo etuŋanan ŋol loitorunit omanikor airiamun kaoni?” Kotema ebulesit atiar, “Ŋes ejakaitika.” Kitin ekegoe lokimukos, kimuko akekonyen. 66Kolimok ebulesit Isaak akiro kere nuabu kiswama. 67Kitoloma Isaak Rebeka owema loka Sara totoke, koyana ŋes, korau akeberu; komina ŋes; kitalakarite Isaak nakedaunia totoke atwanare.
