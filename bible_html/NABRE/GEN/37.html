Ageun 37

37
1 # Age 36:7. Kiboiei Yakob kakwap nakolo kiboionono papake, akwap naka Kanaan. 2#1 Sam 2:22-24. Na ŋes aur naka Yakob. Nakeraar Yosep loka ikar itomon ka lukanykaarei, ecokicncnei esipan nepepe kikeinacan; kojaenena etelepat nepepe kodwe luka Bilika, ka odwe luka Silupa, aŋor nuka papake; kideideonokini Yosep papakec akirokec nueroko. 3#2 Sam 13:18. Ido amina Isirael Yosep aitelekarit idweke kere, naarai ŋes loaurie kamoionuke; kodonyok ekooti loka acerieik nuipu. 4#Age 27:41; 49:23; 1 Sam 17:28; Yok 7:3,5; 1 Yok 2:11; 3:10. Koanyut ikeinacan ebe amina papakec ŋes aitelekarit ikeinacan kere; apotu kolaata, komam bobo ineras kaŋes ejok, dimarai nupeken Uŋet bon.
5Abu Yosep kiruja airujasit, kolimok ikeinacan: kiyatakis bobo aila ŋes, 6Kobala kes ebe, “Keŋaiŋaitos, kopupokisi airujasit na naabu keruja: 7#Age 42:6; 43:26; 44:14. Naarai, koany, bala kieni oni ikes komana, ido koany, konyou ekesitika, kosipokin; ido koany, kopotu ikesikus kirimosi, kokukonokinos ekakesit.” 8Kobalas ikeinacan ŋes ebe, “Ipedori kereka ijo airum sio? arai ipedori teni ijo ayait sio? “Kiyatakis bobo aila ŋes kanuka airujasiake ka akekiro da. 9#Age 46:29. Kiruia bobo aceirujasit, kolimok ikeinacan kobala ebe, “Koany, eruja aceirujasit; ido koany, bala akoloŋ ka elap ka aacer atomon kanadiop kakukonokinos eoŋ.” 10#Age 27:29. Kolimok ŋesi papake ka ikeinacan; kitoron papake ŋes, kobala ebe, “Nanyoin bo airujasit na nairujat ijo? Eoŋ ka totokon ka ikoniinacan kiponi aisudedeonokin jo?” 11#Asw 7:9; Luk 2:19,51. Kirum ikeinacan ekokote kanuken; konye koric papake akirot nin kotauke.
12Koloto ikeinacan acok esipan loka papakec Sekem. 13Kobala Isirael Yosep ebe, “Mere icokiete ikoniinacan esipan Sekem? Bia keyakia mama ejaas.” Kobala ŋesi ebe, “Eoŋ lo.” 14#Age 29:6; 1 Sam 17:18; Age 13:18. Kobala ŋesi ebe, “Aso kolot, koany ikoniinacan, arai ejaas ejok, ka esipan da arai ejai ejok; kinyak akiro.” Kwaŋin kiyakia ŋes anyoun kaditot naka Keburon, kodol Sekem. 15Kodum etuŋanan ŋes, ido koany, etubitoi kokitwi; kinit etuŋanan ŋol ŋes ebe, “lmoot bo inyo?” 16#Eko 1:7. Kotema atiar, “Amoot ikainacan; kolimokinai, kenainait, neicokiorito esipan.” 17#2 Ika 6:13. Kolimok etuŋanan ŋes ebe, “Anyout kesi kane; naarai abu kapup ebalas ebe, ‘Kaloto Dotan.’’’ Kibwapa Yosep ikeinacan, kodumak kes Dotan. 18#1 Sam 19:1; Sab 31:13; Mat 27:1; Mk 14:1; Yok 11:53; Asw 23:12. Kiteluosi ŋes eroko elwana, ido eroko keekuna die kinerut aiar ŋes, 19Kotemanaros atiar, “Koany, abu ca etikerujat je. 20#Awa 1:11; 6:17; 1 Yok 3:12,13. Aso do kopotu kaarata, kacakakisi toma adiopet kapanya, kitemari oni da ebe, ‘Etiaŋ lokadedenan ŋes kenyami’; kasodete kaanyutu kwape ejauniata rwaru airuiasiake.’ 21#Age 42:22. Kopup Reuben akirot nin kitajar kakanikec; kobala cbe, “Kenyekisi aiar.” 22Kotcma Reuben kes atiar, “Sirikibukosi aokot; kocakakisi aipany na naejai modin, konye sirikidokokis akan,” tetere itaiari ŋes kakanikec, ainyakakin papake. 23Kosodi kanen, kedolu Yosep mama ejaas ikeinacan, kolacut ekootike loka acerieik nuipu loanapit; 24Koyaŋaasi ŋes, kocakakis aipany: aonit da aipany komameete akip.
25 # Awa 30:20; Amo 6:6; Yer 8:22. Kiboikinos ainyam atap; kiteluosi akecekonye, kosesenata, ido koany, idiai erioŋet loka Isimaelin loabuni koGilead, ka akaalaikec nuebwokitos luesaetc ka emina ka akinyet, idiaete ayaŋaar Misiri. 26#Age 4:10; Yob 16:18. Kotema Yuda ikeinacan ebe, “Ajokis anyoin nakidumuni oni aiar onacewok ka aimunonoor akeokot? 27#1 Sam 18:17; Age 29:14; 42:21. Aso, ketwegelata Isimaelin, kwabo kenyekisi aidokokin awokokan mamakeŋ; naarai ŋes onacewok, ŋes awokokuan.” Kopuput ikeinacan ŋes, 28#Asw 7:9; Age 39:1; Tub 6:3; Age 45:4,5; Sab 105:17; Mat 27:9. Kitorut nen Imidianin, ikagwelanarak; kibwosut ikeinacan Yosep alemun kaipany, kitwegelata Isimaelin atutubeta akaisaarei nuka epuesa. Koyanaasi Yosep Misiri.
29 # Yob 1:20. Koboŋ Reuben aipany; ido ko any, komamei Yosep kaipany; kocil ikenapen. 30#Yer 31:15; Age 42:13,36. Kobono mama ejaas ikeinacan kobala ebe, “Emamei ikoku; okwe, abei eoŋ ai?” 31Kolemut ekooti loka Yosep, koarata ekoroi lomasenik, kidomakis ekooti aokot; 32#Age 37:23. Koyanaasi ekooti loka acerieik nuipu, koinut papakec; kobalas ebe, “Kipou kadumutu lo: asa kojen arai ŋes eko oti loka ikokukon arai mere ŋes.” 33#Age 44:28. Kojen, kobala ebe, “Ŋes ekooti loka ikakoku; kenyam ŋes etiaŋ loerono: komamei abuonokin keponi Yosep kopoopoai.” 34#Age 37:29. Kocil Yakob ikenapen, kenap epuko kanabet, kokuudian ikekoku apaarasia nuipu. 35#2 Sam 12:17; Age 42:38. Konyout ikedwe kere lusap ka lupesur aitalakar ŋes; konye kogir aitalakario; kobala ebe, “Alomari okatwak mama ejai ikakoku aroko amonyi.” Komony papake akiyo kanuken. 36#Age 39:1. Kitwegelata ŋes Imidianin Potipar koMisiri, ŋes ejakait loka Parao, ekapolon loka iserikalen.
