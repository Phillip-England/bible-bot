Nekemia 7

7
1 # Nek 3:1-32; 6:1. Kosodi kanen, kedaun arwatat adukio, kadau da asipokin ikirigin, koseunai da ikagolok ka ikayook ka Ilebin, 2#Mar 12:7; Sab 101:6; Mat 24:45; 1 Kor 4:2; Age 42:18; Any 18:21; Yob 31:23; Awa 16:6. abu ketitin onaeeka Kanani, ka Kanania ekayaiton loka etogo lokagogon ayait Yerusalem: naarai kerai lomunot, koyonite Edeke aitelekarit luepu. 3Kasodi atemar kes atiar, “Sirieŋaanario irutei luka Yerusalem eroko akoloŋ emwanuuna; naebwoenenata kesi idarete, kogolonokinete ikekia, kotikete katiketa: kibwaanakinete ikedarak kanul luibojete ko Yerusalem, ninitunanan kabeanakinike, ka nininujanan asesena keketogo.” 4Alal ere, kopol da; konye ekidioko ituŋa luajaas toma, komam itogoi da edukuk.
5 # Awa 2:6; Rom 11:36; 1 Kor 4:7; 2 Kor 8:16; Yak 1:17. Kipiiki Ekadeke otauka atukonokin isotia, ka ikayaitok, ka ituŋa kimarai kaurikec. Kasodi adumun eitabo loka aur naka ŋul kaluageunete anyoun, kadum akiro nu iwadikatai ebe:
6 # Esr 2:1-70. Lu kes idwe luka ebuku, luanyounete kaijoreorio kanul kalueioreoritai, luabu Nebukadanesar ekabaka loko Babulon kijoreoo, luabonunete Yerusalem ka Yuda, ninitunanan orekee; 7#Esr 2:2. luapote nepepe ka Serubabel, ka Yesua, ka Nekemia, ka Asaria, ka Raamia, ka Nakamani, ka Morudekai, ka Bilisan, ka Misiperet, ka Bigibai, ka Nekum, ka Baana.
Etiai loka ikiliok luka ituŋa luka Isirael: 8idwe luka Paros, ilukumin iarei ka akwatat ka akaisakanykaarei ka luarei. 9Idwe luka Sepatia akwat auni ka akaisakanykaarei ka luarei. 10Idwe luka Ara, akwat akanykape ka akaisakany ka luarei. 11Idwe luka Pakat-moab, lukodwe luka Yesua ka Yoab, ilukumin iarei ka akwat akanykauni ka itomon ka lukanykauni. 12Idwe luka Elam, elukumit ka akwat aarei ka akaisakany ka luonon. 13Idwe luka Satu, akwat akanykauni ka akaisaoŋon ka lukany. 14Idwe luka Sakai, akwat akanykaarei ka akaisakanykape. 15Idwe luka Binui, akwat akanykape ka akaisaoŋon ka lukanykauni. 16Idwe luka Bebai, akwat akanykape ka akaisaarei ka lukanykauni. 17Idwe luka Asagad, ilukumin iarei ka akwat auni ka akaisaarei ka luarei. 18Idwe luka Adonikam, akwat akanykape ka akaisakanykape ka lukanykaarei. 19Idwe luka Bigibai, ilukumin iarei ka akaisakanykape ka lukanykaarei. 20Idwe luka Adin, akwat akanykape ka akaisakany ka lukany. 21Idwe luka Ater, luka Kesekia, akaisakanykaoŋon ka lukanykauni. 22Idwe luka Kasum, akwat auni ka akaisaarei ka lukanykauni. 23Idwe luka Besai, akwat auni ka akaisaarei ka luonon. 24Idwe luka Karip, akwatat ka itomon ka luarei. 25Idwe luka Gibeon, akaisakanykaoŋon ka lukany. 26#Age 35:6; Rus 2:4; 1 Sam 17:12; 2 Siit 11:6; Mik 5:2; Mat 2:1. Ituŋa luka Betlekem ka Netopa, akwatat ka akaisakanykauni ka lukanykauni. 27#Yos 21:18; 1 Siit 6:54,60; Esr 2:23; Yer 1:1. Ituŋa luka Anatot, akwatat ka akaisaarei ka lukanykauni. 28Ituŋa luka Betasamabet, akaisaoŋon ka luarei. 29Ituŋa luka Kireat-yearim, Kepira, ka Beerot, akwat akanykaarei ka akaisaoŋon ka luuni. 30Ituŋa luka Rama ka Geba, akwat akanykape ka akaisaarei ka lodiopet. 31#1 Sam 13:2; Isa 10:28. Iruna luka Mikimas, akwatat ka akaisaarei ka luarei. 32Ituŋa luka Bet-el ka Ai, akwatat ka akaisaarei ka Iuuni. 33Ituŋa luka Nebo ece, akaisakany ka luarei, 34Ituŋa luka Elam ece, elukumit ka akwat aarei ka akaisakany ka luonon, 35Idwe luka Karim, akwat auni ka akaisaarei. 36Idwe luka Yeriko, akwat auni ka akaisaoŋon ka lukany, 37Idwe luka Lod, ka Kadid ka Ono, akwat akanykaarei ka akaisaarei ka lodiopet. 38Idwe luka Senaa, ilukumin iuni ka akwat akanykaoŋon ka akaisauni.
39 # 1 Siit 24:7. Isasedotin: idwe luka Yedaia, lukokek loka Yesua, akwat akanykaoŋon ka akaisakanykaarei ka luuni. 40#1 Siit 24:14. Idwe luka Imer, elukumit ka akaisakany ka luarei. 41#1 Siit 9:12; 24:9. Idwe luka Pasur, elukumit ka akwat aarei ka akaisaoŋon ka lukanykaarei. 42#1 Siit 24:8; Esr 10:31. Idwe luka Karim, elukumit ka itomon ka lukanykaarei.
43Ilebin: idwe luka Yesua, luka Kadumiel, lukodwe luka Kodeba, akaisakanykaarei ka luonon. 44Ikayook: idwe luka Asap, akwatat ka akaisaoŋon ka lukanykauni. 45Ikagolok: idwe luka Salum, idwe luka Ater, idwe luka Talumon, idwe luka Akub, idwe luka Katita, idwe luka Sobai, akwatat ka akaisauni ka lukanykauni.
46Inetinim: idwe luka Sika, idwe luka Kasupa, idwe luka Tabaot; 47Idwe luka Keros, idwe luka Sia, idwe luka Padon; 48idwe luka Lebana, idweluka Kagaba, idwe luka Salamia; 49idwe luka Kanan, idwe luka Gidel, idwe luka Gakar; 50idwe luka Reaia, idwe luka Resin, idwe luka Nekoda; 51idwe luka Gasam, idwe luka Usa, idwe luka Pasea; 52idwe luka Besai, idwe luka Meunim, idwe luka Nepusesim; 53idwe luka Bakabuk, idwe luka Kakupa, idwe luka Karukur; 54idwe luka Basilit, idwe luka Mekida, idwe luka Karasa; 55idwe luka Barakos, idwe luka Sisera, idwe luka Tema; 56idwe luka Nesia, idwe luka Katipa.
57 # Age 9:25,26; 1 Ika 5:13,14; 2 Siit 2:17,18. Idwe luka ikejaanakinak luka Solomon: idwe luka Sotai, idwe luka Soperet, idwe luka Perida; 58idwe luka Yaala, idwe luka Darakon, idwe luka Gidel; 59idwe luka Sepatia, idwe luka Katil, idwe luka Pokeretkasebain, idwe luka Amon.
60Inetinim kere, ka idwe luka ikejaanakinak luka Solomon, emwaasi akwat auni ka akaisakanykaoŋon ka luarei.
61 # Esr 2:59. Lu kes luanyounete koTel-mela, koTel-karasa, koKerub, koAdon, ka Imer da: konye mam apotu kopedosi aitodun ikekia luka lukapapaakee, karaida auriokee, arai keraas luka Isirael: 62idwe luka Delaia, idwe luka Tobia, idwe luka Nekoda, akwat akanykape ka akaisaoŋon ka luarei. 63#2 Sam 17:27; 19:31-34; 1 Ika 2:7; Esr 2:61. Luka isasedotin: idwe luka Kobaia, idwe luka Kakos, idwe luka Barasilai loamanyit aberu kotoma adwe nuka Barasilai Egileadit, komaikinai ekirorikee. 64Komoata nul neewadikatai kes kotoma kanul kaluemaritai kaurikee, konye mam abu kotakanu: kanuka ŋun koomitos ebe amunaunos, kirenearai kosasedotene. 65#Any 28:30; Leb 8:8; Mar 7:89; 27:18-21; Ikis 33:8; Tub 1:1. Kotema Etirusata ebe kinyeis ainyam iboro lukalaunak noi, aitodol neebwoia esasedoti loejai Urim ka Tumim.
66Ekodet kere ncpepe emwaasi ilukumin akaisaoŋon ka luarei, ka akwat auni ka akaisakanykape, 67komam imarakin ikejaanakinakakee ka akejaanakinakakee, luemwaasi ilukumin ikanykaarei ka akwat auni ka akaisauni ka lukanykaarei: ajaas da ka ikayook ka akayook akwat aarei ka akaisaoŋon ka lukany. 68Emwaasi ibalasinikee akwat akanykaarei ka akaisauni ka lukanykape; inyubunukee, akwat aarei ka akaisaoŋon ka lukany. 69Akaalaikee, akwat aoŋon ka akaisauni ka nukany; asigirianakee, alukumin akanykapc ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisaarei.
70 # Nek 8:9; 10:1. Apotu ice kokapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee koinakinos aswam.
Abu Etirusata koinak idarikin luka esabu elukumit, atiboi akaisakany, ka inapen luka isasedotin akwat akany ka akaisauni toma aiŋadis. 71#Esr 2:69. Apotu ice kokapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee koinakis idarikin ilukumin akaisaarei luka esabu, ka ilatirin ilukumin iarei ka akwat aarei luka epuesa toma aiŋadis naka aswam. 72#Yob 34:10; Rom 2:11; Gal 3:28. Luainakinete ice da kotuna kes idarikin ilukumin akaisaarei luka esabu, ka ilatirin ilukumin iarei luka epuesa, ka inapen luka isasedotin akaisakanykape ka lukanykaarei.
73 # Nek 11:20; Any 23:14-17; Leb 23:24-44; Esr 3:1. Kwaŋin kiboioto isasedotin, ka Ilebin, ka ikagolok, ka ikayook, ka ice kotuna, ka Inetinim, ka Isirael kere koreriakee.
Kedolo elap lokikanyetekaarei ajaas idwe luka Isirael oreriakee.
