1 # Isa 48:2; Mat 4:5. Kiboienenete ikapolok luka itun a ko Yerusalem: kilamilamata eroet icetuŋa da, alemun idiopet kotomon aiboikin Yerusalem ere loka launan, ka kwi da ikanykaoŋon aiboikin oreria ice. 2#Tub 5:9. Kosodetc ituŋa ailipakin arereŋ ikiliok kere luenasikina aiboikin Yerusalem.
3 # 1 Siit 9:2; Nek 7:6; Esr 2:43,55. Lu kes eraas ijaka luka ebuku lueboienenete koYerusalem: konye koreria luka Yuda koiaenena nlnituŋanan alupoke kotoma oreriakec, Isirael, isasedotin, Ilebin, Inetinim, ka idwe luka ikeiaanakinak luka Solomon. 4#1 Siit 9:3; Age 38:29. Kojaenenas koYerusalem ice kodwe luka Yuda, ka lukodwe luka Benyamin. Kodwe luka Yuda; Ataia okoku loka Usia, okoku loka Sekaria, okoku loka Amaria, okoku loka Sepatia, okoku loka Makalalel lokodwe luka Peres; 5ka Maaseia okoku loka Baruk, okoku loka Kol-kose, okoku loka Kasaia, okoku loka Adaia, okoku loka Yoiarib, okoku loka Sekaria, okoku loka Esilonit. 6Odwe luka Peres kere luaiaenenas koYerusalem emwaasi ituŋa luetitin akwat aoŋon ka akaisakanykape ka lukanykauni.
7Lu kes odwe luka Benyamin; Salu okoku loka Mesulam, okoku loka Yoed, okoku loka Pedaia, okoku loka Kolaia, okoku loka Maaseia, okoku loka Itiel, okoku loka Yesaia. 8Kotupite ŋes Gabai, Salai, akwat akanykaoŋon ka akaisaarei ka lukanykauni. 9Kerai Yoel okoku loka Sikiri ekaanyanaranikec: kiareikin Yuda okoku loka Kasenua ayait ere.
10 # 1 Siit 9:10. Lukosasedotin: Yedaia okoku loka Yoiarib, Yakin, 11Seraia okoku loka Kilikia, okoku loka Mesulam, okoku loka Sadok, okoku loka Meraiot, okoku loka Akitub, ekayaiton loka etogo loka Edeke, 12Ikeeeinacan lueswamaenenete aswam naka etogo, akwat akanykauni ka akaisaarei ka luarei: ka Adaia okoku loka Yerokam, okoku loka Pelalia, okoku loka Amisi, okoku loka Sekaria, okoku loka Pasur, okoku loka Malikiya, 13ka ikeinacan, ijaka luka ikekia luka lukapapaakee, akwat aarei ka akaisaoŋon ka luarei: ka Amasisai okoku loka Asarel, okoku loka Akasai, okoku loka Mesilemot, okoku loka Imer, 14ka ikeceinacan, iruna lukirigin luetitin, akwatat ka akaisaarei ka lukanykauni: Sabidiel okoku loka Kagedolim ŋes ckaanyanaranikee.
15Lukolebin: Semaia okoku loka Kasub, okoku loka Asirikam, okoku loka Kasabia, okoku loka Buni; 16#2 Ika 12:15,16; 1 Siit 26:29; 2 Siit 34:13; 1 Kor 4:2. ka Sabetai ka Yosabad lukojaka luka Ilebin, luaanyanarete aswamisio nukokina nukotogo loka Edeke. 17#1 Siit 9:15; Nek 7:44. Ka Matania okoku loka Mika okoku loka Sabidi, okoku loka Asap, lokerai lokapolon aiŋarenikin aimal naelipenenata, ka Bakabukia, lokiareit koinacanake; ka Abuda okoku loka Samua, okoku loka Galal, okoku loka Yedutun. 18#1 Ika 11:13; Esr 9:8; Mat 24:15; Apu 11:2; 21:2. Ilebin kere luajaas ore lokalaunan emwaasi akwat aarei ka akaisakanykauni ka luonon.
19 # 1 Siit 9:17; Nek 7:45; 12:25. Bobo da ikagolok, Akub, ka Talumon, ka ikeeeinacan, luedarete irutei, emwaasi akwatat ka akaisakanykaarei ka luarei, 20Luasalakinete luka Isirael, luka isasedotin, Ilebin, ajaenenas koreria kere luka Yuda, ŋinitunanan kairumeteke. 21#2 Siit 27:3; Esr 2:43,58; Nek 3:26. Konye kiboikinos Inetinim koOpel: kopolokinito Sika ka Gisipa Inetinim.
22Ido ekaanyanaran loka Ilebin ko Yerusalem ŋes Usi okoku loka Bani, okoku loka Kasabia, okoku loka Matania, okoku loka Mika, lokodwe luka Asap, ikayook, loayait aswam naka etogo loka Edeke, 23#Esr 6:8,9. Naarai abu ekabaka kieorakinite nukec, ka aicorakinet naicasit kanuka ikayook, kwape ejaar eipud loka ninipaaran. 24#Age 38:30; 1 Siit 18:17; 23:28. Koiai Petakia okoku loka Mcsesabel, lokodwe luka Sera okoku loka Yuda, kakan naka ekabaka kanuka akiro kere nuka ituŋa.
25 # Age 23:2; Yos 14:15; 15:21. Kidukuroto anyoun koBeer-seba kiton aditot naka Kinom. 31Kiboienenete da idwe luka Benyamin anyoun koGeba ape, koMikimas ka Aiya, koBet-el ka ireriake; 32#Yer 1:1. koAnatot, ka Nob, ka Anania; 33ka Kasor, ka Rama, ka Gitaim; 34Kadid, Seboim, Nebalat; 35#1 Siit 4:14.
