Nekemia 13

13
1 # Ikis 23:3; Amo 2:1-3. Kapaaran kaŋin kosioma toma oitabo loka Musa kopupete ituŋa kere; kodumut iwadikatai nen ebe mam elomanari Emonait ka Emoabit toma atukot naka Edeke ikar kere. 2#Mar 22:5; Yos 24:9; Mar 23:8-11; Ikis 23:5; Sab 109:28; Mik 6:5. Naarai mam apotu kisiriamata idwe luka Isirael ejaas kinyamat ka akipi, konye koropata Baalam aluikin kes, aiken kes: konye kituluo Edeke ekenit araun arereŋ. 3#Nek 9:2; Yak 1:27. Kosodi kanen, kedaut apupun ikisila, kosodete atiakar kotoma Isirael erioŋet kere loka imo.
4Eroko ŋun keiaikina, abu Eliasib esasedoti loaseunitai apolokin agolai nuka etogo loka Edekewok, naarar ekamuran loka Tobia, 5kitemokinite Tobia agola naepol, nelem ibwaanakinete ainakineta nuka akiria, ka lueur maa, ka iboro, ka lukitomoneta luka akima ka ebino ka akinyet, nuainanakinio Elebin, ka kiyaook, ka ikagolok kaieorakinet; ka ainakineta nuikearitai nuka isasedotin. 6#Nek 5:14. Konye kapakio kanun kere kamamei eoŋ koYerusalem: naarai kokaru loka akaisauni ka luarei loka Aratasasata ekabaka loka Babulon, abu kalot mama ejai ekabaka, kosodi ketuboros apaarasia kenitu ekabaka: 7kabu Yerusalem, kajenu aronis naabu Eliasib kiswama kanuka Tobia, aitemokin ŋes agola kalaroi nuka etogo loka Edeke. 8#Sab 69:9; Mk 11:15-17; Yok 2:13. Ketakadik eoŋ noi: ŋes ainakin acakar kina iboro kere luka Tobia. 9#2 Siit 29:5. Kasodi aicorakin, kitesegaasi agolai: kayau do bobo iboro luka etogo loka Edeke, ka ainakineta nuka akiria ka lueur maa.
10 # Nek 10:37; Mal 3:8; 1 Tim 5:17,18; Mar 35:2. Kaienu ebe mam aponi koinakinitete Ilebin akorakinetakee; ŋes akeritotor Ilebin ka ikayook lueswamaete aswam, ninitunanan omanikorike. 11#Awa 28:4; 1 Sam 2:17. Kasodi ketoron ikayaitok, kabala ebe, “Kanukinyo inyeinere etogo loka Edeke?” Katukonoki kes, kebwaik aiboisiokec. 12#Nek 12:44. Kosodete luka Yuda kere ayaun icliopet kotomon yenika akima ka ebino ka akinyet aiŋadisia. 13#1 Kor 4:2. Kadakak ikedarak aiŋadisia, Selemia esasedoti, ka Sadok ekewadikan, lokoLebin da Pedaia: kotupak kes Kanan okoku loka Sakur, okoku loka Matania: naarai aomitai araut lumunon, aswamukec ŋes akoranakin iinacanakec. 14#Nek 5:19. Wu Ekadeke, kiitununei eoŋ kanuka nin, ido sirikimwata aswamisioka nuejok nueswamanakini etogo loka Ekadeke, ka aiiaanakinike.
15 # Any 20:10; Yer 17:21. Kapaarasia kanun abu kaany kotoma Yuda ituŋa luepudapudaete acueta kapaaran nakasabato, kitolomete da ikes, kibwaanakinete iboro asigiriakec: ebino da, imusabibun, ka itinin, ka abwokon nuka iponesio kere, lukitolomete Yerusalem kapaaran nakasabato: karaun ekaienan kamakec kapaaran naagwelanariata inyamat. 16Ajaas da ituŋa luka Turo kanen, luetolomete lukecor, ka iboro luka iponesio kere, kitwegelanarete idwe luka Yuda, ko Yerusalem da kapaaran nakasabato. 17Kasodi ketoron isotia luka Yuda, katema kes atiar, “Aronis anyoin na naiswamaete, amunamun apaaran nakasabato? 18Mere kwape kwaŋin eswamaete lukapapaakus, Edekewok da mam eyauna aronis na kere mamawok, ka ere lo da? Konye yes iyatakinete ayaun anyunyura toma Isirael kanuka amunamun apaaran nakasabato.”
19 # Leb 23:32; Any 31:14-17; Nek 7:3. Kosodi kanen, napikamusi aige nakasabato korutei luka Yerusalem, kecorak aigol ikekia, kecorak da sirikenaar aitodol naedaunor nakasabato: kebwaiki ice kokejaanakinakaka aidar irutei, kinyeis aitolomun abwokon kere kapaaran nakasabato. 20Kosodete ikagwelanarak ka ikagwelak luka iboro luka iponesio kere koperoto kina ka Yerusalem irwan atipet arai aarei. 21Karaun ekajenan mamakec, kabala kes ebe, “Kanukinyo iperioto yesi airimor arwatat? Arai kiswamata bobo kwaŋin, kaeni yes.” Kosodi ageun kapaaran kaŋin mam bobo apotu kopotu kanakasabato, 22#Nek 12:30. Kasodi aicorakin Ilebin aitalaun bonik, aponare aidar irutei, aitalaun apaaran nakasabato. Wu Ekadeke, kiitununei eoŋ kanuka ŋin, kitim kwape ejaar akoniitim naepol noi.
23Kapaarasia kanun da abu kaany lyudayan luamanyitos aŋor nuka Asudod, ka nuka Amon, ka nuka Moab: 24kinerasi idwekec da aŋajep nainyalinyala naka Asudod, kwabo mam apedorete einer aŋajep naka Iyudayan, konye kwape ajaar aŋajep naka ninlteker. 25#Awa 28:4; Ikis 25:2; Esr 7:26; 10:5; Nek 10:29,30. Kasodi ketoron kes, kelamaki da kes, kanaŋanana da ice kakesi, kainyul da ikecetima, kesiyapoik kes kolimito Edeke ebe, “Mam ikoranakinete adwekus odwekec, karaida amanyanakin adwekec odwekus, karaida yes ikulepek. 26#1 Ika 11:1; 2 Sam 12:24,25. Mam kereka abu Solomon eka baka loka Isirael kodiak kotoma kanun? Konye toma atekerin nuipu mam etakanuna ekabaka loariakatar ŋesi, abu Ekedeke komina ŋes, abu Edeke kitarau ŋes ekabaka loka Isirael kere: konye apotu aŋor numo kitapaasi ŋes, 27#Esr 10:2. Aso do kopupunoi yes da iswamaete aronis na naepol kere, aibucar mama ejai Edekewok aimany aŋor numo?”
28 # Nek 12:10. Kerai ediopet kodwe luka Yoiada, okoku loka Eliasib esasedoti lopeti ekamuran loka Sanabalat Ekoronit: kanuka nun abu kerenea ŋes kamakaŋ, 29#Nek 6:14; Mal 2:4. Wu Ekadeke, kiitununei kes, naarai komunamunata esasedotene, ka aitutuket naka esasedotene, ka naka Ilebin.
30 # Nek 10:30; 12:1. Kwaŋin abu ketesegaaria kes mama ejaas imo kere, kaseik aijaanakin naka isasedotin ka naka Ilebin, ŋinituŋanan toma aswamuke: 31ka ainakinet naka akito, kapaarasia nueseunitai, ka nuka araito nugeet. Wu Ekadeke, kiitununei eoŋ kanuka ajokis.
