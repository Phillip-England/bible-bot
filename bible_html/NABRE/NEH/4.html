Nekemia 4

4
1 # Esr 4:1-5; Nek 2:10; Sab 2:1-4; Asw 5:17. Konye kepupu Sanabalat kwape kidukio isio arwatat, konyunyurikin, kisiop noi, kisekel Iyudayan. 2Kiner kakonyen nuka iinaeanake ka ajore naka Samaria, kobala ebe, “Iswamaete inyo Iyudayan lu lukalanyak? Ekotosi kereka abwaun bobo? Ekotosi ainakin asuban? Ekotosi adaun kapaaran adiopet? Ekotosi aisikwarun amor katukon nuka aapoŋor, naarai ieweitai?” 3#Nek 2:10,19. Kosodi Tobia Emonait loedunyas kaŋes kotema atiar, “Kanin da yenedukito kesi, arai kicaka ekue, ebilibilei arwatatakec nakamor.” 4#Sab 123:3,4; Isa 53:3; Luk 16:14; 1 Sam 17:26; Sab 79:12; Awa 3:34; Kos 12:14. Kiira ijo, wu Edekekosi; naarai kimeni: kinyakak amorositikee akwesikee, kokorak anatario kakwap nakaijoreorio: 5#Sab 59:5-13; 69:27; Yer 18:23; 2 Tim 4:14. sirikirapak numam adolitekec, aronisikec da kinyek amwatario kakonyenekon: naarai kitunyunyurikis jo kakonyen nuka ikadukok.
6Kasodete kadukoto arwatat; arwatat kere kimorikikin nepepe aitodol atutubet naka amukururuke: naarai apotu ituŋa kibwaikis itauon aswam,
7Konye kepuput Sanabalat, ka Tobia, ka Iarabun, ka Imonain, ka Isudodain, ebe iyatanakin aswam naka aiteteun arwatatin nuka Yerusalem, ageun da ainwok arorwaitin, konyunyurikinos noi; 8#Sab 2:1-3; Isa 8:9,10; Asw 23:12,13. kinerutu kere nepepe aponare aitiji Yerusalem, aisiyaloŋoŋ lukotoma. 9#Yob 22:27; Sab 50:15; Mat 26:41. Konye kipou kelipata mama ejai Edekekosi, aibwaikin da ikekwenyanarak paaran ka kuare da kanukee.
10Kotema Yuda atiar, “Eŋoŋar agogoŋ naka ikebwokak abwokon, ipu aapoŋor; tape mam kipedori aiduk arwatat.” 11Kotemasi lukasurupokosi atiar, “Mam ejenunete, karaida aanyun, aitodol nekilomaria kidiŋikec, kasodete aiar, ketanatjata aswamukee.” 12Kosodi kanen, kepotu Iyudayan luaraas iidunyetakee, kolimokis kesi sio aatenak atomon kaiboisio kere ebe, “Ibusakinit kobonut mamakosi.” 13Ŋes kebwaikinia ituŋa toma atutubeta nuijesakina kapak naajai kau karwatat, kaiboisio nuelapat, kebwaik do ituŋa kwape ajaatar ikekiakee, koiaas da kikilenakec, ka akwarasikec, ka akabwaikec da. 14#Mar 14:9; Ikis 1:29; 10:17; Yob 37:22; Bur 12:20,21,28,29; 2 Sam 10:12. Kasodi kasesen, kanyou, katema isotia, ka ikayaitok, ka ituŋa ice atiar, “Sirikiyoŋa kes: kiitunitos EJAKAIT, loepollokaŋosibib, kojiosi kanuka iinacanakus, ka idwekus, lusap ka lupesur, ka aŋorokus ka itogoikus da.”
15 # Yob 5:12; Sab 33:10. Kosodi kanen, kepuputu lukasurupokosi ebe ajenun kamakosi, ido etapalau Edeke akeeeinerun, kasodete isio kere aboŋor mama ejai arwatat, ninitunanan toma aswamuke. 16Kosodi kanen, agear kapak kaŋin, eswamaenenete atutubet naka ikakeiaanakinak aswam, kitinito atutubet kakesi akwaras, ka ibukui, ka akabwai, ka ikootin luka asua; koiaananas ikayaitok kau kekek kere loka Yuda. 17#1 Kor 16:13; Pes 6:10. Ŋul luadukenenete arwatat, ka nul luebwokenenete abwokon kigugunatos, kiswamai ŋinituŋanan aswam kadiopekan, kitinite naee da yeniiiisike, 18Ikadukok da, koenikinite ŋinituŋanan ekileŋike anabcteke, kodukenenei kwaŋin. Ŋol da loakusenenei arupepe kiiaenena keoŋ, 19Kalimoki isotia, ka ikayaitok, ka ituŋa ice ebe, “Epol aswam, elal da, kiderakina oni karwatat, elwanikina ijinitunanan ka yeniee: 20#Any 14:14; Yos 23:10; Ikis 1:30; 3:22. kotoma niniibois naiirariata eporoto loka arupepe, kokerununete ayuun sio; kijiikini Edeke oni.”
21Kasodete keswamaenenete aswam: kitinenenete atutubetekec akwaras ageun eeilakide kiton neetakanuniata aacer. 22Ido toma apak kaŋin kalimok ituŋa ebe, “Koperenenei ninitunanan ka ekekejaanakinan toma Yerusalem, koraananas ikedarakakosi kuare, ido kitaaritos paaran.” 23Kanuka nun mam kilaeununei inapcnekosi, karaida eoŋ karaida ikainaean, karaida ikakeiaanakinak, karaida ituŋa lukedarak lukatupito eoŋ, kolosenenei ŋinitunanan atan itiŋ it yenijiisike.
