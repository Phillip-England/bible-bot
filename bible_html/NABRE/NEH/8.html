Nekemia 8

8
1 # Nek 3:26; 12:37; Esr 7:6; Isa 8:20; Yer 8:8,9; 2 Siit 34:15; Mal 4:4. Kotukonokinos ituŋa kere kwapc ediopetunanan aibois naelal, naesesenas kerute loka Akipi; kobalasi Esera ekewadikan ayaun eitabo loka ikisila luka Musa, luabu EJAKAIT kicorak Isirael. 2#Ikis 31:11,12; 1 Pet 3:7; Leb 23:24. Koyau Esera esasedoti ikisila akonyen nuka atukot, ikiliok ka aŋor da, ka kere luapedorete apupun kajenun, kapaaran nasodit naka elap lokikanyetekaarei. 3Kosiom nu koinaren kaibois naelal naesesenas kerute loka Akipi ageun tupuruc sek kiton kidin paaran, kojaas kiliok ka aŋor da, ka nul luapedorete ajenun; kitoikis ituŋa kere apupokin eitabo loka ikisila. 4Kobwo Esera ekewadikan kotut loka akito, loekeitoi kanuka nun; kobwaata Matitia, ka Sema ka Anaia, ka Uria, ka Kilikia, ka Maaseia eiduny kaŋes kakekan nakoteten; kakekan da nakokediany Pedaia, ka Misael, ka Malakiya, ka Kasum, ka Kasabadana, ka Sekaria, ka Mesulam. 5#Tub 3:20. Kopuko Esera eitabo kakonycn nuka ituŋa kere; (naarai ajai kuju kituna kere;) kepuko, kobwaata ituŋa kere. 6#Mar 5:22; Nek 5:13; Mat 6:13; 1 Kor 14:16; Aku 3:41; Any 4:31. Kimala Esera EJAKAIT, Edeke lokapolon. Kobonokis ituŋa kere ebe, “Amen, Amen”; kikianarete akaninikec: kosodete kituluroto akwesikee, komaata EJAKAIT, kitulurito akecekonyen kwap, 7#Leb 10:11. Ido Yesua, ka Bani, ka Serebia, ka Yamin, ka Akub, ka Sabetai, ka Kodia, ka Maaseia, ka Kelita, ka Asaria, ka Yosabad, ka Kanan, ka Pelaia, ka Ilebin, kiteienut ituŋa ikisila; kobwaata ituŋa kaiboisiokee. 8#Kbk 2:2; Asw 8:30-35. Kosiomata citabo, toma okisila luka Edeke, nuepuposi; koyautu apolou, koienunos nuasomio,
9 # Esr 2:63; Nek 7:65; 2 Siit 35:3; Kos 4:6; Leb 23:24; Ikis 16:14; Lim 3:4; Mal 2:13. Kosodi Nekemia lokerai Etirusata, ka Esera esasedoti ekewadikan, ka Ilebin luesisianakincte ituŋa atemar ituŋa kere atiar, “Na ŋes apaaran nakalaunan mama ejai EJAKAIT Ekusideke; sirikibworoto, karaida aimony akiyo.” Naarai amonyete ituŋa kere akiyo naapupunioto akiro nuka ikisila. 10#Est 9:19; Luk 11:41; Apu 11:10. Kosodi atemar kes atiar, “Koloto, konyamata asiepai, komata luewiny, kijukakis akorakineta nin yenimam itemokinitoi ibore: naarai apaaran na nakalaunan mama ejai EJAKAITiwok: kwabo sirikikadakis; naarai aiyalama naka EJAKAIT ŋes agogonokus.” 11Kisililinata Ilebin ituŋa kere ebe, “Kililinata, naarai erai apaaran nakalaunan; kwabo sirikikadakis.” 12Kolototo ituŋa kere ainyam ka aimat, ka aijukar akorakineta, kaitalakar naepol noi, naarai aienutu akiro nualimonokinio kes.
13Kapaaran nakiareit da kotukonokinos ikapolok luka ikekia luka lukapapaa luka ituŋa kere, ka isasedotin, ka Ilebin, neejai Esera ekewadikan, aitoikin aki necjaas akiro nuka ikisila. 14#Age 33:17; Leb 23:34. Koanyut nukiwadikatai okisila, kwape abu EJAKAIT kicorakinia kama ka Musa ebe koperenenete idwe luka Isirael oswamin kopucit loka elap lokikanyetekaarei. 15Koramenenete kolimonokinete koreriakec kere, ka Yerusalem ebe, “Koloto moru, koomata atenuna nuka eseitun, ka atenuna da nuka eseitun loka ais, ka atenuna nuka ikadasia, ka atenuna nuka idukudukun, ka atenuna nuka ikito luetumito, aiswamaa iswamin, kwape kiwadikatere.” 16#Ikis 22:8; Nek 12:39. Kosodete ituŋa alomar, koyanaut kes, kiswamata da iswamin, ninitunanan oswi loka etogoke, ka alaroikee, ka alaroi nuka etogo loka Edeke, kaibois da naelal naka erute loka Akipi, kaibois da naelal naka erute loka Epuraim. 17Kiswamata ekodet loka ŋul kere Iuabonunete kaiioreorio iswamin, kiboikinos oswamin: naarai agear kapaarasia nuka Yesua okoku loka Nun aitodol apaaran ŋin mam idwe luka Isirael eswamaenenete kwaŋin, Kojai ailel naepol noi. 18#Ikis 31:10; Mar 29:35. Ido ŋinipaaran, agear kapaaran nasodit aitodol apaaran nakawasia, asiomenenei ŋesi kotoma oitabo loka ikisila luka Edeke, Koricata epucit apaarasia akanykaarei; apaaran nakikanyetekauni kojai atukonokinet nakayamakina, kwape ajaar aicorakinet.
