Yeremia 5

5
1 # Esk 22:30; Mik 7:2; Sab 12:1. Aso kokerata laije ka laiie korotin luka Yerusalem,
Koanyut bo, kojenut, komoata kaiboisioke nuelalak,
Arai ipedorete aanyun irunanan,
Arai ejai yeniswamai nuebeito, yenemoot amunonut;
Aso etimi eoŋ ere,
2 # Tit 1:16. Karaida kineras atiar, ‘Kwape eiaria EJAKAIT’;
Abeit iyapoete nukaiŋal.
3 # 2 Siit 16:9; Isa 1:5; Yer 7:28; Sep 3:2. Wu EJAKAIT, mere ma esesenete akonikonye abeit?
Inom kes, konye mam itururwete;
Idany kes, konye agiro aicorio:
Etogogonoto akecekonyen aitelekarit atiiba;
Agiro aboŋun,
4 # Yer 8:7. Kasodi einer atiar, “Abeit icanas lu: ebanaaka;
Naarai mam ejenete erot loka EJAKAIT,
Karaida ekisil loka Ekecedeke.
5 # Mik 3:1; Sab 2:3. Alosionori do mama ejaas lukapolok, kenera kakes;
Naarai ejenete kesi erot loka EJAKAIT, ka ekisil loka Ekecedeke.”
Konye abila ŋul eloit kediopetau, anedata aeneta.
6 # Yer 4:7; Kbk 1:8; Kos 13:7. Kanuka ŋun eari rwaru kes eŋatuny loelomuni kamoni,
Eŋasi kes epeet loka amodiŋ,
Eperuni eris koreriakec,
Ŋinituŋanan yenelomuni kotoma enyilinyilio;
Naarai ipu adiakanaretakec,
Eyatakin akecepaar.
7 # Yos 23:7; Yer 12:16; Amo 8:14; Gal 4:8; Ikis 32:15. “Apedori biai aitim jo?
Kajala ikonidwe eoŋ,
Eyapoikis ŋul lumam eraas idekesio:
Abu kesimon kes kosodete adotiar,
Kotukonokinos katukon otogoi luka nudotia.
8 # Esk 22:11; 2 Sam 11:2-4; Yer 13:27. Esubito bala ibalasin lukitadakio tupuruc:
Ŋolituŋanan icicina kaberu naka yenice.
9 # Isa 1:24; Yer 44:22; Esk 7:9. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe:
Mam kereka eticani kanuka ŋun?
Ido mam tope inyani ekatau kateker naikoni nen?
10 # Yer 39:8. “Kodokasi arwatatinike, komudiasi:
Konye sirikitanana cut:
Kolemasi aketenun;
Naarai mam eraas nuka EJAKAIT.
11 # Yer 3:20. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Naarai keŋala noi noi ekek loka Isirael,
Ka ekek loka Yuda.
12 # 2 Siit 36:16; Isa 28:15; Yer 14:13. Anerokis EJAKAIT, kineras atiar, ‘Mam nuiswamai ŋesi; Kwabo mam kidoikini oni aronis,
Kwabo mam kianyuni ekileŋ karaida eteŋei,
13Eraunos inabin da ekwam,
Kwabo emamei akirot kotomakec:
Iswamakinio kes kwaŋin.’’’
14 # Yer 1:9; Kos 6:5; Apu 11:5. Kanuka ŋun ebala kwanata EJAKAIT,
Edeke loka aioresio, ebe:
“Kanu ineratar akirot ŋin,
Koany, etuluori akakiro kaitukukon araun akim,
Ka ituŋa lu da araun akito, icwei kes.
15 # Ikis 28:49; Isa 5:26; Yer 1:15; Isa 39:3. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe:
Koany, ayauni mama iiaas ateker naebuni kama elwana,
Ijo ekek loka Isirael:
Ateker nakagogonon, ateker nakolo sek,
Ateker namam ijeni akecenaiep,
Kwabo mam ijenuni nuineras.
16Ecoekec loka ikoioi erai ailit naesabara,
Eraas kere ikiliok lukirigin.
17 # Leb 26:16; Ikis 28:31; Tub 6:3,4. Ido enyamete ikonidwenyan, ka ikoninyamat,
Lutikenyama ikonidwe lusap ka lupesur:
Enyamete ikonisipanin luka aituk ka luka amerekekin; Enyamete ekonimusabibu, ka etinikon:
Ejepejepeete kekilen ikonireria luebwaitai, luimunokina.”
18“Konye ebala EJAKAIT ebe, karaida kapaarasia kanun, mam ketananete cut. 19#Ikis 29:24; 28:48; 1 Ika 9:8; Yer 13:22. Kosodi kanen, naineriata atiar, ‘Kanukinyo kiswamakinia EJAKAIT Ewokodeke akiro ŋun kere?’ Kosodi Koboŋok kes ebe, ‘Kwape kinyeiniata yesi eoŋ, kijaanakis idekesio lumo kakwapukus, ijaanakinete kwape kwaŋin imo kakwap namere nakus.’’’
20Kolimosi nu kokek loka Yakob,
Kolimonokis koYuda, kineras atiar,
21 # Isa 6:9-10; Esk 12:2; Mk 8:18; Mat 13:14; Yok 12:40; Asw 28:26. “Kiirasi nu, yes ituŋa luibanaaka, luemamei aijen;
Lueiaas akonye, komam ianyunete;
Lueiaas aki komam ipupunete.
22 # Apu 15:4; Yob 26:10; Awa 8:29; Yob 38:8-11. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Mam ca kiyoŋito eoŋ?
Mam imaranete kakonyeneka?
Ebwaik asine araun eikor loka aŋolol,
Kokisil lomam edaun, tetere mam etubor,
Karaida edakununos akailan, mam epedorete aipikor;
Karaida irurumaete, mam epedorete atubor.
23Konye ejaas ituŋa lu itauon luepaarete ido luepaliikis;
Apaasi aloto.
24 # Mat 5:45; Yok 2:23; Age 8:22. Kwabo mam ebalas kotauonokec ebe, “Kayonate EJAKAIT Ewokodeke,
Loeinakini akiru kapakike;
Akiru naka aiporo ka naka akamu da;
Loinadakini oni asabaton nuseuna nuka aidweny.’
25Aremosi numam adolitekus akiro ŋun,
Erebokis aronisiokus ajokis kamakus.
26Naarai edumunos ikaronok kotuŋaka:
Idarete, kwape eperunioto ikalokak akweny;
Elokete atacit, ipokete ituŋa.
27Kwape ilelebunotor akweny aruja,
Kwape kwaŋin ilelebunotor itogoikec aiŋal:
Ŋes apolokorotor, kobarata.
28 # Sab 73:12. Etumitos, inutakina:
Eebo, elitoro kaswamisio nukaronok:
Mam etubete kabeit akiro nuka ikokiok,
Tetere ekeriakaros;
Mam epurokinete luilwaritos nuebeito.
29 # Mal 3:5. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Mam kereka etieani kanuka ŋun?
Mam ekatau inyani ateker naikoni nen?”
30Adoloki akirot nakaumokin ka nasogim akwap;
31 # Esk 13:6; Isa 30:10. Edwarete inabin lukenalak,
Eyaito isasedotin kanuka aisigalikinikec;
Ekotosi ikatuŋa kwaŋin;
Ikwaikinete yesi ai kedolu esaŋe?
