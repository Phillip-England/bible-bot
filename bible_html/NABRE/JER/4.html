Yeremia 4

4
1 # Yok 2:12. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
“Arai ikoto aboŋun mama ajai eoŋ, ijo Isirael,
Ibonuni mama ajai:
Arai kilema italesiakon kakonyeneka,
Ŋes konyet mam irianirianar;
2 # Ikis 10:20; Gal 3:8; Isa 45:23; 48:1; 45:25; 1 Kor 1:31. Ido iyapoi ebe, ‘Kwape ejaria EJAKAIT,’
Kabeit, kotube, kadolit da;
Ilipakinos atekerin arereŋ kotomaken,
Tomakeŋ da ŋes itubusikinotor.”
3 # Kos 10:12; Mat 13:7. Naarai ebala EJAKAIT ituŋa luka Yuda ka Yerusalem da kwanata ebe:
“Kokorioto alupokus nuiukarito,
Sirikiyeata neeiaas ikukwa.
4 # Ikis 10:16; Kol 2:11; Sep 2:2. Kokepas bonik kama ejai EJAKAIT,
Kolemas amunym nuka itauonokus,
Yes ituŋa luka Yuda, ka luibojete koYerusalem:
Kinyek akanyunyura alomun kwape kakim,
Kicwe komamei yenepedori aitudeŋ,
Kanuka aronis naka aswamisiokus.”
5Kolimosi koYuda, kisiarakis akiro koYerusalem, kineras atiar,
“Kokutata yesi arupepe kakwap:
Kiworoto kobalas ebe,
‘Kotukonokinos, kaloto oreria luebwaitai.’
6 # Yer 1:13. Kikeasi aanyunet naesesenas ka Sion:
Kokerata kolakunos, sirikipaikis:
Naarai ayauni aronis naebuni kagolitomei,
Ka amudiar naepol.
7 # Dan 7:4. Adakun eŋatuny kamonike, ekamudiaran loka atekerin;
Ejai orot, alom kaiboisike;
Aitisilior akwapukon, tetere iswapete ireriakon,
Komamei yeniboie kotoma.
8 # Isa 22:12. Kanuka ŋun konapa ipukoi,
Kokuudianata kibwololoto:
Naarai anyunyura naededen naka EJAKAIT
Mam ibelokino kamawok.
9“Kosodi kanen kapaaran kaŋin ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ipirisiar etau loka ekabaka, ka itauon luka ikapolok; isirienunio isasedotin, eumokinete da inabin.” 10#Isa 63:17; Esk 14:9; 2 Ses 2:11. Kasodi eoŋ atemar atiar, “Wu okwe, EJAKAIT Edeke! Abeit iŋalat noi ituŋa lu, ka Yerusalem da, kobala ebe, ‘Ŋaunos kainapakin’; kwabo adoloki ekileŋ aiiarakec.”
11 # Isa 27:8; Yer 51:1; Esk 17:10; Kos 13:5. Kapak kaŋin elimokinio ituŋa lu, ka Yerusalem da ebe, “Ekwam loemwana loebuni kaiboisio nuiceleleete kotoma amodiŋ mama ejai akoku naka ikatuŋa, mere aipiet, karaida aitalaun; 12Ŋes ekwam Loegogoŋ adepar ŋun loebuni kanukan: kwana eoŋ da enerakini kes itubesia.”
13 # Isa 5:28; Ikis 28:49; Aku 4:19; Kos 8:1; Kbk 1:8. Koany, ebuni ŋesi kwape idouon,
Agalinike da kwape etapipirio:
Etiroko ibalasinike adepar imorukaalen.
Itukwi oni! naarai kinatario,
14 # Isa 1:16; Yak 4:8. Wu Yerusalem, kilot ekonitau alemar nukaronok, tetere kitajario.
Itodoli ai eperete akoniwomisio nueroko kotomakon?
15 # Yer 8:16. Naarai elimori eporoto loebuni koDan,
Elimonokini aronis naebuni koukon luka Epuraim:
16 # Yer 5:15. Kitwekenya atekerin;
Koany, kolimonokis Yerusalem ebe,
“Epote ikerereok anyoun kakwap naelwana,
Edolokinete ireria luka Yuda.
17 # 2 Ika 25:1. Kwape ikedarak emanikor esurokinete ŋes airimor;
Naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kepaliiki eoŋ.
18 # Yob 20:6-11; Sab 107:17; Awa 1:31; Isa 50:1; Yer 2:17. Ekonirot ka aswamisiokon kes ayaunete akiro ŋun mamakon;
Ŋin ŋes aronisikon,
Naarai edwar, naarai adoloki otaukon.”
19 # Isa 16:11; Luk 19:41,42. Wu akaijar! Wu akaijar! Kaditarit teni kiton otauka;
Ebala etauka ditidit;
Mam apedori aililiŋ;
Naarai apupi eporoto loka arupepe,
Elala loka ejie.
20 # Sab 42:7; Yer 10:20. Iworot amudiar atupakin amudiar;
Naarai enataritai akwap kere:
Kitiut ikaweman eŋatario,
Aigalisioka da katipet.
21Itodoli ai aanyun aanyunet,
Kapupi eporoto loka arupepe?
22 # Rom 1:22; 16:19; 1 Kor 14:20. “Naarai ebanaka ikatuŋa, mam kajenete;
Eraas idwe lujikit, emamei kes aijen:
Ecoak kesi aswam nueroko,
Konye emamei aijen aswam nuejok.”
23 # Isa 24:19; Age 1:2. Abu kaany akwap, ido koany, ajai atai komam etiakak;
Akuj da amamei aica.
24 # Esk 38:20. Abu kaany ironan, ido koany, komaranata,
Kilokilokos iukon epuai kepuai,
25 # Sep 1:3. Abu kaany, ido koany, komamei ituŋanan,
Kekerata akweny kere nuka apak.
26Abu kaany, ido koany, emanikor lopusuk araun ais, Ketiyanakinos ireriake kere kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, Kakonyen nuka anyunyurake naededen.
27 # Yer 5:10. Naarai ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, “Esilior akwap kere; konye mam etanaŋi cur.
28 # Isa 33:9; 50:3; Yer 12:4; Kos 4:3; Mar 23:19. Kanuka ŋun ibworo akwap,
Irioniar aku; kokuiu:
Naarai abu kalim, abu kanerok,
Ido mam abu keitu, ido mam ejulakini.”
29 # 2 Ika 25:4. Ekerete lukore kere kanuka ecelet loka ikebwanenok ka ikeketak akabwai;
Elomarete amonieik, edokarete atiiban,
Asilior ŋolire,
Emamei da ituŋanan yeniboie kotoma.
30 # 2 Ika 9:30; Aku 1:2,19. Ka jo da, nakiŋatarere, iswamai anyoika?
Karaida kinap inapen lukareŋak,
Karaida kiyogog kiboro luka esabu
Karaida italali akonyekon kekia,
Itubusikina taai;
Kimenete ipailonokon, emootos akoniijar
31 # Isa 1:15; Aku 1:17. Naarai apup eporoto kwape loka aberu naeriebi aidoun, Kwape loka aditar naka ŋin naidouni ekiyaike,
Eporoto loka akoku naka Sion, nainyikonokini iyena, Korionori akekanin, kobala ebe,
“Itukwi eoŋ! Eilori akaijar kakonyen nuka ikaarak.”
