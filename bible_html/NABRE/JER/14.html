Yeremia 14

14
1Akirot naka EJAKAIT naabu kobu mama ejai Yeremia nuka ekure:
2 # Isa 3:26; 15:3,4; Any 11:6; Awa 21:13. “Ibworo Yuda, iiunepepas iruteike,
Iboiete iketuŋa kokwap, kokuudianete;
Ekear aimony naka Yerusalem.
3 # Sab 40:14; 2 Sam 15:30. Ŋukarete isotiakec inyamurenekec aomare akipi:
Edolete atapar, mam edumunete akipi;
Eboŋunete ewokito amotiikee;
Irumito ileic itiriraete,
Imukorete akwesikec.
4Kanuka alup nuecilicilas,
Naarai emamei akiru naetepi akwap,
Irumito ikakoriok ileje,
Imukorete akwesikec.
5Eebo, ewurie amori kais, ejali ikokuke,
Naarai emameete inya.
6 # Yer 2:24. Ebwoete itukoi kokuiu neelapat,
Iyenanakinos adumunia ekwam kwape ikuwei;
Ewariwarete akonyekec,
Naarai emameete ikui.
7 # Sab 25:11. “Karaida kitejenikinete numam adolitekosi aluikin sio,
Kiswama ijo kanuka ekonikiror, wu EJAKAIT:
Naarai ipu akosibononoreta kau;
Kidiaka isio kama ijai,
8 # Yer 17:13; Asw 28:20; 1 Tim 1:1; Sab 46:1. Wu jo amun naka Isirael,
Eketaiaranike kapak naka ican,
Kanukinyo ti kiraunia kwape loiboiororor kakwap,
Ido kwape ekaloton loepaari aidareun akwap aiwalar?
9 # Isa 59:1; Any 29:45. Kanukinyo ti kiraunia kwape etuŋanan loeumokinit,
Kwape lokirigi lomam epedori aitajar?
Konye jo, wu EJAKAIT, ijai kidinikosi,
Kimaikinitai ekonikiror;
Sirikijal sio.”
10 # 1 Ika 17:18; Sab 109:14; Kos 8:13; Bur 8:12. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT ituŋa lu ebe,
“Kwape kwaŋin aminas airim:
Mam kitikokinito akejekee:
Ŋes mam EJAKAIT ecamakinia kes;
Iitunit kwana numam adolitekec, Iticani aronisiokee.”
11 # Any 32:10. Kabala EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, “Sirikilipak ituŋa lu ainakinio aiokis. 12#Awa 1:28; Isa 1:15; Yer 11:11; Mik 3:4. Arai ikanyete eteŋei, mam apupuni aimonyokee; arai da einakinete ainakineta nuieweio ka ainakineta nuka akiria, mam acamuni kes: konye apiari kes kekilen, ka eteŋei, ka atular da.”
13Kasodi eoŋ ebe, “Wu okwe, EJAKAIT Edeke, koany, elimonokinete inabin kes ebe, ‘Mam ianyunete ekileŋ, mam da iiaunete ketenei; konye ainakini yes ainapakin naieasit kaibois kana.’’’ 14#Isa 30:10; Yer 23:21; 2 Ses 2:9-11. Kosodi EJAKAIT atemar eoŋ atiar, “Edwareteinabin nukainal kokirorika; mam eoŋ ejukarit kes, mam abu keeorak kes, karaida einer kakes: kidwarakinete yes aitapoer nakainal, ka aidwar naatai, ka aimod naka itauonokee bonik. 15#Yer 23:15. Kanuka nun ebala kwanata EJAKAIT nuka inabin luedwarete kokirorika, komam eoŋ ejukarit kes, konye ebalasi ebe, ‘Mam ejaunos ekileŋ ka eteŋei kakwap kana’, ebe: Epiari ekileŋ ka eteŋei inabin ŋul. 16#Mat 15:14; Sab 79:3. Ituŋa da luedwarakinete kesi irenearitai korotin luka Yerusalem kanuka eteŋei ka ekileŋ: emamikinete luidaete kes, kes ka aŋorokec ka idwekec lusap ka lupesur. Naarai abukokini kes aronisikec.
17 # Aku 1:16; Yer 8:21. “Ilimokini ijo kes akirot na ebe:
Koleleata akiyo kakonyeka,
Kuare kapaaran da, komam esanere;
Naarai apaki akoku nabekot naka ikatuŋa arorwa naepol, Amusuŋut naerono noi.
18 # Esk 7:15. Arai alomari ais, Koany, luearar kekilen!
Arai alomari ore,
Aso koany, luedekasi eteneil
Naarai irimorete enabi ka esasedoti kere akwap,
Komamei kes aijen.”
19 # 2 Ika 17:20; Sab 78:59; Aku 5:22. Iŋer teni ijo eut Yuda?
Ala cut ekonitau Sion?
Kanukinyo kidanyere sio,
Komamei sio aŋaleun?
Kipou kamunos ainapakin,
Konye mam ajokis ebuno;
Kimunos apak naka aŋaleun, ido koany aburun!
20 # Esr 9:5; 1 Yok 1:9; Dan 9:8. Wu EJAKAIT, kilimori aronisikosi,
Ka numam adolit da nuka lukapapaakosi:
Naarai kipou kadiakasi kama ijai.
21 # Yer 17:12. Sirikiitapian, kanuka ekonikiror;
Sirikitisil eicoloŋ loka aibusesukon:
Kiitu, sirikibil aitutuketekon kasio,
22 # Isa 30:23. Ejaas kereka kotoma aamameak nuka ikapirin nuepedorete ayaun akiru?
Epedori cabo akuj ayaun elimilim?
Mere jo ŋopeŋol, wu EJAKAIT Edekekosi?
Ŋes kidaria jo;
Naarai isubi ijo iboro ŋul kere.
