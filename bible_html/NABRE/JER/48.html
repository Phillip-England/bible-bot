Yeremia 48

48
1 # Isa 15:1; Mar 32:38. Nuka Moab.
Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
Edeke loka Isirael cbe:
“Itukwi Nebo! naarai eswap!
Esurumi Kiriataim ileje, enataritai.
Esurumi Misigab ileje, atiyatiyai;
2Emamei bobo aiturio nako Moab.
Enerakin aronis kotoma Kesibon ebe,
‘Kopotu, kamudiasi kinyek araun ateker!’
Ŋo da, wu Madumen, kiaililinio;
Kirukitori ekileŋ.
3“Eporoto laka ecelet loebuni koKoronaim,
‘Aiŋat, ka amudiar naepo!!’
4Amudiar Moab;
Apupun aimony naka idweke ludidik.
5Naarai kaneedokere Lukit edokarete
Komonyenenete akiyo;
Naarai kaneejesarere Koronaim,
Apotu kopuputu aimony nakiean naka amudiario.
6Kokera! Kitajarata akusiijar!
Kosubata bala ekiot loka amodiŋ!
7 # Sab 49:6,7; 52:7; Yer 9:23; 1 Tim 6:17; Apu 18:17; Mar 21:29. Naarai kanuka amunokina aswamisiokon,
Ka abarakon jo da kikamario:
Ŋoreorio Kemos, ka isasedotinike,
Ka ikapolokoke da nepepe.
8Edolokini ekaŋatan nolire,
Emameun ere loekui;
Eoliori aditot, emudiario da apuuton;
Kwape enerar EJAKAIT.
9“Koinak Moab abebenoka,
Kopororia kolot;
Eraunos ikereria amejei,
Komamei da yeniboie kotoma.
10 # 1 Sam 15:3. “Kikenoi nin yenipalisikini aswam naka EJAKAIT; kikenoi da ijin yenirebokini ekileŋ kinyek abukor aokot.
11 # Sep 1:12. “Ŋamamai Moab ageun keiditeuke,
Ŋililiara akesidak;
Komam ailocoloeoo anyoun kobore kiton obore,
Ido mam ijoreoritai;
Kanuka nun eroko esalit akejijim tomake,
Ido mam auresike iiulakina.
12“Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Aso koany, epote apaarasia nueiukunia nul luebukonorete, ido ebukonorete ŋes; itoonete amotiike, ebwanabwangaete da acupaike. 13#Tub 11:24; Kos 10:6; 1 Ika 12:29; Amo 5:5,6. Irumete Moab ileic kanuka Kemos, kwape erumiata ileic ekek loka Isirael kanuka Bet-el loamunokina.
14 # Sab 33:6; Lim 9:11; Isa 16:6; Yer 9:23. “Ibalasi ai ebe, ‘Kirai ikirigin
Ka Iuetirin kojie’?
15 # Yer 50:27; 51:57; Mal 1:14. Ebala Ekabaka, ekekiror EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe,
Adoku ekamudiaran loka Moab ka ireriake,
Ekokorioros ikatumunakake luseuna oarit,
16Eapiete iean luka Moab adolun,
Arurukun aiticanitoike.
17 # Isa 9:4; 14:4. Kokuudianata ŋes, yes kere luirimorito,
Ka yes da kere luijenete ekekiror;
Kobalas ebe, ‘Abila ca ma ebela lokagogon,
Esuretait loejok!’
18 # Isa 47:1; 15:2; Yer 46:19; Mar 21:30. “Wu jo apese naiboie kotoma Dibon,
Kikokoror anyoun kaibusesukon,
Kiboikin, koiakite akure.
Naarai kibunok ekamudiaran loka Moab,
Amudia abwasinakon nuegogon,
19 # Ikis 2:36; 1 Sam 4:13,16. Wu jo yeniboie kotoma Aroer,
Kobwou korot airot:
Kiŋit loekeri ka nin da naekui;
Kiner ebe, ‘Anyoika bo?’
20 # Mar 21:13. Esurumi Moab ileic, naarai abilibiliun;
Kibwololoro, komonyata!
Kolimosi kotoma Arunon ebe, Eswap Moab.
21“Adoloki atubokinio akwap naka apuutona; Kolon, ka Yasa, ka Mepaat, 22ka Dibon, ka Nebo, ka Bet-dibilataim; 23ka Kiriataim, ka Betgamul, ka Bet-meon; 24#Amo 2:2. ka Keriot, ka Bosura, ka ireria kere luka akwap nako Moab, lueiaas kama elwana, ka lueapiete da. 25Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Atubor amumwarat naka Moab, abila akekan da.
26 # Yer 25:15. “Kitemerata ŋes, naarai itopolooro kama ejai EJAKAIT; imodumodun Moab kalekanake, imuario da. 27#Awa 24:17; Sep 2:8; Yer 2:26. Naarai mam kereka kerai Isirael eimuaritono kamakon? Aponi kereka kodumakinai kidiŋ kikokolak? Naarai nainerenenar nuke, imunimuŋi akonikou.
28 # Sab 55:6,7; Eko 2:14. “Yes luibojete koMoab, konyoutu koreria,
Kiboioto katiiba!
Kosuba bala adudumer naiswamai akaike
Ariri kaituk naka ebur.
29 # Isa 16:6. Kipupu nuka eitur loka Moab,
Ebe iturokina noi;
Aitopolooroke, ka eiturike, aomiarake,
Ka aisoŋolonut da naka crauke.
30Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ajeni ekepali;
Iŋalaŋalai ailabilabake,
Iŋalaŋalaete da akeswamisio.
31 # Isa 15:5. Ŋes ebwololoo kanuka Moab;
Eebo, adelori kanuka Moab kere:
Ebworo kanuka ituŋa luka Kir-kercs,
32 # Mar 21:13. Wu jo emusabibu loka Sibima,
Amonyi akiyo kanukon, adepar aimony naka Yaser;
Apesito akonitenun adepar aŋolol,
Kodolot aŋolol naka Yaser;
Ebirokin ekaŋatan araitokon nuka akamu,
Ka ludwenyanakon.
33 # Isa 16:9,10; Yok 1:12. Alemar aiyalama ka alakara komanikoria lupusuk kakwap nako Moab;
Ido etoonikini ebino kotoma acuesia;
Emameuni yenicakacakai keibwon;
Acela ŋin mam erai acela naka eibwoŋ.
34 # Isa 15:4-6. “Ageun kaimony naka Kesibon, aitodol Eleale, kiton Yakas da ekeseut ikeeeporotoi, ageun koSoar, aitodol Koronaim, kiton Egilat-selisiya, Naarai asilioros da akip nuka Nimirim, 35#Isa 15:2. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Bobo da etanaŋi kotoma Moab yeneinakini asuban kaiboisio nuiceleleete, ka nin da yenisupurikini ikedekesio lukisupurununo. 36#Awa 11:4; Lim 5:13,14; Isa 15:7; Luk 12:20,21. Kanuka nun emonyi ekatau kanuka Moab kwape alamarun, emonyi ekatau kwape alamarun kanuka ituŋa luka Kir-keres: kanuka ŋun aolio abar naatukonokinit.
37 # Yer 47:5; Age 37:34. “Naarai ejai ninikou epat, ebarenito nolipenek: egerer akanin kere, ejai epuko aŋabes, 38#Yer 22:28. Ejai akuudian kokuju otogoi kere luka Moab, korotinike da kere; naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Abwan Moab kwape amot namam kesiyalamikinit. 39Abwana ca! Ebwololoto ca! Ebeloki ca ma Moab asebot kileic! Eraun do Moab naimuario naituburuni ŋul kere luirimorito ŋes.”
40 # Yer 49:22; Dan 7:4; Kos 8:1; Kbk 1:8; Isa 8:8. Naarai inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Koany, eporori kwape kemorukaalet,
Loeterari akebebenoka asurokin Moab.
41 # Isa 13:8; Yer 30:6; Mik 4:9. Erumi Keriot, isirienunio abwasin nukagogon,
Eraunos itauon luka ikirigin luka Moab kapaaran kaŋin, Kwape etau loka aberu naerieba aidoun.
42 # Sab 83:4; Isa 16:13,14; Awa 16:18. Emudiario Moab komam bobo eraun ateker,
Naarai itopolooro kama ejai EJAKAIT.
43 # Isa 24:17,18. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Wu jo loiboie koMoab,
Kibunokinete jo etasuro, ka aipany, ka ataeit da,
44 # Yer 11:23. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
ŋol loekeri kotasuro ibirokin aipany;
Ŋol da loekui kaipany, ipoki atacit:
Naarai ainuni Moab nun,
Okaru loka aitieanioke.
45 # Mar 21:28; 24:17. “Ebwoete luekerete komamei agogoŋ
Kotolim loka Kesibon;
Naarai alom akim koKesibon,
Alomut aipelonoreta nuka akim kokidin Sikon;
Anyamata agor naka Moab,
Ka aboko da naka akwes nuka luecelas.
46 # Mar 21:29; Tub 11:24. Itukwi, ijo Moab!
Aoliosi ituŋa luka Kemos:
Naarai ijoreoritai ikusidwe lusap,
Akusipesur da ijoreoritai.
47 # Yer 49:6,39. Konye ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Abeit enyakuni bobo aijoreorio naka Moab
Kapaarasia nukawasia.”
Adol ber ne atubokinio naka Moab.
