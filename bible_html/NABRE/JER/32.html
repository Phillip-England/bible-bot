Yeremia 32

32
1 # Yer 39:1; 2 Ika 25:1-7. Akirot naka EJAKAIT naabu kobu mama ejai Yeremia kokaru lokitomonet loka Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda, ŋes ekaru lokitomonet kakanykauni loka Nebukadaresar, 2#Nek 3:25; Mat 5:12; Yer 33:1; 37:21. Kapak kaŋin abu aiore naka ekabaka loko Babulon kirigau Yerusalem: koenitete enabi Yeremia kalaro naka ikedarak, naajai togo loka ekabaka loka Yuda, 3#Yer 21:4-7. Naarai Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda, ŋes aenit, kobala ebe, “Kanukinyo kidwaria, kinera ebe: ‘Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Koany, ainakini ere lo akan naka ekabaka loko Babulon, ŋes da irumi: 4#2 Ika 25:4-7; Yer 39:5; 52:9. mam ekui Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda kakan naka Ikaludean, konye abeit einakinio akan naka ekabaka loko Babulon, ineras kaŋes kaituke, eanyunete akekonyen akonyen nuka ŋol. 5#Yer 27:22; 21:4; Awa 21:30. Ido eyaŋaari Sedekia Babulon. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Iboie kaŋina aitodol naaanyanaria: karaida kijioto ka Ikaludean mam kisirereŋio’?”
6Kotema Yeremia atiar, “Abu kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe, 7#Leb 25:24-32; Rus 4:4. Koany, ebuni Kanamel okoku loka Salum mamaikon mama ijai ebala ebe, ‘Kogwel emanikorika loejai Anatot: naarai kibeit ijo apedor naka alakun.’ 8#Yer 1:1. Kosodi do Kanamel okoku loka mamaika kobu mama ajai alaro naka ikedarak, kwape keiaar akirot naka EJAKAIT, kobala ebe, ‘Keŋainait, kogwel emanikorika loejai Anatot, loejai akwap naka Benyamin; naarai airum nakon, ka alakun da nakon; kogwel ijo bon.’ Kasodi eoŋ ajenun ebe nin ŋes akirot naka EJAKAIT.
9 # Age 23:16. Kagwel emanikor lokejai Anatot kama ejai Kanamel okoku loka mamaika, ketemok apesan, isekelin itomon kakanykaarei luka epuesa. 10#Isa 8:2. Kewadika akiro, kepueak, keworou ikajenak, ketemok ŋes epuesa kaitemis. 11Kalema aiwadikaet naepucakinitai naka egwelet, ya nakibwaikinitai aipucet, kwape kejaar ekisil ka epone, ka ace da naepukoro: 12#Yer 36:4. keiaik aiwadikaet naka egwelet Baruk okoku loka Neria, okoku loka Maseia, koseseni Kanamel okoku loka mamaika, kosesenete da ikaienak luewadikaete aiwadikaet naka egwelet, kakonyen nuka Iyudayan kere luibojete kalaro naka ikedarak. 13Keeorak Baruk kakonyenekee, kabala ebe, 14‘Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Kolema aiwadikaeta nu, aiwadikaet na naka egwelet naepueakinitai, ka aiwadikaet na naepukoro, kibwaik amot nakelupe; tetere edaunete apak naepol. 15Naarai inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Egwelio bobo itogoi ka imanikoria luka emusabibu kakwap kana.’
16 # Age 32:9-12; Dan 9:1; Pil 4:6,7. “Ido kadau aijaikin Baruk okoku loka Neria aiwadikaet naka egwelet, abu kelip EJAKAIT ebe: 17#2 Ika 19:15; Age 18:14; Sek 8:6; Mat 19:26; Mk 10:27; Luk 1:37; Rom 4:21. ‘Wu okwe EJAKAIT Edeke! Koany, ijo kisubi akuj ka akwap kapedorikon naepol, ka akanikon naerioro, emamei adiokirot nakikaruni jo: 18#Any 20:6; Ikis 5:9,10; Sab 45:3-6; Isa 9:6; Tit 2:13; Yer 10:16. loitimenenei ilukumin luka ituŋa, itacakini atorobusio nuka idwekec luetupakinete numam adolito nuka lukapapakec: Edeke lokapolon, lokapedor, EJ AKAIT loka ajoresio ŋes ekekiror; 19#Isa 28:29; Yob 34:21; Sab 33:13-15; Awa 5:21; Yer 16:17; 17:10; Sab 62:12; Lim 12:14; Mat 16:27; Yok 5:29. Ekapetan lokapolon, lokapedor aswam: esesenete akekonyen irotin kere luka ituŋa; ainakin ninitunanan kwape ejaatar irotinike, ido kwape ejaatar araito nuka aswamisioke; 20#Any 9:16; 1 Siit 17:21; Isa 63:12. loibu kibwaik aanyuneta ka nukaumokin toma akwap nako Misiri, kiton lolo, kotoma Isirael ka ituŋa da; kodumunia ekiror kwape lolo. 21#Any 6:6; 2 Sam 7:23. Kitolom ikonituŋa Isirael kotoma akwap nako Misiri, kaanyuneta ka nukaumokin, kakan nakagogoŋ, kakan da naerioro, ketasuro loepol; 22#Any 3:8; Yer 11:5. koinak akwap na, nakiyapoikinit lukapapaakec ainakin, akwap naeleleete akile ka esik. 23#Nek 9:26; Dan 9:10. Kolomut kirumata; konye mam apotu korimasi ekoniporoto, karaida alosenen kokisilakon; mam eswamaete adiokirot kotoma kanun kanuieorakinit kes aswam; ŋes ainakini iyauni aronis na kere mamakec. 24#Yer 14:12; Yos 23:15. Koany, adolut itutio ore airum; ainakin ere akan naka Ikaludean luitijiete, kanuka ekileŋ, ka eteŋei, ka atular da; adolo nuibu kiner; ido koany, iany. 25Ido wu EJAKAIT Edeke, ibu kolimok eoŋ ebe, “Kogwelu amana kapesan, konyarau ikajenak”: kwabo ere da ainakin akan naka Ikaludean.’’’
26Kosodi kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Yeremia ebe: 27#Isa 64:8; Sab 115:3.“Koany, arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, Edeke loka lueiaas awat kere: ejai kereka adiokirot nakekaruni? 28#2 Ika 25:1-11; 2 Siit 36:17-21. Ŋes ainakin EJAKAIT inera kwanata ebe: Koany, eiaikini ere lo akan naka Ikaludean, akan da naka Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, ido irumi ŋes. 29#Yer 21:10; 19:13. Ikaludean da, Iuitijiete ere lo, epote inokokinete ere lo, icweikinete, ka itogoi da lukisupurionokiniata Baal lukisupurununo kokujukec, kobukonokinete icedekesio ainakineta numatan, aitunyunyurikin eoŋ. 30#Esk 20:28. Naarai apotu idwe luka Isirael, ka idwe luka Yuda kiswamata nueroko bon kakonyeneka, ameun keiditeukec. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Mam apotu idwe luka Isirael kiswamata idiobore kakaninikee eut dimarai yenikaitunyunyurikin eoŋ, 31#Sep 3:1; 2 Ika 23:27. Naarai ketunyunyuranakini ere lo ka aisideden ameuni kapaaran naadukere kiton lolo; ŋes do acakaria kakonyeneka, 32#Isa 1:4-6; Dan 9:8. Kanuka aronis kere naeswamaete idwe luka Isirael ka idwe luka Yuda aitunyunyurikin eoŋ, kes ka ikabakanakec, ka ikapolokokec, ka isasedotinikec, ka inabinokec, ka ituŋa luka Yuda, ka luibojete da kotoma Yerusalem. 33#Yer 7:13. Kebelokis asebot kwabo mere akonyen; ido karaida kesisianakinit kes, adilonokin tupuruc aisisianakin, konye mam kiirara karaida aeamun aisisianakinio. 34#2 Ika 23:4,5; Yer 7:30-31; 19:1-6; Esk 8:5. Konye ebwaikis italesiakec toma togo loenyaritai ekakiror, amunaasi da. 35#Leb 18:21; 2 Ika 23:10; Yer 7:31; 19:5. Adukoto aiboisio nuiceleleete nuka Baal, nuejaas aditot naka okoku loka Kinom, aitotor ikecesap ka akecepesur akim mama ejai Molek; numam ecorakinit, mam da aomisioitike alomarit otauka aswam nuketale ŋun: aisidiakar Yuda.
36“Aso do inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael nuka ere lo loineras yesi ebe, ‘Einakinitai akan naka eka baka loko Babulon kanuka ekileŋ, ka eteŋei, ka atular da,’ ebe: 37#Ikis 30:3; Yer 23:6. Koany, atukonokini kes aitonyoun kakwapin kere nukereŋearit kanu ka anyunyuraka ka akadedeŋ, ka adwaris naeta ria; ido enyakuni kes bobo aibois na, kesiboik inapakina. 38#Yer 24:7. Eraunos kesi ikatuŋa, eoŋ da araun Ekecedeke. 39#Esk 11:19. Ainakini etau ediopet ka erot ediopet, kayonenenete eoŋ apaarasia kere; tetere ejaunete ejok, kes ka ikeeedwe da Iuetupanakinete, 40#Isa 55:3; Yer 31:33. Ido etutuki kakes aitutuket namam edaun, komam ebelokini ainyein aiswamanakin kes ajokis, ebwaikin ayoŋit eoŋ otauonokec, kenyeis aijal. 41#Ikis 30:9; Yer 24:6. Eebo, esiyalamikini aiswamanakin nuejok, ido abeit, eraikini kes akwap na kekatau kere, ka akaijar kere.
42 # Yer 31:28. “Naarai ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, Kwape ayaunia aronis na kere naepol otuŋa lu, kwape kwaŋin ainunia kes ajokis kere naesumunit. 43#Yer 33:10. Egwelio imanikoria kakwap kana, nainerasi yesi ebe, Esil, emamei etuŋanan karaida etiaŋ; einakinitai akan naka Ikaludean. 44#Yer 17:26; 33:7. Egwelete ituŋa imanikoria kanuka apesan, iwadikaete aiwadikaeta, ipucete kes aipueeta, enyaraunete ikajenak, kakwap naka Benyamin, kotoma aiboisio nuidunyas Yerusalem, ka oreria da luka Yuda, kotoma oreria luka akwap nakiukon, kotoma oreria luka apuutona, koreria da luka Eŋalakimak; naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Etobonuni aijoreoriokec.”
