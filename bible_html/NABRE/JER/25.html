Yeremia 25

25
1 # 2 Ika 24:1; Dan 1:1; Yer 36:1; 46:2. Akirot naabu kobu mama ejai Yeremia nuka ituŋa kere luka Yuda, kokaru lokiwononet loka Yekoiakim okoku loka Yosia, ekabaka loka Yuda: ekaru ŋol ŋes ekaru losodit loka Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon; 2naalimokini enabi Yeremia ituŋa kere luka Yuda, ka ŋul kere luibojete koYerusalem kobala ebe: 3#Yer 1:2.“Ageun kokaru lokitomonet kauni loka Yosia okoku loka Amon, ekabaka loka Yuda, kiton lolo, ikar lu akaisaarei ka luuni, kobunenenei akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai, kenerenena kayes, adilonokini tupuruc einer, konye mam yes ipotu kopuput. 4#2 Siit 36:15; Yer 7:13,25; 11:7; 26:5; 29:19. Ido abu EJAKAIT kiiukanakini yes ikekejaanakinak kere inabin, edilonokini tupuruc aijukunun; konye yes mam ipotu kopuput, karaida aitoikin akiikus akiirar. 5#2 Ika 17:13; Esk 18:30; Luk 13:3-5. Ebala ebe, ‘Kobonutu kinye ninitunanan ekerot loerono, ka aronis naka aswamisiokus, kiboikinos akwap nakiinakinit EJAKAIT yes ka lukapapaakus, ageun apeŋo aitodol ikar kere; 6ido siritupanarete idekesio ice aijaanakin ka aima kes, sirikitunyunyuranakis eoŋ kaswamisio nuka akaninikus. Eoŋ da mam keswamakini yes aronis.’ 7#Yer 1:19; 32:30. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Konye mam ipotu kopuput eoŋ; kitunyunyurikis kaswamisio nuka akaninikus aiswamakin bonik aronis.
8“Kanuka mm ŋes inerar kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe: Naarai mam ipotu kopuput akakiro, 9#Yer 1:15; 40:2; 18:16. ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Koany, ejukari aomare ikekia kere luka ari naka agolitomei, ka Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, ekakejaanakinan, kayau kes aitiji akwap na, ka ŋul da luibojete kotoma, ka atekerin nu kere nuirimorito; ido amudiari cut, ketuluo kes aumokinet, aimuaret, asilio da namam edaun. 10#Yer 7:34; 16:9; Apu 18:22-23. Ido bobo, alemari kamakee eporoto lokaiyalama ka eporoto lokailel, eporoto loka eteran ka eporoto da loka ateran, aruo naka akaŋas, ka aica naka atala, 11#2 Siit 36:21; Yer 29:10; Dan 9:2. Itisilio akwap na kere, eraun aumokinet; ijaanakinete da atekerin nu ekabaka loko Babulon ikar akaisakanykaarei. 12#Yer 29:10; Dan 9:2; Isa 13:19. Kosodi kanen, ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kedaunos ikar akaisakanykaarei, kasodi eticani eoŋ ekabaka loko Babulon, ka ateker ŋin, ka akwap naka Ikaludean, kanuka numam adolitekec, ido etisili ikar kere. 13Ido ayauni kakwap ŋin akakiro kere nuabu kenerite, kere nuewadikatai oitabo lo, loadwari Yeremia mama ejaas atekerin kere, 14#Yer 50:41; 27:7; 50:29; 51:6,24. Naarai atekerin nuipu ka ikabakan lukapolok itubusianete kes, eebo, kes da ba; atacakini kwape ejaatar aswamisiokec, ido kwape ejaar aswam naka akaninikee.”
15 # Yob 21:20; Sab 11:6; 75:8; Isa 51:17,22; Apu 14:10. Naarai kaba la kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael ebe: “Kolem adere na naka ebino loka adedeŋ kakanika, kitamat atekerin kere nukeiukari joo 16#Nak 3:11. Emasete, igetanaros, ikerepikinete kanuka ekileŋ loejukari mamakee.”
17Kasodi alemun adere kakan naka EJAKAIT, ketamat atekerin kere nukejukari EJAKAIT eoŋ: 18#Sab 60:3; Esk 9:8; Dan 9:12; Amo 2:5. ebe Yerusalem ka ireria luka Yuda, ka ikabakanakec, ka ikapolokokec, aituluor asilio, aumokinet, aimuaret, ka ekenit; kwape ejaar lolo; 19#Yer 46:2. Parao ekabaka loko Misiri ka ikekejaanakinak, ka ikapolokoke, ka iketuŋa kere; 20#Yob 1:1; Isa 20:1. ka ituŋa kere luinyalinyala, ka ikabakan kere luka akwap naka Us, ka ikabakan kere luka akwap naka Ipilisitian (Asukelon, ka Gasa, ka Ekuron, ka luapianunos luka Asudod); 21Edom, ka Moab, ka idwe luka Amon; 22ka ikabakan kere luka Turo, ka ikabakan kere luka Sidon, ka ikabakan luka apujan naejai okekwa juwai aŋolol; 23Dedan ka Tema, ka Bus, ka ituŋa kere luebarenete arokio; 24#2 Siit 9:14; Yer 50:37. ikabakan kere luka Arabia, ka ikabakan kere luka ituŋa luinyalinyala luibojete kamodiŋ; 25ikabakan kere luka Simiri, ka ikabakan kere luka Elam, ka ikabakan kere luka Imedin; 26#Yer 51:41. ka ikabakan kere luka ari naka agolitomei, luelwaka ka lueapiete, ameanakin; ka ajakanuto kere nuka akwapin, nuejaas akwap. Akaulokee emasi do ekabaka loka Sesak.
27 # Kbk 2:16. “Ilimokini kes ebe, ‘Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Komata, komerikinos, koleka, kibironoros, mam bobo inyounete, kanuka ekileŋ loejukuni mamakus.’
28“Kosodi arai keneroto aiiaar adere kakanikon aimat, kosodi ilimokini kes ebe, ‘Inera EJAKAIT loka ajoresio kwanata ebe: Kibusakinit yes aimat! 29#Awa 11:31; Yer 49:12; Oba 1:16; Luk 23:31; 1 Pet 4:17; Esk 38:21. Naarai koany, ageuni eoŋ aswam aronis kore loemaikinitai ekakiror, yes da mam kiticanio? Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Mam iswakete aitieanio: naarai anyarakini ekileŋ asurokin kere luibojete kakwap.’
30 # Isa 42:13; Yok 3:16; Amo 1:2; Sab 68:16. “Ŋo da kodwarak kes akiro nu kere, kolimok ebe:
‘Irurumai EJAKAIT kokuiu,
Ikeseuni ekeporoto kaiboisike nakalaunan;
Irurumakini auioke kagogon;
Ereseikini kere luibojete kakwap,
Bala ikepudapudak emusabibu.
31 # Kos 4:1; Mik 6:2; Yok 3:2. Edoli eeelet kiton osaŋe loka akwap,
Naarai epurete EJAKAIT katekerin;
Etubokini luejaas awat kere,
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Apiari lukaronok kekilen.’
32“Tnera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe:
Koany, elomuni aronis anyoun kateker kiton ateker,
Itukutuni etapipirio loepol anyoun kosaneta luka akwap!
33 # Isa 34:2-8; Yer 12:12; Apu 19:17-21. “Ka nul da lueari EJAKAIT kapaaran kaŋin ikaikinos anyoun kosanc loka akwap kiton osaŋe eee loka akwap. Mam ekuudianio, karaida atukonokinio, karaida aidaio; eraunete aoret kakwap.
34“Kibwololoto, yes ikecokok, komonyata;
Kipunupunokinos kotoma okurwon, yes ikayaitok luka esipan:
Naarai adolut cut apaarasia nuka aariokus, ka aisiarisiariokus, Yes da ibiroros bala ibore yenisiyalamikinitai.
35Mam ikecokok edumunete erot lokereara,
Karaida ikayaitok luka esipan neekuiata.
36Eporoto loka aikalal naka ikeeokok.
Ka aibwolol naka ikayaitok luka esipan!
Naarai amunaa EJAKAIT ekecerony,
37 # Sab 97:1-3; Isa 66:15; Bur 12:29. Esiliŋiŋii aujoi nuinapakina
Kanuka anyunyura naededen naka EJAKAIT.
38Enyeki akenopis, kwape eŋatuny,
Naarai araun akecekwap aumokinet
Kanuka adedeŋ naka ekileŋ loereit,
Ido kanuka anyunyurake naededen.”
