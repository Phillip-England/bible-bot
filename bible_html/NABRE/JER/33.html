Yeremia 33

33
1 # Yer 32:2; 37:21. Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT irwan nakiareit mama ejai Yeremia, eroko eenitai kalaro naka ikedarak ebe: 2#Isa 37:26.“Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loiswamai, EJAKAIT lokesubi aiticat, EJAKAIT ŋes ekekiror, ebe: 3#Age 18:17; Ikis 4:7,29; Sab 50:15; Isa 55:6,7; Yer 29:12; Asw 2:21. Konyarai, eoŋ da kabonokini, kapukok nukapolok, ka nuetioko, numam iieni. 4#Yer 32:24. Naarai inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael, nuka itogoi lukore kalo, ido nuka itogoi luka ikabakan luka Yuda, luaponi kotiyanakinai adukio aigalioret aitikokinia itutio, ka ekileŋ, ebe: 5Elomunete Ikaludean ejie, ka aileleb kes kawat nukatwak nuka ituŋa, luadanyi kakanyunyura ka akadedeŋ, naarai ebwa akakonyen kore kalo kanuka aronisikec kere. 6#Yer 30:17. Koany, ainuni aiiar ka aitanaleunio, eoŋ da etaŋaleuni kes; ido apukokini ainapakin ka abeit nuepolok noi teni. 7#Yer 31:34; Isa 1:26. Enyakuni aijoreorio naka Yuda, ka aijoreorio naka Isirael, aduki, kwape sek. 8#Sab 85:2,3; Isa 40:2; Yer 31:34; Sek 13:1; Bur 9:13; Mik 7:18; 1 Yok 1:7-9. Ido elotari numam adolitekec kere, nukediakanariata kama ajai; esioniei numam adolitekec kere, nukediakanariata kama ajai, nukepaariata kama ajai. 9#Esr 1:2; Isa 62:7; 60:5. Ere lo da eraun kama ajai ekiror loka ailel, loka aiturit, loka aibuses da, kakonyen nuka atekerin kere nuka akwap, nuiirarete ajokis kere naeswamak, koyona, komaranata kanuka ajokis kere, ka ainapakin kere nuadumakini ŋes,
10“Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Toma aibois na nainerasi yesi ebe, ‘Eswap, emamei etuŋanan karaida etiaŋ,’ koreria luka Yuda, korotin da luka Yerusalem luesilok, emamei etuŋanan, emamei yeniboie kotoma, emamei da etiaŋ, 11#1 Siit 16:34; 2 Siit 5:13; 7:3; Esr 3:11; Sab 100:5; 106:1; 107:1; 118:1; 136:1; Apu 18:23; Leb 7:12; Yok 2:9; Bur 13:15. iirario bobo eporoto loka ailel ka eporoto loka aiyalama, eporoto loka eteran, ka eporoto da loka ateran, eporoto loka nul luinerasi ebe,
‘Kimalata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio,
Naarai ejok EJAKAIT,
Naarai ejaenena akeitim ikar kere’;
ido eporoto loka nul Iueyaunete asuban nuka aimal toma togo loka EJAKAIT, Naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe, enyakuni bobo aijoreorio naka akwap kwape sek,
12 # Isa 65:10. “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe: Toma aibois na, naeswap, naemamei etuŋanan, karaida etiaŋ, koreriakee kere, ejaun bobo aibois naka ikecokok luituwuriakete ikecesipanin. 13#Yer 17:26; Leb 27:32. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Itorete bobo isipanin akanin nuka ŋol loimari kes, koreria luka akwap nakiukon, koreria luka akwap naka apuutona, koreria luka Eŋalakimak, kakwap naka Benyamin ka aiboisio nuidunyasi kaYerusalem, koreria da luka Yuda.
14 # Yer 23:5-6; 29:10. “Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Koany, epote apaarasia nuetodolikinio akirot nin naejok naabu kener nuka ekek loka Isirael, ido nuka ekek loka Yuda. 15#Isa 4:2. Kapaarasia kanun, kapak kaŋin, etilibikini Daudi Atenut naka adolit; ido itodoli ŋol nuketube ka nukadolit kakwap. 16#Ikis 33:28; Isa 45:17. Kapaarasia kanun itajario Yuda, Yerusalem da iboie inapakina; lo da ŋes ekiror loemaikinio ebe, ‘EJAKAIT ŋes akosidolit.’
17 # 2 Sam 7:12-16; 1 Ika 2:4; 1 Siit 17:11-14. “Naarai inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Mam emamikini Daudi ekiliokit loiboikin oicoloŋ loka ekek loka Isirael ikar kere. 18#Mar 3:5-10; Mal 1:11; Rom 12:1; 1 Pet 2:5,9; Apu 1:6. Ido mam emamikini isasedotin Ilebin ekiliokit kakonyeneka loeinanakini ainakineta nuicweio, aicwe ainakineta nuka akiria, ka ainanakin asuban tir.”
19Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Yeremia, kobala ebe: 20#Age 8:22; Sab 89:37; Isa 54:9; Yer 31:36.“Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Arai ipedorete aibil akaitutuket naka apaaran, ka akaitutuket naka akuare, kinye ajaun apaaran ka akuare kapakikec; 21asa epedori akaitutuket abilio nakolo kitutuki ka Daudi ekakejaanakinan, kinye ajaun kikoku yenirumi koicoloŋoke; ka akaitutuket da nakitutuki ka Ilebin isasedotin, ikakejaanakinak. 22#Age 13:16; 15:5; Yer 31:37. Kwape ajore naka akuj mam epedori aimario, asine da naka aŋolol aitemio; kwape kwaŋin etanyanyaa aur naka Daudi ekakejaanakinan, ka Ilebin lukejaanakinete.”
23Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Yeremia kobala ebe: 24#Age 8:22; Yer 31:35; Sab 74:16; 104:19. “Mam kereka iwomoomoi nuenerata ituŋa lu ebe, ‘Aeaka EJAKAIT ikekia iarei luaseunit’? Amenata kwaŋin ikatuŋa, kinyeis bobo araun ateker kakonyenekec, 25Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Arai mam icasit kakaitutuker naka apaaran ka akuare, arai da mam ebwaikinit aicorakineta nuka akuj ka akwap, 26#Isa 14:1; Yer 31:20; Sek 10:6. aso acakari aur naka Yakob, ka naka Daudi ekakejaanakinan, kenye ayaŋaar kaurike airum aur naka Aburaam ka Isaak, ka Yakob da; naarai enyakuni bobo aijoreoriokee, ido etimi kes.”
