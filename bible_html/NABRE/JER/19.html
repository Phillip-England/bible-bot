Yeremia 19

19
1 # Esk 8:11. Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe, “Kolot, kogwel amot naka elupe naka ekaiean, koyana ice komakio luka ituŋa, ice da komakio luka isasedotin; 2#Yos 15:8; 2 Siit 28:3; Awa 1:20; 2 Ika 23:10; Yer 7:30-32; 32:34-35. koloma neejai aditot naka okoku loka Kinom, naejai eiduny neelomarere orute loka Atiboloka, kitatam kaŋina akiro nukalimokini jo, 3Kinera ebe, ‘Kopuputu yesi akirot naka EJAKAIT, yes ikabakan luka Yuda, ka luibojete koYerusalem. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe, Koany, ayauni aronis aibois na, naisideloori aki nuka ninitunanan yeniirari. 4#Ikis 28:20; Isa 65:11; Yer 17:13; 2 Ika 21:16. Naarai kajalata eoŋ, amunaut aibois na, kisupurununut lukisupurununo kotomake mama ejaas idekesio ice, lumam lem ejenete, kes ka lukapapaakec, ka ikabakan luka Yuda; kilelebata aibois na kaokot nuka lumam akirot. 5#Leb 18:21. Apotu kodukoto aiboisio nuiceleleete nuka Baal, aicweia ikeeedwe kakim araun ainakineta nuicweio nuka Baal: numam eoŋ kecorakinit, karaida einer, mam da aomisioitike alomarit otauka. 6#Yos 15:8. Kanuka ŋun koany, ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Epote apaarasia numam bobo aibois na enyaritere Topet, karaida Aditot naka okoku loka Kinom, konye Aditot naka Earit. 7#Leb 26:17; Ikis 28:25,26; Yer 9:21; 7:33; Sab 79:2; Apu 19:18. Etapaluni ainerun naka Yuda ka Yerusalem kaibois kana; ebirokin eoŋ da kes kekilen kakonyen nuka lukasurupokee, kakan da naka ŋul kaluemootos aiiarakec, Ainakini atesinakee araun akiriŋ naka akweny nuka apak, ka itian da luka akwap. 8#Leb 26:22; 1 Ika 9:8; Yer 18:16; Aku 2:15,16; Sep 2:15. Etarauni ere lo aumokinet, ka aijuar; eumokini ŋinitunanan yenitori nen, isuari kanuka aitieanitoike kere. 9#Leb 26:29; Ikis 28:53; Isa 9:20; Aku 4:10. Ainakini kes konyama awat nuka idwekec lusap ka lupesur, ninituŋanan konyam akuan naka opaperoke, kirigaunitete, kicikakinitete, kanekicikakinete lukasurupokee, ka nul da luemootos akeeeijar.’
10 # Yer 51:63. “Kosodi ijo aibwan amot kakonyen nuka ituŋa kaluiuparos kajo, 11#Sab 2:9; Isa 30:14; Aku 4:2; Yer 7:32. ilimokini kes ebe, ‘Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe: Abwani kwape kwaŋin ituŋa lu ka ere lo, kwape ebwania ituŋanan amot naka ekaiean, namam bobo epedorio aipueakin. Ido idaikinete ituŋa Topet, naarai emamikini aibois kamace, 12Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Eswamakini kwape kwaŋin aibois na, ka luibojete kotoma, aituputoor ere lo kwape Topet, 13#2 Ika 23:10; Sep 1:5; Yer 32:29; 7:18. Ido itogoi luka Yerusalem, ka itogoi luka ikabakan luka Yuda, luamunaaros, eraunos kwape aibois naka Topet, itogoi kere luesupurununere lukisupurununo oswionokee mama ejai aiore kere naka akuj, kobukokis icedekesio ainakineta numatan,’”
14 # 2 Siit 20:5. Kosodi do kobu Yeremia anyoun koTopet, kama kiiukarit EJAKAIT aidwar; kobwo ŋesi kalaro naka etogo loka EJAKAIT kotema ituŋa kere atiar: 15#2 Siit 36:16,17; Yer 7:26; Sek 7:11-14; Asw 7:51,52.“Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe, Koany, ayauni ore lo ka ireriake kere aronis kere naenerak ŋes; naarai apotu kitininikata imurisiŋakec, aneroto apupun akiroka.”
