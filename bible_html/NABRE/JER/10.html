Yeremia 10

10
1Wu ekek loka Isirael, kopuput akirot nakitemari EJAKAIT yes.
2 # Leb 20:23. Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Sirikisisiaut erot loka atekerin,
Sirikiburununut kanuka aanyuneta nuka akuj;
Naarai eburunito atekerin kanukec.
3Naarai iponesio luka ituŋa eraas aamameak:
Naarai eiepep ekitoi kamagoro,
Aswam naka akanin nuka ekeswaman kaep.
4Isirete kesi ŋes kepuesa, ka esabu;
Iketakinete kaseito ka anyulia,
Tetere mam ilokulokun.
5 # Sab 115:5,7; Kbk 2:19. Esubito bala ipirin luikeitoi, mam inerasi:
Ibusakinit adakio, naarai mam epedorete alosit,
Sirikuriaka kes;
Naarai mam epedorete aswam aronis,
Ido emamei tomakec aswam nuejok.”
6 # Any 8:9; Sab 86:8,10. Emamei yenerianas kaio, wu EJAKAIT;
Ipol ijo, epol da ekonikiror kotoma apedor.
7 # Apu 15:4. Iŋai ca ma mam ti kiyoŋ jo, wu Ekabaka loka atekerin?
Naarai kibusakinit:
Naarai kotoma lukacoa kere luka atekerin
Ido kotoma aiakanutokec kere,
Emamei cut yenerianas kajo.
8Konye eraas kwape itian ido ebanabanato;
Aisisianakin aputosia, erai ekomio atai,
9 # Dan 10:5. Ejai epuesa loerukuk loeyaunitai ko Tarusis,
Ka esabu loeyaunitai koupas,
Aswam naka ekeswaman, ka akanin nuka ekatatan esabu; Enapitos igoen lubululu ka lumugeik;
Eraas kere aswam naka ituŋa luepiriaka.
10 # 1 Tim 6:17. Konye EJAKAIT ŋes Edeke lokabeit;
Erai ŋesi Edeke lokaiaran, ekabaka lomam edaun:
Akenyunyura da isimarani akwap,
Mam epedorete atekerin atitiŋikin akeisiop.
11Ilimokini kes kwanata ebe, “Idekesio lumam kesubete aku; ka akwap, eoliorete kakwap, ido kokwap kakui.”
12 # Age 1:1,6; Yob 38:4; Sab 89:11; 136:5; Isa 40:28; Yer 51:15; Asw 14:15. Kanuka apedorike abu kosub alup,
Kanuka acoake abu kiticat akwap,
Kanuka aiieneke abu kopesik akui,
13Naikeseunia eporotoke, ejai airurum naka akipi toma akui, Itonyouni elisina kosaneta luka akwap;
Iswamakini akiru eimilia,
Itolomuni da ekwam kainadisiake.
14Erai ŋinituŋanan bala itian, emamei aijen;
Aputoke naegerer isurumi nolikatatan esabu ileic:
Naarai aputoke naecalara erai aiŋal,
Emamei eiyeŋane kotomakec.
15Eraas aamameak, aswam naka aiŋal:
Kapak naka aiticaniokee eoliorete.
16 # Sab 16:5; 73:26; Yer 51:19; Aku 3:24; Ikis 32:9; Sab 74:2. Mam akorakinet naka Yakob eputosi kanun,
Naarai erai ŋesi lokasuban iboro kere;
Isirael da ŋes ateker naka airumeteke:
EJAKAIT loka aioresio ŋes ekekiror.
17Kotukonok iborokon kokwap,
Ŋo loiboie kore loirigaunitai.
18 # Esk 6:10. Naarai eba la EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Koany, aporocari luibojete kakwap kapak kana,
Etuburuni kes, koriebakinos.”
19 # Mik 7:9. Itukwi abei ai akapipil!
Akamusunut kesiriebi noi.
Konye kabala ebe, “Abeit ŋin ŋes akadek,
Kebusakinit da atitiŋikin.”
20Aŋatar ewemaka, anedas aunoika kere:
Anyout idweka kamaka, ido emameete:
Emamei yeneterari bobo ekawema,
Asipokin aigalisioka.
21Naarai araut ikeeokok itian,
Kwabo mam apotu kinitata EJAKAIT:
Kanuka ŋun ŋes mam kekeriakarotor;
Esiarakinos isipaninikec kere.
22Eporoto loka eemut! Koany ebuni,
Autaar naepol naebuni kakwap naka agolitomei,
Aitisilior ireria luka Yuda,
Aitaraun aibois naka ikuwei.
23 # Sab 17:5; 119:116,117; Awa 16:1. Wu EJAKAIT, ajeni ebe erot loka ituŋanan emamei kotomake ilopet:
Emamei kotoma otunanan yenelosi aitetenar akekeie.
24 # Sab 6:1; Yer 30:11; Kbk 3:2. Wu EJAKAIT, koleeoroi, konye kotube;
Mere kakoninyunyura, kinyek aitidisiar,
25 # Yob 18:21; 1 Ses 4:5; 2 Ses 1:8. Kobukok anyunyurakon okapirin lumam kijenete,
Atekerin da numam iwoneete ekonikiror;
Naarai anyamasi Yakob;
Eebo, anyamasi, adautu ŋes, etisilioto aiboisike.
