51
1Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Koany, etonyouni ekwam loemudiari
Asurokinia Babulon,
Ka nul da kere luibojete ko Kaludea.
2 # 2 Ika 19:7; Yer 4:11. Ejukakini luepiesete Babulon,
Ikekeete ŋes,
Epiarete akwap:
Naarai esurokinete kowaitin kere
Kapaaran naka icano #Yer 15:7; 50:14.
3Lokakabwa sirikiket akabwake,
Ido sirikebwo konapite anapitoke naka asua,
Sirikitimo ikatumunakake;
Komudiasi cut ajoreke kere.
4 # Yer 49:26. Ibirokinos lueario akwap naka Ikaludean,
Luimusunitai da orotinike.
5 # Sab 91:14; Sek 1:15. Naarai Edeke, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio
Mam ejalit Isirael ka Yuda;
Karaida kileleba akwapukec adiakanareta
Kama ejai Ekalaunan loka Isirael,
6 # Apu 18:4; Yer 25:14. “Kokerata anyoun koBabulon,
Kitajar ŋinitunanan akeijar!
Kinyeis amudiario kanumam adoliteke:
Naarai erai apak naka enyaŋit loka EJAKAIT,
Etacakini ŋes eropitike.
7 # Apu 17:2-4; 18:3; Yer 25:16. Kerai Babulon aikopo naka esabu kakan naka EJAKAIT,
Naitemerikinia akwap kere;
Amata atekerin ebinoke;
Kanuka ŋun ikerepikinito atekerin.
8 # Isa 21:9; 1 Ses 5:12; Apu 18:9; Yer 46:11. Ebiror Babulon katipet ido amudiar;
Kibwololoto kanukeŋ!
Kolemikisi ekia kanuka arieboke,
Diat itaŋaleunio,
9 # Apu 18:5; Isa 13:14; Yer 46:16. Tikiitanaleu Babulon,
Konye mam itanaleun,
Koiala do ŋes,
Kaloto ŋinituŋanan obukec:
Naarai adol aketubokinio toma akui,
Ikiariokiton odouon
10 # Sab 37:6. Etolom EJAKAIT adolitewok;
Kopotu, kalimosi koSion
Aswamisio nuka EJAKAIT Edekewok.
11 # Isa 13:17. “Kipena imalia!
Kokapa ibukui!
Esidik EJAKAIT itauon luka ikabakan luka Imedin; naarai elosikinit amudiar Babulon; naarai erai enyaŋit loka EJAKAIT, ainyaŋ eyekaluke.
12 # Nak 2:1. Kikeut emedera asurokinia arwatatin nuka Babulon; Kitogogoŋoto eidare;
Kibwaikis ikedarak;
Kitemokis ikaperunok;
Naarai elosikinit EJAKAIT, ido eswama
Nuenerakinit luibojete koBabulon.
13 # Apu 17:1. Yes luibojete kakipi nuipu, Luidila abar,
Adol awasiakus,
Etiai loka eranakus.
14 # Yer 49:13; Nak 3:15. Eyapo EJAKAIT loka ajoresio elimit elopet ebe,
Abeit kesilelebikini ituŋa, kwape enyede;
Ikalalaete eibwoŋ kamakon.
15 # Age 1:1; Sab 146:5,6; Isa 49:26; Asw 14:15; Kol 1:16,17; Bur 1:2,3; Yob 9:8. “Kanuka apedorike abu kosub alup,
Kanuka acoake abu kiticat akwap,
Kanuka aijeneke da abu kopesik akuj.
16 # Sab 135:7. Naikeseunia eporotoke, ejai airurum naka akipi toma akuj,
Ido itodokaari elisina anyoun kosaŋeta luka akwap.
Iswamakini akiru eimilia,
Itolomuni da ekwam kaiŋadisiake.
17 # Yer 50:2. Araun ninitunanan bala etiaŋ, emamei aiicn;
Aputoke naegerer isurumi nelikatatan-esabu ileje:
Naarai erai aputoke naeealara aiŋal,
Emamei eiyeŋane kotomake,
18 # Yok 2:8. Eraas aamameak, aswam naka aiŋal:
Kapak naka aiticaniokec eoliorete.
19 # Sab 16:5; 73:26; Yer 10:16; Aku 3:24. Mam akorakinet naka Yakob eputosi kaŋun;
Naarai erai ŋesi lokasuban iboro kerc,
Isirael da ŋes ateker naka airumuke;
EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ŋes ekekiror.
20 # Isa 10:5; Yer 50:23. “Irai ijo aepeka ka lujiisika;
Kabilibilia jo atekerin,
Kamudiaria ajakanuto,
21Kabilibilia jo ebalasi ka ekebwanenonike;
22 # 2 Siit 36:17. Kabilibilia jo agali ka ekereneanike;
Kabilibilia jo ekiliokit ka aberu;
Kabilibilia jo emojoŋ ka esapat;
Kabilibilia jo ekatumunan ka apese;
23Kabilibilia jo ekecokon ka ekesipan;
Kabilibilia jo ekakorion ka imoŋin luka ajookotoke;
Kabilibilia jo ikayaitok ka ikapolokitok.
24 # Yer 50:15,16. “Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Atacakini Babulon ka luibojete koKaludea kere kakonyenekus aronisikec kere naapotu kiswamakis Sion.
25 # Isa 13:2; Sek 4:7; Apu 8:8. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Koany, kasurokini jo, ijo eronat ekamudiaran,
Loimudiari akwap kere;
Ekiari eoŋ akakan asurokinia jo,
Kekokori jo katiiban,
Ketuluori jo eron at loidopi.
26Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Mam elemunio kamakon amoru araun naka agor,
Karaida amoru araun apetetait,
Konye isilior do ikar kere,
27 # Isa 13:2; Yer 15:14; 50:41. “Kikeut emedera kakwap,
Kokuta arupepe katekerin,
Kitemokis atekerin ejie kaŋes,
Konyaraut ajakanuto,
Nuka Ararat, ka Mini ka Asukenas ejie kaŋes;
Koyautu ibalasin kwape enyede loikirimigito ajul.
28Kitemokis atekerin ejie kaŋes,
Ikabakan luka Imedin, ka ikayaitokokee, ka ikegaŋitokoke kere,
Ka akwap kere naka ayaiteke.
29 # Yer 50:13. Emarani akwap, erieba;
Naarai itodolikinio nuelosikinit EJAKAIT asurokinia Babulon, Aitisilio akwap nako Babulon,
Komameun yeniboie kotoma.
30 # Isa 19:16; Aku 1:9; Amo 1:5. Enyeis ikirigin luka Babulon ejie,
Iboiete kaneerigatai;
Enoŋarit akecegogoŋ,
Araut kwape aŋor;
Eewei aiboisioke,
Abilas atiketake,
31 # Yer 50:24. Ekerete ikakerak inamunos,
Ekerete da ikijukitono iriamunos,
Alimokin ekabaka loko Babulon ebe
Erumi ereke kowaitin kere.
32Erumutu neesedeny,
Icweitai esege,
Akuriakaros ikajiok.
33 # Isa 21:10; Yok 3:13; Mat 13:30. Naarai inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe:
Esubit akoku naka Babulon bala epejal
Kapak naipudapudaere;
Esalit apak naedit Kodoli aidwenyeke.”
34 # 2 Siit 36:9,10; Yer 24:1. “Kajwenya Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, Kacuiki, kenyeki aonit,
Keliko kwape etiaŋ loeiliili,
Esileleb akoikike kanukaiokakaka,
Kaeaka,”
35Etemari yeniboie koSion atiar,
“Kobeik Babulon akaie nakeswamakinio eoŋ ka akuanaka.”
Etemari Yerusalem da atiar,
“Kobeikis akaokot mama ejaas luibojete koKaludea.”
36 # Sek 1:15; Yer 50:38. Ŋes enerar EJAKAIT kwanata ebe,
“Koany, apuri akirokon, kenyatjakini jo,
Etoonikini akeŋolol, etoonikini da aketan.
37 # Isa 13:22; 2 Siit 29:8; Yer 18:16. Eraun Babulon aapoŋor,
Aibois naiboioto ikuwei,
Aumokinet, aimuario,
Komamei yeniboie kotoma.
38“Kiunete nepepe kwape iŋatunyo ludidik;
Irurumaete kwape idwe luka iŋatunyo.
39 # Dan 5:1,30. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kemwakuut awatekee,
Epoikini kes epucit, etemeri kes,
Kolakaros, koiotooros ajo numam enaŋete,
Komam akwenyun,
40Esijesari kes kwape idwe luka amerekekin aario,
Kwape imerekekin ka ikorwa.
41 # Yer 25:26; 49:25; Isa 13:19; Dan 4:30. “Erumi cama Sesak! #51:41 Sesak ŋes ekiror eee loka Babulon
Esirieŋun eituritono loka akwap kere!
Araun eama Babulon asilio kotoma atekerin!
42 # Isa 8:7,8. Abunok aŋolol Babulon;
Arapaa kanuka aipues naka akailanake.
43Araut ireriake asilio,
Akwap naconit, amodiŋ da,
Akwap namam iboio ituŋanan,
Namam itorit okoku loka ituŋanan,
44 # Yer 50:2. Eticani Be! koBabulon,
Esilekuni ŋes nuabu kilikono.
Mam bobo eleleete atekerin mamake;
Ibiror arwatat naka Babulon.
45 # Apu 18:4. “Yes ikatuŋa, konyoutu kamaken!
Kitajarunos ninitunanan
Kanyunyura naededen naka EJAKAIT.
46 # 2 Ika 19:7. Kinyeis ikusitauon akiilor,
Kwabo sirikiyoŋa kanuka nukabe nuiirario kakwaj,
Naarai epote nukabe kokaru ediopet,
Kosodi kokau kokaru eee epote nukabe,
Ejai akaie da akwap,
Ekayaiton ejiosi kecekayaiton.
47 # Isa 44:23; Yer 50:3. “Aso koany, epote apaarasia,
Nuetepesenia aputosia nuegerer nuko Babulon;
Irumete akwapuke kere ileje;
Luke kere lueario ibirokinos kidinike.
48 # Apu 18:20; Yer 44:28. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kosodete akuj ka akwap,
Ka iboro kere luejaas toma,
Ayoore kanuka aiyalama kanuka Babulon;
Naarai epote ikaŋatak mamakeŋ kari naka agolitomei.
49 # Apu 18:24. Kwape kibiroria Babulon lukeario luka Isirael,
Kwape kwaŋin ibirokinotor lukeario luka akwap kere Babulon,
50 # Yer 44:28. “Yes luikuata ekileŋ,
Koloto, sirikibwaata kopalarito!
Kiitutu EJAKAIT kama elwana,
Ido koloma Yerusalem toma otauonokus.
51 # Sab 44:15. Kitirira oni, naarai kipupu amoromoroo;
Erumata akonyenewok ileic,
Naarai alomasi imo aiboisio nukalaunak
Nuka etogo loka EJAKAIT.’
52“Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Aso do koany, epote apaarasia,
Nuetepesenia aputosiake nuegerer; Idinyasi Iuimusunito
Anatar akwapuke kere,
53 # Yer 49:16; Amo 9:2. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Karaida kedokarit Babulon kiton akuj,
Karaida kitieasit amukurur naka agogoŋuke,
Konye epote ikaŋatak neejai kamakaŋ,
54 # Isa 13:6-9; 15:5; Yer 50:22; Sep 1:10. “Eporoto loka aibworo loebuni koBabulon!
Ka loka amudiar naepol
Loebuni kakwap naka Ikaludean!
55Naarai eŋasi EJAKAIT Babulon,
Ido emudiar eeelet loepol kamaken,
Ido irurumaete akailanakec kwape akipi nuipu,
Edakun eeelet loka ipororoikec.
56 # Ikis 32:35; Sab 94:1. Naarai abunoki ŋes ekaŋatan,
Eebo, abunoki Babulon;
Aŋatar da ikiriginike,
Abilibilunos akabwaikee;
Naarai EJAKAIT ŋes Edeke loetaci,
Abeit eropi.
57 # Yer 46:18; 48:15. Ebala Ekabaka, ekekiror EJAKAIT loka ajoresio cbe,
Eremeri ikapolokoke, ka lukacoake,
Ikayaitokoke, ka ikeganirokoke, ka ikiriginike da;
Ido eiotooros aio numam enaŋete,
Komam ekwenyunete,
58 # Kbk 2:13. “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe,
Etiyatiyao cut arwatat naelal naka Babulon,
Icweio iruteike luiceleleete.
Itaaritos atekerin aamameak,
Itapasitos atekerin kanuka akim.”
59Akiro nuecorakini Yeremia enabi Seraia okoku loka Neria, okoku loka Maseia, naalosio Babulon nepepe ka Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda kokaru lokiwononet loka airumuke. Kerai Seraia ekapolon loka inyamat. 60Kosodi kiwadik Yeremia oitabo aronis kere naebunokin Babulon, kes akiro nun kere nuiwadikatai kanuka Babulon. 61Kotema Yeremia Seraia atiar, “Naidolio toma Babulon mam iswaki asoman akiro nun kere. 62#Yer 50:3,39. Kinera ebe, ‘Wu EJAKAIT, ibu kinera nuka aibois kana, amudiar, kinyek idiotuŋanan kere aiboi kanen, ituŋanan karaida itian, konye kitisiloi do ikar kere.’ 63#Apu 18:21. Kosodi kidau asoman eitabo lo, ienikini amoru kocakak kidin naka Eupurate. 64Ido kinera ebe, ‘Elumori Babulon kwaŋin, kwabo mam ikeuni bobo kanuka aronis naainuni ŋes, ido epasete.’’’
Ananata akiro nuka Yeremia ne.
