Yeremia 46

46
1 # Yer 25:15. Akirot, naka EJAKAIT naabu kobu mama ejai enabi Yeremia kanuka atekenn.
2 # 2 Ika 23:29; 2 Siit 35:20; Yer 25:9,19. Kanuka Misiri, Nuka aiore naka Parao-neko ekabaka loko Misiri, naajai ariri naka ecor Eupurate koKarukemis naadanyi Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon kokaru lokiwononer loka Yekoiakim okoku loka Yosia, ekabaka loka Yuda.
3 # Isa 8:9,10; Yer 51:11,12; Yok 3:9,10; Nak 2:1. “Kokapata ebuku ka eupal,
Kikunyakis ejie!
4Kopesikis nupetan abalasin;
Kibwanenoto, yes ikebwanenok!
Kiderakinos, konapitos abokoikus nuka aisrrun;
Kopwata akwaras, konapitos ikootin luka asua!
5 # Yer 6:25; 20:4; Apu 6:15-17. Kanukinyo aanyunia?
Aburut, abonosi kau.
Ebironor ikiriginikee,
Ido anemesiaros, mam enoliarctc kau.
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Ejai etasuro owaitin kere!
6 # Dan 11:19. Kinyeis luetiroko akerit,
Karaida ekirigi aiku;
Kari naka agolitomei kariri naka ecor Eupurate,
Eguikis, ebiroros.
7 # Dan 11:22. Iŋai bo ŋin yenenyouni kwape Nile,
Kwape ieorin lukibukas akipikec?
8Anyou Misiri kwape Nile,
Ibukas akekipi kwape icorin.
Kobala ebe, Anyouni, karapak akwap kere;
Amudiari ere ka ŋul da luibojete kotoma.
9Kodokasi yesi, ibalasin;
Kikerepikis yesi, agalin!
Kolomasi ikirigin;
Ituŋa luka Etiopia ka Put luekapitos iupalin;
Ituŋa da luka Lud, luekapitos luikesito da akabwai,
10 # Yok 1:15; Ikis 32:42; Isa 34:6. Naarai apaaran ŋin erai naka EJAKAIT,
Edeke loka ajoresio,
Apaaran naka enyaŋit, inyanakini lukasurupoke.
Enyami ekileŋ, imonikini da,
Ido emasi aokotokec itekori eut.
Naarai ejai EJAKAIT, Edeke loka ajoresio, asubanit
Akwap naka ari naka agolitomei,
Kariri naka ecor Eupurate.
11 # Yer 8:22; Isa 47:1. Kodoka Gilead, kolem emina,
Ŋo akoku nabekot nako Misiri!
Itwasamai taai ikee luipu:
Komamei aŋaleun kamakon.
12Eirasi atekerin ikonileic,
Elelebun akoniimony akwap;
Naarai eriama ekirigi ka ekirigi,
Ebiroros kere nepepe.”
13 # Yer 43:10-13; Isa 19:1; Esk 30. Akirot naalimokini EJAKAIT enabi Yeremia, kwape ebunio Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon amudiar akwap nako Misiri.
14“Kolimosi koMisiri, kitisiarikis ko Migidol,
Kitisiarikis koNop, ka Ta-panes;
Kineras atiar, ‘Kobwou, kokapakin;
Naarai anyam ekileŋ iwaitinikon.’
15Kanukinyo amearere ikonikirigin?
Mam ebwouna, naarai EJAKAIT erenet,
16 # Leb 26:37. Esiguik luipu, eebo, ebironoros,
Kobalas ebe, ‘Kanyoutu,
Kabonosi otunawok, akwap nakobuwok,
Akerit ekileŋ lokireit.’
17Konyarata Parao, ekabaka loko Misiri ekekiror, ‘Ekepopolan loitumori apak titai.’
18 # Isa 48:2; Mal 1:14; Yer 10:10; 44:26; 1 Tim 1:17; Mar 23:19. “Ebala Ekabaka, ekekiror EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, ebe, Kwape ajaria, abeit kwape Tabor oroŋan,
Ido kwape Karamel ariri kaŋolol,
Ebuni ŋesi.
19 # Yer 48:18. Wu jo apese naiboie koMisiri,
Kitemok iboro lukinatarere;
Naarai eraun Nop ameje,
Icweio, komamei yeniboikin.
20 # Kos 10:11; Yer 1:14. “Erai Misiri ataok naejok noi;
Konye enyouni ekamonit kagolitomei, kobunok ŋes.
21Ido ituŋake lukeropit kotomake esubito bala itak luetumito; Naarai abonosi kesi da, akerata nepepe,
Mam ebwouna:
Naarai adolok kes apaaran naka icanikec,
Apak naka aiticaniokec.
22 # Isa 29:4. “Tnororaar eporotokec kwape emun loeliat;
Naarai eraeete lukasurupokec alosit aiore,
Elosete kokapito aepei,
Kwape ikaiepak ikito.
23Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Ejepete amagoroke,
Karaida mam epedori aitorio;
Naarai itelekarito kesi emaase kaipues;
Ido mam imarunos.
24Irumete apese nako Misiri ileje;
Ŋaikinio akan naka ituŋa luka agolitomei.”
25 # Esk 30:13. Ebala EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke Ioka Isirael ebe: “Koany, eticani Amon loka No, ka Parao, ka Misiti, ka ikedekesio ka ikabakanake da; Parao ka nul da luemunosi ŋes. 26#Yer 44:30; 48:47; 49:39. Akorakini kes akan naka nul kaluemoete akeceiiar, kakan naka Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, ka akan da naka ikekeiaanakinak, Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kokau iboikinos ituŋa, kwape apaarasia nukolosek.
27 # Yer 30:10-11; Isa 44:2. “Konye sirikiyoŋ, ijo Yakob, ekakejaanakinan,
Kwabo sirikiibur, ijo Isirael:
Naarai koany, ketajari jo kama elwana,
Ka akoniwur da kakwap naka aiioreoriokec.
Eboŋuni Yakob, iyamakin ijamamai,
Emameuni do yenitukuriani.
28Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Sirikiyoŋ, ijo Yakob ekakejaanakinan,
Naarai ajaenena kaio,
Naarai apiari atekerin kere nukerenearit,
Konye jo, mam kapiari eut.
Naarai kaleconori jo motimot,
Kwabo mam kenyeini cut komam keticanenenei.”
