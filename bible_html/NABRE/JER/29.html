Yeremia 29

29
1 # 2 Ika 24:12-16; 2 Siit 36:10. Nu kes akiro nuka ebalua loabu enabi Yeremia kijuka ejai Yerusalem mama ejai aatoŋot naka imakio lueioreoro, ka isasedotin, ka inabin, ka ituŋa kere luejoreorit Nebukadaresar alemar koYerusalem ayaŋaar Babulon. 2Kedau Yekonia ekabaka, ka toto naka ekabaka, ka ibwaein, ka ikapolok luka Yuda ka Yerusalem, ka ikeswamak, ka ikatatak, anyoun koYerusalem. 3Kijuka ebalua kakan naka Elasa okoku loka Sapan, ka Gemaria okoku loka Kilikia, luabu Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda kijuka Babulon mama ejai Nebukadaresar ekabaka loka Babulon, ebe: 4“Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio, Edeke Ioka Isirael mama ejaas luejoreoro kere, luainakin aijoreoreo anyoun koYerusalem ayaŋaar Babulon, ebe: 5Kodukoto itogoi, kiboienenete kanen; kiraata imanikoria, konyama araitokec. 6Komanya aŋor, kowuriata idwe lutelepai ka lupesur; komanyanakis odwekus aŋor, kokorenenete apesurukus adukonokinio, kowurienenete idwe lutelepai ka lupesur; kilelebunos nina kwabo mam inonaros. 7#Dan 6:4; Rom 13:1,5; 1 Tim 2:1,2. Kokototor ere lokesijoreorit ainapakin, kilipakis kama ejai EJAKAIT: naarai kanuka ainapakinike ŋes idumuniata yesi da ainapakinikus. 8#Yer 14:14; 23:21; Pes 5:6. Naarai inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio. Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Kinyeis inabinokus, luejaas kayes, ka ikaroakakus aiŋal yes, kwabo sirikicamut airujasiakus nuirujaete. 9Naarai kidwarakinete nukainal kokirorika: ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Mam keyakienenei kes.
10 # 2 Siit 36:21; Yer 25:11; Dan 9:2. “Naarai ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Kedau Babulon aitodolikinio ikar akaisakanykaarei, kaanyanari yes, ka aitodolikin akakirot naejok mamakus, ainyakun yes bobo aibois na. 11Naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ajeni eoŋ aomisio nuaomit kanukus, aomisio nuka ainapakin, kwabo mere nueroko, ainakin yes amunokina nuepote. 12#Dan 9:3. Iwoneete yesi da eoŋ, ilosete ailip eoŋ, ido eirari yes, 13#Leb 26:39,40; Sab 32:6; Mat 7:7; Yer 24:7; Ikis 4:29. Yes da kimoete eoŋ, kianyunete; arai kito kiimoata kikusitauon kere. 14#Ikis 4:7; Sab 32:6; Isa 55:6; Rom 10:20; Yer 23:3,8; 30:3. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kianyunete eoŋ; ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ebelokini eoŋ aiioreoriokus, katukonokini yes alemun kotoma atekerin kere, ka aiboisio da kere, nukerenearit, ido kenyakuni yes bobo toma aibois nakesiioreoria.
15“Naarai inerata ebe, Kitonyoik EJAKAIT inabin kotoma Babulon. 16Ŋes inerar kwanata EJAKAIT nuka ekabaka loiboie koicolon loka Daudi, ka nuka ituŋa kere lueiaas ore lo, iinacanakus lumam aupara kayes alioreorio: 17#Yer 24:8-10. inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe, Koany, eiukakini kes ekileŋ, ka eteŋei, ka atular, ido etuluori kes bala etini loebos, lomam epedori anyamio, naarai erono noi. 18#Ikis 29:21-28; 1 Ika 9:7,8; 2 Ika 25:8; Aku 2:15,16; Yer 15:4; 24:9. Ido arukitori kes kekilen, ka erenei, ka atular, ainakini kes aisiarisiario kotoma ajakanuto kere nuka akwap, araut ekenit, ka aumokinet, ka aimuario, ka amorosit, kotoma atekerin kere kama kereŋearit. 19#Yer 25:4; 32:33. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Naarai mam apotu kiirasi akakiro, nueiukakinitor kes ikakejaanakinak inabin, adilonokini tupuruc aijukunun kes; ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Konye ipotu konero akiirar. 20#Amo 3:6; Mik 4:10. Aso do kiirasi yesi akirot naka EJAKAIT, yes kere ikijoreoritono, lukayanaari Babulon anyoun koYerusalem. 21#2 Pet 2:1. Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke loka Isirael nuka Akab okoku loka Kolaia, ido nuka Sedekia okoku loka Maaseia, lukidwarakinete yes aiŋal kokirorika, ebe: Koany, ainakini kes akan naka Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon: eari do ŋesi kes kakonyenekus. 22#Age 48:20; Isa 65:15; Dan 3:6. Naarai kanukec ŋes ikenioto ikiioreoritono luka Yuda luejaas Babulon ebe, “Kituput EJAKAIT jo kwape Sedekia ka Akab, luabu ekabaka loko Babulon kitisis kakim.” 23#Sep 3:4; Awa 5:21; Yer 13:27; 16:17; Mal 3:5; Bur 4:13. Naarai kiswamata nukabanaanut kotoma Isirael, ido kiteeloete aŋor nuka iidunyetakec, ido kineras kokirorika akiro nukainal, numam ecorakinit kes. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Eoŋ lo loajeni, ido arai ekaienan.”
24Nuka Semaia Enekelamit ilimokini ebe: 25#2 Ika 25:18; Yer 21:1.“Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Naarai ijuka ibaluan kokirorikon mama ejaas ituŋa kere luejaas Yerusalem, ka neejai Sepania okoku loka Maaseia esasedoti, ido neeiaas isasedotin kere ebe, 26#Yer 20:1; Asw 4:1; Ikis 13:1-5; 2 Ika 9:11; Sek 13:3-6; 2 Siit 16:10; Yer 20:2; Asw 16:24. ‘Kituluo EJAKAIT esasedoti aibois naka Yekoiada esasedoti, koraut ijaka kotogo loka EJAKAIT kanuka ŋinitunanan yenikikerep, ka yenituluor enabi, kibwaik roma oloit ka akito nueridakinete emurisin. 27#Mar 16:3; Asw 4:17. Aso do kanukinyo mam itoronia Yeremia loka Anatot, loituluoro elopet bon enabi kamakus? 28Naarai ejuku mamakosi Babulon ebala ebe, “Erai aijoreoriokus naka apak naepol: asa do kodukoto itogoi, kiboienenete kanen; kiraata imanikoria, konyama araitokec.”’”
29Kosodi Sepania esasedoti kosiom ebalua ŋol kopup enabi Yeremia. 30Kosodi kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Yeremia ebe, 31#Yer 28:15.“Kijukak nul kere ikijoreoritono kobala ebe, “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT nuka Semaia Enekelamit ebe: Naarai kidwarak Semaia, kwabo mam eoŋ ejukunit, kiinak yes amunokina aiŋal; 32#Any 20:5; Rom 2:8; Yer 28:16. ŋes inerar EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Koany, eti cani Semaia Enekelamit, ka akeur; ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Emamuun ituŋananike yeniboikin otuŋa lu, ido mam ŋes eanyuni ajokis naeswamakini ikaruna: naarai ener nukepali mama ejai EJAKAIT.’’’
