Yeremia 52

52
1 # 2 Ika 24:18; 2 Siit 36:11. Kerai Sedekia loka ikar akaisaarei kalodiopet nakeraunor ekabaka; kirumak ikar itomon ka lodiopet ko Yerusalem. Ekiror loka totoke kerai Kamutal akoku naka Yeremia loka Libuna. 2Kiswama nueroko kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kwape ejaatar kere nueswamaenene Yekoiakim. 3#Esk 17:12-16. Kosodi kanen abe it kitodolikinoi anyunyura naka EJAKAIT koYerusalem ka Yuda, kirenea kes kakonyeneke.
Kipaliik Sedekia eka baka loko Babulon. 4#Esk 24:2; 2 Ika 25:1; Sek 8:19; Yer 39:1; 6:3-6; Ikis 28:52-57; Isa 42:24,25. Kosodi kanen kokaru lokikanyet kaonon loka airumuke, kolap lokitomonet, kapaaran nakitomonet naka elap, kobu Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, ŋes ka akejore kere asurokin Yerusalem, kidukuroto airigaun ŋes; kitutuoto itutio lujiis. 5Kwaŋin kirigaut ere aitodol okaru lokitomonet ka lodiopet loka ekabaka Sedekia. 6#Leb 26:14,26; Isa 3:1; Esk 4:16. Olap lokiwononet, kapaaran nakikanyet kaonon kodedeŋar eteŋei kore, komamikis eut ituŋa luka akwap inyamat. 7#Esk 33:21. Kosodete kotiyasi arorwa kore, kokera ikajiok kere, kolomut kore kuare korot loka erute loejai kidin arwatatin aarei, loidunyas kemanikor loka ekabaka, (Kiriamarito Ikaludean ere kere.) Kobeata korot loka Araba. 8Konye korukio aiore naka Ikaludean ekabaka, kodolokis Sedekia kapuutona nuka Yeriko; kisiarakin aioreke kere aijal ŋes. 9#Yer 32:4. Kosodete kikamas ekabaka, koyanaasi neejai ekabaka loko Babulon, Ribula, kakwap naka Kamat; kotubok ŋes akiroke. 10#Yer 39:6,7. Koarak ekabaka loko Babulon odwe luka Sedekia akonyeneke; koar da ikapolok kere luka Yuda Ribula. 11#Esk 12:13. Kogorunun ŋesi Sedekia akonye; koen ekabaka loko Babulon ŋes kirikoi, koyana Babulon, kibwaik toma aenis aitodol apaaran naketwania.
12 # 2 Ika 25:8; Sek 7:5; Yer 39:9. Kosodi kolap kalokikanyet, kapaaran nakitomonet naka elap, ŋes ekaru lokitomonet kakanykaoŋon loka ekabaka Nebukadaresar, ekabaka loko Babulon, abu Nebusaradan, ekapolon loka ikedarak, lokebwoienenei kakonyen nuka ekabaka loko Babulon, kobu Yerusalem. 13#1 Ika 9:8; Sab 74:6-8; Isa 64:10,11; Yer 26:18; Mat 24:1,2. Kiewe etogo loka EJAKAIT, ka etogo loka ekabaka; ka itogoi kere lukotoma Yerusalem, ŋolitogo kere loepol kicweikin kakim. 14Abu aiore kere naka Ikaludean luajaas ka lokapolon loka ikedarak, kotiyatiya arwatatin kere nuka Yerusalem ainamar. 15Kosodi kijoreo Nebusaradan ekapolon leka ikedarak lukican noi kotuna, ka aatoŋot naka ituŋa lukideuna kore, kanul da Iualunakina ekabaka loko Babulon, ka aatoŋot naka ikeswamak. 16Konye kitasal Nebusaradan ekapolon loka ikedarak lukican noi kakwap araut iketemonokinok emusabibu ka ikakoriok da.
17 # 1 Ika 7:15-47; Yer 27:19. Kobilibilata Ikaludean ipirin luka aisinin lukejaas togo loka EJAKAIT, ka aicegeria, ka aŋolol naka aisinit nukejaas togo loka EJAKAIT, koyanaasi aisininikee kere Babulon. 18#Any 27:3. Ido koyanaasi amoti ka initia, ka airudencta, ka atanyia, ka akaalei, ka iboro kere luka aisinit lukijaanakiniata. 19Ka akolin da, ka atiboka nuigaete ekurwon, ka atanyia, ka amotii ka ipirin luka atalai, ka akaalei, ka atiboi da; nukeraas nuka esabu, esabu, ka nukeraas nuka epuesa epuesa, kwaŋin ayaŋaaria ekapolon loka ikedarak. 20Ipirin luarei, aŋolol adiopet, ka imoŋin itomon ka luarei luka aisinin lukeiaas kwap kacegeria, lueswamakinit ekabaka Solomon etogo loka EJAKAIT: aisinin nuka iboro kanul kere mam kepedorete ainiŋio, 21#1 Ika 7:15,21,22; 2 Siit 3:15. Ipirin da, aojau naka epir ediopet kerai ikubitin itomon ka lukanykauni; kolukunite aitemis naka ikubitin itomon ka luarei, aburesuke ibokor iwonon: eorori. 22Kejai akou naka aisinit kokuiuke; aoiau naka akou adiopet ikubitin ikany, kolukunito iboyan ka akamamagan akou, kere luka aisinit, ekoni epir lokiareit nepenen ka akamamagan. 23#1 Ika 7:20; 2 Siit 3:16. Kosiepion kejaas akamamagan akaisakanykaoŋon ka nukanykape; akamamagan kere lukelukunito iboyan emwaasi akwatat.
24 # 1 Siit 6:14; 2 Ika 25:18; Yer 21:1. Kosodi ekapolon loka ikedarak kikama Seraia esasedoti lopeti, ka Sepania esasedoti lokiareit, ka ikagolok luuni; 25Kikama kore ekapolokiton lokeyait ikajiok; ka ituŋa ikanykaarei kanul lukeanyununete akonyen nuka ekabaka, lukedumunio kore; ka ekewadikan loka ekapolon loka aiore, loaramununei ituŋa luka akwap; ka ituŋa akaisakanykape luka ituŋa luka akwap, lukedumunio kokidiŋ ore. 26Kikama Nebusaradan ekapolon loka ikedarak kes, koyau mama ejai ekabaka loko Babulon, Ribula. 27#Leb 26:33. Kodany ekabaka loka Babulon kes, koarak Ribula akwap naka Kamat. Kwaŋin kijoreoroi Yuda anyoun kakwapuke.
28 # 2 Ika 24:2; 2 Siit 36:20. Lu kes ituŋa lukiioreori Nebukadaresar: kokaru lokikanyet kaarei Iyudayan ilukumin iuni ka akaisaarei ka luuni. 29#Yer 39:9. Kokaru lokitomonet kakanykauni loka Nebukadaresar, kijoreo ŋesi koYerusalem ituŋa akwat akanykauni ka akaisauni ka luaarei; 30kokaru loka akaisaarei ka luuni loka Nebukadaresar kiioreo Nebusaradan ekapolon loka ikedarak Iyudayan akwat akanykaarei ka akaisaoŋon ka lukany: ituŋa kerc emwaasi ilukumin iwonon ka akwat akanykape.
31 # 2 Ika 25:27-30; Age 40:13-20. Kosodi kanen kokaru loka akaisauni ka lukanykaarei loka aenio naka Yekoiakin ekabaka loka Yuda olap lokitomonet kaarei, kapaaran nakakaisaarei kanukany naka elap, kokaru lokeraunor Ebil-merodak ekabaka loko Babulon, abu ŋes! kitopoloo akou naka Yekoiakin ekabaka loka Yuda, kolem kaidaris; 32Kinera kaŋes nukasianut, kitopoloo eicoloŋoke aitelekarit iicoloŋo luka ikabakan lukejaas kaŋes Babulon. 33#2 Sam 9:13. Kiloeok inapeneke luka aidaris, konyamcnenei inyamat kakonyeneke, apaarasia kere nuka aijarake. 34Akorakineteke, abu ekabaka loko Babulon koinanak ŋes akorakinet ninipaaran, aitodol apaaran naka atwanareke, apaarasia kere nuka aijarake.
