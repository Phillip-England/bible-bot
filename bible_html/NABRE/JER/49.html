Yeremia 49

49
1 # Esk 21:28-32; 25:1-7; Amo 1:13-15; Sep 2:8-11. Nuka idwe luka Amon.
Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Emameete teni Isirael idwe lusap?
Emamei kereka ekeruman?
Aso kanukinyo irumia Malakam Gad,
Iboioto da ituŋake koreriake?
2 # Amo 1:14. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Aso koany, epote apaarasia
Nuetupupunio elala loka ejie
Asurokin Raba loka idwe luka Amon;
Eraun ameie naesilior,
Icwei akim akepesur.
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kosodi lsirael airum lulem irumito ŋes.
3 # Isa 32:11; Yer 48:7. “Kibwolol, ijo Kesibon, naarai anasi Ai!
Kikalalata, yes apesur nuka Raba!
Konapata ipukoi,
Kokuudianata, kokerata niniibois kere kabwasin!
Naarai ijoreorio Malakam,
Ka isasedotinike ka ikapolokoke da nepepe.
4 # Yer 21:13. Kanukinyo iwolobobotor kaditon,
Aditotokon naelelei,
Ijo apese naebononori kau?
Naemunokina abarake, kobala ebe,
‘Iŋai bo kabunokini?’
5Ebala EJAKAIT Edeke loka aioresio ebe,
Koany, kainuni akurian,
Kotoma kere lukirimorito;
Kireneario yes ŋinituŋanan neelwana noi, ido emamei yenetukonokini Iuiposiaros.
6 # Yer 48:47. Konye ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kokau enyakuni bobo aijoreorio naka idwe luka Amon.”
7 # Amo 1:11; Yob 5:12-14; Oba 1:8; Isa 19:11. Nuka Edom.
Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe:
“Emamei kereka bobo acoa koTeman?
Aolio ca ainerun kotoma omisioitin?
Elicar eut acoakec?
8 # Isa 2:19; Yer 25:23. Kokerata, koboŋ utu, konopa,
Yes luibojete koDedan;
Naarai ainuni Esau ican,
Kapak naetieania ŋes.
9 # Isa 17:6. Arai kiponikinitos ikadenok luka emusabibu
Mam ti kedepanasi ace?
Arai kiponikinitos ikokolak kuare,
Mam tikemudiasi aitodol neeupiata?
10 # Isa 17:14. Konye edokidokoorit eoŋ Esau,
Apukorit akeiboisio nukaiyeiyea,
Mam epedori aiŋop.
Amudiar aurike, ka ikeinaean, ka ikeidunyeta da;
Ŋes da arumor.
11Kinyek ikonidwe eraas ikokiok,
Edari eoŋ koraut luejarete;
Ka akonipuserun da kamunokinos eoŋ.”
12 # Yer 25:29; Oba 1:16. Naarai inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: “Koany, nul lumam ebusakinit aimat kadere abeit emasete; ijo da ŋolloikui cut komam kitieanio? Mam eut ikui aitieanio, konye abeit imasi. 13#Age 22:16; Isa 45:23; 34:6; Amo 6:8; 1:12. Naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Eyapoikin elopet ebe eraun Bosura aumokinet, ka amorosit, ka ameie, ka ekenit da; eraunete ikereria kere amejei nutir.”
14 # Oba 1:1,2. Eira akiro nuepote kama ejai EJAKAIT,
Iyakuunitai ekiyakia katekerin ebala ebe:
“Kotukonokinos nepepe, kosurokis,
Konyout kojioto!
15Naarai koany, ketidisiari jo kotoma atekerin,
Ainakin da ituŋa aimwaŋ jo.
16 # Yob 39:27; Awa 15:25; Amo 9:2. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kimodata jo airukurianetakon nusogim,
Ka aisoŋolonut nakotaukon,
Ŋo loiboie kabuai nuka atiida,
Loirumit aidi naka eukot.
Karaida kiduki akonikai kuiu kwape kemorukaalet,
Ketodokuni kaŋina.
17 # Yer 18:16; 50:13. “Eraun Edom aumokinet: eumokini ŋinituŋanan yenitori, isuari kanuka aitieanetake kere. 18#Age 19:24-25; Ikis 29:23; Yer 50:40; Amo 4:11. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kwape amudiarere Sodom ka Gomora ka ireria kere luidunyatar, emameun ituŋanan yenejaun ŋina, kwabo emameun ikoku yenika ituŋanan yeniboikin nen. 19#Yer 4:17; 12:5; Sek 11:3; Any 15:11; Sab 89:6-8; Yob 41:10. Koany, edokari kwape eŋatuny loebuni kamoni naka Yorodan asurokin aibois naegogoŋ: konye etekeri ŋes katipet; ido adakakini nin yenaseuni ayait ŋes, Naarai iŋai bo eputosi keoŋ? Ido iŋai bo kebwaikini apak? Iŋai da ekeeokon lokabwoikini eoŋ? 20#Yer 50:45. Aso kiirasi ainerun naka EJAKAIT, naeneru kanuka Edom; kanun nuelosikinit mama ejaas luibojete koTeman: abeit iriarete kesi ludidik luka esipan; abeit eburunete anukiakee kanukee. 21Emarani akwap kanuka epororo loka aibirorikec; ejai acalanari, epupunio eporotoke kAŋolol Nakarenan. 22#Yer 4:13. Koany, edokari eporori kwape kemorukaalet, loeterari akebebenoka asurokin Bosura: eraunos itauon luka ikirigin luka Edom kapaaran kaŋin kwape etau laka aberu naerieba aidoun.”
23 # Isa 17:1-3; Amo 1:3-5; Sek 9:1. Nuka Damasiko.
“Erumata Kamat ileje, ka Arapad da;
Naarai eirasi akiro nueroko,
Ajurununata itauon;
Iyalononoete kwape aŋolol namam inapakina.
24 # Isa 13:8. Aleiekar Damasiko, ebelokin akerit,
Ekamu amaran;
Adumu ariebo ka iean,
Kwape aberu naerieba aidoun.
25 # Yer 51:41. Kanukinyo mam inyeinere ere loka aiturit,
Ere loka akaiyalama?
26Ebala EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe,
Kanuka nun ŋes ibironokinotor ikekatumunak orotinike,
Emudiario da ikajiokoke kere kapaaran kaŋin,
27 # Amo 1:4; 2 Ika 13:3. Ido enokokini akim arwatatin nuka Damasiko,
Iewei itogoi lukapolok luka Ben-kadad.”
28 # Yob 1:3. Nuka Kedar, ka ajakanuto nuka Kasor, nuamudiari Nebukadaresar eka baka loko Babulon.
Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Konyoutu, kodokasi Kedar!
Konata idwe lukokide!
29 # Sab 120:5. Elemunete iwemanakee ka isipaninikec;
Aigalisiokee, ka iborokec kere,
Edemarete da akaalaikee:
Ilulukinere ebe, ‘Erum etasuro iwaitin kere!’
30Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kokera, koriariata, konopata,
Yes luibojete koKasor:
Naarai eneru Nebukadaresar ekabaka loka
Babulon nukasurokin yes,
Apet nuelosikinit aiswamakin yes.
31 # Mar 23:9; Ikis 33:28; Tub 18:28; Mik 7:14. “Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Konyoutu, kodokasi mama ejai ateker naijamamai,
Naiboie inapakina;
Naemameete irutei karaida ariino,
Naiboie egitara,
32 # Ikis 28:64; Esk 5:10; Yer 25:23. Eraunete akaalaikec iŋatan,
Isipanin da lukibarenekee lukoran.
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Esiarakini luekebito arokio mama ejaas ikwamin kere,
Ido ainuni kes ican luepote kowaitin kere,
33 # Yer 9:11; Mal 1:3. Eraun Kasor aibois naka ikuwei naiboienenoto,
Ameje namam edaun:
Emamuun ituŋanan ycnejaun ŋina,
Kwabo emamuun ikoku yenika irunanan yeniboikin nen.”
34 # Age 10:22; Yer 25:25. Akirot naka EJAKAIT naabukobu mama ejai enabi Yeremia nuka Elam, napegeuni Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda airum, kobala ebe:
35 # Age 14:1; Esr 4:9; Isa 21:2; Dan 8:2. “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe: Koany, abili akabwa naka Elam, ŋes amun naka agogonokec. 36Ainuni Elam ikwamin iwonon anyoun kariino aoŋon nuka akuj, ido eweliwelari mama ejaas ikwamin ŋul kere; emameun areker namam edolete luerenearitai luka Elam, 37#Yer 48:2. Ainakin Elam aburun kakonyen nuka lukasurupokec, kakonyen da nuka ŋul kaluemooto aiiarakec. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ainuni kes aronis, ŋes akanyunyura naededen, eiukuni ekileŋ arukio kes, aitodol neamudiaria kes. 38#Yer 43:10; Dan 7:9. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ebwaikini ekaicolon Elam, amudiari kanen ekabaka ka ikapolok da.
39“Konye ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kapaarasia nukawasia, enyakuni bobo aijorcorio naka Elam.”
