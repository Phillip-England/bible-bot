Yeremia 18

18
1Akirot naabu kobu mama ejai Yeremia kama ejai EJAKAIT, ebe: 2“Konyou, kijesar togo loka ekajean, kaŋina da kesiirari jo akakiro.” 3Kasodi eoŋ aijesar togo loka ekajean, ido koany, iswamai akeswam kokokoroke. 4#Rom 11:15. Ido naemunaunor amot naiswamai ŋesi kelupe kakan naka ekajean, kirueakini bobo aije amot ace, kwape itoiokitor ekajean aiie.
5Kosodi kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 6#Isa 45:9; 64:8.“Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Wu jo ekek loka Isirael, mam kereka apedori eoŋ aiswamakin yes kwape eswamaa ekaiean lo? Koany, kwape elupe kakan naka ekajean, ikoni ijo nen kakanika, ijo ekek loka Isirael. 7#Yer 1:10. Kapak kaŋin naenerar nuka ateker, ka nuka ajakanut, arutun ka abilibil ka amudiar da; 8#Tub 10:15,16; 2 Siit 12:6,7; Isa 1:16-19; Esk 18:21; Luk 13:3-5; Yok 3:10. arai ibelokin ateker nin, naabu kenerite nuke, ainyein aronisike, eitokini aronis naalosikinit aiswamakin kes. 9Ido kapak kaŋin naenerar nuka ateker ka nuka ajakanut, aiduk ka aira ŋes; 10arai kiswama aronis kakonyeneka, komam erimari eporotoka, kasodi eoŋ aiitokin aiokis naabu kener aitoiok ŋes. 11#2 Ika 17:13; Isa 1:16-19. Aso do kolot, kolimok ituŋa luka Yuda, ka luibojete ko Yerusalem ebe: ‘Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT ebe: Koany, etemok aronis mama iiaas, eneru akirot asurokin yes. Kobonutu do yesi, ninitunanan korotoke lokaronon, kitojokata ikusirotin ka aswamisiokus da.’
12“Konye etemarete ebe, ‘Emamei amun: naarai kitupi akirokosi, kiswamai da ninitunanan kwape ejaar aninik naka eketau lokaronon.’
13 # Yer 2:10; 1 Kor 5:1. “Kanuka ŋun ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
Kinitiŋita yesi atekerin,
Iŋai epupunit akiro nuikote nen?
Eswama abekot naka Isirael akirot nasogim noi.
14Epedori kereka akaka naka Lebanon aimijar katiida naka ais?
Eonikinete kereka akip nuelilim nueleleunete kama elwana?
15 # Yer 2:13; 10:15; 6:16; Ikis 32:21; Isa 53:6. Naarai apotu ikatuŋa kemurwokis eoŋ,
Apotu kisupurikis aamameak lukisupurununo;
Kitiguikis kes korotinikec, kosokono lumoion,
Atubitoi kokitui, nemam erot itutut,
16 # Yer 49:13; Ikis 29:24; 1 Ika 9:8; Aku 2:15; Mik 6:16. Aitaraun akwapukee aumokinet,
Ka aijuar namam enaŋi.
Eumokini ŋinituŋanan yenitori nen,
Kimunimuni akouke.
17 # Yer 13:24; Sab 48:7. Esiarisiari kes akonyen nuka lukasurup
Kwape kekwam lokokide:
Etodikini kes ekapur konye mere akakonyen,
Kapaaran naka epolakee.”
18 # Yer 11:19; Asw 7:51; 2 Tim 4:3; Leb 10:11; Mal 2:7. Kosodete kesi atemar ebe, “Kopotu, kenerutu akiro asurokin Yeremia; naarai mam ikisila eoliorete kaneejai esasedoti, karaida ainerun kama ejaas lukacoa, karaida akirot kaneejai enabi. Kopotu do, kenomata ŋes kaŋajep, kenyeis oni aibwaikin etau akiroke kere.”
19Kibwaikinai eoŋ etau, wu EJAKAIT,
Kiira eporoto loka ŋul kaluketonolopete eoŋ.
20 # Sab 109:4; 35:7. Etacakinio kereka aronis itia ajokis?
Naarai abokikis akaijar aipany.
Kiitu kwape kabwoia kakonyenekon ainerakin kes ajokis, Aibelokin akoninyunyura kamakec.
21Kanuka ŋun kijaik ikecedwe otenei,
Kokorak kes apedor naka ekileŋ;
Aŋorokec kotwakiasi ikecedwe, koraut da apuserun.
Kotularos ikecekiliok,
Kodany ekileŋ ikecekatumunak kojie.
22Kopupunoi aimony kotogoikee,
Naisituriaikinia kes erionerl
Naarai abokata aipany alokia eoŋ,
Ebwaata ataein aisipokia akakeje.
23 # Nek 4:5. Konye wu EJAKAIT, iieni ijo ainerunikee kere aiar eoŋ.
Sirikisioni numam adolitekec,
Sirikimwata da aronisikec kakonyenekon.
Konye kolatarai kes kakonyenekon;
Kitepesen kes kapak naka anyunyurakon.
