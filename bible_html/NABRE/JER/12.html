Yeremia 12

12
1 # Age 18:25; Yob 12:6. Idolit ijo, wu EJAKAIT, naanuriania kamakon:
Konye akoto apurokin bon kamakon akiro:
Kanukinyo ekerianaror erot loka ikaronok?
Kanukinyo iiamamaata kere luiswamaenenete noi nuka aimodi
2 # Isa 29:13. Ibu kira kes, eebo, kidirata alias;
Epoloete, eebo, eraete araito:
Iapiei ijo kaitukekee, Ilwanikina da kaomisiokee.
3 # Yak 5:5. Konye jo, wu EJAKAIT, kijeni eoŋ:
Kiany eoŋ, igimori ekatau kamakon:
Kiriu kes kwape amerekekin nuaarat,
Kotiak kes kanuka apaaran naka earit.
4 # Sab 107:34; Yer 4:25; Kos 4:3. Itodoli ai akwap aibworo,
Akui da nuka akwap kere arinyor?
Kanuka aronis naka nul kaluiboiete kotoma
Ŋes edaunotor itiaŋ ka akweny;
Naarai ebakai ebe, “Mam ŋes eanyuni esanewok lokokau.”
5 # 1 Siit 12:15; Yer 49:19. “Arai ibolito kituna luekerete kakeje, kitapasa jo,
Ikwaikini ai ipedoriabolobol kibalasin?
Ido arai ilatara ijo kakwap naka ainapakin,
Ikwaikini ca ai kaiturokina naka Yorodan?
6Naarai ikoniinacan, ka ekek loka papakon,
Ŋupeŋul kiswamakinete nuka aimod;
Ŋupenul kiluluoto jo:
Sirikiyu kes, karaida kiinerakis jo akiro nukajokak.”
7“Ajal etogoka, aeaka airumeteka;
Ainak naemin ekatau noi akan naka lukasurupoke.
8 # 2 Siit 36:16. Arau airumeteka kamakaŋ kwape eŋatuny kamagoro;
Ekeseu ekeporoto asurokin eoŋ;
Ŋes alaia ŋes.
9Erai kereka airumeteka kamakaŋ kwape ekweny loeerepus
loemejai?
Irigaunete kereka ŋes ikweny luemeiaete asurokin?
Koloto yesi, kotukonokis itiaŋ kere luka ais,
Koyautu kes konyamasi.
10 # Yer 6:3; Isa 5:1,5; 63:18. Apotu ikeeokok luipu komunaasi emanikorika loka emusabibu,
Kipudapudata ewaika kakeje,
Kitaraut ewaika loejok ais naesil.
11 # Isa 42:25; Mal 2:2. Etaraut asilio:
Ibworo mama ajai naarai esil;
Itisilioritai akwap kere,
Naarai emamei ituŋanan yenibwaikinit etau,
12Adoloto ikaŋatak kuju neelapat toma ais:
Naarai iŋinyari ekileŋ loka EJAKAIT ageun kosane ediopet loka akwap kiton osaŋe eee loka akwap;
Emamei akuan nainapakina.
13 # Leb 26:16. Apotu koyeata akima, konye kosakata ikukwa;
Itapasitos atai, mam imedaunete:
Kisurumito araitokus yes ileic,
Kanuka anyunyura naededen naka EJAKAIT.”
14 # Sep 2:8-10; Ikis 30:3; Yer 32:37. Ebala kwanata EJAKAIT asurokin iidunyetaka kere lueroko, luetirorete airumet naesurumit eoŋ ikatuŋa Isirael ebe: “Koany, arutori kes kakwapukee, arutori da ekek loka Yuda kokidinikec. 15#Esk 28:25; Amo 9:14. Kosodi kanen, kadau arutor kes, aboŋuni etimi kes: ido enyakakini kes, ninitunanan airumeteke, ŋinituŋanan da alupoke. 16#Yer 4:2; Yos 23:7; Sep 1:5; 1 Kor 3:9,10; Pes 2:20; 1 Pet 2:5. Kosodi kanen, arai edunyaka kesi aisisia iponesio luka ikatuŋa, iyapoete kolimito ekakiror ebe, ‘Kwape ejaria EJAKAIT’, kwape esisianakiniata kesi ikatuŋa aiyapo kolimito Baal; ŋes konyet edukokinere kidiŋ kikatuna. 17#Sab 2:8,9; Isa 60:12; Luk 19:27; 1 Pet 2:8. Konye ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Arai mam epupete, kasodi eoŋ arutor ateker ŋin, arutori ido amudiari.”
