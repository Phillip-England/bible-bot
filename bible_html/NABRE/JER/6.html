Yeremia 6

6
1 # Yos 18:28; Tub 1:21; Nek 3:14. Kokerata kolakunos, yes idwe luka Benyamin,
Konyout kokidiŋ Yerusalem,
Kokutata arupepe koTekoa,
Kikeasi aanyunet koBet-kakerem:
Naarai etakani aronis ebuni kagolitomei,
Ka amudiar naepol.
2Agelari akoku naka Sion,
Naitemelel naijamamai,
3 # 2 Ika 25:1. Epote ikecokok kisipaninikee luka amerekekin asurokin ŋes;
Ibubuete iwemanakec airimor;
Itadakete kes, ŋinituŋanan kaiboisike.
4“Kokapanakinos ejie kaŋes;
Konyoutu, kadokasi kidin paaran!”
“Itukwi oni! naarai aiotaki akoloŋ,
Aojaaros itolimon luka atibonl”
5“Konyoutu, kadokasi kuare,
Kamudiasi itogoike lukapolok!”
6 # 2 Ika 21:16. Naarai ebala EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe:
“Kojeput ikito, kitutuoto etut,
Asurokin Yerusalem:
Lo ŋes ere lokitieana;
Ileleba emwaŋit pas kidinike.
7 # Sab 55:9-11; Yer 20:8; Esk 7:11; Mik 7:2,3. K wape ibwelibwelio atan akekipi,
Kwape kwaŋin da ibwelibwelio aronisike:
Iirario akaje ka aiŋat kotomake;
Ejai adek ka amusuŋun kakonyeneka tiritir.
8 # Esk 23:18; Kos 9:12. Kituutoor, ijo Yerusalem,
Kinyek ekatau anyoun kama ijai;
Kenyek aitaraun jo asilio,
Akwap namam ituŋanan.”
9Ebala EJAKAIT loka aioresio kwanata ebe:
“Inyogokinete aidepidep eut aatoŋot naka Isirael kwape emusabibu:
Kinyakanyak bobo akonikan katenun kwape yenedeni emusabi bu.”
10 # Any 6:12; Asw 7:60; Yer 7:26; 20:8; Luk 11:45; 2 Tim 4:3,4. Alimokini ŋai ka aikwenyar da tetere epupunete?
Koany, mam akeeeki ekepep,
Kwabo mam epedorete akiirar:
Koany, araun akirot naka EJAKAIT amorosit kamakee;
Mam isiyalamikinito.
11 # Yer 8:10; Sep 1:13. Kanuka ŋun ŋes elelebunor eoŋ anyunyura naka EJAKAIT;
Kapas aitikokinit.
“Kobukok odwe korot,
Ido katukot naka ikatumunak ncpepe:
Naarai eyanaario ekile ka nakowaike,
Lomoion ka ŋol da loileleba apaarasia.
12 # Yer 8:10-12; Sep 1:13. Eluoros itogoikee luka ice,
Nepepe kanorokec ka imanikoriakee:
Naarai ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Ariori akakan mama ejaas luibojete kakwap kana.
13 # Isa 56:11; Mik 3:5. Naarai ageun kaloedit kakesi kiton lokapolon kakesi,
Eraŋani ŋinituŋanan aimedaun;
Ido ageun konabi kiton osasedoti,
Emodi ŋinituŋanan.
14 # Esk 13:10. Etaŋaleut da amusuŋut naka ikatuŋa kaitapapanit,
Kobalas ebe, ‘Ainapakin, ainapakin,’
Kereka emamei ainapakin.
15 # Yer 3:3. Eruma kereka kes ileje naadauniata aswam naketal?
Be! Mam eut erumito ileje,
Ido mam apedorete aitirir.
Kanuka ŋun ebiroros nepepe kanul luebiroros:
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Kapak naeticania kes elataros.”
16 # Yer 18:15; Mal 4:4; Luk 16:29; Isa 28:12; Mat 11:29. Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe,
“Kobwaata yesi korotin, koanyut,
Kinita isokono lukolono,
Neejai erot loejok, koloto kotoma,
Ido idumunete aiyeŋun kotauonokus:
Konye kobalas ebe, ‘Mam kilosi kotoma.’
17 # Isa 21:11; Yer 25:4; Kbk 2:1. Abu kebwaik ikedarak kamakus ebe,
‘Kopuputu eporoto loka arupepe!’
Konye kobalas ebe, ‘Mam kipupuni.’
18Aso do kopuputu, yes atekerin,
Kojen, ijo ekodet, nuedoikinete kes.
19 # Awa 1:31. Kopup, ijo akwap; koany, ayauni aronis otuŋa lu,
Araito nuka akeceomisio,
Naarai mam apotu kiirasi akakiro;
Ido aneroto ekakisil.
20 # Isa 66:3; 60:6; Mik 6:6. Anyoin kereka ajokis naka lukisupurununo luepote mamaka
anyoun koSeba,
Eitola da loebuni kakwap naelwana?
Ainakinetakus nuieweio mam eeamunio,
Asubanakus da mam kesiyalamikinito.
21 # Yob 5:12; Isa 8:14; Yer 13:16. Kanuka nun ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
‘Koany, ebwaikini ikomion akonyen nuka ituŋa kalu:
Iguikinete lukapapa ka odwekee kere nepepe;
Eoliori eidunyet ka epaperoke da.’”
22Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Koany epote ituŋa kakwap naka agolitomei;
Ekwenyuni ateker naepol kowaitin luelwaka luka akwap.
23 # Isa 5:30. Itiŋito akabwai ka akwaras;
Ibworitos kesi, mam itimenenete;
Irurumaete ikeeeporotoi kwape aŋolol,
Ibwanenito ibalasin;
Ekapanakina nclitunanan kwape ekaiiot,
Asurokin jo, wu akoku naka Sion.”
24 # Sab 48:6; Isa 21:3; Yer 4:31; 13:21; 30:6; 49:24. Kipou keirasi eemutukee;
Koleiekekaros akaniniwok;
Kikam aditar,
Ka ariebo kwape naka aberu naidouni.
25 # Tub 5:6. Sirikilomas okitui, karaida alosit korot;
Naarai ejai ekileŋ loka lokasurup ka aitasuro kanoliwai,
26 # Isa 32:11; Yer 4:8; Mik 1:10; Sek 12:10. Wu jo akoku naka ikatuŋa, konap epuko,
Kipunupunokin kotoma okurwon:
Kibworo, kwape ibworor ikoku yenilemura,
Akuudian naepol noi noi,
Naarai kitiuni oni ekamudiaran.
27“Ketarau jo ekegimoron, ekeninon da kotoma otunaka; Tetere ijenuni igimori da ekecerot,
28 # Esk 22:18. Epaliikis kesi kere noi,
Irimete kasabanakin;
Eraas kesi aisinit ka asuat:
Iswamaete kere nukamunaar.
29Ekusi acukut kagogoŋ; Idopori akim asasit;
Iyatanakinete ailiaun atai;
Naarai mam ikaronok edemario.
30 # Kos 9:17; Sek 11:8. Enyaritai epuesa loepalitai,
Naarai agiri EJAKAIT kes.”
