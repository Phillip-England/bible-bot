Yeremia 27

27
1 # Yer 28:1; 2 Ika 24:18-20; 2 Siit 36:11-13. Kageunet naka airum naka Sedekia okoku loka Yosia, ekabaka loka Yuda, kobu akirot na naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Yeremia. 2#Yer 28:10; Esk 4:1. Kabala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: “Kiswamau aunoi ka aiookon, koenik omurisinikon, 3Kijukak ace mama ejai ekabaka loka Edom, ka ekabaka loka Moab, ka ekabaka loka idwe luka Amon, ka ekabaka loka Turo, ka eka baka loka Sidon, kakanin nuka ikiyakia luepote Yerusalem mama ejai Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda. 4Kitetemik kes akiro nu ainar ikapolokokec kobala ebe: Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Ilimokinete lukapolokokus kwanata ebe: 5#Sab 96:5; 115:15,16; Isa 42:5; 45:12; Age 1:29,30; Ikis 2:5; Yos 1:2,3; Dan 4:17. Eoŋ abu kasub akwap, ka ituŋa, ka itiaŋ, lucjaas akwap, kapedorika naepol ka akanika naerioro; ainakini eoŋ nin yenetojokit. 6#Yer 28:14; Dan 2:37,38; Yer 25:9; 43:10; Sab 50:10-12. Kwana da ainak akwapin nu kere akan naka Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, ekakejaanakinan; ainak da itiaŋ luka ais aiiaanakin ŋes, 7#2 Siit 36:20; Dan 5:26; Yer 25:12; 50:27. Atekerin da kere ijaanakincte ŋes ka okokuke, ka orataitike, aitodol needolioto apaarasia nuka akekwap: kosodete itubusianete ŋes atekerin nuipu ka ikabakan lukapolok.
8“Kosodi kanen, ateker arai ajakanut naeneri aijaanakin Nebukadaresar ŋol, ekabaka loko Babulon, ido luenerete aibwaikin imurisiŋakec aiookot naka ekabaka loko Babulon, ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Etieani ateker ŋin, kekilen, ka eteŋei, ka atular, aitodol neesimudiaria ŋes kes. 9#Awa 19:27; Yer 14:14; Mik 3:7; Pes 5:6. Konye yes, sirikiirasi inabinokus, karaida ikaroakakus, karaida airujasiakus, karaida ikadwarakakus, karaida ikacudakakus, lukilimokinete ebe, ‘Mam ijaanakinete ekabaka loko Babulon.’ 10Naarai kidwarakinete yes nukainal, ayaŋaar yes naelwana kakwapukus; kasodi eoŋ aireŋear yes, ido iwoliorete. 11Konye ateker naibwaikini emurisinike ajookot naka ekabaka loko Babulon, kijaanak, ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Enyeini ŋin aiboi kakwapukee: ido ekoriete iboikinos nen.”
12 # Yer 28:1; 38:17. Kalimok Sedekia ekabaka loka Yuda akiro nun kere kwape ajaatar, kabala ebe: “Kibwaikis imurisinakus aiookot naka ekabaka loko Babulon, kijaanakis ŋes ka iketuŋa, tetere iiarete. 13#Awa 8:36; Esk 18:31. Kanukinyo ikototor atwakare, ijo ka ikonituŋa, kanuka ekileŋ, ka eteŋei, ka atular, kwape ineritor EJAKAIT nuka ateker naeneri aijaanakin ekabaka loko Babulon? 14#Yer 14:14; 23:21. Kwabo sirikiirasi akiro nuka inabin luineras kayes ebe, ‘Mam ijaanakinete ekabaka loko Babulon.’ Naarai edwarete kesi nukainal. 15Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Naarai mam ejukununei kes, konye edwarete kokirorika aiŋal: tetere kereneari yes, ido iwoliorete yesi, ka inabin lukidwarakinete.”
16 # 2 Ika 24:13; Yer 28:3; Dan 1:2. Ido da alimokin isascdotin ka ituŋa lu kere ebe, “Inera EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Sirikiirasi akiro nuka jnabinokus lukidwarakinete, ebalas ebe, ‘Koany, iboro lukorogo loka EJAKAIT eapiete bo bo ainyakunio koBabulon,’ naarai edwarete nukainal. 17#Esk 18:31. Sirikiirasi kes; kijaanakis ekabaka loko Babulon, tetere ijarete, Inyo bo ti keraunor ere lo asilio? 18#Age 20:7; Yob 42:8; Isa 37:4; Yak 5:16. Konye arai eraas inabin, ka akirot naka EJAKAIT da arai ejai kakes, kinainaata kito kesi EJAKAIT loka aioresio, kinyeis iboro luesalito kotogo loka EJAKAIT, kotogo da loka ekabaka loka Yuda, koYerusalem da ayanaario Babulon. 19#2 Ika 25:13; Yer 52:17. Naarai inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio nuka ipirin, ka nuka aŋolol, ka nuka aicegeria, ido nuka luapianunos luka iboro luesalito kore kalo, 20#2 Ika 24:14,15; Yer 24:1. lumam iritonorit Nebukadaresar ekabaka loko Babulon, naej oreoria Yekonia okoku loka Yekoiakim ekabaka loka Yuda, alemar koYerusalem, koyanaa Babulon, ka ikapolok kere luka Yuda ka Yerusalem; 21#Leb 26:24; Isa 5:25. eebo, inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio, Edeke loka Isirael, nuka iboro luapianunos kotogo Ioka EJAKAIT, kotogo da loka ekabaka loka Yuda, koYerusalem da ebe: 22#2 Siit 36:21; Dan 9:2; Yer 29:10; 32:5; Esr 1:7. Eyanaario Babulon, ido ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ejaenenas nina, aitodol apaaran naaanyanaria kes, Kasodi alemuni enyakuni bobo aibois na.”
