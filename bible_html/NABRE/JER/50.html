Yeremia 50

50
1 # Isa 13:1; 21:1. Akirot naabu EJAKAIT kiner kama ka Yeremia enabi kanuka Babulon, kanuka akwap naka Ikaludean, ebe:
2 # Isa 46:1; Yer 43:12. “Kolimosi kotoma atekerin, kitejenut da,
Kikiasi emedera, Kiteienut,
Sirikimunonikis: kineras atiar,
‘Aŋatar Babulon,
Erumata Bel ileic,
Eyalonoŋ Merodak,
Erumata aputosiake ileje,
Iyalononoete akeputosia nuegerer.’
3 # Isa 13:17. “Naarai enyouni ateker kagolitomei asurokin ŋes, itisili akekwap, komameun yeniboie kotoma; akerata, aloto, ituŋa ka itiaŋ da.
4 # Kos 1:11; 3:5; Sab 126:5; 105:4; Sek 12:10; 8:21,22; Isa 45:19. “Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kapaarasia kakwa, kapak kaŋin da, epote idwe luka Isirael, kes nepepe ka idwe luka Yuda; emonyete elosete, komootos EJAKAIT Ekecedeke. 5#Yer 31:31; 1 Pet 2:25. Inisete nuka Sion, kotereitos ereetakec nina, koba las ebe, ‘Kopotu, kemorikikinos ka EJAKAIT kaitutuket namam edaun namam imurwokinio.’
6“Araur ikatuŋa amerekekin nukeoliorete; erooliosi ikecokokokec, etapaasi oroŋan: anyout koronat kiton oukor, imurwokinito nekeuriakenenata. 7#Sek 11:5; Yer 2:3; Dan 9:16; Sab 22:4. Anyamata kere lukeanyunete: kobalas lukasurupokec ebe, ‘Mam kigwaunit akiro, naarai adiakasi kama ejai EJAKAIT, aiboisikec nakabeit, ŋes EJAKAIT, amun naka lukapapaakee.’
8 # Apu 18:4; Isa 48:20; Yer 51:6; Sek 2:6. “Kokerata kokidin naka Babulon, konyoutu kakwap naka Ikaludean, kosubata bala ikorwa kakonyen nuka isipanin. 9Naarai koany, etwekenyuni etodokari da atukot naka atekerin nukapolok asurokin Babulon, nuepote kagolitomei: iderakinos asurokin ŋes: ameun kanen eŋatario. Eraunos ikeeekojoi bala luka ikirigin luenaikinito, lumam eboŋunete epalapalato, 10#Apu 17:16. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Eŋatario Kaludea: imonikinos kere luenatarete Ŋes,
11 # Isa 47:6. “Naarai iyalamas, naarai ibwoŋas,
Yes luinatasi akairumet,
Naarai ieuros kwape ataok naieakaeakai akima,
Icieinas kwape abalasin nuegogoŋ;
12Irumete totokus ileje noi;
Itirirai ŋin nakiwuruuni,
Koany, eraun ŋesi awasia naka atekerin,
Amodiŋ, akwap nalarion, elet.
13Kanuka anyunyura naka EJAKAIT mam iboikinoi,
Konye eraun asilio:
Eumokin nin yenitori Babulon,
Isuari icanike kere.
14Kiderakinos asurokin Babulon kowaitin kere,
Yes kere luikesito akabwai;
Kogumata ŋes, sirikitasalata imalia;
Naarai adiaka kama ejai EJAKAIT.
15 # 1 Siit 29:24; 2 Siit 30:8; Aku 5:6; Yer 51:58; Sab 2:1-3; Apu 16:6. Kikeseut elala kowaitin kere,
Alo akanin;
Ebiroros apetetake,
Atiyanar akerwatatin.
Naarai lo ŋes enyaŋit loka EJAKAIT;
Kinyanakis;
Kwape kolo eswamaa,
Kiswamakis ŋes kwaŋin,
16 # Isa 13:14. Komudiasi yeneyei koBabulon,
Ka yenekapit ekurupeta kapak naka aisak:
Kanuka aiyoŋ ekileŋ loereit,
Eboŋori ŋinituŋanan mama ejaas iketuŋa,
Ekeri ninitunanan akwap nakobukee.
17 # Yok 3:12; Mat 9:36; Yer 23:2; 2:15; 1 Pet 2:25; 2 Siit 28:20; 2 Ika 17:6; 24:10,14; Isa 7:17,20; 8:7,8. “Isirael ŋes amerekek namejat; ereneata iŋatunyo, Kisiau ekabaka loka Asuria ainyam, ka Nebukadaresar lo ekabaka loko Babulon, ŋes lokawasia ekoe akekojo. 18Ŋes inerar kwanata EJAKAIT loka ajoresio Edeke loka Isirael ebe: Koany, etieani ekabaka loko Babulon ka akekwap, kwape abu keticania ekabaka loka Asuria. 19#Isa 65:10. Ido enyakuni Isirael bobo toma oronyiake, ido edakenena ŋesi Karamel ka Basan, imonikini eketau koukon luka Epuraim ka Gilead da, 20#Yer 31:34; Isa 1:9. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kapaarasia kanun, kapak kaŋin, emootai numam adolit nuka Isirael, kwabo emameete; aronisio da nuka Yuda, kwabo mam etakanunete: naarai esioniei ŋul lualeleni araun aatoŋot,
21 # Isa 10:6. “Kodokasi asurokin akwap naka Merataim,
Kosurokis da luibojete koPekod,
Ebala EJAKAIT ebe,
Koara, komudiasi eut atupakin kes,
Kiswamata kere nuabu kecorak.
22Ejai eeelet loka ejie toma akwap,
Ka amudiar naepol!
23 # Isa 16:6; Yer 51:20. Aiepar cama anyuli naka akwap kere, abila ca!
Aluor eama Babulon ameje kokidin atekerin!
24 # Dan 5:30. Abu kalok jo, ipok da, ijo Babulon,
Mam ibu kojen:
Itakanu, kikamun da,
Naarai ipegata ka EJAKAIT.
25 # Isa 13:5. Anaa EJAKAIT akeinadis,
Alemu lujiis luka anyunyurake,
Naarai ejai EJAKAIT,
EJAKAIT loka aioresio,
Aswam naelosi aswam kaŋina,
Kakwap naka Ikaludean.
26Kopotu kosurokis anyoun kanoliikoru,
Kodakasi asotoi nuka idulake;
Kotukonosi atukon,
Kosodete amudiar cut:
Sirikitasala imodiobore kere.
27 # Sab 22:12; Isa 34:7; Yer 48:44. Koarata imasenikoke kere;
Kijesaros oarit.
Itukwi kes! Naarai adolu akeeepaaran,
Apak naka aitieaniokec.
28 # Yer 51:10. “Iporotoi luka nul kaluekerete aiku anyoun kakwap nako Babulon, alimor koSion enyaijit loka EJAKAIT Ewokodeke, ainyaŋ eyekaluke.
29 # Apu 18:6; Aku 3:64; 2 Ses 1:6; Isa 47:10. “Konyarautu ikagumak imalia asurokin Babulon, nul kere luikesete akabwai. Kidukuro airigaun; sirikeku imodioke. Kotaeakis kwape ajaar aswamuke; kere nuabu kiswama, kiswamakis ŋes da: naarai iwonono kama ejai EJAKAIT, kama ejai Ekalaunan loka Isirael. 30#Yer 49:26; 51:4. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kanuka nun ŋes ibirokinotor ikekatumunak orotinike, emudiario da ikajiokoke kere kapaaran kaŋin.
31“Ebala EJAKAIT, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ebe,
Koany, kasurokin eoŋ jo, ijo ekewonot,
Naarai adolo akonipaaran,
Apak naketieania.
32 # Yer 21:14. Iguikini ekewonot ibiror,
Ido emameun yenitonyouni:
Esinepikini akim oreriake,
Enyami da nul kere luirimorito.
33“Inera kwanata EJAKAIT loka aioresio ebe: Ereitai idwe luka Isirael ka idwe luka Yuda; ŋul da kere luapotu kijoreosi kes eridakinito; eŋerito aosikin. 34#Apu 18:8; Awa 23:11; Isa 47:4. Egogoŋ Ekalakunanikee, EJAKAIT loka ajoresio ŋes ekekiror. Abeit epuri kanukee, tetere isinapikini akwap, konye isiyalononoi luibojete koBabulon.
35 # Dan 5:30; Isa 37:13. “Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Esurokini ekileŋ Ikaludean,
Ka luibojete koBabulon,
Ka ikapolokoke, ka lukaeoake da.
36Esurokini ekileŋ ikelabilabak,
Koluoros ibaŋiok:
Esurokini ekileŋ ikiriginike,
Eburunete.
37 # Yer 25:20; 51:30; Nak 3:13. Esurokini ekileŋ abalasinikee, ka agalinikec,
Ka ituŋa kere luinyalinyaluna luejaas kokidinike,
Eraunos nul bala aŋor.
Esurokini ekileŋ abarake,
Eŋatario.
38 # Isa 44:27; Apu 16:12; Yer 51:32,36. Eŋaikin akamu akipike, eokete!
Naarai erai akwap naka aputosia nuegerer,
Abanakaros kotoma aputosia,
39 # Apu 18:2; Isa 13:21; Yer 51:37; 25:12. “Kanuka nun iboikinos itian luka ais nepepe kiku wei Babulon, iboikinos ikuului da ŋina: mam bobo ejaikinos ituŋa ikar kere; ido mam iboikinos ituŋa aturio katurio, 40#Age 19:24-25; Ikis 29:23; Isa 1:9; Yer 49:18; Amo 4:11; Sep 2:9; 2 Pet 2:6; Yud 1:7. Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Kwape kolo ebiroria Edeke Sodom ka Gomora ka ireria kere luedunyatar, kwaŋin emameun ituŋanan yeneiaun ŋina, karaida ikoku yenika ituŋanan yeniboiororoi nen.
41 # Yer 6:22; Apu 17:16. “Koany, epote ituŋa kagolitomei;
Ateker napeti,
Isikwenyunio ikabakan luipu
Anyoun kosaneta luka akwap.
42 # Isa 13:18; 5:30. Ekapetc akabwai ka akwaras;
Ededeŋ, emamei da aitim;
Irurumai eporotokec bala aŋolol,
Ibwanenitos abalasin;
Ederakinos mamakon kwape ikajiok,
Ŋo apese naka Babulon.
43 # Yer 49:24. “Eira ekabaka loka Babulon eemutokee,
Alajakakata akaninike;
Adita akirot etauke,
Ka ariebo kwape naka aberu kanaerieba aidoun.
44 # Yob 41:10; Dan 5:2,20. “Koany, edokari kwape eŋatuny loebuni kamo ni naka Yorodan asurokin aibois naegogon; konye etekeri kes katipet; ido adakakini ayait ŋes ŋin yenaseuni. Naarai iŋai tope eputosi keoŋ? Ido iijai kebwaikin apak? Iŋai da ekeeokon lokabwoikin eoŋ? 45#Sab 33:11; Isa 14:24; Yer 51:11; Asw 4:28; Pes 1:11; 1 Kor 1:27. Aso kiirasi ainerun naka EJAKAIT, naeneru kanuka Babulon; kanun nuelosikinit mama ejai akwap naka Ikaludean: Abeit iriete kesi ludidik luka esipan; abeit ekuriakaros aiboisiokec kanukec. 46#Apu 18:9. Emarani akwap kanuka eeelet loka aŋatar Babulon, epupunio adelonorike kotoma atekerin.”
