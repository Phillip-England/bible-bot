Yeremia 17

17
1 # Yob 19:24; Awa 3:3; 2 Kor 3:3. “Iwadikatai aronis naka Yuda kakwara naka asuat; kaitesu naka ekukwai igirakinitai amor nuka itauonokee, ido amumwar nuka itemwanakec. 2#2 Ika 16:4; 2 Siit 24:18; Isa 1:29. Kapak naiitunioto ikeeedwe itemwanakec ka Aserimikee, kama ejaas ikito luepesito, koukon da luiceleleete, 3#Yer 15:13. koronan da luka ais. Ainakini ikoniboro ka abarakon kere anatario, aiboisiokon nuiceleleete kanuka aronis, anatar iikorunokon kere. 4#Yer 16:13. Ŋo da ipaluni kama ejai airumetekon naabu kainakinite jo; kainakini jo aijaanakin lukasurupokon kakwap namam ijeni: naarai esinepu akanyunyura akim naieweenenei ikar kere.”
5 # Isa 30:1. Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe:
“Kikenoi ituŋanan yenemunokina ituŋanan,
Yenitaraun akuan akekan,
Eketau da enyoununei kama ejai EJAKAIT.
6 # Yob 20:17. Naarai eputokinos kekiot kamodiŋ,
Mam eanyuni neeponiata nukaiokak.
Iboikini aiboisio nuilariononos nuka ais,
Akwap naka abalaŋ naemameete ituŋa.
7 # Sab 2:12; Awa 16:20. “Isirerenitoi ituŋanan yenemunokina EJAKAIT,
Ido EJAKAIT ŋes akemun.
8 # Sab 1:3. Naarai eraun bala ekitoi loeraikinitai ariri naka akipi,
Loisiliarit aliasioke ocor,
Kwabo mam eyoni akoloŋ keca noi,
Konye eribiribete akekui;
Kwabo mam iyalononoi kokaru loka ekure,
Kwabo mam inyeini aira araito.”
9 # Age 6:5. Iŋalana etau aitelekarit iboro kere,
Ido epol akedek noi:
Iŋai bo epedori ajenun ŋes?
10 # 1 Sam 16:7; 1 Siit 28:9; Sab 7:9; 139:23; 62:12; Yer 11:20; 20:12; 32:19; Awa 17:3; Rom 8:27; 2:6; Apu 2:23. “Eoŋ EJAKAIT ererei etau, etemi da inereteny,
Ainakini ninitunanan kwape ejaatar ikeponesio,
Kwape ejaatar da araito nuka akeswamisio.”
11 # Luk 12:20. Kwape irukumia etakura abeei numam eurit,
Ikoni nen yenetukonokin abar, kwabo mere kotoma abeit;
Kokidin kapaarasiake etiakas kakes,
Kawasiake da eraun iban.
12Eicoloŋ lokaibuses loitopolooritai ameun kageunet,
Ŋes aiboisiwok nakalaunan,
13 # Sab 73:27; Yer 2:26; Isa 1:28; 65:13; Esk 16:63; 36:32; Dan 12:2; Luk 10:20. Wu EJAKAIT, amun naka Isirael,
Lukinyeinete ijo kere irumete rwaru ileic;
Lukenyounete kere kaneijai iwadikao toma alup,
Naarai enyeis EJAKAIT,
Atan naka akipi nukajarak.
14Kitanaleunoi, wu EJAKAIT, ido aŋaleuni;
Kitajarai, ido etajaruun;
Naarai ijo ekaituritono.
15 # Isa 5:19; Esk 12:22; Amo 5:18; 2 Pet 3:4. Koany, kabalas ebe,
“Ai ca ejai akirot naka EJAKAIT?
Kobu cabo!”
16Eoŋ da mam abu kesunyak ainyein araun ekecokon atupit jo; Mam abu kemiela apaaran naka epola;
Iieni ijo;
Nualomunete kaitukuka ajaenenas akonyenekon.
17Sirikirau loketasuro kamaka:
Ŋo neekerianarakan kapaaran naka aronis.
18Kirumata ileic lukareito,
Konye eoŋ kenyeis ileic airum;
Koburut kes,
Konye eoŋ kenyek aburun:
Kitodolik kes apaaran naka aronis;
Komudia kes kamudiar naka airucakin!
19Kabala EJAKAIT eoŋ kwanata ebe: “Kolot, kobwo korute loka idwe luka ituŋa, neelomununiata ikabakan luka Yuda neelomanariata da, korutei kere luka Yerusalem, 20#Sab 49:1; Yer 19:3; 22:2; Esk 2:7; 3:17; Kos 5:1; Mik 3:1. Kolimok kes ebe: Kiirasi yesi akirot naka EJAKAIT, yes ikabakan luka Yuda, ka Yuda kere, ka luibojete kere koYerusalem, luelomununete korutei kalu. 21#Mar 15:32; Nek 13:15-22. Ebala EJAKAIT kwanata ebe: Kocoito bonik, siribwokenenete lubwokon kapaaran naka asabato, karaida aitolomun korutei luka Yerusalem. 22#Any 20:8-10; 23:12; 31:13; Esk 20:12; Ikis 5:12-14. Ido siribwokenenete lubwokon aitolomun kotogoikus kapaaran naka asabato, sirikiswamata adioswam kere: konye kitalaut apaaran nakasabato, kwape kecorakinitor lukapapaakus. 23Konye mam kes apotu kiirasi, karaida aitoikin akiikec, konye kitininikata imurisiŋakec, kinyeis akiirar, kinyeis da aituutoorio.
24“Kosodi kanen, ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Arai idunyakinito akiiranar eoŋ, komam itolomununete lubwokon kere korutei luka ere kala kapaaran nakasabato, konye italaunete apaaran nakasabato, komam iswamaete adioswam kere kapaaran kaŋin, 25#Ikis 4:40. ŋes konyet elomununiata korutei luka ere kalo ikabakan ka ikapolok luiboikinos oicoloŋ loka Daudi, ibwanenito agalin ka ibalasin, kes ka ikapolokokec, ituŋa luka Yuda, ka luibojete da ko Yerusalem: ejaenena ere lo ikar kere. 26#Sek 7:7; Sab 107:22. Epote kesi koreria luka Yuda, kaiboisio da nuirimorito Yerusalem, kakwap naka Benyamin, kapuutona, koronan, ka Eŋalakimak da, eyaunete ainakineta nuieweio, ka asuban ka ainakineta nuka akiria, ka lukisupurununo, ido eyaunete asuban nuka aimal, togo loka EJAKAIT. 27#Aku 4:11; Amo 1:4; 2 Ika 25:9. Konye arai kiŋeroto akiirar eoŋ komam italaunete apaaran nakasabato, ibwokete da lubwokon ilomunete korutei luka Yerusalem kapaaran nakasabato; kasodi eoŋ ainokokin akim koruteike, epiari da itogoi lukapolou luka Yerusalem, komam ituduneori.”
