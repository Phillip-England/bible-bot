Sepania 2

2
1 # Yok 2:16. Kotukonokinos, eebo, kotukonokinos,
Ŋo ateker namam ileieikin;
2 # 2 Ika 23:26. Eroko elomuna ekisil,
Eroko etuboro apaaran bala agugu,
Eroko ebunokina yes anyunyura naededen naka EJAKAIT, Eroko apaaran naka anyunyura naka EJAKAIT ebunokina yes.
3 # Amo 5:6,15; Sab 76:9; Yok 2:14. Komoata EJAKAIT, yes kere luitasonos luka akwap, Luiswamaete akeicorakineta;
Komoata adolit, komoata da aitasono:
Motoko kitonopjo kapaaran naka anyunyura naka EJAKAIT,
4 # Yer 6:4; Isa 14:29-31; Yer 47:1-7; Esk 25:15-17; Yok 3:4-8. Naarai eialakinjo Gasa,
Eraun Asukelon da ameje:
Ireneaŋo ituŋa luka Asudod kidiŋ paaran,
Eutoŋo da Ekuron,
5 # Esk 25:16; Yos 13:3. Itukwi luibojete kariri naka aŋolol,
Ateker naka Ikeretin!
Kiluik akirot naka EJAKAIT jo, wu Kanaan,
Akwap naka Ipilisitian;
Kamudiari jo komam esali yeniboie kotoma.
6 # Isa 17:2. Eraun ariri naka aŋolol ironyia,
Ejaunos idukurun luka ikecokok,
Ka anukia da nuka isipanin.
7 # Isa 11:11; Kag 1:12; Mik 5:7; 4:10; Any 4:31; Luk 1:68; Sab 126:4; Yer 23:3; Esk 39:25. Eraun ariri naka aŋolol airumet naka aatoŋot naka ekek loka Yuda;
Itadakete isipaninikee kanen:
Eperete kuare otogoi luka Asukelon.
Naarai eanyanari kes EJAKAIT Ekecedeke,
Inyakuni kaijoreoŋokec,
8 # Yer 48:27; 49:1; Esk 25:8. “Apup eoŋ amorosit naka Moab,
Ka amoromor naka idwe luka Amon, Nuamoromorooto ikatuŋa,
Aitopoloor bonik aluikin eikorukec.”
9 # Isa 15:1; Amo 2:1; 1:13; Age 19:24. Kanuka nun ebala EJAKAIT loka ajoresjo,
Edeke loka Isirael ebe,
“Kwape ajaria eoŋ, abeit eraun Moab bala Sodom,
Ka idwe luka Amon bala Gomora,
Airumet naka ikukwa, ka apanya nuka abalaŋ,
Ka ameje naka ikar kere.
Luideunos luka ikatuŋa enaŋete kes,
Aatoŋot naka atekereka irumi kes.”
10Edumunete ŋun kanuka eiturikec,
Naarai amoromoroto ido kitopolooros bonik,
Asurokin ituŋa luka EJAKAIT loka ajoresjo.
11 # Age 49:10; 10:5; Sab 2:8; 22:27; Isa 2:2,3; 11:9,10; Mal 1:1; Yok 4:21; 1 Tim 2:8. Eraun EJAKAIT etasuro mama ejaas;
Naarai isiriebi idekesjo kere luka akwap eteŋei;
Emaete ŋes ituŋa, ŋinituŋanan kaiboisike,
Kes apujana kere nuka atekerin,
12 # Isa 18:1-7. Yes da Iyetjopian, kiaŋo kekileŋika.
13 # Isa 10:5-34; 14:24-27; Nak 1:1–3:19. Eŋori ŋesi akekan aluikin agolitomei,
Emudiari Asuria;
Ituluori ŋesi Ninebe ameie,
Koono da bala amodiŋ.
14 # Isa 13:21. Eperenenete isipanin kokidinike,
Itian kere luka atekerin;
Iboie erere ka eeoe kopirinoke;
Eruo etukuri kadirisan;
Ejaun ekuruk okek;
Naarai alaea aswam naka eres.
15 # Isa 47:8; Apu 18:7. Ŋol ŋes ere loibwona, loeboie komam epodo,
Loabala kotauke ebe, “Ajai eoŋ,
Emamei ece lokiputor eoŋ,”
Buu! Arau ca ma ameje,
Aibois neeperete itiaij!
Ŋin yenitori etubor isuari ŋes,
Iianiiani akanike.
