Aimario 32

32
1 # Mar 21:32; Yos 13:25; 2 Sam 24:5; Isa 16:8,9. Ajaas idwe luka Reuben ka idwe luka Gad ibaren luipu noi: keanyut akwap naka Yaser, ka akwap naka Gilead, ebe arai aibois naebus kanuka ibaren; 2Kopotu idwe luka Gad ka idwe luka Reuben kinerata kaMusa ka Eleasar esasedoti ka ikapolok luka ekodet atiar, 3“Atarot, ka Dibon, ka Yaser, ka Nimura, ka Kesibon, ka Eleale, ka Sebam, ka Nebo, ka Beon, 4#Mar 21:24,34. Akwap naetiji EJAKAIT kakonyen nuka ekodet loka Isirael, erai akwap naibusakinit ibaren, ejaas da ikonikejaanakinak ibaren.” 5Kotemasi atiar, “Arai kidumunit asianut kakonyenekon, koinakinai ikonikejaanakinak akwap na araun airumet; sirikiitodok sio Yorodan.”
6Kotema Musa idwe luka Gad ka idwe luka Reuben atiar, “Do iinaeanakus elosete oiie, kihoiete yesi kane? 7Kanukinyo itakalanyata itauon luka idwe luka Isirael kotoma alosit akwap naainaki kes EJAKAIT? 8#Mar 13:3,17-33; Ikis 1:22. Apotu lukapapakus kiswamata kwaŋin, naejukaria kes koKades-barunea airot akwap. 9#Mar 1:10; 13:24,31; Ikis 1:24. Naarai kedokasi aditot naka Esukol, koanyut da akwap, kitakalanyata itauon Iuka idwe luka Isirael kinyeis alomar akwap naainakinit kes EJAKAIT. 10#Mar 14:11; Ikis 1:34; Sab 95:11; Esk 20:15; Bur 3:8-19. Kinep anyunyura naka EJAKAIT kapaaran kaŋin, kiyapo ebe, 11#Ikis 1:35; Mar 14:24. ‘Abeit emamei kotoma otuŋa lualomunete koMisiri, ageun kaluka ikar akaisaarei ka luedeparito, loeanyuni akwap naabu keyapoik Aburaam, ka Isaak, ka Yakob; naarai mam katuparito eoŋ kekeeetau kere: 12#Ikis 1:36; Yos 14:8,9; Yob 4:7; Sab 37:29; Awa 11:31. Dimarai Kaleb okoku loka Yepune Ekenisit, ka Yosua okoku loka Nun: naarai atupasi EJAKAIT kekeeetau kere.’ 13#Mar 14:33; 26:64. Kinep anyunyura naka EJAKAIT aluikin Isirael, kisirim kes kamodiŋ ikar akaisaoŋon, aitodol neadaikinor nen aturi kere naabu kiswama aronis kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 14#Ikis 1:34. Ido koany, inyout yesi airum aibois naka lukapapakus, ailwek naka ikadiakanarak, aiyatakin bobo anyunyura naededen naka EJAKAIT mama ejai Isirael. 15#Ikis 30:17; Yos 22:16,18; 2 Siit 7:19; 15:2. Naarai arai kipaasi yesi kotoma atupite ŋes, itasalikini bobo ŋesi kes amodiŋ; ido imudiarete yesi ituŋa lu kere.”
16Kikunyut mama ejai ŋesi, kotemasi atiar, “Kibwaikini akosiituk auioi nee, kidukokini ikosidwe ludidik ireria: 17#Yos 4:12,13. Konye isio ikulepek kikapakini eut aiŋarenikin idwe luka Isirael, aitodol nekiyanaaria kes aiboisikec; ido iboikinos ikosidwe ludidik oreria luebwaunitai kanuka luibojete kakwap. 18#Yos 22:4. Mam kibonori oreriakosi, aitodol nairumiata idwe luka Isirael ninituŋanan airumeteke, 19#Yos 12:1; 13:8. Naarai mam sio kirumi kakes okekwa iuwai loka Yorodan; naarai akosiirumet ejai okekwa lo loka Yorodan ari nakokide.” 20#Ikis 3:18; Yos 1:14; 4:12,13. Kotema Musa kes atiar, “Arai kiswamata yesi akirot nin; arai kikapakinos yesi alosit oiie kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, 21Kosodi nolitunanan loekapakina kayesi aidok Yorodan kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, aitodol nairenearia lukasurupoke kakonyeneke, 22#Ikis 3:20; Yos 11:23; 18:1; 22:4,9; Ikis 3:12; Yos 1:15; 13:8,32. Kipikoroi akwap kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: kosodete konyet aboŋun, komam nuigwaunito kama ejai EJAKAIT, kama ejai Isirael da; eraun akwap na airumetekus kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 23#Age 4:7; 44:16; Sab 140:11; Awa 13:21; Isa 3:11; 59:12; Rom 2:9; 1 Kor 4:5. Konye arai mam iswamaete kwaŋin, koany idiakasi kama ejai EJAKAIT: abeit itodun akusidiakaret. 24#Mar 32:16,34. Kodukokis ikusidwe ludidik ireria, kobwaikis akusimerekekin aujoi; kiswamata da ŋun nualomutu kaitukekus.” 25Kinerata idwe luka Gad ka idwe luka Reuben kaMusa atiar, “Iswamaete ikonikeiaanakinak kwape icorakinia ekajakait. 26#Yos 1:14. Ikosidwe ludidik, ka akosiŋor, ka ikosisipanin ka ikosibaren, ejaunos toma oreria luka Gilead: 27#Yos 4:12. Konye edokete ikonikejaanakinak, nolitunanan loekapakina ejie, kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, ejie, kwape ebalar ekaiakait.”
28 # Yos 1:12-15. Koinak Musa Eleasar esasedoti, ka Yosua okoku loka Nun, ka ikapolok luka ikalia luka lukapapaakee luka atekerin nuka idwe luka Isirael aidar akirokee. 29Kolimok Musa kes ebe, “Arai kedokasi idwe luka Gad ka idwe luka Reuben Yorodan nepepe kayes, nolitunanan loekapakina ejie kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kosodi akwap aipikorio kakonyenekus; iinakinete do kes akwap naka Gilead araun airumet: 30Konye arai mam edokete nepepe kayes kokapakina, irumete nepepe kayes kotoma akwap naka Kanaan.” 31Kobonokis idwe luka Gad ka idwe luka Reuben ebe, “Kwape alimokinia EJAKAIT ikonikeiaanakinak, kiswamai isio kwaŋin, 32Kidoki kakapakina kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT alomar akwap naka Kanaan, kosalak airumetekosi okekwa loka Yorodan.”
33 # Ikis 3:12; Yos 12:6; 13:8; 22:4; Mar 21:24; Ikis 2:30-35; 3:1-8; Sab 135:10,11; 136:18. Koinak Musa kes, idwe luka Gad ka idwe luka Reuben, ka atutubet naka ateker naka Manase okoku loka Yosep, ajakanut naka Sikon ekabaka loka Imorain, ka ajakanut naka Og ekabaka loka Basan, akwap kwape ajaatar ireriake ka iikorunokee, kes ireria luka akwap lualukunito. 34#Mar 33:45; Ikis 2:36. Kodukoto idwe luka Gad Dibon, ka Atarot, ka Aroer; 35Ka Aturot-sopan, ka Yaser, ka Yogubeka; 36Ka Bet-nimura, ka Bet-karan: ireria luabwaitai, ka auioi nuabwaitai nuka amerekekin. 37Kodukoto idwe luka Reuben Kesibon, ka Eleale, ka Kiriataim; 38#Isa 46:1; Mar 22:41; Any 23:13. Ka Nebo, ka Baal-meon, (kilocoi ikeeekiroria,) ka Sibima: komaikis ireria luadukete icekiroria. 39#Age 50:23; Mar 26:29; Yos 17:1. Koloto idwe luka Makir okoku loka Manase Gilead, kiruma ŋes, kodemasi ŋes Imorain luajaas toma. 40#Ikis 3:12; Yos 17:1. Koinak Musa Makir okoku loka Manase Gilead; kiboienenei da kanen. 41#Ikis 3:14; Yos 13:30; 1 Siit 2:21; Tub 10:4; 1 Ika 4:13. Kolot Yair okoku loka Manase kirum ireriakee, komaik kes Kabot-Yair, 42#2 Sam 18:18; Sab 49:11. Kolot Noba kirum Kenat, ka ireriake ludidik, komaik ŋes Noba ekekiror obe.
