Aimario 4

4
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron atiar, 2“Kimarasi odwe luka Kokat kotoma odwe luka Lebi, kwape ejaatar ikekiakec, kwape cjaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakee. 3#Age 41:46; Mar 3:40; 8:24; 1 Siit 23:3,24,27; Esr 3:8; Luk 3:23. Ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany, kere luekapakina aiiaanakin, aswam kotoma owema loka airiamunun, 4#Mar 4:15. Na ŋes aswam naka odwe luka Kokat kotoma owema loka airiamunun, koboro lukalaunak eut; 5#Any 26:31; 36:35; Leb 16:2; 2 Siit 3:14; Mat 27:51; Bur 9:3; Any 25:10,16. Naitobwosere idukurun, elomari Aaron ka odweke toma, elaeunete aigalisit naetiakit, erapakiniata ŋes asaduku naka aitejenunet. 6#Any 25:13. Erapakinete da emukule loka etakas #4:6 etakas ŋes etiaŋ. kujuke, ido eburiarete kere kegoe lobululu, irupakinete adaketake, 7#Any 25:23-30; Leb 24:6,8. Epesikinete egoe lobululu omesa loka imugatin luka aitodikin, idokokinete nen atuba ka akaalei ka atanya, ka aikopon nubukununet: ejaunete nen da imugatin ludue: 8Ido erapiarete kere kegoe lokarenan, eburiarete bobo kemukule loka etakas, irupakinete da adaketake. 9#Any 25:31-38. Elemunete egoe lobululu, aimukoria epir loka atala naka aica, ka atalaike, ka aridetake, ka aitudeŋetake, ka itidoike luka akinyet luetwasamaete kesi aiiaanakinia: 10Eburiarete ŋes ka itidoike kere kemukule loka etakas, idokokinete adaketeke. 11#Any 30:1. Epesikinete egoe lobululu otem loka esabu, iburiarete bobo kemukule loka etakas, irupakinete da adaketake: 12Ibwaikinete iboro kere luetwasamaete aiiaanakinia, luejaanakiniata kotoma aibois nakalaunan, toma ogoe lobululu, eburiarete da kere kemukule loka etakas, idokokinete da adaketeke, 13Emudarete ekurwon kotoma otem, epesikinete egoe lomuge: 14Idokokinete nen iboroke kere, lueiaanakiniata ŋes, ieuman luka akim, ka igoluna luka akiriŋ, ka amudeta, ka atanyia, iboro kere luka etem; epesikinete da eimukoret loka emukule loka etakas, irupakinete da adaketake. 15#Mar 7:9; 10:21; Ikis 31:9; 2 Sam 6:13; 1 Siit 15:2,15; 2 Sam 6:6,7; 1 Siit 13:9,10; Mar 3:31. Kedaut Aaron ka odweke arapakin aibois nakalauian ka iboro kere lukotoma aibois nakalaunan, naedolio aitobwot iidukurun; akaulo kanun, epote odwe luka Kokat aidak ŋes: konye mam kes etirorete iboro lukalaunak kinyeis atwakare. Iboro ŋul kes luedakete odwe luka Kokat luka ewema loka airiamunun.
16 # Any 25:6; 30:34; Sab 141:2; Mal 1:11; Apu 5:8; 8:3; Any 29:40; Mar 28:3; Any 30:23. Ido luidari Eleasar okoku loka Aaron esasedoti kes lu, akinyet naka atala, ka lukisupurununo, ka ainakinet nadue naka akiria, ka akinyet nakinyonyoanakina, idari da ewema lokalaunan ka lueja toma kere, aibois nakalaunan ka iboroke da.”
17Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron atiar, 18“Sirikitiakasi ateker naka ikekia luka Ikokatin kotoma oLebin: 19#Mar 4:4. Konye kiswamak kes kwana, tetere eiarere kinyeis atwakare, kikunyakis neejaas iboro lukalaunak noi: elomari Aaron ka odweke toma, kosodete aitodianakin kes ninitunanan akeswam ka ibore yenedaki: 20#Any 19:21; 1 Sam 6:19. Konye mam elomarete toma aanyun iboro lukalaunak, nepeeep da, kinyeis atwakare.”
21Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 22“Kimar odwe luka Gereson da, kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakee, kwape ejaatar ikekiakee; 23#1 Siit 23:3,24,27. Imari kes ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany; kere luekapakina aijaanakin, aswam. Kotoma owema loka airiamunun, 24Na ŋes aswam naka ikekia luka Igeresonin, naiiaanakiniata naedakiata da iboro: 25#Any 26:1-14; 38:9; 40:19; Mar 3:25,26. Edakete kesi aigalisio nuka ewema lokalaunan, ka ewema loka airiamunun, eimukoreteke, ka eimukoret loka emukule loka etakas loejai kujuke, ka aigalisit naka ekek loka ewema loka airiamunun; 26Ka luikaiai alaro, ka aigalisit naka ekek loka erute loka alaro, naidunyas kewema lokalaunan, naidunyas da ketem kolukunite, ka aunoikee, ka iboro kere da lueupatar kaiiaanakinikec, kere nuibusakinit aiswamakin kes, nupenun ijaanakinete. 27Kwape icorakinia Aaron ka odweke kwaŋin ijaanakiniata odwe luka Igeresonin, kotoma kaluedakete kere, kotoma kaluiiaanakinete da; itodikinete kes luedakete kere. 28Ŋin ŋes aswam naka ikekia luka odwe luka Igeresonin kotoma owema loka airiamunun: idarete kesi koanyanari Itamar okoku loka Aaron esasedoti.
29Ka odwe luka Merari da, imari ijo kes kwape ejaatar ikekiakee, kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakee; 30#Age 41:46; Mar 4:23; 8:24; 1 Siit 28:12,13. Imari kes ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany, ninitunanan yenekapakina aijaanakin, aswam kotoma owema loka airiamunun, 31#Mar 3:36,37; Any 26:15; 27:9-19. Lu kes iboro luidarete kesi, kwape eiaar aswamukee kotoma owema loka airiamunun; abaoi nuka ewema lokalaunan, ka akitoke nukirionokina, ka ipirinoke ka aigaŋetakec; 32#Any 25:9; 1 Siit 9:29; Esr 8:24-30. Ka ipirino luka alaro naelukunit, ka aigaŋetakec, ka ikeceloin, ka aunoikee, ka iborokec kere lueupatar, ka luijaanakiniata; ibilete ikiroria luka iboro kere luidarete kesi ka luedakete, 33Ŋin ŋes aswam naka ikekia luka odwe luka Merari, kwape eiaar aijaanakinikec kere kotoma owema loka airiamunun, koanyanari Itamar okoku loka Aaron esasedoti.”
34 # Mar 3:19,27; 4:2. Kimarata Musa ka Aaron ka ikapolok luka ekodet odwe luka Ikokatin kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, kwape ajaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec, 35#Mar 8:24-26; 1 Siit 23:24; 28:13; Luk 3:23; 1 Tim 3:6. Ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany, ninitunanan yenekapakina aijaanakin, aswam kotoma owema loka airiamunun. 36Ŋul da luaponi kimarai kakesi kwape ajaatar ikekiakec emwaasi ilukumin iarei ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisakany. 37Ŋul kes luaponi kimarai luka ikekia luka Ikokatin, kere luapotu kiiaanakis kotoma owema loka airiamunun, luemarete Musa ka Aaron kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT kama ka Musa.
38Ka nul da luaponi kimarai luka odwe luka Gereson, kwape ajaatar ikekiakee, kwape ajaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec, 39Ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany, ninitunanan yenekapakina aijaanakin, aswam kotoma owema loka airiamunun. 40Ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi, kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, kwape ajaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec, emwaasi ilukumin iarei ka akwat akanykape ka akaisauni. 41Ŋul kes luaponi kimarai luka ikekia luka odwe luka Gereson, kere luapotu kijaanakis kotoma owema loka airiamunun, luemarete Musa ka Aaron kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT.
42Ka ŋul da luaponi kimarai luka ikekia luka odwe luka Merari, kwape ajaatar ikekiakee, kwape ajaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec. 43#Mar 4:35-40; Ikis 33:25; 1 Kor 12:8-12; 2 Kor 12:9. Ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany, ninitunanan yenekapakina aijaanakin, aswam kotoma owema loka airiamunun. 44Ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, emwaasi ilukumin iuni ka akwat aarei. 45Ŋul kes luaponi kimarai luka ikekia luka odwe luka Merari, luemarete Musa ka Aaron kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT, kama ka Musa.
46Ŋul kere luaponi kimarai luka Ilebin, luemarete Musa ka Aaron ka ikapolok luka Isirael kwape ajaatar ikekiakec, kwape ajaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec. 47#Rom 12:6-8. Ageun kaluka ikar akaisauni ka luedeparito aitodol luka ikar akaisakany, ŋinituŋanan yenekapakina aswam naka aijaanakin, ka aswam naka aidak iboro kotoma owema loka airiamunun, 48Ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi, emwaasi ilukumin ikanykauni ka akwat akany ka akaisakanykauni. 49#1 Kor 12:4-28. Kwape ajaar aicorakinet naka EJAKAIT aponi kimarai kes kama ka Musa, ŋinituŋanan kwape ajaar aijaanakinike, ido kwape ajaar aidakake: kwaŋin aponi kimarai, kwape kicorakinia EJAKAIT Musa.
