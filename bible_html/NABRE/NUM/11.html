Aimario 11

11
1 # Any 15:23,24; 16:2,3,7,9; Ikis 9:22; Aku 3:39; Leb 10:2; 2 Ika 1:12. Kirurumata ituŋa, kineras nukaronok kaki nuka EJAKAIT: kepupu EJAKAIT, kinep anyunyurake; konok akim naka EJAKAIT kamakee, konyama da atutubeta nukokina nuka iidukurun. 2#Yak 5:16. Komonyata ituŋa mama ejai Musa; kilip Musa EJAKAIT, kodunei do akim. 3Komaikinai ekiror loka aibois ŋin Tabera: #11:3 Tabera Apolouke Ainok. naarai abu akim naka EJAKAIT konok kamakee.
4 # Any 12:38; 1 Kor 10:6. Kikibelik ikatumunak luenyalakina tomakee: komonyata bobo idwe luka Isirael ebe, “Iŋai kitanyami sio akiriŋ? 5#Any 16:3; Pil 3:19; Mat 6:24-34; Rom 8:7. Kiitu agaria nukinyamenenei atai kotoma Misiri; akolil, ka amonyon, ka edia, ka itugulun, ka lukinyalet. 6Konye kwana iriono ekositau; emamei idiobore da: emamei idiobore yenikitei dimarai emanu lo.”
7 # Any 16:31; Age 2:12. Kosubite emanu bala eeokat loka ejad#11:7 Ejad ŋes araito naedit. ka aketakanu da koputosi bala atakanu naka ebedola.#11:7 Ebedola eputos bala emina. 8Kiledokinos ituŋa atukonokin ŋes, kiriata kakanasia, korukoto kokisun, kipooto kamoti, kilunulunata imugatin: kosubite bala enyamete kesi edia loinyalakinitai akinyet. 9#Any 16:13-15. Nakejaununotor akopyo oidukurun kuare, koiaunun emanu da.
10Kopup Musa ituŋa emonyete kokaliakee kere, ŋinituŋanan kokek loka ewemake: kinep noi anyunyura naka EJAKAIT; kokadak Musa. 11#Any 17:4; Ikis 1:12; Yer 15:10,18; Yer 20:7-18; Mal 3:14; 2 Kor 11:28. Kotema Musa EJAKAIT atiar, “Kanukinyo iswamakinia ijo ekonibulesit erono? Kanukinyo da mam eoŋ adumunitor asianut kakonyenekon, tape ijo kisibwoki eoŋ alaŋir naka ituŋa kalu kere? 12#Isa 40:11; Esk 34:23; Yok 10:11; Isa 49:23; 1 Ses 2:7; Age 22:16; 26:3; Any 13:5. Eoŋ kapotuun kereka ituŋa lu kere? ido eoŋ kedouni kes? Tape ijo kitemar eoŋ ebe, ‘Kodakaa kes katorobukon kwape edakia ekasaan ikoku yenenaki, aitodol akwap naibu kisiyapoik lukapapaakee.’ 13#2 Ika 4:43; Mat 15:33; Mk 8:4. Ai bo alemunia eoŋ akirhj naainakini ituŋa lu kere? naarai emonyete mamaka ebe, ‘Koinakinai sic akiriŋ, kanyama.’ 14#Any 18:18. Mam eoŋ apedori bon aibwok ituŋa lu kere, naarai kalanirikinito noi. 15#1 Ika 19:4; Yob 6:8-10; Yok 4:3; Sep 3:15. Arai kiswamanakini ijo eoŋ kwaŋin, kenainair koar katipet, arai adumunit asianut kakonyenekon; kenyek aanyun iean luetia nen.”
16 # Any 24:1,9. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, “Kotukokinai ituŋa akaisakanykaarei komakio luka Isirae!, luijeni ijo ebe eraas imakio luka ituŋa, luepolokinito kes; koyau kes owema loka airiamunun, tetere ebwoete kaŋina nepepe kaio. 17#Age 11:5; Any 19:20; 1 Sam 10:6; 2 Ika 2:5; Yob 32:8; 38:36; Yoe 2:28. Eiesuni eoŋ da einer kaio kanen; alemari ece komoyo loijaatar ijo, kasodi aibwaikin mamakee; ibwokete nepepe kajo alaŋir naka ituŋa, tetere mam jo bon ibwoki. 18#Any 19:10; 16:7; Asw 7:39. Kolimok ituŋa ebe, ‘Kitalaunos eroko moi edoluno, inyamete da akiriŋ; naarai imonyata kaki nuka EJAKAIT ebe, “Iŋai kitanyami sio akiriŋ? naarai aiok kole isio aiboi koMisiri”: kanuka nun kiinaki EJAKAIT akiriŋ, inyamete. 19Mere adiopepaaran bon inyamiata, karaida apaarasia aarei karaida apaarasia akany, karaida apaarasia akaisaarei; 20Konye elap kere, aitodol naelomunia kokumesikus, nekititirikinio cut: naarai ineroto EJAKAIT loejai mamakus, komonyete kakonyeneke ebe, “Kanukinyo kinyounio koMisiri?”’” 21Kotema Musa atiar, “Ituŋa luaiai eoŋ mamakee imwaasi ikiliok ilukumin akwat akanykape; ido itema ijo atiar, ‘Ainakini kes akiriŋ, konyama adaun elap kere?’ 22#2 Ika 7:2; Mat 15:33; Mk 6:37. Earakinio isipanin luka amerekekin ka luka aituk aisimonikinia kes? arai bo etukonokinio agaria kere nuka aŋolol aisimonikinia kes?” 23#Isa 50:2; Esk 12:25; 24:14. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, “Euriana kereka akan naka EJAKAIT? ianyuni do arai itodolikinio akakiro mama ijai arai bo mam.”
24Koloma Musa, kolimok ituŋa akiro nuka EJAKAIT: kotukok imakio luka ituŋa akaisakanykaarei, kisibwo kes aisirimor Ewema. 25#Any 34:5; 40:38; Mar 12:5; 2 Ika 2:15; 1 Sam 10:5,6; Yoe 2:29; Asw 2:17,18; 1 Kor 14:1. Kiiesun EJAKAIT kotoma odou, kiner kaŋes, kolema da ece komoyo loaiaatar ŋesi, kibwaik omakio akaisakanykaarei: kosodi kiboikin emoyo mamakec, kodwarata, kitananata nepenen.
26 # 1 Sam 20:26; Yer 36:5. Konye asalito ituŋa iarei oidukurun, ekiror loka ediopet ŋes Eludad, ece da ŋes Medad: kiboikin emoyo mamakee; ido araas kesi luka nul kaluewadikatai, konye mam apotu koloto owema: kodwarata koidukurun. 27Koker ekatumunan, kolimok Musa ebe, “Edwarete Eludad ka Medad koidukurun.” 28#Mk 9:38; Yok 3:26. Koboŋok Yosua okoku loka Nun, ekeiaanakinan loka Musa ameun koiditeuke ebe, “Ejakaitika Musa, kitikok kes.” 29#Asw 26:29; 1 Kor 14:5. Kotema Musa ŋes atiar, “Kikokotik ijo kanukaŋ? Tiejok ituŋa kere luka EJAKAIT koraunos inabin, tikibwaiki da EJAKAIT emoyoke mamakee!” 30Koboŋosi Musa ka imakio luka Isirael oidukurun.
31 # Sab 78:26. Kokut ekwam loebuni kama ejai EJAKAIT, koyanau alurueik kaŋolol, kitodo kes oidukurun, kwaka alosit naka adiopepaaran epuai, ka alosit naka adiopepaaran epuai da, airimor iidukurun, ikubitin #11:31 ekubit itemi bala 1’ 6”. iarei kuiu kakwap. 32#Any 16:36; Esk 45:11. Konyout ituŋa apaaran ŋin kere, ka akuare da kere, ka apaaran kere naatupakini, kotukonokis alurueik: nin yenatukokinit nuikidioko atukokinit ikomerin#11:32 ekomer itemi alaŋir. itomon: koloikis airimor iidukurun. 33#Sab 78:30; 106:14. Aroko akiriŋ ejai okelakee, aroko da enyakao, kinep anyunyura naka EJAKAIT aluikin ituŋa, kodany EJAKAIT ituŋa katular naepol noi, 34#Ikis 9:22. Komaikinai aibois ŋin ekiror Kiburot-kataaba:#11:34 Kiburot-kataaba: Apolouke Atesina nuka eibel naarai nen aponi kidaikinai ituŋa luikibelikinito.
35 # Mar 33:17; Ikis 1:1. Kolototo ituŋa anyoun koKiburot-kataaba kodoloto Kaserot; kiboikinos Kaserot.
