Aimario 15

15
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Leb 23:10. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Naidolioto akwap naiboikinos, nakainakini yes, 3#Any 29:18,25,41; Leb 1:2,3; 7:16; 23:8,12; Ikis 16:10; Age 8:21; Any 29:18; Leb 1:9; Esk 20:41; 2 Kor 2:15; Pes 5:2; Pil 4:18. Kosodete ainakin EJAKAIT ainakinet kakim, ainakinet naicweio, arai asubanit, aitodolio aiyaposit, arai ainakinet naka akote, arai kopucinokus luicorakinitai, aisuŋuuno EJAKAIT asakat naeur saa, kaituk arai kamerekekin: 4#Leb 2:1; 6:14; Isa 66:20; Any 29:40; Leb 14:10. Loeinakini akeinakinet einakini EJAKAIT ainakinet naka akiria naka idiopet kotomon yenika epa #15:4 epa itemi alaŋir. loka akiria nuidicie nuinyalakinitai ka idiopet koonon yenika ekin #15:4 ekin itemi lueliyak. loka akinyet: 5#Mar 28:7. Ka ebino kanuka ainakinet namatat, idiopet koonon yenika ekin, itemonokinete nepepe kainakinet naicweio arai kanuka asubanit kanuka ŋinimerekek yenididi. 6Arai kanuka emerekek, itemonokini kanuka ainakinet naka akiria iarei kotomon luka epa loka akiria nuidicic nuinyalakinitai idiopet kouni yenika ekin loka akinyet: 7Kanuka ainakinet namatat da iinakini idiopet kouni yenika ekin loka ebino, loka asakat naeur saa mama ejai EJAKAIT. 8#Leb 7:11. Naitemokinio emoŋ lotobait kanuka ainakinet naicweio, arai kanuka asubanit, aitodolio aiyaposit, arai kanuka ainakineta nuka ainapakin mama ejai EJAKAIT: 9Ŋes einakinia ainakinet naka akiria nepepe kemon lotobait naka iuni kotomon luka epa loka akiria nuidicie nuinyalakinitai katutubet naka ekin loka akinyet. 10Iinakini kanuka ainakinet namatat atutubet naka ekin loka ebino, kanuka ainakinet naeinakinere kakim, naka asakat naeur saa mama ejai EJAKAIT.
11Iswamaenenoo kwaŋin kanuka nolimon lotobait, ido kanuka nolimerekek, kanuka ninimcrekek yenikiliokit, arai kanuka ŋinikalol da. 12Kwape ejaar etiai loitemokinete, iswamakinete kwaŋin ŋinidiopet, kwape ejaar etiaiakee. 13Iurian kere iswamaete akiro nun kopone kanol, naeinakiniata ainakinet naeinakinere kakim, naka asakat naeur saa mama ejai EJAKAIT. 14Arai kiboikin imoit nepepe kayes, arai ice kere yenejaun kidinikus katuriokus kere, kosodi ainakin ainakinet naeinakinere kakim, naka asakat naeur saa mama ejai EJAKAIT; kwape iswamaata yesi, iswamai ŋesi da kwaŋin, 15#Leb 24:22; Mar 9:14; Gal 3:28; Kol 3:11. Kanuka atukot, ejaun ekisil ediopet kanukus, ido kanuka imoit yeniboienenei nepepe kayes, ekisil lomam edaun katuriokus kere: kwape iiaatar yesi, ejaun kwaŋin imoit kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, 16#Leb 24:22. Ejaun ekisil ediopet ka aicorakinet adiopet kanukus, ido kanuka imoit yeniboienenei kayes.”
17Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 18“Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Naidolioto akwap naketolomari yes, 19#Yos 5:11,12. Ejaun kwana, ebe, nainyamiata atap naka akwap, iinakinete ainakinet nadakakina mama ejai EJAKAIT. 20#Any 23:19; Ikis 26:2,10; Nek 10:37; Awa 3:9; Leb 2:14; 23:10. Iinanakinete aluŋuret naka agweikus nainumanariata yesi kanuka ainakinet nadakakina: kwape idakuniata ainakinet nadakakina naka epejal, idakunete nin da kwaŋin, 21linanakinete EJAKAIT aluŋuret naka agweikus nainumanariata araun ainakinet nadakakina katuriokus kere.
22 # Leb 4:2; 1 Yok 2:1. Ido naipaariata, komam iricito aicorakineta nun kere, nualimoki EJAKAIT Musa. 23Ŋun kere nukicorak yes EJAKAIT kama ka Musa, ameun kapaaran naesiaunia EJAKAIT aicorakin, anatar aturiokus kere; 24#Leb 4:13,23; Mar 28:15; Esr 6:17; 8:35. Ejaun kwana, arai kibueasi, komam ekodet ejeŋi, einakini ekodet kere emoŋ lotobait kanuka ainakinet naicweio, naka asakat naeur saa mama ejai EJAKAIT, nepepe kainakineteke naka akiria, ka ainakineteke namatat, kwape ejaar ekisil, ka ekoroi ediopet kanuka ainakinet naka adiakaret. 25#Leb 4:20; Dan 9:24; Rom 5:11; Bur 2:17; 10:10-12; 1 Yok 1:7; 2:1,2. Eluei esasedoti ekodet kere loka idwe luka Isirael, isionio da kes; naarai arai aibucaret, ayaut da ainakinetekee, ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT, ka ainakinetekee naka adiakaret kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kanuka aibucaretekec: 26Isionio ekodet kere loka idwe luka Isirael, ka imoit da yeniboienenei kamakee; naarai arai aibucaret naka ituŋa kere komam ejenete.
27 # Leb 4:27-31; Sab 19:13; Luk 12:48. Ido arai kediaka idiopetunanan komam ejeŋi, einakini akinei naka ekaru ediopet araun ainakinet naka adiakaret. 28#Leb 4:35. Eluei esasedoti aijar naibuearit, naediakaria komam ejeŋi, kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, alueio ŋes; ido isionio ŋes. 29Ŋaunos kekisil ediopet kanuka nin yeniswamai ibore komam ejeŋi, nin yeneurikinitai odwe luka Isirael, ido ka imoit yeniboienenei kamakee. 30#Ikis 17:12; Sab 19:13; Rom 10:16; Bur 10:26; 1 Pet 2:10. Konye aijar naepeleikin aswam ibore, arai iuriat arai imoit, einyanakini nin EJAKAIT; egelario ituŋanan nin kotuŋake. 31#Leb 26:15,43; 2 Sam 12:9; Awa 13:13; Isa 30:12; Bur 10:28; Esk 18:20. Naarai amwaŋ akirot naka EJAKAIT, abil da aicorakineteke; egelario ituŋanan ŋin cut, ejaunos numam adoliteke mamake.”
32 # Any 35:2,3. Naajaatar idwe luka Isirael amodiŋ apotu kodumut etuŋanan erarai akito kapaaran nakasabato, 33Koyaut luadumunete ŋes erarai akito mama ejai Musa ka Aaron ido mama ejai ekodet kere. 34#Leb 24:12. Koenai, naarai mam ber alimoritai nuiswamakinio ŋes. 35#Any 31:14,15; Gal 3:5; 1 Ika 21:13; Asw 7:58. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, “Abeit eario etuŋanan: ibiribiri ekodet kere ŋes kamor kokina kiidukurun.” 36Koyau ekodet kere ŋes kina kiidukurun, kibiribiroto ŋes kamor, kotwana; kwape kicorakinia EJAKAIT Musa.
37Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 38#Ikis 22:12; Mat 9:20; 23:5; Luk 8:43,44. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael, kicorak kes aiswamakin isilo atokito nuka inapenekee, anatar aturiokee kere, kitolomikis eusi lobululu osilo luka niuitokiroit: 39#Ikis 29:19; Yob 31:7; Yer 9:14; Esk 6:9; Sab 73:27; 106:39; Kos 2:2; Yak 4:4. Eraun kamakus esilot, tetere iteete ŋes, kiitununoto aicorakineta kere nuka EJAKAIT, kiswamata kes; komam ilosete koipud loka etaukus ka akonyenekus loetapitor yes aelor: 40#Leb 11:44,45; Mat 5:48; Rom 12:1; Kol 1:22; 1 Ses 5:8; 1 Pet 1:15. Tetere iitunete iswamaete da akaieorakineta kere, koraunos lukalaunak kama ejai Edekekus. 41Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke, loketolomuni yes kakwap nako Misiri, araun Edekekus: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.”
