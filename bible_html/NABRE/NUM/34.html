Aimario 34

34
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Age 17:8; Ikis 1:7; Sab 78:55; 105:11. “Kicorak idwe luka Isirael, ebe, Kidoloto akwap naka Kanaan, (nin ŋes akwap nakibei yes araun airumet, akwap nako Kanaan kwape ejaatar iikorunoke.) 3#Yos 15:1; Esk 47:13; Age 14:3; Yos 3:16. Ewaikus loka ari naka eŋalakimak enyouni kamodiŋ naka Sin kiririkina Edom, eikorukus da loka ari naka eŋalakimak enyouni kosane loka Aŋolol naka Abalaŋ kobei kide: 4#Mar 32:8. Ekudoori ekusikor ari naka eŋalakimak naka acinat naka Akurabim, kobei Sin: kodoli ari naka eŋalakimak naka Kades-barunea; kolosi Kasaradar, kobei Asumon: 5#Age 15:18; 1 Ika 8:65; Isa 27:12. Ekudoori bobo eikor koAsumon kiton oeor yenika Misiri, kodoli aŋolol,
6Eikorukus da loka ari nakoto ŋes Aŋolol Nakapolon karirike: eraun ŋol eikorukus loka ari nakoto.
7Lo ŋes eikorukus loka ari naka agolitomei: igirunete anyoun kAŋolol Nakapolon kiton Moru Kor: 8#Mar 13:21; Yos 13:5,6; 2 Sam 8:9; 2 Ika 14:25; 23:33; Isa 10:9; Yer 39:5; Esk 47:15-20. Anyoun koMoru Kor igirunete kiton ncelomaria Kamat; kodoli eikor Sedad; 9#Esk 47:17. Kolosi eikor kiton Sipiron, kodoli Kasar-enan: nol ŋes eikorukus loka ari naka agolitomei.
10Igirunete eikorukus loka ari nakokide anyoun koKasar-enan kiton Sepam: 11#2 Ika 23:33; Yer 39:5; Ikis 3:17; Yos 11:2; 19:35. Ŋesar eikor anyoun koSepam kiton Ribula, kari nakokide naka Ain; iiesar eikor edoli ariri naka aŋolol naka Cineret kobei kide: 12Ŋesar eikor kiton Yorodan, kodoli Aŋolol naka Abalaŋ: eraun ŋin akusikwap kwape ejaatar ikeikoruno luelukunito.”
13 # Yos 14:1-5. Kicorak Musa idwe luka Isirael ebe, “Ŋin ŋes akwap nairumete kailamilam eroet, naeeoraki EJAKAIT ainakin atekerin akanykaoŋon, ka atutubet naka ateker: #Mar 26:52-56; Yos 14:1,2. 14#Mar 32:33; Yos 14:2,3. Naarai adumu ateker naka idwe luka Reuben kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakee, ka ateker naka idwe luka Gad kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec, adumu da atutubet naka ateker naka Manase airumetekee: 15Adumut atekerin aarei ka atutubet naka ateker airumetekee juwai naka Yorodan koYeriko kari nakokide, neelomunia akoloŋ.”
16Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 17#Any 6:23-25. “Lu kes ikiroria luka iruna luekorokoroetc akwap kanukus araun airumet: Eleasar esasedoti, ka Yosua okoku loka Nun, 18Ilemunete yesi ekapolon ediopet loka ninitcker akorokor akwap araun airumet. 19#Age 29:35; 49:8-10; Ikis 33:7; Sab 60:7; 108:8; Mar 13:6,30; Ikis 1:36. Lu kes ikiroria luka ituŋa: loka ateker naka Yuda, Kaleb okoku loka Ycpune. 20#Age 29:33; 49:5; Esk 48:24. Loka ateker naka idwe luka Simeon, Semuel okoku loka Amikud, 21#Age 35:18; 49:27; Ikis 33:12; Sab 68:27; 80:2. Loka ateker naka Benyamin, Elidad okoku loka Cisilon. 22Loka idwe luka Dan ekapolon, Buki okoku loka Yoguli. 23#Age 48:8-22; 49:22; Ikis 33:13; Sab 80:1. Loka idwe luka Yosep; loka ateker naka idwe luka Manase ekapolon, Kaniel okoku loka Epod: 24Loka ateker naka idwe luka Epuraim ekapolon, Kemuel okoku loka Sipitan. 25Loka ateker naka idwe luka Sebulun ekapolon, Elisapan okoku loka Paranac, 26Loka ateker naka idwe luka Isakar ekapolon, Palutiel okoku loka Asan. 27Loka ateker naka idwe luka Aser ekapolon, Akikud okoku loka Selomi. 28Loka ateker naka idwe luka Naputali ekapolon, Pedakel okoku loka Amikud. 29#Ikis 32:8; Asw 17:26. Ŋul kes lueeorakini EJAKAIT akorokor airumet kanuka idwe luka Isirael kakwap naka Kanaan.”
