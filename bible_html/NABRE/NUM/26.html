Aimario 26

26
1 # Mar 1:1-46. Kosodi akaulo katular kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Eleasar okoku loka Aaron esasedoti, atiar, 2#Any 30:12; 38:25; Mar 1:2. “Kimar etiai loka ekodet kere loka idwe luka Isirael, ageun kaluka ikar akaisaarei ka luedeparito, kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakee, kere luepedorete alosit ojie koIsirael.” 3#Ikis 4:46-49; 34:1. Kinerata Musa ka Eleasar esasedoti kakes kapuuton naka Moab kariri naka Yorodan ko Yeriko, ebe, 4“Kimarata etiai loka ituŋa, ageun kaluka ikar akaisaarei ka luedeparito”; kwape kieorakinia EJAKAIT Musa ka idwe luka Isirael luanyounete kakwap nako Misiri.
5 # Age 29:32; 46:8; 1 Siit 5:1; Apu 7:5. Reuben, ekiyai loka Isirael: odwe luka Reuben: Kanok, ekek loka Ikanokin: Palu, ekek loka Ipaluin: 6Kesuron, ekek loka Ikesuronin: Karami, ekek loka Ikaramin, 7Ŋul kes ikekia luka Ireubenin: ŋul Iuaponi kimarai kakesi emwaasi ilukumin akaisaoŋon ka luuni, ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisauni. 8Odwe luka Palu; Eliab. 9#Mar 16:1,2; 27:3; Ikis 11:6; Isa 65:16; Sab 106:17; Yud 1:11. Odwe luka Eliab; Nemuel, ka Datan, ka Abiram. Ŋupenul kes Datan ka Abiram, luanyaraunio kokodety luetepegete Musa ka Aaron nepepe ka Kora, naetepegeata EJAKAIT: 10#Mar 16:38; 1 Sam 2:34; Yob 31:3; Sab 145:20; Yer 29:22; 1 Kor 10:6; 2 Pet 2:6; Yud 1:7. Koona akwap akeituk, kiliko kes nepepe ka Kora, naketwania erioŋet ŋol; kapak naanyamia akim ituŋa akwat aarei ka akaisakany, koraut aanyunet. 11#Any 6:24; 1 Siit 6:22; Esk 18:20. Konye mam odwe luka Kora anotu kotwaka.
12 # Age 46:10; Any 6:15; 1 Siit 4:24. Odwe luka Simeon kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: Nemuel, ekek loka Incmuelin; Yamin, ekek loka Iyaminin; Yakin, ekek loka Iyakinin: 13#Age 46:10. Sera, ekek loka Iserakin: Saul, ekek loka Isaulin. 14Ŋul kes ikekia luka Isimeonin, ilukumin akaisaarei ka luarei, ka akwat aarei.
15Odwe luka Gad kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: Sepon, ekek loka Iseponin: Kagi, ekek loka Ikagin; Suni, ekek loka Isunin: 16Osuni, ekek loka Ikosunin: Eri, ekek loka Ierin: 17#Age 46:16. Arod, ekek loka Irodain: Areli, ekek loka Irelain: 18Ŋul kes ikekia luka odwe luka Gad kwape ajaatar ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi, ilukumin akaisaoŋon ka akwat akany.
19 # Age 38:2; 46:12; 1 Siit 2:3. Odwe luka Yuda, Er ka Onan: kotwakikis Er ka Onan akwap naka Kanaan, 20#Age 49:8; Apu 7:5. Odwe luka Yuda kwape ajaatar ikekiakee; Sela, ekek loka Iselanin: Peres, ekek loka Iperesin: Sera, ekek loka Iserakin. 21Odwe luka Peres, Kesuron, ekek loka Ikesuronin: Kamul, ekek loka Ikamulin. 22Ŋul kes ikekia luka Yuda kwape ajaatar nul luaponi kimarai kakesi, ilukumin akaisakanykaarei ka lukanykape ka akwat akany.
23 # Age 46:13; 49:14. Odwe luka Isakar kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: Tola, ekek loka Itolain: Puba, ekek loka Ipubain: 24Yasub, ekek loka Iyasubin: Simuron, ekek loka Isimuronin. 25Ŋul kes ikekia luka Isakar kwape ajaatar luaponi kimarai kakesi, ilukumin akaisakanykape ka luonon ka akwat auni.
26 # Age 46:14; 49:13. Odwe luka Sebulun kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: Sered, ekek loka Iseredin; Elon, ekek loka Ielonin: Yaleel, ekek loka Iyaleelin. 27Ŋul kes ikekia luka Isebulunin kwape ajaatar nul luaponi kimarai kakesi, ilukumin akaisakanykape ka akwat akany.
28 # Age 41:51; 46:20; 48:3,13-20. Odwe luka Yosep kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: Manase ka Epuraim, 29#Mar 32:39; 36:1. Odwe luka Manase: Makir, ekek loka Imakirin: kouru Makir Gilead: Gilead, ekek loka Igileadin. 30#Yos 17:2. Lu kes odwe luka Gilead: Ieser, ekek loka Ieserin; Kelek, ekek loka Ikelekin: 31Ka Asuriel, ekek loka Isurielain: ka Sekem, ekek loka Isekemin: 32Ka Semida, ekek loka Isemidain; ka Keper, ekek loka Ikeperin, 33#Mar 27:1; 36:11; Yos 17:3. Ka Selopekad okoku loka Keper amameete idwe lusap, dimarai apesur: ikiroria luka apesur nuka Sclopekad kes Mala, ka Noa, ka Kogila, ka Milika, ka Tirusa. 34Ŋul kes ikekia luka Manase: ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi emwaasi ilukumin akaisakany ka luarei ka akwat akanykaarei.
35 # 1 Siit 7:20. Lu kes odwe luka Epuraim kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: Sutela, ekek loka Isutelakin: Beker, ekek loka Ibekerin: Takan, ekek loka Itakanin, 36Lu kes odwe luka Sutela: Eran, ekek loka Ieranin. 37#Ikis 33:13-17. Ŋul kes ikekia luka odwe luka Epuraim kwape ajaatar nul luaponi kimarai kakesi, ilukumin akaisauni ka luarei ka akwat akany, Ŋul kes odwe luka Yosep kwape ajaatar ikekiakec.
38 # Age 46:21; 1 Siit 7:6; 8:1. Odwe luka Benyamin kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: Bela, ekek loka Ibelain; Asubel, ekek loka Isubelain: Akiram, ekek loka Ikiramain: 39#Age 46:21; 1 Siit 7:12. Sepupam, ekek loka Isupamin: Kupam, ekek loka Ikupamin, 40#1 Siit 8:3. Odwe luka Bela kes Arid ka Naaman, Arid ekek loka Iridain; Naaman, ekek loka Inaamanin, 41Ŋul kes odwe luka Benyamin kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: nul luaponi kimaraikakesi emwaasi ilukumin akaisaoŋon ka lukany ka akwat akanykape.
42 # Age 46:23; 49:16; Mar 1:38,39; Ikis 33:22. Lu kes odwe luka Dan kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: Sukam, ekek loka Isukamin. Ŋul kes ikekia luka Dan kwape ajaatar ikekiakec. 43Ikekia kere luka Isukamin, kwape ajaatar nul luaponi kimarai kakesi emwaasi ilukumin akaisakanykape ka luoŋon ka akwat aoŋon.
44 # Age 46:17; 1 Siit 7:30. Odwe luka Aser kwape ajaatar ikekiakec: Imuna, ekek loka Imunamin: Isibi, ekek loka Isibin: Beria, ekek loka Iberian. 45Odwe luka Beria: Keber, ekek loka Ikeberin: Malakiel, ekek loka Imalakielin, 46Ekiror da loka akoku naka Aser ŋes Sera. 47Ŋul kes ikekia luka odwe luka Aser kwape ajaatar luaponi kimarai kakesi, ilukumin akaisakany ka luuni ka akwat aoŋon.
48 # Age 46:24; 1 Siit 7:13. Odwe luka Naputali kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: Yakuseel, ekek loka Iyakuseelin: Guni, ekek loka Igunin: 49#1 Siit 7:13. Yeser, ekek loka Iyeserin: Silem, ekek loka Isilemin. 50Ŋul kes ikekia luka Naputali kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: nul luaponi kimarai kakesi emwaasi ilukumin akaisaoŋon ka lukany ka akwat aoŋon,
51 # 1:46; 2:32. Ŋul kes luaponi kimarai kodwe luka Isirael, ilukumin akwat akanykape ka lodiopet ka akwat akanykaarei ka akaisauni (601,730).
52 # Mar 34:13; Yos 14:1-2. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 53“Mama ejaas ŋul ŋes ekorakinio akwap araun airumet kwape ejaar etiai loka ikiroria. 54#Mar 33:54. Mama ejaas luipu iinakini airumet naepol, mama ejaas luikidioko iinakini airumet naedir: mama ejai ŋinidiopet kwape ejaatar nul luaponi kimarai kaluke einakinio airumeteke. 55#Mar 34:13; Yos 11:23; 14:2. Konye ekorokoro akwap kailamilam eroet: irumete kwape ejaatar ikiroria luka atekerin nuka Iukapapaakec, 56Kwape ejaunor eroet ŋes ekorakinere airumetekec mama ejaas luipu ka luikidioko.”
57 # Age 46:11; Any 6:16; 1 Siit 6:1,16. Lu kes luaponi kimarai luka Ilebin kwape ajaatar ikekiakee: Gereson, ekek loka Igeresonin: Kokat, ekek loka Ikokatin: Merari, ekek loka Imerarin. 58Lu kes ikekia luka Ilebin: ekek loka Ilibunin, ekek loka Ikeburonin, ekek loka Imalin, ekek loka Imusin, ekek loka Ikorakin. Kouru Kokat Amuram. 59Ekiror da loka aberu naka Amuram ŋes Yokebed, akoku naka Lebi, naaurie Lebi kotoma Misiri: kourik ŋesi Amuram Aaron ka Musa ka Miriam kinacikec. 60#Mar 3:2. Kouri Aaron Nadab ka Abiku, ka Eleasar ka Itamar. 61#Leb 10:1-2; Mar 3:4; 1 Siit 24:2. Kotwaka Nadab ka Abiku, naainakiniata akim naitegelikina kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 62#Mar 3:39; 1:49; 18:20-24; 35:2-8; Ikis 10:9; 14:27-29; Yos 13:14; 14:3. Ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi emwaasi ilukumin akaisaarei ka luuni, ŋolikiliokit ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka luedeparito: naarai mam emarakinitai toma odwe luka Isirael, naarai mam ainakinitai kes airumet kodwe luka Isirael.
63Ŋul kes luemarete Musa ka Eleasar esasedoti; luemarete idwe luka Isirael kapuutona nuka Moab kariri naka Yorodan koYeriko. 64#Mar 1:1; Ikis 2:14,15. Konye kotoma kanul amamei idiotuŋanan kakwi luemarete Musa ka Aaron esasedoti; luemarete idwe luka Isirael kotoma amodiŋ naka Sinai. 65#Mar 14:26-35; Ikis 32:49,50; Sab 90:3-8; Rom 11:22; 1 Kor 10:5; Bur 3:17,18; Yud 1:5; Mar 14:30; 32:12; Ikis 1:36,38; Yos 14:10. Naarai abu EJAKAIT kolimu nukee cbe, “Abeit etwakikinete toma amodiŋ.” Mam ediotuŋanan kakesi abu kideun, dimarai Kaleb okoku loka Yepune, ka Yosua okoku loka Nun.
