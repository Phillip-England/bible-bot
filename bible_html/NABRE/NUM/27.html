Aimario 27

27
1 # Mar 14:35; 26:33; 36:1,11; 1 Siit 7:15; Yos 17:3. Kosodi kikunyut adwe nuka Selopekad, okoku loka Keper, okoku loka Gilead, okoku loka Makir, okoku loka Manase, nuka ikekia luka Manase, okoku loka Yosep: lu kes ikiroria luka adweke; Mala, ka Noa, ka Kogila, ka Milika, ka Tirusa, 2#Mar 26:64,65; 14:22-37; Rom 5:12; 6:23. Kobwaata kakonyen nuka Musa, kakonyen nuka Eleasar esasedoti, kakonyen da nuka ikapolok ka ekodet kere, kokek loka ewema loka airiamunun, kobalas ebe, 3“Abu papakosi kotwanik amodiŋ, konye mam ŋes arai opaupakee kanul luapotu kotukokinos nepepe aluikin EJAKAIT korioŋet loka Kora: konye atwani ŋesi da kotoma adiakareteke; komameete idwe lusap. 4Kanukinyo elemarere ekiror loka papakosi kokekike, naarai amameete ŋes idwe lusap? Koinakinai sio airumet koinaean luka papakosi.”
5 # Any 18:15; 25:22; Leb 24:12,13; Awa 3:5,6. Koyau Musa akeeekirot mama ejai EJAKAIT. 6Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 7#Mar 36:2. “Ineras adwe nuka Selopekad nuedolito: abeit iinakini kes airumet koinaean luka papakee; ido itebei airumet naka papakee mamakee. 8Ilimokini idwe luka Isirael ebe, ‘Arai ketwana etuŋanan, komamei ikoku yenisapat, itebei do airumeteke mama ejai akokuke. 9Arai emamei ŋes akokuke; iinakini airumeteke mama ejaas iinaeanake. 10Arai emameete ŋes iinacanake, iinakini do airumeteke mama ejaas iinaean luka papake. 11#Mar 35:29. Arai emameete papake iinaean, iinakini akeirumet mama ejai opajanake loka ekekike, ŋesi da irumi: eraun kama ejaas idwe luka Isirael ekisil loka etube, kwape kieorakinia EJAKAIT Musa.’”
12 # Ikis 3:23-27; 32:48-52; 34:1. Kobala EJAKAIT Musa ebe, “Kodoka moru lo loka Abarim, koany akwap naainak eoŋ idwe luka Isirael. 13#Mar 20:24,28; 31:2; Ikis 10:6; 32:50,52. Kianyu ŋes, kiyanaario mama ejaas ikonituŋa kwape ayaŋaarere onacekon Aaron: 14#Ikis 1:37; 32:51; Sab 106:32; Any 17:7; Mar 20:1,13,24. Naarai ibu kipaliik akakirot kotoma amodiŋ naka Sin, kopeget loka ekodet, aitalaun eoŋ kakonyenekee kakipi.” (Ŋun kes akip nuka Mcriba loka Kades kamodiŋ naka Sin.) 15Kotema Musa EJAKAIT atiar, 16#Mar 16:22; Bur 12:9; Sek 12:1. “Kodakak EJAKAIT, Edeke loka imoyon luka awat kere, etuŋanan ayait ekodet, 17#1 Ika 22:17; Esk 34:5; Mat 9:36; Mk 6:34; Ikis 31:2; 1 Sam 8:20; 18:13; 2 Siit 1:10; 2 Siit 18:16. Loepedori alomar ka alomun da kakonyenekee, loepedori aiŋarenikin kes alomar ka alomun da; tetere mam ekodet loka EJAKAIT esubi bala amerekekin nuemameete ekecokon.” 18#Any 24:13; Age 41:38; Tub 3:10. Kobala EJAKAIT Musa ebe, “Koyaŋa Yosua okoku loka Nun, etuŋanan loejai emoyo, kidokok ŋes akonikan; 19#Ikis 31:7. Kitobwo kakonyen nuka Eleasar esasedoti, kakonyen nuka ekodet kere; kodakak da ŋes apolou kakonyenekee. 20#Mar 11:17; 1 Sam 10:6; 2 Ika 2:15; Ikis 34:9; Yos 1:15. Iinakini ŋes akonipolou, tetere erimari ekodet kere loka idwe luka Isirael. 21#Any 28:30; 1 Sam 14:41; 28:6; Yos 9:14; Tub 1:1; 20:18; 1 Sam 23:9; 30:7; Leb 8:8; Ikis 33:8; 1 Sam 28:6; 1 Sam 22:10. Ebwoi kakonyen nuka Eleasar esasedoti, loinitununei kanuke kotube loka Urim kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: kakirotoke elomariata, kakirotoke da elomuniata, ŋes ka idwe kere luka Isirael nepepe kaŋes, ekodet kere.” 22Kiswama Musa kwape kicorakinia EJAKAIT ŋes; koyana Yosua, kitobwo ŋes kakonyen nuka Eleasar esasedoti, kakonyen da nuka ekodet kere: 23#Ikis 3:28; 31:23; Isa 55:4. Kidokok ŋes akaninike, kodakak apolou, kwape kineria EJAKAIT kama ka Musa.
