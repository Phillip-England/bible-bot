Aimario 16

16
1 # Yud 1:11. Kosodete Kora, okoku loka Isekar, okoku loka Kokat, okoku loka Lebi, nepepe ka Datan ka Abiram, odwe luka Eliab, ka On, okoku loka Pelet, odwe luka Reuben, ayaŋaar ituŋa: 2#Mar 26:9. Konyout kakonyen nuka Musa, nepepe kice kodwe luka Isirael, ikapolok akwat aarei ka akaisakany luka ekodet, luanyaraunitai aurianet, ituŋa luajenas; 3#Sab 106:16; Any 19:6; 29:45. Kotukonokinos aluikin Musa ka Aaron, kolimokis kes ebe, “Idakakina yes nuipu, kwabo elaunit ekodet kere, ninidiopet kakesi, ejai EJAKAIT kakes: kanukinyo do idakakinotor yesi ikulepek aitelekar atukot naka EJAKAIT?” 4#Mar 20:6. Kepupu Musa, kibiror ebuburia: 5#2 Tim 2:19; Leb 21:6-12; Any 28:1; 1 Sam 2:28; Sab 105:26; Leb 10:3; Esk 40:46. Kolimok Kora ka ipaupake kere ebe, “Moi tupuruc itoduni EJAKAIT lueraas luke, ka yenikalaunan, isudunyikini mamake: nol Ioeseuni ŋesi isudunyikini mamake. 6Kiswamata kwanata; koyanaasi yesi aisupuruneta, Kora ka ipaupake kere; 7Kibwaikis akim toma, kibwaikis da lukisupurununo toma kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT moi: ejaun kwana ebe etuŋanan loeseuni EJAKAIT, elauni ŋol; idakakina yes nuipu, yes odwe luka Lebi.” 8Kotema Musa Kora atiar, “Kopupokisi do yes odwe luka Lebi: 9#1 Sam 18:23; Isa 7:13; Ikis 10:8. Erai kereka akirot naedit kamakus, Edeke loka Isirael agitun yes kokodet loka Isirael, aisukunyikin yes mama ejai; aijaanakin toma owema lokalaunan loka EJAKAIT, ka abwoenen kakonyen nuka ekodet aijaanakin kes; 10Kisukuny ŋesi jo, ka iinacanakon kere odwe luka Lebi nepepe kajo? Imootos yesi da esasedotene? 11#Any 16:8; 1 Kor 3:5. Kanuka ŋun ijo ka ipaupakon kere itukonokinos nepepe aluikin EJAKAIT: ka Aaron da, iŋai bo ŋesi loirurumakinitos yesi?”
12Kijuka Musa anyaraun Datan ka Abiram, odwe luka Eliab: kotemasi atiar, “Mam sio kibuni: 13#Any 2:14; Asw 7:27,35. Erai kereka akirot naedit ijo aitonyoun sio kakwap naeleleete akile ka esik, aarakin sio amodiŋ, kwabo ituluoro bon ekapolon lokiyait sio? 14#Any 3:8; Leb 20:24. Bobo da mam jo kitolomarit sio akwap naeleleete akile ka esik, karaida ainakin airum imanikoria ka amanat nuka imusabibun: mere imod ijo ituŋa lu? mam sio kibuni.” 15#Age 4:4,5; 1 Sam 12:3; Asw 20:33; 2 Kor 7:2. Konyunyurikin Musa noi, kolimok EJAKAIT ebe, “Sirikieam ainakinetekec: mam eoŋ ayanaarit adiopesigiria kamakee, karaida aisirieb idiopet kakesi.” 16Kotema Musa Kora atiar, “Kojaun ijo moi ka ekodetekon kere, akonyen nuka EJAKAIT, ijo ka kes, ka Aaron: 17Koyana ninituŋanan kayesi akeisupurunet kibwaik lukisupurununo toma, koyanaasi mama ejai EJAKAIT ninitunanan akeisupurunet, aisupuruneta akwat aarei ka akaisakany; ijo da, ka Aaron, ninirunanan akeisupurunet.” 18Koyanaasi ninirunanan akeisupurunet, kibwaikis akimian toma, kibwaikis da lukisupurununo, kobwaata kokek loka ewema loka airiamunun nepepe ka Musa ka Aaron. 19#Any 16:7,10; Leb 9:6,23. Kotukok Kora ekodet kere kiteos kakes kokek loka ewema laka airiamunun: kitakanikin aibuses naka EJAKAIT ekodet kere.
20Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron atiar, 21#Age 19:17; Yer 51:6; Asw 2:40; Apu 18:4; Any 32:10; 33:5; Sab 73:19; Isa 37:36. “Kogitaros yesi ka ekodet kalo, kamudia kes katipet.” 22#Yob 12:10; Lim 12:7; Isa 57:16; Sek 12:1; Bur 12:9; 2 Sam 24:17; 1 Siit 21:17; Esk 18:20. Kibiroros ebuburia, kotemasi atiar, “Wu Edeke, Edeke loka imoyon luka lukaiarak kere, inyunyurikini ijo ekodet kere kediaka ediopetunanan?” 23Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 24“Kolimok ekodet ebe, Konyoutu kowema loka Kora, ka Datan ka Abiram.”
25Konyou Musa kolot mama ejai Datan ka Abiram; kotupito da ŋes imakio luka Isirael. 26#Age 19:12; Isa 52:11; Asw 2:40; 2 Kor 6:17; Apu 18:4. Kiner kekodet atiar, “Konyoutu, kenaiŋaito, koweman luka ituŋa lu lukaronok, ido sirikitina idioborekec, kinyeis amudiario kotoma adiakaretakec kere.” 27#Yob 9:4; Awa 16:18. Konyout kowema loka Kora, ka Datan ka Abiram, kanoliwai: kolomut Datan ka Abiram, kobwaata kokekia luka iwemanakec, ka akecenor ka odwekec, ka ikeeedwe ludidik. 28#Any 3:12; Ikis 18:22; Sek 2:9; Yer 23:16; Esk 13:17; Yok 5:30. Kotema Musa atiar, “Iienunete kanu ebe EJAKAIT keiukunit eoŋ aswam aswamisio nu kere; naarai mam eswamat kes kanukaŋ, 29#Any 20:5; 32:34; Yob 35:15; Isa 10:3; Yer 5:9; Aku 4:22. Arai ketwaka ituŋa lu atwanare naka ituŋa kere, ido arai kedoikis kes nuedoonokinete ituŋa kere; ŋes atemar ebe mere EJAKAIT kejukunit eoŋ. 30#Yob 31:3; Isa 28:21; Sab 55:15; Apu 9:2. Konye arai kiswamau EJAKAIT ibore yenikitetet, koon akwap akeituk, kosodi ailikor kes, ka luejaatar kere, kolomasi aipany ejarete; ŋes do ijenuniata ebe amwana ituŋa lu EJAKAIT.”
31 # Mar 26:10; 27:3; Ikis 11:6; Sab 106:17. Kosodi kanen, enetakini ŋesi einer akiro nun kere, kocila akwap naabwoiata kesi: 32#1 Siit 6:22. Koona akwap aitukuke, kiliko kes, ka ikaliakec ka ituŋa kere luaraas luka Kora, ka ikeceboro kere. 33Kwaŋin kes, ka luaiaatatar kere kolomasi aipany ejarete: kobunak akwap kes, kooliosi katukot. 34Kokera luka Isirael kere luerimorito kes kepuput aimonyokec: naarai abakas ebe, “Kinyek akwap ailikor oni da.” 35#Mar 11:1; Leb 10:2; Sab 106:18. Kobu akim kama ejai EJAKAIT, konyama ituŋa nul akwat aarei ka akaisakany luainakinete lukisupurununo.
36Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 37“Kolimok Eleasar okoku loka Aaron esasedoti kolema aisupuruneta kaneecweere, kiweliwel akim; naarai eraas nukalaunak; 38#Awa 20:2; Kbk 2:10; Mar 17:10; 26:10; Esk 14:8; 1 Kor 10:11; 2 Pet 2:6. Aisupuruneta ŋun nuka ikadiakanarak nuegelito aiiarakec, itatai kes aipadalikin arapia etem; naarai apotu koinakis kes mama ejai EJAKAIT, kanuka nun eraas nukalaunak: ido eraunos aanyunet mama ejaas idwe luka Isirael.” 39Koyana Eleasar esasedoti aisupuruneta nuka aisinit, nuainakinito luecweio; kotatata kes aitaraun arapetait naka etem: 40#Mar 3:38; 18:4-7; 1 Ika 13:1. Araun aisiitunet mama ejaas idwe luka Isirael, ebe sirielonwait lomam erai loka aur naka Aaron, kikunyu aicwe lukisupurununo kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT; kinyek aiput Kora ka ipaupake: kwape kelimokinia ŋes EJAKAIT kama ka Musa,
41 # Mar 14:2; Sab 106:13,26; Mat 5:11; Asw 21:28; 2 Kor 6:8. Konye kapaaran naatupakini kirurumak ekodet kere loka idwe luka Isirael Musa ka Aaron, ebe, “Iarata yesi ituŋa luka EJAKAIT.” 42#Any 16:7,10; 24:16; 40:34; Leb 9:23; Mar 14:10. Kosodi ketukokin atukot aluikin Musa ka Aaron, kiteoto owema loka airiamunun: ido koany, korapia edou, kotakanu da aibuses naka EJAKAIT. 43Kopotu Musa ka Aaron kinaren kewema loka airiamunun. 44Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 45#Mar 20:6; 1 Siit 21:16; Mat 26:39. “Konyou kokodet kalo, kamudia kes katipet.” Kibiroros ebuburia. 46#Leb 10:6; Mar 1:53; 11:33; 18:5; Yos 7:1; 22:18; 1 Sam 6:19; 2 Sam 24:1; Sab 106:29; 1 Siit 27:24. Kotema Musa Aaron atiar, “Koyaŋa aisupurunetekon, kibwaik akim nakotem toma, kibwaik da lukisupurununo, koyana katipet neejai ekodet, kolueo kes: naarai alomu anyunyura kama ejai EJAKAIT; ageu da atular.” 47Koyaŋa Aaron kwape alimokinia Musa, koker kidin kekodet; ido koany, ageuna atular kotuna: kibwaik lukisupurununo, koluc iruna, 48#2 Sam 24:16,17,25; Yob 33:24; Sab 106:30; Mat 20:28; 1 Ses 1:10; Bur 7:24; Yak 5:16. Kobwo ŋesi kokidin kalukatwak ka lukajarak; kitananai atular. 49Ido luapotu kotwaka kanuka atular emwaasi ilukumin itomon ka luonon ka akwat akanykaarei, komam imarakinitai luatwakete kanuka akiro nuka Kora. 50Kobono Aaron mama ejai Musa okek loka ewema loka airiamunun: kitananai atular.
