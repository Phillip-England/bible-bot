Aimario 18

18
1 # Mar 17:13. Kotema EJAKAIT Aaron atiar, “Ibwoki ijo ka odwekon, ka ekal loka lukapapaakon nepepe kajo numam adolit nuka aibois nakalaunan: ido ijo ka odwekon ibwokete numam adolit nuka esasedotenekus. 2#Age 29:34; Mar 3:6-10. Ikoniinacan da, ateker naka Lebi, ateker naka papakon, kisukuny nepepe kajo, tetere imorikikinio mamakon, kijaanakis jo: konye ijo ka odwekon nepepe kajo iiaunos akonyen nuka ewema loka aitejenunet. 3#Mar 3:25,31; 16:40; 4:15. Idarete kesi akirokon, idarete da Ewema kere: konye mam ikunyakinete die kiboro luka aibois nakalaunan karaida otem, kinyeis atwakare, kes, karaida yes. 4Imorikikinio kes mamakon, idarete ewema loka airiamunun, kanuka aijaanakin kere naka Ewema:, mam eloŋwait ikunyuni diekus. 5#Any 27:21; 30:7; Leb 24:3; 1 Siit 9:19-33. Idarete yesi aibois nakalaunan, ka etem da: tetere mam bobo akanyunyura ebunokini idwe luka Isirael. 6#Mar 3:12,45. Eoŋ da, koany, alema iinacanakon Ilebin kodwe luka Isirael: eraas ainakinet mamakon, lueinakinitai EJAKAIT, aijaanakin ewema loka airiamunun, 7#Bur 9:3,6; 1 Sam 6:19. Idarete ijo ka odwekon esasedotenekus koboro kere luka etem, ka lueiaas toma aigalisit; ijaanakinete da: kainakinit yes aiiaanakin naka esasedotene bala ainakinet: ido eloŋwait loikunyuni die eario.”
8 # Leb 6:16,18; 7:6,32; Ikis 6:11; Any 40:13,15. Kotema EJAKAIT Aaron atiar, “Eoŋ da, koany, kainak jo nuinadakinio nuka ainakineta nukainanakinio eoŋ, kes iboro lukalaunak luka idwe luka Isirael, kainak jo araun ewaikon, ka odwekon da, airumetekus nakitebeikinitai ikar kere. 9#Leb 2:2,3; 4:22,27. Ŋin eraun nakon koboro lukalaunak noi, numam ieweikinitai: niniinakinetekec, ŋes ŋiniinakinetekee naka akiria, ka niniinakinetekee naka adiakaret, ka ŋiniinakinetekee naka aronis, nakainakinete eoŋ, eraun nakalaunan noi kanukon ido kanuka odwekon da. 10Inyamanakini aibois nakalaunan noi: enyami nolikiliokit; eraunos lukalaunak kamakono 11#Any 29:27,28; Leb 7:30,34; 10:14; Ikis 18:3; Leb 22:2. Lukon lu: luedakakinitai luka ainakinetakee, ainakineta da nuijaŋio nuka idwe luka Isirael: kainak jo, ka ikonidwe lusap ka lupesur nepepe kajo, airumetekus nakitebeikinitai ikar kere: ŋin yenelai kokalekon enyami kes, 12#Any 23:19; Ikis 18:4. Akinyet kere naitelekarit aiokis, ka ebino loitelekarit ajokis, akima da, nuegeete kakesi kokaru nueinakinete EJAKAIT, kainak joo 13Araito nuegeete atiokore nuka kere luejaas toma akwap, nueyaununete mama ejai EJAKAIT, eraunos nukon; ŋin yenelai kokalekon enyami kes. 14#Leb 27:28. Ŋinibore yenegitunitai kotoma Isirael eraun yenikon. 15#Any 13:2; 34:20; Leb 27:20,27; Mar 3:13; Any 13:13. Ŋinibore yenepukori aliliget, yenika akuan kere yeneinakinete EJAKAIT, yenika etuŋanan ka etiaŋ da, eraun yenikon: konye sirikinyek alakun ekiyai loka etuŋanan, sirikinyek da alakun ekiyai loka itian lumam elaete. 16#Mar 3:47; Leb 27:2,6; Any 30:13; Leb 27:12; Esk 45:12. Ka nul da luibusakinit alakun kakesi, ilakuni ageun kaluka elap ediopet, kwape eiaunor aimarakon, isekelin ikany, kwape ajaar esekel #18:16 esekel ŋes ape sa. loka aibois nakalaunan (esekel ŋes igeran akaisaarei). 17#Ikis 15:19; Leb 3:2,5. Konye mam ilakuni ekiyai loka emoŋ, arai ekiyai loka amerekek, arai ekiyai loka akinei; eraas lukalaunak: ibwatakini akeeeokot otem, ieweikini da asiepaikee kanuka ainakinet naeinakinere kakim, asakat naeuru saa mama ejai EJAKAIT. 18#Any 29:26-28; Leb 7:31. Akiriŋikec eraun nakon, kwape ka atorob naiianio ka amurwo nakoteten, eraun nakon. 19Ainakineta nuedakakinitai kere nuka iboro lukalaunak, nueinakinete idwe luka Isirael EJAKAIT, kainak jo ka ikonidwe lusap ka lupesur, nepepe kajo, airumet nakitebeikinitai ikar kere: erai aitutuket naka abalaŋ naka ikar kere kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT mama ijai ijo ka akoniwur nepepe kajo.” 20#Ikis 10:9; 14:27; 18:1; Yos 13:14,33; Sab 16:5; 73:26; Esk 44:28. Kotema EJAKAIT Aaron atiar, “Emamuun jo airumet kotoma akwapukee, karaida adumun akorakinet kamakee: Eoŋ ŋes akorakinetekon ka airumetekon da kodwe luka Isirael.
21 # Leb 27:30-33; Ikis 14:22-29; 12:17-19; Nek 10:37; 12:44; Bur 7:5,8. Ido koany, ainak idwe luka Lebi idiopet kotomon koIsirael araun airumet, atacia aijaanakinikee naijaanakinete, ŋes aijaanakin naka ewema loka airiamunun. 22#Leb 22:9. Ido ameari kwana mam idwe luka Isirael ikunyunete die kewema loka airiamunun, kinyeis aibwok adiakaret, kosodete atwakare. 23Konye ijaanakinete Ilebin ewema loka airiamunun, ibwokete numam adolitekec: eraun ekisil katuriokus kere ikar kere, ido emamuun kes airumet kodwe luka Isirael. 24#Ikis 10:9. Naarai ainak Ilebin idiopet kotomon yenika lueinanakinete idwe luka Isirael kanuka ainakinet naedakakinitai mama ejai EJAKAIT araun airumet: kanuka ŋun alimok kes ebe, Emamuun kes airumet kodwe luka Isirael.”
25Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 26#Nek 10:38. “Bobo da ilimokini Ilebin ebe, ‘Naijauniata idwe luka Isirael idiopet kotomon yenikainak yes kamakec araun airumetekus, iinakiniata yesi da ainakinet naedakakinitai mama ejai EJAKAIT, idiopet kotomon yenika idiopet kotomon. 27Imarakinio mamakus ainakinetekus naedakakinitai kosubite bala kerai alos nakopejal, arai nuegeete nuka acuet naka ebino. 28Iinakinete yesi da kwaŋin ainakinet naedakakinitai mama ejai EJAKAIT, idiopet kotomon yenika luiiaunete kodwe luka Isirael; iiaikiniata da Aaron esasedoti ainakinet naedakakinitai naka EJAKAIT. 29Kainakinetakus kere iinanakinete ŋiniinakinet naedakakinitai naka EJAKAIT, kaluitelekarit aiokis kotomakec ŋes atutubet naitalaunitai kotomakec.’ 30Ŋes itemaria kes atiar, ‘Naidakuniata naitelekarit ajokis, ŋes imarakinere oLebin kosubite bala nuiyatakina nuka epejal, ka nuiyatakina nuka acuet naka ebino. 31#Mat 10:10; Luk 10:7; 1 Kor 9:13; 2 Kor 12:13; Gal 6:6; 1 Tim 5:18; 1 Ses 5:12,13. Inyamenenete ŋes kaniniibois, yes ka ikusikalia da; naarai erai eropitikus atacia aijaanakinikus kowema loka amamunun. 32#Leb 19:8; 22:16. Ido mam ibwokete aronis kanuke, kidaut adakakin naitelekarit ajokis kotomake: sirikitisiloto iboro lukalaunak luka idwe luka Isirael, kinyeis atwakare.’”
