Aimario 22

22
1 # Mar 33:48; Age 35:5; Any 23:27; Ikis 2:25; Yos 2:9; Yer 32:21. Koloto idwe luka Isirael, kidukuroto apuutona nuka Moab okekwa loka Yorodan ko Yeriko. 2#Tub 11:25. Koany Balak okoku loka Sipor kere nukiswamakin Isirael Imorain. 3#Any 15:15. Kokurianar Moab noi ituŋa, naarai epu: koiurunun Moab kanuka idwe luka Isirael. 4#Mar 31:8; Yos 13:21. Kobala Moab imakio luka Midian ebe, “Natikwana elosi etuŋat lo ameor eut kere lukirimoritos, kwape ikoeoria emoŋ eduan lokorony.” Balak da okoku loka Sipor ŋes arai ekabaka loka Moab kapaarasia kanun. 5#Mar 31:8; 2 Pet 2:15-16; Yud 11; Ikis 23:4; Yos 13:22; Nek 13:1,2; Mik 6:5. Kijuka ikiyakia mama ejai Balaam okoku loka Beor, Pesor, loiririkina Ecor, kakwap naka idwe luka iketuŋa, anyaraun ŋes ebe, “Koany, ejaas ituŋa lueponito koMisiri: koany, egarito akwap, kes da katereikina eoŋ: 6#Age 12:3; 27:29; Mar 23:7; Ikis 23:4; Yos 24:9; 1 Sam 17:43; Nek 13:2. Aso, kenainait, kobia, kikenokinai ituŋa lu; naarai egogoŋ kesi adepar eoŋ, elemuni kepiko: kasodete aidany, kereŋea kakwap: naarai ajeni ebe yenilipakini ijo arereŋ isirerenio, ido yenikeni ijo ikenio.”
7 # Any 23:8; 1 Sam 9:7,8; Awa 17:23; Isa 56:11; Mik 3:11. Kolototo imakio luka Moab ka imakio luka Midian ekapitos ainakineta nuka aidwar; kopotu mama ejai Balaam, kolimokis ŋes akiro nuka Balak. 8Kotema kes atiar, “Koperoto ne akuare na, kenyakakini eoŋ da yes kwape kalimokinia EJAKAIT.” Kiboikinos lukapolok luka Moab ore loka Balaam. 9#Age 20:3; 31:24. Kobu Edeke neejai Balaam, kotema atiar, “Lukaŋai ituŋa lu luijaas kajo?” 10Kotema Balaam Edeke atiar, “Balak, okoku loka Sipor, ekabaka laka Moab, ŋes keyakikinit, ebe, 11‘Koany, ituŋa luapote koMisiri egarito akwap: kobia cabo kikenokinai; elemuni apedori ejie kakes, kerenea.’” 12#Age 12:2; 22:17; 28:13,14; Mar 23:20; Ikis 23:5; 33:29; Sab 144:15; Rom 11:29. Kotema Edeke Balaam atiar, “Sirikiloto kakes; sirikiken ituŋa: naarai isirerenitai.’ 13Konyou Balaam tupuruc, kolimok lukapolok luka Balak ebe, “Koloto ma yesi bukus: naarai aŋer EJAKAIT acamakin eoŋ alosit kayes.” 14Konyout lukapolok luka Moab, koloto mama ejai Balak, kotemasi atiar, “Aŋer Balaam aupun kasio.”
15Kiyatak Balak bobo aijukar lukapolok, luitelekarito kwi aipues, ka luitelekarito da kapolou. 16Kopotu mama ejai Balaam, kotemasi ŋes atiar, “Inera kwanata Balak okoku loka Sipor, ebe, ‘Kinye adiokirot aitikokin jo abunore neajai eoŋ, kenainait. 17#Ikis 16:19; Sab 49:17; Mat 4:8,9; 16:26. Naarai ketopoloori eoŋ jo koraun lokaibuses noi, kere da nukilimokini eoŋ eswamai: aso bia, kenainair, kikenokinai ituŋa lu.’’’ 18#1 Ika 22:14; 2 Siit 18:13. Koboŋok Balaam kotema ikejaanakinak luka Balak atiar, “Karaida kainaki Balak eketogo ileleba epuesa ka esabu, mam apedori adepar akirot naka EJAKAIT Ekadeke, aiŋoŋar arai aiyatakin. 19Aso ber, kenainaitos, koperoto yes da ne akuare na, tetere ajenuni nuiyatakini EJAKAIT alimokin eoŋ.” 20#Mar 23:12,26; 24:13; Sab 33:10,11; Isa 37:29; 46:10. Kobu Edeke neejai Balaam kuare, kotema ŋes atiar, “Arai eponitos ituŋa ŋul anyarit jo, konyou, koloto kakes, konye akirot ban nakalimokini jo, nipenin iswamai.”
21 # Awa 15:27; 1 Tim 6:9,10; 2 Pet 2:15; Yud 1:11. Konyou Balaam tupuruc, kopesik aibwanenis asigiriake, koloto nepepe kalukapolok luka Moab. 22#Any 4:24; 23:20. Kinep anyunyura naka Edeke kanu alosio: kobwo emalaika loka EJAKAIT korot asurokin ŋes. Ido kibwanenite asigiriake, koupasi kikekeiaanakinak iarei. 23#2 Ika 6:17. Koany asigiria emalaika loka EJAKAIT ebwoi korot, ka ekekileŋ da eutuno kakanike; kinac asigiria kopaa ais: kocar Balaam asigiria ainyakun orot. 24Kosodi kobwo emalaika loka EJAKAIT korot loedidin loejai kidin kimanikoria luka emusabibu, koiai abwas epuai ka epuai. 25#Isa 47:12; 1 Kor 3:19. Koany asigiria emalaika loka EJAKAIT, koridakin arwatat, kogirisor akeju naka Balaam karwatat: koear ŋes bobo, 26#Isa 26:11; Kos 2:6. Kikunya emalaika loka EJAKAIT nediokasilon, kobwo korot loedidin, neamamei apak naibelokinor teten karaida kediany. 27#Awa 14:16; 27:3,4; Yak 1:19. Koany asigiria emalaika loka EJAKAIT, koper kwap ka Balaam; kinep anyunyura naka Balaam, kocar asigiria kekebela. 28#Mat 19:26; 1 Kor 1:19; 2 Pet 2:16. Kitoona EJAKAIT aituk naka asigiria, kotema Balaam atiar, “Kekonak bo eoŋ jo ai, tape kicaracarai eoŋ aatenak nun auni?” 29#Awa 12:10; Lim 9:3; Yer 17:9; Mat 15:19. Kobala Balaam asigiria ebe, “Naarai kitakadik: arai kiija eoŋ kekilen akanika, ti kwana kaar jo.” 30#1 Kor 1:27. Kotema asigiria Balaam atiar, “Mere eoŋ akonisigiria, naibwanen ijo kaiiarakon kere aitodol lolo? Etapit tope eoŋ aikonanakin jo kwaŋin?” Kobala ebe, “Mam.”
31 # Age 21:19; 2 Ika 6:17; 1 Siit 21:16; Luk 24:16; Yob 8:20; Awa 11:2; Yer 3:25. Kosodi kitwekeny EJAKAIT akonye nuka Balaam, koany emalaika loka EJAKAIT ebwoi korot, ka ekekileŋ eutuno kakanike: kitulur akekou, kibiror ebuburia, 32#Ikis 23:4; Awa 28:6; Mik 6:5; Asw 13:10; 2 Pet 2:14,15. Kobala emalaika loka EJAKAIT ebe, “Kanukinyo icaracaraa akonisigiria aatenak ŋun auni? Koany, alom asurokin jo, naarai epagal akonilosit kakonyeneka: 33Kaanyu asigiria eoŋ, abu kibelokin kakonyeneka aatenak ŋun auni: arai kemam ibelokino kamaka, abeit kwana tikaar jo, tiketajar ŋes.” 34#1 Sam 26:21; 2 Sam 12:13. Kobala Balaam emalaika loka EJAKAIT ebe, “Adiaka eoŋ: naarai mam ajeni ebe ibwoi ijo korot asurokin eoŋ: asa kwana, arai kitakadikinit jo, aboŋori do,” 35Kotema emalaika loka EJAKAIT Balaam atiar, “Kolot kituna: konye akirot bon nakalimokini jo, nipenin inera.” Kosodi koloto Balaam kalukapolok luka Balak.
36 # Age 14:17; 18:2; Any 18:7; 1 Sam 13:10; Asw 28:15. Kepupu Balak ebe abu Balaam, koloma airiamun kaŋes ore loka Moab, loejai oikor loka Arunon, loejai osaŋe loka eikor. 37Kobala Balak Balaam ebe, “Mam abu keyakikinite kaimaimait anyaraun jo? Kanukinyo mam ibu kobunio mamaka? Mam kereka apedori eoŋ aitopoloor jo koraun lokaibuses?” 38#1 Ika 22:14; 2 Siit 18:13. Kobala Balaam Balak ebe, “Koany, abu neijai: ejai kwana eoŋ apedor kere einer adiokirot? Akirot naibwaikini Edeke aitukuka, nipenin alimuni.” 39Kolototo Balaam ka Balak, kodolo Kiriatkusot, 40Koinak Balak asuban nuka imoŋin ka imerekekin, kijukak da Balaam, ka ikapolok luajaas kaŋes.
41 # Ikis 12:2; 2 Siit 11:15; Yer 48:35. Kosodi kanen kiwala akwap, koyana Balak Balaam, kitodoka ŋes aiboisio nuiceleleete nuka Baal, kosesen kanen neasaŋitos ituŋa kere.
