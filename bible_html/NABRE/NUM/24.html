Aimario 24

24
1 # Mar 11:25; 1 Sam 19:20; 2 Siit 15:1; Mat 7:21-23; 1 Kor 12:8,10. Keanyu Balaam ebe etoiokit EJAKAIT aisireren Isirael, mam abu kolot, kwape sek, airiamun kaieudeta, konye kiteteo akekonyen amodiŋ. 2Kiteluo Balaam akekonyen, koany Isirael iboiete kwape ajaatar atekerinikee; kobunok ŋes emoyo loka EJAKAIT. 3#Mar 23:7. Kiner akewaraga ebe,
“Inera Balaam okoku loka Beor,
lnera da etuŋanan loetumudakitai akekonye,
4 # 1 Sam 19:24; Esk 1:28; Dan 8:18; 10:15; 2 Kor 12:2; Apu 1:10,11. Inera loepupi akiro nuka Edeke,
Loeanyuni etapo Ioka Lokapedor kere,
Ibirun, kokwenyete akekonye, ebe,
5Ejokuka ca ma iwemanakon, wu Yakob,
Iwemanakon lukalaunak, wu Isirael!
6 # Sab 1:3; Yer 17:8. Elalak bala aditono,
Kwape kamanan nuka ariri naka ecor,
Kwape iluan luera EJAKAIT,
Kwape iresia kariri naka akipi.
7 # 1 Sam 15:32; 2 Sam 5:12; 1 Ika 4:21; 1 Siit 14:2; Isa 2:2; 9:7. Eleleete akip kamotike,
Ejaununos ikinyomoke toma akipi nuipu,
Eojaar ekekabaka adepar Agag,
Itopoloorio da akejakanut.
8Kitolom ŋes Edeke koMisiri;
Ejaas kagogon kwape naka ekosobwan:
Enyami atekerin nuka lukasurupoke,
Emunyumunyei akeeekojo.
Icumi kes kimaliake.
9 # Age 12:3; 49:9; 27:29; Any 23:23; Sab 122:6; Mat 25:40,45. Abu kodipidipakin, koper bala eŋatuny,
Bala aŋatuny; iŋai itonyouni ŋes?
Isirereŋio yenikilipakini jo arereŋ,
Ilamakinio yenikilamakini jo.”
10Kinep anyunyura naka Balak aluikin Balaam, kibapak akekanin: kolimok Balak Balaam atiar, “Abu kanyarau jo aikenokin lukasurupoka, ido koany, ilipak kes arereŋ cut irwan nu auni, 11Aso do, koker aiboisikon: enerunit aitopoloor jo koraun lokaibuses; konye koany, kitikok EJAKAIT jo adumun aibuses.” 12Kotema Balaam Balak atiar, “Mam eoŋ abu kalimok ikonikiyakia da luibu kijukak ebe, 13‘Karaida kainaki Balak eketogo ileleba epuesa ka esabu, mam apedori adepar akirot naka EJAKAIT, aswam nuejok arai nueroko nuaomit eoŋ eloper; nupeinera EJAKAIT, nupeŋun eneri eoŋ da’? 14Koany, alosi kwana mama ejaas ituŋaka: bia, kalimok jo nuepote ituŋa ŋu! aiswamakin ikonituŋa kapaarasia nukakonyen.” 15Kiner akewaraga ebe,
“Inera Balaam okoku loka Beor ebe,
Inera da etuŋanan loetumudakitai akekonye:
16Inera loepupi akiro nuka Edeke,
Loejeni da aijen naka Loejai kuju kakuj; Loeanyuni etapo loka Lokapedor kere, Ibirun, kokwenyete akekonye, ebe:
17 # Yob 19:25-27; Sek 12:10; Yud 1:14; Apu 1:7; Yer 23:5; Dan 2:14; Luk 1:32,33; Bur 1:8; Apu 22:16; Age 49:10; Sab 110:2. Etei ŋes, konye mere kwana:
Aseseni ŋes, konye mere kodie:
Elomuni aacerit kotoma Yakob,
Ikeuni ebela loka ajakanut koIsirael, Iposi agorieik nuka Moab apedor, Ebili da odwe kere luka eŋuŋet.
18 # Age 27:37; 2 Sam 8:14; Sab 60:8-12; Isa 34:5; 63:1; Amo 9:12. Eraun Edom nake,
Ka Seir da eraun nake, luaraas lukasurupoke;
Kiswamai Isirael nukagogon,
19Kotoma Yakob ŋes elomunia ekayaiton,
Emudiari da luideuna kore.”
20Kite Amalek, kiner akewaraga ebe,
“Kerai Amalek nasodit naka atekerin;
Konye esaneke lokakonyen emudiario.”
21Kite da Ekenit, kiner akewaraga ebe,
“Erai aibois naiboionono ijo naegogon,
Ecinakina akaikon atiiba.
22Kwabo icweio Kain
Aitodol nakinataria Asur jo.”
23Kiner akewaraga ebe,
“Wu okwe, iŋai eiari naiswamaa Edeke ŋun?
24 # Age 10:4; Isa 23:1; Dan 11:30; Age 11:14; Leb 26:28; Ikis 28:36; Mat 23:37. Konye epote imeerin kariri naka Kitim,
Epote aitican Asur, ka aitican da Eber,
Ŋes da emudiario.”
25Konyou Balaam, koloto kobono aiboisike: kolot Balak da.
