Aimario 3

3
1Na ŋes aur naka Aaron ka Musa kapaaran naeneratar EJAKAIT kaMusa komoru Sinai. 2#Mar 26:60; Any 6:23; 28:1; 1 Siit 6:3. Lu kes ikiroria luka odwe luka Aaron: Nadab ekiyaike, ka Abiku, Eleasar, ka Itamar. 3#Any 28:41; 40:13,15; Leb 8:1. Lu kes ikiroria luka odwe luka Aaron, isasedotin luaponi kinyonyoikinai akinyet, luatiakuni ŋesi aijaanakin toma aswam naka esasedoti. 4#Leb 10:1-2; Mar 26:61; Ikis 4:24; 1 Siit 24:2; Isa 66:15; 2 Ses 1:8; Bur 12:29. Kotwakikis Nadab ka Abiku akonyen nuka EJAKAIT, naainakiniata akim naitegelikina kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kamodiŋ naka Sinai, komameete kes idwe: kiiaanakis Eleasar ka Itamar kaswam naka esasedoti kakonyen nuka Aaron papakec,
5Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 6#Any 32:26; Mar 8:6; 18:2. “Kisukuny ateker naka Lebi, kibwaik kes akonyen nuka Aaron esasedoti, tetere ijaanakinete ŋes. 7#Mar 1:50; 8:11-26. Ido idarete akiroke, ka akiro da nuka ekodet kere loejai iŋaren kewema loka airiamunun, aijaanakin toma owema lokalaunan. 8Idarete da iboro kere luka ewema loka airiamunun, ka akiro da nuka idwe luka Isirael, aijaanakin roma owema lokalaunan. 9#Mar 8:19; 18:6. Iinakini Ilebin mama ejai Aaron ka odweke: ainakin kes cut mamakeŋ kanuka idwe luka Isirael. 10#Mar 18:7; 1 Siit 6:49; Asw 6:3,4; Rom 12:7; Mar 1:51; 16:40; 1 Sam 6:19; 2 Sam 6:7. Itebeikini Aaron ka odweke, aidarenen esasedotenekec: eario eloŋwait kere loikunyuni die kewema.”
11Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 12#Mar 8:16; 18:6. “Ka eoŋ da, koany, alemu Ilebin kodwe luka Isirael aibois naka ikiyaik luepukorete aliligeta kotoma odwe luka Isirael; eraunos da Ilebin lukan: 13#Any 13:2,12,15; 22:9; 34:20; Leb 27:26; Mar 8:16; Nek 10:36; Esk 44:30; Luk 2:23. Naarai ikiyaik kere eraas lukan; kapaaran naadanyia ikiyaik kere kotoma akwap nako Misiri abu ketalau ikiyaik kere koIsirael kanukan, ituŋanan ka itiaŋ da: eraunos lukan; arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.”
14 # Any 19:1. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa kamodiŋ naka Sinai atiar, 15#Mar 26:62. “Kimar idwe luka Lebi kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec, kwape ejaatar ikekiakec: imari ŋolikiliokit ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit.” 16Kimar Musa kes kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT, kwape kicorakinitere. 17#Age 46:11; Any 6:16; 1 Siit 6:1,16. Lu kes odwe luka Lebi kwape ejaatar ikiroriakec; Gereson, ka Kokat, ka Merari. 18#Any 6:17. Lu kes ikiroria luka odwe luka Gereson kwape ejaatar ikekiakec: Libuni ka Simei. 19Odwe luka Kokat kwape ejaatar ikekiakec; Amuram, ka Isar, ka Keburon ka Usiel. 20Odwe luka Merari kwape ejaatar ikekiakec; Mali ka Musi. Ŋul kes ikekia luka Ilebin kwape ejaatar ikalia luka lukapapaakec.
21Kotoma Gereson ŋes alomunia ekek loka Ilibunin, ka ekek loka Isimein: nul kes ikekia luka Igeresonin. 22Ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi, kwape eiaar etiai loka ikiliok kere, ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit, ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi emwaasi ilukumin ikanykaarei ka akwat akany. 23#Mar 1:53. Luka ekek loka Igeresonin idukurete agule kewema lokalaunan kari nakoto, 24Ekapolon loka ekal loka lukapapaa luka Igeresonin ŋes Eliasap okoku loka Lael. 25#Mar 4:24-26; 1 Siit 9:14-33; Esr 8:28-30; Any 25:9; 26:1-36. Ido kotoma owema loka airiamunun idarenenete odwe luka Gereson ewema lokalaunan, ka Bwema, ka eimukoreteke, ka aigalisit naka ekek loka ewema loka airiamunun, 26#Any 27:9,16; 35:18. Ka luikajaj alaro, ka aigalisit naka ekek loka alaro, naidunyas kewema lokalaunan, naidunyas ketem kolukunite, ka aunoike, kwape eiaar aijaanakinikec kere.
27 # 1 Siit 26:23. Kotoma Kokat ŋes alomunia ekek loka Imuramain, ka ekek loka Isarin, ka ekek laka Ikeburonin, ka ekek loka Iusielin: ŋul kes ikekia luka Ikokatin, 28Kwape ejaar etiai loka ikiliok kere, ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit, emwaasi ilukumin ikanykauni ka akwat akanykape, idarenenete aibois nakalaunan. 29#Mar 1:53. Luka ikekia luka odwe luka Kokat idukurete osiep loka ewema kari naka eŋalakimak, 30Ekapolon loka ekal loka lukapapaa luka ikekia luka Ikokatin ŋes Elisapan okoku loka Usiel. 31#Mar 4:15; Any 25:10-31; 27:1; 30:1. Idarenenete kes asaduku, ka emesa, ka epir loka atala, ka itemwan, ka iboro lukotoma aibois nakalaunan luetwasamaete kesi aiiaanakinia, ka aigalisit, ka aiiaanakinikec kere. 32#Mar 20:25-28; 2 Ika 25:18; 1 Siit 9:20. Eleasar da okoku loka Aaron esasedoti ŋes eraun ekapolon laka ikapolok luka Ilebin, ido eanyanari ŋul luidarete aibois nakalaunan.
33Kotoma Merari ŋes alomunia ekek loka Imalin, ka ekek loka Imusin: nul kes ikekia luka Merari. 34Ŋul luaponi kimarai kakesi kwape ejaar etiai loka ikiliok kere, ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit, emwaasi ilukumin ikanykape ka akwat aarei. 35Ekapolon loka ekal loka lukapapaa luka ikekia luka Imerarin ŋes Suriel okoku loka Abikail; idukurete kesi osiep loka ewema kari naka agolitomei. 36#Mar 1:53. Ido luaseikinitai odwe luka Merari aidarenen kes abaoi nuka ewema lokalaunan, ka akitoke nukirionokina, ka ipirinoke, ka aigaŋetakec, ka iboro kere lueupatar, ka aijaanakinikec kere: 37Ka ipirino luelukunito alaro, ka aigaŋetakec, ka iloinokec, ka aunoikec.
38 # Mar 1:53; 2:3; 18:5; 1 Siit 6:48,49. Ŋul da luidukurete iŋaren kewema lokalaunan kari nakokide, orute loka ewema loka airiamunun kama eniunia akoloŋ, kes Musa ka Aaron ka odweke, idarenenete aibois nakalaunan, ka akiro da nuka idwe luka Isirael: ido eario eloŋwait loikunyuni die kewema. 39#Mar 4:47,48; 26:62. Kere luaponi kimarai luka Ilebin, luemarete Musa ka Aaron kaieorakinet naka EJAKAIT, kwape ajaatar ikekiakee, ikiliok kere ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit, emwaasi ilukumin akaisaarei ka luarei,
40Kolimok EJAKAIT Musa ebe, “Kimar ikiyaik kere lukiliok luka idwe luka Isirael ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit, kimar etiai loka ikiroriakee. 41#Mar 3:12,45. Kolemikinai eoŋ Ilebin (arai eoŋ EJAKAIT) aibois naka ikiyaik kere kodwe luka Isirael; ka ibaren luka Ilebin aibois naka ikiyaik kere luka ibaren luka idwe luka Isirael.” 42Kimar Musa ikiyaik kere kodwe luka Isirael, kwape kieorakinia EJAKAIT. 43Ikiyaik kere lukiliok kwape ejaar etiai loka ikiroriakee, ageun kayenika elap ediopet ka yenedeparit, nul luaponi kimarai kakesi, emwaasi ilukumin akaisaarei ka luarei ka akwat aarei ka akaisakanykaarei ka luuni.
44Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 45#Mar 3:12,41; 1 Sam 1:28. “Kolem Ilebin aibois naka ikiyaik kere kodwe luka Isirael, ka ibaren luka Ilebin aibois naka ibarenekee: eraunos Ilebin lukan: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, 46#Any 13:13; Mar 18:15; 3:39,43. Ido kanuka alakunio naka ikiyaik akwat aarei ka akaisakanykaarei ka luuni luka idwe luka Isirael luedeparitotor kesi etiai loka Ilebin, 47#Leb 27:6; Mar 18:16; Any 30:13; Leb 27:25; Esk 45:12. Ŋauni isekelin ikany kaneejai ŋinituŋanan; ijauni kes kwape ejaar esekel #3:47 esekel ŋes apesa. loka aibois nakalaunan (esekel ŋes igeran akaisaarei): 48Iinakini Aaron ka odweke apesan nuelakunere luedeparitotor kes.” 49#1 Tim 2:6; Tit 2:14; Bur 9:12; 1 Pet 1:18; Gal 4:4,5. Kiiau Musa apesan nulakunet kaneeiaas nul luadeparitotor kesi lualakunete Ilebin: 50#Mar 3:46,47; 1 Sam 12:3,4; Asw 20:33. Kijau ŋesi apesan kaneejaas ikiyaik luka idwe luka Isirael; isekelin elukumit ka akwat auni ka akaisakanykape ka lukany, kwape ejaar esekelloka aibois nakalaunan: 51Kiiaik Musa Aaron ka odweke apesan nulakunet, kwape ajaar akirot naka EJAKAIT, kwape kieorakinia EJAKAIT Musa.
