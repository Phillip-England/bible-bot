Aimario 35

35
1 # Yos 21:1-42. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa kapuutona nuka Moab kariri naka Yorodan koYeriko atiar, 2#Leb 25:32,33; Mar 18:20-24; Ikis 18:1,2; 1 Siit 6:64; Yos 14:3,4; 21:2; Esk 45:1; 48:8. “Kicorak idwe luka Isirael koinakis Ilebin ireria luiboiononoto kotoma kairumetekee; iinakinete do Ilebin ironyia luelukunito ireria ŋul. 3Ibojenenete kesi koreria; ejaunos ironyiakee kanuka ibarenekec ka iborokee ka itianikec kere. 4Ironyia luka ireria luiinakinete yesi Ilebin, enyounete karwatat naka ere kiton kina ikubitin #35:4 ekubit itemi aojau bala 1’ 6’ elukumit alukun ere kere. 5Itemete kina naka ere ikubitin ilukumin iarei kari nakokide, ikubitin ilukumin iarei kari naka eŋalakimak, ikubitin ilukumin iarei kari nakoto, ka ikubitin da ilukumin iarei kari naka agolitomei, kojai ere kidin, ŋul kes ironyiakec luka ireria. 6#Ikis 4:41; Yos 20:2; 21:3; Sab 9:9; 62:7,8; Isa 4:6. Ireria luiinakinete Ilebin koraas ireria ikanykape lukuianara, luiinakinete ekaaran ituŋanan akeriar: iyatakinete ireria akaisaoŋon ka luarei neeiaas ŋul. 7#1 Siit 6:54-81; Yos 21:41. Ireria kere luiinakinete Ilebin imwaasi ireria akaisaoŋon ka lukanykauni: iinakinete kes ka ironyiakec. 8#Yos 21:3; Any 16:18; Mar 26:54; 33:54. Ireria luiinakinete kairumet naka idwe luka Isirael, kaneejaas luipu ilemarete luipu; kaneejaas luikidioko da ilemarete luikidioko: ninitunanan kwape ejaar airumeteke nairumi einakini Ilebin ireriake.”
9 # Ikis 19:2-4; Yos 20:1-9. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 10#Ikis 19:2; Yos 20:2. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Kidokata Yorodan adolore akwap naka Kanaan, 11#Any 21:13. Iseunete ireriakus lueraunos ireria lukuianara; tetere ekaaran loeari ituŋanan komam epelet ekereari ŋina. 12#Ikis 19:6; Yos 20:3. Eraunos ireria luikuianarete yesi kaneejai ekenyaŋaran; tetere mam ekaaran etwani, aitodol naebwoia kakonyen nuka ekodet kanuka atubokinio. 13Ireria luiinakinete eraunos kamakus ireria ikanykape lukuianara. 14#Ikis 4:41; 2 Kor 8:13,14. Iinakinete ireria iuni kajuwai naka Yorodan, iinakinete da ireria iuni kakwap naka Kanaan; eraunos ireria lukuianara. 15#Mar 15:16. Eraunos ireria nul ikanykape luekuianarete idwe luka Isirael, imoit da, ka ikeboiororon kotomakec: tetere ninituŋanan yeneari ituŋanan komam epelet ekereari ŋina.
16 # Any 21:12,14; Leb 24:17; Ikis 19:11,12. Konye arai esenit ŋes kibore kayenika asuat, tape do etwani, erai ŋesi ekaaran; abeit eario ekaaran da. 17Arai da kitin amoru naepedori aiar ituŋanan asenio ŋes, kotwana, erai ŋesi ekaaran: abeit eario ekaaran da. 18Arai da esenit ŋes kakitoi naepedori aiar ituŋanan kotwana, erai ŋesi ekaaran: abe it eario ekaaran da. 19Ekenyaŋaran aokot elopet eari ekaaran: nairiamakinotor kaŋes, eari. 20#Age 4:8; 2 Sam 3:27; 20:10; 1 Ika 2:31; Luk 4:29; Any 21:14; Ikis 19:11; 1 Sam 18:10,11,25; 20:1; 23:7,8; Sab 10:7-10; 30:7,8. Arai eremorit ŋes kanuka alait, arai ibirit ŋes, koŋopikinite, tape etwani; 21Arai esenit ŋes kakan kanuka asurup, tape etwani; abeit eario ŋol lokeseni ŋes; erai ekaaran: eari ŋes ekenyaŋaran aokot nairiamakinotor kaŋes.
22Konye arai eremorit ŋes komamei asurup, arai ibirit ŋes kidiobore komam enopikinit, 23Arai kadiomoru naepedori aiar ituŋanan, komam eanyunit ŋes, kibirio ŋes tape etwani, komam erai lokasurupike, karaida loemootor ŋes aronis: 24#Yos 20:6. Etubokini ekodet ekasenon ka ekenyaŋaran aokot kwape ejaatar itubesia lu: 25#Pes 1:7; Any 29:7; Leb 4:3; 21:10. Eyuuni ekodet ekaaran kakan naka ekenyaŋaran aokot, inyakari da ekodet ŋes oreke loakuiarit loakerearit: iboienenei kenan aitodol naetwania esasedoti lopeti, loinyonyoikinitai akinyet nakalaunan. 26Konye arai kilam ekaaran eikor loka ereke loakuiarit loakerearit; 27#Any 22:2. Kosodi ekenyaŋaran aokot adumakin ŋes kina keikor loka ereke loakuiarit, kosodi ekenyaŋaran aokot ajar ekaaran; emamuun ŋes akirot kanuka aokot; 28Naarai tiebusakinit je asalakinit oreke loakuiarit aitodol naetwania esasedoti lopeti; konye akaulo katwanare naka esasedoti lopeti, eboŋori ekaaran akwapuke naka airumeteke.
29 # Mar 27:11. Eraunos akiro nun ekisil loka etube kamakus anatar aturiokus kotoma aiboisiokus kere. 30#Ikis 17:6; 19:15; Mat 18:16; 2 Kor 13:1; 1 Tim 5:19; Bur 10:28; Apu 11:3. Ŋin yeneari ituŋanan eario ikaaran kakirot naka ikajenak: konye mam ituŋanan eario kanuka akirot naka ekajenan ediopet bon. 31Sirikijaut egelit kanuka aiiar naka ekaaran loebeit atwanare: konye abeit eario. 32#Asw 4:12; Gal 2:21. Sirikijaut egelit kanuka ŋol loekerit oreke loekuiarit, abonunio aiboie akwap, aitodol naetwania esasedoti. 33#Age 4:9-12; Leb 18:25; Ikis 21:23; Sab 106:38; Isa 26:21; Kos 4:2,3; Mik 4:11; Age 9:6. Kwaŋin do mam imunaunete akwap naiboiononoto: naarai emunaarete aokot akwap: mam akwap epedori alueio kanuka aokot nuebukorio kotoma, dimarai kaokot nuka ŋol kaloabukori kes, 34#Leb 18:25; Ikis 21:23; Any 29:45,46; Mar 5:3; Sab 76:2; Isa 8:18; Kos 9:3; 2 Kor 6:16. Sirikimuna da akwap naiboiononoto, naeboienenei eoŋ kokidin; naarai Eoŋ EJAKAIT eboienenei kokidin kidwe luka Isirael.”
