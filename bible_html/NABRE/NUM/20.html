Aimario 20

20
1 # Mar 33:36; 26:59; Any 15:20. Kodoloto idwe luka Isirael, ekodet kere, amodiŋ naka Sin kolap losodit: kiboikinos ituŋa Kades; kotwanik Miriam nen, kidaikinai da nen. 2#Any 17:1-7; 16:2,7-12; Mar 16:19,42; 21:5; 1 Kor 10:10. Komamikis ekodet akip: kotukonokinos nepepe aluikin Musa ka Aaron, 3#Mar 14:2; Any 17:2; Mar 11:1,33; 14:37; 16:32,33,49; Aku 4:9. Kitoŋolopata ituŋa Musa, kobalas ebe, “Tiejok arai katwakito naatwakiata iinaeanakosi kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT! 4#Any 5:21; 17:3; Mar 11:5; Sab 106:21; Asw 7:35,40. Kanukinyo iyauniata atukot naka EJAKAIT amodiŋ na, atwanikin ne, isio ka ibarenekosi da? 5Kanukinyo kitolomuniata sio koMisiri, ayaun sio aibois na nakaronon? emameete aibois na ikinyom, karaida itinin, karaida imusabibun, karaida akamamagan; emameete da adisikip numatan.” 6#Mar 14:5. Konyoutu Musa ka Aaron kakonyen nuka ekodet kolototo okek loka ewema loka airiamunun, kibiroros ebuburia: kitaanyikin kes aibuses naka EJAKAIT, 7Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 8#Any 17:5; Nek 9:15; Sab 78:15,16; Isa 43:20. “Kolem ebela, kotukok ekodet, ijo ka Aaron onacekon, kinerata yesi katiiba kakonyenekee, ebe koinak akekipi; iinuni do kes akipi nuelomunete katiiba: kwaŋin iinakini ekodet ka ibarenekee aimat.” 9#Mar 17:10. Kolem Musa ebela kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kwape kicorakinia.
10 # Sab 106:33. Kotukokis Musa ka Aaron ekodet kakonyen nuka atiiba, kotema kes atiar, “Aso kopuputu, yes lukepali; kitolomuni isio akipi kati iba kana kanukus?” 11#Yak 1:20; Any 17:6; Ikis 8:15; 1 Kor 10:4. Kikeu Musa akekan, kinom atiiba kekebela irwan aarei; kolomut akipi nuipu, komat ekodet ka ikecebaren, 12#Mar 27:14; Ikis 3:26; Leb 10:3; Esk 20:41; 36:23; 1 Pet 3:15. Kobala EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron ebe, “Naarai mam ipotu kiyuut eoŋ, aitalaun eoŋ kakonyen nuka idwe luka Isirael, kanuka ŋun mam yes itolomarete ekodet lo toma akwap naainak kes.” 13#Sab 95:8. Ŋun kes akipi nuka Meriba;#20:13 Meriba Apoiouke Aŋolopa. naarai apotu idwe luka Isirael kitonolopata EJAKAIT, kitodik akelaunit kotomakec.
14 # Tub 11:16,17; Ikis 23:7; Oba 1:10,12. Kijuka Musa ikiyakia anyoun koKades alosit mama ejai ekabaka loka Edom atiar, “Ebala kwanata onaeekon Isirael ebe, Ŋeni aitaaritoi kere naapotu kajaunotor: 15Kwape ejesarotor lukapapaakosi toma Misiri, keboioto kotoma Misiri ikar luipu; kiticanata da Imisirian sio ka lukapapaakosi; 16#Any 2:23; 3:7; Asw 7:35. Ido nakimonyia mama ejai EJAKAIT, abu kopup ekosiporoto, kijuk emalaika, kitolom sio koMisiri: ido ko any, kijai toma Kades, ere loejai osanc loka eikorukon: 17#Mar 21:22. Kenaiijait, koeamak ketoroto toma akwapukon: mam sio kitori toma omanikoria karaida omanikoria luka emusabibu, ido mam kimasi akipi nuka atanin: kideri erot loka eka baka, mam kipaari akan nakoteten karaida akan nakokediany, aitodol nekituboror koikorukon.” 18Kotema Edom ŋes atiar, “Mam itori tomaka, kenye asurokin jo kekilen.” 19#Ikis 2:6,28. Kotemasi idwe luka Isirael atiar, “Kideri isio erot lokapolon: arai kiimat isio akonikipi, eoŋ ka ibareneka, kataeakini jo etiaiakee: koeamakinai eoŋ ketor kakejeneka, komam eswamai ieebore kere.” 20#Age 27:41; 32:6; Tub 11:17; Sab 120:7; Esk 35:5,11; Amo 1:11. Kotema atiar, “Mam itori.” Kolom Edom ka ituŋa luipu asurokin ŋes kakan nakagogoŋ, 21#Ikis 2:27,29; Tub 11:18; Rom 12:18; Bur 12:14. Kimik kwaŋin Edom Isirael aitor oikoruke: kanuka ŋun kibelokin Isirael anyoun kaneajai ŋesi.
22 # Mar 33:37; 21:4. Kolototo anyoun koKades: kodoloto idwe luka Isirael, ekodet kere, moru Kor. 23Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron komoru Kor, kariri naka eikor loka akwap naka Edom atiar, 24#Age 25:8; Mar 27:13; Isa 57:1,2; Bur 12:23. “Eyanaario Aaron mama ejaas iketuŋa: naarai mam elomari toma akwap naainak idwe luka Isirael, naarai ipotu kipaliikis akakirot kakipi nuka Meriba. 25#Ikis 32:50. Aso koyana Aaron ka Eleasar okokuke, kitodoka kes moru Kor: 26Kolae inapen luka Aaron, kitanap kes Eleasar okokuke: eyanaario Aaron mama ejaas iketuŋa, etwanikini nina.” 27Kiswama Musa kwape kicorakinia ŋes EJAKAIT: kodokasi moru Kor kakonyen nuka ekodet kere, 28#Any 29:29; Mar 33:38; Ikis 10:6; Mar 23:10; Yob 30:23; Awa 14:32; Kos 13:14; Apu 14:13. Kolaca Musa inapen luka Aaron, kitanap Eleasar okokuke; kotwanik Aaron nen kuju moru: kodokut Musa ka Eleasar komoru. 29#Age 50:3,10; Ikis 34:8. Keanyu ekodet kere ebe atwana Aaron, kibworoto lukokek loka Isirael Aaron apaarasia akaisauni.
