Mika 7

7
1 # Yer 2:3; Kos 9:10. Wu okwe! Naarai asubit bala naedaunere atukokin araito nuka aiporo,
Bala naedaunere aide emusabibu:
Emamei aibuŋet naanyamat;
Karaida etini loegeit akonore loimiela ekatau.
2 # 1 Sam 24:11; Sab 57:6; Kbk 1:15. Aolio innjanan yeneyoŋit Edeke kakwap,
Emamei da yenitenara etau kotuna:
Eperunete kere aiar;
Emejai ŋinituŋanan onaceke keboi.
3 # Kos 4:18; Isa 1:23. Ejaas akecekanin mama ejai aronis, Ekukuraka aswam;
Ilipi ekapolon, ekoto ekatubon eropit;
Btunanan loepol ŋes da inera nuka aiŋobel nakaronon nakotauke; Kwaŋin ewojiete nepepe.
4Loejok kakesi erai ekodokodoi;
Loitenara aitelekar luce erono kwape abwas naka ikukwa. Adolu apaaran naka ikedarakakon, ŋes aiticaniokon;
Kwana ebuonokinos.
5Sirikimunokinos ipaper,
Sirikigonakinos ekenarenon;
Kidar ikekia luka aitukukon
Kaneejai ŋin naeperi atorobukon.
6 # Age 9:22-24; Mat 10:35-36; Luk 12:53. Naarai itisili okoku papake,
Enyoikini akoku totoke,
Aberu naka okoku eluikini tataake;
Lukasurupo luka ituŋanan kes ituŋa lukokaleke.
7Konye kanukan, aseseni eoŋ EJAKAIT;
Edareuni Edeke loka aitajarioka;
Kapupok Ekadeke eoŋ,
8 # Sab 37:5,6; Awa 24:17; Aku 4:21. Sirikibwoŋ kanukan, ijo lokasurupika;
Arai kebiror, anyouni;
Naeboikinor amuton,
Eraun EJAKAIT aica kamakaŋ,
9Atitinikim anyunyura naka EJAKAIT,
Naarai adiaka kamaken:
Aitodol nekapurokinia akakirot,
Itodolikini eoŋ etube,
Ketolomuni eoŋ toma aica;
Aanyuni da akedolit.
10Kosodi nakasurup aanyun gun,
Koraparito ileic nakabala eoŋ ebe,
“Ai bo ejai EJAKAIT Ekonideke?”
Elakara akakonye aanyun nin;
Ipudapudao ŋes kwape erit lokorotin.
11Apaaran naka aiduk arwatatinikon!
Kapaaran kaŋin italalaario eikorukon.
12Kapaaran kaŋin epote mama ijai
Anyoun koAsuria ka ireria luko Misiri,
Anyoun koMisiri da kiton Ocor,
Anyoun kaŋolol kiton aŋolol,
Koronat da kiton oron at.
13Konye esilior akwap kanuka ŋul kaluiboiete kotoma,
Kanuka araito nuka aswamisiokec,
14 # Mar 23:9; Ikis 33:28. Kitadak ikonituŋa kebelakon,
Esipan loka airumetekon,
Luibojete ilenokino,
Toma amagoro kidin ka Karumel;
Kodakasi kesi toma Basan ka Gilead,
Kwape kapaarasia nupeno,
15Kwape kapaarasia nuilomunia ijo kakwap nako Misiri Etodikini ŋes akiro nukaumokin,
16 # Isa 26:11. Eanyunete atekerin, irumete ileje,
Kanuka agogonukec kere:
Ebunete aitukekee,
Imaiinikinos akiikec.
17Emeorete apua kwape kemun;
Elomunete emaranete kaiboisiokee nunopis,
Kwape lueliatos kakwap:
Epote kaiyor; mama ejai EJAKAIT Ewokodeke,
Ekuriakaros kanukon.
18 # Any 15:11; 34:6,7; Sab 89:6-8; 103:9; Isa 57:16. Iŋai bo Edeke loiputos kaio,
Loisioniei numam adolit,
Loinaci aibucar naka aatoŋot naka airumeteke?
Mam enyunyuranakinenei ikar kere,
Naarai isiyalamikinit aitim,
19 # Rom 6:6. Ibelokin bobo ŋesi, itimi;
Icakaeakai numam adolitewok kokwap kakejeneke.
Icakari ijo aronisiokee kere ataro naka aŋolol.
20 # Rom 11:29. Itodolikini abeit mama ejai Yakob,
Ka aitim da mama ejai Aburaam,
Nuibu kiyapoik lukapapaakos
Ameun kapaarasia nupeno,
