Ilebin 23

23
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Any 32:5; Mar 10:2,3,10; 2 Siit 30:5; Yok 1:14; 2 Ika 10:20; Sab 81:3. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Ipucin lueseunitai luka EJAKAIT, luilimorete araun atukokineta nukalaunak, lupe lu kes ipucinoka lueseunitai. 3#Any 20:8-10; 23:12; 31:15; 34:21; 35:2; Ikis 5:12-14; Luk 13:14; Nek 13:22; Isa 56:2; 58:13; Esk 20:12. Iswamaete aswam apaarasia akanykape: konye apaaran nakikanyetekaarei ŋes nakasabato naka aibworar, atukokinet nakalaunan; siriswamaete adioswam kere; erai nakasabato mama ejai EJAKAIT kaiboisiokus kere.
4 # Any 23:14. Lu kes ipucino lueseunitai luka EJAKAIT, atukokineta nukalaunak, luilimonorete kapakiokec. 5#Any 12:1-13; Ikis 16:1-2; Mar 28:16; Yos 5:10. Olap losodit, kapaaran nakitomonet kaoŋon naka elap eboŋ, ŋes aleleŋ naka EJAKAIT. 6#Any 12:14-20; 23:15; 34:18; Ikis 16:3-8. Kapaaran nakitomonet kakany naka elap kaŋol ŋes epucit loka imugatin lumam itubuunitoi mama ejai EJAKAIT: inyamenenete imugatin lumam itubuunitoi apaarasia akanykaarei. 7#Any 12:16; Mar 28:18; Ikis 16:1. Kapaaran nasodit itukonokinos atukokinet nakalaunan: siriswamaete aswam, naelaŋir. 8Konye iinanakinete ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT apaarasia akanykaarei: kapaaran nakikanyetekaarei ŋes atukokinet nakalaunan; siriswamaete aswam naelaŋir.”
9Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 10#Any 23:16; Mar 15:2,18; Ikis 16:9; Yos 3:15; Awa 3:9; Rom 11:16; 1 Kor 15:20; Yak 1:18; Apu 14:4. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Kidoloto yesi akwap nakainakini yes, isakete nuke da, ŋes konyet iyaŋauniata eisuta loka nugeet nuka aisakakus neejai esasedoti: 11#Any 29:24; Leb 9:21; 10:14. Ijaŋi ŋesi eisuta kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, tetere ecamunio kanukus: kapaaran naetupakini nakasabato ŋes ijaŋia esasedoti eisuta. 12Kapaaran naijaŋiata yesi eisuta, iinakinete imerekek yenikiliokit yenimam eŋwalit yenika ekaru ediopet araun ainakinet naicweio mama ejai EJAKAIT. 13Ainakineteke naka akiria eraun iarei kotomon luka epa#23:13 epa itemi alaŋir. loka akiria nuidicic nuinyalakinitai akinyet, ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT araun asakat naeuru saa: ainakineteke da namatat eraun naka ebino, idiopet kooŋon yenika ekin.#23:13 ekin itemi nueliyak. 14Mam inyamete emugati, karaida imomwa luikawooritai, karaida aluŋuren nukitetiak aitodol apaaran ŋipeŋin, aitodol naiyaŋauniata ainakinet naka Edekekus: erai ekisil lomam edaun anatar aturiokus kaiboisiokus kere.
15 # Any 23:16; 34:22; Ikis 16:9-12; Leb 25:8. Imarete yesi agear kapaaran naetupakini nakasabato, agear kapaaran naiyaŋauniata eisuta loka ainakinet najiaŋio; itodolete asabaton akanykaarei cut: 16#Asw 2:1. Imarete apaarasia akaisakany aitodol apaaran naetupakini nakasabato nakikanyetekaarei; iinakinete do ainakinet naka akiria nuka agwei mama ejai EJAKAIT. 17#Any 23:16; 22:29; Mar 15:17-19; Ikis 26:1,2. Itolomunete kaiboisiokus imugatin iarei lujiaŋio luka iarei kotomon luka epa: eraunos luka akiria nuidicic, ipeete kes kaluitubuunete, araun nugeet mama ejai EJAKAIT. 18Iinakinete imerekekin ikanykaarei ludidik lumam eŋwalito luka ekaru ediopet nepepe kimugatin, ka emoŋ lotobait, ka imerekekin iarei: eraunos ainakinet naicweio mama ejai EJAKAIT, nepepe kainakinetekec naka akiria, ka ainakinetakec numatan, ainakinet naeinakinere kakim, naka asakat naeuru saa mama ejai EJAKAIT. 19#Leb 4:23,28; Mar 28:30. Iinakinete da ekoroi ediopet araun ainakinet naka adiakaret, ka imerekekin iarei lukiliok luka ekaru ediopet araun asubanit naka ainakineta nuka ainapakin. 20#Mar 18:12; Leb 7:31-34; Ikis 18:4. Ijaŋi esasedoti kes nepepe kemugati loka nugeet araun ainakinet naijaŋio mama ejai EJAKAIT, nepepe kimerekekin luarei ludidik; eraunos lukalaunak mama ejai EJAKAIT luka esasedoti. 21Ilimorete kapaaran ŋipeŋin; ejaun atukokinet nakalaunan mamakus: siriswamaete aswam naelaŋir: erai ekisilomam edaun kaiboisiokus kere anatar aturiokus.
22 # Leb 19:9-10; Ikis 24:19-22; Any 23:11; Ikis 15:1-18; Yob 20:19; Sab 112:9; Awa 14:31; Isa 58:7,8; Yak 2:1; Rus 2:15,16. Naisakiata yesi nuka akwapukus, sirikisakata cut agoria nuka emanikorikon, karaida aituk nuedepanara nuka aisakakon: inyeinia lukican, ka imoit: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.”
23Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 24#Mar 10:10; 29:1; Leb 25:9. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Olap lokikanyetekaarei, kapaaran nasodit naka elap ejaun aibworar nakayamakina kamakus, aisiitunet naka aikut arupepen, atukokinet nakalaunan, 25Siriswamaete aswam naelaŋir: iinakinete ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT.”
26 # Leb 16:29-34. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 27#Any 30:10; Leb 16:30; Mar 29:7; Isa 58:3,5; Dan 10:3,12. “Konye kapaaran nakitomonet naka elap lo lokikanyetekaarei ŋes apaaran naka ailuc: eraun atukokinet nakalaunan mamakus, kitakadikis ikusitauon, iinakinete ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT. 28Siriswamaenenete adioswam kapaaran kaŋin: naarai erai apaaran naka ailuc, aitulucio yes kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 29#Age 17:14; Leb 13:46; Mar 5:2; 2 Siit 26:21; 2 Ses 3:6. Naarai ŋolitau kere lomam itakadikina kapaaran kaŋin, egelario kotuŋake. 30Ŋinituŋanan da yeniswamai adioswam kapaaran kaŋin, agelari eoŋ ituŋanan ŋin kotuŋake, 31Siriswamaenenete adioswam: erai ekisil lomam edaun anatar aturiokus kaiboisiokus kere. 32Eraun mamakus nakasabato naka aibworar nakayamakina, kitakadikis ikusitauon: kapaaran nakikanyetekaoŋon naka elap eboŋ, agear eboŋ kiton eboŋ, idarete nakasabatokus.”
33 # Ikis 16:13-15. Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 34#Any 23:16; 34:22; Mar 29:12; Ikis 16:13; Esr 3:4; Nek 8:14; Sek 14:16; Yok 7:2; Bur 11:9. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Kapaaran nakitomonet kakany naka elap kala lokikanyetekaarei, ŋes epucit loka iswamin adaun apaarasia akanykaarei mama ejai EJAKAIT. 35Kapaaran nasodit ejaun atukokinet nakalaunan: siriswamaete aswam naelaŋir. 36#Mar 29:35; 2 Siit 7:8,9; Nek 8:18; Yok 7:37. Iinakinete ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT apaarasia akanykaarei: kapaaran nakikanyetekauni ejaun atukokinet nakalaunan mamakus; iinakinete ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT: erai atukokinet nakayamakin; siriswamaete aswam naelaŋir,
37Ŋul kes ipucin lueseunitai luka EJAKAIT, luilimorete yesi araut atukokineta nukalaunak, ainakin ainakinet naeinakinere kakim mama ejai EJAKAIT, ainakinet naicweio, ka ainakinet naka akiria, ka asubanit, ka ainakineta numatan, ŋiniinakinet kapaaranike. 38Kogelara nukasabaton nuka EJAKAIT, kogelara da ainakinetakus, kogelara aiyaposiokus, kogelara da ainakinetakus nuka akot, nuiinanakinete EJAKAIT.
39 # Any 23:16; Ikis 16:13; Mat 21:8. Konye kapaaran nakitomonet kakany naka elap lokikanyetekaarei, kidaut atukonokin araito nuka akwap, idarete yesi epucit loka EJAKAIT apaarasia akanykaarei: kapaaran nasodit ejaun aibworar nakayamakina, kapaaran nakikanyetekauni da ejaun aibworar nakayamakina. 40#Nek 8:15; Ikis 16:14; Isa 35:10; 66:10; Rom 5:11. Kapaaran nasodit iyaŋaarete araito nuka ikito luejok, atenuna nuka idukudukun, ka atenuna nuka ikito lueburok, ka ikito lukaguurut; iyalamasi yesi kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT Ekusideke apaarasia akanykaarei. 41Kidarete ŋes araun epucit mama ejai EJAKAIT apaarasia akanykaarei ŋ: erai ekisil lomam edaun katuriokus: kidarete ŋes kolap lokikanyetekaarei. 42Kibojete koswamin apaarasia akanykaarei; kere luurikina koIsirael kibojete koswamin: 43#Ikis 31:13; Sab 78:5,6. Tetere ejenunete aturiokus ebe abu kesiboik idwe luka Isirael oswamin, naketolomunia eoŋ kes kakwap nako Misiri: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.”
44Abu Musa kolimok idwe luka Isirael ipucino lueseunitai luka EJAKAIT.
