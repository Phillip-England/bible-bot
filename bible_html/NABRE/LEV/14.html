Ilebin 14

14
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Mat 8:2,4; Mk 1:40,44; Luk 5:12,14; 17:14; Yer 15:19; Esk 44:23. “Lo ŋes ekisil loka yenikatapon kapaaran naka aitesegaarioke: eyaŋaunio mama ejai esasedoti: 3Elomari esasedoti kiŋa kiidukurun; irerei esasedoti, ido koany, arai aŋaleu adek naka atapon kotuŋanan; 4#Mar 19:6; Bur 9:19; Any 12:22; Mar 19:18; Sab 51:7. Kosodi esasedoti aicorakin alemikin ŋin yenekot aitesegaario akweny aarei nuelaete nuejarete, ka akito nuka eres, ka yenikareŋan, ka esop. #14:4 esop esubit bala emopim. 5Icorakini esasedoti aiar adiopet kakweny kotoma amot naka elupe kuju neeleleata akip: 6Akweny naejari da, eyaŋaari ŋes, ka akito nuka eres, ka yenikareŋan, ka esop, idomakini kes ka akweny naejari toma aokot nuka akweny naarakinitai kuju neeleleata akip: 7#Esk 36:25; Bur 9:13; 10:22; Leb 8:11; 2 Ika 5:10,14. Ebwatanakini ŋesi ŋin yenekot aitesegaario katapon irwan akanykaarei, elimokini ebe elai ŋesi, eosikini akweny naejari kais. 8#Leb 13:6; 11:25; Mar 5:2,3; 12:14,15; 2 Siit 26:20,21. Ŋin yenekot aitesegaario ilosi ikenapen, ebareni itimake kere, iloŋi kakip, eraun yenelai: kokau kaŋun elomari oidukurun, konye iboie kokiŋa kewemake apaarasia akanykaarei. 9Ejaun kapaaran nakikanyetekaarei ebe ebareni itimake kere kakouke, ka epenekike, ka abukioke, ebareni itimake kere: ilosi inapeneke, iloŋi akekuan kakip, eraun yenelai.
10 # Mat 8:4; Mk 1:44; Luk 5:14; Leb 2:1; Mar 15:4. Kapaaran nakikanyetekauni elemuni imerekekin iarei ludidik lukiliok lumam eŋwalitos, ka imerekek idiopet yeniberu yenika ekaru ediopet yenimam eŋwalit, ka iuni kotomon luka epa #14:10 epa itemi alaniru. loka akiria nuidicic araun ainakinet naka akiria, nuinyalakinitai akinyet, ka atido naka akinyet. 11Esasedoti loitesegi ibwaikini ituŋanan yenekot aitesegaario, ka iboro ŋul kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, kokek loka ewema loka airiamunun: 12#Leb 5:2-6; 6:6,7; Any 29:24. Elemuni esasedoti idiopet komerekekin ludidik lukiliok, kijaik ŋes araun ainakinet naka aronis, ka atido naka akinyet, ijaŋi kes araun ainakinet naijaŋio kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 13#Isa 53:7; Yok 1:29; 1 Pet 1:19; Apu 5:6; Any 29:11; Leb 1:5; 7:7; 2:3; 7:6. Earakini imerekek yenididi yenikiliokit aibois nearanakina ainakinet naka adiakaret ka ainakinet naicweio, aibois nakalaunan: naarai kwape eraar ainakinet naka adiakaret naka esasedoti, erai kwape kwaŋin ainakinet naka aronis: erai nakalaunan noi. 14#Pes 1:7; Kol 1:14; Bur 9:9-14; Apu 12:11; Any 29:20; Leb 8:23; Rom 12:1; 2 Kor 7:1. Elemuni esasedoti aokot nuka ainakinet naka aronis, ejukokini okobet loka akit nakoteten naka ŋin yenekot aitesegaario, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akekan nakoteten, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akejuke nakoteten. 15Elemuni esasedoti atido naka akinyet ebukokini otau kakanike elopet nakokediany: 16Idomakini esasedoti ebokoritike lokoteten akinyet naejai akanike nakokediany, ebwatanari akinyet kebokoritike irwan akanykaarei kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 17#Rom 6:13-22; 12:1; 1 Kor 6:20. Ejukokini esasedoti akinyet naedeun kakanike okobet loka akit nakoteten naka ŋin yenekot aitesegaario, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akekan nakoteten, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akejuke nakoteten, toma aokot nuka ainakinet naka aronis: 18#Leb 4:26; Mar 15:28; Dan 9:24; Rom 3:23-25; 5:11; 2 Kor 5:18,19; Pes 2:12-22; Kol 1:19-22; Bur 2:17; 9:9-14; 1 Yok 1:7; 4:10. Inyonyoikini akinyet naedeun kakan naka esasedoti akou naka ŋin yenekot aitesegaario; ituluci esasedoti ŋes kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 19Ijaikini esasedoti ainakinet naka adiakaret, ituluci ŋin yenekot aitesegaario kanuka numam alauniteke; kokau eari ainakinet naicweio. 20Ijaikini esasedoti ainakinet naicweio ka ainakinet naka akiria kotem: ituluci esasedoti ŋes, eraun yenelai.
21 # Leb 5:7; 12:8; Yob 34:19; Awa 17:5; 22:2; Mat 11:30; Luk 6:20; 1 Yok 5:3. Arai icana ŋesi, mam epedori adumun ŋun kere, eyaŋaari do imerekek idiopet yenikiliokit araun ainakinet naka aronis ajiaŋio, aitulucio ŋes, ka idiopet kotomon yenika epa loka akiria nuidicic nuinyalakinitai akinyet araun ainakinet naka akiria, ka atido naka akinyet; 22#Leb 12:8; 15:14,15; Luk 2:24. Ajeleik aarei arai ilukei iarei ludidik, kwape epedoria adumun; eraun idiopet ainakinet naka adiakaret, ka yenice da ainakinet naicweio. 23Kapaaran nakikanyetekauni eyauni kes kanuka aitesegaarioke mama ejai esasedoti, okek loka ewema loka airiamunun, kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 24Eyaŋaari esasedoti imerekek yenika ainakinet naka aronis, ka atido naka akinyet, ijaŋi esasedoti kes araun ainakinet naijaŋio kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 25#1 Ses 5:23; 1 Yok 1:7; Apu 1:5; 7:14. Eari imerekek yenika ainakinet naka aronis, elemuni esasedoti aokot nuka ainakinet naka aronis, ejukokini okobet loka akit nakoteten naka ŋin yenekot aitesegaario, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akanike nakoteten, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akejuke nakoteten. 26Ebukokini esasedoti akinyet otau kakanike elopet nakokediany: 27Ebwatanari esasedoti kebokoritike lokoteten akinyet naejai akanike nakokediany irwan akanykaarei kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: 28#1 Kor 6:11. Ejukokini esasedoti akinyet naejai akanike okobet loka akit nakoteten naka ŋin yenekot aitesegaario, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akanike nakoteten, kobokorit da lokapolon loka akejuke nakoteten, toma aibois naka aokot nuka ainakinet naka aronis. 29Inyonyoikini akinyet naedeun kakan naka esasedoti akou naka ŋin yenekot aitesegaario, aitulucio ŋes kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 30#Leb 15:15; Rom 8:3. Ijaikini idiopet kajeleik, arai kilukei ludidik kwape epedoria adumun; 31Kwape epedoria adumun, idiopet araun ainakinet naka adiakaret, ka yenice araun ainakinet naicweio, nepepe kainakinet naka akiria: ituluci esasedoti ŋin yenekot aitesegaario kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 32Ŋol ŋes ekisil loka ŋin yenedek atapon, yenimam epedori adumun luka aitesegaarioke.”
33Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron atiar, 34#Age 17:8; Mar 32:22; Ikis 7:1; 19:1; 32:39; Isa 45:7; Amo 3:6; Mik 6:9. “Naidolioto yesi akwap naka Kanaan, naainakini yes araun airumet, ebwaikini adek naka atapon kotogo loka akwap naka airumetekus; 35#Sab 91:10; Awa 3:33; Sek 5:4. Ebuni elopet etogo elimokini esasedoti ebe, ‘Esubit bala ejai adek naka atapon togoka.’ 36#Mar 19:18; Sab 51:7; Isa 52:11; 1 Kor 5:6,7; 15:33; 2 Kor 6:17; 2 Ses 3:6; 1 Tim 5:22; Apu 18:4. Icorakini esasedoti alemar iboro kere kotogo, eriŋa ŋes elomara aanyun adek, tetere luejaas toma kere mam eraunos lumam elaete: kokau elomari esasedoti aanyun etogo. 37Irerei ŋesi adek, ido koany, arai ejai adek arwatatin nuka etogo, acileta nupiria, arai nuereŋereŋeto, elomarit aputoke toma arwatat; 38ŋes konyet elomunia esasedoti kotogo kiton okek loka etogo, kogoli etogo apaarasia akanykaarei. 39Eboŋuni esasedoti apaaran nakikanyetekaarei, irerei; ido koany, arai kisiarakin adek arwatatin nuka etogo; 40#Yer 15:19; Esk 22:26; 44:23. Kosodi esasedoti aicorakin alemar amor nuejaas adek, acakar da aibois namam elai kiŋa naka ere. 41Itikisikisi ŋesi etogo toma airimor, ebukonorete nuadaut aikisikis kiŋa naka ere aibois namam elai: 42Elemarete amor ace ibwaikinete aibois naka amor kwa; eyauni asiŋe ace, iladai etogo.
43Arai keboŋ bobo adek, atakanun kotogo, kedau ŋesi alemar amor, kedau aikisikis etogo, kedau ailad; 44#Leb 13:51; Sek 5:4. Kosodi esasedoti alomar airere, ido koany, arai isiarakina adek togo, erai atapon naerogirog etogo: mam elai. 45Ekesi etogo, amoruke, ka akitoke, ka asiŋe kere naka etogo; edakari kes kiŋa naka ere aibois namam elai, 46#Leb 17:15; 22:6; Mar 19:7-10,21,22; 1 Kor 15:33. Bobo da ŋin yenelomari togo kapak kere naegololor eraun yenimam elai kiton eboŋ, 47ŋin yeneperi togo ŋol ilosi ikenapen; ŋin yenenyami kotogo kaŋol ilosi ikenapen.
48 # Ikis 32:39; Yob 5:18; Kos 6:1; Mk 5:29; Luk 7:21. Arai elomari esasedoti, irerei, ido koany, mam adek isiarakina togo, kedau ailad etogo; kosodi esasedoti alimokin ebe elai etogo, naarai aŋaleu adek. 49Eyaŋaari akweny aarei aitesegia etogo, ka akito nuka eres, ka yenikareŋan ka esop. 50Earakini adiopekweny amot naka elupe kuju neeleleata akip; 51#1 Ika 4:33; Sab 51:7. Eyaŋaari akito nuka eres, ka esop, ka yenikarenan, ka akweny naejari, idomakini kes aokot nuka akweny naarakinitai, toma akipi nueleleete da, ebwatanakini togo irwan akanykaarei: 52Ido itesegaari etogo kaokot nuka akweny, ka akipi nueleleete, kakweny naejari, kakito nuka eres, ka esop ka yenikaren an, 53Konye eyaŋaari akweny naejari kiŋa naka ere koosikini koporo ais; ituluci etogo kwaŋin: eraun loelai.”
54 # Leb 13:30. Ŋol ŋes ekisil loka epone kere loka adek naka atapon, ka etapitap; 55#Leb 13:47. Ka loka atapon naka anapito, ka loka etogo; 56#Leb 13:2. Ka loka abuunet, ka asalabit, ka aisorit: 57#Any 15:26; Ikis 4:6; 24:8; Sab 78:5; 119:96; Awa 6:23; Esk 44:23. Aisisianakin arai mam elai, arai da elai; ŋol ŋes ekisil loka atapon.
