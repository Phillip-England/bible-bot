Ilebin 13

13
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa ka Aaron atiar, 2#Ikis 28:27; Isa 3:17; Leb 10:10,11; Ikis 17:8,9; Esk 22:26; Mal 2:7; Mat 8:2-4; 10:8; Luk 17:11-16; Yok 13:8-10. “Ketakanu kamuny naka ituŋanan abuun, arai asalabit, arai aisorit, korau kamunyoke adek naka atapon, ŋes konyet eyaŋaunere neejai Aaron esasedoti, arai neejai ediopet kodweke isasedotin. 3Irerei esasedoti adek naejai amunyoke: arai kekwaŋu ijulot yenikotoma adek, aputo naka adek da elomarit toma amunyoke, erai adek naka atapon; irerei esasedoti ŋes, elimokini ebe mam ŋes elai. 4Arai kekwaŋu aisorit naejai amunyoke, aputoke da mam elomarit toma amunyoke, mam ijulotoke ekwaŋuuna, ŋes konyet egolokinio esasedoti ŋin yenejai adek apaarasia akanykaarei. 5Irerei esasedoti ŋes apaaran nakikanyetekaarei: ido koany, arai eanyuni ebe asal adek kwape kwaŋin, komam adek isiarakina amunyoke, ŋes konyet egolokinio bobo esasedoti ŋes apaarasia ace akanykaarei: 6#Leb 11:25; 14:8. Irerei esasedoti ŋes bobo apaaran nakikanyetekaarei: ido koany, arai kenyasuo adek, komam adek isiarakina amunyoke, elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe elai: erai asalabit: ilosi ikenapen, eraun yenelai. 7Konye arai kisiarakin asalabit amunyoke, kodaunite aitodiar mama ejai esasedoti kanuka aitesegaarioke, itodiari bobo mama ejai esasedoti: 8#2 Sam 3:29. Irerei esasedoti, ido koany, arai kisiarakin asalabit amunyoke, elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe mam elai: erai atapon.
9 # 2 Ika 5:3. Kedek ituŋanan atapon, eyaŋaunio mama ejai esasedoti; 10#Mk 1:44; Luk 5:14; 17:14; Mar 12:10,12; 2 Ika 5:27; 2 Siit 26:19,20. Irerei esasedoti, ido koany, arai ejai abuun naekwaŋ amunyoke, etakwaŋu ijulot, kotakanu akiriŋ naejon naejari kaneabuunit, 11Erai atapon naŋo kamunyoke, elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe mam elai: mam egolokini ŋes; naarai erai yenimam elai. 12Arai kisiarakin atapon amunyoke, arapiar amunyoke kere ageun kakou kiton akeje, kwape keanyanaria esasedoti; 13Kosodi esasedoti airere: ido koany, arai arapia atapon akuanake kere, elimokini ebe elai ŋin yenedeka: akwaŋu kere: elai ŋesi. 14Konye naetakanunia akiriŋ naejon, eraun yenimam elai, 15#Ikis 24:8. Irerei esasedoti akiriŋ naejonit, elimokini ebe mam elai: mam akiriŋ naejonit elai: erai atapon. 16#Luk 5:12-14. Arai kijulakin akiriŋ naejonit, akwaŋuun, elosi ŋesi neejai esasedoti, 17#Ikis 32:39; Sab 147:3. Irerei esasedoti ŋes: ido koany, arai kekwaŋu adek, elimokini esasedoti ebe elai ŋin yenedeka: erai yenelai.
18 # Any 9:9; 15:26; Sab 38:3-7; Isa 38:21; Ikis 28:27; 2 Ika 20:7; Yob 2:7. Arai kejau abus akuan toma amunyoke, aŋaleu, 19Toma aibois naka abus kejaun abuun naekwaŋ, arai aisorit, naekwaŋakwaŋat, naereŋereŋet, kosodi itodikinio esasedoti; 20Irerei esasedoti, ido koany, arai elomarit aputoke toma amunyoke, akwaŋu da ijulot, kosodi esasedoti alimokin ŋes ebe mam elai: erai adek naka atapon, elomunit kotoma abus. 21Konye arai kirere esasedoti, ido koany, emameete ijul lukakwaŋak kotoma, mam elomarit toma amuny, konye enyasuorit, egolokini esasedoti ŋes apaarasia akanykaarei: 22Arai kisiarakin amuny, kosodi esasedoti alimokin ŋes ebe mam elai: erai adek. 23#Awa 28:13; Mat 8:4; Luk 5:14; 17:14; 1 Kor 5:5; Gal 6:1; 1 Pet 4:2,3. Konye arai kesal aisorit aiboisike, komam isiarakina, erai apoore naka abus; elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe elai.
24Arai toma amunyoke ejai neenomorit akim, akiriŋ da naenomorit eluor aisorit, naekwaŋakwaŋat, naereŋereŋet, arai naekwaŋ; 25#Any 4:6,7; Mar 12:10; 2 Sam 3:29; 2 Ika 5:27; 2 Siit 26:19; Mat 8:1-3; Mk 1:40-42; Luk 5:12-14. Irerei esasedoti ŋes: ido koany, arai kekwaŋu ijulot kotoma aisorit, elomarit aputoke da toma amuny; erai atapon, elomunit kotoma anomoret: elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe mam elai: erai adek naka atapon. 26Konye arai kirere esasedoti, ido koany, emamei ijulot yenekwaŋ kotoma aisorit, mam elomarit toma amuny, konye enyasuorit; kosodi esasedoti agolokin ŋes apaarasia akanykaarei: 27#Leb 10:10; Yer 15:19; Esk 22:26. Irerei esasedoti ŋes apaaran nakikanyetekaarei: arai kisiarakin amuny, kosodi esasedoti alimokin ŋes ebe mam elai: erai adek naka atapon. 28Arai kesal aisorit aiboisike, komam isiarakina amuny, konye enyasuorit; erai abuunit naka anomoret, elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe elai: naarai erai apoore naka anomoret.
29Kedekakin ekiliokit arai aberu akou arai epenek, 30#Ikis 24:8; Mal 2:7; 1 Kor 12:9; Ikis 28:27; Isa 3:17. Irerei esasedoti adek: ido koany, arai elomarit aputoke toma amuny, kojaas tomake ijul ludos luideridera kosodi esasedoti alimokin ŋes ebe mam elai: erai etapitap, erai atapon naka akou arai naka epenek. 31Arai kirere esasedoti adek naka etapitap, ido koany, aputoke mam elomarit toma amuny, emamei ijulot yenikirionon kotoma, kosodi esasedoti agolokin ŋin yenedeka etapitap apaarasia akanykaarei. 32Apaaran nakikanyetekaarei irerei esasedoti adek: ido koany, arai mam etapitap isiarakina, emameete ijuludos, aputoke da mam elomarit toma amuny. 33#Yob 1:20; Rom 8:13. ŋes konyet ibarenio, konye mam ibareni etapitap; egolokini esasedoti ŋin yenedeka etapitap apaarasia ace akanykaarei. 34Kapaaran nakikanyetekaarei irerei esasedoti etapitap; ido koany, arai mam etapitap isiarakina amuny, aputoke da mam elomarit toma amuny; kosodi esasedoti alimokin ŋes ebe elai: ilosi inapeneke, eraun yenelai. 35Konye arai kisiarakin etapitap amunyoke kodaunite aitesegaarioke; 36Kosodi esasedoti airere ŋes: ido koany, arai kisiarakin etapitap amuny, mam esasedoti emoi ijuludos; erai yenimam elai. 37#Leb 10:10; Yer 15:19; Esk 22:26; 44:23. Konye arai eanyuni ebe asal etapitap, kelomut ijul lukiriokok kotoma; aŋaleu etapitap, elai ŋesi: elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe elai.
38Arai ejaas aisorito toma amuny naka akuan naka ekiliokit arai aberu, aisorito nukakwaŋak: 39Kosodi esasedoti airere; ido koany, arai ekwaŋakwaŋato aisorito nukotoma amuny naka akuanake; erai egwenyigweny, elomunit kamuny; elai ŋesi.
40 # Isa 15:2; Amo 8:10. Arai kidimas ituŋanan itima luka akouke, erai lokepata; konye elai ŋesi. 41Arai kidimas ituŋanan itima koinaren naka akouke, erai Iokepata koreet; konye elai ŋesi, 42Konye arai ejai adek naekwaŋakwaŋat naereŋereŋet kakou nakepata, arai koreet kalokepata; erai ŋin atapon, naelomunit kakouke nakepata, arai koreet kalokepata. 43#Leb 10:10; Esk 22:26. Kosodi esasedoti airere ŋes: ido koany, arai ekwaŋakwaŋat ereŋereŋet abuunet naka adek kakouke nakepata, arai koreeteke kalokepata, kwape ejaar aput naka atapon amuny; 44Erai ituŋanan yenikatapon, erai yenimam elai: abeit elimokini esasedoti ŋes ebe mam elai; ejai adekake akouke.
45 # Esk 24:17,22; Mik 3:7; 1 Ika 8:37; Yob 40:4; 42:6; Isa 6:5; Sab 61:1; 72:12; Luk 17:12,13; Apu 21:4. Ituŋanan yenedek atapon, ecilicila ikenapen, elakara itimake da, erapari eponoi lokokuju, iworoi ebe, ‘Mam alai, mam alai.’ 46#Mar 5:2; 12:14; 2 Ika 7:3; 15:5; 2 Siit 26:21; Luk 17:12; 1 Kor 5:5; 2 Ses 3:6; Bur 12:15. Apaarasia kere nuerokor edeka eraun yenimam elai; mam ŋes elai: iboienenei bon; iboienenei kokiŋa kiidukurun.
47 # Isa 59:6; 64:6; Esk 16:16; Sek 3:4; Rom 1:21-31; Yud 1:23. Anapito naejai adek naka atapon, arai anapito naka ajul, arai anapito naka ebaputa; 48Arai ewojitoi arai icipitai; naka ebaputa, arai naka ajul; arai komukule, arai toma ŋinibore yeniswamatai kemukule; 49Arai erai adek napiria arai naereŋereŋet kanapito, arai komukule, arai neewojitoi, arai neicipitai, arai toma ŋinibore yeniswamatai kemukule; erai adek naka atapon, ibusakinit aitodikin esasedoti. 50#Yer 15:19; Esk 44:23. Irerei esasedoti adek, egolokini yenejai adek apaarasia akanykaarei: 51#Leb 14:44; Esk 16:43. Irerei ŋesi adek apaaran nakikanyetekaarei: arai kisiarakin adek anapito, arai neewojitoi, arai neicipitai, arai komukule, aswam kere naetwasamaere emukule, erai adek naka atapon naiŋemiŋemi; mam elai. 52Icweikini ŋesi anapito, arai ewojitoi, arai icipitai, ajul, arai ebaputa, arai ibore kere yenika emukule, naejai adek: naarai erai atapon naiŋemiŋemi; icweikinio kakim.
53Arai kirere esasedoti, ido koany, mam adek isiarakina anapito, karaida neewojitoi, karaida neicipitai, karaida toma ibore kere yenika emukule; 54Kosodi esasedoti aicorakin ebe ilosete ibore yenejai adek, egolokini bobo apaarasia ace akanykaarei: 55Irerei esasedoti, kodaunite adek ailosio: ido koany, arai mam adek ilocokino kaputoke, komam adek isiarakina, mam ŋes elai: icweikini kakim: eŋemiŋemi, arai epata toma arai kiŋa,
56Arai kirere esasedoti, ido koany, enyasuoro adek kodaunite ailosio, kosodi acilar kanapito, arai komukule, arai neewojitoi, arai neicipitai: 57Arai eriŋa etakani kanapito, arai neewojitoi, arai neicipitai, arai kobore kere yenika emukule, elomunit: icweikini ibore ŋin yenejai adek kakim. 58Anapito da, arai naewojitoi, arai naicipitai, arai ibore kere yenika emukule, yenilosi, arai anyou adek kamake, ŋes konyet ilosere nakiareit, koraun yenelai.”
59Ŋol ŋes ekisil loka adek naka atapon kanapito naka ajul arai naka ebaputa, arai naewojitoi arai naicipitai, arai ibore kere yenika emukule, alimokin ebe elai, arai alimokin ebe mam elai.
