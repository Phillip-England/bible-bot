Ilebin 19

19
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Leb 11:44-45; 20:7,26; 1 Pet 1:16; Any 19:6; Pes 1:4; 1 Ses 4:7. “Kolimok ekodet kere loka idwe luka Isirael ebe, Koraas lukalaunak: naarai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke arai lokalaunan. 3#Any 20:8,12; 31:13; Ikis 5:12,16; Age 2:2; Nek 9:14; 13:15-21; Isa 56:2; 58:13; Mk 2:27; Bur 4:9. Koyoŋitenenei ŋinituŋanan kayesi totoke, ka papake, koricenenete asabatonoka: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke, 4#Leb 26:1; Any 20:3-5,23; 34:17; Ikis 27:15; 1 Kor 10:14. Sirikibelokinos neejaas aputosia, karaida aiswamaun bonik idekesio lucealara: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.
5Naiinakiniata asubanit naka ainakinet naka ainapakin mama ejai EJAKAIT, koinakis tetere kicamunio. 6Enyamio apaaran ŋin naiinakinete ŋes, ka moi da: arai kideun ibore kiton apaaran nakiwuniet, icweio kakim. 7Arai do enyamio kapaaran kanakiwuniet, erai naketal: mam ecamunio, 8Konye ibwoki ŋinituŋanan yenenyami ŋes numam adoliteke, naarai etisil ibore yenikalaunan yenika EJAKAIT: egelario ituŋanan ŋin kotuŋake.
9 # Leb 23:22; Ikis 15:1-18; 24:19-22; Any 23:11; Rus 2:15. Naisakiata nuka akwapukus, sirikisak cut agoria nuka emanikorikon, karaida aituk nuedepanara nuka aisakakon. 10#Sab 41:1; 140:12; Awa 14:31; 22:16; Lim 5:8; Isa 3:12-15; Yak 2:1-9. Siridepidepanari aideŋ emusabibukon, karaida atukonokin araito nuedoununete nuka emanikorikon loka emusabibu; inyeinia kes yenikican ka imoit: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.
11 # Any 20:15,16; Ikis 5:19,20; 1 Ika 13:18; Yer 9:3-5; Asw 5:3,4; Pes 4:25. Sirikokoenenete; karaida aiŋalaŋal, karaida asabanakin. 12#Any 20:7; Ikis 5:11; Mat 5:33. Siriyapoenenete aiŋal kolimito ekakiror, kosodi aitisil ekiror loka Edekekon: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
13 # Ikis 24:14-15; Mal 3:5; Yak 5:4. Siriŋara eidunyetekon, karaida ademar lukeŋ; sirikitepero eropit loka ekejaanakinan loiwotei akuare kere kiton tupuruc. 14#Ikis 27:18; Rom 14:13; 1 Pet 2:17. Sirikiken ikimin, karaida aibwaikin yeniguikini imuduk, konye koyoŋite Ekonideke: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
15 # Any 23:6-8; Ikis 16:19; Sab 82:2; Awa 24:23; Yak 2:9. Siritubenenete numam etenito: sirikinupak ituŋanan yenikican, karaida aiyoŋ ituŋanan yenikagogoŋon, konye itubonokini eidunyetekon kotenan. 16#1 Ika 21:13; Mat 26:60. Siririmio ebeele otuŋakon: karaida abwoanakin aluikin aokot nuka eidunyetekon: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
17 # Mat 18:15; Age 27:41; 1 Yok 2:9,11; 3:15; Luk 17:3. Sirikila onacekon kotaukon: abeit kitoroni ijo eidunyetekon, sirikibwok aronis kanuke. 18#Mat 5:43,44; 19:19; 22:39; Mk 12:31; Luk 10:27; Rom 13:9; 12:17; Gal 5:4; Yak 2:8. Sirikinyaŋ akirot, karaida adiŋakinit idwe luka ikonituŋa, konye komina eidunyetekon kwape iminari bon: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT. 19#Ikis 22:9-11. Koricenenete ikakisila. Sirikisunup ibarenekon akwar kaluegelegela; sirikiye omanikorikon ikinyom luka iponesio iarei: karaida ainap anapito naka iponesio iarei luka egoe luinyalinyalitai.
20Ŋolituŋanan loeyelo kaberu, naerai abulesit, naisinerit okilenike, namam cut elakunitai, karaida alacakinio; iticanio kes; mam eario, naarai mam ŋin alacakinitai. 21Eyauni ŋesi ainakineteke naka aronis mama ejai EJAKAIT, okek loka ewema loka airiamunun, ŋes emerekek araun ainakinet naka aronis. 22Ituluci esasedoti ŋes kemerekek loka ainakinet naka aronis kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT kanuka adiakareteke naadiakaa ŋesi; isionio akediakaret naadiakaa ŋesi. 23Kilomasi yesi akwap, kiraata da ikito luka iponesio kere lunyaman, imarakinete araitokec araun numam akepepukec: esubete bala numam ekepep kamakus adaun ikar iuni; mam enyamenenoo. 24#Ikis 12:17,18. Konye kokaru lokiwoŋonet eraunos araitokec kere nukalaunak, aiturio EJAKAIT. 25Kokaru lokikanyet inyamete yesi araitokec, tetere idumunete eiyasikec: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.
26 # Age 9:4; Leb 7:26-27; 17:10-14; Ikis 12:16,23; 15:23; 18:10; 2 Ika 17:17. Sirinyamenenete ibore kaokotoke: karaida aitwasam icudeta karaida aiŋic aanyuneta. 27#Leb 21:5; Ikis 14:1; Yer 9:26. Sirikikeba akwesikus, karaida amunaar iwaitin luka ipenekakus. 28#Ikis 14:1. Sirikigerata awatekus kanuka lukatwak, karaida aibwaikin aanyuneta: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
29 # Ikis 23:17. Sirikitisil akokukon aitaraun ŋes naadote; kinyek akwap aluor adoteu, ilelebun akwap aronis. 30#Leb 26:2; Lim 5:1. Koricenenete asabatonoka, koyoŋito aiboisika nakalaunan: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
31 # Ikis 18:11; 1 Sam 28:3; 2 Ika 23:4; Isa 8:19. Sirikibelokinos mama ejaas luejaas ajokia, karaida mama ejaas ikacudak; sirikimoata kes kinyeis amunaun: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.
32 # 1 Ika 2:19; Awa 20:29; 23:22; 1 Tim 5:1. Inyouni kakonyen nuka yenabwanut akorwo, kiyapaara kakonyen nuka ekimat, koyoŋ Ekonideke: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
33 # Any 22:21; Ikis 24:17-18; 27:19. Arai iboie imoit kajo kakwapukus, sirikiswamakis ŋes erono. 34#Any 12:48; Ikis 10:19. Eraun imoit yeniboie kayes kwape iuriat kamakus, komina ŋes kwape iminari bon; naarai iraas kolo imo kakwap nako Misiri: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke.
35 # Ikis 25:13-16; Awa 20:10; Esk 45:10. Siritubenenete numam etenito, aitem aojau, ainiŋ ka aitem etiai loilosikin. 36#Ikis 25:13,15; Awa 20:10. Aitemisio nukabeit, imisanin lukabeit, epa #19:36 epa itcmi alaŋir. lokabeit, ekin #19:36 ekin itemi nueliyak. lokabeit, ŋun kere itwasamaete: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke, loketolom yes kakwap nako Misiri. 37#Ikis 6:25; 5:1; Leb 18:4,5. Kidarenenete ikakisila kere ka itubesiaka kere, kiswamaenenete kes: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT,”
