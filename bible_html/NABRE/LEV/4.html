Ilebin 4

4
1Kotema EJAKAIT Musa atiar, 2#Leb 5:15,17; Mar 15:22; 1 Sam 14:27; Sab 19:12. “Kolimok idwe luka Isirael ebe, Arai kediaka ituŋanan komam ejeni, kakirot kere naecorakinit EJAKAIT ebe mam iswamao, kiswama adiopet kakesi: 3#Leb 8:12; 21:10; Bur 5:3; Leb 9:2. Arai kediaka esasedoti loinyonyoikinitai akinyet ayaŋaun abili mama ejaas ituŋa; koinak do ŋesi kanuka adiakareteke naabu kodiaka, emoŋ lotobait lomam eŋwalit mama ejai EJAKAIT araun ainakinet naka adiakaret; 4Eyauni emoŋ lotobait okek loka ewema loka airiamunun kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT; idokokini akanike akou naka emoŋ lotobait, earakini emoŋ lotobait akonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 5#Leb 16:14; Mar 19:4; Bur 9:13. Kosodi esasedoti loinyonyoikinitai akinyet alemun aceokot nuka emoŋ lotobait, eyaŋauni owema loka airiamunun: 6#Isa 42:21; Yok 8:29; Rom 3:24; 2 Kor 5:19; Pes 2:13; Apu 5:9. Idomakini esasedoti ebokoritike toma aokot, ebwatanari aokot irwan akanykaarei kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT koiŋaren kaigalisit naka aibois nakalaunan. 7#Leb 9:9; 16:18; Bur 9:21-25; Leb 5:9. Ejukonokini esasedoti aokot amumwara nuka etem loka lukisupurununo loejai owema loka airiamunun kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: ebukokini aokot kere nuka emoŋ lotobait kwap ketem loka ainakineta nuicweio, loejai okek loka ewema loka airiamunun. 8Elemari ŋesi asiepai kere naka emoŋ lotobait loka ainakinet naka adiakaret; asiepai naerapit lukotoma ka asiepai kere naejai toma, 9Ka iŋalur luarei, ka asiepaikec naeapias kaŋabet, ka asiepai naka emany, nepepe kaiŋalur, ŋul kere elemari, 10Kwape elemaria komoŋ loka asubanit naka ainakineta nuka ainapakin: icweikini esasedoti kes kotem loka ainakineta nuicweio. 11#Any 29:14; Mar 19:5. Emukule loka emoŋ lotobait, ka akiriŋike kere, nepepe kakouke, ka akejeke, ka lukotomake, ka ikijitike, 12#Leb 6:11; Bur 13:11. Emoŋ lotobait kere edakari kiŋa kiidukurun aibois naelai, neebukonokinio ekurwon, kicwe ŋes kakim kakito: neebukonokinio ekurwon, nepenen icweere.
13 # Mar 15:24; Yos 7:11; Leb 5:2-4,17. Arai kibuca ekodet kere loka Isirael, kibwaa da akirot kakonye nuka atukot, kiswamata akirot kere naecorakinit EJAKAIT ebe mam iswamao, egwaunete akiro: 14Naejenunor adiakaret naapotu kodiakasi, einakini atukot emoŋ lotobait araun ainakinet naka adiakaret, koyau kiŋaren kewema loka airiamunun, 15#Any 29:10-19; Leb 1:4; 3:2; Isa 53:6; Mat 8:17; Bur 9:28; 1 Pet 2:24. Idokokinete imakio luka ekodet akaninikec akou naka emoŋ lotobait kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: earakinio emoŋ lotobait akonyen nuka EJAKAIT. 16#Bur 9:12-14. Koyaŋau esasedoti loinyonyoikinitai akinyet aokot nuka emoŋ lotobait owema loka airiamunun. 17Idomakini esasedoti ebokoritike toma aokot, ebwatanari irwan akanykaarei kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT koiŋaren kaigalisit. 18Ejukonokini da aokot amumwara nuka etem loejai kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT, loejai owema loka airiamunun, ebukokini aokot kere kwap ketem loka ainakineta nuicweio loejai okek loka ewema loka airiamunun. 19Elemari ŋesi asiepaike kere, kicweik kotem. 20#Mar 15:25; Dan 9:24; Rom 5:11; Bur 2:17; 10:10-12; 1 Yok 1:7; 2:2. Iswamakini ŋesi emoŋ lotobait kwaŋin; kwape eswamakinia emoŋ lotobait loka ainakinet naka adiakaret, iswamakini lo kwaŋin: ituluci esasedoti kes, isionio kes. 21Edakari emoŋ lotobait kiŋa kiidukurun, kicwe ŋes kwape ecweia emoŋ lotobait losodit; ŋes ainakinet naka adiakaret naka atukot.
22 # Any 18:21; Mar 16:2; Esr 9:2; Asw 3:17; 2 Sam 21:1-3. Arai kediaka ekapolokiton, kiswama komam ejeni adiopet kakiro kere nuecorakinit EJAKAIT Ekedeke ebe mam iswamao, egwauni akirot; 23Arai kitejenun akediakaret naadiakaa ŋesi, eyauni ekoroi lomam eŋwalit araun ainakineteke. 24Idokokini akekan akou naka ekoroi, earakini aibois nearanakinio ainakinet naicweio kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT: ŋes ainakinet naka adiakaret. 25Elemari esasedoti aokot nuka ainakinet naka adiakaret kebokoritike, ejukonokini amumwara nuka etem loka ainakineta nuicweio, ebukokini aokotoke kwap ketem loka ainakineta nuicweio. 26#Leb 3:5; Mar 15:28. Icwei asiepaike kere kotem, kwape kasiepai naka asubanit naka ainakineta nuka ainapakin: ituluci esasedoti ŋes kanuka adiakareteke, isionio ŋes,
27 # Mar 15:27-28; Lim 7:20. Arai da kediaka ituŋanan kere kotuŋa luka akwap komam ejeni, kiswama akirot kere naecorakinit EJAKAIT ebe mam iswamao, egwauni akirot; 28Arai kitejenun akediakaret naadiakaa ŋesi, ŋes konyet eyaunia akinei naberu namam eŋwalit, araun ainakineteke kanuka akediakaret naabu kodiaka. 29#Leb 4:4,24. Idokokini akanike akou naka ainakinet naka adiakaret, earakini ainakinet naka adiakaret aibois naka ainakinet naicweio. 30Elemari esasedoti aokotoke kebokoritike, ejukonokini amumwara nuka etem loka ainakineta nuicweio, ebukokini aokotoke kere kwap ketem, 31#Leb 3:14; Any 29:18; Leb 1:9; Esr 6:10. Elemari da asiepaike kere, kwape elemaria asiepai naka asubanit naka ainakineta nuka ainapakin; icwei esasedoti ŋes kotem araun asakat naeuru saa mama ejai EJAKAIT: ituluci esasedoti ŋes, isionio ŋes.
32 # Isa 53:7; Yok 1:29; Asw 8:32; 1 Pet 1:19; Apu 5:6-14; 13:8. Arai keyau ŋesi imerekek araun ainakineteke naka adiakaret, eyauni yeniberu yenimam eŋwalit, 33Idokokini akanike akou naka ainakinet naka adiakaret, eari ŋes araun ainakinet naka adiakaret kaibois nearanakinio ainakinet naicweio. 34Elemari esasedoti aokot nuka ainakinet naka adiakaret kebokoritike, ejukonokini amumwara nuka etem loka ainakineta nuicweio, ebukokini aokotoke kere kwap ketem: 35#Leb 3:5; Dan 9:24; Rom 5:11; Bur 9:8-28; 1 Yok 1:7; 2:2. Elemari da asiepaike kere, kwape elemaria asiepai naka imerekek yenika asubanit naka ainakineta nuka ainapakin; icwei esasedoti ŋes kotem, kopone loka ainakineta nuka EJAKAIT nueinakinere kakim: ituluci esasedoti ŋes kanuka adiakareteke naabu kodiaka, isionio ŋes.
