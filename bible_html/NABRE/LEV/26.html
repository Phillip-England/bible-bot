Ilebin 26

26
1 # Any 20:4,5; Leb 19:4; Ikis 4:16-19; 5:8; 16:21-22; 27:1; Isa 44:9-20; Sab 97:7. Sirikiswamaut aputosia, karaida asipokin aput naegerer, arai epir, karaida asipokin amoru naegerer akwapukus, akukonokin ŋes; naarai arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke, 2Koricito asabatonoka, koyoŋito aiboisika nakalaunan: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
3 # Ikis 11:13-15; 28:1-14. Arai ilosenenete kokisilaka, koricito akaicorakineta kiswamata kes: 4#Ikis 28:12; Isa 30:23. Kasodi ainakin yes akiru kapakioke, erai akwap amioke, eraete ikito luka emanikor araitokec. 5#Ikis 11:15; Yoe 2:19,26; Yob 11:18; Esk 34:25. Edoli aicomokus toma aisakakus, edoli aisakakus roma apak naka aiye: inyamete atapakus kimonikinos, iboikinos akwapukus inapakina. 6#1 Siit 22:9; Isa 45:7; Sab 3:5; Yer 30:10; Yok 14:27; Rom 5:1; 2 Ika 17:25; Esk 5:17; 14:15,17; Yos 23:10. Ainakini eoŋ ainapakin kakwap, iperete, emamei yenitukuriani yes: etanaŋi eoŋ itiaŋ lueroko kakwap, komam ekileŋ itori akwapukus. 7Irukitotor lukasurupokus, eŋianakinete ekileŋ kakonyenekus. 8Erukitotor ikany kayesi akwatat, erukitotor akwatat kayesi ilukumin itomon: eŋianakinete lukasurupokus ekileŋ kakonyenekus. 9#Any 2:25; 2 Ika 13:23; Age 17:6,7. Etei eoŋ yes, etuuriari yes, etupui yes; etogogoŋi akaitutuket kayes, 10Inyamete luŋorok lueŋadakinitai sek, itolomunete Iuŋorok kanuka lukitetiak. 11#Esk 37:26; Ikis 32:19. Ebwaikini ewemaka lokalaunan kamakus: mam ekatau kisaci yes, 12#2 Kor 6:16; Pes 2:21; Bur 3:6; Esk 11:20. Aupenena eoŋ kayes, araun Ekusideke, yes da ikatuŋa. 13#Esk 34:27. Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke, loabu ketolom yes kakwap nako Misiri, kinyeis araut ibulesinikec; abu kabil akito nuka ajookotokus, ketolot yes kitenara.
14 # Ikis 28:15. Konye arai iŋerete apupun eoŋ, iŋerete da aswam aicorakineta nu kere; Arai igirete ikakisila, arai da esacito ikusitauon ikatubesia, tetere iŋerete aswam aicorakinetaka kere, konye ibilete aitutuketeka; 15#2 Ika 17:15. Eoŋ da keswamakini yes nu; ejukakini yes akurian, eluera ka ekamidi, luedopodopoete 16#Ikis 28:22; 1 Sam 2:33; Yob 31:8. akusikonye, lueinakinete ikusitauon aipirisiar: iyeete ikinyomokus atai, naarai enyamete lukasurupokus, 17#Tub 2:14; Sab 106:41; 53:5. Aluikin eoŋ yes, kidanyio kakonyen nuka lukasurupokus: lukilaito kiyaete yes; ikerete komam yenikirukitor yes.
18 # 1 Sam 2:5. Arai eriŋa iŋerito apupun eoŋ kanuka ŋun, kasodi aiyatakin alecor yes irwan akanykaarei kanuka adiakaretakus, 19#1 Sam 4:10; Isa 25:11. Abili eoŋ apedorikus naiturototor; etuluori akujukus kwape asuat, akwapukus da kwape aisinit: 20#Sab 127:1; Isa 49:4; Ikis 28:18; Kag 1:10. Edacari akusigogoŋ atai: naarai mam akwapukus erai amioke, karaida ikito luka akwap mam eraete araitokec.
21Arai ilosenenete yesi iŋuŋas keoŋ, iŋerete apupun eoŋ; eyatakini eoŋ ainun yes aiticaneta irwan akanykaarei kwape ejaatar adiakaretakus. 22#Ikis 32:24; 2 Ika 17:25; Yer 15:3; Tub 5:6; 2 Siit 15:5; Sek 7:14. Ejukakini yes etiaŋ loka ais, loidadaanari ikusidwe, emudiari ibarenekus, itidisiari etiaikus; esilokoros irotinikus,
23 # Yer 5:3. Arai iŋerete aboŋun kanuka ŋun kere, konye ilosenenete iŋuŋas keoŋ; 24#2 Sam 22:27. Eoŋ da alosi aŋuŋa kayes; adanyi eoŋ yes, eoŋ elopet, irwan akanykaarei kanuka adiakaretakus. 25#Esk 6:3; Ikis 28:22; Mar 14:12; 16:49; Amo 4:10. Kainuni yes ekileŋ, loinyaŋi aitutuket; itukonokinos oreriakus: kejukakini yes atular; kijaikinio yes akan naka lokasurup. 26#Isa 3:1; Mik 6:14. Arai kabil ebelakus loka emugati, epeete aiŋor atomon imugatinikus kokiala ediopet, ijaikinete kesi imugatinikus kaitemis: inyamete komam imonikinos.
27Arai iŋerete apupun eoŋ kanuka ŋun kere, konye ilosenenete iŋuŋas keoŋ; 28#Isa 59:18. Eoŋ da alosi aŋuŋa kayes kesiopite; alecori yes irwan akanykaarei kanuka adiakaretakus. 29#2 Ika 6:29. Inyamete akiriŋ naka odwekus, inyamete da akiriŋ naka adwekus. 30#2 Siit 34:3; 2 Ika 23:20; Sab 78:59. Amudiari aiboisiokus nuiceleleete, ajepejepei ipirinokus luka akoloŋ, acakakini atesinakus atesina nuka aputosiakus; esaci ekatau yes. 31#Nek 2:3; Yer 5:10; 22:5; Sab 74:1-8; Yer 26:6,9; Aku 1:10; Esk 9:6. Etuluori ireriakus luiswapito, etisiliori aiboisiokus nukalaunak, aŋeri aiŋuun asakat naka luejijimikus. 32#Yer 9:11; 25:11. Etisiliori akwap; eumokinete lukasurupokus luiboiete kotoma. 33#Ikis 4:27; 28:64; Sab 44:11; Esk 12:15; 20:23; Sek 7:14. Kesiarakini yes da atekerin, autuni ekileŋ atupakin yes: esilior akwapukus, iswapete ireriakus.
34 # Leb 25:2-4; 2 Siit 36:21. Kosodi akwap alelakin asabatonoke, apak kere naiswapitor, ka yesi da ijaunos kakwap naka lukasurupokus; ibworari do akwap, elelakini asabatonoke, 35Ibworari apak kere naiswapitor; aibworar namam abu kodumunite kasabatonokus, iroko ijaas yesi kotoma, 36#Age 35:5; Yos 2:9-11; Esk 21:7; Awa 28:1. ŋul da luedeunos kayesi, ainakini itauonokec aiilor kakwap naka lukasurupokec: erukitori kes airurum naka akiot naekusi ekwam; ekerete kesi, kwape ekeria etuŋanan ekileŋ; ibiroros komam yenerukitor. 37#Tub 7:22; Isa 10:4; Tub 2:14. Icakacakas bonik, kwape ekeriata ekileŋ, komam yenerukitor: emamuunete yes agogoŋ abwoikin lukasurupokus. 38Ioliorete kotoma atekerin, kinyami yes akwap naka lukasurupokus.
39 # Nek 1:9; Yer 3:25; 20:12. Luedeunos kayesi idokororaros kanumam adolitekec kakwapin nuka lukasurupokus; idokororaros da bala lukapapaakec kanumam adolitekec,
40 # Mar 5:7; 1 Ika 8:33; Yob 34:31; Awa 28:13; Yer 3:12; Luk 15:18; 1 Yok 1:9. Elimorete numam adolitekec; ka numam adolit nuka lukapapaakec, ka aibucaretakec nuapotu kibucasi kamakaŋ, bobo da naarai alototo aŋuŋa keoŋ, 41#Yer 9:25; Esk 44:7; Asw 7:51; Rom 2:29; Kol 2:11; 1 Ika 21:29; 2 Siit 12:6. Eoŋ da alot aŋuŋa kakes, abu ketoloma kes akwap naka lukasurupoke: arai kitasonokinos ikecetauon lumam ekepep, iyuunete aiticanet naka numam adolitekec. 42#Age 17:7-8; 26:3-4; 28:13-14; Any 2:24; 6:5; Sab 106:5; Esk 16:60; Sab 136:23. Kasodi eoŋ akiitun akaitutuket ka Yakob; ka akaitutuket ka Isaak, eituni da akaitutuket ka Aburaam; eituni da akwap, 43Ejalete kesi akwap, elelakini asabatonoke, kapak naeswapitor komameete kesi; iyuunete aiticanet naka numam adolitekec; naarai ajalakis ikatubesia, asacata ikecetauon ikakisila, 44#Ikis 4:31. Konye kaŋun kere, naejaatar kesi akwap naka lukasurupokec, mam eoŋ ajalakini kes, karaida mam asaci kes, amudiar kes cut, aibil akaitutuket kakes: naarai arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekecedeke: 45#Rom 11:28; Esk 20:9. Konye eituni eoŋ aitutuket naka lukapapaakec kanukec, luabu ketolom kakwap nako Misiri kakonyen nuka atekerin, tetere araun eoŋ Ekecedeke: arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.”
46 # Ikis 33:4; Yok 1:17. Ŋun kes aicorakineta ka itubesia, ka ikisila, luabu EJAKAIT kisilak tomake ka idwe luka Isirael komoru Sinai kamaka Musa.
