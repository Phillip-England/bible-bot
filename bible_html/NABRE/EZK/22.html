Esekiel 22

22
1Bobo da kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe, 2“Ŋo da, okoku loka etuŋanan, itubokini, itubokini kereka ijo ere loka aokot? Aso kitejenik ŋes italesiake kere. 3#Mik 6:16. Kinera ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Ere loebukokini aokot kidinike, tetere edolokini apakike, loiswamai aputosia amunaaror! 4#Age 9:6; 2 Ika 21:16; Sab 106:38; 44:13,14; Ikis 28:37; 1 Ika 9:7; 2 Siit 7:20; Yer 18:16; Dan 9:16. Igwaunit akirot naka aokot nuibukoo, imunaar kaputosiakon nuiswama; isukuny apaarasiakon, ido idolok okarukon, ŋes kainakini ketuluori amorosit katekerin, ka yenikisekelo kakwapin kere. 5Lukiapiatar, ka luelwaka, kisekelete, ijo loejai ekiror lokamunaara, ido ileleba autaar.
6 # Isa 1:23; Sep 3:3. “Koany, ikapolok luka Isirael, ninitunanan kwape eiaar apedorike, kejaenenas kotomakon abukor aokot, 7#Any 20:12; 22:21-22; Ikis 5:16; 24:17; 27:16. Kotomakon kemenenenata papa ka toto; kidinikon kiswamanakiniata ipejonon nukamwanir: kotomakon kenyamanakiniata kepali yenemamei papake ka apuserut, 8#Leb 19:30; 26:2. Imwan ikaboro lukalaunak, imunaa da akasabaton. 9Keiaenenas ituŋa Iukasabanakinak kotomakon abukor aokot: kotomakon da ŋes kenyameneniata koronan: kidinikon kiswamaeneniata nukadoteu. 10#Leb 18:19; 20:11; 1 Kor 5:1. Kotomakon kepukoriata amujalal naka lukapapakee: kotomakon kitisilioto aŋor numam alaete kanuka atiakarikec, 11#Yer 5:8. Ido ejai ioiswamai nuketal kaberu naka eidunyeteke; ido ejai eee loemunaari kadoteu aberu naka okokuke; ido ejai eee kotomakon loitisili kinacike, akoku naka papake. 12#Any 22:25; 23:8; Leb 25:36-37; Ikis 16:19; 23:19; 32:18; Sab 106:21; Yer 2:32; Esk 23:35. Kotomakon eropio kes abukor aokot; ibu kotukonok ameda aitedepar, idum amakira kama ejaas iidunyetakon kanuka areit; ido ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kimurwok eoŋ.
13 # Esk 21:17. “Aso do koany, atuari akakanin kamakirakon nakaimod naidum, kaokot da nukejaenenas kidinikon, 14#Esk 21:7; 17:24; Isa 31:3; 1 Kor 10:22. Epedori kereka ekonitau atitiŋikin, arai akonikanin ajaut kagogon, kapaarasia nukeswamakinia? Eoŋ EJAKAIT ener, eswamai da. 15#Ikis 4:27. Kesiarisiari jo katekerin, keweliweli kakwapin; etanaŋi da amodokokokon kotomakon. 16#Any 8:22; Sab 9:16. Kosodi imunaar kanukon, kakonyen nuka atekerin; ijenuni ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.”
17Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe, 18#Isa 1:22.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, arau ekek loka Isirael elele kamaka; eraas kesi kere aisinin, ka adebe, ka asua, ka asasin kokidin oupa; eraas kesi elele loka epuesa. 19Aso do inera kwana ta Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Naarai iraunos kere elele, asa do koany, katukonokini yes kidiŋ Yerusalem, 20Kwape etukonokinerc epuesa ka aisinin, ka asua ka asasin ka adebe kidin oupa, aicukokin akim, aisiealaria; katukonokini yes kwaŋin kakanyunyura, kakadedeŋ; kebwaikini nen, kesicalari. 21Eebo, katukonokini, keeukokini akim naka akanyunyura, icalaros yesi kokidinike, 22Kwape ecalaror epuesa kokidin oupa, iealaros yesi da kwape kwaŋin kokidinike; ijenunete ebe eoŋ EJAKAIT kabukok yes akadedeŋ.”
23Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 24“Okoku loka etuŋanan, kolimok ŋes ebe, Irai ijo akwap namam itesegario ido mam itetepio akiru kapaaran naka aisiop. 25#Kos 6:9; Yer 6:13; Mat 23:14; Asw 20:29; Mik 3:11; Sep 3:3. Enerut inabinoke kokidinike, kwape eŋatuny loegat naepoopoaa imejauna: anyamata aiiar naka ituŋa; anatata iboro luka akais nuipu ka luka etiai loepol; etupuata apuserunuke kokidinike, 26#Mal 2:8; 1 Sam 2:29; Leb 10:10. Epaliikis isasedotinike ikakisila, amunaasi ikaboro lukalaunak; mam etiakitos iboro lukalaunak ka luka apakio kere, mam isisianakinito atiaketait kotoma lumam elaete ka Iuelaetc, ebwaata akecekonye kasabatonoka, kamoromoroo eoŋ da kamakec. 27#Isa 1:23. Esubito ikapolokoke kokidinike bala ipeen luepoopoete imeiauna; abukor aokot, ka amudiar aijar, tetere imakiraete kaimod. 28#Esk 13:10. Ido eladaata inabinoke kanukee aitwakaŋ, eanyunete aamameak, edwarakinete kes nukainal, kobalas ebe, “Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE,’ kwabo mam EJAKAIT inerit. 29#Yer 5:26. Apotu ituŋa luka akwap komwana, kotupasi aiŋat; eebo, esiyaloŋoŋoto lukican ka luilwaritos, ido amwanata imoit atitai, 30#Yer 5:1; Age 18:23. Kamoote ituŋanan kotomakec, yeniketetean abwas, yenebwoi karorwa kakonyeneka kanuka akwap, kenyek amudiar; konye mam abu kaany imodio da. 31Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kanuka nun ijes abukokinia kes akaisiop; kadau kes kakim naka akanyunyura: etebeik ekecerot akwesikee.”
