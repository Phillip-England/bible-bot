Esekiel 32

32
1Kosodi kanen kokaru lokitomonet kaarei, kolap lokitomonet kaarei, kapaaran nasodit naka elap, kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 2#Esk 27:2; 19:3,6; 29:3; 34:18. “Okoku loka etuŋanan, kokuudianik Parao ekabaka loko Misiri, kolimok ŋes ebe:
Kitupusitoi kinatuny yenika atekerin:
Kwabo iputos kapiu naejai anolclin;
Ido ibeluni koeorinikon,
Iticani akipi kakejekon,
Komunaa ieorinikee.
3 # Esk 12:13. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
Kapesikini jo ekaboi
Katukot naka ituŋa luipu;
Kibwosunete kekaboi.
4 # 1 Sam 17:44-46; Isa 14:19; Yer 8:2; Esk 29:5; 39:4. Kenyeini jo kaneeoŋit,
Kaeakar kina ais,
Ketodoikini jo akweny kere nuka apak,
Kesimonikinia da itian luka alwap kere.
5 # Esk 31:12. Aloikini akonikirin oroŋan,
Elelebi aditon kamukururukon,
6Ido ecocoi akwap kaokotokon nuiduio
Kiton oroŋan;
Ilelebi alelesia kere.
7 # Yoe 2:31; Amo 8:9; Isa 13:10; Mat 24:29; Mk 13:24-25; Luk 21:25; Apu 6:12-13; 8:12. Kasodi kadau aitudeŋ jo, arapari akuj,
Etumutonori aacer;
Arapakini edou akoloŋ,
Kwabo mam elap eeauni.
8Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe,
Etumutonori italai kere
Lueeaete lukokuiukon,
Etumutonori akonikwap.
9“Esiyalononoi itauon luka iruna luipu, naetodolio amudiariokon toma atekerin, akwapin numam lem ijeni. 10#Esk 27:35; 26:16. Eebo, esitirirai ituŋa luipu kanukon, ekuriakaros noi ikabakanakec kanukon, naeiliilia ekakileŋ kakonyenekee; emaranete ŋinipak, ŋolituŋanan kanuka akeijar, kapaaran naka aibirorikon. 11#Yer 46:26. Naarai inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Kidolokini jo ekileŋ loka ekabaka loko Babulon. 12#Esk 28:7; 29:19. Ebirori ekonikodet kikilena luka lukapedor; eraas kere lusogim luka atekerin. Eŋasete eitur loko Misiri, emudiario ekodeteke kere.
13 # Esk 29:11. Amudiari da iketian kere
Lueapias akipi nuipu;
Ido mam bobo itieanete akeje nuka ituŋanan,
Karaida imageger luka itiaŋ aitican kes.
14Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe,
Kasodi aisiiriir akeeekipi,
Ka aitelel icorinikee kwape kakinyet.
15 # Any 7:5; Sab 9:16; Esk 6:6,7. Naetisilio akwap nako Misiri,
Ka aitiswap akwap naetwaniarit ailelebake,
Naadanyia kere luibojete kotoma,
Kosodete do ejenunete ebe atai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
16 # 2 Sam 1:17; 2 Siit 35:25; Esk 26:17. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Na ŋes akuudian rtaekuudianete; ekuudianete apesur nuka atekerin; ekuudianikinete Misiri ka ekekodet kere.”
17Kosodi kanen kokaru lokitomonet kaarei, kapaaran nakitomonet kakany naka elap, kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 18#Yer 1:10; Esk 26:20.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, komonyak ekodet loko Misiri, kibironok kwap, ŋes nepepe ka apesur nuka atekerin nuaienas, kiton atutubeta nukokwap nuka akwap, nepepe kanul kaluijesaros Aipany:
19 # Esk 31:2; 28:10. “Iŋai ca ideparit kajokis?
Kiiesar, kiperikin nepepe kalumam ekepitai.’
20Ibirokinos kidinikec luearitai kekilen: eiaikin okileŋ: kiriasi ŋes ka ekekodet kere. 21#Isa 1:31; 2 Ika 25:3-10; Yer 39:2-8; 52:4-14. Ineras kaŋes lukagogon lukotoma lukapedor kokidin Okatwak nepepe kanul kalukeganitok ebe: ‘Ejesaros, aperoto anatata, lumam ekepitai, luearitai kekilen.’
22“Ejai nen Asur ka aketukot kere; irimotito akeilin: kes kere earitai, ebirokin ekileŋ. 23#Isa 14:15. Ejaas akeeeilin toma atutubeta nukokwap nuka Aipany, irimorit aketukot akeilit: kes kere earitai, ebirokin ekileŋ, luayaunete etasuro akwap naka ikajarak.
24 # Yer 49:34. “Ejai nen Elam ka ekekodet kere irimorit akeilit: kes kere earitai, ebirokin ekileŋ, luejesaros mam ekepitai loma atutubeta nukokwap nuka akwap, luayaunete etasuro akwap naka ikaiarak, ibwokito ikeeeleic nepepe kaŋul kaluijesaros Aipany. 25Apesikis ŋes aperis kokidin kaluearitai nepepe kekekodet kere; irimorito ŋes akeilin: kes kere lumam ekepitai, lueari ekileŋ; naarai eyaunio etasurokee akwap naka ikaiarak, ibwokito ikeeeleic nepepe kanul kaluijesaros Aipany; ibwaikinitai kidinikec kaluearitai.
26 # Age 10:2; Esk 27:13. “Ejai nen Mesee, ka Tubal, ka ekeeekodet kere; irimorito kes akeeeilin: kes kere lumam ekepitai lueari ekileŋ: naarai ayaunete etasurokee akwap naka ikajarak. 27#Isa 14:18. Ido mam eperete nepepe kalukapedor luebiroros lumam ekepitai luejesaros Okatwak ekapito luiiisikec, ibwaikinito ikileŋakec aicolon kakwesikee, ejaas numam adolitekec akoiokec; naarai keiai etasuro loka lukapedor kakwap naka ikajarak. 28Konye kibilakinio kidin kalumam ekepitai, iperi nepepe kanul kalueari ekileŋ,
29 # Esk 25:12. “Ejai nen Edom, ikekabakan ka ikekapolok kere, luibwaikinitai kakeeepedor nepepe kanul kalueari ekileŋ: eperete nepepe kalumam ekepitai, ido nepepe kanul kaluijesaros Aipany.
30 # Esk 38:6,15; 28:21. “Ejaas nen ikapolok luka agolitomei, kes kere, ka Isidonin kere, luejesaros nepepe kaluearitai; karaida keyaunete etasuro kanuka agogonukec, irumete ileic; eperete mam ekepitai nepepe kanul kalueari ekileŋ; ibwokito ikeeeleic nepepe kanul kaluiiesaros Aipany.
31 # Esk 14:22. “Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Eanyuni kes Parao, elakar kanuka ekekodet kere: Parao ka akejore kere lueari ekileŋ. 32Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Naarai ebwaik eketasuro akwap naka ikajatak: iperikinio Parao ka ekekodet kere kidin kalumam ekepitai, nepepe kanul kalueari ekileŋ.”
