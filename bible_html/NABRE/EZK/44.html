Esekiel 44

44
1 # Esk 43:1. Kosodi kenyak korot lokorute lokokiŋa loka aiboisnakalaunan, loeseseni kide; ido kegolol. 2Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, “Esalakini erute lo egolol, mam enaario, kwabo emamuun ituŋanan yenelomari, naarai aloma EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael nen; ŋes agolere. 3#Sek 6:12; Pil 2:8; Age 31:54; Esk 46:2,8. Iboikin ekapolon nen, korai ekapolon, anyamanakin atap nen kakonyen nuka EJAKAIT; elomari korot loka agola nalomaret naka erute, ido elomuni korot ŋopeŋol.”
4 # Esk 3:23; 1:28. Kosodi kayana korot lokorute loka agolitomei koinaren ketogo; kasesen, ido koany, kileleba aibuses naka EJAKAIT togo loka EJAKAIT: kebiror ebuburia. 5#Esk 40:4. Katema EJAKAIT eoŋ atiar, “Okoku loka etuŋanan, kibwaik ekonitau noi, kosesen kakonikonye, ido kiira kakoniki ŋun kere nukalimokini jo nuka aicorakineta kere nuka etogo loka EJAKAIT, ka ikekisila kere: ido kibwaik ekonitau noi luelomanarete togo, ka luelomanarete da aibois nakalaunan. 6#Esk 2:5; 1 Pet 4:3. Ido ilimonokini lukepali, kes ekek loka Isirael ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Wu jo ekek loka Isirael, kodautu italesiakus kere, 7#Asw 21:28; Esk 7:20; Leb 26:41; 21:6; 3:16. naarai itolomununete imo, lumam ekepitai kotauon, ido Iumam ekepitai kawat, ajaut toma aiboisika nakalaunan, amunaar ekatogo, naiinanakiniata ikanyamat, atarion, ka aokot da. Ibilata yesi akaitutuket, aiyatakin italesiakus kere. 8#Leb 22:2; Mar 18:3; 2 Tim 4:1. Kwabo mam iricito ikaboro lukalaunak: konye ibwaikis yesi imo araut ikaricak akaibois nakalaunan.
9 # Sab 50:16. “Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Emamuun emoit lomam ekepitai kotau, ido lomam ekepitai kakuan, loelomari aiboisika nakalaunan, emoit kere loejaunun toma odwe luka Isirael. 10#2 Ika 23:8; Nek 9:34; Yer 23:11; Esk 22:26. Konye Ilebin lualuluasi kamakaŋ, luapaasi kamakaŋ atupar aputosiakec, naapaaria Isirael, ibwokete kesi numam adolitekec. 11#1 Siit 26:1; Mar 16:9. Konye eraunete kesi ikejaanakinak kotoma aiboisika nakalaunan, aanyanar korutei luka etogo, kijaanakinete da kotogo: earanakinete nul ainakinet naicweio, ka asubanit kanuka ituŋa, ido ebwoenenete kakonyenekec aijaanakin kes. 12#Sab 106:26. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Naarai kijaanakis kes kakonyen nuka aputosiakec, koraut ekomio kanumam adolit mama ejai ekek loka Isirael; ŋes ekeikinia akakan aluikin kes, ido ibwokete numam adolitekec. 13#Mar 18:3; Esk 32:30. Kwabo mam ikunyunete diekan, aitodol aswam naka esasedoti mamakaŋ, karaida aikunyakin idio koboroka lukalaunak, ka iboro da lukalaunak noi; konye ibwokete ikeceleic, kanuka italesiakee lueswamaenenete, 14#1 Siit 23:28,32. Konye etuluori kes ikaricak etogo, kanuka aijaanakin kere nakotoma, kanuka akiro kere da nuiswamao kanen.
15 # Esk 40:46; 1 Sam 2:35; Ikis 10:8. “Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Konye isasedotin Ilebin, odwe luka Sadok, luaricenenete akaibois nakalaunan, naapaariata idwe luka Isirael kamakaŋ, nul kes ikunyunete diekan, aijaanakin eoŋ; ebwoete kakonyeneka ainakin eoŋ atarion ka aokot. 16#Esk 41:22; Mal 2:7. Elomanarete kesi aiboisika nakalaunan, ikunyununete omesaka, aijaanakin eoŋ, ido ericete akakiro. 17#Any 28:39-43; Leb 16:4. Kosodi kanen naelomanariata orutei luka alaro nakotoma, enapenenete inapen luka ebaputa; kwabo mam enapenenete lukajul, naijaanakiniata korutei luka alaro nakotoma, kotoma da. 18Ejaenenas igoen lukieniena luka ebaputa akwesikee, ka epale loka ebaputa kanabesikec; mam enapenenete ibore kere yenisinyinyiri. 19#Esk 42:14; Any 29:37; Leb 16:23. Kosodi naelomununiata alaro nakokina, ŋes alaro nakokiŋa naka ituŋa, elacenenete inapenekec luejaanakiniata, kinadakis agolai nukalaunak; kosodete ainap inapen ice, tetere mam italaunete ituŋa kinapenekec. 20#Isa 28:7; Leb 21:5. Ido mam ebarenenete akwesikec, karaida aipit itim; konye enerenenete itimakec. 21#Luk 1:15; 1 Tim 3:3; Leb 10:9. Mam emasenenei ediosasedoti ebino, naelomariata alaro nakotoma. 22#Leb 21:7,13-14. Kwabo mam emanyenenete apuserut, karaida naerenearitai; konye komanyenenete abekon nuka aur naka ekek loka Isirael, arai apuserut nakerai apuserut naka esasedoti. 23#Mal 2:7; Leb 10:10. Ido isisianakinete kesi ikatuŋa atiaketait kwape ejaar kidin yenikalaunan ka yenika ituŋa kere, itejenikinete da atiaketait kwape eiaar kidin yenimam elai ka yenelai. 24#Ikis 17:8; 1 Tim 4:12; Esk 22:26. Etubonokinete kesi naeiaatatar ipegeta; etubonokinete kwape ejaatar ikatubesia. Koricito ikakisila ka akaicorakineta opueinoka kere Iuescunitai; kitalaununete da asabatonoka. 25#Leb 21:1-4. Mam ikunyanakinete yenikatwan amunaaror: konye kanuka papa, arai toto, arai ikokukec yenisapat arai yenipese, arai onaee, arai kinaei naemamei ekiliokit, epedorete amunaar. 26#Mar 6:10. Kedau aitesegaario, kimarakinai apaarasia akanykaarei. 27#Leb 4:3. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kosodi kapaaran naelomaria aibois nakalaunan, alaro nakotoma, aijaanakin aibois nakalaunan, einakini ber ainakineteke naka adiakar.
28 # Ikis 10:9; Yos 13:14,33; Esk 45:4; Mar 18:20. “Eiaunete kairumet; arai eoŋ airumetekee: kwabo mam iinanakinete alup kotoma Isirael; arai eoŋ alupokec. 29#Leb 6:18; Mar 18:8-19. Enyamenenete ainakineta nuka akiria, ka ainakineta nuka adiakar, ka ainakineta nuka aronis; ido ŋinibore yenipoliatai koIsirael eraun yenikee. 30#Mar 15:20; Awa 3:9. Ka luegeete kere luka araito kere nugeet, ka ŋiniinakinet kere naka ŋinibore kere, naka ainakinetakus kere, eraununos nuka isasedotin: iinanakinete da isasedotin atapakus nakainumunum, tetere isirerenio ekonitogo. 31#Any 22:31; Leb 22:8. Mam enyamenenete isasedotin ibore kere yenetwani bon, arai yenepoopootai, arai akweny, arai itiaŋ.
