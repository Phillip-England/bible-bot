Esekiel 16

16
1Kobu bobo akirot naka Ejakait mama ajai ebe: 2#Esk 20:4.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, kitejenik Yerusalem italesiake. 3Kiner atiar, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE kaYerusalem ebe: Ageunetekon, ka auruniokon ajai akwap naka Ekanaanit; Emorait ŋes papakon, totokon da Akitit. 4#Kos 2:3. Nakiwuruunerc, kapaaran nakiwuruunere mam aponi kotuboi aliligetekon, kwabo mam aponi kiloŋi kakipi aitalaunia; mam aponi kibwaikin adiobalaŋ, karaida adiokimukorio. 5Amamei akoŋu nakitimit, aiswamakin jo ŋun kere, aisioni jo; konye aponi kocakar kina ais, naarai kilaitai, kapaaran nakiwuruunere.
6Kosodi naetorio, kaany ibelibelo kaokotokon, katema atiar, ‘Karaida kijai aokotokon, kojar’; eebo, katema atiar, ‘Karaida kijai aokotokon, koiar.’ 7Ketupuu kwape aibutunet naka ais, kiyatanakin korau nakapolon, kodolok toma atumun naibus; eburut ikisinakon, apolooros itimakon; konye ijai titai imuialal.
8 # Rus 3:9; Any 19:5. “Kosodi naetorio, kasesen jo, koany, apakikon kerai apak naka amin; kemuko kanapitoka, karap amujalalakon, Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Eebo, keyapoik jo, ketutuk aitutuket kaio, korau nakan. 9Kasodi kelon kakipi; eebo, kelota eut aokotokon, kenyonyoanak akinyet. 10Ketanap da anapito naesonyitai, ketanap amuk nuka emukule, kemuko kebaputa kaloejok, karapak aliiri. 11#Age 24:22; 41:42; Eko 1:10; Isa 13:19; Awa 1:9; Dan 5:7,16,29. Kesiri dakiisiro, kebwaik aikomon akaninikon, erikot da omurisinukon, 12Kebwaik itetio okumesikon, kakiikon da, aboko da naetemelel akoukon. 13#Ikis 32:13; Sab 48:2. Kwaŋin ŋes kiisirere kesabu ka epuesa; kerai anapitokon naka ebaputa kaloejok, ka aliiri, ka anapito naesonyitai; konyamenenei atap naidicic, ka esik, ka akinyet. Kitemelel noi noi, kisirereni aitodol apolou naka ekabakane. 14#Aku 2:15. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kilosikin eemutokon atekerin kanuka atemelelekon; naarai etodolikinitoi, kanuka apolouka naabu kebwaikinite jo.
15 # Ikis 32:15; Mik 3:11; Yer 7:4; 2:20; Isa 1:21; 57:8; Kos 1:2. “Konye ibu komuno atemelelekon, koluor adote kanuka eemutokon, kiporoenenei neejai ŋolidiopet loetori; koraenena naken. 16#2 Ika 23:7; Esk 7:20; Kos 2:8. Ibu kolem inapenekon, kiswamau aiboisio nuiceleleete nuesiritoi kijesesia luegelegela, kodotear kamakec; mam lem ejaenenas nuikote nen, karaida ajaun. 17Ibu kolem da ikoniboro luejok luka akais nuipu luka esabuka ka luka epuesaka lukainakinit jo, kiswamaa aputosia nuka ituŋa, kodotear kakes. 18Ibu kolem inapenekon luesonyitai, korapak kes, kibwaik akakinyet ka lukisupurununoka kakonyenekee. 19#Ikis 32:13. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Ikanyamat da lukainakinit jo, atap naidicie, ka akinyet, ka esik, luketanyamenenei jo, ibu kibwaik kakonyenekee araut asakat naeuru saa. 20#2 Ika 16:3; Isa 57:5; Yer 7:31; Esk 20:26. Bobo da, ibu kolem ikonidwe lusap ka lupesur, lukiwurikinit eoŋ, koinak nul araut asuban mama ejaas kwi ainyam. Kerai kereka akonidoteu ibore yenedit, 21#Age 17:7,11; Any 13:2. tape iari ikadwe, kiiaik kes araut ainakinet naicweio mama ejaas? 22#Yer 2:2; Kos 2:3. Ido kotoma otalesiakon kere ka adoteukon, mam ibu kiitu apaarasia nuka eiditeukon, naijacnenar titai imujalal, kibelibelo kaokotokon,
23“Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Itukwi, itukwi jo! Kosodi kanen akaulo kanukaronokokon kere, 24#Leb 26:30; Sab 78:58; Isa 57:5; Yer 2:20. iduku aibois naikumemei, iswamau aibois naiidiara kanolirot, 25#Awa 9:14; Yer 6:15. Iduku aiboisikon naiidiara asisiarin kere, ituluo atemelelekon araut naketal, ipukok akonikejen mama ejai ŋol kere loitori, iyatanak adoteukon, 26#Esk 8:10; 20:7. Ielokinos kimisirian da, ikoniidunyeta lueburok awat; iyatanak adoteukon, aitunyunyurikin eoŋ. 27Aso do koany, kariik akakan, eŋoŋa akorakinetekon, kejaik jo mama ejai akot naka nun nukilaito, adwe nuka Ipilisitian, nuirumito ileje kanuka eponekon lokadoteu. 28#Tub 10:6; 2 Ika 16:7; Yer 2:18,29; Esk 23:14. Idotear da mama ejaas Iyasurian, naarai mam kipedori atitirikin, eebo, idotear mama ejaas, kwabo mam kiupi. 29Ido bobo iyatanak adoteukon akwap naka Kanaan, kiton Kaludea; kwabo mam nun da kiupete.
30 # Awa 30:20. “Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Ekalanyana ca ma ekonitau! Naarai iswamai nu kere, aswam naka aberu naadote naidelana. 31Naarai iduki akoniibois naikumemei kaninisisiar, kiswamau akoniibois naiidiara kaŋolirot; kwabo mam ibu korai kwape adote, naarai imen eropit. 32Aberu naadote ba! Naisukunyuni ca ma imo aibois naka okilenike! 33#Isa 30:3. Einakinete ituŋa adotea ainakineta; konye jo iinakini ainakinetakon mama ejaas ipailonokon kere, aijodounio, tetere epote neijai ijo kanoliwai kanuka adoteukon. 34Igelara ijo kanor ace kadoteukon, naarai emamei yenikitupuni jo aelokinor: ido naarai ibu korop, kwabo mam kiropio, kanuka ŋun igelara ijo.
35“Aso do ijo adote, kiira akirot naka EJAKAIT. 36#Sab 106:38; Yer 2:34. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Naarai abukor akonisilianut, kopukoroi amuialalakon kanuka adoteukon mama ejaas ipailonokon; ido kanuka aputosia kere nuka italesiakon, kanuka aokot nuka idwekon luiinakinit kes; 37#Isa 47:2,3; Yer 13:22; Aku 1:8; Esk 23:9; Kos 2:10; Nak 3:5. aso do koany, atukonokini ipailonokon kere, luibu kolakanarotor, ka nul kere lukimina, ka nul da kere luibu kolaite; atukonokini kes ciie kajo kowaitin kere, apukokini kes amujalalakon, koanyutu amuialalakon. 38#Age 9:6; Any 21:12; Leb 24:17; Mat 26:52; Apu 13:10. Ido katubokini akiro, kwape etubokinere aŋor nuegircte adukokinikec nuebukorete da aokot; ido kainuni aokot nuka adedeŋ ka ekokote. 39#Esk 23:26. Kakorakini akanin nuka ipailonokon, eeakarete kesi aiboisikon naikumemei, kotiyatiyata aiboisiokon nuiidiara; kilaearete inapenekon, enaŋete ikoniboro lukajokak luka akais nuipu: kinyeinete ijai titai imujalal. 40#Esk 23:10; Yok 8:5. Kiinunete erioŋet asurokinia jo, kibiribirete kamor, kieumueumata eut kikecekilena. 41#Ikis 13:16; 2 Ika 25:9; Yer 39:8; Rom 2:8; Esk 5:8; 23:10,27. Ido ieweete ikonitogoi, itodolikinete etube kamakon kosesenete aŋor nuipu; kesinyeini adoteu, kwabo mam bobo iropi. 42#Esk 5:13. Kwaŋin esimonikinia akadedeŋ kamakon, kinyein ekakokote, eyamakin, komam bobo anyunyurikin, 43#Sab 78:42; Esk 9:10; 11:21. Naarai mam iitununei apaarasia nuka eiditeukon, konye kitakadikin kanun kere; ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Aso koany, ayauni eoŋ ekonirot akoukon.
Mam kereka iyatakinit adoteu kotalesiakon kere? 44Koany, ŋinituŋanan yeninera awaragasia kiwaragi jo ebe, ‘Epone ka toto, ŋes loka akokuke da.’ 45Irai ijo akoku naka totokon, naelait okilenike ka ikedwe; irai ijo inae naka akoniinaean, nuelaete ikiliokokec ka ikecedwe, Kerai totokus Akitit, papakus da kerai Emorait. 46Kinaeikon nakapolon ŋes Samaria, naejai akanikon nakokediany, ŋes ka adweke: kinaeikon nadidi naejai akanikon nakoteten, ŋes Sodom ka adweke. 47#2 Ika 21:9; Esk 5:6,7. Konye mam ilosenenei korotinikec, ido mam iswamaenenei kwape ejaatar italesiakee; konye apak naedit, ibosit ijo adepar kes korotinikon kere, 48#Mat 10:15; Mk 6:11; Luk 10:12. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kwape ajaria, mam kinaeikon Sodom, karaida adweke, eswamaenenete kwape iswamaa ijo ka adwekon, 49#Age 13:10; Luk 16:20. Koany, keraas nu numam adolit nuka kinaeikon Sodom; aiwoŋonut, ka aimonianakin inyamat, ka aijamam naka akerianut kejaas mamake ka adweke; mam ŋes esigalikinit yenikiean ka yenilwaritoi. 50#Age 13:13; 18:20; Yob 18:18. Ido eomikina kesi, kiswamata nuketal kakonyeneka; ŋes kainakini alemar kwape abu ketojokia. 51#Mat 12:41. Kwabo Samaria da mam eswamat atutubet naka aronisiokon; konye jo iyatanakini italesiakon adepar kes, iinakinit akoniinaean atakan bala nuedolito kanuka italesiakon kere luiswamat. 52Ijo da, kibwok alopet ikonileie, naarai iinakinit etube loka ainaeanakon abeneniar; kanuka aronisiokon nuiswamat nuketal adepar kes, ŋes kitelekaritotor ijo kadolit. Eebo, kitirira, ijo da kibwok ikonileie, naarai iinakinit akoniinaean atakan bala nuedolito.
53 # Isa 1:9; Rom 9:29; Yer 20:16. “Ido enyakuni aijoreoriokee, aijoreorio naka Sodom ka adweke, ka aijoreorio naka Samaria ka adweke, ka aijoreorio naka ijoreoritonokon luejaas kakes; 54#Esk 14:22,23. tetere ibwoki ikonileie alopet, kirumata ileje kanuka ŋun kere nuiswamat, naarai italakari kes, 55Eboŋunete do ainaeanakon Sodom ka adweke apoloukee nakolo, eboŋunete da Samaria ka adweke apoloukec nakolo; ibonunete ijo da ka adwekon apoloukus nakolo. 56Mam kereka kiwaragit kinaeikon Sodom kapaaran naka aiwononutukon, 57#2 Ika 16:5; 2 Siit 28:18; Isa 7:1. eroko epukoro akonironis? Kwana da iputokinos kaŋes araut naamoromorot naka adwe nuka Edom, ka nun kere nuirimorito ŋes, ka adwe da nuka Ipilisitian, nukirimorito kimwanito jo, 58Ebala EJAKAIT ebe, Ibu kibwok adoteukon ka italesiakon.
59 # Ikis 29:12-14; Esk 17:13,16. “Naarai inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Keswamakini teni kwape ibu kiswamaa, ijo naimwanit aiyaposit kanuka aibil aitutuket. 60#Any 2:24; Leb 26:41,42; Sab 106:45; Kos 2:15; Yer 32:40. Konye eituni akaitutuket kajo naka apaarasia nuka eiditeukon, ido eticasi kamakon aitutuket namam edaun. 61#Esk 20:43; Eko 8:8; Isa 2:2; 54:1; Gal 4:26. Kosodi konyet iituni irotinikon, kirumata ileic, nakainakinia jo ainaeanakon, nukapolok ka nudidik; kainakini kes araut adwe, konye mere kanuka akoniitutuket. 62#Kos 2:19,20; Asw 7:8; Bur 8:6-10,13. Ido etieasi kamakon akaitutuket: ijenuni do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT. 63#Rom 3:19. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Tetere iituni, itirirai, mam bobo iwoonari akoniituk, kanuka ileieikon; naadaunia aisioni jo kere nuiswamat.”
