Esekiel 17

17
1Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 2“Okoku loka etuŋanan, kolim asogol, kiner awarag mama ejai ekek loka Isirael: 3#2 Ika 24:12. Kiner ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Abu emorukaalet loepol lokejaas abebenoka nuepolok ka akopir nueojak, loeleleba aiul, loka iiesesia luipu kobu Lebanon, koyana aidi naka eres: 4kobila nakokuiu naka aibutunetake, kodaka akwap naka agwelanar; kibwaik ore loka ikagwelanarak. 5#Ikis 8:7-9; Isa 15:7. Koyana da ikinyom luka akwap kira alup nupusuk, abu kosipok neajaas akipi nuipu, Kibwaik kwape aderut. 6Kopoloor, koraun emusabibu loepesit kouriana, kibelokinos atenueikike mamake, koiaas aliasioke kwapuke. Aso koraun emusabibu, kopet atenun, kolibi akidikidin.
7“Keiai da emorukaalet eee loepol, lokejaas abebenoka nuepolok ka aiul nuipu: ido koany, kitukud emusabibu ŋol akeliasio mamake, kolibi atenueikike abear neejai, tetere ieoeoi. Kouko kapanya nueraitai, 8kiraik alup nuejok neajaas akipi nuipu, tetere epesi atenun, ido kora araito, koraun emusabibu loejok. 9#2 Ika 25:7. Kiner ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Isirereŋio? Mam kereka erutuni ŋesi aliasioke, koden akeraito, tetere erinyori? Tetere erinyorete akekwi kere nuepalal nuelibunete; mere kanuka agogoŋ naepol karaida kanuka ituŋa luipu arutun akelias. 10#Esk 19:12; Kos 13:15; Mat 21:19; Mk 11:20; Yok 15:6; Yud 1:12. Eebo koany, kiraikin, isirerenio? Mam kereka erinyori cut, kijaŋ ekwam lokokide? Erinyori kotoma apanya nuapolokini.”
11Bobo da ko bu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe, 12#Esk 2:5; 2 Ika 24:15-20; 2 Siit 36:10-13.“Kolimok ekek lokepali ebe, Mam kereka iienete apolou naka akiro kanun? Kolimok ebe, Koany, abu ekabaka loko Babulon kobu Yerusalem, kikama ekabakake, ka ikapolokoke, koyanau kes neajai Babulon. 13#2 Ika 24:17; 2 Siit 36:13. Koyana ediopet kaur naka ekabaka, kitutukoto kaŋes aitutuket; ido kisiyapo ŋes. (Koyana ikagogoŋok luka akwap: 14#Esk 29:14. aitisil ajakanut, kinyek adakara, konye kicat kanuka airie aitutuketeke.) 15#2 Ika 24:20; Ikis 17:16; Yer 52:3; 37:5-7; Isa 30:1-4; 31:1-3. Konye kipaliik kiyakia ikekiyakia Misiri, koinakinai ibalasin ka ituŋa luipu, Isirerenio kereka ŋes? Ekui tope ŋol loiswamai nuikote nen? Ebili aitutuket, kosodi aiku? 16#Yer 32:5; Esk 12:13. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kwape ajaria, abeit etwanikini loamwani aiyapositike, kobili akeitutuket, kidiŋ Babulon, aibois neiboio eka baka lokitarau ŋes eka baka, 17#Yer 37:7; 52:4; Aku 4:17; Esk 29:6,7. Kwabo mam Parao ka akeiore nakagogon ka ekekodet loepol inarakinetc ŋes kojie, naitutuoto itutio, edukete abwasin amudiaria ituŋa luipu. 18#1 Siit 29:24. Naarai amwaŋ aiyaposit ka aibil aitutuket; ido koany, itogogonit kakanike, konye eswama ŋun kere; mam ekui, 19Aso do inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Kwape ajaria, abeit aiyapositika naabu komwana, ka akaitutuket nakebili, etodolikini akouke, 20#Yos 10:16-18; 2 Siit 33:11; Lim 9:12; Aku 1:13; Esk 12:13; 20:36. Aloki ekaboi mamake, ipoki akatacit, ayanauni Babulon, kipuri kaŋes kaŋina kanuka akediakaret naadiakarit kamakaŋ. 21#Esk 12:14. Ibiroros ikakerakake kere lukorionetake kere kekilen, isiarisiario nul kere luideunos kokwamin kere: ijenunete do ebe eoŋ EJAKAIT ener.”
22 # Isa 11:1; 53:2; Yer 23:5; Sab 2:6. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: “Alemari eoŋ aidi naicelelei naka eres, ebwaikini; abilanari kotoma nukokuiu nuka aibutunetake, naenonok, ka aira kuiu oroŋat loeoj loimukurur. 23#Isa 2:2,3; Mik 4:1. Eraikini oroŋat loimukurur loka Isirael: elibi ŋesi atenun, ka aira araito, eraun eres loejok: iboiete akweny nuka ipoŋesio kere kotolim loka atenueikike. 24Ejenunete ikito kere luka ais ebe eoŋ EJAKAIT etisili ekitoi loeoi, etopoloo ekitoi loeuriana, esirinyori ekitoi loepesit, esilibi da ekitoi loeoŋit. Eoŋ EJAKAIT ener, eswama da.”
