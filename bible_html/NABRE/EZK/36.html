Esekiel 36

36
1“Ŋo da, okoku loka etuŋanan, kodwarak ironan luka Isirael, kiner ebe, Yes ironan luka Isirael, kiirasi akirot naka EJAKAIT. 2#Ikis 32:13; Sab 78:69; Isa 58:14; Kbk 3:19. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Naarai kinerak yes lokasurup ebe, ‘Uus!’ ido, ‘Aiboisio nuiceleleete nukolo sek araut bo do nukosi’: 3asa kodwar, kiner ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Naarai, eebo, naarai kitisiliosi, kojwenyasi kowaitin kere, tetere kirumete nukesalete nuka atekerin, kilimitoi kanaiepa nuka ikeyalak, ka abekoi naerono naka ituŋa: 4#Sab 79:4. kanuka ŋun, yes ironan luka Isirael, kiirasi akirot naka Ejakait EDEKE. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDBKB elimokini ironan ka iukon, ka alelesia, ka aditono, ka aiboisio nuiswapito nuesilok, ka ireria luinyeinitai, luaraas imejan ka amorosito mama ejaas nukesalete nuka atekerin nuirimorito. 5#Ikis 4:24; Isa 66:15,16; Esk 38:19; 35:10; Sek 1:15. Ŋes inerar kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Abeit ener kotoma akim naka ekokoteka asurokin nukesalete nuka atekerin, ka Edom kere, luaseikinos ikulepek akakwap araun airumet naka aiyalama naka itauonokec kere, kanuka epali loka etau, tetere irumete araut ameiat. 6#Sab 123:3,4; Esk 34:29. Kanuka ŋun kodwar nuka akwap naka Isirael, kolimok ironan ka iukon, ka alelesia ka aditono ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Koany, ener kokokoteka, kadedenuka, narai ibwokata ileic luka ikapirin: 7#Esk 20:5. aso inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Ekeu akakan, ebe, Abeit ikapirin lukirimorito, ibwokete kesi ikulepek ikeceleie.
8“Konye yes, ironan luka Isirael, ipesiarete atenueikikus, koraikis ikatuŋa Isirael araitokus; naarai eapie kes aboŋun. 9#Kos 2:21-23; Kag 2:19. Naarai koany, ajai eoŋ owaikus, ebelokin eoŋ mamakus, kikorio kiyeio. 10#Isa 58:12; 61:4; Amo 9:14. Etupui ituŋa kamakus, ekek kere loka Isirael, kere ba: ejaunete ituŋa oreria, iteteununio aiboisio nuiswapito. 11#Yer 31:27; 33:12; Esk 35:9; Kos 2:20; Yok 3:17; 1 Yok 5:20. Etupui ituŋa ka itian da kamakus: iyatanakinos, iwuriaros: ketudumikini yes ituŋa kwape lem ijaatar, ka aiswamakin yes ejok aitelekarit ageunetekus. Ŋenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT. 12#Oba 1:17; Yer 15:7. Eebo, ainakin ituŋa aitorit nekus, kes ikatuŋa Isirael; kirumete, iraun airumetekec, mam bobo itwataniari kes ikeeedwe. 13#Mar 13:32. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
Naarai kilimonokinete ebe, ‘Inyami ijo ituŋa, itwataniari atekerikon idwe’: 14ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Aso mam bobo iyatakini ainyam ituŋa, karaida aitwataniar akoniteker idwe. 15#Isa 60:14; Esk 34:29; Sep 3:19,20. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Mam bobo kesibwoki ileic luka ikapirin, ido mam bobo ibwoki amorosit naka ituŋa, ido mam bobo itiguikini akoniteker.”
16Bobo da kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 17#Yer 2:7; Leb 15:19.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, nakiboio ekek loka Isirael kakwapukec, apotu komunaaros korotinikec ka aswamisiokec da: korau ekecerot kakonyeneka bala namam elai naka aberu naibami. 18#Esk 16:36. Aso kabukoki kes akadedeŋ kanuka aokot nuapotu kobukokis akwap, kanu da amunaarotor kaputosiakec: 19#Esk 22:15; 7:3. kesiarisiar kes atekerin, kiweliwelai anatar akwapin: katubok kes kwape ejaar ekecerot, ido kwape ejaatar akeceswamisio. 20#Isa 52:5; Rom 2:24. Nakedolioto kesi atekerin nualosete, komunaasi ekakiror lokalaunan; naarai kobalas ituŋa ebe, ‘Lu kes ituŋa luka EJAKAIT, anyoutu kesi da kakwapuke.’ 21#Esk 20:9. Konye abu ketim ekakiror lokalaunan, loamunaari ekek loka Isirael kotoma atekerin nualosete,
22 # Ikis 9:5; Sab 106:8. “Aso kolimok ekek loka Isirael ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Mam eoŋ eswamai kanukus, ijo ekek loka Isirael, dimarai kanuka ekakiror lokalaunan, loipotu komunaasi kotoma atekerin nuilosete. 23#Esk 20:41. Etalauni eoŋ ekakiror loepol, loamunaaritai katekerin, loimunaarete yesi kokidinikec, Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Ejenunete do atekerin ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, naetalaunor kamakus kakonyenekee. 24#Esk 34:13. Naarai kalemuni yes katekerin, katukonok alemun kakwapin kere, ketolomik akwapukus. 25#Mar 19:13; Isa 52:15; Yer 33:8; 1 Yok 1:7. Ido kabwasi yes akipi nuelaete, ilaunete: kedetarete kotoma amodokokus kere ka aputosiakus da kere. 26#Sab 51:10; Yok 3:3-5; 2 Kor 3:18; Esk 11:19-20. Kainakinete etau lokitetet, ebwaikini emoyo lokitetet tomakus: ido alemuni etau lokamoru kawatekus, kainakinete etau loka akiriŋ. 27#Esk 37:14; Isa 44:3; Rom 8:4,9; Gal 5:5; Pes 1:13. Ebwaikini ekamoyo tomakus, ketoloto kokisilaka, ido iricete ikatubesia, kiswamata da. 28#Esk 28:25; Yer 30:22. Iboikinos akwap nakolo kainakini lukapapaakus; iraenenas ikatuŋa, eoŋ da araenena Ekusideke. 29#Mat 1:21; Sab 105:16. Ido etajari yes kanumam ailakus kere: anyarauni akima, ka aiyatakin, mam bobo kainunete ebeli. 30#Esk 34:27. Ido esideneriai araito nuka ikito, ka amio nakomanikor, kinyeis bobo amoromoroo katekerin kanuka ebeli. 31#Esk 6:9; Leb 26:39. Kosodete aiitun ikusirotin lueroko, ka akusiswamisio numam ajokuka; itakadikinos kanuka numam adolitekus, ka italesiakus. 32#Ikis 9:5; Dan 9:19. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kojenutu bo, mam eswamai nu kanukus. Kirumata ileje kitiriraata da kanuka ikusirotin, ijo ekek loka Isirael.
33 # Rom 8:30-32. “Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Kapaaran nakedetaria yes kanumam adolitekus kere, esiboikini ituŋa toma oreria, edukio da aiboisio nuiswapito. 34#Yer 25:9. Ekorio akwap naasil, nakerai naesil kakonyen nuka ŋul kere kalukitorenenete. 35#Isa 51:3. Ebalasi do ebe, ‘Arau akwap na naasil bala emanikor laka Eden; ireria da lueswapito luasilok ka luatiyanakina abwai ido eboikinos ituŋa toma.’ 36#Sab 58:11; Esk 17:24; 22:14. Kosodete ejenunete atekerin nukesalete nukirimorito ebe Eoŋ EJAKAIT aduk aiboisio nuatiyanakina, era da neasil: Eoŋ EJAKAIT ener, eswamai da.
37 # Sab 102:17; Isa 45:11,19; Yer 29:11-13; Esk 14:3; Sek 13:9; Mat 7:7,8; Pil 4:6; Yak 4:3. “Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Akoto ekek laka Isirael keŋisenenei eoŋ aiswamakin kes; etupui ituŋa bala esipan. 38Bala esipan loka asuban, bala esipan loka Yerusalem kopueinoke lueseunitai; kwaŋin elelebia ireria lueswapito isipanin luka ituŋa, Ejenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.”
