Esekiel 40

40
1 # Esk 33:21; 1:3. Kokaru loka akaisaarei ka lukany loka aijoreoriokosi, kageunet naka ekaru, kapaaran nakitomonet naka elap, kokaru lokitomonet kaonon akaulo kaipikorio naka ere, kapaaran kanipe kaŋin, kojau akan naka EJAKAIT keoŋ, kayanau ne, 2#Esk 8:3; Apu 21:10. Kaitapoeta nuka Edeke kayau akwap naka Isirael, kebwaik eoŋ oroŋat loeoj noi, kujuke kojai ibore yenesubit bala ere kari naka Eŋalakimak. 3#Dan 10:6; Esk 47:3; Apu 11:1; 21:15. Kayanau eoŋ nen, ido koany, kojai etuŋanan, kosubite atakanuke bala atakanu naka aisinit, loetinit auno naka abirai kakanike, ka agada nakitemis; kobwo korute. 4#Esk 2:7,8; 3:17; 43:10. Kalimok eoŋ etuŋanan ŋol che, “Okoku loka etuŋanan, koany kakonikonye, kiira da kakoniki, kibwaik ekonitau kere nuketodikini; naarai kiyaunio ne tetere ketodikini jo kes; nuianyuni kere kitejenik ekek loka Isirael.”
5 # Isa 26:1. Ido koany, kojai arwatat kina naka etogo kowaitin kere, ido kitinite etuŋanan egada nakitemis naaoj ikubitin ikanykape, ŋolikubit #40:5 ekubit ŋes aitemis naka aojau, bala 1’ 6” aoj ekubit ka alalau naka etau loka akan: kitem ŋesi abures naka arwatat, agada adiopet; ka amukururuke, agada adiopet. 6Kosodi kobu orute loeseseni kide, kodoka kadoketake; kitem okek loka erute, aburesuke agada adiopet: ka ekek eee aburesuke agada adiopet. 7#1 Siit 9:18. Aoj ninigola nakidaret agada adiopet, alal agada adiopet; apak da kokidin kagolai nukidaret ikubitin ikany; ido ekek loka erute aapie kagola nalomaret orute atereikina ketogo, agada adiopet, 8Kitem da agola nalomaret orute atereikina ketogo, agada adiopet, 9Kosodi kitem agola nalomaret orute, ikubitin ikanykauni; ka ategerioke ikubitin iarei; kojai agola nalomaret orute atereikina ketogo. 10#1 Siit 26:12,13; Eko 1:4; Nek 13:5,9,12,13; Isa 26:20; Yer 35:2. Emwaasi agolai nuka erute kari nakokide auni epuwai, ka auni da epuwai; kwa auni araas nuka aitemis adiopet: ategerio da nuka aitemis adiopet epuwai, ka epuwai da. 11Kitem alalau neenaario erute, ikubitin itomon; aojau da naka erute, ikubitin itomon ka luuni; 12ajai apak koiŋaren naka agolai, ekubit ediopet epuwai, ka apak ekubit ediopet epuwai da; emwaasi agolai ikubitin ikanykape epuwai, ka ikubitin ikanykape epuwai da. 13Kitem erute anyoun katunuru naka agola adiopet, kiton atunuru naka ace, alalau naka ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukany; kiteosi ekek ka ekek. 14#Any 27:9; Leb 6:16; Sab 85:4; 84:2; 87:4; 100:4; Isa 54:2; 60:8,9; 62:9; Esk 8:7; 42:1. Kitem neelomarere da, ikubitin akaisaarei, ido kirimorite alaro neelomarere orute. 15Anyoun koinaren naka erute kiton to naka agola nalomaret nakotoma, emwaasi ikubitin akaisakany. 16Ajaas agolai apedon nuagolokino, kejaas da toma ategerio toma orute airimor, kwape kwaŋin acuneta: koiaas apedon toma ammor: kojaas da idukudukun ategerio,
17 # Apu 11:2; Esk 45:5. Kosodi kayau eoŋ alaro nakokina, ido koany, ajaas agolai ka neitutuitoi naeswamakinitai alaro airimor: koiaas agolai akaisauni neitutuitoi, 18Kojai neitutuitoi kawai naka irutei kosesenas aojau naka irutei, neitutuitoi nakokwap. 19Kosodi kitem alalau anyoun koinaren kerute lokokwap kiton kiŋaren naka alaro nakotoma kokina ikubitin akwatat, kide ka agolitomei da.
20Ka erute loka alaro nakokina loeseseni agolitomei kitem aojauke ka alalauke. 21Emwaasi agolaike auni epuwai, ka auni da epuwai, ategerioke ka acunetake koriakasi kaitemis naka erute Iosodit: aoiauke ikubitin akaisakany, alalauke ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukany. 22Apedonoke, ka acunetake, ka idukudukunuke, koriakasi aitemis naka erute loeseseni kide: kojaas adoketa akanykaarei adokaria; koiaas da acunetake kinarenikec. 23Kojai erute alaro nakotoma kosesenas erute eee, kari naka agolitomei, kari da nakokide; kitem ageun korute kiton orute, ikubitin akwatat.
24Kenarenik eoŋ abear eŋalakimak, ido koany, erute loka eŋalakimak: kitem ategerioke ka acunetake kwape ajaatar aitemisio kwa. 25#1 Ika 6:4; Isa 54:12. Koiaas apedon tomake, ido toma acunetake airimor, kwape apedon kwa: aoiauke ikubitin akaisakany, ka alalauke da ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukany. 26Kojaas adoketa akanykaarei adokaria, kojaas acunetake kiŋarenikec; koiaas idukudukun, ediopet epuwai, ediopet da epuwai, kotoma ategerioke. 27Kojai erute alaro nakotoma kari naka eŋalakimak: kitem ageun korute kiton orute kari naka eŋalakimak, ikubitin akwatat.
28Kosodi kayau alaro nakotoma korute loka eŋalakimak: kitem erute loka eŋalakimak kwape ajaatar aitemisio kwa. 29#1 Ika 6:5-10; 1 Siit 28:11,12; 2 Siit 3:9; 31:11; Nek 10:38,39; 12:44; 13:5,9,12,13; Yer 35:2; 36:10. Ka agolaike, ka ategerioke, ka acunetake, kwape ajaatar aitemisio kwa: kojaas apedon tomake ka toma acunetake da airimor: aojauke ikubitin akaisakany, alalauke da ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukany. 30Koiaas acuneta airimor, aojaukee ikubitin akaisaarei kalukany, alalaukee da ikubitin ikany. 31#Sab 92:12; Eko 7:7; Yer 10:5. Atereikina acunetake kalaro nakokiŋa; koiaas idukudukun kategerioke: needokarere koiaas adoketa akanykauni.
32Kayau alaro nakotoma naeseseni kide: kitem erute kwape ajaatar aitemisio kwa: 33agolaike, ka ategerioke, ka acunetake, kwape ajaatar aitemisio kwa: ido ajaas apedon tomake, toma acunetake da airimor: aojauke ikubitin akaisakany, alalauke ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukany, 34Atereikina acunetake kalaro nakokina: kojaas idukudukun kategerioke, epuwai, ka epuwai da: needokarere koiaas adoketa akanykauni.
35Kayau orute loka agolitomei: kitem ŋes kwape ajaatar aitemisio kwa, 36Agolaike, ka ategerioke, ka acunetake; koiaas apedon tomake airimor: aoiauke ikubitin akaisakany, alalauke ikubitin akaisaarei ka lukany. 37Atereikina acunetake kalaro nakokina; koiaas idukudukun kategerioke, epuwai, ka epuwai da; needokarere kojaas adoketa akanykauni.
38 # Leb 1:9; 3:11; Bur 9:14. Kojai agola nepepe kekekike eapie ategerio korutei; nen kilosenenata ainakinet naicweio. 39#Leb 4:2,3; 5:6; 6:6; 7:1; 14:12,13; 19:21,22; Isa 53:5; 2 Kor 5:21; Tit 2:14; Bur 10:12-14; 1 Pet 1:18,19; Apu 5:9; Mar 6:12; Esk 42:13; 44:29. Agola nalomaret naka erute ajaas imesan iarei epuwai, ka imesan da iarei epuwai, aarenena ainakinet naicweio, ka ainakinet naka adiakaret, ka ainakinet da naka aronis. 40Ajaas imesan iarei epuwai kokina, needokarere kalomaret naka erute loka agolitomei; ajaas da imesan iarei epuwai neebeit agola nalomaret orute. 41Ajaas imesan iwonon epuwai, ka imesan da iwonon epuwai eapias kerute; imesan ikanykauni luearanakinio asuban. 42Ajaas imesan iwonon luka ainakinet naicweio, luka amoru nuikee, aojaukee ekubit ediopet ka atutubet, alalaukee ekubit ediopet ka atutubet, amukururukee ekubit ediopet; luebwaanakinete iboro luaaris ainakinet naicweio ka asubanit. 43Itemokinitoi igolun lueojak alalau naka etau loka akan kotoma airimor: koiai akiriŋ naka ainakinet omesan.
44 # 1 Siit 6:31; Kol 3:16; Pes 5:19. Ido kina kerute lokotoma ajaas agolai kanuka ikayook toma alaro nakotoma, naajai osiep loka erute loka agolitomei; kosesenete eŋalakimak: adiopet osiep laka crute lokokide koseseni agolitomei. 45#Leb 8:35; Mar 3:7; Ikis 11:1; 1 Ika 2:3; Apu 1:6. Kosodi ŋesi atemar eoŋ atiar, “Agola na, naeseseni eŋalakimak, erai naka isasedotin, ikedarak luidarete etogo, 46#Mar 18:5; 1 Ika 2:35; Esk 44:15; 43:19; Pes 2:17; Kol 4:12. Agola naeseseni agolitomei erai naka isasedotin, ikedarak luidarete etem: kes idwe luka Sadok, lukotoma odwe luka Lebi Iuikunyakinete aijaanakin EJAKAIT.” 47Kitem alaro, aoiauke ikubitin akwatat, alalauke ikubitin akwatat, koriana kowaitin kere; kojai etem kinaren ketogo.
48Kosodi kayau agola nalomaret togo, kitem ŋinitegerioit naka agola nalomaret, ikubitin ikany epuwai, ka ikubitin ikany da epuwai: alalau naka erute ikubitin iuni epuwai, ka ikubitin iuni da epuwai. 49#1 Ika 6:3; 7:21. Aojau naka agola nalomaret ikubitin akaisaarei, alalauke ikubitin itomon kadiopet; eapie kadoketa nuedokanarere: ajaas ipirin eapias kategerio, ediopet epuwai, ka eee da epuwai.
