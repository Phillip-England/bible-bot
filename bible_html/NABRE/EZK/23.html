Esekiel 23

23
1Kobu bobo akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 2#Yer 3:7; Esk 16:46.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, ajaas aŋor aarei, totokec adiopet: 3#Leb 17:7; Yos 24:14; Esk 20:8. kiporoto Misiri; kiporoto koiditeukec: koridakinai ikecekisin kaŋina, kieikakis atibolok nuka abekonutukec kaŋina. 4Ikecekiroria Akola nakapolon, ka Akoliba kinacike. Koraut nukaŋ, kouriata idwe lusap ka Iupesur. Ikeeekiroria da, Samaria ŋes Akola, Yerusalem da ijes Akoliba.
5 # 2 Ika 15:19; 16:7; Kos 5:13; 8:9; 10:6. “Kiporo Akola nakeraar nakan; kikila ipailonoke, Iyasurian ikeidunyeta. 6Lukenapenenete lubulului, ikayaitok ka ijaka, kes kere ikarumunak luimielai, ikebwanenok luibwanenete ibalasin. 7Kokorak kes akedoteu, kes kere, ituŋa luseuna luka Asuria: ka ŋol da lokikila ŋesi, komunaar kaputosiakee kere. 8Kwabo mam kinyeini akedoteu anyoun kapaarasia nuka Misiri; naarai koperoto kaŋes katumunike, kicikakis atiboloka nuka abekonutuke: kobukokis ŋes akeeedoteu. 9#2 Ika 17:3-6,23. Ŋes kakorakinia akanin nuka ipailonoke, akanin nuka Iyasurian, lukikila. 10#Esk 16:37,41. Kopukosi nul amujalalake: koyanaasi ikedwe lusap ka lupesur, koara ŋes kekilen: koraun naejenio kanor; naarai aponi kotubokinai akiro.
11“Koany kinacike da Akoliba nun, konye kiteleka ŋesi ŋin kapaar naka eikilike, kotoma adoteuke da naitelekarit adoteu naka inacike. 12#2 Ika 16:7. Kikila Iyasurian, ikayaitok ka ijaka, ikeidunyeta, lukenapenenete lukaibuses naepol, ikebwanenok luibwanenete ibalasin, kes kere ikatumunak luimielai. 13Kaany ebe emunaara; kotupasi kesi kere edioperor, 14#Yer 50:2. Konye kiyatak ŋesi akedoteu: naarai koany ituŋa lukinomakinitai arwatat, aputosia nuka Ikaludean nukinomitai keiese Ioirananai. 15Koenitos arokoban aŋabesikec, kienienito akwesikee kigoen lukijesesinei, kes kere kotakanete kwape ikapolok, kwape ejaar aput naka Ibabulonin luka Kaludea, akwap nakobukec, 16Napaanyunia kes kikila, kiyakik ikiyakia koKaludea, 17Kopotu Ibabulonin mama ejai ŋesi kaperis naka amin, komunaasi ŋes kadoteukec, komunaar kakes, kopianik eketau kes. 18#Ikis 32:19; Sab 78:59; Yer 12:8; 15:1; Aku 2:7; Amo 6:8; Sek 11:8. Kwaŋin kopuko akedoteu, kopuko da akemujalal: kopianik ekatau ŋes, kwape apianikinia ekatau kinacike. 19Konye kiyatak adoteuke, kiitu apaarasia nuka atumunike, nueporoo kakwap nako Misiri. 20#Esk 16:26. Kikila ipailonokec, kosubito awatekec bala awat nuka asigiria, kosubite aurikec bala aur naka ibalasin. 21Kwaŋin iitunit adoteu naka atumunikon, nakieikakiniata Imisirian aiburun naka atibolokakon nuka atumunikon.
22 # Isa 10:5,6; Esk 16:37. “Aso do, inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Ŋo Akoliba, koany, etonyouni ipailonokon asurokin jo, luapianiki ekonitau, ayauni kes asurokin jo kowaitin kere; 23Ibabulonin, ka Ikaludean kere, Pekod ka Soa, ka Koa, ka Iyasurian kere nepepe kakes: ikatumunak luimielai, kes kere ikayaitok ka ijaka, ikapolok ka ituŋa luejenikina, kes kere ibwanenito ibalasin. 24#Nak 3:2; 2 Sam 24:14; Yer 47:3; 39:5; Esk 16:38. Epote kesi asurokin jo ekapitos luiiis, kagalin, ka nukakokoron, ka atukot naka ituŋa; iderakioos ejie kaio kojaas kiupalin ka ibukwi ka akopiran owaitin kere: adakakini kes atubokin akiro, kitubokinete kwape ejaatar itubesiakec. 25Ederakini ekokoteka asurokin jo, kiticanete kadeden. Kituborete ikumesikon, ka akiikon; irikakini ekileŋ akonitutubet oaesali. Eyaŋaarete idwekon lusap ka lupesur; icweio akonitutubet naesali kakim. 26#Yer 13:22; Esk 16:39; Apu 17:16. Kilacarete inapenekon, eyaŋaarete lukaseganutukon luejok. 27#Esk 16:41; Mik 5:10. Kwaŋin etanania akonidoteu, ka eporokon lokolo iyauni kakwap nako Misiri: tetere mam iteluori akonikonye mamakee, karaida akiitun bobo Misiri. 28#Yer 21:7; Esk 16:37. Naarai inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Koany, kakorakini jo akanin nuka nul kaluireit, akanin nuka ŋul kaluapianiki etaukon. 29#Esk 16:39. Kiswamakinete jo nukanar, kidemarete aitaaritoikon kere, kijalakinete imujalal atitai: epukorio amuialal naka adoteukon. Eporokon ka adoteukon kere 30#Esk 6:9. kiswamakinito kwaŋin naarai ibu kiporo mama ejaas ikapirin, ido naarai imunaar kaputosiakec. 31#Sab 11:6; Yer 25:15; Dan 9:12. Ibu kotupa erot loka inacikoo; ŋes kainakinia akolike akanikon. 32#Ikis 28:37; Esk 22:4,5. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
Imasi akoli naka inacikon, Naidul naepol;
Kikienare noi kimuario; Naarai igauni ŋesi nuipu.
33Kilelebikin amera ka aiturur.
Akoli naka aitirira ka ajalakinio,
Ŋes akoli naka inacikon Samaria.
34 # Sab 75:8; Isa 51:17. Imasi aikokoor,
Iboiboi abelekekake,
Icili da ikonikisina:
Naarai ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Eoŋ ener. 35#Yer 2:32; 3:21; 1 Ika 14:9; Nek 9:26. Aso inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Naarai kimurwok eoŋ, koeaka eoŋ kaukon, asa do jo da kibwok eporokon ka adoteukon.”
36 # Isa 58:1. Bobo da kalimok EJAKAIT eoŋ ebe: “Okoku loka etuŋanan, itubokini kereka Akola ka Akoliba? Aso kolimok kes italesiakec, 37#Esk 16:20,38. Naarai adoteata, ejaas aokot akaninikec; adoteata aputosiakec; ido etotoroto ikecedwe lusap lukawurikinete akim ainakin nun ainyam. 38#Esk 22:8. Ido oobo keswamakis eoŋ nu: amunaasi akaibois nakalaunan apaaran ŋipeŋin, amunaasi da akasabaton. 39#2 Ika 21:4; Yer 23:11; Esk 44:7. Naarai kedautu aarakin ikecedwe mama ejaas aputosiakee, kosodete alomar aiboisika nakalaunan kapaaran kaŋin amunaar. Ido koany, kwape kwaŋin eswamaata kotogoka. 40#Isa 57:9; Rus 3:3; 2 Ika 9:30; Yer 4:30. Ido bobo ijukakis anyaraun ituŋa luepote kama elwana: luejukakinio ekiyakia, ido koany, kopotu. Kilon kanuka nul, kojuk akonyekon, kisir lukisiro. 41#Awa 7:17; Yer 44:17; Esk 16:18,19; Kos 2:8. Kiboikin aperis nanepuk, kitemonokinitete emesa kinarenike, loibwaikinit lukisupurununoka ka akakinyet. 42Kojai eporoto loka ekodet loijamamai kaŋes: koyaunai ikamerak anyoun kamodiŋ nepepe kimukopin; kitanapai ŋun aikomon akaninikec, ka abokoi da nuejok akwesikee.
43“Kasodi eoŋ einer nuka nin naemoionikinit adoteu ebe, Kwana edoteete kaŋes, ŋin da kakes. 44Kolomasi mama ejai, kwape elomariata mama ejai naadote. Kwape kwaŋin kelomariata mama ejai Akola ka Akoliba, aŋor nukeporok. 45#Esk 16:38; Yok 8:4-7. Etubokinete ikadolitok ŋun akiro kwape etubokinere nudotea, ido kwape etubokinere aŋor nuebukorete aokot; naarai eraas nudotea, ejaas aokot akaninikee.”
46Naarai ebala kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: “Etonyoikini kes ekodet, kainakin aiiliilio ka anatario. 47#Esk 16:41; 24:21; 2 Siit 36:17. Ibiribirei kes ekodet kamor, icutarete kikilenakec; earete ikecedwe lusap ka lupesur, ieweete ikecetogoi kakim. 48#Ikis 13:11; 2 Pet 2:6. Kwaŋin etanania eporo kakwap, tetere isisianakinio aŋor kere kinyeis arokor eporokus. 49Ido kitacakinio eporokus, ibwokete aronisio nuka aputosiakus: ijenunete do ebe arai eoŋ Ejakait EDEKE.
