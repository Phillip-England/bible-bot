Esekiel 27

27
1Kobu bobo akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 2#Esk 26:17.“Ŋo da, okoku loka etuŋanan, kokuudian Turo; 3#Esk 28:2; Isa 23:3. kolimok Turo ebe, Wu jo loiboie kaneelomarere aŋolol, loirai ekagwelanaran loka atekerin mama ejaas apujana, inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
Ijo, wu Turo, iner ebe,
‘Adolit eoŋ kaiokis.’
4Ejaas iikorunokon otau loka aŋololin;
Itodolito ikadukokokon ajokisikon.
5 # Ikis 3:9. Eswamata abaoikon kere
Koberosin lueyaunio koSenir:
Ayaut iresia koLebanon Aiswamakinia jo epir.
6 # Yer 2:10. Eswamata akinolakon koluan luko Basan;
Eswamata atutubetakon nukokwap koteasurin
Lueyaunio kapuiana nuka Kitim,
Kitwalitete kikela luka itomei.
7 # Age 10:4. Korai emederakon loka ebaputa loejok
Loesonyitai loeyaunio koMisiri,
Koraun aanyunetekon;
Ŋesesinei lubulului ka lumugeik
Lueyaunio kapuiana nuka Elisa
Kiswapito jo.
8Keraas ikonikekwanak
Luibojete koSidon ka Arubad;
Ŋo Turo, kejaas lukaeoakon kotomakon,
Keraas ikeŋarenokokon.
9 # Yos 13:5. Imakio luko Gebal, ka lukacoake
Keraas ikanwokakakon kotomakon;
Atakerin kere nuka aŋolol nepepe kikenarenokokec
Kejaas tomakon aisiecanar igwelanarakon.
10 # Yer 46:9; Esk 30:5. “Perusia ka Lud ka Put keiaas aiorekon, ikajiokokon: kikaianakinete ibukui ka akopiran tomakon; kiturito akoniiokis. 11Keiaenenas ituŋa luka Arubad nepepe kaiorekon kuju arwatatinikon airimor, kejaenenas Igamain toma abwasinikon: kikajanakinete ibukuikec arwatatinikon airimor; itodolito ajokisikon.
12 # 2 Siit 20:36. “Kerai Tarusis ekagwelanaranikon kanuka aipues naka abar nakipoŋesio luipu; kisiceanarete epuesa, ka asua, ka adebe, ka asasin kanuka iborokon. 13#1 Siit 1:5; Yok 3:6. Yaban, ka Tubal, ka Mesek, keraas ikagwelanarakakon; kisiceanarete kesi awat nuka ituŋa ka iboro luka aisinin kanuka igwelanarakon. 14#Age 10:3; 1 Siit 1:6. Kisiceanarete lukokek loka Togaruma ibalasin ka ibalasin luka ejie ka inyubun kanuka iborokon, 15#Age 10:7; 1 Siit 1:9. Keraas ituŋa luko Dedan ikagwelanarakakon: keraas apujana nuipu agwelanaret naka akanikon: kiinununete ikela luka itomei ka eboni,#27:15 eboni ŋes ekitoi lokirionon. aisiceanar. 16Kerai Edom ekagwelanaranikon kanuka aipues naka aswamisiokon: kisiceanarete imeralidin, ka lumugeik, ka aswam naesonyitai, ka ebaputa loejok, ka koral,#27:16 imeralidin ka koral eraas amor nukeriai. ka amor da nukareŋak kanuka iborokon. 17#1 Ika 5:9; Asw 12:20. Yuda ka akwap naka Isirael keraas ikonikagwelanarak: kisiceanarete akima nuka Minit, ka Panag, ka esik ka akinyet, ka emim kanuka igwelanarakon, 18Kerai Damasiko ekagwelanaranikon kanuk: aipues naka aswamisiokon, kanuka aipues naka abar nakipoŋesio luipu kebino loka Kelubon, ka ajul nuekwanak nuka amerekekin. 19Kisiecanaret Bedan ka Yaban iusio kanuka iborokon: asua nuepwatat, ekasia, ka akalamu keraas igwelanarakon. 20#Age 25:3. Kerai Dedan ekagwelanaranikon loka igoei luketiai lukibwanenis. 21#Age 25:13; 1 Siit 1:29. Arabia, ka ikapolok kere luko Kedar, keraas ŋul ikagwelanarak luka akanikon; imerekekin ludidik, ka imerekekin ka akinei keraas ikagwelanarakakon luka ŋul. 22#Age 10:7; Sab 72:10,15; Isa 60:6. Ikagwelanarak luko Seba ka Raama keraas ikagwelanarakakon: kisiceanarete luitelekarito kere kalueuru maa, ka amor kere nuketiai, ka esabu, kanuka iborokon, 23#Age 11:31; 25:3; 2 Ika 19:12; Asw 7:4. Karan, ka Kane, ka Eden, ikagwelanarak luko Seba, ka Asur ka Kilimad, keraas ikagwelanara kakon. 24Keraas nul ikagwelanarak luka iboro lueseunitai, iloun lubululu ka luesonyitai, ka asadukun nuka inapen luibusak, nueenitai kaunoi nueswamaunio keres, kalugwelanarakon. 25#Isa 2:16; Apu 18:11-19. Keraas ameerin nuka Tarusi nukalotok nuka igwelanarakon.
Kitelelebi, kitubusikinitete noi
Kotoma otau loka aŋololin.
26 # Sab 48:7. Kiyanaasi ikekwaŋakakon akipi nuipu:
Kibili ekwam lokokide kotau loka aŋololin.
27 # Awa 11:4. Abarakon, ka iborokon igwelanarakon,
Ikekwaŋakakon, ka ikeŋarenokokon,
Ikanwokakakon, ikesiceanarak luka igwelanarakon,
Ka ituŋakon kere lukajiok, lueiaas tomakon,
Nepepe katukotokon kere naejai kidinikon,
Ibirokinos otau loka aŋololin
Apaaran naka aibirorikon.
28Emaranete ironyia
Kanuka eporoto loka aimony naka ikeŋarenokokon.
29 # Apu 18:17. Ŋesunos kameerinikec
Kere luitinito akinolia.
Ikekwaŋak, ka ikeŋarenok kere luka aŋolol,
Ebwoete do kaneoŋit,
30 # Nek 9:1; Aku 2:10; Apu 18:19; Est 4:1. Isiirarete ikeeeporotoi mamakon,
Edelorete,
Iwelanakinos apua akwesikee,
Ibelibelos kokurwon.
31Ebarenos kanukon, enapete ipukoi,
Ido emonyete akiyo kanukon kokadakinito itauonokee
Kaibworo naedwar.
32Kotoma aimonyokee ekuudianete kanukon,
Kikuudianikinete ebe,
“Iŋai ma eputosi bala Turo,
Bala ŋol loisililinio kokidin aŋolol?
33Nakelomuniata iborokon kotoma aŋolol,
Kisimonik ituŋa luipu;
Kanuka aipues naka abarakon ka igwelanarakon
Kitabar ikabakan luka akwapin.
34Kwana kibilata aŋololin
Kataro naka akipi,
Kolumosi igwelanarakon ka atukotokon kere
Nepepe kajo.
35 # Esk 26:15. Kiumokis luibojete kere kapujana,
Akuriakata noi ikabakanakec,
Anyorididoto ireetakee.
36 # Yer 18:16. Kisuasi ikagwelanarak kokidin otuŋa;
Iraun etasuro,
Mam bobo ijaun.’’’
