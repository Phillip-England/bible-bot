Esekiel 41

41
1 # Sek 6:12; Mat 16:18; 1 Kor 3:16; 2 Kor 6:16; Pes 2:21; Apu 3:12. Kayau oyekalu, kitem ategerio, alalaukee ikubitin ikanykape epuwai, ka ikubitin ikanykape epuwai, nakerai alalau naka ewema. 2Alalau naka alomaret ikubitin itomon; isiepiort luka alomaret keraas ikubitin ikany epuwai, ka ikubitin ikany da epuwai; kitem aojauke, ikubitin akaisaoŋon, alalauke ikubitin akaisaarei. 3Kosodi alomar toma, kitem ŋinitegerioit naka alomaret, ikubitin iarei; ka alomaret, ikubitin ikanykape; alalau naka alomaret, ikubitin ikanykaarei. 4#1 Ika 6:20; 2 Siit 3:8; Apu 21:2,3,16,17. Kitem aojauke, ikubitin akaisaarei, alalauke da ikubitin akaisaarei, koiŋaren keyekalu: katema eoŋ atiar, “Na ŋes aibois nakalaunan noi.”
5Kosodi kitem arwatat naka etogo, ikubitin ikanykape; alalau da naka ŋinigola nakosiep, ikubitin iwonon, airimor etogo kowaitin kere. 6#1 Ika 6:5,6. Eeulul agolai nukosiep irwan auni, adiopet kuju kanaee: akaisauni kaŋinikiculet, Kolomasi ŋun arwatat naka etogo kanuka agolai nukosiep airimor, tetere iganio kanen, komam egoijakina arwatat naka etogo. 7Kiyatanakinos agolai nukosiep alalaar airimor, iwosete kuju: naarai airimor etogo eyatanakin aiwot kuju airimor etogo: ŋes ainakin alalau naka etogo iyatanakin kuju: kwaŋin kidoka anyoun kanakokwap kiton kuju itori aieulet nakokidin. 8#Isa 28:16; Esk 40:5. Abu kaany da ebe kejai etut airimor: apeteta nuka agolai nukosiep keraas agada adiopet eut naka ikubitin ikanykape lueojak. 9Abures naka arwatat nakokiŋa naka agolai nukosiep kerai ikubitin ikany: atutubet nakesali kere aibois naka agolai nukosiep nukotogo. 10Ido kidin kagolai ajai abures naka ikubitin akaisaarei airimor etogo kowaitin kere. 11Ketereikina ikekia luka agolai nukosiep aibois nakesali, atereikina da ekek ediopet agolitomei, ka ekek eee eŋalakimak: abures naka aibois nakesali kerai ikubitin ikany airimor.
12Aduket nakejai kinaren kaneatiakaritai kari nakoto alalauke ikubitin akaisakanykaarei; abures da naka arwatat naka aduket kerai ikubitin ikany airimor, aojauke da ikubitin akaisakanykaoŋon.
13Kitem etogo, aojauke, ikubitin akwatat; kaneatiakaritai ka aduket nepepe karwatatinike aojauke ikubitin akwatat. 14Alalau da nakoinaren ketogo kokide, ka alaro kerai ikubitin akwatat.
15Kitem aoiau naka aduket koinaren kaneatiakaritai naejai kauke, ka ibalasanake epuwai ka epuwai, ikubitin akwatat.
Eyekalu da lokotoma, ka agolai nulomaret nuka alaro, 16#1 Ika 7:4; Isa 54:12; Esk 40:16; 1 Kor 13:12. ariri naka ikekia, ka apedon nuagolokino, ka ibalasan luirimorito aiculetakee auni atereikina ariri naka ekek keraparito akito kowaitin kere, anyoun kokwap aitodol apedon; ido keraparitai apedon: 17aitodol apak nakejai kuju kekek kiton togo lokotoma, kokina da, karwatat da kere airimor kotoma, kokina da, kaitemis. 18#Age 3:24; Any 25:22; 1 Sam 4:4; 2 Sam 22:11; 1 Ika 6:29; 2 Siit 3:10; Sab 80:1; 99:1; Esk 10:2,14; 1:10; Apu 4:7-9. Kegeritai ikerubin ka idukudukun; kejai edukudukut kidiŋ ekerubi ka ekerubi, kejaas nollkerubi ireeta iarei; 19kwaŋin kejai ereet loka etuŋanan atereikina edukudukut kepuwai, ka ereet loka ikoku yenika eŋatuny atereikina edukudukut kepuwai. Kiswamatai kwaŋin anatar etogo kere airimor; 20kegeritai ikerubin ka idukudukun anyoun kokwap kiton kuju okek: kikoni nen arwatat naka eyekalu.
21Oyekalu kejaas ategerio nueriakanara owaitin kere; koinaren naka aibois nakalaunan ajai ibore yenasubit bala 22#Any 30:1,8; 1 Ika 7:48; 2 Siit 4:19; Apu 8:3; Awa 9:2; Eko 1:12; Esk 44:16; Mal 1:7,12. etem loka akito, amukururuke ikubitin iuni, aojauke ikubitin iarei; agoriaike, kwapuke, ka arwatatinike keraas nuka akito. Katema eoŋ atiar, “Lo ŋes erŋesa loejai akonyen nuka EJAKAIT,” 23#1 Ika 6:31-35. Ido eyekalu ka aibois nakalaunan da kere ajaas ikekia iarei. 24Araas ikirigin luka abaoi aarei, abaoi aarei nuesukonokinos; abaoi aarei nuka ekirigi ediopet, ka abaoi aarei nuka ekirigi lokiareit. 25Ido kegerakinitai okirigin luka eyekalu, ikerubin ka idukudukun, kwape ageritere arwatatin; ido kinaren kagola nalomaret kokina, kejaas abaoi nueburok nuka akito. 26#Esk 40:16. Ajaas apedon nugolokino, ka idukudukun epuwai ka epuwai da, kowaitin luka agola nalomaret: kikote nen agolai nukosiep nuka etogo, ka abaoi nueburok.
