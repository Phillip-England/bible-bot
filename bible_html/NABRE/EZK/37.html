Esekiel 37

37
1 # Esk 3:14; Luk 4:1. Koiau akan naka EJAKAIT kaeoŋ, kayana kina komoyo loka EJAKAIT, kebwaik aditot naeleleba akojo. 2Ketotor airimor kes: ido koany, epu noi kaditot; ido koany, kookito de. 3#1 Sam 2:6; Rom 4:17; 2 Kor 1:9. Kalimok eoŋ ebe, “Okoku loka etuŋanan, epedorete tope akojo nu aijar?” Kabonok ebe, “Wu Ejakait EDEKE, jo ijeni.” 4#Isa 55:11; Rom 10:17. Kalimok eoŋ bobo ebe, “Kodwarak akoio nu, kolimok ebe, Yes akojo nuiwokito, kiirasi akirot naka EJAKAIT. 5#Sab 104:30. Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE akojo nu ebe: Koany, ketolomikini yes eiyeŋane, ido ijarete. 6#Esk 6:7; Yoe 2:27. Ebwaikini aputio mamakus, ayauni akiriŋ mamakus, karapakinete amuny, akutakini eiyan ane, ido ijarete; ijenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.”
7Aso kadwara kwape kecorakinitere: napadwaria kojai airuruma, ido koany, komaran akwap, kiriamanakinos akoio, akoit mama ejai akoit naeeke. 8Kaany, ido koany, keiaas aputio mamakee, kolom akiriŋ, korapak amuny kokuju: konye kemamei eiyeŋane kotomakec. 9Kosodi kalimok eoŋ ebe, “Kodwarak eiyeŋane, kodwar, okoku loka etuŋanan, kolimok eiyeijane ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Kobia, ijo eiyeŋane, kokwamin iwonon, kiyenak lu luearitai, kojarata.” 10#Apu 11:11. Aso kadwara kwape kecorakinia, koloma eiyeŋane tomakec, kojarata, kobwaata kakecekeje, aiore naailiili noi.
11 # Sab 141:7; Isa 49:14. Kosodi kaIimok eoŋ ebe, “Okoku loka etuŋanan, eraas akojo nu ekek kere loka Isirael. Koany, ebalas ebe, ‘Aokoto akoiowok, aolio amunowok: kipiari eut.’ 12#Isa 26:19; Kos 1:11; Esk 36:24; Esr 1:1; Amo 9:14. Kanuka riun kodwar, kolimok kes ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Koany, apukori ailinakus, ketolomunete kailinakus, yes ikatuŋa; kayaunete kiton akwap naka Isirael. 13Ido ijenunete ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, kadau apukor ailinakus, ka aitolomun kailinakus, yes ikatuŋa, 14#Esk 36:27. Kepikakinete yes ekamoyo, ijarete, kebwaikinete akwapukus. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Ŋenunete do ebe Eoŋ EJAKAIT ener, eswama da.”
15Kobu bobo akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe, 16#Mar 17:2; 2 Siit 11:12-16.“Ka jo da, okoku loka etuŋanan, kolem ebela ediopet, kiwadik toma obela ebe, ‘Loka Yuda ka idwe luka Isirael ipaupake’; kosodi kolem eeebela, kiwadik toma obela, ‘Loka Yosep, ebela laka Epuraim, laka ekek kere laka Isirael ipaupake.’ 17Aso kimorikik nepepe araun ediopebela, korau ediopet kakanikon, 18#Esk 12:9. Kosodete naineratar idwe luka ikonituŋa kaio, ebe, ‘Kiteienikinai apolou naka nun?” 19#Sek 10:6. Kolimok kes ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Koany, alemuni ebela loka Yosep, loejai akan naka Epuraim, ka atekerin nuka Isirael ipaupake; ebwaikini kes ebela loka Yuda, etarauni kes ebela ediopet, eraunete kesi ediopet kakanika. 20#Esk 12:3. Ibelai da luiwadikat eiaunete akanikon kakonyenekec. 21#Esk 36:24. Kolimok kes ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Koany, alemuni idwe luka Isirael kokidin atekerin nualosito, katukonok kowaitin kere, kayau kes toma akwapukec. 22#Isa 11:13; Kos 1:11; Yer 3:18; 23:5,6; Age 49:10; Esk 34:23; Yok 10:16. Ido etuluori ateker adiopet kakwap, koroijan luka Isirael; ekabaka ediopet eraun ekecekabaka kere: mam bobo eraunos atekerin aarei, kwabo mam etiakio eut bobo araun ajakanuto aarei. 23#Isa 2:18; Kos 14:8; Esk 20:43; 36:25; Sek 13:1,2; Esk 36:28,29. Ido mam emunaaros bo bo kanuka aputosiakee, karaida italesiakee, karaida adiakanaretakec kere: konye etaiari kes kotoma aiierukanutukec kere, naeswamaeneniata nukaronok, etesegi kes: asa eraunete do ikatuŋa, eoŋ da araun Ekecedeke.
24 # Esk 34:23,24; Isa 40:11; Yer 23:5; Kos 3:5; Luk 1:32; Sab 78:71,72; Sek 13:7; Yok 10:16; 1 Pet 5:4. “Daudi ekabulesit ŋes erauni ekabakakee; edumunete ekeeokon ediopet. Ido elosenenete kotubcsiaka, ka airie ikakisila, iswamaenenete da. 25#Isa 60:21; Amo 9:15; Yok 12:34. Ido iboikinos akwap nakolo ainakini Yakob ekabulesit, naeboienenoto lukapapaakus; aso iboikinos nen, kes ka ikeeedwe, ka idwe luka idwekec, ikar kere: Daudi da ekabulesit ŋes eraenena ekapolonikee ikar kerc. 26#Sab 89:3; Isa 55:3; Yer 32:40; Esk 34:25; 2 Kor 6:16. Ido bobo etutuki kakes aitutuket naka ainapakin: eraun aitutuket namam enaŋi kamakee: esirereni kes, etupui kes, ebwaikini akaibois nakalaunan kidinikec ikar kere. 27#Leb 26:11; Yok 1:14; 2 Kor 6:16; Apu 21:3. Eiaenena da ekawema kakes: eoŋ da araun Ekecedeke, kes da eraunos ikatuŋa. 28Ejenunete do atekerin ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT loetalaunit Isirael, naejaenenar akaibois nakalaunan kidinikec ikar kere.”
