Esekiel 28

28
1Kobu bobo akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 2#Ikis 8:14; 2 Siit 26:16; Awa 16:18; Dan 5:22,23; Isa 2:12; 31:3; 1 Tim 3:6; Sab 9:20. “Okoku loka etuŋanan, kolimok ekapolon loka Turo ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
Naarai ekeun ekonitau,
Ibu da kiner ebe, ‘Arai eoŋ edeke,
Eboiei koicolon loka Edeke,
Kokidiŋ kaŋololin’;
Kwabo irai etuŋanan, me re Edeke,
Karaida itupusit ijo ekonitau bala etau loka Edeke:
3 # Dan 2:48; Sek 9:2. Koany, irai ijo lokaeoa aitelekar Daniel;
Mam kimunonikinitai jo nukaiyeiyea kere,
4Kanuka acoakon ka ajenunikon idum abar,
Idum da esabu ka epuesa
Kotoma ainadisiakon:
5 # Ikis 8:12-14. Kanuka acoakon naepol, ido kanuka agwelanarikon
Iyatanak abarakon,
Ekeun da ekonitau kanuka abarakon.
6 # Dan 7:25,26; Asw 12:22; 2 Ses 2:4. Aso do inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
Naarai itupusit ijo ekonitau Bala etau loka Edeke;
7 # Esk 30:11. Aso koany, ayauni imo asurokin jo, Lusogim luka atekerin:
Eutunete ikecekileŋa
Asurokin aiokis naka acoakon,
Emunaarete amwekimwekekon.
8Kijesarete jo kiton Aipany;
Itwani atwanare naka nul kaluearitai,
Kotau loka aŋololin.
9Irina kereka ijo inera ebe, ‘Arai eoŋ Edeke,’
Kakonyen nuka yenikiari?
Kwabo irai etuŋanan, mere Edeke,
Kakan naka yenikimusuni.
10 # Esk 31:18. Itwani atwanare naka lumam ekepitai Kakan naka imo,
Naarai ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe,
Eoŋ ŋes ener.”
11Bobo da kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 12#Esk 27:3. “Okoku loka etuŋanan, kokuudianik ekabaka loka Turo, kolimok ŋes ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
“Ibu ijo korai aanyunet naka etiai,
Ileleba acoa, idoliorit kaiokis.
13 # Age 2:8; 3:23,24; Esk 31:8,9; 26:13; Yok 2:3. Ŋai kolo toma Eden, emanikor loka EDEKE;
Kerai ninlmoru naketiai arapetekon,
Asadia, atopasit, ka daimod.
Aberulo, naemelomelot, ka ayaseper,
Asapiro, emeralid, ka karubunikol;
Iminanakinitai kesabu loegerer.
Kitemonokinitoi kapaaran nakisubunere.
14 # Any 25:20; Esk 20:40. Abu kebwaik jo nepepe kekerubi loidari loinyonyoikinitai akinyet;
Ibu koiai oroŋat lokalaunan loka Edeke;
Kirimi kokidin amor nuka akim.
15Ibu kodolit korotinikon
Ageun kapaaran nakisubunere,
Aitodol neatakanuniata numam adolit kamakon,
16Kanuka aipues naka agwelanarikon,
Kilelebi akaie, idiaka;
Ŋes kaeakaria jo kwape ycnemunaara
Anyoun koroŋat loka Edeke;
Kireŋea jo ekerubi loidari
Kokidin kamor nuka akim.
17 # 2 Pet 2:6; Yud 1:7. Ekeun ekonitau kanuka ajokisikon;
Kimuna acoakon kanuka amwekimwekekon:
Kaeaka jo kiton akwap,
Keperik akonyen nuka ikabakan,
Tetere kisesenete.
18 # Amo 1:10. Kanuka aipucs naka numam adolitekon,
Kanuka numam adolit nuka agwelanarikon,
Imunaa aiboisiokon nukalaunak:
Kanuka nun etolom akim kokidinikon,
Kitisisi jo, ketuluo ekurwon kakwap,
Kakonyen nuka nul kere lukisesenete.
19Kiumokinete nul kere
Lukijenete jo kotoma otuŋa:
Iraun etasuro, mam bobo iiaun.”
20 # Yok 3:4-8; Sek 9:1-2; Mat 11:21-22; Luk 10:13-14. Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 21#Isa 23:4,12; Yer 25:22; Esk 32:30.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, kite Sidon, kodwarak ŋes, 22#Any 14:4; Sab 9:16; Esk 39:13; 20:41. kiner ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe:
“Koany, arai eoŋ lokasurupikon, ijo Sidon;
Ketopoloor kokidiŋikon,
Ejenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
Kadau aitodolikin itubcsia kamake,
Ketalaun kamake.
23 # Esk 38:22. Naarai eyakikini ŋes atular
Ka aokot korotinike;
Ibirokinos Iuimusuijitai kidinike,
Kanuka ekileŋ loirimori ŋes kowaitin kere,
Ejenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
24 # Mar 33:55; Yos 23:13. “Mam bobo eiaununete ikodokodo lueremeremete ekek loka Isirael, karaida ikukwa luisiriebcte nul luirimorcte kes, luiswamakinete kes nukepali, Ejenunete do ebe arai eoŋ Ejakait EDEKE.
25 # Isa 11:12; Kos 1:11; Esk 20:41. “Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Naadaunia atukonokin ekek loka Isirael kotoma otuŋa kama isiarakina, ketalaun kamakec kakonyen nuka atekerin, kosodete iboikinos akwapukec nakainakinit ekabulesit Yakob. 26#Isa 65:21; Amo 9:14; Yer 31:5. Iboikinos nen inapakina; eebo, edukete itocoi, iraete imanikoria luka emusabibu. Iboiete inapakina, kadau aitodolikin itubesia kama kanul kere luiswamaete nukepali kowaitin kere. Ejenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekecedeke,”
