Esekiel 43

43
1 # Esk 10:19. Kosodi kokau kayau orute, ŋes erute loeseseni kide, 2#Isa 6:3; Esk 3:23; 9:3; 10:3-4,18-19; 11:22-23; Apu 1:15; 18:1. Ido koany, kobu aibuses naka Edeke loka Isirael korot lokokide: eporotoke da kwa pe eporoto loka akipi nuipu; kiliyau akwap kanuka akeibuses.
3 # Esk 1:4; 3:23; Yer 1:10. Kosuhite bala atakanu naka aitapoet naanyunit, bala aitapoet naabu kaany naabunio ŋesi amudiar ere: ekote aitapoeta bala aitapoet naabu kaany koeor Kebar; kebiror ebuburia. 4#Esk 10:19. Koloma aibuses naka EJAKAIT oyekalu korot loka erute loeseseni kide. 5#Esk 3:12; 1 Ika 8:10. Kadaka Emoyo eoŋ, kayana kiton alaro nakotoma; ido koany, kileleba aibuses naka EJAKAIT togo.
6 # Esk 40:3. Kobwoi etuŋanan kosiepika, keira eporoto loenera keoŋ kotogo. 7#Sab 99:1,5; 68:16; 1 Siit 28:2; Any 25:8; Leb 26:12,30; Esk 39:7; Yer 16:18. Katema eoŋ atiar, “Okoku loka etuŋanan, na ŋes aibois naka eicolonoka, ka aibois naka aicaketa nuka akejeka, naeboio kidin ka idwe luka Isirael ikar kere. Kwabo mam bobo idwe luka Isirael emunaarete ekakiror lokalaunan, karaida kes, karaida ikabakanakec, kanuka eporokee, ido kanuka atesin nuka ikabakanakec kotoma aiboisiokec nuiceleleete; 8#2 Ika 16:14. Kanuka aiduk aririkee naka ekek eiduny karirika, ategerioitikec eiduny kategerioitika, ajai arwatat bon kidin eoŋ ka kes. Amunaasi ekakiror lokalaunan, kanuka italesiakec lueswamaete: kanuka ŋun etanana kes kanuka akanyunyura. 9Kwana kolemasi ca kesi eporokee, ka atesina nuka ikabakanakec neelwana kamakaŋ, asa eboienenei kokidinikec ikar kere.
10 # Esk 40:4. “Ijo da okoku loka etuŋanan, kitodik ekek loka Isirael etogo lo, tetere irumete ileic kanuka numam adolitekec: kitemoto aputoke. 11#Esk 44:5,6; Mat 28:20. Ido arai irumete ileic kanuka ŋun kere nueswamata, kitejenik kes asubunet naka etogo, ka aputoke, ka alomaretake, ka alomunetake, ka asubunetake kere, ka aicorakinetake, ka asubunetake kere, ka ikisilake kere, kiwadik kakonyenekee: tetere ericete asubuneteke kere, ka aicorakinetake kere, ka aswam da. 12#Sab 93:5; Esk 40:2; Apu 21:27. Lo ŋes ekisil loka etogo: kuju aidi naka eronat kiton osaŋeke kere airimor eraun nakalaunan noi. Koany, ŋol ŋes ekisil loka etogo.
13 # Esk 40:5; Any 27:1-2; 2 Siit 4:1. “Nu kes aitemisio nuka etem kokubitin: (ekubit ŋes ekubit ka alalau naka etau loka akan:) kwapuke amukurur ekubit, alalauke ekubit, ka atokitoitike airimor aituk atutubet naka ekubit: nen ŋes kwap naka etem. 14Ageun kokwap naejai kwap kiton amurut nakokwap ikubitin iarei, alalauke ekubit; ido ageun kamurut naedit kiton amurut naepol, ikubitin iwonon, alalau da ekubit. 15Ido etem lokokuju ikubitin iwonon, ido ageun kokiala loka etem kiton kuju ejaunos amumwar aoŋon. 16Aojau naka ekiala loka etem eraun ikubitin itomon kaarei, alalau da ikubitin itomon kaarei, koriakas iwaitinike kere iwonon. 17#Any 20:26. Amurut da koriana alalau ka aojau ikubitin itomon ka luonon; atokitoitike eraun atutubet naka ekubit, kokwapuke ekubit airimor: esesenete akedoketa kide.”
18 # Leb 1:5; Bur 9:18-22; Any 29:35-37. Katema eoŋ atiar, “Okoku loka etuŋanan, inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Nu kes aicorakineta nuka etem: kapaaran naiswamaata, ainakinia ainakineta nuicweio, ka abwatanakin aokot. 19#1 Ika 2:27,35; Isa 61:5; Yer 33:18; Esk 40:46; Any 29:10; Leb 8:14,15. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Iinakini isasedotin Ilebin lukaur naka Sadok, Iueapiete keoŋ aijaanakin eoŋ, emoŋ lotobait araun ainakinet naka adiakaret. 20Ilemuni aokotoke, ibwaikini amumwarake aoŋon, ka agoria aoŋon nuka amurut, ka atokitoit airimor: kwaŋin itesegia ŋes, iluei da. 21#Any 29:14; Bur 13:11. Ilemuni da emoŋ lotobait loka ainakinet naka adiakaret, icweio ŋes kaibois naeseunitai naka etogo, kokina kaibois nakalaunan. 22Kosodi kapaaran nakiareit iinakini ekoroi lomam eŋwalit araut ainakinet naka adiakaret; itesegete etem, kwape etesegitotor komon lotobait. 23Naidaunia aiteseg, iinakini emoŋ lotobait lomam eŋwalit, ka emerekek lomam eŋwalit loelemunio kosipan. 24#Leb 2:13. Isukunyikini kes akonyen nuka EJAKAIT, eeakanakinete isasedotin abalaŋ, einakinete kes araut ainakinet naicweio mama ejai EJAKAIT. 25#Any 29:35. Kapaarasia akanykaarei itemonokinene ŋinipaaran akinei araut ainakinet naka adiakaret: ido itemonokinete emoŋ lotobait, ka emerekek lomam eŋwalit loelemunio kosipan. 26Apaarasia akanykaarei ilueete kanuka etem, itesegarete; kwaŋin ŋes etiakariata. 27#Leb 9:1; Yob 42:8. Kosodi kedautu apaarasia ŋun, edoli apaaran nakikanyetekauni, ka aiyatanakin da, einanakinete isasedotin ainakinetakus nuieweio, ka ainakinetakus nuka ainapakin kotem. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kaeamuni do yes.”
