Esekiel 20

20
1 # Esk 8:1. Kosodi kanen kokaru lokikanyetekaarei, kolap lokikanyet, kapaaran nakitomonet naka elap, kopotu ice komakio luka Isirael aiŋit EJAKAIT, kiboikinos kakonyeneka. 2Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 3#1 Sam 28:6; Sab 50:15; Awa 15:8; Mat 15:8.“Okoku loka etuŋanan, kinera kimakio luka Isirael, kolimok kes ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Iponito aiŋit eoŋ? Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kwape ajaria, mam kinisete eoŋ, 4#Esk 16:2; Mat 23:32. Okoku loka etuŋanan, itubokini kereka ijo kes? Itubokini kereka ijo kes? Kitejenik italesia luka lukapapaakee. 5#Any 6:2-8; 3:8; 20:2; Ikis 4:34; Age 17:7. Kolimok kes ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Apaaran naaseunia Isirael, kekeu akakan mama ejai aur naka ekek loka Yakob, ketejenun mama ejaas kesi kakwap nako Misiri, naekeunia akakan mamakee, kener atiar, Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 6#Any 3:8,17; Yer 32:22; Sab 48:2. Apaaran ŋin kekeu akakan mamakee, aitonyoun kakwap nako Misiri aitolomar akwap naarotakinit kes, naeleleete akile ka esik, ŋes aibuses naka akwapin kere, 7#2 Siit 15:8; Ikis 29:16; Yos 24:14. Kabala kes ebe, Koeaka ŋinituŋanan italesia luimielas akonyekus, ido sirikimunaaros kaputosia nuko Misiri; arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 8Konye kepaliikis, kagiroto akiirar; mam eeakara ninitunanan italesia luimielasi akonyeke, ido mam inyeina aputosia nuko Misiri.
“Kasodi kener abukokini kes akadedeŋ, aitodolikin akanyunyura mamakee kokidin kakwap nako Misiri. 9#Any 32:12; Mar 14:13. Konye eswama eoŋ nun kanuka ekakiror, kinyek amoromoroo kakonyen nuka atekerin nukejaas kesi, nuketejenunor mamakee, ketolomu kakwap nako Misiri. 10Kwaŋin ketolomu kes kakwap nako Misiri, kayana modin, 11#Ikis 4:8; Nek 9:13; Rom 10:5; Leb 18:5. Kainak ikakisila, ketejenu ikatubesia, luejaria ituŋanan arai kiswama kes, 12#Any 31:13-17; 20:8; Ikis 5:12; Nek 9:14. Bobo da kainak akasabaton, araut aanyunet kidinikec ka eoŋ, tetere ejenunete ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT loetalauni kes, 13#Awa 1:25. Konye kepaliik ekek loka Isirael kamodiŋ: mam alosenenete kokisilaka, kogiroto ikatubesia luejaria ituŋanan arai kiswama kes; komunaasi noi akasabaton,
“Kasodi kener abukokini kes akadedeŋ kamodiŋ, amudiar kes. 14Konye eswama eoŋ ŋun kanuka ekakiror, kinyek amoromoroo kakonyen nuka atekerin, kanuetolomunia kes kakonyenekec. 15#Sab 95:11; Mar 14:26-35. Bobo da kekeu akakan mamakec kamodiŋ, kenyek ayaun kes toma akwap naainakinit, naeleleete akile ka esik, ŋes aibuses naka akwapin kere. 16#Mar 15:39; Sab 78:37; Esk 14:3,4; Amo 5:25,26; Asw 7:42,43. Naarai aneroto ikatubesia, komam elosenenete kokisilaka, komunaasi da akasabaton: naarai atupanarete ikecetauon aputosiakec, 17#1 Sam 24:10; Sab 78:38; Nek 9:19. Konye kitim akakoŋu, kenyek amudiar kes, kwabo mam abu kapia kes cut kamodiŋ,
18“Kasodi alimokin ikecedwe kamodiŋ ebe, Sirilosenenete kokisila luka lukapapaakus, ido siriricenenete itubesiakec, kwabo sirikimunaaros kaputosiakec, 19#Ikis 5:32–12:32. Arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke, kolosenenete kokisilaka, koricenenete ikatubesia, kiswamaenenete, 20#Any 20:11; Nek 13:17-19. ido kitalautu akasabaton; eraunete aanyunet kidinikus ka eoŋ, tetere ijenunete ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT Ekusideke. 21#Mar 25:1,2; Ikis 9:23,24. Konye kepaliikis idwe; mam kelosenenete kokisilaka, kwabo mam kericenenete itubesiaka aiswamaenen, luejaria ituŋanan, arai kiswama kes; komunaasi da akasabaton.
“Kasodi kener abukokin kes akadedeŋ, aitodolikin akanyunyura mamakec kamodiŋ. 22#Sab 78:38. Konye kenyaku akakan, keswama kanuka ekakiror, kinyek amoromoroo kakonyen nuka atekerin kanuetolomunia kes kakonyenekec, 23#Ikis 28:64; Sab 106:27; Leb 26:33. Bobo da kekeu akakan mamakec kamodiŋ ebe, eweliweli kes toma atekerin, esiarisiari kes toma akwapin; 24#Esk 6:9; Mk 7:22. Naarai mam apotu kiswamata ikatubesia, konye koneroto ikakisila, komunaasi da akasabaton, ido emielas akecekonye aputosia nuka lukapapaakec. 25#Sab 81:12; Rom 1:24. Bobo da abu kainak kes ikisila lumam ejok, ka itubesia lumam ejariata. 26#2 Ika 17:17; 2 Siit 28:3; Yer 32:35; Esk 16:20,21. Kamuna kes kanuka ainakinetakec, naarai kitotorenenoto ikiyaikec kere akim, tetere etisiliori kes, kojenutu ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT,
27 # Rom 1:23. “Aso do, okoku loka etuŋanan, kinera kekek loka Isirael, kolimok ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Kanuka nu bobo ŋes kamoromorooto lukapapaakus, naarai apotu kepaliikis eoŋ. 28#Isa 57:5; Esk 16:19. Naarai naadaunia ayaun kes akwap, nakekeu akakan ainakin kes, kosodete nakeanyuniata eukot loeoia, ka ekitoi loetumit, koinakis kanen asubanakec, koinakis kanen ainakinetakee nuitunyunyurikinete; kanen da kisupuruut asakatakec nueuru saa, kobukonokinete kanen ainakinetakec numatan. 29Kasodi alimokin kes ebe, Aibois naicelelei nailosete apolouke inyo? Kosodi komaikinai ekekiror Bama #20:29 Apolouke Aibois naicelelei. kiton lolo. 30Aso do kolimok ekek loka Isirael ebe, Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Imunaaros ca kwape kejaar epone loka lukapapaakus? Ido idotearos ca atupar italesiakec? 31#Awa 1:27,28; Sek 7:13; Mat 25:11,12. Naiinanakiniata ainakinetakus, naitotorioto idwekus akim, imunaaros ca kaputosiakus kere kiton lolo, Aso kinisete ca eoŋ, WU ekek loka Isirael? Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kwape ajaria, mam kiŋisete eoŋ.
32 # Esk 11:5. “Kanin da naelomari otauonokus mam ejaun nepetai da; naarai ineras ebe, ‘Kirauni kwape atekerin, kwape ikekia luka akwapin, aijaanakin ikito ka amor.’
33 # Yer 21:5. “Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kwape ajaria, abeit arauni ekabakakus kakan nakagogon, kakan da naerioro, kadeden naebukoro. 34Ido kalemuni katekerin, katukonok yes alemun kakwapin nukiisiarakinio, kakan nakagogon, kakan da naerioro, kadeden naebukoro. 35#Esk 17:20. Kayauni amodiŋ naka atekerin, ido kipuri kaŋina kayes kasesenas kakonyen. 36#Mar 14:21. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Kwape kapurio kalukapapaakus kamodiŋ naka akwap nako Misiri, kwape kwaŋin ŋes kipurio kayes da. 37#Leb 27:32; Yer 33:13. Ketotori yes kwap kebela, ketolomari olomorut loka aitutuket; 38#Esk 34:17; 13:9; Mat 3:12; Sab 95:11; 1 Kor 10:5; Bur 4:6. apiari lukepali kotomakus, ka nul da luediakarete mama ajai: alemuni kakwap naiboioto, konye mam elomarete akwap naka Isirael. Ŋenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT.
39 # Sab 81:12; Amo 4:4; Awa 21:27; Isa 1:13; Yer 7:9-11; Esk 23:38. “Inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe: Ka jo da, wu ekek loka Isirael, koloto kiiaanak ninitunanan aputosiake, amear lolo, arai kiineroro akiirar eoŋ: konye mam bobo imunaarete ekakiror lokalaunan kainakinetakus, ka aputosiakus.
40 # Isa 2:2; 56:7; Esk 17:23; Sek 8:20; Mal 3:4; Rom 12:1. “Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Naarai koronataka lokalaunan, koronat loka aicelel naka Isirael, kanen kejaanakinia ekek kere loka Isirael, kes kere, kakwap: kanen ŋes acamunia kes, kanen da eburaa ainakinetakus, ka araito nugeet nuka ainakinetakus, ka iborokus kere lukalaunak. 41Acamuni yes kwape asakat naeuru saa, nakalemunia ayaun katekerin, katukonok alemun kakwapin nukiisiarakinitai; ketalaunio kamakus kakonyen nuka atekerin. 42#Yer 24:7; Yok 17:3; Esk 36:23; 11:17. Ŋenunete do ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT, naketolomaria akwap naka Isirael, akwap naabu kekeu akakan ainakin lukapapaakus. 43#Esk 16:61; Leb 26:39; Kos 5:15. Kosodete iitunete kaŋina ikusirotin, ka aswamisiokus kere, nuipotu komunaarotor; kikadakinete kanuka aronisiokus kere nuiswamato. 44#Esk 24:24; 36:22. Ebala Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Ŋenunete ebe arai eoŋ EJAKAIT nakeswamakiniata yes kanuka ekakiror, mere kwape ejaatar irotinikus lueroko, karaida kwape ejaatar aswamisiokus nukasilianut.”
45Kobu akirot naka EJAKAIT mama ajai ebe: 46“Okoku loka etuŋanan, kitete akonikonyen eŋalakimak, kolimonok akonikirot kobei eŋalakimak, kodwarak amagoro naka ais nakotoma Eŋalakimak. 47#Yer 21:14; Luk 23:31; Esk 21:4. Kolimok amagoro naka Eŋalakimak ebe, Kiira akirot naka EJAKAIT: inera kwanata Ejakait EDEKE ebe, Koany enokokini akim mamakon, kicwe nolikltoi loepesit kotomakon, ka nolikitoi loeoŋit: mam itudenio aipelonoreta nuka akim naesarani, enyami akim akonyen kere ageun kenalakimak kiton agolitomei. 48Eanyunete awat kere ebe eoŋ EJAKAIT enokokinit: mam itudenio.” 49Kasodi atemar atiar, “Wu okwe Ejakait EOEKB! Kabalas eoŋ ebe, c Mere ŋes eketataman loka awaragasia?’”
