1 Samuel 10

10
1 # 1 Sam 16:13; 2 Ika 9:3; Any 19:6; Ikis 32:9; Yer 10:16. Kosodi Samuel alemun ekube loka akinyet, kobukok akouke, konu ŋes, kotema atiar, “Mere kinyonyoik EJAKAIT jo akinyet araun ekapolon loiyait airumeteke? 2#Age 35:19; Yos 18:28. Nainyounio kaneaiai eoŋ lolo, idumuni ituŋa iarei eiduny kates naka Rakel, koikor loka Benyamin ka Selusa; kilimokinete jo ebe, ‘Asigiria nuibu kolot aimo adumunos: ido koany, enyek papakon apod nuka asigiria epod jo ebe, “Ekwaikini ai kanuka okokuka?” 3#Yos 19:22; Tub 4:6,12; 8:18; Sab 89:12; Age 28:22; 35:1. Ŋes do ilosio ijo kanen, idoli olua loka Tabor, iriamunos kiruna iuni kaŋina luelosete mama ejai Edeke Bet-el, ibwokit ediopet ikalei iuni, ibwokit ece imugatin iuni, ka loce da ibwokit eriagi loka emukule loka ebino: 4#Tub 18:15. Kiyogaete jo, kiinakinete jo imugatin iarei; luijauni jo kakanikec. 5#1 Sam 13:3; 9:12; Any 15:20; 2 Ika 3:15; 1 Kor 14:1. Akaulo kanun ibuni ijo oukot loka EJAKAIT, neejai aibois naka iserikalcn luka Ipilisitian; kosodi kanen naidolio jo ore, iriamunos kerionet loka inabin ijesunos kaibois naicelelei ejaas kesabuli, ka esimat, ka alamaru, ka adeudeu koiŋarenikec; ido kodwarete kesi: 6#Mar 11:25; 1 Sam 16:13; 19:23; Mat 7:22. Kidoikini jo emoyo loka EJAKAIT kagogon, idwari da nepepe kakes, iluor ijo ecetuŋanan, 7#Any 4:8; Age 21:20; Ikis 20:1; Tub 6:12; Mat 1:23. Kikoni nen, nakidoikiniata jo aanyuneta nun, iswamai nuibusakinit aswam; naarai ejai Edeke kaio, 8#1 Sam 11:14; 13:8. Kinarenikini eoŋ alosit Giligal; ido koany, abuni mama ijai ainakin ainakineta nuicweio, ka asuban nuka ainakineta nuka ainapakin; idareuni apaarasia akanykaarei, aitodol neabunio, aitodikin jo nuiswamai.”
9Kikwaikis nen, naanyounio ŋesi kaneaiai Samuel, abu Edeke koinak ŋes ecetau: kodoloto aanyuneta nun kere kapaaran kaŋin, 10#1 Sam 19:20; Mat 7:21-23. Kosodi kedolut oukot, koany, kiriamunos erioŋet loka inabin kaŋes; kodoik ŋes emoyo loka Edeke kagogon, kodwar kakes. 11#Mat 13:54; Yok 7:15; Asw 4:13. Kosodi kanen, keanyut lulem ajenete ŋes ebe koany, edwari nepepe kinabin, kotemanaros ituŋa ebc, “Inyo bo yen yenadoiki okoku loka Kis? Ejai Saul da onabin?” 12#1 Sam 19:23-24; Isa 54:13; Yok 3:8. Koboŋok ediopet loka aibois kaŋin ebe, “Iŋai bo papakec?” Koraun do awaragan ebe, “Ejai Saul da onabin?” 13Kosodi kedau aidwar, kobu aibois naicelelei,
14 # 1 Sam 14:50. Kotema mamai loka Saul ŋes ka ekebulesit atiar, “Ai bo ipotu yesi koloto?” Kotema atiar, “Amoot asigiria: kosodi kiiany ebe mam apotu kodumunos, kapotu mama ejai Samuel.” 15Kotema mamai loka Saul atiar, “Keŋaiŋait kolimokinai nuabu Samue! kolimok jo.” 16Kotema Saul mamaike atiar, “Abu kolimok sio ebe adumunos asigiria.” Konyc akirot naka ajakanut naabu Samuel kinerite, mam abu kolimok ŋes.
17 # Tub 11:11; 20:1; 1 Sam 11:15; 7:5. Konyarau Samuel ituŋa mama ejai EJAKAIT Misipa; 18#Any 3:7,8; 14:11; Ikis 4:34; Tub 6:8; Nek 9:9-12,27,28. Kotema idwe luka Isirael atiar, “Inera kwanata EJAKAIT, Edeke loka Isirael ebe, ‘Abu ketolom Isirael koMisiri, kayu yes kakan naka Imisirian, kakan da naka ajakanuto kere nukimwanito yes’: 19#1 Sam 8:7; 12:12; Luk 18:16. Konye ineroto lolo Edekekus, loabu kitajar yes katiokisiokus kere kocanikus da; ipotu kotemasi ŋes ebe, ‘Mam, konye koseikinai sio ekabaka.’ Aso do kitodiaros akonyen nuka EJAKAIT katekerinikus ka ilukuminikus.”
20 # Yos 7:14; Asw 1:24. Kisukuny do Samuel atekerin kere nuka Isirael, koseunai ateker naka Benyamin. 21Kisukuny ateker naka Benyamin kokekiakec, koseunai ekek loka Imaturin: kisukuny ekek loka Maturi ediopet ediopet; koseunai Saul okoku loka Kis; konye kemoata ŋes mam apotu kodumut. 22#1 Sam 23:2. Kanuka ŋun kinita bobo EJAKAIT ebe, “Ejai kereka etuŋanan loeroko kedoluno?” Koboŋok EJAKAIT ebe, “Koany, enopit koboro.” 23Kokerata koomata ŋes kaŋina; kosodi kebwo ŋesi kotuna, aoja ŋesi adepar ituŋa kere ageun kokeperinike. 24Kotema Samuel iruna kere atiar, “Ianyut yesi ŋol loaseu EJAKAIT, ebe emamei loeputotor kotuna kere?” Kiworoto ituŋa kere ebe,” Kidari Edeke ekabaka.”
25 # Ikis 17:14; 1 Sam 8:11. Kosodi Samuel alimokin ituŋa epone loka ajakanut, kiwadik oitabo, kibwaik akonyen nuka EJAKAIT, Kotwar Samuel ituŋa kere, ninitunanan togoke.
26Kolot Saul da togoke Gibea; kolototo ituŋa lukirigin luatirorit Edeke itauonokee kaŋes. 27#Ikis 13:13; 1 Sam 11:12; Asw 7:35,51,52; 2 Sam 8:2; 1 Ika 4:21; 10:25; 2 Siit 17:5; Sab 72:10; Mat 2:11. Konye odwe luka Belial ice kotemasi atiar, “Ikwaikini ai etuŋanan lo kitajari oni?” Komenata ŋes, mam apotu koinut ainakinet. Konye kililin ŋesi.
