Gifts for the Work
68After they arrived at the Lord’s house in Jerusalem, some of the family heads gave freewill offerings  # 2:68 Nm 15:3; Ezr 1:4 for the house of God in order to have it rebuilt on its original site. 69Based on what they could give, they gave 61,000 gold coins,  # 2:69 Or drachmas, or darics 6,250 pounds  # 2:69 Lit 5,000 minas of silver, and 100 priestly garments to the treasury  # 2:69 Ezr 8:33–34 for the project. 70The priests, Levites, singers, gatekeepers, temple servants, and some of the people settled in their towns, and the rest of Israel settled in their towns.  # 2:70 1Ch 9:2; Neh 11:3
