Luke 17

17
Warnings from Jesus
1He  # 17:1–3 Mt 18:6–7; Mk 9:42; 1Co 11:19 said to his disciples, “Offenses will certainly come,   # 17:1 Lit “It is impossible for offenses not to come but woe   # 17:1 Lk 22:22 to the one through whom they come!   # 17:1 Mt 13:41 2It would be better for him if a millstone were hung around his neck and he were thrown into the sea than for him to cause one of these little ones to stumble.   # 17:2 1Co 8:9 3Be   # 17:3–4 Mt 18:15,21–22 on your guard. If your brother sins,   # 17:3 Other mss add against you rebuke   # 17:3 Lv 19:17 him, and if he repents, forgive   # 17:3 Mt 6:14 him. 4And if he sins against you seven times in a day, and comes back to you seven times, saying, ‘I repent,’ you must forgive him.”
Faith and Duty
5The  # 17:5–6 Mt 17:19–20; Mk 9:28–29 apostles  # 17:5 Lk 6:13 said to the Lord, “Increase our faith.”  # 17:5 Mk 9:24
6 “If you have faith the size of   # 17:6 Lit faith like a mustard seed,”   # 17:6 Mt 13:31; Mk 4:31; Lk 13:19 the Lord said, “you can say to this mulberry tree, ‘Be uprooted and planted in the sea,’ and it will obey you.
7 “Which one of you having a servant tending sheep or plowing will say to him when he comes in from the field, ‘Come at once and sit down to eat’? 8Instead, will he not tell him, ‘Prepare something for me to eat, get ready, and serve me while I eat and drink; later you can eat and drink’?   # 17:8 Lk 12:35–37 9Does he thank that servant because he did what was commanded?   # 17:9 Other mss add I don’t think so 10In the same way, when you have done all that you were commanded, you should say, ‘We are unworthy   # 17:10 Jb 22:2–3; 35:7; Mt 25:30; Rm 11:35 servants; we’ve only done our duty.’ ”
Ten Men Healed
11While traveling to Jerusalem,  # 17:11 Lk 9:51 he passed between  # 17:11 Or through the middle of Samaria and Galilee.  # 17:11 Jn 4:3–4 12As he entered a village, ten men with leprosy  # 17:12 Gk lepros; a term for various skin diseases; see Lv 13–14,# 17:12 Lv 13:45–46 met him. They stood at a distance 13and raised their voices, saying, “Jesus, Master,  # 17:13 Lk 5:5 have mercy on us! ”
14When he saw them, he told them, “Go and show yourselves to the priests.”   # 17:14 Lv 13:2–14:32; Mt 8:4; Lk 5:14 And while they were going, they were cleansed.
15But one of them, seeing that he was healed, returned and, with a loud voice, gave glory to God.  # 17:15 Mt 9:8; Mk 2:12; Lk 2:14; 5:26; Jn 9:24; Ac 4:21; 12:23; Rm 4:20 16He fell facedown  # 17:16 1Sm 20:41; 2Sm 14:22; Mt 17:6; 1Co 14:25 at his feet, thanking him. And he was a Samaritan.  # 17:16 Mt 10:5; Jn 4:9
17Then Jesus said, “Were not ten cleansed? Where are the nine? 18Didn’t any return to give glory to God except this foreigner? ” 19And he told him, “Get up and go on your way. Your faith has saved you.”   # 17:19 Or faith has made you well,# 17:19 Mt 9:22; Mk 10:52; Lk 5:20; 18:42
The Coming of the Kingdom
20When he was  # 17:20–21 Mt 24:23; Mk 13:21 asked by the Pharisees when the kingdom of God would come,  # 17:20 Lk 19:11; Ac 1:6 he answered them, “The kingdom of God is not coming with something observable; 21no one will say,   # 17:21 Lit they will not say ‘See here! ’ or ‘There! ’ For you see, the kingdom of God is in your midst.”   # 17:21 Or within you
22Then he told the disciples, “The days are coming   # 17:22 Dt 32:35; Is 2:12; Jr 7:32; Am 4:2; Lk 5:35; 19:43; 21:6; 23:29 when you will long to see one of the days of the Son of Man, but you won’t see it.   # 17:22 Am 5:18 23They will say to you,   # 17:23–24 Mt 24:23,27; Mk 13:21 ‘See there! ’ or ‘See here! ’ Don’t follow or run after them.   # 17:23 Lk 21:8 24For as the lightning flashes from horizon to horizon and lights up the sky, so the Son of Man will be in his day.   # 17:24 1Co 1:8 25But first it is necessary that he suffer many things and be rejected by this generation.   # 17:25 Mt 16:21; Lk 9:22
26 “Just as it was in the days of Noah,   # 17:26–27 Mt 24:37–38 so it will be in the days of the Son of Man: 27People went on eating, drinking, marrying and being given in marriage   # 17:27 Lk 20:34–35 until the day Noah boarded the ark,   # 17:27 Gn 6:14 and the flood came and destroyed them all. 28It will be the same as it was in the days of Lot:   # 17:28 Gn 19:1–26 People went on eating, drinking, buying, selling, planting, building. 29But on the day Lot left Sodom, fire and sulfur rained from heaven and destroyed   # 17:29 Gn 19:24–25 them all. 30It   # 17:30–35 Mt 24:17,39–41; Mk 13:15 will be like that on the day the Son of Man is revealed.   # 17:30 Mt 16:27; 24:44; 1Co 1:7; 2Th 1:7; 1Pt 1:7; 4:13 31On that day, a man on the housetop, whose belongings are in the house, must not come down to get them. Likewise the man who is in the field must not turn back. 32Remember Lot’s wife!   # 17:32 Gn 19:26 33Whoever tries to make his life secure   # 17:33 Other mss read to save his life,# 17:33 Or tries to retain his life will lose it, and whoever loses his life will preserve it.   # 17:33 Mt 10:39; Jn 12:25 34I tell you, on that night   # 17:34 Lk 1:78–79 two will be in one bed; one will be taken and the other will be left. 35Two women will be grinding grain together; one will be taken and the other left.”   # 17:35 Some mss include v. 36: “Two will be in a field: One will be taken, and the other will be left.”
37“Where, Lord? ”  # 17:37 Lk 17:20 they asked him.
He said to them, “Where the corpse is, there also the vultures   # 17:37 Ezk 29:5; 32:4; 39:17–20 will be gathered.”
