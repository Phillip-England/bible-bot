Joel 1

1The word of the Lord that came  # 1:1 Hs 1:1; Mc 1:1; Zph 1:1 to Joel son of Pethuel:
A Plague of Locusts
2Hear this,  # 1:2 Hs 4:1; 5:1 you elders;
listen, all you inhabitants of the land.
Has anything like this ever happened in your days  # 1:2 Dt 4:32; Jr 2:10; Mk 2:12
or in the days of your ancestors?
3Tell your children about it,  # 1:3 Ex 10:2; Jos 4:22; Ps 78:4
and let your children tell their children,
and their children the next generation.
4What the devouring locust  # 1:4 Ex 10:4; Jl 2:25; Am 4:9 has left,
the swarming locust has eaten;
what the swarming locust has left,
the young locust  # 1:4 Ps 105:34; Nah 3:15–16 has eaten;
and what the young locust has left,
the destroying locust  # 1:4 Ps 78:46; Is 33:4 has eaten.
5Wake up, you drunkards,  # 1:5 Jl 3:3 and weep;
wail, all you wine drinkers,
because of the sweet wine,
for it has been taken from your mouth.
6For a nation has invaded my land,  # 1:6 Jl 2:2,11,25
powerful and without number;
its teeth are the teeth of a lion,
and it has the fangs of a lioness.  # 1:6 Rv 9:7–8
7It has devastated my grapevine
and splintered my fig tree.
It has stripped off its bark and thrown it away;
its branches have turned white.
8Grieve like a young woman dressed in sackcloth,  # 1:8 Is 22:12; Jr 4:8; 6:26
mourning for the husband of her youth.
9Grain and drink offerings have been cut off  # 1:9 Hs 9:4; Jl 2:14
from the house of the  Lord;
the priests, who are ministers of the  Lord, mourn.  # 1:9 Hs 10:5; Jl 2:17
10The fields are destroyed;  # 1:10 Is 24:4
the land grieves;
indeed, the grain is destroyed;
the new wine is dried up;  # 1:10 Hs 9:2
and the fresh oil fails.  # 1:10 Dt 28:51; Hs 2:8–13; Hg 1:11
11Be ashamed, you farmers,  # 1:11 Jr 14:4; 51:23; Am 5:16
wail, you vinedressers,  # 1:11 Or The farmers are dismayed, the vinedressers wail
over the wheat and the barley,
because the harvest of the field has perished.  # 1:11 Is 17:11; Jr 9:12
12The grapevine is dried up,  # 1:12 Hab 3:17
and the fig tree is withered;
the pomegranate,  # 1:12 Hg 2:19 the date palm,  # 1:12 Sg 7:8 and the apple  # 1:12 Sg 2:3  —
all the trees of the orchard #— #have withered.
Indeed, human joy has dried up.  # 1:12 Is 16:10; 24:11
13Dress in sackcloth and lament,  # 1:13 Jr 4:8; Ezk 7:18 you priests;  # 1:13 Jl 2:17
wail,  # 1:13 Jr 9:10 you ministers of the altar.
Come and spend the night in sackcloth,  # 1:13 1Kg 21:27
you ministers of my God,
because grain and drink offerings
are withheld from the house of your God.
14Announce a sacred fast;  # 1:14 Jl 2:15–16
proclaim a solemn assembly!
Gather the elders  # 1:14 Jl 1:2
and all the residents of the land
at the house of the  Lord your God,  # 1:14 2Ch 20:3; Neh 9:1; Jr 36:6,9
and cry out to the  Lord.  # 1:14 Jnh 3:8
The Day of the Lord
15Woe because of that day!  # 1:15 Jr 30:7; Am 5:16
For the day of the  Lord is near
and will come as devastation from the Almighty.  # 1:15 Is 13:6,9; Ezk 30:2–3; Jl 2:1–2,11,31
16Hasn’t the food been cut off
before our eyes,  # 1:16 Is 3:7
joy and gladness  # 1:16 Is 16:10; Jr 48:33
from the house of our God?
17The seeds lie shriveled in their casings.  # 1:17 Or clods; Hb obscure,# 1:17 Is 17:10–11
The storehouses are in ruin,
and the granaries are broken down,
because the grain has withered away.
18How the animals groan!  # 1:18 1Kg 18:5; Jr 12:4; 14:5–6; Hs 4:3
The herds of cattle wander in confusion
since they have no pasture.
Even the flocks of sheep and goats suffer punishment.
19I call to you,  Lord,  # 1:19 Ps 50:15; Mc 7:7
for fire has consumed
the pastures of the wilderness,  # 1:19 Jr 9:10; Am 7:4
and flames have devoured
all the trees of the orchard.
20Even the wild animals cry out to  # 1:20 Or animals pant for; Hb obscure  you,  # 1:20 Ps 104:21; 147:9
for the river beds are dried up,  # 1:20 1Kg 17:7; 18:5
and fire has consumed
the pastures of the wilderness.
