The Anointing at Bethany
1Six days before the Passover, Jesus came to Bethany  # 12:1 Mk 11:1 where Lazarus  # 12:1 Other mss read Lazarus who died was, the one Jesus had raised from the dead.  # 12:1 Jn 11:43–44 2So  # 12:2–8 Mt 26:6–13; Mk 14:3–9 they gave a dinner for him there; Martha  # 12:2 Jn 11:1 was serving them, and Lazarus was one of those reclining at the table with him. 3Then Mary took a pound of perfume, pure and expensive nard, anointed Jesus’s feet, and wiped his feet with her hair.  # 12:3 Mk 14:3; Jn 11:2 So the house was filled with the fragrance of the perfume.
4Then one of his disciples, Judas Iscariot  # 12:4 Mt 26:14; Mk 3:19 (who was about to betray him), said, 5“Why wasn’t this perfume sold for three hundred denarii  # 12:5 A denarius = one day’s wage,# 12:5 Mt 18:28 and given to the poor? ” 6He didn’t say this because he cared about the poor but because he was a thief.  # 12:6 Jn 10:1 He was in charge of the money-bag and would steal part of what was put in it.
7Jesus answered, “Leave her alone; she has kept it for the day of my burial.   # 12:7 Jn 19:40 8For you always have the poor with you,   # 12:8 Dt 15:11 but you do not always have me.”
The Decision to Kill Lazarus
9Then a large crowd of the Jews learned he was there. They came not only because of Jesus but also to see Lazarus, the one he had raised from the dead.  # 12:9 Jn 21:14 10But the chief priests had decided to kill Lazarus also, 11because he was the reason many of the Jews were deserting them  # 12:11 Lit going away and believing in Jesus.

Jesus Predicts His Crucifixion
20Now some Greeks were among those who went up to worship at the festival. 21So they came to Philip,  # 12:21 Mk 3:18 who was from Bethsaida  # 12:21 Mt 11:21; Mk 6:45; 8:22; Lk 9:10; 10:13; Jn 1:44 in Galilee,  # 12:21 Mt 17:22 and requested of him, “Sir,  # 12:21 Col 3:22 we want to see Jesus.” 22Philip went and told Andrew;  # 12:22 Jn 6:8 then Andrew and Philip went and told Jesus.
23Jesus replied to them, “The hour has come for the Son of Man   # 12:23 Mk 2:10 to be glorified. 24Truly I tell you, unless a grain of wheat   # 12:24 Lk 16:7 falls to the ground and dies, it remains by itself. 34Then the crowd replied to him, “We have heard from the law that the Messiah will remain forever.  # 12:34 Ps 89:36; Jn 15:4; 1Jn 2:17 So how can you say, ‘The Son of Man   # 12:34 Mk 2:10 must be lifted up’? Who is this Son of Man? ”
35Jesus answered, “The light will be with you only a little longer.   # 12:35 Ps 118:27; Jn 7:33; 9:4 Walk while you have the light   # 12:35 Ps 36:9; Jn 12:46; 1Jn 2:8 so that darkness doesn’t overtake you.   # 12:35 Php 3:12 The one who walks in darkness doesn’t know where he’s going.   # 12:35 Jn 7:28; 8:14; 1Jn 2:11 36While you have the light,   # 12:36 Jn 7:33; 1Th 5:5 believe in the light so that you may become children of light.” Jesus said this, then went away and hid from them.  # 12:36 Lk 4:30

A Summary of Jesus’s Mission
