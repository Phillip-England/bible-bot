The Law of Love
13Therefore, let us no longer judge one another.  # 14:13 Mt 7:1; Rm 14:3 Instead decide never to put a stumbling block or pitfall in the way of your brother or sister.  # 14:13 1Co 8:13 14I know and am persuaded in the Lord Jesus that nothing is unclean in itself.  # 14:14 Ac 10:15; Rm 14:2,20 Still, to someone who considers a thing to be unclean, to that one it is unclean.  # 14:14 1Co 8:7 15For if your brother or sister is hurt by what you eat, you are no longer walking according to love.  # 14:15 Eph 5:2 Do not destroy, by what you eat, someone for whom Christ died.  # 14:15 1Co 8:11 16Therefore, do not let your good be slandered,  # 14:16 1Co 10:30; Ti 2:5 17for the kingdom of God is not eating and drinking,  # 14:17 1Co 8:8 but righteousness, peace, and joy  # 14:17 Rm 15:13; Gl 5:22 in the Holy Spirit. 18Whoever serves Christ  # 14:18 Rm 16:18 in this way is acceptable to God and receives human approval.  # 14:18 2Co 8:21; Php 4:8; 1Pt 2:12
19So then, let us pursue what promotes peace  # 14:19 Ps 34:14; Rm 12:18; 1Co 7:15; 2Tm 2:22; Heb 12:14 and what builds up one another.  # 14:19 Rm 15:2; 1Co 10:23; 14:3,26; 2Co 12:19; Eph 4:12,29 20Do not tear down God’s work because of food.
