Isaiah 11

11
Reign of the Davidic King
1Then a shoot will grow from the stump of Jesse,  # 11:1 Is 9:7; 11:10; Ac 13:23
and a branch  # 11:1 Is 14:19; 60:21; Jr 23:5; 33:15; Dn 11:7; Zch 3:8; 6:12 from his roots will bear fruit.
2The Spirit of the Lord will rest on him  # 11:2 Is 42:1; 48:16; 61:1; Mt 3:16; Jn 1:32  —
a Spirit of wisdom and understanding,
a Spirit of counsel and strength,
a Spirit of knowledge and of the fear of the  Lord.
3His delight will be in the fear of the  Lord.
He will not judge
by what he sees with his eyes,  # 11:3 Jn 2:25; 7:24
he will not execute justice
by what he hears with his ears,
4but he will judge the poor righteously  # 11:4 Ps 72:2,13–14; Is 3:14
and execute justice for the oppressed of the land.
He will strike the land
with a scepter  # 11:4 Lit the rod from his mouth,  # 11:4 Ps 2:9; Is 49:2; Mal 4:6; Rv 1:16; 19:15
and he will kill the wicked  # 11:4 Gn 18:25; Ps 139:19; Ezk 3:18–19; 18:20–24; 33:8–15
with a command  # 11:4 Lit with the breath from his lips.  # 11:4 Jb 4:9; Is 30:28,33; 2Th 2:8
5Righteousness will be a belt around his hips;  # 11:5 Eph 6:14
faithfulness will be a belt around his waist.
6The wolf will dwell with the lamb,  # 11:6 Is 65:25; Jr 5:6; Hs 2:18; 13:6–9
and the leopard will lie down with the goat.
The calf, the young lion, and the fattened calf will be together,
and a child will lead them.
7The cow and the bear will graze,
their young ones will lie down together,
and the lion will eat straw like cattle.
8An infant will play beside the cobra’s pit,
and a toddler will put his hand into a snake’s den.
9They will not harm or destroy each other
on my entire holy mountain,
for the land will be as full
of the knowledge of the  Lord
as the sea is filled with water.  # 11:9 Ps 98:2–3; Is 45:6; 52:10; 66:18–23; Hab 2:14
Israel Regathered
10On that day the root of Jesse  # 11:10 Is 11:1
will stand as a banner for the peoples.  # 11:10 Is 49:22; 62:10; Jn 3:14–15; 12:32
The nations will look to him for guidance,  # 11:10 Rm 15:12; Rv 5:5
and his resting place will be glorious.  # 11:10 Is 14:3; 28:12; 32:17–18
11On that day  # 11:11 Is 27:12 the Lord will extend his hand a second time to recover the remnant of his people who survive #— #from Assyria, Egypt, Pathros, Cush, Elam, Shinar, Hamath, and the coasts and islands of the west.  # 11:11 Is 10:20–22; 37:4,31–32; 46:3
12He will lift up a banner for the nations
and gather the dispersed of Israel;  # 11:12 Is 56:8; Zph 3:10; Zch 10:6
he will collect the scattered of Judah
from the four corners of the earth.  # 11:12 Rv 7:1; 20:8
13Ephraim’s envy will cease;  # 11:13 Is 9:21; Jr 3:18; Ezk 37:16–17,22; Hs 1:11
Judah’s harassing will end.
Ephraim will no longer be envious of Judah,
and Judah will not harass Ephraim.
14But they will swoop down
on the Philistine flank to the west.
Together they will plunder the people of the east.  # 11:14 Jr 49:28
They will extend their power over Edom and Moab,
and the Ammonites will be their subjects.  # 11:14 Ps 60:8; 83:6
15The Lord will divide  # 11:15 Text emended; MT reads destroy,# 11:15 Or dry up the Gulf of Suez.  # 11:15 Lit the Sea of Egypt
He will wave his hand over the Euphrates
with his mighty wind  # 11:15 Is 7:20; 8:7; Rv 16:12
and will split it into seven streams,
letting people walk through on foot.
16There will be a highway for the remnant of his people
who will survive from Assyria,  # 11:16 Is 19:23; 35:8; 40:3; 62:10
as there was for Israel
when they came up from the land of Egypt.  # 11:16 Ex 14:26–29
