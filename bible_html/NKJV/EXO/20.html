12#Lev. 19:3; Deut. 27:16; Matt. 15:4; 19:19; Mark 7:10; 10:19; Luke 18:20; Eph. 6:2“Honor your father and your mother, that your days may be #Deut. 5:16, 33; 6:2; 11:8, 9long upon the land which the Lord your God is giving you.
13#(Matt. 5:21, 22); 19:18; Mark 10:19; Luke 18:20; Rom. 13:9; (1 John 3:15)“You shall not murder.

15#Ex. 21:16; Lev. 19:11, 13; Matt. 19:18; Rom. 13:9“You shall not steal.

The People Afraid of God’s Presence
18Now #Heb. 12:18, 19all the people #Rev. 1:10, 12witnessed the thunderings, the lightning flashes, the sound of the trumpet, and the mountain #Ex. 19:16, 18smoking; and when the people saw it, they trembled and stood afar off. 19Then they said to Moses, #Gal. 3:19; Heb. 12:19“You speak with us, and we will hear; but #Deut. 5:5, 23–27let not God speak with us, lest we die.”
20And Moses said to the people, #Ex. 14:13; (Is. 41:10, 13)“Do not fear; #Ex. 15:25; (Deut. 13:3)for God has come to test you, and #Deut. 4:10; 6:24; Prov. 3:7; 16:6; Is. 8:13that His fear may be before you, so that you may not sin.” 21So the people stood afar off, but Moses drew near #Ex. 19:16; Deut. 5:22the thick darkness where God was.
The Law of the Altar
22Then the Lord said to Moses, “Thus you shall say to the children of Israel: ‘You have seen that I have talked with you #Deut. 4:36; 5:24, 26; Neh. 9:13from heaven. 23You shall not make anything to be #Ex. 32:1, 2, 4; Deut. 29:17with Me—gods of silver or gods of gold you shall not make for yourselves. 24An altar of #Ex. 20:25; 27:1–8earth you shall make for Me, and you shall sacrifice on it your burnt offerings and your peace offerings, #Ex. 24:5; Lev. 1:2your sheep and your oxen. In every #Deut. 12:5; 16:6, 11; 1 Kin. 9:3; 2 Chr. 6:6place where I record My name I will come to you, and I will #Gen. 12:2bless you. 25And #Deut. 27:5if you make Me an altar of stone, you shall not build it of hewn stone; for if you #Josh. 8:30, 31use your tool on it, you have profaned it. 26Nor shall you go up by steps to My altar, that your #Ex. 28:42, 43nakedness may not be exposed on it.’
