Egypt and Her Allies Will Fall
1The word of the Lord came to me again, saying, 2“Son of man, prophesy and say, ‘Thus says the Lord God:
#Is. 13:6; 15:2; Ezek. 21:12; Joel 1:5, 11, 13“Wail, ‘Woe to the day!’
3For #Ezek. 7:7, 12; Joel 2:1; Obad. 15; Zeph. 1:7the day is near,
Even the day of the Lord is near;
It will be a day of clouds, the time of the Gentiles.
4The sword shall come upon Egypt,
And great anguish shall be in Ethiopia,
When the slain fall in Egypt,
And they #Ezek. 29:19take away her wealth,
And #Jer. 50:15her foundations are broken down.
5“Ethiopia, Libya, Lydia, #Jer. 25:20, 24all the mingled people, Chub, and the men of the lands who are allied, shall fall with them by the sword.”
6‘Thus says the Lord:
“Those who uphold Egypt shall fall,
And the pride of her power shall come down.
#Ezek. 29:10From Migdol to Syene
Those within her shall fall by the sword,”
Says the Lord God.
7“They#Jer. 25:18–26; Ezek. 29:12 shall be desolate in the midst of the desolate countries,
And her cities shall be in the midst of the cities that are laid waste.
8Then they will know that I am the Lord,
When I have set a fire in Egypt
And all her helpers are destroyed.
9On that day #Is. 18:1, 2messengers shall go forth from Me in ships
To make the careless Ethiopians afraid,
And great anguish shall come upon them,
As on the day of Egypt;
For indeed it is coming!”
10‘Thus says the Lord God:
#Ezek. 29:19“I will also make a multitude of Egypt to cease
By the hand of Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon.
11He and his people with him, #Ezek. 28:7; 31:12the most terrible of the nations,
Shall be brought to destroy the land;
They shall draw their swords against Egypt,
And fill the land with the slain.
12#Is. 19:5, 6I will make the rivers dry,
And #Is. 19:4sell the land into the hand of the wicked;
I will make the land waste, and all that is in it,
By the hand of aliens.
I, the Lord, have spoken.”
13‘Thus says the Lord God:
“I will also #Is. 19:1; Jer. 43:12; 46:25; Zech. 13:2destroy the idols,
And cause the images to cease from Noph;
#Zech. 10:11There shall no longer be princes from the land of Egypt;
#Is. 19:16I will put fear in the land of Egypt.
14I will make #Is. 11:11; Jer. 44:1, 15; Ezek. 29:14Pathros desolate,
Set fire to #Ps. 78:12, 43; Is. 19:11, 13Zoan,
#Jer. 46:25; Ezek. 30:15, 16; Nah. 3:8–10And execute judgments in No.
15I will pour My fury on Sin, the strength of Egypt;
#Jer. 46:25I will cut off the multitude of No,
16And #Ezek. 30:8set a fire in Egypt;
Sin shall have great pain,
No shall be split open,
And Noph shall be in distress daily.
17The young men of Aven and Pi Beseth shall fall by the sword,
And these cities shall go into captivity.
18#Jer. 2:16At Tehaphnehes the day shall also be darkened,
When I break the yokes of Egypt there.
And her arrogant strength shall cease in her;
As for her, a cloud shall cover her,
And her daughters shall go into captivity.
19Thus I will #(Ps. 9:16); Ezek. 5:8; 25:11execute judgments on Egypt,
Then they shall know that I am the Lord.” ’ ”
Proclamation Against Pharaoh
20And it came to pass in the eleventh year, in the first month, on the seventh day of the month, that the word of the Lord came to me, saying, 21“Son of man, I have #Jer. 48:25broken the arm of Pharaoh king of Egypt; and see, #Jer. 46:11it has not been bandaged for healing, nor a splint put on to bind it, to make it strong enough to hold a sword. 22Therefore thus says the Lord God: ‘Surely I am #Jer. 46:25; Ezek. 29:3against Pharaoh king of Egypt, and will #Ps. 37:17break his arms, both the strong one and the one that was broken; and I will make the sword fall out of his hand. 23#Ezek. 29:12; 30:17, 18, 26I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations, and disperse them throughout the countries. 24I will strengthen the arms of the king of Babylon and put My sword in his hand; but I will break Pharaoh’s arms, and he will groan before him with the groanings of a mortally wounded man. 25Thus I will strengthen the arms of the king of Babylon, but the arms of Pharaoh shall fall down; #Ps. 9:16they shall know that I am the Lord, when I put My sword into the hand of the king of Babylon and he stretches it out against the land of Egypt. 26#Ezek. 29:12I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations and disperse them throughout the countries. Then they shall know that I am the Lord.’ ”
