48
Division of the Land
1“Now these are the names of the tribes: #Ezek. 47:15From the northern border along the road to Hethlon at the entrance of Hamath, to Hazar Enan, the border of Damascus northward, in the direction of Hamath, there shall be one section for #Josh. 19:40–48Dan from its east to its west side; 2by the border of Dan, from the east side to the west, one section for #Josh. 19:24–31Asher; 3by the border of Asher, from the east side to the west, one section for #Josh. 19:32–39Naphtali; 4by the border of Naphtali, from the east side to the west, one section for #Josh. 13:29–31; 17:1–11, 17, 18Manasseh; 5by the border of Manasseh, from the east side to the west, one section for #Josh. 16:5–10; 17:8–10, 14–18Ephraim; 6by the border of Ephraim, from the east side to the west, one section for #Josh. 13:15–23Reuben; 7by the border of Reuben, from the east side to the west, one section for #Josh. 15:1–63; 19:9Judah; 8by the border of Judah, from the east side to the west, shall be #Ezek. 45:1–6the district which you shall set apart, twenty-five thousand cubits in width, and in length the same as one of the other portions, from the east side to the west, with the #(Is. 12:6; 33:20–22); Ezek. 45:3, 4sanctuary in the center.
9“The district that you shall set apart for the Lord shall be twenty-five thousand cubits in length and ten thousand in width. 10To these—to the priests—the holy district shall belong: on the north twenty-five thousand cubits in length, on the west ten thousand in width, on the east ten thousand in width, and on the south twenty-five thousand in length. The sanctuary of the Lord shall be in the center. 11#Ezek. 40:46; 44:15It shall be for the priests of the sons of Zadok, who are sanctified, who have kept My charge, who did not go astray when the children of Israel went astray, #Ezek. 44:10, 12as the Levites went astray. 12And this district of land that is set apart shall be to them a thing most #Ezek. 45:4holy by the border of the Levites.
13“Opposite the border of the priests, the #Ezek. 45:5Levites shall have an area twenty-five thousand cubits in length and ten thousand in width; its entire length shall be twenty-five thousand and its width ten thousand. 14#Ex. 22:29; Lev. 27:10, 28, 33; Ezek. 44:30And they shall not sell or exchange any of it; they may not alienate this best part of the land, for it is holy to the Lord.
15#Ezek. 45:6“The five thousand cubits in width that remain, along the edge of the twenty-five thousand, shall be #Ezek. 42:20for general use by the city, for dwellings and common-land; and the city shall be in the center. 16These shall be its measurements: the north side four thousand five hundred cubits, the south side four thousand five hundred, the east side four thousand five hundred, and the west side four thousand five hundred. 17The common-land of the city shall be: to the north two hundred and fifty cubits, to the south two hundred and fifty, to the east two hundred and fifty, and to the west two hundred and fifty. 18The rest of the length, alongside the district of the holy section, shall be ten thousand cubits to the east and ten thousand to the west. It shall be adjacent to the district of the holy section, and its produce shall be food for the workers of the city. 19#Ezek. 45:6The workers of the city, from all the tribes of Israel, shall cultivate it. 20The entire district shall be twenty-five thousand cubits by twenty-five thousand cubits, foursquare. You shall set apart the holy district with the property of the city.
21#Ezek. 34:24; 45:7; 48:22“The rest shall belong to the prince, on one side and on the other of the holy district and of the city’s property, next to the twenty-five thousand cubits of the holy district as far as the eastern border, and westward next to the twenty-five thousand as far as the western border, adjacent to the tribal portions; it shall belong to the prince. It shall be the holy district, #Ezek. 48:8, 10and the sanctuary of the temple shall be in the center. 22Moreover, apart from the possession of the Levites and the possession of the city which are in the midst of what belongs to the prince, the area between the border of Judah and the border of #Josh. 18:21–28Benjamin shall belong to the prince.
23“As for the rest of the tribes, from the east side to the west, Benjamin shall have one section; 24by the border of Benjamin, from the east side to the west, #Josh. 19:1–9Simeon shall have one section; 25by the border of Simeon, from the east side to the west, #Josh. 19:17–23Issachar shall have one section; 26by the border of Issachar, from the east side to the west, #Josh. 19:10–16Zebulun shall have one section; 27by the border of Zebulun, from the east side to the west, #Josh. 13:24–28Gad shall have one section; 28by the border of Gad, on the south side, toward the South, the border shall be from Tamar to #Gen. 14:7; 2 Chr. 20:2; Ezek. 47:19the waters of Meribah by Kadesh, along the brook to the #Ezek. 47:10, 15, 19, 20Great Sea. 29#Ezek. 47:14, 21, 22This is the land which you shall divide by lot as an inheritance among the tribes of Israel, and these are their portions,” says the Lord God.
The Gates of the City and Its Name
30“These are the exits of the city. On the north side, measuring four thousand five hundred cubits 31#(Rev. 21:10–14)(the gates of the city shall be named after the tribes of Israel), the three gates northward: one gate for Reuben, one gate for Judah, and one gate for Levi; 32on the east side, four thousand five hundred cubits, three gates: one gate for Joseph, one gate for Benjamin, and one gate for Dan; 33on the south side, measuring four thousand five hundred cubits, three gates: one gate for Simeon, one gate for Issachar, and one gate for Zebulun; 34on the west side, four thousand five hundred cubits with their three gates: one gate for Gad, one gate for Asher, and one gate for Naphtali. 35All the way around shall be eighteen thousand cubits; #Jer. 23:6; 33:16and the name of the city from that day shall be: #Is. 12:6; 14:32; 24:23; Jer. 3:17; 8:19; 14:9; Ezek. 35:10; Joel 3:21; Zech. 2:10; Rev. 21:3; 22:3THE LORD IS THERE.”
