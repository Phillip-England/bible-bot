1“Thus says the Lord to His anointed,
To #Is. 44:28Cyrus, whose #Ps. 73:23; Is. 41:13right hand I have held—

8“Rain#Ps. 85:11 down, you heavens, from above,

The Holy One of Israel, and his Maker:
#Is. 8:19“Ask Me of things to come concerning #Jer. 31:9My sons;
And concerning #Is. 29:23; 60:21; 64:8the work of My hands, you command Me.

And #Gen. 1:26created man on it.

#(Rom. 3:24)Not for price nor reward,”

The Lord, the Only Savior

#Is. 45:5There is no other God.’ ”
