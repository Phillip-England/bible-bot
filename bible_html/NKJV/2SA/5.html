David Reigns over All Israel

The Conquest of Jerusalem

9Then David dwelt in the stronghold, and called it #1 Chr. 11:6–9the City of David. And David built all around from the Millo and inward. 10So David went on and became great, and #2 Sam. 5:7; 1 Kin. 9:15, 24the Lord God of hosts was with #1 Sam. 17:45him.
11Then #1 Sam. 18:12, 28Hiram #1 Kin. 5:1–18king of Tyre sent messengers to David, and cedar trees, and carpenters and masons. And they built David a house. 12So David knew that the Lord had established him as king over Israel, and that He had #1 Chr. 14:1exalted His kingdom #Num. 24:7for the sake of His people Israel.
13And #Is. 45:4David took more concubines and wives from Jerusalem, after he had come from Hebron. Also more sons and daughters were born to David. 14Now #(Deut. 17:17); 1 Chr. 3:9these are the names of those who were born to him in Jerusalem: Shammua, Shobab, Nathan, #1 Chr. 3:5–8Solomon, 15Ibhar, Elishua, Nepheg, Japhia, 16Elishama, Eliada, and Eliphelet.
The Philistines Defeated
17#2 Sam. 12:24Now when the Philistines heard that they had anointed David king over Israel, all the Philistines went up to search for David. And David heard of it #1 Chr. 11:16and went down to the stronghold. 18The Philistines also went and deployed themselves in #2 Sam. 23:14the Valley of Rephaim. 19So David #Gen. 14:5; Josh. 15:8; 1 Chr. 11:15; Is. 17:5inquired of the Lord, saying, “Shall I go up against the Philistines? Will You deliver them into my hand?”
And the Lord said to David, “Go up, for I will doubtless deliver the Philistines into your hand.”
20So David went to #1 Sam. 23:2; 2 Sam. 2:1Baal Perazim, and David defeated them there; and he said, “The Lord has broken through my enemies before me, like a breakthrough of water.” Therefore he called the name of that place Baal Perazim. 21And they left their images there, and David and his men #1 Chr. 14:11; Is. 28:21carried them away.

For then #2 Kin. 7:6; 1 Chr. 14:15the Lord will go out before you to strike the camp of the Philistines.” 25And David did so, as the Lord commanded him; and he drove back the Philistines from #Judg. 4:14Geba as far as #1 Chr. 14:16Gezer.
