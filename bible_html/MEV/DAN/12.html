Daniel 12

12
The Resurrection of the Dead
1“And at that time Michael shall stand up, # Da 10:13 the great prince who stands guard over the sons of your people. And there shall be a time of trouble such as never was since there was a nation even to that time. # Mt 24:21 And at that time your people shall be delivered, # Jer 30:7 everyone who shall be found written in the book. 2Many of those who sleep in the dust of the ground will awake—some to everlasting life, and others to everlasting shame and contempt. # Isa 26:19; Mt 25:46 3Those who are wise # Da 11:33, 35 shall shine as the brightness of the expanse of heaven, # Mt 13:43 and those who turn the many to righteousness as the stars forever and ever. 4But you, Daniel, shut up the words and seal the book until the time of the end. # Da 8:17; 12:9; Rev 22:10 Many shall run to and fro, and knowledge shall increase.”
5Then I, Daniel, looked, and behold, there before me stood two others, the one on this side of the bank of the river, and the other on that side of the bank of the river. 6One said to the man clothed in linen, # Eze 9:2 who was above the water of the river, “How long shall it be to the end of these wonders?” # Da 8:13
7And I heard the man clothed in linen, who was over the water of the river, when he held up his right hand and his left hand to heaven, and swore by Him who lives forever that it would be for a time, times, and half a time. # Da 7:25; Rev 12:14 # 12:7 Or a year, two years, and half a year. And when they finish shattering the power of the holy people, # Da 8:24 all these things shall be finished.
8As for me, I heard, but I could not understand. So I said, “My lord, what shall be the result of these things?”
9And he said, “Go your way, Daniel, for these words are closed up and sealed until the time of the end. # Da 12:4 10Many shall be purified and made white and tried. But the wicked shall do wickedly, # Da 11:35; Rev 22:11 and none of the wicked shall understand, but the wise shall understand. # Hos 14:9
11“From the time that the daily sacrifice shall be taken away and the abomination that makes desolate set up, # Da 9:27; 11:31; Mt 24:15 there shall be one thousand two hundred and ninety days. 12Blessed is he who waits and comes to the one thousand three hundred and thirty-five days.
13“But as for you, go your way until the end. Then you shall rest and rise again for your lot at the end of the age.” # Rev 14:13
