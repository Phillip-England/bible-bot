Ezekiel 7

7
The Day of the Lord’s Wrath
1Moreover the word of the Lord came to me, saying: 2And you, son of man, thus says the Lord God to the land of Israel:
An end, the end is coming # Am 8:2, 10
upon the four corners of the land.
3Now the end has come upon you,
and I will send My anger upon you,
and will judge you according to your ways, # Eze 18:30
and will recompense upon you all your abominations.
4My eye shall not spare you,
nor will I have pity; # Eze 5:11
but I will recompense your ways upon you,
and your abominations shall be in your midst.
And you shall know that I am the Lord! # Eze 6:7
5Thus says the Lord God:
A disaster! A unique disaster!
Behold, it is coming! # 2Ki 21:12–13; Eze 5:9
6An end is coming,
the end has come;
it watches for you;
behold, it has come!
7Your doom has come to you,
O inhabitant in the land;
the time has come, the day of trouble is near, # Isa 22:5; Eze 7:12
and not the joyful shouting on the mountains.
8Now I will shortly pour out My fury upon you, # Eze 9:8; 14:19
and accomplish My anger upon you;
and I will judge you according to your ways,
and will recompense you for all your abominations.
9My eye shall not spare,
nor will I have pity;
I will recompense you according to your ways,
and your abominations that are in your midst.
And you shall know that I am the Lord who strikes.
10Behold, the day is coming!
Behold, your doom has gone out;
the rod has blossomed, # Isa 10:5
pride has budded.
11Violence has risen up
into a rod of wickedness;
none of them shall remain,
none of their people,
none of their wealth,
nor anything eminent among them. # Isa 59:6–8; Zep 1:18
12The time has come,
the day has drawn near.
Do not let the buyer rejoice,
or the seller mourn,
for wrath is against all their multitude. # Isa 5:13–14; Eze 6:11–12
13For the seller shall not return
to that which is sold,
although they both live;
for the vision concerning the whole multitude
shall not return;
nor shall any strengthen his life
by his iniquity. # Lev 25:24–28
14They have blown the trumpet # Jer 4:5
even to make all ready,
but none goes to the battle;
for My wrath is against all the multitude.
15The sword is outside,
and the pestilence and the famine within.
He who is in the field
shall die by the sword;
and he who is in the city, # Jer 14:18; La 1:20
famine and pestilence shall devour him.
16Even when their survivors escape,
they shall be on the mountains
like doves of the valleys, # Isa 38:14
all of them mourning,
every one over his iniquity. # Ezr 9:15
17All hands shall be feeble,
and all knees shall be weak as water. # Jer 6:24; Eze 21:7
18They shall also gird themselves with sackcloth,
and horror shall cover them;
and shame shall be upon all faces,
and baldness upon all their heads. # Isa 15:2–3; Am 8:10
19They shall cast their silver in the streets,
and their gold shall become abhorrent;
their silver and their gold
shall not be able to deliver them
in the day of the wrath of the Lord; # Pr 11:4; Zep 1:18
they shall not satisfy their souls,
or fill their stomachs,
for their iniquity has become a stumbling block.
20They transformed the beauty of His ornaments into pride,
and made the images of their abominations # Jer 7:30
and of their detestable things with it;
therefore I will make it an abhorrent thing to them. # Eze 24:21
21I will give it into the hands of the strangers
as plunder and to the wicked of the earth for destruction, # 2Ki 24:13
and they shall pollute it.
22My face also I will turn from them, # Jer 18:17
and they shall pollute My secret place;
for the robbers shall enter
and defile it.
23Make a chain, # Jer 27:2
for the land is full of bloody crimes,
and the city is full of violence. # Eze 9:9
24Therefore I will bring the worst of the nations,
and they shall possess their houses;
I will also make the pomp of the strong to cease,
and their holy places shall be defiled. # Eze 28:7
25When destruction comes,
then they shall seek peace, but there shall be none.
26Mischief shall come upon mischief,
and rumor shall be upon rumor. # Jer 4:20
Then they shall seek a vision of the prophet;
but the law shall perish from the priest,
and counsel from the elders.
27The king shall mourn,
and the prince shall be clothed with desolation,
and the hands of the people of the land shall tremble.
I will do to them according to their way,
and according to what they deserve I will judge them.
And they shall know that I am the Lord!
