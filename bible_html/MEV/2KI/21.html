2 Kings 21

21
Manasseh, King of Judah
2Ch 33:1–20
1Manasseh was twelve years old when he became king, and he reigned fifty-five years in Jerusalem. His mother’s name was Hephzibah. # Isa 62:4 2He did evil in the sight of the Lord, according to the abominations of the nations whom the Lord dispossessed before the Israelites. 3He went back and rebuilt the high places that Hezekiah his father had destroyed. He erected altars for Baal, made an Asherah pole as Ahab king of Israel had done, and worshipped all the host of heaven and served them. # 2Ki 17:16; 18:4 4He built altars in the house of the Lord, of which the Lord had said, “In Jerusalem I will put My name.” # 2Sa 7:13; 1Ki 8:29 5He built altars for all the host of heaven in the two courts # 1Ki 6:36; 7:12 of the house of the Lord. 6He made his son pass through the fire, # Lev 18:21 was conjuring and seeking omens, and dealt with mediums and soothsayers. # Lev 19:31 He did much evil in the sight of the Lord, provoking Him to anger.
7He put a carved image of Asherah that he had made in the house of which the Lord said to David and to Solomon his son, “In this house and in Jerusalem, which I have chosen out of all tribes of Israel, I will put My name forever. # 1Ki 8:29; 9:3 8I will not make the feet of Israel to wander homeless from the land # 2Sa 7:10 that I gave to their fathers, if only they will be careful to do according to all that I have commanded them and according to all the law that My servant Moses commanded them.” 9But they did not listen. # Pr 29:12 Manasseh led them to do more evil than the nations whom the Lord had destroyed before the Israelites.
10The Lord spoke by His servants the prophets, saying, 11“Because Manasseh king of Judah has done these abominations, # 2Ki 24:3–4 things more evil than all that the Amorites did, # 1Ki 21:26 and has also caused Judah to sin with his idols, 12therefore thus says the Lord God of Israel: Behold, I am bringing evil on Jerusalem and Judah, such evil that the ears of whoever hears about it will tingle. # 1Sa 3:11; Jer 19:3 13I will stretch over Jerusalem the measuring line of Samaria # Isa 34:11; Am 7:7–8 and the level of the house of Ahab, and I will wipe out Jerusalem as one wipes out a bowl, wiping it and turning it upside down. 14I will disregard the remnant # 2Ki 19:4; Jer 23:33 of My inheritance and give them into the hand of their enemies. They shall become plunder and spoil for all their enemies, 15because they have done evil in My sight and have provoked Me to anger, since the day their fathers came out of Egypt, even to this day.”
16Moreover, Manasseh shed innocent blood very much, until he had filled Jerusalem from one end to the other; besides his sin he caused Judah to sin by doing evil in the sight of the Lord.
17Now the rest of the deeds of Manasseh, all that he did, and his sin that he committed, are they not written in the book of the annals of the kings of Judah? 18Manasseh slept with his fathers, and was buried in the garden of his house, in the garden of Uzza. Amon his son reigned in his place. # 2Ki 21:26; 2Ch 33:20
Amon, King of Judah
2Ch 33:21–25
19Amon was twenty-two years old when he became king. # 2Ch 33:21–23 He reigned two years in Jerusalem. His mother’s name was Meshullemeth, daughter of Haruz of Jotbah. 20He did evil in the sight of the Lord, as his father Manasseh had done. 21He walked in all the ways that his father walked, served the idols that his father served, and worshipped them. 22He abandoned the Lord God of his fathers, and he did not walk in the way of the Lord. # 1Ki 11:33; 2Ki 22:17
23The servants of Amon conspired against him and killed the king in his house. # 2Ki 12:20; 14:19 24But the people of the land struck # 2Ki 14:5 all those who conspired against King Amon, and the people of the land made Josiah his son king in his place.
25Now the rest of the deeds of Amon that he did, are they not written in the book of the annals of the kings of Judah? 26And he was buried in his tomb in the garden of Uzza, and Josiah his son reigned in his place. # 2Ki 21:18
