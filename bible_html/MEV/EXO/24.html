Exodus 24

24
The Covenant Confirmed
1Then He said to Moses, “Come up to the Lord, you, and Aaron, Nadab, and Abihu, # Ex 6:23 and seventy of the elders of Israel, # Nu 11:16 and you shall worship from a distance. 2Moses alone shall come near the Lord, # Ex 24:13, 15 but they shall not come near, nor may the people go up with him.”
3Moses came and told the people all the words of the Lord and all the ordinances. Then all the people answered with one voice and said, “All the words which the Lord has said we will do.” # Ex 19:8; 24:7 4Moses wrote all the words of the Lord, # Dt 31:9 and rose up early in the morning, # Ge 28:18 and built an altar at the foot of the mountain with twelve pillars for the twelve tribes of Israel. 5He sent young men of Israel who offered burnt offerings and sacrificed peace offerings of young bulls to the Lord. # Ex 18:12 6Moses took half of the blood and put it in basins, and half of the blood he sprinkled on the altar. # Heb 9:18 7He took the Book of the Covenant and read in the hearing of the people, and they said, “All that the Lord has said we will do, and we will be obedient.”
8So Moses took the blood, and sprinkled it on the people, and said, “Behold, the blood of the covenant # Mt 26:28; Heb 13:20 that the Lord has made with you in accordance with all these words.”
Moses on Mount Sinai
9Then Moses went up with Aaron, Nadab, and Abihu, and seventy of the elders of Israel, # Ex 24:1 10and they saw the God of Israel, # Jn 1:18 and under His feet there was something like a paved work of sapphire stone as clear as the sky itself. # Eze 10:1 11He did not lay His hand upon the nobles of the Israelites. Also they saw God, and they ate and they drank. # Ex 18:12
12The Lord said to Moses, “Come up to Me to the mountain and stay there, and I will give you the stone tablets with law and the commandments which I have written, # Ex 31:18; 32:15–16 so that you may teach them.”
13Moses rose up with Joshua his attendant, and Moses went up to the mountain of God. # Ex 3:1 14He said to the elders, “Wait for us in this place until we return to you. And behold, Aaron and Hur are with you. Whoever has any matters of dispute let him come to them.”
15Moses went up to the mountain, and the cloud covered the mountain. # Ex 19:9, 16 16The glory of the Lord rested on Mount Sinai, # Ex 16:10; Nu 14:10 and the cloud covered it for six days. And on the seventh day He called to Moses from the midst of the cloud. 17Now the appearance of the glory of the Lord was like a consuming fire # Ex 3:2; Heb 12:29 on the top of the mountain to the eyes of the Israelites. 18Moses went into the midst of the cloud and went up to the mountain. And Moses was on the mountain for forty days and forty nights. # Ge 7:4; Ex 34:28; Dt 9:9; Mt 4:2
