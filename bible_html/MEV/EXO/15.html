Exodus 15

15
A Song of Moses
1Then Moses and the Israelites sang this song to the Lord # Ps 106:12; Rev 15:3 and spoke, saying:
“I will sing to the Lord,
for He has triumphed gloriously!
He has thrown the horse and his rider
into the sea!
2The Lord is my strength and song,
and He has become my salvation. # Isa 12:2
He is my God, and I will praise Him;
my father’s God, and I will exalt Him. # Ex 3:15–16
3The Lord is a man of war;
the Lord is His name. # Ps 24:8; 83:18
4Pharaoh’s chariots and his army
He has thrown into the sea;
his chosen captains also
are drowned in the Red Sea.
5The depths have covered them;
they sank to the bottom like a stone. # Ex 14:28; Ne 9:11
6“Your right hand, O  Lord,
is glorious in power.
Your right hand, # Ps 118:15–16 O  Lord,
shatters the enemy. # Ps 2:9
7In the greatness of Your excellence,
You overthrow those who rise up against You.
You send out Your wrath;
it consumes them like stubble. # Isa 5:24
8With the blast of Your nostrils
the waters were gathered together. # Ex 14:21–22
The flowing waters stood upright as a heap; # Ps 78:13
and the depths were congealed in the heart of the sea.
9“The enemy said,
‘I will pursue. I will overtake.
I will divide the spoil; # Jdg 5:30; Isa 53:12
my lust shall be satisfied upon them.
I will draw my sword,
my hand shall destroy them.’
10You blew with Your wind, # Ex 14:21
and the sea covered them;
they sank like lead
in the mighty waters. # Ex 15:5
11“Who is like You, O  Lord, among the gods? # Dt 3:24
Who is like You,
glorious in holiness,
fearful in praises,
doing wonders? # Isa 6:3
12You stretched out Your right hand,
and the earth swallowed them.
13“In Your mercy You have led
the people whom You have redeemed; # Ps 77:20
You have guided them by Your strength
to Your holy dwelling.
14The peoples have heard and are afraid; # Nu 14:14; Dt 2:25
sorrow has taken hold on the inhabitants of Philistia.
15Then the chiefs of Edom were amazed;
the mighty men of Moab, trembling takes hold of them;
all the inhabitants of Canaan are melted away. # Jos 2:9; 5:1
16Fear and dread fall upon them; # Dt 2:25
by the greatness of Your arm
they are as still as a stone,
until Your people pass over, O  Lord,
until the people whom You have purchased pass over. # Ps 74:2
17You shall bring them in, and plant them # Ps 44:2; 80:8
on the mountain of Your inheritance,
in the place, O  Lord, which You have made for Your dwelling,
in the sanctuary, O  Lord, which Your hands have established.
18The Lord will reign
forever and ever.” # Ps 10:16; 29:10
19For the horses of Pharaoh went in with his chariots and with his horsemen into the sea, and the Lord brought back the waters of the sea upon them, but the Israelites walked on dry land in the midst of the sea. # Ex 14:28–29
The Song of Miriam
20Miriam the prophetess, the sister of Aaron, # Nu 26:59 took a timbrel in her hand, and all the women went out after her with timbrels and with dancing. # Jdg 11:34; 1Sa 18:6 21Miriam answered them,
“Sing to the Lord,
for He triumphed gloriously!
The horse and his rider
He has hurled into the sea.” # Ex 15:1
The Waters of Marah and Elim
22Then Moses led Israel from the Red Sea, and they went out into the Wilderness of Shur, # Ge 16:7; 25:18 and they went three days in the wilderness and found no water. 23When they came to Marah, they could not drink of the waters of Marah, for they were bitter. Therefore, the name of it was called Marah. # Nu 33:8; Ru 1:20 24So the people murmured against Moses, # Ex 14:11; 16:2 saying, “What shall we drink?”
25And he cried to the Lord, and the Lord showed him a tree. When he had thrown it into the waters, the waters were made sweet.
There He made for them a statute and an ordinance, and there He tested them. # Ge 22:1; Ex 16:4; Dt 8:2, 16; Jdg 3:4 26He said, “If you diligently listen to the voice of the Lord your God, and do what is right in His sight, and give ear to His commandments, and keep all His statutes, I will not afflict you with any of the diseases with which I have afflicted the Egyptians. # Dt 7:15; 28:60 For I am the Lord who heals you.” # Ex 23:25
27Then they came to Elim, where there were twelve wells of water and seventy palm trees, and they camped there by the waters. # Nu 33:9
