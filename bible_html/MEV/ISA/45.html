Isaiah 45

45
Cyrus, God’s Anointed
1Thus says the Lord to Cyrus, His anointed,
whose right hand I have held # Ps 73:23; Isa 41:13 —
to subdue nations before him
and to loosen the loins of kings,
to open doors before him
so that the gates will not be shut:
2I will go before you
and make the crooked places straight; # Isa 40:4
I will break in pieces the gates of bronze
and shatter the bars of iron. # Ps 107:16
3And I will give you the treasures of darkness
and hidden riches of secret places
so that you may know that I, the Lord,
who calls you by your name, am the God of Israel. # Isa 43:1
4For Jacob My servant’s sake
and Israel My chosen one,
I have even called you by your name;
I have named you,
though you have not known Me. # Isa 41:8–9; Ac 17:23
5I am the Lord and there is no other;
there is no God besides Me. # Isa 44:8
I strengthen you, # Ps 18:39
though you have not known Me,
6so that they may know from the rising of the sun
and from the west # Mal 1:11
that there is no one besides Me.
I am the Lord, and there is no other; # Isa 37:20
7I form the light and create darkness;
I make peace and create calamity;
I, the Lord, do all these things. # 1Sa 2:6; Am 3:6
8Drip down, O heavens, from above, # Ps 72:6
and let the clouds pour down righteousness;
let the earth open up,
and let them bring forth salvation,
and let righteousness spring up together. # Hos 10:12
I, the Lord, have created it.
9Woe to him who strives with his maker,
the potsherd with the potsherds of the earth!
Shall the clay say to the potter, # Isa 64:8
“What are you making?”
Or the thing you are making say,
“He has no hands”? # Isa 29:16
10Woe to him who says to his father,
“What are you begetting?”
Or to the woman,
“To what are you giving birth?”
11Thus says the Lord,
the Holy One of Israel and his maker:
Ask Me of things to come
concerning My sons,
and you shall commit to Me the work of My hands. # Isa 29:23; 43:7
12I have made the earth
and created man on it.
I, even My hands, have stretched out the heavens, # Isa 42:5
and I have ordained all their host. # Ge 2:1; Ne 9:6
13I have raised him up in righteousness, # Isa 41:2
and I will direct all his ways;
he shall build My city,
and he shall let My captives go, # 2Ch 36:22–23
neither for price nor reward,
says the Lord of Hosts.
The Only Savior
14Thus says the Lord:
The products of Egypt and merchandise of Cush
and of the Sabeans—men of stature—
shall come over to you,
and they shall be yours;
they shall come after you;
in chains they shall come over,
and they shall fall down before you.
They shall make supplication to you, saying,
“Surely God is with you, and there is no other; # Isa 49:23; Jer 16:19; Zec 8:20–23
there is no other God.”
15Truly You are a God who hides Yourself, # Ps 44:24; Isa 57:17
O God of Israel, the Savior.
16They shall be ashamed and also humiliated, all of them; # Isa 42:17; 44:9
the craftsmen of idols shall go away in confusion together,
17but Israel shall be saved by the Lord
with an everlasting salvation; # Isa 26:4
you shall not be ashamed nor humiliated # Isa 54:4
to all eternity.
18For thus says the Lord,
who created the heavens,
who is God,
who formed the earth and made it, # Ge 1:2; Isa 42:5
who has established it,
who did not create it in vain,
who formed it to be inhabited:
I am the Lord,
and there is no other.
19I have not spoken in secret # Isa 48:16
in a dark place of the earth;
I did not say to the descendants of Jacob,
“Seek Me in a waste place”;
I, the Lord, speak righteousness;
I declare things that are right.
20Assemble yourselves and come; # Isa 43:9
draw near together, you fugitives of the nations.
They have no knowledge who set up the wood of their carved image,
and pray to a god that cannot save.
21Declare and set forth your case;
let them take counsel together.
Who has declared this from ancient times?
Who has told it from that time?
Have not I, the Lord?
And there is no God besides Me,
a righteous God and Savior;
there is no other besides Me. # Isa 43:11
22Turn to Me and be saved, # Nu 21:8–9; 2Ch 20:12
all the ends of the earth.
For I am God, and there is no other.
23I have sworn by Myself, # Ps 63:11
the word has gone out of My mouth in righteousness
and shall not return,
that to Me every knee shall bow,
every tongue shall take an oath. # Php 2:10–11
24Surely, one shall say, “Only in the Lord
are righteousness and strength.”
Men shall come to him,
and all who are incensed at Him shall be ashamed. # Isa 41:11
25In the Lord shall all the seed of Israel
be justified and shall glory. # Isa 41:16
