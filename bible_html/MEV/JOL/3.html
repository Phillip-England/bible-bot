Joel 3

3
Judging the Nations
1In those days and at that time,
when I restore the fortunes of Judah and Jerusalem, # Jer 16:15; 30:3
2I will gather all the nations,
and bring them down to the Valley of Jehoshaphat.
I will enter into judgment with them there # Isa 66:16
regarding My people and My inheritance Israel,
whom they have scattered among the nations;
they have also divided up My land.
3They have cast lots for My people, # Ob 11
and have traded a boy for a prostitute;
they have sold a girl for wine, # Am 2:6
that they might drink.
4Also, what are you to Me, Tyre and Sidon and all the regions of Philistia? Are you repaying Me for something? If you are repaying Me, I will return your payment swiftly and speedily on your head. # Isa 34:8; 59:18 5For you have taken My silver and My gold, and you have carried off My finest treasures to your temples. # 2Ki 12:18; 2Ch 21:16–17 6You have sold the people of Judah and Jerusalem to the Greeks # Eze 27:13; Joel 3:3 in order to remove them far from their border.
7Behold, I will stir them up from the place to which you have sold them, # Isa 43:5–6; Jer 23:8 and I will return your payment on your head. 8I will sell your sons and your daughters into the hand of the people of Judah, # Isa 60:14 and they will sell them to the Sabeans # Job 1:15 —to a distant nation, for the Lord has spoken.
9Proclaim this among the nations:
Consecrate a war!
Stir up the mighty men!
Let all the men of war draw near and rise. # Isa 8:9–10; Jer 46:3–4
10Beat your plowshares into swords
and your pruning hooks into spears; # Isa 2:4; Mic 4:3
let the weakling say,
“I am a warrior!”
11Hurry and come, all you surrounding nations,
and gather there.
Bring down Your warriors, # Isa 13:3; Zep 3:8 O  Lord.
12Let the nations be roused,
and go up to the Valley of Jehoshaphat; # Joel 3:2
for there I will sit
to judge all the surrounding nations. # Ps 96:13; Isa 2:4
13Swing the sickle,
for the harvest is ripe. # Hos 6:11
Come and tread,
for the winepress is full.
The wine vats overflow,
because their evil is great. # Isa 63:3
14Multitudes, multitudes,
in the valley of decision!
For the day of the Lord is near
in the valley of the decision. # Isa 34:2–8; Joel 1:15; 3:2
15The sun and moon darken,
and the stars withdraw their radiance. # Joel 2:10, 31
16The Lord roars from Zion,
and sounds His voice from Jerusalem, # Am 1:2
and heaven and earth quake. # Eze 38:19
But the Lord is a refuge for His people,
and a stronghold for the Israelites.
The Lord Dwells in Zion
17Then you will know that I am the Lord your God,
who dwells in Zion, My holy mountain.
And Jerusalem will be holy, # Isa 4:3; Joel 2:27
and invaders will never again pass through her.
18And it will be that in that day the mountains will drip sweet wine,
and the hills will flow with milk,
and all the streambeds of Judah will flow with water; # Isa 30:25; 35:6
a spring will proceed from the house of the Lord # Ge 2:10–14; Eze 47:1–12; Zec 14:8; Rev 22:1–2
and will water the Valley of Shittim. # Nu 25:1
19Egypt will become a desolation, # Isa 19:1–15
and Edom a desolate wasteland, # Ob 1–14
because of the violence done to the people of Judah—
they shed innocent blood in their land.
20But Judah will be inhabited forever,
and Jerusalem for generations and generations. # Eze 37:25; Am 9:15
21I will avenge their blood,
which I have not yet avenged. # Isa 4:4; Eze 36:25
The Lord dwells in Zion! # Rev 21:3
