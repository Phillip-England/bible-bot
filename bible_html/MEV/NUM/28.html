Numbers 28

28
Daily Offerings
1And the Lord spoke to Moses, saying: 2Command the Israelites and say to them: My offering, My bread for My sacrifices made by fire, # Lev 3:11 as a pleasing aroma to Me, # Ge 8:21 you will guard to offer to Me at their time. 3You will say to them: This is the fire offering which you will bring near to the Lord: two male lambs in their first year, without blemish, day by day, as a regular burnt offering. # Lev 6:9 4The one lamb you will offer in the morning, and the other lamb you will offer at evening, 5and one-tenth of an ephah # Ex 16:36 # 28:5 Likely about 31/2 pounds, or 1.6 kilograms; and in vv. 13, 21, and 29. of flour as a grain offering, # Lev 2:1 mingled with one-fourth of a hin # 28:5 About 1 quart, or 1 liter; and in vv. 7 and 14. of beaten oil. 6It is a regular burnt offering, which was ordained at Mount Sinai # Am 5:25 as a pleasing aroma, a sacrifice made by fire to the Lord. 7Its drink offering will be one-fourth of a hin for the one lamb. In a holy place you will pour the strong wine to the Lord as a drink offering. 8The other lamb you will offer at evening. As the morning grain offering and its drink offering, you will offer it as an offering made by fire, a pleasing aroma to the Lord.
Sabbath Offerings
9On the Sabbath day two lambs in their first year, without blemish, and two-tenths of an ephah # 28:9 Likely about 7 pounds, or 3.2 kilograms; and in vv. 12, 20, and 28. of flour as a grain offering, mixed with oil, and its drink offering— 10this is the burnt offering of every Sabbath, # Eze 46:4–5 in addition to the regular burnt offering and its drink offering.
Monthly Offerings
11At the beginnings of your months you will offer a burnt offering to the Lord: two young bulls, one ram, seven lambs in their first year, without blemish; # Nu 10:10; Eze 46:6 12three-tenths of an ephah # 28:12 Likely about 11 pounds, or 5 kilograms; and in vv. 20 and 28. of flour as a grain offering, mixed with oil, for one bull; and two-tenths of an ephah of flour as a grain offering, # Nu 15:4–12 mixed with oil, for one ram; 13and one-tenth of an ephah of flour mixed with oil as a grain offering for one lamb; as a burnt offering of a pleasing aroma, a sacrifice made by fire to the Lord. 14Their drink offerings will be one-half a hin # 28:14 About 2 quarts, or 1.9 liters. of wine for a bull, and one-third of a hin # 28:14 About 11/3 quarts, or 1.3 liters. for a ram, and one-fourth of a hin for a lamb. This is the burnt offering of each month throughout the months of the year. 15Also one male goat as a sin offering to the Lord shall be offered, besides the regular burnt offering # Nu 15:24; 28:3 and its drink offering.
Passover Offerings
Ex 12:14–20; Lev 23:4–8; Dt 16:1–8
16On the fourteenth day of the first month is the Passover of the Lord. # Ex 12:2–11, 18 17On the fifteenth day of this month is the feast. Unleavened bread will be eaten for seven days. # Lev 23:6 18On the first day there will be a holy convocation. You will not do any ordinary work, # Ex 12:16 19but you will offer a sacrifice made by fire as a burnt offering to the Lord: two young bulls, and one ram, and seven lambs in their first year. Be sure they are without blemish. # Lev 22:20; Dt 15:21 20And their grain offering will be of flour mixed with oil. Three-tenths of an ephah you will offer for a bull, and two-tenths of an ephah for a ram. 21One-tenth of an ephah you will offer for each of the seven lambs, 22and one goat as a sin offering, to make an atonement for you. 23You will offer these in addition to the burnt offering in the morning, which is for a regular burnt offering. # Nu 28:3 24The same way you will offer daily, throughout the seven days, the food of the sacrifice made by fire, as a pleasing aroma to the Lord. It will be offered in addition to the regular burnt offering and its drink offering. 25And on the seventh day you will have a holy assembly. You will not do any ordinary work. # Ex 12:16; Lev 23:8
Offerings at the Feast of Weeks
Lev 23:15–22; Dt 16:9–12
26And on the day of the firstfruits, when you bring a new grain offering to the Lord at your Feast of Weeks, # Ex 23:16; 34:22 you will have a holy assembly. You will do no ordinary work. 27But you will offer the burnt offering as a pleasing aroma to the Lord: # Lev 23:18–19 two young bulls, one ram, and seven lambs in their first year; 28and their grain offering of flour mixed with oil, three-tenths of an ephah for one bull, two-tenths of an ephah for one ram, 29one-tenth of an ephah for each of the seven lambs; 30and one goat, to make an atonement for you. # Nu 28:15, 22 31Make sure they are without blemish. # Nu 28:19 You will offer them with their drink offerings, besides the regular burnt offering and its grain offering.
