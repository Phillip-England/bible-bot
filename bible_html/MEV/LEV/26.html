Leviticus 26

26
Rewards for Obedience
1You shall not make for yourselves idols; neither set up a carved image nor a standing stone, nor shall you set up any sculpted stone in your land to bow down to it, for I am the Lord your God. # Ex 20:4–5; 23:24; Lev 19:4
2You shall keep My Sabbaths and reverence My sanctuary: I am the Lord. # Lev 19:30
3If you walk in My statutes and keep My commandments and do them, # Dt 11:13–15; 28:1–14 4then I will give you rain in due season, and the land shall yield its increase, and the trees of the field shall yield their fruit. # Ps 67:6; 85:12 5Your threshing shall last till the grape harvest, and the grape harvest shall last till the time for sowing, # Am 9:13 and you shall eat your bread till you are full and dwell in your land safely. # Lev 25:18–19
6I will give peace in the land, and you shall lie down for sleep, and none shall make you afraid; I will remove harmful beasts from the land, # Isa 35:9; Jer 30:10 and the sword shall not go through your land. 7You shall chase your enemies, and they shall fall before you by the sword. 8Five of you shall chase a hundred, and a hundred of you shall put ten thousand to flight, # Dt 32:30; Jos 23:10 and your enemies shall fall before you by the sword.
9I will turn toward you and make you fruitful and multiply you, and I will confirm My covenant with you. # Ge 17:6–7; Ne 9:23 10You shall eat the old harvest long stored, and clear out the old to make way for the new. # Lev 25:22 11I will set My tabernacle among you, # Ex 25:8; 29:45; Jn 1:14 and I shall not abhor you. 12I will walk among you, and I will be your God, and you shall be My people. # Ge 3:8; 2Co 6:16 13I am the Lord your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, that you should not be their slaves, and I have broken the bars of your yoke and made you walk upright. # Ex 20:2; Eze 34:27
Punishment for Disobedience
14But if you will not listen to Me, and will not do all these commandments, # Dt 28:15–68; Mal 2:2 15if you despise My statutes, or if you abhor My judgments, so that you will not do all My commandments, but you break My covenant, # 2Ki 17:15 16then I will do this to you: I will visit you with terror, with wasting disease, and with a fever that shall consume the eyes and cause sorrow of heart, # 1Sa 2:33 and you shall sow your seed in vain, for your enemies shall eat it. # Job 31:8; Ecc 2:21 17I will set My face against you, and you shall be slain before your enemies. They that hate you shall reign over you, and you shall flee when none pursues you. # Lev 17:10; Ps 53:5
18If you will not yet listen to Me after all this, then I will punish you seven times more for your sins. # Lev 26:21, 24 19I will break the pride of your power, # Isa 25:11 and I will make your heaven as iron and your land as bronze. # Dt 28:23 20Your strength shall be spent in vain, # Ps 127:1; Isa 49:4 for your land shall not yield her increase, nor shall the trees of the land yield their fruits.
21If you continue to walk contrary to Me and will not listen to Me, I will bring seven times more plagues upon you according to your sins. 22I will also send wild beasts among you, which shall rob you of your children, destroy your livestock, and make you few in number. # Dt 32:24; 2Ki 17:25 And your roads shall be desolate. # Jdg 5:6
23And if by these things you are not turned to Me, but walk contrary to Me, # Jer 2:30; 5:3 24then I will also walk contrary to you and will punish you yet seven times for your sins. # 2Sa 22:27; Ps 18:26 25I will bring a sword upon you that shall extract vengeance for My covenant. And when you are gathered together within your cities, I will send pestilence among you, and you shall be delivered into the hand of the enemy. # Nu 14:12; Eze 5:17 26When I have broken the supply of your bread, ten women shall bake your bread in one oven, and they shall ration your bread again by weight, and you shall eat and not be satisfied. # Isa 3:1; Mic 6:14
27If you will not listen to Me for all this, but walk contrary to Me, 28then I will walk contrary to you also in fury, # Isa 59:18; 63:3 and I Myself will chastise you seven times for your sins. 29You shall eat the flesh of your sons and the flesh of your daughters. # La 4:10; Eze 5:10 30I will destroy your high places, and cut down your images, and cast your funeral offerings on the lifeless forms of your idols, # 2Ki 23:20; Isa 27:9 and I shall abhor you. 31I will make your cities a waste and bring your sanctuaries to desolation, # 2Ki 25:4–10; Ne 2:3 and I will not smell the aroma of your fragrant offerings. 32I will bring the land into desolation, and your enemies that dwell there shall be astonished at it. # Jer 9:11; 18:16; Mic 3:12 33I will scatter you among the nations # Dt 4:27; Eze 20:23 and I will draw out a sword after you. And your land shall be desolate and your cities a waste. 34Then the land shall enjoy its sabbaths as long as it lies desolate, while you are in your enemies’ land; then the land shall rest and enjoy its sabbaths. # 2Ch 36:21 35As long as it lies desolate it shall rest because it did not rest during your sabbaths when you lived upon it.
36And on those who are left alive of you I will send faintness into their hearts in the lands of their enemies; and the sound of a shaken leaf shall make them flee, and they shall flee as from a sword, and they shall fall when no one pursues. # Lev 26:17; Eze 21:7 37They shall fall one upon one another, as to escape before the sword, though no one pursues, and you shall have no power to stand before your enemies. # Jos 7:12–13; Jdg 2:14 38You shall perish among the nations, and the land of your enemies shall consume you. 39And those who are left of you shall rot away in their iniquity in your enemies’ lands, and also because of the iniquities of their fathers, they shall rot away with them. # Eze 4:17; 33:10
40But if they confess their iniquity, and the iniquity of their fathers, # 1Ki 8:33–36; Pr 28:13 with their treachery that they committed against Me, and also that they have walked contrary to Me, 41and that I also have walked contrary to them and have brought them into the land of their enemies; if then their uncircumcised hearts are humbled, # Eze 44:7; Ac 7:51 and they then accept the punishment of their iniquity, 42then will I remember My covenant with Jacob, and also My covenant with Isaac, and also My covenant with Abraham, and I will remember the land. # Ex 2:24; 6:5 43But the land shall be abandoned by them, and enjoy its sabbaths while it lies desolate without them. They shall make amends for their iniquity, because they despised My judgments and because they abhorred My statutes. # Lev 26:15 44Yet for all that, when they are in the land of their enemies, I will not cast them away, nor will I abhor them to destroy them utterly and to break My covenant with them, # 2Ki 13:23; Ro 11:2 for I am the Lord their God. 45But for their sake I will remember the covenant with their fathers, whom I brought out of the land of Egypt in the sight of the nations, that I might be their God: I am the Lord. # Lev 22:33; 25:38
46These are the statutes and judgments and laws that the Lord made between Himself and the Israelites on Mount Sinai by the hand of Moses. # Lev 25:1; 27:34
