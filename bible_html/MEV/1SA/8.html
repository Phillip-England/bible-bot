1 Samuel 8

8
Israel Demands a King
1And it came about when Samuel was old, that he installed his sons as judges for Israel. # Dt 16:18–19 2Now the name of his firstborn son was Joel, and the name of his second son was Abijah. They were judging in Beer-sheba. 3But his sons did not walk in his way, for they followed after unlawful gain, and they took bribes, and they perverted justice. # Dt 16:19; Ps 15:5
4And all the elders of Israel gathered together and they came to Samuel at Ramah. # 1Sa 7:17 5They said to him, “Behold, you are old and your sons do not walk in your ways. Now, install for us a king to govern us like all the nations.” # Dt 17:14–15; 1Sa 8:19–20
6But the thing was evil in the eyes of Samuel, # 1Sa 12:17 because they said, “Give us a king to govern us.” And Samuel prayed to the Lord. # 1Sa 15:11 7The Lord said to Samuel, “Obey the voice of the people in relation to all that they say to you. For it is not you they have rejected, but Me they have rejected # Ex 16:8; 1Sa 10:19 from reigning over them. 8Just as all the deeds which they have done to Me, from the day I brought them up from Egypt even to this day, in that they have forsaken Me and have served other gods, so they are doing also to you now. 9Now then, obey their voice. Only you will testify against them and proclaim to them the judgment concerning the king who will reign over them.” # 1Sa 8:11–18; 10:25
10So Samuel said all the words of the Lord to the people who were asking from him a king. 11And he said, “This will be the judgment concerning the king who will reign over you: Your sons he will take in order to place them for himself # 1Sa 14:52 in his chariots and as his horsemen, and they will run before his chariot, 12and in order to assign for himself captains of thousands and captains of fifties, and to plow his ground, and to gather in his harvest, and to make his weapons of war and the equipment of his chariots. 13And your daughters he will take for perfumers, and cooks, and bakers. 14And your choicest fields, and vineyards, and olive groves he will take # 1Ki 21:7; Eze 46:18 and give them to his servants. 15And of your seed fields and your vineyards he will take one-tenth of their harvest and will give it to his high officials and to his servants. 16And your menservants and your maidservants, and the best of your young men and asses he will take and make do his work. 17Your flocks he will take one-tenth of, but you will be his for slaves. 18And you will cry out in that day because of your king, whom you have chosen for yourselves, but the Lord will not answer you in that day.” # Isa 1:15; Mic 3:4
19But the people refused to obey the voice of Samuel. # Jer 44:16 And they said, “No! But surely a king will be over us! 20So that we also will be like all the nations! # 1Sa 8:5 And so that our king will govern us, and will go out before us, and will fight our battles.”
21And after Samuel heard all the words of the people, he spoke them in the hearing of the Lord. # Jdg 11:11 22And the Lord said to Samuel, “Obey their voice, and make for them a king.” # 1Sa 8:7; Hos 13:11
So Samuel said to Israel’s fighting men, “Go every man to his city.”
