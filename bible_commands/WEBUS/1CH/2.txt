LABEL:7
VERSE:The son of Carmi: Achar, the troubler of Israel, who committed a trespass in the devoted thing.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The son of Ethan: Azariah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The sons also of Hezron, who were born to him: Jerahmeel, Ram, and Chelubai.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Ram became the father of Amminadab, and Amminadab became the father of Nahshon, prince of the children of Judah;
LABEL:11

LABEL:12

VERSE:Nethanel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,
LABEL:15
VERSE:Ozem the sixth, and David the seventh;
LABEL:16
VERSE:and their sisters were Zeruiah and Abigail. The sons of Zeruiah: Abishai, Joab, and Asahel, three.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Abigail bore Amasa; and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Caleb the son of Hezron became the father of children by Azubah his wife, and by Jerioth; and these were her sons: Jesher, Shobab, and Ardon.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Azubah died, and Caleb married Ephrath, who bore him Hur.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Hur became the father of Uri, and Uri became the father of Bezalel.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he took as wife when he was sixty years old; and she bore him Segub.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Segub became the father of Jair, who had twenty-three cities in the land of Gilead.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Geshur and Aram took the towns of Jair from them, with Kenath, and its villages, even sixty cities. All these were the sons of Machir the father of Gilead.
LABEL:24
VERSE:After Hezron died in Caleb Ephrathah, Abijah, Hezron’s wife, bore him Ashhur the father of Tekoa.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were Ram the firstborn, Bunah, Oren, Ozem, and Ahijah.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Jerahmeel had another wife, whose name was Atarah. She was the mother of Onam.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The sons of Ram the firstborn of Jerahmeel were Maaz, Jamin, and Eker.
LABEL:28
VERSE:The sons of Onam were Shammai and Jada. The sons of Shammai: Nadab and Abishur.
LABEL:29
VERSE:The name of the wife of Abishur was Abihail; and she bore him Ahban and Molid.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The sons of Nadab: Seled and Appaim; but Seled died without children.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The son of Appaim: Ishi. The son of Ishi: Sheshan. The son of Sheshan: Ahlai.
LABEL:32
VERSE:The sons of Jada the brother of Shammai: Jether and Jonathan; and Jether died without children.
LABEL:33
VERSE:The sons of Jonathan: Peleth and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.
LABEL:34
VERSE:Now Sheshan had no sons, but only daughters. Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.
LABEL:35
VERSE:Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant as wife; and she bore him Attai.
LABEL:36

LABEL:37

LABEL:38
VERSE:and Obed became the father of Jehu, and Jehu became the father of Azariah,
LABEL:39
VERSE:and Azariah became the father of Helez, and Helez became the father of Eleasah,
LABEL:40
VERSE:and Eleasah became the father of Sismai, and Sismai became the father of Shallum,
LABEL:41
VERSE:and Shallum became the father of Jekamiah, and Jekamiah became the father of Elishama.
LABEL:42
VERSE:The sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel were Mesha his firstborn, who was the father of Ziph, and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron.
LABEL:43
VERSE:The sons of Hebron: Korah, Tappuah, Rekem, and Shema.
LABEL:44
VERSE:Shema became the father of Raham, the father of Jorkeam; and Rekem became the father of Shammai.
LABEL:45

LABEL:46
VERSE:Ephah, Caleb’s concubine, bore Haran, Moza, and Gazez; and Haran became the father of Gazez.
LABEL:47

LABEL:48

LABEL:53

LABEL:54
