TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:15
TITLE:Proverbs 15
LABEL:1
VERSE:A gentle answer turns away wrath,
VERSE:but a harsh word stirs up anger.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The tongue of the wise commends knowledge,
VERSE:but the mouths of fools gush out folly.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yahweh’s eyes are everywhere,
VERSE:keeping watch on the evil and the good.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A gentle tongue is a tree of life,
VERSE:but deceit in it crushes the spirit.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A fool despises his father’s correction,
VERSE:but he who heeds reproof shows prudence.
LABEL:6
VERSE:In the house of the righteous is much treasure,
VERSE:but the income of the wicked brings trouble.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The lips of the wise spread knowledge;
VERSE:not so with the heart of fools.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The sacrifice made by the wicked is an abomination to Yahweh,
VERSE:but the prayer of the upright is his delight.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The way of the wicked is an abomination to Yahweh,
VERSE:but he loves him who follows after righteousness.
LABEL:10
VERSE:There is stern discipline for one who forsakes the way.
VERSE:Whoever hates reproof shall die.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Sheol and Abaddon are before Yahweh—
VERSE:how much more then the hearts of the children of men!
LABEL:12
VERSE:A scoffer doesn’t love to be reproved;
VERSE:he will not go to the wise.
LABEL:13
VERSE:A glad heart makes a cheerful face,
VERSE:but an aching heart breaks the spirit.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The heart of one who has understanding seeks knowledge,
VERSE:but the mouths of fools feed on folly.
LABEL:15
VERSE:All the days of the afflicted are wretched,
VERSE:but one who has a cheerful heart enjoys a continual feast.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Better is little, with the fear of Yahweh,
VERSE:than great treasure with trouble.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Better is a dinner of herbs, where love is,
VERSE:than a fattened calf with hatred.
LABEL:18
VERSE:A wrathful man stirs up contention,
VERSE:but one who is slow to anger appeases strife.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The way of the sluggard is like a thorn patch,
VERSE:but the path of the upright is a highway.
LABEL:20
VERSE:A wise son makes a father glad,
VERSE:but a foolish man despises his mother.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Folly is joy to one who is void of wisdom,
VERSE:but a man of understanding keeps his way straight.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Where there is no counsel, plans fail;
VERSE:but in a multitude of counselors they are established.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Joy comes to a man with the reply of his mouth.
VERSE:How good is a word at the right time!
LABEL:24
VERSE:The path of life leads upward for the wise,
VERSE:to keep him from going downward to Sheol.
LABEL:25
VERSE:Yahweh will uproot the house of the proud,
VERSE:but he will keep the widow’s borders intact.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Yahweh detests the thoughts of the wicked,
VERSE:but the thoughts of the pure are pleasing.
LABEL:27
VERSE:He who is greedy for gain troubles his own house,
VERSE:but he who hates bribes will live.
LABEL:28
VERSE:The heart of the righteous weighs answers,
VERSE:but the mouth of the wicked gushes out evil.
LABEL:29
VERSE:Yahweh is far from the wicked,
VERSE:but he hears the prayer of the righteous.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The light of the eyes rejoices the heart.
VERSE:Good news gives health to the bones.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The ear that listens to reproof lives,
VERSE:and will be at home among the wise.
LABEL:32
VERSE:He who refuses correction despises his own soul,
VERSE:but he who listens to reproof gets understanding.
LABEL:33
VERSE:The fear of Yahweh teaches wisdom.
VERSE:Before honor is humility.
