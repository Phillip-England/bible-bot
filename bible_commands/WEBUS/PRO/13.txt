TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:13
TITLE:Proverbs 13
LABEL:1
VERSE:A wise son listens to his father’s instruction,
VERSE:but a scoffer doesn’t listen to rebuke.
LABEL:2
VERSE:By the fruit of his lips, a man enjoys good things,
VERSE:but the unfaithful crave violence.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He who guards his mouth guards his soul.
VERSE:One who opens wide his lips comes to ruin.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The soul of the sluggard desires, and has nothing,
VERSE:but the desire of the diligent shall be fully satisfied.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A righteous man hates lies,
VERSE:but a wicked man brings shame and disgrace.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Righteousness guards the way of integrity,
VERSE:but wickedness overthrows the sinner.
LABEL:7
VERSE:There are some who pretend to be rich, yet have nothing.
VERSE:There are some who pretend to be poor, yet have great wealth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The ransom of a man’s life is his riches,
VERSE:but the poor hear no threats.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The light of the righteous shines brightly,
VERSE:but the lamp of the wicked is snuffed out.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Pride only breeds quarrels,
VERSE:but wisdom is with people who take advice.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Wealth gained dishonestly dwindles away,
VERSE:but he who gathers by hand makes it grow.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Hope deferred makes the heart sick,
VERSE:but when longing is fulfilled, it is a tree of life.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Whoever despises instruction will pay for it,
VERSE:but he who respects a command will be rewarded.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The teaching of the wise is a spring of life,
VERSE:to turn from the snares of death.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Good understanding wins favor,
VERSE:but the way of the unfaithful is hard.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Every prudent man acts from knowledge,
VERSE:but a fool exposes folly.
LABEL:17
VERSE:A wicked messenger falls into trouble,
VERSE:but a trustworthy envoy gains healing.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Poverty and shame come to him who refuses discipline,
VERSE:but he who heeds correction shall be honored.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Longing fulfilled is sweet to the soul,
VERSE:but fools detest turning from evil.
LABEL:20
VERSE:One who walks with wise men grows wise,
VERSE:but a companion of fools suffers harm.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Misfortune pursues sinners,
VERSE:but prosperity rewards the righteous.
LABEL:22
VERSE:A good man leaves an inheritance to his children’s children,
VERSE:but the wealth of the sinner is stored for the righteous.
LABEL:23
VERSE:An abundance of food is in poor people’s fields,
VERSE:but injustice sweeps it away.
LABEL:24
VERSE:One who spares the rod hates his son,
VERSE:but one who loves him is careful to discipline him.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The righteous one eats to the satisfying of his soul,
VERSE:but the belly of the wicked goes hungry.
