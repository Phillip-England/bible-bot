TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:17
TITLE:Proverbs 17
LABEL:1
VERSE:Better is a dry morsel with quietness,
VERSE:than a house full of feasting with strife.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A servant who deals wisely will rule over a son who causes shame,
VERSE:and shall have a part in the inheritance among the brothers.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The refining pot is for silver, and the furnace for gold,
VERSE:but Yahweh tests the hearts.
LABEL:4
VERSE:An evildoer heeds wicked lips.
VERSE:A liar gives ear to a mischievous tongue.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Whoever mocks the poor reproaches his Maker.
VERSE:He who is glad at calamity shall not be unpunished.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Children’s children are the crown of old men;
VERSE:the glory of children is their parents.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Excellent speech isn’t fitting for a fool,
VERSE:much less do lying lips fit a prince.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A bribe is a precious stone in the eyes of him who gives it;
VERSE:wherever he turns, he prospers.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He who covers an offense promotes love;
VERSE:but he who repeats a matter separates best friends.
LABEL:10
VERSE:A rebuke enters deeper into one who has understanding
VERSE:than a hundred lashes into a fool.
LABEL:11
VERSE:An evil man seeks only rebellion;
VERSE:therefore a cruel messenger shall be sent against him.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Let a bear robbed of her cubs meet a man,
VERSE:rather than a fool in his folly.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Whoever rewards evil for good,
VERSE:evil shall not depart from his house.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The beginning of strife is like breaching a dam,
VERSE:therefore stop contention before quarreling breaks out.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He who justifies the wicked, and he who condemns the righteous,
VERSE:both of them alike are an abomination to Yahweh.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Why is there money in the hand of a fool to buy wisdom,
VERSE:since he has no understanding?
LABEL:17
VERSE:A friend loves at all times;
VERSE:and a brother is born for adversity.
LABEL:18
VERSE:A man void of understanding strikes hands,
VERSE:and becomes collateral in the presence of his neighbor.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He who loves disobedience loves strife.
VERSE:One who builds a high gate seeks destruction.
LABEL:20
VERSE:One who has a perverse heart doesn’t find prosperity,
VERSE:and one who has a deceitful tongue falls into trouble.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He who becomes the father of a fool grieves.
VERSE:The father of a fool has no joy.
LABEL:22
VERSE:A cheerful heart makes good medicine,
VERSE:but a crushed spirit dries up the bones.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A wicked man receives a bribe in secret,
VERSE:to pervert the ways of justice.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Wisdom is before the face of one who has understanding,
VERSE:but the eyes of a fool wander to the ends of the earth.
LABEL:25
VERSE:A foolish son brings grief to his father,
VERSE:and bitterness to her who bore him.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Also to punish the righteous is not good,
VERSE:nor to flog officials for their integrity.
LABEL:27
VERSE:He who spares his words has knowledge.
VERSE:He who is even tempered is a man of understanding.
LABEL:28
VERSE:Even a fool, when he keeps silent, is counted wise.
VERSE:When he shuts his lips, he is thought to be discerning.
