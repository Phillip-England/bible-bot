BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:9
TITLE:Proverbs 9
LABEL:1
VERSE:Wisdom has built her house.
VERSE:She has carved out her seven pillars.
LABEL:2
VERSE:She has prepared her meat.
VERSE:She has mixed her wine.
VERSE:She has also set her table.
LABEL:3
VERSE:She has sent out her maidens.
VERSE:She cries from the highest places of the city:
LABEL:4
VERSE:“Whoever is simple, let him turn in here!”
VERSE:As for him who is void of understanding, she says to him,
LABEL:5
VERSE:“Come, eat some of my bread,
VERSE:Drink some of the wine which I have mixed!
LABEL:6
VERSE:Leave your simple ways, and live.
VERSE:Walk in the way of understanding.”
LABEL:7
VERSE:One who corrects a mocker invites insult.
VERSE:One who reproves a wicked man invites abuse.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Don’t reprove a scoffer, lest he hate you.
VERSE:Reprove a wise person, and he will love you.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Instruct a wise person, and he will be still wiser.
VERSE:Teach a righteous person, and he will increase in learning.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The fear of Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom.
VERSE:The knowledge of the Holy One is understanding.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For by me your days will be multiplied.
VERSE:The years of your life will be increased.
LABEL:12
VERSE:If you are wise, you are wise for yourself.
VERSE:If you mock, you alone will bear it.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The foolish woman is loud,
VERSE:undisciplined, and knows nothing.
LABEL:14
VERSE:She sits at the door of her house,
VERSE:on a seat in the high places of the city,
LABEL:15
VERSE:to call to those who pass by,
VERSE:who go straight on their ways,
LABEL:16
VERSE:“Whoever is simple, let him turn in here.”
VERSE:As for him who is void of understanding, she says to him,
LABEL:17
VERSE:“Stolen water is sweet.
VERSE:Food eaten in secret is pleasant.”
LABEL:18
VERSE:But he doesn’t know that the departed spirits are there,
VERSE:that her guests are in the depths of Sheol.
