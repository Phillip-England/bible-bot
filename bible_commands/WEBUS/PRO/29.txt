TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:29
TITLE:Proverbs 29
LABEL:1
VERSE:He who is often rebuked and stiffens his neck
VERSE:will be destroyed suddenly, with no remedy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:When the righteous thrive, the people rejoice;
VERSE:but when the wicked rule, the people groan.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Whoever loves wisdom brings joy to his father;
VERSE:but a companion of prostitutes squanders his wealth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The king by justice makes the land stable,
VERSE:but he who takes bribes tears it down.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A man who flatters his neighbor
VERSE:spreads a net for his feet.
LABEL:6
VERSE:An evil man is snared by his sin,
VERSE:but the righteous can sing and be glad.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The righteous care about justice for the poor.
VERSE:The wicked aren’t concerned about knowledge.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Mockers stir up a city,
VERSE:but wise men turn away anger.
LABEL:9
VERSE:If a wise man goes to court with a foolish man,
VERSE:the fool rages or scoffs, and there is no peace.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The bloodthirsty hate a man of integrity;
VERSE:and they seek the life of the upright.
LABEL:11
VERSE:A fool vents all of his anger,
VERSE:but a wise man brings himself under control.
LABEL:12
VERSE:If a ruler listens to lies,
VERSE:all of his officials are wicked.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The poor man and the oppressor have this in common:
VERSE:Yahweh gives sight to the eyes of both.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The king who fairly judges the poor,
VERSE:his throne shall be established forever.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The rod of correction gives wisdom,
VERSE:but a child left to himself causes shame to his mother.
LABEL:16
VERSE:When the wicked increase, sin increases;
VERSE:but the righteous will see their downfall.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Correct your son, and he will give you peace;
VERSE:yes, he will bring delight to your soul.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Where there is no revelation, the people cast off restraint;
VERSE:but one who keeps the law is blessed.
LABEL:19
VERSE:A servant can’t be corrected by words.
VERSE:Though he understands, yet he will not respond.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Do you see a man who is hasty in his words?
VERSE:There is more hope for a fool than for him.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He who pampers his servant from youth
VERSE:will have him become a son in the end.
LABEL:22
VERSE:An angry man stirs up strife,
VERSE:and a wrathful man abounds in sin.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A man’s pride brings him low,
VERSE:but one of lowly spirit gains honor.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Whoever is an accomplice of a thief is an enemy of his own soul.
VERSE:He takes an oath, but dares not testify.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The fear of man proves to be a snare,
VERSE:but whoever puts his trust in Yahweh is kept safe.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Many seek the ruler’s favor,
VERSE:but a man’s justice comes from Yahweh.
LABEL:27
VERSE:A dishonest man detests the righteous,
VERSE:and the upright in their ways detest the wicked.
