TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:21
TITLE:Proverbs 21
LABEL:1
VERSE:The king’s heart is in Yahweh’s hand like the watercourses.
VERSE:He turns it wherever he desires.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Every way of a man is right in his own eyes,
VERSE:but Yahweh weighs the hearts.
LABEL:3
VERSE:To do righteousness and justice
VERSE:is more acceptable to Yahweh than sacrifice.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A high look and a proud heart,
VERSE:the lamp of the wicked, is sin.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The plans of the diligent surely lead to profit;
VERSE:and everyone who is hasty surely rushes to poverty.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Getting treasures by a lying tongue
VERSE:is a fleeting vapor for those who seek death.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The violence of the wicked will drive them away,
VERSE:because they refuse to do what is right.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The way of the guilty is devious,
VERSE:but the conduct of the innocent is upright.
LABEL:9
VERSE:It is better to dwell in the corner of the housetop
VERSE:than to share a house with a contentious woman.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The soul of the wicked desires evil;
VERSE:his neighbor finds no mercy in his eyes.
LABEL:11
VERSE:When the mocker is punished, the simple gains wisdom.
VERSE:When the wise is instructed, he receives knowledge.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The Righteous One considers the house of the wicked,
VERSE:and brings the wicked to ruin.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Whoever stops his ears at the cry of the poor,
VERSE:he will also cry out, but shall not be heard.
LABEL:14
VERSE:A gift in secret pacifies anger,
VERSE:and a bribe in the cloak, strong wrath.
LABEL:15
VERSE:It is joy to the righteous to do justice;
VERSE:but it is a destruction to the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The man who wanders out of the way of understanding
VERSE:shall rest in the assembly of the departed spirits.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He who loves pleasure will be a poor man.
VERSE:He who loves wine and oil won’t be rich.
LABEL:18
VERSE:The wicked is a ransom for the righteous,
VERSE:the treacherous for the upright.
LABEL:19
VERSE:It is better to dwell in a desert land,
VERSE:than with a contentious and fretful woman.
LABEL:20
VERSE:There is precious treasure and oil in the dwelling of the wise,
VERSE:but a foolish man swallows it up.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He who follows after righteousness and kindness
VERSE:finds life, righteousness, and honor.
LABEL:22
VERSE:A wise man scales the city of the mighty,
VERSE:and brings down the strength of its confidence.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Whoever guards his mouth and his tongue
VERSE:keeps his soul from troubles.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The proud and arrogant man—“Scoffer” is his name—
VERSE:he works in the arrogance of pride.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The desire of the sluggard kills him,
VERSE:for his hands refuse to labor.
LABEL:26
VERSE:There are those who covet greedily all day long;
VERSE:but the righteous give and don’t withhold.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination—
VERSE:how much more, when he brings it with a wicked mind!
LABEL:28
VERSE:A false witness will perish.
VERSE:A man who listens speaks to eternity.
LABEL:29
VERSE:A wicked man hardens his face;
VERSE:but as for the upright, he establishes his ways.
LABEL:30
VERSE:There is no wisdom nor understanding
VERSE:nor counsel against Yahweh.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The horse is prepared for the day of battle;
VERSE:but victory is with Yahweh.
