TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:20
TITLE:Proverbs 20
LABEL:1
VERSE:Wine is a mocker and beer is a brawler.
VERSE:Whoever is led astray by them is not wise.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The terror of a king is like the roaring of a lion.
VERSE:He who provokes him to anger forfeits his own life.
LABEL:3
VERSE:It is an honor for a man to keep aloof from strife,
VERSE:but every fool will be quarreling.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The sluggard will not plow by reason of the winter;
VERSE:therefore he shall beg in harvest, and have nothing.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Counsel in the heart of man is like deep water,
VERSE:but a man of understanding will draw it out.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Many men claim to be men of unfailing love,
VERSE:but who can find a faithful man?
LABEL:7
VERSE:A righteous man walks in integrity.
VERSE:Blessed are his children after him.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A king who sits on the throne of judgment
VERSE:scatters away all evil with his eyes.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Who can say, “I have made my heart pure.
VERSE:I am clean and without sin”?
LABEL:10
VERSE:Differing weights and differing measures,
VERSE:both of them alike are an abomination to Yahweh.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Even a child makes himself known by his doings,
VERSE:whether his work is pure, and whether it is right.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The hearing ear, and the seeing eye,
VERSE:Yahweh has made even both of them.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Don’t love sleep, lest you come to poverty.
VERSE:Open your eyes, and you shall be satisfied with bread.
LABEL:14
VERSE:“It’s no good, it’s no good,” says the buyer;
VERSE:but when he is gone his way, then he boasts.
LABEL:15
VERSE:There is gold and abundance of rubies,
VERSE:but the lips of knowledge are a rare jewel.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Take the garment of one who puts up collateral for a stranger;
VERSE:and hold him in pledge for a wayward woman.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Fraudulent food is sweet to a man,
VERSE:but afterwards his mouth is filled with gravel.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Plans are established by advice;
VERSE:by wise guidance you wage war!
LABEL:19
VERSE:He who goes about as a tale-bearer reveals secrets;
VERSE:therefore don’t keep company with him who opens wide his lips.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Whoever curses his father or his mother,
VERSE:his lamp shall be put out in blackness of darkness.
LABEL:21
VERSE:An inheritance quickly gained at the beginning
VERSE:won’t be blessed in the end.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Don’t say, “I will pay back evil.”
VERSE:Wait for Yahweh, and he will save you.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Yahweh detests differing weights,
VERSE:and dishonest scales are not pleasing.
LABEL:24
VERSE:A man’s steps are from Yahweh;
VERSE:how then can man understand his way?
LABEL:25
VERSE:It is a snare to a man to make a rash dedication,
VERSE:then later to consider his vows.
LABEL:26
VERSE:A wise king winnows out the wicked,
VERSE:and drives the threshing wheel over them.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The spirit of man is Yahweh’s lamp,
VERSE:searching all his innermost parts.
LABEL:28
VERSE:Love and faithfulness keep the king safe.
VERSE:His throne is sustained by love.
LABEL:29
VERSE:The glory of young men is their strength.
VERSE:The splendor of old men is their gray hair.
LABEL:30
VERSE:Wounding blows cleanse away evil,
VERSE:and beatings purge the innermost parts.
