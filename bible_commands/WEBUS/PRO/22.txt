TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:22
TITLE:Proverbs 22
LABEL:1
VERSE:A good name is more desirable than great riches,
VERSE:and loving favor is better than silver and gold.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The rich and the poor have this in common:
VERSE:Yahweh is the maker of them all.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A prudent man sees danger and hides himself;
VERSE:but the simple pass on, and suffer for it.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The result of humility and the fear of Yahweh
VERSE:is wealth, honor, and life.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Thorns and snares are in the path of the wicked;
VERSE:whoever guards his soul stays far from them.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Train up a child in the way he should go,
VERSE:and when he is old he will not depart from it.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The rich rule over the poor.
VERSE:The borrower is servant to the lender.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He who sows wickedness reaps trouble,
VERSE:and the rod of his fury will be destroyed.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He who has a generous eye will be blessed,
VERSE:for he shares his food with the poor.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Drive out the mocker, and strife will go out;
VERSE:yes, quarrels and insults will stop.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He who loves purity of heart and speaks gracefully
VERSE:is the king’s friend.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Yahweh’s eyes watch over knowledge,
VERSE:but he frustrates the words of the unfaithful.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The sluggard says, “There is a lion outside!
VERSE:I will be killed in the streets!”
LABEL:14
VERSE:The mouth of an adulteress is a deep pit.
VERSE:He who is under Yahweh’s wrath will fall into it.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Folly is bound up in the heart of a child;
VERSE:the rod of discipline drives it far from him.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Whoever oppresses the poor for his own increase and whoever gives to the rich,
VERSE:both come to poverty.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Turn your ear, and listen to the words of the wise.
VERSE:Apply your heart to my teaching.
LABEL:18
VERSE:For it is a pleasant thing if you keep them within you,
VERSE:if all of them are ready on your lips.
LABEL:19
VERSE:I teach you today, even you,
VERSE:so that your trust may be in Yahweh.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Haven’t I written to you thirty excellent things
VERSE:of counsel and knowledge,
LABEL:21
VERSE:To teach you truth, reliable words,
VERSE:to give sound answers to the ones who sent you?
LABEL:22
VERSE:Don’t exploit the poor because he is poor;
VERSE:and don’t crush the needy in court;
LABEL:23
VERSE:for Yahweh will plead their case,
VERSE:and plunder the life of those who plunder them.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Don’t befriend a hot-tempered man.
VERSE:Don’t associate with one who harbors anger,
LABEL:25
VERSE:lest you learn his ways
VERSE:and ensnare your soul.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Don’t you be one of those who strike hands,
VERSE:of those who are collateral for debts.
LABEL:27
VERSE:If you don’t have means to pay,
VERSE:why should he take away your bed from under you?
LABEL:28
VERSE:Don’t move the ancient boundary stone
VERSE:which your fathers have set up.
LABEL:29
VERSE:Do you see a man skilled in his work?
VERSE:He will serve kings.
VERSE:He won’t serve obscure men.
