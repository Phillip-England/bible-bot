TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:16
TITLE:Proverbs 16
LABEL:1
VERSE:The plans of the heart belong to man,
VERSE:but the answer of the tongue is from Yahweh.
LABEL:2
VERSE:All the ways of a man are clean in his own eyes,
VERSE:but Yahweh weighs the motives.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Commit your deeds to Yahweh,
VERSE:and your plans shall succeed.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Yahweh has made everything for its own end—
VERSE:yes, even the wicked for the day of evil.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Everyone who is proud in heart is an abomination to Yahweh;
VERSE:they shall certainly not be unpunished.
LABEL:6
VERSE:By mercy and truth iniquity is atoned for.
VERSE:By the fear of Yahweh men depart from evil.
LABEL:7
VERSE:When a man’s ways please Yahweh,
VERSE:he makes even his enemies to be at peace with him.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Better is a little with righteousness,
VERSE:than great revenues with injustice.
LABEL:9
VERSE:A man’s heart plans his course,
VERSE:but Yahweh directs his steps.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Inspired judgments are on the lips of the king.
VERSE:He shall not betray his mouth.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Honest balances and scales are Yahweh’s;
VERSE:all the weights in the bag are his work.
LABEL:12
VERSE:It is an abomination for kings to do wrong,
VERSE:for the throne is established by righteousness.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Righteous lips are the delight of kings.
VERSE:They value one who speaks the truth.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The king’s wrath is a messenger of death,
VERSE:but a wise man will pacify it.
LABEL:15
VERSE:In the light of the king’s face is life.
VERSE:His favor is like a cloud of the spring rain.
LABEL:16
VERSE:How much better it is to get wisdom than gold!
VERSE:Yes, to get understanding is to be chosen rather than silver.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The highway of the upright is to depart from evil.
VERSE:He who keeps his way preserves his soul.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Pride goes before destruction,
VERSE:and an arrogant spirit before a fall.
LABEL:19
VERSE:It is better to be of a lowly spirit with the poor,
VERSE:than to divide the plunder with the proud.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He who heeds the Word finds prosperity.
VERSE:Whoever trusts in Yahweh is blessed.
LABEL:21
VERSE:The wise in heart shall be called prudent.
VERSE:Pleasantness of the lips promotes instruction.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Understanding is a fountain of life to one who has it,
VERSE:but the punishment of fools is their folly.
LABEL:23
VERSE:The heart of the wise instructs his mouth,
VERSE:and adds learning to his lips.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Pleasant words are a honeycomb,
VERSE:sweet to the soul, and health to the bones.
LABEL:25
VERSE:There is a way which seems right to a man,
VERSE:but in the end it leads to death.
LABEL:26
VERSE:The appetite of the laboring man labors for him,
VERSE:for his mouth urges him on.
LABEL:27
VERSE:A worthless man devises mischief.
VERSE:His speech is like a scorching fire.
LABEL:28
VERSE:A perverse man stirs up strife.
VERSE:A whisperer separates close friends.
LABEL:29
VERSE:A man of violence entices his neighbor,
VERSE:and leads him in a way that is not good.
LABEL:30
VERSE:One who winks his eyes to plot perversities,
VERSE:one who compresses his lips, is bent on evil.
LABEL:31
VERSE:Gray hair is a crown of glory.
VERSE:It is attained by a life of righteousness.
LABEL:32
VERSE:One who is slow to anger is better than the mighty;
VERSE:one who rules his spirit, than he who takes a city.
LABEL:33
VERSE:The lot is cast into the lap,
VERSE:but its every decision is from Yahweh.
