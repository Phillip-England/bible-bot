TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:11
TITLE:Proverbs 11
LABEL:1
VERSE:A false balance is an abomination to Yahweh,
VERSE:but accurate weights are his delight.
LABEL:2
VERSE:When pride comes, then comes shame,
VERSE:but with humility comes wisdom.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The integrity of the upright shall guide them,
VERSE:but the perverseness of the treacherous shall destroy them.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Riches don’t profit in the day of wrath,
VERSE:but righteousness delivers from death.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The righteousness of the blameless will direct his way,
VERSE:but the wicked shall fall by his own wickedness.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The righteousness of the upright shall deliver them,
VERSE:but the unfaithful will be trapped by evil desires.
LABEL:7
VERSE:When a wicked man dies, hope perishes,
VERSE:and expectation of power comes to nothing.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A righteous person is delivered out of trouble,
VERSE:and the wicked takes his place.
LABEL:9
VERSE:With his mouth the godless man destroys his neighbor,
VERSE:but the righteous will be delivered through knowledge.
LABEL:10
VERSE:When it goes well with the righteous, the city rejoices.
VERSE:When the wicked perish, there is shouting.
LABEL:11
VERSE:By the blessing of the upright, the city is exalted,
VERSE:but it is overthrown by the mouth of the wicked.
LABEL:12
VERSE:One who despises his neighbor is void of wisdom,
VERSE:but a man of understanding holds his peace.
LABEL:13
VERSE:One who brings gossip betrays a confidence,
VERSE:but one who is of a trustworthy spirit is one who keeps a secret.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Where there is no wise guidance, the nation falls,
VERSE:but in the multitude of counselors there is victory.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He who is collateral for a stranger will suffer for it,
VERSE:but he who refuses pledges of collateral is secure.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A gracious woman obtains honor,
VERSE:but violent men obtain riches.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The merciful man does good to his own soul,
VERSE:but he who is cruel troubles his own flesh.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Wicked people earn deceitful wages,
VERSE:but one who sows righteousness reaps a sure reward.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He who is truly righteous gets life.
VERSE:He who pursues evil gets death.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Those who are perverse in heart are an abomination to Yahweh,
VERSE:but those whose ways are blameless are his delight.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Most certainly, the evil man will not be unpunished,
VERSE:but the offspring of the righteous will be delivered.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Like a gold ring in a pig’s snout,
VERSE:is a beautiful woman who lacks discretion.
LABEL:23
VERSE:The desire of the righteous is only good.
VERSE:The expectation of the wicked is wrath.
LABEL:24
VERSE:There is one who scatters, and increases yet more.
VERSE:There is one who withholds more than is appropriate, but gains poverty.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The liberal soul shall be made fat.
VERSE:He who waters shall be watered also himself.
LABEL:26
VERSE:People curse someone who withholds grain,
VERSE:but blessing will be on the head of him who sells it.
LABEL:27
VERSE:He who diligently seeks good seeks favor,
VERSE:but he who searches after evil, it shall come to him.
LABEL:28
VERSE:He who trusts in his riches will fall,
VERSE:but the righteous shall flourish as the green leaf.
LABEL:29
VERSE:He who troubles his own house shall inherit the wind.
VERSE:The foolish shall be servant to the wise of heart.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The fruit of the righteous is a tree of life.
VERSE:He who is wise wins souls.
LABEL:31
VERSE:Behold, the righteous shall be repaid in the earth,
VERSE:how much more the wicked and the sinner!
