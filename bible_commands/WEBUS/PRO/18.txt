TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:18
TITLE:Proverbs 18
LABEL:1
VERSE:A man who isolates himself pursues selfishness,
VERSE:and defies all sound judgment.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A fool has no delight in understanding,
VERSE:but only in revealing his own opinion.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When wickedness comes, contempt also comes,
VERSE:and with shame comes disgrace.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The words of a man’s mouth are like deep waters.
VERSE:The fountain of wisdom is like a flowing brook.
LABEL:5
VERSE:To be partial to the faces of the wicked is not good,
VERSE:nor to deprive the innocent of justice.
LABEL:6
VERSE:A fool’s lips come into strife,
VERSE:and his mouth invites beatings.
LABEL:7
VERSE:A fool’s mouth is his destruction,
VERSE:and his lips are a snare to his soul.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The words of a gossip are like dainty morsels:
VERSE:they go down into a person’s innermost parts.
LABEL:9
VERSE:One who is slack in his work
VERSE:is brother to him who is a master of destruction.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Yahweh’s name is a strong tower:
VERSE:the righteous run to him, and are safe.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The rich man’s wealth is his strong city,
VERSE:like an unscalable wall in his own imagination.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Before destruction the heart of man is proud,
VERSE:but before honor is humility.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He who answers before he hears,
VERSE:that is folly and shame to him.
LABEL:14
VERSE:A man’s spirit will sustain him in sickness,
VERSE:but a crushed spirit, who can bear?
LABEL:15
VERSE:The heart of the discerning gets knowledge.
VERSE:The ear of the wise seeks knowledge.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A man’s gift makes room for him,
VERSE:and brings him before great men.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He who pleads his cause first seems right—
VERSE:until another comes and questions him.
LABEL:18
VERSE:The lot settles disputes,
VERSE:and keeps strong ones apart.
LABEL:19
VERSE:A brother offended is more difficult than a fortified city.
VERSE:Disputes are like the bars of a fortress.
LABEL:20
VERSE:A man’s stomach is filled with the fruit of his mouth.
VERSE:With the harvest of his lips he is satisfied.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Death and life are in the power of the tongue;
VERSE:those who love it will eat its fruit.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Whoever finds a wife finds a good thing,
VERSE:and obtains favor of Yahweh.
LABEL:23
VERSE:The poor plead for mercy,
VERSE:but the rich answer harshly.
LABEL:24
VERSE:A man of many companions may be ruined,
VERSE:but there is a friend who sticks closer than a brother.
