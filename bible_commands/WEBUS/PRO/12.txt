TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:12
TITLE:Proverbs 12
LABEL:1
VERSE:Whoever loves correction loves knowledge,
VERSE:but he who hates reproof is stupid.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A good man shall obtain favor from Yahweh,
VERSE:but he will condemn a man of wicked plans.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A man shall not be established by wickedness,
VERSE:but the root of the righteous shall not be moved.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A worthy woman is the crown of her husband,
VERSE:but a disgraceful wife is as rottenness in his bones.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The thoughts of the righteous are just,
VERSE:but the advice of the wicked is deceitful.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The words of the wicked are about lying in wait for blood,
VERSE:but the speech of the upright rescues them.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The wicked are overthrown, and are no more,
VERSE:but the house of the righteous shall stand.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A man shall be commended according to his wisdom,
VERSE:but he who has a warped mind shall be despised.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Better is he who is little known, and has a servant,
VERSE:than he who honors himself and lacks bread.
LABEL:10
VERSE:A righteous man respects the life of his animal,
VERSE:but the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He who tills his land shall have plenty of bread,
VERSE:but he who chases fantasies is void of understanding.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The wicked desires the plunder of evil men,
VERSE:but the root of the righteous flourishes.
LABEL:13
VERSE:An evil man is trapped by sinfulness of lips,
VERSE:but the righteous shall come out of trouble.
LABEL:14
VERSE:A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth.
VERSE:The work of a man’s hands shall be rewarded to him.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The way of a fool is right in his own eyes,
VERSE:but he who is wise listens to counsel.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A fool shows his annoyance the same day,
VERSE:but one who overlooks an insult is prudent.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He who is truthful testifies honestly,
VERSE:but a false witness lies.
LABEL:18
VERSE:There is one who speaks rashly like the piercing of a sword,
VERSE:but the tongue of the wise heals.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Truth’s lips will be established forever,
VERSE:but a lying tongue is only momentary.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Deceit is in the heart of those who plot evil,
VERSE:but joy comes to the promoters of peace.
LABEL:21
VERSE:No mischief shall happen to the righteous,
VERSE:but the wicked shall be filled with evil.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Lying lips are an abomination to Yahweh,
VERSE:but those who do the truth are his delight.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A prudent man keeps his knowledge,
VERSE:but the hearts of fools proclaim foolishness.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The hands of the diligent ones shall rule,
VERSE:but laziness ends in slave labor.
LABEL:25
VERSE:Anxiety in a man’s heart weighs it down,
VERSE:but a kind word makes it glad.
LABEL:26
VERSE:A righteous person is cautious in friendship,
VERSE:but the way of the wicked leads them astray.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The slothful man doesn’t roast his game,
VERSE:but the possessions of diligent men are prized.
LABEL:28
VERSE:In the way of righteousness is life;
VERSE:in its path there is no death.
