TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:4
TITLE:Proverbs 4
LABEL:1
VERSE:Listen, sons, to a father’s instruction.
VERSE:Pay attention and know understanding;
LABEL:2
VERSE:for I give you sound learning.
VERSE:Don’t forsake my law.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For I was a son to my father,
VERSE:tender and an only child in the sight of my mother.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He taught me, and said to me:
VERSE:“Let your heart retain my words.
VERSE:Keep my commandments, and live.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Get wisdom.
VERSE:Get understanding.
VERSE:Don’t forget, and don’t deviate from the words of my mouth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Don’t forsake her, and she will preserve you.
VERSE:Love her, and she will keep you.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Wisdom is supreme.
VERSE:Get wisdom.
VERSE:Yes, though it costs all your possessions, get understanding.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Esteem her, and she will exalt you.
VERSE:She will bring you to honor when you embrace her.
LABEL:9
VERSE:She will give to your head a garland of grace.
VERSE:She will deliver a crown of splendor to you.”
LABEL:10
VERSE:Listen, my son, and receive my sayings.
VERSE:The years of your life will be many.
LABEL:11
VERSE:I have taught you in the way of wisdom.
VERSE:I have led you in straight paths.
LABEL:12
VERSE:When you go, your steps will not be hampered.
VERSE:When you run, you will not stumble.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Take firm hold of instruction.
VERSE:Don’t let her go.
VERSE:Keep her, for she is your life.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Don’t enter into the path of the wicked.
VERSE:Don’t walk in the way of evil men.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Avoid it, and don’t pass by it.
VERSE:Turn from it, and pass on.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For they don’t sleep unless they do evil.
VERSE:Their sleep is taken away, unless they make someone fall.
LABEL:17
VERSE:For they eat the bread of wickedness
VERSE:and drink the wine of violence.
LABEL:18
VERSE:But the path of the righteous is like the dawning light
VERSE:that shines more and more until the perfect day.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The way of the wicked is like darkness.
VERSE:They don’t know what they stumble over.
LABEL:20
VERSE:My son, attend to my words.
VERSE:Turn your ear to my sayings.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Let them not depart from your eyes.
VERSE:Keep them in the center of your heart.
LABEL:22
VERSE:For they are life to those who find them,
VERSE:and health to their whole body.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Keep your heart with all diligence,
VERSE:for out of it is the wellspring of life.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Put away from yourself a perverse mouth.
VERSE:Put corrupt lips far from you.
LABEL:25
VERSE:Let your eyes look straight ahead.
VERSE:Fix your gaze directly before you.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Make the path of your feet level.
VERSE:Let all of your ways be established.
LABEL:27
VERSE:Don’t turn to the right hand nor to the left.
VERSE:Remove your foot from evil.
