TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:14
TITLE:Proverbs 14
LABEL:1
VERSE:Every wise woman builds her house,
VERSE:but the foolish one tears it down with her own hands.
LABEL:2
VERSE:He who walks in his uprightness fears Yahweh,
VERSE:but he who is perverse in his ways despises him.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The fool’s talk brings a rod to his back,
VERSE:but the lips of the wise protect them.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Where no oxen are, the crib is clean,
VERSE:but much increase is by the strength of the ox.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A truthful witness will not lie,
VERSE:but a false witness pours out lies.
LABEL:6
VERSE:A scoffer seeks wisdom, and doesn’t find it,
VERSE:but knowledge comes easily to a discerning person.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Stay away from a foolish man,
VERSE:for you won’t find knowledge on his lips.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The wisdom of the prudent is to think about his way,
VERSE:but the folly of fools is deceit.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Fools mock at making atonement for sins,
VERSE:but among the upright there is good will.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The heart knows its own bitterness and joy;
VERSE:he will not share these with a stranger.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The house of the wicked will be overthrown,
VERSE:but the tent of the upright will flourish.
LABEL:12
VERSE:There is a way which seems right to a man,
VERSE:but in the end it leads to death.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Even in laughter the heart may be sorrowful,
VERSE:and mirth may end in heaviness.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The unfaithful will be repaid for his own ways;
VERSE:likewise a good man will be rewarded for his ways.
LABEL:15
VERSE:A simple man believes everything,
VERSE:but the prudent man carefully considers his ways.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A wise man fears and shuns evil,
VERSE:but the fool is hot headed and reckless.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He who is quick to become angry will commit folly,
VERSE:and a crafty man is hated.
LABEL:18
VERSE:The simple inherit folly,
VERSE:but the prudent are crowned with knowledge.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The evil bow down before the good,
VERSE:and the wicked at the gates of the righteous.
LABEL:20
VERSE:The poor person is shunned even by his own neighbor,
VERSE:but the rich person has many friends.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He who despises his neighbor sins,
VERSE:but he who has pity on the poor is blessed.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Don’t they go astray who plot evil?
VERSE:But love and faithfulness belong to those who plan good.
LABEL:23
VERSE:In all hard work there is profit,
VERSE:but the talk of the lips leads only to poverty.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The crown of the wise is their riches,
VERSE:but the folly of fools crowns them with folly.
LABEL:25
VERSE:A truthful witness saves souls,
VERSE:but a false witness is deceitful.
LABEL:26
VERSE:In the fear of Yahweh is a secure fortress,
VERSE:and he will be a refuge for his children.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The fear of Yahweh is a fountain of life,
VERSE:turning people from the snares of death.
LABEL:28
VERSE:In the multitude of people is the king’s glory,
VERSE:but in the lack of people is the destruction of the prince.
LABEL:29
VERSE:He who is slow to anger has great understanding,
VERSE:but he who has a quick temper displays folly.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The life of the body is a heart at peace,
VERSE:but envy rots the bones.
LABEL:31
VERSE:He who oppresses the poor shows contempt for his Maker,
VERSE:but he who is kind to the needy honors him.
LABEL:32
VERSE:The wicked is brought down in his calamity,
VERSE:but in death, the righteous has a refuge.
LABEL:33
VERSE:Wisdom rests in the heart of one who has understanding,
VERSE:and is even made known in the inward part of fools.
LABEL:34
VERSE:Righteousness exalts a nation,
VERSE:but sin is a disgrace to any people.
LABEL:35
VERSE:The king’s favor is toward a servant who deals wisely,
VERSE:but his wrath is toward one who causes shame.
