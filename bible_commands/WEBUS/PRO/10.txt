TITLE:Proverbs 10
LABEL:1
VERSE:The proverbs of Solomon.
VERSE:A wise son makes a glad father;
VERSE:but a foolish son brings grief to his mother.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Treasures of wickedness profit nothing,
VERSE:but righteousness delivers from death.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yahweh will not allow the soul of the righteous to go hungry,
VERSE:but he thrusts away the desire of the wicked.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He becomes poor who works with a lazy hand,
VERSE:but the hand of the diligent brings wealth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He who gathers in summer is a wise son,
VERSE:but he who sleeps during the harvest is a son who causes shame.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Blessings are on the head of the righteous,
VERSE:but violence covers the mouth of the wicked.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The memory of the righteous is blessed,
VERSE:but the name of the wicked will rot.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The wise in heart accept commandments,
VERSE:but a chattering fool will fall.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He who walks blamelessly walks surely,
VERSE:but he who perverts his ways will be found out.
LABEL:10
VERSE:One who winks with the eye causes sorrow,
VERSE:but a chattering fool will fall.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The mouth of the righteous is a spring of life,
VERSE:but violence covers the mouth of the wicked.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Hatred stirs up strife,
VERSE:but love covers all wrongs.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Wisdom is found on the lips of him who has discernment,
VERSE:but a rod is for the back of him who is void of understanding.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Wise men lay up knowledge,
VERSE:but the mouth of the foolish is near ruin.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The rich man’s wealth is his strong city.
VERSE:The destruction of the poor is their poverty.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The labor of the righteous leads to life.
VERSE:The increase of the wicked leads to sin.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He is in the way of life who heeds correction,
VERSE:but he who forsakes reproof leads others astray.
LABEL:18
VERSE:He who hides hatred has lying lips.
VERSE:He who utters a slander is a fool.
LABEL:19
VERSE:In the multitude of words there is no lack of disobedience,
VERSE:but he who restrains his lips does wisely.
LABEL:20
VERSE:The tongue of the righteous is like choice silver.
VERSE:The heart of the wicked is of little worth.
LABEL:21
VERSE:The lips of the righteous feed many,
VERSE:but the foolish die for lack of understanding.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Yahweh’s blessing brings wealth,
VERSE:and he adds no trouble to it.
LABEL:23
VERSE:It is a fool’s pleasure to do wickedness,
VERSE:but wisdom is a man of understanding’s pleasure.
LABEL:24
VERSE:What the wicked fear will overtake them,
VERSE:but the desire of the righteous will be granted.
LABEL:25
VERSE:When the whirlwind passes, the wicked is no more;
VERSE:but the righteous stand firm forever.
LABEL:26
VERSE:As vinegar to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes,
VERSE:so is the sluggard to those who send him.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The fear of Yahweh prolongs days,
VERSE:but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.
LABEL:28
VERSE:The prospect of the righteous is joy,
VERSE:but the hope of the wicked will perish.
LABEL:29
VERSE:The way of Yahweh is a stronghold to the upright,
VERSE:but it is a destruction to the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The righteous will never be removed,
VERSE:but the wicked will not dwell in the land.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The mouth of the righteous produces wisdom,
VERSE:but the perverse tongue will be cut off.
LABEL:32
VERSE:The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable,
VERSE:but the mouth of the wicked is perverse.
