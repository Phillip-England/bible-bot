TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:HOS
CHAPTER:6
TITLE:Hosea 6
LABEL:1
VERSE:“Come! Let’s return to Yahweh;
VERSE:for he has torn us to pieces,
VERSE:and he will heal us;
VERSE:he has injured us,
VERSE:and he will bind up our wounds.
LABEL:2
VERSE:After two days he will revive us.
VERSE:On the third day he will raise us up,
VERSE:and we will live before him.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Let’s acknowledge Yahweh.
VERSE:Let’s press on to know Yahweh.
VERSE:As surely as the sun rises,
VERSE:Yahweh will appear.
VERSE:He will come to us like the rain,
VERSE:like the spring rain that waters the earth.”
LABEL:4
VERSE:“Ephraim, what shall I do to you?
VERSE:Judah, what shall I do to you?
VERSE:For your love is like a morning cloud,
VERSE:and like the dew that disappears early.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Therefore I have cut them to pieces with the prophets;
VERSE:I killed them with the words of my mouth.
VERSE:Your judgments are like a flash of lightning.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For I desire mercy, and not sacrifice;
VERSE:and the knowledge of God more than burnt offerings.
LABEL:7
VERSE:But they, like Adam, have broken the covenant.
VERSE:They were unfaithful to me there.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Gilead is a city of those who work iniquity;
VERSE:it is stained with blood.
LABEL:9
VERSE:As gangs of robbers wait to ambush a man,
VERSE:so the company of priests murder on the path toward Shechem,
VERSE:committing shameful crimes.
LABEL:10
VERSE:In the house of Israel I have seen a horrible thing.
VERSE:There is prostitution in Ephraim.
VERSE:Israel is defiled.
LABEL:11
VERSE:“Also, Judah, there is a harvest appointed for you,
VERSE:when I restore the fortunes of my people.
