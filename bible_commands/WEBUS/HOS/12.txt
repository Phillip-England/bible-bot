TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:HOS
CHAPTER:12
TITLE:Hosea 12
LABEL:1
VERSE:Ephraim feeds on wind,
VERSE:and chases the east wind.
VERSE:He continually multiplies lies and desolation.
VERSE:They make a covenant with Assyria,
VERSE:and oil is carried into Egypt.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh also has a controversy with Judah,
VERSE:and will punish Jacob according to his ways;
VERSE:according to his deeds he will repay him.
LABEL:3
VERSE:In the womb he took his brother by the heel,
VERSE:and in his manhood he contended with God.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Indeed, he struggled with the angel, and prevailed;
VERSE:he wept, and made supplication to him.
VERSE:He found him at Bethel, and there he spoke with us—
LABEL:5
VERSE:even Yahweh, the God of Armies.
VERSE:Yahweh is his name of renown!
LABEL:6
VERSE:Therefore turn to your God.
VERSE:Keep kindness and justice,
VERSE:and wait continually for your God.
LABEL:7
VERSE:A merchant has dishonest scales in his hand.
VERSE:He loves to defraud.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Ephraim said, “Surely I have become rich.
VERSE:I have found myself wealth.
VERSE:In all my wealth they won’t find in me any iniquity that is sin.”
LABEL:9
VERSE:“But I am Yahweh your God from the land of Egypt.
VERSE:I will yet again make you dwell in tents,
VERSE:as in the days of the solemn feast.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I have also spoken to the prophets,
VERSE:and I have multiplied visions;
VERSE:and by the ministry of the prophets I have used parables.
LABEL:11
VERSE:If Gilead is wicked,
VERSE:surely they are worthless.
VERSE:In Gilgal they sacrifice bulls.
VERSE:Indeed, their altars are like heaps in the furrows of the field.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Jacob fled into the country of Aram.
VERSE:Israel served to get a wife.
VERSE:For a wife he tended flocks and herds.
LABEL:13
VERSE:By a prophet Yahweh brought Israel up out of Egypt,
VERSE:and by a prophet he was preserved.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Ephraim has bitterly provoked anger.
VERSE:Therefore his blood will be left on him,
VERSE:and his Lord will repay his contempt.
