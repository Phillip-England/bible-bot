TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:HOS
CHAPTER:10
TITLE:Hosea 10
LABEL:1
VERSE:Israel is a luxuriant vine that produces his fruit.
VERSE:According to the abundance of his fruit he has multiplied his altars.
VERSE:As their land has prospered, they have adorned their sacred stones.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Their heart is divided.
VERSE:Now they will be found guilty.
VERSE:He will demolish their altars.
VERSE:He will destroy their sacred stones.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Surely now they will say, “We have no king; for we don’t fear Yahweh;
VERSE:and the king, what can he do for us?”
LABEL:4
VERSE:They make promises, swearing falsely in making covenants.
VERSE:Therefore judgment springs up like poisonous weeds in the furrows of the field.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The inhabitants of Samaria will be in terror for the calves of Beth Aven,
VERSE:for its people will mourn over it,
VERSE:along with its priests who rejoiced over it,
VERSE:for its glory, because it has departed from it.
LABEL:6
VERSE:It also will be carried to Assyria for a present to a great king.
VERSE:Ephraim will receive shame,
VERSE:and Israel will be ashamed of his own counsel.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Samaria and her king float away
VERSE:like a twig on the water.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The high places also of Aven, the sin of Israel, will be destroyed.
VERSE:The thorn and the thistle will come up on their altars.
VERSE:They will tell the mountains, “Cover us!” and the hills, “Fall on us!”
LABEL:9
VERSE:“Israel, you have sinned from the days of Gibeah.
VERSE:There they remained.
VERSE:The battle against the children of iniquity doesn’t overtake them in Gibeah.
LABEL:10
VERSE:When it is my desire, I will chastise them;
VERSE:and the nations will be gathered against them
VERSE:when they are bound to their two transgressions.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Ephraim is a trained heifer that loves to thresh,
VERSE:so I will put a yoke on her beautiful neck.
VERSE:I will set a rider on Ephraim.
VERSE:Judah will plow.
VERSE:Jacob will break his clods.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Sow to yourselves in righteousness,
VERSE:reap according to kindness.
VERSE:Break up your fallow ground,
VERSE:for it is time to seek Yahweh,
VERSE:until he comes and rains righteousness on you.
LABEL:13
VERSE:You have plowed wickedness.
VERSE:You have reaped iniquity.
VERSE:You have eaten the fruit of lies,
VERSE:for you trusted in your way, in the multitude of your mighty men.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Therefore a battle roar will arise among your people,
VERSE:and all your fortresses will be destroyed,
VERSE:as Shalman destroyed Beth Arbel in the day of battle.
VERSE:The mother was dashed in pieces with her children.
LABEL:15
VERSE:So Bethel will do to you because of your great wickedness.
VERSE:At daybreak the king of Israel will be destroyed.
