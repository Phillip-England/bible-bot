TITLE:Titus 1

VERSE:Paul, a servant of God and an apostle of Jesus Christ, according to the faith of God’s chosen ones and the knowledge of the truth which is according to godliness,
LABEL:2
VERSE:in hope of eternal life, which God, who can’t lie, promised before time began;
LABEL:3
VERSE:but in his own time revealed his word in the message with which I was entrusted according to the commandment of God our Savior,
LABEL:4
VERSE:to Titus, my true child according to a common faith: Grace, mercy, and peace from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ our Savior.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I left you in Crete for this reason, that you would set in order the things that were lacking and appoint elders in every city, as I directed you—
LABEL:6
VERSE:if anyone is blameless, the husband of one wife, having children who believe, who are not accused of loose or unruly behavior.
LABEL:7
VERSE:For the overseer must be blameless, as God’s steward, not self-pleasing, not easily angered, not given to wine, not violent, not greedy for dishonest gain;
LABEL:8
VERSE:but given to hospitality, a lover of good, sober minded, fair, holy, self-controlled,
LABEL:9
VERSE:holding to the faithful word which is according to the teaching, that he may be able to exhort in the sound doctrine, and to convict those who contradict him.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For there are also many unruly men, vain talkers and deceivers, especially those of the circumcision,
LABEL:11
VERSE:whose mouths must be stopped: men who overthrow whole houses, teaching things which they ought not, for dishonest gain’s sake.
LABEL:12
VERSE:One of them, a prophet of their own, said, “Cretans are always liars, evil beasts, and idle gluttons.”
LABEL:13
VERSE:This testimony is true. For this cause, reprove them sharply, that they may be sound in the faith,
LABEL:14
VERSE:not paying attention to Jewish fables and commandments of men who turn away from the truth.
LABEL:15
VERSE:To the pure, all things are pure, but to those who are defiled and unbelieving, nothing is pure; but both their mind and their conscience are defiled.
LABEL:16
VERSE:They profess that they know God, but by their deeds they deny him, being abominable, disobedient, and unfit for any good work.
