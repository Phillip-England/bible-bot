BOOK:TIT
CHAPTER:3
TITLE:Titus 3
LABEL:1
VERSE:Remind them to be in subjection to rulers and to authorities, to be obedient, to be ready for every good work,
LABEL:2
VERSE:to speak evil of no one, not to be contentious, to be gentle, showing all humility toward all men.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For we were also once foolish, disobedient, deceived, serving various lusts and pleasures, living in malice and envy, hateful, and hating one another.
LABEL:4
VERSE:But when the kindness of God our Savior and his love toward mankind appeared,
LABEL:5
VERSE:not by works of righteousness which we did ourselves, but according to his mercy, he saved us through the washing of regeneration and renewing by the Holy Spirit,
LABEL:6
VERSE:whom he poured out on us richly through Jesus Christ our Savior;
LABEL:7
VERSE:that being justified by his grace, we might be made heirs according to the hope of eternal life.
LABEL:8
VERSE:This saying is faithful, and concerning these things I desire that you insist confidently, so that those who have believed God may be careful to maintain good works. These things are good and profitable to men;
LABEL:9
VERSE:but shun foolish questionings, genealogies, strife, and disputes about the law; for they are unprofitable and vain.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Avoid a factious man after a first and second warning,
LABEL:11
VERSE:knowing that such a one is perverted and sinful, being self-condemned.
LABEL:12
VERSE:When I send Artemas to you, or Tychicus, be diligent to come to me to Nicopolis, for I have determined to winter there.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Send Zenas the lawyer and Apollos on their journey speedily, that nothing may be lacking for them.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Let our people also learn to maintain good works to meet necessary needs, that they may not be unfruitful.
LABEL:15
VERSE:All who are with me greet you. Greet those who love us in faith.
VERSE:Grace be with you all. Amen.
