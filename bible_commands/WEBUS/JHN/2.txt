LABEL:8
VERSE:He said to them, “Now draw some out, and take it to the ruler of the feast.” So they took it.
LABEL:9

LABEL:11

LABEL:12
VERSE:After this, he went down to Capernaum, he, and his mother, his brothers, and his disciples; and they stayed there a few days.
LABEL:13

LABEL:14

LABEL:16

LABEL:17

LABEL:18

LABEL:19

LABEL:20

LABEL:21

LABEL:22
VERSE:When therefore he was raised from the dead, his disciples remembered that he said this, and they believed the Scripture and the word which Jesus had said.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Now when he was in Jerusalem at the Passover, during the feast, many believed in his name, observing his signs which he did.
LABEL:24
