TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:HAB
CHAPTER:3
TITLE:Habakkuk 3
LABEL:1
VERSE:A prayer of Habakkuk, the prophet, set to victorious music.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh, I have heard of your fame.
VERSE:I stand in awe of your deeds, Yahweh.
VERSE:Renew your work in the middle of the years.
VERSE:In the middle of the years make it known.
VERSE:In wrath, you remember mercy.
LABEL:3
VERSE:God came from Teman,
VERSE:the Holy One from Mount Paran. Selah.
VERSE:His glory covered the heavens,
VERSE:and his praise filled the earth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:His splendor is like the sunrise.
VERSE:Rays shine from his hand, where his power is hidden.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Plague went before him,
VERSE:and pestilence followed his feet.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He stood, and shook the earth.
VERSE:He looked, and made the nations tremble.
VERSE:The ancient mountains were crumbled.
VERSE:The age-old hills collapsed.
VERSE:His ways are eternal.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I saw the tents of Cushan in affliction.
VERSE:The dwellings of the land of Midian trembled.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Was Yahweh displeased with the rivers?
VERSE:Was your anger against the rivers,
VERSE:or your wrath against the sea,
VERSE:that you rode on your horses,
VERSE:on your chariots of salvation?
LABEL:9
VERSE:You uncovered your bow.
VERSE:You called for your sworn arrows. Selah.
VERSE:You split the earth with rivers.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The mountains saw you, and were afraid.
VERSE:The storm of waters passed by.
VERSE:The deep roared and lifted up its hands on high.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The sun and moon stood still in the sky
VERSE:at the light of your arrows as they went,
VERSE:at the shining of your glittering spear.
LABEL:12
VERSE:You marched through the land in wrath.
VERSE:You threshed the nations in anger.
LABEL:13
VERSE:You went out for the salvation of your people,
VERSE:for the salvation of your anointed.
VERSE:You crushed the head of the land of wickedness.
VERSE:You stripped them head to foot. Selah.
LABEL:14
VERSE:You pierced the heads of his warriors with their own spears.
VERSE:They came as a whirlwind to scatter me,
VERSE:gloating as if to devour the wretched in secret.
LABEL:15
VERSE:You trampled the sea with your horses,
VERSE:churning mighty waters.
LABEL:16
VERSE:I heard, and my body trembled.
VERSE:My lips quivered at the voice.
VERSE:Rottenness enters into my bones, and I tremble in my place
VERSE:because I must wait quietly for the day of trouble,
VERSE:for the coming up of the people who invade us.
LABEL:17
VERSE:For even though the fig tree doesn’t flourish,
VERSE:nor fruit be in the vines,
VERSE:the labor of the olive fails,
VERSE:the fields yield no food,
VERSE:the flocks are cut off from the fold,
VERSE:and there is no herd in the stalls,
LABEL:18
VERSE:yet I will rejoice in Yahweh.
VERSE:I will be joyful in the God of my salvation!
LABEL:19
VERSE:Yahweh, the Lord, is my strength.
VERSE:He makes my feet like deer’s feet,
VERSE:and enables me to go in high places.
VERSE:For the music director, on my stringed instruments.
