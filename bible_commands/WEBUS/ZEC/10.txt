TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:ZEC
CHAPTER:10
TITLE:Zechariah 10
LABEL:1
VERSE:Ask of Yahweh rain in the spring time,
VERSE:Yahweh who makes storm clouds,
VERSE:and he gives rain showers to everyone for the plants in the field.
LABEL:2
VERSE:For the teraphim have spoken vanity,
VERSE:and the diviners have seen a lie;
VERSE:and they have told false dreams.
VERSE:They comfort in vain.
VERSE:Therefore they go their way like sheep.
VERSE:They are oppressed, because there is no shepherd.
LABEL:3
VERSE:My anger is kindled against the shepherds,
VERSE:and I will punish the male goats,
VERSE:for Yahweh of Armies has visited his flock, the house of Judah,
VERSE:and will make them as his majestic horse in the battle.
LABEL:4
VERSE:From him will come the cornerstone,
VERSE:from him the tent peg,
VERSE:from him the battle bow,
VERSE:from him every ruler together.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They will be as mighty men,
VERSE:treading down muddy streets in the battle.
VERSE:They will fight, because Yahweh is with them.
VERSE:The riders on horses will be confounded.
LABEL:6
VERSE:“I will strengthen the house of Judah,
VERSE:and I will save the house of Joseph.
VERSE:I will bring them back,
VERSE:for I have mercy on them.
VERSE:They will be as though I had not cast them off,
VERSE:for I am Yahweh their God, and I will hear them.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Ephraim will be like a mighty man,
VERSE:and their heart will rejoice as through wine.
VERSE:Yes, their children will see it and rejoice.
VERSE:Their heart will be glad in Yahweh.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will signal for them and gather them,
VERSE:for I have redeemed them.
VERSE:They will increase as they were before.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will sow them among the peoples.
VERSE:They will remember me in far countries.
VERSE:They will live with their children and will return.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I will bring them again also out of the land of Egypt,
VERSE:and gather them out of Assyria.
VERSE:I will bring them into the land of Gilead and Lebanon;
VERSE:and there won’t be room enough for them.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He will pass through the sea of affliction,
VERSE:and will strike the waves in the sea,
VERSE:and all the depths of the Nile will dry up;
VERSE:and the pride of Assyria will be brought down,
VERSE:and the scepter of Egypt will depart.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I will strengthen them in Yahweh.
VERSE:They will walk up and down in his name,” says Yahweh.
