TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:JOL
CHAPTER:1
TITLE:Joel 1
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh’s word that came to Joel, the son of Pethuel.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Hear this, you elders,
VERSE:and listen, all you inhabitants of the land!
VERSE:Has this ever happened in your days,
VERSE:or in the days of your fathers?
LABEL:3
VERSE:Tell your children about it,
VERSE:and have your children tell their children,
VERSE:and their children, another generation.
LABEL:4
VERSE:What the swarming locust has left, the great locust has eaten.
VERSE:What the great locust has left, the grasshopper has eaten.
VERSE:What the grasshopper has left, the caterpillar has eaten.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Wake up, you drunkards, and weep!
VERSE:Wail, all you drinkers of wine, because of the sweet wine,
VERSE:for it is cut off from your mouth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For a nation has come up on my land, strong, and without number.
VERSE:His teeth are the teeth of a lion,
VERSE:and he has the fangs of a lioness.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He has laid my vine waste,
VERSE:and stripped my fig tree.
VERSE:He has stripped its bark, and thrown it away.
VERSE:Its branches are made white.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Mourn like a virgin dressed in sackcloth
VERSE:for the husband of her youth!
LABEL:9
VERSE:The meal offering and the drink offering are cut off from Yahweh’s house.
VERSE:The priests, Yahweh’s ministers, mourn.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The field is laid waste.
VERSE:The land mourns, for the grain is destroyed,
VERSE:The new wine has dried up,
VERSE:and the oil languishes.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Be confounded, you farmers!
VERSE:Wail, you vineyard keepers,
VERSE:for the wheat and for the barley;
VERSE:for the harvest of the field has perished.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The vine has dried up, and the fig tree withered—
VERSE:the pomegranate tree, the palm tree also, and the apple tree,
VERSE:even all of the trees of the field are withered;
VERSE:for joy has withered away from the sons of men.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Put on sackcloth and mourn, you priests!
VERSE:Wail, you ministers of the altar.
VERSE:Come, lie all night in sackcloth, you ministers of my God,
VERSE:for the meal offering and the drink offering are withheld from your God’s house.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Sanctify a fast.
VERSE:Call a solemn assembly.
VERSE:Gather the elders and all the inhabitants of the land to the house of Yahweh, your God,
VERSE:and cry to Yahweh.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Alas for the day!
VERSE:For the day of Yahweh is at hand,
VERSE:and it will come as destruction from the Almighty.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Isn’t the food cut off before our eyes,
VERSE:joy and gladness from the house of our God?
LABEL:17
VERSE:The seeds rot under their clods.
VERSE:The granaries are laid desolate.
VERSE:The barns are broken down, for the grain has withered.
LABEL:18
VERSE:How the animals groan!
VERSE:The herds of livestock are perplexed, because they have no pasture.
VERSE:Yes, the flocks of sheep are made desolate.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Yahweh, I cry to you,
VERSE:for the fire has devoured the pastures of the wilderness,
VERSE:and the flame has burned all the trees of the field.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Yes, the animals of the field pant to you,
VERSE:for the water brooks have dried up,
VERSE:and the fire has devoured the pastures of the wilderness.
