TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:MIC
CHAPTER:5
TITLE:Micah 5
LABEL:1
VERSE:Now you shall gather yourself in troops,
VERSE:daughter of troops.
VERSE:He has laid siege against us.
VERSE:They will strike the judge of Israel with a rod on the cheek.
LABEL:2
VERSE:But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah,
VERSE:being small among the clans of Judah,
VERSE:out of you one will come out to me who is to be ruler in Israel;
VERSE:whose goings out are from of old, from ancient times.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Therefore he will abandon them until the time that she who is in labor gives birth.
VERSE:Then the rest of his brothers will return to the children of Israel.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He shall stand, and shall shepherd in the strength of Yahweh,
VERSE:in the majesty of the name of Yahweh his God.
VERSE:They will live, for then he will be great to the ends of the earth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He will be our peace when Assyria invades our land
VERSE:and when he marches through our fortresses,
VERSE:then we will raise against him seven shepherds,
VERSE:and eight leaders of men.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They will rule the land of Assyria with the sword,
VERSE:and the land of Nimrod in its gates.
VERSE:He will deliver us from the Assyrian,
VERSE:when he invades our land,
VERSE:and when he marches within our border.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The remnant of Jacob will be among many peoples
VERSE:like dew from Yahweh,
VERSE:like showers on the grass,
VERSE:that don’t wait for man
VERSE:nor wait for the sons of men.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The remnant of Jacob will be among the nations,
VERSE:among many peoples,
VERSE:like a lion among the animals of the forest,
VERSE:like a young lion among the flocks of sheep;
VERSE:who, if he goes through, treads down and tears in pieces,
VERSE:and there is no one to deliver.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Let your hand be lifted up above your adversaries,
VERSE:and let all of your enemies be cut off.
LABEL:10
VERSE:“It will happen in that day”, says Yahweh,
VERSE:“that I will cut off your horses from among you
VERSE:and will destroy your chariots.
LABEL:11
VERSE:I will cut off the cities of your land
VERSE:and will tear down all your strongholds.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I will destroy witchcraft from your hand.
VERSE:You shall have no soothsayers.
LABEL:13
VERSE:I will cut off your engraved images and your pillars from among you;
VERSE:and you shall no more worship the work of your hands.
LABEL:14
VERSE:I will uproot your Asherah poles from among you;
VERSE:and I will destroy your cities.
LABEL:15
VERSE:I will execute vengeance in anger
VERSE:and wrath on the nations that didn’t listen.”
