TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:MIC
CHAPTER:6
TITLE:Micah 6
LABEL:1
VERSE:Listen now to what Yahweh says:
VERSE:“Arise, plead your case before the mountains,
VERSE:and let the hills hear what you have to say.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Hear, you mountains, Yahweh’s indictment,
VERSE:and you enduring foundations of the earth;
VERSE:for Yahweh has a case against his people,
VERSE:and he will contend with Israel.
LABEL:3
VERSE:My people, what have I done to you?
VERSE:How have I burdened you?
VERSE:Answer me!
LABEL:4
VERSE:For I brought you up out of the land of Egypt,
VERSE:and redeemed you out of the house of bondage.
VERSE:I sent before you Moses, Aaron, and Miriam.
LABEL:5
VERSE:My people, remember now what Balak king of Moab devised,
VERSE:and what Balaam the son of Beor answered him from Shittim to Gilgal,
VERSE:that you may know the righteous acts of Yahweh.”
LABEL:6
VERSE:How shall I come before Yahweh,
VERSE:and bow myself before the exalted God?
VERSE:Shall I come before him with burnt offerings,
VERSE:with calves a year old?
LABEL:7
VERSE:Will Yahweh be pleased with thousands of rams?
VERSE:With tens of thousands of rivers of oil?
VERSE:Shall I give my firstborn for my disobedience?
VERSE:The fruit of my body for the sin of my soul?
LABEL:8
VERSE:He has shown you, O man, what is good.
VERSE:What does Yahweh require of you, but to act justly,
VERSE:to love mercy, and to walk humbly with your God?
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice calls to the city—
VERSE:and wisdom fears your name—
VERSE:“Listen to the rod,
VERSE:and he who appointed it.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Are there yet treasures of wickedness in the house of the wicked,
VERSE:and a short ephah that is accursed?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Shall I tolerate dishonest scales,
VERSE:and a bag of deceitful weights?
LABEL:12
VERSE:Her rich men are full of violence,
VERSE:her inhabitants speak lies,
VERSE:and their tongue is deceitful in their speech.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Therefore I also have struck you with a grievous wound.
VERSE:I have made you desolate because of your sins.
LABEL:14
VERSE:You shall eat, but not be satisfied.
VERSE:Your hunger will be within you.
VERSE:You will store up, but not save,
VERSE:and that which you save I will give up to the sword.
LABEL:15
VERSE:You will sow, but won’t reap.
VERSE:You will tread the olives, but won’t anoint yourself with oil;
VERSE:and crush grapes, but won’t drink the wine.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For the statutes of Omri are kept,
VERSE:and all the works of Ahab’s house.
VERSE:You walk in their counsels,
VERSE:that I may make you a ruin,
VERSE:and your inhabitants a hissing.
VERSE:You will bear the reproach of my people.”
