TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:MIC
CHAPTER:1
TITLE:Micah 1
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh’s word that came to Micah of Morasheth in the days of Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah, which he saw concerning Samaria and Jerusalem.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Hear, you peoples, all of you!
VERSE:Listen, O earth, and all that is therein.
VERSE:Let the Lord Yahweh be witness against you,
VERSE:the Lord from his holy temple.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For behold, Yahweh comes out of his place,
VERSE:and will come down and tread on the high places of the earth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The mountains melt under him,
VERSE:and the valleys split apart like wax before the fire,
VERSE:like waters that are poured down a steep place.
LABEL:5
VERSE:“All this is for the disobedience of Jacob,
VERSE:and for the sins of the house of Israel.
VERSE:What is the disobedience of Jacob?
VERSE:Isn’t it Samaria?
VERSE:And what are the high places of Judah?
VERSE:Aren’t they Jerusalem?
LABEL:6
VERSE:Therefore I will make Samaria like a rubble heap of the field,
VERSE:like places for planting vineyards;
VERSE:and I will pour down its stones into the valley,
VERSE:and I will uncover its foundations.
LABEL:7
VERSE:All her idols will be beaten to pieces,
VERSE:all her temple gifts will be burned with fire,
VERSE:and I will destroy all her images;
VERSE:for of the hire of a prostitute has she gathered them,
VERSE:and to the hire of a prostitute shall they return.”
LABEL:8
VERSE:For this I will lament and wail.
VERSE:I will go stripped and naked.
VERSE:I will howl like the jackals
VERSE:and mourn like the ostriches.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For her wounds are incurable;
VERSE:for it has come even to Judah.
VERSE:It reaches to the gate of my people,
VERSE:even to Jerusalem.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Don’t tell it in Gath.
VERSE:Don’t weep at all.
VERSE:At Beth Ophrah I have rolled myself in the dust.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Pass on, inhabitant of Shaphir, in nakedness and shame.
VERSE:The inhabitant of Zaanan won’t come out.
VERSE:The wailing of Beth Ezel will take from you his protection.
LABEL:12
VERSE:For the inhabitant of Maroth waits anxiously for good,
VERSE:because evil has come down from Yahweh to the gate of Jerusalem.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Harness the chariot to the swift steed, inhabitant of Lachish.
VERSE:She was the beginning of sin to the daughter of Zion;
VERSE:for the transgressions of Israel were found in you.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Therefore you will give a parting gift to Moresheth Gath.
VERSE:The houses of Achzib will be a deceitful thing to the kings of Israel.
LABEL:15
VERSE:I will yet bring a conqueror to you, inhabitants of Mareshah.
VERSE:The glory of Israel will come to Adullam.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Shave your heads,
VERSE:and cut off your hair for the children of your delight.
VERSE:Enlarge your baldness like the vulture,
VERSE:for they have gone into captivity from you!
