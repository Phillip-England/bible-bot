TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:MAT
CHAPTER:1
TITLE:Matthew 1
LABEL:1
VERSE:The book of the genealogy of Jesus Christ, the son of David, the son of Abraham.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Abraham became the father of Isaac. Isaac became the father of Jacob. Jacob became the father of Judah and his brothers.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Judah became the father of Perez and Zerah by Tamar. Perez became the father of Hezron. Hezron became the father of Ram.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Ram became the father of Amminadab. Amminadab became the father of Nahshon. Nahshon became the father of Salmon.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Salmon became the father of Boaz by Rahab. Boaz became the father of Obed by Ruth. Obed became the father of Jesse.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Jesse became the father of King David. David the king became the father of Solomon by her who had been Uriah’s wife.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Solomon became the father of Rehoboam. Rehoboam became the father of Abijah. Abijah became the father of Asa.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Asa became the father of Jehoshaphat. Jehoshaphat became the father of Joram. Joram became the father of Uzziah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Uzziah became the father of Jotham. Jotham became the father of Ahaz. Ahaz became the father of Hezekiah.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Hezekiah became the father of Manasseh. Manasseh became the father of Amon. Amon became the father of Josiah.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Josiah became the father of Jechoniah and his brothers at the time of the exile to Babylon.
LABEL:12
VERSE:After the exile to Babylon, Jechoniah became the father of Shealtiel. Shealtiel became the father of Zerubbabel.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Zerubbabel became the father of Abiud. Abiud became the father of Eliakim. Eliakim became the father of Azor.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Azor became the father of Zadok. Zadok became the father of Achim. Achim became the father of Eliud.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Eliud became the father of Eleazar. Eleazar became the father of Matthan. Matthan became the father of Jacob.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Jacob became the father of Joseph, the husband of Mary, from whom was born Jesus, who is called Christ.
LABEL:17
VERSE:So all the generations from Abraham to David are fourteen generations; from David to the exile to Babylon fourteen generations; and from the carrying away to Babylon to the Christ, fourteen generations.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Now the birth of Jesus Christ was like this: After his mother, Mary, was engaged to Joseph, before they came together, she was found pregnant by the Holy Spirit.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Joseph, her husband, being a righteous man, and not willing to make her a public example, intended to put her away secretly.
LABEL:20
VERSE:But when he thought about these things, behold, an angel of the Lord appeared to him in a dream, saying, “Joseph, son of David, don’t be afraid to take to yourself Mary as your wife, for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy Spirit.
LABEL:21
VERSE:She shall give birth to a son. You shall name him Jesus, for it is he who shall save his people from their sins.”
LABEL:22
VERSE:Now all this has happened that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the Lord through the prophet, saying,
LABEL:23
VERSE:“Behold, the virgin shall be with child,
VERSE:and shall give birth to a son.
VERSE:They shall call his name Immanuel,”
VERSE:which is, being interpreted, “God with us.”
LABEL:24
VERSE:Joseph arose from his sleep, and did as the angel of the Lord commanded him, and took his wife to himself;
LABEL:25
VERSE:and didn’t know her sexually until she had given birth to her firstborn son. He named him Jesus.
