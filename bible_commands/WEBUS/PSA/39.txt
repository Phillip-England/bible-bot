BOOK:PSA

TITLE:Psalms 39
LABEL:1
VERSE:I said, “I will watch my ways, so that I don’t sin with my tongue.
VERSE:I will keep my mouth with a bridle while the wicked is before me.”
LABEL:2
VERSE:I was mute with silence.
VERSE:I held my peace, even from good.
VERSE:My sorrow was stirred.
LABEL:3
VERSE:My heart was hot within me.
VERSE:While I meditated, the fire burned.
VERSE:I spoke with my tongue:
LABEL:4
VERSE:“Yahweh, show me my end,
VERSE:what is the measure of my days.
VERSE:Let me know how frail I am.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Behold, you have made my days hand widths.
VERSE:My lifetime is as nothing before you.
VERSE:Surely every man stands as a breath.” Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:“Surely every man walks like a shadow.
VERSE:Surely they busy themselves in vain.
VERSE:He heaps up, and doesn’t know who shall gather.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Now, Lord, what do I wait for?
VERSE:My hope is in you.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Deliver me from all my transgressions.
VERSE:Don’t make me the reproach of the foolish.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I was mute.
VERSE:I didn’t open my mouth,
VERSE:because you did it.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Remove your scourge away from me.
VERSE:I am overcome by the blow of your hand.
LABEL:11
VERSE:When you rebuke and correct man for iniquity,
VERSE:you consume his wealth like a moth.
VERSE:Surely every man is but a breath.” Selah.
LABEL:12
VERSE:“Hear my prayer, Yahweh, and give ear to my cry.
VERSE:Don’t be silent at my tears.
VERSE:For I am a stranger with you,
VERSE:a foreigner, as all my fathers were.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Oh spare me, that I may recover strength,
VERSE:before I go away and exist no more.”
