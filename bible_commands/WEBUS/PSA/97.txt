TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:97
TITLE:Psalms 97
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh reigns!
VERSE:Let the earth rejoice!
VERSE:Let the multitude of islands be glad!
LABEL:2
VERSE:Clouds and darkness are around him.
VERSE:Righteousness and justice are the foundation of his throne.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A fire goes before him,
VERSE:and burns up his adversaries on every side.
LABEL:4
VERSE:His lightning lights up the world.
VERSE:The earth sees, and trembles.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The mountains melt like wax at the presence of Yahweh,
VERSE:at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The heavens declare his righteousness.
VERSE:All the peoples have seen his glory.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Let all them be shamed who serve engraved images,
VERSE:who boast in their idols.
VERSE:Worship him, all you gods!
LABEL:8
VERSE:Zion heard and was glad.
VERSE:The daughters of Judah rejoiced
VERSE:because of your judgments, Yahweh.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For you, Yahweh, are most high above all the earth.
VERSE:You are exalted far above all gods.
LABEL:10
VERSE:You who love Yahweh, hate evil!
VERSE:He preserves the souls of his saints.
VERSE:He delivers them out of the hand of the wicked.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Light is sown for the righteous,
VERSE:and gladness for the upright in heart.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Be glad in Yahweh, you righteous people!
VERSE:Give thanks to his holy Name.
