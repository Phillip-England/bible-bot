TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:75
TITLE:Psalms 75
LABEL:1
VERSE:We give thanks to you, God.
VERSE:We give thanks, for your Name is near.
VERSE:Men tell about your wondrous works.
LABEL:2
VERSE:When I choose the appointed time,
VERSE:I will judge blamelessly.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The earth and all its inhabitants quake.
VERSE:I firmly hold its pillars. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I said to the arrogant, “Don’t boast!”
VERSE:I said to the wicked, “Don’t lift up the horn.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Don’t lift up your horn on high.
VERSE:Don’t speak with a stiff neck.”
LABEL:6
VERSE:For neither from the east, nor from the west,
VERSE:nor yet from the south, comes exaltation.
LABEL:7
VERSE:But God is the judge.
VERSE:He puts down one, and lifts up another.
LABEL:8
VERSE:For in Yahweh’s hand there is a cup,
VERSE:full of foaming wine mixed with spices.
VERSE:He pours it out.
VERSE:Indeed the wicked of the earth drink and drink it to its very dregs.
LABEL:9
VERSE:But I will declare this forever:
VERSE:I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I will cut off all the horns of the wicked,
VERSE:but the horns of the righteous shall be lifted up.
