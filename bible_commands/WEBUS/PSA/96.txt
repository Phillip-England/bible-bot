TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:96
TITLE:Psalms 96
LABEL:1
VERSE:Sing to Yahweh a new song!
VERSE:Sing to Yahweh, all the earth.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing to Yahweh!
VERSE:Bless his name!
VERSE:Proclaim his salvation from day to day!
LABEL:3
VERSE:Declare his glory among the nations,
VERSE:his marvelous works among all the peoples.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For Yahweh is great, and greatly to be praised!
VERSE:He is to be feared above all gods.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For all the gods of the peoples are idols,
VERSE:but Yahweh made the heavens.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Honor and majesty are before him.
VERSE:Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Ascribe to Yahweh, you families of nations,
VERSE:ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name.
VERSE:Bring an offering, and come into his courts.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Worship Yahweh in holy array.
VERSE:Tremble before him, all the earth.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Say among the nations, “Yahweh reigns.”
VERSE:The world is also established.
VERSE:It can’t be moved.
VERSE:He will judge the peoples with equity.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice.
VERSE:Let the sea roar, and its fullness!
LABEL:12
VERSE:Let the field and all that is in it exult!
VERSE:Then all the trees of the woods shall sing for joy
LABEL:13
VERSE:before Yahweh; for he comes,
VERSE:for he comes to judge the earth.
VERSE:He will judge the world with righteousness,
VERSE:the peoples with his truth.
