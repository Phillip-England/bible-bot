TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:84
TITLE:Psalms 84
LABEL:1
VERSE:How lovely are your dwellings,
VERSE:Yahweh of Armies!
LABEL:2
VERSE:My soul longs, and even faints for the courts of Yahweh.
VERSE:My heart and my flesh cry out for the living God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yes, the sparrow has found a home,
VERSE:and the swallow a nest for herself, where she may have her young,
VERSE:near your altars, Yahweh of Armies, my King, and my God.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Blessed are those who dwell in your house.
VERSE:They are always praising you. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Blessed are those whose strength is in you,
VERSE:who have set their hearts on a pilgrimage.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Passing through the valley of Weeping, they make it a place of springs.
VERSE:Yes, the autumn rain covers it with blessings.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They go from strength to strength.
VERSE:Every one of them appears before God in Zion.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh, God of Armies, hear my prayer.
VERSE:Listen, God of Jacob. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Behold, God our shield,
VERSE:look at the face of your anointed.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For a day in your courts is better than a thousand.
VERSE:I would rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God,
VERSE:than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For Yahweh God is a sun and a shield.
VERSE:Yahweh will give grace and glory.
VERSE:He withholds no good thing from those who walk blamelessly.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Yahweh of Armies,
VERSE:blessed is the man who trusts in you.
