TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:63
TITLE:Psalms 63
LABEL:1
VERSE:God, you are my God.
VERSE:I will earnestly seek you.
VERSE:My soul thirsts for you.
VERSE:My flesh longs for you,
VERSE:in a dry and weary land, where there is no water.
LABEL:2
VERSE:So I have seen you in the sanctuary,
VERSE:watching your power and your glory.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Because your loving kindness is better than life,
VERSE:my lips shall praise you.
LABEL:4
VERSE:So I will bless you while I live.
VERSE:I will lift up my hands in your name.
LABEL:5
VERSE:My soul shall be satisfied as with the richest food.
VERSE:My mouth shall praise you with joyful lips,
LABEL:6
VERSE:when I remember you on my bed,
VERSE:and think about you in the night watches.
LABEL:7
VERSE:For you have been my help.
VERSE:I will rejoice in the shadow of your wings.
LABEL:8
VERSE:My soul stays close to you.
VERSE:Your right hand holds me up.
LABEL:9
VERSE:But those who seek my soul to destroy it
VERSE:shall go into the lower parts of the earth.
LABEL:10
VERSE:They shall be given over to the power of the sword.
VERSE:They shall be jackal food.
LABEL:11
VERSE:But the king shall rejoice in God.
VERSE:Everyone who swears by him will praise him,
VERSE:for the mouth of those who speak lies shall be silenced.
