TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:135
TITLE:Psalms 135
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise Yah!
VERSE:Praise Yahweh’s name!
VERSE:Praise him, you servants of Yahweh,
LABEL:2
VERSE:you who stand in Yahweh’s house,
VERSE:in the courts of our God’s house.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Praise Yah, for Yahweh is good.
VERSE:Sing praises to his name, for that is pleasant.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For Yah has chosen Jacob for himself,
VERSE:Israel for his own possession.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For I know that Yahweh is great,
VERSE:that our Lord is above all gods.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Whatever Yahweh pleased, that he has done,
VERSE:in heaven and in earth, in the seas and in all deeps.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He causes the clouds to rise from the ends of the earth.
VERSE:He makes lightnings with the rain.
VERSE:He brings the wind out of his treasuries.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He struck the firstborn of Egypt,
VERSE:both of man and animal.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He sent signs and wonders into the middle of you, Egypt,
VERSE:on Pharaoh, and on all his servants.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He struck many nations,
VERSE:and killed mighty kings—
LABEL:11
VERSE:Sihon king of the Amorites,
VERSE:Og king of Bashan,
VERSE:and all the kingdoms of Canaan—
LABEL:12
VERSE:and gave their land for a heritage,
VERSE:a heritage to Israel, his people.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Your name, Yahweh, endures forever;
VERSE:your renown, Yahweh, throughout all generations.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For Yahweh will judge his people
VERSE:and have compassion on his servants.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The idols of the nations are silver and gold,
VERSE:the work of men’s hands.
LABEL:16
VERSE:They have mouths, but they can’t speak.
VERSE:They have eyes, but they can’t see.
LABEL:17
VERSE:They have ears, but they can’t hear,
VERSE:neither is there any breath in their mouths.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Those who make them will be like them,
VERSE:yes, everyone who trusts in them.
LABEL:19
VERSE:House of Israel, praise Yahweh!
VERSE:House of Aaron, praise Yahweh!
LABEL:20
VERSE:House of Levi, praise Yahweh!
VERSE:You who fear Yahweh, praise Yahweh!
LABEL:21
VERSE:Blessed be Yahweh from Zion,
VERSE:who dwells in Jerusalem.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
