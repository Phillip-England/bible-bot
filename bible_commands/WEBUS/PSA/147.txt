TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:147
TITLE:Psalms 147
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise Yah,
VERSE:for it is good to sing praises to our God;
VERSE:for it is pleasant and fitting to praise him.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh builds up Jerusalem.
VERSE:He gathers together the outcasts of Israel.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He heals the broken in heart,
VERSE:and binds up their wounds.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He counts the number of the stars.
VERSE:He calls them all by their names.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Great is our Lord, and mighty in power.
VERSE:His understanding is infinite.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yahweh upholds the humble.
VERSE:He brings the wicked down to the ground.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Sing to Yahweh with thanksgiving.
VERSE:Sing praises on the harp to our God,
LABEL:8
VERSE:who covers the sky with clouds,
VERSE:who prepares rain for the earth,
VERSE:who makes grass grow on the mountains.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He provides food for the livestock,
VERSE:and for the young ravens when they call.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He doesn’t delight in the strength of the horse.
VERSE:He takes no pleasure in the legs of a man.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Yahweh takes pleasure in those who fear him,
VERSE:in those who hope in his loving kindness.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, Jerusalem!
VERSE:Praise your God, Zion!
LABEL:13
VERSE:For he has strengthened the bars of your gates.
VERSE:He has blessed your children within you.
LABEL:14
VERSE:He makes peace in your borders.
VERSE:He fills you with the finest of the wheat.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He sends out his commandment to the earth.
VERSE:His word runs very swiftly.
LABEL:16
VERSE:He gives snow like wool,
VERSE:and scatters frost like ashes.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He hurls down his hail like pebbles.
VERSE:Who can stand before his cold?
LABEL:18
VERSE:He sends out his word, and melts them.
VERSE:He causes his wind to blow, and the waters flow.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He shows his word to Jacob,
VERSE:his statutes and his ordinances to Israel.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He has not done this for just any nation.
VERSE:They don’t know his ordinances.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
