TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:71
TITLE:Psalms 71
LABEL:1
VERSE:In you, Yahweh, I take refuge.
VERSE:Never let me be disappointed.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Deliver me in your righteousness, and rescue me.
VERSE:Turn your ear to me, and save me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Be to me a rock of refuge to which I may always go.
VERSE:Give the command to save me,
VERSE:for you are my rock and my fortress.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Rescue me, my God, from the hand of the wicked,
VERSE:from the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For you are my hope, Lord Yahweh,
VERSE:my confidence from my youth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I have relied on you from the womb.
VERSE:You are he who took me out of my mother’s womb.
VERSE:I will always praise you.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I am a marvel to many,
VERSE:but you are my strong refuge.
LABEL:8
VERSE:My mouth shall be filled with your praise,
VERSE:with your honor all day long.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Don’t reject me in my old age.
VERSE:Don’t forsake me when my strength fails.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For my enemies talk about me.
VERSE:Those who watch for my soul conspire together,
LABEL:11
VERSE:saying, “God has forsaken him.
VERSE:Pursue and take him, for no one will rescue him.”
LABEL:12
VERSE:God, don’t be far from me.
VERSE:My God, hurry to help me.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Let my accusers be disappointed and consumed.
VERSE:Let them be covered with disgrace and scorn who want to harm me.
LABEL:14
VERSE:But I will always hope,
VERSE:and will add to all of your praise.
LABEL:15
VERSE:My mouth will tell about your righteousness,
VERSE:and of your salvation all day,
VERSE:though I don’t know its full measure.
LABEL:16
VERSE:I will come with the mighty acts of the Lord Yahweh.
VERSE:I will make mention of your righteousness, even of yours alone.
LABEL:17
VERSE:God, you have taught me from my youth.
VERSE:Until now, I have declared your wondrous works.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Yes, even when I am old and gray-haired, God, don’t forsake me,
VERSE:until I have declared your strength to the next generation,
VERSE:your might to everyone who is to come.
LABEL:19
VERSE:God, your righteousness also reaches to the heavens.
VERSE:You have done great things.
VERSE:God, who is like you?
LABEL:20
VERSE:You, who have shown us many and bitter troubles,
VERSE:you will let me live.
VERSE:You will bring us up again from the depths of the earth.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Increase my honor
VERSE:and comfort me again.
LABEL:22
VERSE:I will also praise you with the harp for your faithfulness, my God.
VERSE:I sing praises to you with the lyre, Holy One of Israel.
LABEL:23
VERSE:My lips shall shout for joy!
VERSE:My soul, which you have redeemed, sings praises to you!
LABEL:24
VERSE:My tongue will also talk about your righteousness all day long,
VERSE:for they are disappointed, and they are confounded,
VERSE:who want to harm me.
