TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:94
TITLE:Psalms 94
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh, you God to whom vengeance belongs,
VERSE:you God to whom vengeance belongs, shine out.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Rise up, you judge of the earth.
VERSE:Pay back the proud what they deserve.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yahweh, how long will the wicked,
VERSE:how long will the wicked triumph?
LABEL:4
VERSE:They pour out arrogant words.
VERSE:All the evildoers boast.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They break your people in pieces, Yahweh,
VERSE:and afflict your heritage.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They kill the widow and the alien,
VERSE:and murder the fatherless.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They say, “Yah will not see,
VERSE:neither will Jacob’s God consider.”
LABEL:8
VERSE:Consider, you senseless among the people;
VERSE:you fools, when will you be wise?
LABEL:9
VERSE:He who implanted the ear, won’t he hear?
VERSE:He who formed the eye, won’t he see?
LABEL:10
VERSE:He who disciplines the nations, won’t he punish?
VERSE:He who teaches man knows.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Yahweh knows the thoughts of man,
VERSE:that they are futile.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Blessed is the man whom you discipline, Yah,
VERSE:and teach out of your law,
LABEL:13
VERSE:that you may give him rest from the days of adversity,
VERSE:until the pit is dug for the wicked.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For Yahweh won’t reject his people,
VERSE:neither will he forsake his inheritance.
LABEL:15
VERSE:For judgment will return to righteousness.
VERSE:All the upright in heart shall follow it.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Who will rise up for me against the wicked?
VERSE:Who will stand up for me against the evildoers?
LABEL:17
VERSE:Unless Yahweh had been my help,
VERSE:my soul would have soon lived in silence.
LABEL:18
VERSE:When I said, “My foot is slipping!”
VERSE:Your loving kindness, Yahweh, held me up.
LABEL:19
VERSE:In the multitude of my thoughts within me,
VERSE:your comforts delight my soul.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Shall the throne of wickedness have fellowship with you,
VERSE:which brings about mischief by statute?
LABEL:21
VERSE:They gather themselves together against the soul of the righteous,
VERSE:and condemn the innocent blood.
LABEL:22
VERSE:But Yahweh has been my high tower,
VERSE:my God, the rock of my refuge.
LABEL:23
VERSE:He has brought on them their own iniquity,
VERSE:and will cut them off in their own wickedness.
VERSE:Yahweh, our God, will cut them off.
