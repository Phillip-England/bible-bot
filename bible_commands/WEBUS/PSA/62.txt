LABEL:1
VERSE:My soul rests in God alone.
VERSE:My salvation is from him.
LABEL:2
VERSE:He alone is my rock, my salvation, and my fortress.
VERSE:I will never be greatly shaken.
LABEL:3
VERSE:How long will you assault a man?
VERSE:Would all of you throw him down,
VERSE:like a leaning wall, like a tottering fence?
LABEL:4
VERSE:They fully intend to throw him down from his lofty place.
VERSE:They delight in lies.
VERSE:They bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:My soul, wait in silence for God alone,
VERSE:for my expectation is from him.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He alone is my rock and my salvation, my fortress.
VERSE:I will not be shaken.
LABEL:7
VERSE:My salvation and my honor is with God.
VERSE:The rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Trust in him at all times, you people.
VERSE:Pour out your heart before him.
VERSE:God is a refuge for us. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Surely men of low degree are just a breath,
VERSE:and men of high degree are a lie.
VERSE:In the balances they will go up.
VERSE:They are together lighter than a breath.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Don’t trust in oppression.
VERSE:Don’t become vain in robbery.
VERSE:If riches increase,
VERSE:don’t set your heart on them.
LABEL:11
VERSE:God has spoken once;
VERSE:twice I have heard this,
VERSE:that power belongs to God.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Also to you, Lord, belongs loving kindness,
VERSE:for you reward every man according to his work.
