TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:125
TITLE:Psalms 125
LABEL:1
VERSE:Those who trust in Yahweh are as Mount Zion,
VERSE:which can’t be moved, but remains forever.
LABEL:2
VERSE:As the mountains surround Jerusalem,
VERSE:so Yahweh surrounds his people from this time forward and forever more.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For the scepter of wickedness won’t remain over the allotment of the righteous,
VERSE:so that the righteous won’t use their hands to do evil.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Do good, Yahweh, to those who are good,
VERSE:to those who are upright in their hearts.
LABEL:5
VERSE:But as for those who turn away to their crooked ways,
VERSE:Yahweh will lead them away with the workers of iniquity.
VERSE:Peace be on Israel.
