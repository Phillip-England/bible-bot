TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:34
TITLE:Psalms 34
LABEL:1
VERSE:   I will bless Yahweh at all times.
VERSE:His praise will always be in my mouth.
LABEL:2
VERSE:My soul shall boast in Yahweh.
VERSE:The humble shall hear of it and be glad.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Oh magnify Yahweh with me.
VERSE:Let’s exalt his name together.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I sought Yahweh, and he answered me,
VERSE:and delivered me from all my fears.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They looked to him, and were radiant.
VERSE:Their faces shall never be covered with shame.
LABEL:6
VERSE:This poor man cried, and Yahweh heard him,
VERSE:and saved him out of all his troubles.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Yahweh’s angel encamps around those who fear him,
VERSE:and delivers them.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Oh taste and see that Yahweh is good.
VERSE:Blessed is the man who takes refuge in him.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Oh fear Yahweh, you his saints,
VERSE:for there is no lack with those who fear him.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The young lions do lack, and suffer hunger,
VERSE:but those who seek Yahweh shall not lack any good thing.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Come, you children, listen to me.
VERSE:I will teach you the fear of Yahweh.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Who is someone who desires life,
VERSE:and loves many days, that he may see good?
LABEL:13
VERSE:Keep your tongue from evil,
VERSE:and your lips from speaking lies.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Depart from evil, and do good.
VERSE:Seek peace, and pursue it.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Yahweh’s eyes are toward the righteous.
VERSE:His ears listen to their cry.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Yahweh’s face is against those who do evil,
VERSE:to cut off their memory from the earth.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The righteous cry, and Yahweh hears,
VERSE:and delivers them out of all their troubles.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Yahweh is near to those who have a broken heart,
VERSE:and saves those who have a crushed spirit.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Many are the afflictions of the righteous,
VERSE:but Yahweh delivers him out of them all.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He protects all of his bones.
VERSE:Not one of them is broken.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Evil shall kill the wicked.
VERSE:Those who hate the righteous shall be condemned.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Yahweh redeems the soul of his servants.
VERSE:None of those who take refuge in him shall be condemned.
