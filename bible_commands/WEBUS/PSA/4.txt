TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:4
TITLE:Psalms 4
LABEL:1
VERSE:Answer me when I call, God of my righteousness.
VERSE:Give me relief from my distress.
VERSE:Have mercy on me, and hear my prayer.
LABEL:2
VERSE:You sons of men, how long shall my glory be turned into dishonor?
VERSE:Will you love vanity and seek after falsehood? Selah.
LABEL:3
VERSE:But know that Yahweh has set apart for himself him who is godly;
VERSE:Yahweh will hear when I call to him.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Stand in awe, and don’t sin.
VERSE:Search your own heart on your bed, and be still. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Offer the sacrifices of righteousness.
VERSE:Put your trust in Yahweh.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Many say, “Who will show us any good?”
VERSE:Yahweh, let the light of your face shine on us.
LABEL:7
VERSE:You have put gladness in my heart,
VERSE:more than when their grain and their new wine are increased.
LABEL:8
VERSE:In peace I will both lay myself down and sleep,
VERSE:for you alone, Yahweh, make me live in safety.
