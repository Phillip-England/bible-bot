TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:137
TITLE:Psalms 137
LABEL:1
VERSE:By the rivers of Babylon, there we sat down.
VERSE:Yes, we wept, when we remembered Zion.
LABEL:2
VERSE:On the willows in that land,
VERSE:we hung up our harps.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For there, those who led us captive asked us for songs.
VERSE:Those who tormented us demanded songs of joy:
VERSE:“Sing us one of the songs of Zion!”
LABEL:4
VERSE:How can we sing Yahweh’s song in a foreign land?
LABEL:5
VERSE:If I forget you, Jerusalem,
VERSE:let my right hand forget its skill.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Let my tongue stick to the roof of my mouth if I don’t remember you,
VERSE:if I don’t prefer Jerusalem above my chief joy.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Remember, Yahweh, against the children of Edom in the day of Jerusalem,
VERSE:who said, “Raze it!
VERSE:Raze it even to its foundation!”
LABEL:8
VERSE:Daughter of Babylon, doomed to destruction,
VERSE:he will be happy who repays you,
VERSE:as you have done to us.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Happy shall he be,
VERSE:who takes and dashes your little ones against the rock.
