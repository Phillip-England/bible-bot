VERSE:Oh come, let’s sing to Yahweh.
VERSE:Let’s shout aloud to the rock of our salvation!
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let’s come before his presence with thanksgiving.
VERSE:Let’s extol him with songs!
LABEL:3
VERSE:For Yahweh is a great God,
VERSE:a great King above all gods.
LABEL:4
VERSE:In his hand are the deep places of the earth.
VERSE:The heights of the mountains are also his.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The sea is his, and he made it.
VERSE:His hands formed the dry land.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Oh come, let’s worship and bow down.
VERSE:Let’s kneel before Yahweh, our Maker,
LABEL:7
VERSE:for he is our God.
VERSE:We are the people of his pasture,
VERSE:and the sheep in his care.
VERSE:Today, oh that you would hear his voice!
LABEL:8
VERSE:Don’t harden your heart, as at Meribah,
VERSE:as in the day of Massah in the wilderness,
LABEL:9
VERSE:when your fathers tempted me,
VERSE:tested me, and saw my work.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Forty long years I was grieved with that generation,
VERSE:and said, “They are a people who err in their heart.
VERSE:They have not known my ways.”
LABEL:11
VERSE:Therefore I swore in my wrath,
VERSE:“They won’t enter into my rest.”
