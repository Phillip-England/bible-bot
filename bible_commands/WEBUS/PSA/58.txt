TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:58
TITLE:Psalms 58
LABEL:1
VERSE:Do you indeed speak righteousness, silent ones?
VERSE:Do you judge blamelessly, you sons of men?
LABEL:2
VERSE:No, in your heart you plot injustice.
VERSE:You measure out the violence of your hands in the earth.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The wicked go astray from the womb.
VERSE:They are wayward as soon as they are born, speaking lies.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their poison is like the poison of a snake,
VERSE:like a deaf cobra that stops its ear,
LABEL:5
VERSE:which doesn’t listen to the voice of charmers,
VERSE:no matter how skillful the charmer may be.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Break their teeth, God, in their mouth.
VERSE:Break out the great teeth of the young lions, Yahweh.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Let them vanish like water that flows away.
VERSE:When they draw the bow, let their arrows be made blunt.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Let them be like a snail which melts and passes away,
VERSE:like the stillborn child, who has not seen the sun.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Before your pots can feel the heat of the thorns,
VERSE:he will sweep away the green and the burning alike.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The righteous shall rejoice when he sees the vengeance.
VERSE:He shall wash his feet in the blood of the wicked,
LABEL:11
VERSE:so that men shall say, “Most certainly there is a reward for the righteous.
VERSE:Most certainly there is a God who judges the earth.”
