TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:52
TITLE:Psalms 52
LABEL:1
VERSE:Why do you boast of mischief, mighty man?
VERSE:God’s loving kindness endures continually.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Your tongue plots destruction,
VERSE:like a sharp razor, working deceitfully.
LABEL:3
VERSE:You love evil more than good,
VERSE:lying rather than speaking the truth. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:You love all devouring words,
VERSE:you deceitful tongue.
LABEL:5
VERSE:God will likewise destroy you forever.
VERSE:He will take you up, and pluck you out of your tent,
VERSE:and root you out of the land of the living. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The righteous also will see it, and fear,
VERSE:and laugh at him, saying,
LABEL:7
VERSE:“Behold, this is the man who didn’t make God his strength,
VERSE:but trusted in the abundance of his riches,
VERSE:and strengthened himself in his wickedness.”
LABEL:8
VERSE:But as for me, I am like a green olive tree in God’s house.
VERSE:I trust in God’s loving kindness forever and ever.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will give you thanks forever, because you have done it.
VERSE:I will hope in your name, for it is good,
VERSE:in the presence of your saints.
