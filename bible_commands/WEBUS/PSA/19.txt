TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:19
TITLE:Psalms 19
LABEL:1
VERSE:The heavens declare the glory of God.
VERSE:The expanse shows his handiwork.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Day after day they pour out speech,
VERSE:and night after night they display knowledge.
LABEL:3
VERSE:There is no speech nor language
VERSE:where their voice is not heard.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their voice has gone out through all the earth,
VERSE:their words to the end of the world.
VERSE:In them he has set a tent for the sun,
LABEL:5
VERSE:which is as a bridegroom coming out of his room,
VERSE:like a strong man rejoicing to run his course.
LABEL:6
VERSE:His going out is from the end of the heavens,
VERSE:his circuit to its ends.
VERSE:There is nothing hidden from its heat.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Yahweh’s law is perfect, restoring the soul.
VERSE:Yahweh’s covenant is sure, making wise the simple.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh’s precepts are right, rejoicing the heart.
VERSE:Yahweh’s commandment is pure, enlightening the eyes.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The fear of Yahweh is clean, enduring forever.
VERSE:Yahweh’s ordinances are true, and righteous altogether.
LABEL:10
VERSE:They are more to be desired than gold, yes, than much fine gold,
VERSE:sweeter also than honey and the extract of the honeycomb.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Moreover your servant is warned by them.
VERSE:In keeping them there is great reward.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Who can discern his errors?
VERSE:Forgive me from hidden errors.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Keep back your servant also from presumptuous sins.
VERSE:Let them not have dominion over me.
VERSE:Then I will be upright.
VERSE:I will be blameless and innocent of great transgression.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart
VERSE:be acceptable in your sight,
VERSE:Yahweh, my rock, and my redeemer.
