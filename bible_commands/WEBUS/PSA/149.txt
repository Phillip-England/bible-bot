TITLE:Psalms 149
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise Yahweh!
VERSE:Sing to Yahweh a new song,
VERSE:his praise in the assembly of the saints.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let Israel rejoice in him who made them.
VERSE:Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Let them praise his name in the dance!
VERSE:Let them sing praises to him with tambourine and harp!
LABEL:4
VERSE:For Yahweh takes pleasure in his people.
VERSE:He crowns the humble with salvation.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Let the saints rejoice in honor.
VERSE:Let them sing for joy on their beds.
LABEL:6
VERSE:May the high praises of God be in their mouths,
VERSE:and a two-edged sword in their hand,
LABEL:7
VERSE:to execute vengeance on the nations,
VERSE:and punishments on the peoples;
LABEL:8
VERSE:to bind their kings with chains,
VERSE:and their nobles with fetters of iron;
LABEL:9
VERSE:to execute on them the written judgment.
VERSE:All his saints have this honor.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
