TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:81
TITLE:Psalms 81
LABEL:1
VERSE:Sing aloud to God, our strength!
VERSE:Make a joyful shout to the God of Jacob!
LABEL:2
VERSE:Raise a song, and bring here the tambourine,
VERSE:the pleasant lyre with the harp.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Blow the trumpet at the New Moon,
VERSE:at the full moon, on our feast day.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For it is a statute for Israel,
VERSE:an ordinance of the God of Jacob.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He appointed it in Joseph for a covenant,
VERSE:when he went out over the land of Egypt,
VERSE:I heard a language that I didn’t know.
LABEL:6
VERSE:“I removed his shoulder from the burden.
VERSE:His hands were freed from the basket.
LABEL:7
VERSE:You called in trouble, and I delivered you.
VERSE:I answered you in the secret place of thunder.
VERSE:I tested you at the waters of Meribah.” Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:“Hear, my people, and I will testify to you,
VERSE:Israel, if you would listen to me!
LABEL:9
VERSE:There shall be no strange god in you,
VERSE:neither shall you worship any foreign god.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I am Yahweh, your God,
VERSE:who brought you up out of the land of Egypt.
VERSE:Open your mouth wide, and I will fill it.
LABEL:11
VERSE:But my people didn’t listen to my voice.
VERSE:Israel desired none of me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:So I let them go after the stubbornness of their hearts,
VERSE:that they might walk in their own counsels.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Oh that my people would listen to me,
VERSE:that Israel would walk in my ways!
LABEL:14
VERSE:I would soon subdue their enemies,
VERSE:and turn my hand against their adversaries.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The haters of Yahweh would cringe before him,
VERSE:and their punishment would last forever.
LABEL:16
VERSE:But he would have also fed them with the finest of the wheat.
VERSE:I will satisfy you with honey out of the rock.”
