TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:76
TITLE:Psalms 76
LABEL:1
VERSE:In Judah, God is known.
VERSE:His name is great in Israel.
LABEL:2
VERSE:His tabernacle is also in Salem.
VERSE:His dwelling place in Zion.
LABEL:3
VERSE:There he broke the flaming arrows of the bow,
VERSE:the shield, and the sword, and the weapons of war. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Glorious are you, and excellent,
VERSE:more than mountains of game.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Valiant men lie plundered,
VERSE:they have slept their last sleep.
VERSE:None of the men of war can lift their hands.
LABEL:6
VERSE:At your rebuke, God of Jacob,
VERSE:both chariot and horse are cast into a dead sleep.
LABEL:7
VERSE:You, even you, are to be feared.
VERSE:Who can stand in your sight when you are angry?
LABEL:8
VERSE:You pronounced judgment from heaven.
VERSE:The earth feared, and was silent,
LABEL:9
VERSE:when God arose to judgment,
VERSE:to save all the afflicted ones of the earth. Selah.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Surely the wrath of man praises you.
VERSE:The survivors of your wrath are restrained.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Make vows to Yahweh your God, and fulfill them!
VERSE:Let all of his neighbors bring presents to him who is to be feared.
LABEL:12
VERSE:He will cut off the spirit of princes.
VERSE:He is feared by the kings of the earth.
