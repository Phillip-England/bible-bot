VERSE:Yahweh, how my adversaries have increased!
VERSE:Many are those who rise up against me.
LABEL:2

VERSE:“There is no help for him in God.”  Selah.
LABEL:3

VERSE:my glory, and the one who lifts up my head.
LABEL:4

VERSE:and he answers me out of his holy hill. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I laid myself down and slept.
VERSE:I awakened, for Yahweh sustains me.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I will not be afraid of tens of thousands of people
VERSE:who have set themselves against me on every side.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Arise, Yahweh!
VERSE:Save me, my God!
VERSE:For you have struck all of my enemies on the cheek bone.
VERSE:You have broken the teeth of the wicked.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Salvation belongs to Yahweh.
VERSE:May your blessing be on your people. Selah.
