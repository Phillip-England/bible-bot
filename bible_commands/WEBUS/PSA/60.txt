TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:60
TITLE:Psalms 60
LABEL:1
VERSE:God, you have rejected us.
VERSE:You have broken us down.
VERSE:You have been angry.
VERSE:Restore us, again.
LABEL:2
VERSE:You have made the land tremble.
VERSE:You have torn it.
VERSE:Mend its fractures,
VERSE:for it quakes.
LABEL:3
VERSE:You have shown your people hard things.
VERSE:You have made us drink the wine that makes us stagger.
LABEL:4
VERSE:You have given a banner to those who fear you,
VERSE:that it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:So that your beloved may be delivered,
VERSE:save with your right hand, and answer us.
LABEL:6
VERSE:God has spoken from his sanctuary:
VERSE:“I will triumph.
VERSE:I will divide Shechem,
VERSE:and measure out the valley of Succoth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine.
VERSE:Ephraim also is the defense of my head.
VERSE:Judah is my scepter.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Moab is my wash basin.
VERSE:I will throw my sandal on Edom.
VERSE:I shout in triumph over Philistia.”
LABEL:9
VERSE:Who will bring me into the strong city?
VERSE:Who has led me to Edom?
LABEL:10
VERSE:Haven’t you, God, rejected us?
VERSE:You don’t go out with our armies, God.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Give us help against the adversary,
VERSE:for the help of man is vain.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Through God we will do valiantly,
VERSE:for it is he who will tread down our adversaries.
