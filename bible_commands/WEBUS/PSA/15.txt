TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:15
TITLE:Psalms 15
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh, who shall dwell in your sanctuary?
VERSE:Who shall live on your holy hill?
LABEL:2
VERSE:He who walks blamelessly and does what is right,
VERSE:and speaks truth in his heart;
LABEL:3
VERSE:he who doesn’t slander with his tongue,
VERSE:nor does evil to his friend,
VERSE:nor casts slurs against his fellow man;
LABEL:4
VERSE:in whose eyes a vile man is despised,
VERSE:but who honors those who fear Yahweh;
VERSE:he who keeps an oath even when it hurts, and doesn’t change;
LABEL:5
VERSE:he who doesn’t lend out his money for usury,
VERSE:nor take a bribe against the innocent.
VERSE:He who does these things shall never be shaken.
