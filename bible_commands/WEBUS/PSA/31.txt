TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:31
TITLE:Psalms 31
LABEL:1
VERSE:In you, Yahweh, I take refuge.
VERSE:Let me never be disappointed.
VERSE:Deliver me in your righteousness.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Bow down your ear to me.
VERSE:Deliver me speedily.
VERSE:Be to me a strong rock,
VERSE:a house of defense to save me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For you are my rock and my fortress,
VERSE:therefore for your name’s sake lead me and guide me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Pluck me out of the net that they have laid secretly for me,
VERSE:for you are my stronghold.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Into your hand I commend my spirit.
VERSE:You redeem me, Yahweh, God of truth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I hate those who regard lying vanities,
VERSE:but I trust in Yahweh.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I will be glad and rejoice in your loving kindness,
VERSE:for you have seen my affliction.
VERSE:You have known my soul in adversities.
LABEL:8
VERSE:You have not shut me up into the hand of the enemy.
VERSE:You have set my feet in a large place.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Have mercy on me, Yahweh, for I am in distress.
VERSE:My eye, my soul, and my body waste away with grief.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For my life is spent with sorrow,
VERSE:my years with sighing.
VERSE:My strength fails because of my iniquity.
VERSE:My bones are wasted away.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Because of all my adversaries I have become utterly contemptible to my neighbors,
VERSE:a horror to my acquaintances.
VERSE:Those who saw me on the street fled from me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I am forgotten from their hearts like a dead man.
VERSE:I am like broken pottery.
LABEL:13
VERSE:For I have heard the slander of many, terror on every side,
VERSE:while they conspire together against me,
VERSE:they plot to take away my life.
LABEL:14
VERSE:But I trust in you, Yahweh.
VERSE:I said, “You are my God.”
LABEL:15
VERSE:My times are in your hand.
VERSE:Deliver me from the hand of my enemies, and from those who persecute me.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Make your face to shine on your servant.
VERSE:Save me in your loving kindness.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Let me not be disappointed, Yahweh, for I have called on you.
VERSE:Let the wicked be disappointed.
VERSE:Let them be silent in Sheol.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Let the lying lips be mute,
VERSE:which speak against the righteous insolently, with pride and contempt.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Oh how great is your goodness,
VERSE:which you have laid up for those who fear you,
VERSE:which you have worked for those who take refuge in you,
VERSE:before the sons of men!
LABEL:20
VERSE:In the shelter of your presence you will hide them from the plotting of man.
VERSE:You will keep them secretly in a dwelling away from the strife of tongues.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Praise be to Yahweh,
VERSE:for he has shown me his marvelous loving kindness in a strong city.
LABEL:22
VERSE:As for me, I said in my haste, “I am cut off from before your eyes.”
VERSE:Nevertheless you heard the voice of my petitions when I cried to you.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Oh love Yahweh, all you his saints!
VERSE:Yahweh preserves the faithful,
VERSE:and fully recompenses him who behaves arrogantly.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Be strong, and let your heart take courage,
VERSE:all you who hope in Yahweh.
