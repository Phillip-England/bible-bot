TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:115
TITLE:Psalms 115
LABEL:1
VERSE:Not to us, Yahweh, not to us,
VERSE:but to your name give glory,
VERSE:for your loving kindness, and for your truth’s sake.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Why should the nations say,
VERSE:“Where is their God, now?”
LABEL:3
VERSE:But our God is in the heavens.
VERSE:He does whatever he pleases.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their idols are silver and gold,
VERSE:the work of men’s hands.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They have mouths, but they don’t speak.
VERSE:They have eyes, but they don’t see.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They have ears, but they don’t hear.
VERSE:They have noses, but they don’t smell.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They have hands, but they don’t feel.
VERSE:They have feet, but they don’t walk,
VERSE:neither do they speak through their throat.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Those who make them will be like them;
VERSE:yes, everyone who trusts in them.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Israel, trust in Yahweh!
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:10
VERSE:House of Aaron, trust in Yahweh!
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:11
VERSE:You who fear Yahweh, trust in Yahweh!
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Yahweh remembers us. He will bless us.
VERSE:He will bless the house of Israel.
VERSE:He will bless the house of Aaron.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He will bless those who fear Yahweh,
VERSE:both small and great.
LABEL:14
VERSE:May Yahweh increase you more and more,
VERSE:you and your children.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Blessed are you by Yahweh,
VERSE:who made heaven and earth.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The heavens are Yahweh’s heavens,
VERSE:but he has given the earth to the children of men.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The dead don’t praise Yah,
VERSE:nor any who go down into silence,
LABEL:18
VERSE:but we will bless Yah,
VERSE:from this time forward and forever more.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
