TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:143
TITLE:Psalms 143
LABEL:1
VERSE:Hear my prayer, Yahweh.
VERSE:Listen to my petitions.
VERSE:In your faithfulness and righteousness, relieve me.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Don’t enter into judgment with your servant,
VERSE:for in your sight no man living is righteous.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For the enemy pursues my soul.
VERSE:He has struck my life down to the ground.
VERSE:He has made me live in dark places, as those who have been long dead.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Therefore my spirit is overwhelmed within me.
VERSE:My heart within me is desolate.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I remember the days of old.
VERSE:I meditate on all your doings.
VERSE:I contemplate the work of your hands.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I spread out my hands to you.
VERSE:My soul thirsts for you, like a parched land. Selah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Hurry to answer me, Yahweh.
VERSE:My spirit fails.
VERSE:Don’t hide your face from me,
VERSE:so that I don’t become like those who go down into the pit.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Cause me to hear your loving kindness in the morning,
VERSE:for I trust in you.
VERSE:Cause me to know the way in which I should walk,
VERSE:for I lift up my soul to you.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Deliver me, Yahweh, from my enemies.
VERSE:I flee to you to hide me.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Teach me to do your will,
VERSE:for you are my God.
VERSE:Your Spirit is good.
VERSE:Lead me in the land of uprightness.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Revive me, Yahweh, for your name’s sake.
VERSE:In your righteousness, bring my soul out of trouble.
LABEL:12
VERSE:In your loving kindness, cut off my enemies,
VERSE:and destroy all those who afflict my soul,
VERSE:for I am your servant.
