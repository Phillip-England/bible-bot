TITLE:Psalms 68
LABEL:1
VERSE:Let God arise!
VERSE:Let his enemies be scattered!
VERSE:Let them who hate him also flee before him.
LABEL:2
VERSE:As smoke is driven away,
VERSE:so drive them away.
VERSE:As wax melts before the fire,
VERSE:so let the wicked perish at the presence of God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:But let the righteous be glad.
VERSE:Let them rejoice before God.
VERSE:Yes, let them rejoice with gladness.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Sing to God! Sing praises to his name!
VERSE:Extol him who rides on the clouds:
VERSE:to Yah, his name!
VERSE:Rejoice before him!
LABEL:5
VERSE:A father of the fatherless, and a defender of the widows,
VERSE:is God in his holy habitation.
LABEL:6
VERSE:God sets the lonely in families.
VERSE:He brings out the prisoners with singing,
VERSE:but the rebellious dwell in a sun-scorched land.
LABEL:7
VERSE:God, when you went out before your people,
VERSE:when you marched through the wilderness... Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The earth trembled.
VERSE:The sky also poured down rain at the presence of the God of Sinai—
VERSE:at the presence of God, the God of Israel.
LABEL:9
VERSE:You, God, sent a plentiful rain.
VERSE:You confirmed your inheritance when it was weary.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Your congregation lived therein.
VERSE:You, God, prepared your goodness for the poor.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The Lord announced the word.
VERSE:The ones who proclaim it are a great company.
LABEL:12
VERSE:“Kings of armies flee! They flee!”
VERSE:She who waits at home divides the plunder,
LABEL:13
VERSE:while you sleep among the camp fires,
VERSE:the wings of a dove sheathed with silver,
VERSE:her feathers with shining gold.
LABEL:14
VERSE:When the Almighty scattered kings in her,
VERSE:it snowed on Zalmon.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The mountains of Bashan are majestic mountains.
VERSE:The mountains of Bashan are rugged.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Why do you look in envy, you rugged mountains,
VERSE:at the mountain where God chooses to reign?
VERSE:Yes, Yahweh will dwell there forever.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The chariots of God are tens of thousands and thousands of thousands.
VERSE:The Lord is among them, from Sinai, into the sanctuary.
LABEL:18
VERSE:You have ascended on high.
VERSE:You have led away captives.
VERSE:You have received gifts among people,
VERSE:yes, among the rebellious also, that Yah God might dwell there.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Blessed be the Lord, who daily bears our burdens,
VERSE:even the God who is our salvation. Selah.
LABEL:20
VERSE:God is to us a God of deliverance.
VERSE:To Yahweh, the Lord, belongs escape from death.
LABEL:21
VERSE:But God will strike through the head of his enemies,
VERSE:the hairy scalp of such a one as still continues in his guiltiness.
LABEL:22
VERSE:The Lord said, “I will bring you again from Bashan,
VERSE:I will bring you again from the depths of the sea,
LABEL:23
VERSE:that you may crush them, dipping your foot in blood,
VERSE:that the tongues of your dogs may have their portion from your enemies.”
LABEL:24
VERSE:They have seen your processions, God,
VERSE:even the processions of my God, my King, into the sanctuary.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The singers went before, the minstrels followed after,
VERSE:among the ladies playing with tambourines,
LABEL:26
VERSE:“Bless God in the congregations,
VERSE:even the Lord in the assembly of Israel!”
LABEL:27
VERSE:There is little Benjamin, their ruler,
VERSE:the princes of Judah, their council,
VERSE:the princes of Zebulun, and the princes of Naphtali.
LABEL:28
VERSE:Your God has commanded your strength.
VERSE:Strengthen, God, that which you have done for us.
LABEL:29
VERSE:Because of your temple at Jerusalem,
VERSE:kings shall bring presents to you.
LABEL:30
VERSE:Rebuke the wild animal of the reeds,
VERSE:the multitude of the bulls with the calves of the peoples.
VERSE:Trample under foot the bars of silver.
VERSE:Scatter the nations who delight in war.
LABEL:31
VERSE:Princes shall come out of Egypt.
VERSE:Ethiopia shall hurry to stretch out her hands to God.
LABEL:32
VERSE:Sing to God, you kingdoms of the earth!
VERSE:Sing praises to the Lord—Selah—
LABEL:33
VERSE:to him who rides on the heaven of heavens, which are of old;
VERSE:behold, he utters his voice, a mighty voice.
LABEL:34
VERSE:Ascribe strength to God!
VERSE:His excellency is over Israel,
VERSE:his strength is in the skies.
LABEL:35
VERSE:You are awesome, God, in your sanctuaries.
VERSE:The God of Israel gives strength and power to his people.
VERSE:Praise be to God!
