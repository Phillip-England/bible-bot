TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:29
TITLE:Psalms 29
LABEL:1
VERSE:Ascribe to Yahweh, you sons of the mighty,
VERSE:ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name.
VERSE:Worship Yahweh in holy array.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice is on the waters.
VERSE:The God of glory thunders, even Yahweh on many waters.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice is powerful.
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice is full of majesty.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice breaks the cedars.
VERSE:Yes, Yahweh breaks in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He makes them also to skip like a calf;
VERSE:Lebanon and Sirion like a young, wild ox.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice strikes with flashes of lightning.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice shakes the wilderness.
VERSE:Yahweh shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yahweh’s voice makes the deer calve,
VERSE:and strips the forests bare.
VERSE:In his temple everything says, “Glory!”
LABEL:10
VERSE:Yahweh sat enthroned at the Flood.
VERSE:Yes, Yahweh sits as King forever.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Yahweh will give strength to his people.
VERSE:Yahweh will bless his people with peace.
