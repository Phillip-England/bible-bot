TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:55
TITLE:Psalms 55
LABEL:1
VERSE:Listen to my prayer, God.
VERSE:Don’t hide yourself from my supplication.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Attend to me, and answer me.
VERSE:I am restless in my complaint,
VERSE:and moan
LABEL:3
VERSE:because of the voice of the enemy,
VERSE:because of the oppression of the wicked.
VERSE:For they bring suffering on me.
VERSE:In anger they hold a grudge against me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:My heart is severely pained within me.
VERSE:The terrors of death have fallen on me.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Fearfulness and trembling have come on me.
VERSE:Horror has overwhelmed me.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I said, “Oh that I had wings like a dove!
VERSE:Then I would fly away, and be at rest.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Behold, then I would wander far off.
VERSE:I would lodge in the wilderness.” Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:“I would hurry to a shelter from the stormy wind and storm.”
LABEL:9
VERSE:Confuse them, Lord, and confound their language,
VERSE:for I have seen violence and strife in the city.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Day and night they prowl around on its walls.
VERSE:Malice and abuse are also within her.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Destructive forces are within her.
VERSE:Threats and lies don’t depart from her streets.
LABEL:12
VERSE:For it was not an enemy who insulted me,
VERSE:then I could have endured it.
VERSE:Neither was it he who hated me who raised himself up against me,
VERSE:then I would have hidden myself from him.
LABEL:13
VERSE:But it was you, a man like me,
VERSE:my companion, and my familiar friend.
LABEL:14
VERSE:We took sweet fellowship together.
VERSE:We walked in God’s house with company.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Let death come suddenly on them.
VERSE:Let them go down alive into Sheol.
VERSE:For wickedness is among them, in their dwelling.
LABEL:16
VERSE:As for me, I will call on God.
VERSE:Yahweh will save me.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Evening, morning, and at noon, I will cry out in distress.
VERSE:He will hear my voice.
LABEL:18
VERSE:He has redeemed my soul in peace from the battle that was against me,
VERSE:although there are many who oppose me.
LABEL:19
VERSE:God, who is enthroned forever,
VERSE:will hear and answer them. Selah.
VERSE:They never change
VERSE:and don’t fear God.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He raises his hands against his friends.
VERSE:He has violated his covenant.
LABEL:21
VERSE:His mouth was smooth as butter,
VERSE:but his heart was war.
VERSE:His words were softer than oil,
VERSE:yet they were drawn swords.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Cast your burden on Yahweh and he will sustain you.
VERSE:He will never allow the righteous to be moved.
LABEL:23
VERSE:But you, God, will bring them down into the pit of destruction.
VERSE:Bloodthirsty and deceitful men shall not live out half their days,
VERSE:but I will trust in you.
