TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:22
TITLE:Psalms 22
LABEL:1
VERSE:My God, my God, why have you forsaken me?
VERSE:Why are you so far from helping me, and from the words of my groaning?
LABEL:2
VERSE:My God, I cry in the daytime, but you don’t answer;
VERSE:in the night season, and am not silent.
LABEL:3
VERSE:But you are holy,
VERSE:you who inhabit the praises of Israel.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Our fathers trusted in you.
VERSE:They trusted, and you delivered them.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They cried to you, and were delivered.
VERSE:They trusted in you, and were not disappointed.
LABEL:6
VERSE:But I am a worm, and no man;
VERSE:a reproach of men, and despised by the people.
LABEL:7
VERSE:All those who see me mock me.
VERSE:They insult me with their lips. They shake their heads, saying,
LABEL:8
VERSE:“He trusts in Yahweh.
VERSE:Let him deliver him.
VERSE:Let him rescue him, since he delights in him.”
LABEL:9
VERSE:But you brought me out of the womb.
VERSE:You made me trust while at my mother’s breasts.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I was thrown on you from my mother’s womb.
VERSE:You are my God since my mother bore me.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Don’t be far from me, for trouble is near.
VERSE:For there is no one to help.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Many bulls have surrounded me.
VERSE:Strong bulls of Bashan have encircled me.
LABEL:13
VERSE:They open their mouths wide against me,
VERSE:lions tearing prey and roaring.
LABEL:14
VERSE:I am poured out like water.
VERSE:All my bones are out of joint.
VERSE:My heart is like wax.
VERSE:It is melted within me.
LABEL:15
VERSE:My strength is dried up like a potsherd.
VERSE:My tongue sticks to the roof of my mouth.
VERSE:You have brought me into the dust of death.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For dogs have surrounded me.
VERSE:A company of evildoers have enclosed me.
VERSE:They have pierced my hands and feet.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I can count all of my bones.
VERSE:They look and stare at me.
LABEL:18
VERSE:They divide my garments among them.
VERSE:They cast lots for my clothing.
LABEL:19
VERSE:But don’t be far off, Yahweh.
VERSE:You are my help. Hurry to help me!
LABEL:20
VERSE:Deliver my soul from the sword,
VERSE:my precious life from the power of the dog.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Save me from the lion’s mouth!
VERSE:Yes, you have rescued me from the horns of the wild oxen.
LABEL:22
VERSE:I will declare your name to my brothers.
VERSE:Among the assembly, I will praise you.
LABEL:23
VERSE:You who fear Yahweh, praise him!
VERSE:All you descendants of Jacob, glorify him!
VERSE:Stand in awe of him, all you descendants of Israel!
LABEL:24
VERSE:For he has not despised nor abhorred the affliction of the afflicted,
VERSE:neither has he hidden his face from him;
VERSE:but when he cried to him, he heard.
LABEL:25
VERSE:My praise of you comes in the great assembly.
VERSE:I will pay my vows before those who fear him.
LABEL:26
VERSE:The humble shall eat and be satisfied.
VERSE:They shall praise Yahweh who seek after him.
VERSE:Let your hearts live forever.
LABEL:27
VERSE:All the ends of the earth shall remember and turn to Yahweh.
VERSE:All the relatives of the nations shall worship before you.
LABEL:28
VERSE:For the kingdom is Yahweh’s.
VERSE:He is the ruler over the nations.
LABEL:29
VERSE:All the rich ones of the earth shall eat and worship.
VERSE:All those who go down to the dust shall bow before him,
VERSE:even he who can’t keep his soul alive.
LABEL:30
VERSE:Posterity shall serve him.
VERSE:Future generations shall be told about the Lord.
LABEL:31
VERSE:They shall come and shall declare his righteousness to a people that shall be born,
VERSE:for he has done it.
