VERSE:God presides in the great assembly.
VERSE:He judges among the gods.
LABEL:2
VERSE:“How long will you judge unjustly,
VERSE:and show partiality to the wicked?” Selah.
LABEL:3
VERSE:“Defend the weak, the poor, and the fatherless.
VERSE:Maintain the rights of the poor and oppressed.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Rescue the weak and needy.
VERSE:Deliver them out of the hand of the wicked.”
LABEL:5
VERSE:They don’t know, neither do they understand.
VERSE:They walk back and forth in darkness.
VERSE:All the foundations of the earth are shaken.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I said, “You are gods,
VERSE:all of you are sons of the Most High.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Nevertheless you shall die like men,
VERSE:and fall like one of the rulers.”
LABEL:8
VERSE:Arise, God, judge the earth,
VERSE:for you inherit all of the nations.
