TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:59
TITLE:Psalms 59
LABEL:1
VERSE:Deliver me from my enemies, my God.
VERSE:Set me on high from those who rise up against me.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Deliver me from the workers of iniquity.
VERSE:Save me from the bloodthirsty men.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For, behold, they lie in wait for my soul.
VERSE:The mighty gather themselves together against me,
VERSE:not for my disobedience, nor for my sin, Yahweh.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I have done no wrong, yet they are ready to attack me.
VERSE:Rise up, behold, and help me!
LABEL:5
VERSE:You, Yahweh God of Armies, the God of Israel,
VERSE:rouse yourself to punish the nations.
VERSE:Show no mercy to the wicked traitors. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They return at evening, howling like dogs,
VERSE:and prowl around the city.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Behold, they spew with their mouth.
VERSE:Swords are in their lips,
VERSE:“For”, they say, “who hears us?”
LABEL:8
VERSE:But you, Yahweh, laugh at them.
VERSE:You scoff at all the nations.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Oh, my Strength, I watch for you,
VERSE:for God is my high tower.
LABEL:10
VERSE:My God will go before me with his loving kindness.
VERSE:God will let me look at my enemies in triumph.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Don’t kill them, or my people may forget.
VERSE:Scatter them by your power, and bring them down, Lord our shield.
LABEL:12
VERSE:For the sin of their mouth, and the words of their lips,
VERSE:let them be caught in their pride,
VERSE:for the curses and lies which they utter.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Consume them in wrath.
VERSE:Consume them, and they will be no more.
VERSE:Let them know that God rules in Jacob,
VERSE:to the ends of the earth. Selah.
LABEL:14
VERSE:At evening let them return.
VERSE:Let them howl like a dog, and go around the city.
LABEL:15
VERSE:They shall wander up and down for food,
VERSE:and wait all night if they aren’t satisfied.
LABEL:16
VERSE:But I will sing of your strength.
VERSE:Yes, I will sing aloud of your loving kindness in the morning.
VERSE:For you have been my high tower,
VERSE:a refuge in the day of my distress.
LABEL:17
VERSE:To you, my strength, I will sing praises.
VERSE:For God is my high tower, the God of my mercy.
