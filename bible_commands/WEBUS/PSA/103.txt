TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:103
TITLE:Psalms 103
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, my soul!
VERSE:All that is within me, praise his holy name!
LABEL:2
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, my soul,
VERSE:and don’t forget all his benefits,
LABEL:3
VERSE:who forgives all your sins,
VERSE:who heals all your diseases,
LABEL:4
VERSE:who redeems your life from destruction,
VERSE:who crowns you with loving kindness and tender mercies,
LABEL:5
VERSE:who satisfies your desire with good things,
VERSE:so that your youth is renewed like the eagle’s.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yahweh executes righteous acts,
VERSE:and justice for all who are oppressed.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He made known his ways to Moses,
VERSE:his deeds to the children of Israel.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh is merciful and gracious,
VERSE:slow to anger, and abundant in loving kindness.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He will not always accuse;
VERSE:neither will he stay angry forever.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He has not dealt with us according to our sins,
VERSE:nor repaid us for our iniquities.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For as the heavens are high above the earth,
VERSE:so great is his loving kindness toward those who fear him.
LABEL:12
VERSE:As far as the east is from the west,
VERSE:so far has he removed our transgressions from us.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Like a father has compassion on his children,
VERSE:so Yahweh has compassion on those who fear him.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For he knows how we are made.
VERSE:He remembers that we are dust.
LABEL:15
VERSE:As for man, his days are like grass.
VERSE:As a flower of the field, so he flourishes.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For the wind passes over it, and it is gone.
VERSE:Its place remembers it no more.
LABEL:17
VERSE:But Yahweh’s loving kindness is from everlasting to everlasting with those who fear him,
VERSE:his righteousness to children’s children,
LABEL:18
VERSE:to those who keep his covenant,
VERSE:to those who remember to obey his precepts.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Yahweh has established his throne in the heavens.
VERSE:His kingdom rules over all.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, you angels of his,
VERSE:who are mighty in strength, who fulfill his word,
VERSE:obeying the voice of his word.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, all you armies of his,
VERSE:you servants of his, who do his pleasure.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, all you works of his,
VERSE:in all places of his dominion.
VERSE:Praise Yahweh, my soul!
