TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:12
TITLE:Psalms 12
LABEL:1
VERSE:Help, Yahweh; for the godly man ceases.
VERSE:For the faithful fail from among the children of men.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Everyone lies to his neighbor.
VERSE:They speak with flattering lips, and with a double heart.
LABEL:3
VERSE:May Yahweh cut off all flattering lips,
VERSE:and the tongue that boasts,
LABEL:4
VERSE:who have said, “With our tongue we will prevail.
VERSE:Our lips are our own.
VERSE:Who is lord over us?”
LABEL:5
VERSE:“Because of the oppression of the weak and because of the groaning of the needy,
VERSE:I will now arise,” says Yahweh;
VERSE:“I will set him in safety from those who malign him.”
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yahweh’s words are flawless words,
VERSE:as silver refined in a clay furnace, purified seven times.
LABEL:7
VERSE:You will keep them, Yahweh.
VERSE:You will preserve them from this generation forever.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The wicked walk on every side,
VERSE:when what is vile is exalted among the sons of men.
