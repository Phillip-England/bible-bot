TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:33
TITLE:Psalms 33
LABEL:1
VERSE:Rejoice in Yahweh, you righteous!
VERSE:Praise is fitting for the upright.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Give thanks to Yahweh with the lyre.
VERSE:Sing praises to him with the harp of ten strings.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Sing to him a new song.
VERSE:Play skillfully with a shout of joy!
LABEL:4
VERSE:For Yahweh’s word is right.
VERSE:All his work is done in faithfulness.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He loves righteousness and justice.
VERSE:The earth is full of the loving kindness of Yahweh.
LABEL:6
VERSE:By Yahweh’s word, the heavens were made:
VERSE:all their army by the breath of his mouth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He gathers the waters of the sea together as a heap.
VERSE:He lays up the deeps in storehouses.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Let all the earth fear Yahweh.
VERSE:Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For he spoke, and it was done.
VERSE:He commanded, and it stood firm.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Yahweh brings the counsel of the nations to nothing.
VERSE:He makes the thoughts of the peoples to be of no effect.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The counsel of Yahweh stands fast forever,
VERSE:the thoughts of his heart to all generations.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Blessed is the nation whose God is Yahweh,
VERSE:the people whom he has chosen for his own inheritance.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Yahweh looks from heaven.
VERSE:He sees all the sons of men.
LABEL:14
VERSE:From the place of his habitation he looks out on all the inhabitants of the earth,
LABEL:15
VERSE:he who fashions all of their hearts;
VERSE:and he considers all of their works.
LABEL:16
VERSE:There is no king saved by the multitude of an army.
VERSE:A mighty man is not delivered by great strength.
LABEL:17
VERSE:A horse is a vain thing for safety,
VERSE:neither does he deliver any by his great power.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Behold, Yahweh’s eye is on those who fear him,
VERSE:on those who hope in his loving kindness,
LABEL:19
VERSE:to deliver their soul from death,
VERSE:to keep them alive in famine.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Our soul has waited for Yahweh.
VERSE:He is our help and our shield.
LABEL:21
VERSE:For our heart rejoices in him,
VERSE:because we have trusted in his holy name.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Let your loving kindness be on us, Yahweh,
VERSE:since we have hoped in you.
