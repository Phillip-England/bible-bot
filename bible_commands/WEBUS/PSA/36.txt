TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:36
TITLE:Psalms 36
LABEL:1
VERSE:A revelation is within my heart about the disobedience of the wicked:
VERSE:There is no fear of God before his eyes.
LABEL:2
VERSE:For he flatters himself in his own eyes,
VERSE:too much to detect and hate his sin.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit.
VERSE:He has ceased to be wise and to do good.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He plots iniquity on his bed.
VERSE:He sets himself in a way that is not good.
VERSE:He doesn’t abhor evil.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Your loving kindness, Yahweh, is in the heavens.
VERSE:Your faithfulness reaches to the skies.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Your righteousness is like the mountains of God.
VERSE:Your judgments are like a great deep.
VERSE:Yahweh, you preserve man and animal.
LABEL:7
VERSE:How precious is your loving kindness, God!
VERSE:The children of men take refuge under the shadow of your wings.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They shall be abundantly satisfied with the abundance of your house.
VERSE:You will make them drink of the river of your pleasures.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For with you is the spring of life.
VERSE:In your light we will see light.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Oh continue your loving kindness to those who know you,
VERSE:your righteousness to the upright in heart.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Don’t let the foot of pride come against me.
VERSE:Don’t let the hand of the wicked drive me away.
LABEL:12
VERSE:There the workers of iniquity are fallen.
VERSE:They are thrust down, and shall not be able to rise.
