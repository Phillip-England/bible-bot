TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:9
TITLE:Psalms 9
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will give thanks to Yahweh with my whole heart.
VERSE:I will tell of all your marvelous works.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will be glad and rejoice in you.
VERSE:I will sing praise to your name, O Most High.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When my enemies turn back,
VERSE:they stumble and perish in your presence.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For you have maintained my just cause.
VERSE:You sit on the throne judging righteously.
LABEL:5
VERSE:You have rebuked the nations.
VERSE:You have destroyed the wicked.
VERSE:You have blotted out their name forever and ever.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The enemy is overtaken by endless ruin.
VERSE:The very memory of the cities which you have overthrown has perished.
LABEL:7
VERSE:But Yahweh reigns forever.
VERSE:He has prepared his throne for judgment.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He will judge the world in righteousness.
VERSE:He will administer judgment to the peoples in uprightness.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yahweh will also be a high tower for the oppressed;
VERSE:a high tower in times of trouble.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Those who know your name will put their trust in you,
VERSE:for you, Yahweh, have not forsaken those who seek you.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Sing praises to Yahweh, who dwells in Zion,
VERSE:and declare among the people what he has done.
LABEL:12
VERSE:For he who avenges blood remembers them.
VERSE:He doesn’t forget the cry of the afflicted.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Have mercy on me, Yahweh.
VERSE:See my affliction by those who hate me,
VERSE:and lift me up from the gates of death,
LABEL:14
VERSE:that I may show all of your praise.
VERSE:I will rejoice in your salvation in the gates of the daughter of Zion.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The nations have sunk down in the pit that they made.
VERSE:In the net which they hid, their own foot is taken.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Yahweh has made himself known.
VERSE:He has executed judgment.
VERSE:The wicked is snared by the work of his own hands. Meditation. Selah.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The wicked shall be turned back to Sheol,
VERSE:even all the nations that forget God.
LABEL:18
VERSE:For the needy shall not always be forgotten,
VERSE:nor the hope of the poor perish forever.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Arise, Yahweh! Don’t let man prevail.
VERSE:Let the nations be judged in your sight.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Put them in fear, Yahweh.
VERSE:Let the nations know that they are only men. Selah.
