TITLE:Psalms 142
LABEL:1
VERSE:I cry with my voice to Yahweh.
VERSE:With my voice, I ask Yahweh for mercy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I pour out my complaint before him.
VERSE:I tell him my troubles.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When my spirit was overwhelmed within me,
VERSE:you knew my route.
VERSE:On the path in which I walk,
VERSE:they have hidden a snare for me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Look on my right, and see;
VERSE:for there is no one who is concerned for me.
VERSE:Refuge has fled from me.
VERSE:No one cares for my soul.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I cried to you, Yahweh.
VERSE:I said, “You are my refuge,
VERSE:my portion in the land of the living.”
LABEL:6
VERSE:Listen to my cry,
VERSE:for I am in desperate need.
VERSE:Deliver me from my persecutors,
VERSE:for they are too strong for me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Bring my soul out of prison,
VERSE:that I may give thanks to your name.
VERSE:The righteous will surround me,
VERSE:for you will be good to me.
