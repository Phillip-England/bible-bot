TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:48
TITLE:Psalms 48
LABEL:1
VERSE:Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised,
VERSE:in the city of our God, in his holy mountain.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Beautiful in elevation, the joy of the whole earth,
VERSE:is Mount Zion, on the north sides,
VERSE:the city of the great King.
LABEL:3
VERSE:God has shown himself in her citadels as a refuge.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For, behold, the kings assembled themselves,
VERSE:they passed by together.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They saw it, then they were amazed.
VERSE:They were dismayed.
VERSE:They hurried away.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Trembling took hold of them there,
VERSE:pain, as of a woman in travail.
LABEL:7
VERSE:With the east wind, you break the ships of Tarshish.
LABEL:8
VERSE:As we have heard, so we have seen,
VERSE:in the city of Yahweh of Armies, in the city of our God.
VERSE:God will establish it forever. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:We have thought about your loving kindness, God,
VERSE:in the middle of your temple.
LABEL:10
VERSE:As is your name, God,
VERSE:so is your praise to the ends of the earth.
VERSE:Your right hand is full of righteousness.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let Mount Zion be glad!
VERSE:Let the daughters of Judah rejoice because of your judgments.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Walk about Zion, and go around her.
VERSE:Number its towers.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Notice her bulwarks.
VERSE:Consider her palaces,
VERSE:that you may tell it to the next generation.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For this God is our God forever and ever.
VERSE:He will be our guide even to death.
