TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:83
TITLE:Psalms 83
LABEL:1
VERSE:God, don’t keep silent.
VERSE:Don’t keep silent,
VERSE:and don’t be still, God.
LABEL:2
VERSE:For, behold, your enemies are stirred up.
VERSE:Those who hate you have lifted up their heads.
LABEL:3
VERSE:They conspire with cunning against your people.
VERSE:They plot against your cherished ones.
LABEL:4
VERSE:“Come,” they say, “let’s destroy them as a nation,
VERSE:that the name of Israel may be remembered no more.”
LABEL:5
VERSE:For they have conspired together with one mind.
VERSE:They form an alliance against you.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites;
VERSE:Moab, and the Hagrites;
LABEL:7
VERSE:Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek;
VERSE:Philistia with the inhabitants of Tyre;
LABEL:8
VERSE:Assyria also is joined with them.
VERSE:They have helped the children of Lot. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Do to them as you did to Midian,
VERSE:as to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the river Kishon;
LABEL:10
VERSE:who perished at Endor,
VERSE:who became as dung for the earth.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Make their nobles like Oreb and Zeeb,
VERSE:yes, all their princes like Zebah and Zalmunna,
LABEL:12
VERSE:who said, “Let’s take possession of God’s pasture lands.”
LABEL:13
VERSE:My God, make them like tumbleweed,
VERSE:like chaff before the wind.
LABEL:14
VERSE:As the fire that burns the forest,
VERSE:as the flame that sets the mountains on fire,
LABEL:15
VERSE:so pursue them with your tempest,
VERSE:and terrify them with your storm.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Fill their faces with confusion,
VERSE:that they may seek your name, Yahweh.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Let them be disappointed and dismayed forever.
VERSE:Yes, let them be confounded and perish;
LABEL:18
VERSE:that they may know that you alone, whose name is Yahweh,
VERSE:are the Most High over all the earth.
