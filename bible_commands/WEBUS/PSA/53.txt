TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:53
TITLE:Psalms 53
LABEL:1
VERSE:The fool has said in his heart, “There is no God.”
VERSE:They are corrupt, and have done abominable iniquity.
VERSE:There is no one who does good.
LABEL:2
VERSE:God looks down from heaven on the children of men,
VERSE:to see if there are any who understood,
VERSE:who seek after God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Every one of them has gone back.
VERSE:They have become filthy together.
VERSE:There is no one who does good, no, not one.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Have the workers of iniquity no knowledge,
VERSE:who eat up my people as they eat bread,
VERSE:and don’t call on God?
LABEL:5
VERSE:There they were in great fear, where no fear was,
VERSE:for God has scattered the bones of him who encamps against you.
VERSE:You have put them to shame,
VERSE:because God has rejected them.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Oh that the salvation of Israel would come out of Zion!
VERSE:When God brings back his people from captivity,
VERSE:then Jacob shall rejoice,
VERSE:and Israel shall be glad.
