TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:105
TITLE:Psalms 105
LABEL:1
VERSE:Give thanks to Yahweh! Call on his name!
VERSE:Make his doings known among the peoples.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing to him, sing praises to him!
VERSE:Tell of all his marvelous works.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Glory in his holy name.
VERSE:Let the heart of those who seek Yahweh rejoice.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Seek Yahweh and his strength.
VERSE:Seek his face forever more.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Remember his marvelous works that he has done:
VERSE:his wonders, and the judgments of his mouth,
LABEL:6
VERSE:you offspring of Abraham, his servant,
VERSE:you children of Jacob, his chosen ones.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He is Yahweh, our God.
VERSE:His judgments are in all the earth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He has remembered his covenant forever,
VERSE:the word which he commanded to a thousand generations,
LABEL:9
VERSE:the covenant which he made with Abraham,
VERSE:his oath to Isaac,
LABEL:10
VERSE:and confirmed it to Jacob for a statute;
VERSE:to Israel for an everlasting covenant,
LABEL:11
VERSE:saying, “To you I will give the land of Canaan,
VERSE:the lot of your inheritance,”
LABEL:12
VERSE:when they were but a few men in number,
VERSE:yes, very few, and foreigners in it.
LABEL:13
VERSE:They went about from nation to nation,
VERSE:from one kingdom to another people.
LABEL:14
VERSE:He allowed no one to do them wrong.
VERSE:Yes, he reproved kings for their sakes,
LABEL:15
VERSE:“Don’t touch my anointed ones!
VERSE:Do my prophets no harm!”
LABEL:16
VERSE:He called for a famine on the land.
VERSE:He destroyed the food supplies.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He sent a man before them.
VERSE:Joseph was sold for a slave.
LABEL:18
VERSE:They bruised his feet with shackles.
VERSE:His neck was locked in irons,
LABEL:19
VERSE:until the time that his word happened,
VERSE:and Yahweh’s word proved him true.
LABEL:20
VERSE:The king sent and freed him,
VERSE:even the ruler of peoples, and let him go free.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He made him lord of his house,
VERSE:and ruler of all of his possessions,
LABEL:22
VERSE:to discipline his princes at his pleasure,
VERSE:and to teach his elders wisdom.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Israel also came into Egypt.
VERSE:Jacob lived in the land of Ham.
LABEL:24
VERSE:He increased his people greatly,
VERSE:and made them stronger than their adversaries.
LABEL:25
VERSE:He turned their heart to hate his people,
VERSE:to conspire against his servants.
LABEL:26
VERSE:He sent Moses, his servant,
VERSE:and Aaron, whom he had chosen.
LABEL:27
VERSE:They performed miracles among them,
VERSE:and wonders in the land of Ham.
LABEL:28
VERSE:He sent darkness, and made it dark.
VERSE:They didn’t rebel against his words.
LABEL:29
VERSE:He turned their waters into blood,
VERSE:and killed their fish.
LABEL:30
VERSE:Their land swarmed with frogs,
VERSE:even in the rooms of their kings.
LABEL:31
VERSE:He spoke, and swarms of flies came,
VERSE:and lice in all their borders.
LABEL:32
VERSE:He gave them hail for rain,
VERSE:with lightning in their land.
LABEL:33
VERSE:He struck their vines and also their fig trees,
VERSE:and shattered the trees of their country.
LABEL:34
VERSE:He spoke, and the locusts came
VERSE:with the grasshoppers, without number.
LABEL:35
VERSE:They ate up every plant in their land,
VERSE:and ate up the fruit of their ground.
LABEL:36
VERSE:He struck also all the firstborn in their land,
VERSE:the first fruits of all their manhood.
LABEL:37
VERSE:He brought them out with silver and gold.
VERSE:There was not one feeble person among his tribes.
LABEL:38
VERSE:Egypt was glad when they departed,
VERSE:for the fear of them had fallen on them.
LABEL:39
VERSE:He spread a cloud for a covering,
VERSE:fire to give light in the night.
LABEL:40
VERSE:They asked, and he brought quails,
VERSE:and satisfied them with the bread of the sky.
LABEL:41
VERSE:He opened the rock, and waters gushed out.
VERSE:They ran as a river in the dry places.
LABEL:42
VERSE:For he remembered his holy word,
VERSE:and Abraham, his servant.
LABEL:43
VERSE:He brought his people out with joy,
VERSE:his chosen with singing.
LABEL:44
VERSE:He gave them the lands of the nations.
VERSE:They took the labor of the peoples in possession,
LABEL:45
VERSE:that they might keep his statutes,
VERSE:and observe his laws.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
