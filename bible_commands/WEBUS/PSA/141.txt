TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:141
TITLE:Psalms 141
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh, I have called on you.
VERSE:Come to me quickly!
VERSE:Listen to my voice when I call to you.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let my prayer be set before you like incense;
VERSE:the lifting up of my hands like the evening sacrifice.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Set a watch, Yahweh, before my mouth.
VERSE:Keep the door of my lips.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Don’t incline my heart to any evil thing,
VERSE:to practice deeds of wickedness with men who work iniquity.
VERSE:Don’t let me eat of their delicacies.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Let the righteous strike me, it is kindness;
VERSE:let him reprove me, it is like oil on the head;
VERSE:don’t let my head refuse it;
VERSE:Yet my prayer is always against evil deeds.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Their judges are thrown down by the sides of the rock.
VERSE:They will hear my words, for they are well spoken.
LABEL:7
VERSE:“As when one plows and breaks up the earth,
VERSE:our bones are scattered at the mouth of Sheol.”
LABEL:8
VERSE:For my eyes are on you, Yahweh, the Lord.
VERSE:I take refuge in you.
VERSE:Don’t leave my soul destitute.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Keep me from the snare which they have laid for me,
VERSE:from the traps of the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Let the wicked fall together into their own nets
VERSE:while I pass by.
