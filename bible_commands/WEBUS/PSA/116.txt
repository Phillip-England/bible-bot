BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:116
TITLE:Psalms 116
LABEL:1
VERSE:I love Yahweh, because he listens to my voice,
VERSE:and my cries for mercy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Because he has turned his ear to me,
VERSE:therefore I will call on him as long as I live.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The cords of death surrounded me,
VERSE:the pains of Sheol got a hold of me.
VERSE:I found trouble and sorrow.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Then I called on Yahweh’s name:
VERSE:“Yahweh, I beg you, deliver my soul.”
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yahweh is gracious and righteous.
VERSE:Yes, our God is merciful.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yahweh preserves the simple.
VERSE:I was brought low, and he saved me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Return to your rest, my soul,
VERSE:for Yahweh has dealt bountifully with you.
LABEL:8
VERSE:For you have delivered my soul from death,
VERSE:my eyes from tears,
VERSE:and my feet from falling.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will walk before Yahweh in the land of the living.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I believed, therefore I said,
VERSE:“I was greatly afflicted.”
LABEL:11
VERSE:I said in my haste,
VERSE:“All people are liars.”
LABEL:12
VERSE:What will I give to Yahweh for all his benefits toward me?
LABEL:13
VERSE:I will take the cup of salvation, and call on Yahweh’s name.
LABEL:14
VERSE:I will pay my vows to Yahweh,
VERSE:yes, in the presence of all his people.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Precious in Yahweh’s sight is the death of his saints.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Yahweh, truly I am your servant.
VERSE:I am your servant, the son of your servant girl.
VERSE:You have freed me from my chains.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I will offer to you the sacrifice of thanksgiving,
VERSE:and will call on Yahweh’s name.
LABEL:18
VERSE:I will pay my vows to Yahweh,
VERSE:yes, in the presence of all his people,
LABEL:19
VERSE:in the courts of Yahweh’s house,
VERSE:in the middle of you, Jerusalem.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
