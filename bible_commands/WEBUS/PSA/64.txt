TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:64
TITLE:Psalms 64
LABEL:1
VERSE:Hear my voice, God, in my complaint.
VERSE:Preserve my life from fear of the enemy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Hide me from the conspiracy of the wicked,
VERSE:from the noisy crowd of the ones doing evil;
LABEL:3
VERSE:who sharpen their tongue like a sword,
VERSE:and aim their arrows, deadly words,
LABEL:4
VERSE:to shoot innocent men from ambushes.
VERSE:They shoot at him suddenly and fearlessly.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They encourage themselves in evil plans.
VERSE:They talk about laying snares secretly.
VERSE:They say, “Who will see them?”
LABEL:6
VERSE:They plot injustice, saying, “We have made a perfect plan!”
VERSE:Surely man’s mind and heart are cunning.
LABEL:7
VERSE:But God will shoot at them.
VERSE:They will be suddenly struck down with an arrow.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Their own tongues shall ruin them.
VERSE:All who see them will shake their heads.
LABEL:9
VERSE:All mankind shall be afraid.
VERSE:They shall declare the work of God,
VERSE:and shall wisely ponder what he has done.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The righteous shall be glad in Yahweh,
VERSE:and shall take refuge in him.
VERSE:All the upright in heart shall praise him!
