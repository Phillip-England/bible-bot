TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:91
TITLE:Psalms 91
LABEL:1
VERSE:He who dwells in the secret place of the Most High
VERSE:will rest in the shadow of the Almighty.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will say of Yahweh, “He is my refuge and my fortress;
VERSE:my God, in whom I trust.”
LABEL:3
VERSE:For he will deliver you from the snare of the fowler,
VERSE:and from the deadly pestilence.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He will cover you with his feathers.
VERSE:Under his wings you will take refuge.
VERSE:His faithfulness is your shield and rampart.
LABEL:5
VERSE:You shall not be afraid of the terror by night,
VERSE:nor of the arrow that flies by day,
LABEL:6
VERSE:nor of the pestilence that walks in darkness,
VERSE:nor of the destruction that wastes at noonday.
LABEL:7
VERSE:A thousand may fall at your side,
VERSE:and ten thousand at your right hand;
VERSE:but it will not come near you.
LABEL:8
VERSE:You will only look with your eyes,
VERSE:and see the recompense of the wicked.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Because you have made Yahweh your refuge,
VERSE:and the Most High your dwelling place,
LABEL:10
VERSE:no evil shall happen to you,
VERSE:neither shall any plague come near your dwelling.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For he will put his angels in charge of you,
VERSE:to guard you in all your ways.
LABEL:12
VERSE:They will bear you up in their hands,
VERSE:so that you won’t dash your foot against a stone.
LABEL:13
VERSE:You will tread on the lion and cobra.
VERSE:You will trample the young lion and the serpent underfoot.
LABEL:14
VERSE:“Because he has set his love on me, therefore I will deliver him.
VERSE:I will set him on high, because he has known my name.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He will call on me, and I will answer him.
VERSE:I will be with him in trouble.
VERSE:I will deliver him, and honor him.
LABEL:16
VERSE:I will satisfy him with long life,
VERSE:and show him my salvation.”
