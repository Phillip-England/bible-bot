TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:41
TITLE:Psalms 41
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is he who considers the poor.
VERSE:Yahweh will deliver him in the day of evil.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh will preserve him, and keep him alive.
VERSE:He shall be blessed on the earth,
VERSE:and he will not surrender him to the will of his enemies.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yahweh will sustain him on his sickbed,
VERSE:and restore him from his bed of illness.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I said, “Yahweh, have mercy on me!
VERSE:Heal me, for I have sinned against you.”
LABEL:5
VERSE:My enemies speak evil against me:
VERSE:“When will he die, and his name perish?”
LABEL:6
VERSE:If he comes to see me, he speaks falsehood.
VERSE:His heart gathers iniquity to itself.
VERSE:When he goes abroad, he tells it.
LABEL:7
VERSE:All who hate me whisper together against me.
VERSE:They imagine the worst for me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:“An evil disease”, they say, “has afflicted him.
VERSE:Now that he lies he shall rise up no more.”
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yes, my own familiar friend, in whom I trusted,
VERSE:who ate bread with me,
VERSE:has lifted up his heel against me.
LABEL:10
VERSE:But you, Yahweh, have mercy on me, and raise me up,
VERSE:that I may repay them.
LABEL:11
VERSE:By this I know that you delight in me,
VERSE:because my enemy doesn’t triumph over me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:As for me, you uphold me in my integrity,
VERSE:and set me in your presence forever.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Blessed be Yahweh, the God of Israel,
VERSE:from everlasting and to everlasting!
VERSE:Amen and amen.
