TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:140
TITLE:Psalms 140
LABEL:1
VERSE:Deliver me, Yahweh, from evil men.
VERSE:Preserve me from violent men:
LABEL:2
VERSE:those who devise mischief in their hearts.
VERSE:They continually gather themselves together for war.
LABEL:3
VERSE:They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent.
VERSE:Viper’s poison is under their lips. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Yahweh, keep me from the hands of the wicked.
VERSE:Preserve me from the violent men who have determined to trip my feet.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The proud have hidden a snare for me,
VERSE:they have spread the cords of a net by the path.
VERSE:They have set traps for me. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I said to Yahweh, “You are my God.”
VERSE:Listen to the cry of my petitions, Yahweh.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Yahweh, the Lord, the strength of my salvation,
VERSE:you have covered my head in the day of battle.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh, don’t grant the desires of the wicked.
VERSE:Don’t let their evil plans succeed, or they will become proud. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:As for the head of those who surround me,
VERSE:let the mischief of their own lips cover them.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Let burning coals fall on them.
VERSE:Let them be thrown into the fire,
VERSE:into miry pits, from where they never rise.
LABEL:11
VERSE:An evil speaker won’t be established in the earth.
VERSE:Evil will hunt the violent man to overthrow him.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I know that Yahweh will maintain the cause of the afflicted,
VERSE:and justice for the needy.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Surely the righteous will give thanks to your name.
VERSE:The upright will dwell in your presence.
