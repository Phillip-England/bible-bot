TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:30
TITLE:Psalms 30
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will extol you, Yahweh, for you have raised me up,
VERSE:and have not made my foes to rejoice over me.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh my God, I cried to you,
VERSE:and you have healed me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yahweh, you have brought up my soul from Sheol.
VERSE:You have kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Sing praise to Yahweh, you saints of his.
VERSE:Give thanks to his holy name.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For his anger is but for a moment.
VERSE:His favor is for a lifetime.
VERSE:Weeping may stay for the night,
VERSE:but joy comes in the morning.
LABEL:6
VERSE:As for me, I said in my prosperity,
VERSE:“I shall never be moved.”
LABEL:7
VERSE:You, Yahweh, when you favored me, made my mountain stand strong;
VERSE:but when you hid your face, I was troubled.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I cried to you, Yahweh.
VERSE:I made supplication to the Lord:
LABEL:9
VERSE:“What profit is there in my destruction, if I go down to the pit?
VERSE:Shall the dust praise you?
VERSE:Shall it declare your truth?
LABEL:10
VERSE:Hear, Yahweh, and have mercy on me.
VERSE:Yahweh, be my helper.”
LABEL:11
VERSE:You have turned my mourning into dancing for me.
VERSE:You have removed my sackcloth, and clothed me with gladness,
LABEL:12
VERSE:to the end that my heart may sing praise to you, and not be silent.
VERSE:Yahweh my God, I will give thanks to you forever!
