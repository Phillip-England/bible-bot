TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:66
TITLE:Psalms 66
LABEL:1
VERSE:Make a joyful shout to God, all the earth!
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing to the glory of his name!
VERSE:Offer glory and praise!
LABEL:3
VERSE:Tell God, “How awesome are your deeds!
VERSE:Through the greatness of your power, your enemies submit themselves to you.
LABEL:4
VERSE:All the earth will worship you,
VERSE:and will sing to you;
VERSE:they will sing to your name.” Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Come, and see God’s deeds—
VERSE:awesome work on behalf of the children of men.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He turned the sea into dry land.
VERSE:They went through the river on foot.
VERSE:There, we rejoiced in him.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He rules by his might forever.
VERSE:His eyes watch the nations.
VERSE:Don’t let the rebellious rise up against him. Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Praise our God, you peoples!
VERSE:Make the sound of his praise heard,
LABEL:9
VERSE:who preserves our life among the living,
VERSE:and doesn’t allow our feet to be moved.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For you, God, have tested us.
VERSE:You have refined us, as silver is refined.
LABEL:11
VERSE:You brought us into prison.
VERSE:You laid a burden on our backs.
LABEL:12
VERSE:You allowed men to ride over our heads.
VERSE:We went through fire and through water,
VERSE:but you brought us to the place of abundance.
LABEL:13
VERSE:I will come into your temple with burnt offerings.
VERSE:I will pay my vows to you,
LABEL:14
VERSE:which my lips promised,
VERSE:and my mouth spoke, when I was in distress.
LABEL:15
VERSE:I will offer to you burnt offerings of fat animals,
VERSE:with the offering of rams,
VERSE:I will offer bulls with goats. Selah.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Come and hear, all you who fear God.
VERSE:I will declare what he has done for my soul.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I cried to him with my mouth.
VERSE:He was extolled with my tongue.
LABEL:18
VERSE:If I cherished sin in my heart,
VERSE:the Lord wouldn’t have listened.
LABEL:19
VERSE:But most certainly, God has listened.
VERSE:He has heard the voice of my prayer.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Blessed be God, who has not turned away my prayer,
VERSE:nor his loving kindness from me.
