TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:14
TITLE:Psalms 14
LABEL:1
VERSE:The fool has said in his heart, “There is no God.”
VERSE:They are corrupt.
VERSE:They have done abominable deeds.
VERSE:There is no one who does good.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh looked down from heaven on the children of men,
VERSE:to see if there were any who understood,
VERSE:who sought after God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:They have all gone aside.
VERSE:They have together become corrupt.
VERSE:There is no one who does good, no, not one.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Have all the workers of iniquity no knowledge,
VERSE:who eat up my people as they eat bread,
VERSE:and don’t call on Yahweh?
LABEL:5
VERSE:There they were in great fear,
VERSE:for God is in the generation of the righteous.
LABEL:6
VERSE:You frustrate the plan of the poor,
VERSE:because Yahweh is his refuge.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Oh that the salvation of Israel would come out of Zion!
VERSE:When Yahweh restores the fortunes of his people,
VERSE:then Jacob shall rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.
