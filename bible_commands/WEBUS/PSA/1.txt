TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:1
TITLE:Psalms 1
SECTION:BOOK 1
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is the man who doesn’t walk in the counsel of the wicked,
VERSE:nor stand on the path of sinners,
VERSE:nor sit in the seat of scoffers;
LABEL:2
VERSE:but his delight is in Yahweh’s law.
VERSE:On his law he meditates day and night.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He will be like a tree planted by the streams of water,
VERSE:that produces its fruit in its season,
VERSE:whose leaf also does not wither.
VERSE:Whatever he does shall prosper.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The wicked are not so,
VERSE:but are like the chaff which the wind drives away.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Therefore the wicked shall not stand in the judgment,
VERSE:nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For Yahweh knows the way of the righteous,
VERSE:but the way of the wicked shall perish.
