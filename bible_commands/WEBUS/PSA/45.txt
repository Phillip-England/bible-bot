TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:45
TITLE:Psalms 45
LABEL:1
VERSE:My heart overflows with a noble theme.
VERSE:I recite my verses for the king.
VERSE:My tongue is like the pen of a skillful writer.
LABEL:2
VERSE:You are the most excellent of the sons of men.
VERSE:Grace has anointed your lips,
VERSE:therefore God has blessed you forever.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Strap your sword on your thigh, O mighty one,
VERSE:in your splendor and your majesty.
LABEL:4
VERSE:In your majesty ride on victoriously on behalf of truth, humility, and righteousness.
VERSE:Let your right hand display awesome deeds.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Your arrows are sharp.
VERSE:The nations fall under you, with arrows in the heart of the king’s enemies.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Your throne, God, is forever and ever.
VERSE:A scepter of equity is the scepter of your kingdom.
LABEL:7
VERSE:You have loved righteousness, and hated wickedness.
VERSE:Therefore God, your God, has anointed you with the oil of gladness above your fellows.
LABEL:8
VERSE:All your garments smell like myrrh, aloes, and cassia.
VERSE:Out of ivory palaces stringed instruments have made you glad.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Kings’ daughters are among your honorable women.
VERSE:At your right hand the queen stands in gold of Ophir.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Listen, daughter, consider, and turn your ear.
VERSE:Forget your own people, and also your father’s house.
LABEL:11
VERSE:So the king will desire your beauty,
VERSE:honor him, for he is your lord.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The daughter of Tyre comes with a gift.
VERSE:The rich among the people entreat your favor.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The princess inside is all glorious.
VERSE:Her clothing is interwoven with gold.
LABEL:14
VERSE:She shall be led to the king in embroidered work.
VERSE:The virgins, her companions who follow her, shall be brought to you.
LABEL:15
VERSE:With gladness and rejoicing they shall be led.
VERSE:They shall enter into the king’s palace.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Your sons will take the place of your fathers.
VERSE:You shall make them princes in all the earth.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I will make your name to be remembered in all generations.
VERSE:Therefore the peoples shall give you thanks forever and ever.
