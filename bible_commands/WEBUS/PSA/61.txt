TITLE:Psalms 61
LABEL:1
VERSE:Hear my cry, God.
VERSE:Listen to my prayer.
LABEL:2
VERSE:From the end of the earth, I will call to you when my heart is overwhelmed.
VERSE:Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For you have been a refuge for me,
VERSE:a strong tower from the enemy.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I will dwell in your tent forever.
VERSE:I will take refuge in the shelter of your wings. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For you, God, have heard my vows.
VERSE:You have given me the heritage of those who fear your name.
LABEL:6
VERSE:You will prolong the king’s life.
VERSE:His years will be for generations.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He shall be enthroned in God’s presence forever.
VERSE:Appoint your loving kindness and truth, that they may preserve him.
LABEL:8
VERSE:So I will sing praise to your name forever,
VERSE:that I may fulfill my vows daily.
