TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:108
TITLE:Psalms 108
LABEL:1
VERSE:My heart is steadfast, God.
VERSE:I will sing and I will make music with my soul.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Wake up, harp and lyre!
VERSE:I will wake up the dawn.
LABEL:3
VERSE:I will give thanks to you, Yahweh, among the nations.
VERSE:I will sing praises to you among the peoples.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For your loving kindness is great above the heavens.
VERSE:Your faithfulness reaches to the skies.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Be exalted, God, above the heavens!
VERSE:Let your glory be over all the earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:That your beloved may be delivered,
VERSE:save with your right hand, and answer us.
LABEL:7
VERSE:God has spoken from his sanctuary: “In triumph,
VERSE:I will divide Shechem, and measure out the valley of Succoth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Gilead is mine. Manasseh is mine.
VERSE:Ephraim also is my helmet.
VERSE:Judah is my scepter.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Moab is my wash pot.
VERSE:I will toss my sandal on Edom.
VERSE:I will shout over Philistia.”
LABEL:10
VERSE:Who will bring me into the fortified city?
VERSE:Who will lead me to Edom?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Haven’t you rejected us, God?
VERSE:You don’t go out, God, with our armies.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Give us help against the enemy,
VERSE:for the help of man is vain.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Through God, we will do valiantly,
VERSE:for it is he who will tread down our enemies.
