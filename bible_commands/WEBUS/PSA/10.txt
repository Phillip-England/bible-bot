TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:10
TITLE:Psalms 10
LABEL:1
VERSE:Why do you stand far off, Yahweh?
VERSE:Why do you hide yourself in times of trouble?
LABEL:2
VERSE:In arrogance, the wicked hunt down the weak.
VERSE:They are caught in the schemes that they devise.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For the wicked boasts of his heart’s cravings.
VERSE:He blesses the greedy and condemns Yahweh.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The wicked, in the pride of his face,
VERSE:has no room in his thoughts for God.
LABEL:5
VERSE:His ways are prosperous at all times.
VERSE:He is arrogant, and your laws are far from his sight.
VERSE:As for all his adversaries, he sneers at them.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He says in his heart, “I shall not be shaken.
VERSE:For generations I shall have no trouble.”
LABEL:7
VERSE:His mouth is full of cursing, deceit, and oppression.
VERSE:Under his tongue is mischief and iniquity.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He lies in wait near the villages.
VERSE:From ambushes, he murders the innocent.
VERSE:His eyes are secretly set against the helpless.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He lurks in secret as a lion in his ambush.
VERSE:He lies in wait to catch the helpless.
VERSE:He catches the helpless when he draws him in his net.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The helpless are crushed.
VERSE:They collapse.
VERSE:They fall under his strength.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He says in his heart, “God has forgotten.
VERSE:He hides his face.
VERSE:He will never see it.”
LABEL:12
VERSE:Arise, Yahweh!
VERSE:God, lift up your hand!
VERSE:Don’t forget the helpless.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Why does the wicked person condemn God,
VERSE:and say in his heart, “God won’t call me into account”?
LABEL:14
VERSE:But you do see trouble and grief.
VERSE:You consider it to take it into your hand.
VERSE:You help the victim and the fatherless.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Break the arm of the wicked.
VERSE:As for the evil man, seek out his wickedness until you find none.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Yahweh is King forever and ever!
VERSE:The nations will perish out of his land.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Yahweh, you have heard the desire of the humble.
VERSE:You will prepare their heart.
VERSE:You will cause your ear to hear,
LABEL:18
VERSE:to judge the fatherless and the oppressed,
VERSE:that man who is of the earth may terrify no more.
