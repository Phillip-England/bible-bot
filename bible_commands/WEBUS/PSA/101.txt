TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:101
TITLE:Psalms 101
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will sing of loving kindness and justice.
VERSE:To you, Yahweh, I will sing praises.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will be careful to live a blameless life.
VERSE:When will you come to me?
VERSE:I will walk within my house with a blameless heart.
LABEL:3
VERSE:I will set no vile thing before my eyes.
VERSE:I hate the deeds of faithless men.
VERSE:They will not cling to me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A perverse heart will be far from me.
VERSE:I will have nothing to do with evil.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I will silence whoever secretly slanders his neighbor.
VERSE:I won’t tolerate one who is arrogant and conceited.
LABEL:6
VERSE:My eyes will be on the faithful of the land,
VERSE:that they may dwell with me.
VERSE:He who walks in a perfect way,
VERSE:he will serve me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He who practices deceit won’t dwell within my house.
VERSE:He who speaks falsehood won’t be established before my eyes.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Morning by morning, I will destroy all the wicked of the land,
VERSE:to cut off all the workers of iniquity from Yahweh’s city.
