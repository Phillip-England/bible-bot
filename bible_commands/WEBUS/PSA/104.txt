TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:104
TITLE:Psalms 104
LABEL:1
VERSE:Bless Yahweh, my soul.
VERSE:Yahweh, my God, you are very great.
VERSE:You are clothed with honor and majesty.
LABEL:2
VERSE:He covers himself with light as with a garment.
VERSE:He stretches out the heavens like a curtain.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He lays the beams of his rooms in the waters.
VERSE:He makes the clouds his chariot.
VERSE:He walks on the wings of the wind.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He makes his messengers winds,
VERSE:and his servants flames of fire.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He laid the foundations of the earth,
VERSE:that it should not be moved forever.
LABEL:6
VERSE:You covered it with the deep as with a cloak.
VERSE:The waters stood above the mountains.
LABEL:7
VERSE:At your rebuke they fled.
VERSE:At the voice of your thunder they hurried away.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The mountains rose,
VERSE:the valleys sank down,
VERSE:to the place which you had assigned to them.
LABEL:9
VERSE:You have set a boundary that they may not pass over,
VERSE:that they don’t turn again to cover the earth.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He sends springs into the valleys.
VERSE:They run among the mountains.
LABEL:11
VERSE:They give drink to every animal of the field.
VERSE:The wild donkeys quench their thirst.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The birds of the sky nest by them.
VERSE:They sing among the branches.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He waters the mountains from his rooms.
VERSE:The earth is filled with the fruit of your works.
LABEL:14
VERSE:He causes the grass to grow for the livestock,
VERSE:and plants for man to cultivate,
VERSE:that he may produce food out of the earth:
LABEL:15
VERSE:wine that makes the heart of man glad,
VERSE:oil to make his face to shine,
VERSE:and bread that strengthens man’s heart.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Yahweh’s trees are well watered,
VERSE:the cedars of Lebanon, which he has planted,
LABEL:17
VERSE:where the birds make their nests.
VERSE:The stork makes its home in the cypress trees.
LABEL:18
VERSE:The high mountains are for the wild goats.
VERSE:The rocks are a refuge for the rock badgers.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He appointed the moon for seasons.
VERSE:The sun knows when to set.
LABEL:20
VERSE:You make darkness, and it is night,
VERSE:in which all the animals of the forest prowl.
LABEL:21
VERSE:The young lions roar after their prey,
VERSE:and seek their food from God.
LABEL:22
VERSE:The sun rises, and they steal away,
VERSE:and lie down in their dens.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Man goes out to his work,
VERSE:to his labor until the evening.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Yahweh, how many are your works!
VERSE:In wisdom, you have made them all.
VERSE:The earth is full of your riches.
LABEL:25
VERSE:There is the sea, great and wide,
VERSE:in which are innumerable living things,
VERSE:both small and large animals.
LABEL:26
VERSE:There the ships go,
VERSE:and leviathan, whom you formed to play there.
LABEL:27
VERSE:These all wait for you,
VERSE:that you may give them their food in due season.
LABEL:28
VERSE:You give to them; they gather.
VERSE:You open your hand; they are satisfied with good.
LABEL:29
VERSE:You hide your face; they are troubled.
VERSE:You take away their breath; they die and return to the dust.
LABEL:30
VERSE:You send out your Spirit and they are created.
VERSE:You renew the face of the ground.
LABEL:31
VERSE:Let Yahweh’s glory endure forever.
VERSE:Let Yahweh rejoice in his works.
LABEL:32
VERSE:He looks at the earth, and it trembles.
VERSE:He touches the mountains, and they smoke.
LABEL:33
VERSE:I will sing to Yahweh as long as I live.
VERSE:I will sing praise to my God while I have any being.
LABEL:34
VERSE:Let my meditation be sweet to him.
VERSE:I will rejoice in Yahweh.
LABEL:35
VERSE:Let sinners be consumed out of the earth.
VERSE:Let the wicked be no more.
VERSE:Bless Yahweh, my soul.
VERSE:Praise Yah!
