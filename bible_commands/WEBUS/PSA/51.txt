TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:51
TITLE:Psalms 51
LABEL:1
VERSE:Have mercy on me, God, according to your loving kindness.
VERSE:According to the multitude of your tender mercies, blot out my transgressions.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Wash me thoroughly from my iniquity.
VERSE:Cleanse me from my sin.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For I know my transgressions.
VERSE:My sin is constantly before me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Against you, and you only, I have sinned,
VERSE:and done that which is evil in your sight,
VERSE:so you may be proved right when you speak,
VERSE:and justified when you judge.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Behold, I was born in iniquity.
VERSE:My mother conceived me in sin.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Behold, you desire truth in the inward parts.
VERSE:You teach me wisdom in the inmost place.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Purify me with hyssop, and I will be clean.
VERSE:Wash me, and I will be whiter than snow.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Let me hear joy and gladness,
VERSE:that the bones which you have broken may rejoice.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Hide your face from my sins,
VERSE:and blot out all of my iniquities.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Create in me a clean heart, O God.
VERSE:Renew a right spirit within me.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Don’t throw me from your presence,
VERSE:and don’t take your Holy Spirit from me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Restore to me the joy of your salvation.
VERSE:Uphold me with a willing spirit.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Then I will teach transgressors your ways.
VERSE:Sinners will be converted to you.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Deliver me from the guilt of bloodshed, O God, the God of my salvation.
VERSE:My tongue will sing aloud of your righteousness.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Lord, open my lips.
VERSE:My mouth will declare your praise.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For you don’t delight in sacrifice, or else I would give it.
VERSE:You have no pleasure in burnt offering.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit.
VERSE:O God, you will not despise a broken and contrite heart.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Do well in your good pleasure to Zion.
VERSE:Build the walls of Jerusalem.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Then you will delight in the sacrifices of righteousness,
VERSE:in burnt offerings and in whole burnt offerings.
VERSE:Then they will offer bulls on your altar.
