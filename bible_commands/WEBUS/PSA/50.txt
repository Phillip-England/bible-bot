TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:50
TITLE:Psalms 50
LABEL:1
VERSE:The Mighty One, God, Yahweh, speaks,
VERSE:and calls the earth from sunrise to sunset.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Out of Zion, the perfection of beauty,
VERSE:God shines out.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Our God comes, and does not keep silent.
VERSE:A fire devours before him.
VERSE:It is very stormy around him.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He calls to the heavens above,
VERSE:to the earth, that he may judge his people:
LABEL:5
VERSE:“Gather my saints together to me,
VERSE:those who have made a covenant with me by sacrifice.”
LABEL:6
VERSE:The heavens shall declare his righteousness,
VERSE:for God himself is judge. Selah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:“Hear, my people, and I will speak.
VERSE:Israel, I will testify against you.
VERSE:I am God, your God.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I don’t rebuke you for your sacrifices.
VERSE:Your burnt offerings are continually before me.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I have no need for a bull from your stall,
VERSE:nor male goats from your pens.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For every animal of the forest is mine,
VERSE:and the livestock on a thousand hills.
LABEL:11
VERSE:I know all the birds of the mountains.
VERSE:The wild animals of the field are mine.
LABEL:12
VERSE:If I were hungry, I would not tell you,
VERSE:for the world is mine, and all that is in it.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Will I eat the meat of bulls,
VERSE:or drink the blood of goats?
LABEL:14
VERSE:Offer to God the sacrifice of thanksgiving.
VERSE:Pay your vows to the Most High.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Call on me in the day of trouble.
VERSE:I will deliver you, and you will honor me.”
LABEL:16
VERSE:But to the wicked God says,
VERSE:“What right do you have to declare my statutes,
VERSE:that you have taken my covenant on your lips,
LABEL:17
VERSE:since you hate instruction,
VERSE:and throw my words behind you?
LABEL:18
VERSE:When you saw a thief, you consented with him,
VERSE:and have participated with adulterers.
LABEL:19
VERSE:“You give your mouth to evil.
VERSE:Your tongue frames deceit.
LABEL:20
VERSE:You sit and speak against your brother.
VERSE:You slander your own mother’s son.
LABEL:21
VERSE:You have done these things, and I kept silent.
VERSE:You thought that I was just like you.
VERSE:I will rebuke you, and accuse you in front of your eyes.
LABEL:22
VERSE:“Now consider this, you who forget God,
VERSE:lest I tear you into pieces, and there be no one to deliver.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Whoever offers the sacrifice of thanksgiving glorifies me,
VERSE:and prepares his way so that I will show God’s salvation to him.”
