TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:6
TITLE:Psalms 6
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh, don’t rebuke me in your anger,
VERSE:neither discipline me in your wrath.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Have mercy on me, Yahweh, for I am faint.
VERSE:Yahweh, heal me, for my bones are troubled.
LABEL:3
VERSE:My soul is also in great anguish.
VERSE:But you, Yahweh—how long?
LABEL:4
VERSE:Return, Yahweh. Deliver my soul,
VERSE:and save me for your loving kindness’ sake.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For in death there is no memory of you.
VERSE:In Sheol, who shall give you thanks?
LABEL:6
VERSE:I am weary with my groaning.
VERSE:Every night I flood my bed.
VERSE:I drench my couch with my tears.
LABEL:7
VERSE:My eye wastes away because of grief.
VERSE:It grows old because of all my adversaries.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Depart from me, all you workers of iniquity,
VERSE:for Yahweh has heard the voice of my weeping.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yahweh has heard my supplication.
VERSE:Yahweh accepts my prayer.
LABEL:10
VERSE:May all my enemies be ashamed and dismayed.
VERSE:They shall turn back, they shall be disgraced suddenly.
