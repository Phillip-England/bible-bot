VERSE:To you, Yahweh, I lift up my soul.

VERSE:Don’t let me be shamed.
VERSE:Don’t let my enemies triumph over me.

VERSE:Yes, no one who waits for you will be shamed.
VERSE:They will be shamed who deal treacherously without cause.

VERSE:Show me your ways, Yahweh.
VERSE:Teach me your paths.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Guide me in your truth, and teach me,
VERSE:for you are the God of my salvation.
VERSE:I wait for you all day long.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yahweh, remember your tender mercies and your loving kindness,
VERSE:for they are from old times.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Don’t remember the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions.
VERSE:Remember me according to your loving kindness,
VERSE:for your goodness’ sake, Yahweh.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Good and upright is Yahweh,
VERSE:therefore he will instruct sinners in the way.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He will guide the humble in justice.
VERSE:He will teach the humble his way.
LABEL:10
VERSE:All the paths of Yahweh are loving kindness and truth
VERSE:to such as keep his covenant and his testimonies.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For your name’s sake, Yahweh,
VERSE:pardon my iniquity, for it is great.
LABEL:12
VERSE:What man is he who fears Yahweh?
VERSE:He shall instruct him in the way that he shall choose.
LABEL:13
VERSE:His soul will dwell at ease.
VERSE:His offspring will inherit the land.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The friendship of Yahweh is with those who fear him.
VERSE:He will show them his covenant.
LABEL:15

VERSE:for he will pluck my feet out of the net.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Turn to me, and have mercy on me,
VERSE:for I am desolate and afflicted.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The troubles of my heart are enlarged.
VERSE:Oh bring me out of my distresses.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Consider my affliction and my travail.
VERSE:Forgive all my sins.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Consider my enemies, for they are many.
VERSE:They hate me with cruel hatred.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Oh keep my soul, and deliver me.
VERSE:Let me not be disappointed, for I take refuge in you.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Let integrity and uprightness preserve me,
VERSE:for I wait for you.

VERSE:out of all his troubles.
