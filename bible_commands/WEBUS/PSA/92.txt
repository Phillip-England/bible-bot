TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:92
TITLE:Psalms 92
LABEL:1
VERSE:It is a good thing to give thanks to Yahweh,
VERSE:to sing praises to your name, Most High,
LABEL:2
VERSE:to proclaim your loving kindness in the morning,
VERSE:and your faithfulness every night,
LABEL:3
VERSE:with the ten-stringed lute, with the harp,
VERSE:and with the melody of the lyre.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For you, Yahweh, have made me glad through your work.
VERSE:I will triumph in the works of your hands.
LABEL:5
VERSE:How great are your works, Yahweh!
VERSE:Your thoughts are very deep.
LABEL:6
VERSE:A senseless man doesn’t know,
VERSE:neither does a fool understand this:
LABEL:7
VERSE:though the wicked spring up as the grass,
VERSE:and all the evildoers flourish,
VERSE:they will be destroyed forever.
LABEL:8
VERSE:But you, Yahweh, are on high forever more.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For behold, your enemies, Yahweh,
VERSE:for behold, your enemies shall perish.
VERSE:All the evildoers will be scattered.
LABEL:10
VERSE:But you have exalted my horn like that of the wild ox.
VERSE:I am anointed with fresh oil.
LABEL:11
VERSE:My eye has also seen my enemies.
VERSE:My ears have heard of the wicked enemies who rise up against me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree.
VERSE:He will grow like a cedar in Lebanon.
LABEL:13
VERSE:They are planted in Yahweh’s house.
VERSE:They will flourish in our God’s courts.
LABEL:14
VERSE:They will still produce fruit in old age.
VERSE:They will be full of sap and green,
LABEL:15
VERSE:to show that Yahweh is upright.
VERSE:He is my rock,
VERSE:and there is no unrighteousness in him.
