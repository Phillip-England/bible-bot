TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:28
TITLE:Psalms 28
LABEL:1
VERSE:To you, Yahweh, I call.
VERSE:My rock, don’t be deaf to me,
VERSE:lest, if you are silent to me,
VERSE:I would become like those who go down into the pit.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Hear the voice of my petitions, when I cry to you,
VERSE:when I lift up my hands toward your Most Holy Place.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Don’t draw me away with the wicked,
VERSE:with the workers of iniquity who speak peace with their neighbors,
VERSE:but mischief is in their hearts.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Give them according to their work, and according to the wickedness of their doings.
VERSE:Give them according to the operation of their hands.
VERSE:Bring back on them what they deserve.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Because they don’t respect the works of Yahweh,
VERSE:nor the operation of his hands,
VERSE:he will break them down and not build them up.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Blessed be Yahweh,
VERSE:because he has heard the voice of my petitions.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Yahweh is my strength and my shield.
VERSE:My heart has trusted in him, and I am helped.
VERSE:Therefore my heart greatly rejoices.
VERSE:With my song I will thank him.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh is their strength.
VERSE:He is a stronghold of salvation to his anointed.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Save your people,
VERSE:and bless your inheritance.
VERSE:Be their shepherd also,
VERSE:and bear them up forever.
