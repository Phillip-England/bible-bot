TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:57
TITLE:Psalms 57
LABEL:1
VERSE:Be merciful to me, God, be merciful to me,
VERSE:for my soul takes refuge in you.
VERSE:Yes, in the shadow of your wings, I will take refuge,
VERSE:until disaster has passed.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I cry out to God Most High,
VERSE:to God who accomplishes my requests for me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He will send from heaven, and save me,
VERSE:he rebukes the one who is pursuing me. Selah.
VERSE:God will send out his loving kindness and his truth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:My soul is among lions.
VERSE:I lie among those who are set on fire,
VERSE:even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows,
VERSE:and their tongue a sharp sword.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Be exalted, God, above the heavens!
VERSE:Let your glory be above all the earth!
LABEL:6
VERSE:They have prepared a net for my steps.
VERSE:My soul is bowed down.
VERSE:They dig a pit before me.
VERSE:They fall into the middle of it themselves. Selah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:My heart is steadfast, God.
VERSE:My heart is steadfast.
VERSE:I will sing, yes, I will sing praises.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Wake up, my glory! Wake up, lute and harp!
VERSE:I will wake up the dawn.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will give thanks to you, Lord, among the peoples.
VERSE:I will sing praises to you among the nations.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For your great loving kindness reaches to the heavens,
VERSE:and your truth to the skies.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Be exalted, God, above the heavens.
VERSE:Let your glory be over all the earth.
