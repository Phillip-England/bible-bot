TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:145
TITLE:Psalms 145
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will exalt you, my God, the King.
VERSE:I will praise your name forever and ever.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Every day I will praise you.
VERSE:I will extol your name forever and ever.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Great is Yahweh, and greatly to be praised!
VERSE:His greatness is unsearchable.
LABEL:4
VERSE:One generation will commend your works to another,
VERSE:and will declare your mighty acts.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I will meditate on the glorious majesty of your honor,
VERSE:on your wondrous works.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Men will speak of the might of your awesome acts.
VERSE:I will declare your greatness.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They will utter the memory of your great goodness,
VERSE:and will sing of your righteousness.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh is gracious, merciful,
VERSE:slow to anger, and of great loving kindness.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yahweh is good to all.
VERSE:His tender mercies are over all his works.
LABEL:10
VERSE:All your works will give thanks to you, Yahweh.
VERSE:Your saints will extol you.
LABEL:11
VERSE:They will speak of the glory of your kingdom,
VERSE:and talk about your power,
LABEL:12
VERSE:to make known to the sons of men his mighty acts,
VERSE:the glory of the majesty of his kingdom.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom.
VERSE:Your dominion endures throughout all generations.
VERSE:Yahweh is faithful in all his words,
VERSE:and loving in all his deeds.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Yahweh upholds all who fall,
VERSE:and raises up all those who are bowed down.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The eyes of all wait for you.
VERSE:You give them their food in due season.
LABEL:16
VERSE:You open your hand,
VERSE:and satisfy the desire of every living thing.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Yahweh is righteous in all his ways,
VERSE:and gracious in all his works.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Yahweh is near to all those who call on him,
VERSE:to all who call on him in truth.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He will fulfill the desire of those who fear him.
VERSE:He also will hear their cry, and will save them.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Yahweh preserves all those who love him,
VERSE:but he will destroy all the wicked.
LABEL:21
VERSE:My mouth will speak the praise of Yahweh.
VERSE:Let all flesh bless his holy name forever and ever.
