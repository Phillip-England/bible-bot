TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:79
TITLE:Psalms 79
LABEL:1
VERSE:God, the nations have come into your inheritance.
VERSE:They have defiled your holy temple.
VERSE:They have laid Jerusalem in heaps.
LABEL:2
VERSE:They have given the dead bodies of your servants to be food for the birds of the sky,
VERSE:the flesh of your saints to the animals of the earth.
LABEL:3
VERSE:They have shed their blood like water around Jerusalem.
VERSE:There was no one to bury them.
LABEL:4
VERSE:We have become a reproach to our neighbors,
VERSE:a scoffing and derision to those who are around us.
LABEL:5
VERSE:How long, Yahweh?
VERSE:Will you be angry forever?
VERSE:Will your jealousy burn like fire?
LABEL:6
VERSE:Pour out your wrath on the nations that don’t know you,
VERSE:on the kingdoms that don’t call on your name,
LABEL:7
VERSE:for they have devoured Jacob,
VERSE:and destroyed his homeland.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Don’t hold the iniquities of our forefathers against us.
VERSE:Let your tender mercies speedily meet us,
VERSE:for we are in desperate need.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Help us, God of our salvation, for the glory of your name.
VERSE:Deliver us, and forgive our sins, for your name’s sake.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Why should the nations say, “Where is their God?”
VERSE:Let it be known among the nations, before our eyes,
VERSE:that vengeance for your servants’ blood is being poured out.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let the sighing of the prisoner come before you.
VERSE:According to the greatness of your power, preserve those who are sentenced to death.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Pay back to our neighbors seven times into their bosom
VERSE:their reproach with which they have reproached you, Lord.
LABEL:13
VERSE:So we, your people and sheep of your pasture,
VERSE:will give you thanks forever.
VERSE:We will praise you forever, to all generations.
