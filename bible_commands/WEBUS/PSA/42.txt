TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:42
TITLE:Psalms 42
SECTION:BOOK 2
LABEL:1
VERSE:As the deer pants for the water brooks,
VERSE:so my soul pants after you, God.
LABEL:2
VERSE:My soul thirsts for God, for the living God.
VERSE:When shall I come and appear before God?
LABEL:3
VERSE:My tears have been my food day and night,
VERSE:while they continually ask me, “Where is your God?”
LABEL:4
VERSE:These things I remember, and pour out my soul within me,
VERSE:how I used to go with the crowd, and led them to God’s house,
VERSE:with the voice of joy and praise, a multitude keeping a holy day.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Why are you in despair, my soul?
VERSE:Why are you disturbed within me?
VERSE:Hope in God!
VERSE:For I shall still praise him for the saving help of his presence.
LABEL:6
VERSE:My God, my soul is in despair within me.
VERSE:Therefore I remember you from the land of the Jordan,
VERSE:the heights of Hermon, from the hill Mizar.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Deep calls to deep at the noise of your waterfalls.
VERSE:All your waves and your billows have swept over me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh will command his loving kindness in the daytime.
VERSE:In the night his song shall be with me:
VERSE:a prayer to the God of my life.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will ask God, my rock, “Why have you forgotten me?
VERSE:Why do I go mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?”
LABEL:10
VERSE:As with a sword in my bones, my adversaries reproach me,
VERSE:while they continually ask me, “Where is your God?”
LABEL:11
VERSE:Why are you in despair, my soul?
VERSE:Why are you disturbed within me?
VERSE:Hope in God! For I shall still praise him,
VERSE:the saving help of my countenance, and my God.
