TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:16
TITLE:Psalms 16
LABEL:1
VERSE:Preserve me, God, for I take refuge in you.
LABEL:2
VERSE:My soul, you have said to Yahweh, “You are my Lord.
VERSE:Apart from you I have no good thing.”
LABEL:3
VERSE:As for the saints who are in the earth,
VERSE:they are the excellent ones in whom is all my delight.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their sorrows shall be multiplied who give gifts to another god.
VERSE:Their drink offerings of blood I will not offer,
VERSE:nor take their names on my lips.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yahweh assigned my portion and my cup.
VERSE:You made my lot secure.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places.
VERSE:Yes, I have a good inheritance.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I will bless Yahweh, who has given me counsel.
VERSE:Yes, my heart instructs me in the night seasons.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I have set Yahweh always before me.
VERSE:Because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Therefore my heart is glad, and my tongue rejoices.
VERSE:My body shall also dwell in safety.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For you will not leave my soul in Sheol,
VERSE:neither will you allow your holy one to see corruption.
LABEL:11
VERSE:You will show me the path of life.
VERSE:In your presence is fullness of joy.
VERSE:In your right hand there are pleasures forever more.
