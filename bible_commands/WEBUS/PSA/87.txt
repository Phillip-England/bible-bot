BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:87
TITLE:Psalms 87
LABEL:1
VERSE:His foundation is in the holy mountains.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh loves the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Glorious things are spoken about you, city of God. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I will record Rahab and Babylon among those who acknowledge me.
VERSE:Behold, Philistia, Tyre, and also Ethiopia:
VERSE:“This one was born there.”
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yes, of Zion it will be said, “This one and that one was born in her;”
VERSE:the Most High himself will establish her.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yahweh will count, when he writes up the peoples,
VERSE:“This one was born there.” Selah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Those who sing as well as those who dance say,
VERSE:“All my springs are in you.”
