TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:74
TITLE:Psalms 74
LABEL:1
VERSE:God, why have you rejected us forever?
VERSE:Why does your anger smolder against the sheep of your pasture?
LABEL:2
VERSE:Remember your congregation, which you purchased of old,
VERSE:which you have redeemed to be the tribe of your inheritance:
VERSE:Mount Zion, in which you have lived.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Lift up your feet to the perpetual ruins,
VERSE:all the evil that the enemy has done in the sanctuary.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Your adversaries have roared in the middle of your assembly.
VERSE:They have set up their standards as signs.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They behaved like men wielding axes,
VERSE:cutting through a thicket of trees.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Now they break all its carved work down with hatchet and hammers.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They have burned your sanctuary to the ground.
VERSE:They have profaned the dwelling place of your Name.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They said in their heart, “We will crush them completely.”
VERSE:They have burned up all the places in the land where God was worshiped.
LABEL:9
VERSE:We see no miraculous signs.
VERSE:There is no longer any prophet,
VERSE:neither is there among us anyone who knows how long.
LABEL:10
VERSE:How long, God, shall the adversary reproach?
VERSE:Shall the enemy blaspheme your name forever?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Why do you draw back your hand, even your right hand?
VERSE:Take it from your chest and consume them!
LABEL:12
VERSE:Yet God is my King of old,
VERSE:working salvation throughout the earth.
LABEL:13
VERSE:You divided the sea by your strength.
VERSE:You broke the heads of the sea monsters in the waters.
LABEL:14
VERSE:You broke the heads of Leviathan in pieces.
VERSE:You gave him as food to people and desert creatures.
LABEL:15
VERSE:You opened up spring and stream.
VERSE:You dried up mighty rivers.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The day is yours, the night is also yours.
VERSE:You have prepared the light and the sun.
LABEL:17
VERSE:You have set all the boundaries of the earth.
VERSE:You have made summer and winter.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Remember this, that the enemy has mocked you, Yahweh.
VERSE:Foolish people have blasphemed your name.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Don’t deliver the soul of your dove to wild beasts.
VERSE:Don’t forget the life of your poor forever.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Honor your covenant,
VERSE:for haunts of violence fill the dark places of the earth.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Don’t let the oppressed return ashamed.
VERSE:Let the poor and needy praise your name.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Arise, God! Plead your own cause.
VERSE:Remember how the foolish man mocks you all day.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Don’t forget the voice of your adversaries.
VERSE:The tumult of those who rise up against you ascends continually.
