TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:32
TITLE:Psalms 32
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is he whose disobedience is forgiven,
VERSE:whose sin is covered.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Blessed is the man to whom Yahweh doesn’t impute iniquity,
VERSE:in whose spirit there is no deceit.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When I kept silence, my bones wasted away through my groaning all day long.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For day and night your hand was heavy on me.
VERSE:My strength was sapped in the heat of summer. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I acknowledged my sin to you.
VERSE:I didn’t hide my iniquity.
VERSE:I said, I will confess my transgressions to Yahweh,
VERSE:and you forgave the iniquity of my sin. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For this, let everyone who is godly pray to you in a time when you may be found.
VERSE:Surely when the great waters overflow, they shall not reach to him.
LABEL:7
VERSE:You are my hiding place.
VERSE:You will preserve me from trouble.
VERSE:You will surround me with songs of deliverance. Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will instruct you and teach you in the way which you shall go.
VERSE:I will counsel you with my eye on you.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Don’t be like the horse, or like the mule, which have no understanding,
VERSE:who are controlled by bit and bridle, or else they will not come near to you.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Many sorrows come to the wicked,
VERSE:but loving kindness shall surround him who trusts in Yahweh.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Be glad in Yahweh, and rejoice, you righteous!
VERSE:Shout for joy, all you who are upright in heart!
