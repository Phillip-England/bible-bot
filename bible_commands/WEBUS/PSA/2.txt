VERSE:Why do the nations rage,
VERSE:and the peoples plot a vain thing?
LABEL:2
VERSE:The kings of the earth take a stand,
VERSE:and the rulers take counsel together,
VERSE:against Yahweh, and against his Anointed, saying,
LABEL:3
VERSE:“Let’s break their bonds apart,
VERSE:and cast their cords from us.”
LABEL:4
VERSE:He who sits in the heavens will laugh.
VERSE:The Lord will have them in derision.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Then he will speak to them in his anger,
VERSE:and terrify them in his wrath:
LABEL:6
VERSE:“Yet I have set my King on my holy hill of Zion.”
LABEL:7
VERSE:I will tell of the decree:
VERSE:Yahweh said to me, “You are my son.
VERSE:Today I have become your father.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Ask of me, and I will give the nations for your inheritance,
VERSE:the uttermost parts of the earth for your possession.
LABEL:9
VERSE:You shall break them with a rod of iron.
VERSE:You shall dash them in pieces like a potter’s vessel.”
LABEL:10
VERSE:Now therefore be wise, you kings.
VERSE:Be instructed, you judges of the earth.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Serve Yahweh with fear,
VERSE:and rejoice with trembling.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Give sincere homage to the Son, lest he be angry, and you perish on the way,
VERSE:for his wrath will soon be kindled.
VERSE:Blessed are all those who take refuge in him.
