TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:110
TITLE:Psalms 110
LABEL:1
VERSE:Yahweh says to my Lord, “Sit at my right hand,
VERSE:until I make your enemies your footstool for your feet.”
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh will send out the rod of your strength out of Zion.
VERSE:Rule among your enemies.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Your people offer themselves willingly in the day of your power, in holy array.
VERSE:Out of the womb of the morning, you have the dew of your youth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Yahweh has sworn, and will not change his mind:
VERSE:“You are a priest forever in the order of Melchizedek.”
LABEL:5
VERSE:The Lord is at your right hand.
VERSE:He will crush kings in the day of his wrath.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He will judge among the nations.
VERSE:He will heap up dead bodies.
VERSE:He will crush the ruler of the whole earth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He will drink of the brook on the way;
VERSE:therefore he will lift up his head.
