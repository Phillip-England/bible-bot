TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:89
TITLE:Psalms 89
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will sing of the loving kindness of Yahweh forever.
VERSE:With my mouth, I will make known your faithfulness to all generations.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I indeed declare, “Love stands firm forever.
VERSE:You established the heavens.
VERSE:Your faithfulness is in them.”
LABEL:3
VERSE:“I have made a covenant with my chosen one,
VERSE:I have sworn to David, my servant,
LABEL:4
VERSE:‘I will establish your offspring forever,
VERSE:and build up your throne to all generations.’” Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The heavens will praise your wonders, Yahweh,
VERSE:your faithfulness also in the assembly of the holy ones.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For who in the skies can be compared to Yahweh?
VERSE:Who among the sons of the heavenly beings is like Yahweh,
LABEL:7
VERSE:a very awesome God in the council of the holy ones,
VERSE:to be feared above all those who are around him?
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh, God of Armies, who is a mighty one, like you?
VERSE:Yah, your faithfulness is around you.
LABEL:9
VERSE:You rule the pride of the sea.
VERSE:When its waves rise up, you calm them.
LABEL:10
VERSE:You have broken Rahab in pieces, like one of the slain.
VERSE:You have scattered your enemies with your mighty arm.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The heavens are yours.
VERSE:The earth also is yours,
VERSE:the world and its fullness.
VERSE:You have founded them.
LABEL:12
VERSE:You have created the north and the south.
VERSE:Tabor and Hermon rejoice in your name.
LABEL:13
VERSE:You have a mighty arm.
VERSE:Your hand is strong, and your right hand is exalted.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Righteousness and justice are the foundation of your throne.
VERSE:Loving kindness and truth go before your face.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Blessed are the people who learn to acclaim you.
VERSE:They walk in the light of your presence, Yahweh.
LABEL:16
VERSE:In your name they rejoice all day.
VERSE:In your righteousness, they are exalted.
LABEL:17
VERSE:For you are the glory of their strength.
VERSE:In your favor, our horn will be exalted.
LABEL:18
VERSE:For our shield belongs to Yahweh,
VERSE:our king to the Holy One of Israel.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Then you spoke in vision to your saints,
VERSE:and said, “I have given strength to the warrior.
VERSE:I have exalted a young man from the people.
LABEL:20
VERSE:I have found David, my servant.
VERSE:I have anointed him with my holy oil,
LABEL:21
VERSE:with whom my hand shall be established.
VERSE:My arm will also strengthen him.
LABEL:22
VERSE:No enemy will tax him.
VERSE:No wicked man will oppress him.
LABEL:23
VERSE:I will beat down his adversaries before him,
VERSE:and strike those who hate him.
LABEL:24
VERSE:But my faithfulness and my loving kindness will be with him.
VERSE:In my name, his horn will be exalted.
LABEL:25
VERSE:I will set his hand also on the sea,
VERSE:and his right hand on the rivers.
LABEL:26
VERSE:He will call to me, ‘You are my Father,
VERSE:my God, and the rock of my salvation!’
LABEL:27
VERSE:I will also appoint him my firstborn,
VERSE:the highest of the kings of the earth.
LABEL:28
VERSE:I will keep my loving kindness for him forever more.
VERSE:My covenant will stand firm with him.
LABEL:29
VERSE:I will also make his offspring endure forever,
VERSE:and his throne as the days of heaven.
LABEL:30
VERSE:If his children forsake my law,
VERSE:and don’t walk in my ordinances;
LABEL:31
VERSE:if they break my statutes,
VERSE:and don’t keep my commandments;
LABEL:32
VERSE:then I will punish their sin with the rod,
VERSE:and their iniquity with stripes.
LABEL:33
VERSE:But I will not completely take my loving kindness from him,
VERSE:nor allow my faithfulness to fail.
LABEL:34
VERSE:I will not break my covenant,
VERSE:nor alter what my lips have uttered.
LABEL:35
VERSE:Once I have sworn by my holiness,
VERSE:I will not lie to David.
LABEL:36
VERSE:His offspring will endure forever,
VERSE:his throne like the sun before me.
LABEL:37
VERSE:It will be established forever like the moon,
VERSE:the faithful witness in the sky.” Selah.
LABEL:38
VERSE:But you have rejected and spurned.
VERSE:You have been angry with your anointed.
LABEL:39
VERSE:You have renounced the covenant of your servant.
VERSE:You have defiled his crown in the dust.
LABEL:40
VERSE:You have broken down all his hedges.
VERSE:You have brought his strongholds to ruin.
LABEL:41
VERSE:All who pass by the way rob him.
VERSE:He has become a reproach to his neighbors.
LABEL:42
VERSE:You have exalted the right hand of his adversaries.
VERSE:You have made all of his enemies rejoice.
LABEL:43
VERSE:Yes, you turn back the edge of his sword,
VERSE:and haven’t supported him in battle.
LABEL:44
VERSE:You have ended his splendor,
VERSE:and thrown his throne down to the ground.
LABEL:45
VERSE:You have shortened the days of his youth.
VERSE:You have covered him with shame. Selah.
LABEL:46
VERSE:How long, Yahweh?
VERSE:Will you hide yourself forever?
VERSE:Will your wrath burn like fire?
LABEL:47
VERSE:Remember how short my time is,
VERSE:for what vanity you have created all the children of men!
LABEL:48
VERSE:What man is he who shall live and not see death,
VERSE:who shall deliver his soul from the power of Sheol?  Selah.
LABEL:49
VERSE:Lord, where are your former loving kindnesses,
VERSE:which you swore to David in your faithfulness?
LABEL:50
VERSE:Remember, Lord, the reproach of your servants,
VERSE:how I bear in my heart the taunts of all the mighty peoples,
LABEL:51
VERSE:With which your enemies have mocked, Yahweh,
VERSE:with which they have mocked the footsteps of your anointed one.
LABEL:52
VERSE:Blessed be Yahweh forever more.
VERSE:Amen, and Amen.
