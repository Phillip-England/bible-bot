TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:138
TITLE:Psalms 138
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will give you thanks with my whole heart.
VERSE:Before the gods, I will sing praises to you.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will bow down toward your holy temple,
VERSE:and give thanks to your Name for your loving kindness and for your truth;
VERSE:for you have exalted your Name and your Word above all.
LABEL:3
VERSE:In the day that I called, you answered me.
VERSE:You encouraged me with strength in my soul.
LABEL:4
VERSE:All the kings of the earth will give you thanks, Yahweh,
VERSE:for they have heard the words of your mouth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yes, they will sing of the ways of Yahweh,
VERSE:for Yahweh’s glory is great!
LABEL:6
VERSE:For though Yahweh is high, yet he looks after the lowly;
VERSE:but he knows the proud from afar.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Though I walk in the middle of trouble, you will revive me.
VERSE:You will stretch out your hand against the wrath of my enemies.
VERSE:Your right hand will save me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh will fulfill that which concerns me.
VERSE:Your loving kindness, Yahweh, endures forever.
VERSE:Don’t forsake the works of your own hands.
