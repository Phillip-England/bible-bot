BOOK:LAM
CHAPTER:5
TITLE:Lamentations 5
LABEL:1
VERSE:Remember, Yahweh, what has come on us.
VERSE:Look, and see our reproach.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Our inheritance has been turned over to strangers,
VERSE:our houses to aliens.
LABEL:3
VERSE:We are orphans and fatherless.
VERSE:Our mothers are as widows.
LABEL:4
VERSE:We must pay for water to drink.
VERSE:Our wood is sold to us.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Our pursuers are on our necks.
VERSE:We are weary, and have no rest.
LABEL:6
VERSE:We have given our hands to the Egyptians,
VERSE:and to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Our fathers sinned, and are no more.
VERSE:We have borne their iniquities.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Servants rule over us.
VERSE:There is no one to deliver us out of their hand.
LABEL:9
VERSE:We get our bread at the peril of our lives,
VERSE:because of the sword in the wilderness.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Our skin is black like an oven,
VERSE:because of the burning heat of famine.
LABEL:11
VERSE:They ravished the women in Zion,
VERSE:the virgins in the cities of Judah.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Princes were hanged up by their hands.
VERSE:The faces of elders were not honored.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The young men carry millstones.
VERSE:The children stumbled under loads of wood.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The elders have ceased from the gate,
VERSE:and the young men from their music.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The joy of our heart has ceased.
VERSE:Our dance is turned into mourning.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The crown has fallen from our head.
VERSE:Woe to us, for we have sinned!
LABEL:17
VERSE:For this our heart is faint.
VERSE:For these things our eyes are dim:
LABEL:18
VERSE:for the mountain of Zion, which is desolate.
VERSE:The foxes walk on it.
LABEL:19
VERSE:You, Yahweh, remain forever.
VERSE:Your throne is from generation to generation.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Why do you forget us forever,
VERSE:and forsake us for so long a time?
LABEL:21
VERSE:Turn us to yourself, Yahweh, and we will be turned.
VERSE:Renew our days as of old.
LABEL:22
VERSE:But you have utterly rejected us.
VERSE:You are very angry against us.
