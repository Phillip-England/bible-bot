TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:LAM
CHAPTER:2
TITLE:Lamentations 2
LABEL:1
VERSE:How has the Lord covered the daughter of Zion with a cloud in his anger!
VERSE:He has cast the beauty of Israel down from heaven to the earth,
VERSE:and hasn’t remembered his footstool in the day of his anger.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The Lord has swallowed up all the dwellings of Jacob
VERSE:without pity.
VERSE:He has thrown down in his wrath the strongholds of the daughter of Judah.
VERSE:He has brought them down to the ground.
VERSE:He has profaned the kingdom and its princes.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He has cut off all the horn of Israel in fierce anger.
VERSE:He has drawn back his right hand from before the enemy.
VERSE:He has burned up Jacob like a flaming fire,
VERSE:which devours all around.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He has bent his bow like an enemy.
VERSE:He has stood with his right hand as an adversary.
VERSE:He has killed all that were pleasant to the eye.
VERSE:In the tent of the daughter of Zion, he has poured out his wrath like fire.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The Lord has become as an enemy.
VERSE:He has swallowed up Israel.
VERSE:He has swallowed up all her palaces.
VERSE:He has destroyed his strongholds.
VERSE:He has multiplied mourning and lamentation in the daughter of Judah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He has violently taken away his tabernacle,
VERSE:as if it were a garden.
VERSE:He has destroyed his place of assembly.
VERSE:Yahweh has caused solemn assembly and Sabbath to be forgotten in Zion.
VERSE:In the indignation of his anger, he has despised the king and the priest.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The Lord has cast off his altar.
VERSE:He has abhorred his sanctuary.
VERSE:He has given the walls of her palaces into the hand of the enemy.
VERSE:They have made a noise in Yahweh’s house,
VERSE:as in the day of a solemn assembly.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Yahweh has purposed to destroy the wall of the daughter of Zion.
VERSE:He has stretched out the line.
VERSE:He has not withdrawn his hand from destroying;
VERSE:He has made the rampart and wall lament.
VERSE:They languish together.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Her gates have sunk into the ground.
VERSE:He has destroyed and broken her bars.
VERSE:Her king and her princes are among the nations where the law is not.
VERSE:Yes, her prophets find no vision from Yahweh.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The elders of the daughter of Zion sit on the ground.
VERSE:They keep silence.
VERSE:They have cast up dust on their heads.
VERSE:They have clothed themselves with sackcloth.
VERSE:The virgins of Jerusalem hang down their heads to the ground.
LABEL:11
VERSE:My eyes fail with tears.
VERSE:My heart is troubled.
VERSE:My bile is poured on the earth,
VERSE:because of the destruction of the daughter of my people,
VERSE:because the young children and the infants swoon in the streets of the city.
LABEL:12
VERSE:They ask their mothers,
VERSE:“Where is grain and wine?”
VERSE:when they swoon as the wounded in the streets of the city,
VERSE:when their soul is poured out into their mothers’ bosom.
LABEL:13
VERSE:What shall I testify to you?
VERSE:What shall I liken to you, daughter of Jerusalem?
VERSE:What shall I compare to you,
VERSE:that I may comfort you, virgin daughter of Zion?
VERSE:For your breach is as big as the sea.
VERSE:Who can heal you?
LABEL:14
VERSE:Your prophets have seen false and foolish visions for you.
VERSE:They have not uncovered your iniquity,
VERSE:to reverse your captivity,
VERSE:but have seen for you false revelations and causes of banishment.
LABEL:15
VERSE:All that pass by clap their hands at you.
VERSE:They hiss and wag their head at the daughter of Jerusalem, saying,
VERSE:“Is this the city that men called ‘The perfection of beauty,
VERSE:the joy of the whole earth’?”
LABEL:16
VERSE:All your enemies have opened their mouth wide against you.
VERSE:They hiss and gnash their teeth.
VERSE:They say, “We have swallowed her up.
VERSE:Certainly this is the day that we looked for.
VERSE:We have found it.
VERSE:We have seen it.”
LABEL:17
VERSE:Yahweh has done that which he planned.
VERSE:He has fulfilled his word that he commanded in the days of old.
VERSE:He has thrown down,
VERSE:and has not pitied.
VERSE:He has caused the enemy to rejoice over you.
VERSE:He has exalted the horn of your adversaries.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Their heart cried to the Lord.
VERSE:O wall of the daughter of Zion,
VERSE:let tears run down like a river day and night.
VERSE:Give yourself no relief.
VERSE:Don’t let your eyes rest.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Arise, cry out in the night,
VERSE:at the beginning of the watches!
VERSE:Pour out your heart like water before the face of the Lord.
VERSE:Lift up your hands toward him for the life of your young children,
VERSE:who faint for hunger at the head of every street.
LABEL:20
VERSE:“Look, Yahweh, and see to whom you have done thus!
VERSE:Should the women eat their offspring,
VERSE:the children that they held and bounced on their knees?
VERSE:Should the priest and the prophet be killed in the sanctuary of the Lord?
LABEL:21
VERSE:“The youth and the old man lie on the ground in the streets.
VERSE:My virgins and my young men have fallen by the sword.
VERSE:You have killed them in the day of your anger.
VERSE:You have slaughtered, and not pitied.
LABEL:22
VERSE:“You have called, as in the day of a solemn assembly, my terrors on every side.
VERSE:There was no one that escaped or remained in the day of Yahweh’s anger.
VERSE:My enemy has consumed those whom I have cared for and brought up.
