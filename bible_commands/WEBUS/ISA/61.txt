TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:ISA
CHAPTER:61
TITLE:Isaiah 61
LABEL:1
VERSE:The Lord Yahweh’s Spirit is on me,
VERSE:because Yahweh has anointed me to preach good news to the humble.
VERSE:He has sent me to bind up the broken hearted,
VERSE:to proclaim liberty to the captives
VERSE:and release to those who are bound,
LABEL:2
VERSE:to proclaim the year of Yahweh’s favor
VERSE:and the day of vengeance of our God,
VERSE:to comfort all who mourn,
LABEL:3
VERSE:to provide for those who mourn in Zion,
VERSE:to give to them a garland for ashes,
VERSE:the oil of joy for mourning,
VERSE:the garment of praise for the spirit of heaviness,
VERSE:that they may be called trees of righteousness,
VERSE:the planting of Yahweh,
VERSE:that he may be glorified.
LABEL:4
VERSE:They will rebuild the old ruins.
VERSE:They will raise up the former devastated places.
VERSE:They will repair the ruined cities
VERSE:that have been devastated for many generations.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Strangers will stand and feed your flocks.
VERSE:Foreigners will work your fields and your vineyards.
LABEL:6
VERSE:But you will be called Yahweh’s priests.
VERSE:Men will call you the servants of our God.
VERSE:You will eat the wealth of the nations.
VERSE:You will boast in their glory.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Instead of your shame you will have double.
VERSE:Instead of dishonor, they will rejoice in their portion.
VERSE:Therefore in their land they will possess double.
VERSE:Everlasting joy will be to them.
LABEL:8
VERSE:“For I, Yahweh, love justice.
VERSE:I hate robbery and iniquity.
VERSE:I will give them their reward in truth
VERSE:and I will make an everlasting covenant with them.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Their offspring will be known among the nations,
VERSE:and their offspring among the peoples.
VERSE:All who see them will acknowledge them,
VERSE:that they are the offspring which Yahweh has blessed.”
LABEL:10
VERSE:I will greatly rejoice in Yahweh!
VERSE:My soul will be joyful in my God,
VERSE:for he has clothed me with the garments of salvation.
VERSE:He has covered me with the robe of righteousness,
VERSE:as a bridegroom decks himself with a garland
VERSE:and as a bride adorns herself with her jewels.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For as the earth produces its bud,
VERSE:and as the garden causes the things that are sown in it to spring up,
VERSE:so the Lord Yahweh will cause righteousness and praise to spring up before all the nations.
