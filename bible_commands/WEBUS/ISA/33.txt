TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:ISA
CHAPTER:33
TITLE:Isaiah 33
LABEL:1
VERSE:Woe to you who destroy, but you weren’t destroyed,
VERSE:and who betray, but nobody betrayed you!
VERSE:When you have finished destroying, you will be destroyed;
VERSE:and when you have finished betrayal, you will be betrayed.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yahweh, be gracious to us. We have waited for you.
VERSE:Be our strength every morning,
VERSE:our salvation also in the time of trouble.
LABEL:3
VERSE:At the noise of the thunder, the peoples have fled.
VERSE:When you lift yourself up, the nations are scattered.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Your plunder will be gathered as the caterpillar gathers.
VERSE:Men will leap on it as locusts leap.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yahweh is exalted, for he dwells on high.
VERSE:He has filled Zion with justice and righteousness.
LABEL:6
VERSE:There will be stability in your times, abundance of salvation, wisdom, and knowledge.
VERSE:The fear of Yahweh is your treasure.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Behold, their valiant ones cry outside;
VERSE:the ambassadors of peace weep bitterly.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The highways are desolate.
VERSE:The traveling man ceases.
VERSE:The covenant is broken.
VERSE:He has despised the cities.
VERSE:He doesn’t respect man.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The land mourns and languishes.
VERSE:Lebanon is confounded and withers away.
VERSE:Sharon is like a desert, and Bashan and Carmel are stripped bare.
LABEL:10
VERSE:“Now I will arise,” says Yahweh.
VERSE:“Now I will lift myself up.
VERSE:Now I will be exalted.
LABEL:11
VERSE:You will conceive chaff.
VERSE:You will give birth to stubble.
VERSE:Your breath is a fire that will devour you.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The peoples will be like the burning of lime,
VERSE:like thorns that are cut down and burned in the fire.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Hear, you who are far off, what I have done;
VERSE:and, you who are near, acknowledge my might.”
LABEL:14
VERSE:The sinners in Zion are afraid.
VERSE:Trembling has seized the godless ones.
VERSE:Who among us can live with the devouring fire?
VERSE:Who among us can live with everlasting burning?
LABEL:15
VERSE:He who walks righteously
VERSE:and speaks blamelessly,
VERSE:he who despises the gain of oppressions,
VERSE:who gestures with his hands, refusing to take a bribe,
VERSE:who stops his ears from hearing of bloodshed,
VERSE:and shuts his eyes from looking at evil—
LABEL:16
VERSE:he will dwell on high.
VERSE:His place of defense will be the fortress of rocks.
VERSE:His bread will be supplied.
VERSE:His waters will be sure.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Your eyes will see the king in his beauty.
VERSE:They will see a distant land.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Your heart will meditate on the terror.
VERSE:Where is he who counted?
VERSE:Where is he who weighed?
VERSE:Where is he who counted the towers?
LABEL:19
VERSE:You will no longer see the fierce people,
VERSE:a people of a deep speech that you can’t comprehend,
VERSE:with a strange language that you can’t understand.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Look at Zion, the city of our appointed festivals.
VERSE:Your eyes will see Jerusalem, a quiet habitation,
VERSE:a tent that won’t be removed.
VERSE:Its stakes will never be plucked up,
VERSE:nor will any of its cords be broken.
LABEL:21
VERSE:But there Yahweh will be with us in majesty,
VERSE:a place of wide rivers and streams,
VERSE:in which no galley with oars will go,
VERSE:neither will any gallant ship pass by there.
LABEL:22
VERSE:For Yahweh is our judge.
VERSE:Yahweh is our lawgiver.
VERSE:Yahweh is our king.
VERSE:He will save us.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Your rigging is untied.
VERSE:They couldn’t strengthen the foot of their mast.
VERSE:They couldn’t spread the sail.
VERSE:Then the prey of a great plunder was divided.
VERSE:The lame took the prey.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The inhabitant won’t say, “I am sick.”
VERSE:The people who dwell therein will be forgiven their iniquity.
