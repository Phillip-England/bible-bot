TITLE:Isaiah 21
LABEL:1
VERSE:The burden of the wilderness of the sea.
VERSE:As whirlwinds in the South sweep through, it comes from the wilderness, from an awesome land.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A grievous vision is declared to me. The treacherous man deals treacherously, and the destroyer destroys. Go up, Elam; attack! I have stopped all of Media’s sighing.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Therefore my thighs are filled with anguish. Pains have seized me, like the pains of a woman in labor. I am in so much pain that I can’t hear. I am so dismayed that I can’t see.
LABEL:4
VERSE:My heart flutters. Horror has frightened me. The twilight that I desired has been turned into trembling for me.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They prepare the table. They set the watch. They eat. They drink. Rise up, you princes, oil the shield!
LABEL:6
VERSE:For the Lord said to me, “Go, set a watchman. Let him declare what he sees.
LABEL:7
VERSE:When he sees a troop, horsemen in pairs, a troop of donkeys, a troop of camels, he shall listen diligently with great attentiveness.”
LABEL:8
VERSE:He cried like a lion: “Lord, I stand continually on the watchtower in the daytime, and every night I stay at my post.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Behold, here comes a troop of men, horsemen in pairs.” He answered, “Fallen, fallen is Babylon; and all the engraved images of her gods are broken to the ground.
LABEL:10
VERSE:You are my threshing, and the grain of my floor!” That which I have heard from Yahweh of Armies, the God of Israel, I have declared to you.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The burden of Dumah.
VERSE:One calls to me out of Seir, “Watchman, what of the night? Watchman, what of the night?”
LABEL:12
VERSE:The watchman said, “The morning comes, and also the night. If you will inquire, inquire. Come back again.”
LABEL:13
VERSE:The burden on Arabia.
VERSE:You will lodge in the thickets in Arabia, you caravans of Dedanites.
LABEL:14
VERSE:They brought water to him who was thirsty. The inhabitants of the land of Tema met the fugitives with their bread.
LABEL:15
VERSE:For they fled away from the swords, from the drawn sword, from the bent bow, and from the heat of battle.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For the Lord said to me, “Within a year, as a worker bound by contract would count it, all the glory of Kedar will fail,
LABEL:17
VERSE:and the residue of the number of the archers, the mighty men of the children of Kedar, will be few; for Yahweh, the God of Israel, has spoken it.”
