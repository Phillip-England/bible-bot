TRANSLATION:WEBUS
BOOK:ISA
CHAPTER:35
TITLE:Isaiah 35
LABEL:1
VERSE:The wilderness and the dry land will be glad.
VERSE:The desert will rejoice and blossom like a rose.
LABEL:2
VERSE:It will blossom abundantly,
VERSE:and rejoice even with joy and singing.
VERSE:Lebanon’s glory will be given to it,
VERSE:the excellence of Carmel and Sharon.
VERSE:They will see Yahweh’s glory,
VERSE:the excellence of our God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Strengthen the weak hands,
VERSE:and make the feeble knees firm.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Tell those who have a fearful heart, “Be strong!
VERSE:Don’t be afraid!
VERSE:Behold, your God will come with vengeance, God’s retribution.
VERSE:He will come and save you.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Then the eyes of the blind will be opened,
VERSE:and the ears of the deaf will be unstopped.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Then the lame man will leap like a deer,
VERSE:and the tongue of the mute will sing;
VERSE:for waters will break out in the wilderness,
VERSE:and streams in the desert.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The burning sand will become a pool,
VERSE:and the thirsty ground springs of water.
VERSE:Grass with reeds and rushes will be in the habitation of jackals, where they lay.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A highway will be there, a road,
VERSE:and it will be called “The Holy Way”.
VERSE:The unclean shall not pass over it,
VERSE:but it will be for those who walk in the Way.
VERSE:Wicked fools shall not go there.
LABEL:9
VERSE:No lion will be there,
VERSE:nor will any ravenous animal go up on it.
VERSE:They will not be found there;
VERSE:but the redeemed will walk there.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Then Yahweh’s ransomed ones will return,
VERSE:and come with singing to Zion;
VERSE:and everlasting joy will be on their heads.
VERSE:They will obtain gladness and joy,
VERSE:and sorrow and sighing will flee away.”
