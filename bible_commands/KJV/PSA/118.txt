VERSE:That his mercy endureth for ever.

VERSE:That his mercy endureth for ever.

VERSE:The LORD answered me, and set me in a large place.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The LORD  is on my side;
VERSE:I will not fear: What can man do unto me?
LABEL:7
VERSE:The LORD taketh my part with them that help me:
VERSE:Therefore shall I see my desire upon them that hate me.
LABEL:8

VERSE:Than to put confidence in man.
LABEL:9

VERSE:Than to put confidence in princes.

VERSE:But in the name of the LORD will I destroy them.

VERSE:But in the name of the LORD I will destroy them.
LABEL:12

VERSE:For in the name of the LORD I will destroy them.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Thou hast thrust sore at me that I might fall:
VERSE:But the LORD helped me.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The LORD  is my strength and song,
VERSE:And is become my salvation.

VERSE:The right hand of the LORD doeth valiantly.
LABEL:16

VERSE:The right hand of the LORD doeth valiantly.
LABEL:17

VERSE:And declare the works of the LORD.
LABEL:18

VERSE:But he hath not given me over unto death.
LABEL:19

VERSE:I will go into them, and I will praise the LORD:
LABEL:20

VERSE:Into which the righteous shall enter.

VERSE:And art become my salvation.
LABEL:22

VERSE:Is become the head stone of the corner.

VERSE:This is the LORD's doing;
VERSE:It is marvellous in our eyes.
LABEL:24

VERSE:We will rejoice and be glad in it.
