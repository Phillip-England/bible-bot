TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:32
TITLE:Psalm 32
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is he whose transgression is forgiven,
VERSE:Whose sin is covered.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Blessed is the man unto whom the LORD imputeth not iniquity,
VERSE:And in whose spirit there is no guile.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When I kept silence, my bones waxed old
VERSE:Through my roaring all the day long.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For day and night thy hand was heavy upon me:
VERSE:My moisture is turned into the drought of summer. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I acknowledged my sin unto thee,
VERSE:And mine iniquity have I not hid.
VERSE:I said, I will confess my transgressions unto the LORD;
VERSE:And thou forgavest the iniquity of my sin. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For this shall every one that is godly pray unto thee in a time when thou mayest be found:
VERSE:Surely in the floods of great waters they shall not come nigh unto him.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou art my hiding place; thou shalt preserve me from trouble;
VERSE:Thou shalt compass me about with songs of deliverance. Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will instruct thee and teach thee in the way which thou shalt go:
VERSE:I will guide thee with mine eye.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Be ye not as the horse, or as the mule, which have no understanding:
VERSE:Whose mouth must be held in with bit and bridle, lest they come near unto thee.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Many sorrows shall be to the wicked:
VERSE:But he that trusteth in the LORD, mercy shall compass him about.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Be glad in the LORD, and rejoice, ye righteous:
VERSE:And shout for joy, all ye that are upright in heart.
