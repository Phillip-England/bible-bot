VERSE:The LORD reigneth; let the people tremble:
VERSE:He sitteth between the cherubims; let the earth be moved.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The LORD  is great in Zion;
VERSE:And he is high above all the people.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Let them praise thy great and terrible name;
VERSE:For it is holy.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The king's strength also loveth judgment; Thou dost establish equity,
VERSE:Thou executest judgment and righteousness in Jacob.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Exalt ye the LORD our God,
VERSE:And worship at his footstool; For he is holy.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Moses and Aaron among his priests, and Samuel among them that call upon his name;
VERSE:They called upon the LORD, and he answered them.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He spake unto them in the cloudy pillar:
VERSE:They kept his testimonies, and the ordinance that he gave them.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Thou answeredst them, O LORD our God:
VERSE:Thou wast a God that forgavest them, though thou tookest vengeance of their inventions.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Exalt the LORD our God, and worship at his holy hill;
VERSE:For the LORD our God is holy.
