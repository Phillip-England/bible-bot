TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:96
TITLE:Psalm 96
LABEL:1
VERSE:O Sing unto the LORD a new song:
VERSE:Sing unto the LORD, all the earth.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing unto the LORD, bless his name;
VERSE:Shew forth his salvation from day to day.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Declare his glory among the heathen,
VERSE:His wonders among all people.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For the LORD  is great, and greatly to be praised:
VERSE:He is to be feared above all gods.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For all the gods of the nations are idols:
VERSE:But the LORD made the heavens.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Honour and majesty are before him:
VERSE:Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Give unto the LORD, O ye kindreds of the people,
VERSE:Give unto the LORD glory and strength.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Give unto the LORD the glory due unto his name:
VERSE:Bring an offering, and come into his courts.
LABEL:9
VERSE:O worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness:
VERSE:Fear before him, all the earth.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Say among the heathen that the LORD reigneth:
VERSE:The world also shall be established that it shall not be moved: He shall judge the people righteously.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad;
VERSE:Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Let the field be joyful, and all that is therein:
VERSE:Then shall all the trees of the wood rejoice
LABEL:13
VERSE:Before the LORD: for he cometh, for he cometh to judge the earth:
VERSE:He shall judge the world with righteousness, and the people with his truth.
