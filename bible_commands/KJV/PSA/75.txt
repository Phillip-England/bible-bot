TITLE:Psalm 75
LABEL:1
VERSE:Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks,
VERSE:Unto thee do we give thanks:
VERSE:For that thy name is near
VERSE:Thy wondrous works declare.
LABEL:2
VERSE:When I shall receive the congregation
VERSE:I will judge uprightly.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved:
VERSE:I bear up the pillars of it. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly:
VERSE:And to the wicked, Lift not up the horn:
LABEL:5
VERSE:Lift not up your horn on high:
VERSE:Speak not with a stiff neck.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For promotion cometh neither from the east, nor from the west,
VERSE:Nor from the south.
LABEL:7
VERSE:But God is the judge:
VERSE:He putteth down one, and setteth up another.
LABEL:8
VERSE:For in the hand of the LORD  there is a cup, and the wine is red;
VERSE:It is full of mixture; and he poureth out of the same:
VERSE:But the dregs thereof,
VERSE:All the wicked of the earth shall wring them out, and drink them.
LABEL:9
VERSE:But I will declare for ever; I will sing praises to the God of Jacob.
LABEL:10
VERSE:All the horns of the wicked also will I cut off;
VERSE:But the horns of the righteous shall be exalted.
