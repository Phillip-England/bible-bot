BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:48
TITLE:Psalm 48
LABEL:1
VERSE:Great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised
VERSE:In the city of our God, in the mountain of his holiness.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth,
VERSE:Is mount Zion, on the sides of the north,
VERSE:The city of the great King.
LABEL:3
VERSE:God is known in her palaces for a refuge.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For, lo, the kings were assembled,
VERSE:They passed by together.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They saw it, and so they marvelled;
VERSE:They were troubled, and hasted away.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Fear took hold upon them there,
VERSE:And pain, as of a woman in travail.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou breakest the ships of Tarshish
VERSE:With an east wind.
LABEL:8
VERSE:As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the LORD of hosts, in the city of our God:
VERSE:God will establish it for ever. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God,
VERSE:In the midst of thy temple.
LABEL:10
VERSE:According to thy name, O God, so is thy praise unto the ends of the earth:
VERSE:Thy right hand is full of righteousness.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let mount Zion rejoice,
VERSE:Let the daughters of Judah be glad, because of thy judgments.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Walk about Zion, and go round about her:
VERSE:Tell the towers thereof.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Mark ye well her bulwarks, consider her palaces;
VERSE:That ye may tell it to the generation following.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For this God is our God for ever and ever:
VERSE:He will be our guide even unto death.
