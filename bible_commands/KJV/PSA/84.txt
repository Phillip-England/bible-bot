VERSE:Even thine altars, O LORD of hosts, my King, and my God.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Blessed are they that dwell in thy house:
VERSE:They will be still praising thee. Selah.
LABEL:5

VERSE:in thee; In whose heart are the ways of them.
LABEL:6

VERSE:The rain also filleth the pools.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They go from strength to strength,
VERSE:Every one of them in Zion appeareth before God.
LABEL:8
VERSE:O LORD God of hosts, hear my prayer:
VERSE:Give ear, O God of Jacob. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Behold, O God our shield,
VERSE:And look upon the face of thine anointed.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For a day in thy courts
VERSE:Is better than a thousand.
VERSE:I had rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God,
VERSE:Than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For the LORD God is a sun and shield:
VERSE:The LORD will give grace and glory: No good thing will he withhold from them that walk uprightly.

VERSE:Blessed is the man that trusteth in thee.
