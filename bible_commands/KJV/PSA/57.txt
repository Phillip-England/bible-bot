TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:57
TITLE:Psalm 57
LABEL:1
VERSE:Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: For my soul trusteth in thee:
VERSE:Yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I make my refuge, until these calamities be overpast.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will cry unto God most high;
VERSE:Unto God that performeth all things for me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He shall send from heaven, and save me
VERSE:From the reproach of him that would swallow me up. Selah.
VERSE:God shall send forth his mercy and his truth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:My soul is among lions:
VERSE:And I lie even among them that are set on fire,
VERSE:Even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows, and their tongue a sharp sword.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens;
VERSE:Let thy glory be above all the earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They have prepared a net for my steps; My soul is bowed down:
VERSE:They have digged a pit before me, into the midst whereof they are fallen themselves. Selah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed:
VERSE:I will sing and give praise.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Awake up, my glory; awake, psaltery and harp:
VERSE:I myself will awake early.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will praise thee, O Lord, among the people:
VERSE:I will sing unto thee among the nations.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thy mercy is great unto the heavens,
VERSE:And thy truth unto the clouds.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens:
VERSE:Let thy glory be above all the earth.
