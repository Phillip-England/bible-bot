BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:135
TITLE:Psalm 135
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the name of the LORD;
VERSE:Praise him, O ye servants of the LORD.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Ye that stand in the house of the LORD,
VERSE:In the courts of the house of our God,
LABEL:3
VERSE:Praise the LORD; for the LORD  is good:
VERSE:Sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For the LORD hath chosen Jacob unto himself,
VERSE:And Israel for his peculiar treasure.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For I know that the LORD  is great,
VERSE:And that our Lord is above all gods.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Whatsoever the LORD pleased, that did he in heaven, and in earth,
VERSE:In the seas, and all deep places.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He causeth the vapours to ascend from the ends of the earth;
VERSE:He maketh lightnings for the rain; He bringeth the wind out of his treasuries.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Who smote the firstborn of Egypt,
VERSE:Both of man and beast.
LABEL:9
VERSE:  Who sent tokens and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt,
VERSE:Upon Pharaoh, and upon all his servants.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Who smote great nations,
VERSE:And slew mighty kings;
LABEL:11
VERSE:Sihon king of the Amorites, and Og king of Bashan,
VERSE:And all the kingdoms of Canaan:
LABEL:12
VERSE:And gave their land for an heritage,
VERSE:An heritage unto Israel his people.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Thy name, O LORD, endureth for ever;
VERSE:And thy memorial, O LORD, throughout all generations.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For the LORD will judge his people,
VERSE:And he will repent himself concerning his servants.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The idols of the heathen
VERSE:are silver and gold, the work of men's hands.
LABEL:16
VERSE:They have mouths, but they speak not;
VERSE:Eyes have they, but they see not;
LABEL:17
VERSE:They have ears, but they hear not;
VERSE:Neither is there any breath in their mouths.
LABEL:18
VERSE:They that make them are like unto them:
VERSE:So is every one that trusteth in them.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Bless the LORD, O house of Israel:
VERSE:Bless the LORD, O house of Aaron:
LABEL:20
VERSE:Bless the LORD, O house of Levi:
VERSE:Ye that fear the LORD, bless the LORD.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Blessed be the LORD out of Zion,
VERSE:Which dwelleth at Jerusalem.
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD.
