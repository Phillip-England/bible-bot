VERSE:Blessed is every one that feareth the LORD;

VERSE:Happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.
LABEL:3

LABEL:4
VERSE:Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that feareth the LORD.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The LORD shall bless thee out of Zion:
VERSE:And thou shalt see the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yea, thou shalt see thy children's children,
VERSE:And peace upon Israel.
