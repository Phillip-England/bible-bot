TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:140
TITLE:Psalm 140
LABEL:1
VERSE:Deliver me, O LORD, from the evil man:
VERSE:Preserve me from the violent man;
LABEL:2
VERSE:Which imagine mischiefs in their heart;
VERSE:Continually are they gathered together for war.
LABEL:3
VERSE:They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent;
VERSE:Adders' poison is under their lips. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Keep me, O LORD, from the hands of the wicked;
VERSE:Preserve me from the violent man; Who have purposed to overthrow my goings.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The proud have hid a snare for me, and cords; They have spread a net by the wayside;
VERSE:They have set gins for me. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I said unto the LORD, Thou art my God:
VERSE:Hear the voice of my supplications, O LORD.
LABEL:7
VERSE:O GOD the Lord, the strength of my salvation,
VERSE:Thou hast covered my head in the day of battle.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Grant not, O LORD, the desires of the wicked:
VERSE:Further not his wicked device; lest they exalt themselves. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:  As for the head of those that compass me about,
VERSE:Let the mischief of their own lips cover them.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Let burning coals fall upon them:
VERSE:Let them be cast into the fire; Into deep pits, that they rise not up again.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let not an evil speaker be established in the earth:
VERSE:Evil shall hunt the violent man to overthrow him.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I know that the LORD will maintain the cause of the afflicted,
VERSE:And the right of the poor.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Surely the righteous shall give thanks unto thy name:
VERSE:The upright shall dwell in thy presence.
