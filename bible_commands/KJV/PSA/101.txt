TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:101
TITLE:Psalm 101
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will sing of mercy and judgment:
VERSE:Unto thee, O LORD, will I sing.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will behave myself wisely
VERSE:In a perfect way.
VERSE:O when wilt thou come unto me?
VERSE:I will walk within my house with a perfect heart.
LABEL:3
VERSE:I will set no wicked thing before mine eyes: I hate the work of them that turn aside;
VERSE:It shall not cleave to me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A froward heart shall depart from me:
VERSE:I will not know a wicked person.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Whoso privily slandereth his neighbour,
VERSE:Him will I cut off:
VERSE:Him that hath an high look
VERSE:And a proud heart will not I suffer.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Mine eyes shall be upon the faithful
VERSE:Of the land, that they may dwell with me:
VERSE:He that walketh in a perfect way,
VERSE:He shall serve me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He that worketh deceit shall not dwell within my house:
VERSE:He that telleth lies shall not tarry in my sight.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will early destroy all the wicked of the land; That I may cut off
VERSE:All wicked doers from the city of the LORD.
