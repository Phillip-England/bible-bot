TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:147
TITLE:Psalm 147
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD: For it is good to sing praises unto our God;
VERSE:For it is pleasant; and praise is comely.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The LORD doth build up Jerusalem:
VERSE:He gathereth together the outcasts of Israel.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He healeth the broken in heart,
VERSE:And bindeth up their wounds.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He telleth the number of the stars;
VERSE:He calleth them all by their names.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Great is our Lord, and of great power:
VERSE:His understanding is infinite.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The LORD lifteth up the meek:
VERSE:He casteth the wicked down to the ground.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Sing unto the LORD with thanksgiving;
VERSE:Sing praise upon the harp unto our God:
LABEL:8
VERSE:Who covereth the heaven with clouds, who prepareth rain for the earth,
VERSE:Who maketh grass to grow upon the mountains.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He giveth to the beast his food,
VERSE:And to the young ravens which cry.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He delighteth not in the strength of the horse:
VERSE:He taketh not pleasure in the legs of a man.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The LORD taketh pleasure in them that fear him,
VERSE:In those that hope in his mercy.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Praise the LORD, O Jerusalem;
VERSE:Praise thy God, O Zion.
LABEL:13
VERSE:For he hath strengthened the bars of thy gates;
VERSE:He hath blessed thy children within thee.
LABEL:14
VERSE:He maketh peace in thy borders,
VERSE:And filleth thee with the finest of the wheat.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He sendeth forth his commandment upon earth:
VERSE:His word runneth very swiftly.
LABEL:16
VERSE:He giveth snow like wool:
VERSE:He scattereth the hoarfrost like ashes.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He casteth forth his ice like morsels:
VERSE:Who can stand before his cold?
LABEL:18
VERSE:He sendeth out his word, and melteth them:
VERSE:He causeth his wind to blow, and the waters flow.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He sheweth his word unto Jacob,
VERSE:His statutes and his judgments unto Israel.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He hath not dealt so with any nation:
VERSE:And as for his judgments, they have not known them.
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD.
