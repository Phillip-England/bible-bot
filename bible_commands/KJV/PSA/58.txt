TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:58
TITLE:Psalm 58
LABEL:1
VERSE:Do ye indeed speak righteousness, O congregation?
VERSE:Do ye judge uprightly, O ye sons of men?
LABEL:2
VERSE:Yea, in heart ye work wickedness;
VERSE:Ye weigh the violence of your hands in the earth.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The wicked are estranged from the womb:
VERSE:They go astray as soon as they be born, speaking lies.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their poison is like the poison of a serpent:
VERSE:They are like the deaf adder that stoppeth her ear;
LABEL:5
VERSE:Which will not hearken to the voice of charmers,
VERSE:Charming never so wisely.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Break their teeth, O God, in their mouth: Break out the great teeth of the young lions, O LORD.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Let them melt away as waters which run continually: when he bendeth his bow to shoot his arrows, let them be as cut in pieces.
LABEL:8
VERSE:As a snail which melteth, let every one of them pass away: Like the untimely birth of a woman,
VERSE:That they may not see the sun.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Before your pots can feel the thorns,
VERSE:He shall take them away as with a whirlwind, both living, and in his wrath.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The righteous shall rejoice when he seeth the vengeance:
VERSE:He shall wash his feet in the blood of the wicked.
LABEL:11
VERSE:So that a man shall say, Verily there is a reward for the righteous:
VERSE:Verily he is a God that judgeth in the earth.
