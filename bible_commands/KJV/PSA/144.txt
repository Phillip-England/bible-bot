TITLE:Psalm 144
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed be the LORD my strength,
VERSE:Which teacheth my hands to war, and my fingers to fight:
LABEL:2
VERSE:My goodness, and my fortress; My high tower, and my deliverer; My shield, and he in whom I trust;
VERSE:Who subdueth my people under me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:  LORD, what is man, that thou takest knowledge of him!
VERSE:Or the son of man, that thou makest account of him!
LABEL:4
VERSE:Man is like to vanity:
VERSE:His days are as a shadow that passeth away.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Bow thy heavens, O LORD, and come down:
VERSE:Touch the mountains, and they shall smoke.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Cast forth lightning, and scatter them:
VERSE:Shoot out thine arrows, and destroy them.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Send thine hand from above;
VERSE:Rid me, and deliver me out of great waters, from the hand of strange children;
LABEL:8
VERSE:Whose mouth speaketh vanity,
VERSE:And their right hand is a right hand of falsehood.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will sing a new song unto thee, O God:
VERSE:Upon a psaltery and an instrument of ten strings will I sing praises unto thee.
LABEL:10
VERSE:  It is he that giveth salvation unto kings:
VERSE:Who delivereth David his servant from the hurtful sword.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Rid me, and deliver me from the hand of strange children,
VERSE:Whose mouth speaketh vanity, and their right hand is a right hand of falsehood:
LABEL:12
VERSE:That our sons may be as plants grown up in their youth;
VERSE:That our daughters may be as corner stones, polished after the similitude of a palace:
LABEL:13
VERSE:  That our garners may be full, affording all manner of store:
VERSE:That our sheep may bring forth thousands and ten thousands in our streets:
LABEL:14
VERSE:  That our oxen may be strong to labour;
VERSE:That there be no breaking in, nor going out; That there be no complaining in our streets.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Happy is that people, that is in such a case:
VERSE:Yea, happy is that people, whose God is the LORD.
