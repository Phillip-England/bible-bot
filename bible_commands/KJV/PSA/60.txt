BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:60
TITLE:Psalm 60
LABEL:1
VERSE:O God, thou hast cast us off, thou has scattered us,
VERSE:Thou hast been displeased; O turn thyself to us again.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Thou hast made the earth to tremble; thou hast broken it:
VERSE:Heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Thou hast shewed thy people hard things:
VERSE:Thou hast made us to drink the wine of astonishment.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Thou hast given a banner to them that fear thee,
VERSE:That it may be displayed because of the truth. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:That thy beloved may be delivered;
VERSE:Save with thy right hand, and hear me.
LABEL:6
VERSE:God hath spoken in his holiness; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem,
VERSE:And mete out the valley of Succoth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine;
VERSE:Ephraim also is the strength of mine head; Judah is my lawgiver;
LABEL:8
VERSE:Moab is my washpot; Over Edom will I cast out my shoe:
VERSE:Philistia, triumph thou because of me.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Who will bring me into the strong city?
VERSE:Who will lead me into Edom?
LABEL:10
VERSE:  Wilt not thou, O God, which hadst cast us off?
VERSE:And thou, O God, which didst not go out with our armies?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Give us help from trouble:
VERSE:For vain is the help of man.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Through God we shall do valiantly:
VERSE:For he it is that shall tread down our enemies.
