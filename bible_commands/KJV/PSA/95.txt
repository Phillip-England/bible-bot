BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:95
TITLE:Psalm 95
LABEL:1
VERSE:O come, let us sing unto the LORD:
VERSE:Let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving,
VERSE:And make a joyful noise unto him with psalms.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For the LORD  is a great God,
VERSE:And a great King above all gods.
LABEL:4
VERSE:In his hand are the deep places of the earth:
VERSE:The strength of the hills is his also.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The sea is his, and he made it:
VERSE:And his hands formed the dry land.
LABEL:6
VERSE:O come, let us worship and bow down:
VERSE:Let us kneel before the LORD our maker.
LABEL:7
VERSE:For he is our God;
VERSE:And we are the people of his pasture, and the sheep of his hand.
VERSE:To day if ye will hear his voice,
LABEL:8
VERSE:Harden not your heart,
VERSE:As in the provocation, and as in the day of temptation in the wilderness:
LABEL:9
VERSE:When your fathers tempted me,
VERSE:Proved me, and saw my work.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Forty years long was I grieved with this generation,
VERSE:And said, It is a people that do err in their heart, and they have not known my ways:
LABEL:11
VERSE:Unto whom I sware in my wrath
VERSE:That they should not enter into my rest.
