TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:2
TITLE:Psalm 2
LABEL:1
VERSE:Why do the heathen rage,
VERSE:And the people imagine a vain thing?
LABEL:2
VERSE:The kings of the earth set themselves, and the rulers take counsel together,
VERSE:Against the LORD, and against his anointed, saying,
LABEL:3
VERSE:Let us break their bands asunder,
VERSE:And cast away their cords from us.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He that sitteth in the heavens shall laugh:
VERSE:The Lord shall have them in derision.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Then shall he speak unto them in his wrath,
VERSE:And vex them in his sore displeasure.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Yet have I set my king upon my holy hill of Zion.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I will declare the decree: the LORD hath said unto me,
VERSE:Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Ask of me, and I shall give thee the heathen for thine inheritance,
VERSE:And the uttermost parts of the earth for thy possession.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Thou shalt break them with a rod of iron;
VERSE:Thou shalt dash them in pieces like a potter's vessel.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Be wise now therefore, O ye kings:
VERSE:Be instructed, ye judges of the earth.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Serve the LORD with fear,
VERSE:And rejoice with trembling.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Kiss the Son, lest he be angry, and ye perish from the way,
VERSE:When his wrath is kindled but a little. Blessed are all they that put their trust in him.
