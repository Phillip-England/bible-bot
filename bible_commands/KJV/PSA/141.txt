TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:141
TITLE:Psalm 141
LABEL:1
VERSE:  LORD, I cry unto thee: make haste unto me;
VERSE:Give ear unto my voice, when I cry unto thee.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let my prayer be set forth before thee as incense;
VERSE:And the lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrifice.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Set a watch, O LORD, before my mouth;
VERSE:Keep the door of my lips.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Incline not my heart to any evil thing,
VERSE:To practise wicked works with men that work iniquity: And let me not eat of their dainties.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Let the righteous smite me;
VERSE:It shall be a kindness: And let him reprove me;
VERSE:It shall be an excellent oil, which shall not break my head:
VERSE:For yet my prayer also shall be in their calamities.
LABEL:6
VERSE:When their judges are overthrown in stony places,
VERSE:They shall hear my words; for they are sweet.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Our bones are scattered at the grave's mouth,
VERSE:As when one cutteth and cleaveth wood upon the earth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:But mine eyes are unto thee, O GOD the Lord:
VERSE:In thee is my trust; leave not my soul destitute.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Keep me from the snares which they have laid for me, and the gins of the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Let the wicked fall into their own nets, whilst that I withal escape.
