TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:19
TITLE:Psalm 19
LABEL:1
VERSE:The heavens declare the glory of God;
VERSE:And the firmament sheweth his handywork.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Day unto day uttereth speech,
VERSE:And night unto night sheweth knowledge.
LABEL:3
VERSE:There is no speech nor language,
VERSE:Where their voice is not heard.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their line is gone out through all the earth,
VERSE:And their words to the end of the world.
VERSE:In them hath he set a tabernacle for the sun,
LABEL:5
VERSE:Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber, and rejoiceth as a strong man to run a race.
LABEL:6
VERSE:His going forth is from the end of the heaven, and his circuit unto the ends of it:
VERSE:And there is nothing hid from the heat thereof.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The law of the LORD  is perfect, converting the soul:
VERSE:The testimony of the LORD  is sure, making wise the simple.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The statutes of the LORD  are right, rejoicing the heart:
VERSE:The commandment of the LORD  is pure, enlightening the eyes.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The fear of the LORD  is clean, enduring for ever:
VERSE:The judgments of the LORD  are true and righteous altogether.
LABEL:10
VERSE:More to be desired are they than gold, yea, than much fine gold:
VERSE:Sweeter also than honey and the honeycomb.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Moreover by them is thy servant warned:
VERSE:And in keeping of them there is great reward.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Who can understand his errors?
VERSE:Cleanse thou me from secret faults.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins; Let them not have dominion over me:
VERSE:Then shall I be upright, and I shall be innocent from the great transgression.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Let the words of my mouth, and the meditation of my heart, be acceptable in thy sight,
VERSE:O LORD, my strength, and my redeemer.
