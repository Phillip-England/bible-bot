BOOK:PSA

TITLE:Psalm 30
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will extol thee, O LORD; for thou hast lifted me up,
VERSE:And hast not made my foes to rejoice over me.
LABEL:2
VERSE:O LORD my God,
VERSE:I cried unto thee, and thou hast healed me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:O LORD, thou hast brought up my soul from the grave:
VERSE:Thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Sing unto the LORD, O ye saints of his,
VERSE:And give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For his anger endureth but a moment; in his favour is life:
VERSE:Weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning.
LABEL:6
VERSE:And in my prosperity I said, I shall never be moved.
LABEL:7
VERSE:  LORD, by thy favour thou hast made my mountain to stand strong:
VERSE:Thou didst hide thy face, and I was troubled.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I cried to thee, O LORD;
VERSE:And unto the LORD I made supplication.
LABEL:9
VERSE:What profit is there in my blood,
VERSE:When I go down to the pit?
VERSE:Shall the dust praise thee?
VERSE:Shall it declare thy truth?
LABEL:10
VERSE:Hear, O LORD, and have mercy upon me:
VERSE:LORD, be thou my helper.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou hast turned for me my mourning into dancing:
VERSE:Thou hast put off my sackcloth, and girded me with gladness;
LABEL:12
VERSE:To the end that my glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent.
VERSE:O LORD my God, I will give thanks unto thee for ever.
