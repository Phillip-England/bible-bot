TITLE:Psalm 115
LABEL:1
VERSE:Not unto us, O LORD, not unto us, but unto thy name give glory,
VERSE:For thy mercy, and for thy truth's sake.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Wherefore should the heathen say,
VERSE:Where is now their God?
LABEL:3
VERSE:But our God is in the heavens:
VERSE:He hath done whatsoever he hath pleased.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their idols are silver and gold,
VERSE:The work of men's hands.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They have mouths, but they speak not:
VERSE:Eyes have they, but they see not:
LABEL:6
VERSE:They have ears, but they hear not:
VERSE:Noses have they, but they smell not:
LABEL:7
VERSE:They have hands, but they handle not: Feet have they, but they walk not:
VERSE:Neither speak they through their throat.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They that make them are like unto them;
VERSE:So is every one that trusteth in them.
LABEL:9
VERSE:O Israel, trust thou in the LORD:
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:10
VERSE:O house of Aaron, trust in the LORD:
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Ye that fear the LORD, trust in the LORD:
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The LORD hath been mindful of us: he will bless us;
VERSE:He will bless the house of Israel; He will bless the house of Aaron.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He will bless them that fear
VERSE:the LORD, both small and great.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The LORD shall increase you more and more,
VERSE:You and your children.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Ye are blessed of the LORD
VERSE:Which made heaven and earth.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The heaven, even the heavens, are the LORD's:
VERSE:But the earth hath he given to the children of men.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The dead praise not the LORD,
VERSE:Neither any that go down into silence.
LABEL:18
VERSE:But we will bless the LORD
VERSE:From this time forth and for evermore.
VERSE:Praise the LORD.
