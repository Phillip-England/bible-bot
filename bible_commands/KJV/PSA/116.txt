VERSE:I love the LORD,
VERSE:Because he hath heard my voice and my supplications.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Because he hath inclined his ear unto me,
VERSE:Therefore will I call upon him as long as I live.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The sorrows of death compassed me,
VERSE:And the pains of hell gat hold upon me:
VERSE:I found trouble and sorrow.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Then called I upon the name of the LORD;
VERSE:O LORD, I beseech thee, deliver my soul.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Gracious is the LORD, and righteous;
VERSE:Yea, our God is merciful.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The LORD preserveth the simple:
VERSE:I was brought low, and he helped me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Return unto thy rest, O my soul;
VERSE:For the LORD hath dealt bountifully with thee.
LABEL:8
VERSE:For thou hast delivered my soul from death,
VERSE:Mine eyes from tears, and my feet from falling.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will walk before
VERSE:The LORD in the land of the living.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I believed, therefore have I spoken: I was greatly afflicted:
LABEL:11
VERSE:I said in my haste, all men are liars.
LABEL:12
VERSE:What shall I render unto the LORD
VERSE:For all his benefits toward me?
LABEL:13
VERSE:I will take the cup of salvation,
VERSE:And call upon the name of the LORD.
LABEL:14
VERSE:I will pay my vows unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Precious in the sight of the LORD  is the death of his saints.
LABEL:16
VERSE:O LORD, truly I am thy servant;
VERSE:I am thy servant, and the son of thine handmaid: Thou hast loosed my bonds.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I will offer to thee the sacrifice of thanksgiving,
VERSE:And will call upon the name of the LORD.

VERSE:I will pay my vows unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people,
LABEL:19
