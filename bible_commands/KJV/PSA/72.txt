TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:72
TITLE:Psalm 72
LABEL:1
VERSE:Give the king thy judgments, O God,
VERSE:And thy righteousness unto the king's son.
LABEL:2
VERSE:He shall judge thy people with righteousness,
VERSE:And thy poor with judgment.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The mountains shall bring peace to the people,
VERSE:And the little hills, by righteousness.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He shall judge the poor of the people,
VERSE:He shall save the children of the needy,
VERSE:And shall break in pieces the oppressor.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They shall fear thee as long as the sun and moon endure,
VERSE:Throughout all generations.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He shall come down like rain upon the mown grass:
VERSE:As showers that water the earth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:In his days shall the righteous flourish;
VERSE:And abundance of peace so long as the moon endureth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He shall have dominion also from sea to sea,
VERSE:And from the river unto the ends of the earth.
LABEL:9
VERSE:They that dwell in the wilderness shall bow before him;
VERSE:And his enemies shall lick the dust.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The kings of Tarshish and of the isles shall bring presents:
VERSE:The kings of Sheba and Seba shall offer gifts.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Yea, all kings shall fall down before him:
VERSE:All nations shall serve him.
LABEL:12
VERSE:For he shall deliver the needy when he crieth;
VERSE:The poor also, and him that hath no helper.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He shall spare the poor and needy,
VERSE:And shall save the souls of the needy.
LABEL:14
VERSE:He shall redeem their soul from deceit and violence:
VERSE:And precious shall their blood be in his sight.
LABEL:15
VERSE:And he shall live, and to him shall be given of the gold of Sheba:
VERSE:Prayer also shall be made for him continually; and daily shall he be praised.
LABEL:16
VERSE:There shall be an handful of corn in the earth upon the top of the mountains;
VERSE:The fruit thereof shall shake like Lebanon: And they of the city shall flourish like grass of the earth.
LABEL:17
VERSE:His name shall endure for ever:
VERSE:His name shall be continued as long as the sun:
VERSE:And men shall be blessed in him: All nations shall call him blessed.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Blessed be the LORD God, the God of Israel,
VERSE:Who only doeth wondrous things.
LABEL:19
VERSE:And blessed be his glorious name for ever:
VERSE:And let the whole earth be filled with his glory; Amen, and Amen.
LABEL:20
VERSE:The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended.
