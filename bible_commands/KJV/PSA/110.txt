VERSE:The LORD said unto my Lord,
VERSE:Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion:
VERSE:Rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power,
VERSE:In the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: Thou hast the dew of thy youth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent,
VERSE:Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The Lord at thy right hand
VERSE:Shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies;
VERSE:He shall wound the heads over many countries.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He shall drink of the brook in the way:
VERSE:Therefore shall he lift up the head.
