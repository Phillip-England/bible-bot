VERSE:Hear me when I call, O God of my righteousness:
VERSE:Thou hast enlarged me when I was in distress; Have mercy upon me, and hear my prayer.

VERSE:How long will ye love vanity, and seek after leasing? Selah.

VERSE:The LORD will hear when I call unto him.
LABEL:4

VERSE:Commune with your own heart upon your bed, and be still. Selah.

VERSE:Offer the sacrifices of righteousness,
VERSE:And put your trust in the LORD.
LABEL:6
VERSE:  There be many that say,
VERSE:Who will shew us any good?
VERSE:LORD, lift thou up
VERSE:The light of thy countenance upon us.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou hast put gladness in my heart,
VERSE:More than in the time that their corn and their wine increased.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will both lay me down in peace, and sleep:
VERSE:For thou, LORD, only makest me dwell in safety.
