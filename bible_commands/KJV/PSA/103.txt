TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:103
TITLE:Psalm 103
LABEL:1
VERSE:Bless the LORD, O my soul:
VERSE:And all that is within me, bless his holy name.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Bless the LORD, O my soul,
VERSE:And forget not all his benefits:
LABEL:3
VERSE:Who forgiveth all thine iniquities;
VERSE:Who healeth all thy diseases;
LABEL:4
VERSE:Who redeemeth thy life from destruction;
VERSE:Who crowneth thee with lovingkindness and tender mercies;
LABEL:5
VERSE:Who satisfieth thy mouth with good things;
VERSE:So that thy youth is renewed like the eagle's.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The LORD executeth righteousness and judgment
VERSE:For all that are oppressed.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He made known his ways unto Moses,
VERSE:His acts unto the children of Israel.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The LORD  is merciful and gracious,
VERSE:Slow to anger, and plenteous in mercy.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He will not always chide:
VERSE:Neither will he keep his anger for ever.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He hath not dealt with us after our sins;
VERSE:Nor rewarded us according to our iniquities.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For as the heaven is high above the earth,
VERSE:So great is his mercy toward them that fear him.
LABEL:12
VERSE:As far as the east is from the west,
VERSE:So far hath he removed our transgressions from us.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Like as a father pitieth his children,
VERSE:So the LORD pitieth them that fear him.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For he knoweth our frame;
VERSE:He remembereth that we are dust.
LABEL:15
VERSE:  As for man, his days are as grass:
VERSE:As a flower of the field, so he flourisheth.
LABEL:16
VERSE:For the wind passeth over it, and it is gone;
VERSE:And the place thereof shall know it no more.
LABEL:17
VERSE:But the mercy of the LORD  is from everlasting to everlasting upon them that fear him,
VERSE:And his righteousness unto children's children;
LABEL:18
VERSE:To such as keep his covenant,
VERSE:And to those that remember his commandments to do them.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The LORD hath prepared his throne in the heavens;
VERSE:And his kingdom ruleth over all.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Bless the LORD, ye his angels, that excel in strength,
VERSE:That do his commandments, hearkening unto the voice of his word.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Bless ye the LORD, all ye his hosts;
VERSE:Ye ministers of his, that do his pleasure.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Bless the LORD, all his works in all places of his dominion:
VERSE:Bless the LORD, O my soul.
