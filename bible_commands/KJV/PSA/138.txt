TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:138
TITLE:Psalm 138
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will praise thee with my whole heart:
VERSE:Before the gods will I sing praise unto thee.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will worship toward thy holy temple, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth:
VERSE:For thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.
LABEL:3
VERSE:In the day when I cried thou answeredst me,
VERSE:And strengthenedst me with strength in my soul.
LABEL:4
VERSE:All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O LORD,
VERSE:When they hear the words of thy mouth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the LORD:
VERSE:For great is the glory of the LORD.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Though the LORD  be high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly:
VERSE:But the proud he knoweth afar off.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me:
VERSE:Thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The LORD will perfect that which concerneth me:
VERSE:Thy mercy, O LORD, endureth for ever: Forsake not the works of thine own hands.
