TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:142
TITLE:Psalm 142
LABEL:1
VERSE:I cried unto the LORD with my voice;
VERSE:With my voice unto the LORD did I make my supplication.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I poured out my complaint before him;
VERSE:I shewed before him my trouble.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, then thou knewest my path.
VERSE:In the way wherein I walked have they privily laid a snare for me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I looked on my right hand,
VERSE:And beheld, but there was no man that would know me:
VERSE:Refuge failed me;
VERSE:No man cared for my soul.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I cried unto thee, O LORD:
VERSE:I said, Thou art my refuge and my portion in the land of the living.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Attend unto my cry;
VERSE:For I am brought very low:
VERSE:Deliver me from my persecutors;
VERSE:For they are stronger than I.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Bring my soul out of prison,
VERSE:That I may praise thy name:
VERSE:The righteous shall compass me about;
VERSE:For thou shalt deal bountifully with me.
