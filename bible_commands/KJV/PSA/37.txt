VERSE:Yea, thou shalt diligently consider his place, and it shall not be.

VERSE:And shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace.

VERSE:And their bows shall be broken.

LABEL:16

VERSE:Than the riches of many wicked.

VERSE:And in the days of famine they shall be satisfied.

VERSE:But the righteous sheweth mercy, and giveth.

VERSE:And they that be cursed of him shall be cut off.

VERSE:And he delighteth in his way.

VERSE:For the LORD upholdeth him with his hand.
