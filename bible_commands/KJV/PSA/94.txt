BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:94
TITLE:Psalm 94
LABEL:1
VERSE:O LORD God, to whom vengeance belongeth;
VERSE:O God, to whom vengeance belongeth, shew thyself.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Lift up thyself, thou judge of the earth:
VERSE:Render a reward to the proud.
LABEL:3
VERSE:  LORD, how long shall the wicked,
VERSE:How long shall the wicked triumph?
LABEL:4
VERSE:  How long shall they utter and speak hard things?
VERSE:And all the workers of iniquity boast themselves?
LABEL:5
VERSE:They break in pieces thy people, O LORD,
VERSE:And afflict thine heritage.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They slay the widow and the stranger,
VERSE:And murder the fatherless.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Yet they say, The LORD shall not see,
VERSE:Neither shall the God of Jacob regard it.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Understand, ye brutish among the people:
VERSE:And ye fools, when will ye be wise?
LABEL:9
VERSE:He that planted the ear, shall he not hear?
VERSE:He that formed the eye, shall he not see?
LABEL:10
VERSE:He that chastiseth the heathen, shall not he correct?
VERSE:He that teacheth man knowledge, shall not he know?
LABEL:11
VERSE:The LORD knoweth the thoughts of man,
VERSE:That they are vanity.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Blessed is the man whom thou chastenest, O LORD,
VERSE:And teachest him out of thy law;
LABEL:13
VERSE:That thou mayest give him rest from the days of adversity,
VERSE:Until the pit be digged for the wicked.
LABEL:14
VERSE:For the LORD will not cast off his people,
VERSE:Neither will he forsake his inheritance.
LABEL:15
VERSE:But judgment shall return unto righteousness:
VERSE:And all the upright in heart shall follow it.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Who will rise up for me against the evildoers?
VERSE:Or who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?
LABEL:17
VERSE:Unless the LORD  had been my help,
VERSE:My soul had almost dwelt in silence.
LABEL:18
VERSE:When I said, My foot slippeth;
VERSE:Thy mercy, O LORD, held me up.
LABEL:19
VERSE:In the multitude of my thoughts within me
VERSE:Thy comforts delight my soul.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Shall the throne of iniquity have fellowship with thee,
VERSE:Which frameth mischief by a law?
LABEL:21
VERSE:They gather themselves together against the soul of the righteous,
VERSE:And condemn the innocent blood.
LABEL:22
VERSE:But the LORD is my defence;
VERSE:And my God is the rock of my refuge.
LABEL:23
VERSE:And he shall bring upon them their own iniquity, and shall cut them off in their own wickedness;
VERSE:Yea, the LORD our God shall cut them off.
