TITLE:Psalm 82
LABEL:1
VERSE:God standeth in the congregation of the mighty;
VERSE:He judgeth among the gods.
LABEL:2
VERSE:How long will ye judge unjustly,
VERSE:And accept the persons of the wicked? Selah.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Defend the poor and fatherless:
VERSE:Do justice to the afflicted and needy.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Deliver the poor and needy:
VERSE:Rid them out of the hand of the wicked.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They know not, neither will they understand; They walk on in darkness:
VERSE:All the foundations of the earth are out of course.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I have said, Ye are gods;
VERSE:And all of you are children of the most High.
LABEL:7
VERSE:But ye shall die like men,
VERSE:And fall like one of the princes.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Arise, O God, judge the earth:
VERSE:For thou shalt inherit all nations.
