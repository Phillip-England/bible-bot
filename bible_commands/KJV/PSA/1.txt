TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:1
TITLE:Psalm 1
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners,
VERSE:Nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.
LABEL:2
VERSE:But his delight is in the law of the LORD;
VERSE:And in his law doth he meditate day and night.
LABEL:3
VERSE:And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water,
VERSE:That bringeth forth his fruit in his season;
VERSE:His leaf also shall not wither;
VERSE:And whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The ungodly are not so:
VERSE:But are like the chaff which the wind driveth away.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment,
VERSE:Nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous:
VERSE:But the way of the ungodly shall perish.
