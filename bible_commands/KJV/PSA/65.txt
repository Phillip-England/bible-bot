TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:65
TITLE:Psalm 65
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise waiteth for thee, O God, in Zion:
VERSE:And unto thee shall the vow be performed.
LABEL:2
VERSE:O thou that hearest prayer,
VERSE:Unto thee shall all flesh come.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Iniquities prevail against me:
VERSE:As for our transgressions, thou shalt purge them away.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Blessed is the man whom thou choosest, and causest to approach unto thee,
VERSE:That he may dwell in thy courts: We shall be satisfied with the goodness of thy house, even of thy holy temple.
LABEL:5
VERSE:  By terrible things in righteousness wilt thou answer us, O God of our salvation;
VERSE:Who art the confidence of all the ends of the earth, and of them that are afar off upon the sea:
LABEL:6
VERSE:Which by his strength setteth fast the mountains;
VERSE:Being girded with power:
LABEL:7
VERSE:Which stilleth the noise of the seas,
VERSE:the noise of their waves, and the tumult of the people.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They also that dwell in the uttermost parts are afraid at thy tokens:
VERSE:Thou makest the outgoings of the morning and evening to rejoice.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Thou visitest the earth, and waterest it:
VERSE:Thou greatly enrichest it
VERSE:With the river of God, which is full of water:
VERSE:Thou preparest them corn, when thou hast so provided for it.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Thou waterest the ridges thereof abundantly: Thou settlest the furrows thereof:
VERSE:Thou makest it soft with showers: Thou blessest the springing thereof.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou crownest the year with thy goodness;
VERSE:And thy paths drop fatness.
LABEL:12
VERSE:They drop upon the pastures of the wilderness:
VERSE:And the little hills rejoice on every side.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The pastures are clothed with flocks;
VERSE:The valleys also are covered over with corn; They shout for joy, they also sing.
