TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:97
TITLE:Psalm 97
LABEL:1
VERSE:The LORD reigneth; let the earth rejoice;
VERSE:Let the multitude of isles be glad thereof.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Clouds and darkness are round about him:
VERSE:Righteousness and judgment are the habitation of his throne.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A fire goeth before him,
VERSE:And burneth up his enemies round about.
LABEL:4
VERSE:His lightnings enlightened the world:
VERSE:The earth saw, and trembled.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The hills melted like wax at the presence of the LORD,
VERSE:At the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The heavens declare his righteousness,
VERSE:And all the people see his glory.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Confounded be all they that serve graven images,
VERSE:That boast themselves of idols: Worship him, all ye gods.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Zion heard, and was glad;
VERSE:And the daughters of Judah rejoiced because of thy judgments, O LORD.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For thou, LORD, art high above all the earth:
VERSE:Thou art exalted far above all gods.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Ye that love the LORD, hate evil:
VERSE:He preserveth the souls of his saints; He delivereth them out of the hand of the wicked.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Light is sown for the righteous,
VERSE:And gladness for the upright in heart.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Rejoice in the LORD, ye righteous;
VERSE:And give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness.
