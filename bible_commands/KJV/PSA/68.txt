TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:68
TITLE:Psalm 68
LABEL:1
VERSE:Let God arise, let his enemies be scattered:
VERSE:Let them also that hate him flee before him.
LABEL:2
VERSE:As smoke is driven away, so drive them away:
VERSE:As wax melteth before the fire, so let the wicked perish at the presence of God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:But let the righteous be glad; let them rejoice before God:
VERSE:Yea, let them exceedingly rejoice.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Sing unto God,
VERSE:Sing praises to his name:
VERSE:Extol him that rideth upon the heavens
VERSE:By his name JAH, and rejoice before him.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A father of the fatherless, and a judge of the widows,
VERSE:Is God in his holy habitation.
LABEL:6
VERSE:God setteth the solitary in families: He bringeth out those which are bound with chains:
VERSE:But the rebellious dwell in a dry land.
LABEL:7
VERSE:O God, when thou wentest forth before thy people,
VERSE:When thou didst march through the wilderness; Selah:
LABEL:8
VERSE:The earth shook, the heavens also dropped at the presence of God:
VERSE:Even Sinai itself was moved
VERSE:At the presence of God, the God of Israel.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Thou, O God, didst send a plentiful rain, whereby thou didst confirm thine inheritance,
VERSE:When it was weary.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Thy congregation hath dwelt therein:
VERSE:Thou, O God, hast prepared of thy goodness for the poor.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The Lord gave the word:
VERSE:Great was the company of those that published it.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Kings of armies did flee apace:
VERSE:And she that tarried at home divided the spoil.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Though ye have lien among the pots, yet shall ye be as the wings of a dove
VERSE:Covered with silver, and her feathers with yellow gold.
LABEL:14
VERSE:When the Almighty scattered kings in it,
VERSE:It was white as snow in Salmon.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The hill of God is as the hill of Bashan;
VERSE:An high hill as the hill of Bashan.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Why leap ye, ye high hills? This is the hill which God desireth to dwell in;
VERSE:Yea, the LORD will dwell in it for ever.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The chariots of God are twenty thousand, even thousands of angels:
VERSE:The Lord is among them, as in Sinai, in the holy place.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Thou hast ascended on high, thou hast led captivity captive: Thou hast received gifts for men;
VERSE:Yea, for the rebellious also, that the Lord GOD might dwell among them.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Blessed be the Lord,
VERSE:Who daily loadeth us with benefits, even the God of our salvation. Selah.
LABEL:20
VERSE:  He that is our God is the God of salvation;
VERSE:And unto GOD the Lord belong the issues from death.
LABEL:21
VERSE:But God shall wound the head of his enemies,
VERSE:And the hairy scalp of such an one as goeth on still in his trespasses.
LABEL:22
VERSE:The Lord said, I will bring again from Bashan, I will bring my people again
VERSE:from the depths of the sea:
LABEL:23
VERSE:That thy foot may be dipped in the blood of thine enemies,
VERSE:And the tongue of thy dogs in the same.
LABEL:24
VERSE:They have seen thy goings, O God;
VERSE:Even the goings of my God, my King, in the sanctuary.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The singers went before, the players on instruments followed after;
VERSE:Among them were the damsels playing with timbrels.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Bless ye God in the congregations,
VERSE:Even the Lord, from the fountain of Israel.
LABEL:27
VERSE:There is little Benjamin with their ruler, the princes of Judah and their council,
VERSE:The princes of Zebulun, and the princes of Naphtali.
LABEL:28
VERSE:Thy God hath commanded thy strength:
VERSE:Strengthen, O God, that which thou hast wrought for us.
LABEL:29
VERSE:Because of thy temple at Jerusalem
VERSE:Shall kings bring presents unto thee.
LABEL:30
VERSE:Rebuke the company of spearmen,
VERSE:The multitude of the bulls, with the calves of the people,
VERSE:Till every one submit himself with pieces of silver:
VERSE:Scatter thou the people that delight in war.
LABEL:31
VERSE:Princes shall come out of Egypt;
VERSE:Ethiopia shall soon stretch out her hands unto God.
LABEL:32
VERSE:Sing unto God, ye kingdoms of the earth;
VERSE:O sing praises unto the Lord; Selah:
LABEL:33
VERSE:To him that rideth upon the heavens of heavens, which were of old;
VERSE:Lo, he doth send out his voice, and that a mighty voice.
LABEL:34
VERSE:Ascribe ye strength unto God: His excellency is over Israel,
VERSE:And his strength is in the clouds.
LABEL:35
VERSE:O God, thou art terrible out of thy holy places:
VERSE:The God of Israel is he that giveth strength and power unto his people. Blessed be God.
