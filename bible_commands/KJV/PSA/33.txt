TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:33
TITLE:Psalm 33
LABEL:1
VERSE:Rejoice in the LORD, O ye righteous:
VERSE:For praise is comely for the upright.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Praise the LORD with harp:
VERSE:Sing unto him with the psaltery and an instrument of ten strings.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Sing unto him a new song;
VERSE:Play skilfully with a loud noise.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For the word of the LORD  is right;
VERSE:And all his works are done in truth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He loveth righteousness and judgment:
VERSE:The earth is full of the goodness of the LORD.
LABEL:6
VERSE:By the word of the LORD were the heavens made;
VERSE:And all the host of them by the breath of his mouth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He gathereth the waters of the sea together as an heap:
VERSE:He layeth up the depth in storehouses.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Let all the earth fear the LORD:
VERSE:Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For he spake, and it was done;
VERSE:He commanded, and it stood fast.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The LORD bringeth the counsel of the heathen to nought:
VERSE:He maketh the devices of the people of none effect.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The counsel of the LORD standeth for ever,
VERSE:The thoughts of his heart to all generations.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Blessed is the nation whose God is the LORD;
VERSE:And the people whom he hath chosen for his own inheritance.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The LORD looketh from heaven; he beholdeth all the sons of men.
LABEL:14
VERSE:From the place of his habitation he looketh upon all the inhabitants of the earth.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He fashioneth their hearts alike;
VERSE:He considereth all their works.
LABEL:16
VERSE:There is no king saved by the multitude of an host:
VERSE:A mighty man is not delivered by much strength.
LABEL:17
VERSE:An horse is a vain thing for safety:
VERSE:Neither shall he deliver any by his great strength.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Behold, the eye of the LORD  is upon them that fear him,
VERSE:Upon them that hope in his mercy;
LABEL:19
VERSE:To deliver their soul from death,
VERSE:And to keep them alive in famine.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Our soul waiteth for the LORD:
VERSE:He is our help and our shield.
LABEL:21
VERSE:For our heart shall rejoice in him,
VERSE:Because we have trusted in his holy name.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Let thy mercy, O LORD, be upon us,
VERSE:According as we hope in thee.
