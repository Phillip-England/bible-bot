BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:91
TITLE:Psalm 91
LABEL:1
VERSE:He that dwelleth in the secret place of the Most High
VERSE:Shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress:
VERSE:My God; in him will I trust.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler,
VERSE:And from the noisome pestilence.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust:
VERSE:His truth shall be thy shield and buckler.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night;
VERSE:Nor for the arrow that flieth by day;
LABEL:6
VERSE:  Nor for the pestilence that walketh in darkness;
VERSE:Nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday.
LABEL:7
VERSE:A thousand shall fall at thy side, and ten thousand at thy right hand;
VERSE:But it shall not come nigh thee.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold
VERSE:And see the reward of the wicked.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Because thou hast made the LORD, which is my refuge,
VERSE:Even the most High, thy habitation;
LABEL:10
VERSE:There shall no evil befall thee,
VERSE:Neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For he shall give his angels charge over thee,
VERSE:To keep thee in all thy ways.
LABEL:12
VERSE:They shall bear thee up in their hands,
VERSE:Lest thou dash thy foot against a stone.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder:
VERSE:The young lion and the dragon shalt thou trample under feet.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him:
VERSE:I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He shall call upon me, and I will answer him:
VERSE:I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him, and honour him.
LABEL:16
VERSE:With long life will I satisfy him,
VERSE:And shew him my salvation.
