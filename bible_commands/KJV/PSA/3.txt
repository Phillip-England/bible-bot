VERSE:  LORD, how are they increased that trouble me!

LABEL:2

VERSE:There is no help for him in God. Selah.
LABEL:3

VERSE:My glory, and the lifter up of mine head.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I cried unto the LORD with my voice,
VERSE:And he heard me out of his holy hill. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I laid me down and slept;
VERSE:I awaked; for the LORD sustained me.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people,
VERSE:That have set themselves against me round about.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God:
VERSE:For thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the cheek bone;
VERSE:Thou hast broken the teeth of the ungodly.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Salvation belongeth unto the LORD:
VERSE:Thy blessing is upon thy people. Selah.
