TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:42
TITLE:Psalm 42
LABEL:1
VERSE:As the hart panteth after the water brooks,
VERSE:So panteth my soul after thee, O God.
LABEL:2
VERSE:My soul thirsteth for God, for the living God:
VERSE:When shall I come and appear before God?
LABEL:3
VERSE:My tears have been my meat day and night,
VERSE:While they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?
LABEL:4
VERSE:When I remember these things, I pour out my soul in me:
VERSE:For I had gone with the multitude, I went with them to the house of God,
VERSE:With the voice of joy and praise,
VERSE:With a multitude that kept holyday.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
VERSE:And why art thou disquieted in me?
VERSE:Hope thou in God:
VERSE:For I shall yet praise him For the help of his countenance.
LABEL:6
VERSE:O my God, my soul is cast down within me:
VERSE:Therefore will I remember thee from the land of Jordan, and of the Hermonites, from the hill Mizar.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Deep calleth unto deep at the noise of thy waterspouts:
VERSE:All thy waves and thy billows are gone over me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:  Yet the LORD will command his lovingkindness in the daytime,
VERSE:And in the night his song shall be with me, and my prayer unto the God of my life.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will say unto God my rock, why hast thou forgotten me?
VERSE:Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
LABEL:10
VERSE:  As with a sword in my bones,
VERSE:Mine enemies reproach me;
VERSE:While they say daily unto me,
VERSE:Where is thy God?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
VERSE:And why art thou disquieted within me?
VERSE:Hope thou in God;
VERSE:For I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God.
