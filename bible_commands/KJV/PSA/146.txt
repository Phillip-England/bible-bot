TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:146
TITLE:Psalm 146
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD. Praise the LORD, O my soul.
LABEL:2
VERSE:While I live will I praise the LORD: I will sing praises unto my God while I have any being.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man,
VERSE:In whom there is no help.
LABEL:4
VERSE:His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth;
VERSE:In that very day his thoughts perish.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Happy is he that hath the God of Jacob for his help,
VERSE:Whose hope is in the LORD his God:
LABEL:6
VERSE:Which made heaven, and earth, the sea, and all that therein is:
VERSE:Which keepeth truth for ever:
LABEL:7
VERSE:Which executeth judgment for the oppressed:
VERSE:Which giveth food to the hungry.
VERSE:The LORD looseth the prisoners:
LABEL:8
VERSE:The LORD openeth the eyes of the blind:
VERSE:The LORD raiseth them that are bowed down:
VERSE:The LORD loveth the righteous:
LABEL:9
VERSE:The LORD preserveth the strangers; He relieveth the fatherless and widow:
VERSE:But the way of the wicked he turneth upside down.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The LORD shall reign for ever, even thy God, O Zion,
VERSE:Unto all generations.
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD.
