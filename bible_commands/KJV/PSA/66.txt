TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:66
TITLE:Psalm 66
LABEL:1
VERSE:Make a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands:
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing forth the honour of his name: Make his praise glorious.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Say unto God, How terrible art thou in thy works!
VERSE:Through the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves unto thee.
LABEL:4
VERSE:All the earth shall worship thee,
VERSE:And shall sing unto thee; They shall sing to thy name. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Come and see the works of God:
VERSE:He is terrible in his doing toward the children of men.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He turned the sea into dry land:
VERSE:They went through the flood on foot: There did we rejoice in him.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He ruleth by his power for ever; His eyes behold the nations:
VERSE:Let not the rebellious exalt themselves. Selah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:O bless our God, ye people,
VERSE:And make the voice of his praise to be heard:
LABEL:9
VERSE:Which holdeth our soul in life,
VERSE:And suffereth not our feet to be moved.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thou, O God, hast proved us:
VERSE:Thou hast tried us, as silver is tried.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou broughtest us into the net;
VERSE:Thou laidst affliction upon our loins.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Thou hast caused men to ride over our heads;
VERSE:We went through fire and through water: But thou broughtest us out into a wealthy place.
LABEL:13
VERSE:I will go into thy house with burnt offerings:
VERSE:I will pay thee my vows,
LABEL:14
VERSE:which my lips have uttered, and my mouth hath spoken, when I was in trouble.
LABEL:15
VERSE:I will offer unto thee burnt sacrifices of fatlings, with the incense of rams;
VERSE:I will offer bullocks with goats. Selah.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Come and hear, all ye that fear God,
VERSE:And I will declare what he hath done for my soul.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I cried unto him with my mouth,
VERSE:And he was extolled with my tongue.
LABEL:18
VERSE:If I regard iniquity in my heart,
VERSE:The Lord will not hear me:
LABEL:19
VERSE:  But verily God hath heard me;
VERSE:He hath attended to the voice of my prayer.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Blessed be God, which hath not turned away my prayer,
VERSE:Nor his mercy from me.
