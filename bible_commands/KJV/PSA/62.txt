TITLE:Psalm 62
LABEL:1
VERSE:Truly my soul waiteth upon God:
VERSE:From him cometh my salvation.
LABEL:2
VERSE:He only is my rock and my salvation;
VERSE:He is my defence; I shall not be greatly moved.
LABEL:3
VERSE:How long will ye imagine mischief against a man?
VERSE:Ye shall be slain all of you: As a bowing wall shall ye be, and as a tottering fence.
LABEL:4
VERSE:They only consult to cast him down from his excellency:
VERSE:They delight in lies: They bless with their mouth, but they curse inwardly. Selah.
LABEL:5
VERSE:My soul, wait thou only upon God;
VERSE:For my expectation is from him.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He only is my rock and my salvation:
VERSE:He is my defence; I shall not be moved.
LABEL:7
VERSE:In God is my salvation and my glory:
VERSE:The rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Trust in him at all times; ye people,
VERSE:Pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. Selah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Surely men of low degree are vanity,
VERSE:and men of high degree are a lie: To be laid in the balance, they are altogether lighter than vanity.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Trust not in oppression, and become not vain in robbery:
VERSE:If riches increase, set not your heart upon them.
LABEL:11
VERSE:God hath spoken once; Twice have I heard this;
VERSE:That power belongeth unto God.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Also unto thee, O Lord, belongeth mercy:
VERSE:For thou renderest to every man according to his work.
