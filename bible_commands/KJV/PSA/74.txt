TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:74
TITLE:Psalm 74
LABEL:1
VERSE:O God, why hast thou cast us off for ever?
VERSE:Why doth thine anger smoke against the sheep of thy pasture?
LABEL:2
VERSE:Remember thy congregation, which thou hast purchased of old;
VERSE:The rod of thine inheritance, which thou hast redeemed;
VERSE:This mount Zion, wherein thou hast dwelt.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Lift up thy feet unto the perpetual desolations;
VERSE:Even all that the enemy
VERSE:hath done wickedly in the sanctuary.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Thine enemies roar in the midst of thy congregations;
VERSE:They set up their ensigns for signs.
LABEL:5
VERSE:  A man was famous according as he had lifted up axes upon the thick trees.
LABEL:6
VERSE:But now they break down the carved work
VERSE:Thereof at once with axes and hammers.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They have cast fire into thy sanctuary,
VERSE:They have defiled by casting down the dwelling place of thy name to the ground.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They said in their hearts, Let us destroy them together:
VERSE:They have burned up all the synagogues of God in the land.
LABEL:9
VERSE:We see not our signs: There is no more any prophet:
VERSE:Neither is there among us any that knoweth how long.
LABEL:10
VERSE:O God, how long shall the adversary reproach?
VERSE:Shall the enemy blaspheme thy name for ever?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Why withdrawest thou thy hand, even thy right hand?
VERSE:Pluck it out of thy bosom.
LABEL:12
VERSE:For God is my King of old,
VERSE:Working salvation in the midst of the earth.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Thou didst divide the sea by thy strength:
VERSE:Thou brakest the heads of the dragons in the waters.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Thou brakest the heads of leviathan in pieces,
VERSE:And gavest him to be meat to the people inhabiting the wilderness.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Thou didst cleave the fountain and the flood:
VERSE:Thou driedst up mighty rivers.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The day is thine, the night also is thine:
VERSE:Thou hast prepared the light and the sun.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Thou hast set all the borders of the earth:
VERSE:Thou hast made summer and winter.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Remember this, that the enemy hath reproached, O LORD,
VERSE:And that the foolish people have blasphemed thy name.
LABEL:19
VERSE:O deliver not the soul of thy turtledove unto the multitude of the wicked:
VERSE:Forget not the congregation of thy poor for ever.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Have respect unto the covenant:
VERSE:For the dark places of the earth are full of the habitations of cruelty.
LABEL:21
VERSE:O let not the oppressed return ashamed:
VERSE:Let the poor and needy praise thy name.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Arise, O God, plead thine own cause:
VERSE:Remember how the foolish man reproacheth thee daily.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Forget not the voice of thine enemies:
VERSE:The tumult of those that rise up against thee increaseth continually.
