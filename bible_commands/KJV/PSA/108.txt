TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:108
TITLE:Psalm 108
LABEL:1
VERSE:O God, my heart is fixed;
VERSE:I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Awake, psaltery and harp:
VERSE:I myself will awake early.
LABEL:3
VERSE:I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people:
VERSE:And I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For thy mercy is great above the heavens:
VERSE:And thy truth reacheth unto the clouds.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens:
VERSE:And thy glory above all the earth;
LABEL:6
VERSE:That thy beloved may be delivered:
VERSE:Save with thy right hand, and answer me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:God hath spoken in his holiness;
VERSE:I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine;
VERSE:Ephraim also is the strength of mine head;
VERSE:Judah is my lawgiver;
LABEL:9
VERSE:Moab is my washpot;
VERSE:Over Edom will I cast out my shoe; Over Philistia will I triumph.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Who will bring me into the strong city?
VERSE:Who will lead me into Edom?
LABEL:11
VERSE:  Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off?
VERSE:And wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?
LABEL:12
VERSE:Give us help from trouble:
VERSE:For vain is the help of man.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Through God we shall do valiantly:
VERSE:For he it is that shall tread down our enemies.
