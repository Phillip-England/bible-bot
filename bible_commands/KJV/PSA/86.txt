TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:86
TITLE:Psalm 86
LABEL:1
VERSE:Bow down thine ear, O LORD, hear me:
VERSE:For I am poor and needy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Preserve my soul; for I am holy:
VERSE:O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Be merciful unto me, O Lord:
VERSE:For I cry unto thee daily.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Rejoice the soul of thy servant:
VERSE:For unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive;
VERSE:And plenteous in mercy unto all them that call upon thee.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Give ear, O LORD, unto my prayer;
VERSE:And attend to the voice of my supplications.
LABEL:7
VERSE:In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee:
VERSE:For thou wilt answer me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Among the gods there is none like unto thee, O Lord;
VERSE:Neither are there any works like unto thy works.
LABEL:9
VERSE:All nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord;
VERSE:And shall glorify thy name.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thou art great, and doest wondrous things:
VERSE:Thou art God alone.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Teach me thy way, O LORD; I will walk in thy truth:
VERSE:Unite my heart to fear thy name.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with all my heart:
VERSE:And I will glorify thy name for evermore.
LABEL:13
VERSE:For great is thy mercy toward me:
VERSE:And thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest hell.
LABEL:14
VERSE:O God, the proud are risen against me,
VERSE:And the assemblies of violent men have sought after my soul; And have not set thee before them.
LABEL:15
VERSE:But thou, O Lord, art a God full of compassion, and gracious,
VERSE:Longsuffering, and plenteous in mercy and truth.
LABEL:16
VERSE:O turn unto me, and have mercy upon me;
VERSE:Give thy strength unto thy servant, and save the son of thine handmaid.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Shew me a token for good; That they which hate me may see it, and be ashamed:
VERSE:Because thou, LORD, hast holpen me, and comforted me.
