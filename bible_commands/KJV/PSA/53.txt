TITLE:Psalm 53
LABEL:1
VERSE:The fool hath said in his heart,
VERSE:There is no God.
VERSE:Corrupt are they, and have done abominable iniquity:
VERSE:There is none that doeth good.
LABEL:2
VERSE:God looked down from heaven upon the children of men,
VERSE:To see if there were any that did understand, that did seek God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Every one of them is gone back: they are altogether become filthy;
VERSE:There is none that doeth good, no, not one.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Have the workers of iniquity no knowledge?
VERSE:Who eat up my people as they eat bread: They have not called upon God.
LABEL:5
VERSE:There were they in great fear, where no fear was:
VERSE:For God hath scattered the bones of him that encampeth against thee:
VERSE:Thou hast put them to shame,
VERSE:because God hath despised them.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Oh that the salvation of Israel were come out of Zion!
VERSE:When God bringeth back the captivity of his people,
VERSE:Jacob shall rejoice,
VERSE:And Israel shall be glad.
