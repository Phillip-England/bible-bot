TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:26
TITLE:Psalm 26
LABEL:1
VERSE:Judge me, O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity:
VERSE:I have trusted also in the LORD; therefore I shall not slide.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Examine me, O LORD, and prove me;
VERSE:Try my reins and my heart.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes:
VERSE:And I have walked in thy truth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I have not sat with vain persons,
VERSE:Neither will I go in with dissemblers.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I have hated the congregation of evil doers;
VERSE:And will not sit with the wicked.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I will wash mine hands in innocency:
VERSE:So will I compass thine altar, O LORD:
LABEL:7
VERSE:That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving,
VERSE:And tell of all thy wondrous works.
LABEL:8
VERSE:  LORD, I have loved the habitation of thy house,
VERSE:And the place where thine honour dwelleth.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Gather not my soul with sinners,
VERSE:Nor my life with bloody men:
LABEL:10
VERSE:In whose hands is mischief,
VERSE:And their right hand is full of bribes.
LABEL:11
VERSE:But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity:
VERSE:Redeem me, and be merciful unto me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:My foot standeth in an even place:
VERSE:In the congregations will I bless the LORD.
