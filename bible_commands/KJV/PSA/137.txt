TITLE:Psalm 137
LABEL:1
VERSE:By the rivers of Babylon, there we sat down, yea, we wept,
VERSE:When we remembered Zion.
LABEL:2
VERSE:We hanged our harps
VERSE:Upon the willows in the midst thereof.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For there they that carried us away captive required of us a song; And they that wasted us required of us mirth, saying,
VERSE:Sing us one of the songs of Zion.
LABEL:4
VERSE:How shall we sing
VERSE:The LORD's song in a strange land?
LABEL:5
VERSE:If I forget thee, O Jerusalem,
VERSE:Let my right hand forget her cunning.
LABEL:6
VERSE:If I do not remember thee, let my tongue cleave to the roof of my mouth;
VERSE:If I prefer not Jerusalem above my chief joy.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Remember, O LORD, the children of Edom in the day of Jerusalem;
VERSE:Who said, Rase it, rase it, even to the foundation thereof.
LABEL:8
VERSE:O daughter of Babylon, who art to be destroyed;
VERSE:Happy shall he be, that rewardeth thee as thou hast served us.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Happy shall he be, that taketh and dasheth thy little ones
VERSE:Against the stones.
