TITLE:Psalm 41
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is he that considereth the poor:
VERSE:The LORD will deliver him in time of trouble.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The LORD will preserve him, and keep him alive; and he shall be blessed upon the earth:
VERSE:And thou wilt not deliver him unto the will of his enemies.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The LORD will strengthen him upon the bed of languishing:
VERSE:Thou wilt make all his bed in his sickness.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I said, LORD, be merciful unto me:
VERSE:Heal my soul; for I have sinned against thee.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Mine enemies speak evil of me,
VERSE:When shall he die, and his name perish?
LABEL:6
VERSE:And if he come to see me, he speaketh vanity:
VERSE:His heart gathereth iniquity to itself; When he goeth abroad, he telleth it.
LABEL:7
VERSE:All that hate me whisper together against me:
VERSE:Against me do they devise my hurt.
LABEL:8
VERSE:An evil disease, say they, cleaveth fast unto him:
VERSE:And now that he lieth he shall rise up no more.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted,
VERSE:Which did eat of my bread, hath lifted up his heel against me.
LABEL:10
VERSE:But thou, O LORD, be merciful unto me,
VERSE:And raise me up, that I may requite them.
LABEL:11
VERSE:By this I know that thou favourest me,
VERSE:Because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity,
VERSE:And settest me before thy face for ever.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Blessed be the LORD God of Israel
VERSE:From everlasting, and to everlasting. Amen, and Amen.
