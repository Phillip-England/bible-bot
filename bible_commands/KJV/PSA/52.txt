TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:52
TITLE:Psalm 52
LABEL:1
VERSE:Why boastest thou thyself in mischief, O mighty man?
VERSE:The goodness of God endureth continually.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Thy tongue deviseth mischiefs;
VERSE:Like a sharp razor, working deceitfully.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Thou lovest evil more than good;
VERSE:And lying rather than to speak righteousness. Selah.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Thou lovest all devouring words,
VERSE:O thou deceitful tongue.
LABEL:5
VERSE:God shall likewise destroy thee for ever,
VERSE:He shall take thee away, and pluck thee out of thy dwelling place, and root thee out of the land of the living. Selah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The righteous also shall see, and fear,
VERSE:And shall laugh at him:
LABEL:7
VERSE:Lo, this is the man that made not God his strength;
VERSE:But trusted in the abundance of his riches, and strengthened himself in his wickedness.
LABEL:8
VERSE:But I am like a green olive tree in the house of God:
VERSE:I trust in the mercy of God for ever and ever.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will praise thee for ever, because thou hast done it:
VERSE:And I will wait on thy name; for it is good before thy saints.
