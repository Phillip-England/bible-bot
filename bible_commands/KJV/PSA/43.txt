CHAPTER:43
TITLE:Psalm 43
LABEL:1
VERSE:Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation:
VERSE:O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.
LABEL:2
VERSE:For thou art the God of my strength: why dost thou cast me off?
VERSE:Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
LABEL:3
VERSE:O send out thy light and thy truth: Let them lead me;
VERSE:Let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Then will I go unto the altar of God, unto God my exceeding joy:
VERSE:Yea, upon the harp will I praise thee, O God my God.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
VERSE:And why art thou disquieted within me?
VERSE:Hope in God:
VERSE:For I shall yet praise him, Who is the health of my countenance, and my God.
