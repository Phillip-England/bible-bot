TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:28
TITLE:Psalm 28
LABEL:1
VERSE:Unto thee will I cry, O LORD my rock; be not silent to me:
VERSE:Lest, if thou be silent to me, I become like them that go down into the pit.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Hear the voice of my supplications, when I cry unto thee,
VERSE:When I lift up my hands toward thy holy oracle.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Draw me not away with the wicked, and with the workers of iniquity,
VERSE:Which speak peace to their neighbours, but mischief is in their hearts.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Give them according to their deeds,
VERSE:And according to the wickedness of their endeavours:
VERSE:Give them after the work of their hands;
VERSE:Render to them their desert.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Because they regard not the works of the LORD, nor the operation of his hands,
VERSE:He shall destroy them, and not build them up.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Blessed be the LORD,
VERSE:Because he hath heard the voice of my supplications.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The LORD  is my strength and my shield; my heart trusted in him, and I am helped:
VERSE:Therefore my heart greatly rejoiceth; and with my song will I praise him.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The LORD  is their strength,
VERSE:And he is the saving strength of his anointed.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Save thy people, and bless thine inheritance:
VERSE:Feed them also, and lift them up for ever.
