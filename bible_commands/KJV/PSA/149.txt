TITLE:Psalm 149
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song,
VERSE:And his praise in the congregation of saints.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let Israel rejoice in him that made him:
VERSE:Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Let them praise his name in the dance:
VERSE:Let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people:
VERSE:He will beautify the meek with salvation.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Let the saints be joyful in glory:
VERSE:Let them sing aloud upon their beds.
LABEL:6
VERSE:  Let the high praises of God be in their mouth,
VERSE:And a twoedged sword in their hand;
LABEL:7
VERSE:To execute vengeance upon the heathen,
VERSE:And punishments upon the people;
LABEL:8
VERSE:To bind their kings with chains,
VERSE:And their nobles with fetters of iron;
LABEL:9
VERSE:To execute upon them the judgment written:
VERSE:This honour have all his saints.
VERSE:Praise ye the LORD.
