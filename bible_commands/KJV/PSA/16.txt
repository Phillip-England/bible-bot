TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:16
TITLE:Psalm 16
LABEL:1
VERSE:Preserve me, O God:
VERSE:For in thee do I put my trust.
LABEL:2
VERSE:  O my soul, thou hast said unto the LORD,
VERSE:Thou art my Lord: My goodness extendeth not to thee;
LABEL:3
VERSE:  But to the saints that are in the earth,
VERSE:And to the excellent, in whom is all my delight.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their sorrows shall be multiplied that hasten after another god:
VERSE:Their drink offerings of blood will I not offer,
VERSE:Nor take up their names into my lips.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The LORD  is the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup:
VERSE:Thou maintainest my lot.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places;
VERSE:Yea, I have a goodly heritage.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I will bless the LORD, who hath given me counsel:
VERSE:My reins also instruct me in the night seasons.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I have set the LORD always before me:
VERSE:Because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Therefore my heart is glad, and my glory rejoiceth:
VERSE:My flesh also shall rest in hope.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell;
VERSE:Neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou wilt shew me the path of life: In thy presence is fulness of joy;
VERSE:At thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore.
