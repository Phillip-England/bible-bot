BOOK:TIT
CHAPTER:2
TITLE:Titus 2
SECTION:Sound Doctrine
LABEL:1
VERSE:But speak thou the things which become sound doctrine:
LABEL:2
VERSE:that the aged men be sober, grave, temperate, sound in faith, in charity, in patience.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The aged women likewise, that they be in behaviour as becometh holiness, not false accusers, not given to much wine, teachers of good things;
LABEL:4
VERSE:that they may teach the young women to be sober, to love their husbands, to love their children,
LABEL:5
VERSE:to be discreet, chaste, keepers at home, good, obedient to their own husbands, that the word of God be not blasphemed.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Young men likewise exhort to be sober minded.
LABEL:7
VERSE:In all things shewing thyself a pattern of good works: in doctrine shewing uncorruptness, gravity, sincerity,
LABEL:8
VERSE:sound speech, that cannot be condemned; that he that is of the contrary part may be ashamed, having no evil thing to say of you.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Exhort servants to be obedient unto their own masters, and to please them well in all things; not answering again;
LABEL:10
VERSE:not purloining, but shewing all good fidelity; that they may adorn the doctrine of God our Saviour in all things.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For the grace of God that bringeth salvation hath appeared to all men,
LABEL:12
VERSE:teaching us that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we should live soberly, righteously, and godly, in this present world;
LABEL:13
VERSE:looking for that blessed hope, and the glorious appearing of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ;
LABEL:14
VERSE:who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify unto himself a peculiar people, zealous of good works.
LABEL:15
VERSE:These things speak, and exhort, and rebuke with all authority. Let no man despise thee.
