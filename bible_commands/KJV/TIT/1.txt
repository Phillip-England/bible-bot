LABEL:6
VERSE:if any be blameless, the husband of one wife, having faithful children not accused of riot or unruly.
LABEL:7
VERSE:For a bishop must be blameless, as the steward of God; not selfwilled, not soon angry, not given to wine, no striker, not given to filthy lucre;
LABEL:8
VERSE:but a lover of hospitality, a lover of good men, sober, just, holy, temperate;
LABEL:9
VERSE:holding fast the faithful word as he hath been taught, that he may be able by sound doctrine both to exhort and to convince the gainsayers.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For there are many unruly and vain talkers and deceivers, specially they of the circumcision:
LABEL:11
VERSE:whose mouths must be stopped, who subvert whole houses, teaching things which they ought not, for filthy lucre's sake.
LABEL:12
VERSE:One of themselves, even a prophet of their own, said, The Cretians are alway liars, evil beasts, slow bellies.
LABEL:13
VERSE:This witness is true. Wherefore rebuke them sharply, that they may be sound in the faith;
LABEL:14
VERSE:not giving heed to Jewish fables, and commandments of men, that turn from the truth.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Unto the pure all things are pure: but unto them that are defiled and unbelieving is nothing pure; but even their mind and conscience is defiled.
LABEL:16
VERSE:They profess that they know God; but in works they deny him, being abominable, and disobedient, and unto every good work reprobate.
