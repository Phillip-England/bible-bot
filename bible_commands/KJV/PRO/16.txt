BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:16
TITLE:Proverbs 16
LABEL:1
VERSE:The preparations of the heart in man,
VERSE:And the answer of the tongue, is from the LORD.
LABEL:2
VERSE:All the ways of a man are clean in his own eyes;
VERSE:But the LORD weigheth the spirits.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Commit thy works unto the LORD,
VERSE:And thy thoughts shall be established.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The LORD hath made all things for himself:
VERSE:Yea, even the wicked for the day of evil.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Every one that is proud in heart is an abomination to the LORD:
VERSE:Though hand join in hand, he shall not be unpunished.
LABEL:6
VERSE:By mercy and truth iniquity is purged:
VERSE:And by the fear of the LORD  men depart from evil.
LABEL:7
VERSE:When a man's ways please the LORD,
VERSE:He maketh even his enemies to be at peace with him.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Better is a little with righteousness
VERSE:Than great revenues without right.
LABEL:9
VERSE:A man's heart deviseth his way:
VERSE:But the LORD directeth his steps.
LABEL:10
VERSE:A divine sentence is in the lips of the king:
VERSE:His mouth transgresseth not in judgment.
LABEL:11
VERSE:A just weight and balance are the LORD's:
VERSE:All the weights of the bag are his work.
LABEL:12
VERSE:  It is an abomination to kings to commit wickedness:
VERSE:For the throne is established by righteousness.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Righteous lips are the delight of kings;
VERSE:And they love him that speaketh right.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The wrath of a king is as messengers of death:
VERSE:But a wise man will pacify it.
LABEL:15
VERSE:In the light of the king's countenance is life;
VERSE:And his favour is as a cloud of the latter rain.
LABEL:16
VERSE:How much better is it to get wisdom than gold!
VERSE:And to get understanding rather to be chosen than silver!
LABEL:17
VERSE:The highway of the upright is to depart from evil:
VERSE:He that keepeth his way preserveth his soul.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Pride goeth before destruction,
VERSE:And an haughty spirit before a fall.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Better it is to be of an humble spirit with the lowly,
VERSE:Than to divide the spoil with the proud.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He that handleth a matter wisely shall find good:
VERSE:And whoso trusteth in the LORD, happy is he.
LABEL:21
VERSE:The wise in heart shall be called prudent:
VERSE:And the sweetness of the lips increaseth learning.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Understanding is a wellspring of life unto him that hath it:
VERSE:But the instruction of fools is folly.
LABEL:23
VERSE:The heart of the wise teacheth his mouth,
VERSE:And addeth learning to his lips.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Pleasant words are as an honeycomb,
VERSE:Sweet to the soul, and health to the bones.
LABEL:25
VERSE:There is a way that seemeth right unto a man,
VERSE:But the end thereof are the ways of death.
LABEL:26
VERSE:He that laboureth laboureth for himself;
VERSE:For his mouth craveth it of him.
LABEL:27
VERSE:An ungodly man diggeth up evil:
VERSE:And in his lips there is as a burning fire.
LABEL:28
VERSE:A froward man soweth strife:
VERSE:And a whisperer separateth chief friends.
LABEL:29
VERSE:A violent man enticeth his neighbour,
VERSE:And leadeth him into the way that is not good.
LABEL:30
VERSE:He shutteth his eyes to devise froward things:
VERSE:Moving his lips he bringeth evil to pass.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The hoary head is a crown of glory,
VERSE:If it be found in the way of righteousness.
LABEL:32
VERSE:  He that is slow to anger is better than the mighty;
VERSE:And he that ruleth his spirit than he that taketh a city.
LABEL:33
VERSE:The lot is cast into the lap;
VERSE:But the whole disposing thereof is of the LORD.
