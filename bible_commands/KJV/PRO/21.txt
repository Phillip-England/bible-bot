TITLE:Proverbs 21
LABEL:1
VERSE:The king's heart is in the hand of the LORD,
VERSE:As the rivers of water: he turneth it whithersoever he will.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Every way of a man is right in his own eyes:
VERSE:But the LORD pondereth the hearts.
LABEL:3
VERSE:To do justice and judgment
VERSE:Is more acceptable to the LORD than sacrifice.
LABEL:4
VERSE:An high look, and a proud heart,
VERSE:And the plowing of the wicked, is sin.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The thoughts of the diligent tend only to plenteousness;
VERSE:But of every one that is hasty only to want.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The getting of treasures by a lying tongue
VERSE:Is a vanity tossed to and fro of them that seek death.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The robbery of the wicked shall destroy them;
VERSE:Because they refuse to do judgment.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The way of man is froward and strange:
VERSE:But as for the pure, his work is right.
LABEL:9
VERSE:  It is better to dwell in a corner of the housetop,
VERSE:Than with a brawling woman in a wide house.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The soul of the wicked desireth evil:
VERSE:His neighbour findeth no favour in his eyes.
LABEL:11
VERSE:When the scorner is punished, the simple is made wise:
VERSE:And when the wise is instructed, he receiveth knowledge.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The righteous man wisely considereth the house of the wicked:
VERSE:But God overthroweth the wicked for their wickedness.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Whoso stoppeth his ears at the cry of the poor,
VERSE:He also shall cry himself, but shall not be heard.
LABEL:14
VERSE:A gift in secret pacifieth anger:
VERSE:And a reward in the bosom strong wrath.
LABEL:15
VERSE:  It is joy to the just to do judgment:
VERSE:But destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The man that wandereth out of the way of understanding
VERSE:Shall remain in the congregation of the dead.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He that loveth pleasure shall be a poor man:
VERSE:He that loveth wine and oil shall not be rich.
LABEL:18
VERSE:The wicked shall be a ransom for the righteous,
VERSE:And the transgressor for the upright.
LABEL:19
VERSE:  It is better to dwell in the wilderness,
VERSE:Than with a contentious and an angry woman.
LABEL:20
VERSE:  There is treasure to be desired and oil in the dwelling of the wise;
VERSE:But a foolish man spendeth it up.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He that followeth after righteousness and mercy
VERSE:Findeth life, righteousness, and honour.
LABEL:22
VERSE:A wise man scaleth the city of the mighty,
VERSE:And casteth down the strength of the confidence thereof.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Whoso keepeth his mouth and his tongue
VERSE:Keepeth his soul from troubles.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Proud and haughty scorner is his name,
VERSE:Who dealeth in proud wrath.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The desire of the slothful killeth him;
VERSE:For his hands refuse to labour.
LABEL:26
VERSE:He coveteth greedily all the day long:
VERSE:But the righteous giveth and spareth not.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The sacrifice of the wicked is abomination:
VERSE:How much more, when he bringeth it with a wicked mind?
LABEL:28
VERSE:A false witness shall perish:
VERSE:But the man that heareth speaketh constantly.
LABEL:29
VERSE:A wicked man hardeneth his face:
VERSE:But as for the upright, he directeth his way.
LABEL:30
VERSE:  There is no wisdom nor understanding
VERSE:Nor counsel against the LORD.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The horse is prepared against the day of battle:
VERSE:But safety is of the LORD.
