TRANSLATION:KJV
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:29
TITLE:Proverbs 29
LABEL:1
VERSE:He, that being often reproved hardeneth his neck,
VERSE:Shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice:
VERSE:But when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father:
VERSE:But he that keepeth company with harlots spendeth his substance.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The king by judgment establisheth the land:
VERSE:But he that receiveth gifts overthroweth it.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A man that flattereth his neighbour
VERSE:Spreadeth a net for his feet.
LABEL:6
VERSE:In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare:
VERSE:But the righteous doth sing and rejoice.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The righteous considereth the cause of the poor:
VERSE:But the wicked regardeth not to know it.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Scornful men bring a city into a snare:
VERSE:But wise men turn away wrath.
LABEL:9
VERSE:  If a wise man contendeth with a foolish man,
VERSE:Whether he rage or laugh, there is no rest.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The bloodthirsty hate the upright:
VERSE:But the just seek his soul.
LABEL:11
VERSE:A fool uttereth all his mind:
VERSE:But a wise man keepeth it in till afterwards.
LABEL:12
VERSE:If a ruler hearken to lies,
VERSE:All his servants are wicked.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The poor and the deceitful man meet together:
VERSE:The LORD lighteneth both their eyes.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The king that faithfully judgeth the poor,
VERSE:His throne shall be established for ever.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The rod and reproof give wisdom:
VERSE:But a child left to himself bringeth his mother to shame.
LABEL:16
VERSE:When the wicked are multiplied, transgression increaseth:
VERSE:But the righteous shall see their fall.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Correct thy son, and he shall give thee rest;
VERSE:Yea, he shall give delight unto thy soul.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Where there is no vision, the people perish:
VERSE:But he that keepeth the law, happy is he.
LABEL:19
VERSE:A servant will not be corrected by words:
VERSE:For though he understand he will not answer.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words?
VERSE:There is more hope of a fool than of him.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child
VERSE:Shall have him become his son at the length.
LABEL:22
VERSE:An angry man stirreth up strife,
VERSE:And a furious man aboundeth in transgression.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A man's pride shall bring him low:
VERSE:But honour shall uphold the humble in spirit.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul:
VERSE:He heareth cursing, and bewrayeth it not.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The fear of man bringeth a snare:
VERSE:But whoso putteth his trust in the LORD shall be safe.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Many seek the ruler's favour;
VERSE:But every man's judgment cometh from the LORD.
LABEL:27
VERSE:An unjust man is an abomination to the just:
VERSE:And he that is upright in the way is abomination to the wicked.
