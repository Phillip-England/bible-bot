BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:12
TITLE:Proverbs 12
LABEL:1
VERSE:Whoso loveth instruction loveth knowledge:
VERSE:But he that hateth reproof is brutish.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A good man obtaineth favour of the LORD:
VERSE:But a man of wicked devices will he condemn.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A man shall not be established by wickedness:
VERSE:But the root of the righteous shall not be moved.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A virtuous woman is a crown to her husband:
VERSE:But she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The thoughts of the righteous are right:
VERSE:But the counsels of the wicked are deceit.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The words of the wicked are to lie in wait for blood:
VERSE:But the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The wicked are overthrown, and are not:
VERSE:But the house of the righteous shall stand.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A man shall be commended according to his wisdom:
VERSE:But he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.
LABEL:9
VERSE:  He that is despised, and hath a servant, is better
VERSE:Than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread.
LABEL:10
VERSE:A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast:
VERSE:But the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread:
VERSE:But he that followeth vain persons is void of understanding.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The wicked desireth the net of evil men:
VERSE:But the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The wicked is snared by the transgression of his lips:
VERSE:But the just shall come out of trouble.
LABEL:14
VERSE:A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth:
VERSE:And the recompence of a man's hands shall be rendered unto him.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The way of a fool is right in his own eyes:
VERSE:But he that hearkeneth unto counsel is wise.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A fool's wrath is presently known:
VERSE:But a prudent man covereth shame.
LABEL:17
VERSE:  He that speaketh truth sheweth forth righteousness:
VERSE:But a false witness deceit.
LABEL:18
VERSE:There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword:
VERSE:But the tongue of the wise is health.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The lip of truth shall be established for ever:
VERSE:But a lying tongue is but for a moment.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Deceit is in the heart of them that imagine evil:
VERSE:But to the counsellors of peace is joy.
LABEL:21
VERSE:There shall no evil happen to the just:
VERSE:But the wicked shall be filled with mischief.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Lying lips are abomination to the LORD:
VERSE:But they that deal truly are his delight.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A prudent man concealeth knowledge:
VERSE:But the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The hand of the diligent shall bear rule:
VERSE:But the slothful shall be under tribute.
LABEL:25
VERSE:Heaviness in the heart of man maketh it stoop:
VERSE:But a good word maketh it glad.
LABEL:26
VERSE:The righteous is more excellent than his neighbour:
VERSE:But the way of the wicked seduceth them.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting:
VERSE:But the substance of a diligent man is precious.
LABEL:28
VERSE:In the way of righteousness is life;
VERSE:And in the pathway thereof there is no death.
