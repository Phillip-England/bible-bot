BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:22
TITLE:Proverbs 22
LABEL:1
VERSE:A good name is rather to be chosen than great riches,
VERSE:And loving favour rather than silver and gold.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The rich and poor meet together:
VERSE:The LORD  is the maker of them all.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A prudent man foreseeth the evil, and hideth himself:
VERSE:But the simple pass on, and are punished.
LABEL:4
VERSE:By humility and the fear of the LORD
VERSE:Are riches, and honour, and life.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Thorns and snares are in the way of the froward:
VERSE:He that doth keep his soul shall be far from them.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Train up a child in the way he should go:
VERSE:And when he is old, he will not depart from it.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The rich ruleth over the poor,
VERSE:And the borrower is servant to the lender.
LABEL:8
VERSE:He that soweth iniquity shall reap vanity:
VERSE:And the rod of his anger shall fail.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He that hath a bountiful eye shall be blessed;
VERSE:For he giveth of his bread to the poor.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Cast out the scorner, and contention shall go out;
VERSE:Yea, strife and reproach shall cease.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He that loveth pureness of heart,
VERSE:For the grace of his lips the king shall be his friend.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The eyes of the LORD preserve knowledge,
VERSE:And he overthroweth the words of the transgressor.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The slothful man saith, There is a lion without,
VERSE:I shall be slain in the streets.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The mouth of strange women is a deep pit:
VERSE:He that is abhorred of the LORD shall fall therein.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Foolishness is bound in the heart of a child;
VERSE:But the rod of correction shall drive it far from him.
LABEL:16
VERSE:He that oppresseth the poor to increase his riches,
VERSE:And he that giveth to the rich, shall surely come to want.
SECTION:Words of the Wise
LABEL:17
VERSE:Bow down thine ear, and hear the words of the wise,
VERSE:And apply thine heart unto my knowledge.
LABEL:18
VERSE:For it is a pleasant thing if thou keep them within thee;
VERSE:They shall withal be fitted in thy lips.
LABEL:19
VERSE:That thy trust may be in the LORD,
VERSE:I have made known to thee this day, even to thee.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Have not I written to thee excellent things
VERSE:In counsels and knowledge,
LABEL:21
VERSE:That I might make thee know the certainty of the words of truth;
VERSE:That thou mightest answer the words of truth to them that send unto thee?
LABEL:22
VERSE:Rob not the poor, because he is poor:
VERSE:Neither oppress the afflicted in the gate:
LABEL:23
VERSE:For the LORD will plead their cause,
VERSE:And spoil the soul of those that spoiled them.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Make no friendship with an angry man;
VERSE:And with a furious man thou shalt not go:
LABEL:25
VERSE:Lest thou learn his ways,
VERSE:And get a snare to thy soul.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Be not thou one of them that strike hands,
VERSE:Or of them that are sureties for debts.
LABEL:27
VERSE:If thou hast nothing to pay,
VERSE:Why should he take away thy bed from under thee?
LABEL:28
VERSE:Remove not the ancient landmark,
VERSE:Which thy fathers have set.
LABEL:29
VERSE:Seest thou a man diligent in his business? he shall stand before kings;
VERSE:He shall not stand before mean men.
