TITLE:Proverbs 15
LABEL:1
VERSE:A soft answer turneth away wrath:
VERSE:But grievous words stir up anger.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The tongue of the wise useth knowledge aright:
VERSE:But the mouth of fools poureth out foolishness.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The eyes of the LORD  are in every place,
VERSE:Beholding the evil and the good.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A wholesome tongue is a tree of life:
VERSE:But perverseness therein is a breach in the spirit.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A fool despiseth his father's instruction:
VERSE:But he that regardeth reproof is prudent.
LABEL:6
VERSE:In the house of the righteous is much treasure:
VERSE:But in the revenues of the wicked is trouble.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The lips of the wise disperse knowledge:
VERSE:But the heart of the foolish doeth not so.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination to the LORD:
VERSE:But the prayer of the upright is his delight.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The way of the wicked is an abomination unto the LORD:
VERSE:But he loveth him that followeth after righteousness.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Correction is grievous unto him that forsaketh the way:
VERSE:And he that hateth reproof shall die.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Hell and destruction are before the LORD:
VERSE:How much more then the hearts of the children of men?
LABEL:12
VERSE:A scorner loveth not one that reproveth him:
VERSE:Neither will he go unto the wise.
LABEL:13
VERSE:A merry heart maketh a cheerful countenance:
VERSE:But by sorrow of the heart the spirit is broken.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The heart of him that hath understanding seeketh knowledge:
VERSE:But the mouth of fools feedeth on foolishness.
LABEL:15
VERSE:All the days of the afflicted are evil:
VERSE:But he that is of a merry heart hath a continual feast.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Better is little with the fear of the LORD
VERSE:Than great treasure and trouble therewith.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Better is a dinner of herbs where love is,
VERSE:Than a stalled ox and hatred therewith.
LABEL:18
VERSE:A wrathful man stirreth up strife:
VERSE:But he that is slow to anger appeaseth strife.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The way of the slothful man is as an hedge of thorns:
VERSE:But the way of the righteous is made plain.
LABEL:20
VERSE:A wise son maketh a glad father:
VERSE:But a foolish man despiseth his mother.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Folly is joy to him that is destitute of wisdom:
VERSE:But a man of understanding walketh uprightly.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Without counsel purposes are disappointed:
VERSE:But in the multitude of counsellors they are established.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A man hath joy by the answer of his mouth:
VERSE:And a word spoken in due season, how good is it!
LABEL:24
VERSE:The way of life is above to the wise,
VERSE:That he may depart from hell beneath.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The LORD will destroy the house of the proud:
VERSE:But he will establish the border of the widow.
LABEL:26
VERSE:The thoughts of the wicked are an abomination to the LORD:
VERSE:But the words of the pure are pleasant words.
LABEL:27
VERSE:He that is greedy of gain troubleth his own house;
VERSE:But he that hateth gifts shall live.
LABEL:28
VERSE:The heart of the righteous studieth to answer:
VERSE:But the mouth of the wicked poureth out evil things.
LABEL:29
VERSE:The LORD  is far from the wicked:
VERSE:But he heareth the prayer of the righteous.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The light of the eyes rejoiceth the heart:
VERSE:And a good report maketh the bones fat.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The ear that heareth the reproof of life
VERSE:Abideth among the wise.
LABEL:32
VERSE:He that refuseth instruction despiseth his own soul:
VERSE:But he that heareth reproof getteth understanding.
LABEL:33
VERSE:The fear of the LORD  is the instruction of wisdom;
VERSE:And before honour is humility.
