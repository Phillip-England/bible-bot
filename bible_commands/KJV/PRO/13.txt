TITLE:Proverbs 13
LABEL:1
VERSE:A wise son heareth his father's instruction:
VERSE:But a scorner heareth not rebuke.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth:
VERSE:But the soul of the transgressors shall eat violence.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He that keepeth his mouth keepeth his life:
VERSE:But he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing:
VERSE:But the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A righteous man hateth lying:
VERSE:But a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Righteousness keepeth him that is upright in the way:
VERSE:But wickedness overthroweth the sinner.
LABEL:7
VERSE:There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing:
VERSE:There is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great riches.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The ransom of a man's life are his riches:
VERSE:But the poor heareth not rebuke.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The light of the righteous rejoiceth:
VERSE:But the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Only by pride cometh contention:
VERSE:But with the well advised is wisdom.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished:
VERSE:But he that gathereth by labour shall increase.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Hope deferred maketh the heart sick:
VERSE:But when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed:
VERSE:But he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The law of the wise is a fountain of life,
VERSE:To depart from the snares of death.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Good understanding giveth favour:
VERSE:But the way of transgressors is hard.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Every prudent man dealeth with knowledge:
VERSE:But a fool layeth open his folly.
LABEL:17
VERSE:A wicked messenger falleth into mischief:
VERSE:But a faithful ambassador is health.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth instruction:
VERSE:But he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul:
VERSE:But it is abomination to fools to depart from evil.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He that walketh with wise men shall be wise:
VERSE:But a companion of fools shall be destroyed.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Evil pursueth sinners:
VERSE:But to the righteous good shall be repaid.
LABEL:22
VERSE:A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children's children:
VERSE:And the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Much food is in the tillage of the poor:
VERSE:But there is that is destroyed for want of judgment.
LABEL:24
VERSE:He that spareth his rod hateth his son:
VERSE:But he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul:
VERSE:But the belly of the wicked shall want.
