VERSE:But a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.

VERSE:Treasures of wickedness profit nothing:
VERSE:But righteousness delivereth from death.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish:
VERSE:But he casteth away the substance of the wicked.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He becometh poor that dealeth with a slack hand:
VERSE:But the hand of the diligent maketh rich.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He that gathereth in summer is a wise son:
VERSE:But he that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Blessings are upon the head of the just:
VERSE:But violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The memory of the just is blessed:
VERSE:But the name of the wicked shall rot.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The wise in heart will receive commandments:
VERSE:But a prating fool shall fall.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He that walketh uprightly walketh surely:
VERSE:But he that perverteth his ways shall be known.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow:
VERSE:But a prating fool shall fall.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The mouth of a righteous man is a well of life:
VERSE:But violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Hatred stirreth up strifes:
VERSE:But love covereth all sins.
LABEL:13
VERSE:In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found:
VERSE:But a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Wise men lay up knowledge:
VERSE:But the mouth of the foolish is near destruction.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The rich man's wealth is his strong city:
VERSE:The destruction of the poor is their poverty.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The labour of the righteous tendeth to life:
VERSE:The fruit of the wicked to sin.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He is in the way of life that keepeth instruction:
VERSE:But he that refuseth reproof erreth.
LABEL:18
VERSE:He that hideth hatred with lying lips,
VERSE:And he that uttereth a slander, is a fool.
LABEL:19
VERSE:In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin:
VERSE:But he that refraineth his lips is wise.
LABEL:20
VERSE:The tongue of the just is as choice silver:
VERSE:The heart of the wicked is little worth.
LABEL:21
VERSE:The lips of the righteous feed many:
VERSE:But fools die for want of wisdom.
LABEL:22
VERSE:The blessing of the LORD, it maketh rich,
VERSE:And he addeth no sorrow with it.
LABEL:23
VERSE:  It is as sport to a fool to do mischief:
VERSE:But a man of understanding hath wisdom.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him:
VERSE:But the desire of the righteous shall be granted.
LABEL:25
VERSE:As the whirlwind passeth, so is the wicked no more:
VERSE:But the righteous is an everlasting foundation.
LABEL:26

VERSE:So is the sluggard to them that send him.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The fear of the LORD prolongeth days:
VERSE:But the years of the wicked shall be shortened.
LABEL:28
VERSE:The hope of the righteous shall be gladness:
VERSE:But the expectation of the wicked shall perish.
LABEL:29
VERSE:The way of the LORD  is strength to the upright:
VERSE:But destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:30
VERSE:The righteous shall never be removed:
VERSE:But the wicked shall not inhabit the earth.
LABEL:31
VERSE:The mouth of the just bringeth forth wisdom:
VERSE:But the froward tongue shall be cut out.
LABEL:32
VERSE:The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable:
VERSE:But the mouth of the wicked speaketh frowardness.
