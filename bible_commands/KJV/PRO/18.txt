BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:18
TITLE:Proverbs 18
LABEL:1
VERSE:Through desire a man, having separated himself, seeketh
VERSE:And intermeddleth with all wisdom.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A fool hath no delight in understanding,
VERSE:But that his heart may discover itself.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When the wicked cometh, then cometh also contempt,
VERSE:And with ignominy reproach.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The words of a man's mouth are as deep waters,
VERSE:And the wellspring of wisdom as a flowing brook.
LABEL:5
VERSE:  It is not good to accept the person of the wicked,
VERSE:To overthrow the righteous in judgment.
LABEL:6
VERSE:A fool's lips enter into contention,
VERSE:And his mouth calleth for strokes.
LABEL:7
VERSE:A fool's mouth is his destruction,
VERSE:And his lips are the snare of his soul.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The words of a talebearer are as wounds,
VERSE:And they go down into the innermost parts of the belly.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He also that is slothful in his work
VERSE:Is brother to him that is a great waster.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The name of the LORD  is a strong tower:
VERSE:The righteous runneth into it, and is safe.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The rich man's wealth is his strong city,
VERSE:And as an high wall in his own conceit.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Before destruction the heart of man is haughty,
VERSE:And before honour is humility.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He that answereth a matter before he heareth it,
VERSE:It is folly and shame unto him.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The spirit of a man will sustain his infirmity;
VERSE:But a wounded spirit who can bear?
LABEL:15
VERSE:The heart of the prudent getteth knowledge;
VERSE:And the ear of the wise seeketh knowledge.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A man's gift maketh room for him,
VERSE:And bringeth him before great men.
LABEL:17
VERSE:  He that is first in his own cause seemeth just;
VERSE:But his neighbour cometh and searcheth him.
LABEL:18
VERSE:The lot causeth contentions to cease,
VERSE:And parteth between the mighty.
LABEL:19
VERSE:A brother offended is harder to be won than a strong city:
VERSE:And their contentions are like the bars of a castle.
LABEL:20
VERSE:A man's belly shall be satisfied with the fruit of his mouth;
VERSE:And with the increase of his lips shall he be filled.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Death and life are in the power of the tongue:
VERSE:And they that love it shall eat the fruit thereof.
LABEL:22
VERSE:  Whoso findeth a wife findeth a good thing,
VERSE:And obtaineth favour of the LORD.
LABEL:23
VERSE:The poor useth intreaties;
VERSE:But the rich answereth roughly.
LABEL:24
VERSE:A man that hath friends must shew himself friendly:
VERSE:And there is a friend that sticketh closer than a brother.
