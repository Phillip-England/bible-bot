BOOK:1TI
CHAPTER:2
TITLE:1 Timothy 2
LABEL:1
VERSE:I exhort therefore, that, first of all, supplications, prayers, intercessions, and giving of thanks, be made for all men;
LABEL:2
VERSE:for kings, and for all that are in authority; that we may lead a quiet and peaceable life in all godliness and honesty.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour;
LABEL:4
VERSE:who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus;
LABEL:6
VERSE:who gave himself a ransom for all, to be testified in due time.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Whereunto I am ordained a preacher, and an apostle, (I speak the truth in Christ, and lie not;) a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and verity.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will therefore that men pray every where, lifting up holy hands, without wrath and doubting.
LABEL:9
VERSE:In like manner also, that women adorn themselves in modest apparel, with shamefacedness and sobriety; not with broided hair, or gold, or pearls, or costly array;
LABEL:10
VERSE:but (which becometh women professing godliness) with good works.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let the woman learn in silence with all subjection.
LABEL:12
VERSE:But I suffer not a woman to teach, nor to usurp authority over the man, but to be in silence.
LABEL:13
VERSE:For Adam was first formed, then Eve.
LABEL:14
VERSE:And Adam was not deceived, but the woman being deceived was in the transgression.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Notwithstanding she shall be saved in childbearing, if they continue in faith and charity and holiness with sobriety.
