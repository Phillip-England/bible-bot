TITLE:Proverbs 13
LABEL:1
VERSE:A wise son heareth his father’s instruction;
VERSE:But a scoffer heareth not rebuke.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth;
VERSE:But the soul of the treacherous shall eat violence.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He that guardeth his mouth keepeth his life;
VERSE:But he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing;
VERSE:But the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A righteous man hateth lying;
VERSE:But a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Righteousness guardeth him that is upright in the way;
VERSE:But wickedness overthroweth the sinner.
LABEL:7
VERSE:There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing:
VERSE:There is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great wealth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The ransom of a man’s life is his riches;
VERSE:But the poor heareth no threatening.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The light of the righteous rejoiceth;
VERSE:But the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.
LABEL:10
VERSE:By pride cometh only contention;
VERSE:But with the well-advised is wisdom.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished;
VERSE:But he that gathereth by labor shall have increase.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Hope deferred maketh the heart sick;
VERSE:But when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Whoso despiseth the word bringeth destruction on himself;
VERSE:But he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The law of the wise is a fountain of life,
VERSE:That one may depart from the snares of death.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Good understanding giveth favor;
VERSE:But the way of the transgressor is hard.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Every prudent man worketh with knowledge;
VERSE:But a fool flaunteth his folly.
LABEL:17
VERSE:A wicked messenger falleth into evil;
VERSE:But a faithful ambassador is health.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth correction;
VERSE:But he that regardeth reproof shall be honored.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul;
VERSE:But it is an abomination to fools to depart from evil.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Walk with wise men, and thou shalt be wise;
VERSE:But the companion of fools shall smart for it.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Evil pursueth sinners;
VERSE:But the righteous shall be recompensed with good.
LABEL:22
VERSE:A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children’s children;
VERSE:And the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the righteous.
LABEL:23
VERSE:Much food is in the tillage of the poor;
VERSE:But there is that is destroyed by reason of injustice.
LABEL:24
VERSE:He that spareth his rod hateth his son;
VERSE:But he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul;
VERSE:But the belly of the wicked shall want.
