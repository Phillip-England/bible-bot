TITLE:Proverbs 12
LABEL:1
VERSE:Whoso loveth correction loveth knowledge;
VERSE:But he that hateth reproof is brutish.
LABEL:2
VERSE:A good man shall obtain favor of Jehovah;
VERSE:But a man of wicked devices will he condemn.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A man shall not be established by wickedness;
VERSE:But the root of the righteous shall not be moved.
LABEL:4
VERSE:A worthy woman is the crown of her husband;
VERSE:But she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The thoughts of the righteous are just;
VERSE:But the counsels of the wicked are deceit.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The words of the wicked are of lying in wait for blood;
VERSE:But the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The wicked are overthrown, and are not;
VERSE:But the house of the righteous shall stand.
LABEL:8
VERSE:A man shall be commended according to his wisdom;
VERSE:But he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Better is he that is lightly esteemed, and hath a servant,
VERSE:Than he that honoreth himself, and lacketh bread.
LABEL:10
VERSE:A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast;
VERSE:But the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.
LABEL:11
VERSE:He that tilleth his land shall have plenty of bread;
VERSE:But he that followeth after vain persons is void of understanding.
LABEL:12
VERSE:The wicked desireth the net of evil men;
VERSE:But the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.
LABEL:13
VERSE:In the transgression of the lips is a snare to the evil man;
VERSE:But the righteous shall come out of trouble.
LABEL:14
VERSE:A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth;
VERSE:And the doings of a man’s hands shall be rendered unto him.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The way of a fool is right in his own eyes;
VERSE:But he that is wise hearkeneth unto counsel.
LABEL:16
VERSE:A fool’s vexation is presently known;
VERSE:But a prudent man concealeth shame.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He that uttereth truth showeth forth righteousness;
VERSE:But a false witness, deceit.
LABEL:18
VERSE:There is that speaketh rashly like the piercings of a sword;
VERSE:But the tongue of the wise is health.
LABEL:19
VERSE:The lip of truth shall be established for ever;
VERSE:But a lying tongue is but for a moment.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Deceit is in the heart of them that devise evil;
VERSE:But to the counsellors of peace is joy.
LABEL:21
VERSE:There shall no mischief happen to the righteous;
VERSE:But the wicked shall be filled with evil.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Lying lips are an abomination to Jehovah;
VERSE:But they that deal truly are his delight.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A prudent man concealeth knowledge;
VERSE:But the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.
LABEL:24
VERSE:The hand of the diligent shall bear rule;
VERSE:But the slothful shall be put under taskwork.
LABEL:25
VERSE:Heaviness in the heart of a man maketh it stoop;
VERSE:But a good word maketh it glad.
LABEL:26
VERSE:The righteous is a guide to his neighbor;
VERSE:But the way of the wicked causeth them to err.
LABEL:27
VERSE:The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting;
VERSE:But the precious substance of men is to the diligent.
LABEL:28
VERSE:In the way of righteousness is life;
VERSE:And in the pathway thereof there is no death.
