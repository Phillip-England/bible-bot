TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PRO
CHAPTER:29
TITLE:Proverbs 29
LABEL:1
VERSE:He that being often reproved hardeneth his neck
VERSE:Shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:When the righteous are increased, the people rejoice;
VERSE:But when a wicked man beareth rule, the people sigh.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father;
VERSE:But he that keepeth company with harlots wasteth his substance.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The king by justice establisheth the land;
VERSE:But he that exacteth gifts overthroweth it.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A man that flattereth his neighbor
VERSE:Spreadeth a net for his steps.
LABEL:6
VERSE:In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare;
VERSE:But the righteous doth sing and rejoice.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The righteous taketh knowledge of the cause of the poor;
VERSE:The wicked hath not understanding to know it.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Scoffers set a city in a flame;
VERSE:But wise men turn away wrath.
LABEL:9
VERSE:If a wise man hath a controversy with a foolish man,
VERSE:Whether he be angry or laugh, there will be no rest.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The bloodthirsty hate him that is perfect;
VERSE:And as for the upright, they seek his life.
LABEL:11
VERSE:A fool uttereth all his anger;
VERSE:But a wise man keepeth it back and stilleth it.
LABEL:12
VERSE:If a ruler hearkeneth to falsehood,
VERSE:All his servants are wicked.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The poor man and the oppressor meet together;
VERSE:Jehovah lighteneth the eyes of them both.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The king that faithfully judgeth the poor,
VERSE:His throne shall be established for ever.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The rod and reproof give wisdom;
VERSE:But a child left to himself causeth shame to his mother.
LABEL:16
VERSE:When the wicked are increased, transgression increaseth;
VERSE:But the righteous shall look upon their fall.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Correct thy son, and he will give thee rest;
VERSE:Yea, he will give delight unto thy soul.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Where there is no vision, the people cast off restraint;
VERSE:But he that keepeth the law, happy is he.
LABEL:19
VERSE:A servant will not be corrected by words;
VERSE:For though he understand, he will not give heed.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words?
VERSE:There is more hope of a fool than of him.
LABEL:21
VERSE:He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child
VERSE:Shall have him become a son at the last.
LABEL:22
VERSE:An angry man stirreth up strife,
VERSE:And a wrathful man aboundeth in transgression.
LABEL:23
VERSE:A man’s pride shall bring him low;
VERSE:But he that is of a lowly spirit shall obtain honor.
LABEL:24
VERSE:Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul;
VERSE:He heareth the adjuration and uttereth nothing.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The fear of man bringeth a snare;
VERSE:But whoso putteth his trust in Jehovah shall be safe.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Many seek the ruler’s favor;
VERSE:But a man’s judgment cometh from Jehovah.
LABEL:27
VERSE:An unjust man is an abomination to the righteous;
VERSE:And he that is upright in the way is an abomination to the wicked.
