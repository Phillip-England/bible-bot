LABEL:2
VERSE:in hope of eternal life, which God, who cannot lie, promised before times eternal;
LABEL:3
VERSE:but in his own seasons manifested his word in the message, wherewith I was intrusted according to the commandment of God our Saviour;
LABEL:4
VERSE:to Titus, my true child after a common faith: Grace and peace from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Saviour.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For this cause left I thee in Crete, that thou shouldest set in order the things that were wanting, and appoint elders in every city, as I gave thee charge;
LABEL:6
VERSE:if any man is blameless, the husband of one wife, having children that believe, who are not accused of riot or unruly.
LABEL:7
VERSE:For the bishop must be blameless, as God’s steward; not self-willed, not soon angry, no brawler, no striker, not greedy of filthy lucre;
LABEL:8
VERSE:but given to hospitality, a lover of good, sober-minded, just, holy, self-controlled;
LABEL:9
VERSE:holding to the faithful word which is according to the teaching, that he may be able both to exhort in the sound doctrine, and to convict the gainsayers.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For there are many unruly men, vain talkers and deceivers, specially they of the circumcision,
LABEL:11
VERSE:whose mouths must be stopped; men who overthrow whole houses, teaching things which they ought not, for filthy lucre’s sake.
LABEL:12
VERSE:One of themselves, a prophet of their own, said,
VERSE:Cretans are always liars, evil beasts, idle gluttons.
LABEL:13
VERSE:This testimony is true. For which cause reprove them sharply, that they may be sound in the faith,
LABEL:14
VERSE:not giving heed to Jewish fables, and commandments of men who turn away from the truth.
LABEL:15
VERSE:To the pure all things are pure: but to them that are defiled and unbelieving nothing is pure; but both their mind and their conscience are defiled.
LABEL:16
VERSE:They profess that they know God; but by their works they deny him, being abominable, and disobedient, and unto every good work reprobate.
