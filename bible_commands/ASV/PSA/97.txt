TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:97
TITLE:Psalms 97
LABEL:1
VERSE:Jehovah reigneth; let the earth rejoice;
VERSE:Let the multitude of isles be glad.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Clouds and darkness are round about him:
VERSE:Righteousness and justice are the foundation of his throne.
LABEL:3
VERSE:A fire goeth before him,
VERSE:And burneth up his adversaries round about.
LABEL:4
VERSE:His lightnings lightened the world:
VERSE:The earth saw, and trembled.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The mountains melted like wax at the presence of Jehovah,
VERSE:At the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The heavens declare his righteousness,
VERSE:And all the peoples have seen his glory.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Let all them be put to shame that serve graven images,
VERSE:That boast themselves of idols:
VERSE:Worship him, all ye gods.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Zion heard and was glad,
VERSE:And the daughters of Judah rejoiced,
VERSE:Because of thy judgments, O Jehovah.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For thou, Jehovah, art most high above all the earth:
VERSE:Thou art exalted far above all gods.
LABEL:10
VERSE:O ye that love Jehovah, hate evil:
VERSE:He preserveth the souls of his saints;
VERSE:He delivereth them out of the hand of the wicked.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Light is sown for the righteous,
VERSE:And gladness for the upright in heart.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Be glad in Jehovah, ye righteous;
VERSE:And give thanks to his holy memorial name.
