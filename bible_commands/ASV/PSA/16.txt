TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:16
TITLE:Psalms 16
LABEL:1
VERSE:Preserve me, O God; for in thee do I take refuge.
LABEL:2
VERSE:  O my soul, thou hast said unto Jehovah, Thou art my Lord:
VERSE:I have no good beyond thee.
LABEL:3
VERSE:As for the saints that are in the earth,
VERSE:They are the excellent in whom is all my delight.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their sorrows shall be multiplied that give gifts for another god:
VERSE:Their drink-offerings of blood will I not offer,
VERSE:Nor take their names upon my lips.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Jehovah is the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup:
VERSE:Thou maintainest my lot.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places;
VERSE:Yea, I have a goodly heritage.
LABEL:7
VERSE:I will bless Jehovah, who hath given me counsel;
VERSE:Yea, my heart instructeth me in the night seasons.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I have set Jehovah always before me:
VERSE:Because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Therefore my heart is glad, and my glory rejoiceth:
VERSE:My flesh also shall dwell in safety.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thou wilt not leave my soul to Sheol;
VERSE:Neither wilt thou suffer thy holy one to see corruption.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou wilt show me the path of life:
VERSE:In thy presence is fulness of joy;
VERSE:In thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore.
