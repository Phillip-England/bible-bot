VERSE:To see if there were any that did understand,

VERSE:There is none that doeth good, no, not one.

VERSE:Who eat up my people as they eat bread,
VERSE:And call not upon God?
LABEL:5
VERSE:There were they in great fear, where no fear was;
VERSE:For God hath scattered the bones of him that encampeth against thee:
VERSE:Thou hast put them to shame, because God hath rejected them.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Oh that the salvation of Israel were come out of Zion!
VERSE:When God bringeth back the captivity of his people,
VERSE:Then shall Jacob rejoice, and Israel shall be glad.
