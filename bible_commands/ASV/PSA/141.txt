TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:141
TITLE:Psalms 141
LABEL:1
VERSE:Jehovah, I have called upon thee; make haste unto me:
VERSE:Give ear unto my voice, when I call unto thee.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let my prayer be set forth as incense before thee;
VERSE:The lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrifice.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Set a watch, O Jehovah, before my mouth;
VERSE:Keep the door of my lips.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Incline not my heart to any evil thing,
VERSE:To practise deeds of wickedness
VERSE:With men that work iniquity:
VERSE:And let me not eat of their dainties.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Let the righteous smite me, it shall be a kindness;
VERSE:And let him reprove me, it shall be as oil upon the head;
VERSE:Let not my head refuse it:
VERSE:For even in their wickedness shall my prayer continue.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Their judges are thrown down by the sides of the rock;
VERSE:And they shall hear my words; for they are sweet.
LABEL:7
VERSE:As when one ploweth and cleaveth the earth,
VERSE:Our bones are scattered at the mouth of Sheol.
LABEL:8
VERSE:For mine eyes are unto thee, O Jehovah the Lord:
VERSE:In thee do I take refuge; leave not my soul destitute.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Keep me from the snare which they have laid for me,
VERSE:And from the gins of the workers of iniquity.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Let the wicked fall into their own nets,
VERSE:Whilst that I withal escape.
