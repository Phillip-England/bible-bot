BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:43
TITLE:Psalms 43
LABEL:1
VERSE:Judge me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation:
VERSE:Oh deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.
LABEL:2
VERSE:For thou art the God of my strength; why hast thou cast me off?
VERSE:Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
LABEL:3
VERSE:Oh send out thy light and thy truth; let them lead me:
VERSE:Let them bring me unto thy holy hill,
VERSE:And to thy tabernacles.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Then will I go unto the altar of God,
VERSE:Unto God my exceeding joy;
VERSE:And upon the harp will I praise thee, O God, my God.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
VERSE:And why art thou disquieted within me?
VERSE:Hope thou in God; for I shall yet praise him,
VERSE:Who is the help of my countenance, and my God.
