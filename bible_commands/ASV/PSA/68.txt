TITLE:Psalms 68
LABEL:1
VERSE:Let God arise, let his enemies be scattered;
VERSE:Let them also that hate him flee before him.
LABEL:2
VERSE:As smoke is driven away, so drive them away:
VERSE:As wax melteth before the fire,
VERSE:So let the wicked perish at the presence of God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:But let the righteous be glad; let them exult before God:
VERSE:Yea, let them rejoice with gladness.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Sing unto God, sing praises to his name:
VERSE:Cast up a highway for him that rideth through the deserts;
VERSE:His name is Jehovah; and exult ye before him.
LABEL:5
VERSE:A father of the fatherless, and a judge of the widows,
VERSE:Is God in his holy habitation.
LABEL:6
VERSE:God setteth the solitary in families:
VERSE:He bringeth out the prisoners into prosperity;
VERSE:But the rebellious dwell in a parched land.
LABEL:7
VERSE:O God, when thou wentest forth before thy people,
VERSE:When thou didst march through the wilderness; [Selah
LABEL:8
VERSE:The earth trembled,
VERSE:The heavens also dropped rain at the presence of God:
VERSE:Yon Sinai trembled at the presence of God, the God of Israel.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Thou, O God, didst send a plentiful rain,
VERSE:Thou didst confirm thine inheritance, when it was weary.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Thy congregation dwelt therein:
VERSE:Thou, O God, didst prepare of thy goodness for the poor.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The Lord giveth the word:
VERSE:The women that publish the tidings are a great host.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Kings of armies flee, they flee;
VERSE:And she that tarrieth at home divideth the spoil.
LABEL:13
VERSE:When ye lie among the sheepfolds,
VERSE:It is as the wings of a dove covered with silver,
VERSE:And her pinions with yellow gold.
LABEL:14
VERSE:When the Almighty scattered kings therein,
VERSE:It was as when it snoweth in Zalmon.
LABEL:15
VERSE:A mountain of God is the mountain of Bashan;
VERSE:A high mountain is the mountain of Bashan.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Why look ye askance, ye high mountains,
VERSE:At the mountain which God hath desired for his abode?
VERSE:Yea, Jehovah will dwell in it for ever.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The chariots of God are twenty thousand, even thousands upon thousands:
VERSE:The Lord is among them, as in Sinai, in the sanctuary.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Thou hast ascended on high, thou hast led away captives;
VERSE:Thou hast received gifts among men,
VERSE:Yea, among the rebellious also, that Jehovah God might dwell with them.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Blessed be the Lord, who daily beareth our burden,
VERSE:Even the God who is our salvation. [Selah
LABEL:20
VERSE:God is unto us a God of deliverances;
VERSE:And unto Jehovah the Lord belongeth escape from death.
LABEL:21
VERSE:But God will smite through the head of his enemies,
VERSE:The hairy scalp of such a one as goeth on still in his guiltiness.
LABEL:22
VERSE:The Lord said, I will bring again from Bashan,
VERSE:I will bring them again from the depths of the sea;
LABEL:23
VERSE:That thou mayest crush them, dipping thy foot in blood,
VERSE:That the tongue of thy dogs may have its portion from thine enemies.
LABEL:24
VERSE:They have seen thy goings, O God,
VERSE:Even the goings of my God, my King, into the sanctuary.
LABEL:25
VERSE:The singers went before, the minstrels followed after,
VERSE:In the midst of the damsels playing with timbrels.
LABEL:26
VERSE:Bless ye God in the congregations,
VERSE:Even the Lord, ye that are of the fountain of Israel.
LABEL:27
VERSE:There is little Benjamin their ruler,
VERSE:The princes of Judah and their council,
VERSE:The princes of Zebulun, the princes of Naphtali.
LABEL:28
VERSE:Thy God hath commanded thy strength:
VERSE:Strengthen, O God, that which thou hast wrought for us.
LABEL:29
VERSE:Because of thy temple at Jerusalem
VERSE:Kings shall bring presents unto thee.
LABEL:30
VERSE:Rebuke the wild beast of the reeds,
VERSE:The multitude of the bulls, with the calves of the peoples,
VERSE:Trampling under foot the pieces of silver:
VERSE:He hath scattered the peoples that delight in war.
LABEL:31
VERSE:Princes shall come out of Egypt;
VERSE:Ethiopia shall haste to stretch out her hands unto God.
LABEL:32
VERSE:Sing unto God, ye kingdoms of the earth;
VERSE:Oh sing praises unto the Lord; [Selah
LABEL:33
VERSE:To him that rideth upon the heaven of heavens, which are of old;
VERSE:Lo, he uttereth his voice, a mighty voice.
LABEL:34
VERSE:Ascribe ye strength unto God:
VERSE:His excellency is over Israel,
VERSE:And his strength is in the skies.
LABEL:35
VERSE:O God, thou art terrible out of thy holy places:
VERSE:The God of Israel, he giveth strength and power unto his people.
VERSE:Blessed be God.
