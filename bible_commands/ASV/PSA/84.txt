VERSE:How amiable are thy tabernacles,
VERSE:O Jehovah of hosts!

VERSE:My heart and my flesh cry out unto the living God.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yea, the sparrow hath found her a house,
VERSE:And the swallow a nest for herself, where she may lay her young,
VERSE:Even thine altars, O Jehovah of hosts,
VERSE:My King, and my God.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Blessed are they that dwell in thy house:
VERSE:They will be still praising thee. [Selah

VERSE:In whose heart are the highways to Zion.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Passing through the valley of Weeping they make it a place of springs;
VERSE:Yea, the early rain covereth it with blessings.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They go from strength to strength;
VERSE:Every one of them appeareth before God in Zion.
LABEL:8
VERSE:O Jehovah God of hosts, hear my prayer;
VERSE:Give ear, O God of Jacob. [Selah
LABEL:9
VERSE:Behold, O God our shield,
VERSE:And look upon the face of thine anointed.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For a day in thy courts is better than a thousand.
VERSE:I had rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God,
VERSE:Than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For Jehovah God is a sun and a shield:
VERSE:Jehovah will give grace and glory;
VERSE:No good thing will he withhold from them that walk uprightly.
LABEL:12
