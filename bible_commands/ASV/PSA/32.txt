BOOK:PSA

TITLE:Psalms 32
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is he whose transgression is forgiven,
VERSE:Whose sin is covered.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Blessed is the man unto whom Jehovah imputeth not iniquity,
VERSE:And in whose spirit there is no guile.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When I kept silence, my bones wasted away
VERSE:Through my groaning all the day long.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For day and night thy hand was heavy upon me:
VERSE:My moisture was changed as with the drought of summer. [Selah
LABEL:5
VERSE:I acknowledged my sin unto thee,
VERSE:And mine iniquity did I not hide:
VERSE:I said, I will confess my transgressions unto Jehovah;
VERSE:And thou forgavest the iniquity of my sin. [Selah
LABEL:6
VERSE:For this let every one that is godly pray unto thee in a time when thou mayest be found:
VERSE:Surely when the great waters overflow they shall not reach unto him.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou art my hiding-place; thou wilt preserve me from trouble;
VERSE:Thou wilt compass me about with songs of deliverance. [Selah
LABEL:8
VERSE:I will instruct thee and teach thee in the way which thou shalt go:
VERSE:I will counsel thee with mine eye upon thee.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Be ye not as the horse, or as the mule, which have no understanding;
VERSE:Whose trappings must be bit and bridle to hold them in,
VERSE:Else they will not come near unto thee.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Many sorrows shall be to the wicked;
VERSE:But he that trusteth in Jehovah, lovingkindness shall compass him about.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Be glad in Jehovah, and rejoice, ye righteous;
VERSE:And shout for joy, all ye that are upright in heart.
