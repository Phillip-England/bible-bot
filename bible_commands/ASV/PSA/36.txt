TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:36
TITLE:Psalms 36
LABEL:1
VERSE:The transgression of the wicked saith within my heart,
VERSE:There is no fear of God before his eyes.
LABEL:2
VERSE:For he flattereth himself in his own eyes,
VERSE:That his iniquity will not be found out and be hated.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit:
VERSE:He hath ceased to be wise and to do good.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He deviseth iniquity upon his bed;
VERSE:He setteth himself in a way that is not good;
VERSE:He abhorreth not evil.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, is in the heavens;
VERSE:Thy faithfulness reacheth unto the skies.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Thy righteousness is like the mountains of God;
VERSE:Thy judgments are a great deep:
VERSE:O Jehovah, thou preservest man and beast.
LABEL:7
VERSE:How precious is thy lovingkindness, O God!
VERSE:And the children of men take refuge under the shadow of thy wings.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house;
VERSE:And thou wilt make them drink of the river of thy pleasures.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For with thee is the fountain of life:
VERSE:In thy light shall we see light.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Oh continue thy lovingkindness unto them that know thee,
VERSE:And thy righteousness to the upright in heart.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let not the foot of pride come against me,
VERSE:And let not the hand of the wicked drive me away.
LABEL:12
VERSE:There are the workers of iniquity fallen:
VERSE:They are thrust down, and shall not be able to rise.
