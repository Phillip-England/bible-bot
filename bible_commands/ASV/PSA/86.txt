TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:86
TITLE:Psalms 86
LABEL:1
VERSE:Bow down thine ear, O Jehovah, and answer me;
VERSE:For I am poor and needy.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Preserve my soul; for I am godly:
VERSE:O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Be merciful unto me, O Lord;
VERSE:For unto thee do I cry all the day long.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Rejoice the soul of thy servant;
VERSE:For unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive,
VERSE:And abundant in lovingkindness unto all them that call upon thee.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Give ear, O Jehovah, unto my prayer;
VERSE:And hearken unto the voice of my supplications.
LABEL:7
VERSE:In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee;
VERSE:For thou wilt answer me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:There is none like unto thee among the gods, O Lord;
VERSE:Neither are there any works like unto thy works.
LABEL:9
VERSE:All nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord;
VERSE:And they shall glorify thy name.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thou art great, and doest wondrous things:
VERSE:Thou art God alone.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Teach me thy way, O Jehovah; I will walk in thy truth:
VERSE:Unite my heart to fear thy name.
LABEL:12
VERSE:I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with my whole heart;
VERSE:And I will glorify thy name for evermore.
LABEL:13
VERSE:For great is thy lovingkindness toward me;
VERSE:And thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest Sheol.
LABEL:14
VERSE:O God, the proud are risen up against me,
VERSE:And a company of violent men have sought after my soul,
VERSE:And have not set thee before them.
LABEL:15
VERSE:But thou, O Lord, art a God merciful and gracious,
VERSE:Slow to anger, and abundant in lovingkindness and truth.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Oh turn unto me, and have mercy upon me;
VERSE:Give thy strength unto thy servant,
VERSE:And save the son of thy handmaid.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Show me a token for good,
VERSE:That they who hate me may see it, and be put to shame,
VERSE:Because thou, Jehovah, hast helped me, and comforted me.
