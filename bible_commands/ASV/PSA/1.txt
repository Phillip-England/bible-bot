BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:1
TITLE:Psalms 1
SECTION:BOOK I
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the wicked,
VERSE:Nor standeth in the way of sinners,
VERSE:Nor sitteth in the seat of scoffers:
LABEL:2
VERSE:But his delight is in the law of Jehovah;
VERSE:And on his law doth he meditate day and night.
LABEL:3
VERSE:And he shall be like a tree planted by the streams of water,
VERSE:That bringeth forth its fruit in its season,
VERSE:Whose leaf also doth not wither;
VERSE:And whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The wicked are not so,
VERSE:But are like the chaff which the wind driveth away.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Therefore the wicked shall not stand in the judgment,
VERSE:Nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For Jehovah knoweth the way of the righteous;
VERSE:But the way of the wicked shall perish.
