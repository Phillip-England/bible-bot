VERSE:Sing aloud unto God our strength:
VERSE:Make a joyful noise unto the God of Jacob.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Raise a song, and bring hither the timbrel,
VERSE:The pleasant harp with the psaltery.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Blow the trumpet at the new moon,
VERSE:At the full moon, on our feast-day.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For it is a statute for Israel,
VERSE:An ordinance of the God of Jacob.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He appointed it in Joseph for a testimony,
VERSE:When he went out over the land of Egypt,
VERSE:Where I heard a language that I knew not.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I removed his shoulder from the burden:
VERSE:His hands were freed from the basket.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou calledst in trouble, and I delivered thee;
VERSE:I answered thee in the secret place of thunder;
VERSE:I proved thee at the waters of Meribah. [Selah
LABEL:8
VERSE:Hear, O my people, and I will testify unto thee:
VERSE:O Israel, if thou wouldest hearken unto me!
LABEL:9
VERSE:There shall no strange god be in thee;
VERSE:Neither shalt thou worship any foreign god.
LABEL:10
VERSE:I am Jehovah thy God,
VERSE:Who brought thee up out of the land of Egypt:
VERSE:Open thy mouth wide, and I will fill it.
LABEL:11
VERSE:But my people hearkened not to my voice;
VERSE:And Israel would none of me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:So I let them go after the stubbornness of their heart,
VERSE:That they might walk in their own counsels.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Oh that my people would hearken unto me,
VERSE:That Israel would walk in my ways!
LABEL:14
VERSE:I would soon subdue their enemies,
VERSE:And turn my hand against their adversaries.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The haters of Jehovah should submit themselves unto him:
VERSE:But their time should endure for ever.
LABEL:16
VERSE:He would feed them also with the finest of the wheat;
VERSE:And with honey out of the rock would I satisfy thee.
