BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:41
TITLE:Psalms 41
LABEL:1
VERSE:Blessed is he that considereth the poor:
VERSE:Jehovah will deliver him in the day of evil.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Jehovah will preserve him, and keep him alive,
VERSE:And he shall be blessed upon the earth;
VERSE:And deliver not thou him unto the will of his enemies.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Jehovah will support him upon the couch of languishing:
VERSE:Thou makest all his bed in his sickness.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I said, O Jehovah, have mercy upon me:
VERSE:Heal my soul; for I have sinned against thee.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Mine enemies speak evil against me, saying,
VERSE:When will he die, and his name perish?
LABEL:6
VERSE:And if he come to see me, he speaketh falsehood;
VERSE:His heart gathereth iniquity to itself:
VERSE:When he goeth abroad, he telleth it.
LABEL:7
VERSE:All that hate me whisper together against me;
VERSE:Against me do they devise my hurt.
LABEL:8
VERSE:An evil disease, say they, cleaveth fast unto him;
VERSE:And now that he lieth he shall rise up no more.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Yea, mine own familiar friend, in whom I trusted,
VERSE:Who did eat of my bread,
VERSE:Hath lifted up his heel against me.
LABEL:10
VERSE:But thou, O Jehovah, have mercy upon me, and raise me up,
VERSE:That I may requite them.
LABEL:11
VERSE:By this I know that thou delightest in me,
VERSE:Because mine enemy doth not triumph over me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:And as for me, thou upholdest me in mine integrity,
VERSE:And settest me before thy face for ever.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Blessed be Jehovah, the God of Israel,
VERSE:From everlasting and to everlasting.
VERSE:Amen, and Amen.
