BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:26
TITLE:Psalms 26
LABEL:1
VERSE:Judge me, O Jehovah, for I have walked in mine integrity:
VERSE:I have trusted also in Jehovah without wavering.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Examine me, O Jehovah, and prove me;
VERSE:Try my heart and my mind.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes;
VERSE:And I have walked in thy truth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:I have not sat with men of falsehood;
VERSE:Neither will I go in with dissemblers.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I hate the assembly of evil-doers,
VERSE:And will not sit with the wicked.
LABEL:6
VERSE:I will wash my hands in innocency:
VERSE:So will I compass thine altar, O Jehovah;
LABEL:7
VERSE:That I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard,
VERSE:And tell of all thy wondrous works.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Jehovah, I love the habitation of thy house,
VERSE:And the place where thy glory dwelleth.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Gather not my soul with sinners,
VERSE:Nor my life with men of blood;
LABEL:10
VERSE:In whose hands is wickedness,
VERSE:And their right hand is full of bribes.
LABEL:11
VERSE:But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity:
VERSE:Redeem me, and be merciful unto me.
LABEL:12
VERSE:My foot standeth in an even place:
VERSE:In the congregations will I bless Jehovah.
