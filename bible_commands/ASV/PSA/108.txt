TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:108
TITLE:Psalms 108
LABEL:1
VERSE:My heart is fixed, O God;
VERSE:I will sing, yea, I will sing praises, even with my glory.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Awake, psaltery and harp:
VERSE:I myself will awake right early.
LABEL:3
VERSE:I will give thanks unto thee, O Jehovah, among the peoples;
VERSE:And I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For thy lovingkindness is great above the heavens;
VERSE:And thy truth reacheth unto the skies.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens,
VERSE:And thy glory above all the earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:That thy beloved may be delivered,
VERSE:Save with thy right hand, and answer us.
LABEL:7
VERSE:God hath spoken in his holiness: I will exult;
VERSE:I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine;
VERSE:Ephraim also is the defence of my head;
VERSE:Judah is my sceptre.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Moab is my washpot;
VERSE:Upon Edom will I cast my shoe;
VERSE:Over Philistia will I shout.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Who will bring me into the fortified city?
VERSE:Who hath led me unto Edom?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Hast not thou cast us off, O God?
VERSE:And thou goest not forth, O God, with our hosts.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Give us help against the adversary;
VERSE:For vain is the help of man.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Through God we shall do valiantly:
VERSE:For he it is that will tread down our adversaries.
