TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:33
TITLE:Psalms 33
LABEL:1
VERSE:Rejoice in Jehovah, O ye righteous:
VERSE:Praise is comely for the upright.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Give thanks unto Jehovah with the harp:
VERSE:Sing praises unto him with the psaltery of ten strings.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Sing unto him a new song;
VERSE:Play skilfully with a loud noise.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For the word of Jehovah is right;
VERSE:And all his work is done in faithfulness.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He loveth righteousness and justice:
VERSE:The earth is full of the lovingkindness of Jehovah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:By the word of Jehovah were the heavens made,
VERSE:And all the host of them by the breath of his mouth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He gathereth the waters of the sea together as a heap:
VERSE:He layeth up the deeps in store-houses.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Let all the earth fear Jehovah:
VERSE:Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of him.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For he spake, and it was done;
VERSE:He commanded, and it stood fast.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Jehovah bringeth the counsel of the nations to nought;
VERSE:He maketh the thoughts of the peoples to be of no effect.
LABEL:11
VERSE:The counsel of Jehovah standeth fast for ever,
VERSE:The thoughts of his heart to all generations.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Blessed is the nation whose God is Jehovah,
VERSE:The people whom he hath chosen for his own inheritance.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Jehovah looketh from heaven;
VERSE:He beholdeth all the sons of men;
LABEL:14
VERSE:From the place of his habitation he looketh forth
VERSE:Upon all the inhabitants of the earth,
LABEL:15
VERSE:He that fashioneth the hearts of them all,
VERSE:That considereth all their works.
LABEL:16
VERSE:There is no king saved by the multitude of a host:
VERSE:A mighty man is not delivered by great strength.
LABEL:17
VERSE:A horse is a vain thing for safety;
VERSE:Neither doth he deliver any by his great power.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Behold, the eye of Jehovah is upon them that fear him,
VERSE:Upon them that hope in his lovingkindness;
LABEL:19
VERSE:To deliver their soul from death,
VERSE:And to keep them alive in famine.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Our soul hath waited for Jehovah:
VERSE:He is our help and our shield.
LABEL:21
VERSE:For our heart shall rejoice in him,
VERSE:Because we have trusted in his holy name.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Let thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, be upon us,
VERSE:According as we have hoped in thee.
