TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:138
TITLE:Psalms 138
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will give thee thanks with my whole heart:
VERSE:Before the gods will I sing praises unto thee.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will worship toward thy holy temple,
VERSE:And give thanks unto thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth:
VERSE:For thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.
LABEL:3
VERSE:In the day that I called thou answeredst me,
VERSE:Thou didst encourage me with strength in my soul.
LABEL:4
VERSE:All the kings of the earth shall give thee thanks, O Jehovah,
VERSE:For they have heard the words of thy mouth.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Yea, they shall sing of the ways of Jehovah;
VERSE:For great is the glory of Jehovah.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For though Jehovah is high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly;
VERSE:But the haughty he knoweth from afar.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me;
VERSE:Thou wilt stretch forth thy hand against the wrath of mine enemies,
VERSE:And thy right hand will save me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Jehovah will perfect that which concerneth me:
VERSE:Thy lovingkindness, O Jehovah, endureth for ever;
VERSE:Forsake not the works of thine own hands.
