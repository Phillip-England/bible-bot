BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:142
TITLE:Psalms 142
LABEL:1
VERSE:I cry with my voice unto Jehovah;
VERSE:With my voice unto Jehovah do I make supplication.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I pour out my complaint before him;
VERSE:I show before him my trouble.
LABEL:3
VERSE:When my spirit was overwhelmed within me,
VERSE:Thou knewest my path.
VERSE:In the way wherein I walk
VERSE:Have they hidden a snare for me.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Look on my right hand, and see;
VERSE:For there is no man that knoweth me:
VERSE:Refuge hath failed me;
VERSE:No man careth for my soul.
LABEL:5
VERSE:I cried unto thee, O Jehovah;
VERSE:I said, Thou art my refuge,
VERSE:My portion in the land of the living.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Attend unto my cry;
VERSE:For I am brought very low:
VERSE:Deliver me from my persecutors;
VERSE:For they are stronger than I.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Bring my soul out of prison,
VERSE:That I may give thanks unto thy name:
VERSE:The righteous shall compass me about;
VERSE:For thou wilt deal bountifully with me.
