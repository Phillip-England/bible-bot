VERSE:I hate the work of them that turn aside;

VERSE:I will know no evil thing.

VERSE:Him that hath a high look and a proud heart will I not suffer.

VERSE:He that walketh in a perfect way, he shall minister unto me.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He that worketh deceit shall not dwell within my house:
VERSE:He that speaketh falsehood shall not be established before mine eyes.
