VERSE:O God thou hast cast us off, thou hast broken us down;
VERSE:Thou hast been angry; oh restore us again.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Thou hast made the land to tremble; thou hast rent it:
VERSE:Heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Thou hast showed thy people hard things:
VERSE:Thou hast made us to drink the wine of staggering.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Thou hast given a banner to them that fear thee,
VERSE:That it may be displayed because of the truth. [Selah
LABEL:5
VERSE:That thy beloved may be delivered,
VERSE:Save with thy right hand, and answer us.
LABEL:6
VERSE:God hath spoken in his holiness: I will exult;
VERSE:I will divide Shechem, and mete out the valley of Succoth.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Gilead is mine, and Manasseh is mine;
VERSE:Ephraim also is the defence of my head;
VERSE:Judah is my sceptre.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Moab is my washpot;
VERSE:Upon Edom will I cast my shoe:
VERSE:Philistia, shout thou because of me.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Who will bring me into the strong city?
VERSE:Who hath led me unto Edom?
LABEL:10
VERSE:Hast not thou, O God, cast us off?
VERSE:And thou goest not forth, O God, with our hosts.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Give us help against the adversary;
VERSE:For vain is the help of man.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Through God we shall do valiantly;
VERSE:For he it is that will tread down our adversaries.
