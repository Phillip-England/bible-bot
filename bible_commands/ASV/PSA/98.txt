TITLE:Psalms 98

VERSE:Oh sing unto Jehovah a new song;
VERSE:For he hath done marvellous things:
VERSE:His right hand, and his holy arm, hath wrought salvation for him.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Jehovah hath made known his salvation:
VERSE:His righteousness hath he openly showed in the sight of the nations.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He hath remembered his lovingkindness and his faithfulness toward the house of Israel:
VERSE:All the ends of the earth have seen the salvation of our God.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Make a joyful noise unto Jehovah, all the earth:
VERSE:Break forth and sing for joy, yea, sing praises.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Sing praises unto Jehovah with the harp;
VERSE:With the harp and the voice of melody.
LABEL:6
VERSE:With trumpets and sound of cornet
VERSE:Make a joyful noise before the King, Jehovah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof;
VERSE:The world, and they that dwell therein;
LABEL:8
VERSE:Let the floods clap their hands;
VERSE:Let the hills sing for joy together
LABEL:9
VERSE:Before Jehovah; for he cometh to judge the earth:
VERSE:He will judge the world with righteousness,
VERSE:And the peoples with equity.
