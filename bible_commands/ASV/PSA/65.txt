TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:65
TITLE:Psalms 65
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise waiteth for thee, O God, in Zion;
VERSE:And unto thee shall the vow be performed.
LABEL:2
VERSE:O thou that hearest prayer,
VERSE:Unto thee shall all flesh come.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Iniquities prevail against me:
VERSE:As for our transgressions, thou wilt forgive them.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Blessed is the man whom thou choosest, and causest to approach unto thee,
VERSE:That he may dwell in thy courts:
VERSE:We shall be satisfied with the goodness of thy house,
VERSE:Thy holy temple.
LABEL:5
VERSE:By terrible things thou wilt answer us in righteousness,
VERSE:O God of our salvation,
VERSE:Thou that art the confidence of all the ends of the earth,
VERSE:And of them that are afar off upon the sea:
LABEL:6
VERSE:Who by his strength setteth fast the mountains,
VERSE:Being girded about with might;
LABEL:7
VERSE:Who stilleth the roaring of the seas,
VERSE:The roaring of their waves,
VERSE:And the tumult of the peoples.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They also that dwell in the uttermost parts are afraid at thy tokens:
VERSE:Thou makest the outgoings of the morning and evening to rejoice.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Thou visitest the earth, and waterest it,
VERSE:Thou greatly enrichest it;
VERSE:The river of God is full of water:
VERSE:Thou providest them grain, when thou hast so prepared the earth.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Thou waterest its furrows abundantly;
VERSE:Thou settlest the ridges thereof:
VERSE:Thou makest it soft with showers;
VERSE:Thou blessest the springing thereof.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou crownest the year with thy goodness;
VERSE:And thy paths drop fatness.
LABEL:12
VERSE:They drop upon the pastures of the wilderness;
VERSE:And the hills are girded with joy.
LABEL:13
VERSE:The pastures are clothed with flocks;
VERSE:The valleys also are covered over with grain;
VERSE:They shout for joy, they also sing.
