BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:112
TITLE:Psalms 112
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye Jehovah.
VERSE:Blessed is the man that feareth Jehovah,
VERSE:That delighteth greatly in his commandments.
LABEL:2
VERSE:His seed shall be mighty upon earth:
VERSE:The generation of the upright shall be blessed.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Wealth and riches are in his house;
VERSE:And his righteousness endureth for ever.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness:
VERSE:He is gracious, and merciful, and righteous.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Well is it with the man that dealeth graciously and lendeth;
VERSE:He shall maintain his cause in judgment.
LABEL:6
VERSE:For he shall never be moved;
VERSE:The righteous shall be had in everlasting remembrance.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He shall not be afraid of evil tidings:
VERSE:His heart is fixed, trusting in Jehovah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:His heart is established, he shall not be afraid,
VERSE:Until he see his desire upon his adversaries.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He hath dispersed, he hath given to the needy;
VERSE:His righteousness endureth for ever:
VERSE:His horn shall be exalted with honor.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The wicked shall see it, and be grieved;
VERSE:He shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away:
VERSE:The desire of the wicked shall perish.
