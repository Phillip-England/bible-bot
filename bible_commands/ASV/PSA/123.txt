VERSE:Unto thee do I lift up mine eyes,
VERSE:O thou that sittest in the heavens.

VERSE:Behold, as the eyes of servants look unto the hand of their master,

VERSE:So our eyes look unto Jehovah our God,
VERSE:Until he have mercy upon us.
LABEL:3

VERSE:For we are exceedingly filled with contempt.
LABEL:4

VERSE:With the scoffing of those that are at ease,
VERSE:And with the contempt of the proud.
