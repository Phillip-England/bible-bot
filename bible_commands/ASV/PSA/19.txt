BOOK:PSA

TITLE:Psalms 19
LABEL:1
VERSE:The heavens declare the glory of God;
VERSE:And the firmament showeth his handiwork.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Day unto day uttereth speech,
VERSE:And night unto night showeth knowledge.
LABEL:3
VERSE:There is no speech nor language;
VERSE:Their voice is not heard.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their line is gone out through all the earth,
VERSE:And their words to the end of the world.
VERSE:In them hath he set a tabernacle for the sun,
LABEL:5
VERSE:Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his chamber,
VERSE:And rejoiceth as a strong man to run his course.
LABEL:6
VERSE:His going forth is from the end of the heavens,
VERSE:And his circuit unto the ends of it;
VERSE:And there is nothing hid from the heat thereof.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The law of Jehovah is perfect, restoring the soul:
VERSE:The testimony of Jehovah is sure, making wise the simple.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The precepts of Jehovah are right, rejoicing the heart:
VERSE:The commandment of Jehovah is pure, enlightening the eyes.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The fear of Jehovah is clean, enduring for ever:
VERSE:The ordinances of Jehovah are true, and righteous altogether.
LABEL:10
VERSE:More to be desired are they than gold, yea, than much fine gold;
VERSE:Sweeter also than honey and the droppings of the honeycomb.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Moreover by them is thy servant warned:
VERSE:In keeping them there is great reward.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Who can discern his errors?
VERSE:Clear thou me from hidden faults.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins;
VERSE:Let them not have dominion over me:
VERSE:Then shall I be upright,
VERSE:And I shall be clear from great transgression.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart
VERSE:Be acceptable in thy sight,
VERSE:O Jehovah, my rock, and my redeemer.
