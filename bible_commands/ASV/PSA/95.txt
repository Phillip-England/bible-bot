VERSE:Oh come, let us sing unto Jehovah;
VERSE:Let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Let us come before his presence with thanksgiving;
VERSE:Let us make a joyful noise unto him with psalms.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For Jehovah is a great God,
VERSE:And a great King above all gods.
LABEL:4
VERSE:In his hand are the deep places of the earth;
VERSE:The heights of the mountains are his also.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The sea is his, and he made it;
VERSE:And his hands formed the dry land.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Oh come, let us worship and bow down;
VERSE:Let us kneel before Jehovah our Maker:
LABEL:7
VERSE:For he is our God,
VERSE:And we are the people of his pasture, and the sheep of his hand.
VERSE:To-day, oh that ye would hear his voice!
LABEL:8
VERSE:Harden not your heart, as at Meribah,
VERSE:As in the day of Massah in the wilderness;
LABEL:9
VERSE:When your fathers tempted me,
VERSE:Proved me, and saw my work.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Forty years long was I grieved with that generation,
VERSE:And said, It is a people that do err in their heart,
VERSE:And they have not known my ways:
LABEL:11
VERSE:Wherefore I sware in my wrath,
VERSE:That they should not enter into my rest.
