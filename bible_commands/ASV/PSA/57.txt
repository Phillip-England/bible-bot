TITLE:Psalms 57
LABEL:1
VERSE:Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me;
VERSE:For my soul taketh refuge in thee:
VERSE:Yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I take refuge,
VERSE:Until these calamities be overpast.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will cry unto God Most High,
VERSE:Unto God that performeth all things for me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He will send from heaven, and save me,
VERSE:When he that would swallow me up reproacheth; [Selah
VERSE:God will send forth his lovingkindness and his truth.
LABEL:4
VERSE:My soul is among lions;
VERSE:I lie among them that are set on fire,
VERSE:Even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and arrows,
VERSE:And their tongue a sharp sword.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens;
VERSE:Let thy glory be above all the earth.
LABEL:6
VERSE:They have prepared a net for my steps;
VERSE:My soul is bowed down:
VERSE:They have digged a pit before me;
VERSE:They are fallen into the midst thereof themselves. [Selah
LABEL:7
VERSE:My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed:
VERSE:I will sing, yea, I will sing praises.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Awake up, my glory; awake, psaltery and harp:
VERSE:I myself will awake right early.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will give thanks unto thee, O Lord, among the peoples:
VERSE:I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thy lovingkindness is great unto the heavens,
VERSE:And thy truth unto the skies.

VERSE:Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens;
