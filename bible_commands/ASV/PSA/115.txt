TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:115
TITLE:Psalms 115
LABEL:1
VERSE:Not unto us, O Jehovah, not unto us,
VERSE:But unto thy name give glory,
VERSE:For thy lovingkindness, and for thy truth’s sake.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Wherefore should the nations say,
VERSE:Where is now their God?
LABEL:3
VERSE:But our God is in the heavens:
VERSE:He hath done whatsoever he pleased.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their idols are silver and gold,
VERSE:The work of men’s hands.
LABEL:5
VERSE:They have mouths, but they speak not;
VERSE:Eyes have they, but they see not;
LABEL:6
VERSE:They have ears, but they hear not;
VERSE:Noses have they, but they smell not;
LABEL:7
VERSE:They have hands, but they handle not;
VERSE:Feet have they, but they walk not;
VERSE:Neither speak they through their throat.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They that make them shall be like unto them;
VERSE:Yea, every one that trusteth in them.
LABEL:9
VERSE:O Israel, trust thou in Jehovah:
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:10
VERSE:O house of Aaron, trust ye in Jehovah:
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Ye that fear Jehovah, trust in Jehovah:
VERSE:He is their help and their shield.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Jehovah hath been mindful of us; he will bless us:
VERSE:He will bless the house of Israel;
VERSE:He will bless the house of Aaron.
LABEL:13
VERSE:He will bless them that fear Jehovah,
VERSE:Both small and great.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Jehovah increase you more and more,
VERSE:You and your children.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Blessed are ye of Jehovah,
VERSE:Who made heaven and earth.
LABEL:16
VERSE:The heavens are the heavens of Jehovah;
VERSE:But the earth hath he given to the children of men.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The dead praise not Jehovah,
VERSE:Neither any that go down into silence;
LABEL:18
VERSE:But we will bless Jehovah
VERSE:From this time forth and for evermore.
VERSE:Praise ye Jehovah.
