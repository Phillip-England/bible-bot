TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:147
TITLE:Psalms 147
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye Jehovah;
VERSE:For it is good to sing praises unto our God;
VERSE:For it is pleasant, and praise is comely.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Jehovah doth build up Jerusalem;
VERSE:He gathereth together the outcasts of Israel.
LABEL:3
VERSE:He healeth the broken in heart,
VERSE:And bindeth up their wounds.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He counteth the number of the stars;
VERSE:He calleth them all by their names.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Great is our Lord, and mighty in power;
VERSE:His understanding is infinite.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Jehovah upholdeth the meek:
VERSE:He bringeth the wicked down to the ground.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Sing unto Jehovah with thanksgiving;
VERSE:Sing praises upon the harp unto our God,
LABEL:8
VERSE:Who covereth the heavens with clouds,
VERSE:Who prepareth rain for the earth,
VERSE:Who maketh grass to grow upon the mountains.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He giveth to the beast his food,
VERSE:And to the young ravens which cry.
LABEL:10
VERSE:He delighteth not in the strength of the horse:
VERSE:He taketh no pleasure in the legs of a man.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Jehovah taketh pleasure in them that fear him,
VERSE:In those that hope in his lovingkindness.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Praise Jehovah, O Jerusalem;
VERSE:Praise thy God, O Zion.
LABEL:13
VERSE:For he hath strengthened the bars of thy gates;
VERSE:He hath blessed thy children within thee.
LABEL:14
VERSE:He maketh peace in thy borders;
VERSE:He filleth thee with the finest of the wheat.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He sendeth out his commandment upon earth;
VERSE:His word runneth very swiftly.
LABEL:16
VERSE:He giveth snow like wool;
VERSE:He scattereth the hoar-frost like ashes.
LABEL:17
VERSE:He casteth forth his ice like morsels:
VERSE:Who can stand before his cold?
LABEL:18
VERSE:He sendeth out his word, and melteth them:
VERSE:He causeth his wind to blow, and the waters flow.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He showeth his word unto Jacob,
VERSE:His statutes and his ordinances unto Israel.
LABEL:20
VERSE:He hath not dealt so with any nation;
VERSE:And as for his ordinances, they have not known them.
VERSE:Praise ye Jehovah.
