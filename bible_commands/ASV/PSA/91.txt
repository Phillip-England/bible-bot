BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:91
TITLE:Psalms 91
LABEL:1
VERSE:He that dwelleth in the secret place of the Most High
VERSE:Shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.
LABEL:2
VERSE:I will say of Jehovah, He is my refuge and my fortress;
VERSE:My God, in whom I trust.
LABEL:3
VERSE:For he will deliver thee from the snare of the fowler,
VERSE:And from the deadly pestilence.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He will cover thee with his pinions,
VERSE:And under his wings shalt thou take refuge:
VERSE:His truth is a shield and a buckler.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night,
VERSE:Nor for the arrow that flieth by day;
LABEL:6
VERSE:For the pestilence that walketh in darkness,
VERSE:Nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday.
LABEL:7
VERSE:A thousand shall fall at thy side,
VERSE:And ten thousand at thy right hand;
VERSE:But it shall not come nigh thee.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold,
VERSE:And see the reward of the wicked.
LABEL:9
VERSE:For thou, O Jehovah, art my refuge!
VERSE:Thou hast made the Most High thy habitation;
LABEL:10
VERSE:There shall no evil befall thee,
VERSE:Neither shall any plague come nigh thy tent.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For he will give his angels charge over thee,
VERSE:To keep thee in all thy ways.
LABEL:12
VERSE:They shall bear thee up in their hands,
VERSE:Lest thou dash thy foot against a stone.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder:
VERSE:The young lion and the serpent shalt thou trample under foot.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him:
VERSE:I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.
LABEL:15
VERSE:He shall call upon me, and I will answer him;
VERSE:I will be with him in trouble:
VERSE:I will deliver him, and honor him.
LABEL:16
VERSE:With long life will I satisfy him,
VERSE:And show him my salvation.
