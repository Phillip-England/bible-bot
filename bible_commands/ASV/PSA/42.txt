TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:42
TITLE:Psalms 42
LABEL:1
VERSE:As the hart panteth after the water brooks,
VERSE:So panteth my soul after thee, O God.
LABEL:2
VERSE:My soul thirsteth for God, for the living God:
VERSE:When shall I come and appear before God?
LABEL:3
VERSE:My tears have been my food day and night,
VERSE:While they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?
LABEL:4
VERSE:These things I remember, and pour out my soul within me,
VERSE:How I went with the throng, and led them to the house of God,
VERSE:With the voice of joy and praise, a multitude keeping holyday.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
VERSE:And why art thou disquieted within me?
VERSE:Hope thou in God; for I shall yet praise him
VERSE:For the help of his countenance.
LABEL:6
VERSE:O my God, my soul is cast down within me:
VERSE:Therefore do I remember thee from the land of the Jordan,
VERSE:And the Hermons, from the hill Mizar.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Deep calleth unto deep at the noise of thy waterfalls:
VERSE:All thy waves and thy billows are gone over me.
LABEL:8
VERSE:  Yet Jehovah will command his lovingkindness in the day-time;
VERSE:And in the night his song shall be with me,
VERSE:Even a prayer unto the God of my life.
LABEL:9
VERSE:I will say unto God my rock, Why hast thou forgotten me?
VERSE:Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
LABEL:10
VERSE:As with a sword in my bones, mine adversaries reproach me,
VERSE:While they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?
LABEL:11
VERSE:Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
VERSE:And why art thou disquieted within me?
VERSE:Hope thou in God; for I shall yet praise him,
VERSE:Who is the help of my countenance, and my God.
