VERSE:Stormy wind, fulfilling his word;
LABEL:9
VERSE:Mountains and all hills;
VERSE:Fruitful trees and all cedars;
LABEL:10
VERSE:Beasts and all cattle;
VERSE:Creeping things and flying birds;
LABEL:11

VERSE:Princes and all judges of the earth;
LABEL:12
VERSE:Both young men and virgins;

VERSE:Let them praise the name of Jehovah;
VERSE:For his name alone is exalted;
VERSE:His glory is above the earth and the heavens.
