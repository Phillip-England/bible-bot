TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:145
TITLE:Psalms 145
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will extol thee, my God, O King;
VERSE:And I will bless thy name for ever and ever.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Every day will I bless thee;
VERSE:And I will praise thy name for ever and ever.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised;
VERSE:And his greatness is unsearchable.
LABEL:4
VERSE:One generation shall laud thy works to another,
VERSE:And shall declare thy mighty acts.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Of the glorious majesty of thine honor,
VERSE:And of thy wondrous works, will I meditate.
LABEL:6
VERSE:And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts;
VERSE:And I will declare thy greatness.
LABEL:7
VERSE:They shall utter the memory of thy great goodness,
VERSE:And shall sing of thy righteousness.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Jehovah is gracious, and merciful;
VERSE:Slow to anger, and of great lovingkindness.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Jehovah is good to all;
VERSE:And his tender mercies are over all his works.
LABEL:10
VERSE:All thy works shall give thanks unto thee, O Jehovah;
VERSE:And thy saints shall bless thee.
LABEL:11
VERSE:They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom,
VERSE:And talk of thy power;
LABEL:12
VERSE:To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts,
VERSE:And the glory of the majesty of his kingdom.
LABEL:13
VERSE:Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom,
VERSE:And thy dominion endureth throughout all generations.
LABEL:14
VERSE:Jehovah upholdeth all that fall,
VERSE:And raiseth up all those that are bowed down.
LABEL:15
VERSE:The eyes of all wait for thee;
VERSE:And thou givest them their food in due season.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Thou openest thy hand,
VERSE:And satisfiest the desire of every living thing.
LABEL:17
VERSE:Jehovah is righteous in all his ways,
VERSE:And gracious in all his works.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Jehovah is nigh unto all them that call upon him,
VERSE:To all that call upon him in truth.
LABEL:19
VERSE:He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him;
VERSE:He also will hear their cry and will save them.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Jehovah preserveth all them that love him;
VERSE:But all the wicked will he destroy.
LABEL:21
VERSE:My mouth shall speak the praise of Jehovah;
VERSE:And let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever.
