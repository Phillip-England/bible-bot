VERSE:The meek shall hear thereof, and be glad.

VERSE:And let us exalt his name together.
LABEL:4

VERSE:And delivered me from all my fears.

VERSE:And their faces shall never be confounded.

VERSE:And saved him out of all his troubles.

VERSE:And delivereth them.

VERSE:Blessed is the man that taketh refuge in him.
LABEL:9

VERSE:For there is no want to them that fear him.
LABEL:10

VERSE:But they that seek Jehovah shall not want any good thing.
LABEL:11

VERSE:I will teach you the fear of Jehovah.
LABEL:12

VERSE:And loveth many days, that he may see good?

VERSE:And thy lips from speaking guile.
LABEL:14

VERSE:Seek peace, and pursue it.
LABEL:15

VERSE:And his ears are open unto their cry.
LABEL:16

VERSE:To cut off the remembrance of them from the earth.

VERSE:And delivered them out of all their troubles.

VERSE:And saveth such as are of a contrite spirit.
LABEL:19

VERSE:But Jehovah delivereth him out of them all.
LABEL:20

VERSE:Not one of them is broken.

VERSE:And they that hate the righteous shall be condemned.
