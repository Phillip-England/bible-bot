BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:66
TITLE:Psalms 66
LABEL:1
VERSE:Make a joyful noise unto God, all the earth:
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing forth the glory of his name:
VERSE:Make his praise glorious.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Say unto God, How terrible are thy works!
VERSE:Through the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves unto thee.
LABEL:4
VERSE:All the earth shall worship thee,
VERSE:And shall sing unto thee;
VERSE:They shall sing to thy name. [Selah
LABEL:5
VERSE:Come, and see the works of God;
VERSE:He is terrible in his doing toward the children of men.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He turned the sea into dry land;
VERSE:They went through the river on foot:
VERSE:There did we rejoice in him.
LABEL:7
VERSE:He ruleth by his might for ever;
VERSE:His eyes observe the nations:
VERSE:Let not the rebellious exalt themselves. [Selah
LABEL:8
VERSE:Oh bless our God, ye peoples,
VERSE:And make the voice of his praise to be heard;
LABEL:9
VERSE:Who holdeth our soul in life,
VERSE:And suffereth not our feet to be moved.
LABEL:10
VERSE:For thou, O God, hast proved us:
VERSE:Thou hast tried us, as silver is tried.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou broughtest us into the net;
VERSE:Thou layedst a sore burden upon our loins.
LABEL:12
VERSE:Thou didst cause men to ride over our heads;
VERSE:We went through fire and through water;
VERSE:But thou broughtest us out into a wealthy place.
LABEL:13
VERSE:I will come into thy house with burnt-offerings;
VERSE:I will pay thee my vows,
LABEL:14
VERSE:Which my lips uttered,
VERSE:And my mouth spake, when I was in distress.
LABEL:15
VERSE:I will offer unto thee burnt-offerings of fatlings,
VERSE:With the incense of rams;
VERSE:I will offer bullocks with goats. [Selah
LABEL:16
VERSE:Come, and hear, all ye that fear God,
VERSE:And I will declare what he hath done for my soul.
LABEL:17
VERSE:I cried unto him with my mouth,
VERSE:And he was extolled with my tongue.
LABEL:18
VERSE:If I regard iniquity in my heart,
VERSE:The Lord will not hear:
LABEL:19
VERSE:But verily God hath heard;
VERSE:He hath attended to the voice of my prayer.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Blessed be God,
VERSE:Who hath not turned away my prayer,
VERSE:Nor his lovingkindness from me.
