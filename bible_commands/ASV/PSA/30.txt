BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:30
TITLE:Psalms 30
LABEL:1
VERSE:I will extol thee, O Jehovah; for thou hast raised me up,
VERSE:And hast not made my foes to rejoice over me.
LABEL:2
VERSE:O Jehovah my God,
VERSE:I cried unto thee, and thou hast healed me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:O Jehovah, thou hast brought up my soul from Sheol;
VERSE:Thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Sing praise unto Jehovah, O ye saints of his,
VERSE:And give thanks to his holy memorial name.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For his anger is but for a moment;
VERSE:His favor is for a life-time:
VERSE:Weeping may tarry for the night,
VERSE:But joy cometh in the morning.
LABEL:6
VERSE:As for me, I said in my prosperity,
VERSE:I shall never be moved.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou, Jehovah, of thy favor hadst made my mountain to stand strong:
VERSE:Thou didst hide thy face; I was troubled.
LABEL:8
VERSE:I cried to thee, O Jehovah;
VERSE:And unto Jehovah I made supplication:
LABEL:9
VERSE:What profit is there in my blood, when I go down to the pit?
VERSE:Shall the dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth?
LABEL:10
VERSE:Hear, O Jehovah, and have mercy upon me:
VERSE:Jehovah, be thou my helper.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Thou hast turned for me my mourning into dancing;
VERSE:Thou hast loosed my sackcloth, and girded me with gladness;
LABEL:12
VERSE:To the end that my glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent.
VERSE:O Jehovah my God, I will give thanks unto thee for ever.
