CHAPTER:25
TITLE:Psalms 25
LABEL:1
VERSE:Unto thee, O Jehovah, do I lift up my soul.
LABEL:2
VERSE:O my God, in thee have I trusted,
VERSE:Let me not be put to shame;
VERSE:Let not mine enemies triumph over me.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Yea, none that wait for thee shall be put to shame:
VERSE:They shall be put to shame that deal treacherously without cause.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Show me thy ways, O Jehovah;
VERSE:Teach me thy paths.
LABEL:5
VERSE:Guide me in thy truth, and teach me;
VERSE:For thou art the God of my salvation;
VERSE:For thee do I wait all the day.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Remember, O Jehovah, thy tender mercies and thy lovingkindness;
VERSE:For they have been ever of old.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Remember not the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions:
VERSE:According to thy lovingkindness remember thou me,
VERSE:For thy goodness’ sake, O Jehovah.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Good and upright is Jehovah:
VERSE:Therefore will he instruct sinners in the way.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The meek will he guide in justice;
VERSE:And the meek will he teach his way.
LABEL:10
VERSE:All the paths of Jehovah are lovingkindness and truth
VERSE:Unto such as keep his covenant and his testimonies.
LABEL:11
VERSE:For thy name’s sake, O Jehovah,
VERSE:Pardon mine iniquity, for it is great.
LABEL:12
VERSE:What man is he that feareth Jehovah?
VERSE:Him shall he instruct in the way that he shall choose.
LABEL:13
VERSE:His soul shall dwell at ease;
VERSE:And his seed shall inherit the land.
LABEL:14
VERSE:The friendship of Jehovah is with them that fear him;
VERSE:And he will show them his covenant.
LABEL:15
VERSE:Mine eyes are ever toward Jehovah;
VERSE:For he will pluck my feet out of the net.
LABEL:16
VERSE:Turn thee unto me, and have mercy upon me;
VERSE:For I am desolate and afflicted.
LABEL:17
VERSE:The troubles of my heart are enlarged:
VERSE:Oh bring thou me out of my distresses.
LABEL:18
VERSE:Consider mine affliction and my travail;
VERSE:And forgive all my sins.
LABEL:19
VERSE:Consider mine enemies, for they are many;
VERSE:And they hate me with cruel hatred.
LABEL:20
VERSE:Oh keep my soul, and deliver me:
VERSE:Let me not be put to shame, for I take refuge in thee.
LABEL:21
VERSE:Let integrity and uprightness preserve me,
VERSE:For I wait for thee.
LABEL:22
VERSE:Redeem Israel, O God,
VERSE:Out of all his troubles.
