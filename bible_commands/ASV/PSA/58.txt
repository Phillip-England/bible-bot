BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:58
TITLE:Psalms 58
LABEL:1
VERSE:Do ye indeed in silence speak righteousness?
VERSE:Do ye judge uprightly, O ye sons of men?
LABEL:2
VERSE:Nay, in heart ye work wickedness;
VERSE:Ye weigh out the violence of your hands in the earth.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The wicked are estranged from the womb:
VERSE:They go astray as soon as they are born, speaking lies.
LABEL:4
VERSE:Their poison is like the poison of a serpent:
VERSE:They are like the deaf adder that stoppeth her ear,
LABEL:5
VERSE:Which hearkeneth not to the voice of charmers,
VERSE:Charming never so wisely.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Break their teeth, O God, in their mouth:
VERSE:Break out the great teeth of the young lions, O Jehovah.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Let them melt away as water that runneth apace:
VERSE:When he aimeth his arrows, let them be as though they were cut off.
LABEL:8
VERSE:  Let them be as a snail which melteth and passeth away,
VERSE:Like the untimely birth of a woman, that hath not seen the sun.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Before your pots can feel the thorns,
VERSE:He will take them away with a whirlwind, the green and the burning alike.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The righteous shall rejoice when he seeth the vengeance:
VERSE:He shall wash his feet in the blood of the wicked;
LABEL:11
VERSE:So that men shall say, Verily there is a reward for the righteous:
VERSE:Verily there is a God that judgeth in the earth.
