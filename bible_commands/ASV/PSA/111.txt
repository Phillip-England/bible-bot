TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:111
TITLE:Psalms 111
LABEL:1
VERSE:Praise ye Jehovah.
VERSE:I will give thanks unto Jehovah with my whole heart,
VERSE:In the council of the upright, and in the congregation.
LABEL:2
VERSE:The works of Jehovah are great,
VERSE:Sought out of all them that have pleasure therein.
LABEL:3
VERSE:His work is honor and majesty;
VERSE:And his righteousness endureth for ever.
LABEL:4
VERSE:He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered:
VERSE:Jehovah is gracious and merciful.
LABEL:5
VERSE:He hath given food unto them that fear him:
VERSE:He will ever be mindful of his covenant.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He hath showed his people the power of his works,
VERSE:In giving them the heritage of the nations.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The works of his hands are truth and justice;
VERSE:All his precepts are sure.
LABEL:8
VERSE:They are established for ever and ever;
VERSE:They are done in truth and uprightness.
LABEL:9
VERSE:He hath sent redemption unto his people;
VERSE:He hath commanded his covenant for ever:
VERSE:Holy and reverend is his name.
LABEL:10
VERSE:The fear of Jehovah is the beginning of wisdom;
VERSE:A good understanding have all they that do his commandments:
VERSE:His praise endureth for ever.
