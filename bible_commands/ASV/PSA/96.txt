TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:96
TITLE:Psalms 96
LABEL:1
VERSE:Oh sing unto Jehovah a new song:
VERSE:Sing unto Jehovah, all the earth.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Sing unto Jehovah, bless his name;
VERSE:Show forth his salvation from day to day.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Declare his glory among the nations,
VERSE:His marvellous works among all the peoples.
LABEL:4
VERSE:For great is Jehovah, and greatly to be praised:
VERSE:He is to be feared above all gods.
LABEL:5
VERSE:For all the gods of the peoples are idols;
VERSE:But Jehovah made the heavens.
LABEL:6
VERSE:Honor and majesty are before him:
VERSE:Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Ascribe unto Jehovah, ye kindreds of the peoples,
VERSE:Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength.
LABEL:8
VERSE:Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name:
VERSE:Bring an offering, and come into his courts.
LABEL:9
VERSE:Oh worship Jehovah in holy array:
VERSE:Tremble before him, all the earth.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Say among the nations, Jehovah reigneth:
VERSE:The world also is established that it cannot be moved:
VERSE:He will judge the peoples with equity.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice;
VERSE:Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof;
LABEL:12
VERSE:Let the field exult, and all that is therein;
VERSE:Then shall all the trees of the wood sing for joy
LABEL:13
VERSE:Before Jehovah; for he cometh,
VERSE:For he cometh to judge the earth:
VERSE:He will judge the world with righteousness,
VERSE:And the peoples with his truth.
