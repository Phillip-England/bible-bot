TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:12
TITLE:Psalms 12
LABEL:1
VERSE:Help, Jehovah; for the godly man ceaseth;
VERSE:For the faithful fail from among the children of men.
LABEL:2
VERSE:They speak falsehood every one with his neighbor:
VERSE:With flattering lip, and with a double heart, do they speak.
LABEL:3
VERSE:Jehovah will cut off all flattering lips,
VERSE:The tongue that speaketh great things;
LABEL:4
VERSE:Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail;
VERSE:Our lips are our own: who is lord over us?
LABEL:5
VERSE:Because of the oppression of the poor, because of the sighing of the needy,
VERSE:Now will I arise, saith Jehovah;
VERSE:I will set him in the safety he panteth for.
LABEL:6
VERSE:The words of Jehovah are pure words;
VERSE:As silver tried in a furnace on the earth,
VERSE:Purified seven times.
LABEL:7
VERSE:Thou wilt keep them, O Jehovah,
VERSE:Thou wilt preserve them from this generation for ever.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The wicked walk on every side,
VERSE:When vileness is exalted among the sons of men.
