TRANSLATION:ASV
BOOK:PSA
CHAPTER:29
TITLE:Psalms 29
LABEL:1
VERSE:Ascribe unto Jehovah, O ye sons of the mighty,
VERSE:Ascribe unto Jehovah glory and strength.
LABEL:2
VERSE:Ascribe unto Jehovah the glory due unto his name;
VERSE:Worship Jehovah in holy array.
LABEL:3
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah is upon the waters:
VERSE:The God of glory thundereth,
VERSE:Even Jehovah upon many waters.
LABEL:4
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah is powerful;
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah is full of majesty.
LABEL:5
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah breaketh the cedars;
VERSE:Yea, Jehovah breaketh in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
LABEL:6
VERSE:He maketh them also to skip like a calf;
VERSE:Lebanon and Sirion like a young wild-ox.
LABEL:7
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah cleaveth the flames of fire.
LABEL:8
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah shaketh the wilderness;
VERSE:Jehovah shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.
LABEL:9
VERSE:The voice of Jehovah maketh the hinds to calve,
VERSE:And strippeth the forests bare:
VERSE:And in his temple everything saith, Glory.
LABEL:10
VERSE:Jehovah sat as King at the Flood;
VERSE:Yea, Jehovah sitteth as King for ever.
LABEL:11
VERSE:Jehovah will give strength unto his people;
VERSE:Jehovah will bless his people with peace.
